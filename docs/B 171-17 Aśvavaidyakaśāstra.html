Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīraivantāya (!) ||

ataḥ paraṃ tu[[ra]]ṅgānāṃ (!), kathyate vāhane vidhiḥ |

yena te siddhim āyānti karmmayogyā bhavanti ca ||

|| thava nalivo śeḍaṃgayayā vidhi deva ādi revanta dayakayā khaṃ hlaye thva hlāyāthyaṃ yānasā śaḍaṃyā samastaṃ siddhi juyu va || 1 ||

śloka ||

śuklapakṣatrayodaśyāṃ pratipat paṃcamī tathā |

dhṛtiyoge siddhiyoge vāra bhāṣkaraśukrayo (!) || (exp. 1.1–4)

End

❖ triśāraṃ ta gavāṃ (!) kṣīraiḥ kvāthaiv-vatriphalaiḥ pivet |

tridinānta bhavet phoṭaḥ saptāhād vā kilāsake (!) ||

nīlāṃ guṃjā ca kāsīsaṃ dhutturaṃ hanmapāditaṃ (!) |

sūy-yabhaktā ca cāṃgerī piṣṭvā tulyāni repayet (!) ||

sphoṭasthānapraśītyarthasaptarātraṃ punaḥ punaḥ ||

svetakuṣṭaṃ nihanty āsu sādhyāsādhyaṃ na saṃśayaḥ ||

guāphalognicūrṇṇaṃ ca lepitaṃ śvetakuṣtanut |

śilāpāmārgabhasmāpi liptaṃ śvitraṃ vināśayet || athaśvitralepaḥ || (exp. 43:1–4)
