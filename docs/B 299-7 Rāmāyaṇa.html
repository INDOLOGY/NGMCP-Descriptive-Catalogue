Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || || rāmāya namaḥ || ||

gacchatā mātulakulaṃ bharatena tadānaghaḥ |

śatrughno nitya śatru(2)ghno nītaḥ prītipuraskṛtaḥ ||

mātulenāśvapatinā putrasnehena lālitaḥ |

tatrāpi nivasaṃ tau tau tarppamāṇau (3) ca kāmataḥ |

bhrātarau smaratāṃ vīrau vṛddhaṃ daśarathaṃ nṛpaṃ |

rājāpi tau mahātejā sasmāra proṣitau sutau |

ubhau (4) bharata śatrughnau maheṃdravaruṇopamau |

sarva eva tu tasyeṣṭāś catvāraḥ puruṣarṣabhāḥ | (fol. 1v1–4)

End

ukṣiṣṭaṃ (!) vā pramattaṃ vā tāpasaṃ dharmacāriṇaṃ ||

adaṃtyasmin mahāraṇye tān nivāraya rāghava (3) ||

eṣa paṃthā maharṣīṇāṃ phalānyāharatāṃ vane ||

anena tu vanaṃ durgaṃ gaṃtuṃ rāghava te kṣamaṃ ||

itī(4)va taiḥ prāṃjalibhis tapasvibhir

dvijaiḥ kṛta svastyayanaḥ paraṃtapa |

vanaṃ sabhāryaḥ pravi(5)veśa rāghavaḥ

salakṣmaṇaḥ sūrya ivābhramaṃḍalaṃ || ||(fol. 232v2–5)

Colophon

ityārṣe rāmāyaṇe vālmīki(6)nā viracite ayodhyākāṇḍe ekonaviṃśatyuttaraśatatamaḥ sarggaḥ || || saṃvat 17(7)(83) samaya kārttika saptamyāṃ śukravāsare liṣitaṃ gorṣāgrāme narabhūpāla sāha rā(8)jye bhojyamāne śubhaṃ || 2 || rāmakṛṣṇagovindasahāya || || || || || || || (fol. 232v5–8)
