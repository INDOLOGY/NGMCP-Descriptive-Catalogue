Beginning

❖ namo bhagavate vāsudevāya ||

jitas te puṇḍarīkākṣa namas te viśvabhāvana |

namas te mudgavīkeśa(!) mahāpuruṣapūrvaja ||

jitam bhagavatā tena hariṇā lokadhāriṇā |

ajena viśvarūpeṇa nirgguṇena guṇātmanā ||

prāptarājyasya rāmasya rākṣasānāṃ vadhe kṛte |

ājagmur ṛṣayaḥ sarvve rāghavaṃ pratinandituṃ || (fol. 1v)

End

asmin kāvye vālmīkiviracite bālakāṇḍamakāṇḍa-uttarakāṇḍās(!) trayaḥ kāṇḍā bhavanti | sargāṇām ekacatvāriṃśadadhikāni pañcaśatāni aṃkato pi ślokaḥ || 541 ||

prakīrṇṇakopākhyānika uttarakāṇḍa iti || ||

karaṇakaṃ javikāsanabhāskaraḥ(!) sa khalu lekhayati sma jinaṃ sudhī(!) |

daśarathātmajacārukathāṃ mudā sacivaviṣṇuhariḥ suhidāṃ(!) hitaḥ || ||

munibānarase varṣay āṣāḍhamāsi pāṇḍare |

kāme gurau rāmakathāṃ lekhasiddhi (!) tadābhavat || (fol. 157v)
