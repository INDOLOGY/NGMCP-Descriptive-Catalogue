Excerpts

Beginning

śrīguruprasanna ||

maunaṃ vyākhyāna kaṭitaparabrāhmītatvaṃ yuvānaṃ ||

varaśiṣṭhāṃ teva sadṛsigaṇair āvṛtaṃ brahmaniṣṭhaiḥ ||

ācāreṃdraṃkaragalitaṃ cinmudrāmānaṃdamūrttiṃ ||

svātmārāmaṃ munītavadanaṃ dakṣiṇāmūrtim iḍe || 1 ||

viśvaṃ darpaṇadṛṣyamānanagarītulyaṃ nijāṃtargataṃ

paśyan nātmanī māyayā bahir īvo bhūtaṃ yathā nidrayā ||

yaḥ sākṣāt kurute prabodhasamaye svātmānam otmavādvayaṃ ||

tasmai śrīgurumūrtaye nama idaṃ śrīdakṣiṇāmūrttaye || 2 || (fol. 1r1–10)

End

vaṭaviṭatisamīpe bhūmibhāge niṣaṇṇam ||

sakalamunijanānāṃ jñānadātāram ārāt ||

tribhūvanagurum īśaṃ dakṣiṇāmūrttidevaṃ ||

jananamaraṇadukhacchedadakṣaṃ namāmi || 12 || (fol. *2v11–13, exp. 4)

Colophon

iti śrīmatparamahaṃsaparivrājakācāryaśrīśaṃkarācāryaviracitaṃ śrīdakṣiṇāmūrtistotram saṃpūrṇaṃ || (fol. *2v13–14, exp. 4)
