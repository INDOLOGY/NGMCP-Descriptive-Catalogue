Excerpts

Beginning

oṁ nama[ḥ] nṛtyanāthāya || navarasāya nama[ḥ] ||


śṛ[ñ]gārahāsyakaruna(!)raudravīrabhayānakā[ḥ] |

vibhatsādbhutaśāntāś ca na navanāṭyarasā[ḥ] smṛtāḥ || ||


śṛ[ñ]gārāj jāyate hāso raudāś ca karunā(!) rasaḥ |

bhayānako ʼpi vibhatso †dvirād† utpadyate ʼdbhutaḥ || (fol. 1v1–4)


End

rakāraṃ tv aśivaṃ vidyāt gakāraṃ saktim ucyate ||

śivasak(!)tisamāyuktaṃ ragarūpa(!) namo ʼstu te ||


rāgarūpa[ṃ] paraṃ brhma rāgarūpa[ṃ] nirañjanaṃ |

rāgarūpa[ṃ] brahmā viṣṇu rāgarūa sadāśiva || ||


nakāraṃ pavanaṃ proktaṃ ekāraṃ kamalāsanaṃ

nakāraṃ naṃdikeśaṃ ca ākaraṃ madhusūdanaṃ || ||


nṛtyadevasamāyukta na jānāti mati[ṃ] kuruḥ(!)

vedānāṃ prāpyate svarggaṃ nṛtyaṃ bhaktiparāṃ gati[ḥ] || || (fol. 6r4–6v2)
