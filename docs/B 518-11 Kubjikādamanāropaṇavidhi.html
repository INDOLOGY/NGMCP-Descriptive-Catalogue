Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṁ namaḥ caṇḍikāyai ||


kṛtayuge hemasūtrāni(!) tretāyāṃ rajatas tathā |

dvāpare māsaśūtrāṇi(!) kastākaryāsam(!) eva ca ||


oṁkāraṃ prathamaṃ tantu dvitīyaṃ somadevatā |

tṛtīyaṃ vahnidaivatvaṃ caturtha[ṃ] brahmadevatā ||


pañcame nāgadaivatvaṃ ṣaṣṭhamaṃ ca śikhidhvajaṃ(!) |

saptamaṃ sūryyadevatvaṃ aṣṭhamaṃ ca sadāśivaṃ || (fol. 4t1–4)


End

japahomārccane hiīnaṃ kṛtanitya(!) mayā tava |

akṛtaṃ vācyahīnañ ca tan nyūrṇa(!) parameśvarī ||


dhanadhānyasūtaṃ dharmmakīrttim āyur yaśaśriyaṃ ||

turaṃgān danti‥ putrāt kubjikāyā(!) prayacchame || ||


atra gandhādi || vindūtrayaṃ 3 || ekāny ekatyādi(!) || nyādisvivalivisarjanaṃ || ācamanādisūryyārghaḥ

|| candanāi āśīrvvāde viya || jathmaktisamayabhojyaṃ || dakṣīṇā(!) || (exp. 20b9–21t4)


Colophon

iti pavitraprakṣālaṇavidhiḥ samāptaḥ || atha pavitra(!) (exp. 21t4–5)
