❖ oṃ nao brahmaṇe namaḥ ||


tato viṣṇupratiṣṭhādinayajñasuva(rṇakalaśa)dhvajāvarohanavidhi likhyate ḥ ||


divasānukrameṇa aṣṭamātṛkā ārādhanaṃ || hṅathu kuhnu yajamāna ādi samasta kuṭumbādi snāna


vasta hele rusi pāya mālaḥ || || brāhaṃaṇa yajamāna daivajña ācārya yayo(!)dakavidhithyeṃ yāya ḥ ||


śatabhidīkāvāṅāvatāthe || kalasa cosyaṃ tāthe thaṃḍila cosyaṃ tāthe ekabhakta yāya mālaḥ || thvate


hṅathu kuhnuyā vidhiḥ || || (exp. 3B1–13)
