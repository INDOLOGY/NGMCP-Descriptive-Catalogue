«Beginning»


śrīgaṇeśāye(!) namoḥ(!) ||

atha gocaraphalajātakābharaṇe〈ḥ〉 || ||

nṛjanmarāśeḥ khacarai[ḥ] pracārair

yad gocaraṃ sāṃhitikaiḥ praṇītam ||

sthūlaphala(!) tat kilama(!) pravacmi

bālāvabodapradam abhragānām || 1 ||


gatir bhayaṃ śrīr vyasanaṃ ca dainyaṃ

śatrukṣayo yānam ati(!)va pi(!)ḍā

kāntikṣayo bhīṣṭaviśiṣṭasiddhir

labdhir vyayo rkasya phalakrameṇa〈ḥ〉 || 2 || (fol. 1v1–4)


«End»

kaṭipradeśe jaghane ca jīvaḥ

kaviṣṇuguhe sthalam utkayugme

janur udeśe nalini śasūnuś

cāreṇa vā janmani vīttanīyaḥ || 9 ||


yadā yadā syāt pratikulavarttī

khāṅge svadoṣeṇa karoti pi(!)ḍām

īdaṃ tu pūrvaṃ pravicārya sarvaṃ

praśnaprasūtyādiṣu kalpe(!)nīyam || 10 || (fol. 2v5–8)


«Colophon»

ī(!)tyaṣṭakavargaphalānī(!) || (fol. 2v8)
