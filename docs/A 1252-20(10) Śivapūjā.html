Excerpts

Beginning

atha śivapūjanaṃ

oṃ harāya nama mṛdāharaṇaṃ

oṃ maheśvarāya nama saṃghaṭanaṃ

oṃ śaṃbhave nama prati(2)ṣṭhāpanaṃ

oṃ śūlapāṇaye nama āvāhanaṃ

oṃ pinākapāṇaye nama ihā gaccha (a)ha tiṣṭa paśupa(3)taye nama (exp. 97t, ll. 1-3)

End

bhīmāya ākāsamūrttaye paśupataye yajamānamūrttaye

mahādevā(5)ya somamūrttaye nama īśānāya sūryamūrttaye nama

haro maheśvaraśaṃbhū ⟪⟫(6)śūlapāṇi pinākadhṛk śivaḥ paśupatiś caiva

mahādeveti visarjjanaṃ (exp. 97b, ll. 4-6)

Colophon

iti śivapūjanaṃ || (exp. 97b6)
