Manuscript Features

This text has been written by two different hands. After 46v, there is one half-damaged folio may be related to some Yogasūtravyākhā.

Sāṅkhyasūtravṛtteḥ pustakam idaṃ śrīdaivajñakeśaripaṇḍitasya || has been written on 1r.

Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

vināyakaṃ namaskṛtya viṣṇuṃ sūryyaṃ sarasvatīṃ ||
lakṣmīṃ gaṃgāṃ maheśānaṃ vṛttiṃ karttuṃ samārabhe || 1 ||

puruṣaḥ khalu vairāgyā(2)n mokṣasādhanaśāstre pravarttate |
vairāgyaṃ ca dvidhā syāt | śokādinā vā janmāntarīyaduritakṣayād vā | tathā ca śrutiḥ yad ahar eva virajet tad ahar eva (3) pravrajed iti | śāstre pravṛttañ ca
paramavairāgyān mucyate | tathāha pataṃjaliḥ | dṛṣṭānuśravikaviṣayavitṛṣnasya (!) vaśīkārasaṃjñā vairā(4)gyaṃ | (fol. 1v1–4)

End

yad vā tad veti karmakṣayād vā jñānād vā anyato (vadeti) svasvāmibhāvocchityā saṃsārocchittiḥ puruṣārthaḥ | taducchittiḥ puruṣārtha iti vīpsādhyāyaparisamāptau ||

(7) eṣa satkārasiddhāntaḥ sarva eva nirūpitaḥ |
tantrataḥ pratipāditvān nahi syāt punar uktitā || 1 ||    || (fol. 46v6–7)

Colophon

iti śrīkāpilasāṅkhyapravacanasūtravṛttau ṣaṣṭho dhyāyaḥ | (8) samāpteyaṃ sāṅkhyasūtravṛttiḥ ||    ||

vidhuvedamunīndrasammite śakakāle tapasi sphuṭe dale |
jvalanasya tithau raver dine likhanam (!) pūrṇam abhūd idan drutam || 1 śrīkṛṣṇa. (fol. 46v7–8)
