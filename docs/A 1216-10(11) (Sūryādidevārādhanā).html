mohāndhakāra maganānāṃ ja(exp.109b1)nānāṃ jñānalakṣmībhiḥ
kṛtamudharaṃ yena tannaumi suryyabhāskaraṃ || 1 || (2) ||
yo sokṣirāmbu madhya, śvapati jalanidhau |
nāgaparyaṃka bhogaiḥ (3) śeṣasyāya phanāgre jvalati phaṇimani,
vighate svāsavāte, brahmā(4)dyaistuyamāne, tridaśamunigaṇai jhagjajuśama mantraṃ viṣṇu trailokya nā(5)thaṃ kaluṣalukaharaṃ, pātu māṃ padmanābhaṃ || 2 ||
myarukāñcana datvānāṃ, gavā(exp.110t1)koṭi satyarapiḥ |
