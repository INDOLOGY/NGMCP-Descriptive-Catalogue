Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namo gaṇeśāya ||

ratināthacakravartī

nicitya kavipriyām etāṃ |

kavitāchandaḥśikṣāṃ

nānā(2)prekṣāvatāṃ vācaḥ ||

mukundakaṃdarppaguṇena rūpake,

kāvyadvayaṃ vikramarāmayor guṇaiḥ |

kṛtvā ma(3)hānāṭakapadyam ātmanaḥ

sapadyagadyaiḥ prababandha yaḥ kaviḥ ||

khalānalaṃ prasādya(tā)munā sa(4)tām avipriyā |

nigadyate kavipriyā vipaścitā kavipriyā ||

rītiḥ śarīraṃ kavitāramaṇyā

(5) bhūṣāvidhiṃ yāṃ hy upamādivācaḥ |

imāṃ samantād rasanā hi dūtī

yathāguṇaṃ saṃghaṭayet sadasya (!) (6)|| (fol.1v1–6)

End

†rāvaṇāṃgulyachadanaṃ (2) śatruyajñād viyojanaṃ |

sahasraṃ jānhavībabhruśeṣaśīṣāmbujacchadāḥ† |

†ravikeraṇā(3)rjunakaravedaśākhendratṛṣṇayaḥ† |

ratināthakaver iti priyātanayeva hi yā ka(4)vipriyā |

kavibhāvavidhiprakāśakaś caramaḥ kathitas tadarbhakaḥ ||  ❁  || (fol.83r1–4)

Colophon

iti śrīratināthacakravarttikṛtā kavipriyā samāptā ||   ||
