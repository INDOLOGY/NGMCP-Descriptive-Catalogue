Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

atredam avadheyaṃ śrībhāgavataṃ nā[mā]ṣṭādaśapurāṇāntargataṃ kiṃtv atiriktaṃ vedāṣṭādaśapurāṇamahābhārataracayitur apy ajātacittaprasādaprasattijanakatvāt tathā hi sūta uvāca

dvāpare samanuprāpte tṛtīye yugaparyayo

jātaḥ parāśarād ye(!)gī vyāsavyāṃ kalayā hareḥ

sa kadācit sarasvatyā upaspṛśya jalaṃ śuciḥ

vivikta eka āsīna udite ravimaṃḍale

parāvarajñaḥ sa ṛṣiḥ kālenāvyaktaraṃhasā

yugadharmavyatikaraṃ prāptaṃ bhuvi yuge yuge

bhautikānāṃ ca bhāvānāṃ śaktihrāśaṃ ca tatkṛtaṃ

aśraddadhānān nisatvān durmedhān hrasitāyuṣaḥ

durbhagāṃś ca janān vīkṣya munir divyena cakṣuṣā (fol. 1v1–7)


End

ata eva janmādyasya yata iti ślokāvatāraṇikāgraṃthadarśanena śrīdharasvāminām ayam evāśayaḥ pratibhāti avatāraṇagraṃthas tu atha nānāpurāṇaśāstraprabaṃdhaiś cittapraśāṃtim alabhamānas tatra tatrāparituṣyan nāradopadeśataḥ śrīmadbhagavadguṇavarṇanapradhānaṃ śrībhāgavataśāstraṃ prāripsur vedavyāsas tatparipādya paradevatānusmaraṇalakṣaṇamaṃgalam ācaratīti dik (fol. 5v4–8)


Colophon

iti śrītarkavāgīśabhaṭṭācāryaveṇīdattaviracitaṃ śrībhāgavataparākramaprakaraṇaṃ upādhyāyasatyadhareṇa saṃpāditam etat śubham śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ (fol. 5r8–9)
