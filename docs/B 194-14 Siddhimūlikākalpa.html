Manuscript Features

Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrībhavānīśaṃkarābhyāṃ namaḥ ||

śrīśaṃkararuguruṃ natvā audyanārthanavikramāt(!) |
sādhakānāṃ hitārthāya nṛpānāṃ(!) jayadāyakaṃ || 1 ||
sādhanaṃ vividhaś caiva vidyāviṃducitanmate(!) |
kalpamudrādhanaprāpti(!) guṭikātilakāñjanaṃ || 2||
pādukākhaḍgavetālavīrasādhanapūrvakam ||
kṛtvā gehe śmaśāne vā nirbhaye ranam(!) āviśet || 3 ||
asādhyaṃ tasya vai nāsti śatru(!) kāpi(!) mahītale || ||
śrīdevyauvāca(!) || (fol. 1r1–6)

End

nāsti tasmāt paraṃ lokaṃ siddhimūlamahauṣadhīṃ ||
pratyak(!)bhūtāmṛtaṃ dīvyaṃm(!) idaṃ stotuṃ na śakyate |
siddhamūlamukhe yasya bhuktimukti bhaved dhruvaṃ ||
caitanyasphuṭitaṃ yasya hṛdaye harayel labhet |
īdṛśaḥ paramaṃ kalpakalpānāṃ sārasaṃgrahā ||
satyam uktaṃ mahādevis(!) tava snehād idaṃ vaca(!) || (fol. 13v5-14r3)

Colophon

iti parākrame saṃgraha || sāre atirahasye śivahṛdayasthite samastasandeho(!)prasamane kanyādhipāvatāre citāmbaraciravivaraṇe siddhamūlikākalpaṃ samāptaṃ || || śivaṃ || || śrīmahācaṇḍikāyai namaḥ || (fol. 14r3–5)

Beginning

śrīmahācaṇḍikāyai namaḥ || atha saṃvitsvīkaraṇavidhiḥ tatra śrīguruṃ pañcamudrābhiḥ praṇamya asya śrīsaṃvinmayī bālāmantrasya ānandabhairava ṛṣiḥ śirasi || paṃkti chanda mukhe || saṃvinmayī śrīvārādevatā hṛdaye || aiṃ bījaṃ nābhau hrīṃ śaktiḥ guhye || śrīṃ kīlakaṃ pādayoḥ || mama sveṣṭadevatāṅgatvena || (fol. 14r5-14v3)

End

uttare || tatas tattvacatuṣṭayai ||

oṃ aiṁ ātmatattvaṃ śodhayāmi namaḥ svāhā || oṃ klīṁ vidyātattvaṃ śodhayāmi namaḥ svāhā ||
oṃ sauḥ śivatattvaṃ śodhayāmi namaḥ svāhā || oṃ aiṁ klīṁ sauḥ sarvatattvaṃ śodhayāmi namaḥ svāhā ||
iti svīkṛtya punaḥ ṣaḍaṅgaṃ vidhāya pañcamudrāṃbhi(!) praṇamya kṛtāñjali paṭhet ||

saṃvid devī garīyasī guṇamayī vaiguṇyavidhvaṃsinī ||
māyāmohamadāndhatākārasamanī tāpatrayonmūlinī || (!)
vāgdevī vadanāmbujeṣu rasanāsaṃbodhanī dīpikā ||
brahmajñānavivekasiṃdhuvijayī vijñānamūrtyai namaḥ || || (fol. 15v5–16r6)

Colophon

iti vijayākalpe saṃvitsvīkaraṇamantranyāsadhyānavidhiḥ samāptaḥ || śubhaṃ || mantranyāsavidhiḥ || (fol. 16r6–16v1)
