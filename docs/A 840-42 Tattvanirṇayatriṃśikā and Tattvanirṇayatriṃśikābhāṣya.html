Excerpts

Beginning of the root text

śrīḥ śaivagranthaḥ śivabhairavakṛtatriṃśikā vimalānandakṛtabhāṣyasahitā || oṁ namaḥ paradevatāyai ||

ādimadhyāntarahitā ādimadhyāntavarttinī
yā vastubhāvarahitā tāṃ vande śakticinmayīm || 1 ||

asty eva sā parā sūkṣmā sūṣmatvān nopalabhyate |
jñaptimātrā parā sūkṣmā cittacetāntarasthitā (fol. 1v6–7)

Beginning of the commentary

prathamaṃ tāvacchāstrasyādau namaskāro vyākhyāmate | ādiś ca madhyaś ca antaś ca te bhavanti ādimadhyāntās tair ādimadhyāntai rahitā vinirmuktā yā sā ādimadhyāntarahitā | tatra ādiśabdena sṛṣṭiḥ, madhyaśabdena sthitiḥ, antaśabdena pralayaḥ |(fol. 1v8–10)

End of the root text

gamāgamavinirmuktaṃ paripūrṇaṃ taducyate ||
paripūrṇasya tatvasya bharitāvastha ucyate || 25 ||

evaṃ hi bhairavyokteṣu śivoktaśaktiśāsane ||
nirṇītaṃ śīvasadbhāvamanmathā naiva mucyati || 28 ||

siddhānteṣv atha vāmadakṣiṇapathe pūrvā ʼpare śāsane
nirṇītaṃ śivabhairaveṇa prabhuṇā maccātisūkṣme śive ||

taccaivātiśayena nirṇayam iha jñātvā kṛtā triṃśikā
sarvānām anukampanāya vimalānandena bhāṣyo ʼpi ca || (fol. 13v22–23, 26–27, 35–38)

End of the commentary

evaṃ śabdena anena prakāreṇa bhairavokteṣu tantreṣu śivoktaśivaśāsane śuddhaśaiveṣu ca śāktaśāsane śāktaśabdena pūrvānvayapaścimānvaya uttarānvayeṣu nirṇitaṃ śivasadbhāvaṃ purvoktena pramāṇapadayogena nirṇītaṃ śivasadbhāvaṃ tasmādbiaravokte śivokte śāktaśāktaśāsaneṣv api nirmathya teṣāṃ śāstrāṇām anumatiṃ jñātvā asti triṃśikākhyeṣv api pramāṇapadayogena sūṣmaśivasya nirṇayaṃ vyākhyātam evaṃ vinā bhiravokte śāktaśāktaśāsane mokṣāṃ na bhaved yadi mokṣaṃ na bhavet tadā na vastavyam iti || 28 || (fol. 13v28–34)

Colophon

iti tatvanirṇayatriṃśāikākhyā bhāṣyena saha samāptā | (fol. 13v38)
