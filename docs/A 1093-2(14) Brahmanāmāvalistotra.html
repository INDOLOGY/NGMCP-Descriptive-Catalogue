Manuscript Features

On the exp. 57 is available a 2 pictures of the śrīyantra and is written 4 stanzas quoted from śrīmadbhāgavat

śrībhagavān uvāca ||

jñānaṃ paramaguhyaṃ te yad vijñānasamanvitaṃ ||
sa rasyaṃ tad aṅgaṃ ca gṛhāṇa gaditaṃ mayā ||

…

After the colophon is available more few stanzas

vedāṃtatarkkoktibhir āgamaiś ca ||
nānā vidhaiḥ śāstrakadambakaiś ca ||

dhyānādibhiḥ satkaraṇair na gamyaṃ ||
…

Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||    ||

sakṛc chravaṇamātreṇa brahmajñānaṃ yatokṣavet (!) ||
brahamanāmāva(2)līmālā sarveṣāṃ mokṣasiddhaye || 1 ||

asaṅgo ham asaṅgo ham asaṅgo haṃ punaḥ punaḥ ||
sac ci(3)d ānandarūpo ham aham evāham avyayaḥ || 2 ||

nityaṃ śuddha vimukto haṃ nirākāro ham avyayaḥ ||
(4) bhūmānandasvarūpo ham ahaṃ evāham avyayaḥ || 3 || (exp. 57,fol. 52r1–4)

End

brahma satyaṃ jagan mithyā jīvo brahmai(6)va nāparaḥ ||
anena vedyaṃ sac chāstrād iti veḍāṃtaḍiṃḍimaḥ || 21 ||

ahaṃ jyotir bahir jyotiḥ pratya(7)g jyotiḥ parātparaḥ ||
jyotijr jyotiḥ svayaṃ jyotir ātmajyotiḥ śivo smy ahaṃ || 22 || (exp. 58, fol. 52v5–7)

Colophon

iti śrīśaṃ(8)karācāryakṛtau brahmanāmāvalīstotram ||    || (fol. 52v7–8)
