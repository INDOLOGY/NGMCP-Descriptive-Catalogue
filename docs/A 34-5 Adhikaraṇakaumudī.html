Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namaḥ śrīkṛṣṇāya ||

purāṇam ajaraṃ devaṃ kāmadaṃ kāmavairiṇam |

svanidānaṃ nidānan taṃ yoginaṃ bhoginaṃ numaḥ ||

āste deva smaraparavaśasmeratāruṇyanṛtyat-

prauḍhottaṅsastanabharanamatkāminīkāmakeliḥ |

yuṣmatsvairāparavaśamanāḥ kiṃ jagannāthabhāvam

bhāvībhāvañ caraṇakamale tāvake māmakīnaḥ ||

dharmmaśāstre ʼdhikaraṇaṃ vicāreṣūpakārakam |

viduṣā devanāthena nirbbandhena vitanyate ||

parisaṃkhyāniyamayos tathā vidhyanuvādayoḥ |

bhedakaṃ kathyate samyak paryyudāsaniṣedhayoḥ ||

prasaṅgatantrayor bhedas tatsāhityasahatvayoḥ |

śrutiliṅgādibhedañ ca teṣām eva balābalam || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

tad evaṃ

ratnākare kalpatarau śrīdata(!)raviṇā yayoḥ |

vākpatau ni

haracaraṇarajasvī bālabhāve yaśasvī

taruṇimani tapasvī sarvvakāle yaśasvī |

vividiṣati budho yaṃ dīpayāmāsa tasminn

adhikaraṇakadambaiṣ kaumudīr ddevanāthaḥ ||

somabhaṭṭopadiṣṭena pathā saṃsarato dhunā |

mīmāṃsāviṣamāraṇye tāraṇaṃ mama bhāratī || (fol. 52r–v2)

Colophon

iti mahāmahopādhyāyatarkapañcānanaṭhakkuraśrīdevanāthakṛtādhikaraṇakaumudī samāptā || ○ ||

vedabāhurasakṣmābhir lakṣite śakavatsare |

lilekha locanaḥ svārthe-m-etan mārggādime hani || (fol. 52v2–3)
