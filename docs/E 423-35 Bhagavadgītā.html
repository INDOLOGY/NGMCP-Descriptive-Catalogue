Manuscript Features

The available folios are 1–13, 26–56.

In the beginning of the text there is pūjāvidhi. The text is not complete in the middle. The text reaches up to the 12th śloka of third adhyāya. In fol. 13r and 26v there is last part of 7th adhyāya. 8 to 18 adhyāya is completely available.

Half parts of fols. 12v–13r and 26v–27r are cut.
