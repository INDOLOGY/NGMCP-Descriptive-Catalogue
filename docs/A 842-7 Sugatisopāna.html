Manuscript Features

The last stanza (33v10) of the text states that the text was expanded by Hākū Śukla.

ādau gaṃgātī[r]thamaraṇaphalam

daṃpatīvṛṣotsarganirṇayaḥ

…

b Mahārājādhirāja Gaṇeśvara; A 1281/17

There are two exposures of fols. 27v–28r.

Excerpts

Beginning

oṁ mahāviṣṇave namaḥ ||

oṃ ||

vedasmṛtipurāṇādi dvṛṣṭvā lokahitaiṣiṇā ||

kṛtaṃ sugatisopānaṃ śrīgaṇeśvaramantriṇā ||

gaṅgāsmaraṇaṃ narakādaśarnaphalam |

gaṅgāgaṇṅeti kīrtanaṃ sarvapāpavimuktipūrvakarudraloakagamanaphalam | janma-tritayārjitapāpakṣayaphalaṃ vā | gaṅājalekṣaṇapāpadhvaṃsapūrvakadīptiphalam | (fol. 1v1–2)

End

tato vaiśvadevabalyanantaraṃ ca saptaprabhṛtiyathāśaktibrāhmaṇān bhojayitvā jñātibhiḥ saha svayaṃ bhuñjīta ||    || 

idaṃ sugatisopānaṃ śrīgaṇeśvaranirmitam |

saṃpūrṇaṃ pustakaṃ yātaṃ hākūśuklena vistṛtam ||    || (fol. 33v9–10)

Colophon

iti saprakriyamahāsaṃdhivigrahikaṭhakkuradevādityātmajamahāmahatukamahā-sāmantādhipatimahārājādhirājaśrīgaṇeśvarakṛtaṃ sugatisopānam idaṃ sampūrṇam ||     || śubham ||     ||     ||     ||     || (fol. 33v10–11)
