Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namo mahāgaṇeśāya ||

niṣpratyūham upāsmahe bhagavataḥ kaumodakīlakṣmaṇaḥ

kokaprīticako[[ra]]pāraṇāpaṭū jyotiṣmatīlocane |

yābhyām arddhavibodhamugdha(2)madhuraśrīr arddhanidrāyito,

nābhīpalvalapuṇḍarīkasukulaṣ (!) †kamboḥ sapatnīkṛtaḥ† ||

api ca ||

viramati mahākalpe nābhīpathaikaniketanas

tribhuvanapuraḥ śilpī (3) yasya pratikṣaṇam ātmabhūḥ ||

kim adhikaraśā (!) kīdṛt kasya vyavasthitir ityasā

vudaram aviśa (!) draṣṭuṃ tasmai jagannidhaye namaḥ ||

nāndyante sūtradhāraḥ || 2 || alam ativistareṇa (4) bho bho lavaṇodavelāvanālītamālakandalasya (!) tribhuvanamaulimaṇḍalamahānīlamaṇeḥ kamalākucakalaśakelikasturikāpatrāṅkurasya, bhagavataḥ śrīpuruṣottamasya (5) yātrā(yām) upasthānīyāḥ (!) sabhāsadaḥ kutasścid viṣādāgate kalahakandalanāmnā kuśīlavena, raudrabībhatsabhayānakādbhutabhūyiṣṭhaṃ kam api prabandham abhinayatā ni(6)tyaṃ kilāyaṃ udvejito lokaḥ, tat kasyacid abhimatarasabhāvātiśayabhājaḥ preṅkhaṇakasya prayogānujñayā nāṭyavedopādhyāyabahurūpā[[syāṃ]]ntevāsī (!) madhyadeśīyaḥ su(2r1)carito nāma bharataputro ham anugṛhye | (fol. 1v1–2r1)

End

api ca ||

devasyā[[t]]mabhuvaḥ kamaṇḍalujalasrotāṃsi maṃdākinī-

gaṅgābhogavatīmayāni punate yāvat trilokīm imām (6) |

tāvad vīrayaśorasāyanamadhusyandaḥkavīnām ayaṃ

jāgarnu śrutiśaṣkulivalayitavyomāvagahī guṇaḥ ||

vāsiṣṭhaḥ evam astv iti || || (7) iti niṣkrāntās sarvve (fol. 100v5–7)

Colophon

nāyakanando nāma saptamo ʼṅkaḥ || 7 || || || || (fol. 10v7)
