Beginning

etām eva purā dhyātvā vidyātrailokyamohinīṃ ||

trailokyaṃ mohayāmāsa kāmārīṃ(!) bhagavān hariḥ || 1 ||

End

lobhān mohāc ca garvād vā vaidyuṣyād vā prakāśayet ||

Colophon

śrī ||

adhāre taruṇārkkabimbaruciraṃ hemaprabham vāgbhavaṃ

vījaṃ manmatham indragopasadṛśaṃ hṛtpaṅkaje saṃsthitaṃ ||

randhre brahmapadasthaśaktim aparaṃ somaprabhāṃ bhāsvaraṃ

ye dhyāyanti padatrayaṃ tava śive te yānti mokṣaṃ padaṃ || 1 ||    ||     ||

pañca || praṇava(!) ||
