mohanī namaḥ ||

vauṃ puṣpaṃ nama ||

kṣatrābali paṭhet ||

oṃ namaḥ si(2)ddhimahākālikāyai

trailokyāyai namaḥstre, tribhūvana namite, kālike viśvacaṇḍe,

vā(3)me vāmāntarastha, tridaśavara nūte, naumite pādayugme |

hratsvadīrghaviśrargge akacata(4)ṭapayaśekṣaḥ prakāśāntarasthe,

mūṇḍe hūṃ viśvarūpe matiparadaradeśe ṣatāgotta(5)rīya | (fol. 6r, ll. 1-5)

iti (4) śrīsiddhilakṣmīmate prakāśe jayadrathe vidyāpīṭhe pratyaṃgiryyākṣetrābali pīṭhastavaṃ (5) samāptaṃ || || (fol. 7v3-5)

saṃhāramūdrāna bali visarjjanaṃ || ||

bali choya vidhi thva || dathupāta kvāche ||

maṅga(5)laco gaṇeśa || kvāche nāśvara || anantaliṅgako bhairava ||
