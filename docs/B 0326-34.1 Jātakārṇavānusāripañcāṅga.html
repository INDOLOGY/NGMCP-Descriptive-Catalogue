«Beginning»


śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

śrīsuryyāya namaḥ ||

śrīsāradādevyai namaḥ || ||


nepālabhākhāna jātakārṇṇavana paṃcā(2)ga jyāya ||


kalyādyabdagaṇa prabhākarahataḥ caitrādimāsair yyutaḥ

tristhaḥ khādrihṛtāptaptayuk surahṛtai labdhādhi(3)māsai yutaḥ ||

khatrighraḥsa tithidvidhāśivahataḥ trivyāmaśailod dhṛtai,

hīnolabdhadināvamai śitaniśārddhaśrā(4)vaṇo aharggaṇaḥ || ||


kaligatābda taya 12 na guṇa yāya patrā jyāyagū caitra śukla pratipadākuhnu jyāya(5) jusā ganemvā caitrādi māsana, svathāsa taya,

kvasa coṃgulīsa 70 ne bhāga kāyā labdha, dathutaṃsa tayāgulīsa tane (6) thukīsa 33 na bhāga kayā laṃbdha adhimāsa dha

kaligatābdayā kvasa coyā taya thva adhimāna cosa cogulīsa ta(7)nā biya, thvakīsa 30 guṇa yāya, tithina gane caitra śukla pratipadāyā

jyānāgu ju tithina ne mvāo 30 guṇa yānā(8)gulīsa nithā taya chagū chasa coṃgulī 11 guṇa yāya 703 na bhāga kāya, labdha ogūoma dīna

dhaka adhimāsayā(9) kvasaṃ coyātaya thvaoma dinanaṃ cosa coṃgulīsa pāda yānā biya, pāda yānā śeṣa arddharātrīyā aharggaṇa

jula(1)oma dinayā kvasaṃ aharggaṇa dhakaṃ coyā taya thva aharggaṇasa 7 na bhāga kāya śeṣa śukravāraṃnisyaṃ niya vāra culāoi (2)

atha bhagnaballi dayake || hnāpāṃ suryyabhagna dayake || (fol. 1r1-v2/exp. 4)


«End»


udayāstacakramārgga dayake ||

śi(7)ghrocca pikayāva śighroccana udaya asta cakramārgasa pāda yāya śeṣa aṃgādina ghaṭi vighaṭisa udaya asta cakramā(8)rga juyu,

thva bhaviṣya dhakā coyā taya thukunhu juyu dhaka siya || ||

thva grahaṣaṣṭa jyāya || || || (fol. 22v6-8/exp. 25)
