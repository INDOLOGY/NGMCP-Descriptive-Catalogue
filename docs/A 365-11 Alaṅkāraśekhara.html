kāvyajñaśikṣayābhyāsa iti hetus tadudbhave

śaktiḥ puṇyaviśeṣaḥ sa eva pratibhety ucyate. vyutpattiḥ śakir (!) abhyāsas tri(2)tayaṃ kāvyasādhanam ity api. śrīpādastu

pariśīlitakāvyavartmanāṃ

sīraṃ ciraṃ yamau rakṣaḥ kaṃṭhaḥ khaṃjanakekinoḥ
