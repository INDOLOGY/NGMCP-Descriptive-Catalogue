Manuscript Features

The available folios are 1–4, 6–11, 15–19, 21–28.

There are two folios from some another MS of Āyurveda. These fols. are placed at the last.

The MS contains many scribal errors.

Beginning

|| śrīgaṇeśāya namo || || ||

śrīdhanaṃtariguruve namo || || ||

ālasyabhramakaṃ pamohajaḍatāsarvāṃgasaṃdhivethā

romāṃco vadanaṃ vivarṇaṃ madhuraṃ sokastathā tāṇḍava ||

śaithilya vapuṣo jvaro mṛdutaro maṃdagniruṣṇaspṛhā

ṇidrā svalomalobhavac ca virasā jihma samireṅgitam || || ||

End

jarjarikṛtatailārdra varṣābhūmūlikā api ||

dhṛtā yoni muṣe rāmāviśalyā kurute kṣaṇāt || iti viśalyākaraṇaṃ || ||

sutikopadrave jāte kirāṃ tāriṣṭha caṃṇḍanaiḥ ||
