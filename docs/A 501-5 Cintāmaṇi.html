Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namo gaṇeśāya || ||

kalayatu śubhavṛṃdaṃ kālikā kāmadātrī

viśadayatu yaśo me sarvadeśe mukundaḥ ||

ramayatu kamalā me sadmani prītijālair

haricaraṇarajobhiḥ kṣālitaṃ taṃ manos tu || 1 ||

asti vīranṛpe kavau kaluṣite yas te mahīmaṇḍale |

dhūrtair ddharmavilopanāya kṛtibhir mithyā jagad vaṃcyate ||

yad vācaspatiśaṃkaraprabhṛtibhiḥ khyātais tathā maithilaiḥ

smārtair itya anumoditaṃ tithigataṃ tatvaṃ vivicyocyate || 2 ||

vāṇīprasādam āsādya smārttasūktīr vilokya ca ||

māsi māsi tithes tatvaṃ maheśena vivicyate || 3 || (fol. 1r1–3)


End

snānaṃ kariṣye niyatā brahmavratā satī ||

bhokṣyāmi naktaṃ bhūśayyāṃ kariṣye prāṇināṃ dayām iti ||

asya naktavratatvāt sāyāhnavyāpinīṃ grāhyā ||

iti śivaṃ || || (fols. 30r6–7)


Colophon

iti mahāmahopādhyāyasa..raśrīmaheśakṛte titihitattve ciṃtāmaṇau āṣāḍhamāsavivekaḥ samāptaḥ || || śubhaṃ bhūyāt || || śrī śāke 1720 adhikaśrāvaṇakṛṣṇe 9 śanau likhitaṃ suṃdarānaṃdajaineneti || || || (fol. 30r7–9)
