Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrigaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

bhairava uvāca ||

sahasranāmataḥ pūrvvam paṭhanīyan tu yad bhavet |

tad ahaṃ saṃpravakṣyāmi divyaṃ kavacam uttamam || 1 ||

kavacasyasya (!) deveśi mutpādyāḥ pūrvvavan matāḥ ||

viśeṣaḥ sarvvarakṣāyai viniyogaḥ prakīrttitaḥ || 2 ||

śiro me pātu cāmuṇḍā lalāṭaṃ kālikāvatu ||

trinetrā netrayugalaṃ ghrāṇaṃ pātu mamāṃbikā || 3 ||

kapolau pātu kalyāṇī karṇṇau me kāmadāvatu |

mukhaṃ muktipradā pātu bhogadā dantavāsīnī (!) || 4 || (fols. 1v1–2r1)

End

yaḥ svarṇaguṭikāṃ kṛtvā likhitaṃ vāgrajanmanā |

pūjayitvā vidhānena maṃtrasaṃskārapūrvvakaṃ |

kaṇthe dakṣabhuje vāpi dhārayet sādhakottamaḥ |

pūjānte kīrttayec cāpi kīrttayec cāpi nityaṃ bhaktiparāyaṇaḥ || 15 ||

sa bhavet sarvasiddhīśo nātra kāryyā vicāraṇā |

na tasya durllabhaṃ kiṃcin nāsādhyaṃ vā carācare || 16 ||

vidyate para[[me]]śāni tasmād yatnena sādhayet |

etatkavacamajñātvā yo japet śvetakālikāṃ || 17 ||

śatakoṭiprajaptopi maṃtras tasya na siddhyati |

tasmāt sarvvaprayatnena kavacaṃ pariśīlayet || 18 || || (fol. 3r4–4r1)

Colophon

iti śrīvāḍavānalīye mahātaṃtre śrīśvetakālīkavacaṃ samāptam || ❖ || oṃ || ❖ ❖ ❖ ❖ ❖ ❖ ❖ ❖ ❖ || (fol. 5v1–4)
