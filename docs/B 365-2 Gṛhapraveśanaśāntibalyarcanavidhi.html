❖ oṃ namaḥ śrī mahāgaṇeśāye ||
śrī 3 gurupādukāṃbhyāṃ namaḥ ||

atha gṛ(2)hapraveśa pūjāvidhirllikyate ||    ||
patavāsana coya, vāstu suddhā vali (3) mālako boya ||    ||
yajamāna puṣpabhājana || adyādi || vākya ||
śrīsa(4)mvarttā maṇḍalānte kramapadanihitā nanda śaktiḥ subhīmā sṛṣṭaṃ nyāya (5) catuṣkaṃ akula kulagataṃ paṃcakaṃ cānya ṣaṭkaṃ |
catvārā paṃcakonyaṃ puna(6)rapi caturaṃ tattota maṇḍalena saṃsṛṣṭaṃ yena tasmai namata guruvaraṃ bhai(1)ravaṃ śrī kujeśa ||    ||
