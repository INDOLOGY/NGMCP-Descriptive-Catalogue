Excerpts

«Beginning»


oṁ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

bhujagapativimuktasvacchanirmokavallo

vilasitam anukurvan yasya gaṅgā pravāhaḥ ||

śirasi sarasabhāsvan mālatīdāmalakṣmīṃ

laghayati himagauras so ʼstu vaṃ sādhyasiddhyai || 1 ||

kalpāvasānasamaye sthitaye kavīnān

dehāṃ taraṅkim api yā sṛjati prasannā ||

yasyāḥ prasādaparamāṇur api pratiṣṭhām

abhyeti kām api namāmi srasvatī tām || 2 || (fol. 1v1–3)


«End»


sudhiyā janamejayena yatnād

dhṛtisiṃhena saman nirūpiteyaṃ ||

vidito bahudṛśvabhiḥ kavīndraḥ

bhuvikoṣānumataḥ śramo madīyaḥ || 277 ||


hitvā mahāśābdikatābhimānaṃ

mātsaryyamanputramuhunidhāya ||

hārāvalīṃ yaḥ prakaroti kaṇṭhe

vidagdha koṣṭhīparaṃ sa bhāti || 278 || (fol. 13r9–v1)


«Colophon»


iti śrīśābdikamahāmahopādhyāyaporuṣottamade[va]praṇītā hārāvalīvaryyana sitā ? || || || śubham || || (fol. 13v2)
