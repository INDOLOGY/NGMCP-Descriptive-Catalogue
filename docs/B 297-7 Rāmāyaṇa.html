Excerpts

Beginning

|| || śrīrāmajaya (!) || ||

śṛtvā hanumato vākyaṃ yathāvad abhibḥāṣitaṃ |

rāmaḥ prītisamāyukto vākyam uttaram avravī(2)t |

kṛtaṃ hanumatākāryaṃ sumahad bhuvi durlabhaṃ |

manasā pi yad anyena na śakyaṃ dharaṇītale |

na hitaṃ paripaśyāmi ya(3)s tareta mahodadhiṃ |

anyatra garuḍād vāyor [an]yatra (!) ca hanūmataḥ |

devadānavayakṣāṇāṃ gaṃdharvoragarakṣasāṃ |

apradhṛṣyāṃ (4) purīṃ laṃkāṃ rāvaṇena surakṣitāṃ | (fol. 1v1–4)

End

praty āharati visrabdhaṃ balaṃ viṣṇoḥ pravṛddhatāṃ |

vedavic ca bhaved vipraḥ kṣatriyo rājyam āpnuyāt |

dhanaṃ (7) dhānyaṃ tathā vaiśyaḥ śūdraḥ sukham avāpnuyāt |

ślokaṃ pādaṃ paṭhed yas tu viṣṇor lokaṃ sa gachati (!) |

ārogyam āyuṣyaka(1)raṃ yaśasyaṃ

saubhrātradaṃ putravibardhanaṃ ca |

śrotavyam etan niyataiś ca sadbhir

ākhyānam ūrjam balam ṛddhikāmaiḥ ||

kuṭumbavṛddhiṃ dha(2)nadhānyasaṃcayaṃ

striyaś ca mukhyāḥ sukham uttamaṃ sadā |

labheta lakṣmīṃ vipulāṃ narottamo

yathābhiṣekaṃ bhuvi rāmalakṣmaṇāv i(3)ti || || (fol. 327v6–328r3)

Colophon

ity ārṣe rāmāyaṇe laṃkākāṃḍe maharṣivālmīkiviracite ābhyudayike parvvaṇi rāmābhiṣekaḥ samāptaḥ || || (fol. 328r3)
