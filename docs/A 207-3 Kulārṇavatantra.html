Manuscript Features

in intact formation.

fol. 60v is re-foliated as 82 and 70 also and text continues in Devanagari script in the exposure 84b, continues foliation from 71.

On the exposure two is written ❖ vinādravyādhivāsena yo (!) japanti smanti ca |

yo (!) japani mahāmūḍhā (!) teṣāṃ duḥkhaṃ pade pade ||

Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīparadevatāyai

guruṃ gaṇapatiṃ durggāṃ vaṭukaṃ śivam acyutaṃ |

brahmāṇaṃ girijāṃ lakṣmīṃ vāṇīṃ vande vibhūtaye ||

a(2)nadyāyākhilādyāya māyine gatamāyine ||

arūpāya sarūpāya śivāya gurave namaḥ || 1 ||

parāprāsādamantrāya saccidā(3)nandacetase |

agniṣomātmarūpāya tryembakāya (!) namo namaḥ || 12 || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

sādhakānāṃ hitārthāya bhuktimuktiphalaikṣiṇāṃ (!) ||

yaś coddhāmnāyamahātmyaṃ (!) paṭhitvā cakrasaṃnidhau ||

bhaktyā paramayā devi yaḥ śṛṇoti sa kaulikaḥ ||

vratadāne tapas tīrthayajña(1)dā[nā]rccanādi ca ||

tatphalaṃ koṭiguṇitaṃ labhate nātra saṃśayaḥ ||

tas sannidhau sa nivaśet || nātra kāryā vicāraṇāt (!) || (fol. 157r4–5 and v1)

Colophon

||     || iti śrīkulārṇave mahārahasye sapādalakṣagraṃthe paṃcamakhaṇḍe nāmavāsanādikathanaṃ nāma saptadaśollā(3)saḥ || 17 ||

samāptam || śubham ||      || śubham ||     || (fol. 157v2–3)
