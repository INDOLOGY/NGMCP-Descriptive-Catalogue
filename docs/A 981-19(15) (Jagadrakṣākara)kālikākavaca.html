Excerpts

Beginning

śrībhadrakālyai namaḥ ||

bhavaduḥkhamahodvighna saṃtaptān karuṇārṇava ||

tvāṃ tu yāce ham īśaikaṃ trātum arhasi vāgjalaiḥ || 1 ||

kathitaṃ kālikā devyāḥ kāmadaṃ kavacaṃ ma[[ha]]t ||

rahasyaṃ kavacaṃ brūhi kasmai cintayadīritaṃ || 2 ||

prayogasaṃghair bhavatā sādhako vimukhīkṛtaḥ ||

sādhanāt tad idānīṃ me pratyakṣīkaraṇaṃ vada || 3 ||

yat syā (!) devatāpratyakṣe na tatkarmaśatair api ||

vada tattadupāyaṃ me yena syād iṣṭadarśanaṃ || 4 ||

devatābhajanaṃ samyak yo na jānāti tattvataḥ ||

ruṣtotyarthaṃ sa kiṃ kuryāt tuṣṭo vā saṃsṛtau prabho || 5 ||

śiva uvāca ||

kathidā na mayā proktaṃ kavacaṃ kāmadaṃ śive ||

tathāpi na nivṛttis te praśne giribhuvaḥ kimāḥ (!) || 6 || (fol. 21v1–6)

End

dadyād advaitabhāvāya parastrīsvāratāya ca ||

viśuddhāya kulīnāya niḥsaṃdahahṛ (!) ʼdviṣe (!) || 62 ||

ṣaḍgeheṅgesti likhitaṃ bhurjje kavacam adbhutaṃ ||

rocanālaktasiṃdūra mṛgīṃdu (!) kuṃkumadravaiḥ || 63 ||

cauradasyugrahārāti jalādhivyādhivaṃhnibhīḥ ||

na tasya jāyate kvāpi svayaṃ śrīr nivaśaty api || 64 ||

yatphalaṃ labhate martyo devasaṃdhārcane ʼvanau ||

kālikā kavacanyāsāt tatphalaṃ syā (!) na saṃśayaṃ || 65 ||

rahasyaṃ kavacaṃ maṃtro rahasyaṃ kavacastutiḥ ||

rahasyaṃ kavacaṃ dhyānaṃ devyā vetti sa siddhibhāk || 66 ||

rahasyaṃ kavacaṃ vidyā rahasyaṃ kavacaṃ dhanaṃ ||

rahasyaṃ kavacaṃ jñānaṃ rahasyaṃ kavacaṃ kulaṃ || 67 ||

rahasyaṃ tiṣṭhati kavacaṃ so kutaścid bhayaḥ sudhīḥ ||

jarāmaraṇanirmuktaḥ śāpānugrahakṛd bhavet || 68 || (fol. 24r6–24v3)

Colophon

iti śrīkālīrahasye kālikākavacaṃ jagadrakṣākaraṃ saṃpūrṇam || 15 || (fol. 24v3)
