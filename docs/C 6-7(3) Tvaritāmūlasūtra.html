Excerpts

Beginning

(///jaṃmā(!) hi ṣannāraṃ lakṣe pūrṇṇe ʼgnimadhyataḥ ||)

dṛśyate tvaritā devī pūrṇṇāhutyāṃ vaśānataḥ |

tataś ca hṛṣṭamanasā arghan dadyāt subhāvitaḥ ||

vīrohṛ(dya)bhita(!)cittas tu tac ca sāhasam ācaret |

uktavatī tadā devī sādhu putra mahātapaḥ ||

ārādhitā subhaktena utiṣṭhet(!) tiṣṭha gamyatāṃ |

khecaratvaṃ mahāsiddhiṃ sahāyasahito vraja ||

tataś cottiṣṭhate vīraḥ sahāyasahitonabhaṃ |

tejo rā|simahādīpto vrajate yatra rocate ||

svacchandasarvvagonityaṃ śvatvam akṣayaṃ labhet |

asthicarmmāvaśeṣaṃ tu śuṣkapiṇḍāś ca dehinaḥ ||

sādhakānucarā ye tu sahāyā mṛtyum āgatāḥ ||

tepyarghabindusaṃspṛṣṭā(!) tapa(!)śān(ty u)pabṛṃhitāḥ ||

devyā(!) śaktisamopetā maṃtratejaḥsamanvitāḥ |

divyā(!)rūpadharo bhūtvā trinetramukuṭojvalā ||

utpatanti mahātmānaḥ khecaratvaṃ sudurllabhaṃ |

krīḍante sarvvalokeṣu śivaśaktyopabṛṃhitāḥ || (fol.198r1-198v2)

End

rahasyaṃ paramaṃ hy etat sarvvañjānottamaṃ khaga ||

aparīkṣa(!) na dātavyaṃ kāryaṃ guptataraṃ tvayā |

dātavyaṃ tu suśiṣyāya bhaktāya ca tapasvine ||

nivetitātmano(!) caiva (trotalaṃ) tatra muttamaṃ(!) |

mūlavidyā tu vidyāṅgāḥ sapta eva prakīrttitāḥ ||

ṣaṭsaṃkhyātās tu guhyāṅgāḥ dūtayaṣ tu daśa smṛtāḥ |

ekaṃ paṃcānanaṃ viṃsacaturbhir adhikāni ca ||

akṣarāṇāṃ tu caikatra tṛsaptaty(!) adhikaṃ śataṃ ||

śataiḥ saptabhir ākhyātā(!) paṭlair navatis tathā ||

lakṣādhikā (!) samuddhṛtya sārāt sāraṃ susiddhidaṃ || ❁ || (fol.200r1-5 )

Colophon

ityādye trotale mahātantre tvritāmūlasūtre navamapaṭalaḥ samāptaḥ || ❁ ||

maṅgalamahāśrīḥ || samvat 317? śrāvaṇaśuklatṛtīyāyāṃ || śaniścaravāre ||

rājādhirājaparameśvaraśrīmallakṣmīkāmadesya(!) vijayarājye ||

śrījasabrahmabhāro nāmnasya pustakam idaṃ || || subham astu || ||

(fol.200r5-200v2)
