Excerpts

«Beginning»


hakaṃ ||

tasmān nityaṃ prakarttavyaṃ kaulābhyāṃ saṃvarāṇane |

bahiḥ pūjā mahādevi śaivoktavidhinā priye ||

nādākṣaṃ paramaṃ bījaṃ sarvvasatvesv avasthitaṃ |

taṃ viditvā mahādevi sarvapāpakṣayo bhavet || (fol. 3r1–2)


«End»


pitṛdevāgni(bhaktaś) ca †asaṃtoṣī jagad (vitaḥ)† ||

śivoktaśāsane raktagurubhaktadṛḍhavarataḥ ||

ahaṃkāraparityaktaḥ saṃvitbhaktabahuśrutaḥ ||

eṣa devi mayā proktaṃ saucācāreṣu brāhmaṇā |

vadisyāmi varārohe trayā(‥)raṃ punaḥ priye ||

antakaule niyan proktaṃ a(bhyā)maṃ sarvadā priye ||

nāḍīcakreṣu yat proktaṃ proktaṃ yad vaṃsanirṇṇaye |

piṇḍapadādivijñāne (fol. 12v3–5)


«Colophon»

The colophon is lost since the text runs up to fol. 12.
