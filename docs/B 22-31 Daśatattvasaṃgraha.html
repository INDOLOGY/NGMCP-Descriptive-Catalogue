Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namo vajrasattvāya |

vajrasatvaṃ guruṃ natvā taddharmmasūtrasaṃhatim |
daśatatvāni kathyante jñānapādakrameṇa tu ||
rakṣācakrañ ca cakrañ ca jāpa ṣekau haṭhau baliḥ |
pratyaṅgiraḥ puṭodghāta tatvāni kramaśo daśaḥ (!) || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

niyataṃ durgatiparaṃ |

kariṣyamāṇakarmatvaṃ jñāyate yadi niścitaṃ |
tadvirodho py acintyatvā⁅ccha⁆++ karmaṇo yataḥ ||

iti sampuṭodghātavidhiḥ |

daśatatvāni saṃhṛtya yanmayāsāditaṃ śu++ |
daśatatvaparijñātā samyak tenāstv asau jana | (last folio 1–3)

Sub-Colophons

iti lekhyapratyaṅgirā || 8 || (fol. 38v2)

Colophon

kṛtir iyaṃ daśatattvasaṃgrahaḥ paṇḍitakṣitiga⁅rbha⁆syeti || || (last folio 3–4)
