Beginning

|| || śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

karodvānanaṃ cārucaṃdrāvataṃsaṃ

sravadbaṃdupālītamila bhṛṃgamālāṃ

aparṇāsutaṃ vighnaphagaṃ(!) haraṃtaṃ

gaṇeśeśaṃ sureśaṃ tamādyaṃ prapadye || 1 ||

śrījānakīśaṃ śaraṇāgataikaduḥkhāpahaṃ śrīgirijādhavaṃ ca |

smṛtvātha pūjyau pitarau guruṃś ca śrībhātarau sūryasamānarūpau | 1 | (fol. 1v1–2)


End

graṃthān bahūn suvitatān suciraṃ vibhāvya

kaṣṭena yaḥ kavivarair adhigamyaterthaḥ |

nirmatsaraṃ budhavarāḥ parito virārya

graṃthād urūkuru tataṃ vihitā prayāsam | 7 |

divi paṃcakalpataravaḥ paraṃ śrūtā jayasiṃhavarman yateḥ samānate katham anyathā suragaṇāṃbikāpurevanitaṃ sadādhvaramiṣyat samāyayau || 8 || (fol. 36v5–7)
