Beginning

❖ oṁ namaḥ śrīgaṇeśāya ||


oṁ kālikādevyai namaḥ ||


kailāsaśikhare ramye nānāpuṣpopaśobhite ||

kalpapādapamadhyasthe nānāratnopaśobhite || 1 ||


maṇimaṇḍapamadhyasthe munigaṃdharvasevite ||

taṃ kadācit sukhāsīnaṃ bhagavaṃtaṃ jagadguruṃ || 2 ||


kapālakhaṭvāṅgadharaṃ caṃdrārddhakṛtaśekharaṃ ||

triśūlaḍamaruhastaṃ mahāvṛṣabhavāhanaṃ || 3 || (fol. 1v1–5)


End

iha loke naravaraḥ paścān mukto bhaved dhruvaṃ ||

anyatra yatra vakṣāmi viśeṣaṃ siddhidaṃ kalau || 49 ||


mṛtacarmāsane martyaḥ sādhakaṃ upaviśyate(!) ||

imaṃ mamtramayaṃ devyāḥ sahasrākhyaṃ stavottamaṃ || 49 ||


sākṣād bhagavatī śyāmā darśanaṃ jāyate sadā ||

deyaṃ putrāya śiṣyāya gurubhaktiratāya ca || 50 ||


sādhakāya ca dātavyaṃ na deyaṃ yasya kasyacit ||

abhaktebhyo ʼpi putrebhyo datvā narakam āpnuyāt || 51 ||


sulabhasya phalaṃ nāsti durllabhasya kathaṃ bhavet ||

satyaṃ satyaṃ mahāsena karmmaṇā manasā girā || 50 ||


abhaktāya kulīnāya datvā kuṣṭībhaven naraḥ || (fol. 20v4–21r6)


Colophon

iti śrīrudrayāmale mahātaṃtre kārttikeyamahādevasamvāde trailokyavijayābhidhaṃ maṃtragarbhaśyāmāsahasranāmastotraṃ samāptam || śubham astu || ||


gopyā gopyā punargopyā gopyā gopyā punaḥ punaḥ ||
