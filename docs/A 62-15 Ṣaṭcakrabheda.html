Excerpts

«Beginning:»'


śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

śrīgurupādukābhyo namaḥ ||


atha taṇtrānusāreṇa ṣaḍcakrādikramoddhataḥ ||

ucyate paramānandanirvāhaprathamāṃkuraḥ ||


meror bāhyapradeśe śaśimihiraśire savyadakṣe niṣaṇne

madhye nāḍī suṣumnā tritayaguṇamayī candrasūryāgnirūpā

dhattūrasmerapuṣpāgrathitatamavapuḥ kandamadhyāt śirasthā

vajrākhyā merudeśe śirasśi pariṇatā madhyame syo jvalantī || 2 || (fol. 1v1–4)


«End:»


lākṣābhaṃ paramāmṛtaṃ paraśivāt pītvā punaḥ kuṇḍalī

pūrṇānandamahodayāt kulapathān mūle viśet sundarīṃ |

tad divyāmṛtadhārayā sthiramatiḥ saṃtarppayed daivataṃ

yogī yogaparaṃparāsu viditaṃ brahmāṇḍabhāṇḍasthitaṃ || 54 ||


jñātvaitat kramam uttamaṃ yatamanā yogī samādhau sthitaḥ

śrīdīkṣāgurupādapadmayugalā(nanda)modaprakāśodayāt ||

saṃsāre na hi jāyate na hi punaḥ saṃksīyate saṃkṣaye

pūrṇānadaparaṃparāsu muditas cāste satām agraṇī


yo dhīte niśi saṃdhyayor api divā yogī svabhāvasthito

mikṣajñānanidānam etad amalaṃ śuddhaṃ suguptaṃ kramaṃ ||


śrīmat śrīgurupādapadmayugalā laṃbī rasaṃ tattvato

jñātvā cāsya paraṃ svadaivatapade ceto narīnṛtyate || 55 (fol. 6v2–7)


«Colophon:»


iti śrīpūrṇānadaviracite cintāmaṇi samāptaḥ || || ṣaṭcakrabhedaḥ | (fol. 6v8)
