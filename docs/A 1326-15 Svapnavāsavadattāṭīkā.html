Manuscript Features

Fols. 2–6, 15–19 and 59–65 are available.

A scattered folio, smaller in size, (having figure 19, in the upper left-hand margin under the abbreviation vāsada. and in the lower right-hand margin under the word rāma) not related to our particular text (Svapnavāsavadattāṭīkā) but seems to be related to the Svapnavāsavadattā (we did not try to find out the fact, but from the abbreviation and context, we guessed) appears as a regular folio between fols. 18 and 59 reading:

ghanasthalīṣu janīṣu viśrātākathānubaṃdhatayā pravarttamānānekajanagṛhagamana-tvareṣu samāvāsitakukkuṭeṣu niṣkuṭeṣu kṛtayaṣṭisamārohaṇeṣu barhiṇeṣu …
