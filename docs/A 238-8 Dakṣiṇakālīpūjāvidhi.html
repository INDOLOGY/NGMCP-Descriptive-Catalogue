(oṃ) namo dakṣiṇakālikāyai ||


prakṣyālya pāṇipādau pūjāsthānam āgatyāca(mya) sāmānyārghasthāpanaṃ kuryāt || athāstreṇa


pātraṃ prakṣālya nama iti pūrayitvā gandhapuṣpādi datvā tīrthamantreṃa tīrtham āvāhya praṇavena


dhenumudrāṃ pradarśayet || tatostreṇa dvāraṃ prokṣya oṃ dvāradevatābhyo namaḥ iti saṃpūjya


astramantreṇa vāmapādaghātatrayeṇa bhaumān bighnān utsārya astrādibhiś cāntarīkṣagān bighnān


utsārya mūlamantrasaṃcitadivyadṛṣṭyā divyān bighān utsārya apasarpantviti paṭhitvā vikirān vikīrya


dakṣiṇapādapurasaram aṅgasaṃkocanayāgamaṃḍapaṃ praviśya brahmaṇe vāstupuruṣāya namaḥ iti


naiṛtyāṃ saṃpūjyāsanaṃ kuryāt || (exp. 3t1–3b3)
