Excerpts

«Beginning of the root text:»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

bhedātīte sapadigalite puṇyapāpe viśīrṇe

māyāmoho (!) kṣayam adhigate naṣṭasaṃde(5)havṛttau ||

śabdātīte triguṇarahitaṃ prāpyatattvarthabodhaṃ

nistraiguṇye pathi vicaratāṃ ko vidhiḥ ko niṣedhaḥ 1 (exp 2,4–5)

«Beginning of the commentary:»

artha

bheda bhanyākoḥ ta maḥ jīva bhinnu iśvara (!) bhinna ho bhannu tyo buddhi naṣṭa bhayāpachi puṇyaṃ pāpaṃ pani naṣṭa bhayāpa(2)chi māyā bhanyāki jhuṭo pani satya dekhāunyā moha bhanyāko jaḍaśariralāī ātmā ho bhannu (exp. 2,1–2)

«End of the root text:»

satyaṃ satyaṃ param amṛtaṃ (!) śāntikalyānarūpaṃ (!)

māyāra(6)ṇye dahanamaline śāntikalyānadīpaṃ (!) ||

tejorūpaṃ vimalasadanaṃ vyāsaputrāṣṭakaṃ ya (!)

prātakāle (!) pa(7)ṭhati manasā yāti nirvāṇamārgam || 8 || 0 || (exp. 6b5–7)

«End of the commentary:»

pṛthivi jala vāyu ākāśa jo cha brahmarūpa ho mṛtyule (8) rahita śāṃtikalyāṇarūpabhayāko jo rūpa bhayāko ⟨ko⟩ mala kehi na bhayāko sthāna brahma choḍi kehi china vyāsa(9)putrale racyākoṣṭaka jo manuṣya paḍhos brahmakana pāucha (!) || 8 || (exp. 4b3, 8–9)

«Colophon of the root text:»

iti śukāṣṭaka (!) śubhaṃ || ○ || ○ || ○ || ○ || ○ || (exp. 4b7)

«Colophon of the commentary:»

iti śukāṣṭaka (!) śubhaṃ (exp. 4b 9)
