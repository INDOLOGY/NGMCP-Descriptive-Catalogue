Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||    ||

oṃ asya indrākṣīstotramantrasya purandara-ṛṣir anuṣṭupchanda indrākṣīdevatā lakṣmīr bījaṃ bhuvaneśvarīti śaktir bhavānīkīlakaṃ mama sarvābhīṣṭasiddhyarthe pāṭhe viniyogaḥ ||

śirasi purandara-ṛṣaye namaḥ

mukhe anuṣṭupchandase namaḥ

…

dhyānam

indrākṣīṃ dvibhujāṃ devīṃ pītavastravibhūṣitām ||
vāmahaste vajradharāṃ dakṣiṇena varapradām ||

indrākṣīṃ yuvatīṃ dhyāye[n] nānālaṅkārabhūṣitām ||
prasannavadanāmbhojām apsarogaṇasevitām || 1 ||

anyac ca

dvibhujāṃ saumyavadanāṃ pāśāṅkuśadharāṃ parām ||
trailokyamohinīṃ devīm indrākṣīnāmakīrttitām || (fol. 1v1–2v7)

End

sadā saṃmohinī devī sundarī bhuvaneśvarī ||
etair nāmapadair divyaiḥ stutā śakreṇa dhīmatā || 9 ||

śatam āvartayed yas tu mucyate vyādhibandhanāt ||
āvṛttena sahasreṇa labhate vāñchitaṃ phalam || 10 ||

lakṣam ekaṃ japed yas tu sākṣād devī bhaviṣyati ||
trikālaṃ yaḥ paṭhen nityaṃ tasyādhīnāś ca sampadaḥ || 11 ||

āyur ārogyam aiśvaryaṃ jñānaṃ buddhir yaśobalam ||
prāptam indreṇa sarvasvam indrākṣyāś ca prasādataḥ || 12 ||

rājā ca vaśam āpnoti ṣaṇmāsān nātra saṃśayaḥ ||
raṇe rājakule dyūte sarvatra vijayī bhavet || 13 ||

kṣayāpasmārakuṣṭāditāpajvaranivāraṇam ||
etat stotraṃ japen nityaṃ sarvavyādhivināśanam || 14 || (fol. 4r2–v5)

Colophon

iti śrīmatpurandaraviracitam i[n]drākṣīstotraṃ samāptam ||    || śubham || ❁ || ❁ || ❁ || (fol. 4v5–6)
