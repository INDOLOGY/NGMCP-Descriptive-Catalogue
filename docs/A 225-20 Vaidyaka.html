Beginning

❖ mūlikādi hlāya ||

śubhe, āraṇya(!) ekānte, prābhāte mantrayuktitaḥ |
saṃgrājyam auṣadhaṃ siddhaṃ ( no cakṣati ca ) kā(2)ṣṭhavat ||

bhiṅa gusa, ekānta yāṅa, mantraṇa saṃyukta yāṅa, thathyeṃ kāyāvāsaraṇa siddhijuraṃ ||

ity evaṃ si(3)ddhamūlānāṃ, vidhirmantraś ca kathyate ||

thathyaṃleyā guli leyā guli, vāsarayā, vidhiṃ mantra hlāsyaṃ haya ||    ||(fol. 1r1–3)

❖śrīganēśāya namaḥ || oṃ asya śrī siddhisamayāmantrasya bhairavaṛṣir a(7)nuṣṭup chandaḥ ānandarūpiṇī parālakṣṃīr devatā aiṃ bījaṃ, hrīṃ kīlakaṃ, samādhisukhasiddhyarthe jape viniyogaḥ ||(fol. 2v6–7)

End

jamgamādi jantuyā che gu(5)li sālu si dudu cvo mala thvate ādikāya mālanāva nakāyā jīrṇṇavāṅave rasakāya || (fol. 2v4–5)

trivarṣe viṣṇurūpaṃ tu caturvarṣe tu rudratāṃ
pañcavarṣe varārohe ī(4)śvaratvam avāpnuyāt
sadāśivo bhavet ṣaḍbhir jarāmaraṇavarjitaḥ
evaṃ krameṇa deveśi bhakṣayet si(5)ddhamūlikāṃ
śubhaṃ kimadhikaṃ || vijayākalpa || (fol. 7r3–5)
… triphalā vālāla, arjjunasiṃ yā vo seṃ hlāsa, lāhā, vilisipu, uṇalahā, thvate samabhāga yāṅā va ccheduvane dhūpayāya, ṭaṃsa kūsida kvamoyuva ||    || e ||    ||
