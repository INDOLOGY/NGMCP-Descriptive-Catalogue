śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

śrībhagavān ājñā garchan | uddhavajī jiteṃdriya bhai praṇavābhyāsādi gari ma viṣaya manalāī niścaya gari amana bhai yoga dhāraṇa gari abhyāsa garnyā jogīlāī siddhi āuṃcha bhanī śrībhagavānbāṭa ājñā garyāmā uddhavajī soddha garchan 1

he prabho siddhi kati chan dhāraṇa bhanyāko kasto ho

yogīlāī siddhi dinyā hajurai hunuhuṃcha tas artha malāī ājñā havas 2

śrībhagavān ājñā garchan
