iyam iha loke ācaṃdrārkaṃ tiṣṭhatu ||

asya vaktṛṇāṃ śro(3)tṛṇāṃ ca śubhaṃ bhavatu ||

ye subodhāṃ paṭhaṃti māpagryāṃ (!) jātakapaddhatiṃ ||

horāvitpa(4)davī (!) yāṃti loke mānaṃ yaśaś ca te || spaṣṭārtham idaṃ || it graṃthapāṭhakasya (5) phalaśrutidvārā graṃthapraśaṃsā || ❁ || (fol. 70r2–5)
