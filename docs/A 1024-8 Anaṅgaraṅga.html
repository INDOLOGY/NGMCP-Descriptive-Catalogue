Excerpts

Beginning of the commentary

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||    ||
nirvighnapūrvaka graṃtha samāpta havas bhannā nimitta kāmadevake smaraṇa rupa maṃgalācaraṇa gariṃcha atisuṃdara vilāsa bhayākā sava prāṇīkā cittamā vaḍo tejasvī bhayākā śamvara nāmā daityakanamāryā (fol. 1v1–3)

Beginning of the root text

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

atilalitavilāsaṃ viśvacetonivāsaṃ
samarakṛtavikāśaṃ śamvarākhyapraṇāśam
ratinayanavirāmaṃ saṃtataṃ cābhirāmaṃ
prasabhavijitavāmaṃ śarmadaṃ naumi kāmaṃ || 1 || (fol. 1v4–6)

End of the root text

yāva chaṃkaramauligā suranadī gaurī tadarddhāṃ ga⟨ṃ⟩gā
yāvat premavatī payodhitanayā vaikuṃṭhasaṃge ratā ||
vedābhyāsarato viraṃcir a⁅p⁆i bhūr yāvad dineśaḥ śaśī
bhūyāt tāvad anaṃgaraṃgaka iti prītyai sadā kāmināṃ || 60 ||(fol. 62v7, 63r4)

End of the commentary

jāṃhāsamma bhūmi sūrya candramā jāhāsamma rahanyāchan tāṃhāsamma yo anaṃgaraṃga nāmā graṃtha kāmadevakā nānāvidha vilākana jāṃnyābhayākā strī puruṣako + + + + vaṭhāvas || 60 || (fol. 63r2–6)
