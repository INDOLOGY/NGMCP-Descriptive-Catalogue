Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrīgurave namaḥ ||

sadāśiveśvaro rudro viṣṇubrahmāsanākulaḥ ||

gaurīśo trīśammitrīśoḥ (!) kapilānandagurus tataḥ ||

siddhiśāsanapiṃgākhyo manuś ca puṣpadantakaḥ |

śāntirogasti saṃjñaś ca paṃcārthākhyagurus tataḥ ||

vidyācāryyo vratācāryyo durvvāso kauśiko mataḥ |

jaigīśo pi vratācāryya (!) kauṇḍinyācāryya eva ca || (exp. 3t1–4)

End

svasvaśaktisamāyogāt nāmāmbā ca padāntikā |

varābhayakarā sarvva śivād iti gurukramaṃ ||

gurukramaṃ paṭhed yas tu trisaṃdhyāṃ śraddhayānvita |

nyāsapūjājapādīnāṃ anantaphalam āpnuyāt || 8 || (exp. 4b2–4)

Colophon

iti śrīpāṇḍupate gurukramaṃ samāptaṃ || || (exp. 4b4–5)
