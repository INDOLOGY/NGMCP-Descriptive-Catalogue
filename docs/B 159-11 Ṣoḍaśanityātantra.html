Manuscript Features

Available folios 175 and 176.

Fols. 175v and 175r are in reverse order.

A folio (figure in the lower right-hand margin of the verso) related to the text but not in its proper place appears between fols. 175 and 176.

In the same way, a next folio related to the text but not in its proper place (foliation is same as in the previous folio) appears in a different hand after fol. 176r.

iti ṣoḍaśanityātaṃtre śrīkādimate asṭādaśa paṭalaḥ (fol. 90r4(the first one).

Excerpts

Beginning

tte tat tattvaṃ svātmasātkṛtam ||

iti ṣoḍaśanityātaṃtreṣu kādimate catustriṃśat paṭalam ||

atha ṣoḍaśanityānāṃ svātmatve vāsanāṃ śṛṇu ||

yayā tanmayatāsiddhiḥ pratyakṣā bhavati dhruvam || 1 ||

gurūr(!) ādyā bhavec chaktiḥ sāvimarśamayī matā ||

†bhavatvaṃ† tasya dehasya raṃdhratvenāvabhāsate || 2 ||

balidevyaḥ svamāyāḥ syuḥ paṃcamījanakātmikā ||

kurukullā bhaven mātā puruṣārthās tu sāgarāḥ || 3 || (fol. 175r1–4)

End

evaṃ kṛte bhaved vidyā sarvodhvā vīravaṃdite ||

sarvādyas(!)tād vilonmā(!) sā madhyasthā vyākulakramāt || 41 ||

tābhiḥ sarvābhir apy āsu siddhayaty evābhivāṃchita⟨ṃ⟩m ||

lalitābhedajātāni tad yantroktāny aśeṣaṭaḥ || 42 ||

nityānityābhidāś cāpi tad yaṃtrapaṭaloditāḥ ||

anyās tu tatra tatraiva draṣṭavyā yatra coṃ(!)ditāḥ || 43 ||

eṣāṃ paṃcaprakā (fol. 176v9–12)
