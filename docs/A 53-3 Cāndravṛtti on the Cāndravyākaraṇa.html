Manuscript Features

This MS covers parts of the third, fifth, and sixth adhyāya of the Cāndravṛtti. The sequence of the folios is out of order. The extant portions of the MS are as follows:

fol. 38 (covering 3.1.37–66)
foll. 41; 43; 45; 46; 42; 48; 47; 44 (covering 3.2.62–3.4.67)
foll. 82; 81; 83–85 (covering 5.4.78–6.1.40).

The sūtras are cited in full, but sometimes portions of the Vṛtti are missing. All folios are damaged to various degrees.
