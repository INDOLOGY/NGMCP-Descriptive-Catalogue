Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||


makhādikarmmavijñānakumudobdodhahetave ||

namastasmai gaṇēśāṅghrin natvā candramarīcaye || 1 ||


hutīṣṭibhaktismṛtibudhdilajjā

śāntyādiyoṣiddharavallabho ʼham ||

brabīmi saṃskāravidhin niṣekād

brāhmam praṇamyābajbhavāṅghripadmam || 2 || (1v1–3)


End

ācamyācamya bhakṣasyāvra(!)somamadhuparkkavat |

ājyasaṃskāra evāyaṃ ṣaḍāvayavalakṣaṇā(!) |


praṇāvāje [ʼ]pi rāmo [ʼ]stu brūyād ācārya eva saḥ(!) |

adho sādhītyāhar ahaḥ saha śiṣyair jayed guruḥ ||


pratīkaṃ me vicakṣaṇaṃ jiṅkāye madhuyahu ca |

vācā hy api dadāmy aṃtam idaṃ maṃtram upāṃśutaḥ ||


svrakraṇasavakaṃ rāṅāti tasya syāt prārthane vidhi |

liṃgād āpyāyate śeṣa iti sūtravidāṃ mataṃ || ❁❁ || (fol. 135v1–4)


Colophon

iti tatvārthasaṃpūrṇareṇukāryeṇa yajvanā kṛtāyāṃ sūtravivṛtau samāptā gṛhyakārikā || śubhaṃ

bhavat || śrīrā śrīrāma || ❁ || (fol. 135v4–5)
