Manuscript Features

This MS covers only 10-12 paṭalas of the text. The scribe abruptly stops in the second line of fol. 9v.

Excerpts

Beginning

evam āsādya susthānam ādau bhāgyādhirohaṇaṃ |
ātmanaḥ śreyase kāryaṃ guruṇā vidhipūrvakaṃ |
bhāgyamūlam idaṃ sarvaṃ dṛṣṭādṛṣṭapradaṃ nṛṇāṃ |
na hi bhāgyavihīnānāṃ bhavanti bhrasam(!) ṛddhayaḥ |
ato guruprasādena śrīmatā soram(!) muddhṛtaṃ |
tad iha〇 pravadām(!) etan mahābhāgyādhirohaṇaṃ |
nādo manoratho dhāma vyoma dhāma tridhā gataṃ |
kālaḥ sthoro vāḍaveśaḥ sākṣī śrītithibodhakaḥ |
nirodhakṛn mantrabījaṃ〇 sphuṭam ekatra yojayet |
candragarbbābhidhānena devaḥ kāmeśvaro bhavet |
yad anādipaṃ sūkṣmam acintyaṃ dheya(!)varjjitaṃ |
avyaktaṃ vyaktim āyātaḥ sa eṣa parameśva〇raḥ | (fol. 1v1-4)

End

vāgbhavaṃ yad iha proktaṃ yogīnīnāṃ〇 śarīrajaṃ |
tad eva samyag vijñāya yajet tripurabhairavīṃ |
tasminn eva sukhāsīnāṃ candrabimbopari sthitāṃ |
mahābindupade līnāṃ tata(!) sukhānandanirbharāṃ |
dhyātvā〇 yastvā(!) vidhivat kāmavat kāmukeśvaraḥ |
kṣobhayen nātra saṃdeho līlayā bhuvanatrayaṃ |
daśanāt(!) svaśanād eva kīrttanād api yoṣitaḥ |
sarvvās tu pratimuhyanti mriyante ca viśeṣataḥ |
etad guhyataraṃ kāryaṃ yato nāsty atra bhājanaṃ ||
bhāgyādhirohaṇājasravidhāne devyās tv anusmaraṇo nāma dvādaśamaḥ paṭalaḥ || ○ ||
evaṃ vijñānadakṣo pi prāptarddhir api saṃsadi |
guruḥ spaṣṭākṣaraṃ yadā vinā vācaṃ na śobhate |
ato bravīmi saṃsiddhāṃ vijayañ ca (fol. 9r3–v2)

Sub-colophons

bhāgyādhirohaṇe mantroddhāro nāma daśamaḥ paṭalaḥ || ○ || (fol. 4r1–2)

mahābhāgyādhirohaṇo nāma yogapaṭala ekādaśamaḥ || ○ || (fol. 7v3)

bhāgyādhirohaṇājasravidhāne devyās tv anusmaraṇo nāma dvādaśamaḥ paṭalaḥ || ○ || (fol. 9r5–v1)
