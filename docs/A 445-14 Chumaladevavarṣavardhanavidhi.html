❖ śrī 3 siddhigaṇeśāya namaḥ ||
śrīgurupādukābhyāṃ namaḥ ||    ||

chumalayā devalacheyā gajuli chāyā varṣavarddhana paṃcopacārapūjā(3)yāta thva sāphula dayakā ||    ||
jajamāna pūṣapabhājana ||
oṃ adyādi (4) || vākya ||
śrīsamvarttāmaṇḍalānte kramapadasahitā nandaśakti subhī(5)mā sṛṣṭiṃnyāya catuṣkaṃ akulakulagataṃ paṃcakaṃ cāṃnya ṣaṭkaṃ catvā(2r1)ro pañcakaṃnyaṃ pun arapi caturaṃ tat tato maṇḍalena saṃsṛṣṭiṃ yena tasmai (2) namita guruvaraṃ bhairavaṃ śrīkujeśaṃ || (fol. 1v1–2r2)

End

yajamāna ādina mālakvasta svāna biya || (5) vācana laṃkha kāyāva taya || mohanī taya ||    || laṃkhana hāye || ūkā(52v1)raṃ vāyuvije || ceta || śrīkhaṇḍa || sidhara || vīreśvarī mahāvīra || moha(2)nī || trailokyamohanī || svāna taya || gauraṃ bhṛkṣara || svāna mālako(3)stā biya ||    || astramantrana bali thvaya || nośiya || bali bhokalu(3)ya || sākṣi thāya ||    ||
(fol. )
