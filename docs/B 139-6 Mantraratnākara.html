Excerpts

Beginning

jñai,r abhivītaṃ, divyabhūṣaṇollaśite ||

ātmābhinnatayetthaṃ, dhyātvaikāntam athāṣṭavarṇṇañ ca ||

japed ity arthaḥ || || 

atha vākyapūjā ||

arghapātrasthāpanaṃ kṛtvā trailva(!)kyamohanāya vidmahe, smarāya dhīmahi, tan no viṣṇuḥ pracodayād iti viṣṇugāyatryātmānaṃ pūjopakaraṇāni bhūmiñ cābhyūkṣapet || mānapūjāṃ kṛtvā trailva(!)mohanāyety uktagāyatrīṃ śaṃjapya mūlamaṃtraṃ japtvā manaskṛtya<ref name="ftn1">Manasikṛtya is grammatically correct form.</ref> yantram uddharet || vilipya gandhatoyena likhed aṣṭadalāṃbujaṃ || karṇnikāyān tu ṣaṭkoṇaṃ, ṇa(!) sādhyaṃ tatra manmathaṃ || (fol. 1r1–4)

End

āvāhanaprakaraślok⟨k⟩aṃ mātṛkāmbhojaṃ trikoṇaṣaṭkoṇagarbbhaṃ pūjāyantraṃ ||

pīṭhaśaktināśanamantras tu ekākṣarīvat || ||

dhyānaṃ ||

navaratnamayadvīpaṃ, smared ikṣurasāmbudhau ||

tadvīcidhau tarppantaṃ, mandamārutasevitaṃ ||

mandārapārijātādikalpavṛkṣalatākulaṃ ||

udbhūtaratnacchāyābhi,r aruṇīkṛta (fol. 29v8–9)
