Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

śrītripurasundarīcaraṇakamalebhyo namaḥ || ||

kāṃtāḥ kacattavayapuṣpasugaṃdhigaṃdhar (!)

nnubrabhramar (!) bhramarakarburakaṃdarāryā ||

gaṃdharvayakṣasurasiddhakirīṭakoṭi

saṃghaṭṭaduṣṭacaraṇāya namaḥ śivāya || 1 ||

namāmi sadguruṃ śāṃtaṃ pratyakṣaṃ śivarūpiṇaṃ ||

śirasā yogapīṭhasthaṃ muktikāmārthasiddhaye || 2 ||

śire siṃdūrasaṃdohajagaccaitanyakāriṇī |

tāṃ namāmi mahādevīpaṃcamīm ātmarūpiṇīṃ || 3 || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

gaṃdharvarūpavān bhūtvā saṃpūjyam amareśvarī ||

rakṣāmaṃtraṃ paṭhitvā tu durllabhaṃ labhate dhruvaṃ || 141 ||

ati duḥkālaye ghore bhīmeni ivaṃdhane ||

tatra yastu phaṭhed bhaktyā dhruvaṃ mucyeta baṃdhanāt || 142 ||

attyantarogayuktau vā yadi devīṃ prapūjayet ||

samyag vā ślokam ekaṃ cāsadyo rogātpramucyate || 143 ||

mohanastaṃbhanākarṣamāraṇoccāṭanaṃ tathā ||

kalpanād eva jāyaṃte sarvasiddhi pāmuṃ (!) labhet || 144 ||

idaṃ tripurasuṃdarjyā (!) stavarājamanoharaṃ ||

rakṣāmaṃtraṃ ca śubhadaṃ śivena parikīrttitaṃ || 145 ||

chaṃtraṃ mūrddhni sahasrapatrakamalaṃ vaktraṃ sudhāvarṣaṇaṃ

divyastrīparipāyyamānamamidaṃ cātmānamevaṃ sadā ||

dhyānayetmālayaṃ bhaje bhagavatīṃ ratnāmapūrvāsane

stotraṃ yastu samastam āsu labhate rājaspadaṃ (!) bhūtale || 146 || (fol. 8r4–10)
