Excerpts

Beginning

|| śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ |

devaḥ pūrvaṃ purā rātiḥ puratrayajayodyame |

anarcanād gaṇeśasya jato vighnākulaḥ kila || 1 || (fol. 1v1–2)

End

anyathā śaraṇaṃ nāsti tvam eva śaraṇaṃ mama |

tasmāt kāruṇyabhāvena rakṣa rakṣa gaṇeśvara || 14 ||

yad akṣara[ṃ] pada[ṃ] bhraṣṭaṃ svaraṃ vyaṃjanavarjitaṃ |

tat sarvaṃ kṣamyatāṃ deva prasīda gaṇanāyaka || 15 || (fol. 12r11–13)

Colophon

iti śrīpadmapurāṇe mahāgaṇapatisahasranāmastotraṃ saṃpūrṇaṃ || ❁ ||

śrīmahāgaṇapati[ḥ] prīyatāṃ || ❁ ||    ||    ||     ||     ||     || (fol. 12rv1–2)

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || (the latter veneration formula seems to have been added later)
