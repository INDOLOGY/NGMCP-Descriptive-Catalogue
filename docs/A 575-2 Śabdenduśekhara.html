Manuscript Features

The text runs from the beginning of the Saṃjñā chapter to the end of the Samāsāśrayavidhi chapter.

A stamp of the Nepal Rastriya Pustakalaya appears on fol. 1r.

Excerpts

Beginning

|| śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

nāgeśaḥ kila nāgeśabhāṣitārthavicakṣaṇaḥ

śivabhaṭṭasuto dhīmān upādhyāyopanāmakaḥ

yācakānāṃ kālpataror (!) arikaṣahutāsanāt |

śṛṅgaverapurādhīśarāmato labdhajīvakaḥ |

natvā phaṇīśam īśānaṃ kaumudyarthaprakāśakam |

manoramomārdhadehaṃ tanve śabdenduśekharaṃ | (fol. 1v1–2)

End

ākṛtīti, tena kaṃ karotīti vigrahe vūjo ḍupratyaya kiṣku, śatāt parāṇi paraśātāni///7 tparanipāta ityādi siddhaṃ, tatra paṃcamīsamāse lokāśrayatvālliṃgasya liṃgasya viśeṣyanighnatāṃ anyathā paravālliṃgatā (!) syāt, aṃtye tu samyag eveti (fol. 383v9–10)
