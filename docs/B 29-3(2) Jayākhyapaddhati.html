Manuscript Features

This text begins on fol. 108r3 leaving the sixth verse of the 30th paṭala of the Jayākhya juhuyāt kuṅkume (30.6c).

Excerpts

Beginning

yāgālayaṃ hi viśveśa gṛhāṇa racitaṃ mayā |
āsamāptiṃ bhaja vibho kriyāṅgānāñ ca sarvvataḥ |
āmantri (!) si de〇veśa kṛto yāgo yathāvidhiṃ (!)
taṃma (!) harasva bhagavan sarvvakarmaprapūraṇaṃ |
āmantrita jagannātha salakṣmyādigaṇai (!) saha |
sānidhyaṅ kuru deveśa pavitrārthaṃ māmā(!)dhvare |
samakṣaṃ bhagavatā bhaktyā svaprabhuṃ pūjayāmy ahaṃ ||
saṃnidhānam ata kāryaṃ madanugrahakāmyayā ||

iti prārthanā || (fol 108r3–5)

End

sarvvakāmārthasaṃdohe iha kāsyapi raṃmyatā ||
nava pañca catasro vā vinā ku(mbha) kva cin matāṃ ||
astrāmbhasā tataḥ snātvā hutvā mantrañ ca tatmayīṃ ||
śaktiśatyādimṛtīnāṃ dhyātvā tatvaṃñ ca vinyaset |
hṛdā saṃpuṭayogena svamantrena tu vinyaset ||
sanidhākṛtyaṃ sarodha saṃpūjyas tatra vinyaset |
sāntikumbhāmbhasā siñcet pādanyāsam ata smṛtam ||
prāsādañ ca tatra kuryāt tasya lakṣaṇam ucyate || devaḥ || (fol. 116r4–6)

Colophon

iti śrījayākṣarasaṃhitāyā ⟪śrī⟫ savidhi samāptaḥ paṇḍitena sodhanīya śubhaḥ || samvat 515 māghaśudi ekādasyā tithau || || (fol. 116r6)
