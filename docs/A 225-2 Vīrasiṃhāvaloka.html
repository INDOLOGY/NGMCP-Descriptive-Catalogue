Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

yac-cakṣur bhuvanasya divyam amalaṃ dhāmaśrutīnāṃ pana (!)

yad brahmaikaniketanaṃ ca payasāṃ bījaṃ yad ekaṃ mataṃ

satreṣv agni hutasya veda viditasthānaṃ ca yat sarvvadā

ta(2)d vaḥ pātu sanātanaṃ harimahas tattvaprabodhodayaṃ 1

daivajñāgamadharmaśāstranigamāyurvedadugdhodadhīn

āmathyas phuradālabuddhikiraṇā viśvopakārojjvalaṃ |

ā(3)lokyāmṛtamātanoti vibudhair āsevayam alpadbhutaṃ

śrīmaṃtomaradevaśarmmatanayaḥ śrīvīrasiṃho nṛpaḥ 3

iha khalu puruṣārthacatuṣṭayāya dhīmatāṃ pravṛttiḥ | (fol.1v1–3)

End

jñātaṃ jñavyaṃ (!) kuḍavād ūrdvaṃ prasthādiṣv iti mā(1)navaḥ ||

dviguṇyena tulāmāna (!) iti mānavido viduḥ |

droṇī droṇāṭhaka(!) prasthaṃ kuḍavaś ca palaṃ picuḥ ||

ṣāṇako māṣakaś caiva yathā pūrvve caturguṇaṃ |

samyak prayogaḥ sarvveṣāṃ siddhir ākhyāta(2)karmaṇāṃ ||

siddhir ākhyāti sarveṣu guṇair yukto bhiṣagvaraḥ || (fol.68v17–69r2)

Colophon

|| iti śrītomaravaṃśāvataṃsa nipubhūta (!) bhairava śrīvīrasiṃhadevaviracite graṃthe (3) vīrasiṃhāvaloke jyotiḥśāstrakarmavipākāyurvedoktaprayogo (!) miśrakādhyāyaḥ samāptaḥ || śubham astu saṃvat 1801 mī(4)tī (!) aṣāḍha vāra somārake (!) pustakaṃ samāpta (!) || śrī rūpanārāyaṇa gopāla vanābhyāṃ yatibhyām idam ālikhe || śrīkṛṣṇo jayati ||(5)

śrīrāmo jayati || nārāyana (!) namastu te || śrīnārasiṃhyā (!) namaḥ idaṃ pustaga (!) līṣīta (!) nārāyaṇe namastu te | gaṃgāgaurī jālapāmāi gaṃga sahāi (fol.69r2–5)
