Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || || 

śrībhairava uvāca || 

adhunā devi vakṣye haṃ rahasyaṃ paramādbhutaṃ || 

yattat sarve(2)ṣu taṃtreṣu yāmalādiṣu bhāṣitaṃ || 

purā devīrahasyāsyaṃ taṃtraṃ maṃtroccvigrahaṃ || 

tatvaṃ (!) śrīṣoḍaśākṣa(3)ryyā sarvasvaṃ mama pārvati  || 

aprakāśyaṃ mahātatvaṃ (!) paramoccaphalapradaṃ || 

bhogāpavargadaṃ loke sādha(4)kānāṃ sukhāvahaṃ || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

śrībhairava uvāca || 

imaṃ devirahasyākhyaṃ (!) taṃtrarājaṃ maheśvari || 

sarvasiddhimayaṃ gopyaṃ gopanīyaṃ maheśvari || 

adātavyo mabhaktebhyo (!) durātmabhyo maheśvari || 

svaputrebhyo nyaśiṣibhyo (1) na deyaṃ tu mumukṣubhiḥ || 

idaṃ sāraṃ hi taṃtrāṇāṃ tattvaṃ sarvasvam uttamaṃ || 

rahasyaṃ devi durgāyā gopanīyaṃ (2) svayonivat || (fol. 187r8–9&1–2)

Colophon

|| iti śrīrudrayāmalataṃtre devīrahasye ṣaṣṭitamaḥ paṭalaḥ || || 60 || || śubhaṃ (fol. 187v2)
