Manuscript Features

On the front cover-leaf is written:

śaktibījādikā devī pātu nityaṃ ramākṣarī
brahmāṇī pātu me pūrve vaiṣṇavī bahnikoṇake

māheśvarī tathā yāmye indrāṇī naiṛte tathā
paścime pātu niyataṃ nārasiṃhī bhayaṃkarī

mārute pātu vārā‥ kaubere śivadūtikā
aiśānyā bhairavī devī pātu trailokyavanditā

urdhve pātu mahādevī mahiṣamarddinī
iti te kathitaṃ devī kavacaṃ devadurllabhaṃ

yaḥ paṭhet sādhakaḥ śreṣṭo rūpavān vijayī naraḥ
datvā puṣpāṃjaliṃ devyai yaḥ paṭhet kavacaṃ paraṃ

…

abhaktebhyo svaśiṣyebhoḥ datvā mṛtyum avāpnuyāt

Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

śrībālātripurasundaryyai namaḥ ||

śrīdevy uvāca |

bālāpūjāyutā nātha vidyāñ ca sakalās tava ||
sāṃprataṃ śrotum icchāmi kavacaṃ maṃtravigrahaṃ || 1 ||

trailokyamohanaṃ nāma sarvāpadvinivāraṇaṃ ||
puraiva sūcitaṃ nātha kṛpayā me prakāśaya || 2 ||    ||

śrībhairava uvāca ||    ||

devadānavavdyādhṛk pūjite prāṇavallabhe ||
trailokyamohanaṃ nāma ka[[va]]caṃ śrūyatāṃ paraṃ || 3 ||

sarvavidyāmayaṃ devi tava maṃtramayam dhruvaṃ ||
sarvarakṣākaraṃ sarvasiddhividyāpradāyakaṃ || 4 || (fol. 1v1–6)

End

paṃcavarṣasahasrāṇaṃ pūjāphalam avāpnuyāt ||
bhūrje vilikya guṭikāṃ svarṇasthāṃ dhārayed yadi || 22 ||

puruṣo dakṣiṇe bāhau yoṣiddhāmabhuje tathā ||
tadgātraṃ prāpya śastrāṇi brahmāstrādīni bharavi || 23 ||

mālyāni kusumāny eva bhavaṃti sukhadāni ca ||
tasya g⟨e⟩ṛ sthirā lakṣmī bāṇīvaktre vased dhruvaṃ || 24 ||    || (fol. 3r4–3v1)

Colophon

iti śrībhaiavataṃtre śrībālākavacaṃ sampūrṇam ||    || śubham ||    || śrīrāmaḥ ||    || rāmaḥ ||    || rāmaḥ ||    || rāmaḥ ||    || rāmaḥ ||    || (fol. 3v2–3)
