Beginning

❖ namaḥ sarvvajñāya ||

sārvaṃ praṇamya sarvvajñaṃ durggaguptasya sūnunā |

saṃhitā siddhasāreyaṃ raviguptena vakṣyate ||

āyurvedodadhin tarttum aśaktā ye lpamedhasaḥ |

teṣām iyaṃ pratārāya vihitā tantranau (!) dṛḍhā ||

brahmā provāca yaṃ svargge vedam āyurnibandhanam |

śiṣyebhyaḥ kathayāmāsa kāśirājo pi taṃ kramāt ||

tasya tv aṃgāni śālākyaṃ kāyabhūtacikitsitam |

śalyāgadavayobālarakṣābījavivarddhanam ||

puruṣo vyādhyadhiṣṭhānaṃ mahābhūtaguṇātmakam |

śārīramānasāgantusahajā vyādhayo matāḥ ||

śārīrā jvarakuṣṭhādyāḥ krodhādyā mānasāḥ smṛtāḥ |

āgantavo vighātotthāḥ sahajāḥ kṣuttṛḍādayaḥ || (fol. 1v1–3)
