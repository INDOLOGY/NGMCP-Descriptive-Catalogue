Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ śrīsaraśvatyai namaḥ śrīkāśī viśvanāthāya namaḥ śriannapurṇā[[yai]](!) namaḥ śrīgurubhyo namaḥ śrīśārṅgadhara(!) likhyate || ❁ ||

śrīyaṃ(!) sa dadyād bhavatāṃ purārir yadaṃgatejaḥ prasare bhavānī ||

virājate nirmalacaṃdrikāyāṃ mahauṣadhīva jvalitā himādrau || 1 ||

prasiddhayogā munibhiḥ prayuktāḥ cikitsakair ye bahuśonubhūtāḥ

vidhīyate śārṅgadhareṇa teṣāṃ susaṃgrahaḥ sajjana raṃjanāyā || 2 ||

(fol.1v1-6)

End

āyurvedasamudrasya guḍharthasyāni(!) saṃyataḥ |

jñātaḥ kaiścid budhais tas(!) tu kṛtā vividhasaṃhitā ||

kaṃcid arthaṃ tato nītvā kṛteyaṃ sa hi mayā ||

kṛpākaṭākṣavikṣepam asyāṃ vvraddhaṃti(!) jaṃtavaḥ |

vividhagadārttidaridranāśaṃ yā hariramaṇīva karoti saūbhagaṃ yaḥ ||

vilasatu sa śārṅgadharasya saṃhitā sa kavihṛdayeṣu sarojanirmmaleṣū || ❁ ||

(fol.155r2-6)

Colophon

iti śrīśārṅgadharacikitsā samāpta(!)||

śrībhavānīśaṃkarābhyāṃ namaḥ || śubham astu || 6 || śrīkalyāṇam astu ||

mohanajibhahālajamiṭhālahena svārthaṃ paropakārārthaṃ śārṅadharākhyaṃ pustakaṃ yal likhitaṃ tat śrīmatkāśyāṃ kṣayasavatsare pauṣamāsi śuklapakṣe trītiyāyāṃ(!) samāptaṃ || || śubhaṃ bhavatu || śrīsadāśivārpaṇam astu (fol.155r6-11)
