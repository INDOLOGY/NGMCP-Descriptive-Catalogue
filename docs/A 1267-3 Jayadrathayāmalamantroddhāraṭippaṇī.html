«Beginning»


❖ oṃ namo vīranāthāya namaḥ ||

paṃcaśrotaḥprapaṃcottulyakāladhvaninidhanapade yeṣu vidyāśrayeṣu |

śaś cettānyaṃ ca yāto mukhet saptamaḥ |

kuṇḍalārṇṇā yasya vyaktāḥ

karāgreṣv apaharatu savaḥ sarvvavighnān naśed yaḥ ||

yān yuṣmān akṣamāyā ṣaḍrahasi mahākuṇḍalīhaṃ sarūpaīr

aṣṭābhir vāgbhavendraiḥ kalayati kila yā tulatāṃ vāglatāyāḥ |

ādyaspandākhyadivyāraṇimathitamahāhaṃsagarbhapraveśān

sābhivyaktā caturddhā nivasatu satataṃ vāci vāgīśvarī vaḥ || 2 ||

ādau nādamayaṃ tatombaravapuḥ paṃcāśadātmakramāt

mantraiḥ paṃcabhir utthitākṛti punaḥ sādāśive dhāmni yaḥ |

śabdabrahmavilāsa ity abhibhutakailāśaśailāśanaiḥ

śrīśrīkaṇṭha iti śrutaṃ jayati tad viśvaprakāśaṃ mahaḥ || 3 || (fol. 1v1–6)


«End»

śrīmadaparājitāṃ vidyāṃ rājñīmantrottamātraṃ jayāvijayājayantīnāṃ caturthe śrī mreṃ amṛte amṛtodvahe srāṃ jīvati nra āpyāyati mrau ajite sraḥ aparājite || khphreṃ svāhā | puṣpe kuṃkumacandanakarppūrāgurukastūrikā rocanā kumārīpaṃcakaśca kaṃ bhojayitvā prativarṇaṃ kramāryyapananavidhinātyaparājitāṃ lekhayet śikhāyāṃ bandhayed rasaṃgrāme śakram api jayati ||

iti śubha ||

āgamād āgataṃ sarvvaṃ yatra paśyanti dīkṣitā |

samāgacchati muktiś ca soyam āgamam ucyate ||

āṅmaryyādā iti proktāḥ samajñānārthavācakaḥ |

tattvam āgamyate yena sa āgama iti smṛtaḥ ||

jyogi khaṃ grahaṇaṃ vidyā gāruḍaviṣatakṣaṇaṃ

śive tattvaparijñānaṃ kaule vedhaprakīrttitaṃ || (fol. 64r2–7)


«Colophon»

saṃ 704 mārgga śukla 6 ravau || || (fol. 64r7)
