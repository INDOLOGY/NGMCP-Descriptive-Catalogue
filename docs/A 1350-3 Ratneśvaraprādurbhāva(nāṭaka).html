Manuscript Features

The manuscript contains four aṅkas of the nāṭaka. Many folios are lost in the middle.

The handwriting changes in fol. 12r.

In fols. 21, 28, 55v and 56v the letters are slightly disrupted.

Excerpts

Beginning

nṛtyeśvarāya namaḥ ||    ||

atha nāndīślokaḥ ||

yasyāṅge girinandinī suranadī śīrṣe sadā rājate,
bhāle candrakalā śrutau phaṇipatiḥ kaṇṭhe mahānīlimā |
prītir bhaktajane ratiḥ pitṛivane keliś ca sārdhaṅganaiḥ (!)
sosmān pātu sadāśivas trijagatām īśo vibhūs sarvadaḥ ||    ||

nāndima || mālava || (ja yū ya a pra e pra ru) || jayajaya | karakairavaśaśadharasundaradeha, āre | himakaramaṇḍitapiṅgalajaṭataṭanaṭagṛharacitasugeho raviśaśianalanayane || (fol. 1v1–4)

End
