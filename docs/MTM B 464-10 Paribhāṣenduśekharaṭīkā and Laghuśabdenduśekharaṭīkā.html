Manuscript Features

This MTM contains the following texts:

1. Paribhāṣenduśekharaṭīkā (fols. 1–19 (1st foliation) and 1–21 (2nd foliation)

2. Laghuśabdenduśekharaṭīkā (fols. 1–67 3rd foliation)

Fols. 3r, 11v, 2 (3rd foliation), 7r (3rd foliation) contain considerable marginal notes.

Few lines of fols. 19r and 19v (1st foliation) are missing.

A folio, numbered as figure 13 in the recto side of the upper left-hand margin, is inserted between fols. 13 and 14. The inserted folio contains ṭippaṇī (comment or notes) of the text.

The Foliation of the MS appears as follows:

Fols. 1–19: figures on the lower right-hand margin of the verso under the word rāmaḥ

Fols. 1–22: figures in the extreme lower right-had margin of the verso; it seems to be added later

Fols. 1–67: The foliation of these folios are not in proper order. At the beginning, it runs continuously but somewhere from the middle it is done randomly.

There are two exposures of fols. 55v–56r (3rd foliation).
