yadipādvā dvitaya prasādata || dvikilāṃkatritaya nama || skṛtaṃ kṛtaṃ kalasthīla niryyalavāli vinduvat || thva pṛthvī maṇḍala sa || sahastu rājāpatisyaṃ thvanamhaṃ rājāyā carana kamalasa || savarapaṃcāthvathathiṃgvarāṃ cakravartti śrīvamnta, vikramakeśarināmadasyaṃ cogva, indranila, marakatapadamarāga vajravaidurjamuktāratna sasuharana kuvalayā, saṃlpattir thededayakayu, (fol. 1v1–6)
