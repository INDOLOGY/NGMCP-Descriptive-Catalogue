Manuscript Features

On the exp. 2 is written

sarvatīrtheṣu sa snātaḥ sarvayajñeṣu dīkṣitaḥ |

abhayaṃ sarvabhūtebhyo dattamatra digāṃdara (!)

paṃgvandhabālavṛddhānāṃ rogiṇāṃ †mvadarihniṇāṃ†

yo puṣṭāṃti (!) sadā vaiśya te modaṃte sadā divi

narakepi ciraṃ magnāḥ pūrvajāyakulānvaye (!)

tadaiva yānti te svargaṃ yadārcati †su no hariṃ†

Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

puṣpadhanvā vijayate naṃgenāpy akhilaṃ jagat ||

vaśīkṛtaṃ yena haṭhāt sa surāsuramānuṣaṃ || 1 ||

puṃsāṃ sarvasukhaika(2)sādhanaphalāḥ saundaryagarvoddhura-

krīḍālolavilāsinījanamanaḥsphītapramodāvahāḥ ||

gunjadbhṛṃgavinidrapaṃkajabharasphārollasaddī(3)rghikā-

aṃ(3)kolatailabhāvitamuṣitaṃ go 'rākṛtikumudakuṃdamalaṃ ||
