Manuscript Features

There are five separate foliations 1st up to 59, 2nd up to 14, 3rd up to 7, 4th up to 5 and 5th up to 5.

There are two exposures of the fols. 8v–9r (1st foliation), 5v–6r (4th foliation).

There are tabular illustrations on fols. 29r (1st foliation) and 4v (5th foliation).

There is an impress of the seal of Nepal National Library dated 2013 [VS] on the front cover leaf.
