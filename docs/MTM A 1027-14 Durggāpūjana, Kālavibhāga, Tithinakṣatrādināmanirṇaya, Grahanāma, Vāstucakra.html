Manuscript Features

This MTM contains the following texts:

1. Durggāpūjana (fol. 1v1–4r4)

2. Kālavibhāga ( fol. 101v4–5)

3. Tithinakṣatrādināmanirṇaya (fol. 1v1–4r5, exp. 13)

4. Grahanāma (exp. 16t1–17b1)

5. Vāstucakra (exp. 17b2–4)

In the left hand margin of most of the leaves, there are the summary titles of the text.
