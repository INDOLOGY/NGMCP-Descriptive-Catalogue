Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīvāgīśvarāya ||

oṃ nama gaṇesāhāyaḥ(!) ||

oṃ namo śrībhailavāya ||

oṃ namo guruve namaḥ || ❁ ||


pūrāpurvvakālasa nikhadhanagara dhāyāgu desa chaguri da(2)sya coṅa, thva deśasa virasyana dhāyāhma rājā basarapaṃ coṅa || thvayā putra rājakumāla, nara, dhāyāhma rājakumāra dasye coṅa, thva gathiṃhma rājakumāra dhārasā, anega gu(3)nana saṃjukta juyāo coṅa, jura, lvāyagurisa heo, tejaoṃta dhārasā, kāmadeothyaṃ, vānarāka, thvayā sarilasa khuna dhārasāṃ chunaṃ madu, māhātyāgī vicakṣana mā(4)hādharmātmā, thathiṃnahma nara rājakumāra basarapaṃ coṅa || 1 || (fol. 1v-1-4)


«Middle:»

thanali oṃkārāṃtarasa, parameśvara damana likhiśvarayā, kṛpā āsikhana, atyaṃtana suṃdari, ratidevio samāna juyāo conahma, damayaṃti kaṃ(7)nyā jarama juraṃ, thvanaṃri datta, damanaputra jarama juraṃ, thuti putri putrayā sahitana ānaṃndana bijyākatvaṃ juraṃ || ❁ || (fol. 3r6-7)


End

tao 2 dhana dharma dāna yānāo saṃkhvāpā, paṃjādā[[na]] yityādiṃ yānāo mahāsukhana conaṃ || thanari, nara rājāna jurasāṃ prajāroka, mantrīprabhiti sahita yāṅāo(2) rājya bhoge yāṅāo sadāsarvakāra, thugu prakārana, karunācita yāṅāo, dharmmana jhitaya yāṅāo punyayā sarila juyā, mahāharṣana sukha ānandana bijyā(3)ka juro || || thva nara rājāyā vyākhyāna, gvahmasyanaṃ kanio, gvahmasyanaṃ ṅenio, thvamita gvaverasaṃ, arakṣina dhāyāgu dayuo makhu, rakṣmina saṃjukta(4) juio, pāpa dayāo conasāṃ, pāpamocana juyuo, thvahma manuṣyayā manavāṃchā purṇṇa juyīo dhaka thva śāstrana dhāraṃ || || (fol. 51v1-4)


Colophon

iti śrīnaravyākhyā(5)na samāpta || || śubha || saṃvat 951 miti āṣāḍha śuklayā || cauthiyāṃ mahāpuṇyati || ❁ ||thau || madyānakṣetre (6) buddhavāra || ❁ || thva ṣuhnu coyadhunakā juro || ❁ || likhita erayā śrīmānadevasaṃskārita śrīcakramahāvihāla nivāśita śrīvajrā(7)cāryya śrījñānadattadevana likhitaṃ || ❁ || dānapati eradesayā, carācheyā cakradhara utrāpanthakutideśasa dikavelasa dharmacitta utpatti juyāo thvana (fol. 51v4-7)
