Title (Vājasaneyi-)Prātiśākhyabhāṣya

Remarks also styled Mātṛmodaka (Pārṣadabhāṣya), but here called Mātṛkodaka Pārṣadabhāṣya in two colophons; commentary on Kātyāyana’s Vājasaneyiprātiśākhya, sometimes styled Śuklayajuḥprātiśākhyam

Author Uvaṭa, son of Bhaṭṭavajraṭa (the colophons have Uaṭa and Ūaṭa)

Subject Vyākaraṇa

Language Sanskrit

Place of Deposit NAK

Manuscript Features

Foll. 49–50, 101, and a few foll. at the end of this MS are missing.

Most of the foll. are worm-eaten to a certain extent. Of some 40 foll, pieces have broken off.

Exp. 2 shows a cover leaf, exp. 3 the back of fol. 1. The text starts on exp. 4 (fol.1v).

The last extant fol. is fol. 114 (exp. 113 below –114 above), commenting up to sūtra 8.63. Thus, the commentary on sūtras 8.64–69 is missing.

Exp. 114 below –115 show fol. 113 (sūtras 8.43–58).

Exp. 116 shows another cover leaf.
