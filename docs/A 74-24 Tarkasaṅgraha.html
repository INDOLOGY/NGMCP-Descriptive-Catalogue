Title Tarkasaṃgrahadīpikāprakāśa

Remarks The Tarkasaṃgrahadīpikāprakāśa, also known as Nīlakaṇṭhaprakāśikā, or simply as Nīlakaṇṭhī, is a commentary on the Tarkasaṃgrahadīpikā, itself an autocommentary by Annambhaṭṭa on his own Tarkasaṃgraha.

Manuscript Features

The preserved part of the MS contains Nīlakaṇṭha’s commentary on sections 1–56 (including the discussion of asiddha) and the very end of the commentary.

The MS is dated; however, only the year is indicated, both as ŚS 1742 and VS 1877 (in the second case only saṃvat is written). This corresponds to 1820 AD.
