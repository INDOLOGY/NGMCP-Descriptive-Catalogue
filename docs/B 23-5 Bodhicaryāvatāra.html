«Beginning»

❖ namo mañjunāthāya ||

sugatān sasutān sadharmmakāyān 

		praṇipatyādarato ʼkhilāñ(!) ca vandyān |

sugatātmaja<sahaja>sambarāvatāraṃ 

kathayiṣyāmi yathāgama(!) samāsāt ||

na hi kiñcid  apūrvam atra vācyan

na ca saṃgranthanakauśalaṃ mamāsti |

ata eva na me parārtha<<ā..>>cintā

svamano bhāvayituṃ kṛtaṃ mayedam ||

mama tāva⌠⌠d a⌡⌡nena yāti vṛddhiṃ

kuśalaṃ bhāvayituṃ prasādavegaḥ ||

atha matsamadhātur eva paśyed

aparo ⌠ ʼ⌡py enam ato ⌠ ʼ⌡pi sārthako ⌠ʼ⌡yaṃ || (fol. 1v1–4)
