Excerpts

Beginning

«mūla:»

ṣrīgurubhyo namaḥ || vaśiṣṭha uvācaḥ ||

dikkālādyanavacchinnānantacinmātramūrttaye |
svānubhūtyekamānāya namaḥ śāntāya tejase || 1 ||

ahaṃ baddho vimuktaḥ syām iti yasyāsti niścayaḥ |
nātyantam ajño no [’]tajñaḥ so smin śāstre dhikāravān || 2 || (fol. 1v5–7)

«commentary:»

oṃ so [’]haṃ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ❖ || dikkāleti || koho kālāntasamayamā śrīvaśiṣṭhamūni jo chan so yekai paramātmā pani ananta bhai vyāpaka bhayākā, āphai tejasvarupa bhai, cinmūrtti caitanya brahmako anubhava gari, paihle maṃgalācaraṇakā ritile gari śānta svarūpa bhayākā śrīrāmacandrakana śikṣā gardachan || 1 || aham iti || ahaṃ ma baddha bhairahyāko chu so kauna prakārale mukta huṃlā (fol. 1v1–4)

End

«mūla:»

yathā na putrikāśūnyaḥ staṃbho nutkīrṇaputrikāḥ (!) ||
tathā bhātaṃ jagad brahma tena śūnyaṃ na tatpadam || 34 ||
saumyāṃbhasi yathā vīcir na cāsti na ca nāsti caḥ ||
tathā jagad brahmaṇīdaṃ śūnyāśūnyaṃ pada (!) gatam || 35 || || (fol. 31v4–6)

«commentary:»

saumyāṃbhasiti || sundara nirmala jalamā taraṃga hunchan pani huṃdainan pani, yestai tarahale garikana brahmamā pani yo jagat jo cha so laya huṃdā śūnya huncha, pheri śṛṣṭi huṃdā yo jagat sava deṣiṃcha, tapani śūnyāśūnyeko kāraṇa jagatkai ho brahmamā chaina bhani śrīvasiṣṭhamuni jo cha so śrīrāmacandrakana kahyāko yo mūla ṭikā bhāṣāko anusaṃdhāna gari śrīambaragira yogīle āphnā satsaṃgamā kahaṃdā bhayā || 35 || ❁ || (fol. 31v8–32r1)

Colophon

«mūla:»

iti śrīyogavāśiṣṭhasāre jñānatatvanirūpaṇaṃ nāma daṣamaḥ prakarṇam || 10 || iti śrīleṣanārthe padmadāsabhaktaḥ (fol. 31v6, 32r3–4)

«commentary:»

iti śrīyogavāśiṣṭhasāre jñānatatvanirūpaṇaṃ nāma daṣamaḥ prakarṇam || 10 || ❁ || iti śrīsamvat 1912 sāla miti kārtika śudi 11 roja 1 tasmīn dine likhitaṃ pustakaṃ śuddho vā maśuddho vā mama deṣa na diyete || śrīrāmāya namaḥ śrīlakṣmaṇāya naḥ śrīhanumānaḥ (fol. 32r2, 5–6)
