Manuscript Features

Some folios are damaged in the right margins. The last folio (after no. 19) is not numbered, and covered with praises of gods on the recto. The verso is left blank.

Beginning

oṃ namo vighneśāya ||

nārāyaṇaṃ namaskṛtya narañ caiva narottamaṃ |
devīṃ sarasvatīñ caiva tato jayam udīrayet ||
jayati parāśarasūnuḥ satyavatīhṛdayanandano vyāsaḥ |
yasyāsyakamalagalitaṃ vāgmayam amṛtaṃ jagat pibati ||
ṛṣayo naimiṣāraṇye śrutvā nāmasahasrakaṃ |
mukhyaṃ śivarahasyañ ca bhūyo devarṣim abruvan |
svāyambhuva kṛtārthā sma(!) tvadanugrahato vayaṃ |
yadd(!) rahasyaṃ harer nnamnāṃ(!) śrutaṃ tvanmukhapaṅkajāt |
idānīm īdṛśaṃ dharmmaṃ harer atyantatoṣaṇaṃ |
vrataṃ kiñ cid vadāsmākaṃ dṛḍhabhaktir yyathā harau ||
nārada uvāca || (fol. 1v1-3)
