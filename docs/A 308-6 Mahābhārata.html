Manuscript Features

The text starts from the 29th adhyāya of Śalyaparvan and runs up to the end of the Śalyaparvan (64th adhyāya). Those adhyāyas are known as Tīrthayātrā(upa)parvan (29th adhyāya–53rd adhyāya) and Gadāyuddha(upa)parvan (54th adhyāya–64th adhyāya) (POONA EDITION).

Excerpts

Beginning

|| śrīgurugaṇeśāya namaḥ ||     ||

nārāyaṇaṃ namaskṛtya naraṃ caiva narottamaṃ ||

devīṃ sarasvatīṃ caiva tato jayam udīrayet || 1 ||

dhṛtarāṣṭra uvāca ||

hateṣu sarvasainyeṣu pāṇḍuputrai raṇājire ||

mama śainyāvaśiṣṭās te kim akurvata saṃjaya || 2 ||

kṛtavarmā kṛta(!)ś caiva droṇaputraś ca vīryavān |

duryodhanas tu maṃdātmā rājā kim akarot tadā || 3 ||

saṃjaya uvāca ||

saṃprādravatsu dāreṣu kṣatriyāṇāṃ mahātmanāṃ |

vidrute śibire śūnye bhṛśodvignās trayo rathaḥ || 4 ||

niśamya pāṇḍuputrāṇāṃ tadā vaijayināṃ svanam ||

vidrutaṃ śibiraṃ dṛṣṭvā sāyāhne rājagṛddhinaḥ || 5 ||

sthānaṃ nārocayaṃs tatra tatas te hradam abhyayuḥ |

dudhiṣṭhiro pi dharmātmā bhrātṛbhiḥ sahito raṇe | (fol. 1v1–4)

End

rājño niyogād yodha(!)vyaṃ brāhmaṇena viśeṣataḥ ||

varttatāṃ kṣatradharmeṇa hy evaṃ dharmavido viduḥ ||

rājñas tu vacanaṃ śrutvā kṛpaḥ śāradvatas tataḥ ||

drauṇiṃ rājño niyogena saināpatye bhyaṣecayat ||

so bhiṣikto mahārāja pariṣvajya nṛpottamaṃ ||

prayayau siṃhanādena diśaḥ savā(!) vinodayan ||

duryodhano pi rājeṃndra śoṇitena pariplutaḥ ||

tāṃ niśāṃ pratipede tha saravabhūtabhayāvahāṃ ||

apakramya tu te tūrṇaṃ tasmād āyodhanān nṛpaḥ(!) ||

śokasaṃvignamanasaś ciṃtādhyānaparābhavan || 46 || (fol. 55r1–7)

Colophon

iti śrīmahābhārate śatasāhasryāṃ saṃhitāyāṃ vaiyāsikyāṃ gadāparvaṇi gadāyuddhaṃ samāptaṃ ||     ||

śubham astu satataṃ || || ||

śāke vahniśaśāṃkaśailaśaśabhṛd yukte maghau śyāmale

pakṣe śaivatithau śanau ca divase matsye sthite bhāskare ||

śrīdaivajña(budhājñavāra)m amṛtānaṃdābhidho lelikhat 

śrīmadbhīmavalīsuyodhanaripuṃ prāptā †upad† yatra tat || 1 || (fol. 55r7–*55v4)
