Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namo gaṇeśāya ||

yaṃ dhyāyaṃti [ni]raṃ[taraṃ] munigaṇāḥ paśyaṃti yaṃ yogino

yaṃ gāyaṃti parasparaṃ śrutigaṇāṃ(!) [ve]dāntavedyaṃ paraṃ ||

brahmādyaiḥ parisevitaṃ (!) suraguru (!) tyaktaṃ guṇaiḥ sāśvataṃ (!)

vante (!) tam puraṣottamaṃ (!) trijagatām ānandakandaṃ mahaḥ ||

natvā devendravandyaṃ tribhuvanajanakaṃ viśvanāthaṃ maheśaṃ

gau(!)viśveśavaṃdyāṃ tribhuvanajananīṃ bhāratīñ ca praṇamya ||

natvā gairīsutan ta (!) kaṃ(!)ripativadanaṃ vighnarājaṃ gaṇeśaṃ

natvā devān aśeṣān akhilabhayaharā (!) granthavaryyaṅ karomi ||

vilokya bahutantrāni(!) ya[du]nāthena dhīmatā ||

mantraratnākaro granthaḥ kriyate sārasaṃgrahaḥ || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

iha saṃttahitaḥ(!) svacittavṛtti-

pratimāyāḥ pratijalpanaṃ karoti |

gamanaṃ ca naraḥ puraḥ pareṣāṃ

punar utthāpanam apy aho mṛtasya || 

sthānasāsya jñānamātreṇa puṃsāṃ

saṃsāre[ʼ]smin asaṃbhavo naiva bhūayaḥ ||

bhūtagrāmasamtato[ʼ]bhyāsato[ʼ]smin

karttuṃ harttuṃ syāc ca śaktiḥ samagrāḥ || || || (fol. 181r8–9)

Colophon

iti śrīgauḍadeśīyamahāmahopādhyāyaśrīvidyābhūṣaṇa-bhaṭṭāccāryyātmaja||śrīyadunāthacakravarttiviracite śrīmantraratnākare daśamas taraṃgaḥ samāptaś cāyaṃ graṃthaḥ ||  || || || || (fol. 181r9–10)
