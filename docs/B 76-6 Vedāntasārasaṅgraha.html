śrīgurubhyo namaḥ || ||

oṃ

śrīakhaṃḍaṃ saccidānaṃdam avāṅmanasagocaraṃ ||

ātmānam akhilādhāram āśraye [ʼ]bhīṣṭasiddhaye ||

yad ālaṃbodaraṃ haṃti satāṃ pratyūhasaṃbhavaṃ |

tad ālaṃbe dayālaṃbaṃ laṃbodarapadāṃbujaṃ ||

arthato [ʼ]py advayānaṃdam atītadvaitalakṣaṇaṃ ||

ātmārāmam ahaṃ vaṃde śrīguruṃ śivavigrahaṃ ||

vedāṃtaśāstrasiddhāṃtasārasaṃgraha ucyate ||

prekṣāvaṃtāṃ mumukṣūṇāṃ sukhabodhopapattaye ||

asya śāstrānusāratvād anubaṃdhacatuṣṭayaṃ ||

yad eva mūlaśāstrasya nirdiṣṭaṃ tad ihocyate || (fol. 1v1–7)

End

ity uktvā svaguruṃ latvā (!) bhūyo bhūyaḥ punaḥ punaḥ ||

kṛtārtho [ʼ]smīti śiṣyo [ʼ]sau sukhaṃ tasthau samāhitaḥ ||

muktaṃ śiṣyaṃ parijñāya guruḥ saṃtuṣtamānasaḥ ||

vṛttiṃ pratyaṅmukhīṃ kṛtvā ramayāmāsa nirguṇe ||

iti vedāṃtaśāstrasya tātparyaṃ guruśiṣyayoḥ ||

saṃvādena vitirṇaṃtiṃ (!) sādhūnāṃ muktisādhanaṃ ||

śrīguruṃ sadvayānaṃdaṃ sadānaṃdaṃ ca tadguruṃ ||

tadguruṃ śaṃkarānaṃdaṃ praṇamāmi punaḥ punaḥ || ❖ || (fol. 82r9–82v4)
