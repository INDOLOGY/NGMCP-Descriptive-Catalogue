Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīgaṇeśāya ||

visphūrttrimūrttimukhamaṃmṛtabhādyabiṃbaṃ (!)

ṣaṭanetravedacaraṇaṃ (!) haranaṃ (!) dirūḍhaṃ (!) |

sindūrabhasmaracitaṃ yugāṅgarūpaṃ

prātaḥ smarāmi śivaśaktimukhāraviṃdaṃ || 1 ||

bhrūcāpacaṃcalaiṣaṭ (!) śarakāmaśubhraṃ

gaṇḍasthalaṃ lalitamauktikayuktahāraṃ |

śubhraṃ jaṭāśiraśikeśavirājamānaṃ

prātaḥ smarāmi || 2 || (exp. 23t1–4)

End

vedādiśāstramaṇimantramagamyagamyaṃ

cidbrahmavyāpakajagatañcajananījayaṃ (!) ca |

bhaktir bhaje ca bhayabhaṃjanatadvadiṣṭaṃ

prātaḥ smarāmi || 10 ||

nityaṃ paṭhet stavam idaṃ varayugārūpaṃ (!)

trailokya (!) yasyatitaraṃti (!) varaṃ bhavanti |

siddhir bhavati bhagavān dhruvaṃ ca dvisthaṃ

prātaḥ smarāmi || 11 || (exp. 23b8–24t3)

Colophon

iti śrīśaṅkarācāryyaviracitaṃ śivaśaktistotraṃ saṃpūrṇṇaṃ || śubham astu || (exp. 24t3–4)
