Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīvajrastvāya || ||

vajrasatvovācaḥ ||

nilañjananilākālaṃ śunyaśunyamahāśunyanirārambanirākāramamūrūtitatvan nahi pṛthivī || || mama vajraśarīraikaśiroṣṇīṣasvargavayaṃ pātāle pādasthāpanaṃ mudaran tu matyamaṇḍalaṃ || || (fols. 1v1–2r1)

End

pūrvvadvāre yamāntakaḥ dakṣiṇadvāre prajñāntakaḥ || paścimadvare padmāntakaḥ uttaradvāre vighnāntakaḥ || || īśānakoṇe acala || agne kone tarjjirājaḥ naiṛtyastha nīladaṇḍaḥ vāyūvya ca mahāvala || || ete tathagatavyāptadigvidisaṃ saṃsthito bhavantu vatu pṛthivīmaṇḍalaṃ bhavantuḥ vajra satva kacaḥ || ❖ || (fol. 4r5–v5)

Colophon

iti śrī śrī śrī vajrasatvakayasya tathāgatavyāptaṃ śatakaṃ parisamāptaḥ || ❖ || śubhamaṅgalaṃ bhavantuḥ || ❁ || (fol. 4v4–5)
