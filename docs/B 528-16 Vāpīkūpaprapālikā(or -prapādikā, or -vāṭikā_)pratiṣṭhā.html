Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṁ namaḥ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||


atha vāpi(!) kūpapraṇālikā pratisthālikṣate ||

tatrāpi pūjayet vāstu vibme bhāskarabhājite ||


īśasvāgnimṛgāś caiva pitṛrogam anudditiḥ ||

īśādīśāntakīleṣu sārddhadvipadanāyakā || (exp. 3b1–8)


End

oṁ ambe ambike || dhūpa dīpaphalanaivedy(!) || oṁ manojyutiḥ pratiṣṭḥā || jayastotraṃ ||


oṁ nāgapāśāmako nityaṃ jalarājo mahābalaiḥ |

nimagnaś caiva vikhyāto nāgarājāyate namaḥ ||


yathākāryasthaṇḍilārccanavidhe tatsarvvaparipūrṇṇam astu || || || tato ratyādi || svasvamantreṇā

pūjayet || || tato arthapadāsādhanaṃ || || ghṛtāhuti ghṛtāhuti pūrṇṇā‥lāhuti saṃ‥huti yathākarmma

daivadatasāmṛttikāsnānavijaṃ nyāsa daśamarkka thirahāre || (exp. 14t16–14b12)
