Manuscript Features

The available fols. are: 1–8, 1–38 and 48–87.

The MS contains the text from the strīpratyaya, kāraka, avyayībhāvasamāsa, tatpuruṣasamāsa, ajantapuṃliṅga to avyaya and samāsāśraya to dvirukta chapter.
