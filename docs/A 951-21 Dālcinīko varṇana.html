Excerpts

Beginning

śrī ❖

dālcīniko varṇana

tvacaṃ tvag valkalaṃ bhṛṃgaṃ varāṃgaṃ mukhaśodhanam

śakalaṃ saiṃhalaṃ vanyaṃ surasaṃ rāmavallbham ||

utkaṭaṃ bahugandhaṃ ca vijjulaṃ ca vanapriyaṃ (!)

lāṭaparṇaṃ gandhavalkaṃ varaṃśītaṃ tathā nṛpe ||

mukhaśodhyaṃ priyañ caiva dhanvaṃtarinighaṃṭake

hṛdayaṃ guḍapriyaṃ cocaṃ tanukaṃ svarṇabhūmikā ||

rāmāpriyaṃ dhanāhvañ ca laṭaparṇañ ca keyake

manaḥ priyaṃ tathā dravye gurutvak svarṇabhūmikam ||

madane tu dāru sitā svādvī ca tanutvak bhave (!) (exp. 1a:1–11)

End

(3) dālacīni, puṣkaramūla, śaṭhī inko cūrṇa mukhamā li rākhanāle kaṃṭha śuddha huncha svara rāmro huncha

(4) dālacīni 5 tolā, jvānu 2|| tolā, sukhamelako dānā 3 tolā, jīrā 2 tolā, alaiṃcī 1 tolā, virenuna 2 tolā,milāi khānāle pyāsa, dāha chuṭcha amalā sameta milāi khāye dhātusthānako garmi chuṭcha Shī udaya (exp.1a:50–56)
