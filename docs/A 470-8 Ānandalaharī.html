Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīparadevatāyai⟨ḥ⟩ ||

śivaḥ śaktyā yukto yadi bhavati śaktaḥ prabhavituṃ
na ced evaṃ devo na khalu kuśalaḥ spanditum api |
atas tvām ārādhyāṃ hariharaviriṃcyādibhir api
praṇaṃtuṃ stotuṃ vā katham akṛtapuṇyaḥ prabhavati || 1 ||

tanīyāṃsaṃ pāṃśuṃ tava caraṇapaṃkeruhabhavaṃ
viriṃciḥ saṃcinvan viracayati lokān avikalān |
vahaty enaṃ śauriḥ katham api sahaśreṇa śirasā
haraḥ saṃkṣudhyainaṃ bhajati bhasitoddhūlanavidhiṃ || 2 ||

avidyānām antas timiramihiradvīpanaka(!)rī
jaḍānāṃ caitanyastabakamakarandaśrutiśilā |
daridrāṇāṃ cinta(!)ma ||(!)ṇiguṇanikā janmajaladhau
nimagnānāṃ daṃṣṭrā muraripuvarāhasya bhavatī || 3 || (fols. 1v1–2r2)

End

taḍitvantaṃ śaktyā timiraparipanthisphuraṇayā
sphuran nānāratnābharaṇapariṇaddhendradhanuṣaṃ
tamaḥ śyāmaṃ meghaṃ kam api maṇipūraikaśaraṇaṃ
niṣeve varṣantaṃ haramihirataptaṃ tribhuvanaṃ || [1]39 ||

tava svādhiṣṭhāne hutavaham adhiṣṭhāya nirataṃ
tam īḍe saṃvarttaṃ jananiṃ(!) mahatīṃ tāṃ ca samayāṃ |
yadā loke lokān dahati mahati krodhakalile
dayārddrāya dṛṣṭiḥ śiśiram upacāraṃ racayati || [1]40 ||

tavādhāraṃ mūlaṃ saha samayayā lāsyaparayā
navātmānaṃ vande navarasamahātāṇḍavanaṭaṃ |
ubhābhyām etābhyāṃ ubhayam uddiśya dayayā
sanāthābhyāṃ jajñe janakajananīmaj jagad idaṃ || [1]41 || (fol. 10r1–v3)

Colophon

ity ānandalaharī samāpta(!) ||    || śrīparadevatā | prītir astu ||    || śrīi(!)ṣṭadevatā prītir astu || (fol. 10v3–4)
