Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

atha saṃnyāsapaddhati vyākhyāsyāmaḥ ||

oṃ nārāyaṇaṃ padmabhavaṃ vaśiṣṭaṃ

śaktiṃ ca tatputraparāśaraṃ ca ||

vyāsaṃ śukaṃ gauḍapadaṃ mahāṃtaṃ

goviṃdayogīṃdram athāsya śiṣyaṃ || 1 ||

śrīśaṃkarācāryam athāsya śiṣyaṃ |

pādaṃ ca hastāmalakaṃ ca śiṣyaṃ

taṃ toṭakaṃ vārttikakāram anyaṃ

asmadguruṃ saṃtatam ānato smi || 2

śrītoṭakācāryam athopaśāṃtaṃ |

nārāyaṇaṃ naṃdagiriṃ ca śiṣyaṃ

hartyutaraśrīharaśiṣyadevā

tadāgrajñānaṃ vimalaṃ ca tasya || 3 || (fols. 1v1–6)


End

tatrānubhavakṣetra syād viśvarūposya devatā |

devī caitanyaśaktiḥ syād ācāryasadgurus tathā | 22 ||

sakṣetraṃ śravaṇaṃ snānaṃ janmottpattivināśakaṃ

pūrṇānaṃdakrameṇaiva saṃnyāsa tatra cāśrayāt || 23 ||

pūrvācāryaiḥ kṛtā caiṣā saptāmnāyapraṇālikā |

saptāmnāye tu yo vetti sa yogī pūrṇa ucyate || 24 || (fol. 4r3–7)


Colophon

iti śrīmatsaṃkarācāryapraṇālikā saptāmnāyasaṃpūrṇaṃ || || likhitaṃ voharāmūlānaṃdaṃ || || dodhapure || saṃ 1890 jyeṣṭa suda paṃcadasī || || oṃ tatsat hariyoṃ (!) || || oṃ || || oṃ || (fol. 4r7–9)
