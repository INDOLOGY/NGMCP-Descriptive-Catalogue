Excerpts

«Beginning»


❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīśrīśrīsiddhilakṣmīdevyai namaḥ ||


śrīgurupādukābhyāṃ namaḥ || ||


koṭyāhutisiddhāgniyajñadinakṛtyavidhir likhyate ||


tritattvena ācamya || khphreṁ hrāṁ ātmatattvāya svāhā || khphreṁ hrīṁ vidyātattvāya svāhā ||


khphreṁ hrauṁ śivatattvāya svāhā || sūryārghaṃ adyādi || vākya ||


varṇāntaṃ bījam uddhṛtya tasyopari śivaṃ nyaset ||


ādimadhyāvasāneṣu agnitraya(vi)bhūṣitaṃ ||


marmmaṇā dīpitaṃ kṛtya sāvitryāparabhūṣitaṃ ||


eṣa kūṭavaraṃ sreṣṭhaṃ mahāmārttaṇḍabhairavaḥ ||


arghaṃ namaḥ || puṣpaṃ namaḥ || (fol. 1r1–7)


«End»


kṣetri āvāhana ||


oṁ hrīṁ hūṁ hāṁ phreṁ ksoṁ kroṁ namaḥ svāhā ||


śrīlakṣṃī pratidehayogaparamānandāravindodbhavā ||


rāmā ratnaśiromaṇiḥ khalu mahālakṣmīr mahāmaṅgalā ||


madhye śaktipayodharā tribhuvanālaṃkārasambandhitā ||


senduś candrakalādharā guṇarasā śrīkālikā yoginī ||


tataḥ saṃkhyāhuti || || ṛtvigjanena agnisūtraṃ paṭhet ||


ṛtvigjanādibhojanaṃ kārayet || || (fol. 132r7–131v1)



«Colophon(s):»


iti dinakṛtyavidhiḥ samāptaḥ || (fol. 132v1)
