Excerpts

Beginning

mūla aṃśa

gaurīśravaḥ ketakapatrabhaṃgamākṛṣyahastenadadanumkhāgre ||
vighnānmukhānmuhūrttā kalitadvitīyadaṃtapraroho haratudvipāsyaḥ || 1 ||

kriyākalāpa pratipattihetuṃ || (fol. 1v8–9)

ṭīkā aṃśa

oṃ namaḥ śrī gaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

|| kailāsepūrṇarākāhimakara rucire vīkṣyavījaṃ svakīyaṃ
bhūyobhūyopi dhāvan pratibhaṭa karaṭisparddhayācaṇḍamuṇḍa ||

mādhāvatvatvadaṃghri prahatibhirabhito dhūyate saudharitrī
tvaṃvāvāgnirniruddhā kapaṭataraṭilaḥ kelayo naḥ punaṃtu || (fol. 1v1–2)

End

mūla aṃśa

tadātmaja udāradhīrvivudhanīlakaṇṭhā +++++ rāmābhidhaḥ ||

girīśanagarevarebhujabhujeṣu caṃdrairmiteśakeviniramādimaṃ khalu muhūrtaciṃtāmaṇim || 491 || (fol. 118r7–8)

ṭīkā aṃśa

tadātmaja iti || tasmādanaṃtadaivajñāsyātmajo rāmābhidho girīśanagare vārāṇasyāṃ muhurttaciṃtāmaṇināma +++++ dvāviṃśatyadhikapaṃcadaśāmiteśake viniramādakārṣīt śeṣaṃ sphūṭam || 491 ||(fol. 118r9–10)

Colophon

iti daivajñānantasutadaivajñarāmavira ++++ pramitākṣarāyāṃ gṛhapraveśa prakaraṇaṃ samāptimavrājīt ||   || śakeṃdrakṛta vatsare tithituraṃga candrairmite pataṃga ++++ sare pavana bhojidhiṣṇye(!) tathā saṭīkamimamālikhat khalu muhūrttacintāmaṇim || śakābde 1715 māghakṛṣṇa pratipadi bhṛgu ++++ vidvajjanai śodhanīyam || śubhaṃ bhavatu sarvadā (fol. 118r10–13)
