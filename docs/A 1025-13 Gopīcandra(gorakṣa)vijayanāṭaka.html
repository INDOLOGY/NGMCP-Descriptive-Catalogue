nija yuvati yutāṅgaṃ masta kanyasta canyaṃ saka ///

dhināthaṃ || || thana sunya me || rāga mārava || || navamāna || ///

End

|| lu 9 || ||

thana ārati || || me rāga ||

mā(141r4)runā dhanāśrī || co ||

ārati śrīnṛtyanātha sahāya diyāo pavana jogara gāya ||

antara jyoti(5)ra'i bhuvana jagāya cāda sururja dui manahirukāya ||

bhuvanalakṣmīsuta bhāskara gāya māyā(6)cāti mātha rijhāya || ||

thana dya || me rāga || paṃcama || kha ||

bhāskara narapati siroma(7)ṇi bhūpe

pāiro sakara guṇa manasija rupe ||

nepārala adhipati sumati surāje

tāhā(141v1)ro varana kare kavira samāje ||

miti muṇi pakṣe vasu samvata nepāre

miriro jyaṣṭhela su(2)ddha digavāle ||

saṃpūrṇṇa hairo nāca govicandra nāme ||

yogatatva vicāriyā kehe guṇadhāma(3) || ||

Colophon

iti gopicandagvarakhavijaya nāṭāke saptamoṅka || saṃpūrṇṇaṃ ||
