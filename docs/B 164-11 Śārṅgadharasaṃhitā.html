Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

śrīdhanvaṃtarāya (!) namaḥ || ||

karaśirasācyutamīśaṃ bhavabhavapuṣṭi kṣayālayamupāyaṃ ||

svarggāpavargayor api tāpatrayanāśanārthaṃ(!) abhivaṃde || 1 ||

sagaṇagaṇeśaṃ matidaṃ vighnasamūhāṃś ca haṃti yaḥ smaraṇe ||

tadanugurorapi caraṇaṃ śaraṇaṃ gadahīna manujānāṃ || 2 ||

ye durjjanāḥ paraguṇānanusūya yaṃti

tepi svabhāva vaśagāḥ kimuvāraṇīyāḥ ||

ye śāstrabodha guṇadoṣa vicāradakṣās

te sajjanāḥ praṇatibhi khalu vaṃdanīyāḥ || 3 ||

śrīvāstavyakulaprakāśamaṇayoḥ ye pūrvajā viśrutāḥ

śrīhammīrapure mahājananate yeṣāṃṣthite kathyate |

ye kāmaṃ madavihvalodbhavaraviḥ kṣīrādhikṛd yogatas

tat sāścarya śrūcāṃdrakāṃta vitatāṃ vikhyāta kīrttigatāḥ(!) || 4 || (fol.1v1-6)

End

śārṅgadharācāryeṇa kiṃcidarthaṃ svabuddhyā ekadeśīya prayojanaṃ nītvā āhṛtya iyaṃ saṃhitā kṛtā nivaddhetyabhiprāyaḥ na tu kapola kalptā || etena muni praṇīta ca saṃkṣepaṃ ca darśitaṃ || atha kavirvijñaptim āha krapākaṭākṣa vikṣepam api kurvvaṃtvityādi ||asyāṃ saṃhitāyām sādhavaḥ sajjanāḥ kṛpālaṭākṣavikṣepaṃ kṣapayā kṛtā katāṣasya dṛṣṭe || kiṃcid bhāgasya vikṣeponmīlanaṃ kurvatu(!) || anumatārtha kriyā || avaśyam evaṃ bhāvaṃ sūcayati tenāvaśyam eva kaṭākṣavikṣepaṃ kurvaṃtu | yataḥ || kaṭākṣamātrāvalokanenāpi hṛdayaṃgamā kṣamā bhavaṃti kim uta sakalaṃ dṛṣṭvā etena graṃthasyāsamīcīnatā darśitā || idānīṃ kavireva nijagraṃthatthotkṛṣṭatvamāha(!) || (fol.85r4-84v1)
