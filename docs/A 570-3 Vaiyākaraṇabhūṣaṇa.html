Manuscript Features

There are occasional corrections and annotations, both by the scribe himself and a second hand. Above the foliation in the left-hand margin, the abbreviation vai° bhū° appears on each verso. Above the foliation in the right-hand margin, the word rāma appears. As regards foliation, the scribe has skipped numbers “58” and “95”, the text running, however, continuously. From fol. 152 onwards, there is a second foliation, which has been deleted. The writing on fol. 15r has been rubbed off.

On the back of folio 1, which originally belonged to some other MS, the following words are inscribed in Nāgarī characters: śrīkṛṣṇajośī vaiyyākaraṇabhūṣaṇaprāraṃbhaḥ || patre 174. Thus, this MS once might have been the property of this said śrīkṛṣṇajośī (cf. also the colophon).
