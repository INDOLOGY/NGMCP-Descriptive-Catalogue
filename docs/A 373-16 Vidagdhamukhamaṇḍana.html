Manuscript Features

We find this verse in the recto side of second exposure and verso side is filled with some stanzas related to Vyākaraṇa.

❖ vayovikāraṃ sahajaṃ jahāti

śeṣaś ca roṣaṃ nalinaṃ (!) ca pamkam |

mācaṃcalaṃ (!) paṃkajanābhe (!) yogāt

satsaṃgatiḥ kiṃ na karoti puṃsām ||

Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śivāya ||

siddhauṣadhāni bhavaduḥkhamahāgadānāṃ

puṇyātmanāṃ paramakarṇṇarasāyanāni |

prakṣālanaikasalilāni manomalānāṃ

(2) śauddhodaneḥ pravacanāni cirañ jayanti ||

jayanti sataḥ sukṛtaikabhājanam (!)

parārthasampādanasadvratasthitāḥ |

karasthanīropamaviśvada(3)rśino

jayanti vaidaddhababhuvaḥ kaver ggaraḥ ||

ākrānteva mahopalena muninā sapteva durvvāsasā

sātatyam bata mudriteva ja(4)tunā nīteva mūrchāṃ viṣaiḥ ||

baddhevātanurajjubhiḥ paraguṇān vaktun na śaktā satī

jihvā lauhaśalākayā khalamukhe vi(5)ddheva saṃlakṣyate || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

spheṭayitvākṣaraṅ (!) kiñcit punar anyasya dānataḥ |

yatrāparo bhaved arthaś cutadattākṣaraṃ (!) hi tat ||

sadāgati(3)hatocchrāyas tamaso vaśyatāṃ gataḥ |

astam eṣyati dīpo yaṃ vidhur ekaś cirasthitaḥ ||

pūrṇṇacandramukhī ramyā kāminī ni(4)rmmalāmbarā ||

karotu (!) kasya na svāntam ekāntamadanottaram (!) || (fol. 34r2–4)

Colophon

iti śrīdharmmadāsaviracite vidagdhamukhamaṇḍane catu(5)rthaḥ paricchedaḥ || 7 || †nadī jalaṃ keśavanāriketur nagāhvayo nāma nagārisūnuḥ |

eṣo ʼṅganāveśadharaḥ pramāthī jitvā (6) vayaṃ neṣyati cādya gāvaḥ† || (fol. 34r4–6)
