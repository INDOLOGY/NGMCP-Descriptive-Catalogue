Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || oṃ nomo anādihaṃsāḥ ||

haṃsarupāji jagadiśāḥ ||  taṃ sadguru paramahaṃ(2)sāḥ ||

paramapareśā paripūrṇā || 1 ||

ubhaya pakṣe viṇadekhaḥ tujhe śobhati dhnipākhaḥ

śu(3)ddha satvā ho unicokhaḥ || svarūpa suresvasojvalaḥ || 2  ||

haṃsa volije śubhravarṇaḥ || tujhi haṃ(4)satā vilakṣaṇaḥ || sā duniyāṃ sakalavarṇaḥ || haṃsapaṇa tuja śobhe || 3 || (fol. 1r1–4)
