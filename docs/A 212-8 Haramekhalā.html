Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śivāyaḥ ||

nivadhi (!) jagadbhāvābhāvodayavyaya viśramakrama

nirupama krīḍā sauṇḍopyakhaṇḍa samasthitiḥ ||

satatama pṛthagbhogyabhogāvarodha rasātmakaṃ

kam api diśatād ānandaṃ vāna vyandu (!) vibhūṣaṇaḥ ||

vyaktārthato vidadhato haramekhalāyā

buddheḥ svabhāva carati haramekhalāyāḥ ||

santo yathā haracirād abhiyogatāntāḥ

syuḥ saṃśayādi virahād abhiyogatāntā

vidyānidhe jagati sarvaguro dvijendra

cūḍāmaṇeḥ svayamanugrahatodhigamya | (!)

tad vastu sundaradhiyāṃ smṛtaye cikīrṣāḥ

sayyāniviṣṭam athakotra manobhimānaḥ || ||

thvate ṭikākārasyaṃ dayakā śloka || || (fol.1v1–5)

«Sub-colophon:»

iti haramekhalāyāṃ kautūhalādiprakāśe prathamaḥ | sambaṃdhābhidhānābhidheyaprayojana samuddeśo nāma || ❁ || (fol.2v6–7)
