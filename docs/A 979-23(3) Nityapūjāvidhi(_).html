⁅gaṇeśāya namaḥ⁆ ||

pīṭhe ma .. dalaṃ saṃsthāpya pūjayet | vindobhūpari maṃ maṃdūkāya namaḥ | evaṃ kaṃ kālāgnibhadrāya namaḥ | mūṃ mūlaprakṛtyai namaḥ | āṃ ācāraśaktaye namaḥ | aṃ anantāya namaḥ pṛṃ pṛthivyai namaḥ | sudhāsindhave namaḥ || ratnadvīpāya namaḥ || maṇimaṃḍapāya namaḥ || (fol. 1v1–3)

gaccha gaccha paraṃ sthānaṃ svasthānaṃ parameśvarī |

yatra brahmādayo devā nāveduḥ paramaṃ padam ||

mūlena skanti (!) nītvā | manasā saṃpūjya tadā tā (!) tayā sukhī vihared iti nityapūjāvidhiḥ || (fol. 3v17–18)
