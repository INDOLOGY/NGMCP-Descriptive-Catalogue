Manuscript Features

This MTM contains the following texts:

1. #Tāntrikakāṇḍaviṣayakaprakīrṇapatra?

2. #Śaivatantraviṣayakaprakīrṇapatra?

3. # Bauddhaviṣaykaprakīrṇakapatra?

The third manuscript can be easily placed in the 9th century on the paleographic basis.

The right-hand side of the leaf is broken off.
