Excerpts

Beginning

❁ oṃ namo bhagavatyai āryyamahāpratyajñirāyai(!) ||

namaḥ śrīsarvabuddhabodhisatvebhyaḥ || ||

śrīsāhasrapramarthanāṃ bhagavati(!) paṃcādibuddhātmikāṃ-

sāhasrādhikamārabhaga(!)caturāṃ sāhasrakoṭī(!)gaṇāṃ ||

sāhasrāyutakoṭiḥ(!)lokanivayāvuddhārayantī(!) kṣaṇe-

sāhasrādhipatīśvarīṃ praṇamatāṃ tāṃ viśvarūpāṃ sadā ||

mahāvaro jñānasamādhidatto jaya jalaṃ janmamayaṃ vidāryya ||

†viveśa nivṛtyata vipraśāttāṃ taṃ śāstanāṃga† śirasā namāmi ||

sāhasraśīrṣā dvisahasrabhujāṃ niyutakoṭiśatadivyanetrāṃ |

tathāgatoṣṇīṣaṣaśītātapatrāṃ pratyaṃjñirā(!) tāṃ praṇatosmi nityaṃ ||                                                                  (fol.1v1-5 )

End

|| idam avocad bhagavān sarvabuddhabodhisatvānāṃ yaḥ sadevanāgayakṣagandharvāsuragaruḍakinnaramahoragamanuṣyāmanuṣyaḥ(!)

ye cānye grahanakṣatramātṛkāgaṇapatayāś(!) caḥ(!)saptamātaro yakṣiṇīḥ(!)rākṣasādipiśācīsahitāḥ samagrāḥ sasainyāḥ saparivārāḥ loko

bhagavatoḥ śrīvajrasatvabhāṣitam abhyanandann iti || ||

(fol.162r6-162v3)

Colophon

ity āryyaḥ sarvatathāgatoṣṇīṣaśrītātapatrānām āparājitāyāṃ †laṃrkarā†lokāyāṃ

mahāpratyajñirāyāṃdharmopadeśaśāstreḥ(!)supariśuddhadharmmakāyajñānamū(rti)

manu(bhata)radharmmadeśanādhiṣṭhitaḥ śrīśākyamunibhāṣito(!)paramārthā nāma

vinirggatamahāpratyajñirā mahāvidyārājñīḥ(!)sapādarakṣāduddhṛtaḥ(!) samāptaḥ || ||

ye dharmā hatu(!)prabhavā hetusteṣān tathāgataḥ ||

hyavadat teṣāṃ yo nirodha evaṃvādimahāśramaṇaḥ || ||

tvaṃ mātā tvaṃ pitā tvaṃ jagati gurur api tvaṃ ca bandhuś ca mitraṃ

tvaṃ nāthas tvaṃ vidhātā hitam aghaharaṇaṃ tvaṃ padaṃ saṃpadāṃ ca |

tvaṃ vaikalpaṃ pada(!) tvaṃ varaguṇanilayo †ghasta†doṣas tvam eva

tvaṃ dīnānāthacintāmaṇir api śaraṇaṃ tvāṃ gatohaṃ jinendraḥ || ||

śubhaṃ maṃgalaṃ bhavatuḥ(!) || (fol.162v4-163r5 )
