Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṁ namo nṛharaye haraye || ||


nārāyaṇaṃ namaskṛtya naraṃ caiva narottamaṃ ||

devīṃ sarasvatīṃ caiva tato jayam udīraayet |


taptahāṭakakeśāgrajvalatpāvakalocanaḥ |

vajrādhikanakhasparśa divyasiṃha namoʼstu te |


nakhamukhavilikhitadititanayoraḥ-

paripatasṛgaruṇīkṛtagātraḥ|

himadharagirir iva gairikayukto

naraharir aharahar avatu sa yuṣmān 2


himavadvāsinaḥ sarve munayo vedapāragāḥ |

trikālajñā mahātmāno naimiṣāraṇyavāsinaḥ |3 (fol. 1v1–4)


End

kiṃ kiṃ siṃhas tataḥ kiṃ narasadṛśavapur devacitraṃ gṛhītaḥ |

naivaṃ (dhik kān)[[jīvet kramam anupatitaḥ]] soʼpi satyaṃ harīśaḥ |

cāpaṃ cāpaṃ nakhāṅgaṃ jhaṭiti (hahahahaṃ) karkaśatvaṃ nakhānām

ityevaṃ daityanāthaṃ nijanakhakuliśair jaghnivān yaḥ sa voʼvyāt 28 (fol .120r3–5)


Colophon

oṁ tat sad iti śrīnārasiṃhapurāṇe ādye dharmārthakāmamokṣapradāyini parabrahmasvarūpiṇi

idam ekaṃ suniṣpannaṃ dhyeyo nārāyaṇaḥ sadā || na vāsudevāt param asti kiṃcit phalastutir nāma catuḥṣaṣṭitamoʼdhyāya ityetanārasiṃhapurāṇam ❁ /// ṇatoʼmarācalācalamite śākavare śucau śucau śrīprāṇanāthena mayā dvijanmanā pustaṃ nṛsiṃhākhyapurāṇamadvayaṃ taṃ manmahe narahariṃ jagadekabījaṃ yadīhayā viśvamidaṃ vibhāti

yo līlayā sṛja /// karmavacobhir aikyāt 2

oṁ namaḥ śrīmannṛharaye haraye ❁ samāptam abhūn nṛsiṃhapurāṇaṃ (fol. 120r5–10)
