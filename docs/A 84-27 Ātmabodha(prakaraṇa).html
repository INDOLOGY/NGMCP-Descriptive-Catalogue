Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

tapobhiḥ kṣīṇapāpānāṃ śāṃtānāṃ vītarāgiṇām ||

mumukṣūṇāmapekṣo(!)yam ātmabodho vidhīyate || 1 ||

asyārthaḥ ||

nityanaimittikasvajātidharmakṛchracāṃdrāyaṇādirūpa tapasyāle pāpakṣayabhayākā

putrakalatrādiviṣe asakta bhayākā, kevala mokṣako icchā garnyā ādamilāī cāhinyā |

ātmabodha nāma graṃtha yogeśvara śaṃkarācārya ājñā garchan || 1 ||

(fol. 17r1-17v2)

End

digdeśakālādyanapekṣasarvagaṃ śītādihṛn nityasukhaṃ niraṃjanam ||

yasvātmatīrthaṃ bhajate viniḥkriyaḥ(!) sa sarvvavit sarvagatomṛto bhavet || 68 ||

ātmā jñānakana chekanyā pāpakṣaya nimittaprayādi(!) tīrthayātrādiko udyoga garnu bhanaulā ta kahaṃchan jo manuṣya karmarahi⟨hi⟩ta bhai āphai ātmarūpatīrthakana sevā garchan | sarvajña sarvavyāpī sukhaduḥkhādi dvaṃdva duḥkhaphālanyā nityānaṃdasvarūpa bhayāko | estā ātmatīrthadeṣi bāki kehi chaina bujhnu || 68 || ||

(fol. 60r1-60v5)

Colophon

iti ātmabodhabhāṣā samāptaṃ || śubhaṃ (fol. 60v6)
