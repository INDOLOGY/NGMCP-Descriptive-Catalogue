śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

yan dhyāyanti surāsurāś ca nikhilā yakṣā[ḥ] piśācoragāḥ<ref>MSpc, piśocoragāḥ MSac</ref> ||

rājānaś ca tathā munīndranivahāḥ sarvvārthadaṃ siddhaye ||

bhaktānāṃ varadābhayapradakaraṃ pāśāṃkuśālaṃkṛtaṃ

caṃcaccāmaravīṣamānam(!) aniśaṃ śohaṃ(!) śucantyaṃ (!) śivaṃ || 1 ||


babhāra iṣṭā(!) munipuṅgavān yor(!)

adhyāyamantraiś ca durāsahaujāḥ ||

jājvalyamāno divi dṛśyato yaḥ

sa bhārgavo mām avatā[c] charaṇyaḥ || 2 ||


yasya prasādād itino (!) bhavanti

jānanti daityā vividhāñ ca māyāṃ |

yo labdhavān mantravaraṃ maheśān

mṛtyuṃjayaṃ devavarāṃ gaṇeśān || 3 || (fol. 1v1–4)


«End»


namoʼstubhyaṃ(!) mahālakṣmī(!) ratnakuṇḍalaśobhite ||

viṣṇornarddhaśavāśīca (!) śīghraṃ mokṣaṃ dadasva me || 6 ||

namas tubhyaṃ mahādurgge mahāduṣṭara(!)tāriṇī (!) ||
