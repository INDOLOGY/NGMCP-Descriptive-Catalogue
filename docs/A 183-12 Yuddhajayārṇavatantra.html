devy uvāca ||


bhagavan devadeveśa śaśāṃkakṛtaśeṣara ||

svarodayaṃ paraṃ guhyaṃ munīnām api kiṃ punaḥ ||

maryānāṃ svalpabuddhināṃ pūrvam uktaṃ tvayā vibho || 5 || (fol. 1v1-5)
