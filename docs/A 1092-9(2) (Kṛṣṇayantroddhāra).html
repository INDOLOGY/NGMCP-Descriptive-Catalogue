Beginning

❖ śrīkṛṣṇayā yantroddhāra thva julo ||

piṇḍaṃ mūle navītaṃ dahanapurayuge ko(2)śarāje daśārṇṇe,
kuryyāt padmaṃ daśārṇṇaṃ sphurita ḍaśadalaṃ kāmavīje navī(3)taṃ |
padmaṃ kiṃjalka saṃsthaṃ svarakṛti dalavat prollasat ṣoḍaśārṇṇaṃ
ki(4)ñjalka vyaṃjanod yad vikṛtayugadaleṣvarpitānuṣṇavarṇṇaṃ
pāśāṃkuśā(5)bhyāmāvītaṃ kṣoṇīpurayugāśriṣu
aṣṭākṣareṇa lasitaṃ yantraṃ go(17b1)pālavaindave ||

thuti yantroddhāra śrīkṛṣṇayā ||
thva yantra luṁcakisa catu(2)(śā)na ilāo kiya julo || (exp. 17t1–17b2)
