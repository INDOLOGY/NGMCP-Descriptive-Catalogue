api ca paramatattvaṃ yogināṃ bhānur ekaḥ || 1 || ||



jayati sarvotkarṣeṇa varttate kaḥ ayaṃ bhānuḥ sūryaḥ kiṃ viśiṣṭaḥ ekaḥ advitīyaḥ kiṃ kurvan vyaṃjyan


prakāśayan kān padārthān kābhiḥ ātmabhābhiḥ svadīptibhiḥ kva jagati kiṃ viśiṣṭān padārthān gūḍhān adṛśyān


kasmin sati aṃdhakāre kayā hetubhūtayā janaghanaghṛṇayā ghanā cāsau ghṛṇā ca ghanaghṛṇā janānāṃ ghanaghṛṇā
