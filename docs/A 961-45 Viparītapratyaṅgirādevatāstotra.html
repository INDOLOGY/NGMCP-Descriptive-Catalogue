Manuscript Features

At the end of the manuscript, there is a folio of skaṃdapurāṇīyaketupaṃcaviśatnāmastotra; which runs as : śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || || oṁ namo bhagavate vikaṭe vīrapālīke prasīda prasīda ||

...

tasya naśyati bādhāś ca sarvān ketuprasādataḥ ||

dhanadhānyapaśunāṃ ca bhavt buddhir na śaṃsayaḥ || 5 ||


iti skamdapurāṇe ketupaṃcaviṃśatināmastotram saṃpūrṇam || || (exp. 6b1–8)

Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya nama[ḥ] || ||


oṁ asya śrīpratyaṃgirāstotramaṃtrasya śrībhairava ṛṣi [anuṣṭupchaṃdaḥ] 2 huṁ bi(!)jaṁ hrīṁ śaktaye kīṁ kīlakaṃ mama śatrukṣayārte jape viniyoga[ḥ] || || oṁ bhairavāya ṛṣaye namaḥ śirasi || oṁ anuṣṭup chaṃdase namaḥ mukhe || oṁ śrīpratyaṃgirādevatāya namaḥ hṛdaye || oṁ huṁ bījāya namaḥ nābhau || oṁ hrīṁ śaktaye namaḥ guhye || (fol. 1v1–5)


End

paramaṃtrataṃtrayaṃtraviṣacūrṇasarvaprayogādīnām anyeṣāṃ yatkṛtaṃ kāritaṃ tanmastake niyātinīḥ(!)sarvahiṃsām ākarṣiṇī ahitānāṃ ca nāśinī duṣṭātmanāṃ ca nāśinī yat karoti yat kiṃcit kariṣyati virūpaṃ kārayiṣyati vā anumodayati vā karmaṇā manasā vācā ye devāsurarākṣasās tiryak pretasarvahiṃsakā virūpakaṃ kurvaṃti mama maṃtrataṃtrayaṃtraviṣacūrṇasarvaprayogādinām ātmahastena vā yaḥ karoti kariṣyati kārayiṣyati vā tān sarvān aneṣāṃ(!) nivartitvā patati mastake || 25 ||(fol. 5r5–5v5)


Colophon

iti śrībhairavataṃtre śrīviparītapratyaṃgirādevatāstotraṃ(!) saṃpūrṇam || ❁ || 1967 sāla jyeṣṭhaśuklasaptamīmaṃgalavāra(!) || || (fol. 5v5–6)
