Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

lakṣmīm ālokya lubhyan nigamam upahasan śocayan yajñajaṃtūn

kṣatraṃ śoṇākṣipaśyan samitidaśamukhaṃ vīkṣya romāṃ(2)camaṃcan ||

hṛtvā haiyaṃgavīnaṃ cakitam apasaran mlekṣaraktair digaṃtān

siṃcan daṃtena bhūmiṃ tilam iva tulayan pātu māṃ pītavāsāḥ || 1 ||

bhāratyāḥ (3)śāstrakāṃtāraśrātāyāḥ śaityakāriṇī ||

kriyate bhānunā bhūri rasārasataraṃgiṇī || 2 ||

vāṇīkamalinī bhānor ekṣā rasataraṃgiṇī ||

haṃ(4)sāḥ kṛtadhiyas tatra yuktam atra pratīyatāṃ || 3 ||

girāṃ devī (!) taraṃgiṇyāṃ vāraya krūravāraṇān ||

yad bhaviṣyati lokānām āvilo viralo rasaḥ || 4 || (fol. 1r1–4)

End

vidvadvāridharāḥ snehaṃ tathā kurvaṃtu saṃtataṃ ||

labhate vipulāṃ vṛddhiṃ yathā rasataraṃgiṇī (2) || ||

avagāhasva vāgdevi divyāṃ rasataraṃgiṇīṃ ||

asmatpadyena padmena rachaya śrutibhūṣaṇaṃ || ||

yāvad bhānoḥ kṛ(3)tā kāpi kāliṃdī bhuvi naṃdate ||

tāvat tiṣṭhatu me bhānor eṣā rasataraṃgiṇī || ❁ || (fol. 30r1–3)

Colophon

iti śrīkavika(4)lāsanāthagaṇapatināthatanayabhānudattaviracitāyāṃ (!) rasataraṃgiṇyām aṣṭamas taraṃgaḥ || ||

naṃdarturasabhū(5)śāke ūrjje gnīśatithau śubhāṃ ||

nṛpājñayāliṣad (!) gaṃgāviṣṇū rasataraṃgiṇīṃ || ||

svasti śrīrājarājasya ma(6)hārājasya bhūpateḥ ||

trivikramasya bhavatu vinodabahulā śubhā || ||

śubham astu || ❁ || || ❁ || || (fol. 30r3–6)
