Beginning

śrīmaṃjugurave namaḥ || ||

bhagavān āha || ||


kaś cāsau bhikṣavo ʼrthaviniścayo nāma dharmaparyyāyaḥ || || ||

yad uta(!) || paṃcaskandhā[ḥ] [[1]] || paṃcopādānaskandhāḥ [[2]] || aṣṭādaśadhātavaḥ [[3]] || dvādaśāyatanāni [[4]] || dvādaśāṅgapratītyasamutpādaḥ ⌠⌠5⌡⌡ || catvāryy āryasatyāni ⌠⌠6⌡⌡ || (fol. 1v1–3)



End

yadutārthaviniścayaṃ nāma dharmaparyyāyam iti †ya nā† yad uktam idaṃ ta(!) pra[[tyu]]ktaṃ bhikṣavo ʼraṇyāyatanāni śūnyāgārāṇi parvatakaṇḍa(!)ragiriguhāpatālavrajānyasvavakāśaśmaśānavanapraṣṭhā - - - bhikṣavo mā pramānya(!)rthadhvaṃ || mā paścād vipratisāriṇo bhaviṣyatha || idam askā(!)kam anuśāsanam asmin baladharmaparyyāye bhāṣyamāne(!) paṃcānāṃ bhikṣa - - - tāni vimuktāni || || idam avocat bhagavān āttamanās te ca bhikṣavaḥ sā ca sarvāvatī parṣat sadevamānuṣāsuragaṃdharvaś ca loko bhagavato bhāṣitam abhyanandann iti || || (fol. 29v8–30r4)



Colophon

āryya-arthaviniścayanāmadharmaparyyāya(!) samāptam(!) || || śubhaṃ bhūyāt || || ❁ (fol. 30r5–6)
