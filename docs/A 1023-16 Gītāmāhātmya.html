Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

ṛṣaya ūcuḥ || ||

gītāyāś caiva māhātmyaṃ yathāvat sūta no vada ||

purāṇe muninā proktaṃ vyāsena (2) śrutinoditam || 1 || ||

sūta uvāca ||

pṛṣṭaṃ ca bhavabhītānāṃ mahad gopyaṃ purātanam ||

śakyate kena vai vaktuṃ gītāmāhā(3)tmyam uttamam || 2 ||

kṛṣṇo jānāti vai samyak kiṃcit kuntīsuto rjjunaḥ ||

vyāso vā vyāsaputro vā yājñavalkyo tha mai(4)thilaḥ || 3 ||

anye śravaṇataḥ śrutvā loke śaṃkīrttayaṃti ca ||

tasmāt saṃkīrttayāmy adya vyāsadevyād (!) yathāśrutam || 4 ||

(5) sarvopaniṣadho (!) gāvo dogdhā gopālanandanaḥ ||

pārtho vatsa[[ḥ]] sudhīr bhoktā dugdhaṃ gītāmṛtaṃ mahat (fol. 1v1-5)

End

gītāyāḥ paṭhanaṃ kṛtvā mahātmyaṃ (!) naiva yaḥ paṭhet ||

vṛthā pā(2)ṭhaphalaṃ tasya śramam eva bhaved dhruvam || 87 ||

etanmahātmyasaṃyukto (!) gītāpāṭhaṃ karoti yaḥ ||

śraddhayā yaḥ śṛṇoty evaṃ durllabhaṃ (!) gatim āpnu(3)yāt || 88 ||

śrutvā paṭhitvā gītāṃ ca mahātmyaṃ (!) yaḥ śṛṇoti ca ||

tasya puṇyaphalaṃ loke bhaved vai manasepsitam. || 89 || ○ || (fol. 4v1–3)

Colophon

iti śrīvārāhapurāṇe sūtasaunakasamvāde gītāmahātmyaṃ (!) samāptaṃ śubham ||

śāke 1762 vaiśākhe 15 śanivāsare śubham (fol. 4v4)
