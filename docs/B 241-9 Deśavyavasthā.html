Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

ādiśivaracite tapasyākhaṇḍe devīpraśnānantaraṃ śivena kathitam

rahasyātirahasyaṃ ca proktaṃ devi ta(2)vāgrataḥ

deśavyavasthā deveśi kathyate śṛṇu yatnataḥ 1

vaidyanātha (!) samārabhya bhuvane śāntagaṃ śive

tāvad aṅgābhidho deśo yātrāyāṃ nahi (3) duṣyati 2

vatnākaraṃ (!) samārabhya brahmaputrāntagaṃ pare

vaṅgadeśo mayā proktaḥ sarvasiddhipradarśakaḥ3 (fol. 1v1–3)

End

pūrve caiva jagannnāthas tūttare ʼmarakaṇṭakam

kāṃcīpuraṃ madhyabhāge mohanīvarttam eva ca 76

lalitā(2)yā mukhaṃ tac ca vidyāgaṇavibhūṣitam

kāśmīrakṣetram uddiṣṭaṃ mahāmaṃtravibhūṣitam 77

navalakṣamahāvidyā sagaṇaiḥ pari(3)bhūṣitam

paṃcaprasthāni deveśi kathitāni mayā tava 78 (fol. 4r1–3)

iti saṃkṣepadeśavyavasthā śubham (fol. 4r3)
