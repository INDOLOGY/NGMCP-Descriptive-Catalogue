The text Kātyāyanapariśiṣṭa is also known as Pratijñāsūtravyākhyā. The MS contains the root text (sūtras) and commentary.

Exposures of fols 5v–7r are out of focus.

Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

praṇamya śirasā devaṃ sarvavighnavināśinam ||

viṣṇudattohaṃ pratijñāsūtravyākhyāṃ samārabhe || 1 ||

atha prātiśākhyasūtrakathanānantaram pratijñāyata iti pratijñānāma sūtraṃ vidhāsyata iti śiṣyabuddher ekā pratākriyate (!) | adhyayanādikarmasu svarasaṃskārasaṃskṛtānāṃ śabdānāṃ prayoge adṛṣṭaviśeṣapratipādanād anena svarasaṃskārajñānam ity anvarthasaṃjñā | (fol. 1v1–3)

End

visargeṣvīṣad virāma iti | aham antarjam ityādi saṃyuktākṣaraparā ʼkārasyāpīṣaddīrghatā bhavatīty āha | padādyasya saṃyuktākārasyeddīrghatā bhavatīti | iṣad dīrghatā bhavatīti dvirabhyāsaḥ parisamāpty arthaḥ || 3 || ❖ || (fol. 3r8–10)
