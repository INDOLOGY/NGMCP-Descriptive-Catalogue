Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrī 3 nṛtyanāthāya namaḥ || ||

nāndi me || ||

gavarimā || kāmoda || co ||

jaya jagadīśa naṭe(fol. 1r1)śa || dhru ||

devī bachi hmasa tala josā ḍamaru śūla,

śirasa caṃdramā bhiṅa thūla,

bina tisā thāna(2)thāna surakhu jaṭāsa,

thathiṅahma lokayā āsa ||

dhuti tala dhucheguli bihma kokhi jāṁsa,

ā(3)sana chi dohalayā hmāsa,

gaṃgādhara mahimā hlāla raṇajīta,

yāya māla jagatayā hīta || me (4) pu 1 || ||

End

thithiṃ, āliṃgana yāya || ||

mahāaye pārvvatyā(fol. 10v2)dilokapani āva, ādiśaktiyā bhāvanā yāya nuyo ||

sarvvaīśvara jivakhe || ||

mahāde(3)vādiyā, devabhāva me || ||

suravaramā || bhairavī || kha e co ||

girivaranaṃdini gaurī mṛḍāni,

di(4)tisutanāśini siṃhavāhini ||

jaya 2 tāriṇi devi bhavāni ||dhru ||

tribhuvaṇapālini jaya śi(5)vāni,

raṇajita vinati yāva dukha hāni || me pu 17 ||

Colophon

iti śrīharagaṇakathā prathamo'ṅkassaṃpū(fol. 10v6)rṇṇaṃ || ||

saṃvat 875 bhādrapada śukla dvitīyā || ||

śubham astu sarvvadā || || || śubha ||
