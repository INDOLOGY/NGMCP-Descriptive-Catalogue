Manuscript Features

Only fols. 1‒7 are available . (Fol. 7v has not been microfilmed.)

The text runs from the very beginning up to stanza 6 of the 2nd sarga.

The manuscript is slightly damaged: the edges are broken, but without any loss of akṣaras.

Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

yaḥ samastaviduṣāṃ śiromaṇir yena rājati samā(!) vipaścitāṃ ||

taṃ mahīśvarakṛtāṃ dhrivaṃdanaṃ nīlakaṇṭhapitaraṃ namāmy ahaṃ || 1


śṛṃgāraikapradhānaṃ yad ṛtusaṃhāranāmakaṃ ||

kāvyaṃ tat kālidāsīyaṃ (vyā)khyāsye haṃ yathāmati ||


apracāratamomagnā kālidāsakṛtir yataḥ ||

kriyate taś caṃdrikeyaṃ vibudhānada(!)dāyinī || 3 ||

(fol. 1v1‒3)
