Manuscript Features

Most of the pages are covered with unrelated stanzas and mantras. On fol. 7v/8r (pp 14-15) we find a part of the Aparādhastotra. The writing in this manuscript looks as if written by a very unskilled person or a child. The paper is lined. Pages 1 and 2 have been left blank.
