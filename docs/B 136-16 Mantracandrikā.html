Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

natvā gurupadāmbhojaṃ gaṇeśaṃ śāradāṃ śivāṃ
śivaṃ sūryyaṃ hṛṣīkeśaṃ tanyate maṃtracaṃdrikāṃ 1

graṃthagraṃthāntarād vākyān nānārthaṃ pratibudhya ca
saukaryārthaṃ ca saṃkṣepāt sārasāraṃ pratanyate 2

vande ham sakalāṃ sveṣṭāṃ paramātmasvarūpiṇīṃ
yasyāḥ kaṭākṣamātreṇa sādhakaḥ kiṃ na sādhayet 3 (fol. 1v1–3)

End

śāṃtidaṃ sutadaṃ strīṇāṃ bhūtapretabhayāpahaṃ
garbharakṣākaraṃ śuddhaṃ param asyān na vidyate

mustā mothaṃ niśā haridrāḥ vahniś citrakamūlaṃ yaṣṭi(!) yaṣṭimadhuḥ rohiṇī kaṭukī. phalatrayaṃ harro(!) varaḍā(!) amalā(!) prasthaparimitaṃ ghṛtaṃ samyak pācayet
maṃtrasādhitam uktaphaladaṃ iti paṃcagavyam (fol. 233r8–10)

Colophon

iti śrīmaṃtracaṃdrikāyāṃ yaṃtrakaraṇādhikāraḥ saptaviṃśas taraṃgaḥ samāptaḥ 27 śūbham bhūyāt ❁ (fol. 233r10–11)
