❖ sādhaka coṅavanya verasa hṅathukuhnu rusi dhyanya sa ṣāyake chorābhari (2) śatikuhnu hnāpā daṅāva osatana puṅāva kasāgā ṅayāva (3) rājagubājuyā chesa muṅāva saghaṃ[[na]] kāyāva, pyāhṅāva⟪va⟫ṅāo rāyaku va(4)ṅāva pujābhara kāya, gohma upādhyāhā jura ohmana pujābhara joṅā(5)o kharakana ritakāo bājana thātakāo, sādhaka conaonya vara si(6)dhayakāva tarasā vara dvāhā oṅāva pitavāsana coya gāgamaṇḍara śo(4t1)yāo cone cāhnasayā pujāśa muḍācāyyana caṃṇḍamahāroṣarṇa degu yāya (2) upādhyāhāna vajravirāśini degu yāya pāthakana triyodasā mayāya (3) deva caṃṇḍavi pramukhana pujā yāya
