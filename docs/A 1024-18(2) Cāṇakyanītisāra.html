❖ oṃ namo sarvvajñāyaḥ ||

praṇamya śilasā burddhaṃ trailokyādhipati prabhuṃ |

nānāsāstraṃ dhṛtaṃ vakṣa, rājanitiḥ samurccaya || o ||

trailokayā a(2)dhipati juraṃ, buddhatvaṃ namaskāra yāṅāva nānā śāstrasa pikāsyaṃ taṣya rājanisti muṅa kayā kha jena hlāya || 1 ||

adhiśyai(vam i)(3)daṃ śāstraṃ narojñāspati tatvata |

dhalmopadeśa bitayaṃ(!) kāryyākāryya śubhāśubhaṃ || o ||

niścayana manuṣyana thva śāstra dhararapaṃ tatva (4) siraṅāva dharmma noṃ adharmma noṃ siyuva upadeśavata juyuva kārjja akārjja ṣubha aśubha siyuva || 2 || (exp. 63t1–4)
