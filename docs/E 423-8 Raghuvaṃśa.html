Manuscript Features

001. Raghuvaṃśa (exps. 2–34t)

002. Śiśuhitaiṣiṇī (exps. 34b–189b)

There are two exposures of fols. 53v–54r, 141v–142r, 142v–143r, 152v–153r, 180v–181r, 189v–190r, 199v–200r, 203v–206r, 212v–213r, 217v–218r, 235v–236r, 261v–262r and 267v–268r, and three exposures of fols. 50v–51r. The exposures of fols. 189v–190r, 195v–196r, 199v–200r and 204v–205r are very dark.

The available folios of the Raghuvaṃśa are 50–55, 92–93, 95–97, 99, 103–117, 129–130; fol. 95 is damaged in the right margin but with no loss of text, and fol. 103 is damaged on the left side with considerable loss of text.

8.10–93 on fols. 50r–55v
