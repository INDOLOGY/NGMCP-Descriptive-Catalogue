Title Sarpasaṃhitā

Remarks The text is ascribed to the Sāmaveda

In the Preliminary title list the title was given as Nāgarājavaruṇapūjāvidhi (Inv. No. 98393), Parjanyasūkta (Inv. No. 98394), (Sāmaveda)Sarpasaṃhitā (Inv. No. 98395). Nāgarājavaruṇa is the name of one of nine nāgas and it is one of sub-colophons here. Parjanyasūkta, a hymn to Prajāpati, is to be recited in this pūjā (see End)
