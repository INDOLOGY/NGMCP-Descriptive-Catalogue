Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namo bhavānyai ||

vedānteṣu yam āhur ekapuruṣaṃ vyāpya sthitaṃ rodasī
yasminn īśvara ity ananyaviṣayaḥ śabdo yathārthākṣaraḥ |
antar yaś ca mumukṣubhir nviyamita(!)prāṇādibhir mṛgyate
sa sthāṇuḥ sthirabhaktiyogasulabho niḥśreyasāyāstu vaḥ ||

nāndyante sūtradhāraḥ || alam ativistareṇa , māriṣa , pariṣad eṣā pūrvveṣāṃ [[kavī(nāṃ)]] dṛṣṭarasaprabandhā , aham asyāṃ kālidāsagrathitavastunā , vikramorvvavaśīnānmā navena troṭakenopasthāsya , tad ucyatāṃ pātravarggaḥ , sveṣu sveṣu ⁅sthā⁆neṣv avahitair bhavadbhir bhavitavyam ,

praviśvya naṭaḥ , yathājñāpati(!) bhāvaḥ ,

sūtra , yāvad āryyavidagdhamiśrān śirasā praṇipatya vijñāpayāmi ||

praṇayiṣu dākṣiṇyavaśād athavā sadvastupuruṣabahumānāt |
śṛṇuta janā avadhānāt kriyām imāṃ kālidāsasya || || (fol. 1v1-2r1)

Sub-Colophons

iti niṣkrāntāḥ sarvve prathamo 'ṅkaḥ || śrīḥ || (fol. 8v3-4)

End

kupitā na tu kopakāraṇaṃ sakṛd apy ātmagataṃ (sma)rāmy ahaṃ |
prabhutā ramaṇeṣu yoṣitāṃ na hi bhāvasthalitāny apekṣate ||

sasambhramam upaviśya , (jā)nubhyāṃ sthitvā , kupiteti punaḥ paṭhitvā , avalokya ca , kathaṃ kathāvicchedakāriṇī svakāryye vyāsaktā , athavā a.. khalv idam ucyate |

mahad api paraduḥkhaṃ śītalaṃ samyag āhuḥ
praṇayam agaṇayitvā yan mamāpadgatasya |
adharam iva madāndhā pattim(!) eṣā pra° (fol. 41v24)
