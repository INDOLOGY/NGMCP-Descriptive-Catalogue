Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

oṁ asya śrī-ādityahṛdayākhyastotrasyāgastya ṛṣir anuṣṭup cha⟨ṃ⟩nda ādityahṛdayabhūto bhagavān brahma devatā nirastāseṣavighnajayābrahmavidyāsiddhau sarvatra śatruparājayasiddhau ca pāthe viniyoga[ḥ] ||

gāyatrī-aṅguṣṭhābhyān namaḥ || 1 ||

gāyatrītarjanībhyā⟨ṃ⟩n nama || 2 ||

gāyatrīmadhyamābhyāṃ namaḥ || 3 ||

gāyatrī-anāmikābhyāṃ namaḥ || 4 ||

gāyatrīkaniṣṭhikābhyān namaḥ || 5 ||

gāyatrīkaratalakarapṛṣṭhābhyān namaḥ || 6 ||

gāyatrīhṛdayāye(!) namaḥ || 7 ||

gāyatrīśirase svāhā || 8 ||

gāyatrīśikhāyai vaṣaṭ || 9 ||

...

tato yuddhapariśrāntaṃ samare cintayā sthitam ||

rāvaṇañ cāgrato dṛṣtvā yuddhāya samupasthitam || 1 ||

daivataiś ca samāgamya draṣṭum abhyāgato raṇam ||

upagamyābra[[vī]]d rāmam agastyo bhagavāṃs tadā || 2 ||

rāma rāma mahābāho śṛṇu guhyaṃ sanātanaṃ |

yena sarvān arīn vatsa samare vijayiṣyase || 3 || (fol. 1v1–7 and 2r2–6)

End

ādityaṃ prekṣya japtvedaṃ paraṃ harṣam avāptavān ||

trir ācamya śucir bhūtvā dhanur ādāya vīryavān || 29 ||

rāvaṇaṃ prekṣ[y]a hṛṣṭātmā jayārthaṃ samupāgamat ||

sarvayatnena mahatā vṛtas tasya vadhe bhavat || 30 ||

atha ravir avadan nirīkṣya rāmaṃ

muditamanāḥ paramaṃ prahṛṣyamāṇaḥ ||

niśicarapatim akṣayaṃ divitvā

suragaṇamadhyagato vacas tvarati || 31 || (fol. 4v1–6)

Colophon

ity ārṣe śrīmadrāmāyaṇe vālmīkīye ādikāvye yuddhakāṃḍe ādityahṛdayakathanaṃ nāma ṣaḍuttaraśatatamaḥ sargaḥ || (1) (fol. 4v6–7)
