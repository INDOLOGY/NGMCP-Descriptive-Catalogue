Manuscript Features

The first cover-leaf contains two stanzas on commendation of Sūrya beginning with:

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

namaḥ savitre jadekacakṣu(2)ṣe

jagatprasūtisthitināśahetave ||

trayīmayāya (3) triguṇātmadhāriṇe

viriṃcinārāyaṇaśaṃkarātmane

...
