Beginning

❖ oṃ namo nārāyaṇāya || ○ ||

purāṇāgamavākyāni parāmṛṣyan budhaiḥ saha |
yogīśvareṇa kṛyate dānavākyasamuccayaḥ ||
utpādyajñānasāmagrī jñātavyā vākyasammatā |
prasaṅgād grahahomādivākyam adhy (!) atra darśitaṃ |

tatra khuraśṛṅgavivarjitakṛṣṇacarmma 1 tathā svalpakṛṣṇacarmma 1 gomayena liptāyāṃ tu bhuvi kuśān āstīrya mṛgājinadvayaṃ prā(!)grīvaṃ sthāpanīyaṃ | tathopari guḍena prāṅmukhī udakyādāsavatsā dhenuḥ kalpanīyā | tatrai(!)ttamā dhenuḥ |

daśaraktikamāṣamānena guḍapalasahasra 4 tathā madhyama (!) guḍapalasahasra 3 adhama (!)guḍapalasahasra 2 caturthāṃśena vatsaḥ | dhenupucchapradeśe dānapratigrahau | (fols. 1v1–2r1)

End

oṃ adyādityāya grahāya | etāni gandhapuṣpadhūpadīpanaivedyādīni namaḥ |

oṃ somāya grahāya pūrvoktāni namaḥ | sarveṣām eva grahāṇāṃ pūjā | oṃ amukasagotrāyetyādi | etat amṛtamahāśāntipratiṣṭhārthaṃ dakṣiṇāṃm (!) ett (!) suvarṇṇan tubhyam ahaṃ saṃpradade | oṃ amukasagotrāṃ amukavedādhyāyinaṃ amukadevaśarmāṇaṃ duṣṭabṛhaspate (!) śubhapraśamanārthaṃ aśvatthasamidhahomakarmakartuṃ tvām ahṃ vṛṇe | oṃ amukasagotrāyetyādi | etat bṛhaspatihomapratiṣṭhārthaṃ suvarṇṇam idaṃ dakṣiṇān tubhyam ahaṃ saṃpradade | evam anyeṣv api duṣṭagraheṣu samidviśeṣeṇa homayan (!) iti || || (fols. 41v4–42r4)

iti dānavākyaṃ samāptam iti śubham || (fol. 42r4)
