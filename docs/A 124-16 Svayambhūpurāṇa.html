†ākāśanirmalābhūtaniśprapañcaguṇāśraye†


pañcabhūtātmakaṃ śāntas tasmai caityālayo(!) namaḥ ||

«End»


pratidine pratimāse prativarṣe sadā sadā〈ḥ〉 ||

†pṛthai† sumaṃgalavare śubhapūrṇayukte


swvarge ʼpi uṣṇiṣapade suralokaśreṣṭaḥ ||
