Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namo bhagavate vāsudevāya || śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || || śrīsarasvatyai namaḥ ||

yaḥ kātaṃtrasudhānidhānakaladhyaḥ(!)sāhityaratnālayaḥ
sānaṃdāsphuṭagadyapadyaracanāprāgalbha(!)śabdodayaḥ ||
ṣaṭkāvyāmṛtapūrapūrṇavadanaḥ sadvṛṃdacaṃdrodayas
tasyeyaṃ kavikarppaṭīkaracanā kaṃṭhe pratisthāpyatāṃ(!) || 1 ||

yatnād imāṃ kaṃṭhagatāṃ vidhāya
śrutopadeśād viditopadeśaḥ ||
ajñātaśabdārthaviniścaye[ʼ]pi
ślokaṃ karoty eva sabhāsu satyaṃ || 2 ||

ādau tāvad anuṣṭupchandasā caṃdravarṇanam ārabhyate ||

tatra tāvat prathamapāde pañcākṣare || karppūrapūra | hiṃḍīrapiṃḍa || rajanīkara || saṃbūkaṃṭha(!)|| gaṃgāpravāha || (fol. 1v1–3)


End

caturthacaraṇe || tarkodarka(!) || viśuddhaṣṇa(!)racitaḥ || śreyān sarve(!)janeṣu nirmalamatiḥ || śaktyā nirjitaḥ śatrusāranivahaḥ punaḥ | vāgīśvaro[ʼ]yaṃ nṛpaḥ | śrībhīmadevo nṛpaḥ || jīvād(!) ayaṃ bhūpatiḥ | bhīmo yamo lokyatāṃ || vijñānapārāyaṇaḥ || vidvān ayaṃ rājate || || || (fol. 10v2–4)
