Excerpts

Beginning

iti bahuvacanāṃtaṃ evaṃ (ṣadyadam idaṃ sūtraṃ ṣadyadatvam) eva yuktaṃ yataḥ

saṃhitaikapade nityā nityā dhātūpasargayoḥ

nityā samāse vākye tu sā vivakṣām apekṣae(!) iti

yadyapi a i kau ca matvrtha ityādau samāsepi saṃdhir na tathāpy asārvatrikoyaṃ vidhir iti kvacit tu ekāca iti saṃdhir eva dṛśyate etatsūtraṃ kāryadvayavidhāyakavarṇaparigaṇanā nirūpakaṃ cety arthaḥ

tad evāha anena anenoktavakṣyamāṇasūtrasanuccayena pratyāhāragrahaṇāya varṇā akṣarāṇi parisāmastyena gaṇyaṃte saṃkhyīkriyaṃte paripādya(!) prakāśyaṃta itysrthaḥ ca punas teṣāṃ pūrvvoktānāṃ a i u ṛ ḷ akṣarāṇāṃ samānasaṃjñā ⟪samānasaṃjñā samāna⟫ iti nāma vidhīyate kriyate ⟪kriyate⟩⟩  athavābhidhīyate kathyate nanu paṃcānām akārādīnāṃ nyāsavaisadṛśye(!) kathaṃ samānasaṃjñā saṃjāghaṭīti atrocyate ekamātratvenasamānatvam eteṣāṃ nanu

(fol.7r1-8 )

End

nanu lakārosti rakāro nāsti rād yapo dviḥ kathaṃ lagati tatrāha ralayor iti rkāralakārayoḥ sāvarṇyaṃ sadṛśatvaṃ vā jñeyaṃ tena rād yapo dvir iti kakārasya dvitvena tavalkkāra iti siddhaṃ rakāralakārayoḥ upacārāt vāgrahaṇād (rā)vede ṛkāraḷkārayor api sāvarṇyaṃ vaktavyaṃ hotṛ ḷkāraḥ savarṇe hotṝkāraḥ atra ṛḷvarṇayo(!) savarṇatvepi ralayor ity anena sāvarṇyaṃ ṛḷvarṇasthāninoḥ prāyaḥ sāvarṇyaṃ ralayor bhavet tathā vede savarṇatvaṃ syāt ṛkāraḷkārayoḥ pari aṃkaḥ paryyaṃkaḥ evaṃ pali aṃkaḥ palyaṃkaḥ ubhaya… (fol.21v4- 8 )
