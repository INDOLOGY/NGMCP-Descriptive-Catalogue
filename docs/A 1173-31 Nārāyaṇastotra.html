«Text:»

❖ oṃ namo nārāyaṇāya || mārkaṇḍeya uvāca ||

naraṃ nṛsiṃhaṃ naranāthamacyutaṃ pralambabāhuṃ kamalāyatekṣaṇaṃ |

kṣitīśvarairarcitapādapaṅkajaṃ namāmi viṣṇuṃ puruṣaṃ purātanam || 1 ||

jagatpatiṃ kṣīrasamudramandiraṃ rathāṅgapāṇi(!) munivṛndavanditaṃ |

śriyaḥ patiṃ śrīdharamacyutaṃ prabhuṃ namāmi govinda manantavarccasaṃ || 2 ||

ajaṃ vareṇyaṃ jaraduḥkhanāsanaṃ guruṃ purāṇaṃ puruṣottamaṃ prabhuṃ |

sahasrasūryadyutimacyutaṃ tanuṃ namāmi bhaktyā harimadya mādhavaṃ || 3 ||

puruṣṇutaṃ(!) puṇyavatāṃ parāṅgatiṃ kṣiterddharaṃ(!) lokadharaṃ prajāpatiṃ | (!)

paraṃparāṇāmapi kāraṇaṃ hariṃ namāmi lokatrayakarmmasākṣiṇaṃ || 4 ||

bhogetyanantasya payonidhau mudā purā hi śete bhagavānanādikṛt |

kṣīrodavīcī kalikāmbunākṣitaṃ taṃ śrīnivāsaṃ praṇamāmi keśavaṃ || 5 ||

yo nārasiṃhaṃ vapurāsthito mahān

purā murārir mmadhukaiṭabhāntaka |

samastalokārttiharaṃ hiraṇyakaṃ

jaghāna viṣṇuṃ satataṃ namāmi taṃ || 6 ||

anantamavyaktamatīndriyaṃ prabhu

sparśādinīlādivihīnamekaṃ |

yasyedṛśīśasya hareḥ svarūpaṃ

vadanti tajñāḥ praṇamāmi taṃ hariṃ || 7 ||

ānandamekaṃ tvajaraṃ vidātmakaṃ

hṛdālayaṃ yogibhireva pūjitaṃ |

aṇoraṇīyān mamabuddhisakṣayaṃ

namāmi bhaktaṃ priyamāśva(!) hariṃ || 8 ||

iti śrīnārasiṃhapurāṇe nārāyaṇastotraṃ samāptam iti || || (fol.1–2)
