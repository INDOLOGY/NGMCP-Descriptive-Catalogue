Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namo bhagavatyai ca ||

yad yogibhir bbhavabhayārttivināśayogya,m

āsādya vanditam atīvaviviktacittaiḥ |

tad vaḥ punātu haripādasarojayugma,m

āvirbbhavatkramavilaṅghitabhūr bbhuvaḥ svaḥ ||

pāyāt sa vaḥ sakalakalmaṣabhedadakṣaḥ,

kṣīrodakukṣiphaṇibhoganiviṣṭamūrttiḥ |

śvāsāvadhūtasalilāt kalikākarālaḥ,

sindhuṣ pranṛtyam iva yasya karoti saṅgāt ||

prapadye devam īśānaṃ, śāśvataṃ dhruvam avyayaṃ ||

maheśvaraṃ mahātmānaṃ, viśvasya jagataṣ patim ||

tapaḥsvādhyāyanirataṃ, mārkkaṇḍeyaṃ mahāmuniṃ |

vyāsaśiṣyo mahātejā, jaiminiṣ paryyapṛcchataḥ(!) || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

jaiminir uvāca ||

bhārate cābhavan yan me sandehaṃ cchedanaṃ dvijaḥ |

tad bhavadbhiṣ kṛtaṃ yan na kayid (!) anyaṣ kariṣyati ||

yūyaṃ dīrghāyuṣaḥ santu prajāvṛddhiviviktatāḥ |

sāṃkhyayoge tathā cāstu buddhir avyabhicāriṇī ||

pitṛśāpakṛtā ddukkhā,r (!) ddaurmmanasyaṃ vyapaitu vaḥ ||

etāvad uktyā (!) vacanaṃ sa jagāma svam āśramaṃ |

cintayan paramodāraṃ śakṣiṇāṃ vākyam īritaṃ || || (fol. 235r7–v1)

Colophon

iti śrīmārkkaṇḍeyapurāṇaṃ samāptaṃ || ○ || saṃvat 929 āṣāḍhakṛṣṇacaturthī ādityavāra thva kuhnu śrīviryyanārāyaṇava jhāyatā kṛṣṇāvatīna dāna yāṅā julo || || || || śubham astu || || || || (fol. 235v1–3)
