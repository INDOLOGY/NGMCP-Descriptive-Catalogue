Manuscript Features

The text is not in standard Sanskrit while giving measurements of idols ("pratimā"), and we have not marked these mistakes below in the excerpt.

From fol. 7r–12v, the manuscript is damaged on the lower right-hand margin of the verso and upper right-hand margin of the recto of each folio, causing a lost of minimal amount letters.

On the front cover-leaf is written:

"buddhadevatākālakṣaṇa

"pramāṇakṛ? ❖ namo buddhāya || namo dhamāya namo saṃghāya"

"devalakṣaṇa devalakṣaṇa" || || ||

"buddhadevapratimālakṣaṇaḥ" || "ātreyatilakabauddha"

On the back cover-leaf is written:

(" .. sarva la .. jñāyā ha?")

2 ("sūdhamadrāta anyā"

"āhāraniyamāni ca" |

"tathādhyāvacanadvārā"

.. "vajasya? budhen narā" ||)

"devapramimālakṣaṇa"
