Excerpts

Beginning

-thā vṛttaṃ himālaye ||

śrīpārvaty uvāca ||

kathaṃ pūrvaṃ mayā cīrṇaṃ vratānām uttamaṃ vrataṃ ||

tat sarvaṃ śro(2)tum ichāmi (!) tvatsakāśād vṛṣadhvaja ||

īśvara uvāca ||

asti tatra mahān divyo himavān parva(3)teśveraḥ (!) |

nānāsiṃhasamāyukto nānādrumasamākulaḥ |

nānāpakṣigaṇair yukto nānāmṛgani(4)ṣevitaḥ ||

yatra devā (!) sagaṃdharvā (!) siddhacāraṇaguhyakāḥ || (fol. 4r1–4)

End

tato (!) kuryāt svayaṃ devi baṃdhu(9)bhiḥ saha bhojanaṃ |

sarvān kāmān avāpnoti saubhāgyaṃ labhate dhruvaṃ ||

mṛtavatsā tu yā nārī (12r1) putraṃ prāpya cirāyuṣaṃ |

na punar garbham āyāti sā gauryyā saha līyate || || || (fol. 11v8–12r1)

Colophon

iti skaṃdapu(2)rāṇe umāmaheśvarasaṃvāde sodyāpanaṃ haritālikāvrataṃ saṃpūrṇaṃ || ❁ || ❁ || ❁ || || (fol. 12r1–2)
