«Complete transcript»


śivāya namaḥ || ||


agastir uvāca || ||


adya me saphalaṃ janma adya me saphalaṃ tapaḥ

adya me saphalaṃ jñānaṃ śaṃbhos tvatpādadarśanāt 1


kṛtārtho yaṃ kṭrārtho haṃ kṛtārtho haṃ na saṃśayaḥ

adya te pādapadmasya darśanād bhaktavatsalaḥ 2


śivaḥ śaṃbhuḥ śivaḥ śaṃbhuḥ śaṃbhuḥ śaṃbhuḥ śivaḥ śivaḥ

iti vyāharato nityaṃ dināny āyāṃtu yāṃtu me 3


śive bhaktiḥ śive bhaktiḥ śive bhaktir bhave bhave

sadā bhūyāt sadā bhūyāt sadā bhūyāt suniścalā 4


ājanmamaraṇaṃ yas tu mahādevo na daivatam

mā janiṣṭa suto vaṃśe jāto drāg vipadyatām 5


jātasya jāyamānasya garbhasthasyāpi dehinaḥ

mābhūn mama kule janma yasya śaṃbhur na daivatam 6


vayaṃ dhanyā vayaṃ dhanyā vayaṃ dhanyā japatpate

ādidevo mahādevo yad asmatkuladaivatam 7


hara⟨ḥ⟩ śaṃbho mahādeva viśveśāmaravallabha⟨ḥ⟩

śiva śaṃkara sarvātman nīlakaṇṭha namo stu te 8


mṛtyuṃjayāya rudrāya śitikaṇṭhāya śaṃbhave

amṛteśāya sarvāya mahādevāya te namaḥ 9


mahādeva mahādeva mahādevety a[[yaṃ]] dhvaniḥ

sa eva muktikāṃtāyāḥ pāṇigrahaṇaḍiṇḍimaḥ 10


nīlakaṇṭhāṣṭakam idaṃ yaḥ paṭhec chivasannidhau

śivalokam avāpnoti śivena saha modate 11


iti śivastutiḥ samāpto(!) yam śubhaṃ bhavatu || ❁ || ❁ || ❁ || ❁ || ❁ || (fols. 1–3)
