Manuscript Features

The MS contains many synonymous notes especially above the word of the main text and sometimes it appears as a marginal note. We may call it Ṭippaṇam, i.e. a short commentary.

Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ svasti || śrīgaṇapataya (!)  namaḥ || śrīgurubhyo namaḥ ||

prabhavaviratimadhyajñānavadhyā (!) nitāṃtaṃ

(2) viditaparamatatvā (!) yatra te yogino pi |

tam aham iha nimittaṃ viśvajanmāṃtyayānām (!) 

anumi(3)tam abhivaṃde bhagrahaiḥ kālam īśam || 1 ||

vilokya garggādimunipraṇītaṃ

varāhalallādikṛtaṃ (4) ca śāstraṃ |

daivajñakaṃṭhābharaṇārtham eṣā

viracyate jyotiṣaratnamālā || 2 || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

iti lalitasuvṛttāṃ nirmmalā (!) ratnamālām

udaharad adhikā(2)rthāt sadguṇā (!) ratnakośāt |

nijagurupadabhaktaḥ śrīpatī (!)  sā sudīghaṃ (!)

caturagaṇakakaṃṭhe rājatām ujvala(3)śrī (!) || 13 ||

bhrātar adyatanavipranirmmitaṃ,

śāstram etad iti mā vṛthā tyaja |

āgato yam ṛṣibhāṣito rthato,

nā(4)paraṃ kim api kīrttitaṃ mayā || 14 ||

suvṛttayā śrīpatibaddhayānayā,

kaṃṭhasthayā jyotiṣaratnamālayā |

(5) alakṣaṇo py arthaparicyuto py alaṃ,

sabhāsu bhūmnā gaṇako virājate || 15 ||     || (fol. 62v1–5)

Colophon

iti śrīśrīpatibha(6)ṭṭaviracitāyāṃ jyotiṣaratnamālāyāṃ pratiṣṭhāprakaraṇaṃ viṃśatitam (!) samāptaḥ || ❖ || śubham astu sarvvadā ||     || (fol. 62v5–6)
