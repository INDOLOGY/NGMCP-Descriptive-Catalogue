Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrībhavānī śāṅka[rā]bhyāṃ ||

vātayā śubhaphala salaṃyāhmaṃ sacoyātatina thanā pikāsyaṃtayā ||

uttaroṣṭha thaṃthahmathusi || sṛkka, hve, sarvvakāmaviva ||

lalāṭadhāya, hmosata, the 2, 3, 4 dhanya theṅa duna 3 conasā niśreṇīnāma,

kāmanā phalaviva || mnuva hmosataḍo antarajaya || nigāla, devamaṇi nāma śubha || (fol.1v1–4)

End

asmāditi, thva vāsarathaṃ ṅā prayogana salaṃ rajalapara ṅāva, bhidyotrakāyā va, saharayāya, thvanali māsalaṃ yāhmaṃ dayaṃ sarvāṅgasa, pyākapaṃkana lepana yāke || karmaṇeti, thva guli prakāraṇa, cā matholasalaṃ yāke, prayogayāta ṅāva, salaṃcātholapiva, salaṃcā tholake karmasa, thva mahāprayoga atyanta bhiṃṅa || iti garbhadhāraṇopāyaḥ || (fol.82r1-4)

Colophon

iti nānāgraṃthasamuccayam aśvaśāstraṃ samāptaṃ ||

śrī śrīsumati jayajitāmitramalladevasana thva pustaka dayakā ||

bhāgīrāma pradhāṃnāṃgayā velasa saṃvat 810 kārttika sudi 9 || || (fol.82r4-6)
