Manuscript Features

The MTM contains the following texts:


1. Devīkavaca (fols. 1–2r1)

2. Sāradāstotra; extracted from the Brahmapurāṇa (fols. 2r2–3v)

Excerpts

«Beginning of 1. Devīkavaca»

❖ oṁ namaḥ śrīdevyai ||


viṣṇur uvāca ||


aṃguṣṭhe pātu me gaurī, tarjjanyāṃ kālikāvatu |

madhyamāyāṃ mahākālī, anāmāyāṃ maheśvarī ||


kaniṣṭhāyāṃ mahādevī, dakṣahaste jayā tathā |

vijayā vāmahaste tu, mama rakṣatu sarvvadā || (fol. 1r1–3)


«End of 2. Sāradāstotra»


sarvvapāpavinirmmuktaṃ, śubhagolokavatsalaḥ |

vāṃchitaṃ phalam āpnoti, ṣaḍbhi[r] māsair na saṃśayaḥ || 10 || (fol. 3r7–3v1)


«Colophon of 1. Devīkavaca»


iti viṣṇubhāṣitaṃ devyāḥ kavacaḥ samāptaḥ || || (fol. 2r1)


«Colophon of 2. Sāradāstotra»


iti śrībrahmapurāṇe sāradāstotraṃ samāptaṃ || || (fol. 3v2)
