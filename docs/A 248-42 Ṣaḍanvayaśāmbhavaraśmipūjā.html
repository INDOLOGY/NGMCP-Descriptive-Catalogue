«Beginning: »

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

vedārthasārthaviparītavilokanena

prāyo bhavaed yajanalopam avekṣya śāstraṃ ||

tatkūṭam aṭṭaviśajī(!)karaṇena jāta-

dānakṣamasva tava pādayugena yāce ||

sahasrāre mahāpadme tatopari rahasyavit ||

caraṇadvayasaṃbhūtau svecchāvigrahadhāriṇau ||

devadevyau tathā nāthā na svamaṃtrayutān smaret ||

guruñ ca gurupatnīñ ca devadevīṃ vibhāvayet ||

prātaḥprabhṛtisāyāntam ityādi ||

iti saṃprārthanāpūrvaṃ praṇamya gurupādukām ||

bahir nigacchet śuddhātmā puṇyadeśāntadūrataḥ

kṛtvāvaśyakam āsthāya śaucācamanapūrvakaṃ ||

gaṇḍūṣaṃ dantakāṣṭhañ ca kṛtvā cācamya vāgyataḥ || (fol. 1v1–4)

«End: »

sarvvavyāhṛtisaṃsthāto bhūdevyā saṃbhuśaktayaḥ ||

namo nama manasi sthitā nāthāya yatkar(ma) kāryaparāya svāhā ||

caraṇaṃ pavitram ityādi vedāgamavacanam ||

śāṃbhavaṃ caraṇaṃ dhyātvā saccidānandalakṣaṇam |

pavitraṃ paramaṃ sākṣāt purāṇaṃ paramāmṛtam ||

yena pūtā mahātmānaḥ kṣaṇāt saṃsārasāgarāt ||

samuttīrya parānandaṃ bhuñjante paraśāṃbhavam ||

tasmiṃścitte layaṃ kṛtvā tadrūpo ham iti smaret ||

ityānandaparaḥ sarve prāṇabuddhir layo bhavet || || (fol. 14r1–4)
