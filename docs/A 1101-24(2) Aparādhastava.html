Manuscript Features

A 1101/24 contains three mss:

  • [A] A 1101-24(1) (Saṅkṣepa)Pārthivapūjāvidhi (incomplete)
  • [B] A 1101-24(2) Aparādhastava (incomplete)
  • [C] A 1101-24(3) Kālajñāna (complete)

Their folios are photographed in the following order:

B1r (blank) – B1v – C1r (blank) – C1v – C2v – C2r – A2v – A2r –C3v – C3r –B4r – B4v (blank)


Fols. 2 and 3 of [B] are missing.

Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

ādau karmaprasaṃgāt kalayati kaluṣaṃ māṛkukṣau sthitasya

tanmūtrāmedhyamadhye vyathayati nitarāṃ jāṭharo jātavedāḥ ||

yad yad vā tatra duḥkhaṃ prabhavati sutarāṃ śakyate kena vaktuṃ

kṣaṃtavyo meparādhaḥ śiva śiva śiva bho śrīmahādeva śaṃbho || 1 ||

(fol. 1v1-3)

End

... saṃduḥ(!) kīrtibhājaṃ jaḍaṃ

kāruṇyākarabhūpate paśupate doṣākaraṃ pāhi ṃām || 17 || (fol. 4r1)

Colophon

iti śrīcaraṇaśaṃkaraproktaṃ aparādhastavaṃ sampūrṇam || śubham ||

saṃvat 1866 māghaśuklapratipadi sūrye dine likhitaṃ, māghavasyedaṃ pustakam śubham || śrīsāmbāśivārpaṇam astu || śubhṃ bhūyāt

(fol. 4r1-3)
