Excerpts

Beginning

|| śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

vaidyaṃ potas tathā nāga[ḥ] kṣāṃti[ḥ] śakyo yathākramaṃ ||
paṃcaratnam idaṃ proktaṃ boddhavyaṃ paṃḍitaiḥ sadā || 1 ||

vaidyaṃ pānarataṃ naṭaṃ kupaṭhitaṃ svādhyāyahīnaṃ dvijaṃ
yuddhe kāpuruṣāṃ(!) hayaṃ gatarayaṃ mūrkham parivrājakaṃ ||
rājānaṃ ca kumaṃtribhiḥ parivṛtaṃ deśaṃ ca sopadravaṃ
bhāryyāṃ yauvanagarvitāṃ pararatāṃ muṃcaṃtu śīghraṃ budhāḥ || 1 || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

dhanvaṃtari[ḥ] kṣapaṇako marasiṃha-śaṃkū
vetālabhaṭṭa-ghaṭakharppara-kālidāsāḥ ||
khyāto varāhamihīro(!) nṛpateḥ sabhāyāṃ
ratnāni vai vararucir nnava vikramasya || 10 || (fol. 4r2–4)

Colophon

iti navaratnaṃ samāptaṃ ||
śrīsaṃvat 1724 samaye likhito yaṃ graṃthaḥ || śubhaṃ || (fol. 4r4–5)
