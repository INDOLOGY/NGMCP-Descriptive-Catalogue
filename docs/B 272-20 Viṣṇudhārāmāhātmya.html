Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || || ātreya uvāca ||

śrīśaile kārttike māsi yaḥ kuryāt tu pradakṣiṇāṃ ||

sa tīrtharājavadarīgamanasya phalaṃ labhet ||

gire pradakṣiṇā tatra koṭijanmāghanāśinī ||

iti (2) sūryyamukhodgītaṃ kurukṣetre śrutaṃ mayā ||

kasmin māsi tithau kasyāṃ snātvā kṛtvā pradakṣiṇāṃ ||

naro labhet phalaṃ kutra vadarīgamanasya yat ||

tat kṣetraṃ pāvanaṃ manye guro mayi kṛpāṃ kuru || ||

(3) sanatkumāra uvāca ||

brahmāvartte tu sahasi brahmalokaikakāraṇe ||

ekādaśyāṃ tu yaḥ kuryāt sa labhed vadarīphalaṃ || (fol. 1v1–3)

«Sub-colophon:»

iti sanatkumārasaṃhitāyāṃ tīrthamāhātmye uttarakhaṇḍe viṣṇudhārāmāhāmye (4) brahmakuṇḍotpattir nāma caturtho dhyāyaḥ || 4 || (fol. 4v3–4)

End

brahmalokaṃ vrajaṃty atra brahmatīrthaniṣevanāt ||

devā (!) paśyata madrupam enaṃ śaṃvaraśāyinaṃ ||

jyeṣṭhe rājīvane snāto yaḥ paśyati ca parvasu (3) ||

saṃtyajya sarvapāpāni purā kṛtāni cātrasaḥ || (!)

gavāṃ lakṣaphalaṃ prāpya mama lokaṃ sa gachati (!) || ||

skaṃda uvāca ||

viṣṇupadhyāṃ tataḥ snātvā devarṣir yādavaiḥ samaṃ (4) ||

yayau sadarppakaḥ kṛṣṇa suprabhānagaraṃ punaḥ || || (fol. 8r2–4)

Colophon

iti himavatkhaṇḍe viṣṇudhārāmāhātmye skaṃdāgatsyasaṃvādeḥ (!) pradhyumnakṛtatīrthayātrākramo nāma ṣa(5)ṣṭhodhyāyaḥ || 6 || śubham bhavatu || siddhiṃ bhūyāt || || || (fol. 8r4–5)
