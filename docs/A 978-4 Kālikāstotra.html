Manuscript Features

The spaces where the akṣaras are written are remained blank and the remaining parts of the folios are painted with black ink. On the every side of the folios there are two lines, which are painted like the lines made up of bricks.

Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

ullasad danujavṛndakhedinī

bhūriduḥkhabhavabhītibhedinī ||

kaṇṭhalambitakapālamālikā

maṃgalaṃ mama karotu kālikā || 1 ||

śoṇitādragajacarmmavāsinī (!)

mattabhairavamahāṭṭahāsinī ||

daityabāhukṛtakāś ca śālikā

maṃgalaṃ mama karotu kālikā || 2 || (exp. 1left, t 1–7)

End

rājase sakalasargasajane

sātvikī ca jagatām sulakṣaṇe ||

nāmasiṃhajagatāṃ surolikā

maṃgalaṃ mama karotu kālikā || 7 ||

bhaktinamramuniyakṣadevatā

siddhakinnarasamūhasevitā ||

śuṃbhadaityamakarandarolikā

maṃgalam mama karotu kālikā || 8 || (exp. 1right, b 5–11)
