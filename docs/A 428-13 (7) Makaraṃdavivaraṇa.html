Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśa jaya 2

prajñāyataḥ prāpya kṛtapratijñaṃ

sparddhāṃ vidhatte pra(2)sabhaṃ pratijñaṃ ||

ajño pi taṃ śrīśivanāmadheyaṃ

gurūpamaṃ svīyaguruṃ bhaje(3)haṃ || 1 ||

śrīmachivāt samadhigamya varaprasādaṃ

vṛttāṃśubhir vivaraṇā(4)bhinavāravṛṃdaṃ ||

etad divākaravikāsitam āryavarya

bhṛṃgā bhajaṃtu (5) makaraṃdapipāśayaiva || 2 ||

pṛṣṭhaschitāsan na śakomiteṣṭa

śakā(6)vaśeṣapramakoṣṭakasthaṃ ||

tithyādi vārādi savallikaṃ tad

yojyaṃ (7) samīpastha śakasya kaṃde || 3 || (fol. 1v1–7)

End

kasyāṃ diśi darśanam adarśanata(8)yā || yāsyatītyetaduktaṃ grahalāghave || laghu (golpadūnād) udeti pūrve

syāt (9)(bhū)ri gatiḥ graha pratīcyāṃ ||

bhūyān laghugaḥ paratra nāstaṃ prācyāṃ bhūrijavo(10) laghu prayāti || 130 || iti udayāstasādhanaṃ|| (1)dvivedapaṃceṃdu 1542 vihīna…(5)iti saṃvatsarādirānayanaṃ ||❁(exp.114b:7–115:5)
