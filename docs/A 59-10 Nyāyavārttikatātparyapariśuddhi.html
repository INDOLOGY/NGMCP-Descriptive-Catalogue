Beginning

pratyakṣādipramāṇety arthākṣipte pi ca prakṛtam astīti svarūpakathanārthaṃ, lakṣaṇaṃ hi jijñāsuparipādanāya, sā cāvijñātasāmanya(!) viśeṣe nāsti | sāmānyajñānañ ca tallakṣaṇādhīnam atas tallakṣaṇam viśeṣavibhāgañ ca kṛtvā jijñāsām utthāpya viśeṣalakṣaṇāvatāraḥ tathā ca sāmānyalakṣaṇa〇vibhāgoddeśajñānasya jijñāsādijananadvārā viśeṣalakṣaṇajñānaṃ prati hetutvāt tadgocarayo(r api) [hetuhetu](madbhā)vaṃ vivakṣitvoktaṃ hetutveneti, vyavacchedo nāsyāvidheya ity ata āha〇 prayojanam iti | (fol. 1v1-3)

End

yady asau pratipādakaḥ kathaṃ svahṛdayaguhāniḥkṣiptasamayā(n eva śa)bdān uccārayet, jānāty eva yenāviditasamayā (ete pratipādakāḥ ///4 vipralambhaḥ, tathāpi samayāntarakaraṇe ka upayogaḥ, prasiddhasamayeair eva vipralambhasaṃbhavāt | yatrāpi guptasamayai(r) vyavahāraḥ, tatrāpi na pratipādyaṃ prati samayo gopyaḥ api tv aviśvastān pratī(ti) [hṛdi ni](dhāyā)ha tasmād iti ||
