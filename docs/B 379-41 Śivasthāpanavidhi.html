❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīmahābhairavāya ||


atha śivasthāpanayā vidhiḥ ||


dina soyāva makasa upādhyāsyaṃ ādina prohita munakāva jajamānasyaṃ goyatasyaṃ


devaradayakeyā ājñā phone prohita sakalasyaṃ ājñā viya || || thvanali punaḥ dina soyāva


kārmopanihaṃṅāva devaro hokoyeracchoya || karmmiparisyaṃ dava roho lūyakāva vovalajyājuko


yāye || || thvanali sindūrajātrā yāṅā (exp. 2:1–11)
