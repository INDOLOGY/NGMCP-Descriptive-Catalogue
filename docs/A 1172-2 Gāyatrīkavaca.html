Manuscript Features

In the beginning of the MS there is hayagrīvastotra. The text is as follows:

prācī sandhyā kācidantar niśyayāḥ (!)

ajñādṛṣṭer añjitaḥ śrīr apūrvā ||

ktī vedān pātu me vājivaktrā

gīśākhya vāsudevasya mūrttiḥ || 1 ||

aṇatājñānasaṃdohadhvāṃtadhvaṃsanakarmaṭham ||

māmi turagagrīvaṃ hariṃ sārasvatapradam || 2 ||

okadvayam idaṃ prātar aṣṭāviṃśativārakaṃ ||

ayataḥ paṭhato nityaṃ kṛtsnā vidyā prasiddhyati || ||

iti śrīhayagrīvastotram śubham || || || || ||

On fol. 4v there is written: paṃ bhuvaneśvarabhaṭṭarāī.
