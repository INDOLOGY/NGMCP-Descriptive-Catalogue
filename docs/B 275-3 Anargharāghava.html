Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namah śrīrāmacandrāya ||

niḥpratyuham upāsmahe bhagavataḥ kaumodakīlakṣmanaḥ

koka(2)prīticakorapāraṇapaṭu(!) jyotismatī locane ||

yāmbhyāṃ arddhavivodhamugdhamadhuraśrīr arghani(3)drāyito,

nābhipalvapuṇḍarīkamukulaḥ kaṃvoḥ sapatnīkṛtaḥ ||

api ca ||

viramati(4)mahākalpe nābhīpathaikaniketaḥ

tribhuvanapuraḥ śīlpī yasya pratikṣaṇam ātmabhūḥ |

(5) kim adhikaraṇā kīdṛk kasya vyavasthitir ity asāv

udaram aviśad draṣṭuṃ tasmai jagannidhaye namaḥ || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

api ca ||

devasyātmabhuvaḥ kamaṇdalujalastotāṃsi mandāki(3)nī,

gaṅgābhogavatī mayā nipunate yāvatrilokīm(!) imāṃ |

tāvad dīrayaśo rasāyanamadhusya(4)ndaḥ kavīnām ayaṃ,

jāgarttu śrutiśaṅkalīvalayitabhyomāvagāhī guṇaḥ || ○ || || ○ ||

(5)iti niḥkrāntāḥ sarvve (fol. 167v2–5)

Colophon

nāyakāndo nāma tarkkapañcānanaśrībhaṭṭācāryyamaudgalyago(6)trasambhavena śrīmurāriśarmmaṇā viracitaḥ saptamoʼṇkaḥ samāpta iti || ❁ ||

(68r1)sarvvagīrvvāṇavarddhitamahāgarvvaprauḍharatnaprabhābharitabhavānīcaraṇasarasiruhamadhuvratnṛpendra(2)mallamahārājādhiṣṭhite nepālādhirvāsinā śrīviśvambharopādhyāyena dijanmanā navaviva(3)ravāhaṅkite saṃvatsare ʼsmin phālguṇaśukladaśamyāṃ some naipāyikavāraṇapaṃcāna(4)nabhaṭṭācāryamurārikṛtanāṭakagrantho vyalikhi || (fol. 167v5–168r4)
