Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

sabhā praśne (!) na vaktavyaṃ kuṭilānā (!) tathā niśi ||

nāparāhne (!) aviśvasyā (!) tvaritā (!) na vadet kadā || 1 ||

phalapuṣpayuto yo hi daivajñaṃ paripṛchati (!)

(2) tasyaiva kathayet praśnaṃ satyo bhavati nānyathā (!) 2

satyaṃ satyaṃ punaḥ satyam udhṛtya vaca ucyate

vedācchāstraṃ paraṃ nāsti na praśnajjyotiyo (!) cyate || 3 ||

(3) ādau ciṃtāparijñānaṃ kṛtvā praśnaṃ vicārayet

vyomadṛṣṭir bhavej jīvo mūlaṃ bhūmyāvalokane 4 (fol. 1v1–3)

End

mūle yojyaṃ100 dvādaśahir bhāgo daya (!) ladhvaṃ8 (!) śeṣamāsa0 ladhvaṃ (!) pakṣasaṃjñaṇ śeṣaṃ divasā eva triṃśalladhvaṃ (!) mūle triṃśad bhā(9)jye yojyaṃ30 saptaśeṣe vārasaṃjñaśuklapakṣe gurūtaḥ (!) kṛṣṇapakṣe śukrataḥ syād iti ladhvaṃ (!) saptabhājyaṃ mūle yojyaṃ11 ṣaṣṭhibhir bhāgaṃ śe(10)ṣe ghasrādikaṃ syur iti spaṣṭārthaṃ || (fol. 6r8–10)
