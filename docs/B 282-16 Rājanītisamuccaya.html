Excerpts

Beginning

[❖ oṃ namaḥ sarasvatyai ||

praṇamya śirasā viṣṇu, trailokyādhipatiprabhuṃ

nānāśāstro dhṛtaṃ vakṣye, rājanīti samuccayaṃ || 1 ||

trailokyayā adhipatijuraṃ nārāyaṇa ṭoṃ namaskārayāṅāva nānāśāstra,

sapiṃkāsyaṃtayā, rājanīti muṃṅatayā khaṃjura lhāya || ||

adhītyaiva midaṃ śāstraṃ, naro jñāsyati tatvataḥ |

dharmmopadeśa vinayaṃ, kāryākāryaśubhāśubhaṃ || 2 || (fol. 1r1-5 )

… hitayāya kāmanāna janahlāya gvanaṣu manaṣyana (!) thva śāstra dharayaṃ sayāmātraṇa ṅeṅa hmayā māmana hitayāṅā thyaṃ thva śāstrana hitayā tatvaṃ || || (fol. 1v1–2)

End
