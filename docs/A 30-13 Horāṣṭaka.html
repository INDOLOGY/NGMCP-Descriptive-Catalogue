Manuscript Features

The writing on fol. 8r has been partly rubbed off.

There is an additional fol. added to this MS (exp. 10 bottom), written in another hand, which was possibly used only as a cover leaf.

On the index cards the title of the text has erroneously been transcribed Hīrāṣṭakam.
