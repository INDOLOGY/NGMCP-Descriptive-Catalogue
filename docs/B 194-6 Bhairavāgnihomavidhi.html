❖ oṁ namaḥ śrīmahābhairavāya ||


sūryyārgha || adyādi jathāvidhi || oṁ hrāṁ hrīṁ hrūṁ has hreṁ mhreṁ sa ▒ hraṁ saḥ


śrīmahāmātaṅgabhairavāya namaḥ || gurave ācāryya idaṃ māsnaṃ namaḥ || purṣpaṃ namaḥ ||


+coyake || purṣpabhājanaṃ || || tato kalasapūjā || akhaṇḍamaṇḍalākāraṃ tyādi || uru sumaraṇaṃ || (exp. 21A1–11)

hrāṁ rāṁ rīṁ rūṁ agni svāhā || āhūti || ghṛtāhūti || ||

homapātra adhomukhī kṛtvā śiroveṣṭako kāya tāmbūra arghapātrodakena kuṇḍa bhrāmya adhomukhī kṛtvāgre oṃ hrāṁ


garcha garcha suraṃ śreṣṭha ārtmasaṃsāravāhana ||


jatra saṃbhun trayodeva tatra garchati hutāśana ||
