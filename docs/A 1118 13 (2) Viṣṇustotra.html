Excerpts

«Beginning»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

narāyaṇaṃ sahasrākṣaṃ, padmanābhaṃ purātanaṃ ||

praṇato smi ṛṣīkeśaṃ, kiṃ me mṛtyuḥ kariṣyati || 1 ||

govindaṃ puṇdarīkākṣaṃ, anntam ajam avyayaṃ ||

keśavaṃ ca prapanno smin kiṃ me mṛtyuḥ kariṣyati || 2 || (fol. 4v1–4)


«End»

bhūtātmānāṃ mahātmānaṃ, jagadyonim ayonijaṃ ||

«Colophon»
