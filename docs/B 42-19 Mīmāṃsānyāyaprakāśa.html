Beginning

|| śrīgaṇeśāya nama (!) ||

yatkṛpāleśamātreṇa puruṣārthacatuṣṭayaṃ

prāpyate tam ahaṃ vande govidaṃ (!) bhaktavatsalaṃ 1

anaṃtaguṇasampannam anaṃtabhajanapriyaṃ

anaṃtarupiṇaṃ vaṃde gurum ānaṃdarūpiṇaṃ 2

iha khalu paramakāruṇikena bhagavatā jaiminy ṛṣiṇā athāto dharmajijñāsety ādinā dvādaśasvādhyāyeṣu (!) dharmo vicāritaḥ (fol. 1v1–3)

End

yat karoṣi yad aśnāsi yaj juhoṣi dadāsi yat |

yat tapasyasi kauṃteya (!) tat kuruṣva madarpaṇam

iti smṛteḥ | asyāś cāṣṭakādismṛtivat prāmāṇyād ity anyatra vistaraḥ |

kvāhaṃ mandamatiḥ kveyaṃ prakriyā bhaṭṭasammatā |

tasmādbhakter vilāsoyaṃ govindagurupādayoḥ || 1 ||
