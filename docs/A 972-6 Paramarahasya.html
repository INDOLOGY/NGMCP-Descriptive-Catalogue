Manuscript Features

It is noticed that that the fol. 33 is mentioned two-times but text is not repeated. That's why we could say that the fol. 33 (which is mentioned second time) might be the fol. 34 and it should be run so on.

Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīnāthapādukebhyo namaḥ

kailāśaśikharāsīnaṃ. trikuṭaṃ śaktisaṃgamaṃ

tasyābdaṃ paṅkajaṃ divyaṃ. karṇikottuṅgake(2)ṣaraṃ (!)

dalāvyuṣṭakakoṭīnāṃ. (!) †śobhākārasukiñjaraṃ†

tasyārdhe saṃsthito devaḥ kaulīśaḥ kaulikīsahaḥ (!) (fol. 1v1–2)

End

paratattve mano budhyā, nirālambe tu bandhayet

vadhabandham iti khyātaṃ, paratattve niyāmi(3)taḥ

evaṃ ādīny anekāni tattvaparyyāyavācakāḥ

na hi saṃjāyate mūḍhā, vilome niratā narāḥ

sāsitāḥ (!) pīṭhajais tatra yogi(4)nībhi (!) niyāmitāḥ

bhūyo bhūyas tu saṃsāre, vyathākīrsma (!) bhramanti te (fol. 51r2–4)

Colophon

iti mudrāpīṭhamatasāre kubjikāmate paramarahasye pañcamaḥ (5) paṭalaḥ samāptaḥ saṃvat 954 phārgunaśudi (!) 12 śaniścaravāsare 7 evaṃ dine saṃpūrṇa (!) śubham (fol. 51r4–5)
