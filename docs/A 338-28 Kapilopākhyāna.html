Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||    ||

atha dhyānayogaḥ ||

antakaraṇa śuddhabhayāko yogī
mithyā jagatmā hunago viyogī ||
tyo satva guṇakā bhajanaile gardā
harīcaraṇakā saraṇaimā pardā || 1 ||

ajñānakā dvāra ṣulīgayāko
sākṣīsvarupale sthir bhai rahyāko ||
ajñānako kārya mania banyāko
manile saṃsāra khadāgaryāko || 2 || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

bhaktile pūrṇa bhai man harilāī dīnu
sarvatra kehi haridekhi pharak nalīnu ||
jo dhyānamā ḍuvirahaṃcha puruṣ jagatmā
tyo antyakāla pugijāncha harītaṣatmā || 30 ||

dāsa hu prabhu hajurakā bhramale bhulyāko
śrīrudrakānta paneūu abhimāna caḍhyāko ||
āthakā kṛpāle abhimāna chuṭigayāko
pheri caraṇamā haridāsa bhulyāko || 31 || (fol. 7v5–8r4)

Colophon

iti śrīmat bhāgavate mahāpurāṇe tṛtīyaskaṃdhe kāpileyopākhyāne aṣṭāṅgayoge dhyānayogo nāmaḥ (!) aṣṭāviṃśodhyāyaḥ || 28 ||    || (fol. 8r4–6)
