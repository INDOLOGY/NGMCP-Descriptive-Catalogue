Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namaḥ śrīsarvabuddhabodhisattvebhyaḥ ||

namaḥ śrībhagavatyai āryaprajñāpāramitāyai ||

tadyathā ||

śrutismṛtigativijaye svāhā

śrutaṃ mayaikasmaye gṛddhrakūṭe tathāgataḥ

vijahāradharmadhātau gaṃbhīre buddhagocare ||

budhisattvam uccaye yā mahākuladevatathā sarasvatyā ca mahādevatayā || śriyā ca mahādevatā dṛḍhayā ca nahāpṛthivīdevatayā rītyā ca mahādevatayā || evaṃ pramukhābhir mahādevatābhir aneka devanāgayakṣarākṣasagandharvāsuragaruḍakinnaramahoragamanuṣyāmanuṣyaiḥ sārddhaṃ | athāyuṣmānānando bhagavantam etad avocet || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

ākā[[śa]]tulyā gagaṇa(!)svabhāvā

māyāmarīci(!)dakacandrakalpa ||

sarve ca sattvāḥ sukhinaḥ svabhāvā

mahāntaśūnyāḥ svayanāyakasya ||

atha bhagavān āsanād⟨y⟩utthāya brahmasvareṇāvocat || sādhu sādhu te kuladevate śāstā dadāmi sādhu te kuladevate punaś ca sādhvīti || || idam avocat bhagavān āttamanās te ca bodhi sattvā bodhisattvasamuccayā kuladevatā sarasvatī mahādevīpramukhā[ḥ] sā ca sarvāvatīparṣad asuragaruḍakinnaramahoragādipramukhā bhagavato bhāṣitam abhyanandan iti || || (fol. 90v5–91r2)

Colophon

āryasuvarṇaprabhāsottamaḥ sūtrendrarājaḥ parisamāptaḥ ||

ye dharmā hetuprabhā(!)vā

hetu[s] teṣāṃ tathāgata hyavadat

teṣāṃ ca yo nirodhaḥ

evaṃ vādī mahāśramaṇaḥ || || śubham || || śreyostu saṃvat 1030 pauṣamāse śuklapakkṣe pū(!)rṇamāsi maṃgalavārasa mahābuddhayā paṃḍitaśrībhuvanānandana o kuli bahili sa cona hma ⟪…⟫ coyā viyā jula || thva pustaka dayakā pāṭha yāṅā dharmana parivāra cchiyāṃ saptabṛddhiṃ pūrṇa juyamāla || thva pustaka sunānaṃ lobha yāya madu jula || || śubhamastu || || (fol. 91r2–6)
