Beginning

[ṭīkāṃśaḥ]

śrīsītārāmābhyāṃ namaḥ | śrīrāmānujāya namaḥ ||
śrīrāghavaṃ daśarathātmajamaprameyaṃ sītāpatiṃ raghukulānvaya ratnadīpaṃ ||
ājānuvāhum araviṃdadalāyatākṣaṃ
rāmaṃ niśācaravināśakaraṃ namāmi || 1 ||
kūjaṃtaṃ rāmarāmeti madhuraṃ madhurākṣaraṃ ||
āruhya kavitāśākhāṃ vaṃde vālmīkikokilaṃ || 2 || (fol. 1v1–4)

[mūlāṃśaḥ]

tapaḥ svādhyāyanirataṃ tapasvīvāgvidāṃvaraṃ
nāradaṃ paripapracha vālmīkir munipuṃgavaṃ
konvasmin sāṃprataṃ loke guṇavān kaś ca vīryavān
dharmajñaś ca kṛtajñaś ca satyavākyo dṛḍhavrataḥ 2 (fol. 4r7 and 4v6)

gaṃgāvaraṇaṃ puṇyaṃ sāgarasyāpi pūraṇaṃ 2
