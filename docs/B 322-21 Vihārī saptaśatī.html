Excerpts

Beginning of the basic text

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||    || atha vihārī śatasayika tilaka mūla dohā ||    ||

†merī bhava bādhā haro rādhā nāga rasoī ||
jātana (7) kī kyāi pare śyāma harana duti hoe || 1 || (fol. 1v6–7)

Beginning of the commentary on the basic text

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || śrīgurugaṇeśo jayati ||    ||
śrītilaka kavi kī uktiḥ || dohā ||

śrīgurugaṇeśa ke vacana ko citta me dha(2)ri keṃ dhyāna ||
karatu vihārī satasayā ṭīkā vudhi anumāna || 1 ||

so rādhā nāgara śrīkṛṣṇa ko nāma hai. so merī bhava kahiye saṃsā(3)ra(tā) kī bādhā kahiye pīḍā. haro kahiye. dūrakaro. isa ko iha matalaba hai. ki mero mokṣakarau || athavā. merī kahie mamatā. ehī (4)saṃsāra meṃ baḍī vyathā hai. (fol. 1v1–4)

End of the basic text

†chipyo chapā karachi tichiyo (9) tamasasi haarana sahyāri
hasati hasati cali sarisaṣimuṣataiṃ ācaruḍāri || 151 ||

(24r5) arī ṣarī saṭapaṭa ṣarī vidhu ādhemaga heri.
saṅgala gemadhupīne nai bhānine gali adheri || 152 ||

yuva(6)ti jonhū mai mili gai nai kula ho tilaṣāe
sodhe kī ḍorī lagī anīca nīsaṃga jāe† || 153 ||    || (fol. 23v8–24r7)

End of the commentary on the basic text

†saṣī ko vacana saṣī prati || yuvati kahi paināyikā. so cāṃdanī mai mili gaī maule (4) kau nahī deṣāī dīti hai. soṃdho kahiyai sugaṃdha. tā koḍo resai | ālī kahie saṭī te saṃga jāti hai | unmīlita mudrālaṃkārako (5) saṃkaraḥ† || 153 || (fol. 24r3–5)
