Excerpts

Beginning

śrī devy uvāca ||

prāṇabaṃdho dayāsiṃdho guhyaśāstravicārakaḥ ||

kālikāvacanaṃ divyaṃ śatrusaṃhāradāruṇaṃ || 1 ||

kathayasvāsamāsena yena me śaktiruttamā ||

īśvara uvāca ||

śṛṇu vakṣyāmi kavacaṃ sarvaśatruvidāraṇaṃ || 2 ||

sarvaśāṃtikaraṃ sākṣāt mahāpātakanāśanaṃ

sarvavyādhipraśamanaṃ sarvasaṃpatpradāyakaṃ || 3 ||

caturvargapradaṃ sākṣāt mahāpāṭakanāśanaṃ || ||

oṃ asya śrīdakṣiṇakālikāyāḥ kavacasya śrībhagavān mahākālaṛṣiḥ anuṣṭupchadaḥ ādyā śaktiḥ śrībhagavavatī devatā hrīṃ bījaṃ svāhā śaktiḥ sarvasamṛddhaye viniyogaḥ ||

klīṃ pātu mastake devī śikhāyāṃ krīṃ maheśvarī || 4 ||

bhrūmadhye klīṃ maheśāni pātu māṃ cakṣuṣor dvayoḥ ||

huṃ pātu nāśikāraṃdhre huṃ jihvāyāṃ maheśvarī || 5 ||

hrīṃ pātu tālumūle ca hrīṃ pātu oṣṭayugmakaṃ ||

dakṣiṇe kālike pātu sarvāṃge kālasauṃdarī ||

krīṃ pātu nāsikādvaṃdvaṃ krīṃ pātu karṇayugmakaṃ ||

krīṃ pātu jihvāmūlaṃ ca huṃ pātu vadane punaḥ || 7 || (fols. 24v4–25r2)

End

na jāyate vyādhis tasya duḥkhaśokabhayaṃ kvacit ||

gorocanākuṃkumair vā raktanaṃdanakair api || 48 ||

pāvakair vā maheśāni likhed yas tu viśeṣataḥ ||

aṣṭamyāṃ maṃgaladine caturdaśyāṃ mathāpi (!) vā || 49 ||

yuddhe vā devadeveśi likhed deśikasattamaḥ ||

maghāyāṃ śravaṇāyāṃ vā revatyāṃ vā viśeṣataḥ || 50 ||

siṃharāśau gate caṃdre karkaṭastho divākara ||

mīnarāśau guru gate vṛścikasthe śaniścare || 51 ||

likhitvā dhārayad yas tu gurūṇāṃ ca maheśvarī ||

dhyānadhāraṇayogena dhārayed yas tu bhaktita (!) || 52 ||

acirāt tasya siddhi (!) syān nātra kāryā vicāraṇā ||

vādī mūkaṭiyoṣitastapayatīḥ pakṣmāvaro śasyati || 53 ||

garvī (!) kharvati sarvavic ca jaḍati vaiśvānaraḥ śītati ||

ācāryāṣṭakasiddhirūpaparamaṃ siddhāṣṭakaṃ tadbhaje || 54 ||

tāṃ vahne bhavabhītibhaṃjanakarīṃ kālī girīsapriyaṃ (!) || 55 || (fol. 27r1–7)

Colophon

iti viśvasārataṃtre dakṣiṇakālikākavacaṃ śaṃpūrṇam || 16 || (fol. 27r8)
