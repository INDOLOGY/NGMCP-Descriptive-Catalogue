❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīvajrasattvāyai ||

oṃ namaḥ śrī-aṣṭamātṛkāyai || ||

aṣṭapīthapūjāvidhim āha || ādau gurumaṇḍala(!) || trisamādhitvaṃ dhunaṅā o vidhithyaṃ kramana dhuna ṅā o balikrarma(!) yāya || madye sayogāmbarajñāneśvarīpramukhana aṣṭamātṛkā bhairava gaṇeśa kumāla (!) mahākāla kṣatrapāla siṃghini byāghrini(!) thvate staṃ jāya dhūpa yāya ākarṣaṇa pādyādi puṣpabyāsapūjā lāsyā stuti tarpaṇa || (fol. 1v1–4)

«End: »

oṃ namaḥ śrī-aṣṭamātṛkāyai ||
