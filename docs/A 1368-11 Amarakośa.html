Manuscript Features

1. This text (folio 1-11) contains from beginning of prathamakāṇḍa to 6th śabdādivarga.

2. There are 2 prakīrṇṇapatra (folio 1 and 2) from any other text at the last.

Excerpts

«Beginning»

oṃ śrīdevyai namaḥ ||

yasya jñānadayāsindhor agādhasyānaghā guṇāḥ | sevyatām akṣayo dhīrāḥ sa śrīye cāmṛtāya ca ||

gvanasa ātmāsa jñānano dayāno, mahāsamudratvaṃ thyam pari(2)pūrarapañ vanaṅāna, niṣpāpa jusyaṃ vannagva guṇa tholva | amo parameśvara | tathāgatatva | akṣaya avināsī jusya vannagva, iha lokasa lakṣmī, paralokasa mokṣa gāva, jñānīlokana |(3) sevarapagvana || || (fol. 1v1-3)


«End»

pravandhaḥ kalpanākathā || paṇḍiyāna ciṅā grantha ||

pravalikā prahelikā hiyānī dhāya ||

smṛtis tu dharmasaṃhitā | vyavahāra dharmasumatiḥ ||

sa(8)māhṛtis tu saṃgrahaḥ nānā śāstrasa pikāsyaṃ muṅatā granthayā nāma samāhṛti dhāye || saṅgrahana dhāye ||

samasyā tu samasyārthā || memvana dvayakā granthasa magā(kra) taṃ || (fol. 11v7-8)
