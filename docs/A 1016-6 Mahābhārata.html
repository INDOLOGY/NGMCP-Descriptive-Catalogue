Excerpts

Beginning

oṁ śrīgaṇeśāyā (!) namaḥ

nārāyaṇaṃ namaskṛtya naraṃ caiva narottamaṃ
devīṃ sarasvatīṃ caiva tato jayam udīraya(!)t 1

janamejaya uvāca ||

tam apratimasatvaujobalavīryasamaṃn(!)vitaṃ
hataṃ devavrataṃ śrutvā pāṃcālyena śikhaṃḍinā 1

dhṛtarāṣṭras tato rājā śokavyākulalocanaḥ
kim aceṣṭata viprarṣe hate pitari vīryyavān 2

tasya putro hi bhagavan bhīṣmadroṇamukhai rathaiḥ
parājitya maheṣvāsān pāṃḍavān rājyam icchati || 3

tasmin hate tu bhagavan ketau sarvadhanuṣmatāṃ
yad aceṣṭata kauravyas tan me bru(!)hi tapodhana 4 (fol. 1v1–4)

End

saṃjaya uvāca

yuddhaṃ kṛtvā mahad ghoraṃ paṃcāhāni mahābalaḥ
brāhmaṇo nihato rājan brahmalokam avāptavān 1

svadhīte yat phalaṃ vede tad asminn api parvaṇi
kṣatriyāṇām abhīrūṇāṃ yuktam (atra sad yaśaḥ) 2

ya idaṃ pataṭhe (!) prva śṛṇuyād vāpi nityaśaḥ
samucyate mahāpāpaiḥ kṛtair ghoraiś ca karmmabhiḥ 3

yajñāvāptir brāhmaṇasyeha nityaṃ
ghore yuddhe kṣatriyāṇāṃ yaśaś ca
śeṣau varṇau kāmam iṣṭaṃ labhete
putrān pautrān nityam iṣṭāṃs tathaiva 4 (fol. 288v8–11)

…
navaślokasahastrāṇi tathā navaśatāni ca
ślokā nava tathāśītiḥ saṃkhyātā(!) tattvadarśinā 9

droṇaparvaṇi viprebhyo bhojanaṃ paramārcitaṃ
śarāś ca deyā rājeṃdra cāpāny asidharās tathā 10

aśvā rathā gajāś caiva vāsāṃsy ābharaṇāni ca 10 (fol. 289r6–8)

Colophon

iti śrīmahābhārate śatasāhastryāṃ saṃhitāyāṃ vaiyāsikyāṃ droṇaparva samāptaṃ 203 (fol. 289r8–9)
