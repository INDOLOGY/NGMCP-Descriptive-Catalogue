śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

praṇamya paramātmāna(!) bāladhīvṛddhisiddhaye ||

indrādayopi yasyāntaṃ na yayuḥ sabdavāridheḥ ||

prakriyān tasya kṛtnasya kṣamo vaktuṃ naraḥ kathaṃ ||

tatra tāvat saṃjñā saṃvyavahārāya sagṛhyate(!) ||

a i u ṛ ḷ samānāḥ || anena pratyāhāragrahaṇāya varṇāḥ parigaṇyaṃnte(!) ||

teṣāṃ samānasaṃjñā ca vidhīyate || naiteṣu sūtreṣu sandhir anusandheyo vivakṣitatvād vivakṣitas tu sandhir bhavatīti niyamāt || laukikaprayoganiṣpattaye samayamātratvāc ca || hrasvadīrghaplutabhedāḥ savarṇāḥ || eteṣāṃ hrasvadīrghaplutabhedāḥ parasparaṃ savarṇā bhaṃṇyaṃnte(!) || lokāc cheṣasya siddhir iti vakṣyati || tato lokata eva hrasvādisaṃjñā jñātavyā(!) || ekamātro hrasvaḥ || dvimātro dīrghaḥ || trimātraḥ plutaḥ || vyaṃjanaṃ chārddhamātrakaṃ || || eṣām anyepy udāttādibhedāḥ || uccair upalabhyamāna udāttaḥ || nīcair anudāttaḥ || samavṛtyā svaritaḥ || e ai o au saṃdhyakṣarāṇi || eṣāṃ hrasvā na santi || ubhaye svarāḥ || akārādayaḥ pañca catvāra ekārādaya ubhaye svarā ucyaṃte ||                                                                                                 (fol.1v1-2r6)
