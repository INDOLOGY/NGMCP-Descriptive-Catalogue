Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṁ namaḥ sarvvajñāya ||

samāptā vasuvikrāntavikramiparipṛcchā prajñāpāramitā nirddeśaḥ sarvvasatvasantoṣaṇād bodhisatvapiṭakāt ||

nairātmyaṃ yatra dharmāṇāṃ dharmmarājena deśitaṃ

laṃkāvatāra(!) tat sūtram iha yatnena likhyate |

evaṃ mayā śrutam ekasmin samaye bhagavān laṃkāpure samudramalayasikhare viharati sma || nānāratnagotrapuṣpapratimaṇḍite mahamahatā(!) bhikṣusaṃghena sārddha(!) mahatā ca bodhisatvagaṇena nānābuddhakṣetrasaṃnnipatitaiḥ bodhisatvair mahāsatvair anekasamādhivasitābalābhijñāvikrīḍitai(!) mahāmatibodhisatvapūrvvaṅga[[mai]]s sarvvabuddhapāṇya[[bhiṣikā]]bhiṣiktaiḥ svacittadṛsyagocaraparijñānāca(!)kuṣalair nānāsatvacittacaritrarūpanayavinayadhāribhiḥ paṃcadharmmasvabhāvavijñāna- nairātmyādvayagatiṅgataiḥ (fol. 1v1‒2r3)

End

bhūmipreveśān labhate abhijñā vasitāni ca ||
jñānamāyopamaṃ | kāyam abhiṣiktaṃ ca saugatam |

nivarttate yadā cittaṃ nivṛttaṃ pasyato jagat ||
muditāṃ labhate bhūmiṃ labhante buddhabhūmiṃ ca ||

āśrayena nirvṛttena viśvarūpo maṇir yathā |
karoti savtakṛtyāni pratibiṃbaṃ yathā jale ||

sadasatpakṣanirmmuktam ubhayaṃ nobhayaṃ na ca |
pratyekaśrāvakīyābhyāṃ niṣkāntā(!) saptamī bhavet |

pratyātmadṛṣṭadharmānāṃ bhūtabhūmiviśodhitaṃ |
bāhyatīrthavivinirmuktaṃ mahāyāṇaṃ vinirddiśet ||

parāvṛttir vikalpasya cyutināsa
(fol. 231v2–4)
