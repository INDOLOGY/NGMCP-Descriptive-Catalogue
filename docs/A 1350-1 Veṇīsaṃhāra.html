Foliation figures in the lower right-hand margin of the verso under the word rāma; the sides of many folios are broken, so the folio numbers no longer appear.

Place of Deposit NAK

Accession No. 3/609

Manuscript Features

Available folios 3–6, 8–53

The manuscript starts near the beginning of the first aṅka and runs up to almost the end of the work. At the end, some sentences and two verses are lost.

In many folios, the letters are slightly disrupted on the right side.

'On the top left of fol. 3r is written bhiśmaparvako ṭīkā bhanī calānamā leṣeko ('in the register, listed as a commentary on the Bhismaparva).
