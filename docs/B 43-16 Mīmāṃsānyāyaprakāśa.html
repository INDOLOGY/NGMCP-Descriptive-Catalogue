Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

yatkṛpāleśamātreṇa puruṣārthacatuṣṭayaṃ ||

prāpyate tam ahaṃ vaṃde goviṃdaṃ bhaktavatsalaṃ || 1 ||

anaṃtaguṇasampannam anaṃtabhajanapriyaṃ ||

anaṃtarūpi(2)ṇaṃ vaṃde gurum ānaṃdarūpiṇaṃ || 2 ||

iha khalu paramakāruṇikena bhagavatā jaiminiṛṣiṇā athāto dharmajijñāsety ādinā dvādaśasvadhyāyeṣu dharmo vicāritaḥ | tatra vede(3)na prayojanam uddiśya vidhīyamānortho dharmaḥ |  yathāāgādi | (fol. 1v1–3)

End

yat karoṣi yad aśnāsi (9) yaj juhosi dadāsi yat |

yat tapasyasi kaūṃteya tatkuruṣva madarpaṇam

iti smṛteḥ | 

asyāś cāṣṭakādismṛtivat prāmāṇyād ity anyatra vistaraḥ ||

kvā(10)haṃ maṃdamatiḥ kveyaṃ prakriyā bhaṭṭasammatā

tasmād bhakter vilāso ʼyaṃ goviṃdagurupādayoḥ || 1 ||

graṃtharūpo madīyo ʼyaṃ vāgvyāpāraḥ suśobhanaḥ ||

anena (11) prīyatāṃ devo goviṃdo bhaktavatsalaḥ || 2 || (fol. 31r8–11)

Colophon

iti śrīanantadevasūnunā āpadevena kṛtaṃ mīmāṃsānyāyaprakāśasaṃjñakaṃ mīmāṃsāprakaraṇaṃ samāptat (!) śubham astu || saṃvat 1786 || vidhirasāyaṇo ||  yadaiṃdravāyavāgraṃthaprathamamaharitpādāvahargrahaṇam vivakṣita tadaṇḍakamilitavadāvṛttir bhavati ...(fol. 31r11–12)
