śrī ❖

palāśako varṇana

palāśaḥ kiṃśukaḥ parṇo vātapotho ʼ tha yājñikaḥ

triparṇo vakrapuṣpaś ca pūtadur brahmavṛkṣakaḥ

brahmopanetā kāṣṭhadrur nṛpanāmanighaṃṭake

kṣāraśreṣṭho raktapuṣpo bījasnehaḥ samidvaraḥ ||

trivṛntaś ca tathā dhanve dravye karmī prakīrtitaḥ

brahmapādapa saṃjñas tu made ca samid uttamaḥ ||

palāśako phūlako cūrṇa dūdhamā bhijāudai sukāudai garnu ekāisa dina pugepachi ekatolā cūrṇa dūdhasaṃga khānu bhīmasena jattiko baliyo huncha | (exp.1b:24–29)
