Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrīgurubhyo namaḥ ||

oṃ namo nāṭeśvarāya ||

atha nṛtyasiddhi vidhānaṃ ||

nṛtyasiddhi hṅathukuhnu ||

adhivāsa(2)na karma yāya ||

sūryyārgha || guru namaskārādi || navātmā nyāsa ||

jalapātra arghapātra || bhūtaśuddhi || ātamapūjā (3) || āvāhana || trideva 3 ||

bali dvaṃdusa 1 || pañcalaṅgapūjā ṣaḍṛṅgaṇa ||

rajana thānasā, mathānasā caturasrama ce(4)ta siṃdhurana buyāva cakra coya || (fol. 1v1–4)

«Extracts:»

pehnu maphesyaṃ nāṭeśvarasa hlāya, pyākhana huya juroṃ ||     ||

iti si(7)ddhividhāna juro ||     || (fol. 51r6–7)

❖ caturthī karuṇa kāya vidhiḥ ||

khovalu dakva māla || gāyanapani sohmaṃ || karuṇa kāra vane cānasa || (8) hṅathvathyaṃ nṛtyeśvarapūjāna yāya || tālapūjā yāya ||

mantra ||

oṃ vaṁ viṣṇave tāladharātmane namaḥ ||

karuṇa mūlamantreṇa triyāñja(9)li || (fol. 51r7–9)

End

thvate nakasa pyākhana piṃdāne vidhiḥ ||     ||     || (4)

daśakathā neyā rividhāna ||

nakasa nāṭēśvarasake ni pūjā yāya ||

hṅathvathyaṃ laṃgasālāsa pyākhana huya māra ||

pyākhana ma(5)hurasā || tālana vidhithyaṃ yāya māra ||

thvate dhunaṅāva samaya cepana thiyake || svāna kokāya ||

āchusa biya || thvate daśaka(6)thaṃ neyā vidhiḥ ||     ||     ||

karuṇa kāyā chesa svānake sakalyaṃ ṅoyāva, nadī vaharape ||

thvanali tālapa(7)tiṅa padohana pūjā ||

nakasa hṅathvathyaṃ nāṭēśvarasake pūjā ||

hṅathvathyaṃsa karmma khaṅa yāya māla ||

dhūpaḥ dīpaḥ jāpa(8)ḥ stotraḥ ||     ||

tāla bahuli khiṃ pvaṃgā gācheṃ sutra ādinayā,

tālapati ācāḍahātoṃ biye, ācāryasyaṃ devaṭoṃ (9) hlāya ||

ghāghara. khaḍi pvaṃho mohonī kaṃda dakva samastaṃ duṃta ||

cupe pūjā || paśu tarppaṇa || samaya dakṣiṇā || citra, siṃdhara (52v1) sagona, svāna || sākṣi thāya || bhojya yāya || kalaṃka choya || (fol. 52r8–52v1)

«Sub–colophons:»

iti nṛtyeśvarastotra samāptaṃ ||     || (fol. 8v5)

iti sthaṇḍili ṅāva kalaśāhutividhi ||     ||     || (fol. 33r9)

Colophon

thvate kathyapyākhana dayakeyā vidhiḥ samāptaḥ || śubhamastu ||     || (fol. 52v1)
