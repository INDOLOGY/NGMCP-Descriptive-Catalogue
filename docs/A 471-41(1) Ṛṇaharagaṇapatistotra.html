Excerpts

Complete transcript

|| śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

kailāsaparvate ramye śaṃbhuṃ caṃdrārdhaśekharaṃ ||
ṣaḍāmnāyasamāyuktaṃ papracha nagakanyakā || 1 ||

śrīpārvaty uvāca ||

deveśa parameśāna sarvaśāstrārthapāraga ||
upāyaṃ ṛṇanāśasya kṛpayā vada sāṃprataṃ || 2 ||

śi-

(fol. 1v1–5; exp. 3t)

-------

devaḥ pārvatīputro ṛṇanāśaṃ karotu me || 2 ||
tripurasya vadhāt pūrvaṃ śaṃbhunā sā ca pūjitaḥ ||
sa devaḥ pārvatīputro ṛṇanāśaṃ karotu me ||
hiraṇyakaśipo śryādau vadhārthaṃ viṣṇunārcitaḥ ||
sa devaḥ | mahiṣasya vadhe devyā gaṇanāthaḥ prapūjitaḥ |
sa devaḥ | tārakasya vadhāt pūrvaṃ kumāreṇa prapūjitaḥ |
sa devaḥ vṛtrasya ca vadhārthāya śakreṇa paripūjitaḥ |
sa devaḥ pārvatīputraḥ

(folio no. unknown; exp. 3b)
