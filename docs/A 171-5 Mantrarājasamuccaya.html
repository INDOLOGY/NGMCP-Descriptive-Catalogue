Manuscript Features

On the fols. 164r–167r (exps. 157b–160b) are written the table of content of uttarārddha (Mantrarājasamuccaya)

pra(śnākṣa)raṃ raudrayuktaṃ tithivāraṃ ca miśritam. ||
navabhis tu hared bhāgaṃ śeṣe na phalam ucyate || 1 ||

saptatṛtīya (!) kathayaṃti vārttā naveka (!) paṃcaturitaṃ vadaṃti. || (!)
...

The fols. 62r and 62v are in reverse order.
