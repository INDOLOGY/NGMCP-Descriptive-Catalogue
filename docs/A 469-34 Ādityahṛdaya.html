Manuscript Features

There are impresses of the seal of Chandra Shumshere on the front and back cover-leaves, together with the year 1970 [VS].

On the front cover-leaf is written:

amāvāsyā(!) somavāre bhaumavāre caturthikā |
saptamī(!) ravivāre ca sūryaparvaśataiḥ samā(!)

Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

śrīsarasvatyai namaḥ

śrīsūryyāya namaḥ

arjuna uvāca

jñānaṃ ca dharmaśāstrāṇāṃ guhyād guhyataraṃ tathā
mayā kṛṣṇa parijñātaṃ vāṅmayaṃ sacarācaraṃ 1

sūryyastutimayaṃ nyāsaṃ vaktum arhasi keśava
bhaktyā pṛchā(!)mi deveśa kathayasva prasādataḥ 2

sūryyabhaktiṃ kariṣyāmi kathaṃ sūryyaṃ prapūjayet
tad ahaṃ śrotum ichā(!)mi tvatprasādena yādava 3

śrībhagavān uvāca

rudrādidaivataiḥ sarvaiḥ pṛṣṭena kathitaṃ mayā
vakṣye[ʼ]haṃ karmmavinyāsaṃ śṛṇu pāṃḍava yatnataḥ 4

asmākaṃ yat tvayā pṛṣṭam ekacitto bhavārjuna
tad ahaṃ saṃpravakṣyāmi ādimadhyāvasānakaṃ 5 (fol. 1v1–6)

End

śan no devī(!) namas tubhyaṃ jagaccakṣur namo[ʼ]stu te
padmāsanaḥ [[padmakaraḥ]] padmagarbhasamu(!)dyutiḥ
saptāśvarathasaṃyukto dvibhujaḥ syāt sadā raviḥ [1]49

ādityasya namaskāraṃ ye kurvaṃti dine dine
teṣāṃ janmasahasreṣu dāridryaṃ nopajāyate [1]50

namo dharmavidhāne ca namas te kṛtasākṣiṇe
pratyakṣadevadevāya bhāskarāya namo namaḥ [1]51

udayagirim upetaṃ bhāskaraṃ padmahastaṃ
sakalabhuvananetraṃ raktapadmopade(!)yaṃ
timiraka[[ri]]mṛgendraṃ bodhakaṃ padminīnāṃ
suravaram abhivaṃde suṃdaraṃ viśvadīpaṃ 152

anyathā śaraṇaṃ nāsti tvam eva śaraṇaṃ mama
tasmāt kāruṇyabhāvena rakṣa rakṣa surottama 153 (fols. 9v9–10r6)

Colophon

iti śrībhaviṣyottarapurāṇe śrīkṛṣṇārjunasaṃvāde ādityahṛdayastotraṃ saṃpūrṇam ❁ śubham ❁ (fol. 10r6–7)
