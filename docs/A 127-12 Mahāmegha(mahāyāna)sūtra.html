oṁ namo ʼcinyasāgarebhyaḥ(!) sarvvasamyak sambuddhebhyaḥ ||

evaṃ mayā śrutam ekasmin samaye bhagavān nandopanandanāgarājabhavane viharatisma || śrāvasi

ratnagarbhamahāmeghamaṇḍalakūṭāgāre mahatā bhikṣusaṃghena sārdhaṃ mahatā ca bodhisattvasaṃghena

sārdhaṃ mahatā ca nāgarājagaṇena sārdhaṃ (fol. 1v1-2)



«End»


imāni tathāgatanāmāni likhitvā śucini āsane sthāpayitvā saptadhūpakaṭacchūktām utkṣipet ākāśe

pañca sapta ca vārāṃs tathāgatanāmāni parivarttayet || mahatī pūjāṃ kṛtvā anāvṛṣṭau saptāhaṃ

avyavacchinnaṃ pravarttayitavya(!) devo varṣiṣyati || || (fol. 29v4-5)
