Manuscript Features

On exp. 2 is written:

padmanāthakesaridvijena paṇḍitāllabdham idaṃ pustakaṃ hārāvalyāḥ śubham astu ikṣumatyāstīre nepālamaṇḍale 1980 vaikramābde

There are two exposures of fols. 1v–2r and 20v–21r.

Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

hārāvalī || ||

bhujagapativimuktasvacchanirmokavallī-

vilasitam anukurtvan yasya gaṅgāpravāhaḥ ||

śirasi sarasabhāsvan mālatīdāmalakṣmīr

laghayati himagauras stu [ʼ]stu vaḥ sādhyasiddhyai || 1 ||

kalpāvasānasamaye sthitaye kavīnān

dehāntaraṃ kim api yā sṛjati prasannā ||

yasyāḥ prasādaparamāṇur api pratiṣṭhām

adhyetikām api namāmi sarasvatīṃ tām || 2 || (fol. 1v1–4)


End

sudhiyā janamejayena yatnād

dhṛtisiṃhena samaṃ nirūpiteyam ||

vidito bahudṛśvabhiḥ kaviṃdrair

bhuvi koṣānumataḥ śramo madīyaḥ || 77 ||

hitvā mahāśābdikatābhimānaṃ

mātsaryam anyatra muhur nidhāya ||

hārāvalī yaḥ prakaroti kaṃṭhe

vidagdhagoṣṭhīṣu paraṃ sa bhāti || 278 || || (fol. 23r3–6)
