Excerpts

Beginning of the root text

duḥkhatrayābhighātāj

jijñāsā tadapaghātake hetau |

dṛṣṭe [[sāpārthā cen

naikāṃtātyaṃtato bhāvāt. |]] (fol. 1v5)

Beginning of the commentary

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

ajām ekāṃ lohitaśuklakṛṣṇāṃ

bahvīḥ prajāḥ sṛjamānāṃ namāmaḥ.

ajā ye tāṃ juṣamāṇāṃ bhajaṃte

jahaty enāṃ bhuktabhogāṃ numa(2)s tān 1

kapilāya mahāmunaye

munaye śiṣyāya tasya cāsuraye

pañcaśikhāya tatheśvara-

kṛṣṇāyaite namasyāmaḥ 2 (fol. 1v1–2)

End of the root text

saptatyāṃ khalu ye rthāḥ

te rthāḥ kṛtsnasya ṣaṣṭitaṃtra(15)sya

ākhyāyikāvirahitāḥ

paravādavivarjitāś cāpi (fol. 24r14–15)

End of the commentary

ekatvam arthavattvapārārthyaṃ ca pradhānam adhikṛtyoktaṃ anyatvam akartṛtvaṃ (4) bahutvaṃ ceti puruṣam adhikṛtyoktaṃ astitvaṃ yogo viyogaś ceti ubhayam adhikṛtya śeṣavṛttiḥ sthitir iti sūkṣmaṃ śarīraṃ sthūlaṃ śarīraṃ adhikṛtya.

manāṃsi (5) kumudānīva bodhayaṃtī satāṃ sadā.

śrīvācaspatimiśrāṇāṃ kṛtiḥ sā tattvakaumudī. (fol. 24v3–5)

Colophon

iti śrīsakalaśāṣtrapāraṃgatāgrasaravācaspativiracitā tattvakau(6)mudī sāṃkhyapadapūrvā samāptā ||     || graṃthasaṃkhyā 1125  ||     ||     ||     ||     ||     || (fol. 24v5–6)
