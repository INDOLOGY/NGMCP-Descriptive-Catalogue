Beginning

śrīmadgurave dakṣiṇāmūrttaye namaḥ | |

❖ devyuvāca

devadeva mahādeva bhairavānandadāyakaḥ

dakṣiṇākālikāyās (!) tu kavacaṃ maṃtragarvitaṃ (!) 1

dvāviṃśatyakṣarasyāsya manoḥ kavacam uttamaṃ |
