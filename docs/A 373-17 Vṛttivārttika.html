Excerpts

Beginning

svasti śrīmanmahāgaṇādhipataye namaḥ

viśvaṃ prakāśayantī vyāpārair llakṣanābhidhādhvananaiḥ

nayanair iva haramūrttir vibudhopāsyā sarasvatī ja(2)yati 1

vṛttayaḥ kāvyaśaraṇāvalaṃkāraprabaṃdhṛbhiḥ

abhidhālakṣaṇāvyaktir iti tisro nirūpitāḥ 2

tatra kvacit kvacid vṛddhair viśeṣāna sphu(3)ṭīkṛtān,

niṣṭaṃkayitum asmābhiḥ kriyate vṛttivārttikam 3

tatra śaktyā pratipādakattvam abhidhā, sā tridhā,

rūḍhir yogo yogarūḍhiś ca. akha(4)ṇḍaśaktimātreṇaikārthaprtipādakatvaṃ rūdhiḥ (fol. 1v1–4)

End

tasmād etādṛśasthale‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥ganena kaścid doṣa iti tv arthalakṣaṇopapā(4)dana prayāsaḥ, na caivaṃ sarvatra viṣa[[ya]]viṣayipadasāmānādhikaraṇasthale‥‥‥‥hārād abhedapratīter vaktuṃ śakyatvāt kvacid api sāropa(5)lakṣaṇā na syād iti vācyam, iṣṭāpatteḥ (fol. 21v3–5)

Colophon

iti vṛtti⁅śa⁆‥‥lakṣaṇāvṛttir dvitīyaḥ paricchedaḥ samāptaḥ (fol. 21v5)
