Excerpts

«Beginning of the root text:»

śriyaḥ patiḥ śrīmati śāsituṃ jagaj

jagaṃnivāso vasudevasadmani || ||

vasan dadarśāvataraṃtam aṃbarād

dhiraṇyagarbhāṃgabhuvaṃ muniṃ hariḥ || || || || || ||(fol. 2v6–7)

«Beginning of the commentary:»

|| śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

iṃdīvaradalaśyāmam iṃdirānandakadalaṃ (!)

vandārujanamaṃdāraṃ vande [ʼ]haṃ yadunaṃdanaṃ 1

daṃtācalena dharaṇītalam unnamayya

pātālakeliṣu dhṛtādivarāhalīlaṃ ||

ullāghanotphaṇaphaṇādharagīyamānaṃ

krīḍāvadānam ibharājamukhaṃ namāmaḥ 2 (fol. 1v1–2)

…

śriyaḥ lakṣyāḥ pati (!) anena rukmiṇīrūpayā śriyā sameta iti sūcitaṃ ||

rāghavatve bhavet sītā rukmiṇī kṛṣṇajanmanītī (!) purāṇāt || jagannivāsa (!) jagatām ādhāraḥ || kukṣisthākhilabhuvana iti bhāvaḥ (fol. 2r8–2v1)

«End of the root text:»

oṃm (!) ity uktavato [ʼ]tha śārṅgiṇa iti vyāhṛtya vācaṃ nabhas-

tasminn utpatite puraḥ suramuvāv idoḥ (!) śriyaṃ bibhrati |

śatrūṇām aniśaṃ vināśapiśunaḥ kruddhasya caidyaṃ prati

vyomnīva bhrūkuṭichalena vadane ketuś cakārāspadam || 75 || || (fol. 34v5–6 and 35r6–7)

«End of the commentary:»

atra kaviḥ kāvyavarṇanam ākhyānapūrvakaṃ sargasamāptikaṃ kathayatiḥ (!) || ititi (!) || || || itiśabdaḥ parisamāptau māghakṛtāḥv (!) iti kapi(!)nāmakathanaṃ || mahākāvya iti mahachadena (!) lakṣaṇasapattiḥ (!) || śiśupālarvedha (!) iti kāvyavyavasthākathanaṃ prathamaḥ sarggaḥ samāpta iti śeṣaḥ || evam uttaratrāpi draṣṭavyaṃ || (fol. 35r8–11)

«Colophon of the commentary:»

iti śrīpadavākyapramāṇapārāvārīṇaśrīmahopādhyāyakolācalamallināthasūriviracite māghakāvyavyākhyāne sarvaṃkaṣāsme (!) prathamaḥ sarggaḥ samāptaḥ śubham astu || || || (fol. 35r11–12)
