Beginning

oṃ namaḥ śrīsarvabudhhabodhisattvebhyaḥ || ||

pṛthvīdhātusattayā yuṣmañchāravatīputra pūrvāntato bodhisattvo nopaiti | pṛthvīdhātu śūnyatayā pūrvāntato bodhisattvo nopaiti

pṛthvīdhātu viviktatayā pūrvvāntato bodhisattva nopaiti pṛthvīdhātusvabhāvatayā pūrvvāntatobodhisattva nopaiti ||

pṛthvīdhātusattayā aparāntato bodhisattvā nopaiti pṛthvīdhātuśūnyatayā aparāntato bodhisattvo nopaiti pṛthvīdhātuviviktatayā

aparāntato bodhisattvo nopaiti pṛthvīdhātusvabhāvatayā aparāntato bodhisattvo nopaiti ||(fol. 1v1–3)


End

śrotāpattiphalabalāsamudāgamatayā prajñāpāramitābalā samudāgamatā draṣṭavyā āveśīkabuddhadharmabalā samudāgamatayā

prajñāpāramitābalā samudāgamatā draṣṭavyā anāgāmiphalabalāsamudāgamatayā prajñāpāramitābalā samudāgamatā draṣṭavyā

arhatvabalāsamudāgamatayā prajñāpāramitābalā samudāgamatā draṣṭavyā pratyekabodhibalāsamudāgamatayā

prajñāpāramitābalā samudāgamatā draṣṭavyā mārgākārajñatābalā samudāgamatayā prajñāpāramitābalā samudāgamatā

draṣṭavyā anutarā saṃmyaksaṃbodhibalāsamudāgamatayā prajñāpāramitābalā samudāgamatā draṣṭavyā anenāpi bhagavat

paryāyeṇa mahāpāramiteyaṃ bodisattvānāṃ mahāsattvānāṃ yaduta prajñāpāramiteti ❁ (fol. 451v12–16)


Colophon

iti śrīḥśatasāhasryāḥ prajñāpāramitāyā ṣaḍviṃśatitamaḥ parivarttadvitiyakhaṇḍaḥ(!) samāptam iti dvitiyaḥ(!) ❁

(fol. 451v16)
