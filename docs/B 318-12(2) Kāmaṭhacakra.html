Excerpts

Beginning

athātaḥ saṃpravakṣyāmi cakraṃ paramadurllabhaṃ |
kauśalaṃ trijagad dī(6)paṃ sabhāpratyakṣakālakaṃ ||

kāmaṭhākhyaṃ likhec cakraṃ vyomākārastha madhyagaṃ |
aṣṭāviṃśatiṛkṣāṇi savyamārgeṇa gaṇyate || (exp. 37t5–6)

End

cakrākāre likhec cakraṃ catur nāḍyaikamadhyagaṃ |
tatas tasmāt phalaṃ vācyaṃ keḍhācakreṣu niścitaṃ ||

ma(1)dhyatrīṇigṛhe śatruḥ tadbāhye tadgṛhād bahiḥ |
tatpure grāmamadhye pi caturthe grāmabāhyataḥ |

cauryyakarmmasu naṣṭeṣu śatrūṇāṃ śāsaneṣu ca ||
ca(2)krakeḍheti vijñātaṃ svaraśāstre prakīrttitaṃ || (exp. 38t10–38b2)

Colophon

iti rudrayāmale koḍhācakraṃ ||    ||    || (exp. 38b2)
