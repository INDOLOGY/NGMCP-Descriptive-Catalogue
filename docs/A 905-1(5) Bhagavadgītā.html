Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīpuruṣottamāya ||

śrībhagavān uvāca ||

bhūya eva mahābāho śṛṇu me paramaṃ vaca[ḥ] |
yat te 'haṃ prīyamānāya vakṣyāmi hitakāmyayā || 1 ||

na me viduḥ suragaṇāḥ prabhavaṃ na maharṣayaḥ |
aham ādir hi devānāṃ maharṣīṇāñ ca sarvvaśaḥ || 2 ||

yo mām ajam anādyañ ca vetti loke maheśvaraṃ |
asaṃmūḍhaḥ sa martt[y]eṣu sarvvapāpaiḥ pramucyate || 3 || (exp. 20t8–20b3)

Middle

atha vā bahunaitena kiṃ jñānena tavārjuna |

madanugrahāya paramaṃ guhyam adhyātmasaṃjñitaṃ |
