Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

śrīmaṃgalamūrttir jayati ||

śrīgaṇapataye ⟨na⟩ namaḥ

tasyai paradevatāyai namaḥ ||

gurucaraṇasarorūhebhyo namaḥ ||

yat karna(!)mārutahatā girayo yugāṃte

karṇaībhavaṃti kila menuhimālayādyāḥ ||

śuṣyaṃti saptasaritāṃ patayo ʼpi nūnaṃ

vaṃde ʼṃtarāyarā(!)manāya gaṇeśvaraṃ taṃ || 1 ||


namo bhālekṣaṇe vahnijvālājaṭilamaulaye ||

tripurāṃtakajaga[d]dhvaṃsīpaṃḍitāya pinākine || 2 ||


raso rasāyanaṃ yogaṃ maṃtrāvarttir athaṃjanāṃ ||

sidhyaṃti viśvarūpākhye prasanne sati nānyathā || 3 || (fol. 1v1–6)


End

atha maṃtraḥ || oṁ hrīṁ āgaccha āgaccha kākabalī svāhā ||


lipte paṭe dhare devīṃ gauravarṇāṃ dhṛtotpalāṃ ||

sarvālaṃkāriṇīṃ divyāṃ samālikhyārcayet tataḥ || 76 ||


jātipuṣpaiś ca dhūpāṛghaṃ sahasraṃ pariva[r]ttayet ||

saptahaṃ (!) maṃtram etasyā(!) kuryād arcāṃ śubhānvitaḥ ||


arddharātre gate devī samāgatya prayacchati ||

paṃcaviṃśati†dī‥nārān† pratyahaṃ toṣitā satī || 78 ||


‥ ‥ rīkī japamālā || maṃtra(!) || oṁ hrīṁ ratipriye svāhā || || 36 || || (fol. 8r5–9)


Colophon

iti śrīparamaśaivācāryaśrīnīlakaṃṭhapaṇḍitaviracitāyāṃ ‥ ‥ ‥ sahaśra(!)siddhasāvarataṃtrasya ‥ dhṛte nidhipradīpe yakṣiṇīkalpaḥ samāptaṃ(!) || ❁ (fol. 8r9–10)
