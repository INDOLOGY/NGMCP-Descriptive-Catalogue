Beginning

ca kātyāyanaḥ ||

tailam udvarttanaṃ deyaṃ brāhmaṇebhyaḥ prayatnataḥ | tair abhyaṃgaḥ prakarttavyo varjakālaṃ na ciṃtayet ||

abhyaṃgaprakaraṇe sureśvaro [ʼ]pi ||

sāgnisāmagādīnām agnyasannidhau savyādinā daivapitṛmātāmahaśrāddhe yaḥ paṃktimūrddhanyaḥ prathamopaveśitadevadvija ity arthaḥ || śākhyaṃtare tu paiṭhīnasiḥ | pitṛpaṃktimūrddhanyas tasya ⟨sya⟩ pāṇāv anagnimān iti prāgdarśitaṃ | nanv agnau karaṇa(!) kiṃ pratiśrāddhaṃ kiṃ vaikapaṃktāv iti || tad uktaṃ || daivapūrvaṃ hi tat kuryāt pitṛbhyas tadanaṃtaraṃ || (fol. 34v9–11)
