Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya nama

śrībhairavāya namaḥ ||

oṁ tīkṣmādaṃṣṭreti prārthanaṃ bhairavasya ||

yaṃ yaṃ || ghaṃ jaktirūpaṃ dasāṃ dāṃsavāṃsatamṛsikaṃ yājamānaṃ ||

saṃ saṃ saṃtyaṃ hānayurti siramukuṭajaṭāḥ sokharaṃ caṃdrabiṃbaṃ ||

daṃ daṃ daṃ daṃ dīrghkājaṃ vikṛtanasramukhaṃ urdharo yaṃ karālaṃ

paṃ paṃ paṃ pāpanāśapramatasatataṃ bhairavaṃ kṣetrapālaṃ || 1 || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

bhairavāsṭakam idaṃ puṇyaṃ sa ye māsaṃ ya paṭhe naraḥ

sa yāti paramaṃ sthānaṃ yatra devo maheśvaraṃḥ || (9) || (fol. 3r4–5)

Colophon

iti śrīkāśikhaṃḍe kālabhairavastotraṃ saṃpurṇaṃ || || || || (fol. 3r5–6)
