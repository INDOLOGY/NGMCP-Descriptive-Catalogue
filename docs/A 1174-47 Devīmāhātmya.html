Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

oṃ rātriṃ prapadye punar bhūmayo bhūkampā śikhaṃḍinīpārāhastām(?) yuvatiṃ kumāriṇīm ādityacakṣuṣe vātaḥ prāṇāya somo gaṃdhāyāmaḥ ..hāya mato nujñāya pṛthivyai śariraṃ(!) || oṃ śāṃti 3 iti rātrisūktam ||

mārkkaṃḍeya uvāca ||
sārvarṇiḥ(!) sūryyatanayo yo manuḥ kathyateḥ(!) ṣṭama(!) |
niśāmaya tadutpatiṃ(!) vistarād gadato mama || 1 ||
mahāmāyānubhāvena yathā manvatarādhipa(!) ||
sa babhūva mahābhāgaḥ sāvarṇis tayonayo(!) rave(!) || 2 ||
svārociṣe ṃtare pūrvvaṃ caitravaṃśasamudbhavaḥ ||
suratho nāma rājābhūt samaste kṣitimaṃḍale || 3 ||
tasya pālayataḥ samyak prajāḥ putrān ivaurasān ||
babhūvuḥ śatravo bhūpāḥ kālāvidhvaṃsinas tathā || 4 ||
tasya tair abhavad yuddham atiprabaladaṃḍinaḥ ||
nyūnair api satair yuddhe kolāvidhvaṃsibhir jitaḥ || 5 || (fol. 1v1-2r2)

Sub-Colophons

iti mārkkaṇḍeyapurāṇe sāvarṇīke(!) manvatar(!) devīmāhātmye madhukeṭabhavadhaḥ || 1 || (fol. 9r5-6)

iti mārkkaṃḍeyapurāṇe sāvarnike manvaṃtare devīmāhātmye mahisurasainye(!) vadhaḥ || 3 || (fol. 16r2-3)

iti mārkaṇḍeyapurāṇe devīmahātmye(!) mahiṣāsuravadhaḥ || 3 || (fol. 20v4-5)

etc. etc.

End

devy uvācaḥ(!) ||
ahaṃ vibhūtyā bahubhir iha rūpair yadā sthitā ||
tat saṃhṛtaṃ mayaikaiva tiṣṭhāmy ajāstirobhavā(!) || 8 ||

ṛṣir uvācaḥ(!) ||
tataḥ pravavṛte yuddhaṃ devyāḥ śuṃbhasye(!) cobhayo(!) ||
paśyatāṃ sarvadevānāṃm(!) asurāṇāṃ ca dāruṇam ||
śaravarṣai(!) śiteś(!) śastrais tathāstrai(!) caiva dāruṇaiḥ ||
tayo(!) yuddham adbhūpaḥ(!) sarvalokabhayaṃkaraṃ || (fol. 50v2-7)
