Beginning

śriparamātmane namaḥ ||

lakṣmīkāntaṃ namaskṛtya yathāmati viracyate |

vāśiṣṭhasāravivṛtiḥ paropakṛtaye mayā || 1 ||

vaṣiṣṭhena yatīndreṇopadiṣṭād rāghavaṃ prati ||

sāram uddhṛtavān kaś cit brahmavidyāmahārṇṇavāt || 2 ||

tatrādau vāśiṣṭhasārākhyaṃ grantham āripsus tatpratipādyeṣṭadevatānatirūpaṃ maṅgalam ācarati || dikkāleti || evaṃvidhāya śāntāya guṇātītāya tejase brahmasvarūpāya namaḥ || tejaḥśabdenātra brahmaiva na bhautikaṃ tejaḥ tasya vakṣamāṇa(!)lakṣaṇānupapatteḥ || kiṃbhūtāya tejase dikkālādibhir anavacchinnānaśyattayāparicchinnā anantā avināśinī cinmātra jñānamūrttiḥ svarūpaṃ yasya tasmai (fol. 1v1–3, 6–8)

End

viśvam avidyamānam api brahmaṇi sthitaṃ tena viśvena hīnaṃ tatpadaṃ brahmapadaṃ nāsti || 32 || yatheti || eka evārthaḥ || 33 || saumyeti || saumye sthite jale yathā vīcir asti nāsti ca tathedaṃ jagad brahmaṇi asti nāsti ca | ajñānadaśāyām astijñānadaśāyāṃ nāstīty arthaḥ tenedaṃ brahma śūnyāśūnyaṃ padaṃ gataṃ jagatā hīnaṃ sahitaṃ cety arthaḥ || vyāvahārikatātvikadaśā || bhyām (!) ity arthaḥ || 34 || (fol. 27r3, 9–10)
