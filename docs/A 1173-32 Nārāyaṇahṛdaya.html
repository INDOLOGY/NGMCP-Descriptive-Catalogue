Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāye (!) namaḥ

hari oṃ-m asya śrīnāyaṇahṛdayastotramaṃtrasya brahmāṛṣiḥ anuṣṭup chandaḥ śrīlakṣmīnārāyaṇo devatā śrīlakṣmīnāyaṇaprasādasiddhyarthaṃ jape viniyogaḥ

ath nyāsaḥ

nārāyaṇa paraṃ jyotir iti aṃguṣṭhābhyām namaḥ nārāyaṇāparaṃ bhrahmeti tarjanībhyāṃ namaḥ nārāyaṇa parodeva iti madhyamābhyām namaḥ nārāyaṇaparaṃ dhāmeti anāmikāº nārāyaṇaparodharma iti kaniṣṭhikābhyām namaḥ viśvaṃ nārāyaṇāyeti karatalakarapṛṣṭhābhyāṃ namaḥ (fol. 1–1:2r3)

End

iti guhyatamaṃ śāstraṃ prātpaṃ(!) brahmādikaiḥ purā

tasmāt sarvaprayatnena gopayet sādhayet sudhīḥ 28

yatraitat pustakaṃ tiṣṭhellakṣmīnārāyaṇātmakaṃ

bhūtapaiśācavetālaṃ na sthiraṃ tatra sarvathā 29

lakṣmṛdayaproktena vidhinā sādhayetsudhīḥ

bhṛguvārepi rātrauca pūjayet pustakaṃ (!) dvayaṃ 30

sarvadā sarvadā nityaṃ gopayetsādhayetsudhī

gopanāt sādhanālloke dhanyo bhavati tatvataḥ (!) 31 (fol. 9v4–10v4)

Colophon

iti atharvaṇa rahasye uttarakāṃḍe nārāyaṇahṛdayastotraṃ saṃpūrṇaṃ ❁ mārgaśirṣa śukla 3 bhṛgudine saṃvat 1927 mīrajāpura viṃdhakṣetre gaṃgātīre jānakīrāme (!) likhitaṃ śrīrāmaprītyarthaṃ śubhaṃ bhavatu ❁ śrīlakṣmīnārāyanāprasadāstu ❁ pustakamidaṃ śrīkṛṣṇa jośī (fol.10v4:11r6)
