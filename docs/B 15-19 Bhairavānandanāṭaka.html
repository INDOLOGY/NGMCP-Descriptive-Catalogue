Beginning

❖ oṃ namo nāṭeśvarāya ||

tripuradanujaṃ hatvā nṛtyan maheśvaranāyake ,
pravaramunibhiḥ ddevai(!) hṛṣyan manobhir abhīkṣate |
nayanaśubhagā nāṭye yā bhūt mahotsavakāraṇī ,
bhavatu satataṃ seyaṃ śiddhir mamāpi ca tadvatī ||

api ca ||

prakīrṇṇaḥ kāmārer(?) amaraṇi(?)karair aṃghriyugale
vilolāliḥ kalpadrumakuśumapuṣpāñjalir ayaṃ |
vibhūtyai bhūyād vaḥ pitṛvipinabhasmāṅkitatanor
niśānāthajyotsnāmilitamukuṭas(!) preṣṭhaphaladaḥ ||

api ca || (fol. 1v1-2r1)

tataḥ praviśati nādy(!)ante sūtradhāraḥ || puṣpāṃjalir abhināya ||

dharmādijīvanmuktyaṃ tapañ ca varggapradāyine |
yogināṃ jñānagaṃmyāya(!) namo stu 'paraśambhave(!) ||

agrato m(!) avalokya || alam atipallavena | (2r4-2v3)
