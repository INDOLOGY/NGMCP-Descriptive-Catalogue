Excerpts

Beginning

///ṣadhaṃ ||

māsadvayāt tathā cūrṇaṃ hīnaviryyatvam(!) āpnuyāt ||

hīnatvaṃ guṭikālehau labhete vatsarāt paraṃ |

hīnāḥ syur ghṛtatailādhyāś caturmāsādhikās tathā ||

auṣadhyo aghupākāṃsyur nirvīryyā vatsarāt paraṃ ||

purāṇāḥ syur guṇair yuktā āsavā dhātavo rasāḥ ||

vyādhe(!) yuktaṃ yad dravyaṃ gaṇoktam api tat tyajet ||

anuktam api yuktaṃ tad yojayet tatra tad budhaiḥ || ||

iti yuktāyuktavicāraḥ || ||(fol.4r1- 4)

End

viṣaharīvarttiḥ ||

āyurvedasamudrasya gūḍhārthamaṇisaṃcayaḥ ||

jñātaḥ kaiścin na vibudhiḥ kṛtā vividhasaṃhitā || 112 ||

kaṃcid arthaṃ tato nītvā kṛteyaṃ saṃhitā mayā ||

kṛpākaṭākṣavikṣepam asyāṃ kurvaṃtu sādhavaḥ || 113 ||

vividhagadārttidaridranāśanīyā hariramaṇīva karoti yogaratnaiḥ ||

vilasatu śārṅgadharasya saṃhitā sā kavihṛdayeṣu sarojanirmaleṣu || 114 ||(fol.93r3-6)

Colophon

iti dāmodarasūnunā śārṅgadhareṇa viracitāyām saṃhitāyāṃ cikitsāsthāne netraprasādhanakarmavidhir nāma dātriṃśodhyāyaḥ || 332 || śubham astu || || || ||(fol.93r6-7)
