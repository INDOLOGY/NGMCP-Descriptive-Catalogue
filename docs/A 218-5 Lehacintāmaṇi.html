Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namaḥ śivāya ||

dhyātvā śivaṃ paramatatva(!)vicāravaidyaṃ

caṇḍīm abhiṣṭaphaladā (!) sagaṇaṃ gaṇeśa (!)

dhanvantariṃ munivaraṃ muni(2)suśrutādin (!)

ātreyam ugratapasaṃ manasā praṇamya || ||

rogam ādau parikṣeta tatonantaram auṣadham ||

tataḥ karmma bhiṣak paścāt (3) jñānapūrvaṃ samācaret ||

karmmajā vyādhayaḥ kecid doṣajāḥ santi cāpare

karmmadoṣodbhavāś cānye karmmajā daiva hetukāḥ ||

yathā śā(4)stras tu nirṇītaṃ yathā vyādhi cikitsitaṃ

na samaṃ yānti yo vyādhi (!) sajñeya karmayo (!) budhaiḥ || (fol.1v1–4)

End

eraṇḍād(1)yo nirūha || ||

jvararchardyam atīsāre mūḍhaśalyārdditeṣu ca ||

hṛd grahaṇī roge durbbale vyādhikaeṣite ||

kṣīve raktātisāre ca tathā murchāti(2)karṣite ||

rogeś ceteṣu śatata (!) nirūho na praśasyate ||

nirūhakalpanādhyāyaḥ || || iti sārodhṛta lehaciṃtāmaṇiḥ (3) || samāptam || || śubham astu || (fol.228r9–228v3)

Colophon

vāgbhaṭṭavṛndacarakottama viśvanātha

śrīcakrapāṇiva śuśruta(!) siddhasārāt ||

āhatya(4) sarvakuśalāt khalu vaidyaśāstrāc

chrīdevasiṃha kṛta sārasamuccaye ʼsmin || || samāptam || || ❁ || ❁ || || (fol. 228v3–-4)
