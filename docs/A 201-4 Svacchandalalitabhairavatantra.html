Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ mahābhairavāya ||

kailāsaśikharāsīnaṃ, bhairavaṃ vigatāmayam |

caṇḍanandimahākālagaṇeṣavṛṣabhṛṅgibhi ||

kumārendrayamāditya brahmaviṣṇupura(2)ḥsaraiḥ

stūyate paramātmānaṃ gaṇamātṛniṣevitaṃ ||

sṛṣṭisaṃhārakarttāraṃ sthitivilayakāraṇam |

anugrahakaraṃ devaṃ puṇarttārtti vināśanaṃ ||

muditāṃ bhairavaṃ dṛṣṭvā (3) devī vacanam avratīt(!) |

yat tvayā kathitam mahyaṃ svacchandam parameśvaram || (fol. 1v1-3) 

End

evaṃ saṃkṣepataḥ proktaṃ [mela]kan tu varānane |

satatābhyāsayogena vadanti carukaṃ svakaṃ ||

ya(7)sya saṃprāśanād devi vīreṇa sadṛśo bhavet ||

tasmād dhyānārccanaṃ homaṃ japañ ca varavarṇṇini |

bhāvitātmānas tataḥ sidhyanti mantriṇaḥ || || śubha || (fol. 137v6-7)

Colophon

samvat 785 āṣāḍha śukla paṃcamyāṃ tithau megha nakṣatre bṛhaspativārau (fol. 137v7)
