Excerpts

«Beginning»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

mahimnaḥ pāraṃ te paramaviduṣo yadya sadṛśī

stutir brahmādīnām api tadavasannās tvayi giraḥ ||

athāvācyaḥ sarvaḥ svamatipariṇāmāvadhigṛṇan

mamāpy eṣa stotre hara nirapavādaḥ parikaraḥ || 1 || (fol. 1v1–5)


«End»

kusumadaśananāmā sarvagaṃdharvarājaḥ

śaśidharavaramauler devadevasya dāśaḥ ||

svagurunijamahimno bhraṣṭaye vāsya roṣāt

stavanam idam akārṣīd divyadivyaṃ mahimnaḥ || 34 ||

suravaramunipūjyaṃ svargamokṣaikahetuṃ

yadi paṭhati manuṣya prāṃjalir nānyacetāḥ ||

vrajati śivasamīpaṃ kinnaraiḥ stūyamānaḥ

stavanam idam amoghaṃ puṣpadantapraṇītam || 35 || (fol. 12r5–v5)


«Colophon»

iti puṣpadaṃtācāryaviracitaṃ mahimnaḥstotram || (fol. 12v5)
