namasya(!) hariṃ naivadhātāram īśaṃ,


na vahniṃ na cārkaṃ na ceṇdrādidevān ||


śivodirītān ekavākyaṃ prabaṃdhais


tvadrccāvidhikevalaṃ kiṃ tu manye || 23 ||
