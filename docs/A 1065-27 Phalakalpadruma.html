Excerpts

Beginning

|| śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

siddhīśaṃ naumi matidāṃ śrīmat tripurasuṃdarīṃ |

phalakalpadrumaṃ (2) vakṣye phalaśāstravidāṃ mude || 1 ||

khyātaḥ kavir giridharas tat sūnur muralīdharaḥ ||

tatsūnur bhūdharo vidvān tat putraś ca sudhādharaḥ || (3)2 ||

tad ātmajo harṣadharo karod gurjaragau(4)ḍakaḥ |

nīlakaṃṭhakṛter vyākhyāṃ kāśyāṃ śrīphalavarddhinīṃ || 3 ||

tasyās tattvaṃ gṛhītv edaṃ la(5)ghuṃ graṃthaṃ karoti ca |

sūnyabdapraśnaśāstrāṇāṃ samatyaikyaṃ vicārayan || 4 || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

phalakalpadrumaḥ putraḥ kanyā śrīphalavarddhinī | 1082 |

phalasiddhyai vijaya(10)te śrīsaddharṣadharasya hi |

tryabdhyahīṃduśake pūrṇaṃ vaiśākhasyābhavat site | 1083 |

gurau puṣye (11) ca saptamyāṃ phalakalpadrumaṃ mṛduṃ | (!)

vande brāhmīṃ vaṃde kṛṣṇāṃ vaṃde śaivīṃ vaṃde rāmāṃ | (!)

vaṃde gaurīṃ vaṃ(12)de devīṃ vaṃde buddhiṃ vaṃde siddhiṃ || || (!) śrīḥ || (fol. 53v9–12)
