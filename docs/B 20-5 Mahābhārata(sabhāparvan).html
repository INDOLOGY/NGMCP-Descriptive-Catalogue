Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṁ namo nārāyaṇāya ||

nārāyaṇaṃ namaskṛtya narañ caiva narottamaṃ |

devīṃ sarasvatīñ caiva tato jayam udīrayet ||

vaiśampāyana uvāca ||

tato bravīn mayaḥ pārthaṃ vāsudevasya sannidhau |

prāñjaliḥ ślakṣṇayā vācā pūjayitvā punaḥ punaḥ |

asmāt kṛcchrāc ca saṃrabdhāt pāvakāc ca didhakṣataḥ |

tvayā trāto smi kaunteya brūhi kiṃ karavāṇi te |

arjjuna uvāca ||

kṛtam etat tvayā sarvvaṃ svati te stu vrajāsura |

prītimān bhava no nityaṃ prītimanto vayañ ca te ||

maya uvāca ||

yuktam etat tvayi vibho yathāttha puruṣarṣabha |

prītipūrvvam ahaṃ kiñcit karttum ichāmi bhārata |

ahaṃ hi viśvakarmā vai dānavānaṃ mahākaviḥ |

so haṃ tvatkṛte kartuṃ kiñcid icchāmi pāṇḍava || (fol. 1v1–5)


End

gadāvegañ ca bhīmasya nālaṃ soḍhuṃ narādhipāḥ |

śamo me rocate nityaṃ pārthais tatra na vigrahaḥ |

kurubhyo hi sadā manye pāṇḍavāñ balavattarān

tathā hi balavān rājā jarāsandho mahādyutiḥ ||

bāhupraharaṇe (lauha)bhīmena nihato yudhi

tasya te śama e /// vair bbharatarṣabha |

ubhayoḥ pakṣayor yuktaṃ kriyatām ///

evaṃ kṛte mahārāja paraṃ śreyas tvam āpsyasi |

evaṃ gāvalgaṇe kṣattā dharmārthasahitaṃ vacaḥ |

uktavān na gṛhītañ ca mayā putrahitepsayā || (fol. 191v3–192r1)


Colophon

iti śrī mahābhā /// sāhasryāṃ saṃhitāyāṃ vaiyāsikyāṃ pāṇḍavānāṃ vanapraveśaḥ samā(hiktāṃ) sabhāparvva /// samāptāni | lekhakasya śarīre śubham astu || || salaṃ 335 bhādravadi 6 budhe edine (!) erkaharāgrāme yad upādhyāyaśrī /// reṇa likhitam idaṃ pustakam iti || || ... (fol. 192r1–3)
