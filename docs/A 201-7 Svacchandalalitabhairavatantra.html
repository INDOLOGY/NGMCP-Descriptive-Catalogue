Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śambhave

kailāsaśikharāsīnaṃ bhairavaṃ vigatāmayaṃ

caṇḍanandimahākālagaṇeṣavṛṣabhṛṅgirāṭ

kumārendrayamādityabrahmaviṣṇupurassaraiḥ

sphū(2)yati(!) paramaśānaṃ gaṇamātṛniṣevitaṃ ||

sṛṣṭisaṃhārakarttāraṃ sthitirvileyakāraṇaṃ

anugrahakaraṃ devaṃ praṇatārttivināśanaṃ

muditāṃ bhairavaṃ dṛṣṭvā devī(3) vacanam avravīt

ya tvayā kathitaṃ mahyaṃ svacchandaṃ parameśvaraṃ (fol. 1v1-3) 

End

evaṃ saṃkṣepataḥ proktaṃ melakan tu varānane

satatobhyāsayogena vadanti caru(4)kaṃ svakaṃ

yasya saṃprāsanād devi vīreṇa sadṛśo bhavet ||

tasmād dhyānārccanaṃ homaṃ japañ ca varavarṇṇini ||

karmmaṇāṃ bhāvitātmena tataḥ sidhyanti(5) mantriṇaḥ (fol. 1135v4-5)
