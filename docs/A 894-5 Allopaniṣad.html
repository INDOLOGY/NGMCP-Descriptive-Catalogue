Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

oṃ asmallāṃ ille mitrābaruṇādi vyānidhatte ||

illalle baruṇo rājā punardaduḥ ||

hvayāmi mitro illāṃ illalloṃ illāṃ baruṇo mitro tejas kāmāḥ || 1 || (fol. 1:1–4)

End

hariḥ oṃ allā illallā anādi svarūpāya atharvaṇīvyākhyāṃ hūṃ hrīṃ alla huṃ ajanmānāṃ paśusiddhān jalacarān adṛṣṭaṃ kuru 2 phaṭ asura saṃhāriṇī huṃ || allo rasula mahaṃmada rakabarasmaalle allo illalleti illallāḥ || (fol. 1:18–22)

Colophon

atharvaṇarahasye allopaniṣadaṃ samāptaṃ || (fol. 1:22–23)
