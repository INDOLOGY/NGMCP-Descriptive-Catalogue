Manuscript Features

Scattered folios of the dikarī -śabdakhaṇḍa are available on the first 8 exposures (expos. 1–10)

Another copy of the dinakarī starts on the exposure 18 after the end on ata eva guṇātmikākarmāsmikā vā [padārthavibhāga] (expos.11t–18t)

Another copy available up to dravyavibhāgavyākhyā (expos. 18b–36t) and another copy begins from the end of the pratyakṣakhaṇḍa and available up to anumānakhaṇḍa (expos. 37b–63)

Beginning

|| śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

lakṣmīpādayugaṃ praṇamya pitaraṃ śrībālakṛṣṇābhidhaṃ
bhāradvājakulāṃvudhau vidhum iva śrīgauravāsyāṃbujāt ||
jñātvā śeṣamataṃ mi(2)tena vacasā siddhāṃtamuktāvalī
gūḍhārthāṃ stanute yathāmatimahādevaḥ pareṣāṃ kṛte 1

tatra tāvat bhāṣāparichedavyākhyānaṃ (!) cikīrṣuḥ śrīviśvanāthapaṃcā(3)nano nirvighnaparisamāptaye kṛtasyeśvaranirdśarūpamaṃgalasya śiṣyāpy evaṃ kuryur iti śiṣyaśikṣyāyai nibaṃdhanaṃ kurvan niḥśvaraṃ prārthayate || (fol. 1r1–3)
