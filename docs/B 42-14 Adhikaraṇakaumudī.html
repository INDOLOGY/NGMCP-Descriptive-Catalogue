Beginning

oṃ namaḥ śrīkṛṣṇāya ||     ||

purāṇam ajaraṃ devaṃ kāmadaṃ kāmavairiṇam ||

anidānaṃ nidānan taṃ yoginaṃ bhoginaṃ numaḥ || [[1]]

āste devaḥ smaraparavaśa[[ḥ]] smeratāruṇyanṛtyat

prauḍho tuṅgastanabhāranamat kāminī kāmakeliḥ ||

yuṣmat sevā paravaśamanāḥ kiṃ jagannātha tāvad

bhāvī bhāvaś caraṇakamale tāvake māmakīnaḥ || [[2]]

dharmaśāstre dhikaraṇaṃ vicāreṣūpakārakam ||

viduṣā devanāthena nibbandhena vitanyate || [[3]] (fol. 1v1–4)

End

haracraṇarajasvī bālabhāve yaśasvī

taruṇimanitapasvī sarvakāle yaśasvī ||

vividiṣati budho yaṃ dīpayāmāsa tasminn(!)

adhikaraṇakadambaiḥ kaumudīṃ devanāthaḥ

somabhaṭṭopadiṣṭena pathā saṃsarato dhunā ||

mīmāṃsāviṣa⟪yā⟫[[mā]]raṇye śaraṇaṃ mama bhāratī ||     || (fol. 59v2–5)

Colophon

iti mahāmahopādhyāyatarkapaṃcānanavaiṣṇavaśrīdevanāthakṛtādhikaraṇakaumudī samāptā ||

lekhakapāṭhakayor maṃgalam || ❁ || śubhaṃ || (fol. 59v5–6)
