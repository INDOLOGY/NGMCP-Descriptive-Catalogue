Excerpts

«Beginning of the root text:»

oṃ atha puruṣo ha vai nārāyaṇo akāmayata prajāḥ sṛjeyeti || ❖ || || nārāyaṇāt prāṇo jāyate manaḥ sarvendriyāṇi ca || khaṃ vāyur jyotir āpaś ca pṛthvī viśvasya dhāriṇī || nārāyaṇād brahmā jāyate nārāyaṇād rudro jāyate nārāyaṇāt prajāpatiḥ prajāyate nārāyaṇād indro jāyate nārāyaṇād aṣṭau vasavo nārāyaṇād ekādaśarudrā nārāyaṇāt dvādaśādityāḥ sarvā devatāḥ sarve ṛṣayaḥ sarvāṇi chandāṃsi sarvāṇi ca bhūtāni nārāyaṇadevasamutpadyante nārāyaṇe pralīyante nārāyaṇe pralīyante || (fol. 1v5, 2r4–5, 2v3–6)

«Beginning of the commentary:»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

nārāyaṇopaniṣadaṃ bhedaparvatabhedanīm

vyākhyāsye brahmavidyāyā jananīṃ pāpahāriṇīṃ || 1 ||

saṃbandho viṣayo tadvat sādhikāriprayojanaḥ

sarvopaniṣadāṃ tulyas tasmān nātrocyate pṛthak

puruṣaḥ paripūrṇo deśakālavastuparichedaśūnya ity arthaḥ || (fol. 1v1–3)

«End of the root text:»

oṃ namo nārāyaṇāyeti maṃtrapāṭhako vaikuṃṭhabhuvanaṃ gamiṣyati tad idaṃ paraṃ puruṣaṃ puṇḍarīkaṃ vijñānaghanaṃ tasmāt taḍidābhāsamātraṃ brahmaṇyo devakīputro brahmaṇyo madhusūdana iti sarvabhūtastham evaikaṃ nārāyaṇaṃ kāraṇarūpam akāraṇaṃ paraṃ brahma om ity etad arthaṃ śiro yo dhīte prātar adhīyāno rātrikṛtaṃ pāpaṃ nāśayati sāyam adhīyāno divasakṛtaṃ pāpaṃ nāśayati madhyaṃdinam ādityābhimukhodhīyānaḥ paṃcamahāpātakopapātakāt pramucyate sarvavedapārāyaṇaṃ labhate nārāyaṇo sāyujyam āpnoti | nārāyaṇo sāyujyam āpnoti || || ❖ || || ||

oṃ pratyagānandetyādinā nārayaṇe sāyujyam āpnoti itthaṃ oṃ nārāyaṇāya namaḥ ||

na saṃbhāṣet striyaṃ kāṃcit pūrvadṛṣṭāṃ na ca smaret

kathāṃ ca varjjaye[t] tāsāṃ na paśyel likhitām api || || (fols. 5v2–7, 6r2–4)

«End of the commentary:»

nama iti dve akṣare nārāyaṇāyeti paṃcākṣarāṇi spaṣṭaṃ adhyeti sapāṭhaṃ sārthaṃ ca smarati anayavṛtaḥ apavṛtto kīrttibhāgyāgādidoṣeṇāpi bhūtādr ity arthaḥ tadvīparīto napavṛtaḥ sarvaṃ nikhilaṃ śatasaṃvatsaraparyantaṃ āyuḥ śarīrāt prāṇānāmanirgamanirgamakāraṇaṃ rātigachati na kevalaṃ jīvanaṃ kiṃ tu rāyasyoṣaṃ suvarṇādipuṣṭiṃ gopater bhāvas taṃ paśupatitvaṃ videṃte prāpnoti kiṃ suvarṇagobhiḥ puṣṭo na pavavo dīrghāyuḥ prajāśūnya syād ityāśaṃkā nety āha prajāyateḥ santānavato gṛhasthasya bhāvaḥ prājāpatyaṃ tatra kevalamanaṃ lokaṃ vindate kiṃ tu paramaprītyā hatataḥ kīrtisuvarṇagoprajāyuḥ prātthanantaraṃ jīvanmṛto cāmṛtatvaṃ brahmajñānaṃ tasmāt sādhyaṃ phalam aśnute prāpnoti tato mṛtatvam aśnute (fols. 5r1–2, 8, 5v1, 8, 6r1, 6)

«Colophon of the root text:»

oṃ iti śrīmatparamahaṃsaparivrajakācāryaśrīśaṃkarānandabhagavataḥ kṛtir nnārāyaṇopaniṣaddīpikā samāptaṃ śubham || likhitaṃ padmakeśaraśarmaṇā | hariḥ (fol. 6r2–4)

«Colophon of the commentary:»

ityābhyāsa upaniṣat samāptyarthaḥ śubham samvat 1930 miti śrāvaṇakṛṣṇatithau 11 nārāyaṇa (fol. 6r6)
