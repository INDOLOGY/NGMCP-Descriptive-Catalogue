oṃ namo bha[[ga]]vate vāsudevāya ||
merupṛṣṭhe su〇khāsīnaṃ brahmā lokapitāmahaṃ |
sa[[na]]tkumāraḥ śrīmantam pṛcchate parameśvaraṃ ||

sanatkumāra uvāca ||

ko ’yaṃ viṣṇu (!)〇 paro devaḥ sarvvaśāstreṣu paṭhyate |
kin tu mūrttir amūrttir (vvā) ajāto jāta eva vā ||
saguṇan tu kathaṃ proktaṃ nirgguṇaṃ vā ja〇dguruṃ (!) |
kin tu sūkṣma (!) vijānīyāt kiṃ sthūlam ihocyate ||
kiṃ viṣṇus sakalaḥ proktaḥ kin tu niṣkalam eva vā ||
kim a〇vyaktaṃ vijānīyād vyaktarūpaṃ tu kiṃ bhavet ||
kin tu nityam anityaṃ vākṣaro ’nyaṃ kṣara vā ||
sarvagāḥ kin tu vijñe〇yaḥ kiṃ vāsarvagataḥ smṛtaḥ |
kin tu varṇṇātmako viṣṇuḥ kiṃ vā varṇṇavivarjitaḥ ||
kathaṃ vā sthāpyate viṣṇur llo〇kanātha jjagaṃ gururṃ ||
kasmi(ṃ) dravye tu karttavyā kasyā vai pratimā śubhāḥ(!) |
kiṃ pramāṇaṃ tu vijñeyam aṅgapratyaṅgayos tathā ||
sarīraṃ kiṃ pramāṇan tu karttavyaṃ lakṣaṇānvitaṃ |
mukuṭasya pramāṇan tu śirasaś ca kathaṃ bhavet ||
lalāṭan tu punas tasya karttavyaṃ kiṃ pramāṇataḥ |
nāsikā tu kathaṃ kāryā netre vaiva pitāmahā ||
gaṇḍayoḥ kīm pramāṇan tu karṇṇayoś ca kathaṃ bhavet |
oṣṭho kiṃ tasya karttavyo cipuke kiṃ tu lakṣaṇaṃ ||
bāhoś caiva tad argulyā(!) grīvāyāḥ kiṃ pramānataḥ |
vakṣaś caiva kathaṃ kāryaṃ stanakau nābhimaṇḍalaṃ ||
jaṭharasya pramānan tu kaṭyā vai liṅgam eva ca |
ūrukau(!) jānunī caiva nalakau gulphapādayoḥ |
aṅgulyā nakham aṅguṣṭho sarvalakṣaṇam eva ca |
vāhanaṃ nu punas tasya karttavyan tu janārdane ||
āyudhāni ca devasya kīdṛśāni tu kārayet |
sarvalakṣaṇasaṃpaṃnaḥ kathaṃ viṣṇur vinirdiśet ||
prasāde tu kathaṃ kāryaḥ śobhanaṃ yāgamaṇḍapaṃ |
piṇḍikā tu kathaṃ kāryā garbhasūtraṃ kathaṃ bhavet ||
grahanakṣatralagnaś ca tithivāraṃ tathaivaca |
maṇḍapasya vibhāgan tu vedāś caiva tu lakṣaṇaṃ ||
sthāpakasya tu cihṇāni ye + (mū)rttiparāḥ smṛtāḥ |
etat samāsena bhagavat vaktum arhasi || ○ ||
