Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||


śrīgurubhyo namaḥ |


yasya niśvasitaṃ vedā yo vedebhyo ʼkhilaṃ jagat |

nirmametam ahaṃ vaṃde vidyātīrthamaheśvaraṃ || 1 ||


devāgātuvido gātuṃ yajñāya viṃdata |

manasaspatinā devena vātāgha jñaḥ prayujyatāṃ ||


prakṛtāḥ sarve devāḥ pūrvārddhena uttareṇa tu paramātmā pratipādyate he devāḥ pūrṇamāsasya darśasya vā [[a]]karmaṇo

devatāḥ agnyādayaḥ yato yūyaṃ mārgavettārasthā ʼtaḥ prakṛtayajñāya mārgaṃ labhadhvaṃ labdhāyajñaṃ pravarttayatety

arthaḥ | (fol. 1v1–5)


End

aṃśa maṃta hi tuṃḍulāḥ oṣadhīśasya somasya acaya vā madhurarasavaṃtastha || dacasyatveti vyākhyātaṃ agraye juṣṭam

adhivapāṃrmi(!) dhānyam asi dhinuhi devā ‥dhū taṃḍulasaṃdhātadevānāṃ priyaṃ tvāṃ adhivapāmi yaṣṭuṃ yatas tvaṃ

prītisādhanam asi ato devān prīṇaya | prāṇāya tvā | he upale prāṇāya tvāṃ prohāmi peṣṭuṃ

uchvāsaniśvāsatatsaṃbaṃdhirūpāḥ prāṇavṛttayaḥ eva uttaratra || dīrghāmanu ghāṃ || devo vaḥ vyākhyāttaḥ

| ada kṣe || anupapahatena cakṣuṣā yuṣmān avekṣe paśyāmi || avyavakti ītī tān || he patnidāsī vātaṃḍuleṣu kim apiy

apreveśayaṃti | paṣaṇaṃ(!) kuru tāni ca piṣṭāni sūkṣmāṇi kuru || (fol. 14v10–15r4)
