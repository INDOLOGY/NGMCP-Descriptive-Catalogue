Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ siddhiḥ || bhairavāya namaḥ ||

oṃ hrīṃ śrīṃ aiṃ phreṃ hraṃ hlīṃ klīṃ kṣaḥ |

praṇipatya anādināthaṃ anāthabhūtaughamoghanirmathanam |
satatānanditamanasaṃ sasurāsuravanditāṃhṛ(!)yugam || ||

śrīkubjikā uvāca ||

śiṣyacittābjasaṃghātadurvikāsyavikāsine |
sadoditapraśāntāya namaḥ śrīnāthabhānave |
veṣṭitā devadevesa ajñānapaṭalair aham |
vāmadakṣiṇasiddhāntaiḥ sāṃprataṃ varttate kudhīḥ |
yat tat kulakramāyātaṃ kulālītanutāṃ gataṃ |
sūtrasaṃgrahanirmmuktakalpasandhavivarjitam |
aparaṃ śuddhadivyaughaṃ niḥkaivalyaṃ kathaṃ prabho |
pūrvavṛttāntasaṃyuktaṃ yathāyātaṃ kuleśvara |
pīṭhāni pīṭhanāthaś (!) ca pīṭhadevyaś ca pālakāḥ |
bālavṛddhakumārākhyaṃ kramaṃ dīpasamanvitaṃ |
tathā pīṭhādhipair pīṭhair ddīpadevyā samanvitaṃ | (fol. 1v1–2r1)

End

unmanāpātrimaṃ gṛhya biṃdumastakabhū⁅ṣi⁆tam |
māyābīja (!) dvitīyan tu lakṣmībīja (!) tṛtīyakam |
caturthaṃ vāgbhavaṃ proktaṃ kulavyoman tu pañcamaṃ |
śakteś caturthako gṛhya jātaṃ vesi (!) saṃsthitaṃ |
biṃdunā sirasākrāntaṃ ṣaṣṭhamaṃ parikīrttitaṃ |
punaḥ śakteś caturthan tu pṛthivyurdhvaś (!) ca laṃkṛtam |
māyāyā bheditaṃ kṛtvā ʼkṣaraṃ saptamaṃ smṛtaṃ |
kāmarājāṣṭamaṃ proktaṃ kathākhyaṃ navamaṃ smṛtam |
icchāśaktisamāyuktaṃ vidyā devī navākṣarā |
tīvratejā mahogrā ca gopanīyā prayatnataḥ |
ādivaktrodbhavā devī paścimānvaya pūjitāṃ (!) ||
granthānāṃ rakṣaṇā proktā pāraṃparyā (!) kramāgatā ||
puroktaṃ yan mayā devi ādivaktrodbhavaṃ mahat ||
granthānān tu śataṃ sārdhaṃ teṣāṃ te syā varānanā || ○ || (fol. 68v1–5)
