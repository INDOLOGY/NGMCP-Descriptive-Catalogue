Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgurubhyo namaḥ ||

śaṃbho svāgatam āsyatām ita ito vāmena padmodbhava-
kroṃcāre(!) kuśalaṃ sukha(!) surapate vitteśa no dṛśyate ||
itthaṃ svapnagatasya kaiṭabharipoḥ śrutvā jananyā giraḥ
kiṃ kiṃ bālaka (ja)lpasīty anucitaṃ thūthūkṛtaṃ pātu vaḥ || 1 ||

taptaṃ kair na tapobhiḥ phalitaṃ tadbhogabālāyām ||
yat tāsāṃ kucakuḍye nīlanicolīyatī brahma || 2 ||<ref>The last pāda seems to be unmetrical.</ref>

śrīkṛṣṇaṃ stavalāvaṇyaṃ nijau(!) vā staumi locane ||
tat pratikṣaṇasaṃvarddhi te ca tatsthānadāyinī || 3 ||

harimukhasakalakalānidhi suṣamāyūṣapāṇamukhabhājām ||
abhavad gopavadhūnāṃ kevalakaivalyalābhataḥ śaṃkā || 4 ||

gurujanakathitam apayaśo gṛhapaticaritaṃ ca dāruṇaṃ kim api ||
vismārayati samastaṃ śiva śiva muralī murārāteḥ || 5 || (fol. 1v1-4) <references/>

End

jāmātur mātur ākhyā pitur atha kathaya bhraṣṭar iṃdrānuja tvaṃ
deveśeti kṣamābhṛnnagarayuvatibhir gātukāmābhir ukte ||
lajjā<ref>Two long syllables are missing after lajjā°.</ref> nanānāṃ smaraharavadanaṃ paśyatām āśu teṣām
anyonaṃ(!) bhūpate te kalayatu kuśalaṃ ko pi vācāṃ vicāraḥ || 42 ||

kastūrīyaṃti bhāle tadanu nayanayoḥ kajjalīyaṃti karṇa-
prānte nīlotpalīyanty urasi marakatālaṃkṛtīyaṃti devyāḥ ||
romālīyaṃti nābher upari hari° <references/>
