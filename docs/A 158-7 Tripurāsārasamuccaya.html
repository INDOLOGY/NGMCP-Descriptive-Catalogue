Beginning

❖ śrītripurāyai namaḥ ||

prahvaprācīnavarhiḥ pramukhasuravarānīkakoṭīr akoṭi
śliṣṭaspaṣṭeṃdranīlāmalamaṇimadhupaśreṇijuṣṭḥaṃ ruṣīṣṭa (!) |

End

janmādhāraguhodarā (!) jamukha āmrāntamātrātrayā (!).
vṛtyā valātiśaktitantuniratasūtrākṣamālāyutaḥ (!) ||
maṃtraś ca surasoditaḥ pratipadaṃ tatpratyaśāsukhaṃ (!)
liṅgaṃ ca sthitam ity ayaṃ vijayate nityo jayas traipuraḥ ||    ||    || (fol. 45r7–v1)
