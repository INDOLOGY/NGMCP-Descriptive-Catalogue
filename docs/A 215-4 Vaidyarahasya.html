Beginning

śrīgurubhyo namaḥ || śrīsāradāyai namaḥ ||

yannāma smaraṇaṃ samastaduritadhvaṃsāvahaṃ puṇyadaṃ

bhaktānām abhayaṃkaraṃ sukhakaraṃ sarvārthasiddhipradaṃ ||

taṃ natvārjjunam īśvaraṃ gurukṛpā ʼvāptaiḥ susiddhair varair

yogai(!) vaidyarahasyam adya tanute vidyāpatiḥ kautukāt || 1 ||

asmadgraṃthād ādṛtāṃn(!)siddhayoga[[n]] nānyagraṃthe sthāpayiṣyaṃti kecit || ||

nātra sthāne ye vadiṣyaṃti teṣāṃ nāṣaṃ kuryād īśvarobhīṣṭasiddhai || 2 ||

(fol.1v1-4)

End

dīrghadīrghās tu bahavo graṃthāḥ saṃti tathāpi me ||

sāṃpradāyika(!)yogānāṃ saṃgrahārtham ayaṃ śramaḥ || 6 ||

catuḥ paṃcāśadbhir munividhuśatenādhisahiter(!)

gatebde || 1754 || bhūpārkān nabhasi śitapakṣopaṇitithau || ||

iti śrīmadvaṃśīdharatanujavidyāpatikṛto

bhavat pūrṇo graṃthaḥ sakalabhiṣagānandajanakaḥ || 2 ||                                                                        (fol.101r1-3)
