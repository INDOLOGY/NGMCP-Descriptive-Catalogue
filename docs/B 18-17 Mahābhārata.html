Manuscript Features

The left margins are worm-eaten and broken, which frequently results in loss of foliation.

Written by several hands.

There are a few additions in the margins.

putrapautraparivṛtaḥ sādhūnāñ caiva sammataḥ |
brāhmaṇo labhate vidyāṃ kṣatriyo vijayī bhavet |
vaiśyas tu labhate lābhaṃ śūdro vallabhatām iyāt ||
pitṝn uddiśya paṭhatām gayāyāṃ piṇḍado bhavet |
kurukṣetre ca yat proktaṃ gaṅgāsāgarasaṅgame |
śrāddhaṃ kṛtaṃ pitṝṇān tu phalam āpnoti mānavat |
ity ukto(!) bhagavān kṛṣṇaḥ pūjitas tair nnarādhipaiḥ |
babhūva tūṣṇīṃ te sarvve jagmuś caivā(!) yathāgatam || (fol. 160v3-161r1)
