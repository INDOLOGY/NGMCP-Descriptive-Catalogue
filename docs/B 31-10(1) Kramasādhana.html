Manuscript Features

1. Kramasādhana (fols. 2r1–18v4)

2. Pañcakramasādhana (fols. 18v4–41v5)

There are impresses of the seal of Bhim Shumshere dated 1988 [VS] on fols. 2r and 41v.

Upper right-hand part of fols. 18r,18v,19r and 19v are damaged.

Available folios are 2–20, 22, 25–30, 41.

There are two exposures of the fols. 3v–4r, 11v–12r, 17v–18r.

Alphabetical foliation are given in the middle of left-hand margin on the verso.

On the exp. 23b appears a stamp make an impression This book belongs to Pt. Siddhi Harsha .
