Manuscript Features

On the folios of 1v–18v holds the text of Vāstuśāstra. ( up to exp. 19) exp. 19b–21t holds the text of Gurubhujaṅgaprayātastotra.(on the exp. 19b- 21t) exp. 21b–26t holds the text of the Devyā daṇḍaka(stotra). exp. 26b – holds the text of tripurāsārasamuccayavyākhyā by Nāga Bhaṭṭa.

Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīsaccidānandacetase ||


sudhāsindhumadhye sahasrārapadme

niviṣṭaṃ tathā raktakiñjalkapuñje |

sphurad raśmijāle susūkṣmaṃ sudhāṃśau

maheśaṃ sadā haṃ guruṃ bhāvayāmi || 1 ||


suṣumnākhyanāḍyā yadādhārabhūtaṃ

tadantargataṃ divyam atyantaguhyam

surair apy agamyaṃ sugopyaṃ yad etat

sadā suprakāśaṃ guruṃ cintayāmi || 2 || (exp. 19b1–3)


End

agamya śrutīnāṃ mahimnas tavedaṃ

na jānanti pāraṃ mahāyonoṅgaṃ |

guroḥ stotram icchāmi karttuṃ ca tattvaṃ

kṣamasveti mandaḥ kilāhaṃ tavāpi || 23 ||


prasādesti kiṃ vā na labhyaṃ tvadīye

vipaccāpi sampat tavānugrahaś cet |

na cec candracūḍarddhisiddhiḥ sadāste

prasādena tena priyaḥ sampadā yat || 24 ||


idaṃ stotrarājaṃ trisaṃdhyaṃ paṭhed yaḥ

śṛṇotīti vā bhaktiyuktena tasmai |

padārthān samastān dadātī svakīyān

gurur vedasārān śriyañcāṣṭasiddhiṃ || 25 || (exp. 21t4–8)


Colophon

iti śrigurubhujaṅgaprayātastavarājaṃ samāptaṃ || || śubha || śrīguruḥ prasanno bhūyāt(!) || || (exp. 21t8)
