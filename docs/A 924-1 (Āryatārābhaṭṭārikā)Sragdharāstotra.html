Excerpts

«Begining: »

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrī-āryatārāyai ||

bālārkkālokatāmrapravarasuraśiras cārucūḍāmaṇiśrīḥ

sam(yaksaṃparkkarāgā)naticiraracitāraktakaḥ vyaktabhakti[ḥ] |

bhaktyā pādau tavārye karaputama(!)kuṭoṭopabhugnottamāṅgaṃ⟨ḥ⟩

tāriṇyā yaccharaṇye navanutikusumasragbhir abhyarccayāmi || 1 ||

durllaghe duḥkhavahnau vinipatitatanū durbhagaḥ kāndiṇīkaḥ

kiṃ kiṃ mūḍhaḥ karo(mityusakṛd) api kṛtārambhavaiyarrthyakhinnaḥ ||

śrutvā bhūyaḥ parebhyaḥ kṣatana iva vyomnicandrārkkalakṣmī

mālokāśānibaddhaḥ paragatigamanaḥ tvāṃ śraye pāpahantrīṃ || 2 ||(fol. 1v1–2r3)

«End: »

kalyāṇānandasindhuprakaṭaśaśikare śrībharāṃ dehadṛṣṭiṃ

puṣṭiṃ jñānopadeśe kuru ghanakaruṇe dhvaṃsaya dhvāntam antaḥ |

tatstotrāmbhaḥ pavitrīkṛtamanasi mayi śreyasaṃsthānam ekam

dṛṣṭaṃ yasmād amoghaṃ janani tava guṇastotramātaṃʼ prajānām || 36 ||

saṃstutya tvatguṇaughāvayavam aniyate (yacaraṃ)kāṃ mayā yat<ref name="ftn1">Unmetrical pāda</ref>

puṇyaṃ puṇyārhavāṃchāphalamadhurarasāsvādamuktibhogyaṃ |

lokas tenāryalokeśvaracaraṇatale svastika svasticihnām

ahnānāyaṃ prayāyāt sugatasutamahīṃ tāṃ sukhāvaty upākhyāṃ || 37 || (fol. 11v1–12r2)

«Colophon: »

ity āryatārābhaṭṭārikāyāḥ śragdharāstotraṃ parisamāptaṃ || ❁ ||

ye dharmā hetuprabhavā

hetu[s] teṣāṃ tathāgata | he(!)vadat

teṣāñ ca yo nirodhaḥ

evaṃ vādī mahāśraṇaṃ || ||

lekhapāṭhakasya(!) śubhaṃ bhūyāt || || (fol. 12r2–4)
