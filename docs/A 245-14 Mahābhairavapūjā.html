«Beginning:»'


❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīśrīmahāgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||


śrīgurupādukābhyāṃ namaḥ || śrī śrī śrīmahābhairavāya namaḥ || || yajamānapuṣpabhājana ||

oṃ adyādi || vākye || yajamānasya mānavagotra śrīśrījayabhūpatīndramallavarmaṇa tolana(!)pratiṣṭhā

viśvajātrā śrīśrīśrīākāśamahābhairavaprītyarthaṃ pañcopacārapūjā karttuṃ puṣpabhājanaṃ

samarpayāmi || ||


śrī saṃvarttāmaṇḍalānte kramapadanihitānandaśaktiḥ subhīmā

sṛṣṭaṃ nyāyacatuṣkaṃ akulakulagataṃ paṃcakaṃ cānyaṣaṭkaṃ |

catvāraḥ pañcakonyaṃpunar api caturaṃ tattvato maṇḍalena

saṃsṛṣṭaṃ yena tasmai namata guruvaraṃ bhairavaṃ śrīkujeśa || || (fol. 1v1–2r5)


«End:»


svāna taya ||


kvākāśaḥ kva samīraṇaḥ kva dahanaḥ kvāpaḥ kva viśvambharaḥ

kva brahmā kva janārddanaḥ kva bhujagaḥ kvendra kva devāsurāḥ ||

kalpāntālabhaṭānatapramuditaḥ śrīsiddhiyogeśvaraḥ

krīḍānāṭakanāyako vijayate devo mahābhairavaḥ || ||


svāna mālakostā viya || || astramantrana bali thvaya || bali visarjana yāya || || no siya ||

bali bhoka rūya || || sākṣi thāya || || (fol. 127r5–128r1)


samvat 827 caitraśukla dvitīyā aśvinī nakṣatra viṣkambhayoga somavāra bhelaju khatasa

toraṇa tayā śrīśrī jayabhūpatīndramalladevasana || (fol. 128v1–2)


«Colophon(s):»


iti śṛīśrīśrīmahābhairavapūjāparddhati samāpataḥ(!) || || śubhaṃ bhavatu sarvadā || ||

❖ samvat 827 śrāvaṇa śudi 5 sahta nakṣatra siddhiyoga aṃgāravāra thva kuhnu

bhairavaska thāvane toraṇa tayā dina śrīśrī jayabhūpatīndramalladeva sana || (fol. 128r1–5)
