Beginning

❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

hṛdaye yatpreraṇayā samudyato[[bhūt]] vimūḍhatarabuddhiḥ ||

tatpadakamaṃ vande gokulanāthasya vedādeḥ || 1 ||

hanumān(!)nāṭakasāgaraśabdārthāgādhatāṃ sugādhatvaṃ ||

netuṃ kṛtasāhasatāṃ mama sudhiyaḥ kṣaṃtum arhaṃtu || 2 ||

tāvac chrīrāmacandraguṇacakravālavarṇanecchukaviḥ

svābhīṣṭadevatānāṃ †māṃtaritāśīḥpūrvvā†nāṃdīm avatārayati ||

kalyāṇānām ityādi dvādaśapadais taduktaṃ nāṭakāvatāre ||

aṣṭābhir daśabhir vāpi, nāṃdī dvādaśabhiḥ padaiḥ ||

āśīr namaskṛyā(!)vastunirddeśo vāpi tat pakham(!) iti ||

nāṃdīmaṃgalavācanam iti || (fol. 1v1-5)

End

māthurakulasaṃbhūtiḥ śuddhacaturvvedibodhavikhyātaḥ

he mauraso vijayate kamalāpatir iśvara(!)premā || 1 ||

tatputreṇa mayeyaṃ †mohanāmnārtharalānāṃ† ||

maṃjūṣākapivihitāpy abhidhāna†rālena saṃvivṛtirati† || 2 ||

etadgrathārthoktiśramaikavijñaḥ patiḥ śriyo rāmaḥ ||

tuṣṭā nijapadabhaktiṃ diśatudayāvān urukasvalpaṃ || 3 ||

(fol. 139r4-7 )
