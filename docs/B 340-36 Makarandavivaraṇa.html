Excerpts

Beginning

svasti śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

śrīsarasvatyai namo nama (!) sāṣṭāṃgatayā || ||

prajñāṃ yataḥ prāpya kṛtapratijña

sparddhāṃ vidhatte prasabhaṃ pratijñaṃ ||

ājñopitaṃ (!) śrīśivanāmadheyaṃ

gurūpamaṃ svīyaguruṃ bhajeyam || 1 ||

śrīmacchivāt samadhigatya varaprasādaṃ

vṛttāṃśubhir vivaraṇābhinavāraviṃdaṃ ||

etad divākaravikāśitam āryyavaryya-

bhṛṃgā bhajaṃtu makaraṃdapipāsavo ye || 2 ||

|| anya (!) ca || ||

sadhyo [ʼ]navadhyāmalapadyagobhir

jyotirvidāryyeṇa divākareṇa ||

prakāśyate ʼtho makarandasūkti-

sarojam ānamya guruṃ śivākhyam || 3 ||

pṛṣṭhasthitāsannaśakonite [ʼ]ṣṭa-

śakāvaśeṣapramakoṣṭhakasthaṃ ||

tithyādivārādisavallikaṃ tad

yojyaṃ samīpasthaśakasya kande || 4 || (fol. 1v1–2r2)

End

yo bhukta kāla///saṣaḍbhāt

ṣaḍbhādhiko ṣṇakarabhogyasamanvito [ʼ]sau ||

madhyodayena sahi///

syād rātipātasamayas tv asakṛt sphuṭaḥ syāt || 12 ||

kasyāṃdi/// naṃ vā yāsyatītīdam uktaṃ grahalāghave || ||

laghugolpa inādude///rigatigrahaḥ pracīcyāṃ (!) ||

bhūpāllaghugaḥ paratra vāstaṃ prācyāṃ bhūrija/// ti || 13 ||  || (fol. 22v5–23r4)

Colophon

iti śrīdaivajñanṛsiṃhasutadivākaradaivajñaviracita///vivaraṃ (!) sampūrṇaṃ śubham || || śrīśāke 1718 śrāvaṇaśuklacatudarśyāṃ (!) /// likhitaṃ paśupatijyotirvidā iṭācapali vasan śubham (fol. 23r4–6)
