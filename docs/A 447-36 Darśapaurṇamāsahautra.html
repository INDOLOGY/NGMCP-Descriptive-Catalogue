Beginning

Śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ |

Atha darśapaurṇamāsasya hautraprayogo likhyate || ||

Ardhyo devatā acakṣvatā+++rvyam urupāṃ śrutāṃ ca yajamānasya pravaranakṣatranāmadheyāni ca tataḥ prāmudagāhavanīyād avasṭhāya prāṅgā+ yajñopavītyārkṣamatir eikatyikapadārthāś ca | ājyabhāgānaṃtaraṃ agni agniṣomau agniṣomau tāsāṃ madhye agnīṣōmautrapāśu | śeṣam ucchaiḥ ||  (fol. 1v1–4)

End

Devayajyayā+pahūto bhūyasi haviṣkaraṇa+daṃ madevāhavir ajuṣaṃtāmiti tasmin tu(!)pahūtā || āhavanīye samidham ādadhāti | yathetaṃ niṣkrāmet | (fol. )
