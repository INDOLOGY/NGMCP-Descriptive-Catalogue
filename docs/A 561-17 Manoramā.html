Manuscript Features

The available numbered folios are 1–7, 9–47, 43–101, 1–32, 1–19, 1–35, 2–3, 38, 31, 32, 33, 34, 35, 36 and 37. Fols. 1–7 and 9–47 (between these is a repeated page) are on reel no. A 561/17. The remaining folios are on reel no. A 562/1.

The Kārakaprakaraṇa, Strīpratyayaprakaraṇa and Samāsaprakaraṇa chapters have separate pagination 1–32, 1–19, 1–35.

After fol. 35v of the Samāsaprakaraṇa chapter there are 17 folios on which the page number is not given.

Again a separate pagination restarts 2–3; and then some random pages follow: 38, 31, 32, 33, 34, 35, 36 and 37. After these pages there are two folios on which the page number is not given.

In the centre of the last page is written samāsasya pustakaṃ samāptaṃ ||. Under this are seven pairs of pūrṇavirāmas. Under these lines is a pair of horizontal lines, under which is written: śrīgaṇādhipataye namaḥ|. To the left of the latter is written: śrīrāmacandrāya namaḥ, vertically from top to bottom; to the right is written: śrīrāmānujāya na[maḥ], vertically from bottom to top.

At the bottom of fol. 20v of the Kārakaprakaraṇa chapter there is a drawing. ( CHECK if it looks like a picture, put in illustrations)
