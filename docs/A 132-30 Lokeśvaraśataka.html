«Beginning»


❖ namo gaṇeśāya namaḥ ||


oṁ namo lokeśvarāya ||


śrīmatpātalakerasya || lokanā pravṛcchati ||


mānepā nihitāsthāya tārādevi mahākṛpā ||


śrīmatpātālakaparvvatasalokesoravidyāka, atikṛpāvanta tārādevina ||


manukṣayāta kalyānayāya, arthana, lokeśvalayāke, uyāta khaṃ ṅene || ||


bhothākula, manukṣayā utpāta || bhiṅamabhiṅa ||


sānti asāṃti, chalaponasyaṃ , ādesa dayake mā pūjāvidhiyeṃ,


chalapolarjnā pūvagathiṃ ṅaḍvālasā, kṛpāsamudrethiśca || (exp3.p1–4t4)


«End»


te yo saṃyajūle dharapo saṃthajūre, thaṅāla khasutasā,


svāmisiyiṅo, dhanaphauyai śānti jara jāganayāya,


brāhmanayā lū dānaviya, saṃnyāsinake, gherasahitanake , 41||


thvate uyāssāta, lokeśvalasyaṃ, mannakṣayāte vothayāna,


tana, cosyaṃtana, tarā devikaṃ ṅā khathate jūro ||


saṃmvat 913 āṣāḍha di 5 coya dhunakā jūro || || (exp.23t3-23b3)
