Manuscript Features

The foliation of the text is not regular, so in the excerpt is cited exposure numbers.

Folio, numbered 14 which is blank (after the fol. 12) is wrongly numbered.

The verso side of fol. 28 is blank.

The bottom folio of exp. 28 might be misplaced. It is not in proper place.

Most of the lines of the verso part of the fol. 33 is deleted by scribe.

There are two exposures of fols. 4v–5r, 10v–11r, 30v–31r and exp. 28b.

Fols. 13, 14, and 25–33 are in reverse order.

Available folios 2–3, 4, 8–10, 10–14, 14–16, 22–26, 28, 30–36.
