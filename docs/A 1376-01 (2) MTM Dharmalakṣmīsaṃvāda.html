Manuscript Features

1. The MTM contains following texts:

(1) Sahadevana dayakā chāya (exp. 4)

(2) Dharmalakṣmīsaṃvāda (exps. 6t–11b)

(3) Aṅgaparīkṣā (exps. 11b –13t)

(5) #Āyurvaidikacikitsā (exps. 20–29)

Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namo sāladāyai ||

jatya satya, tato lakṣmī, tato hari, tato dhamyai jato jaya,

gathe ga(2)the dhālasā, gana gana jate satya yākahma yākaṃ || śrī lakṣmītvaṃ bijyāta, gana lakṣmī bi(3)jyāta ana śrīkṛṣṇa bijyāka, gana kṛṣṇatvaṃ bijyāta ana dharmmatva bijyāta anega ja(4)yajaya juo || (exp. 6t1–4)


End

mevuyā darvya (6) mevuyā davyaṃ ṣa mayakaṃ, mocakāyā pāpana, aputra juyāo, nisantāna juraṃ (7) || gathe mevuyātaṃ madayaka, dukha biraṃ, thatheṃ thvahma manukṣayā kuṭuṃmba sa(b1)mastaṃ nāsa juraṃ || thuti pāpa mayāsyaṃ puṇya yāsā thotuli(!) jarnmasa, sukha sa(2)spati bhuktipada lātaṃ paralokasa, svarggavāsa juro || (exp. 11t5–11b2)

Colophon

iti dharmmao lakṣmī (3) saṃvāda khaṃ samāptaḥ || || (exp. 11b2–3)
