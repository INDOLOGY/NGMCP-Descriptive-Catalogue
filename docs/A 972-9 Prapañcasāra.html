Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

śaṃkarācāryebhyo namaḥ

avighnam astu ||

akacaṭatapādyaiḥ saptabhiḥr vvarṇavargair

(viracita)(2)mukhavyāhā pādamadhyā(suhṛktā) ||

sakalajagad adhīśā śāsvatā viśvayonir

vitaratu pariśuddhiṃ ceta++(3)radāvaḥ || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

itthaṃ mūlaprakṛtyakṣararavikṛtalipivratetagra(8)harkṣatrādyāvaddhabhūtindriyaguṇaratricaṃdrāgnisamprate rūpaiḥ | (!)

maṃtrais tad devi prakṛtisvabhā(1)vato (!)

vibhinnabhāvadivasam (!) utthitaujasaḥ ||

jaganti puṣyaṃti ravindu vahnyo

namos tu (2) tasmai paripūrṇatejase || (fol. 164v7–8, 165r1–2)

Colophon

|| iti śrīmat paramahaṃsaparivrājakācāryyagovinda(3)bhagavatapūjyapādaśiṣyaparamahaṃnaparivrājakācāryyaśrīmatśaṃkarabhagavataḥ (4) (!) kṛtau prapañcasāre ṣaṭtrīṃśaḥ paṭalaḥ samāptaḥ || samāptaś cāyaṃ graṃthaḥ || || śubham || (5) || iti samvat 1954 sāla miti caitraśudi 7 roja 3 tad dine jirṇṇa uddhāra liṣita(6)m || || ❁ ||   || (fol. 165r2–6)
