śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||


madhye sudhābdhimaṇimaṇḍaparatnavedyāṃ


siṃhāsanoparigatāṃ paripītavarṇāṃ ||


pītāmbarābharaṇamālyavibhūṣaṇāṇgīṃ


devīṃ namāmi dhṛtamudgaravairijihvāṃ || 1 ||


kailāśaśikharāsīnaṃ gauryā vāmāṅgasaṃsthitaṃ


bhāratipativālmīki śivāsaṃnutam iṣṭadaṃ || 2 ||


bhairavāṣṭavṛtaṃ devaṃ mātṛmaṇḍalaveṣṭitaṃ


mahāpāśupatākrāntaṃ pramathair āvṛtaṃ prabhuṃ || 3 ||


natvā stutvā kumāraś ca idaṃ vacanam avravīt ||


cāpavaryeṣu nipuṇair yuddhacaryābhayaṅkaraiḥ || 4 ||


nānā māyāvinaṃ caiva jetum icchāmi rākṣasaṃ


tasyopāyaṃ ca tad vidyāṃ vada me karuākara || 5 || (fol. 1v1–9)
