Excerpts

Beginning

śrīkṛṣṇāya namaḥ ||

hariharasarasvatī

jagadgur īḍyaḥ paramahaṃsānāṃ

sa jagannāthapadottara-

sa(2)ravaatīpadasaṃbodhaḥ 1

karman di (!) kaṇṭhālaṃkāraṃ sāraṃ vedāntavāridheḥ

racayaty amalaṃ grantham advaitā(3)mṛtasaṃjñakaṃ 2

āsīḍ yativaraḥ kaścid vivekāśramasaṃjñakaḥ

yat prasādena bahavo muktimārga(4)m upāgatāḥ 3 (fol. 1r1–4)

End

jitāhāro jitakro(7)dho jagannāthasarasvatī |

yenāyaṃ racito graṃtha advaitāmṛtasaṃjñakaḥ || 70 ||

etasya kavalānyaṃ cāsvasya sādara(8)to gatāḥ |

amṛtatvaṃ prayānty āśu vibudhā yatisattamāḥ || 71 || (fol. 24r6–8)

Colophon

iti śrīmat paramahaṃsaparivrājakācāryahari(9)harasarasvatīpriyaśiṣyajagannāthaviracite ʼdvaitāmṛte paṃcamaḥ kavalaḥ samāptaḥ || || (fol. 24r8–9)
