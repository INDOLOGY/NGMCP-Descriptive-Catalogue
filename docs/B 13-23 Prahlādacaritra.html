Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namo nārāyaṇāya ||

maitreya uvāca ||

kathitā bhavatā vaṃśā, mānasānāṃ mahāmune,, |

kāraṇaṃ cāsya jagato viṣṇur eva sanātanaḥ||

yac caitad bhagavān āha prahlādaṃ daityasatyamaṃ |

dadāha nāgnir nnāstraiś cakṣuṇṇas tatyāja jīvitaṃ || …

śrotum icchāmi yasyaitac caritaṃ tigmatejasaḥ ||

kinnimittam asau śastrai,r vvikṛto ditijair mmune | (fol. 1r1-4)

«Sub-colophons:»

iti śrīmahāvaiṣṇave, viṣṇupurāne prathame ṃśe prahlādacarite ṣoḍaśo dhyāyaḥ ||

(fol. 2r2-3)

iti śrīviṣṇupurāne prathame ṃśe saptadaśo dhyāyaḥ || (fol. 9v1)

iti śrīviṣṇupurāne prathame ṃśe aṣṭādaśo dhyāyaḥ || (fol. 12r3)

iti śrīviṣṇupurāne prathame ṃśe prahlādacarite ekonaviṃśo dhyāyaḥ|| (fol. 18r4-5)

End

ahorātrakṛtaṃ pāpaṃ prahrāda(!)caritan tataḥ |

śṛṇvata paṭhaśva (!) maitreya, vyapohati na saṃśayaḥ ||

paurṇṇamāsyām amāvāsyām aṣṭamyām athavā paṭhet |

dvādaśyām vā tad āpnoti gopradānaphalaṃ dvija ||

prarlādaṃ (!) sakalāpattve, yathā rakṣitavān hariḥ |

tathā rakṣati yat tasya, śṛṇoti caritaṃ yadā || || (fol. 21r1-3)

Colophon

iti śrīviṣṇupurāne prathame ṃśe prahlādacarite viśatitamo(!) dhyāyaḥ || prahlādacaritraṃ samāptam iti || (fol. 21r4)
