[about battle dated AD 1762]

(❖)(!) rivandī karttumicho (!) ravanipatibṛḍhasyājñayā vṛddhavairaṃ

kaṃpūsainyaṃ tvakasmād ahamahamikayā duṇḍikhelāt pradhāvat ||

kastvaṃ kohaṃ kva yūyaṃ vayamapi sahasā bhaṣmasād yāma hūṃ hūṃ

saṃviśyāgāramadhyaṃ parikaram alabhad yad yadādattadārāt

suptāṃś codvāsya devān bala iva bhavatā jyeṣṭhaṣaṣṭyāṃlakābde dvāṣaṣṭyāvāridāsthe (!) 1762 pradhanamajanihā ʼdvaitavairaṃ prajānām || 2 || ||

Beginning

–dīneṣu dayālavas pṛśanti śatataṃ cālpopi na śrīmado

svasthāḥ santi ca yauvanodaya mahāvyādhi prakopepi ye
