Manuscript Features

On the exposure 89b and 90 is available incomplete text seems to related to the āyurveda begins as oṃ namo mañjunāthāya śrīgaṇēśāya mahābhairavāya namaḥ puresanadyāya | bhāga | rurati‥ ohobo 1 śijalarati 1 thuti sabhāgana kāre juro ||...(exp 90,1)  || || śijararati oho rati 1 thoti tākāre rati 1 rurati 6 bhigoresana julo ||  rojavāre ṛlivo 1 koroja 4 hi gurivāre marivo 1 podharavācchi mesavāyake pireresana cchāya || ||-

At the beginning of the text, damaged fols.of 5r–6v are re-scribed and placed. Oroginal damaged folios are placed on exp. 87b–89t.

Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīgurave ||

guruṃ gaṇapatiṃ durggāṃ vaṭukaṃ śivam acyutaṃ ||

brahmāṇaṃ girijāṃ lakṣmīṃ vāṇīṃ vande vibhūtaye ||

anādyāyā(2)khilādyāya māyine gatamāyine ||

arūpāya sarūpāya śivāya gurave namaḥ ||

parāprasādamantrāya saccidānandace(3)tase  ||

agnī somasvarūpāya tryabakāya (!) namo namaḥ  || || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

sādhakānāṃ hitārthāya bhuktimuktiphalāṃ || (!)

yaś corddhvāmnaya (!) mahātmyaṃ (!) paṭhitvā cakrasannidhau ||

bhaktā (!) ca parayā devi (8) yaḥ śṛṇoti sa kaulikaḥ ||

vratadānatapstīrthaṃ yajñadānārccanādiṣu ||

tatphalaṃ koṭiguṇitaṃ labhate nātra saṃśayaḥ

(exp. 86,1) tatsannidhau sannivaśen nātra kāryyā vicāraṇā || (fol. 82r7–8 and v1)
