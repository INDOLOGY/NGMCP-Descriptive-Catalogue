Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ nama(!) śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

oṃ vaṭukabhairavāya namaḥ

īśvarovāca

merupṛṣte sukhāsīnaṃ devadevaṃ maheśvaram
śaṃkaraṃ paripapraccha pārvatī parameśvaraṃ 1

pārvaty uvāca

bhagavan sarvadharmajña(!) sarvaśāstrāgamādiṣu
āpaduddhārakaṃ maṃtraṃ sarvasiddhipradaṃ nṛṇāṃ 2

sarveṣāṃ caiva bhūtānāṃ hitārthaṃm(!) vā(!)chitaṃ mayā
viśeṣatas tu rājñāṃ vai(!) śāntipuṣṭivibarddhanaṃ 3

aṃganyāsaṃ karaṃ(!)nyāsaṃ dehanyāsaṃ samanvitam

īśvarovāca

śṛṇu devī(!) mahāmaṃtraṃm(!) āpaduddhārahetukaṃ 4

sarvaduḥkhapraśamanaṃ sarvaśatruvināśanaṃ
apasmārādirogāṇāṃ jvarādīnāṃ viśeṣataḥ 5 (fol. 1v1–6)

End

rājaśatruvināśārthaṃ japen māsāṣṭakaṃ yadi
rātrau vāratrayaṃ devi nāśayaty †ava†śatravaḥ

japen māsatrayaṃ devī(!) rājānaṃ vaśam ānayet
dhanārthī ca sutārthī ca dārārthī cāpi mānavaḥ

japen māsatrayaṃ devi vāram ekaṃ tathā niśi
rogī rogāt pramucyante devi satyaṃ na saṃśayaḥ

nigaḍaiś cāpi baddhāya kārāgṛha(!) nipātitā
śṛ(!)khalābaṃdhanaṃ prāptaḥ paṭhec caiva divāniśi

yaṃ yaṃ kāmayate kāmaṃ paṭhet stotram anuttamam
taṃ taṃ kāmam avāpnoti sādhako nātra saṃśayaḥ

aprakāśyaṃ paraṃ guhyaṃ na deyaṃ yasya kasyacit
satkulīnāya śāṃtāya ṛgvedaṃ bhaverjitaḥ (!)

dadyāt stotram idaṃ puṇyaṃ sarvakāmaphalapradaṃ
evṃ śrutvā tato devī nāmāṣṭaśatam uttamam

saṃtoṣaṃ paramaṃ prāpya bhairavasya mahātmanaḥ(!)
jajāpa parayā bhaktyā sadā sarveśvareśvarī

bhairavo pi prahṛṣṭo bhūt svayaṃ ca maheśvaraḥ (fol. 6v1–9)

Colophon

iti śrīrudrayāmale viśvasāroddhārakalpe umāmaheśvarasaṃvāde vaṭukabhairavaāpaduddhārastavarājaḥ(!) samāptam śubham (fol. 6v9–right-hand margin)
