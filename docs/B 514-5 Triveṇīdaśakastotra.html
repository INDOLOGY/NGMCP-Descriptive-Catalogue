Beginning

❖ oṁ śrīguru ājñā || ||

oṁ āśāmanavila varjjaraparakā golācāroṣuṭa vaṁ(exp. 2top1)

orā vasepānī vādhu urā hanumanta[[hara]] hāke

bhima bole unapāre ugāke

śrīmu(2)romantra phaṭ svāhā || 108 ||

gvara 4 tyāphi mantra japarapāo gurisi māna jurau(3)

jarnāni bhaya juro || ||

nā 1 2 3 4 śrīgaṇe(4)

svasti śrīgaṇeśāyanama ||

khemātmānaṃ paramātmānaṃ jivātmānaṃ(5)

kaṣṭio sāghelao sā bhāga yāṅa tālasa buyāo

mata phayā aṃjalana thvasa oālā(6)o tonake misā masitale thvana kāyāo juyuo

ghyala kaṣṭi ṅāyā dāka thva svatā(7) sā bhāga yāṅao thvate choyakāo phayā aṃjalana oālāo thvao nāpaṃ tvanake(8) tyao thva saktā takāoṃ tyao thvato nakaṃ sarvvadā thvana kālakhe || ||

sāghelaosa(9) dhū gugulā bhāgīṇa colasa dudu jhihnuto tvane thva prayoga lāyu hnāsasa thane(10) jio ||(buttom1)

❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

muktā ma[[yā]] kṛta mu⟪⟫kti veṇī bhaktā bhaya prātaniva(2)dha veṇī |

End

śrīmambhavāni sātayaka veṇī lakṣmī(exp. 3top5)srasva⟪⟫tyabhimāna veṇī |

mātastriveṇī trayaratna veṇī śrīmatprayāge jaya(6)ti triveṇī || 9 ||

triveṇīdasakastrotra sāyaṃ prāta paṭhet nara tasya veṇīti(7)

saṃtuṣṭhā dadātyartha canuṣṭhaya || 10 ||

Colophon

iti śrīsaṃkarācārya viraci(exp. 3top8)taṃ triveṇīdasakastotraṃ saṃpurṇṇa || ||
