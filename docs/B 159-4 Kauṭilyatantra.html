Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrīgaṇapataye namaḥ ||

praṇamya śirasā bhaktyā, mūladevaṃ mahopamaṃ |

vakṣye kauṭilyakaṃ taṃtraṃ kuṭilāśayamarddanaṃ ||

uddhṛtya sarvvataṃtrebhyas sāram ādāya yatnataḥ ||

kriyatae saṃgrahaḥ satyaḥ śrīmadvijñānaśaktinā ||

mantraṃ taṃtraṃ prayogaṃ ca, yaṃtraṃ bhedaṃ ca lāghavaṃ |

ṣaḍvidha(!) kathyate karmma sopānaṃ sopadeśakaṃ ||

na deyaṃ yatra kutrāpi, aparīkṣya budhaiḥ sadā |

taṃtraṃ vai durllabhaṃ loke, trailokyasādhakaṃ paraṃ || (fol. 73v or 1v1–5)

End

†mūla āṇaṃ mahilāṇa[[kuhilāṇa]] iḍahā† |

†jāyate jva(!)saṃtāpadbindukarase† nanu ||

viḍālīlālasaṃyuktaṃ, dugdhapānāt kṣayo bhavet ||

pāṃdūrogo bhaved dugdhaṃ, kaṭiṇīkṣārabhakṣaṇāt ||

vajrīkṣīreṇa pippalyāḥ śiraḥ kleśakṣayo bhavet || (fol. 91r or *19r6–7 91v or *19v1)

Colophon

iti śrīvijñānaśaktibhāṣitaṃ kauṭilyābhidhaṃ taṃtraṃ samāptaṃm(!) iti śubham astu ||     || (fol. 91v or *19v1–2)
