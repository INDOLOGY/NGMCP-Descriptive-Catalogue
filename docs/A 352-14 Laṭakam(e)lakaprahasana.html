Excerpts

Beginning

|| || śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

gaurī cuṃvanacaṃcalaṃ calavalac caṃdraprabhāmaṃḍalaṃ

vyāvalāt phaṇikuṃḍalaṃ ratirasapra(2)svinnagaṃḍasthalaṃ ||

prauḍhapremaparaṃparāparicayaprotphullanetrāṃcalaṃ○

śaṃbhor astu vibhūtaye trijagatām unmattagaṃgaṃ śi(3)raḥ || 1 ||

apica ||

rakṣā [[kṣā]]valitāḥ parityajaṭāḥ koyaṃ ⟪mada⟫valid vikramaḥ

kaupīnaṃ tyaja muṃca muṃca nakharavyāpāram āsthā(4)nikaṃ |

ity udbhrātaṃ ivollapannavatuvo caṃḍaṃ nikuṃjodare

rudrāṇī suratapravaṃdhavikalaḥ khaṃḍeṃḍucūḍāmaṇiḥ || 2 || (fol.1v1–4)

End

apica ||

bhūdevaḥ svastivādais tirayatu duritaṃ nirjvarāḥ saṃtu saṃtaḥ

saṃtu prauḍhārivīra vra(2)javijayakalā śālinaḥ kṣoṇipālāḥ ||

āstāṃ vidva(!)prakāṃḍa śravaṇapuṭacamatkārikā(3)vyaṃ kavīnām

astu vyāmohaśāṃtiḥ sṛjatu hṛdi mudaṃ niścalaś caṃdracūḍaḥ || ||

iti niḥkrāṃ(4)tāḥ (!) sarve dvitīyaḥ saṃdhiḥ || || prathamoṃkaḥ || || (fol.17r1–4)

Colophon

iti laṭakamelakaṃ nāma prahasanaṃ samāptaṃ || || likhitaṃ keśavamiśreṇa nāvāpārāṃtargātaphariṃhāvavṛttimālinā || || subham astu || || graṃthaharisarma śuklasya || || rāmanāma satya || || (fol.17r4–6)
