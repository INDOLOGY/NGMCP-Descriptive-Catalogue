Title Tīrthayātrotsarga

Author Lakṣmīnātha dvija

Subject stotra

Language Sanskrit

  1. 1.Though this text is about caturṣaṣṭhi liṅgayātra of Nepālamaṇḍala [See śloka no. 60 above ] There are mentioned of some more tīrthas also . As this text was composed by Lakṣmīnātha śarmā in AD1961 on the occasion of utsarga (completion ) of the pilgrimage, the title is given as Tīrthayātrotsarga.
  2. Here the date is given in śaka era as dvi hyāṣṭa dharaṇī tapasāṃ sita śaṃkara, which is identified as,
      1. dvi ‘two’ = 2
      2. hyāṣṭa = hya (metathesised form of ahi ‘snake’=8) and
          1. aṣṭa ‘eight’ = 8
      3. dharaṇī ‘earth’ = 1

That is , ŚS 1882 ( AD1961 )

      1. tapasāṃ (corrupt form of tapasya ) = Phālguṇa
      2. sita = śuklapakṣa
      3. śaṃkara = mahādeva (11) = Ekādaśī

3. 'Tī ' in left margin, a short from of Tirthayātrotsarga.
