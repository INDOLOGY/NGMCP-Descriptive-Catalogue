After the text there are some verses on praise of Devī on exp. 5t–5b.

Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīpaṃcamīdevyai ||

namāmi sadguruṃ śāntaṃ pratyakṣaṃ śivarūpiṇaṃ |

śirasthayogapīṭhasthaṃ muktikāmārthasiddhaye ||

śivasiṃdūrasaṃdoha svāṅgasundarabhāsūrāṃ |

karuṇāpūrṇakallolakaṭākṣāṃ kūlanāyikāṃ (!) || (exp. 2t1–3)

End

rakṣāṃ yacchaṃ tu dākinītva ca gatā bhukte sthitāṃ rākinī

tvākinyāmiṣasaṃsthitā śritavrathā me dasthitā kākinī |

puṇyam tu sthiśatā sākinī svatu dinaṃ hākin yatho majjago śukraṃ yacchatu yākinī parivṛtī śrībhairavī jīvitāṃ || (exp. 4b6–9)

Colophon

iti śrīrudrayāmale caturāśītisāhasre rahastātirahasye śrīmahādevakṛte śrītripurasundaryāyaṃ paṃcamīstavarājaṃ saṃpūrṇaṃ || ❖ || ○ || || || (exp. 4b9–10)
