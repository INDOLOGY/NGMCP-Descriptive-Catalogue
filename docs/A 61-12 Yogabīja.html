«Beginning:»'


(śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ) ||

yogabījae (…)

sadāśiva jagannātha karuṇāmṛtavāridhe

sarve jīvāḥ sukhair duḥkhair māyājālena veṣṭitāḥ


teṣāṃ mutiḥ kathaṃ deva kṛpayā vada śaṃkara

sarvasiddhikaro mārgo māyālanikṛntaka


janmamṛtyujarāvyādhināśakaḥ sukhadaś ca yaḥ

nānā mārgās tvayā dva kathitās tu maheśvara


adhunā mokṣamārgaṃ hi brūhi yogavidāṃ varaḥ


īśvara uvāca

baddhā yena vimucyaṃte devi mārgam ataḥ paraṃ

tad ahaṃ kathayiṣ〉āmi tava prītyā sureśvari (fol. 1v1–5)


«End:»


dīkṣāmantravihīnasya na vinā malaśodhanaṃ

tasya pūrvavaśāt karma prasahyati muhur muhuḥ


malas trividham ādhatte svayam ātmā vaśottare

aviditvā paraṃ tattvaṃ tatra gatvā na mucyate ||


nopavāsādi tīrthebhyo na vratātmani śodhan ||

anaśane somasūrye rāhugraste tathaiva ca ||


śaraṇāgatasaṃbaṃdhe gograhe vā jalāgniṣu ||

devāyatanamūleṣu vārāṇasyāṃ jaleśvare ||


kedārādiṣu sarvatra kurukṣetre raṇe pi vā ||

na muktir iti bhūtānāṃ vṛṭhā kliśyaṃti te janāḥ ||


kiṃtu svargam avāpyaṃte vartaṃte ca guṇāṣṭadhā ||

ṛṇaśeṣe pravartteta martye bhuvanamaṇḍale ||


śubhāśubhe ca saṃprāpte bhuṃjaṃte suḥkhaduḥkhayoḥ

karmakṣayaṃ vinā teṣāṃ kathaṃ muktir vidhīyate śubhaṃ bhūyāt ❁ (fol. 4r8–4v2)
