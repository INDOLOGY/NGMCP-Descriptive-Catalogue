Manuscript Features

On the fol. 5v there are two stanzas one related to the Dharmaśāstra and next is to the Jyotiṣa.

There are some stanzas added by second hand with its commentary on the fol. 7v which belongs to the same text Śrutabodh. The MS having the only one folio is also dated. The MS reads as the colophon iti śrutabodhacaṃdograṃthasya vyākhyā samāptā śu saṃvat 1871 kārttika kṛ 7.
