Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

bhāvārthāḥ karmaśabdās tebhyaḥ kriyārprartiyai (!) taiṣatvartho vidhīyate || vininiyogaprayuktyāptaṃ nāmapi yady api pramāṇādhīnasiddhikatvam asti tathāpi pramāṇanirupaṇānaṃtara dharmasvarupaviśeṣasya prathamaṃ jijñāsitatvāt tadarthaṃ bhedanirupaṇam eva kartavyaṃ (fol. 1v1–3)

End

sūtrārth(3)as tu kāṭhakam ityādi saṃjñayāṃ (!) aṣtākapāladvādaśākapālādinā rūpeṇa kārir yādyadhyayaneṣu bhūbhojanādi(4)dharmaviśeṣeṇa punar uktyā uditānuditahomanindayā ekasmin karmaṇi sarvaśākhāgatānām aṃgānāṃ anuṣṭhā(5)maśaktyā (!) agnicayanādeḥ śākhābhedāt samāptibhedena uditānuditahomayo (!) svakālātikrame prāyaści(6)ttavidhānena iṣṭayajñānām aniṣṭayajñānām kacchadeśāhedarśanādirūpānyārthādarśanana (!) ca śākhāntare karmabheda syā(7)d iti ||     || (exp. 288, fol. 137v2–7)

Colophon

iti nidalati(3)lakasutabhaṭṭamahopādhyāyatanūbhavasya veṃkaṭādriyatvagurucaraṇānujasya somānātha (!) sarvatomukhayājina (!) kṛtau śā(4)stradipikāvyākhyāyāṃ (!) mayūkhasaṃjñitāyāṃ prathamodhyāyasya caturthapādaḥ samāptaḥ || 18 || śrīmācandrasahāya || (exp. 148, fol. 143v2–4)

iti niddakulalakasūrubhaṭṭamahopādhyāyatanūbhavasya veṃkaṭādriyaścajvi(!)gurucaraṇā(8)nujasya somānāthasarvatomukhayājinaḥ kṛtau śāstradīpikākhyāyāṃ mayūkhamālikākhyāyāṃ yā dvitīyasyādhyā(9)yasya caturthapādaḥ || adhyāyaś ca saṃpūrṇāḥ || śrīkṛṣṇārpaṇam astu || (exp. 286, fol. 137v7–9)
