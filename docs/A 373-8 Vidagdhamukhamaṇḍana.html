Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namo gaṇeśāya ||

siddhauṣadhāni bhavaduḥkhamahāgadānāṃ

puṇyātmanāṃ paramakarṇṇarasāmṛtāni |

prakṣālanaikasalilā⁅ni ma⁆nomalānaṃ

(2) śaiddhodaneḥ pravacanāni ciraṃ jayanti ||

jayaṃti santaḥ sukṛtaikabhājanaṃ (!)

parārthasaṃpādanasadvratasthitāḥ |

karasthanīropamaviśvadarśi(3)no

jayanti vaidagdhyabhuvaḥ kaver giraḥ ||

ākrānteva mahopalena muninā śapteva durvvāsasā,

sātatyaṃ bata mudriteva jatuṃnā (!) nīteva mūrcchāṃ vi(4)ṣaiḥ |

baddhevātanurajjubhiḥ paraguṇān vaktuṃ na śaktā satī,

jihvā lauhaśalākayā khalamukhe viddheva saṃlakṣate (!) || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

sphoṭayitvākṣaraṃ kiñcit punar anyasya dānata(5)ḥ |

yatrāparo bhaved arthaś cyutadattākṣaraṃ hi tat ||

sadāgatihatochāya,s tamaso vaśyatāṃ gataḥ |

astam eṣyati dīpo yaṃ, vidhur ekaś ciraṃ sthi(6)taḥ ||

pūrṇṇacandramukhī ramyā kaminī nirmmalāṃbarā |

karoti kasya na svāntam ekāntavadanotsavaṃ ||

cyutadattākṣarajātiḥ || || || (fol. 23r4–6)

Colophon

iti dharmmadāsaviracite vidagdhamukhamaṃḍane, karttṛguptākṣaracyutakādilakṣaṇa nirṇayo nāma caturthaḥ paricchedaḥ samāptaḥ || ❁ ||

(2) saṃ 736 vaiśā, śudi paṃcami (!), ādrā, śukarmma, bṛhaspati, śvakunhu saṃpūrṇṇa śubhaṃ || || sarasvatyai namaḥ || durggāyai namaḥ || (fol. 23v1–2)
