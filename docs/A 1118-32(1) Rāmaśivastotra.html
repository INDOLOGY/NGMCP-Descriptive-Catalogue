Manuscript Features

This MTM contains the following texts:

1. "Rāmaśivastotra" (exps. 3–5) 2. "Jagannāthāṣṭāka" (exps. 6–7)

On the front cover-leaf is written:

"yasya jñānadayāsidhor(!) aghādhasyā naghāguṇā" etc.


On the back cover-leaf is written:

rāmo rāma ta

"rāmo rājamani(!) sadā vijayate rāmaṃ ramesaṃ bhaje

ādau rāmatapovanādigamanaṃ hatvā /// kāṃcanaṃ ||" and etc.

Excerpts

«Beginning»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

śiva hare śiva rāma sakhe prabho

tṛvidhi(!)tāpanivāraṇa he prabho ||

aja janeśvarapādapa pāhi māṃ

narahare vijayaṃ kuru me varaṃ || 1 ||

kamalalocana rāma dayānidhe

haratu rogajare †kṣakra†gopate ||

śivatano bhavaśaṃkara pāhi māṃ

narahareº || 2 || (fol. 1r1– 4)


«End»

prātar utthāya yo bhaktyā paṭhed vai stotram uttamaṃm(!) ||
