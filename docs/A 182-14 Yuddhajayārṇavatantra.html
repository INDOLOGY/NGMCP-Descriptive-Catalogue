Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṁ namaḥ sūryyāya ||


mohāndhakāramagnānāṃ janānāṃ jñānaraśmibhiḥ |

kṛtam uddharaṇaṃ yena tan naumi śivabhāskaraṃ ||


kailāsaśikharāsīnaṃ devadevaṃ maheśvaraṃ |

gaṇasiddhasamākīrṇamunisaṃghaniṣevitaṃ ||


tadutsaṅge gatā devī pārvvatīpativallabhā ||

anugrahārthaṃ lokānāṃ papraccha parameśvaraṃ ||


devy uvāca ||


bhagavan deva deveśa śaśāṅgakakṛtaśeṣara |

brūhi me paramaṃ jñānaṃ vispaṣṭārthaṃ maheśvara ||


svarodayaṃ paraṃ guhyaṃ munīnām api kiṃ punaḥ ||

martyānāṃ svalpabuddhīnāṃ pūrvvam uktaṃ tvayā vibho || (fol. 1v3–4)


End

anyat tu kathitaṃ sarvaṃ svarodayavinirṇṇayaṃ |

anaṣṭa sā ca vaśatā nyasyamānam udīritaṃ ||


rahasyaṃ paramaṃ devi sarvvasaṃśayacchedanaṃ |

guhyād guhyataraṃ gūḍḥaṃ gopanīyaṃ prayatnataḥ ||


na deyaṃ pāpaśīlāya krūrakarmaratāya ca |

anāstikāya bhaktāya sādhūddiṣṭakararāya ca ||


gurūbhaktāya sān(!)tāya satyavrataparāya ca |

dātavyam eva deveśi śāstraṃ paramadūrllabhaṃ ||


siddhaḥ kuvalayākhyāya bhaktimān vṛṣabhadhvaja |

tena svaredaye śāstre maryalokāvatāritaṃ || (fol. 60v3–61r1)


Colophon

iti yuddhajayārṇavatantre prakīrtṇādhikāro nāma daśamaḥ paṭalaḥ samāptaḥ || || śubham astu

sarvvadā || || samvat 727 caitrakṛṣṇadaśamyāṃ tithau śrībhaktapaṭṭanādhivāsī daivajñaśivavarmmaṇā

likhitam idaṃ saṃpūrṇaṃ || || sadā jayo (ʼ)stu || (fol. 61r1–2)
