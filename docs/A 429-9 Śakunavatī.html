Manuscript Features

1. There are two texts of the same subject mixed in this microfilm. Actually they are two manuscripts. There are illustrations of individual style in both of them. Here they are catalogued in one mentioning them text 1 and text 2. They are parallel texts. Parallel entries of the same subject are given in Middle Excerpts.

2. The following are double exposures: exps. 6/7, 8/9, 13/14 and 88/89
