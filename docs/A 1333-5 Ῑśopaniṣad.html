Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ⁅hari⁆ḥ oṃ ||

pūrṇam adaḥ pūrṇam idaṃ pūrṇāt pūrṇam udacyate ||
pūrṇasya pūrṇam ādāya pūrṇam evāvaśiṣyate ||

oṃ śāṃtiḥ śāṃtiḥ śāṃtiḥ ||

[[oṃ]] īśāvāsyam idaṃ sarvaṃ yat kiṃ ca jagatyāṃ jagat ||
tena tyakte[[na]] bhuṃjīthā mā gṛdhaḥ kasya svid dhanaṃ ||
kurvann eveha karmāṇi jijīviṣec chatam samāḥ ||
evaṃ tvayi nānyatheto sti na karma lipyate nare ||
asuryā nāma te lokā aṃdhena tamasā vṛtāḥ ||
tāṃs te pretyābhigacchaṃti ye ke cātmahano janāḥ || (fol. 1v1-8)

End

vāyur anilam amṛtam athedaṃ bhasmāṃtaṃ śarīraṃ ||
oṃ krato smara kṛtaṃ smara krato smara krtam smara ||
agne naya supathā rāye asmān viśvāni deva vayunāni vidvān ||
yuyodhy asmaj juhurāṇam eno bhūyiṣṭhaṃ te nama uktiṃ vidhema ||

cha || yajurvedakaṇvaśākhā īśopaniṣat samāptā || hariḥ oṃ ||
pūrṇam adhaḥ pūrṇam idaṃ || oṃ śāṃtiḥ śāṃtiḥ śāṃtiḥ || || oṃ tat sat || (fol. 3r6-3v3)
