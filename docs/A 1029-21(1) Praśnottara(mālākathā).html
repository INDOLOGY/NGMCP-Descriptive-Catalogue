Manuscript Features

Actually the text is based on spiritual question-answer and is not as colophon. In this text the writer himself has maintained spiritual question-answer quoting the various disciplines like as Viṣnupurāṇa, Śrīmadbhāvatamahāpurāṇa, Bhaktirasāmṛtasindu, Rāmagītā and so on. Also we find a date Samvat1902 in the middle of the text.

Excerpts

Beginning

śrīkṛṣṇāya namaḥ ||

śrotāko praśna ||

saṃprati kalakatāso ekajana brāhmaṇa dhanavāna ehi vṛndāvana meṃ āe haiṃ ihākā devālaya me aura brāhmaṇoko dhanadvārā pūja gaye haiṃ evaṃ bhojanādi gahye haiṃ pīche sune ke vuha brāhmaṇa phiṃraṃgī kā mata āśraya kie haiṃ evaṃ phiraṃgīlikī vilāyata gaya haiṃ phiraṃgīke sātha sahabhojana karate haiṃ
(fol.1v1–5)

End

ham sava vātakā pramāṇavacana likhā hai vinā pramāṇa kucha nahī likhā hai jo jo sthāname saṃdeha hovai so soi pustaka dekhiṃge jo vāme pramāṇa namilai so hama sava pramāṇa karadaige, parantu durāgrahī kau samajhānekā sādhya nahī hai = aura kautuka manoraṃjanakā liyai jo jo laukika itihāsa likhi haiṃ sohī śāstrakā upayogī hai so vijña apakṣapāti loga sava vicāra karaiṃge kim adhikam vijñeṣu
(fol.82r6–14)

Colophon

iti paśupāśamokṣṇaṃ samāptam miti jeṣṭa badī 11 saṃ 1941 śubham (fol. 82r14)
