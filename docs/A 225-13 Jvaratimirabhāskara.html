Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya nama (!)

sphurat tripurasundarīcaraṇayugmam ārādhayan

praṇamya ca rujāṃ ripuṃ prathamavaidyam aṃbhodhijaṃ

vilo(2)kya vividhāgan (!) karaṇavīrakuṃbhātmajaś

cikitsitam ati nijāṃ prathayati ha coṃḍābhidhaḥ (!)

kāraṇamaṃḍalavitam (!)

ālakṣma(3)rathaṃ bheṣajo dyotam

jvaratimirabhāskarāhvaṃ

graṃthaṃ tanute sarogiṇas trātuṃ

saṃti yady api taṃtrāṇi suvistīrṇāny a(4)nekasaḥ

tahāpi bālabodhāya sāmudhṛtya (!) liṣyate (fol1vr1–4)

End

ityāgnidāhadine

yoginīpadapāṭherasayugasarabhūmānavarṣe daśamyāṃ (!)

śuklāyāṃ-m āśvīnasya tridaśagurudine(9) yoginīpattanasya

bhūpe śrīrājamalle vilasati vasudhāmaṃḍale kuṃbhasūnuḥ

kāyasthaś caṃḍanāmā jvaratimiraha(10)raṃ bhāskaraṃ rasavidhitaḥ (!) (fol. 49v8–10)
