Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

atha viṣṇuśarmmaṇoktaṃ vigrahas tāvat kathitaḥ

sandhir adhunā vidhīyate yasyāyam ādhyaślokaḥ ||

ihāṃ dekhi uprāṃta samdhi kahuṃ bhani rājaputraharule viṣṇuśarmā paṇḍitakana bhanyā ra viṣṇuśarmā kahaṃchan he rājaputra ho sunnuhavas rājavigraha maile kahi cukyāṃ ava saṃdhi kahaṃchu. jaunā saṃdhi prakaraṇako yo prathama śloka ho kaun bhanaulā ta suna || (fol. 1v1–4 )

End

anyac ca || aru pani ||

śrīmān dhavalacandrosau jīyān māṇḍaliko ripūn ||

yenāyaṃ saṃgraho yatnāl lekhayitvā pracāritaḥ ||

saubhāgyamān dhavalacandra nāma bhayāko. maṃḍalako prabhu jo cha ripukana jitos. jasle yo kathā saṃgraha lekhāikana lokamā pracāra garyo. ||

imāṃ nītikathāṃ kaṇṭhe yaḥ kuryād vibudho janaḥ ||

sa modate sabhāmadhye bhūpānāṃ pūjito bhavet || 1 ||

es nītikathākana jo jana kaṃṭha garos. so sabhākā bicamā. śobhākana prāpta havas rājāharuko pūjya havas. || || (fol. 21v8–12 )

Colophon

iti hitopadeśe nītisāre saṃdhir nāma kathāsaṃgrahaś caturthaḥ samāptaḥ ||

śubhaḥ || || || || (fol. 21v12 )
