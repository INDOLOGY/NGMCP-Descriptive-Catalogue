Manuscript Features

On the front page it is written (probably by a librarian of NAK):

dharmapālāvadānaṃ. dharmasatyalakṣmīsaṃvāda (nevārī. bhā. sa)
aśvaghoṣakathā. mahāyānasūtrasahitā (nevārī. bhā. sa)

The first folio is numbered "8".

Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ nama rokanāṃthāya ||
thanaṃri danaṃ ccharai poraṇa mahimāṣa jina jura śāṃvinatiyāya || (fol. 8v1–2)

End

Colophon

iti śrī sākyamunī buddha bhagatārakasya avīṃcīkrīta upoṣadhadevaputra aṣṭamivrata mahātmā dharmmapālavaddhāna caturtham adhyāyo ||   || (fol. 59r4–5)
