«Beginning»


mūla aṃśa

oṃ svasti śrī gaṇeśāya namaḥ || adhigatabhidā(!) pūrvācaryānupetya sahastradhā saridiva


mahībhedāt saṃprāpya sauriya(!) dod gatājayati bhagavatpāda śrīmanmukhāṃvuja nirgatā janana(!)


haraṇī sūktie brahmā dvayaika parāyaṇā || 1 || || (fol. 2r7-8)


ṭīkā aṃśa

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||


śrī vāgīśvaryai namaḥ || śrī setukṛte namaḥ || śrī viśveśvarāya namaḥ || śrī mānave namaḥ ||


śrī veda vyāsāya namaḥ || śrī śaṃkarācāryāya namaḥ śrī gurubhyoḥ namaḥ || śrī sarva vedāṃta


pravarttakebhyo namaḥ ||


vedavedhya mumākāṃte(!) kumārābhyāmalaṃkṛtaṃ ||


naṃdīśa pramukhaiḥ sevyaṃ praṇamāmīṣṭa siddhaye || 1 ||


śaṃkaraṃ śaṃkarācārya vyāsaṃ nārāyaṇātmakaṃ |


sarasvatīṃ ca vrahmaṇaṃ praṇamāmi punaḥ punaḥ || 2 || (fol. 1v1-3)


«End»


tasmāt iti || udāhṛta śruti sūtrābhāṣyādi svāra(!) syāt ityarthaḥ ||


avirodhitvam aiśvarya viśeṣaṇaṃ || tadanuguṇeti eiśvaryānuguṇetyarthaḥ ||


samṛddham īśvara bhāvāpatti viśeṣaṇaṃ ||


śrīkrṣṇacaraṇa dvaṃdve smartṛṇāṃ maṃgalaprade ||


yogidhyeye kṛtir iyaṃ alaṃkārārthamarpitā || 1 ||


śrīkrṣṇā manasā dhyātvā śrīkrṣṇaṃ saṃpraṇamya ca ||


vyākhyātoyaṃ paricchedaḥ śrīkrṣṇa parituṣtaye || 2 || (fol. 18v2-5)


«Colophon»


mūla aṃśa


iti śāstra siddhāṃtaleśa saṃgrahe apyaya(!) dīkṣita viracite prathamaḥ paricchedaḥ samāptaḥ || (fol. 125v9-10)


ṭīkā aṃśa


iti śrīmat paramahaṃsa parivrājakācarya śrīmat svayaṃprakāśānaṃda sarasvatī divya śrī caraṇāraviṃda


saṃlagna rajobhūtasyācyutakṛṣṇānaṃda tīrtha kṛtau śāstra siddhāṃta leśa graṃtha vyākhyāyām


krṣṇālaṃkārāṇyāyāṃ caturthaḥ parichedaḥ samāptaḥ || ❖ || śubhambhūyāt || ❖ ||(fol. 18v5-7)
