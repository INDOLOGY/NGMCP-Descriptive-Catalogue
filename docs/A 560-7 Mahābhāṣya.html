Lines per Folio 11–12

Foliation figures on the verso, in the upper left margin after the marginal titles and in the lower right margin; the marginal titles are: bhāṣya pra, bhāṣya prathama, bhāṣya prathamādhyā, bhāṣya prathamādhyāya., bhāṣya prathama adhyāya, bhā. pra., ma. bhā. pra., ma. bhā. pra. a.

Scribe Śrīpati

Place of Deposit NAK

Accession No. 1/1087

Manuscript Features

Only sutras 1.1.1–1.1.5 were copied (the commentary on 1.1.5 is not complete).

The pages of the manuscript are slightly dark. In the middle of the first page is written śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ likhitam idaṃ śrīpatinā. In the upper left of the page is written (in a bold, large hand but not clear): śrīga. namaḥ.

Beginning

||60|| śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

vṛddhirādaic || kuttvaṃ kasmān na bhavati coḥ kuḥ pudasyati (!) || bhatvāt || kathaṃ bhasaṃjñā ayasmayādīni chaṃdasīti | chandasīty ucyate na cedam chaṃdaḥ | chaṃdovat sūtrāṇi bhavaṃti | yadi bhasaṃjñā vṛddhirādaijadeṃguṇa iti jastvam api na prāpnoti | (fol. 1v1–3)
