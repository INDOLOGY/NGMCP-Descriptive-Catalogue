Manuscript Features

The remaining space on 2r and 2v is covered with stanzas dedicated to Lakṣmī, which begin:

lakṣmīṃ kṣirasamudrarājatanayāṃ (śrī)raṃgadhāmeśvarīṃ ,
dāśībhūtasamastadevavartitās(!) trailokyadīpāṃkurāṃ |
iṣat(!) mandakaṭākṣarabdha(!)vibhavā brahmendragaṃgādharas(!) ,
tās(!) trailokyakuṭumbinīṃ manasijāṃ vande mukundapriyāṃ || || ||
