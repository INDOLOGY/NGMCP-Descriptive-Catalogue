Beginning

gaurīśaṃkarābhyāṃ namaḥ

ātmīyaṃ caraṇaṃ dadhāti purato nimnonnatāyām bhuvi

svīyenaiva kareṇa karṣti taroḥ puṣpaṃ śramāśaṇkayā.

ta(2)lpe kiñ ca mṛgatvacā viracite nidrāti bhāgair nijair

antaḥ premabharālasāṃ priyata[[mā]]m aṇke dadhāno haraḥ ,1 ||

vidvatkulamanobhṛ(3)ṇgarasavyāsaṃgahetave ||

eṣā prakāśyate śrimadbhānunā rasamañjarī || 2 ||

tatra raseṣu śṛṅgārasyābhyarhitatvena tadālambanavibhā(4)vatvena nāyikā tāvan nirūpyate, sā ca trividhā || svīyā parakīyā sāmānyavanitā ceti, || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

mādhvīkasyandasandhohasundarīṃ rasamañjarīm,

tato (!) yasya gaṇeśvaraḥ kavikulālaṃkāracūḍāmaṇir

deśo yasya videhabhūḥ surasaritkallolalolomitā,

(9)padyena svakṛtena tena kavina (!) śrībhānunā yojitā
