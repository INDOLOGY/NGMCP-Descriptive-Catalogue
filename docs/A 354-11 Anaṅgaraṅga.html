Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

atilalitavilāsaṃ viśvacetonivāsaṃ

samarakṛtavikāśaṃ śaṃbarākhyapraṇāśam ||

ratinayanavirāmaṃ saṃ(2)tataṃ cābhirāmaṃ

prasabhavijitabāmaṃ śarmadaṃ naumi kāmaṃ || 1 ||

lodīvaṃśāvataṃso hataripuvanitānetravāriprapūra-

prādurbhūtāṃburāśiḥ (3) śamitaparayaśolīlayā pāvitaś ca ||

tatputraḥ khyātakīrter ahamadanṛpateḥ kāmasiddhāṃtavidvān

jī⟪yā⟫[[vyā]]c chrīlāḍakhānaḥ kṣiti(4)patimukuṭair ghṛṣṭapādāraviṃdaḥ || 2 ||

asyaiva kautukanimittam anaṃgaraṃga-

graṃthavilāsijavallabham ātanoti ||

śrīmān mahāka(5)vir aśeṣakalāvi[[da]]gdhaḥ

kalyāṇamalla iti [[bhūpa]]munir yaśasvī || 3 ||… (fol. 1v1–5)

padminī citriṇī cātha śaṃ(2r1)khinī hastinī tataḥ ||

pūrvapūrvatarās tāsu śreṣṭhās tallakṣma cakṣmahe || 7 || (fol. 1v9–2r1)

End

suviditaparamārthaḥ kāmaśāstrasya vidvān

vividharativinodaiḥ kāminīnāṃ manāṃsi ||

anudinam anurāgād raṃ(4)jayed yaḥ salīlaṃ

phalam avikalam eva prāpnuyān mānava[[ḥ]] saḥ || 62 ||

yāvac chaṃkaramauligā suranadī gaurī tadarddhāṃgagā

yāvat premavatī pa(5)yodhitanayā vaikuṃṭhasaṃge ratā ||

vedābhyāsarato viraṃcir api bhūd (!) yāvad dineśaḥ śaśī

bhūyāt tāvad anaṃgaraṃgaka iti prītyai sadā kāminām || (6) || 63 || || (fol. 26v3–6)

Colophon

iti śrīmallāḍamallavinodāya mahākavikalyāṇamallaviracito naṃgaraṃgaḥ samāptaḥ || || || ❁ || || (fol. 26v6)
