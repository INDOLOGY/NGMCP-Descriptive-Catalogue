Excerpts

Beginning

❖ svasti || || śrīgaṇapataye namaḥ || ||

śrīsarasvatyai namaḥ || śrīgurubhyo nnamaḥ || ||

prabhavaviratimadhyajñānabaṃ(dhā) nitāntaṃ

viditaparamatattvā yatra te yogino pi |

(2) tam aham iha nimittaṃ, viśvajanmātyayānām

manumitam abhivande bhagrahaiḥ kālam īśaṃ || 1 ||

ahaṃ śrī śrīpatināmā taṃ kālam abhivande namas karomi |

kiṃ bhūtam īśaṃ svāmi(3)naṃ sthāpakam ity arthaḥ || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

sasvadvākyaṃ (!) pramāṇaṃ prakaṭayadumater vedavedāntavettuḥ

sūnu śrī(2)lunigasyā cutavaranā nati śrīmahādevanāmā (!)

tatprokta (!) ratnamālā || ruciravivaraṇe ‥ jjanaṃ bhojanānau

tvamīnā †durjjane do† prakaraṇam amaraṃsthāpanavyoma(3)yugmaṃ ||

 (fol. 174r1–3)

Colophon

|| iti śrīpatibhaṭṭaviracitāyāṃ bhaṭṭotpalaratnamālāṭīkāvivaraṇaṃ (!) devapratiṣṭhāprakaraṇa (!) viṃśatitamo dhyāyaḥ || || ❁ || iti ⟪ga⟫gaṇa(4)kacakracūḍāmaṇiśrīpatināmāviracitaṃ ratnamālāvivaraṇaṃ ṭīkāraṃ (!) samāptaṃ || || yādṛśaṃ pustakaṃ dṛṣṭvā tādṛśaṃ likhitaṃ mayā ||

yadi śuddham aśuddhaṃ vā mama (5) doṣo na dīyate || || śubham astu sarvvadā || ||

hrasvadīrghaparīkṣāyāṃ mama buddhiḥ palāyitā ||

śuddhāśuddhaparijñāne kathaṃ śaktir bhaviṣyati ||(fol. 174r3–5)
