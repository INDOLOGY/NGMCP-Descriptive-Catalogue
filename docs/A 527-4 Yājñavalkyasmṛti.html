pūrve saṃgatim āha stheti viśeṣasya rājñaḥ guṇadharmāḥ prajāparipālanādayaḥ †cena† nityanaimittikasamuccayaḥ tadadhikāreti teṣu dharmeṣ vadhikārity(!)arthaḥ mukhena dvāra teṣāṃ nityanaimiti(!)kānāṃ na tu guṇadharmāṇāṃ ata eva gṛhasthām pra(!)miṇām ity ādāv uktaṃ. tato viśeṣatikṣyāmāyāḥ pūrvaṃ sarvān madhye tannirūpaṇam api kṛtaṃ evaṃ cādhyādhyāya prati pādyenāsyāpavādikī saṃgatir iti bhāvaḥ tatrādāv āśaucapadārthajijñāsai cety ata āhāśauceti (fol. 1v1–4)

samuditasaṃsargas tu na hetus tatsaṃjñāyāḥ pratyekam eva karaṇāt yo yenety anena prāyaścittasya pratyekasaṃsarga eva vidhānāc ca vyabhicāro pi na dvitīyavākyatvā[t] prāktatvasyānyatrābhāvāc catuṣṭayamātṛvṛttitvasya tasya pūrvavākyato lāvbhas tadrupakadharmāvachinnacatuṣṭayasaṃsargasya pratyekaṃ hetutvāt ity āśayena samādhatte syād evam ity ādiśabdaiketi prakṛtety ādinaivam iti evaṃ nety arthaḥ | nanv etaṃ tasyāduṣṭatvāpatirata †āhāt† iti tato hāniḥ yāti saṃtathoktatvāt ata evāha yāti sābhāve iti māṇavaṃ mā haivasvibhir iti upasaṃharati pāti sābhāve pīti tasmād ity ādiḥ mūlādy anuktatvāt saptapramāṇaṃ tatsvarūpam āhanata ce (fol. 85v7–86r2)
