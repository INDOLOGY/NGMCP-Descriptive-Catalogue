śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || śrīhanumate namaḥ || ṛṣayaḥ ūcuḥ ||

jhaṣe(!) lohagiriṃ prāptaḥ sītāvirahakātaraḥ ||

bhagavān kiṃ vyadhād rāmas tatsarvaṃ brūhi vistaraṃ 1

vālmīki uvāca

māyāmanujadehoyaṃ dadaśāmre(!) kapīśvaraṃ |

hanumaṃtaṃ jagatsvāmiṃ bālārkasamatejasaṃ 2

sasatvaraṃ samāgatya sāṣṭāṃgaṃ praṇipatya ca |

kṛtāṃjalipuṭo bhūtvā hanumān rāmam abravīt 3

hanumānuvāca

dhanyosmi kṛtakṛtyosmi dṛṣṭvā tvatpādapaṃkajaṃ |

yogināmapyagamyaṃ ca saṃsārabhayanāśanaṃ 4

puauṣottamadeveśakarttavyaṃ tannivedyatāṃ || (fol. 1v1–5)
