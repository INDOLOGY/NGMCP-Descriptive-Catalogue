Excerpts

Beginning

°sya ca ||

kuṇḍaghirgharikāyāś ca vināyakahradan tathā |
kūpaṃ sindhubhavañ caiva puṇyaṃ brahmasaras tathā ||
rudrāvāsaṃ tathā tīrthaṃ nāga+++ lomakaṃ |
brahmāvarttaṃ kuśāvarttan dadhikarṇṇodapānakaṃ ||
śṛṅgatīrtham mahātīrthaṃ tīrthaśreṣṭho(!) mahānadī |
divyaṃ brahmasaraḥ puṇyaṃ gayāśīrṣākṣayam vaṭaṃ |
dakṣiṇañ cottarañ caiva gomayaṃ bhūpaśāntidaṃ |
kapilāhradaṃ gṛdhravaṭaṃ sāvitrīhradam eva ca ||
prabhāsanaṃ śītavanaṃ yonidvārañ ca dhenukaṃ |
ramyakaṃ kokilākhyañ ca mataṅgahradam eva ca ||
pitṛkūpaṃ raṅgatīrthaṃ śatatīrthaṃ sumālinaṃ | (fol. 6r1-4)

Sub-Colophons

ity ādibrahmapurāṇe svayaṃbhūṛṣisamvāde ādityamāhātmyavarṇṇanaṃ || || (fol. 32r3)

ity ādibrahmapurāṇe svayaṃbhūṛṣisamvāde mārttaṇḍasya ekaviṃśatināmānukīrttanam || || (fol. 35r5)

End

māritaṃteyataḥ proktam etad aṇḍaṃ tvayodite(!) |
tasmāt munisutas te 'yaṃ mārttaṇḍo vai bhaviṣyati ||
haniṣyaty asurāṃś cāpi yajñabhāgaharāṃs tv arīn |
devān īśasyeti(!) raver ggaganāt samupāgataṃ ||
praharṣam atulaṃ jātā(!) dānavāś ca kṛtaujasaḥ |
tato yuddhāya daiteyān ājahāra śatakratuḥ ||
saha devair mmudā yuktā dānavāś ca samabhyayuḥ |
teṣāṃ yuddham abhūd ghoraṃ devānām asuraiḥ saha || (fol. 38v2-5)
