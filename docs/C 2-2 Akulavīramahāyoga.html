Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namo gurave ||

śrīmatsahajānandaṃ svasaṃvedyasadbhāvasarvvagambhīrasādhanaṃ sacarācaravyāpakaṃ māyājālavinimmuktaṃ gaṃbhīraguruṃ vande || 〇 ||

athāta saṃpravakṣyāmi akulavīra mahadbhuta |
guhyāt guhyataraṃ guhyaṃ siddhasantānasa〇ntati ||
anugrahā[r]tha lokānāṃ mīnanāthena bhāṣitaṃ ||
gopaṇīyaṃ prayatnena yadicche〇t sthā(!)svataṃ padaṃ ||
saṃsārārṇṇavamagnānāṃ sārabhūta mahāsrayaḥ |
yathā nadīnadā sarve 〇 sāgare samupāgatāḥ ||
tathā tv akulavīre ca sarvapamāṃ(!) kṣayaṃ gatāḥ |
(fol. 1v1–6)

End

nātra saṃsayaḥ ||
paramāmṛtasa[ṃ]tṛptā sahajānaṃdā ca kevalā |
na jarās teṣāṃ na mṛtyuś ca na soko duḥkha(m e)va ca ||
sarvvavyādhiharaś caiva na punarbhava〇saṃbhavaḥ |
akulavīraṃ sthitaṃ divyaṃ siddhanāthaprasādataḥ ||
sarvata sarvadā suddhaḥ sarvataḥ sarvadā 〇 prabhuḥ ||
(fol. [14]r1–3)

Colophon

iti macchendrapādāvatārite kāmarūpīḥ sthāne yoginīprasādā labdhe akulavīraṃ 〇 mahāyogaṃ samāptaḥ || ❁ ||

❖ oṃ hruṃ hruṃ madya bhuṃ bhruṃ namaḥ ||
madyabhāṇḍaṃ viṃsatādhikammatavāraṃ 〇 parijapya |
kapāla mahāañjananavolatthāṃpolucakakolāginīranta(?) pretabhedabhede mahābheda vasa 2 mahā vasa gauri vasa trailoka vasa vajrana vasa || mekopyalana saṃkhola savāla || ❁ ||
(fol. [14]r3–7)
