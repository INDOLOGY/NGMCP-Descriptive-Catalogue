Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṁ namo gaṅgāyai ||


agastya uvāca ||


vinā snānena gaṅgāyāṃ, nṛṇāñ janmanirarthakaṃ |

upāyāntaram (atyanyad) yena snānaphalaṃ labhet || 1 ||

asaktānāñ ca paṅgūnām ālasyopahatātmanāḥ(!) |

dūradeśāntarasthānā[ṃ] gaṅgāsnānaṅ katham bhavet || 2 || (fol. 1v1–3)


End

gaṅgāsnānapratinidhiḥ stotram etan mayeritaṃ |

sisnāsur jjāhnavī na syād etat stotraṃ japet sudhī[ḥ] || 209 || || (fol. 16v3–4)


Colophon

iti śrīskandapurāṇe, kāśīkhaṇḍe gaṅgāsahasranāma manaikonatriṃśatitamo ʼdhyāyaḥ || ❁ || śubham astu sarvvadā bhavataḥ || samvat 852 mārgaśudi || || ❖ oṁ namaḥ śrīrāmacaṃdrāya

śuklāmbaradharaṃ viṣṇuṃ śaśaivarṇṇañ catu[r]bbhujaṃ | prasannavadanaṃ dhyāyet sarvva | (fol. 16v4–6)
