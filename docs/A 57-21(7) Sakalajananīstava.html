namaḥ śrīnityāyai || ○ ||

ajānananto yānti kṣayam avasam anyonyakalahair

jaganmātar jjanmajvarabhayamataḥ kaumudi vayaṃ
namas te kurvvāṇāḥ śaraṇaṃ upayāmo bhagavatīm || 1 ||

vacastarkkāgamyaḥ sarasaparamānandavibhavaḥ
