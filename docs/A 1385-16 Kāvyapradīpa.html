Excerpts

«Beginning of the root text:»

niyatikṛtaniyamarahitāṃ

hlādaikamayīmananya parataṃtrām

navarasarucirāṃ nirmitim

ādadhatī bhāratī kaver jayati 1 (fol. 1v3–4)

«Beginning of the commentary:»

śrīmanmahāgaṇādhipataye namaḥ || ||

sonodevyāḥ prathamatanayaḥ keśavasyātmajanmā

śrīgovindo rucikarakaveḥ snehapātraṃ kanīyān

śrīmannārāyaṇacaraṇayoḥ samya(2)g ādhāya cittaṃ

natvā sārasvatam api mahaḥ kāvyatattvaṃ vyanakti 1

vacana sandarbha viśeṣarūpasya gramthasya prāripsitatvena stotum ucitāyāḥ sevyāmānāś ca vāgdevyā āspadabhūtāṃ (3) kavibhāratīṃ tad abhinnatvenādhyavasitāṃ prāripsitapratibandhakaduritaśāntaye granthakṛt saṃstauti

(fol. 1v1–3)

End

apare tu himapayaḥ śaṃkhādiṣu śuklādiguṇaḥ paramārthato bhinna eva pākāc chuklaṃ rūpaṃ naṣṭaṃ śyāmam utpannam iti pratīteḥ naceyaṃ bhrāntir bādhakābhāvāt na cotpādavināśau samavāyasya pratīyete itiy uktaṃ tad anullekhāt tathā cānugata pratyayānurodhene tatrāpi śuklatvādijātiḥ lāghavāc ca tatraiva saṃketa ityupeyam evaṃ guḍataṃḍu- ||| (fol. 6v11–13)

Colophon

iti mahāmahopādhyāyaśrīgovindakṛte kāvyapradīpe kāvyalakṣaṇanirṇayaḥ prathamaḥ ullāsaḥ || (fol. 5r6–7)
