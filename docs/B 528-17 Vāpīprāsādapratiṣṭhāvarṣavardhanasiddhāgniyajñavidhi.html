Beginning

❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||


śrīgurubhyo namaḥ || ||


atha varṣavarddhanadhvajāvalohana(!) likhyate || || mālakkavasaye || || kaṇḍasacatusama ādina

vātralāvasidhalanaśrīcakracāṣa(!) || || yajamānapūṣpabhājana || adyādi || vākya |

mānavagotrayajamānasya śrīśrījayabhūpatīndramalladevavarmmaṇaḥ

śrīśrīśrīsveṣṭadevatāprītikāmanayā pāṣāṇanirmmita vā(!) prī

prasādasuvarṇakalaśadhvajāvalohanasiddhāgni ayutāhutiyajñavarṣavarddhananimittyarthaṃ kartuṃ

puṣpabhājanaṃ samarpayāmi || || (fol. 1v1–2r1)
