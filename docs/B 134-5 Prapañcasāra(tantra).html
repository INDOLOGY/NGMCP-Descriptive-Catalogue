Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ (mahāgaṇeśāya namaḥ) ||

akacaṭatapayādyais saptabhir varṇṇavarggair

vviracitamukhavāhā pādamadhyākhyahṛtkā |

sakalajagadadhīśa śāśvato viśvayonir

vvitaratu pariśuddhiṃ cetasaḥ sāradā vaḥ ||

athābhavan brahmaharīśvarākhyāḥ

purā pradhānāt pralayāvasāne

guṇaprabhinnā jagato sya sṛṣṭi-

sthitikṣayaspaṣṭaniviṣṭaceṣṭāḥ || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

yadāśrayas taṃ prakṛtiprabhāvato

vibhinnatārāṃ samavasthitaujasaḥ ||

jaganti puṣṇanti ravīnduvahnyo

namo stu tasmai paripurṇṇatejase || (fol. 177v3–4)

Colophon

|| iti śrīprapañcasāre caturtriṃśatiḥ (!) paṭalaḥ saṃpūrṇṇaḥ || 34 || śrīcakrarājyasya || samāptaś cāyaṃ granthaḥ ||

saṃsārasindhau(!) staraṇaikahetūn

vande gurūn mūrddhni śivasvabhāvān

rajāṃṣi yeṣāṃ padapaṅkajānāṃ

tīrthābhiṣekaśriyam āvahanti || ||

saṃvat 806  mārgaśira śudi 14 āditya(!)ra saṃpūrṇana coyā dina  || (fol. 177v4–6)
