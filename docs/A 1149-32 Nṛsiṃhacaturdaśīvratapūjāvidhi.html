«Beginning»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

atha nṛsiṃhacaturdaśīvratapūjā || ||

gaṃdhapuṣpākṣatai⌠r⌡ bhūmāv ādhāraśaktiṃ saṃpūjyārghyaṃ datvā punaḥ saṃsthāpya gaṃdhādi kṣiptvā tilakuśayavajalāny ādāya deśakālau saṃkīrtyā ʼmukasaṃvatsare uttarāyaṇe vasaṃtartau vaiśākhamāse śuklapakṣe caturddaśyāṃ tithau amukagotro ʼmukarāśir amukaśarmāhaṃ śrutismṛtipurāṇoktaphalāvāptaye nṛsiṃha caturddaśīvrataṃ kariṣye || tadaṃgatvena suvarṇapratimāyāṃ nṛsiṃharūpiṇo viṣṇoḥ yathāmilitopacāraiḥ pūjanaṃ kariṣye || (fol. 1v1–7)


«End»

nṛsiṃha devadeveśa tava janmadine śubhe ||

upavāsaṃ kariṣyāmi sarvabhogavivarjitaḥ || ||

atha viś(!)arjana(!) ||

āvāhanaṃ na jānāmi(!)ti visarjanam ||

atha dakṣiṇādānaṃ || || adya kṛtaitat vaiśākhaśuklacaturddaśi(!)nṛsiṃhavratapūjāsāṃgatāsiddhyarthaṃ dakṣiṇātāmraṣaṃḍaṃ ādityadaivate yathānāmagotrāya yathānāmaśarmaṇe brāhmaṇāya tubhyam ahaṃ saṃpradade || || (fol. 4r2–7)


«Colophon»

liṣitaṃ vidyādharasya miti jeṣṭakṛṣṇa 3 sāke 1736 śubham astu || || || | (fol. 4r7–8)
