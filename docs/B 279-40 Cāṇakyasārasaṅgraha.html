Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīganeśāya ||

praṇamya śirasā viṣṇuṃ trailokyādhipatiṃ prabhuṃ ||
nānāśāstrodhṛtaṃ vakṣye rājanī(2)ti samuccayaṃ || 1 ||

trailokyayā adhipati juraṃ viṣṇutvaṃ namaskāla yāṅāva nānā śāstrasa pikāsyaṃ rājanī(3)ti muṅa tayā khaṃ jena hlāya || 1 || (fol. 1r1–3)

End

agnirāpaḥ strīyo mūrkhā, sarppa rājakulāni ca |
nityame(5)va pacāraṇa, sadyaḥ prāṇa harāniṣaṭ || 94 ||

me, laṃkha, strī, mūrkha, sarppa, rājakula, thvate hnithaṃ caralapā(6)na, tatkāranaṃ prāna mokeva ||    ||

śuṣkamāṃsa striyo vṛddhā, bālārkka taruṇo dadhi |
prabhāte maithunaṃ nidrā sadya(7)ḥ prāṇa harāṇiṣaṭ || 95 ||
