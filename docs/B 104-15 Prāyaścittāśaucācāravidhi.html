oṃ namo śrī vajrasatvāya || ||

vajrasatvaṃ namaskṛtya jagantrāṇaṃ mahākṛpaṃ (Exp.1 r 1) ||

prāyaścittaṃ vidhiṃ vakṣye śaucācārañca suvrata || 1 ||

ācāryya paramo(2)devaḥ pujanīyaḥ prayatnata ||

svayaṃvajradharo rājā sākṣādarupena saṃsthitaḥ |(3) 2 |

atha bhāṣā ||

vajrasatvayāta namaskāra gathihma vajrasatva dhālasā jaga(4)t saṃsāra surakṣā yānā bijyāka tavadhana kṛpā dasyaṃ cona ohmasta na(ma)skā(5)ra yānā prāyaścita hlāya śauca yāyeta ācāra siyeketa aneka vrata yāye(6)ta samasta devayāsena tavadhane deva julaṃ ācārayātaṃ yatna yānāo pujā (7) yāyemāla gathiṃhma ācāryya dhālasā thamathya thamanaṃ vajra dhararapa thava ru(Exposer 1r1)ṣa

sākṣāddivyadehasaṃ cona || ||

prāyacittasaucāravidhi prāyacitta udaya prakarana iti || ||

idānī goghna(exp. 18r 4) prāyaścittaviṣaye vyavasthāpyatte || 1 ||
