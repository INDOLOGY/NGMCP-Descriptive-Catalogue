Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśo jayati || ||

śrīmadviṭhṭhalam ānamya pāṇinyādimunīn gurūn ||

prakriyākaumudīṃ kurmaḥ pāṇinīyānusāriṇīṃ || 1 ||

aiuṇ ṛḷk eoṅ aiauc hayavaraṭ laṇ ñamaṅaṇanam jhabhañ ghaḍhadhaṣ jabagaḍadaś khaphachaṭhathacaṭatav kapay śaṣasar hal ||

iti pratyāhārasūtrāṇi | hakārādiṣv akāra uccāraṇārthaḥ | laṇmadhye tvitsaṃjñakaḥ || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

sādhunipuṇābhyām arcāyāṃ saptamyaprateḥ ||

ābhyāṃ yoge saptamī syāt | arcāyāṃ na tu prateḥ || mātari sādhur nipuṇo vā || arcāyāṃ kiṃ || sādhur bhṛtyo rājñas tatvakathanena || aprataḥ prasaktaḥ | prasitaḥ | tatparaḥ utsuko vā hariṇā harau vā || nakṣatreva lupi || lubaṃtān nakṣatrāt tṛtīyā syād vā || adhikaraṇatvāt pakṣe saptamī || puṣpe puṣpeṇa vā gacchet || puṣpayukte kāla ity arthaḥ || lupi kiṃ || puṣpe śaniḥ || akarmakadhātubhir yoge deśakālādhvabhāvebhyo vā dvitīyeti kecit || nadīṃ nadyāṃ vā casati || rātriṃ rātrau vā śete || krośaṃ krośe vā vasati || yāgaṃ yāge vā tiṣṭhati || iti vibhaktyarthāḥ || ||

samarthaḥ padavidhiḥ ||

padayoḥ padānāṃ vā vidhiḥ samarthonvita eva syāt || samāsaś caturddhā || pūrvapadārthapradhānovyayībhāvaḥ || uttarapadārthapradhānas tatpuruṣaḥ || śubham || ❖ || (fol. 37r5–12)
