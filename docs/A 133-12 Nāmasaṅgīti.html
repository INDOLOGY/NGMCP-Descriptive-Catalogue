Manuscript Features

There are two exposures of fols. 34v–35r, 63v–64r, *86v–87r *92v–93r and *133v–134.

After fol. 64, the foliation is not done.

Excerpts

Beginning

āryamañjuśriyaṃ natvā jñānendu†pravatāpinīṃ† ||

tathāsya nāmasaṃgītigambhīrośaradharmmiṇī ||

yogasyārthakriyā || vilo(kyānyati)bandhanaṃ ||

jātakaṃ ceti vṛttaṃ ca stotrakāramataṃ tathā ||

vijñānavādam akhilaṃ tathā madhyamakaṃ ca yat ||

upadeśaṃ ca saṃsmṛtya prārthitena mayādhunā ||

vyākhyānaṃ kriyate tasyā gambhīrodāravarttanaṃ ||

satvānāṃ ca hitārthāya jñānī(!)nāṃ mokṣaprāptaye ||

idānīm asyāḥ saṃgīteḥ sarīravyavasthāpanam abhidhīyate ||

tad yathā || adhyepa(!)ṇāprativacanaṃ ṣaṭkalāvalokanamāyājālābhisaṃbodhikramaḥ bodhicittaḥ śrīvajrasatva(dvāreṇāt) viśuddhadharmmadhātujñānaṃ mahāvairocanasvabhāvena | ādarśajñānam a(kṣo)bhyasvabhāvena | (fol. 1v1–9)

End

śreyomārga iti || virvvāna(!)hetubhāvatvāt |

gambhīrodāravaipulyamahārtho jagadarthakṛt ||

buddhānāṃ viṣayo hy eṣa sarvvasaṃbuddhadeśita iti ||

tatra evārtho hi yasmāt | gaṃbhīrodāravaipulyas tasmād buddhānām viṣay⟪ā⟫agocaro nānyeṣāṃ śrāvakādīnāṃ jagadrthakṛd iti || mahākaruṇāparigṛhītatvāt sarvvabuddhais traikālyabarhibhir dda[r]śitaḥ sarvvasaṃbuddhadarśita iti ||         || (exp.141b1–5)

Colophon

āryanāmasaṃgītitī(!)kāyāṃ nāmamantrārthāvalokinyām upasaṃhārādhikāraḥ caturdaśa(!) samāptaḥ ||         || (exp. .141b5–6)
