Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śubha yā va, mvaṇu sapāṅana saṃma hāyake || ||

aṃvala kuṭaḍaphala, colamāsa, vadiyāla thvate laṃkhana neyā va mvaṇu sapāṅana saṃmano vayā saṃno(2) yukhe || || aṃvala dune yāva, mvaṇu hluye thālasavālā va moṇu hlusyaṃ saṃmahāyake bhiṃnake cikanalācake || || arkka duduna māsacona vo (3) lāva, puṭapāka yāṅā va hākasyaṃ dāṭayāṅace a | o cona o ceka o vālā va, mvaṇusate lāva mvaṇusyākvaroyalā akhe || || (fol.1r1–3)

End

sehlāsa, śīla, devadāra, aguli, sākhara, kasatī samabhāgana, thvate (4) kuthā ṅāva , dhūpayāya āvana hvāhlākā āśavathālāśa || || kākolā 1 jīraphola thvane yā pye o sacchi vo kapū(5)ra thvate neyā va, āśava thālāśa voye || || thvate āśava vidhi ||

(fol. 55r3–5)

Colophon

|| iti haramekhalā samāptaḥ || || śubham astu || || (fol.55r5)
