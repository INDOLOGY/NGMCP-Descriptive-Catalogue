Excerpts

Beginning

. . . aneka saṃśayochedi (!) parokṣārthasya darśakaṃ |

sarvasya locanaṃ śāstraṃ yasya nāstyaṃdha eva saḥ |

yauvanaṃ dhanasaṃpattiḥ prabhutvam avivekitā |

ekaikam apyanarthāya kimu yatra catuṣṭayaṃ |

ityākarṇyātmanaḥ putrāṇām anadhigata śāstrāṇām

anuṣṭhānenodvignamanāḥ sa rājā ciṃtayāmāsa |

ko ʼarthaḥ putreṇa jātena yo na vidvān na dhārmikaḥ |

kāṇena cakṣuṣā kiṃ vā cakṣuḥ pīḍaiva yat phalaṃ |

sa jālo yena jātena jāti(!)vaṃśa samunnatiṃ |

parivartini saṃsāre mṛtaḥ ko vā na jāyate | (fol. 2r1–5)

End

prāleyoddhaḥ sutāyāḥ praṇayam iva satiṃ ścandramauliḥ sa yāvad

yāvallakṣmīr murārer jalada iva taḍit mānase visphuraṃtī |

yāvat svarṇāvaloyaṃ davadahanasaro yasya sūryaḥ sphuliṃgas

tāvan nārāyaṇena pracaratu caritaḥ saṃgrahoyaṃ kathānāṃ |

aparaṃca |

śrīmān dhavala caṃdrosau jīyāt māṃḍaliko ripūn |

yenāyaṃ saṃgraho yatnāl-lekhayitvā pracāritaḥ | (fol. 83v4–7)

Colophon

| iti hitopadeśe saṃdhir nāma caturtha kathāsaṃgrahaḥ samāptaḥ ||

|| bhīmasyāpi raṇe bhaṃgo munirapi matibhramaḥ |

yadi śuddham aśuddhaṃ vā śodhanīyaṃ mahāvudhaiḥ | |

śrīsamvat 1862 sāla miti āṣāśu (!) śuśubhāṃ (fol. 83v7–9)
