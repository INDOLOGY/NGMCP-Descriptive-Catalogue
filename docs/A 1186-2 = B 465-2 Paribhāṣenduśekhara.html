nāṃ samūho yauvanam || tyantāt tu aṇipuṃvadbhāvād yauvanam ity eva bhikṣādipāṭhasāmarthyān na puṃvad iti vṛttis tu bhāṣyaviruddhaḥ |

Excerpts

«Beginning»


śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

natvā sāmbaśivaṃ brahma nāgeśaḥ kurute sudhīḥ ||

bālānāṃ sukhabodhāya paribhāṣenduśekharam ||

prācīnavaiyākaraṇataṃtre vācanikāni atra pāṇinīyataṃtre jñāpakanyāyasiddhāni

bhāṣyavārttikayorupanibaddhāni yāni paribhāṣārūpāṇi tāni vyākhyāyante ||

nanu laṇ a i uṇ sūtrayorṇakārasyaivopādāne ʼṇ iṇgrahaṇeṣu saṃdehādanirṇayo ʼta āha |

vyākhyānato viśeṣapratipattir nahi saṃdehādalakṣaṇam || (fol. 1v1–4)


«End»


samṛdyagrahaṇe sārddhāstisro mātrā iṇ tisro mātrā iti laṇsūtre bhāṣyoktestathā ota śyanīti sūtraṃ śitīti vaktavyam tatrāyamarthaḥ ṣṭhivuklmviti sūtre śitīti na vaktavyam bhavatīti bhāṣyena(!) kevalaṃ mātrālāghavaṃ yāvadayamapyarthakaiyaṭokteḥ prāyeṇeti || || (fol. 53v1–3)


«Colophon»


iti śrīmadupādhyāyopanāmakasatīgarbhajanāgojibhaṭṭakṛ[taḥ] paribhāṣenduśekharaḥ saṃpūrṇaḥ || || śubham astu || || likhitam idam pustakaṃ pokharyālopanāmakaramāvallabhaśarmaṇā iti sudhībhir vibhāvanīyam || ❁ (fol. 53v3–5)
