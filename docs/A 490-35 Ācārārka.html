Excerpts

«Beginning»

❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

śrīmātāpitṛbhyāṃ namaḥ || nirvighnaṃ bhūyāt ||

oṃ || jānakīnayanayugmagocaraṃ māninānnayanayoragocaraṃ ||

nīlamegharucirachaviṃ sadā bhāvayema nasirāpayaṃ mudā || 1 ||

uccair aparṇāmukhacaṃdrabiṃbaṃ

parāmṛśaṃtaṃ nijapāṇipallavaiḥ ||

gaṃgādharaṃ naumi bhujaṃgarāhu

prakṛṣṭabhītyā cakitaṃ muhur muhuḥ || 2 || (fol. 1v1–4)


«End»

proktaṃ dharmavinirṇaye tadakhilaṃ spaṣṭaṃ parajñaptaye |

saṃdiṣṭaṃ gurudatta⌠⌠maṃtra⌡⌡kṛpayā śārdūlavikrīḍitaṃ

tena śrīravimaṃḍalāṃtaragato nārāyaṇaḥ prīyatāṃ || (fol. 86v1–3)


«Colophon»

iti śrīmadbhāradvājamahādevabhaṭṭātmajasakalavidyānidhānaśrīdivākarakṛta ācārārārkeṣṭamabhāgakṛtyaṃ ||

yatpādapaṃkajaparāgapavitradeho

hitvā kalevaramidaṃ kila dārṣadaṃ sā |

jagrāha gautamavadhūḥ kamanīyarūpaṃ

taṃ jānakīpatim ajaṃ manasā smarāmi || ||

oṃ tat sat brahmārpaṇaṃ kṛtvā ||

yajjanmabhiḥ prabhṛtimohavaśaṃ gatena

nānāparādhaśatam ācaritaṃ mayā te |

aṃtarbahiś ca sakalaṃ tava paśyato hi

kṣaṃtu⌠ṃ⌡ tvam arhasi hare karuṇāvaśena ||

sa tvaṃ prasīda bhagavan kuru mapyanāthe (!)

viṣṇo kṛpāṃ paramakāruṇikaḥ kila tvaṃ |

saṃsārasāgaranimagnamanaṃtadīnam uddhartum a (fol. 86v3–10)
