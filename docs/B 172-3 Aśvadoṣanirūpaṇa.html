Excerpts

Beginning

❖ satraṃyā doṣana hlāya ||

kakudāvartta dhāyā |

ḍusaṃ phulyaṃ mākva || hnasalivatevu deho khasyavuḍvāvahmalā aṃguli ṭvaṃhā cahokha | calasayāṅaṃ thyaṃvu, ṅānaphava ||

guḍijhāmarasathyaṃ | vayara sathyaṃ | vani vaṃcu, dvala 2 dhāsyaṃ cānu, kiliṭāyāthyaṃ | keśāntasa | mastakasāthva, carmma śṛṃgī dhāya | (fol. r1–3)

End

vyāghrābha, dhūcchavaḍithyaṃ || dvikarrṇḍīhna savāva ||

dvikhurī, ṭāyavāva || stanīghoḍyā dudu dava || kūku ḍhusī ||

tittiri saṃnibha, titihuḍitthyaṃ cyāka || vānarākṣa, mākaḍamikhā | viḍālākṣa, bhaṭi mikhā || dvipadraṃṣṭra vāuḍvaṃ mahāva || ||(fol.1v1–4)
