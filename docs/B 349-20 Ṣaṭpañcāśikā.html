❖ svasti śrīmaṃgalamūrttaya (!) namaḥ ||    ||

padārtha(!) pṛthu yaśasā ||    || (fol. 1v1–2)

asyārtha (!) ||

varāhamihi(3)rakā chorā pṛthuyaśanāmā (!) inale śrīsūryyamahā avadhāna garīkana namaskāra(4) garyā kyāna bhanyā praśna garnāṇimitya (!) āunyā arulokakā kāraṇale praśna(5)ko artha dṛḍhagarṇā nimityale yo graṃtha banāyā pheri pṛthuyaśasā (!) kasṭā (!) thiyā bhanyā lokaviṣe mahājaśavantaḥ (!) || 1 ||(fol. 1v2–5)

End of the root text
