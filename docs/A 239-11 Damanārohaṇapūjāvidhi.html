❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīguruve(!) ||


atha damanārohaṇavidhi (!) likhyate ||


prathama sindhrana lāyalaye vidhithyaṃ vasape mālako || thvanali saṃkhyāhutito vidhithyaṃ kālāgni yāye || ||

tato devārccana || kalaśa(++)ādina saudhaiyāṅgana pūjayitvā dhūpa dīpa naivedya āyastotra || || paṭavāsana

svastika coyā yā devana sijaralaṃ ḍāsa jima khutāvāsaranaṃ lepalapaṃṭe || raktacandana karppūla kasturi
