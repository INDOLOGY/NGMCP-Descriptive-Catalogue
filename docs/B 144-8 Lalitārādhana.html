Excerpts

Beginning

oṁ mahācaṇḍikāyai namaḥ ||

maulau candramaṇiḥ śrutau phaṇimaṇiḥ koṇe maṇiḥ †svaddhanī†

hāre kūrmmamaṇiḥ kare ratnamaṇiḥ kaṭyāṃ †mṛgośo†maṇiḥ <ref name="ftn1">Pāda b is unmetrical.</ref>

vastre hastimaṇir ggale viṣamaṇis trailokyarakṣāmaṇiḥ

devyā devaśikhāmaṇir vijayate bhaktaikacintāmaṇiḥ ||

kāraṇaṃ jagatāṃ natvā śrīguruṃ nāsti vistarāt ||

lalitārādhanaṃ vakṣye yoginīhṛdayāśrayāt ||     ||

śrī-īśvara uvāca ||

pūjāsaṃketam adhunā kathayāmi tavānaghe ||

yasya vijñānamātreṇa jīvanmuktaḥ pramodate ||

ityādi tatra śrīmān sādhakevaryya<ref name="ftn2">For sādhakavaryyaḥ</ref> brāhme muhu(!)[r]tte cotthāya buddhyāvaśyakaṃ kṛtvā hastau pādau prakṣālyācamya dhaute vāsasī paridhāyācamya svāsanam āstīryya prāṅmukhodaṅmukho vā upaviśya śrīguruṃ dhyāyet ||    || (exp. 3t1–3b1)

End

aiṁ klīṁ hrīṁ tripurabhagavatī<ref name="ftn3">Should be dative case ending.</ref> svāhā || sundarīkullukāmaṃtrasya ānanda ṛṣiḥ gāyatrī chandaḥ kullukātripurasundarīdevatā aiṁ bījaṃ hrīṁ śakti[ḥ] klīṁ kīlakaṃ || ṣaḍaṃgaṃ || aiṁ klīṁ hrīṁ dvivāreṇa || atha setu[ḥ] || viprasya oṁ || atha mahāsatu hrīṁ || atha jihvāśodhanamaṃtra[ḥ] || śrīṁ oṁ śrīṁ oṁ śrī oṁ 10 vāṃraṃ || iti sundarīpakṣe || atha bharavīpakṣe || oṁ hmauṁ oṁ 10 vāṃraṃ iti bhairavīpakṣe mukhaśodhanamaṃtra[ḥ] || (exp. 46b3–7)
