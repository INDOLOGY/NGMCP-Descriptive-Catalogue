Excerpts

«Beginning»


ṅukāva misā ku duyāva, pada.. khvāla.. biyāva syāṅāva rājāna kvāhā vayāva dhālaṃ he pāpiṣṭa(2) rājā je cha masayā rājā ||


yeṃ rājyānmāda(!) vasādaśeṣaviduṣāṃ

murddhāvipisyameyatrāsīdevahala(3)

mātaha dhunāsto... kusānami... ... da

kulakalaṃkamarinnanomurayasya latatāva

śrīśivagupta rāli(4)te tanurddhāri nāhaṃ ... ||

thvayā artha || ||

samasta paṇḍitayā cosa coṅahma je chana rājyāyā urtta(5)magvali ro muraya .. chana avahādā yātaṃ || || (fol. 109r1-5/exp. 22)



«End»


cānakyana maṃntrī rākṣasa bese vayāva kusumāpua vaṅāva candragupta(2)yāta rājyābhiseṣa dhaka biraṃ || thvanaṃli cānakyana

cintalaparaṃ mantri rākṣasa tavadhāṅa buddhiva(3)ntava viroḍha yāṅāva candragupta gathe rājā yuyuva je brāhmana tapaśyā tolatāva

rājika(4)rccarccā yāṅāva gathe cona āvabha yāya mantri rākṣasava malayaketuva ana snehanaṃ mantri yāṅā(5)va talo maloyaketu

rākṣasava phāyāva rākṣasa mantriva thithiṃ bhinakāva tayāva upāya yāyamā(6)la dhakaṃ aneka yatna yātaṃ || || (fol.

116v1-6/exp. 30)



«Colophon»


iti sakatālamaṃntrikākathā samāptaṃ || ||

śubham astu || || (fol. 116v7/exp. 30)

sakataravikatāra vairocana mundrā ||
