Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || oṁ ||

yasyāṅkurāgamavidaḥ paripūrṇaśakter
aṃśe kiyaty apiniviṣḥam amuṃ prapaṃcaṃ ||
tasmai tamālarucibhāsurakaṃdharāya
nārāyaṇasahacarāya nama[ḥ] śivāya || 1 ||

ārkeṇa kṛṣṭadhanurāhitamohanāstraṃ
mārān nirīkṣya madanaṃ kupitasya kiṃcit ||
śaṃbhoḥ samādhisamaye vikasatkṛśānu
jvālākalāpajaṭilaṃ [[vadanaṃ]] smarāmi || 2 || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

caturthapādena sauryaṃ caruṃ nirvaped ityādiṣu anupadiṣṭadharmeṣu pratyakṣavacanasya nāmnaś cātideśikasyābhāve [ʼ]pi kutaś cid vaidikāt karmaṇo dharmapāpakaṃ vacanam astīty ānumāniakavacanarūpeṇa codakenātideśaḥ sāmānyato nirūpitaḥ aṣṭame tu kasya (fol. 93v9–11)
