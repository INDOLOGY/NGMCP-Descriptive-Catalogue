Beginning

namaḥ śrīkālikāyai || ||

kailāśaśikhare ramye devadevaṃ jagatprabhuṃ ||

kena buddhyā kālavaśyaṃ brūhi me parameśvara || 1 ||

īśvara uvāca ||

sādhu pṛṣṭaṃ tvayā skaṃda gopyād gopyataraṃ tvidaṃ ||

mama priyatvāt ṣaḍvaktra vaktavyaṃ tūttamottamaṃ || 2 ||

kavacaṃ kālikāyās tu kālavaṃcanam uttamaṃ ||

kālikākālikānāmapaṭhatāṃ sarvadā jayaḥ || 3 || (fol. 31v6–32r3, exp. 32t–b)
