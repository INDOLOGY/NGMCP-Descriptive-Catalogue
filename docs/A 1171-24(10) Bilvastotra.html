Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || atha bilvāṣṭakam ||
bilvapatraiḥ praśastaiś capuṣpaiś ca tulasi(!)dalaiḥ ||
tilākṣatai(!) yajed deva(!) jīvanmukto na saṃśayaḥ || 1 ||
tridalaṃ triguṇākāraṃ trinetraṃ ca tridhāyudham ||
trijanmapāpasaṃhāraṃ ekabilvaṃ śivārpaṇaṃ || 2 ||
triśākhair bilvapatraiś ca achidraiḥ komalaiḥ śubhaiḥ ||
tava pūjāṃ kariṣyāmi ekapatraṃ śivārpaṇam || 3 ||
akhaṇḍair bilvapatraiś ca pūjitaṃ nandiśaṅkaram ||
mucyate sarvapāpebhya ekabilvaṃºº || 4 ||
jñānamūrtiṃ śivaṃ devaṃ pitāmahasudurlabham ||
yaśaḥśrīprāptikāmena ekabiºº || 5 || (fol. 15v17–1r6)

End

kṣīrasāgarasaṃbhūtaṃ bilvapāpapraṇāśanam ||
īśvarārthaṃ vicinvāmi(!) ekabilvaṃºº || 16 ||
ekena bilvapatreṇa śivaliṅgārcanaṃ kṛtam ||
trailokyaṃ tasya vaśyaṃ ca bhaven nāsty atra saṃśayaḥ || 17 || (fol. 1r19–1v3)

iti śrīpadmapurāṇe bilvastotraṃ śubham ||
