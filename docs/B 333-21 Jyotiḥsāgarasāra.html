Manuscript Features

On the exposure 2 is written:

atha tālaphalākāro kaṇṭakair bahubhiś cito |
dīrghadaṇḍa sarvalohair bhuśuḍyastraṃ (!) nigadyate ||
… daśame vaidhṛti proktā ravipāto vidhīyate ||

After the colophon is available undefined text related to jautiṣa:

❖ śrī sūryāya namaḥ |
kalpādyabdagaṇaḥ prabhākara12hata(!) caitrādimāsair yutas
triṣṭhaḥ khādri70hṛtāptayukasura33 hṛtair labdhādhimāsair yutaḥ|
… krameṇaiteṣāṃ bījāny ucyaṃte || nayana-

Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

jagadīśaṃ raviṃ natvā lokānugrahakāriṇaṃ |
jyotiḥsāgarasāro yaṃ mathureśe(2)na tanyate ||

raviḥ somo maṇgalaś ca budho jīvaḥ sitaḥ śaniḥ |
eteṣāṃ nāmato vārāḥ saptaiva kathitāḥ (3) punaḥ ||

śukrendubudhajīvānāṃ vārāḥ sarvvatra śobhanāḥ |
bhānubhūsutamandānāṃ śubhakarmmasu keṣv api || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

atha gurucakraṃ ||

śīrṣe catvāri rājyaṃ jaladhir api kare dakṣiṇe cāpi saukhyaṃ
ka(5)ṇṭhe caiva vibhūtiṃ madanaśarasitaṃ vakṣasi prītisaṃghān |
pādasthe ṣṭsu pīḍāṃ punar api jaladhir vāmahaste (6) ca mṛtyur
netrai trīṇi padasthāḥ sukham atha nijabhe vākyataḥ saṃkramasthāḥ ||

iti gurusaṃcāraphalaṃ || (fol. 31v4–6)

Colophon

iti jyotiḥsāgararasāre pañcamo dhyāyaḥ ||
iti Mathureśabhāṭṭācāryaviracite jyotiḥṣāgarasāraḥ samāptaḥ ||    || śubham astu ||
samvat 836 jyeṣṭhakṛṣṇa 10 likhitam idaṃ pustakaṃ vipra śrībrahmadevaśarmmaṇā ||    || śubhaṃ ||    || (fol. 43v6)
