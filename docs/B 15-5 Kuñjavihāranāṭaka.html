Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṁ namas tasyai || śrīgurubhyo namaḥ ||


paradevatāyai namaḥ ||

sāvaraṇārddhanārīśvarīśvaranṛtyānandasvarūpebhyo namaḥ ||

śrīkuñjavihāranāmanāṭakaṃ likhyate ||

prathamaṃ nāndīgītaṃ || rājavijaya || jani ||

analas ruja śaśitinihnu nayana jana abhimatade ayācahuvadane ||

varābhaya śūla hātha ḍamaru vajāve tāhi śivaparasāda saba sukha pāve || (fol. 1v1–3)


End

jarāmaraṇa jamarāja parābhavacintaha isave upāya,

na rahe ajñāna mahātama āratite jahi maṇgalapāya ||

māyāpāsapasāradedekhi kahu dinadine upajutarāse

gurugame bujhi nṛpati jagajotimala śivaka bhagatipae sāsa || luja || (fol. 11r3–11v1)


Colophon

iti kuñjavihāranāmanāṭakaṃ saṃpūrṇṇaṃ || || (fol. 11v1)
