nyāyāṃbudhīkṛtasetuṃ

hetuṃ śrīrāmam akhilasaṃpatteḥ |

tātaṃ tribhuvanagītaṃ

tarkālaṃkāram ādarān natvā ||

(2) śrīmatā mathurānāthatarkavāgīśadhīmatā |

viśadīkṛtya darśyante dvitīyamaṇiphakkikāḥ ||

ānvikṣikīpaṇḍitamaṇḍalīṣu (!)

yadyapi idaṃ bahuṣu [[bahubhir]] bahudhā ca[[rvi]]taṃ [[tat]] jñāyate kaiścit sāmānya(4)to hetvābhāsāntaṃ, tathāpi tannānā⟪jñāna⟫[[sthāna]]vitatam aśeṣa[[prakāśakaṃ]] bahutara[[ku]]tarkasambalitam asāṃpradāyikaṃ [[cāto]] vyāmohāyaiva kevalaṃ sarveṣāṃ bhavatīti sa(5)rvā[[rthajighṛkṣayā sattarkam āmūla]]vyākhyānāya [[vaiśadyāya ca]] mamāpi paraṃ nibaṃdhaḥ | (fol. 1v1–5)

End

api ceti || || śaṃkātirikteti || ||

na cāsmanmate sādhyāprasiddhiḥ pararītyaiva paraṃ praty abhidhānāt svamate tu śaṃkāriktabahirbhāvena sādhyaṃ bodhyaṃ (10) na sādhakaṃ na siddhaṃ vyāptasyāvyāpyatve niścitasya pakṣadharmatve niścayena tatreti vyabhicārasaṃśayasāmagrīsatvena [na] tatra vyāptiniścaya ity arthaḥ || (fol. 91v9–10)

Colophon

iti tarkarahasyaṃ saṃpūrṇatām agāt || || (fol. 91v10)
