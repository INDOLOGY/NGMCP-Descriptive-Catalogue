Manuscript Features

There is a prakīrṇa folio of a sanskrit nāṭaka. The story is related with the characters of Sudhanvā, Prabhāvatī, Rukmiṇī and Śrīkṛṣṇa. Full text of the folio is given at the end after colophon.
