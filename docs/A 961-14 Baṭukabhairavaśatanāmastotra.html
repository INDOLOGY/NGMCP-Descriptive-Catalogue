Excerpts

Beginning

śrīvaṭukabhairavāya namaḥ ||


śrībhairava uvāca ||


śṛṇu devi pravakṣāmi bhairavasya mahātmanaḥ ||

baṭūkasya śataṃ nāma aṣṭottaram anuttamam || 1 ||


āpaduddhārakaṃ tv edaṃ(!) sarvakṣatranivarhaṇam ||

apamṛtyuharaṃ divyam ādhivyādhivināśanam || 2 ||


viśeṣā(!)vasya jananaṃ vivāde vijayapradam ||

mārībhaye caurabhaye vātāgnijalaje bhaye || 3 ||


sakṛtpaṭḥanamātreṇa sarvopadravanāśanam ||

dhanasaṃpattijananaṃ putrapautravivarddhanam || 4 || (fol. 1v1–4)


End

āpaduddhārajanakaṃ vaṭukasya prakīrtitam ||

etac ca śṛṇuyād brūyāj ji(!)raved vāsthāpayed gṛhe || 33


dhārayed vā gale vāhau tasya sarvasamṛdhyate ||

na tasya duritaṃ kiṃcin na cauranṛpajaṃ bhayam || 34 ||


na cālyamṛtyudoḥ satyo(!) ḍākinībhyo bhayaṃ na hi ||

kuṣmāḍṇādigrahādibhyo nāpaṛtyur na ca jvarāḥ || 35 ||


māsam ekaṃ trisaṃdhyāṃ ca śucir bhūtvā paṭḥen naraḥ ||

sarvadāridryanirmukto nidhiṃ paśyati bhūtale || 36 ||


māsadvayam adhīyāś ca pādukāsiddhim āpnuyāt ||

añjanaṃ guṭhikāsiddhaṃ dhātuvāḍaṃ rasāyanam || 37 ||


sārasvataṃ ca vetālaṃ vāhanaṃ vīrasādhanam ||

kāryasiddhir maṃtrasidhir maṃtraṃ caiva samīhitam || 38 ||


varṣaṃ māsaṃ ca adhiyāt prāpnuyāt sādhakottamaḥ ||

iti te kathitaṃ devi sārāt sārataraṃ param || 39 ||


kālasaṃkarṣiṇītaṃtre kalikalmaṣanāśanam ||

[[sarvavighnaharaś caiva vāñcitārthasya dāyakaḥ || 4]] (fol. 3r4–3v4)
