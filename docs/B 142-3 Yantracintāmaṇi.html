Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ gaṇesāya ||    ||

yaṃ dhyāyanti surā[surā]ś ca nikhilā yakṣā[ḥ] piśācoragāḥ
rājaṃnaś(!) ca tathā muni[[ndrani]]vahā sarvvārthadaṃ siddhaya(!) |
bhaktānā[ṃ] varahāṃ(!)bhayapradakaraṃ pāśāṃkuśālaṃkṛtaṃ
tvaṃ carvvā(!)maravīkṣyamānam aniśaṃ so haṃ śraya(!) śaṃkaraṃ || 1 ||

babhāra duḥsthān munipuṃgavān yo rudhyāya(!) mantraiś ca durāsahojāḥ |
jājvalyamāno divi dṛśyate yaḥ sa bhāgamogemā(!) maccharaṇya[ḥ] || 2 || (fol. 1v1–2)

End

iti śāntādhikāra(!) duṣṭasatvamocanaṃ nāma vi[ṃ]śatimayantraṃ ||    ||

cintāmaṇau yantravare suyantra(!)
śrīcandracūtasya mukhād vinigate |
tasmin supīṭhī kila saptamīmāṃ
cakāra dāmodaravipravaryaḥ ||    ||

śrīśāntikādhikāra .. pūrṇṇaḥ kṛtaḥ saptamaḥ sayaṃtra |
sukhāni bhātāni dāmodarapaditena ||    ||

iti yantracintāmaṇau nāmni mahākalpe pratyakṣasiddhiprade umāmaheśvarasaṃvādeddhṛte(!) samāpto yaṃ ||    ||

oṃ namaḥ śrī vande devyai ||    ||

āpādamastakanibaddhajalaḥ kṣaṇena
mokṣaṃ prayoti(!) nītatraiś(!) ca suveṣṭitāṃga(!)
yasyājñayā jagati devamanuṣyasiddhās
tāṃ bhaktiyuktamanasā praṇato smi va- (fol. 31v6–10)

Sub-colophon

iti śrīcintāmaṇimahākalpe pratyakṣasiddhiprade umāmaheśvarasamvāde dāmodalapaṇḍitoddhṛta prathamapiṭhikāpaṭalaḥ || 1 || (fol. 2v3–4)

iti caturthapīṭhikāyāṃ vaśīkaranādhikāre rājākopaśamanaṃ bījasaṃpuṭaṃ nāma dvitīyayantraṃ || (fol. 3r8–9)

iti śrīvaśyādhikāre ca[tu]rthapīṭhikāyāṃ jāvajjīvaṃsvāmivaśya nāma tṛtīyayantraṃ || (fol. 3v5–6)

iti śrīyantracintāmaṇau nāmni mahākalpa(!) pratyakṣasiddhiprade umāmaheśvarasaṃvāde dāmodalapaṇḍitoddhṛte dvitiya(!)pīṭhikāpaṭalaḥ || 2 || (fol. 4r9–v1)

iti śrīvaśyādhikāre rājamohanaṃ nāma caturthayantra[ṃ] || (fol. 6r8)
