Excerpts

«Beginning of the root text:»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

saṃketo lakṣaṇā cārthe padavṛttiḥ vṛttyā padapratipādya eva padārtha ity abhidhīyate, idaṃ padam imam arthaṃ bodhayatv iti asmāc chabdād ayam artho boddhavya iti vecchāsaṃketarūpā vṛttiḥ tatrādhunikasaṃketaḥ paribhāṣā tayā cārthabodhakaṃ padaṃ pāribhāṣikam, (fol. 1v1–3)

«Beginning of the commentary:»

padavṛttir ity atra padapadopādānaṃ tattatpadanirūpitatvalābhā(yā)nyathā pade saṃketo ghaṭapadavṛttiḥ syāt evam evottaratrāpi padopādānaṃ, vṛttyeti, vṛttiprayojyatve sati padaprayojyā yā pratipattis tadviṣaya ity arthaḥ (fol. 1v-upper margin)

«End of the root text:»

na tu saṃbandhāntaram iti syāt,

tadādipadāt samavāyam aviṣayīkṛtyāpi saṃbaṃdhāntareṇa gotvādiviṣayakabodhodayād iti cet evaṃ sati jñānaśaktir evādaraṇīyā

tathā ca gavādipadasya samavāyena gotvādiprakārakajñāna eva śakter upagamāt tatoʼbhrāntānāṃ saṃbaṃdhāntareṇa na tatprakārakadhīs tatsaṃbaṃdhena tatprakārakajñānaśaktigrahasyaiva tādṛśabodhahetutvād iti śivam, (fol. 45r3–7)

«End of the commentary:»

gavādipadasyeti jātivācakapadasyety arthas tena tadāditas tathānanubhave[ʼ]pi na kṣatiḥ jñānaśaktipakṣo[ʼ]gre mūla eva prapaṃcito dūṣitaś ceti nātra punar doṣodbhāvanā kṛtā vyākhyātaṃ ca tatra tatrāsmābhir itīhopararmyate śivam astu (fol. 48r4–6)

«Colophon of the root text:»

iti śrīgadādharabhaṭṭācāryyaviracitaḥ śaktivādaḥ || (fol. 45r7)

«Colophon of the commentary:»

iti sukṛtidattakṛtā śaktivādaṭī(!)ppaṇī (fol. 48r6)
