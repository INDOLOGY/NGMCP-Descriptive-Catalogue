Beginning

Śrīśivāya namaḥ || ❁ ||

ṛṣabha uvāca || ❁ ||

namaskṛtya mahādevaṃ visvavyāpinam īśvaram ||
vakṣye śivamayaṃ varma sarvarakṣākaran nṛṇām || 1 ||

śucaudeśe samāsīno yathāvat kalpitāsanaḥ ||
jitendriyo jitaprāṇaś cintayec chivam avyayam || 2 || (fol. 1v1–3)

Bhasmadhāraṇasāmarthyāc chatra(!)sainyaṃ vijeṣyasi ||

iti bhadrāyuṣe samyag yu(!)paśāsya samātṛkaṃ ||
