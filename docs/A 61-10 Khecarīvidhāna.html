Excerpts

«Beginning:»'


oṃ namaḥ


śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||


oṃ namaḥ śivāya ||


atha devīṃ pravakṣyāmi vidyāṃ khecarisaṃ jñitāṃ ||


yayā vijñātamātreṇa trailoke smin jarāmaraḥ 1


mṛṭyuvyādhijarāgrastaṃ dṛṣṭvā viśvaṃ imaṃ priye


buddhiṃ dṛḍhatarāṃ kṛtvā khecarīṃ tu samāśrayet 2


jarāmṛtyugaraghnī yā khecarī vetti bhūtale


graṃthataś cārthataś caiva tadābhyāsaprayogataḥ 3


tāṃ sarvabhāvena guruṃ matvā tāṇ ca samāśrayet


durlabhā khecarīmudrā tamadbhāsāś ca durlabhā 4 (fol. 1–6)



«End:»


nirguṃṭhāpatram ekaikaṃ trikālaṃ paribhakṣayet


dvādaśābdād bhaved devi jarāmaraṇavarjjitaḥ 10


nirguṃṭhyānalanirmuṃḍī sāmymaṃ saṃsādhayed rajaḥ


śarkarāghṛtamadhvarkaṃ vatsarā(t) palitāpahaṃ 11


ṣaṇmāṣagaṃdhakaṃ svarṇaṃ tilakaṃ rudralocanaṃ


madhuratrayutaṃ cūrṇam ajarāmarapadapradaṃ 12


rasaśālmaliniryāsagaṃdhakamadhuratrayaiḥ


bhakṣayet prātar utthāya ṣaṇmāṣād ajarāmaraḥ 13 || || (fol. 20v1–6)



«Colophon:)»


iti śrīmahā ādināthena nirūpite mahākālayogaśāstre khecaryāṃ vidyāyām auṣadhayogo nāma


caturthaḥ paṭalaḥ 4 ||


īśvara uvāca śrīkhecarī samarpaṇa sa tu khecarīmantragraṃthokta some mātuvasaṃvarṇam ity ādi


gamanasaphalaṃ ṣaḍākṣaraṃ(!) khecarī bījaṃ hrīkārā khearīpaṭale pa (fol. 20v6–9)
