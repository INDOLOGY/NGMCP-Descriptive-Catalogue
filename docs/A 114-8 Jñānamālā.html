Manuscript Features

The available folios are 2–50, 53, 55–64, 66–70, 74–75, 77 and 79.

The sides of fols. 50, 53, 67, 68, 69, 74, 75, 77 and 89 are broken. In some of these folios the letters are broken. Letters are rubbed away on fol. 36v.

There are two exposures of fols. 2r and 50v–51r (one exposure of fol. 2r precedes the initial index card).

maile sohi kuro nayā garidiñāṃ .. .. li bhāṣāmāhā,
