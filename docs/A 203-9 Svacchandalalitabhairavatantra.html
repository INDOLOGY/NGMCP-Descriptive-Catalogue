Excerpts

Beginning

|| || śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||


oṁ namo mahābhairavāyai ||


kailāsasikharāsīnaṃ bhairavaṃ vigatāmayaṃ ||

caṃḍanandimahākālagaṇeśavṛṣabhṛṃgibhiḥ ||


kumārendrayamādityabrahmaviṣṇupuraḥsaraiḥ ||

stūyate paramātmānaṃ gaṇamātṛviśeṣataḥ ||


sṛṣṭisaṃhārakarttāraṃ sthitivilayakāraṇaṃ ||

anugrahakaraṃ devaṃ praṇatāṛttvināśanaṃ || (fol. 1v1–3)


End

anye taṃtra[ṃ] samutthāya sādhayanti na saṃśayaḥ ||

evaṃ saṃkṣepataḥ proktaḥ prerakan tu varānane ||


satato ʼbhyāsayogena dadanti <ref>apāṇinīyaprayoga</ref> carukaṃ svayaṃ ||

yasya saṃprāsanā devi vīreśasadṛso bhavet ||


tasyā dhyānā[r]ccanāhomaṃ japaṃ ca varavarṇini ||

karttavyaṃ †bhātitātmena† tataḥ siddhyanti mantriṇaḥ || (fol. 171r3–5)


<references/>


Colophon

iti svacchandalalitabhairave mahātantre mantrapīṭhe chosmādhikāroḥ(!) paṃcadaśapa⟨ta⟩[ṭa]laḥ || samāptaḥ || 15 || ❁ ||


yādṛśaṃ pustakaṃ dṛṣṭvā tādṛśaṃ likhitaṃ mayā

yadi śuddham aśuddhaṃ vā mama doṣo na dīyate || ❁ || (fol. 171r5–7)
