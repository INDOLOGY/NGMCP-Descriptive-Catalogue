Excerpts

Beginning

śrīnamaḥ śītāṃśave || ||

praṇamya śrīśaśimauliṃ taṃ devadānavavaṃditaṃ ||
pūrṇimāvratarājasya pravakṣyāmi praśaṃsanaṃ ||
aṣṭāśīti mahāviprai(!) pṛchamāno(!) 'gnidevatā ||
bhagavan śrībṛhadbhāno kathaṃ somo 'bhavat purā || ||

agnir uvāca ||

śrūyatāṃ muniśārdūla somotpattiṃ ca pāvanaṃ ||
vyākhyātaṃ vistaraṃ ceṃdor vacāṃsi sāmprataṃ dvijāḥ ||
mahāpralayasaṃbhūte naṣṭe sthāvarajaṃgame ||
caturāśīti (la)kṣāṇāṃ yogīnāṃ jīvabījakaṃ ||
saṃhṛtyo(!) parirabhyātha prakṛtitvam upāgamat |
sā devī prakṛtir māyā purāṇeṣv api gīyate ||
tannābhāvārpitaṃ bījam īśānecchāprabhāvataḥ |
tato yajñe jagatsraṣṭā divyapadmodarasthitaḥ || (fol. 1v1-6)


End

bhaginībhiḥ saha strībhir devastrībhiḥ samāhitāḥ |
mārgaśīrṣāditaḥ kṛtvā paurṇamāsīvrataṃ śubhaṃ |
varṣaike sati saṃpūrṇe kārttikīpūrṇimātithau |
udyāpanavidhānena kṛtvā pūrṇaphalaṃ labhet |
amāvāsyāṃ tathā naṣṭe caṃdraḥ punar ajāyate(!) |
patnī ‥tavratenaiva kṣayo 'pi varddhatāṃ gataḥ |
kṣayavṛddhisvarūpo 'pi bhadram eveti vādibhiḥ |
devair brahmādibhir nityaṃ caṃdragatyānumohitaḥ |
kṣayavṛddhiś ca jagataḥ kālajñānaṃ bhavatv iti |
etac candrasya māhātmyaṃ paurṇamāsīvratasya ca ||
vācayec chṛṇuyād vāpi mucyate sarvapātakaiḥ || (fol. 6r5-6v3)


Colophon

ity āgneyapurāṇe paurṇamāsīvratamāhātmyaṃ samāptam || || mārgaśīrṣidaśamyām alekhi || bhaṭṭasyedaṃ pustakam iti || śrīmadvahnipurāṇato vratam idaṃ kurvaṃtu bhaktā janāś cāṃdrīyaṃ suhitaṃ manorathakara⁅ṇī⁆pūrṇimāyāṃ sadā sadbhaktārtham alekhi vai parahitaṃ svārthaṃ ca bhaṭṭena sadvidyāyurgguṇino gadaṃtu viduṣā cintākhyakanādarān(?) (fol. 6v3-6v5)
