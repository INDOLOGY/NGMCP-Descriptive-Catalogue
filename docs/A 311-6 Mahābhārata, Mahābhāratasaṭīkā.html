«Beginning of the root text:»


|| śrīgaṇēśāya namaḥ ||


nārāyaṇaṃ namaskṛtya naraṃ caiva narottamaṃ ||

devīṃ sarasvatīṃ caiva tato jayam udīrayet || 1 ||


janamejaya uvāca ||


gate bhagavati vyāse dhṛtarāṣtro mahipatiḥ ||

kim aceṣṭata viprarṣe tan me vyākhyātum arhasi || 1 || (fol. 1v2–3)


«Beginning of the commentary:»


|| śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||


gate bhagavati vyāse ity ādi strīparve ||


tatra prathamo dhyāyo vyākhyātaprāyaḥ gate || 1 || 3 || (fol. 1v1)


«End»


«End of the root text:»
