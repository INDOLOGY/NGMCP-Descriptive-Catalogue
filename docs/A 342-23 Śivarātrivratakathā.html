This text contains some minor texts other than those of above MTM

❖ Oṃ namaḥ || vasatana here | dina ādityavārana herasā | tā matuyivava |

(23v:1) śvamavārana | herasā | kekakāka daiva | aṃgāravārana | herasā

mina (2) nayiva ||

  1. Three lines containing stuti to Harihara (23v:2-4) in Sanskrit.
  2. Six lines in bigger letter containing quotes (uvāca) of Yudhisthira (23v:5-7 and 24v:1-3) in Sanskrit.
  3. Five lines containing a stuti of Bhagavatī (24v:4-8) in Sanskrit.
  4. Name of deities in Sanskrit of each lunar day of the waxing fortnight as

pratipate | catumikha

dutiyā | vidhātā [The last page 27v ]

Publications in Newari similar to this story. ( Kayastha, 1977 and 2000)
