Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśagurudevatābhyo namaḥ ||    || śrīvāgīśvaryai namaḥ ||    ||

gajamukham amarapravaraṃ
siddhivaraṃ vidhnahartāraṃ ||
gurum avaga(2)manayanapradam
iṣṭakarīm iṣṭadevatāṃ vande ||

āyurvedāgamanaṃ
krameṇa yenā bhavadbhūmau |
prathamaṃ likhāmi tam
ahaṃ nānā taṃ(3)trāṇi saṃdṛśya |

āyurvedasya lakṣaṇaṃ āha |

āyur hitāhitaṃ vyādhir nidānaṃ śamanaṃ tathā |
vidyate yatra vidvadbhiḥ sa ā(4)yurveda ucyate || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

atha ba(8)lavṛddhi nidānam āha |

doṣasāmyākaraaṃ yat tu vahnisāmyakaraṃ ca yat |
dhātupuṣṭikaraṃ dravyaṃ balaṃ tad abhivarddha(9)yet |

atha balavallakṣaṇam āha |

kṛśopi balavān kāścit (!) sthūlopy alpa balo yataḥ |
tasmāc ceṣṭā paṭutve(1)na balavantaṃ vidur budhāḥ || (fol. 177v7–178r1)

Colophon

iti śrīmiśralaṃṭakanatanaya śrīman miśrabhāvaviracite bhāvaprakāśe ṣa(2)ṣṭaṃ(!) prakaraṇam sāmāptam (!) samāptoyaṃ pūrvakhaṇḍaḥ ||    ||
śubhāni bhūyāsuḥ || 1784 || 1919 || 1 || (fol. 178r1–2)
