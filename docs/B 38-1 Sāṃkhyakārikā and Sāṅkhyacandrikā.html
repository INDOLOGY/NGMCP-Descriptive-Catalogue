Manuscript Features

The date of copying is mentioned as a part of the colophon with erroneous reading, manvakendumita, which must have been intended manvagendumite (ŚS 1714).


Excerpts

«Beginning of the root text:»

śrīḥ

duḥkhatrayābhidhātāj jijñāsā tadapaghātake hetau || (fol. 1v5)

dṛṣṭe sāpārthā cen naikāntatotyantatā(!) bhāvāt || 1 || (fol. 2r5)


«Beginning of the commentary:»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ |

śrīrāmagovindasutīrthapādakṛpāviśveśād upalabhya bodham ||

śrīvāsudevād adhigatya sarvaśāstrāṇi vaktuṃ kim api spṛhāṇaḥ || 1 ||

prakṛtiṃ puruṣaṃ caiva natvācāryān gurūṃs tathā ||

nārāyaṇaḥ sākhyamūle tanute sākhya(!)caṃdrikāṃ(!) || 2 || (fol. 1v1–2)


«End of the root text:»

saptatyāṃ kila ye rthās te ’thāḥ kṛtsna ṣaṣṭitaṃtrasya ||

ākhyāyikāvirahitāḥ paravādavivarjitāś cāpi || 72 || (fol. 28r5)


«End of the commentary:»

karaṇānām asāmarthyam aṣṭāviṃśatidhā matā

iti ṣaṣṭiḥ padārthānām aṣṭābhiḥ saha siddhibhir iti ||

tathā caitat ṣaṣṭipadārthavikyenām nedaṃ prakaraṇaṃ kiṃ tu ttaṃtram(!) eveti siddham || 72 || (fol. 28r7‒9)


«Colophon of the root text:»

iti sāṃkhyacaṃdrikā simāptā || ❁||


«Colophon of the commentary:»

iti rāmagovindatīrthaśiṣyanārāyaṇatīryakṛtā sāṃkhyacaṃdrikā samāptā || ❁ ||

manva〈ke〉[ge]ndumita(!) varṣa(!)

śrāvaṇe kṛṣṇa(!) bāṇa(!) ravisaṃjñavāsare ||

sāṃkhyatatvadvijarājacandrikāṃ

lekhivān padmanidhiḥ suddīvaraḥ(!) || 1 || (fol. 28r9-10)
