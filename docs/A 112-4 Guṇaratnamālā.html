Manuscript Features

NGMPP catalogue card is not available for this reel number.

Available folios 12–33, 41, *42, 42, *43, *44 and 35–39.

Folios are not in sequential order and difficult to find out proper place after fol. 33.

The right-hand and left-hand part of the most of folio is badly damaged with the considerable loss of text that is why it is difficult to arrange the folios in sequential order.
