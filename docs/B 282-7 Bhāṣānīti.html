Beginning

–savisyaṃ tasyaṃ, śirāphura savisyaṃ śirāphura savīślāmayāsyaṃ,

rājaputratvaṃ lheḍa o otvaṃ juro, thya sayāśasa,, vānaraco gva,

thva sandedana ḍeṅa rājaputrayā ʼvvālasa, bhojinī jujuyaṃ

thva, thva savānarana, hetiyanena, bhojini ʼkhyākhyāpana punarvvārahanaṃ,

|| thvayāupākhyāna || gvachinaṃ deśayā, jyātha sikaramiyā,

mha camo, nyāsya atiśudarī, parapuruṣa o, rataju o, thva sikramiyā,

heti hetāna thvayā mha camova yā o,, veherana svayamaphayāva, kṣamha camhayā,,atikucarita juro, mārasā kudumasanādānayā hune dhāsyaṃ, vāravāraṃ hāṅāva,, svava makhu… (fol. 22v5–7)
