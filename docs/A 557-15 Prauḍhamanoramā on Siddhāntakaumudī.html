Manuscript Features

The text runs from the beginning of the work to the end of the Ajantastrīliṅga chapter.
On the right margin of fol. 102v is written 101. On fols. 108 and 109 is written respectively: 908 and 909. The handwriting changes at fol. 125r.
On fols. 1r, 111r and 164v is a stamp of Chandra Shamsher.
Fols. 76r–78v contain effaced letters in the background.

Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||    ||

dhyāyaṃ dhyāyaṃ paraṃ brahma smāraṃ smāraṃ guror giraḥ ||
siddhāṃtakaumudīvyākhyāṃ kurmmaḥ prauḍhamanoramāṃ || 1 ||

hayavaraṭ hakāropadeśaḥ aṭaśhaiṇgrahaṇeṣu (!) hakāragrahaṇārtha arheṇa apravāye(!) pīti ṇatvaṃ | devā hasaṃti | bhobhago iti ror yatvaṃ | devo hasati | haśi cety utvaṃ, lilihiḍhve lilihidhve | vibhāṣe iti vā u. (fol. 1v1–4)

End

raiśabdasya strīliṅgatāyā api smaraṇād iti bhāvaḥ tathā ca kṣīradvāmī rātye narāḥ strītyeke iti striyāṃ naustaraṇis tarir ity amaraḥ ||    || (fol. 164r3–4)

Sub-colophon

ity ajaṃtā strīliṃgāḥ || śubham (fol. 164r4–5)
