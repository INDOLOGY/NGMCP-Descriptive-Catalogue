Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namo nārāyaṇāya ||

yā sraṣṭuḥ sṛṣṭir ādyā vahati vidhihutaṃ yā havir yā ca hotrī
ye dve kālaṃ vidhattaḥ śrutiviṣayaguṇā yāsthitā vyāpya viśvaṃ |
yām āhuḥ sarvvabījaprakṛtir iti yayā prāṇinaḥ prāṇavantaḥ
pratyakṣābhiḥ prasannas tanubhir avatu va⟪ḥ⟫s tābhir aṣṭābhir īśaḥ ||

nāndyante sūtradhāraḥ alam atiprasaṅgena

nepathyābhimukham avalokya, ārye yadi nepathyavidhānam adhyavasitaṃ tad ihāgamyatāṃ, praviśya naṭī, ajja iamhi āṇavedu ajjo ko ṇioo aṇuciṭṭhiadu tti

sūtraḥ ārye rasabhāvaviśeṣadīkṣāguros tatrabhavataḥ śrīvikramādityasāhasāṅkasyābhirūpabhūyiṣṭhā pariṣat, asyāñ ca kālidāsagrathitavastunābhijñānaśakuntalena upasthātavyam asmābhiḥ tat pratipātram ādhīyatāṃ yatnaḥ (fol. 1v1–5)

End

rājā aham api yathāśakti śreyase kariṣye, mārī, vatsa kiṃ te priyam upakaromi rājā bhagavann ataḥ paraṃ priyam asti tathāpi yadi bhagavān prasannataraḥ param api karttum icchati, tad etad astu,

pravarttāṃ (!) prakṛtihitāya pārthivaḥ sarasvatī śrutimahatāṃ mahīyasāṃ |
mamāpi ca kṣapayatu nīlalohitaḥ punarbbhavaṃ parigatabhaktir ātmanā ||

mārī vatsa evam astu

iti niḥkrāntāḥ sarvve

saptamoṅkaḥ samāptaḥ || || (fol. 65r2–4)

Colophon

lasaṃ 343 budhe edine vai hāṇṇale śrīvāsude⟪va⟫venā(!)likhitaṃ || (fol 65r4–5)
