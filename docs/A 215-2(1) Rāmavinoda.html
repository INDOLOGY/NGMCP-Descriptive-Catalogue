Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || || śrīgurave namaḥ || ||

siddhibuddhidāyaka sakalagavarīputra gaṇeśa ||

vighnavidāraṇa sukhakaraṇa harṣadharī praṇameśa || 1 ||

śrīdhanvaṃtari caraṇayuga praṇamo dhari ānaṃda ||

roganasaiti sunāmate savajanako suṣakaṃda || || 2 ||

vividhaśāstradeṣīkarī sugama karauṃ adhikāra ||

rāmavinodaha graṃtha rahu sakala jīva sukhakāra || 3 || 

(fol.1v1-5)

End

atha saṃvat dohā ||

gagana pāṇi phunidīpa sasi hi maruta magasiramāsa ||

suklapakṣa tairasadinai budhavāra dina jāsa 100

maradāno aru mahābalī avaraṃga sāha niraṃda ||

tāsu rāja maiharṣa sauṃracyo śāstra ānaṃda || 1 ||

suraguṇesā cosadā jagataguru jinacaṃda ||

pravala paḍū reparagaṇodīpaiṃ jyauṃ ravicaṃda || 2 ||

uttaradisaṣu rāsāna maivannū desa pradhāna

sajala bhūmi hai sarvadā sakkī sahareṃ subhathāna || 3 ||

paradukha bhaṃjaṇakai liye kipāgraṃthasuṣakaṃda ||

ciralagaye rahi jo sadā jālaga merudi niṃdā 4

(fol.141r8-141v3)

Colophon

iti śrīpaṃḍitapadmaraṃgasiṣyarāmacaṃdreṇa viracitaṃ śrīrāmavinode ||...

saṃvat 1875 sāla miti māgha vadi 2 budhavāsare liṣitaṃ śubham ||

(fol. 141v4-142r7)
