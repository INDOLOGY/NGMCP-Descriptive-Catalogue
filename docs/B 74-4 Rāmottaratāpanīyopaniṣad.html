Excerpts

Beginning of the root text

❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || || atha parārdhaṃ || ||

nirguṇāsikarītvādau saguṇopāstir īritā ||

na tasyāś ca maṃtrasādhyatvād vihitas tajjayaḥ (2) pura || (!)

mamtrārthas tu dvitīyasyāṃ tṛtīyopaniṣady api ||

turyāyāṃ kathitaṃ yaṃtraṃ paṃcamyāṃ tu prapūjanaṃ ||

tad vyākhyānān mayā rāmaḥ pūjitaḥ prī(3)yatāṃ sadā ||

ity ar(!)tharvaṇarahasye pūrvatāpanīyopaniṣat samāptā || 1 || (fol. 1v1–3)

Beginning of the commentary

satyaṃ tārakatattvam om idam aṇus thūlaṃ catuḥpādakaṃ,

jāgratsvapnasuṣu(4)ptigaṃ smṛtivaśā(!) viśvādyatītaṃ vibhuṃ |

bhūnāsāṃta upāsyamānam amaraiḥ stutyaṃ turīyātmakaṃ

bhaktānugrahavigrahaṃ janakajājānīṃ bhaje rāgha(5)vaṃ || 1 ||

(10) ānandanidhivāmnīyaṃ (!) ṭīkā prodyāyate (!) mayā |

rāmacandramaṇīṃ prāpya karttuṃ sāmrājyam advayaṃ || 5 || (fol. 1v3–5, 10)

End of the root text

yaḥ oṃ namo bhagavte vāsudevāya yo mahāviṣṇuḥ || 44 || yaḥ paramātmā | 45 | yo jñānātmā | 46 | yaḥ saccidānandaikarasātmā (3) | 47 | ityetair brahmā saptacatvāriṃśan maṃtrair nityaṃ devaṃ stauti tasya mahādevaḥ prīto bhavati svātmānaṃ darśayati | tasmād ya etair maṃtrair nityaṃ devaṃ stau(4)ti sa devaṃ paśyati somṛtattvaṃ ca gacchati | (fol. 21r2–4)

End of the commentary

viśveśapādapadmo ya makarandaughaviṃdubhiḥ |

raṃjitāyāṃ sadā kāśyāṃ ṭīkānaṃdananaiḥ(!) kṛtāḥ || 3 ||

yasyopadśadīdhityā tārakaṃ brahmakāśate |

kāśyāṃ kāśīnivāsāya namas tasmai pinkine || 4 || (fol. 21r11–21v1)

Colophon of the commentary

iti śrīmad rāmacandracaraṇānugṛhītaparamahaṃṣaparivrājakācāryānamdavanaviracitā śrīrāmottaratāpanīyopaniṣadānandanidhiṭīkā samāptāḥ (!) || || (3) || śubhaṃ bhūyāt || || saṃ 899 naṣṭaśrāvaṇakṛṣṇadaśamīśanivāsare ṭīke yaṃ samāptā || śrī śrī śrīrāmāya namaḥ || ❁ || (fol. 21v2–3)
