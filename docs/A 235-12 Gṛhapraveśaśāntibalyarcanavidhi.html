Beginning

❖ oṁ namaḥ śrī 3 mahāgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||


śrīgurupādukābhyāṃ namaḥ ||


atha gṛhapraveśapūjāvidhi[r] likhyate ||


patavāsane coya vāstusu(!)ddhāvalimālakā voya || || yajamānapuṣpabhājana(!) || adyādi || vākya(!) ||


śrīsaṃvarttāmaṇḍalānte kramapadasahitānandaśaktisubhīmā

sṛṣṭaṃ nyāyacatuṣkaṃ akla[kula]gataṃ paṃcakaṃ cāṇya[ṣa]ṭkaṃ |

catvāro(!) paṃcako [ʼ]nyaṃ punar api caturaṃ statvato(!) maṇḍaledaṃ

saṃsṛṣṭaṃ yena tasmai namataguruvaraṃ bhairavaḥ(!) śrīkujeśaṃ || || (fol. 1v1–2r2)


End

sanno [ʼ]stu dvipade nityaṃ sanno rājyas tathaiva ca |

yajamāṇasaṃbhūto [ʼ]yaṃ suputrapaśūbāndhavaḥ ||


rakṣa tvaṃ sarvvakāleṣu daśanabhairava namo [ʼ] te ||

supratiṣṭhā varadā bhavantu || ||


kumbharapūjācchoya || sarvvamaṅgalamāṃgalye śive sarvvārthasādha(!)ke |

kaumārīdevyai svasthānavāso bhavantu || ||

sākṣithāya || vākya || kṛtakarmaṇe sākṣiṇe śrīsūryyāya arthan namaḥ puṣpan namaḥ sākṣiṇe namaḥ ||

|| || (fol. 171r1–171v4)
