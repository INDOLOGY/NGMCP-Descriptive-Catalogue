śrīgaṇēśāya namaḥ ||


|| athāśvalāyano bhagavantaṃ parameṣṭhinaṃ parisametyovāca ||


adhī hi bhagavan brahmavidyāṃ variṣṭhāṃ sadā sadbhiḥ sevyamānāṃ nigūḍḥāṃ


yathādhiyā sarvapāpaṃ vyapohya parātparaṃ puruṣaṃ yāti vidvān || tasmai


sahovāca pitāmahaś ca śraddhābhaktidhyānayogādavabhinanna(!) karmaṇā na


prajayā dhanena tyāgenaike amṛtatvamānasuḥ


pareṇa nākaṃ nihitaṃ guhāyāṃ vibhrājate yadyatayo viśaṃti ||


vedāntavijñāna suniścitārthāḥ sanyāsayogādya tapaḥ śuddhasatvām || 1 ||


(exp. 1a:1-6)



«End»


tridhā roravīti maho devo martyāṃñ āviveśa || 2 ||


tridhā hitaṃ paṇibhir guhyamānaṃgavidevāso ghṛtamanna vindan ||


indra ʼeka gūṃ sūrya ʼekaṃ jajānavvenka svadhayāniṣṭatakṣuḥ ||


yo devānām prathamaṃ purastādviśvādhiyorudro maharṣiḥ ||


hiraṇyagarbhaṃ paśyatajāyamāna gūṃ sanodevasyubhayāsmṛtyā saṃpuna (exp.47b1-4)
