Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

bhāle yasya kalānidhir madhumilad bhṛṅgāvalī gaṃḍayoḥ

kaṃṭhe hir vilasaty alaṃ padayuge gīrvāṇaceto gaṇāḥ ||

brahmā pi trijagat sisṛkṣur abhajan nirbighnasaṃsiddhaye

śrīman maṃgalamūrttim ādya†malayaṃ† taṃ naumi bhaktapriyaṃ | 1 |

yan nāmākṣararaśmibhis tanugataiś cañcatkalāvām bhaved

bhaktasvāntaniśākaro hatatamās tatroccaradbhiḥ kramāt ||

natvā tāṃ bhuvaneśvarīm api gurūn siddhāntasatsundaraṃ

sujñānandakaraṃ karomi caturaṃ jñānādhirājaḥ sphuṭaṃ || 2 || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

tithyaṃtanāḍīhatabhuktaliptāḥ

pūrṇāṃgabhaktāḥ svaphalena hīnāḥ ||

rāhuḥ śaśīnau(!) sahitau bhavetāṃ

tithyaṃtakāḷe †likṣikau† tau | 13 |

svarbhānū nita śubhrabhānujabhujājjīvābhratārāhatā

trijyāptā śāśisāyakaḥ savitamaḥ(!) śītāṃśugolotthadik

grāhyagrāhakamānasaṃ yutidalaṃ syāt sāyakeno nitaṃ

channaṃ grāhyavihīnam etad uditaḥ khagrāsasaṃjño budhaiḥ | 14 |

vimbayogadalavāṇavargayor

antarālapadam abhraṣaḍguṇaṃ |

vyarkacandragatibhājitaṃ bhavec
