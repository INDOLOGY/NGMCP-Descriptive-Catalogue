There is a line in Nepali language after the colophon, which states that: yo pustka śrībhairavarāja juju upādhyā[ya]le baḍo muskīlasaṃga talāsa garī ṣojī mileko


Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||


skaṃda uvāca ||


deva deva jagannātha sarvakāryyasusaṃbhava ||

āpadduḥkhadaridrādipīḍitānāṃ nṛṇāṃ vibho || 1 ||


varddhaṃte sukhasaṃpattidhanadhānyakaraṃ sadā ||

viśeṣataḥ kaliyuge śāṃtipuṣṭipradāyakaṃ || 2 ||


baktum arhasy aśeṣena(!) mamānaṃdakaraṃ paraṃ || ||

śrīśivo(!)[[vā]]ca || (fol. 1v1–5)


End

daṃṣṭrācandraprabhāvaprakaṭitasuvṛhattālupātālamūle ||

śambhor vaktraṃ trinetraṃ tridaśabhayaharaṃ śaṅkaraṃ dakṣiṇāṅgaḥ || 20 ||


idaṃ hṛdayam ajñātvā mama pūjā karoti yaḥ ||

na sidhyati tadāloke kalpakoṭiśatair api || 21 ||


evaṃ dhyātvā prasanno [ʼ]smi sarvasiddhim avāpnuyāt ||

gopanīyaṃ prayatnena mātṛjāraparo yathā || 22 || (5v4–6r4)


Colophon

iti śrīśivarahasyataṃtre śi[[va]]proktaṃ śaṃkarahṛdayaṃ samāptaṃ || śubham || (fol. 4r4–5)
