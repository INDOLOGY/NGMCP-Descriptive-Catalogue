Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||


śrīdattātreya uvāca ||


kailāsaśikharāśī(!)naṃ devadevaṃ maheśvaraṃ

dattātreyaṃ paro pṛchac chaṃkaraṃ lokaśaṃkaraṃ || 1 ||


kṛtāṃjaripuṭo bhūtvā pṛchate bhaktivatsalaḥ ||

bhaktānaṃ ca hitārthāya kalpataṃtraś ca kathyate || 2 ||


kalau siddhimahākalpaṃ taṃtravidyāvicārakam |

kathayaṃ(!)ti mahādeva devadevo maheśvaraḥ || 3 || (fol. 1v1–5)


End

uttarasyāṃ ca digbhāge marīco nāma rākṣasaḥ ||

tasya mūtrapurīṣābhyāṃgatas taṃbho bhava svāhā || 1 ||


anena maṃtraṃ yo saptajalāṃjaliṃ agnimadhye jalaṃ nikṣiped agniṃ śāmyati ||


gṛhītvā ravivāre tu śvetakavarimūlakaṃ ||

dhārayed dakṣiṇo ʼhaṃ hasto agnibhayaṃ na jāyate || 2 || ||


«Sub-Colophon»


iti śrīdattātreyataṃtre īśvaradattātreyasaṃvāde prathamaḥ paṭalaḥ || 1 || (fol. 3v3–4)


Colophon

iti śrīdattātreyataṃtre īśvaradattātreyasaṃvāde vyāghrādinivāraṇaṃ nāma dvāviṃśatitamaḥ paṭalaḥ || 22 || || śubham astu sarvadā ||


giryantarikṣāṃkasomair mite vikramavatsare ||

śuci māse tīladale durgāyāṃ bhaumavāsare || 1 ||


dattātreyyābhidhaṃ taṃtraṃ pārvatīpatinoditaṃ ||

viśvaharṣo ʼlikhad vaṃdyo padmādāsamude mudā || 2 || śubham || (fol. 59v1–5)
