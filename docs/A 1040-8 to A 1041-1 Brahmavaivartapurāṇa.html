Manuscript Features

The MS contains the following chapters of the Brahmavaivartapurāṇa:

  1. Brahmakhaṇḍa, 30 adhyāyas (64 folios)
  2. Prakṛtikhaṇḍa, 64 adhyāyas (172 folios)
  3. Gaṇeśakhaṇḍa, 49 adhyāyas (98 folios)

The ink is partially faded on the first and last pages.
