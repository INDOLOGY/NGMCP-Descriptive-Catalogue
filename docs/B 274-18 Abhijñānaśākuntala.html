Beginning

|| śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

yattredhāj (!) janidaśadhāgataṃ yad yaj jātaṃ

daśavidham eti ṣoḍaṣatvaṃ (!) yad ghītaṃ

samama(2)hadādikasya cādyaṃ tejas

taj jayati himo sma (!) rūpam agryaṃ || 1 ||

uddiśyāmaranimnagāṃ gatavatoḥ śrutvā ka(3)liṃ śaiśave

yaḥ sākṣātkaravāṇi tām iti jaṭājūṭopakaṃṭhaṃ gataḥ

pītva puṣkalapuṣkareṇa na kim apy †a(4)treṭhi† vismāpayan

mitror (!) vigrahavagrahaṃ vihitavān pāpad gatāsyaḥ sa vaḥ || 2 ||

naṭyadedābdhim ālo(5)kya tā ḍanaṃ (!) yo vinirmamau

syātmanābhinayaṃtaṃ taṃ praṇamāmi mahānaṭaṃ || 3 || (fol. 1v1–5)…

(8)atha nāṭakādau gūrvaraṃgāṃgabhūtām (!) āśīrūpāṃ caturasratālānusiṇīm aṣṭapadīṃ sūtradhāro (9) nāṃdīṃ paṭhati || yā sṛṣṭir iti 1 || yā tanu (!) sraṣṭu (!) brahmaṇaḥ ādyāsēṣṭir jalam ity arthaḥ
