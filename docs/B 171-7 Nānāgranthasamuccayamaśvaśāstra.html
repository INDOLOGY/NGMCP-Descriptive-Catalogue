Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ sarvvajñāya ||

praṇamya śaṃkaraṃ bhaktyā śaṅkaraṃ sarvvadehināṃ |

śivāya jagato jātāṃ śivāñcāpi viśeṣataḥ ||

sukhāvabodhaśabdārthaṃ granthavistaravarjitaṃ |

lakṣaṇaṃ vājidehasthaṃ saṃkṣepeṇa yathākramaṃ ||

cikitsāñ ca samāsena siddhauṣadhi samanvitāṃ |

muniproktāni śāstrāṇi samyagālokya vājināṃ ||

śrīmad vijayadattasya putreṇa kriyate ʼdhunā |

śrīmatā jayadattena vāhānāṃ hitamic-chatā || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

akharayā guli mahāpariśrama, myaṃvanamase va, ākhara sapariśrama yā kamhana tuseva, goto the dhārasāṃ mocā garbbhasa thaṃ ne hmaṃ gālinī misā janana mocāvuyakeyā vyathā masev athyaṃ, sāgara laṃghana yāṅāyā pariśrama hanumāna naju kva selā thvato thyaṃ thva graṃtha avahelā yāyamate va pariśrama seyamāla || (fol. 97v4–6)

Colophon

samvat 738 āṣāḍhakṛṣṇāṣṭamī aśvinī, ādityavāra kvahnu vijarakvcche, nārāyaṇasiṃha josina thva graṃtha coya nataṃ ṇana saṃpūrṇṇayāṅā guro || śivamastu || (fol. 97v6–7)
