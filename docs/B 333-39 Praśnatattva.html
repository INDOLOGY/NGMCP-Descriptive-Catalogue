Beginning

❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

bhāratimaṃjulacaṃcaladehe

bhūṣitabhūrivibhūṣaṇasāre

tattvasaroruhavāsinitatvaṃ (!)

darśaya kundanibhe praṇatir me 1

(2) cakrapāṇir iti satyadharasya

khyāta ātmaja ihocyutabhaktaḥ

prārthitaḥ sa kurute bahuśiṣyaiḥ

praśnatatvam (!) atilāghavam ādyam 2

daivajñam ānamya phalaprasūnais

tithyā(3)dipatraṃ viditaprabhāvam

abhyarcya bhaktyā vinayāvanamraḥ

praṣṭā 'malāntaḥkaraṇaṃ supṛchat (!) 3 (fol. 1v1–3)

End

paṭhitam apaṭhitam vā pāṭhanāya pravṛtte

sphurati mama vaco vai yādṛśaṃ yasya śaktyā

idam api hi samastaṃ praśnatattvaṃ mad ī(4)yaṃ

sphurati vibudhavaktre tādṛśaṃ tasya śaktyā 38

indirāramaṇapārvatīpate

devi khecaraguro gaṇanātha

mat kṛtaṃ saphalam astv iti yuṣmān

(5) prārthayām iha kṛtāṃjalir adhvā 39

śrī mat satyadharādyaṃ

suṣuve śādhvīha satyarūpākhyā

upakṛtaye śiṣyānām (!)

cakrapāṇinā tena ra(6)citaṃ hi 40 (fol. 15v3–6)

Colophon

iti cakrapāṇiracite vibudhapadaprade praśnatattve vai miśrapraśnādhyāyaḥ sulabha iha dvādaśaś cāgāt 12 (7) śubhaṃ iti praśnatatvagranthaṃ (!) saṃpūrṇam saṃvat 949 pauṣavadī 2 roja 5 śrīmaheśvararājena likhitaṃ śubham bhūyāt (fol. 15v6–7)
