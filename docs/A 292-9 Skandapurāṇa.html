The MS contains 1–25 adhyāyas of Kedārakhaṇḍa of the Skandapurāṇa.

Excerpts

Beginning

90 || oṃ namaḥ śivāya || ||

yasyājñayā jagatsraṣṭā viraṃciḥ pālako hariḥ ||

saṃharttā kālarudrākhyo namas tasmai pinākine ||

tīrthānām uttamaṃ tīrthaṃ kṣetrāṇāṃ kṣetram uttamaṃ ||

tatraiva naimiṣārṇye śaunakādyās tapodhanāḥ ||

dīrghasatraṃ prakurvaṃtaḥ satriṇāṃ sumatena saḥ ||

teṣāṃ saṃdarśanotsukyād āgato hi mahātapāḥ ||

vyāsaśiṣyo mahāprājño lomaśo nāma nāmataḥ ||

tam āgataṃ te dadṛśur mmunayo dīrghasatriṇaḥ ||

uttasthur ju(!)gapat sarve munayas te samutsukāḥ || (fol. 1v1–5)


End

sūta uvāca ||

vyāsaḥ prasādāc chrutam asti sarvaṃ

mayā dvijāḥ śāṃkararūpam adbhutaṃ ||

suvistṛtaṃ cādbhutavedagarbhaṃ

jñānātmakaṃ paramam eva hi puṇyarūpaṃ ||

śraddhayā parayopetā arcayaṃti śivapriyam ||

śṛṇvaṃti caiva ye bhaktyā śaṃbhor mmāhātmyam adbhutaṃ ||

śivaśāstram idaṃ viprās te yāṃti paramāṃ gatiṃ || || (fol. 273r4–6)


Colophon

iti śrīskaṃdapurāṇe kedārakhaṃḍe śaivaśāstre paṃcaviṃśodhyāyaḥ saṃpūrṇaḥ || || ||

śāke 1564 mārgaśīrṣapratipadi śukre kṛṣṇapakṣe gorṣānagare śivakiṃkaramahārājaśrīḍaṃbarasāhamahograpratāpājñayā śrīmanorathaśarmmaṇā likhitedaṃ śivaśāstrapustakaṃ || || śubham astu || || śrīr astu || (fol. 273r6–9)
