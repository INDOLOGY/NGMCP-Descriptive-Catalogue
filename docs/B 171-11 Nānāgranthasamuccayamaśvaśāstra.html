Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ sarvvajāya (!)

praṇamya śaṃkaraṃ bhaktyā śaṃkaraṃ sarv-vadehināṃ |

śivāya jagato jātaṃ śivaṃ cāpi viśeṣataḥ ||

sukhāvabodhaśabdārthaṃ grantha vistaravarjjitaṃ |

lakṣaṇaṃ vājidehestha saṃkṣepena (!) yathākramaṃ ||

kastamanasyaṃ sukhanasayake arthanata vatavatrattha dvākka vāṅa śaḍayābhyaṃ sacogva lakṣaṇa sakṣapana seyake paripāṃṭhina juroṃ || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

karaṃjādi ghera ||

śaḍaṃyāroya lakṣaṇa seyake nau, vāsaranoṃ jayadatta śāstrasa piṃkāsyaṃ nepāla bhākhāna tayā, the majī ca datasā, guṇavantalokasyaṃ jiyakaṃ hlokākemāla || e || || śubhaṃ || (fol. 56r3–5)
