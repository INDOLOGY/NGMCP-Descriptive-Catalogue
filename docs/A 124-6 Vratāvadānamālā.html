Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namoḥ(!) śrīvasuṃdharāyai

aśoko thopaguptaṃ taṃ vyajijñapat kṛtāṃjaliḥ
dāridraduḥkhaśamanaṃ vrataṃ kathaya sadguro
iti vijñāpitas tena prāvadat sa (sa)tyakaḥ
śru(!)yatāṃ nṛpaśārdu(!)la vasuṃdharāvrataṃ mahat⟨aṃ⟩
yasyā vrataprabhāvena sūryodayaḥ sa saprajaḥ
duḥkhadāridramukto bhūd rājyo mahotsavonvitaḥ
ghoṣirārāma-m-āsīno bhagavān śākyakeśarī
yathā vada(!) bhikṣugaṇaṃ sa bhūpālaṃ tathā krava(!)
purā śrīnāmni nagara(!) sū[r]yodayābhidho nṛpaḥ
pracaṇḍatanayo vi(!)ro bhūt prātāpī yathārthataḥ
tena purā mahīpāla kāmavyākulacetasā
madāṃdhena pramattena agamyāgamanaṃ kṛtaṃ
kāmārthe prakṛtā hiṃsādattādānaṃ kṛtaṃ tathā (fol. 1v1–6)

End

tataḥ kṣaṇāt puṣpavimāna[ṃ] prāptaṃ rājñā svaputrāya rājyābhiṣekaṃ datvā svāsane sthāpayitvā prajādharmārthaṃ svapade niyujya puṣpavimānam āruhya tābhyāṃ devībhyāṃ saha vratibhiś ca sārddhaṃ tuṣitāstu(?)vanam agamat śrīdevībhajanaṃ kṛtvā sukhenātiṣṭhat tatas tena candrodayābhidhānabhūpena rājyapālanaṃ kṛtvā sukhena vratam ādhāya sthitaḥ tataḥ prabhṛti loke śokabhūpa⟨ḥ⟩ śrīvasuṃdharāvrataṃ pracāritaṃ tad adyāpi saṃpravarttitaṃ yatprabhāvād dāridraduḥkhamuktir abhūt (fol. 13v4–8)

Colophon

iti śrīvratāvadānamālāyāṃ śrīvasuṃdharāvratakathanaṃ samāptaḥ

iti samvat 1954 sāla miti bhādra śudi 1 roja 1 śubhm⟨ḥ⟩ (fol. 13v8–9)
