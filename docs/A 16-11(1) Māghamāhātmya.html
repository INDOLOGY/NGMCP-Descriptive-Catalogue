Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namo bhagavate vāsudevāya⟨ḥ⟩ ||

nārāyaṇaṃ namaskṛtvā(!) naraṃ caiva naro⟨r⟩ttamaṃ |
devī[ṃ] śarasvatī[ṃ] caiva⟨ṃ⟩, tato jayam udīrayet ||
dīlipa iti vikhyāto raghuvaṃśe narādhipa[ḥ] |
ekadā mṛgayāyāto ga〇ndhārākṣamahāvane ||
hrīyamānas tato dūraṃ dharmmena mṛgarūpiṇā⟨ṃ⟩ |
tṛṣārtto bhūpatis tatra vanamadhye śarovare ||
dṛṣṭvā snātvā jala⟪ā⟫ṃ pī〇tvā nivartye(!) devatarppanaṃ | (fol. 1r1–3)

vaśiṣṭha uvāca ||
māghasnānaṃ paraṃ śleyo(!) loke(!)dvayahitāvaha(!) |
yajñadānaphalaṃ ye ca prāptukāmā nalottamāḥ(!) ||
mṛgārkkanudite(!) sūrye snā〇tu māghe tu sarvvadā | (fol. 2r1–2)

Sub-colophon

iti padmapurāṇottarakhaṇḍe vaśiṣṭhadilīpasaṃvāde ṣaṣṭho dhyāyaḥ || (fol. 7v)

iti padmapurāṇottarakhaṇḍe vaśiṣṭhadvilipa(!)saṃvāde saptamo dhyāya[ḥ] || (fol. 23r)

iti padmapurāṇottarakhaṇḍe vaśiṣṭhadvilīpa(!)saṃvādeṣṭamo dhyāya[ḥ] || (fol. 27v)

iti padmapurāṇottarakhaṇḍe vaśiṣṭhadvilīpa(!)samvāde navamo dhyāya[ḥ] || (fol. 39r)

End

param idam itihāsaṃ pāvanaṃ tīrthabhūtaṃ
vṛjinavilayahetuṃ yaḥ śṛṇotiha(!) nītyaṃ(!) |
sa bhavati kha〇lu pūrṇṇaḥ sarvvakāmair abhīṣṭai[r]
jjayati ca suralokaṃ du[r]llabhaṃ dharmmahīnai[ḥ] ||

iti padmapurāṇottarakhaṇḍe vaśiṣṭhadvilīpa(!)saṃvāde daśamo dhyāya(!) || (fol. 49v3–5)
