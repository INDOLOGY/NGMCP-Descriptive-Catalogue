Excerpts

«Beginning»


śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

vyāsa uvāca || ||


naimiṣe sūtam āsīnam abhivādya kṛtāṃjaliḥ ||

kathāmṛtarasāsvādakuśalaḥ śaunako ʼbravīt || 1 ||


śaunaka uvāca


ajñānadhvāṃtavidhvaṃsakoṭisūryasamaprabha ||

sūtākhyā hi kathāṃ divyāṃ mama karṇa rasāyanaṃ || 2 ||


bhaktijñānavirāgāptyai viveko varddhate kathaṃ ||

māyāmohanirāsaś ca vaiṣṇavaiḥ kriyate yathā || 3 || (fol. 1v1–3)


«End»


iti ca paramaguhyaṃ sarvasiddhāntasiddhaṃ

sapadi nigaditaṃ te śāstrapuṃjaṃ vilokya ||

jagati śukakathāto nirmalaṃ nāsti kiṃcit

pibata ramata nityaṃ dvādaśaskaṃdhasāram || 104 ||


enāṃ yo niyatatyā śṛṇoti bhaktyā

yaś caināṃ kathayati sādhuvaiṣṇavāgre ||

te smyag vidhikaranāt phalaṃ labhaṃte

yāthārthyaṃ nahi bhuvane kim apy asādhyam || 105 ||


kāyena vācā manasendriyair vā

buddhyātmanā vā prakṛteḥ svabhāvāt

karomi yad yat sakalaṃ parasmai

nārāyaṇāyeti samarpayāmi || 106 || || (fol. 24r4–7)


«Colophon»


iti śrīpadmapurāṇe uttarakhaṇḍe śrībhāgavatamāhātmye saptāhayajñavidhikathanaṃ nāma ṣaṣṭho ʼdhyāyaḥ || ||

vāṇāgagotrābjamite śake madhau

bahnes tithau (śyāmala)pakṣake dvijaḥ ||

śrīdevadatto jayadaṃ hereḥ pradaṃ

saptāhamāhātmyam alīlikhad varam ||


śubham astu || (fol. 24r7–8)
