Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

harako pānalenaiva bhasmībhūyā karosmaraḥ || (!)

arddhanārīśarīraṃ hi yasya tasyai namostu te || 1 ||

samyag ārādhitaḥ kāmaḥ sugaṃdha kusumādibhiḥ ||

vidadhāti vara strīṇāṃ mānagranthi vimocanam || 2 ||

aneka kāmaśāstrāṇāṃ sāram ākṛṣya yatnataḥ ||

bālavyut[[pattaye]] strīṇāṃ cittasantoṣāṇāya ca || 3 ||

kāmaśāstraṃ pravakṣāmi nāti saṃkṣipta vistṛtam ||

āyurvedāgame taddhi yad vinā vṛṣyatā vṛthā || 4 || (fol1v1–3)

«Ending:»

saṃdhyākāle baliṃ dadyāt kukkuṭāṃ vāruṇīyutaṃ ||

tataś cullhyāṃ lohapātre taile dhatturabījakai || 25 ||

ukṣiptvā viṃśat palaṃ pācyaṃ tad rasaṃ māṃsa pīḍitaṃ ||

samārabhyāsya maṃdāgnau yāvat sūryo[[dayo]] bhavet || 26 ||

haṭhā jāgaraṇaṃ kuryād anyathā tan na sidhyati ||

prātaruddhṛtya guṭikāṃ kṣīrabhāṇḍe vinikṣipet || 27 ||

tatkṣiraṃ śuṣyati kṣipraṃ etat pratyayakārakaṃ ||

ratikāle mukhaṃ dhāryā guṭikā vīryyarodhinī || 28 ||

kṣīraṃ pītvā ramed rāmāṃ kāmavyākulitānvitāṃ ||

mukhasthāṃ dhārayed dhaste tadā vīryaṃ na muñcati || 329 || ||  (fol.42r2–5)

Colophon

iti vīryarodhinī guṭikā || ||

iti saurāṣṭradeśodbhava kulāvataṃsasa upādhyāya dvijavarya mādhava viracite āyurvedaprakāśe upaśārīrake strīlakṣaṇa sevanādi kāmaśāstrasya viṣaya nirupanādhyāyaḥ samāptaḥ || ○ || (fol. 42r4–5)

baddhamuṣṭi kaṭīgrīvā taptaḍṛṣṭīr adhomukhaḥ ||

kaṣṭena likhitaṃ graṃthaṃ yatnena pratipālayet ||

|| samvat 1983 sāla jyeºº śudi 10 roja 1 śubham bhuyāt(!) || ❁ ||
