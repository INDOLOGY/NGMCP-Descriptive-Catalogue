Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ bhavāṇī (!)śaṃkarābhyāṃ namaḥ ||

astyuttarasyāṃ diśi devatātmā himālayo nāma nagādhirājaḥ ||

pūrvvāparau toyanidhi viagāhya (!) sthitaḥ pṛthivyā iva mānadaṇḍa ||

yaṃ sarvvaśailāḥ parikalpyavatsaṃ marou sthite dogdhari dohadakṣe |

bhāsvanti ratnāni mahauṣadhīś ca pṛthupadiṣṭāṃ duduhurddharitrīṃ ||

anantaratna prabhavasya yasya himanna saubhāgyavilopi jātaṃ |

eko hi doṣo guṇasannipāte nimajjatīndoḥ kiraṇeṣvivāṅkaḥ || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

pūrvaṃ yathādevapaterniyogaṃ mūrddhnā vahat kārmukavāṇahastaḥ ||

prāptastathaivāśugajendragāmi probhiyamāno navayauvanena ||

atha vivudha gāṇā⟨ya⟩stān inandumaulir viṃsṛjya

kṣitidharapatikanyām ādadānaḥ kareṇa ||

kanaka kalaśarakṣā bhaktiśobhā sanāthaṃ

lṣitiviracitaśaiyyaṃ kautukāgāramāgā

navapariṇaya lajjā bhūṣaṇān tatra gaurī

vadanamapaharantīn tatkṛtākṣepamīśaḥ

api śayana sakhībhyodattavācaṃ kathaṃcit

prathamamukhavikā || || ❁ || (fol. 51r4–51v2)

Colophon

iti śrīkumārasaṃbhve mahākāvye saptamaḥ sarggaḥ samāptaḥ || ❁ ||

samvat 859 caitramāse kṛṣṇapakṣe saptamyāṃ tithau mūlapada rohiniṇakṣetre (!) parighapada śivagoge jāthā karṇa yūktatre (!) budhavāle (!) meṣalāśigate (!) saṃvitari dhanulāśigate candramasi (!) || śubha || (fol. 51v2–4)
