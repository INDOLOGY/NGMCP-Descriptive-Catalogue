Excerpts

«Beginning of the 1. Gītagovinda-daśāvatāra»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

meghair meduram aṃbaraṃ vanabhuvaḥ śyāmāstamāladrumair

naktaṃ bhīrur ayaṃ tvam eva tadimaṃ rādhe gṛhaṃ prāpaya ||

itthaṃ nandanideśataś calitayoḥ pratyadhvakuñjadrumaṃ

rādhāmādhavayor jayaṃti yamunākūle rahaḥkelayaḥ ||1 || (exp. 3t1-5)


«End of 4. #Devīvandanā»

muṃḍamālini naragabhūṣiṇi nāgakuṃḍalas[ś]obhini khaḍgadhāriṇi danujanāśini bevalokaki pālini || 3 || janma janma pāpe durgā mokṣasiddhī(!)varadāyini nṛpati rājendravikramasāhā vagatakai pratipālini || 4 || devi mātā (fol 13r2-13v5)


«Sub-colophon of the 1. Gītagovinda-daśāvatāṛa»

iti śrīgītagovinde daśāvatāraḥ || ||


«Sub-colophon of the 2. Kālikāstotra»

iti sakalanivāraṇaṃ kālikāstotraṃ || || (fol 11r5-11r5)


«Sub-colophon of the 3. Pañcadevavandanā»

śubham astu pāthakāya ||

«Sub-colophon of the 4. Devīvandanā»
