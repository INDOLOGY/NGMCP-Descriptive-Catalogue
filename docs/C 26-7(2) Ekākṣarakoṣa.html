Manuscript Features

The first folio of another MS of the same text is kept together.

The manuscript contains (parts of) four texts:

  • Syādyantakoṣasāra
  • Ekākṣarakoṣa
  • Rūpasiddhikroḍa
  • Dharmaśāstra (prakīrṇa)

Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śivāya ||

āgamebhyo bhidhānebhyo, dhātubhyaḥ śabdaśāsanāt |
bhavaty ekākṣaraṃ nāmābhidhānam abhi[[(dhī)]]yate ||
kaḥ prajāpatir uddiṣṭaḥ ko vāyur iti śabditaḥ |
kaś cātmāli(!) samākhyātaḥ kaḥ prakāśa udāhṛtaḥ || (fol. 1v1–2)

End

ḷkāro devayānīnāṃ ḹ mātā ca prakīrttitaḥ(!) |
kāroekāraś ca bhaved viṣṇu,r aikāraḥ syān maheśvaraḥ ||
okāras tu bhaved brahmā, aukāra ibha ucyate |
am īśo paramaṃ brahma, aḥ syāc caiva maheśvaraḥ || (fol. 3v4–4r1)

Colophon

ity ekākṣaraśabdakoṣaḥ samāptaḥ || || śubham astu || (fol. 4r1)
