Manuscript Features

1. The MTM contains following texts:

(1) Sahadevanaṃ dayakā chāya (exp. 2–5)

(2) Dharmalakṣmīsaṃvāda (exps. 6t–11b)

(4) Roganidānaṭippaṇī (exps. (exp. 20 left–34)

Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namo sāladāyai ||

jatya satya, tato lakṣmī, tato hari, tato dhamyai jato jaya,

gathe ga(2)the dhālasā, gana gana jate satya yākahma yākaṃ || śrīlakṣmītvaṃ bijyāta, gana lakṣmī bi(3)jyāta ana śrīkṛṣṇa bijyāka, gana kṛṣṇatvaṃ bijyāta ana dharmmatva bijyāta anega ja(4)ya jaya juo || (exp. 6t1–4)

End

mevuyā darvya (6) mevuyā davyaṃ ṣa mayakaṃ, mocakāyā pāpana, aputra juyāo, nisantāna juraṃ (7) || gathe mevuyātaṃ madayaka, dukha biraṃ, thatheṃ thvahma manukṣayā kuṭuṃmba sa(11b1)mastaṃ nāsa juraṃ || thuti pāpa mayāsyaṃ puṇya yāsā thotuli jarnmasa, sukha sa(2)spati(!) bhukti pada lātaṃ paralokasa, svarggavāsa juro || (exp. 11t5–11b2)
