Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrīśrīśrīgaṇesāya namaḥ ||

ṅātāpolala devalasa, śrī 3 siddhilakṣmīdevī(2)ske, ghaṇṭhā duteyātā kalaśārccanavidhi || patavāsana mālako coya, jā pūjā(3) ādina boya || ||

jajamāna puṣpabhājana yācake || adyādi || vākya ||

śrī(4)samvattāmaṇḍalānte kramapadanihitānandaśaktiḥ subhīmā

sṛṣṭiṃ nyāye ca(5)tuṣkaṃ tvakulakulagataṃ paṃcakaṃ cānya ṣaṭkaṃ |

catvāraḥ pañcakoṇyaṃ punar api(6) caturaṃs tatvato maṇḍalena,

saṃsṛṣṭaṃ yena tasmai namata guruvaraṃ bhairavaṃ śrīkuje(7)śaṃ || (fol. 1v1-7)

End

ūkāraṃ vāyu bījaṃtyādi || ceta ||

śrīkhaṇḍacanda(4)naṃ divyaṃ || sidhra ||

vīreśvarī mahāvīra || mohanī ||

trailokye mo(5)hanībhyāṃ || ... ||

siddhārthaṃ dadhipāvanaṃ || svāna ||

utphullāṃ(6)buja karṇṇiko || ||

bali chāya || bali thāya 2sa choya || kaumārī (7) visarjjana ||

sarvvamaṃgalamāṅgalya || ||

sākṣī thoya || || (fol. 35v3-7)

Colophon

iti (fol. 36r1) kalaśārccanavidhiḥ samāptaḥ || || ... || ||
