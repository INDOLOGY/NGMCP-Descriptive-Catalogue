❖ arthaḥ(!) dathuyāguli bali likhyate ||

brāhmaṇa ācāryya josi thvate(2)sanaṃ sthirahārāva vayāva || maṇḍapanapi urttare soya majirasā (3) pūrrva soyaṃ teva || bali biya pāṭha yāya caṇḍi || gajura chākuhnuyā (4) velā anukramana hṅā liṃ teva || thva yāya ||    || one hṅāonaṃli (5) dinapatiṃ rajasārāsa karmmācana yāya dhunakāva baliya pā(6)ṭha yāya ||    ||
(fol. 1v1–6)

saṃhāra mu(6)drayā bali visarjjana ||
