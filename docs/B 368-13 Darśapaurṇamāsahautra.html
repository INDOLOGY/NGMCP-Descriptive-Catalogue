Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

atha hautraprayogaḥ

hotar ehīty ukte prāgudagāhavanīyād avasthāya prāṅmukho yajñopavītyācamya dakṣiṇavṛd vihāraṃ prapadyata etat tīrtham ityācakṣate tasya nityāḥ prāṃcaś ceṣṭa aṃkadhāraṇā ca yajñopavītaśauce ca vihā(rā)d avyāvṛttiś ca tatra cet karmaikāṅgavacane dakṣiṇaṃ pratīyād anādeś prapadyāmi hṛtatareṇa pādena vedī śreṇyottara‥ ‥ ‥ samānni⟨ṃ⟩dhāya prapadena bahiṃr(!) ākramya saṃhita pāṇinī dhārayan nākāśavaty aṅguli hṛdayasaṃmitā vaṃkasaṃhitāṃ vā dyāvā pṛthivyoḥ saṃdhim īkṣamāṇa etaddhotusthānam adhvaryu preṣito japati (fol. 1v1–5)

End

apāś cāgne syanabhi śastīś ca satyam itvamayā asi || ayā sā vayasā kṛto yāsan havyamohiṣe yāno dhehi bheṣajaṃ svāhā || aho devāºº || ṛkkaṃ(!) || idaṃ viṣṇur vicaºº | ṛk || bhuḥ svāhā bhura(!) svāhā || sva[ḥ] svāhā || bhurbhuvasvaḥ svāhā || oṃ ca me svaraś ca me yajñā pacate namaś ca || yat te nyūnaṃ tasmai ta japayate tiriktaṃ tasmai te namāḥ(!) || ity upasthāya tīrthe niṣkramataḥ || (fol. 7v2–5)
