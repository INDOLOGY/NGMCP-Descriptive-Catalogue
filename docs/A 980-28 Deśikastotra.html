Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

śrīśiva uvāca ||

atha devi mahāguhyaṃ śṛṇu sarvārthasādhakaṃ ||

śāṃtidaṃ deśikastotraṃ sārabhūtaṃ vadāmi te || 1 ||

deśikastotramaṃtrasya paramātmā ṛṣiḥ smṛtaḥ ||

chaṃdas tu devī gāyatrī devatādeśikottamaḥ || 2 || (fol. 1v1–5, exp. 3t)

End

yo japet pratyahaṃ bhaktyā suprasanno bhaved guruḥ ||

svātmajñānaṃ ca labhate sarvaśatrujayaṃ tataḥ || 39 ||

maṃtrāṇāṃ siddhibhedaṃ ca bhogaṃ yogaṃ japaṃ tathā ||

āyur ārogyasiddhiṃ ca saubhāgyaṃ labhate dhruvaṃ || ❖ || (fol. 4v6–9, exp. 6t)

Colophon

ity ākāśabhairavakalpe deśikastotraṃ saṃpūrṇaṃ || || śivaṃ bhavatu || (fol. 4v9–10, exp. 6t)
