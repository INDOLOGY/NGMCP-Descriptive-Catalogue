Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ


purākailāsa(!)śikhare tārā papraccha(!) śaṃkaraḥ

guptabhāvena deveśi kathayasva tam ādara(!) 1 ||


śrītārovāca


asti guhyataraṃ hy etan niḥsṛtaṃ makharparjarāt(!)

idaṃ rahasyaṃ paramaṃ kathayāmi mayā prabho 3


śrūyatāṃ sāvadhānena kavacaṃ manmukhāśrutaṃ

madhukaiṭabhayor yuddhe kathitaṃ padmajanmani 4 (fol. 1v1–5)


End

caraṇau me pātu nityaṃ vidyāṃntakaṃ samāhūya

tāro me pātu satataṃ aṣṭāṃgaṃ parameśvarī 23


idaṃ kavacaṃ devakathitaṃ manmukhāt sṛjaṃ

prasādād yadi deveśa yatra kutra prakāśyate 23


yiginīs tu [sa]dā hantu paṃcavadhakāriṇī

yadi bhāgyavasāl labdhaṃ kenacit sādhakena vai 24


tadā tasya mahāsiddhiṃ vapraṣyaṃ(!) jāyate dhruvaṃ

nisid(!)dhebhyo na dātavyaṃ na prakāśyaṃ kadācana 25


yadi dadyān nisiddhebhyos tathāha vadhakariṇī

dadyāc chāntāya śiṣyāya taṃtramaṃtrayutāya ca 26


gurūbaktipu(!)jā caiva tadā siddhir anuttamam

ārahasyaṃ kathitaṃ sarvaṃ anantaphaladāyakaṃ 27


gopitavyaṃ tvayā deva na prakāśyaṃ kadācana ❁ (fol. 3r4–3v4)


Colophon

iti śrīyoginitaṃtre vajrayoginīkavacaṃ samāptam ❁ (fol. 3v5)
