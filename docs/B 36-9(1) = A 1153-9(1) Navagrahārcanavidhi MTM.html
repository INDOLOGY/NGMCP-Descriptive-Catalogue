Manuscript Features

The text is copied in corrupt Sanskrit. The apparent errors are not always marked in the Excerpt below.

This is the first text of this MTM which is listed in the PTL as a title of our MS. But there are other two texts too, which are not listed in the PTL separately.

Fol. 6 of the first text is missing.

The date of the copying mentioned in the colophon is in letters (akṣarātmakāṅka). Since the colophon contains a lot of scribal errors, we are not able to pick up the date out of it. On the basis of paleography, nevertheless, we could assign the date of this MS to the 15th century.
