Manuscript Features

Only the last folio is damaged in the margin.

At the beginning three extra folios with Prajñāpāramitā are found which are numbered 1-3. Fol. 3v ends after two and a half lines: nāmadheyamātreṇāyavyayaṃ kuryāṃ yad uta bodhisatva iti ||

Excerpts

Beginning

namo bhagavatyai āryaprajñāpāramitāyai ||

nirvikalpe namas tubhyaṃ prajñāpāramite 'mite |
yā tvaṃ sarvānavadyāṅgi niravadyair nirīkṣyase ||
ākāśam iva nirlepān niṣprapañcān nirakṣarāṃ |
yas tvām paśyati bhāvena sa paśyati tathāgataṃ ||
tava cārye guṇāḍhyāyā buddhasya ca jagadguroḥ |
na paśyanty antaraṃ santaś candracandrikayor iva ||
kṛpātmakāḥ prapadya tvāṃ buddhadharmapuraḥsarīm |
sukhenāṃ(!) yānti māhātmyam atulam bhaktivatsale ||
sakṛd apy āśaye śuddhe yas tvām vidhivad īkṣyate(!) |
tenāpi niyataṃ siddhiḥ prāpyate 'moghadarśane ||
sarveṣām api vīrāṇāṃ parārthe niyatātmanāṃ |
poṣikā janayitrī ca mātā tvam asi vatsalā ||
yad buddhā lokaguruvaḥ(!) putryas(!) tava kṛpālavaḥ |
tena tvam asi kalyāṇī sarvasatvapitāmahī ||
sarvapāramitābhis tvaṃ nirmalābhir anindite |
candralekheva tārābhir anuyātāsi sarvadā ||
vineyañ janam āsādya tatra tatra tathāgataiḥ |
bahurūpā tvam evaikā nānānāmabhir īḍyase ||
prabhāṃ prāpyeva dīptāṃsor avaśyāyodabindavaḥ |
tvām prāpya pralayaṃ yānti doṣā vādāś ca v(ā)dināṃ ||
tvam evaṃ trāsajananī bālānām bhīmadarśanā |
āśvāsajananī cāsi viduṣāṃ saumyadarśanā ||
yasya tvay apy abhiṣvaṅgas tvannāthasya na vidyate |
tasyāmba katham anyatra rāgadveṣau bhaviṣyataḥ || (fol. 1v1-4) (exp. 008)

End

enāṃ prajñāpāramitāṃ śroṣyanty udgrahīṣyanti dhārayiṣyanti vācayiṣyanti paryavāpsyanti pravarttayiṣyanti deśayiṣyanty upadekṣyanty uddekṣyanti svādhyāsyanti likhiṣyanti satka+++ +rukariṣyanti mānayiṣyanti pūjayiṣyanty arcayiṣyanty apacāyiṣyanti | puṣpadhūpagandhamālyavilepanacūrṇṇacīvaracchatradhvajaghaṇṭāpatākābhiḥ samantāc ca dīpamālābhir bahuvidhābhiś ca pūjābhir iti || idam avocad bhagavān āttamanās te ca maitreyapramukhabodhi+++hāsatvā āyuṣmāṃś ca subhūtir āyuṣmāṃś ca śāriputra āyuṣmāṃś cānandaḥ śakraś ca devānāṃ indraḥ sadevamānuṣāsuragandharvaś ca loko bhagavato bhāṣitam abhyanandann iti || || (fol. 216v4-6)

Colophon

āryāṣṭasahasrikāyāṃ prajñāpāramitāyāṃ parīndanāparivartto nāma dvātriṃ++++++ samāptā ceyaṃ bhagavaty āryāṣṭasahasrikā prajñāpāramitā sarvatathāgatajananī sarvabodhisatvapratyekajinaśrāvakāṇāñ ca mātā | dharmamudrā dharmolko dharmanābhir ddharmabherī dharmanetrī dharmara+nidhānam akṣayo dharmakośo dharmācintyādbhutadarśananakṣatramālā sarvasukha+++++ sadevamānuṣāsuragandharvaś ca(!) lokavanditāṃ prajñāpāramitāṃ samyag udgṛhya paryavāpya ca dhārayitvā pravartyaināṃ viharantu sadhārthina iti ||

ye dharmā hetuprabhavā hetun teṣān tathāgatā hy avadat
