///.ājaṃ maṇḍalatrayabhūṣitaṃ |

aghore ca padaṃ dadyāt dvitīyapraṇavaṃ punaḥ ||
śaṃbhoś catus tṛtīyaṃ ca procyantena (!) samāyutaṃ |
paramaghore++++śan turyārghamaṇḍalaiḥ ||
ghorarūpe tato dadyāt ślokāntañ ca punarnnyaset |
ghoramukhi padaṃ dadyāt padaṃ ca bhīmabhīṣaṇe ||
+++++⁅daṃ⁆ dadyād aṃbikā sena (!) saṃyutā |
aṃbikā jhaṃṭasaṃyuktā rephau varārccitau punaḥ ||
kevala ślokadvikaṃ sarephaṃ dvādaśāṃkhyakaṃ |
śikhi+phajhaṃṭakhena (!) dvitīyapraṇavaṃ punaḥ |
dvādaśamaṃ ślokaṃ trikaṃ śikhipatisaṃyutaṃ |
ślokasya yugalaṃ dadyād rphāktaṃ dvādaśākhyakaṃ
śikhirephajhaṃṭakhena dvādaśākhyan tathā punaḥ |
