Excerpts

«Beginning of the basic text:»

śrīheraṃbaṃ mādhavaṃ vāmadevaṃ

natvā devīṃ bhāskaraṃ śiṣyatuṣṭyai |

brūte krṣṇānandajoratnadīpaṃ

(6) gobhūmeghone śake saurasūtrāt || 1 || (fol. 2v5–6)

«Beginning of the commentary text:»

svasti śrīgurugaṇeśābhyān namaḥ || ||

aviralamadadhārāsvinnagaṇḍasthalaṃ taṃ

bhramad alikulabhaṃbhārāvasaṃ sevyamā(2)nam ||

abhimataphaladānotkaṇṭhitasvāntam īśaṃ

gajamukham abhivande tuṃdilaṃ śrīgaṇeśam || 1 || (fol. 1v1–2)

«End of the basic text:»

maṃtrī meṣe karkaṭe sasyapothār-

drāyāṃ meghe śoraseśas tulāyāṃ ||

dhānye śośve nīrase(6)śo mṛgāsye

vāraḥ proktas tatphalaṃ kheṭatulyam || 5 || (fol. 144r5–6)

«End of the commentary text:»

sasyaṃ cāgnyadhānyaṃ iti bhedaḥ tat teṣāṃ rājñādīnāṃ phalaṃ śubhāśubhasūcakarūpaṃ kheṭaiḥ grahaiḥ tu(2)lyaṃ jñeyaṃ pāpagrahe aśubhaphalaṃ śubhagrahe śubhaphalam |

tathā ca jaganmohane

caitrādimesādikuliratauli-

mṛ(3)gādivārādhipatiḥ krameṇa | 

rājā ca mū- (fol. 144v1–3)

Colophon
