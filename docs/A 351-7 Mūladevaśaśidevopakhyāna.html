Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṁ namaḥ śrīnṛtyanāthābhyāṃ ||

nāndī || mālava || co ||

haraṇadāraka taragaṇayā thāku(2)ra yāse, thathena gaṇeśa jula nāma,

bigini haralapa siddhi bihune chena, pāravatīdevī(3) jura nāma ||

babuju sadāśiva pyākhanasa atilaya, vibhutina lepalapu deva,

javakhe(4) hmosatasa candramā tasetala, dudharapu esa bhope yava ||

gaṅgāyā rahuli cona tahāka che (5) jaṭasa, dhucheguli pāchāva vāhāna,

tilahila biyā bhiṅa lāhāti thāyathāyasa, mo(6)lamāla kokhā che īśāna ||

jagatacandana dhāva hṅapā hāra nāndi me jagata mohalape(r2) āve,

ādibhavāniyā tutetalasa nehma, vāsa yāya chena dayakāva || || (fols. 19v1-20r2)

iti prathamāṅkaḥ || || (fol. 19v2)

iti dvitī(fol. 36v6)yāṃka || ||

atha tṛtīyadivase || ||

End

(fol. 50v2) śirijla (3) śarīra īsara savakā, mana nṛpa avayava dāse,

manahi pāvaya puṇya adharama apajasa, ma(4)na vase pāvaya tapāse ||

jagataprakāśa āsa kaela tohara, candraśekharasiṃha bhāe,

ja(5)gatajananipada hethahilākhaha, duhujanaka duhu kāe || || (fol. 50v2-5)

Colophon

iti śrīśrījagatprakāśa kṛ(fol. 50v6)taṃ mūladevaśaśidevavyākhyānanāṭakaṃ

samāptaṃ ||  ||

śubham astu sarvvadeva || ||
