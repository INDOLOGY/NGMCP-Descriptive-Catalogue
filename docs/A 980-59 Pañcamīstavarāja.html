Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

ānandam ānandakaraprasannaṃ jñānasvarūpaṃ nijabhāvayuktaṃ ||

yogīṃdramīdyaṃ bhavarogavaidyaṃ śrīmadguruṃ nityam ahaṃ namāmi || 1 ||

guruṃ gaṇapatiṃ durggāṃ baṭukaṃ śivam acyutaṃ ||

brahmāṇaṃ girijāṃ lakṣmīṃ vāṇīṃ vedavibhūtaye || 2 ||

śrīnāthādigurutraye gaṇapatiṃ pīṭhatrayaṃ bhairavaṃ

siddhibhyo baṭukatrayaṃ padayugaṃ dyutikramaṃ maṃḍalaṃ || 3 ||

hetave jagatām eva saṃsārārṇavasetave ||

prabhave sarvavidyānāṃ śaṃbhave gurave namaḥ || 4 || (exp. 2t1–6)

End

atyantaṃ rogayukto yaḥ yadi devīṃ prapūjayet ||

samyag vā ślokam ekaṃ vā sadyo rogāt pramucyate || 149 ||

mohanaṃ staṃbhanā karmmaṃ maraṇoccāṭanaṃ tatyā ||

kalpanād eva jāyante sarvvasiddhis tadā bhavet || 150 ||

idaṃ tripurasundaryyā stavarājaṃ manoharaṃ ||

rakṣāmaṃtraṃ ca subhadaṃ (!) śivena parikīrttitaṃ || 151 ||

chatrṃ mūrddhni sahasrapatrakamalaṃ rakṣāmṛtaṃ varṣaṇaṃ

divyastrīparicaryyamānam aniśaṃ caimānam (!) ekaṃ sadā ||

dhyāyen mānasapaṃkaje bhagavatīṃ raktā ca pūrvāsane

sāhaṃ yastu samastam astu labhate rājya sphuṭaṃ bhūtale || 152 ||

ākāśe svaprakāśesu kalaguṇamayīṃ sundarīṃ raktanetrāṃ

nityaṃ padmāsanasthāṃ taruṇaravinibhāṃ raktacaṃdraprabhāṃ tāṃ ||

ātmānaṃ sarvvabhāvaiḥ sakalaguṇayutāṃ sarvvadā yo hi paśye (!)

sākṣād devaḥ śivo vā madanadahanakṛt khecaro jāyate saḥ || 153 || || || (exp. 6b3–10)

Colophon

iti śrīrudrayāmale mahāstotre pañcamīstavarāja (!) samāptam iti || || yādṛśaṃ pustakaṃ dṛṣṭvā tādṛśaṃ likhitaṃ mayā || yadi śuddham aśuddham vā mama doṣo na dīyate || śubham astu || || śrir astu || || (exp. 6b11–12)
