Excerpts

Beginning

❖ namo buddhāya ||

tathāgatānāṃ padam ārurukṣur āśritya ratnatrayam ādareṇa |

bodhau nidhāyāvicalaṃ manaś ca kuryāt parātmavyatihāram ādau ||

tataḥ paraṃ dānavidhau prayogaḥ kāryas tathā lokahitonmukhena |

yathā svagātrāṇy api yācitasya na yogasaṃkocavirūpatā syāt ||

mātsaryadoṣopacayāya yat syān na tyāgacittaṃ paribṛhayed vā |

tat tyaktam evārhati bodhisatvaḥ parigrahacchadmamayaṃ vighātaṃ ||

tad bodhisatvaḥ katham ādadīta ratnam vanam vā divi vāpi rājyaṃ |

yat tyāgacittapratipakṣadakṣaṃ sambodhimārgāvaraṇaṃ karoti || (fol. 1v1–2)

End

ko nāma mārakalinānabhibhūtacetāḥ

saṃbuddhadharmmaguṇaratnanidhānabhūtaṃ |

sarvvajñayānamayayānam anarthapaṅkād

ākrośṭum arhati na cej jagatā(!) asya vairaṃ ||

lokārthasādhanapare jinarājavaṃśe

prajñānimīlitanayeṣu pariskhalatsu |

cittaṃ narasya karuṇāmṛdu kasya na syāt

tanmohadoṣaśamanāya dṛḍhaṃ ca vīryaṃ ||

prajñāyā janayati yaḥ parāṃ viśuddhiṃ

nirmokṣaḥ katham iva tasya dūrataḥ syāt |

naivāsmāt parataram asti śīghram anyat

tasmād bhajata vimokṣakāṃkṣiṇo hi || (fol. 10v6–7)

Colophon

prajñāpāramitāsaś(!) cāyaṃ pāramitāsamāsaḥ || ❁ ||

viśuddhamaunīndramanastaḍāgaprasūta❁

sūtrāntasaroruhebhyaḥ |

ādāya śūrabhrameṇa samyaṅ madhūkṣitaṃ pāramitāsamāse || ❁ || (fol. 10v7)
