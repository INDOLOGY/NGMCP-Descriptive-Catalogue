Manuscript Features

The manuscript contains several pūjavidhis, each with a new foliation. The second begins: oṃ namaḥ śivāya || bhūtiśuddhiṃ vidhāya || trikoṇaṣaṭlonāṣṭadalacaturasramaṇḍalam ullikhya oṃ etc. and covers two folios.

The next folio contains yantras and a list, which refers the letters of the alphabet to names of deities and mantras.

The third text begins with oṃ namāḥ(!) mahābhairavāya || oṃ sahāṃ hṛdayāya nama || and covers two folios. It is probably incomplete and ends: pāśāṃkuśakapālamudrābharayavarā pibantī siddhimanāśaktibhiḥ parivāritam | aiṃ.
