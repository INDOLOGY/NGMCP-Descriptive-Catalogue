Manuscript Features

The MS is damaged on the right-hand side with some loss of the text.

Fols. 13–16 and 18–26 are available.

The MS contains the text from Cā. 6.2.35 to Cā. 6.2.144 and 2.1.1 to Cā. 2.1.84. The text is not continuous but the folio is continuous after the end of the second pāda of the sixth adhyāya in fol. 20r (exp. 8b).
