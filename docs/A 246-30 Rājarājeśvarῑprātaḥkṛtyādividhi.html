Manuscript Features

MS contains the text about daily worship to the “Rajarājeśvarī” and the procedures to be followed by Sadhaka

from the early morning to the bedtime.


Excerpts

«Beginning»


atha prātaḥkṛtyaṃ


brāhme muhūrtte cotthāya prātaḥkṛtyaṃ samācaret

prātaḥ śirasi śukle ʼbje dvinetraṃ dvibhujaṃ guruṃ


parasannavadanaṃ śāṃtaṃ samaret tannāmapūrvakaṃ

namoʼ stu gurave tasmai iṣṭadevasvarūpiṇe


yasya vākyāmṛtaṃ haṃti viṣaṃ saṃsārasaṃjñitaṃ

ahaṃ devi na cānyo ʼsmi brahmaivāsmi na śokavā(!)k (fol. 1v1–3)


«End»


iti śayanavidhiḥ


āpo hiṣṭhā mayo bhuvaḥ stāna? urje dya?dātu naḥ mahe ranā(!)ya cakṣuseḥ(!) oṁ jevaḥ? śivatamorasaḥ tasya bhājayate hana


usatīriva mātaraḥ tasa(!)māraṃgamāmāyo?jasya kṣayāya jinatha? āpo jana ‥ athāsanaḥ (!) iti visarasajan (!) (fol. 27r9–v3)


«Colophon»

iti śayanavidhiḥ (fol. 27r7)
