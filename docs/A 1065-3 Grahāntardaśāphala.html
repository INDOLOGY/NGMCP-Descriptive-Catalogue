Manuscript Features

On the exposure 2 is written

mūladaśā nijavarṣair

guṇitā viṃśaty adhikaśatabhaktā ||

śeṣe khatriguṇite [[120 anena]] bhakte

syāntardaśikā jñeyā,

kāntaṃ prāha kapotikākulatayā nāthāṃtathāṃtakālo dhunā

vyādho dhodhṛtacāpasaṃdhṛtaśaraḥ śyenaḥ praribhrāmyati ||

itthaṃ satyahitā sadaṣṭaiṣūṇā śyeno pi tenāhatas

tūrṇaṃ tau tu himālayaṃ pratigatau daivī vicitrā gatiḥ ||

re re vānara ko bhavān ahamare...

...koṭis tu kīṭāyate ||

After the colophon are available two stanzas about nakṣatranāma and paramāṇvādi

Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

netrona2 janmo ūnavā9vaśeṣe (!)

ādyā daśā yā hi [[di]]vākasadeḥ

bhānuḥ śaśi bhū(2)misutotha rāhur

jīvaṃ śanir jñaṃ ca śikhī ca śukraḥ || 1 ||

atha grahadaśāphalaṃ || ||

udvignacittaḥ pari(3)naṣṭapittaḥ

śarīrarogī svajanair viyogī ||

nṛpīḍito rājajanaiḥ pravāsī

naropaghātī ca raver daśāyāṃ || 1 || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

rā(2)jamānyajanair yogo dvijendraiś ca sukhāgamaḥ ||

bhūmilābhaḥ putralābhaḥ ketor antargate gurau || 8 ||

vātapi(3)ttakṛtāpīḍā svajanaiḥ saha vigrahaḥ ||

videśagamanaṃ vāpi ketor antargate śanau || 9 ||

suhṛdbandhusa(4)māyogo bhūmir vittaṃ ca vigrahaḥ ||

dehapīḍā bhaven nityaṃ ketor antargate budhe || 10 || (fol. 14r1–4)

Colophon

iti ketv antargrahadaśāphalam || || svasti śrīśāke 1775 saṃvat 1910 sāla miti bhādraśudi 13 roja 5 (fol. 14r4–5)
