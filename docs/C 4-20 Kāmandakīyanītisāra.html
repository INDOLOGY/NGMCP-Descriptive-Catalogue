Beginning

-sannaṃ kāntijananaṃ sandhyeva śaśimaṇḍalaṃ ||
manaḥ prahlādayantībhir mmandaṃ yāntībhir apy alaṃ ||
mahānto pi hi bhidyante strībhiradbhir ivācalāḥ ||
mṛgayā'kṣās tathā pānaṃ garhitāni mahībhujāṃ ||
dṛṣṭas tebhyas tu vipadaḥ pāṇḍunaiṣa〇dhavṛṣṇiṣu ||
kāma[ḥ] krodhas tathā lobho harṣo māno madas tathā |
ṣaḍvarggam utsṛjed enaṃ tasmin tyakte sukhī nṛpaḥ |
(fol. 5r1–3)

tiṣṭheyu〇r yatra sañcārāḥ paracaryyā savāhinaḥ ||
