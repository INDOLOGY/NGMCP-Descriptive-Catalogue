Excerpts

Beginning

|| oṃ || śrīpārśvan natvā ||

uccair asyatimaṃdatāmarasatāṃ jāgratkalaṃkair ava,-

dhvaṃsaṃhastayate ca yā sumanasām ullāsinī mānase |

duṣṭodyan ma(2)danāśanārccir amalā lokatrayī darśikā,

sā netratritayīva khaṃḍaparaśor vāgdevatā dīvyantu (!) | 1

haṃho cinmayacittacaṃdramaṇayaḥ saṃbarddhaya(3)ddhvaṃ rasān

re re svairiṇi nirvicārakavite māsmat prakāśī bhava |

ullāsāya vicāravīcinicayālaṃkāravārāṃnidheś

caṃdrālokamayaṃ svayaṃ (4) vitanute pīyūṣavarṣaḥ kṛtī || 2 (fol. 1v1–4)

End

jayaṃ(4)ti yājñikaśrīman mahādevāṃgajanmanaḥ ||

sūktapīyūkha(!)varṣasya jayadevakaver giraḥ | 6

mahādevaḥ satrapramukhamakhavidhyaikacaturaḥ

sumi(5)trā tadbhakti praṇihitamatir yasya pitarau |

praṇītas tenāsau sukavijayadevena daśabhiś(!)

ciraṃ caṃdrālokaḥ sukhayatu mayūṣair daśadiśaḥ 7 (fol. 8r3–5)

Colophon

iti śrīpīyūkhavarṣa (!) paṃḍita śrījayadevaviracite caṃdrālokālaṃkāre abhidhāsvarupābhidhānonāma daśamo mayūkhaḥ | 10 | graṃ. 290

haṃsarājasya śiṣyeṇa keśavena matipradaṃ |

abde naṃdadvimunyeke likhi pustaṃ jagannute (!) | 1 kalyāṇam astu srva saṃghasya śrī (fol. 8r6)
