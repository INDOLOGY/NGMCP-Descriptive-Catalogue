Beginning

oṃ namo (!) gaṇapataye ||

yenā kriyaṃta satataṃ gṛhya karmadāsāḥ |
vācāmagocara caritrapavitritāya
tasmai namo bhagavate kusumāyudhāya ||

iti nītiśatake ajñaprakaraṇadaśakam || (fol. 19r5)
