Beginning

❖ svasti śrīmahāgaṇapataye namaḥ ||    ||

yasya jñānadayāsiṃdhor agādhasyā naghāguṇāḥ ||
sevyatām a(2)kṣayo dhīrāḥ saśriye cāmṛtāya caḥ (!) || 1 ||

samāhṛtyānya taṃtrāṇi saṃkṣiptaiḥ pratisaṃskṛtaiḥ ||
saṃpūrṇam ucyate(3) varggān nāmaliṃgānuśāsanaṃ || 2 ||

prāyaśorubhedena sāhacaryyāc ca kutracit ||
strīpuṃnapuṃsakaṃ jñeyaṃ(4) tad viśeṣavidhe kvacit || 3 ||

bhedākhyānāyanadvaṃdo naikaśeṣo na śaṃkaraḥ ||
kṛtotra bhinnaliṃgānām a(5)nuktānāṃ kramād ṛte || 4 || (exp.2a:1–5)

End

taddhitārthe dviguḥ saṃkhyā sarvvanāma tad antakāḥ ||

guṇadravyakriyāyogopādhibhiḥ paragāminaḥ ||
(5)kṛtaḥ karttaryasaṃjñāyāṃ kṛtyāḥ karttari karmmaṇi || 3 ||

anādyaṃtās tena raktādyarthe nānārthabhedakāḥ ||
(6)ṣaṭsaṃjñakās triṣusamāḥ yuṣmad asmat tiṅ avyayam || 4 ||

paraṃ virodhe śeṣaṃ tu jñeyaṃ śiṣṭaprayogataḥ (7)||    || (fol.78v3–7)
