Beginning

❖ oṁ namo brahmaṇe namaḥ ||


sū(!)drasya vivāhakarmmmavidhir llikhyate ||


pehnuṅhavapīṭhapūjācchoya(!) || ṅhathvakohnuprohitathaṃkādimālakoṭhasuṃsiṃ pācake māla ||

ṅhathvakohnupihimasamopanimātalhuyake || || copūjāvidhi || ccheṃsake mita mimāthaṃkādi

yihimasamopani puṣpabhājanayācake || adyāḍi || oṁ śivaṃ śivakaraṃ || (exp. 3: 1–11)


End

Tvaṃ gatiḥ sarvabhūtānāṃ saṃsthi tvaṃ ca cārācare(!)

Antaścārena(!) bhūtānāṃ dṛṣṭā tvaṃ parameśvara || ||


Karmaṇā manasā vācā tato nyāyākṛtaṃ mama ||

Akṛtaṃ vākyahīnan tu tatra pure(!) hutāśanaḥ ||


Mantrahīnaṃ kriyāhīnaṃ bhakta(!)hīnaṃ tathaiva ca ||

Japahomārccanāhīnaṃ kṣyamyatāṃ parameśvara || ||
