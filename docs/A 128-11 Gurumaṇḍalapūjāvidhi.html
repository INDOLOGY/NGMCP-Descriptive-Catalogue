❖ oṃ śrī 3 herukāyaḥ nitekarmapūjāvidhi || ||

suthaṃ danāo thao (2) ātmā suci yāṅāo iṣṭadevatāyā jāpa yāya siloka bo(3)ne || ||

thanasana sanāna yāya || ||

oṃ vajrahāsa huṁ dataoṃ yāya (4) hmutu cole ||

oṃ haṃ svāhā nosi siya || || magathi chiya || || (5)

oṃ savabudha codrāmaniya mahāmakuto huṃ || || ṅyāsa yāya || || (exp. 3t1–5)


«End»

jāki jo(2)ṅā hājarape oṃ vajrasato samaya manupālena oṃ vajrasato te(3)nopatididha me bhava sāsato me bhava sutakṣo me bhava anunakata(4)saṃ me bhava, sarvasidhi me prayaṃca, sarvakrama sucitaṃ śrī yaṃkula (5) oṃ ha 3 ho 3 bhagavana savatathāgata vajramāmi mutavaji (exp.14t1) bhamahāsamesatoā || ||

cita lāhāti buya oṃ ā huṁ va(2)jramudalatramu maṃdra dvaya svānaṃ chuya cita cherasa taya (exps. 13b2–14t2)
