Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīgopālāya || || 

avayktam avyayaṃ śāṃtaṃ nitāṃtaṃ yogināṃ priyaṃ |

sarvānaṃda svarupeyaṃ (!) taṃ vande brahmasarvvagaṃ ||

vividha vi(2)budhavaṃdyāṃ bhāratī vaṃdyamānaḥ

pracuracaturbhāvaṃ ⟪hā⟫ [[dā]]tu kāmo janebhyaḥ |

narapatir iti loke khyātanāmā vidhāsya

narapaticayaca(3)ryyānāmakaṃ śāstram etat ||

śrutvādau yāmalān sapta tathā yuddhajayārṇavaṃ |

kaumārīkauśalaṃ caiva yoginījayasaṃbhavaṃ || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

dakṣiṇāṃ kārayet tatra svaśaktyā khecarān prati ||

evaṃ kṛtvā vidhānena bhuktiyu(5)ktyā (!) vidhānataḥ ||

hṛṣṭas tuṣṭāḥ prajāyaṃte grahāḥ sarveṣu sarvadā ||

evaṃ sarvaprakāreṇa kṛtaṃ yasyābhiṣecanaṃ ||

tad ādi tasya puṃsas tu (6) grahapīḍā na jāyate ||

grahās tuṣṭā na kurvaṃti duṣṭār iṣṭādi ceṣṭitam ||

yato grahamayaṃ sarvaṃ narāṇāṃ tu śubhāśubhaṃ || (fol. 70v4–6)

Colophon

|| iti abhi(7)ṣekamaṃḍalavidhi || ❁ || || iti narapatijayacaryyāyāṃ svarodayaḥ sampūrṇaḥ || ❁ || || ❁ || śubhaṃ (8)

yādṛśī pustakaṃ dṛṣṭvā tādṛśī likhitaṃmayā

yadi śuddhm aśuddhaṃ vā śodhanīyaṃ mahad budhiḥ || ||

śrīrāmacandrāya namaḥ || ||

śivaṃ bhūyā (!) (9)

śyāmadevasya putreṇa lalitāpuranivāsinā (!) |

likhitaṃ vīradevena pustakaṃ śubhadām amuṃ || ||

śrīdvijavara(‥‥‥)sya pustakam idamṃ (fol. 70v7–9)
