dhṛtarāṣṭra uvāca ||

«Beginning of the commentary:»

dharmakṣetre iti || || bhao saṃjaya dharmmabhūmau kurukṣetre hamāra putra ra pāṇḍavaka putra yudhyakai ikṣā karate sekai sābhel || so vārttā mosau kahu bhani dhṛtarāṣṭra rājā saṃjaya sai puchata hai || (fol. 3r6–7)

«End of the basic text:»

yatra yogeśvaraḥ kṛṣṇo yatra pārtho dhanurddhara (!) ||

«End of the commentary:»

dhanuṣadhārī pārtha jo arjjuna virājate, tatra tehi sthāna | śrī hai, rājya hai tāhi, lakṣmī hai, tāhi vijaya kalyāṇa hai, tāhi vibhūti hai, aneka vṛddhi hai, nītinirṇaya hai, tāhi niścaya mere mati he mahārāja śrīkṛṣṇake śaraṇarāṣe setumāraputraka rakṣā hoise hama deṣe hai niścaya || 78 || (fol. 121r8–10)
