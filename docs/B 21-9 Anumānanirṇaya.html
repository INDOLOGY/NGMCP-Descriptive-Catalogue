Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namaḥ śivāya ||

śakṛtkarivikarṣaṇasphuritagopasīmanti⁅nī-
pa⁆yodharamahīdharadvitayamagnadṛkcetasaḥ |
vinamrasuradhoraṇīmukuṭamṛṣṭapādāśrayaḥ ,
punantu padapāṃśavaḥ sapadi kaṃsavidhvaṃsinaḥ ||

bhūrībhūtaṃ dvaitacintāturāṇām ekībhūtaṃ sārabhūtaṃ surāṇāṃ |
ātmībhūtaṃ gopasīmantinīnāṃ śyāmībhūtaṃ brahma nirvyājam īḍe ||

ārādhya yādavakiśoram atiprayatnād
abhyasya gotamamataṃ saha jaiminīyaṃ |
sāraṃ vivicya matayor anayor aśeṣaṃ
vācaspatir vviṣadayaty anumānamārggaṃ ||

svarūpataṣ kāraṇato viṣayāt phalataḥ kramāt |
svārthan tataḥ parārthañ ca kathopakaraṇānvitaṃ ||

atha pratyakṣamūlakatvāt tadanantaram anumānaṃ nirūpyate | tatrānumānam anumitikaraṇam anumititvan tu jātiḥ , anuminomīty anugatānuvyavasāyasākṣiketi prāñcaḥ || navyās tu ākāravyaṅgyajātivādivimatenānumititvaṃ lakṣaṇaṃ kin tu vyāptipakṣadharmmatāviśiṣṭajñānajanyā pramā ābhāsajanyā sadarthānumitiḥ , saṃṅgrāhyaiva(!) anumitis tatkaraṇam anumānaṃ | ābhāsāvyāptiś ca na doṣaḥ , tasyā[[m]] asaṃṅgrāhyatvāt(!) pramāṇanibandhādayo py evaṃ (fol. 1v1-6)

End

tathā hi tvaṃ cauraḥ puruṣatvād ity ukte uttaravādy āha tvam api puruṣatvāc caura iti , atra hi paroktaṃ svadoṣam anuddhṛtyābhyupetya taṃ parasyāpi prasañjayati anabhyupagame hi kathaṃ puruṣatvaṃ cauratve [[tva]](?) āpādakīkuryāt , asminn evānekāt , nanu tvam api caura iti vyabhicārodbhāvanaparaṃ saduttaram evedaṃ , tathā hi , puruṣatvaṃ na cauratvasādhakam anyathā tata eva tavāpi cauratvāpatter ity<ref>ac: itt</ref> evaṃ paratvād iti cet , na , anena ma(ttānu)jñāne (fol. 85v4-6)
