Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

svargāpavargavistārau sāmarasyodarodareḥ ||

bhavarogaharau vaṃde caṃḍikā candraśeṣarau (!) || 1 ||

rasoparasa lohānāṃ tailamūla viṣaiḥ saha ||

sa śādhya (!) pratyayopetaṃ kathyate rasa rasāya[[naṃ]] (!) || || 2 ||

vaidyānāṃ ca hitārthāya vyādhitānāṃ hitāya ca ||

vādināṃ kautukārthāya vṛddhānāṃ dehasiddhaye || 3 ||

maṃtriṇāṃ matraṃsiddhyarthaṃ (!) vividhāścaryakārakam ||

hato haṃti jarāmṛtyuṃ mūrcchito vyādhighātakaḥ || 4 ||

hale ca khegate baṃdhuḥ konyaḥ sūtāt kṛpākaraḥ ||

yāvat sūtaṃ na śuṃddhaṃ na ca gaganemṛtamūrcchitaṃ baṃdhaḥ (!) || 5 ||

(fol.1v1-4)

End

yāmāṃte pūrvavad dalpaṃ svayaṃ niryāti na svayaṃ ||

†sāvuṇī ṭaṃkaṇaṃ† tutthaṃ pūrvavan madhunā yutaṃ || 93 ||

tadvad vartiś ca piṇḍaṃ ‥‥svirālpaharaṃ bhavet ||

putraṃ jīvaphalāmajjātoyaiḥ piṣṭvā prapācayet || 94 ||

abhyaṃtaragataṃ haṃti hy asthisalyotthitaṃ viṣaṃ || ||

siddhe rasaiḥ sakalaroga nivāraṇāya

proktaṃ sajastam anumuya (!) hitaṃ narāṇāṃ ||

dhanvaṃtareḥ sakalakīrtikaraṃ nirīkṣya

mārātisārarasasāgarasiddhakhaṇḍaṃ || 1 || śrī || (fol.37v8-38r2)
