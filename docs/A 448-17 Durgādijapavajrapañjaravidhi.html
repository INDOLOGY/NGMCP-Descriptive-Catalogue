Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṁ namaḥ śrīdurggāyai ||

devā ūcuḥ ||

namo devi jagaddhātri, jagattrayamahāraṇe |

maheśvari mahāśakte, daityadrumakuṭhārike || 1 ||

trailokyavyāpini śive, śaṃkhacakragadādhari |

suśārṅgavyagrahastāgre, namo viṣṇusvarūpiṇe || 2 || (fol. 1r1–4)

End

tasyāpadāṃ sahasrāṇi nāśayiṣyanti devatāḥ |

kāśyāṃ yasyāsti vai prema tena kṛtvāpadāṃ guruṃ || 74 ||

śrotavyā(!)m idam ākhyānaṃ vajrapañjalasaṃjñitam ||      || (fol. 12v3–5)

Colophon

iti śrīskandapurāṇe kālīkhaṇḍe durggāvijayā nāma vajrapaṃjarastuti,dvisaptatitamo dhyāyaḥ || (fol. 12v5)
