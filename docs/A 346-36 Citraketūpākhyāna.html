Manuscript Features

Citraketūpākhyāna: One split into Two

This manuscript written with single hand has split into two parts as below:

A 346/15 – parts of Aṅkas 1–2, 4–6
A 346/36 – parts of Aṅkas 3–4

Both of them makes complete the manuscript. It is split in the following way:

Aṅkas 1–2: Exposures 15–28 (A 346/15)
Aṅkas 3–4: All of Exposures 35 (A 346/36)
Aṅkas 4–6: Exposures 1–13 (A 346/15)

The last parts of exp. 12 and exp. 13 fully are written with second hand.
