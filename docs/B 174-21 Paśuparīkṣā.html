Excerpts

Beginning

namaś caṇḍikāyai

caṇḍikākalpe

paśuparikṣāṃ (!) ⟪pra⟫vakṣyāmi. pūrvaṃ śaṃkarabhāṣitāṃ

pūrvvaṃ paśuṃ pari(!)kṣeta. paśurūpasvabhāvataḥ

caṇḍirūpā kathaṃ rāṣṭra(!) kathaṃ jayaparājayau

paśuyāgavidhiproktaḥ karmakāryaṃ(!) kathaṃ bhavet

kathaṃ doṣaguṇaṃ (!) deva. bhagavān vaktum arhasi (fol. 1v1–3)

End

bhairavyā saṃhitāyāñ ca pṛthag pṛthag udīritā

nirūpyatās (!) te na mayā graṃthagauravabhītita (!) 

dvijāty anupayogitvād a(!)dhikyāt syāhasasya(!) ca

tathā ca śudrakṛtyatvān mṛtau nityatayā tathā

etasyān aṃtaraṃ yad yat kṛtyaṃ tat tad dhruve dhunā

itthaṃm(!) uktavidhānena dattvā paśubalim budhaḥ (fol. 46r1–4)

Colophon

ity ādināthaviracitāyāṃ mahākālasaṃhitāyāṃ paśunirṇaya (!) samāptaḥ śubhaṃ (fol. 46r4–5)

tntracūḍāmaṇau ghaṇṭāpūjanamantro yathā

tato jayadhvanipadaṃ maṃtraṃ mātognigehinī(!)

ekādaśākṣaro maṃtro dhaṇṭādyāḥ sarvasiddhidaḥ

pūjayitvā svamaṃtreṇa ghaṇṭāntaṃ nādapūrvvakaṃ

dīpaṃ dadyāt tu vibhave nānā vādyapuraḥsaraṃ

āvāhanārghadhūpeṇa puṣpanaivedyadīpayoḥ

japakālaṃ vinānyatra kuryād asyāḥ pravādanaṃ

nānayā ca vinā pūjāṃ kārayet siddhilālasāḥ iti
