Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

śriyaṃ diśatu vo devaḥ śrīnābheyajinaḥ sadā

mokṣamārgaṃ satāṃ brūte yadāgama(2)padāvalī || 1 ||

śrīmān śrī (!) ādi[[nā]]thaḥ śriyam iha diśatu śreyasīṃ bhūyasīṃ vo

bibhrāṇaḥ saura(3)bheyaṃ hṛdayamataśivaḥ śaṃkaraḥ śaṃkabhaḥ (!) ||

sevyo (bhṛtyair) vibhūtyā parikalitatanuvarṇanīyo (!) (hyahī(4)nair)

bibhradvarṇaṃ suvarṇaṃ tuhinaśikhariṇo bhāti yaḥ kāmatejā || 1 ||

prabhāvavibhāvāvāsaṃ pratibhā(5)dānadakṣaṇaṃ (!) |

spṛśāmi śirasā saucaṃ gurupādarajaḥ kaṇaṃ || 2 ||

bālānām avabodhāya sva(6)syārthasmaraṇāya ca |

vāgbhaṭālaṃkṛteḥ kurvve vyākhyāṃ saṃkṣepayuktitaḥ || 3 ||

tatra śāstrasyāvi(7)ghnena parisamāptyarthaṃ śrībāhaḍadevo viśiṣṭeṣṭadevatānamaskāram āha || śriyaṃ diśatu || 1 || (fol. 1v1–7)

End

ataḥ kathaṃ etāvaṃta (8) evālaṃkārāḥ | ityāśaṃkyāṃtaram āha ||

arthena yenety ādi || yena a(9)rthena kāvyaṃ kṛtināṃ manassu aticamakaroti |

atyaṃtaṃ camatkārakāra(44v1)kaṃ bhavati | sa eva arthaḥ tasmin kāvye haṃta iti harṣe anayaiva diśā anenaiva ma(2)duktena prakāreṇa alaṃkāratvena abhyūhayaṃ vā || jñāyatāṃ etāvatā yatra kāvye yo(3) ʼrthaḥ āścaryakārī sa tatra alaṃkāratvena jñeyaḥ || 53 || (fol. 44r7–44v3)

Colophon

iti śrīkharataragache śrī(4)jinarājasūripaṭṭe śrījinavarddhanasūriviracitāyāṃ vāgbhaṭtālaṃkāraṭī(5)kāyāṃ caturthaḥ paricchedaḥ samāptaḥ || ❁ || śrī (!) || saṃvat 1756 varṣe māsamāghavi (!) tṛtīyāyāṃ bhṛgudine likhitaṃ bhaṭṭamaṃgalajisutabhaṭṭanārāyāyaṇas tena pu(6)stakaṃ alekhi || paṭhanārthaṃ || ❁❁ || ❁❁ || (fol. 44v3–7)
