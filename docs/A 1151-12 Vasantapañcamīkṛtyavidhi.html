Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

atha vasaṃtapaṃcamīkṛtyavidhiḥ || ||

tatrādau yajamānaḥ kṛtanityakṛ(!)ya upalipte prāṃgaṇe dīpaṃ prajvālya maṃḍapasyeśānabhāge kalasam ekaṃ sthāpayet tatsthāpanaprakāras tu oṁ bhūr asīti maṃtreṇa bhūmisaṃskaraṇaṃ oṁ dhānyam asīti dhānyaṃ saṃsthāpyājighrakalaśam iti tadupari abraṇaṃ bahir bhāge dadhyakṣata(!)vibhuṣitaṃ kalaśaṃ sthāpayitvā varuṇasyottaṃbhanam iti nirmalatīrthādijalaṃ pakṣipya oṁ yā oṣadhīḥ pūrvājāteti sarvauṣadhīḥ | (fol. 1v1–7)


End

evaṃ pūjāṃ nirvatya tato vasaṃtarāgaṃ brāhamaṇamukhena vā śāṃtamukhena śrutvā taṃ vastrādibhiḥ saṃtoṣya kalaśasthajalenāmrapallavenābhiṣiṃcet tato dakṣiṇāṃ dadyā (fol. 3v7–9)
