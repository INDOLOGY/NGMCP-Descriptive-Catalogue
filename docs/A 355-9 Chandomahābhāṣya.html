Beginning

///jñasya phalaṃ na hītyādivākair
asyādhyay yanamāvaśyakam | atha śrībhagavān vyāsaḥ
ṣaḍaṃghādau tatkarva praṇāmaṃ tat chāstropajīvyaparibhāṣṃ ca nibaddhavānityādau tadvayākhyā || mayarasatajabhanalagasaṃmmitam bhramati vaṅmayaṃ (!) jagati yasya || sa jayati piṃgalanāgaḥ śivaprasādāviśuddhamatiḥ ||
dvipādam iti dhvanyate || (fol. 2r1–4)

End

yatrargham na bhavāti tadā saikaṃ kṛētvārdham kāryaṃm
gurunirveśyaḥ evaṃ yāvasveṣṭākṣarapūrtiḥ |
yathā caturakṣarasya ṣaṣṭobhedsaḥ kīdṛṛśneṣadyamadhar me laghuḥ saikatrayārdhe guruḥ | dyor ardhe laghuḥ |
saika saika syārdhe guruḥ śūnyaśeṣe Iṣṭākṣara paryaṃtam gurubhiḥ puūraṇiyam ity artha /// (fol. 63v10–12)

Colophon
