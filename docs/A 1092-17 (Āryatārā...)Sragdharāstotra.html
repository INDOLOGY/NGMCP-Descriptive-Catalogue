Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīāryyatārāyaiḥ ||     ||

bālārkkālokatā supravala su(2)ragirā dhārucuḍāmaṇiśrīḥ

sampatsaṃparkarāgā naticiraraci(3)tā raktakavyaktabhaktī (!)

bhaktyā pādau tavārthakaraputakupatā prabha(4)gnottamāṅgaḥ

ṣṭāriṇyā (!) paṅkarandhena ca nutikusumaḥ śragbhira (!) (5) sarvvayāmī ||     ||

he āryyatārīṇī jipani sarana oya jogye ju(2r1)yāo bijyākahma bhakti bhāvana chalapolayā pādapadma nīpāsaṃ naolaci(2)ta stotra svarupa svāna mālāna jina pujā yāya dhakaṃ vandanā gālasa co(3)nahma sarvajñamitrayā ati duḥkha juyāo vinati yātaṃ chalapolayā (4) tuti pāli nipā gathiṅa dhālasā bālaṣa śrīsuryya khayatheṃ raktavarṇṇa (5) taodhaṃ devalokapani sila tao ati suṃdhara ratnayā svarūpa sa[[pati]](2v1)na silasa yama juo varṇṇa sthira juo alalayā varṇṇa samuhatheṃ juyāo (2) coṅāhma he janaṇī ji gathiṃhma dhālasā lāhāta nipāṃ gomunakāo si(3)lasa tasyaṃ kvachunāo coṅahma ||     || (fol. 1v1–2v3)

End

he janani chalapolayā guṇasam (4) (saṃgata) dayakā śloka punyana madhura vāṃchā phala lānāo, śrī 3 ā(5)ryyāvalokīteśvarayā vacanayā tale svasti cihmaṃ dayāo cona svakhā(37v1)vati bhuvanasaṃ svasti vyāpta juyāo cona thugu svakhāvati bhūvanasa (2) thva śloka yākahma conahmaṃ cokuhmaṃyāta sukhāvati bhū(3)vanasa julasāṃ thva totrayākapanista vāsa lāyamāla julaṃ || 17 (4) ||     ||

(fol. 37r3–37v3)

Colophon

iti śrīsarvajñamitra viracitaṃ śrī 3 āryyatārābhaṭṭari(5)kāśragdhārā

stuti saṃpūrṇṇa samāptaḥ || o ||

ye dharmmā hetu prabhava (38r1)hetu teṣāṃ tathāgata |

hevadatteṣāñ ca yo nirodha evaṃvādi mahā(2)śravaṇaṃ || o || (fol. 37v4–2)
