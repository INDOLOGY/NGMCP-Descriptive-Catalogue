Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇēśāyya namaḥ ||    ||

yatprakāśasukhābhinnaṃ yanmaṃtreṇa prakāśitaṃ || 1 ||
yajurveda śākhābheda taittirīyakopaniṣadaṃ vyācikhyāsu bhagavān bhāṣyakārastat pratipādyaṃ brahmajagajjanmādikaraṇatvena taṭasthālakṣaṇena maṃdamatīna pratisāmānyenopalakṣitaṃ satyajñānādinā ca svarūpalakṣaṇena viśeṣato viniścita namaskāra balena saṃkṣepato darśayati || (fol. 1v1–3)

oṃ yasmājjātaṃ jagatsarvaṃ yasminneva pralīyate ||
yenedaṃ dhāryate caiva tasmai jñānātmane namaḥ || 1 ||

yair ime gurubhiḥ pūrvaṃ padavākyapramāṇataḥ ||
vyākhyātā sarvavedāṃtās tānnityaṃ praṇatosmyahaṃ || 2 || (fol. 1v5–6)

End

niyamābhāvān na hi pratibaṃdhakṣayādeva vidyopadyatena tvīśvaraprasādatapodhyānādyanuṣṭhānāditi niyamostyahiṃsā brahmacaryādīnāṃ ca vidyāṃ pratyūpakārakatvātsākṣādeva ca kāraṇatvātśravaṇamanananididhyāyasanāmataḥ siddhānyāśramāṃtarāṇI sarveṣāṃ cādhikāro vidyāyāṃ paraṃ ca śreyaḥ kevalāyā vidyāyā eveti siddhaṃ || (fol. 22r8–10)

yaduktamitydinā |
karmaṇiyatnādhikyasyānyathāsiddhatvādvikalpavidhātakatvaṃ na saṃbhavaatītyarthaḥ | idānīṃ gṛhasthāśramakarmaṇāṃ bahiraṃgatvaṃ sanyāsāśrama karmaṇāṃ tvaṃ taraṃgavidyāsādhanatvamiti viśeṣaṃ darśayituṃ codyamudbhāvayati | karmanimitatvādityādinā | śāṃti dvayasyā paunarukyamāha śannomitra iti || tad paraṃbrahmamāmaparavidyārtrhinamāvīdarakṣat || (fol. 22r1,11)
