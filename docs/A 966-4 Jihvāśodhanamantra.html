Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||
atha jihvāśodhanaṃ ||
sarasvatītaṃtre || ||
aparaikaṃ(!) pravākṣyāmi mukhaśodhanam uttamaṃ ||
yad akṛtvā mahādevi japapūjā vṛthā bhavet || 1 ||
aśyddhajihvayā devi yo japet sa tu pāpakṛt ||
tasmāt sarvaprayatnena jihvāśodhanam ācaret || 2 ||
śrīdevy uvāca ||
devadevamahādeva śūlapāṇe pinākadhṛk ||
pṛthakpṛthak mahādeva kathayasva dayānidhe || 3 ||
śodhanaṃ sarvavidyānāṃ mukhasya vada me prabho ||
śrīmahādeva uvāca ||
mahātripurasuṃdaryyā mukhasya śidhanaṃ subhe ||
śrībījaṃ praṇavaṃ lakṣṃīṃ tāraṃ śrīpraṇavaṃ tathā ||
imaṃ ṣaḍakṣaraṃ mantraṃ sundaryyā daśadhā japet || 5 ||
bhairavyāḥ śṛṇu cārvaṅgi mukhaśodhanam uttamaṃ ||
praṇavaṃ ca hamauḥ bījaṃ praṇavaṃ suravandite || 6 || (1v1-7)

End

anyathā maṃtraviṭyuktā jihvā staṃbhati(!) sarvadā || (!)
bhakṣaṇe dūṣitā jihvā mithyāvakyena dūṣitā ||
kalahe duṣitā jihvā tat kathaṃ prajapen mantraṃ || tac chodhanam nācaryyana(!) japet pāmaraḥ kvacit || 20 || (!)
śaivaśāktavaiṣṇavādīnāṃ sarveṣām avaśyakatvam eva ||
anyathā prajapen mantraṃ mohena yadi bhāmini || 21 ||
sarvaṃ tasya vṛthā devi mantrasiddhir nna jāyate ||
ante narakam āpnoti satyam eva na cānyathā || 22 ||
devo yadi japen mantram akṛtvā mukhaśodhanaṃ ||
patanaṃ tasya vai devi kiṃ punar martyavāsināṃ || 23 || (2r9-2v5)

Colophon

iti jihvāśodhanamaṃtraḥ | oṃ namo bhagavate || + || śrīrāmacaṃdrāya namaḥ || (2v5)
