Manuscript Features

The manuscript contains two Śanaiścarastotras and at the end, on fol. 12r-12v, a Caṇḍikāstotra (incomplete), which begins: oṃ namaḥ caṇḍīkā(!)devīyaḥ(!) | mahālakṣmī mahādevī saubhāgyasurasūndarī(!) | vaidūryyapādukārūḍhā siṃhasanasana(!)sthitā ||

Missing folios: 5, 9, 13(ff).
