Excerpts

«Beginning»

svasti śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || āsyā rātrau ⌠⌠amukagotrasya⌡⌡ amukaśarmaṇo sakaladuritopasargāya dvāntīpūrvaka ⌠⌠saka⌡⌡lamanorathasiddhyarthe yathāsaktir gandhapuṣpadhupadipatāṃbulaṃ yajñopavitavastra⌠⌠pu⌡⌡ganaivedyādibhīraṃgadevatāpurvakapurvāṃgakṛtaśrīsatyanārāyaṇapujanamahaṃ kariṣye || (fol. 1v, 1–4)


«End»

janārdanaṃ janānaṃdaṃ jānakivallabhaṃ jayaṃ

praṇamāmi sadā satyanārāyaṇamataṃ paraṃ

durgamo viṣame ghore śatrubhiḥ paripiḍite |

vividhāpatsu duḥkheṣu tathānyeṣv api yad bhayāṃ

nāmānyetāni sakirtya ipsītaṃ phalam āpnuyāt ||

varadaṃ satyadevaṃ vaṃdehaṃ kāsaṃ prasama |

lilayanaṃtatāṃ viśvaṃ yena tasmai namo nama || (fol. 2v8–v3)
