Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ nṛtyanāthāya || ||

udyaccandrārddhacuḍo vidhara varajo dvīpacarmmottarīyo

bhūtapretādisaṅgo [vṛṣabhā]tanugato gaaura( 1r1)karppūrakāyaḥ |

pañcāsyo bhānetro ḍamaru japadharo maṅgalā jevanātho

hāḍāruṇḍāṅgadābho navarasakuśalaḥ pātuvo nṛtya(2)nāthaḥ || ||

atha gopīcandanāṭakaṃ likhete ||

jārokodhi ( 1v7)janamiyā bolāilo rāe ||

āilo he gvavindacanda baṃgero adhipati upanāpadumā laiyākeli karaṃti || ||(8)

iti gopīca(14v2)ndra prathamoṅkaḥ ||

End

mahārājādhirāja śrīśrīnivāśamalladeva ke śubhāśīrvvāda diyā sasta indriya samasta pāpa (52v1) śamana kariyā parambrahma cintā kariyā paramānandate śamādhi kariyā thākite jāivo || ||

khava koṇasa ||

yāvattakāra (2) pṛthivī āpa teja vāyu ākāśa candra sūryya thāke tāvatkāla paryyanta subhikṣahaiyā samasta prajāloka sukhe thāko amī śrī(3)ghra jāyavo || ||

Colophon

samvat 810 bhādrapada śukla trayodaśyāṃtithau vudhavāsare || li[[khi]]ta saṃpūrṇṇa miti || dvijavala śrīvi[[śva]]nā(52v4]thena ṛṣitaṃ śubhamastu sarvvadā || || ||

❖ halaḍa, kaśurī, kaḍavasī kīva, vaṃśaliocana, puskarāmura, karkkaṭasiṃ, samabhāga (5) kastiḥ bulāva nake sāthaṃthapaṅa mośoka loya rāvakhaṃ ||(6)

Added with different hand:

śrīśrī sumati jaya jitāmitramalladevasana thva pustaka dayakā || bhāgīrāma paramānayā verasa || śubhaṃ || (7)
