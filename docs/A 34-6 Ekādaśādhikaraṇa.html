Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śivāya ||

prayojanābhisandhānatvāt || vācābhyuccayalakṣaṇaṃ vṛttaṃ, tantrāvāpalakṣaṇaṃ varttayiṣyāmaḥ | tatra ke cit kāryatantratām anye prayājādipadānutantratām anye nuṣṭhānatantratām adhyāyā(n na) ityāhuḥ | tatrādau tantrāvāpa(!)nurūpam eva vivecanīyam | yadi sakṛttvam eva tantratvaṃ some dīkṣaṇīyādīnāṃ tatntratvaṃ syāt, athānekopakārakatve satya anekatvaṃ, tarhi prasaṅge pi tantratvapraṅgaḥ(!), anekaśeṣatve saty evam anekopakārakaṃ tantram iti cet, na, puroḍāśakapālenavyabhicārāt | anekoddeśenānuṣṭhānasakṛttvaṃ tantratvam iti cet, na, kathan tarhi kāryānuṣṭhānayos tantratvaṃ syāt (fol. 1v1–4)

End

tan nātmāśrita iti, tasmād utpattya pūrvvāśrita evopakāro jāyate, tadantagraharūpasyānyathāśritatvakalpanāyāṃ pramāṇābhāt, sa tv aneka eva jātivadanekāśrita iti ke cit, sannipātijanya x pratikaraṇaṃ bhidyate, tatsāhityasyaivopakārāt, vikṛtau bhedanātideśo bhavati | anye ⁅ca⁆ pratikaraṇaṃ bheda evāsya | ekaprakaraṇaśāstrāntatayā tv ekatvam ucyate | sannipātitvād upakārakābhyān tu, pratikaraṇaṃ bhinnā evopakāviśeṣā labhyante | yathā ca prakṛtivikṛtyor upakāravyatibhede pi sa evopa ity ucyate, yathā pratikaraṇaṃ bhede pi | (fol. 9v1–4)

Colophon

iti miśramahāmahopādhyāyaśrīmurārikṛtau ekādaśādyadhikaraṇaṃ samāptam iti || ||

mahopādhyāyaśrīratnākarātmajena vāgdevatārādhanapareṇa śrīharikareṇa likhitam idaṃ pustakam || (fol. 9v4–5)
