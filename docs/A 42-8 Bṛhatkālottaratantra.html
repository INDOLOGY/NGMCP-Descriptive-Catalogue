Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ paraśivāya ||

kārttikeya uvāca ||

sarvvajñāya namas tubhyaṃ sarvvakattre (!) namo stu te |

aviluptasvarūpāya cinmātrāya namo stu te ||

aviluptasvarūpāya cinmātrā〇ya namo stu te ||

pramānātītadehāya namaḥ śāntāya tejase |

satsavi(!)karaṇātītahānādānāntarātmane ||

anādinidhane〇śāya śivāya paramātmane |

namas tatvasamārūpāya (!) vibhāgāyā(!)mitātmane ||

jñāni(!)jñātṛtathājñeyavyatiriktā(!) vai namaḥ 〇 |

jñaptimātrasvarūpāya ānandabrahmaṇe namaḥ |

avidyāvyatiriktāya vidyādehāya vai namaḥ |

bhagavan devadeveśa sarvānugrahaśaṅkara |

kathitan tu mahātantra (!) vistareṇāśivāpahaṃ | (fol. 1v1–5)

End

śivabhaktivihīnāya gurubhaktivivarjjite |

nindānvite kumārgasthe dīrgha〇sūtre tathaiva ca |

na deyaṃ śivasadbhāvam anyāyapathavartine |

dātāro narakaṃ yānti yatrottāraṃ na vidyate |

yasmāt prayatnam āsthā (!) na deyaṃ yasya kasya cit |

śivaṃ gurutarīyaś ca guro bhaktiṃ suniścalā |

tasyājñā deyam idaṃ jñānaṃ nānyasya tu kadā cana || ○ ||

iti kālottare mahātantre aghorāstrādiśāstravyuṣṭiparipālanāpaṭalaṃ || || samāptaṃ cedaṃ bṛhatkālottaraṃ nāma 〇 śivasiddhāntam itiḥ || ❁ ||

(second-last folio, v5 – last folio, r2)

Colophon

śreyo stu || samvat 538 śrāvaṇakṛṣṇe | trayodaśyāyā tithau || punavasunakṣatre || , śu〇ddhiyoge || āditavāsareḥ || śrījuvarājāddhirājaśrīśrījayajyotimalladevasya rājyavijayarājye likhita pustakaṃ 〇 ||
