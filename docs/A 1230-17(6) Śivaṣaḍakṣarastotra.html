Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śṛīgurubhyo namaḥ || ۞ || (5)

❖ svetaṃ sveta vilepamāllavaśanaṃ vāmena raktotpalaṃ,

vibhratyā priya itare(6)ṇa tarasā śriṣṭaṃ prasannānanaṃ |

hastābhyāṃm abhayaṃ varaṃ ca dadhataṃ śambhuś ca rupaṃ pa(7)raṃ,

hālālohita locanotpala yugaṃ dhyāyac chirasthaṃ guruṃ || 1 || (exp. 18t4–7)

End

yatra tatra sthito deva yagatvyāpi maheśvara |

yo guru sarvvadā (9) vānāṃ yakārāya namo namaḥ || 6 ||

ṣadākṣaram idaṃ proktaṃ, yaḥ paṭhec chiva sannidhau | (10)

pāpaṃ tasye vinimuktaṃ śivaloke mahiyate || 7 || (exp. 21b8–10)

Colophon

iti ṣaḍākṣarastotaṃ samāptaṃḥ || (exp. 21b10)
