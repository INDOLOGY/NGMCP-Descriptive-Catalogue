Manuscript Features

The text runs from the beginning of the Avyayībhāvasamāsa chapter to the end of the Samāsāśrayavidhi chapter. Sutras and vārttikas are highlighted in another ink.

The handwriting of this manuscript and of fols. 9r–39v (first foliation) of reel nos. A 560/10 (Inventory no. 28579) and A 560/11 (Inventory no. 28583) seems to be the same, and there is continuation of text from 10 to 11 to the present manuscript.
