Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||     ||

atha śavasādhanam ||     ||

lakṣmīvināyakaṃ vaṃde sānaṃdaṃ vighnanāśanaṃ

somapriyaṃ śaśidharaṃ devadevaṃ jagadgurum ||     ||

aṣṭamyāṃ caturddaśyāṃ vā kujavāre prathamapraharābhyaṃtare.

guruṃ devīṃ ca natvā viśveśvarasya yātrāṃ kuryyāt ||

śūnyāgāre nadītīre parvate nirjjanasthale vilvamūle śamaśāne śamaśānasamīpe raṇasthale vā aṣṭamyāṃ caturddaśyāṃ vā bhaumavāre niśi uttamaṃ siddhisādhanaṃ kuryāt || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

tataḥ snātvā gṛhaṃ gatvā dināṃtare rātrau /// -śūkarānyatamo [[yo]] yācitasthitas taṃ

piṣṭamayaṃ kṛtvā dadyāt | valimaṃtras tu | agrimarātra- /// -ṣāṃ yajamāno haṃte imaṃ valiṃ gṛhṇaṃtu || piṣṭamayaṃ valiṃ datvopoṣaṇaṃ kṛtvā parehninityakri- /// kṛtvā paṃcagavyaṃ pītvā vibha[[vā]]nusāreṇa paṃcaviṃśatisaṃkhyākān daśasaṃkhyākān saptasaṃkhyā (fol. 10v3–6)
