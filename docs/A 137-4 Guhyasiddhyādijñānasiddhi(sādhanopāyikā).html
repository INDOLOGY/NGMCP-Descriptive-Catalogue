Manuscript Features

Available folios 3–20.

The NGMPP catalogue card is not available for this reel number.

There are two exposures of fols. 10v–11r.

Fols. 19v and 19r are in reverse order.

In the NAK catalogue card the reel number of this entry is mentioned as A 1012/5. But the reel no. A 1012/5 contains the different text the 'Varāhapurāṇa'.

The real reel number of this entry is A 137/4 and it is microfilmed in reel number A 112/5 as well. We could know it from the Accession Number.

Excerpts

Beginning

varddhacetasa[ḥ]

prāpnuvanti na te satvās tat padaṃ siddhidam padan

anye pi cātra dṛśyante paryyupāśya gurū(!) dṛḍhan

praṇāmapūjāsatkārair yyāvat prāptaṃ samīhitan

prāpte tu tatpade divye purato pi vyavasthitaṃ

na jānanti durātmānaḥ kāyaṃ kasmād ihāgataḥ

dṛṣṭvāpy a(!)kākinan dūre praṇāmaṃ kurvvate dṛdha(!)n

bahu(!)nān tu punar madhye svāgate pi daridratā (fol. 3r1–3)

End

pūjām asyaiva kāyasya kūryyāt nityaṃ samāhitaḥ

ma⟪chi⟫kṣikāchardisanmiśrai[r] †vidyutrādyaiś† bhāvitaiḥ

paṃcapradīpasaṃyuktaiḥ pūjayed vajradhāriṇaṃ

†śravatvāsvayas† tu kusumaiḥ sakṣīraviśvasambhavaiḥ

pūjayed devatāṃ tena dehasthāntatvabhāvanaiḥ

parasvaharaṇaṃ kuryāt paradārā (fol. 20v9–11)
