«Beginning»


śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||


mūlenācamya mūlena prāṇān āyamya huṃ phaṭ iti daśadigbandhaḥ ||


mūlena trir vyāpakaṃ kuryāt || choṭi o || hasakṣamalavarayūṃ bhītiguruṃ śirasā dhyātvā ||


nyāsaṃ kuryāt ||


asya śrīnakulīvāgīśvarīmaṃtrasya mahācamasakaholakahola ṛṣiḥ ||


śrīnakulīvāgīśvarī devatā || anuṣṭup chaṃdaḥ || aiṃ bījaṃ || sauḥ śaktiḥ ||


kliṃ kīlakaṃ || nakulaī(vāgīśvarī)prasādasidhyarthe jape viniyogaḥ ||


mūlena karau saṃśodhya || mūlena trir vyāpakaṃ kuryāt ||


sasarparīramatiṃ bādhamānā bṛhanmimāyajamadagnidattā ||


pādādijānuparyaṃtaṃ nyaset || āsūryasya duhitā tatāna


śravo deveṣv amṛtam ajuryaṃ || jānvādinābhiparyaṃtaṃ nyaset || (fol. 1v1–2r2)


«End»


oṁ namo bahgavate malayakālabhairavāya bhūtapretapiśācoccāṭanāya


yaṃtrarakṣaṇāyāṣṭamahābhairavāya śākinī ḍākinī chiṃdhi 2


paramaṃtraprayaṃtraparadvaṃdvaparahomaparajapaparavādān chiṃdhi 2


navanāthasiddhabhūtabhairavyāya huṃ phaṭ svāhā || 12 ||


tāraṃ madhye svasādhyaṃ vasupuṭayugalaṃ cālikhet koṇaṣaṭke


vāgbījaṃ kesarāṇām aca iti yugaśo maṃtravarṇān daleṣu ||


halbhiḥ prollāsivṛttaṃ vṛttaṃ vikṛtalipiyutai rakṣarair veṣṭitaṃ bhūs


tad yaṃtraṃ nākulākhyaṃ vimalataravacaḥ śrīpadaṃ khedahāri || 1 ||


jagadaṃba (fol. 7v9–8r9)
