Manuscript Features

In exps. 6, 7 and 8 there is a portion of a text, which is written in Newari language.

Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namostu te mahāgauri raktāṃgī raktavāsinī |

prātarvvāninirūpā ca madhyāhne yauvanī smṛtā ||

saṃdhyāyāṃ vṛddhā rūpā ca jagadvyāpi namostu te |

kālarātri tvam evāsi viṣṇumāyā tvam eva hi ||

tvam eva devi prakṛtiḥ viśvaṃ vyāpya vimohitā ||

sarvvavyāpini tvaṃ devi parāparamikā (!) śive || (exp. 4t1–3)

End

śyāmā raktā śaktihastā mayūrā brahmacāriṇī |

kaumārībālarūpī ca namastestu pare śive ||

aparādhasahasrāṇi kriyate [ʼ]harnniśaṃ mayā |

dāśoyam iti māṃ matvā kṣamasva parameśvarī ||

sarvvamaṃgalamaṃgale || (exp. 5t5–8)

Colophon

iti rudrajāmale (!) kumārīmahāmāyāstotraṃ samāpta (!) || (exp. 5t8)
