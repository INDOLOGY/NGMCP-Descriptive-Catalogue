Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

vāsudevaṃ ramākāṃtaṃ natvā laugākṣibhāskaraḥ ||

kurute jaimininaye praveśāyārthasaṃgraham || 1||


atha paramakāruṇiko bhagavān jaiminir dharmavivekāya dvādaśalakṣaṇīṃ praṇināya || tatrādau dharmajijñāsāṃ sūtrayāmāsa || athāto dharmajijñāseti ||
