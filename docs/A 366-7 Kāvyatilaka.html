Beginning

///setikrameṇa dīpter ādhikyaṃ śuṣketyādivīraraudrādāv ojaḥ saṃbhedena śuṣkeṃdhanāgnivat śṛṃgārakaruṇādau mādhuryyasaṃbhedena svacchajalavat yo guṇotpaṭityeva (!) (2) citaṃ vyāpnoti sa prasādo guṇaḥ na caivaṃ hāsyabhayānakādbhuteṣu prasādamātraguṇeṣu guṇāṃtarasaṃbhedābhāvāc citavyāpanaṃ (!) na syād iti vācyaṃ hāsye śṛṃgāravibhāvādipra(3)bhavatayā mādhuryyasya vikāśadharmmatayā vīrādiprabhavatayā ca ojasaś ca satvāt bhayānakādbhutayoś ca vīrabībhatsaśṛṃgāravibhāvādiprabhavatayaujaso mādhuryyasya (4) ca satvād

śṛṃdārād dhi bhaved dhāsyo raudrāc ca karuṇo rasaḥ

vīrādyair adbhutotpattir bībhatsāt tu bhayānaka iti (fol. 2r1–4)

End

vastutaḥ kāvyaṃ lakṣyate (7) svarūpato viśeṣataś ca jñāpyate neneti kāvyalakṣaṇaṃ prakṛtagraṃtha iti śivaṃ pratipadam upanīyājñātapūrvaṃ nigarvaṃ vyaraci tilakam etac cā(8)ha kāvyaprakāśe ||

maṇiviśadasuvarṇaṃ yuktimuktāvadātaṃ

ramayatu padapadamāsaṃgivāgdevatāyāḥ

maṇivad viśadasuvarṇaṃ

tilakaṃ kṛtam atra (9) yuktimuktābhiḥ

(mūrddhā) ye paridadhate

bhavabhūṣaṇatāṃ vrajaṃti te dhanyāḥ |

baddha (!) śrījayarāmeṇa rāmeṇeva mahodadheḥ

alaṃkārāṃbudhau setuḥ saṃ(10)caraṃtu budhāḥ sukhaṃ ||

alaṃkāranibaṃddho (!) ʼyaṃ racito nyāyavistaraiḥ

nyāyālaṃkāranipuṇaiḥ kṛpayā parigṛhyatāṃ || ||   || ❁ ||  ||   || (11) ||  || || (fol. 69v6–11)
