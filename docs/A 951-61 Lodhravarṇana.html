Excerpts

Beginning

śrī ❖

lodhako varṇana

lodhro rodro bhillataruś cillakaḥ kāṃḍakīlakaḥ

tirīṭo lodhrako vṛkṣa śaṃbaro hastirodhrakaḥ

tilakaḥ kāṃḍahīnaś ca śāvaro hemapuṣpakaḥ

bhillī śāvarakaś caiva prokto rājanighaṃṭake

śāvarī tilvakatarus tathā śāvarapādapaḥ

tirīṭakastathā prokto dhanvantarinighaṃṭake

kānīna paṭṭikāś cillī keyadevanighaṃṭake

tathā madanapāle tu satatodbhava nāmakaḥ

tilyo gālava saṃjñaś ca prokto bhāva prakāśake

gaṇe śukraḥ śāvaraś ca mārjano ʼmarakośake (exp. 1a1–12)

End

(4) lodha, candana, pipalāmūla, atisa, inko cūrṇa cini, maha, ghyu, dūdha, mā māthi khānu jīrṇa jvara śānta huncha

(5) lodhra, indra jau, pipalā, cutro, śuṭho, kuṭakī, ṭhulo kuḍoko bokrā inko kvātha ra kalka milāi ghyu pakāunu yo ghyu siddha bhaepachi sevana garnāle saba prakārako atisāra nāśa huncha shī(!) udauadeva (exp.1a48–54)
