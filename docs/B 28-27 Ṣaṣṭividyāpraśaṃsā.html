Excerpts

«Beginning»

❖ oṁ namas tripurāyai ||

vidyutpuñjanibhāṃ namāmi satataṃ prāgbhārasūṣmāṃ(!) girāṃ

mūrtyābhagnamṛṇālatantunibhayā nirbhidyaliṅgatrayaṃ |

prāpyordhvañ ca visarggabhāgamukulaṃ pīyūkhadhārārasaṃ

pītvā vidgatacuddrumadravamayīṃ śaktiṃ parāṃ kālikāṃ ||

surāsurabhujaṅgādyair munīndraiḥ siddhakinnaraiḥ |

pādāṅgam arccitaṃ yasyāḥ tāṃ nityāṃ sarvva⌠⌠dā⌡⌡ namaḥ ||

aikārāsanam ārūḍhāṃ vyomavibhramamālinīṃ |

nityānandaparādevīṃ nityānityavarapradāṃ || (fol. 1v1–5)


«End»

ṣe(!)carī bhūcarī caiva tṛtīyā khecarī tathā |

dadate sādhaken(drāṇāṃ) iti devi na śaṃsayaḥ(!) ||

aṣṭasiddhiṃ prayacchanti tasyaiva kuladevatāḥ |

etat te kathitaṃ devi bhuktimuktiphalapradaṃ ||

ayogyasya na dātavyam ityājñā pārameśvari ||

iti ṣaṣṭhaḥ paṭalaḥ ||❁ || (fol. 15r1–3)

«Colophon»

iti rudrajā(!)male sapādalakṣagranthe vidyāpīṭhāvatāre uttarabhāgini(!)tyuktaṃnaprakaraṇe ṣaṣṭividyāpraśamsāyām uttaraṣaṭkaṃ samāptam iti || ❁ ||

likhitam iti tapodhanaśrīviśveśvareṇa yuvarājaśrījayāryuṇadevasyārthena likhitaṃ alpagranthakāreṇa (vistaraḥ) saṃ 479 śrāvaṇakṛṣṇadaśamī śivaṃ bhavatu || (fol. 15r3–6)
