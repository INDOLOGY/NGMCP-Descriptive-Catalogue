Beginning

śrīsarasvatye[[na]] (!) namaḥ || || 

oṃ hrīṃ śrīṃ vāgvādini svāhā oṃ aiṃ hirṃ vada vada vāgvādinī svā(2)hā oṃ siddhiṃ oṃ śṛṅgaṃ iti maṃtreṇa pūgīphalam (!) abhimaṃtrya pṛcchakahaste datvā paścā(3)d iti vadet || he pṛchaka pūgīphalaṃ (!) nikṣipyaiteṣāṃ paṃcakaṇāmadhye nikṣipa(4)ṇaṃ (!) punar dvitīyapaṃcake nikṣipya tāṃ ubhayor aṃkayor melane kṛte yadyekādaśa bha(5)vaṃti 11 tadā bho pṛchaka avadhāraya tava vāṃchitārtho bhaviṣyati na saṃdeha |  (fol. 1v1–5)

End

nigaditam ṛ(3)ṣivākyāt sāram etad rahasyaṃ

sakalasakunasārajñānasāre vivekiṃ ||

pha(4)ladharasahito yaḥ pṛcchate nirvikalpaṃ

bhavati sakalasatyaṃ nānyathām eti satyam || (fol. 3r12–4)

Colophon

|| iti śrīkeralīpraśnaḥ | samāptaḥ śubham astu || || || ||  (fol. 3v5)
