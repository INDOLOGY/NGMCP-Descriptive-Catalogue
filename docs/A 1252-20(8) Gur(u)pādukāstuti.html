Excerpts

Beginning

herambaṃ kṣetrapālaṃ ca bāgīśaṃ(!) vaṭukaṃ tathā

śrīgurūnātham ānaṃdabhairavaṃ bhairavīpriyaṃ 1

praṇaṃmya saṃvin (6) mārgasthān nāgam ajñān guruttamān

prāyaścittaṃ pravakṣāmi sarvvataṃtrā virodhata 2

sādhakānāṃ ca putrāṇāṃ (7) dīkṣitānāṃ śivāgame

yoginībīramelāpe(!) pramāda sulabha khalu 3 (exp. 94b, ll. 4-7)

End

śrīguropādukāstrotraṃ prātar uthāya yaḥ paṭhet

nasyaṃti sarvvapāpāni vahninā tū(3)larāśivat 29

paṭhitavyaṃ prayatnena śrotavyaṃ bhaktiśālibhi

bhogamokṣārthabhis tasmāt sadtri(4)rāgapāragai 30 (exp. 96b, ll. 2-4)

Colophon

iti śrīguropādukāstuti(!) ||

ārogyaṃ bhavad dhetā tata vakṛta saphalaṃ bhavaḥ (5)

premanidhis tu vacanād dṛḍhabhaktiḥ prajñāyate

idaṃ pustakaṃ likhitaṃ śrīpremanidhiḥ ṭaṃkapā(6)ṇisya

paṭhanārthaṃ siddhir astu kalyāṇam astu śubha bhūyāt || śrī || (exp. 96b, 4-6)
