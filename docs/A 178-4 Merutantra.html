Manuscript Features

On the front page it is written:

svasti śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || śrīdevyai namaḥ || sarasvatyai namaḥ || rāmacandramanmaṅgalamūmurttaye namaḥ || śrīgaṇeśo jayati ||    ||

Excerpts

Beginning

|| śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||    ||

yayā sat sad ivābhāti na ca bhāti kadā[pi] sat |
yasmin jñāne na sā bhāti tasyai tasmai namo namaḥ | 1 |

jalaṃdhareṇa vijite surāsuranare khile |
sūkṣmarūpeṇa taiḥ sarvaṃ maheśaṃ śaraṇaṃ gatāḥ | 2 |

divyāṇy abdasahasrāṇi samādhisthaṃ vilokya taṃ
prārthitā cārccitā tais tu vāmadakṣiṇamārgibhiḥ | 3 | (fol. 1v1–3)

End

kuṃbhodakena karttāram abhiṣiṃcen manuṃ smaran |
karttā ca dakṣiṇāṃ dadyāt puṣkalāṃ toṣahetave |

palānāṃ tu daśāṃśena mukhyaṃ brāhmaṇabhojanaṃ |
viṃśatyaṃśea madhyaṃ syāc chatāṃśenāparaṃ smṛtaṃ |

iti bho kathitaṃ devā dīpadānaṃ mahīkṣituḥ |
ataḥ paraṃ kiṃ vaktavyaṃ tat pṛcchata surottamāḥ || (fol. 232v9–10)

Colophon

iti śrīmahāmāyāmahākālānumate merutaṃtre śivapraṇīte kārttavīryyamaṃtrakathanaṃ nāma paṃcatriṃśaḥ prakāśaḥ || 35 ||
merau śīvapraṇīte smin meruṇā prakaṭīkṛte |
merubhūte ca taṃtrāṇāṃ mahīśam anunirṇayaḥ ||    ||
śrīgurūśrīdakṣiṇāmūrtti prītir astu ||    || (fol. 323v11–12)

Sub-colophon

iti śrīmahāmāyānumate merutaṃtre śivapraṇīte vyavasthāprakāśaḥ prathamaḥ || 1 || (fol. 3v11)

iti śrīmahāmāyāmahākālānumate merutantre śivapraṇīte saṃskāraprakāśo dvitīyaḥ || 2 || (fol. 9r11–12)
