Excerpts

Beginning

|| śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

yatkṛpāleśamātreṇa puruṣārthacatuṣṭayaṃ ||

prāpyate tam ahaṃ vaṃde goviṃdaṃ bhaktavatsalaṃ || 1 ||

anaṃtaguṇa(2)saṃpannam anaṃtabhajanapriyaṃ ||

anaṃtarūpiṇaṃ vaṃde gurum ānaṃdarūpiṇaṃ || 2 ||

iha khalu paramakāruṇikena bhagavatā jaimi(3)niṛṣiṇā athāto dharmajijñāsety ādinā dvādaśasvadhyāyeṣu dharmo vicāritaḥ || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

yat karoṣi yad aśnāsi yaj juhosi dadāsi yat

yat tapasyasi kauṃteya tat kuruṣva mad arpaṇam iti (9)smṛteḥ |

asyāś cāṣṭākādismṛtivat (!) prāmāṇyād ity atra vistāraḥ (!) |

kvāhaṃ maṃdamatiḥ kveyaṃ prakriyā bhaṭṭasaṃmatā ||

tasmād bhakter vilāso ʼyaṃ goviṃdaguru(10)pādayoḥ || 1 ||

graṃtharūpo madīyo ʼyaṃ vāgvyāpāraḥ suśobhanaḥ ||

anena prīyatāṃ devo goviṃdo bhaktavatsalaḥ || 2 || ❁ || || (fol. 47v8–10)

Colophon

iti śrīanaṃtadevasununā (!) āpa(11)devena kṛtaṃ mīmāṃsānyāyaprakāśasaṃjñakaṃ mīmāṃsaprakaraṇaṃ samāptaṃ || ❁ || śrīgurudevadatta || || śubhaṃ bhavatu || ❁ || 〇 || 〇 || 〇 || 〇 || (fol. 47v10–11)
