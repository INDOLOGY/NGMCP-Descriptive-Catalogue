Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||     ||

atha du(!)rvāṣṭamīvratam ||

tatra prayogaḥ || śuklāṃbaradharam iti viṣṇuṃ prārthya | oṁ viṣṇuḥ 3 adyahetyādi | amukīdevī(!)haṃ | akhaṃḍasaubhāgyaphala(!)vāptaye putrapautrādivṛddhyarthaṃ adyārabhya śarīrāvasānaparyaṃtaṃ du(!)[r]vāṣṭamīvratam ahaṃ kariṣye

idaṃ vrataṃ mayā deva kṛtaṃ prītyai tava prabho ||

nirvighnaṃ siddhim āyātu tvatprasādāt sureśvara⟨ḥ⟩ || (fol. 1r1–3)

End

aṣṭamyāṃ phalapuṣpaiś ca kha[r]jūrair nārikelakaiḥ ||

drākṣāmalakapitthaiś ca vadarair lakucais tathā ||

nāraṃgair jatukari āmrair bījapūraiś ca dāḍimaiḥ ||

dadhyakṣataiḥ (dhva)jābhiś ca dhūpair naivedyadīpakaiḥ ||

maṃtreṇā[[ne]]na rājeṃdra śṛṇuṣvāvahi (fol. 1v8–9)
