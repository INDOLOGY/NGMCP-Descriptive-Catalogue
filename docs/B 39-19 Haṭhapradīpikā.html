On the exposure 3t is available stanzas

❖ śrī gaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

taḍillekhātanvīṃ tapanaśaśivaiśvānaramayīṃ

niṣaṇṇāṃ ṣaṇṇāmapyuparikamalānān tava kalāṃ ||

mahāpadmāṭavyāmuditamalayā yena manasā

mahāntaḥ paśyanto dadhati paramāhlādalaharīṃ || 1 ||

❖ jyotirāndam (!) ākāśaṃ kepi sūnya (!) vadanti ca |

cakṣuṣio (!) pi na paśyanti cātmāna (!) dehasaṃsthitaṃ || ātmā dṛśyate cātmā-
