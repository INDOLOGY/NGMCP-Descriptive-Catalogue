End

idam atiśayaguhyaṃ nisṛtaṃ vyāsavaktrād

ruciratarapurāṇaṃ prītaye vaiṣṇavānāṃ |

ciram amarasamūhair arcitāṃghrer murāres

sakalabhuvanabhartuś cakriṇaḥ prītaye stu || (fol. 168r)

Colophon

samāpto yaṃ purāṇaikadeśaḥ kriyāyogasārākhyo graṃthaḥ ||    ||

vilokyārthadṛṣṭau ṣaḍādarśabhūtāny alekhītareśāṃ mayā pustakāni |

namo bhagavate vāsudevāya || namo rāmāya || (fol. 168r)
