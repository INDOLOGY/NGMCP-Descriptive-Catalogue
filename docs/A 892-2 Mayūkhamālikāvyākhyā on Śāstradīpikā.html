Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

athātaḥ ‥ ‥ paruṣārthayor (!) jijñāsāhetuhetumadbhāvasaṃgatyād doṣalakṣaṇānaṃtaraṃ prayuktilakṣaṇam ārabhyata iti prasiddha iti paryasmi(2)n naṣṭā ye padārthānāṃ śeṣaśeṣībhāve siddhesaty ayaṃ padād audanenān utpādyate 'yamanetyetyevam asmin adhyāye ’nuṣṭhāyanāparapayopa‥ yukti ha‥ te ity arthaḥ prasiddhe śeṣaśeṣitve ityane(3)na vivakṣitasya hetuhetumadbhāvasya śarūpā 'nupapattimatvā samarthayate (fol. 1v1–3)

End

puroḍāśayor upāśupāte guṇatvaṃ saṃbhavati ta(9)smāt puroḍāśau kevalam upalakṣaṇatayo pādīyate tataś ca tad upasahita kāla eva guṇaḥ sacopalakṣaṇāyāpāye pyanayetaity ekapuroḍāśāpāta (!) pi syāt yathā śaṃkhavelā(10)yām āgaṃtavyam iti kālaviśeṣopalakṣaṇatayāt te śaṃkhadhvenau (!) kvacid dine devād akṛte pi tad upalakṣītakālasyānayāpyavagamyate tadvad ity artha (!) ||    || (fol. 103r8–10)

Colophon

iti śrīnidi(11)lakulatilakasurubharaṇyahopādhyāyatanabhavasya (!) vekatādiyajvagurucaraṇānupjasya (!) somanāthayajvanaḥ kṛtau śāstradīpikāvyākhyāyāṃ ma(yū)khamallikākhyā(12)yāṃ daśamasyādhyāyasyāṣṭamaḥ pādaḥ ||    || śubha || (fol. 103r10–12)
