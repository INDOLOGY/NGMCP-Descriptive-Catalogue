Excerpts

Beginning

rakṣitātmā cayo rājā rakṣyān yaś cānurakṣati ||

prajāś ca tasya bardhante dhruvaṃ ca mahad aśnute || 18 ||


yasya yodhāḥ susaṃtuṣṭāḥ sāṃtvitāsūpatarpitāḥ ||

alpenāpi sadaṃḍena mahīṃ jayati pārthiva || 3 || || (fol. 1v1–2r2)


End

saṃdhyām upāsya śṛṇuyāc cārāṇāṃ gūḍhabhāśi(!)taṃ ||

gītanṛtyaiś ca bhuṃjīta paṭhet svādhyāyam eva ca || 1 ||


rājadharme yathā sarvāṇi bhūtāni dharādhārayate samam

tathā sarvāṇi bhūtāni bibhrataḥ pārthivaṃ vratam 1


āpaddharme bhīṣmaḥ yasya traivārṣikaṃ bhaktaṃ paryāptaṃ bhṛtyavṛttaye adhikaṃ cāpi vidyate sa

somaṃ pātum arhasi 1 (10v1–23v2)
