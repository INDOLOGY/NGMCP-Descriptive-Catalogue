Manuscript Features

The preserved part of the MS containsVidagdhamukhamaṇḍana 1.56–2.5 and part of 2.6. It seems to have been prepared by the same scribe who wrote the other MS of the Vidagdhamukhamaṇḍana in this MTM (see A 22/01b). The two folios are not numbered; however, the symbol x is added in the middle of the right-hand margin of what may be taken to be the verso sides (and were microfilmed as such).
