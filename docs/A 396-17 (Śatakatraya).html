Beginning

|| oṃ || śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

yāṃ cintayāmi satataṃ mayi sā viraktā
sā pyanyam ichati janaṃ sa janonyasaktaḥ ||
asmat kṛte ca parituṣyati kācid anyā
dhik tāṃ ca taṃ ca madanaṃ ca imāṃ ca māṃ ca || 1 ||

ajñaḥ sukham ārādhyaḥ sukhataram ārādho(!) viśeṣajñaḥ ||
jñānalavadurvidagdhaṃ brahmāpi bhartṛ (!) naraṃ na raṃjayati || 2 || (fol. 1v1–2r1)
