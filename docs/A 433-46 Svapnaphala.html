śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

svapnādhyāyaṃ pravakṣāmi (!) yathā keśamabhāṣitaṃ ||

dvitīye cāṣṭabhir māsais tri(3)bhir māsais triyāmakeḥ (!) ||
