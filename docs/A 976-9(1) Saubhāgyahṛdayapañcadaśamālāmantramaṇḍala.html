○ || nārāyaṇākhyanāmeṃduḥ pā ○ || tu māṃ satataṃ paraṃ || 1 ||

|| kiṃ kiṃ duḥkhaṃ danujadalane kṣīyate na smṛtāyāṃ

kā kā kīrttīḥ kulakamalini vyāpyate na stutāyāṃ ||

kā kā siddhiḥ suravaranute prāpyate nārcitāyāṃ

kaṃ kaṃ yogaṃ tvayi na labhate cittam ālaṃbitāyāṃ || 1 ||

○ || śrīpaṃcadaśamālā || śrī

graṃthasaṃkhyā 4000

saṃvat 1766 śrāvaṇaśuklapaurṇamāsyāṃ tithau bhaumavāsare paṃcadaśamālāpustakaṃ || lekhakahastena lekhayitvā dakṣiṇāṃ ru. |||.) datvā saṃgṛhītaṃ śubhadaṃ varadaṃ bhavatu ||

śrīmannārāyaṇadaivajñeneti jñeyaṃ ||
