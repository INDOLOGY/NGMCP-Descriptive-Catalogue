gandharvvā liṣitas totra sūnavyādhamahāstapam ||

ārūḍhārdevyavimānaṃ(!) gacha(!) vāsāsanaṃ mavyādhyā(!)
svargagato dṛṣṭvā dharmako manavismayaṃ gata ||
