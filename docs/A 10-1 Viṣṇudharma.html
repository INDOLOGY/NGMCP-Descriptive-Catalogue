Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namo bhagavato(!) vāsudevāya ||

nārāyanan namaskṛtya naraṃ caiva narottamaṃ |

devīṃ śarāśva〇tī caiva tato jayam udīrayet ||

kṛtābhisekaṃ tanayaṃ rājña pārikṣa(!) hi |

draṣṭum abhyāyayuḥ prītyā śauna〇kādyā maharṣayaḥ ||

tān āgatāṃ sa rājarṣiḥ pādyārghair ahirarcitāṃ |

sukhopaviṣṭaviśrāntān kṛtasaṃpraśnasatkṛtān ||

tatkathābhir(!) kṛtāhlāda(!) praṇipatya kṛtāṃjaliṃ |

satānīko〇 ʼtha prapaccha nārāyanakathaṃ(!) purān ||

yam āśritya jagannātha mama pūrvvapitāmahāḥ |

vipakṣāpahṛtaṃ rājñaṃ(!)〇 avāpuḥ puruṣottamāt || (fol. 1v1–2)

End

sarvā bādhā tathā pā〇pam akhilaṃ manujeśvaraḥ |

viṣṇudharmo vyayo hanti saṃsmṛtāḥ paṭhitāḥ śrutāḥ ||

etat te sarva〇m ākhyātaṃ rahasya(!) parama(!) hare(!) ||

nātaḥ parataraṃ kiṃ cic chravyaṃ śrutisukhāvaha(!) |

atroktavidhiyuktasya puruṣasya vipaścitaḥ ||

na durlabhaṃ naravyāghra paramaṃ brahma śāśvatam〇 (fol. 126r5–v1)

Colophon

viṣṇudharmeṣu śāstramahātmya(!) | mahādharmaśāstre śatatasāhasryāsaṃhitāyāṃ(!) dānadharmmajapaniyama〇svādhyāyādiṣu viṣṇudharmānukīrttanaṃ nāma namo namaḥ ||    ||

viṣṇudharme(ṣv a)mī vṛttāḥ kṛyāprayogapravṛttiḥ acyutāmbarīṣasamvādaḥ ||    ||〇

sugatīdvādaśī || etc. (fol. 126v1–2)

vaivasvatagāthā || puṣpādivibhāgaḥ || māndhātarājyāprāpti(!)hetutrivikramavrataḥ ||

samāptaṃ cedaṃ viṣṇudharmam iti ||    || oṃ namo bhagavate vāsudevāya ||    || (fol. 127r2–3)
