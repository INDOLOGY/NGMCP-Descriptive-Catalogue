Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namaḥ śivāya ||

śrīkulagaṇādhipataye namaḥ ||    ||

///
nandaśaktisubhāṃ mā sṛṣṭinyāye catuṣkam akulakulagataṃ pañcakaṃ cānya ṣaḍkaṃ ||
catvāraḥ paṃcako nyaḥ punar api caturaḥ ṣoḍaśājñāṃ bhiṣekaṃ (!) . . . . . . mūrtimadhye . . . . . . . . puṣpaṃ svamudrāḥ ||
kālaṃ kaumāravṛddhaṃ paramaśivakalā cakradevīkramāṇāṃ ||
śrīnāthaṃ candrapuryāṃ nava nava kalitaṃ yugmabhedas tu sāram ||
. . . . . . . . . yuge koṃkaṇe vādhikāram || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

yebhya (!) santi mahākaulaṃ dhyānayuktā dṛḍhavratāḥ ||
te vrajanti khamārgeṇa gurvārādhanatatparaḥ ||

viḍaṃvaṃ tvanyathātmānaṃ vādhū . . . . . dviyoddhṛtā ||
etat tantre varārohe śāstrakoṭi śatair api ||

labhateta kulāmnāya gurupāramparācyutāḥ ||
tasmāt sarvaprayatnena gurumānyaḥ sadā priye ||

pustakāt paraṃjñānaṃ prāpyate muktimuktidam ||
pañcakrodbhavaṃ tyajya ṣaṣṭhe kulavinirṇaye ||

saptametu mahākaula na bhūto na bhaviṣyati ||    || (fol. 6v10–11 and 7r1)

Colophon

iti śrīmate ṣaṭsāhastre daśamaḥ paṭala (!) (fol. 7r1)
