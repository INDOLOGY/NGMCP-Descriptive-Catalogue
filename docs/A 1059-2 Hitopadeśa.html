Manuscript Features

The manuscript consists of two parts:

  • [A] 48 folios: Hitopadeśa, 4th paricheda "Saṃdhi" (maginal title hi.saṃ)
  • [B] 60 folios: Hitopadeśa, 2nd paricheda "Suhṛdbheda" (marginal title hi.su)

Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||    ||

atha suhṛdbhedaḥ || rājaputrair uktaṃ ||
ārya mitralābhaḥ śrutas tāvad asmābhir idānīṃ su[hṛ]dbhedaṃ śrotuṃ naḥ kutūhalam asti ||    ||

rājaputraharu bhamchan. he guru tapāile ājñā garyāko mitralābha sunyuṃ. ava suhṛdbheda sunnakanaichā bhairahecha ||    ||

viṣṇuśarmovāca || śuhṛdbhedaṃ śṛṇu tat kathayāmi ||    ||

viṣṇuśarmā nāmā paṃḍita bhaṃchan. he rājaputra ho timiharu suna. ma suhṛdbheda kahaṃchu ||    ||
(fol. B1v1–4)

End

aparaṃ ca ||
śrīmān dhavalaś caṃdro sau jīyān māṃḍaliko ripūn ||
yenāyaṃ saṃgraho ratnāl lekhayitvā pracāritaḥ ||

aru pani bhamchu. maṃḍalakā rājā bhayākā jo dhavalacaṃdra bhanyāko śrī lakṣmīle jukta bhaikana. savai śatruharukana jitnu jun dhavalacaṃdrale jatnasita yo kathāko saṃgraha hitopadeśa nāma bhayāko leṣāikana saṃsāramahā pracāra garidiyā ||
(fol. A47v6–48r1)

Colophon

iti hitopadeśe śiśujñānodayadāyini dvitīyakathāparichedaḥ || 2 || śubham astu ||    ||
(fol. B60r7–8)

iti hitopadeśe śiśujñānodayadāyini saṃdhir nāma caturthaḥ parichedaḥ ||    || śubham astu ||    ||
saṃvat 931 miti āṣāḍha vadi 9 nhusa majasyanitolayā. machiṃdrana hitopadeśa saphūli. mitralābha ādi. suhṛdbheda. vigraha. saṃdhi. thamana soyayāta. pe guliṃ saphūlidayakā julo || śubham ||
(fol. A48r1–4)
