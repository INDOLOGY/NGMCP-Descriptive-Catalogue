Excerpts

«Beginning»


śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

śrīrāma uvāca ||


devadeva mahādeva siddhibuddhiphalaprada ||

pūrvaṃ saṃsūcitā ṛddhirasāyanavarā <ref>non pāṇini-use</ref> parā || 1 ||


yasya sādhanamātreṇa gṛhāt tulyo naro bhavet ||

tāṃ siddhiṃ vada me deva yadi tvaṃ bhaktavatsala[ḥ] || 2 ||


pūrvan tu kathitaṃ deva ratnatantraṃ tvayā mama ||

guṭikāḥ kathitāḥ pūrvaṃ sahasradvitayaṃ śiva || 3 ||


pāradāḥ kathitāḥ pūrvaṃ ṣaṭśataṃ mṛtirūpakāḥ ||

dhātūnām aṣṭakalpās tu pūrvam eva prakāśitāḥ || 4 || (fol. 1v1–3)


<references/>

«End»


athātaḥ saṃpravakṣyāmi kalpo divyarasasya ca ||

kāmadhenur divyarūpā rasāyanakarā parā || 1 ||


tasyāḥ saṃgrahamātreṇa rasāyanakaro bhavet ||

atha dvādaśamāṣais tu pātran tu ghṛtapātravat || 2 ||

raupyapātraṃ purā kṛtvā tatra niṃbūrasaṃ suta ||

divyaniṃbūphalānāṃ hi śataṃ dvādaśa saṃkhyayā || 3 ||


tat pātre tu rasaṃ datvā tv agnau saṃsthāpayet punaḥ ||

rase jīrṇe dvijaśreṣṭha punarniṃbūrasaṃ dadet || 4 || (fol. 11r9–v2)


«Sub-Colophon»

iti svarṇataṃtre kānīnakakalpaḥ ekādaśaḥ || || (fol. 11r9)


«Colophon»

The colophon is lost.
