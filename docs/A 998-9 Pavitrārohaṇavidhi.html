śrībhairavovāca ||

pavitrārohanaṃ puṇyaṃ pravakṣāmi(!) samāsata(!)

ākhāḍe uttamaś ceva(!) śrāvane madhyaman tathā
kanyasaṃ bhādrapadye(!) tribhi māse pavitrakaṃ ||<ref name="ftn1"> One syllable, probably the last one, is missing in pāda c. </ref>

ubhayo rthaprasidhī (vaṃ)..(?)seṣe kārttike smṛtaṃ ||

samvacchare(!) kṛte pūjājapadhyānatriyādibhi(!) ||
