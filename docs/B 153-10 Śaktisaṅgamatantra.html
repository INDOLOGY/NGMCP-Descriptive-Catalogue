Manuscript Features

There are three exposures of fols. 12v–13r and two exposures of fols. 15v–16r and 23v–24r.

Folio number 23 appears two times in the MS. The first one is continuous folio of the text and the second one is inserted from other MS. It is also written in a different hand.

The MS is written in a different hand from fol. 32r.

Available folios 1–42

The NGMPP catalogue card refers to the BSP 4.2, pp. 155ff. for the MS. But, it is not mentioned there in the BSP. Furthermore, it seems that the reference is given only to indicate the general section of the Saktisaṃgamatantra.

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||     ||
