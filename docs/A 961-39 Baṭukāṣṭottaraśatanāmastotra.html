Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya nama[ḥ] || ||


śrīvaṭukāya nama[ḥ] || ||


śrī īś(!)vara uvāca || ||


śṛṇu devī(!) pravakṣāmi bhairavasya mahātmana[ḥ] ||

vaṭukasya śataṃ nāmnām aṣṭottaram anuttamam || 1 ||


āpaddhudhārakam idaṃ sarvaśatrunivāraṇam ||

apamṛtyuharaṃ divyam ādhivyaṃ(!)dhinivarhaṇam || 2 ||


viśeṣāvasyajananaṃ vivāde vijayapradam ||

māribhaye caurabhayavātāgnijalaje bhaye || 3 ||


sakṛtpaṭhanamātrena(!) sarvopadravanāśanam ||

dhanasaṃpattijananam putrapautravivarddhanam || 4 || (fol. 1v1–2r3)


End

māsadvayam adhīyānaḥ pādukāsiddhimān bhavet ||

ajñjar(!)na guṭikā khaḍgadhātuvādarasāyanam || 38 ||


sārasvataś caiva tālaṃ vāhanavirasādhanam ||

kāryasiddhir maṃtasiddhir maṃtrasiddhis tathaiva ca || 39 ||


varṣamātram adhīyāta(!) hy āpnuyāt sādhakottamaḥ ||

etat te kathitaṃ devī(!) guhyāguhyāharaṃ paraḥ(!) || 40 ||


kālasaṃkarṣaṇi taṃtre kalikalmaṣnāśanam ||

sarvavighnaharaś caiva vāñchitārthasya dāyakam || 41 || (fol. 7v1–8r8)


Colophon

iti kālasaṃkarṣaṇīyataṃtre haragaurisamvāde vaṭukāṣṭottaraśatanāmastotraṃ samāptam śubham || || śrī || śrīvaṭukāya nama[ḥ] || || 1943 || sāla || || (fol. 8r3–5)
