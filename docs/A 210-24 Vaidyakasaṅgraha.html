Excerpts

Beginning

…‥ rttanacchraprayojanaḥ (!) gva hmayāguśanaṃ dataḥ

o hmā samastanaṃ mānyakayā oḥ dhanūvaṃsa rājāyā kāya cchitha juleḥ niguni jura ṅāsachra prayajana || 41 ||

kiṃkūlyana misālana vidyāhinaṃ ju yo nalaḥ |

akulinopī yo vidyāṃsvaḥ rvadavate hitī pūjayat || ||

gvaṅka manuṣya yākana aṃta juyā o cchāya yaḥ vidyāhīna jura ṅā o chra prayojana vidyāvaṃta jurasāḥ gunavaṃta jura sāḥ gathe de vo pūjā yātaḥ athyeṃ pūjā yāyi vaḥ māṃnyā yāyu vaḥ akuli jurasāṃ || 42 || (exp.1:1–6)

End

thvate nighārayā pracāra hlānya || muhma phasakana bhaṃṭākimi mophalini khatamāra taṃ mohalini khatanāra aṃvalamūra carasanā khārā ghera thva ghā saṃbhāvi kha || || saṃjitakāṃṭhayā pāṅ take manakā vayā cākūsanake esanadere khvāṅakerākhana hmo kanana yā va yaṃsana dere bhaṃṭā kvāthadāyā vatito take || (exp.15:4–7)
