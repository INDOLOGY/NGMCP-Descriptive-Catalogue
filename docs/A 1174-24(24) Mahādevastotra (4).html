Manuscript Features

The manuscript contains 32 Stotras, see A 1174-24 Stotrasaṃgraha for a complete list.

In many places a and e are indistinguishable, likewise ā and o.

Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śivāya || ||

jaya vṛṣabhadhvaja hatamakaradhvaja
puramathana pramathādhipa deva ||
deva trijagannātha triśaktinātha
tridaśa namasya namasya namas te || 1 ||<ref> Pādas a and c are unmetrical.</ref>

jaya śaśiśeṣara surāpagādhara
sarvvagate śarvveśa maheśa |
śarvvānīśa(!) sarvvajagadīśa
nīlakaṇṭha hara namo namas te || 2 ||

jaya juga(!)nāyaka maṅgaladāyaka
maṅgaleśa caturānana rakṣa |
raktārakta viṣakta vināsana
viśvanātha viśvesa namas te || 3 ||<ref>In both stanzas above and in the one below the metre is unclear and every pāda is different.</ref> (fol. 71v10-72r2) <references/>

End

jaya bhūtādhipa bhūtivibhūṣita
bhujaṃgarāja bhujabhūṣaṇa bhavya |
bhaved bhīma tripurādhīśa
triguṇa tryambaka nātha namas te || 11 || (fol. 72r8)

Colophon

iti śrīmahādevastotra sampūrṇṇam || śubhaḥ śivaprīti || || ( (fol. 72r8-9)
