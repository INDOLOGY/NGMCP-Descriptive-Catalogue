Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

atha śālagrāmaparīkṣā || bramapurāṇavārāhāpurāṇayoḥ ||

dvāradeśe same cakre (2) dṛśyete nāṃtarīyake ||

vāsudevaḥ sa vijñeyaḥ śukramaś cātiśobhanaḥ || 1 ||

dve cakre hy eka saṃlagne pūrvabhāge tu (3) puṣkaraḥ ||

saṃkarṣaṇaḥ savijñeyo raktābhaś cātiśobhanaḥ || 2 ||

pradyumnaḥ sūkṣmacakras tu pītadīptis tathaiva ca ||

(4) suṣiraṃ chidrabahulaṃ dīrghakāyas tu yo bhavet || 3 ||

aniruddhas tu nīlābho varttulaś cātiśobhanaḥ ||

rekhātra(5)yasamāyukto devaḥ padmena lāṃchitaḥ || 4 || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

tasmāt tvaṃ naraśārdūla bhaktyā saṃpūjayet sadā || (!)

mṛga(9)rājaṃ mahāvīryaṃ sarvakāmaphalapradaṃ ||

brāhmaṇāḥ kṣatriyāḥ vaiśyāḥ śūdrā padmāṃtvajātayaḥ ||

saṃpūjya taṃ suraśre(1)ṣṭaḥ (!) prāpnuvaṃty abhivāṃchitam ||

devattvam amare⟨n⟩ satvaṃ gaṃdharvatvaṃ ca bho dvijāḥ ||

yakṣavidyādharatvaṃ ca tathānyac ca prayachati (!)

(2)dṛṣṭvā taṃ tu namaskṛtya saṃpūjya narakeśavaḥ ||

prāpnuvaṃti narā rājyaṃ svargamokṣaṃ ca dullabham (!) || || (fol. 29v8–30r2)

Colophon

iti śālagrā(2)mamāhātmyaṃ samāptam || || śubham || (fol. 30r2–3)
