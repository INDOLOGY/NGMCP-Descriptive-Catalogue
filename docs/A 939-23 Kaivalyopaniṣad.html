Excerpts

«Beginning of the root text:»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

athāśvalāyano bhagavantaṃ parameṣṭhinaṃ parisametyovāca || 1 ||

adhīhi bhagavan brahma vidyāṃ variṣṭhāṃ sadā sadbhiḥ sevyamānān nigūḍhām || 2 || || ||

yayā cirāt sarvapāpaṃ vyapohya parāt paraṃ puruṣam upaiti vidvān || 3 ||

tasmai sa hovāca pitāmahaś ca || 4 ||

śraddhābhaktidhyānayogād eva hi || 5 ||

na karmaṇā na prajayā dhanena tyāgenaike amṛtatvam ānaśuḥ || 6 ||

pareṇa nākaṃ nihitaṃ guhāyāṃ vibhrājate yad yatayo viśanti ||

vedāntavijñānasuniścitārthāḥ sannyāsayogād yatayaḥ śuddhasatvāḥ || 7 ||

oṃ namo bhagavate vāsudevāya || (fols. 1v5–6, 2r6–8, 2v5–6)

«Beginning of the commentary:»

svasti śrīman maṅgalamūrttaye namaḥ || oṃ namaḥ śivāya || ||

oṃ kaivalyākhyopaniṣadaṃ kaivalyārthāvabodhinīṃ ||

vyākhyāsye kevalas tena kaivalyātmā prasīdatu || 1 ||

bhagavatī śruti sukhapratipatyarthaṃ kaṃcanāśvalāyanam urarīkṛtya nimittikṛtya ākhyāyikām avatārayati brahmavidyāyām āstikyaṃ janayituṃ || atha sādhanacatuṣṭayasaṃpatyanantaraṃ āśvalāyano ṛgvedācāryabhavantaṃ pūjārhaṃ parameṣṭhinaṃ sarvotkṛṣṭa sthānanivāsinaṃ parisametya śāstrīyeṇa vidhinā samīpam āgatya uvāca uktavān papra[c]cha || (fol. 1v1–4, 7)

«End of the root text:»

samastasākṣiṃ sadasadvihīnaṃ prayāti śuddhaṃ paramātmarūpaṃ ||

yaḥ śatarudrīyam adhīte so ʼgnipūto bhavati || 28 ||

suvarṇasteyāt pūto bhavati | brahmahatyātaḥ pūto bhavati | kṛtyākṛtyātpūto bhavati || 29 ||

tasmād avimuktam āśrito bhavati | antyāśramī sarvadā sakṛd vā japet || anena jñānam āpnoti saṃsārārṇavanāśanam || tasmād evaṃ viditvainaṃ kaivalyaṃ padam aśnute kaivalyaṃ padam aśnute || || (fols. 8v6–8, 9r5–6)

«End of the commentary:»

vāśabdo adhikārisāmarthyānusāreṇa vyavasthitivikalpitety arthaḥ anena rudrādhyāya japena jñānaṃ ahaṃ brahmāsmi iti sākṣātkārarūpaṃ prāpnoti saṃsārārṇavanāśanam | saṃsārasāgaraṃ śoṣaṇaṃ yasmād rudrādhyāyajapo aśeṣapāpanivarhaṇadvārā brahmajñānahetus tasmāt tata evaṃ viditvā uktena prakāreṇa trinetradhyanarudrādhyāyādyayanādinā viditvā sākṣātkṛtya enaṃ paramātmānaṃ kaivalyaṃ kevalasyātmano bhāvaḥ puruṣābhilāṣaviṣayasarvapuruṣārthasamāptibhūtaṃ aśnute prāpnoti kaivalyaṃ padaṃ kaivalyaṃ padaṃ aśnute vyākhyātaṃ padābhyāsa upaniṣatparisamāptyarthaḥ || || (fol. 9v3–4, 8–11)

«Colophon of the root text:»

iti kaivalyopaniṣat || saṃvat 1931 ākhāḍakṛṣṇa 10 likhitam idaṃ pustakaṃ padmakesaraśarmaṇā śubhaṃ | (fol. 9v7)

«Colophon of the commentary:»

iti śrīmatparamahaṃsaparivrājakacāryaśrīmadvidyāraṇyaviracitā kaivalyopaniṣad dīpikā samāptā || (fol. 9v11)
