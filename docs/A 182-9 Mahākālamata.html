Excerpts

Beginning

❖ namaḥ sarvajñāya ||


sukhāsīnaṃ mahāprājñaṃ mahābhāgaṃ mahāmatim ||

abhigamya mahātmānaṃ ṛṣayaḥ saṃśitavrataḥ ||


prapracchu taṃ mahākālaṃ daridrāṇāṃ hite ratāḥ ||

ṛṣaya ūcuḥ ||

draṣṭukāmasya bhagavan dravyo ʼsmākan na vidyate ||


uopāyan tu samācakṣva samāsena śanaiścara ||

ye n ate prīṇayiṣyāmi pratigrahavidhānataḥ ||


ṛṣīṇāṃ vacanaṃ śrutvā mahākālaprabhāvataḥ ||

mahīṃ vasanti nidhānāni(!) pṛthivyāṃ sthāpitāni tu ||


śilātaleṣu caityeṣu drumeṣu parvateṣu vā ||

iṣṭakapratimāṣvāpi liṅge vāpi tathetare || (fol. 1v1–3)


End

rājato vā agnito vā udakato vā mupahāraṃ(!) kurvanti || anyonyaṃ vā kaliṃ utpadyate ||


eṣa doṣam avisarjayitvā yadi gacchati devatā ||

eṣa vai kathitaṃ sarvaṃ sarvaśāstravinirṇayam ||


yathoktena vidhāne prapūjya sukham edhate ||

etad guhyaṃ mahāśāstraṃ mahākālena bhāṣitaṃ ||


mahādevāt prayātaṃ labdhaprakāśāt bhavadvidhau || (!)

eṣa vo vihato(!)pāyo mayā vai ṛṣipuṅgavaḥ (!) ||


etena tu upāyena nidhi(!) utpādya caiva hi ||

yajjñāpya japasaṃpanno vividhāptu (!) pṛthak pṛthak ||


tataḥ guhyaṃ mahāśāstraṃ mahākālena bhāṣitaṃ ||

parituṣṭo (!) ca ṛṣayaḥ śāstraṃ guhya mahāphalam ||

sarvakarma iti vyavasthitā (fol. 12v4–6)


Colophon

iti || mahākālamataṃ samāptam śubham || || (fol. 12v6–7)
