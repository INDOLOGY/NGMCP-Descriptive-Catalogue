Excerpts

Beginning

-sahiṣṇavaḥ ||
arthimārgacanā(!)kalpavṛkṣas tvaṃ dikṣu viśrutaḥ ||
śatāni paṃca saṃprāptās tvām uddiśyāśayā vayaṃ ||
alaṃkārāṃśukayugaṃ pratyekaṃ naḥ samīhitaṃ ||
adhunaivāvilaṃbena dīyatāṃ yadi śakyate ||
ity uktas taiḥ sadācāraḥ so vatīrya turaṃgamāt ||
sādhu pūjā[ṃ] vidhāyeṣāṃ(!) dhīmān kṣaṇam aciṃtayat ||
bhagavaṃtam adṛṣṭvaiva gacchāmi svagṛhaṃ yadi ||
āsannāmṛtapānasya taṃ vighnaṃ katham utsahe ||
adatvā priyam arthibhyo vrajāmi yadi nistrapaḥ ||
kathaṃ karomi dānasya tāṃ svayaṃ vratakhaṇḍanāṃ ||
iti cintayatas tasya bhitvā bhūmiṃ samudgataḥ ||
nāgarājaḥ svayaṃ śeṣaḥ prādād arthisamīhitaṃ || (fol. 1r1–4)

Sub-colophons

iti kṣemeṃdraviracitāyām avadānakalpalatāyāṃ paṇḍitāvadānaṃ nāma catvāriṃśapallavaḥ ||   || (fol. 3r9–v1)

iti kṣemendraviracitāyāṃ bodhisatvādānakalpalatāyāṃ kanakavarṇāvadānaṃ tricatvāriṃśapallavaḥ ||   || (fol. 4r6–7)

iti kṣemendraviracitāyāṃ bodhisatvāvadānakalpalatāyāṃ hiraṇyapāṇyavadānaṃ catuścatvāriṃśapallavaḥ ||   || (fol. 4v9–5r1)

iti kṣemendraviracitāyāṃ bo° ajātaśatrupitṛdrohāvadānaṃ paṃcacatvāriṃśapallavaḥ ||   || (fol. 6v8–9)

iti kṣemendraviracitāyāṃ bodhi° kṛtajñāvadānaṃ ṣaṣṭhacatvāriṃśapallavaḥ ||   || (fol. 8r8)

iti kṣemendra° śālistaṃbāvadānaṃ saptacatvāriṃśapallavaḥ ||   || (fol. 9v3–4)

iti kṣemendraviracitāyāṃ bodhisatvāvadānakalpalatāyāṃ sarvārthasiddhāvadānam aṣṭacatvāriṃśapallavaḥ ||   || (fol. 11r2–3)

iti kṣemeṃdraviracitāyāṃ bodhisatvāvadānakalpalatāyāṃ hastakāvadānam ūnapaṃcāśapallavaḥ ||   || (fol. 14r1)

End

dvaṃdvāśrayāṇi kuṭilāni viśṛṃkhalāni
tīkṣṇāni caṃcalatarāṇi malīmasāni ||
saṃmohanāni nayanānukṛtivratāni
strīṇāṃ vicitracaritāni duraṃtarāṇi ||

kathayitveti sarvajñas trilokyakuśalotsukaḥ ||
dideśa dharmamaryādām āryavaryābhinaṃditāṃ ||

satyaṃ vadet parahitapraṇayopayuktaṃ
hiṃsāviraktam(!) ucitaṃ caritaṃ bhajeta ||
kuryān matiṃ virativairaviṣātidāsāṃ(!)
saṃciṃtaye[n] niyatam aṃtahitāya śāṃtiṃ ||   || (fol. 16v3–5)

Colophon

iti kṣemendraviracitāyāṃ bodhisatvāvadānakalpalatāyāṃ ṣaḍdaṃtāvadānaṃ paṃcāśapallavaḥ || 50 ||   || (fol. 16v5)
