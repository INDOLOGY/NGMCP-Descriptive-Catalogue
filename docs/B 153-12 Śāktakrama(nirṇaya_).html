Beginning

mṛtābhāve viṣṭaraṃ ca śavarūpaṃ prakalpayet ||

athāṃtaryajanaṃ vakṣ[y]e dṛṣṭādṛṣṭaphalapradaṃ ||

vajramudrā samākhyātā vidvadbhiḥ parikīrttitā |
