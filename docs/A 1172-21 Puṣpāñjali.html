«Beginning»

atha puṣpāṃjaliḥ

namos tv anantāya sahasramūrtaye |

sahasrapādākṣiśirorubāhave ||

sahasranāmne puruṣāya śāśvate

sahasrakoṭī yugadhāriṇe namaḥ || 1 ||

namo brahmaṇyadevāya gobrāhmaṇahitāya ca ||
