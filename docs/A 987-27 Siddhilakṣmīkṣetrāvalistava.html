Excerpts

Beginning

❖ na ❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīsiddhikālikādevya ||

oṃ krīṃ trailokyāyai namaste tribhuvananamite kālike viśvacaṇḍe

vāmānte vāmasasve tridaśavaranute naumi te pādayugme |

hrasve dīrghe visarge akacaṭatapaṇe kṣaḥpraśāntāntarasthe |

muṇdasthe krūṃ svarūpe neti parasarade śeṣanāgottarīye || (fol. 21r1–5)

End

yā vīre vīravandyā ṛṣigamanayato yogapīṭheyapīṭhaṃ

śrutvā tvaṃ tasya nityaṃ vitaravarṇṇe kāmarūpāvatīrṇṇaṃ ||

nāmnā kṣatrāvalayaṃ paṭheta śubhayasaṃ maṃtrasiṃhaṃ praṇītaṃ |

divyā divyābhivighnā prabhavam iha bhave devadevī prasādāt || (fol. 23r3–7)

Colophon

iti śrībhairavatasi śiracchede vidyāpīṭhe jayadrathajāmale viśvalakṣmīkrame siddhilakṣmīdevyā kṣatrāvalistavaṃ samāptaḥ || || || tra || || (fol. 23r7–23v3)
