Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

śrībhavānīśaṃkarābhyāṃ namaḥ ||

yasyājñayā jagatsraṣṭā viriṃci(!) pālako hariḥ |

saṃharttā kālarudrākhyo namas tasmai pinākine ||

tīrthānām uttamaṃ tīrthaṃ kṣetrāṇāṃ kṣetram uttamaṃ ||

tatraiva naimiṣāraṇye śaunakādyāḥ tapodhanāḥ ||

dīrghasatraṃ prakurvvaṃtaḥ satriṇāṃ svamatena saḥ |

teṣāṃ saṃdarśanautsukyād āgato hi mahātapāḥ | (fol. 1v1–2)

End

ṛṣaya ūcaḥ(!) ||

evam uktaṃ tvayā sūta, caritaṃ śaṃkarasya ca |

anena caritenaiva, saṃtṛptā(!) smo na saṃśayaḥ ||    ||

sūta uvāca ||

vyāsaprasādāc chrutam asti sarvvaṃ,

mayā tataṃ śaṃkararūpaḥm(!) adbhutaṃ ||

suvistṛtaṃ cādbhutavedagarbhaṃ

jñānātmakaṃ paramaṃ puṇyarūpaṃ || (!)

śraddhayā parayopetāḥ arcayanti śivaṃ priyaṃ |

śṛṇvanti caiva yaṃ bhaktyā śaṃbhor māhātmyam adbhutaṃ |

śivaśāstram idaṃ viprās te yāṃti (paramāṃ) gati(!) ||     || (fol. 98r6–98v2)

Colophon

iti śrīskandapurāṇe kedārakhaṇḍe śaivaśāstre paṃcaviṃśo [ʼ]dhyāyaḥ ||     || mama (‥) śaṃkarapri(!)tir astu ||         ||

svasti śrīsamvat 1947 sāla mītī(!) vaisāṣa(!) vadī(!)10 roja2 (fol. 98v2–3)
