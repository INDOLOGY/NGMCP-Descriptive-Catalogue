Excerpts

Beginning

❖ namaḥ śrīvajrasatvāya ||

vande śrīkuliśeśvaraṃ †(smasuremārāha)†bhavān alpada

krodhādhāvata dikṣu maṅgalagiro gāyanta vajrāṅganāḥ ||

śrīmadvajrabhṛto mahimnijagadāḥ dhātuṃ mahāmaṇḍale<ref name="ftn1">Unmetrical pāda</ref>

niṣpratyūham ihābhayasya mahasā vajrāvalī mīlatu ||

antarbahivajrabhṛte vajrā<ref name="ftn2">Unmetrical pāda</ref>

paraṃparābhir dvigatā hṛdīyaṃ |

yajjyotir antas timiraṃ nirasye

śrīvajrabhṛt mūrttimatī vibhartti || (exp. 4, fol. 1r1–3)

End

...trivajravajraghaṇṭālakṣaṇādi vidhiḥ || ||

iti paramamunīnāṃ vag udanvantyagādhe

śrutimadhinapariyuḥ kepi kepi plavan te |

sakṛpam atimahimnām māta†⟨ṃ⟩ran tarn†nimajjyā-

mṛtam idam upanītaṃ prītaye te bhajantau(!) ||

sukṛtam uditam uccair jyotir antarjvalad yat

prasṛtam iha mahimnā mākara(syāṇḍatāsmaṃ)

vilasatu mama vajrapreyasī sargadhāmni

trijagadabhayabhartur maṇḍale viśvamūrtteḥ || ||

Colophon

mahāpaṇḍitābhayākaraguptaracitā vajrāvalī nāma maṇḍalopai(!)kā samāptā || || (fol.83v: exp. 88:5)
