❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namah || ||


śrīgurubhyo namaḥ || ||


maṇḍapagoya vidhi ||


mālako vasape || || yajamānana pādaprakṣāraṇaṃ || purohita josi ācāryya pādārgha || ||


yajamāna puṣpabhājanaṃ || adyādi vākya || mānavagotra yajamānasya śrīśrī


jayabhūpatīndramalladevavarmmaṇaḥ śrīśrīśrīsveṣṭadevatāprītikāmanayā pṣāṇanirmita


vāpīprāsādasuvarṇnakalaśadhvajāvalohana siddhāgni ayutāhuti yajñe maṇḍapārambhana


kalaśārccana balyārccana karttuṃ puṣpabhājanaṃ samarpayāmi || ||



śrī saṃvarttāmaṇḍalānte kramapadanihitānandaśaktiḥ subhīmā ||


sṛṣṭaṃ nyāyacatuṣkaṃ akulakulagataṃ paṃcakaṃ cānyaṣaṭkaṃ ||


catvāro pancakonyaṃ punar api caturaṃ tattvato maṇḍaledaṃ ||


saṃsṛṣṭaṃ yena tasmai namata guruvaraṃ bhairavaṃ śrīkujeśaṃ || (fol. 1v1–2r4)
