Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśo jayati

vāsudevaṃ ramākāṃtaṃ natvā laugākṣibhāskaraḥ ||

kurute jaimininaye praveśāyārthasaṃgraham || 1 ||

atha paramakāruṇiko bhagavāñ jaiminir dharmavivekāya dvādaśalakṣaṇīṃ praṇināya tatrādau dharmajijñāsāṃ sūtrayāmāsa athāto dharmajijñāseti (fol. 1v1–2)


End

so yaṃ dharmo yad uddiśya vihitas taduddeśena kriyamāṇas taddhetuḥ

īśvarārpaṇabuddhyā kriyamāṇas tu niḥśreyasahetuḥ

na ca tadarpaṇabuddhyānuṣṭhāne pramāṇābhāva iti vācyam

yat karoṣi yad aśnāsi yaj juhoṣi dadāsi yat

yat tapasyasi kauṃteya tat kṛṣva madarpaṇam

iti bhagavadgītāsmṛter eva pramāṇatvāt smṛticaraṇe tatpramāṇyasya śrutimūlakatvena

vyavasthāpanād iti śīvam

bālānāṃ sukhabodhāya bhāskareṇa sumedhasā

racito yaṃ samāsena jaiminīyārthasaṃgrahaḥ 1 || || (fol. 13v11–14r3)


Colophon

iti śrīmahopādhyāyalaugākṣibhāskaraviracitaṃ pūrvamīmāṃsārthasaṃgrahanāmakaṃ prakaraṇam agāc caramavarṇadhvaṃsaṃ || || saṃvat 1896 vaiśākhaśuklapratipattithau likhitam idaṃ gabhastīśvarasamīpe || śubham astu (fol. 14r3–4)
