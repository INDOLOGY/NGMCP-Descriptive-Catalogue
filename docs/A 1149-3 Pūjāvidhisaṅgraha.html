Excerpts

«Beginning»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

atha kārttike ākāśadīpo puṣkarapurāṇe |

tulāyāṃ tilatailena sāyaṃkāle samāgate |

ākāśadīpaṃ yo dadyāt phalam ekaṃ hariṃ prati |

mahatīṃ śriyam āpnoti rūpasaubhāgyasaṃpadam iti | (exp. 3t1–3)


«End»

tvadīyaṃ vastu govinda tubhyam eva samarpaye |

gṛhāṇa sumukho bhūtvā prasīda puruṣottama |

dakṣiṇāṃ saṃkalpya | arghabhramaṇaṃ kṛtvā maṃtrahīnaṃ kriyāhīm ityādi paṭhitvā | kāyena vāceti paṭhitvā abhiṣekaṃ kuryyāt || śubham astu || (fol. 15b8–10)
