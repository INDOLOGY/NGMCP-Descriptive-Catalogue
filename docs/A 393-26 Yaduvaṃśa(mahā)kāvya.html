Excerpts

Beginning

///bhāthalevateva bhūhaṃ sā yauvanaśrīr nnahuṣaṃ navodayaṃ |

ārūrucac cārurucā tayā gṛhaṃ navoḍhaya(!) vātitarāṃ sa sundaraḥ ||

ajīgrahat pāṇitalaṃ mahājanas taṃ bhānumanyā nahuṣaṃ hṛhasthitau |

reme tayā kośalavaṃśajātayā nṛpopi śailātmajayā yathā haraḥ ||

mahāpratāposya diśācaronupānanītamatyodatale durāśayāt |

tathāpi citte na dadhau sa vikriyāṃ svabhāvato namradhiyo hi sādhavaḥ ||

(fol. 20r1-5)

End

śrīśaṅkaro janayitā janatī ca yasya

śrīrohiṇībudhaśivātmajatulyakīrtteḥ |

śrīkāśināthadharaṇitridaśasya tasya

śrīvaṃśado vijayate yaduvaṃśa eṣaḥ ||

kavikāvyabadhāvyagrā sadā muṅgarasākulā |

vandanīyā dvayor jjiavhā khalasya ca bhayasya ca ||

(fol. 64v5-8)

Colophon

iti śrīyaduvaṃśe mahākāvya(!) śrīkāśīnāthakṛtau daśamaḥ sarggaḥ || ||

(fol. 64v8)
