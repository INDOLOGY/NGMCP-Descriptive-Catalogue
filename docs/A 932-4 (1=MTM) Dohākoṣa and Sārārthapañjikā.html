Manuscript Features

Fols. 1, 6 and 13 are missing.

The order of the leaves in the microfilms are: 2–5, 7–9, 17, 10–12 and 14–16.

The MTM contains the following texts:

1. Dohākoṣa of Tillopā with the commentary Sārārthapañjikā

2. Dohākoṣa of Sarahapāda with the commentary Viṣamapadabhañjikā by Advayavajra
