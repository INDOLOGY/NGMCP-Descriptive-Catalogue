Manuscript Features

The subject of the text has been wrongly mentioned as Jyotiṣa in catalogue cards.

Fols. 2–12, 14, 17–18, 20, 23–140 and 142 are available.

Fol. 47 has been mentioned twice to the two successive folios.

There are two exposures of fols. 57–58r.

Beginning
