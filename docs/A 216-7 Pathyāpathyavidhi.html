Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

śvetāṃgaṃ śvetavastraṃ sitakusumagaṇaiḥ pūjitaṃ śvetagaṃdhaiḥ
kṣīrāc caucatnapīṭhe surataruvimale ratnasiṃhāsanasthaḥ ||
(2)durbhiḥ pāśāṅkuśeṣṭā bhayadhṛtiruciraṃ candrmaulī trinetraṃ
dhyāyecchākṣārtham īśaṃ gaṇapatim amalaṃ śrīśayetaṃ prasannam || 1 || (fol. 1v1–2)

Ending

amlaṃ ca sarvaṃ lavaṇaṃ ca sarvaṃ
svadaṃ ca nānāvidham ānayāmi
nidrābhayaṃdhūmakaṭukṣudhā ca
viṣāturo naiva bhajet kadā(1)cit ||  (fol. 23v9–24r1)

Colophon

iti viṣaroge pathyāpathyavidhi || || samāptoyaṃ ||
samvat 1908 sāla miti vaiśāṣa vadi 3 roja 6 viśā(2)ṣā nakṣatre maṣanavāhālavāstavyaṃ arjjavegī thākumani svahastākṣaraṃ samāptaṃ || ||
yādṛśaṃ pustakaṃ …śubham (fol. 24r1–2)
