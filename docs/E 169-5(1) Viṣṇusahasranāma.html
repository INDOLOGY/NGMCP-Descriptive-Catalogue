Beginning

❖ oṃ namo bhagavate vāsudevāya ||

vaiśampāyana uvāca ||

śrutvā dharmmān aśeṣeṇa, pāvanāni ca sarvvaśaḥ |
yudhiṣṭhiraḥ śāntanavaṃ, putar(!) evābhyabhāṣata ||

kim etaṃ daivataṃ loke kiṃ vāpy ekaṃ parāyaṇaṃ |
