Manuscript Features

On the first exposure is written:

atha nīlakaṇṭhīpustakamidaṃ jyotirvid ḍhuṃḍhīrājajaśarmaṇas tatputrasya śrīkṛṣnaśarmaṇaḥ || and rough about the āṃdhapraśna(sya)lakṣaṇa and on the last 60th exposure is about rough paper related to the jyautiṣa is mentioned bhayāta bhabhoga …
