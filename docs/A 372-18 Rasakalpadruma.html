Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||    ||
vaṃdetatkimapijyoti riticitraṃcitāptaye |
yatsmṛterevabhūyāṃsitamāṃsi harati drumaṃ || 1 ||
ādarśaprativiṃvavatriguṇamāgutpattimadhyātyayaṃ
kurvannirvikṛti pratītamamalaiḥ satvena padhyogibhiḥ |
āste sannihitaṃkhadhyuti sudhīvācāmagamyaṃ sadā
sadājyotistatparamaṃ namāmikaraṇa vyāpārapāresthitam || 2 ||
natvāguruniṣṭadevān vrajāsāraṃkaromyaham ||
rasakalpadrumaṃ nāmagraṃthaṃ graṃtha vidāṃ priyam || 3 ||
(fol. 1v1–4)

End

vācaspateḥ ||
sādhāraṇataruvuddhayānamayāvaddhastavālavālopi |
lajjayasimāmidānīṃ caṃpaka bhavanādhivāsibhiḥ kusumaiḥ || 92 ||
śaṃkaramiśrāṇāṃ || yannādṛtastumalināmalinā śayena
kiṃ tena caṃpaka viṣādimurī karoṣI |
viśvāmirāmanava nīradanīlaveṣāḥ
keśāḥ kuśeśayadṛśā kuśalī bhavaṃtu || keśavasya || 93 ||    ||
(fol. Last r7–9)
