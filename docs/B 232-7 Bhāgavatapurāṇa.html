mahābhārata caturvedo vo mahābhārata vo laṃṅāva bhāratagariṣṭa juraṃ thva khaṃ catur


varṇana tuṅeteva brāhmaṇa madayakaṃ hlāyaṃ mateva ṅenevumateva narakavāsa juyuṃ


brāhmaṇa ṭoṃ dayakāva caturvarṇanaṃ ekacittayāṅaṅeṃ nivajuroṃ hlāyuva juroṃ pūrvvajanmayā


saptajanma ṭoyāvu pāpadokovu moraṃ kīrttiṃ rātaṃ ihatra sukha paratrasa sanyāsīna lāṅāthyaṃ


vaikuṇṭhi puli prāpta juyu sārūpyaṃ yuṃ | bhārata saṃpūrṇa yāṅaṅaṃ nemaphātasāno


ślokapādamātra || (exp. 2t1–6)


«End: »


pitṛyātaṃ nāgayakṣayātaṃ lavani ghṛtathyaṃ manuṣyayātaṃ viraṃ || vyāsasa ājñānaṃ nāradaṛṣi


ṭoṃ purāṇapaṃḍitajuraṃ indrāditetisakoṭi devatā sake asita ṛṣi devā ṛṣi vyāsasa ājñāna


pitṛlokadākovu bhāratakanaṃ || yakṣarākṣasa nāgavyāsasa ājñāna vyāsasaputra śukasyaṃ kanaṃ


bhārata || manuṣyalokayātaṃ vyāsasa ājñāna vaiśaṃpāyaṇasyaṃ janamejaya rājā ṭoṃ kanaṭoṃ ||


thva itihāsa khaṃ (exp. 31b4–8)
