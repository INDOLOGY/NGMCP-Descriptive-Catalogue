catuṣpīṭhoktavidhinā śiṣyābhyarthanayā mayā |
sukhasādhanasaṃkṣiptan tadārādhanam ucyate ||

tatra sādhakaś catuṣpīṭhatantroktavidhinā labdhābhiṣeko gurubhakto gambhīradharmādhiyukto yogālaṅkārabhūsito nihārārāmādimanonukūlapradeśe sukumārāṣana(!)niṣaṇṇo maṇḍalacakram adhyāsīnaṃ yogāmbarārādhanaṅ kuryā+ + tatrādau tāvat sūryakaratale rakta-akāram vicintyaś cetādidakṣiṇakarāṅgulīṣu krameṇa nyaset || sūṃ kṣūṃ yūṃ hūṃ aḥ | bījanirjātau pañcasūcikam vajram vibhāvayet || tathā vāme candrakaratale śuklasak[[ā]]racandramaṇḍale rocanādi(!) nyaset | (smryūṃ hmryūṃ ymryūṃ kṣmryūṃ) saḥ | tenaiva bījanirmitarocanādibījena padmam vibhāvayet || karasodhanavidhiḥ || tataḥ kaṇṭhe raktahrīḥkāreṇa aṣṭadalapadmaṃ jihvāpadmadale huṃkāreṇa vajram | tatmadhye oṃ aḥ jihvāsamvārttani hūṃ phaḍ iti || jihvāsodhanavidhiḥ || (fol. 1v1-2r1)
