Manuscript Features

The first two folios bear some unidentified text with Hindi language and some Tāntrikamantrās.

There is one side of a folio which is placed in between 44v and 45v bears Devīstotra as well after the colophon in 46r.

The numbers 30-36,38 and 39 are appeared double but text is not repeated.

Fol. 10v-11r is missing.
