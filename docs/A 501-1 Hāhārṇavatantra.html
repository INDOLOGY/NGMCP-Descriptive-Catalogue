«Beginning»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

śrī devy uvāca ||

deva deva jagannātha namas te śaśiśekhare ||

pinākapāṇi sarvāṃto trinetravṛṣabhadhvajaḥ ||

tvatprasādān mahacchreya nṝṇāṃ siddhikaraṃ paraṃ ||

śrotum icchāmi deveśa chinnāyā vidhim uttamaṃ || 2

maṃtrapadānāṃ maṃtrāṇāṃ puraścaraṇakaṃ śubhaṃ ||

yāgārcanaṃ prayogaṃ ca vada me parameśvara || 3 || (fol. 1v1–3)


«End»

tato makārapaṃcena pūjayitvā subhaktitaḥ ||

daṃḍapraṇāmastotrādikrameṇa soṣayet priye || 38 ||

evaṃ kṛte tribhir māsaṃ pratyakṣasvapnadarśanaṃ ||

yāṃ yāṃ prārthayate kāmaṃ tāṃ tāṃ siddhi (!) na saṃśaya || 39 ||

triguṇāyā mahatkarmam īdṛk manye na varttate ||

satyaṃ satyaṃ paunaḥ satyaṃ satyam etan mayoditaṃ || 40 ||

navati dinatas tasya pratyakṣaṃ svapnadarśanaṃ ||

yāṃ yāṃ ciṃtayate tāṃ tāṃ siddhis tasya na saṃśayaḥ || 41 ||

aprakāśyam idaṃ devi madrūpaṃ vṛṣabhāsanaṃ ||

vīrasādhanatatkoṭiguṇāchīghraḥ phalapradaṃ || 42 ||

na deyaṃ yasya kasyāpi mahachī(!)ghraḥ phalapradaṃ ||

nāstikāya abhaktāya daṃbhācāraratāya ca || 43 ||

aprakāśya mahādevi prakāśāt siddhināśanaṃ ||

bhaktāya ca svaśiṣyāya mahan mārgaṃ pradarśayet || 44 ||

īdṛśaṃ sādhanaṃ devi nānyat kiṃcin na dṛṣyate ||

madrūpivṛṣabhārūḍhaṃ sādhanaṃ bhuvi durllabhaṃ || 45 ||

tavasnehān mayā khyātaṃ na deyaṃ yasya kasyacit || 46 || (fol. 11v11–12r4)
