End

ānaṃtyāt sa vaṃśaḥ śabdena śakyaṃtenuśāsituṃ |

bālavyutpattaye ʼsmābhiḥ saṃkṣipyoktaṃ yathāmatiḥ || 1 ||

prakriyā kaumudī seyaṃ rāmacaṃdraprakāśitā |

asadvaca svamovaṣyā taptaccakorapriyā ciraṃ || 2 ||

jayati subhagamūrttir mugdhahāsāvaloka

praśamitajanatāpo viṭhṭhalaḥ svātmadīpaḥ |

sa cakitam iva lakṣmī sevate yatpadābjaṃ

lalitakaratalābhyāṃ sādhu saṃvāhayaṃti || 3 ||

mīmāṃsāyugalān jayād akaṇabhuk pronktajña sadvākyadā

durjñeyas tva na śāstra - - manasāṃ śabdādhike chāvanaṃ ||

śabdādhāra visārya sādhuvacanaṃ dhvāṃtābhināśanjamā

tatpādyaṃ purarāmacaṃdrajanitā sā prakṛyā kaumudī || 4 || ❖ || || (fols. 41v4–42r2)
