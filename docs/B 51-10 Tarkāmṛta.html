Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||     ||

brahmādyā nikhilārcitās tridaśasaṃdohāḥ sadābhīṣṭadāḥ

svā ʼjñānapraśamāya yatra mana(2)so⟪ḥ⟫ vṛttīḥ samastā dadhuḥ ||

śrīviṣṇoś caraṇāṃbujṃ bhavabhayadhvaṃsai[[ka]]bījaṃ paraṃ.

hṛtpadme vinidhāya taṃ nirupamaṃ tarkkāmṛtaṃ tanyate || 1 (3) ||

atha śrutuḥ śrūyate || ātmā vāre draṣṭavyaḥ śrotavyo maṃtavyo nididhyāsi[[ta]]vya iti || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

kriyāsaṃgatasyaivakārasyātyaṃtāsaṃ(2)yogavyavacchedo ʼrthaḥ || yathā nīlaṃ sarojaṃ bhavaty eva || viśeṣaṇasaṃgatasyāyogavyavacchedaḥ || yathā śaṃkhaḥ pāṃḍura eveti || viśeṣya(3)saṃgatasyānyayogavyavacchedaḥ || yathā padārtha (!) eva dhanurddhara ityādau || evaṃ diśā sarvatra bodhyaḥ ||     || (fol. 12v1–3)
