Manuscript Features

The text in this MS starts from the beginning of the text and reaches up to the beginning of the 4th chapter.

Fols. 4, 9 and 10 are damaged on the left side with considerable loss of text (especially fol. 4).


Excerpts

Beginning

❖ namaḥ sarvvajñāya ||

yaḥ sarvvathā sarvvahatāndhakāraḥ

saṃsārapaṅkāj jagad ujjahāra |

tasmai namaskṛtya yathārthaśāstre

śāstram pravakṣyāmy abhidharmakoṣaḥ(!) ||


prajñāmalā s⁅ā⁆nucarā’bhidharmas

tatprāptaye yāpi ca yac ca śāstraḥ(!) |

tasyārthato’smin samanupraveśāt

sa vāśrayo syety abhidharmakośaḥ ||


dharmāṇām pravicayam antareṇa nāsti

kleśānāṃ yata upaśāntaye ’bhyupāyaḥ |

kleśaiś ca bhramati bhavārṇṇave ’tra lokas

taddhetor uditaḥ kilaiṣaśāstrā ||

(fol. 1v1‒3)


«Sub-colophons»

dhātunirdeśo nāma prathamakośasthānaḥ || ○ || (fol. 3v6)

itīndriyanirdeśo nāma dvitīyaṅ kośasthānaḥ | ❁ (fol. 7v1)

iti lokanirdeśo nāma tṛtīyaṅ kośaḥ sthānaḥ | (fol. 12v1)


End

svāni bhūtāny upādāya kāyavākkarma sāśravaṃ |

anāśravaṃ yatra jāta’vijñaptir anupāttikā ||

naiṣyandikī ca satvākhyā niṣyandopāttabhūtajā |

samādhijaupacayikā’nupāttā bhinnatabhūtajā ||

nāvyākṛtā’sti vijñaptis tridhānyaśubham(!) punaḥ |

kāme rūpe py avijñaptir vvijñaptiḥ savicārayor ||

kāme pi nivṛtā nāsti samutthānam asad yataḥ |

paramārthaśubho mokṣaḥ svato mūlaṃ hyapatrapā ||

samprayogeṇa

(fol. 12r4‒6)
