«Beginnings:»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

atha mūlagraṃtha prāraṃbhaḥ

praṇamya jagadutpattisthiti saṃhārakārakam

svargāpavargayordvāraṃ trailokyaśaraṇaṃ śivam 1

nānā munīnāṃ vacanairdānīṃ

samāsata sadbhiṣajāṃ niyogāt

sopadravāriṣṭa nidānaliṃgo

nibadhyate roga viniścayo ʼyaṃ 2

nānā taṃtra vihīnānāṃ bhiṣajām alyamedhasām

sukhaṃ vijñātumātaṃkam ayameva bhaviṣyati 3

nidānaṃ pūrvarūpāiI rūpāṇyūpaśayastathā

saṃpprāptiś cetivijñānaṃ rogāṇāṃ paṃcadhāsmṛtam 4 (fol.1v1–11)

End

tatpātācchopaśāntiḥ syāt punaḥ sthānāṃtare bhavet

sa snāyuka iti khyātaḥ kriyoktā tu visarpavat 17

bāhvor yadi pramādena jaṃghayostudyate kvacit

saṃkocaṃ khaṃjatā caiva chino tantuḥ karotyasau 18

vātenaśyāvarukṣaḥ sarugathadahanā nīlapītaḥ sadā hoya (!)

śvetaśleṣmaṇāsyā pṛthugarimayuto doṣayugmād viliṃgaḥ

raktāccā raktakāntiḥ samadhika dahanaḥ sarvaja sarvaliṃgo

rogo ʼsāvaṣṭadhetthaṃ munibhir abhihitaḥ snāyukas taṃtukīṭaḥ 19 (fol.127v13:128r2)

Colophon

iti pariśiṣṭa nidāṇaṃ samāpta samāptoyaṃ graṃthaḥ śubham śrīsaṃvat 1945 sāla miti āṣāḍha vadi 5 ro. 7 etaddine likhitaṃ samāptaṃ lekhakoyaṃ pustaka śrīkālidāsasśubham (!) (fol.128v3–5)
