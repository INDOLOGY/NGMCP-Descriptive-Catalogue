Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

śrīgurucaraṇakamalebhyo namaḥ ||

sukhasthitaṃ jagaṃnnāthaṃ (!) kailāse girijaikadā ||

āliṃgyāha kathaṃ vāsaṃ dattvārddhāṃgetha vaṃcase || 1 ||

majjaniṃ kāntikāṃ pāhur (!) brahmādyās tatvadarśitaḥ ||

jātāsannā kathaṃ tasmai kena vādya prasīdati || 2 ||

śrutveti vacanaṃ devyā vismito phullalocanaṃ ||

uvāca prahasaṃ devas tanmanonaṃdayanti ca || 3 ||

īśvara uvāca ||

lakṣmaṇe śarasaṃbhinne vilapaṃtaṃ raghūttamaṃ ||

caturvargātmikā kālī prādurbhūtāṃ janaprabhā || 4 ||

harṣayaṃtī tamūcemuṃ (!) caturvarṇātmakaṃ manaṃ ||

rāmagṛhānasaḍbhaktyā trailokyavijigīṣayā || 5 ||

rāma uvāca ||

mātaś cen me prasannāsi kālike kālanāśinī ||

rahasyaṃ kṛpayācakṣva japāmyarcāmyahaṃ sadā || 6 ||

tathā vārcā japadhyānā mātṛkābhūtaśodhanaiḥ ||

valitarppaṇahomādyaiḥ prasīdes tadvinā vada || 7 || (fol. 39r7–39v5)

End

stotraṃ vā dhārayed aṃge puṣṭakaṃ (!) vārgṛherccayet (!) ||

avyāhatājño niḥpāpaḥ satkaviḥ subhagaḥ sukhī || 44 ||

dhārmikaḥ śatrujichūraḥ kamanīyo dhanī bhavet ||

strīṇāṃ vadhyādidoṣeṣu śiśor bālagrahādiṣu || 45 ||

daurbhāgye duṣṭakṛd doṣe dhāraṇaśubhadaṃ sadā ||

vanojjuṭaśmaśānādau yaḥ kāliṃ vidhivaj japet || 46 ||

aiśvaryaṃ śivavan prāpya bhaven mṛtyujarātigaḥ ||

mohanoccāṭanādīni yāni karmāṇi cechati (!) || 47 ||

tāni siddhyaṃti manasā japan maṃtraṃ stavaṃ sadā ||

parameśvarasaudaryā paramārcyaparāyana || 48 ||

parameśṭhīpadaṃ prāpya parameśitvamṛchati ||

vipulapulakayukta evamaṃtrī (!) yadi bhavati sadā maharddhisiddhiṃ (!) ||

sa bhavati naradeva ekavaṃdyaḥ sakalasukham avāpya mokṣam eti || 50 || (fol. 41v1–7)

Colophon

iti kulācāracaṃdrodaye paramasiddhāgame kālīrahasye kālīstavaṃ saṃpūrṇaṃ || 23 || (fol. 41v7)
