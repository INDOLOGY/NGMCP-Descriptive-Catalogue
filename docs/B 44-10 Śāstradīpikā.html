Excerpts

Beginning of the commentary on prathamādhyāya

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

namo dharmāya mahate śreyas saṃdohadāyine
pratyakṣāgamyarūpāya vedavedyāya te namaḥ

śrī(2)kṛṣṇavāsudevendravāṇīgurupadāmbujān
dhyāyaṃ dhyāyaṃmitar vākyair vyākurve śāśtradīpikāṃ

dharmamīmāṃsā(3)yāṃ devatādigrahādipaṃcakanirākaraṇaṃ na vāstaṃ vīriti (!) manurudhya kiṃtu vigrahabuddhi ced ddevatāyāgadeśa(4)m upeyāt adhvaryādivad adhyakṣam upalabhyeta na copalabhyate (exp. 3, fol. 1v1–4)

Beginning

||    || śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||    ||

oṃ namaḥ śrībhavānyo (!) ||

āmnāyasya kriyārthatvād ānarthakyam atadarthānāṃ tasmā(2)d anityam ucyate ||

athāto dharmajijñāsetyatra pramāṇasvarūpasādhanaphalaiḥ sābhāsair dharmo jijñāsya cena pra(3)tijñātaḥ tatrāsmin adhyāye pramāṇaṃ nirūpyate kiṃ pramāṇaṃ dharma iti tena padeva kiṃcit dharmapramāṇanirūpaṇam a(4)vādamaṃtrasmṛtināmadheyādigocaraṃ tatsarvam atraiva saṃgataṃ (fol. 1v1–4)

End

śabdātarābhyāsaguṇaiḥ (!) sasaṃkhyair
nāmnā ca dūreṇā (!) ca sā(57v1)pavādaḥ ||
bhedā (!) kriyāṇām iti sādhito yam
ataḥ paraṃ syād viniyogaciṃtā ||    || (exp. 133, fol. 57r9–57v1)

Colophon

iti śrīpārthasārathimiśravi(2)racitāyāṃ śāstradīpikāyāṃ dvitīyasyādhyāyasya caturthaḥ pādaḥ sā [[saṃvat 1928 śubham ]] śrīrāmacaṃdrasahāya || (exp. 134, fol. 57v1–2)
