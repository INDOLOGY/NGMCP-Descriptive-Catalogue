Manuscript Features

The MTM contains the following texts:


1. Śivakavaca (exps.3–8b)

2. Avadhūtabhujaṅgaprayātastotra (exps. 9–10b)


The end cover-leaf has been microfilmed between exps. 7 and 9.

Excerpts

«Beginning of 1. Śivakavaca»


❖ oṁ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||


oṁ devānāṃ devam īśānaṃ ādimadhyāṃtavarjjitaṃ |

bhāvagamyaṃ namaskṛtya, papṛ(!)cchedaṃ ṣaḍānanaḥ || (exp. 3t1–2)


«End of 2. Avadhūtabhujaṃgaprayātastotra»


avadhūto [ʼ]vatāro bhujaṃgaprayātaṃ

paṭhed yas tu bhaktyā samādhāya cittaṃ ||

bhaved yogasiddhiṃ bhaven manyamāno

bahūtāpadaṃ prāpyate tatprasādāt || 11 || (exp. 10b2–4)


«Colophon of 1. Śivakavaca»


iti śrīmaheśvaraproktaṃ śivakavacaṃ saṃpūrṇṇaṃ || ||

śubham astu || || (exp. 8b1–2)


«Colophon of 2. Avadhūtabhujaṃgaprayātastotra»

iti śrīpārvvatībhairavasaṃvāde avadhūtabhujaṃgapratana(!)stotraṃ samāptaṃ || || śrīśrīsadāśivāya namaḥ || || śubham astu || || (exp. 10b5–6)
