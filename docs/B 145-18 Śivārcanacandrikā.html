Manuscript Features

  • Abbreviation of the marginal title appears: śi.caṃ. In the upper left-hand and word rāmaḥ is in the lower right-hand margin of the verso of the fourth foliation.
  • Foliations: (74 + 494 + 32 + 41)
  • MS contains the table of contains in the first two exposures and in the last one exposure.
  • MS contains the chapters up to the ṣaṭcatvāriṃśaḥ prakāśakaḥ in 494 folios.
  • One folio appears containing the stuti of pañcāyanadevatā and dakṣiṇamūrttijapavinoyoga in different hands.
  • vaiṣṇavamantrakhanḍa of the śivārcanacandrikā appears in separate 32 folioations in different hands; ends as:
garuḍaṃ śabdam uccāryya pravadec ca pracodayāt ||
gāyatrya(!)ṣā samākhyātā siddhidā mūlamaṃtrataḥ ||
  • Again vaiṣṇavamatra appears in different hands in another 41 foliations; ends as:
garudaṃ śabdam uccāryya pravade(!) ca pracodayāt ||
gāyatrya(!)ṣā samākhyātā siddhidā mūlamaṃtrataḥ ||
  • Two exposures on the first foliation of fols.13v–14r, 29v–30r, 35v–36r, 63v–64r, 67v–68r, 73v–74r
  • On the exp. 85 is written:
etacchivārcanacandrikāpustakaṃ nepālarājaguruhemarājaśarmaṇo jñeyam vai. saṃvat 1970
  • Two exposures on the second foliation of fols. 13v–14r, 81v–82r, 102v–104r, 112v–113r, 124v–125r, 133–134r, 145v–146r, 147v–148r, 149v–150r, 156v–157r, 183v–184r, 184v–185r, 185v–186r, 243v–244r, 255v–256r, 299v–300r, 316v–317r, 318v–319r, 321v–322r, 335v–336r, 339v–340r, 343v–345r, 351v–352r, 388v–389r, 393v–394r, 441v–442r, 450v–451r, 462v–463r, 470v–471r, 478v–479r,
  • Two exposures on the third foliation of fols. 18v–19r,
  • Two exposures on the fourth foliation of fols. 32v–33r, 38v–39r,

Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || 

śrīgurubhyo na[ma]ḥ

śrīśaṃbhave namaḥ ||

avighnam astu || ||

atha śauramantrā udhriyaṃta(!)

nandikeśvaramate ||

kailāśaśikhalā(!)sīnam arddhanārīśvaraṃ prabhuṃ |

brahmopendramahendrādyair devai(!) vidyādharais tata(!)

[mā]navair munibhiḥ siddhai(!) rakṣaubhīr apsaro gaṇaiḥ |

heraṃbaskaṃdapūrvvādigaṇair bhaktyā susevitaṃ |

akṣakrīḍārasā+dalāla saṃparameśvaraṃ |<ref name="ftn1">scribe leaves letters; unmetrical stanza </ref> (fol. 1v1–3)

End

saṃsārasāgaraṃ ghoraṃ tarttu⟨ṃ⟩m icchaṃti ye narāḥ ||

abhyarcya devatās teṣāṃ potā ete mayā kṛtāḥ ||

eteṣv ekaṃ samāruhya tallokadvīpam etya ca ||

tatra bhuktvākhilān bhogān muktiratnam avāpnuyāt ||

suṃdarācāryyaśiṣyeṇa [[śrī]]nivāsena dhīmatā ||

ṣaḍcatvāriṃśad agamat praṇītāyāṃ prakāśakaḥ || (fol. 493v11, 494r1–2)

Colophon

|| iti śrīsuṃdarācāryacaraṇāraviṃdadvaṃdvāṃtevāsinā śrīśrīnivāsabhaṭṭena viracitāyāṃ śrīśivārcanacaṃdrikāyāṃ ṣaṭcatvāriṃśaḥ prakāśakaḥ sampūrṇam(!) || || śubham || (fol. 494r2–3)
