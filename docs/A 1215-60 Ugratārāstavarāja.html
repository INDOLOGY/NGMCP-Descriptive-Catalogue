jaḍānāṃ jaḍatāhaṃsi<ref>Read -hantri?</ref> bhaktānāṃ bhaktavatsale ||

hūṃhūṃkāramaye devī<ref>A correction mark above "ī". Read devi.</ref> balihomapriye namaḥ ||
