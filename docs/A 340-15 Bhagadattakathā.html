Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ siddhi śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

praṇauṃ gaṇapati sārada māya ||

bramāviṣṇuke lāguṃ pāya ||

mātāpitāke vaṃdupāya

janmajanma harivṃdupāya || 1 ||

riṣi vyāsakakatḥā anusāri ||

bhāratha bhāṣo mahāpurānā

jo bhagadaṃta kini ranamāri

sarasa kathā suṣadeva vicāri || 2 ||

jujhi dusāsana ranamahi parau ||

mahābāhu korava saṃghārau ||

sugani baiṭhi vujhāva pāsā ||

kaisaiṃ ho mitra tuhma dharahu udāsā || 3 || (fol. 1v1–6)

End

bhāratha kathāko sunahu saṃvāda

je sune tihi putra kalyāṇa

je kathe tihi gaṃgā aśnana paṃḍavajī tirana bhayo tavahiṃ ||

nārāyaṇaparapaṃca ṣelyā javahiṃ ||

bhagadaṃta hi pāravatiko paṭhāu ||

nārāyaṇa paṃḍo garavāne jāne || bhagadaṃta jujhyā ai tavahi āni || (fol. 14r6–14v2)

Colophon

iti bhagadaṃtakathā saṃpurṇa subham astu jathā prati tatthāp liṣitaṃ mama doṣo na diyate sudhaṃ vā asudhaṃ vā || rāma rāma rāma rāma rāma liṣitaṃ prānagiri guru praśāde guruve (!) namaḥ viṣṇuve nama (!) | rāma rāma rāma rāma rāma rāma
