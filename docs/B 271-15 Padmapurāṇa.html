Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namaḥ vighneśvarāya namonamaḥ ||    ||

śaunaka uvāca ||
sūta sūta mahābhāgā vyāsaśiṣya hyakalmaṣa ||
tvadīyavadanāṃbhojān nānā khyānāni śṛṇyatāṃm (!) || 1 ||

tṛpti (!) na jāyate bhūyaḥ śravaṇecchā pravarddhate ||
kārttikasya ca māhātmyaṃ tulāsaṃsthā (!) divākare || 2 ||

māghamāsasya māhātmyaṃ makarasthe vibhāvasau ||
vaiśāṣamāsamāhātmyaṃ tathā meṣe gate ravau || 3 ||

tatra tatra ca ye dharmāḥ kathitāḥ sarvvaśas tvayā ||
etebhyo ʼpyadhikaṃ kaścin māsaś cet tava saṃmataḥ || 4 || (fol. 1v1–6)

End

sūta uvaca ||    ||
sanatkumāraḥ pītvaivaṃ śivavākyāmṛtaṃ param ||
śrutidvārā cāpa modaṃ kṛtakṛtyo babhūva ha || 36 ||

nabhomāsaṃ stuvan śaṃbhuṃ smaran sahṛdaye śivam ||
śaṃkareṇā ʼbhyanujñāto yayu (!) devarṣisattamaḥ || 37 ||

idaṃ rahasyaṃ paramaṃ nākhyeyaṃ yasyakasyacit ||
bhavatāṃ yogvatāṃ(!) dṛṣṭvā mayai tat kathitaṃ prabho || 38 || (fol. 118v2–7)

Colophon

iti śrīpadmapurāṇe īśvarasanatkumārasaṃvāde śrāvaṇamāhātmye anukramaṇikākathanaṃ nāma triṃśo ʼdhyāyaḥ śrāvaṇamāhātmya samāpta (!) ||    || śubham astu ||    || śrīgaṃgeśagirijeśvaraprītir astu ||    ||    || śrīumāśaṃkara praśannos tu (fol. 118v7–119r2)

nepālavarṣe śaśikhabhracandre
māse śuce kṛṣṇa śubhe dinesmin
tithau ca kāma śaśidevaṛkṣe
vyāghāṭa (!) yoge ravisūnuvāre ||

karke ca sūryye mithune ca candre
viprottama śrī aśvinīkumāraḥ || (!)
nabhasya māhātmya likhaṃ suvidyāṃ
ratepate yai ca suprītikāmā || (!) (fol. 118v7–119r5)
