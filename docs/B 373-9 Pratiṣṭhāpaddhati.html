Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

praṇamya gurupādābjaṃ samāsavyāsavarjjitā ||

kriyate sarvadevānāṃ pratiṣṭhāpaddhatir mayā || 1 ||

prāsādasya tad aṅgānām ādau karmakriyāgatam ||

satkriyāparihāryatvād dhvajādīnāṃ tathāntataḥ || 2 ||

samāhṛtyānyatantrāṇi tatprasiddhapadais tathā ||

vākyair [a]⟪ā⟫pi yathāśakti śiṣyānugrahahetave || 3 ||

tatra

sarvāṅganāṃ yataḥ siddiḥ prāyeṇācāryato bhavet ||

prāsādādivinirmāṇe tatpratiṣṭhādikarmasu || 4 || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

dinacatuṣṭayaparyantaṃ dīnānāthebhyo bhojanaṃ jñātibandhubāndhavebhyaś ca dadyāt || || caturthe hni samidbhir ājyena yavaiḥ kṛṣṇatilaiś ca hutvā śaktyā dakṣiṇāṃ dadyāt || ||

sarvān kāmānn avāpnoti phalaṃ cottamam aśnute || ||

yaścaikam api rājendra bṛkṣa[ṃ] saṃsthāpayennaraḥ ||

so pi svarge vasen martyo yāvad indrāś caturddaśaḥ || ||

aśvattham ekaṃ picumandam ekaṃ

nyagrodham ekaṃ daśaciṃciṇīkam || ||

kapitthabilvāmalakatrayaṃ ca

paṃcāmravāṭī narakaṃ na paśyet || ||

bhūtān bhavyān bhaviṣyāṃś ca tārayed romasaṃmitām || ❁ ||

paramāṃ siddhim āpnoti punarāvṛttidurlabhām || || (fol. 94v6–11)

Sub-colophon

iti śrīraghusūrisūnutrivikramasūriviracitāyāṃ pratiṣṭhāpaddhato(!) vāpīkūpataḍāgādyudyāpanam || atha bṛkṣo(!)sthāpanam ucyate || (fol. 94r6–7)
