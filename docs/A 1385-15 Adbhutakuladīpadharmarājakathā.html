Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namaḥ śrīdharmmarājāya || ||

natvā gurutriratnañ ca kathayāmi samāsataḥ (2)||

sarvasatvānukampāya dharmarājasya satkathām || 1 ||

atha dhīmān mahāsatvo jina śrīrā(3)ja ātmavit ||

jayaśṛīyaṃ yatin natvā sāñjalīr evam avravīt || 2 ||

bhadanta śrotum icchāmi (4) saṃgharatnasya sanmateḥ ||

śrīmato lokanāthasya māhātmyaṃ guṇam uttamam || 3 || (fol.1v1–4)

End

iti (vi)jñāpitaṃ śrutvā dīpaṃkaramu(7)nīśvaraḥ ||

pravilokya janān sarvān āśirvādaṃ dadantu (!) vai || 14 ||

dānapuṇyānubhāvena (8) yāyāsu sugatālaye ||

ityāśirvacanaṃ datvā pratyagamat(!) munīśvaraḥ || 15 ||

kuladīpo (9)pi taṃ dānaṃ visarjanaṃ kṛtaṃ tataḥ ||

etat puṇyānubhāvena kuladīpa (!) divaṃ yayau || (fol. 98r6–9)

Colophon

i(1)ti śrīvikramānandaviracitāyāṃ adbhutakuladīpadharmmarājakathāyāṃ dānadharmānusaṃśā u(2)nna(!)viṃśo ʼdhyāyaḥ || 19 || || samāptā ceyaṃ adbhutakuladīpadharmarājakathām (!) i(3)ti || śubham || || bhūyāt || śubha nepāli samvat 1035 miti (4) śrāvaṇakṛṣṇatrayodaśi (!) maṃgavārasa || gurusaphu vamojim thva saphu chāppe yānā sidhayakayakā (5) dina jula || ❁ || śubham || (fol. 98r9–98v5)
