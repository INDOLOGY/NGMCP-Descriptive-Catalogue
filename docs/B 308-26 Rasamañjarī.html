Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

ātmīyaṃ caraṇaṃ dadhāti purato nimnontāyāṃ bhuvi

svīyenaiva (2) kareṇa karkṣati taroḥ puṣpaṃ śramāśaṃkayā ||

talpe kiṃ ca mṛgatvacā viracite nidrāti bhāgair ni(3)jair

aṃtaḥ prema⟪bha⟫[[ra]]sālasāṃ priyatamām aṃge dadhāno haraḥ || 1 ||

vidvajjanamanobhṛṅgarasavyāsaṃgahe(4)tave ||

ekṣā prakāśyate śrīmadbhānunā rasamaṃjarī || 2 ||

tatra raseṣu śṛṃgārasyābhyarhitatvena tadā(5)laṃbanavibhāvatvena nāyikā tāvan nirūpyate || sā ca trividhā || svīyā parakīyā sāmānyā ceti || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

śikṣā yathā ||

sānaṃda(9)m āli vanamālivilokanāya

nirgacha (!) kuṃjam iti kiṃ nu vicārayethāḥ ||

jhaṃkā(10)riṇo madhuliho divase bhramaṃti

rātrau punaś capalacaṃcupudāś cakorāḥ ||

parihāso (11) yathā ||

†āgārabhittilikhitāsunivena† yasya kaḥ

†samamodaśasu† mūrtiṣu loka- (fol. 14v8–11)
