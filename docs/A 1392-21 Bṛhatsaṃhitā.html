Beginning

karādinakṛto divādimadhyāntagāminaḥ srigdhāḥ
avyucchitrātrmṛjavo vṛṣṭikarāsrehyamoghākhyāḥ 11

kalmāṣavabhrukapilāvicitram āstriṣṭhaharitaśavalābhāḥ
tridivānuvandhino vṛṣṭaye ’lpabhayadātu (!) saptāhāt 12

tāmrāvalapatimṛtyuṃ pītārūṇasannibhāś ca tadvayasanam
haritāḥ paśusasyavadhaṃ dhūmasavarṇāgavāṃ nāśam 13 (fol. 40r1–3)

End

antaracakraṃ virutaṃ śvaceṣṭitaṃ virutam atha śīvāyāś ca
caritaṃ mṛgāśvakariṇāṃ vāyasaviddyottaraṃ tataḥ 11

pāko nakṣatraguṇās tithikaraṇaguṇāḥ
sadhibśayajanmaguṇāḥ gocaras tathā
grahāṇāṃ kathito nakṣatraparuṣaś ca 12

śatam idam adhyāyānām uparipāṭikramād anukrāntam
atha ślokasahastrāṇy āvaddhānyūnacatvāri 13 (fol. 204v1–3)

Sub-colophon

Colophon

iti srīvarāha. bṛhatsaṃ. upasaṃhāro nāma ṣaḍadhikaśatatamo ’dhyāyaḥ 116 (fol. 204v4)
