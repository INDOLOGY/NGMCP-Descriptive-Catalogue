Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

yo[ʼ]naṃto[ʼ]naṃtaśaktiḥ sṛjati jagad idaṃ pālayaty aṃtarātmā
saṃviśyāṃte nipīya (khakamahimagatataḥ) satyacinmūrttir āste |
yo[ʼ](nugaḥ) sajjanānāṃ paramahitatamaḥ pāpinām ugramūrttiḥ
sāsmākaṃ vāṃchitāni pradiśatu bhagavān ātmadaḥ śrīnṛsiṃhaḥ || 1 ||

yanmūlapra⟨pra⟩vahatpavitrapayasaḥ saṃsevanād eva me
rāgadveṣamadābhidhā grahagaṇā (neśusra)saṃtāpinaḥ ||
yatsaṃsārapariśramāahadayāṃ saṃśītalāmodabhāg
vaṃde tatpuruṣottamāśramaguroḥ pādāravindadvayaṃ || 2 ||

kṛtvā chāṃdogyanāmnya(!) upaniṣado vojñagocarāṃ ṭīkāṃ ||
bhūyo[ʼ]lpabuddhigamyāṃ nityanaṃdo mitākṣarāṃ kurve ||    || 3 || (fol. 1r1–7)

End

yanmahyādikamāśritaṃ bahuguṇaṃ brahmacaryādibhiryukto hṛdyavarudhya mūrddhagatatayānādbhyāṃ ʼpyasautraṃ padaṃ | kāmaṃ krīḍati yasturoyamamalaṃ saikaṃ śatābde guraustvitvāde vapatirbubodha tadahaṃ dhyāye nṛsiṃhāvidhaṃ || kārya brahmapadāṃtatuṃgaphalavadhyānaprabhedācalaṃ viśvodbhūti vibhūtivādavilāsaṃ dvīcītaraṃ gokulaṃ dainyamuṃceta bhojanāgurutariṃ cārohatālaṃśramai (mahitāmanavadyasvadyamaṇaye) chāṃdogyaratnākaraṃ ||
(fol. 123v4–9)

Colophon

iti śrīparamahaṃsaparivrājakācārya śrīpuruṣottamāśramapūjyapādaśiṣya nityānamdāśramaviracitāyāṃ chāṃdogyaṭīkāyāmitākṣarāyāmaṣtamodhyāyaḥ samāptaḥ || śubhaṃ bhavatu || sāmavedāya namaḥ || śrīgaṇeśāyanamaḥ || ❁
(fol. 123v9–11)
