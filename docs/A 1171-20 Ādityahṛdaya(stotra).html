Excerpts

Beginning

paṃ bhubaneśvara bhaṭtarāī
āditya ṛdaya(!) || (fol. 1r)
śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || oṃ ādityāya namaḥ ||
tato yuddhapariśrāntaṃ samare caṃtayā sthitaṃ ||
rāvaṇaṃ cāgrato dṛṣṭvā yuddhāya samupasthitam || 1 ||
daivataiś ca samāgamya draṣṭum abhyāgato raṇam ||
upagamyābravīd rāmam agastyo bhagavāṃs tadā || 2 ||
rāma rāma mahābāho śrṇu guhyaṃ sanātanam ||
yena sarvān arīn vatsa samare vijayiṣyase(!) || 3 ||
ādityahṛdayaṃ puṇyaṃ sarvaśatruvināśanam ||
jayāvahaṃ japan nityam akṣayam paraṃmaṃ(!) śivam || 4 ||
sarvamaṃgalamāṃgalyaṃ sarvapāpapranāśanam ||
ciṃtāśokapraśamanam āyurvardhanam uttamam || 5 ||
raśmimantaṃ samudyantaṃ devāsuranamaskṛtam ||
pūjayasva vivasvantaṃ bhāskaraṃ bhuvaneśvaraṃ || 6 ||
sarvadevātmako hy eṣa tejasvī raśmibhāvanaḥ ||
eṣa devāsuragaṇān lokān yāti gabhastibhiḥ || 7 || (fol. 1v1–2r3)

End

etac chrutvā mahātejā naṣṭaśoko ʼbhavat tadā ||
dārayāmāsa suprīto rāghavaḥ prayatātmavān || 28 ||
ādityaṃ prekṣya japtvedaṃ paraṃ harṣam avāptavān ||
trir ācamya śucir bhūtvā dhanur ādāya vīryavān || 29 ||
rāvaṇaṃ prekṣya hṛṣṭātmā jayārthaṃ samupāgamat ||
sarvayatnena mahatāṛtas tasya vadhe ʼbhavat || 30 ||
atha ravikhaṃdan(!) nirī[[kṣasa]]maṃ
muditamanāḥ paramaṃ prahṛṣyamāṇaḥ ||
niśicarapatisaṃkṣayaṃ viditvā
suragaṇamadhyagato† vacas tvareti† || 31 || (fol. 4r1–6)

Colophon

iti śrīmadrāmāyaṇe vālmīkīye ādikāvye yuddhakāṇḍe
