Manuscript Features

This manuscript contains dhyāna mantras of various gods including Indra, Umā, Varuṇa, Kubera, Agni, Naiṛtya, Vāyu, Īśāna and Argha. On exps. 13-15 and 17 are drawn tāntrika yantras.

Excerpts

Beginning

indrayā dhyāna || airāvatagajārūḍhaṃ svavarṇavarṇakiritinasahasraśayanaṃ śakravajrapānivibhāvayet. indrāya vajrahastāya surādhipataye airāvatāsanāya namaḥ || (exp. 1t1–3)

End

rudra sadalapumasajivā māśivasagatikādāyā racośe devajuro svasti kuruāt sadā virghni svasti mārtaṇḍareva ca || eiva ca || yoginā svasti sarvva svastiyā śca śatrujā- (exp. 19t1–3)
