Excerpts

Beginning

pārvaty uvāca ||

devādidevasarvvajña (!) vasirmmānavasaṃjñakaḥ (!) ||

proktas tvayā hi kaṭhino devatātṛptikārakaḥ ||

tadabhāve phalaṃ tadvat kathaṃ syāt tad vadasva me ||

sahasrābdamahāmāyā saṃtuṣṭā jāyate yathā || ||

śiva uvāca ||

kālajñatvāt kukkutoyaṃ balirmmānavarūpakaḥ ||

vidhinā mānuṣasyeva cakraśodhanapūrvvakaṃ || (fol. 21r4–6)

End

cakrākṣarāṇi saṃsmṛtya namonta gopayet tanuṃ ||

saṃśuddhe kukkuṭe devi chūrikm abhimaṃtrayet ||

durgayā tvaṃ dhṛtā devi sarvvadaityāntakārake ||

atas tvāṃ pūjayiṣyāmi kukkuṭasya valiṃ hara ||

kārajñānāya vidmahe kukkuṭatvāya dhīmahi || tanno valiṃ pracodayāt || karṇṇe gāyatrīṃ prajapya balir ddeyaḥ || || (fol. 21v1–3)

Colophon

iti kukkuṭavaliḥ samāptaḥ || ||❖ || (fol. 21v3–4)
