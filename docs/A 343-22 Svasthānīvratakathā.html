Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīsvasthānī parameśvaryyai ||
oṃ namaḥ śivāya ||

kailāśaparvvatayā tvapalasa bijyāka, gathiṃ tvā(2)pala nānā dhātuna svabhālāka kalpavṛkṣayā vana kāmadhenu dasyaṃ coṃ cintāmaṇi thva parvvata anā sama(3)sta śobhālāka thathiṃ thāyasa bijyāka jagat saṃsārayā nātha samasta sesyaṃ bika parameśvara || (fol. 1r1–3)

End

he mahāgaurī thvate phala lāka śrīsvasthānī(2)vrata mahāuttama gohmana thva vrata pṛthvīsa yātā ohmāyā sukha avasyaṃ juyuva lakṣmī paśu ādi(3)na putra vidyā āyu yasa vala lāyu śrīsvasthānīvrata prabhāvana thva hlāko samastaṃ nissandeha(4)na śrīsvasthānīyā kathā nitya hlāyuva gohmaṃ manukṣana hlākahmaṃyāṃ ṅeṅahmaṃyāṃ samasta pāpa phu(5)yuva dhanadhānya vṛddhi juyuva putrapautrao sahitana sukha lāyuva || (fol 11v1–5)
