«Beginning: »


❖ śrīnamas tasyai gaṇeśāya ca ||


yad romavivare kīṭā iva brahmāṇḍakoṭayaḥ |


tad agādhakṛpāsindhūm apāraṃ samupāsmahe ||


natvā guruṃ paraguruñ ca parāparākhyaṃ ||


tasmāt parañ ca parameṣṭhīguruṃ praṇamya ||


niskṛṣya sāram akhilāgamaśāstrasindhoḥ ||


śrīmad ramāramaṇa eṣa samucciniti ||


vastūni dūratara dūratameṣu gatvā


nānā vidhāny upacinoti janaḥ prayatnaiḥ ||


ekatra tāni militāni puro bhilabhyaḥ


daivena ke ca sudhiyo vimukhī bhavanti ||


tatrādau samupāsya devatānirṇaye mantravicāraḥ pravarttate ||


atas taṃ nirṇayārthaṃ puro haracakrāmabhidhīyate || (fol. 1v1–4)


«End: »


svapnādīnā mantralābhe dīkṣoktakrameṇa kalaśaṃ saṃsthāpya vaṭapatreṇa kuṃkumena


likhitvā daśasaṃskāraṃ vidhāya guror mantragrahaṇaṃ kuryāt athavā taddevatokta


sarvvacakravicāritamantrāntaraṃ gṛhṇīyāt upāsyasyaikamantratvāt svapnepi mantrakulena


upāsyopadeśāt tanmantram api japet || bahumantravataḥ puṃsaḥ kā kathā śiva eva saḥ ||


ityādi puraskāraśravaṇād iti saṃkṣepaḥ || atha mantrasaṃskāraḥ || phetkāriṇītantre ||



jananaṃ jīvanaṃ caivaṃ tāḍanaṃ bodhanaṃ tathā ||


athābhiṣeko vimalīkaraṇā(pyāyanā)pyāyanaṃ tataḥ ||


tarppaṇaṃ dīpanaṃ caiva śodhanaṃ daśamaḥ smṛtaṃ ||


mantrāṇāṃ mātṛkāvarṇād uddhāro jajanaṃ (!) smṛtaṃ |


mantravarṇaṃś ca pratyekaṃ praṇavāntaritān tathā ||


( kṛtvā ca vajrarūpeṇa jī(vanaṃ) parikīrttitaṃ || )


mantravarṇā (fol. 7v4–8)
