Manuscript Features

This MTM contains the following texts :

1. Kārttavīryārjunastotra (fols. 1–8; exps. 4–11)

2. Kārttavīryārjunastotra (fols. 1–5; exps. 12–16)

There are two separate foliations for each text; the first runs up to 8 and the second up to 6.

The end portions of the both texts are missing.

There are impresses of the seal of Chandra Shumshere dated 1970 [VS] on the front and back cover-leaves and also on exp. 11b.

Excerpts

Beginning of 1. Kārttavīryārjunastotra

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

oṁ asya śrīkārttavīryārjjunakavacasya dattātreyaṛṣir anuṣṭup chandaḥ śrīkārttavīryārjuno viśṇuś cakravarttī devatā śrīkārttavīryārjunaprītyā mamābhīṣṭasiddhyarthe pāṭhe viniyogaḥ |

avyāt sarvabhayāt prabhākaranibhaḥ pradyotano dyotana(!)

svarṇasrakparivītakaṃdharadharo raktāṃśukoṣṇīṣavān ||

nānāratnavibhūṣitakarasahasrārddhāntavāṇāsano

vāṇāntārddhasahasrabāhur aniśaṃ bhūvallabho na[ḥ] prabhuḥ |

kārttavīryārjunacaraṇābhyāṃ namaḥ || (fol. 1v1–4; 1st foliation)
