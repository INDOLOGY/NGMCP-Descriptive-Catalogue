Manuscript Features

The NGMPP catalogue card refers to the BSP 4.2, pp. 68ff for this entry. But, this entry does not exist in the BSP. It seems that the reference is given only to refer the section of the Mahākālasaṃhitā.

Available folios 1–8, 10, 12–25, 28–35, 37, 39–40, 42–58, 61–64, 68–69, 88–137, 139, 141–151

Two exposures of fols. 10v–11r, 18v–19r and 55v–56r.

After the colophon appears a folio not related to the text. It is eligible as well.

The NGMPP catalogue card (located as exp. 124) is written missing folls 9, 11, 19, ⟪‥‥⟫ 38, 39. But, it is not enough information about the missing folios.
