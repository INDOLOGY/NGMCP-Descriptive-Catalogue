Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namo nārāyanāya ||

nārāyaṇaṃ namaskṛtya naraṃ caiva narottamaṃ |

devīṃ sarasvatīṃ caiva tato jayam udīrayet || ||

vaiśampāyana uvāca ||

tato bravīt mayaḥ pārthaṃ vāsudevasya sannidhau |

prāñjaliḥ ślakṣṇayā vācā pūjayitvā punaḥ punaḥ || ||

maya ⟪ru⟫ uvāca ||

a[[smāt]] kṛṣṇāt susaṃkruddhāt pāvakāc ca didhakṣataḥ ||

tvayā trāto smi kaunteya brūhi kiṃ karavāṇi te || || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

tathā hi balavān rājā jarāsaṃdho mahāvaraḥ |

bāhupraharaṇenaiva bhīmasenapātitaḥ ||

tasya te śama evāstu pāṇḍavair bharatarṣabha |

ubhayoḥ pakṣayor yuktaṃ kriyatām āviṣaṃkayā (!) ||

evaṃ gālvagaṇe kṣattā dharmārthasahītaṃ (!) vacaḥ |

uktavān hi hītaṃ caiva mayā putrahitepsayā || (fol. *176r2–4)

Sub-colophon

iti śrīmahābhārate sabhāparvvasāhasryāṃ saṃhitāyāṃ vaiśiṣkāṃ (!) sabhāparvaṇi || 76 || asyānantaraṃ āraṇyakaṃ parvva bhaviṣyati || yasyāyaṃ pratisandhiḥ || ||

janamejaya uvāca ||

purāḥ śūtajitā (!) pārthāḥ kopitāś ca durātmajiḥ | dhārttarāṣtraiḥ sadāmātya nikṛtya dvijasattama || || (fol. *176r4–6)

Colophon

samāptam idaṃ sabhāparvvapustaka mitiḥ || samvat 824 vaiśākamāse śukrera pakṣa śatamuraviśarttamyātithuśidhaṃḥ || || rikhita puruṣottamaḥ || śrī 3 śreṣṭhadevasalana śubha || (fol. *176r6–*176v2)
