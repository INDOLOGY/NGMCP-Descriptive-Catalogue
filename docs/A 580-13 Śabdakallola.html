Manuscript Features

The first folio of the manuscript is missing, so the initial part of the text is lost.
On the otherwise blank page between the two foliations is a stamp of the Nepal Rastriya Pustakalaya and the words: sārasvatānusārī śabdakallolaḥ nandanakṛtaḥ.
On exp. 2 is written: sārasvatānusārī śabdakallollāsa.
Fols. 2–18 (first foliation) run from shortly after the beginning of the Saṃjñāprakaraṇa chapter to the end of the Samāsaprakaraṇa chapter.
Fols. 1–17 (second foliation) contain text from the Bhvādi chapter.
The handwriting of the two foliations differs.
Vertical lines are drawn on the top of headmarks to separate words.
