Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

atha paṃcamanyāsaḥ ||

hrīṃ śrīṃ klīṃ hrūṃ chrīṃ strīṃ khphreṃ aṃ āṃ anantaśatakoṭimahābhūtegaṇaparivāritāsitaṃgabhairavakulapūjitavanditabhāvitasaṃstutāṃgabhairavabrahmavatīguhyakālikāśrīpādukāṃ pūjayāmi || śrīṃ hrīṃ klīṃ hūṃ hauṃ phreṃ haṃ kṣaṃ || asitaṃgamahābhairavabrahmavatīparabrahmanirvvāṇaguhyakālībhyāṃ hrūṃ phaṭ namaḥ svāhā khphreṃ strīṃ chrīṃ hūṃ klīṃ śrīṃ hrīṃ || dakṣapādāṃguṣṭhe || (fol. 46v1–6)

End

śrūguhyakālikābhyāṃ śrīpādukāṃ pūjayāmi kṣaṃ laṃ haṃ saṃ ṣaṃ śaṃ vaṃ laṃ raṃ yaṃ maṃ bhaṃ vaṃ phaṃ paṃ naṃ dhaṃ daṃ thaṃ taṃ ṇaṃ ḍhaṃ ḍaṃ ṭhaṃ ṭaṃ ñaṃ jhaṃ jaṃ chaṃ caṃ ṅaṃ ghaṃ gaṃ khaṃ kaṃ aḥṃ aṃṃ auṃ eiṃ eṃ lṝṃ lṛṃ ṝṃ ṛṃ ūṃ uṃ īṃ iṃ āṃ aṃ khphreṃ strīṃ chrīṃ hūṃ klīṃ śrīṃ hrīṃ || vyāpakaṃ || (fol. 56v1–3)

Colophon

iti sāmarasyakulayoginīmahādevatānyāsaḥ pañcamaḥ || || śubham astu || (fol. 56v3–4)
