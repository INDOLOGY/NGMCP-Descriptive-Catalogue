Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || śrīrāmacandrāya namaḥ

kūṃjaṃtaṃ rāmarāmeti madhuraṃ madhurākṣaram.
āruhya kavinā śākhāṃ vande vālmīki kokilaṃ 1

vālmīker munisiṃhasya kavitāvanacāriṇaḥ
śṛṇvan rāmakathānādaṃ konayāti parāṃgatiṃ 2

yaḥ pivan satataṃ rāmacaritāmṛtasāgaram.
goṣyadīkṛtavārīśaṃ maśakī kṛtasaṃkṣayaṃ
rāmāyaṇamahāmālā ratnaṃ vande nilātmajaṃ 4 (fol. 1v1–5)

End

idaṃ pavitraṃ pāpaghnaṃ purāpa . . vaiś ca sammitaṃm
yaṃ paṭhed rāmacaritaṃ sarvapāpaiḥ pramucyateḥ 108

etad ākhyānaṃ āyuṣyaṃ paṭhan rāmāyaṇaṃ naraḥ
sa putrapautrasagaṇaḥ petyasvarge mahīyate 109

paṭhan dvijo vā gṛṣabhatvamīyāt satkṣatriyobhūmipatitvamīyāt
vaṇig janaḥ puṇyapatitvamīyājjanaś ca śudropi mahattvamīyāt 100 (fol. 10r6–10v4)

Colophon

ityārṣe śrīmadrāmāyaṇe vālmikīye ādikāvye nāradavākyasaṃkṣepo nāma prathamaḥ sargaḥ śrīrāmacandrārpaṇam astu (fol. 10v4–5)
