Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

śabdabrahmacidānandam adhiṣṭhānam upāsmahe ||

yasya varṇāḥ padaṇ vākyaṃ vivartāḥ saṃcakāśati ||

mahābhāṣyamataṃ bhartṛhariṇāpi prakāśitam ||

ālokyasarvataṃtrāṇi sphoṭa⟪ratnaṃ⟫ [[tattvaṃ]] nirūpyate ||

iha khalu padād vākyāc cārthapratipattiḥ sarvānubhavasiddhāḥ sarvavādisaṃmatā ca tatra varaṇā na pratyekam artham abhidadhati | prathamavarṇād evārthāvagatau

varṇāntaravaiyarthyāpatteḥ | na militā ucanīca(!)pradhvaṃsināṃ kramikāṇāṃ melanābhāvāt | nāpy ekaśrutigocarāḥ | nadī rasa ityādau dīna sara iti ekasmṛtiviṣayatvenāviśeṣāpatteḥ | ato varṇābhivyaṅgyapadasphoṭo vā vākyasphoṭo vā ʼnavayava eko nityorthavācaka ityāha || ||

na pratyekaṃ na militā na vaika śrutigocarāḥ ||

arthasya vācakā varṇāḥ kin tu sphoṭaḥ sa ca dvidhā || 1 ||

padasphoṭo vākyasphoṭaś ca |

tatra padasphoṭavādi(!) padasphoṭa eva nityonavayavaḥ paramārthataḥ san nadhiṣṭhānaṃ varṇavākyayos tatrādhyāsād vivarta iti tadvibhāga āvidyakaḥ kevalapadasphoṭebhya eva ākāṃkṣādimahimnā vākyārthaḥ samullasati iti manyate |

(fol.1v1-7)

End

ata evāgame pi

śabdabrahma yadā pare sukṛtinaś caitanyam antargataṃ

tad vo vyād aniśaṃ śaśāṅkasadanaṃ vācām adhīśaṃ mahaḥ

ityalam ativistareṇa ||

prāktaṃtrasūktiratnānāṃ mālākṛ(ṣṇa)vinirmitā ||

viduṣām adviṣāṃ kaṇṭhe bhāsatām asatām api ||

kriyākārakabhāvena yat sarvatrānubhūyate ||

sa pratyag ātmā jayati śabdabrahmamayaṃ mahaḥ ||

śabdabrahmābhidhānaṃ yad bhāṣyakārādisaṃmatam ||

śrīśeṣakṛṣṇaviduṣā sphoṭatatvaṃ nirūpitam ||

vidvdbhiḥ sadasadvyaktikāribhiḥ sārahāribhiḥ ||

rāgavidveṣarahitaiḥ dūṣitābhūṣitās tu vā ||

(fol.14v5-15r2)

Colophon

iti sphoṭatatvaṃ śubham || śrīḥ || ||
