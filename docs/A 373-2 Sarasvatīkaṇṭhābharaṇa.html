Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

dhvanir varṇāḥ padaṃ vākya,m ity āspadacatuṣṭayaṃ |

yasyāḥ sūkṣmādivhedena vāgdevīn tāṃ upāsmahe ||

nirddoṣaṃ guṇavat kāvya,m laṃkārair alaṃ(2)kṛtaṃ |

rasānvitaṃ kaviḥ kurvvan kīrttiṃ prītiṃ ca viṃdati ||

doṣāḥ padānāṃ vākyānāṃ, vākyārthānāṃ ca ṣoḍaśa |

heyāḥ kāvye kavīndrair yye, tān evādau pracakṣmahe (3) ||

asādhu cāprayuktaṃ ca, kaṣṭaṃ cānarthakaṃ ca yat

anyārthakam apuṣṭārtha,m asamarthaṃ tathiva ca || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

śāsaane (92r1) pi guruṇi vyavasthitaṃ kṛtyavastuṣu niyukṣva(!) kāmataḥ ||

tvatprayojanadhanaṃjayād anya eṣa †iti tāñ ca āvagāḥ† ||

atyantābhāvasya sāmarthyābhāvasya ca pradhvaṃ(2)sābhāvo yathā ||

anāptapuṇyopacayair durāpā phalasya nirdūtarajāḥ (!) savitrī ||

tulyābhavad darśanasaṃpad eṣā vṛṣṭer ddivo vītavalāhakāyāḥ ||

ta etenātidur(3)bbādhā itito vyākṛtāḥ || 7 ||

arthālaṃkṛtayo py etāś caturvviṃśatisaṃjñayā ||

kathitāḥ kāvyavittānāṃ cittaprahlādahetave || || (fol. 92r1–3)

Colophon

iti mahārājadhirāja(4)śrībhojadevaviracite sarasvatīkaṇṭhābharaṇālaṃkāre ʼrthālaṃkāra vivecano nāma tṛtīyaḥ paricchedaḥ samāptaḥ || 3 || || śubham || || ○ || (5)saṃvat 1832 pauṣaśudi 8 śukravāre likhitam idaṃ pustakaṃ sampūrṇam iti || śubham bhūyāt || || (fol. 92r3–5)
