Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrīmahābhairavapāduke(!)bhyo namaḥ ||

saṃvid ullāsito yena cidghanānandasāgarāt |

svataṣkulāc cidānandāc chaktisrāntaravan numaḥ ||

śrīsamvarttāmaṇdalānte kramapadanihitānandaśaktiḥ subhīmā

sṛksūkṣmādyaṃ catuṣkaṃ akulakulagataṃ pañcakaṃ cānyaṣaṭkaṃ |

catvāraḥ pañcakonyaḥ punar api caturas tattvato maṇḍaledaṃ

saṃsṛṣṭaṃ yena tasmai namata gurūvaraṃ bhiravaṃ śrīkujeśaṃm (!) || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

sarvvavighnavinirmukto sarvvaduḥkhavivarjitaḥ |

sarvvadvaṃdvavihīnaś ca śatruto na bhayaṃ ⟨bhayaṃ⟩ bhaye(!)t

sarvakāmān avāpnoti sarvvasiddhiphalaṃ labhet

pūjanāt tantrarājasya labhate śāśvataṃ padaṃ ||

triṣ(!)kālaṃ maṇḍalaṃ kṛtvā tantrasāra(!) prapūjayet |

pūjanāt siddhim āpnoti tuṣyanti ca marīcayaḥ (fol. 222r2–4)

Colophon

|| iti śrīśrīkaṇṭhanāthāvatārite śrīcandradvīpavinirgate yoginīguhye vidyāpīṭhe śrīmatasāragarbhanirṇaye śrīcaturvviṃśatsahsrasaṃhitāsārasamuccaye ṣaṭsāhasre śrīmatottare paṃcaviṃśatimaḥ(!) paṭalaḥ samāptaḥ | (fol. 22r4–5)
