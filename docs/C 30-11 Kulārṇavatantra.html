Excerpts

Beginning

śrīparadevatāyai namaḥ ||

guruṃ gaṇapatiṃ durggāṃ vaṭukaṃ śivam acyutaṃ |

brahmāṇaṃ girijāṃ lakṣmīṃ vāṇī (!) vande vibhūtaye ||

anādyā yā khilādyā māyine gatamāyine |

arūpāya sarūpā (!) śivāya gurave namaḥ ||

parāprāsādamantrāya saccidānandacetase |

agnīṣomātmarūpāya tryaṃbakāya namo namaḥ ||

śrīdevy uvāca ||

bhagavan devadeveśa paṃcakṛtyavidhānakaḥ |

sarvvadā bhaktisulabha śaraṇāgatavatsala ||

kuleśaparameśāna karuṇāmṛtavāridhe |

asāre ghorasaṃsāre sarvvaduḥkhamalīmase ||

nānāvidhaśarīrasthā anantājvajāyaṃteḥ (!) |

jāyante ca vilīyante teṣām anto na vidyate || (fol. 1r1–6)

End

devi yad vidyate prājña (!) tattat kiṃcit mayoditaṃ ||

sādhakānāṃ hitārthāya bhuktimuktiphalekṣiṇāṃ ||

paścārddhāmnāyamahātmyaṃ paṭhitvā cakrasannidhau ||

bhaktyā paramayā devi yaḥ śṛṇoti sa kaulikaḥ ||

vratadānatapastīrthaṃ yajñadānārccanādiṣu ||

tatphalaṃ koṭiguṇitaṃ labhate nātra saṃśayaḥ ||

tatsannidhau sa nivaset nātra kāryā vicāraṇā || || (fol. 137r5–8)

Colophon

iti śrīkulārṇṇave mahārahasye .. .. .. .. .. .. pañcamakhaṇḍe nāmavāsarādikaḥ || tan nāma saptadaśollāsasamāptaḥ || || samvat 771 śrāvaṇaśulkapakṣe daśamī bṛhaspativāla anurādhānakṣatre thva kuhnu || thva pustakasaṃpūrṇṇa yāṅā || śubha || (fol. 137r8–9)
