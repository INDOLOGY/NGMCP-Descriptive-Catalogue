Manuscript Features

There are two extra folios at the beginning, of which the first is numbered as fol. 1 and belongs to a Kuśopadeśa manuscript. The second is illegible. Extra folios are written by another hand.

Excerpts

Beginning

❖ namo lokanāthāya ||

adya praṇamya girijā karuṇāmayī sā
trailokyaśṛṣṭikaranīṃ jananīsvarūpīm |
premānabandha(!) mama duḥkhav(i)mocanārthaṃ
saṃkīrttiyāmi(!) yuvatiṃ maraṇāgateṣu || 1 || (fol. 1v1-) (exp. 005)

adyāpi tā(!) kanakacampakadāmagaurīṃ
phullāraviṃdavadanān tanuromarājīm |
suptosthitā(!)madanavihvalalālasāṅgī(!)
vidyā(!) pramādaguṇitām iva cintayāmi || 2 ||

adyāpi tāṃ śaśimukhīn navayo(!)vanāḍhyā(!)
pīnastanīm punar ahaṃ yadi gaurakāntim |
paśyāmi māṃmatha(!)śarānalapīḍitāṅgī(!)
gātrāṇi saṃprati karomi suśītalāni || 3 ||

adyāpi tā(!) yadi punaḥ kamalāyatākṣīm
paśyāmi pīvarapayodharabhārakhiṃnām |
saṃpīḍya bāhugalena(!) pibāmi vaktraṃ
unmattavat madhukaraḥ kamalaṃ yathaiṣṭam(!) || 4 || (fol. 1v5) (exp. 005)

End

adyāpi nojjhati haraḥ kila kālakūṭam

ambhonidhir vahati durvva⟪‥⟫havāḍavāgnim
aṅgīkṛtaṃ sukṛtino paripālayaṃti || 56 ||

īṣatmīlitalocanena yuvatī vyāvarttitabhrūlatā
saṃdaṣṭa(!)dharavedanā pralapitaṃ hā heti maṃdākṣaram |
tanvaṃjñā(?)suratāvasānasamaye dṛṣṭaṃ mukhaṃ yat mayā
svedāṅgīkṛtapāṇḍugaṇḍapulakaṃ tat kena vismāryate(!) || 57 || ❁ || (fol. 9r2-5)

Colophon

iti caurapañcāsikaḥ ❖ || ślokaḥ samāptaḥ ||❁ || (fol. 9r5-9v1)
