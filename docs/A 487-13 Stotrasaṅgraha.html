Manuscript Features

The MS contains the following stotras:

1. Mahāmāyāstotra 2. Bhairavāṣṭaka 3. Bhairavastotra 4. Āpaduddhārastavarāja 5. Tāriṇīkavaca


Excerpts

Beginning of 1. Mahāmāyāstotra

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīsiddhilakṣmī ||

yā devī siddhilakṣmī haraśiraśirayā jñānaśaktipareśī

icchāśaktisuṣumnā gatiśirayaguṇāḥ kuṇḍalī mārggasaṃsthā ||

.. .. .. .. .. devī .. .. .. śivayutā śaktisīlīnagarbbhā

yasyāḥ krīḍārthamātat sṛjatilayabhṛto yā vidhatte staneśī || 1 ||

yat sūkṣmāṃ karṇṇam ekaparamaparagatāṃ tat suṣumnātarasthaṃ |

dūtai tatvai dvibhinnaṃ śravadāmṛtamayaṃ yoginā tatvagamyaṃ | (exp. 1t1–5)


End of 5. Tāriṇīkavaca

mūrttitr ayaṃ vidadhatī saṃpūjya ca subhakṣitaḥ |

kavacasyāsyapāṭhena manas teṣāṃ susiddhidaḥ || 18 ||

devī balapradā śīghraṃ .. .. tmadāyinī || (exp. 22t5–b1)


Colophon of 5. Tāriṇīkavaca

iti śivoktaṃ tāriṇīkavacaṃ samāptaṃ || || ❖ || (exp. 22b1)
