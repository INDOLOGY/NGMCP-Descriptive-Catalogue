Excerpts

«Beginning»

atha caturthadivase prātar nityakarma samāpya | pūjāmaṇḍale svāsane upaviśya || ācamya prāṇānāyamya || dīpakalaśagaṇeśādīnāṃ puṣpāṃjaliṃ samarcya || pūrvoktapūjāvidhinā uttarapūjāṃ vidhāya || havanaṃ kuryāt || (fol. 19v4–8)


«End»

arghyaṃ dadyād arundhatyai dṛṣṭvā caiva praṇamya ca ||

sakhibhir brāhmaṇaiḥ sārddhaṃ bhuñjīta vijitendriyā ||

jalena pāraṇaṃ kṛtvā ʼśnīyāt || || (fol. 23v9–12)


«Colophon»


iti vaṭasāvitrīvratapūjodyāpanavidhiḥ || ||

iti samvat 1999 sāla phālgina vadi 1 ravivāsare phāguna gate 10 likhitam idaṃ bhuvaneśvaraśarmaṇā || śubhaṃ bhatu sarveṣām || || (fols. 23v12–24r2)
