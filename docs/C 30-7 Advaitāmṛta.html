Excerpts

Beginning

śrīkṛṣṇāya namaḥ ||

hariharasarasvatī jagadgururīḍyaḥ paramahaṃsānāṃ ||

sajagannāthapadottarasarasvatīśabdasaṃvedyaḥ || 1 ||

karmandikaṇṭhālaṃkāraṃ sāraṃ vedāntavāridheḥ

racayatyamalaṃ grantham advaitāmṛtasaṃjñakaṃ || 2 ||

āsīd yativaraḥ kaścid vivekāśramasaṃjñakaḥ ||

yatprasādena bahavo muktimārgam upāgatāḥ || 3 ||

sakhocitaṃ maṭhaṃ loke kvacanāpi vicārayan ||

nāvāyataṃ mano yatra yates tasya prasīdatī || 4 ||

navāṃ kāṃ kāṃ kanandatrigajasaṃkhyāvilokitāḥ ||

maṭhās tena jagatyasmiṃs teṣu lebhe sthitiṃ na saḥ || 5 ||

evaṃ girivarāraṇyaṃ saritsāgaravāpikāḥ ||

paśyatā kila tenaikṣi paṃcajanyābhidho maṭhaḥ || 6 || (fol. 1v1–6)

End

sahariharasarasvatīṃdra caṃ (!) ca dyumaṇīkarābhihatājñatāṃdhakāraḥ ||

jayati paramahaṃsayogivaryo naraharisaṃjñacidātmasaktacittaḥ || 69 ||

jitāhāro jitakrodho jagannātha sarasvati

yenāyaṃ racito graṃthaṃ advaitāmṛtasaṃjñakaḥ || 70 ||

etasya kavalānyaṃ cāsvāghnasādarato gatāḥ

amṛtatvaṃ prayāṃtyāśu vibudhā yatisattamāḥ || 71 || (fol. 37v4–7)

Colophon

iti śrīmatparamahaṃsaparivrājakācārya hariharasasvatīpriyaśiṣyajagannāthasarasvatīviracite ʼdvaitāmṛte paṃcamaḥ kavalaḥ samāptam śubham (fol. 37v7–8)
