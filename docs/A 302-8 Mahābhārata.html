Manuscript Features

The text starts from the 29th adhyāya and runs up to the end (64th adhyāya) of Śalyaparvan. Those adyāyas are known as Tīrthayātrā(upa)parvan and Gadāyuddha(upa)parvan (POONA EDITION).
