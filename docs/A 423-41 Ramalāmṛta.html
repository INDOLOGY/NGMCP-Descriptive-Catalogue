Manuscript Features

On the exp. 2 is written

nakhaṃ kakhest mokalākadaṃnadaṃtarīkhakaṃ

jamātam aṣṭamaṇḍalaṃ śareṃdumadiraṃ (!) sadā 1

tad aṣṭakhaṃḍamalī bhavejjamīrakuṃḍalī

nṛpāṃkavāracāriṇī manaḥ kathāptasāriṇī 2

On the exp. 3t, is described seven formulas to forecast the ramala and on the fols. 1r–8v is listed forecast of ramala according to the number opened.
