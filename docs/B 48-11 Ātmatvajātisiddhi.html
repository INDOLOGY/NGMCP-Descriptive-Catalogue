Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

praṇamyeśapadāṃbhojaṃ mahādevena tatvataḥ ||

īśasādhāraṇā[[tma]]tvajātau yuktir nigadhyate || 1 ||

nanu kathaṃ jīveśvarasādhāraṇātmatvajāti(!) siddhā ||

na ca jīvātmanisiddhāyātmatvajāteḥ śrautātmavyavahāravaleneśvare saṃvaṃdhakalpanāt tasyā eva lāghavenātmapadaśakayatāvacchedakatvakalpanāc ceśvarātmasādhāraṇātmatvajātisiddhir iti vācyaṃ || pramāṇābhāvena jīvātmany eva tasyā asiddheḥ || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

agrāvacchedenottaradeśasaṃyogānurodhenātmasaṃyogābhāvasyāvabhyupeyatvāt vṛkṣādisaṃyogasya ca bhavadbhiḥ pratibaṃdhakatvānaṃgīkārāt, athopadarśitalāghavād ātmasaṃyogatvena pratibaṃdhakatve siddhe tatrāvacchedakabhedena saṃyogabhedābhyupagamān na doṣaḥ na cānekasaṃ ///
