Beginning

❖ oṃ vighneśāya namaḥ ||

atha vyavahārāt pratyekam eva grahapūjanakramo likhyate ||

tatra sūryasya pūjākramo likhyate ||

kuśatrayatilajalāny ādāya oṃ adyāmukagotrasyāmukaśarmmaṇo janmarāśisu sūryagrahasūcitasakaladuritopaśamadīrghā〇yuṣṭvasarvvavidhasaumanasyapraptikāmaḥ | athavā, amukasya janmarāśyapekṣāṣṭamarāśisu sūryagrahasūcitaduritopaśmapūrvvakasūryaprītidīrghāyuṣṭvadha〇nārogyasarvvavidhasaumanasyakāmaḥ sūryapūjanam ahaṃ kariṣye || iti saṅkalpya sūryapratimāṃ tāmramayī⟪ṃ⟫m agre dhṛtvā oṃ sūryo sīti nāma dhṛtvā oṃ manojūti〇r ityādimantraṃ paṭhitvā, oṃ bhagavn sūrya pratiṣṭhito bhaveti pratiṣṭhāpya, etc.

(fol. 1r1–4)

End

oṃ adya kṛtaitacchāntikahomakarmmapratiṣṭhārtham idaṃ hiraṇyam agnidaivatam amukagotrāyāmukaśarmmaṇe brāhmaṇāya hotre dakṣiṇān tubhyam ahaṃ sampradade oṃ svastīti prativacanaṃm (!) | tato yajamānaḥ sūryādinavagrahādhidevatāpratyadhidevatāvināyakādipañcakapūjanaṃ pañcopacāravastrādibhiḥ kuryāt tataḥ puṣpāñjaliṃ gṛhītvā oṃ yāntu devagaṇā i[[tyā]]dinā visarjjayet tato yajamāno brāhmaṇebhyo yathā〇śaktibhakṣyādikaṃ dadyāt || ○ || (fols. 28v–29r)

Colophon

śubham astu || lasaṃ 387 e jyeṣṭhabadi 10 kuje e dine mahārādhirāja(!)śrīmadrāmabha(!)devapāde (bhī)gotāpānthakṛtavaijaulīgrāme mahāmahopādhyāyaśrīrucipatiśarmm⟪asyā⟫[[ā]]tmajena sadupādhyāyaśrīharapatiśarmmālikhitaiṣā pustīyaṃ || samudravasuharanetrāṅkitāyāṃ lakṣmaṇasenasammatāyām iti || (fol. 29r2–5)
