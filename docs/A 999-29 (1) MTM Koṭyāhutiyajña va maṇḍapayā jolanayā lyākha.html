Manuscript Features

1. The MTM contains following texts:

001. Koṭyāhutiyajñava maṇḍapayā jolanayā lyākha (exps. 1-16, 32)
002. Saphuliyā dharota (exps. 17-29)
003. Gṛhavāstulakṣaṇa (exps. 33-63)

2. There is a document of pañjikā of Bhagavatīguthi in 10 lines written in second hand at the last (exp. 64) of the manuscript, which begins as below:

❖ samvata 8(2)3 mārggasila kṛṣṇa 8 || śrībhagavati guthiyā paji ||
pūjājoraṃ mārako, disti, karṇapatakā, pañcapatā(2)pa gvarajā, banejora, dhana, jajaṃ, tāya potāya.....

3. The following are double exposures: exps. 26/27, 49/50, 54/55, 59/60, 65/66/67/68
