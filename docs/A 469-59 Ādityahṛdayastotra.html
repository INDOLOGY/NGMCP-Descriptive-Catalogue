svasti śrīgaṇeśāyan nama ||

ādityaṃ ca namaskāraṃ jagadvyāpi jagadisvaraṃ ||
†pramakṣabrahmarūṇa† ādityāya namo nama || 1 ||

brahmarūpāya brahmātmā brahmaṇe brahmaṇe brahmatomuṣa ||
brahmamāyā paraṃbrahma ādiºº || 2 ||

surādi īśvaraṃ devaṃ devadevaṃ mahesvaraṃ ||
lokanāthaṃ kṛpāsiṃdhuḥ ādityāyaºº || 3 ||

anātho nāthanāthañ ca nāthonāthaś ca vāla ||
tvaca dhārayate sarva ādityāyaºº || 4 ||

visesvaro virabhadroḥ visvadhāvisvatomuṣa ||
visvamāyā visvavījaṃ ādiºº || 5 ||

nirguṇaṃ nirmalaṃ devaṃ nirvikalpā nirāsmayet ||
nirāmayo nirā ‥ ‥ ādi || 6 ||

ṣaṣṭamāsaṃ kṛyate jāpyaṃ monaṃ ca vrata vārayet ||
brahmacaryasucitmutaṃ ādiḥºº ||

diptas tu vachanaṃ śrīmon mahāna sarvajogināṃ ||
māināṃ sarvalokasya ādiºº || 8 ||

vidyārthi labhate vidyā putrārthi putra labhyate ||
mokṣārthi labhate mokṣaṃ ādityāyāºº || 9 ||

ekakālaṃ dvikālaṃ vā trikālaṃ bhaktisādhanaṃ ||
kuṣṭirogakṣayaṃ jāṃti ādityāyaºº || 10 ||

sarvadhāmaamṛdhyaś ca sarvasatrukasādhaṃ ||
sarvagrahanivāreṇa ādityāºº || 11 ||

sarvaśāntikaraṃ devaṃ sadā rapuṣṭivardhana ||
satataṃ maṃgalaṃ āyu ādityāya namo nama || 12 ||

iti śrīvedācāryyaviracitaṃ ādihṛdayastotraṃ samāptaṃ ||

yādṛsaṃ postakaṃ dṛṣyā tādṛsaṃ likhito mayā ||
jadi śuddhaṃm aśuddhaṃ vā mama doṣo na diyate ||

jalaṃ rakṣe tailaṃ rakṣe rakṣaṃ sitalāṃghanāt ||
mūrṣahaste na dātavyaṃ evaṃ vadhati postakaṃ ||    ||
