Excerpts

«Beginning of the root text:»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

rūpaṃ dṛśyaṃ locanaṃ dṛg dṛgdṛśyaṃ dṛk ca mānasaṃ ||

dṛśyā dhīvṛttayaḥ sākṣī dṛg eva na tu dṛśyate || 1 ||

nīlapītasthūlasūkṣmahrasvadīrghādibhedataḥ ||

nānāvidhāni rūpāṇi paśyel locanam ekadhā || 2 || (fol. 1v4–5, 2r4–5)

«Beginning of the commentary:»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

tatra tāvad bhagavān śaṃkarāccārya (!) avidyāviṣadharamuṣitaprabodhasya jagato nugrahāya vākyasudhānāmaśāstrasaṃgrahaṃ cakāra || tatrāyam ādyaślokaḥ rūpaṃ dṛśyam eva bhavati locanaṃ dṛg eva bhavati locanaṃ dṛśyam eva bhavati || (fol. 1v1–3)

«End of the root text:»

jale phenasya tad dharmā dravādyas tat taraṃgake ||

tasyaiva vilaye nīre tiṣṭhaṃttete (!) yathā purā || 46 ||

prātibhāsikajīvasya laye syur vyāvahārike ||

tal laye saccidānaṃdāḥ paryavasyaṃti sākṣiṇi || 47 || (fol. 15v4–6, exp. 22b)

«End of the commentary:»

yathā prātibhāsika jīvasya laye saccidānaṃdā vyāvahārike ||

tallaye sākṣiṇi paryavasyaṃti iti prasiddhaṃ || 47 ||

yat tu vākyasudhām etāṃ vivṛtyaviśadaiḥ padaiḥ || puṇyaṃ mayārjitaṃ kiṃcit tad brahmaṇi samarpitam || || (fol. 15v3, 7–8, exp. 22b)

«Colophon of the commentary:»

iti śrīmacchaṃkarācāryaciracitaṃ vākyaṃ (!) sudhānāmaprakaraṇaṃ saṃpūrṇam || || daṃtā || (fol. 15v9, exp. 22b)
