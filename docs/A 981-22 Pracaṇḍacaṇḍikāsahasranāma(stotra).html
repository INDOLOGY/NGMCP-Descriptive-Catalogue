Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namo gaṇeśāya ||

pārvaty uvāca ||

pracaṇḍacaṇḍikā vidyā pūrvvoktā sarvvasammatā |

tasyā nāmasahasran tu maheśaṃ kathayasva me ||

kailāśaśikhare ramye nānāpātrasamanvite |

siddhate parvvasarvvaiś ca sevite parvvatottame || 2 ||

nānādhātusamākīrṇṇe pāvane kānanottame |

gaṅgānirmmanapāśāne (!) śīkarāmbukaṇāvṛte || 3 ||

dhyānāṃ (!) paratam āsīnaṃ prasannamukhapaṅkajaṃ |

vivyucchaṭābhinayanaṃ trinetraṃ śūladhāriṇaṃ || 4 ||

kapālaṃ paraśur vvīṇāṃ mṛgapātaiś ca bhūṣitaṃ |

ḍamaruṃ pāśakaṃ śaṅkhaṃ yogibhiḥ parimaṇḍitaṃ || 5 || (fol. 1r1–4)

End

tadā bhavati santuṣṭā skandamātā sarasvatī |

kāmākhyān vā jayed vidyāṃ lakṣmyādyāḥ sarvvasiddhidāḥ || 185 ||

uṣpoṣya vidhinā dhīmān pūjayitvā kumārikāṃ ||

tadyonau pūjayed vidyāṃ ṣaṭsahasraṃ dine dine || 186 ||

na doṣo vidyate tatra brahmaṇā bhāṣitaṃ purā ||

ācārāṣṭakarūpeṇa siddhirūpo na saṃśayaḥ || 187 || (fol. 8v1–3)

Colophon

iti śrī pracaṇḍacaṇḍikāḥ sahasranāmastotraṃ samāptaṃ || ||

❖ pūrvvapāṇṇārddhakarṇikāmbhaṃ maṇḍalaṃ caṇḍarociṣaḥ |

javākusumaṃ saṃkāśaṃ nava vandhūkasannibhaṃ ||

tanmadhye yonisaṃkāśaṃ trimārggam abhidhīyate ||

tasyā madhye mahādevī cchinnamastām avasthitāṃ ||

karakacchapikāyuktaṃ madhye padmāsanasthitāṃ ||

yoganidrāsamāyuktāṃ hṛdayaṃ sthitalocanāṃ ||

pīnavakṣojayugalāṃ dhyānaṃ vāniḥ (!) karaṃ smṛtaṃ ||

dhyeyam avajaṭīnāntu gṛhasthānāṃ niśāmaye |

antare svaśarīrasya nābhinīlajasaṅgatāṃ ||

nirllepā nirgguṇā prañā bālacandrasamaprabhāṃ ||

samādhimātragamyāntāṃ guṇatritayaveṣṭitāṃ |

kalātītaguṇātītāṃ muktimārggapradāyinīṃ || ||

samvat 805 pauṣaśukla 13 saṃpūrṇṇa || || (fol. 8v4–8)
