Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

pañcaviṃśatitatveṣu (!) janmanā jñānam āptavān ||

ādisṛṣṭau namas tasmai kapilāya maharṣaye ||

iha kaścid brāhmaṇa(2)s trividhena duḥkhenābhibhūtaḥ || sāṃkhyācāryyaṃ kapilamaharṣiśaraṇam upāgataḥ || atha kulanāmagotrasvādhyāyan nivedyāha || he bhagavan kim i(3)ha paraṃ kiṃ yāthātathyaṃ kiṃ kṛtakṛtya (!) syām iti ||

kapilauvāca (!) ||

athātas tatvasamāsasāṃkhyāsūtraṃ (!) vyākhyāsyāmaḥ || aṣṭau prakṛtayaḥ | ṣoḍaśa (4) vikārāḥ || puruṣaḥ || (fol. 1r1–4)

End

anena trividhena duḥkhenābhibhūtasya vijñāsā (!) samutpannā jñātum icchā jijñāsā (2) || yathā tṛṣitasya pātum icchā yā sāṃ (!) || etat samāsato niḥśreyasajñānaṃ yad bhātvā punar janma na syād iti ||

etan maharṣer vijñānaṃ kapi(3)lasya mahātmanaḥ ||

yac chrutvā brāhmaṇaśreṣṭaḥ kṛtakṛtyo bhava (!) tadā ||

(anuṣṭupu) chaṃdasām atra jñayaṃ (!) ślokaśatatrayam (4) || (fol. 7v1–4)

Colophon

iti samāsasāṃkhyāsūtravṛttiḥ (!) || samāptaḥ (!) || || || (fol. 7v4)
