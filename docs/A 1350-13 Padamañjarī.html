Remarks

Author Bṛhaspati/Rāyamukuṭa/Lokanātha

Subject Koṣa

Language Sanskrit

Text Features Two incomplete manuscripts of Amarakoṣaṭīkā kept as one. One of them is of the Padamañjarī, but the second cannot be identified. It should be noted that the second is very close to the Padamañjarī. So, it may be a commentary written earlier to the former.

Beginning

///liṅgānuśāsanaṃ tayoḥ parīkṣā vidhātavyety arthaḥ nāmāni śabdāḥ liṅgyate strīpratyayādibhir vakṣyate idaṃ siddhaṃ strīpuṃnapuṃsakaṃ aprāmāṇikatāṃ nirupyan asādhyamati anveti pārāyaṇādiśāstrāṇi samukṣipya (!) sarveṣāṃ nāmaliṅgānāṃ paryyāptatvāt saṃpūrṇṇaṃ vargair upalakṣitaṃ sampūrṇṇaṃ vā sajātīyasamūho varhaḥ etc. (fol. 1v)

upasarge daḥ kiḥ || uttamarṇṇe dhanagrahaḥ śārtham adhamarṇṇena sthāpitaṃ vastu (yadbhuktaṃ) vyasanaṃ vipat tataḥ pīḍā mānasī vyathā adhiṣṭhānam ā/// (fol. 84v)
