Excerpts

Beginning

yamagamayaṃ. hatavidhvastaṃ ca tadgṛham anuvicaranvepamānamadhuragātrīṃ viśālalocanām abhiniśamya tadā liṅganasukham anububhūṣus tām ādāya garbhagṛham avikṣam | tasmin neva kṣaṇe tavāsmi navāmbuvāhas tanitagambhīreṇa svareṇānuhṛhītah iti. śrutvā ca smṛtvā ca devo rājavāhanaḥ katham asi kārkaśyena karṇīsutam apy atikrāntāḥ ity abhidhāya punar aveṣyopahāravarmāṇam ācakṣva, tadedānīm avasara ity abhāṣata | (fol. 23r1–4)

«Sub-colophon:»

iti daṃḍinaḥ kṛtau daśakumāracarite dvitīya ucchvāsaḥ |

                                                                           (fol. 23r4–5)

iti daṃḍinaḥ kṛtau daśakumāracarite caturtha ucchāsa ||

                                                               (fol. 39v7–8)

End

deva devasyānveṣaṇāya dikṣu bhraman nabhraṃkaṣasyāpi viṃdhyapārśvarūḍhasya vanaspater adhaḥ pariṇatapataṃgabālapallavāvataṃsite paścimadigaṅganāmukhena palvalāṃbhasy upaspṛśyopāsya saṃ                                                                                  (fol. 33r1–3)
