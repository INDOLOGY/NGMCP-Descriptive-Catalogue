Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇesā(!)ya namaḥ || oṃ namo bhagavate vāsudevāya ||

atha saṃnyāsagrāhyapaddhati likhyate ||

oṃ prathamaṃ gṛhe mātṛpitṛbhaginībhāryāputrakalatrāṇi prārthayati tadanaṃtaram ātmagṛhagrāmādipradakṣiṇaṃ kṛtvānaṃtaraṃ krośapramāṇam uttaradiśaṃ gatvā tatra snānaṃ kṛtvā devārcanādikaṃ kṛtvā devapitrādismaraṇaṃ kṛtvā punar guru dṛśyate (fol. 1v1–7)


End

padmāsano haṃ siṃhāsano haṃ śivo haṃ bhairavo haṃ viṣṇusvarūpo haṃ ātmāparipūrṇo haṃ ṛgvedayajurvedasāmaveda-atharvavedaatharvaṇo haṃ oṃ hrāṃ hrāṃ hrāṃ paṭ svāhā iti siṃhāsanayogapaṭamaṃtraḥ saṃpūrṇaḥ (fol. 51r5–v2)


Colophon

iti śrīśaṃkarācāryaviracitaṃ saptasūtraṃ samāptam || || || || || (fol. 51v2–3)
