Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||    ||

vaiśaṃpāyana uvāca ||

āryāstavaṃ pravakṣyāmi yathoktam ṛṣibhiḥ purā⟨ḥ⟩ ||
nārāyaṇīṃ namasyāmi devīṃ tribhuvaneśvarīṃ || 1 ||

tvaṃ siddhiḥ śrīr dhṛtiḥ kīrttir hrīr vidyā sannatir matiḥ ||
saṃdhyā rātriḥ prabhā nidrā kālarātris tathaiva ca || 2 ||

āryā kātyāyanī devī kauśikī brahmacāriṇī ||
jananī siddhasenasya ugracāramahātapāḥ || 3 ||

jayā ca vijayā caiva tuṣṭiḥ puṣṭiḥ kṣamā dayā ||
jyeṣṭā yamasya bhaginī nīlakauśeyavāsinī<ref>For nīlakauśeyavāsasī</ref> || 4 ||

bahurūpā virūpā ca anekavidhacāriṇī ||
virūpākṣī viśālākṣī bhaktānāṃ parirakṣaṇī || 5 || (fols. 1v1–2r1)

End

pravāse rājabaṃdhe ca śatru(!)ṇāṃ ca marddane ||<ref>Pāda a is unmetrical.</ref>
prāṇātyayeṣu sarveṣu tvaṃ hi rakṣā(!) na saṃśayaḥ || 27 ||

tvayi me hṛdayaṃ devīṃ<ref>Should be vocative case ending.</ref> tvayi cittaṃ manas tvayi ||
rakṣa māṃ sarvabhūtebhyaḥ praśādaṃ ca kuruṣva me || 28 ||

idaṃ devyā stavaṃ puṇyam itihāsasamanvitaṃ ||
yaḥ paṭhet prātar utthāya śuciḥ prayatamānasaḥ || 2 ||

tribhir māsair kāṃkṣitaṃ hi phalaṃ vai saṃprayacchati ||
ṣaḍbhir māsaiḥ variṣṭhaṃ tu varam ekaṃ prayacchati || 30 ||

arcitā navabhir māsai[r] divyacakṣuḥ prayacchati ||
saṃvatsareṇa siddhiṃ tu yathākāmaṃ prayacchati || 31 (fols. 5v1–6r2)

Colophon

iti harivaṃśe āryyāstotraṃ saṃpūrttim agamat || śubham ||    || (fol. 6r3)
