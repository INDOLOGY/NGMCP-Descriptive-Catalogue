Manuscript Features

The manuscript covers from the beginning of the Bhvādi chapter to the end of the Lakārārtha chapter. The sūtras are highlighted in another ink. The numbers of the sūtras are written above them.

Short notes are written in the margins.

On fol. 1r is written

vyākaraṇa siddhāntakaumudī (uttarārdham). 115 patra. tiṅanta

śrīrāmaḥ ||

phalavyāpārayor ekaniṣṭhatāyām akarmakaḥ ||

dhātus tayor dharmibhede sakarmaka udāhṛtaḥ || ❖ ||
