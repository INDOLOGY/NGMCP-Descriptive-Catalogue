Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

upādhiṃ nirūpya pakṣatāṃ nirūpayituṃ śiṣyāvadhānāya pratijānīte | vyāptyanaṃtaram iti | vyāptinirūpaṇānaṃtaram ity arthaḥ | kvacit tathaiva (āha) | pakṣadharmatā | pakṣadharmaḥ | pakṣapadasya śāstrakārīyaparibhāṣāviṣayatāvacchedako [ʼ]numitijanako dharma iti yāvat |

bhāvārthasya | vivakṣitatvāt | etena vyāptipakṣadharmayor anumityātmakaikakāryānukūlatvam eva saṃgi(!)tar(!) iti sūcitaṃ. Anukūlatvaṃ ca kāraṇakāraṇatāvacchedakasādhāraṇaṃ prayojakatvaṃ tac ca kāraṇatāvacchedakavyāvṛtānyathāsiddhicatuṣṭayarāhitye sati niyatapūrvavṛttitvaṃ | (fol. 1v1–4)

End

anumitinirūpai(!)kakāryakāritvasaṃgatyā pakṣatānirūpaṇānaṃtaram anumitihetutvena parāmarśaṃ nirūpayati | pakṣadharmasyeti | pakṣadharmasya pakṣasaṃbaṃdhasya | vyāptiviśiṣṭajñānaṃ | vyāptiviśiṣṭe jñānaṃ | pakṣavyāpti viśiṣṭobhayavaiśiṣṭyāvagāhijñānam iti yāvat || tad evānumitihetur ity arthaḥ | tena pakṣaviśeṣyakaparāmarśaliṃgaviśeṣyakaparāmarśayor ubhayor evāsaṃha (!) | vahnivyāpyo dhūmaḥ dhūmavāṃś ca parva[ta] iti jñānasya mīmāṃsaka⟪ma⟫naye tu (fol. 10v3–6)

Sub-colophon

ity anumānaparicchede pakṣatārahasyaṃ | (fol. 10v3)
