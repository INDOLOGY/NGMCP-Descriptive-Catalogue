Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṁ namaḥ śrīguruve ||

atha deśabali vidhi likhyate || ||

yajamāna puśpabhājana || adyādi (2) vākya ||

śrīsamvattā || śyāmāraktā || utphullāṃ || pūrvvandakṣiṇa ||

siddhirastu || yathāvānatyādi (3) || ||

laṃkhana hāya || ūkāraṃ vāyuvījaṃtyādi || ||

ceta || śrīkhaṇḍa candanaṃ divyaṃtyādi || || (fol.1v1-3)

End

svaātmā āśīrvvāda ||

ambapūrvvagataṃ pa(3)daṃ bhagavati caitanya rūpātmako

jñānecchā vahulā tathva hariharo brahmāmarīcitra(4)kaṃ ||

bhāso bhairavaṃ pañcakan tadanu ca śrīyoginī pañcakaṃ,

candrārkkā ca marīci ṣaṭva na(5)malaṃ mā pātu nityaṃ kuja || ||

vela julaṅāva astrena, bali visarjjana yāya || || (6)

sākṣi thāya || ||

bali deśa ṅuyakaṃ holake || bājana surddhā || || (fol. 72v2-6)

«Sub-colophons:»

iti śrī 3 siddhilakṣmīmate prakāśe jayadrathavidyāpīṭhe pra(fol. 5v7)tyaṃgaryyā kṣatro balistava samāptaḥ || ||

iti pa(fol. 16v3)ścima degurividhiḥ || ||

iti śrīśivaśakti samarasatvaṃ mahāmāyāstotraṃ samāptaḥ || || (fol. 61vt4)

iti śrīpīṭhanṛtyadevyā nata pīṭhāvatārastotraṃ samāptaḥ || || (fol. 67v1)

Colophon

iti deśabali(fol. 72v7)vidhiḥ samāptaḥ || || || e || ||

śubhaṃ bhavatu sarvvadāṃ || ||

❖ adyādi || vākya || mānavagotra yajamānasya śrīśrījayabhūpatīndra varmmaṇaḥ

śrī 3 sveṣṭa(fol. 73r2)devatā prītyarthaṃ navagṛha praveśa aśvasālā pratiṣṭhā

deśabalyārccana pūjā nimityarthaṃ (3) puṣpabhājanaṃ samarppayāmi || ||
