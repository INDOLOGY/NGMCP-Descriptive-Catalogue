Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namaḥ śivāya ||

śivādisthāpane yāge dīkṣāyāṃ ca vi |śeṣataḥ |

sarvveṣām api varṇṇānām anugrahakaraḥ paraḥ |

lakṣakoṭisahasrāṇi viṃśatir arbudāni ca ||

koṭikha | rvvābdavṛṃdaṃ ca maṃtrān (vīsād)vinirgatāḥ |

dvāsaptatisahasrāṇi koṭilakṣaṃ tathārbudam |

ayutāni ca paṃcāśan mantā | dvakrād vinisṛtāḥ |

tṛṃśa | t(!) koṭisahasrāṇi saptāṣṭakaśatāni ca |

ayutāni ca ṣaṭtṛṃśa(!) maṃtrā rudrād vinirggatāḥ |

nava koṭisahasrāṇi paṃcā | sīty(!) ayutāni ca |

koṭiśatāni paṃcāsan maṃtrā vāmād viniḥsṛtāḥ |

saptakoṭisahatrāṇi saptatṛṃśacchatāni(!) ca |

śataṃ sahasralakṣaṃ ca sadyojātād vinirggatāḥ |

paṃcavaktrodbhavair mmaṃtraiḥ śivoktaiḥ siddhimuktidaiḥ |

....gā||disaṃsthāpyaṃ nānyair vvedādivāditaiḥ ||

(dva || aiśaṃ) vaktraṃ tathāghoraṃ guhyaṃ vā jātasaṃ(jña)kaṃ |

paṃcavaktrāṇi devasya paṃcāgamavivṛddhaye |

kā||mikaṃ yagajaṃ cintyaṃ kāraṇaṃ vā jitaṃ tathā |

(fol.1v1-2r2 )

End

bhūtapretapiśācānāṃ rākṣasānāṃ ca sādhane |

(eavaṃ) te kathito nyāso(vāghulāyāṃ) na saṃśayaḥ |

jñānapaṃcā(ghaghaghamikā) vatsa na deyā na deyā yasya kasyacit |

vāṣulāyāṃ mahāsena sa|dbhāvaṃ paramaṃ mahat |

śivasyānādurūpasya upāyo nāstyataḥ paraṃ |

sarvvatatvaprayogeṇa maṃtrayogena suvrata

kathitaṃ tava vatseśa saṃpradāyaṃ paraṃ mahat |

ye yatra saṃ|sthitāṃ(!) satvās teṣāṃ maṃtraṃ śivo(nagha) |

ciṃtāmaṇisamaprakhyaṃ jñātavyaṃ nāstyataḥ paraṃ |

eavaṃ jñātvā | mahāsena mucyate hyavicārataḥ |

saṃpradāyaṃ mayā tubhyaṃ putrasnehāt prakāśitaṃ |

satyaṃ satyaṃ punaḥ satyaṃ kathitaṃ tava suvrate || ❁ ||

(fol.5v6-6r3 )

Colophon

iti kālottare jñānapaṃcāsikā samāptā || ❁ ||

(fol.6r3-4 )
