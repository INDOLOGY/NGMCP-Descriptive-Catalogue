Excerpts

Beginning

(mūla-text) oṃ namas tasyai || oṃ janako ha vaideho bahudakṣiṇeneje tatra ha kurupañcālānāṃ brāhmaṇā abhisametā babhūvus tasya ha janakasya vaidehasya vijijñāsā babhūva. kaḥ svid eṣāṃ brāhmaṇānām anūcānatama iti (fol. 1v)

(commentary) oṃ namas tasmai tasyai ca || evam āgamapradhānena madhukāṇḍenoktam evārtham upapatyā nirūpayituṃ tatpradhānaṃ munikāṇḍam ārabhyate || (fol. 13r)

End

(mūla-text) tiṣṭhamānasya tadvida iti || 28 || navame brāhmaṇe tṛtīyo dhyāyaḥ || (fol. 13r)

(commentary) navamaṃ brāhmaṇaṃ || iti śrīśaṅkarācāryyakṛtāyāṃ bṛhadāraṇyakavyākhyāyāṃ mitākṣarāyāṃ pañcamo (?) dyāyaḥ samāptaḥ | lipir vvaṃśamaṇeḥ || ṣubham astu || śrīr astu ||

etāvat sarasi saroruhasya kulaṃ bhitvāmbhaḥ sapadi vinirggataṃ bahir yyat |
āmodo vikasanam indirānivāsas tat sarvvaṃ dinakarakṛtyam āmanati || (fol. 35v)

Colophon
