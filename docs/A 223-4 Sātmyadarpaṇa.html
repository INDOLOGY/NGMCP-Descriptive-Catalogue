Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

natvā viśveśvaraṃ śyāmāṃ guruṃ vāgīśvarīṃ harim ||

śiṣyāṇāṃ sukhabodhāya kriyate sātmyadarpaṇaṃ || 1 ||

svarasa puṭa kalkaś ca kvāthaś ca himaphāṃṭakau ||

jñeyākaṣāyāḥ pañcaite laghaḥvaḥ syur yathottaram || 2 ||

|| tad yathā ||

āhṛtā tat kṣaṇāt dravyāt kṛṣṭāt kṣusmāt samuddharet ||

vastraniṣpīḍito yaś ca svaraso rasa ucyate || 3 ||

kuḍavaṃ cūrṇitaṃ dravyaṃ kṣiptaṃ dviguṇe jale ||

ahorātraṃ sthitaṃ tasmād bhaved vā rasa uttamaḥ || 4 || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

tatas tasmiś caturjātaṃ jātikośaṃ lavaṃgakaṃ ||

karpūraṃ kuṃkumaṃ vāpi yathālābhaṃ niyojayet ||

taṃ yathāgnibalaṃ martyaḥ pivet sarvakṣayāpahaṃ ||

māṃsena saha cānnena snigdhena madhurena(!) ca ||

naro navativarṣīyopy anena daśakāminī ||

pratyehaṃ (!) ramayaty eva yuveva na ca hīyate || paṃcasāyaka || || (fol. 186r1–3)

Colophon

iti śrīviśvanāthakṛte sātmyadarpaṇe āsāvādyariṣṭadhyāyo dvādaśa (!) || ||

sarasa kvātha kalkas tu cūrṇaṃ ca guṭikā rasā ||

avalehādi pākaś ca pethyā paṃthyo hi kīrttanam || (!)

ghṛtatailaṃ prehādyāsa vāsa iti prakīrtitā ||

iti saṃkṣepataḥ prokta sātmyadarpaṇasaṃgrahaḥ ||

śiṣyāṇāṃ sukhabodhāya viśvanāthena dhīmatā ||

iti sātmyadarpaṇa samāptam || || śubham || ❁ || ||(fol. 186r3–5)
