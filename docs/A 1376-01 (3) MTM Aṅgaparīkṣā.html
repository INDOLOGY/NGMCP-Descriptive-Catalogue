1. The MTM contains following texts:

(1) Sahadevana dayakā chāya (exp. 4)

(2) Dharmalakṣmīsaṃvāda (exps. 6t–11b)

(3) Aṅgaparīkṣā (exps. 11b –13t)

Beginning

❖ namo mañjughokhāya namo 3 || moḍa tuka(4)yā, māyaṃ lābha dayio, jao hnasapota tukayā, strī lābha dayu, khao hnasapata (5) tukayā jolaṇa kayu || nivā tukayā, lakṣmī rābha dayu || jao mikhā tu(6)tasā dhana rābha dayu || khao mikhā tutasā, nugvaḍasa vyādhi madayu || (exp. 11b3–6)


End

khao tutasā pāhāna oio || barahni tutasā, jao tuka bhiṅa || khao tutasā (5) pāhāna oyio || byāli yirasa, jao mikhā tutasā mabhiṅa || khao mikhā tutasā bhiṅa (6) bācāsa jao mikhā tusā rājāna mānya yāyio juro || (exp. 13t4–6)

iti aṃgaparikṣā samāpta || (exp. 13t6)
