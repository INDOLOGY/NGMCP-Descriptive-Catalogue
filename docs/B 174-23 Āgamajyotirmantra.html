Manuscript Features

MS contains the scattered folios of the Hāhārāvatantra. Available prakaraṇas of the Hāhārāvatantra are: Puraścaraṇavidhi, Vaṭukabhairava aparājitā(!)?, Āgamajyotirmantra

īśvara uvāca ||

śṛṇu devī (!) pravakṣāmi yan māṃ tvaṃ paripṛcchasi |
