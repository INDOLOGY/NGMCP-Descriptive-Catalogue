Excerpts

«Beginning»


oṁ namaḥ śrīvajrasatvāya || ||


sahajasiddhi[ṃ] pravakṣyāmi satvānugrahahetunā ||


homayāgatayonīta ādikarmikavarjitaṃ ||


sahajavastu kharūpaṃ tu sāṃtāsiddhir ucyate ||(!)


sahajasiddhi[r] bhavet tasmāt āvāsa[ḥ] sahaja[ḥ] smṛtaḥ ||


†khadhātvā† bhagavan dhyātvā madhye kurvīta bhāvanā ||


cakrapūrv[o] yathā nāsaṃ devatānāṃ yathodayaṃ || (fol. 1v1-3)


«End»


satvānām aśeṣānāṃ bhaveyaṃ janmajanmani ||(!)


anenāśayacāreṇa yadi buddho bhavāmy ahaṃ ||


iti likhita-viṣalagurukramaṇaṃtayoga-vṛhad guṇāśeṣamaṃtrasadbhāvamataṃ ca saṃbhūtaṃ ||


aciṃtādvayabhāgaṃ tu upapannakramo yogamā++yonīti garbhadevatārūpaṃ ||


ātmanaḥ smaraṇārtham asmaj janajaḍasataṃ buddhānām || || (fol. 18r7-10)


«Sub colophon»


iti śrītatvasiddhināmaprakaraṇaṃ prāvṛtataṃtra[ṃ] samāptam || śubham || (fol. 14v9)


«Colophon»


ity aciṃtyādvayakramopadeśaḥ samāptam(!) || ||


kṛtir iyaṃ śrīkuddālapādānām iti prāvṛtarājam || bhūyāt || (fol. 18r10)
