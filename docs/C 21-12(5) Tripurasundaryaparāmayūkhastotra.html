Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namaḥ śrīparadevatāyai ||

bindau tridhāmasadane paraśaṃbhuśakte
rudyāśapīṭhavaragāṃ sūttarīyakhaṇḍāṃ |
śrīpādukāsthitilayodbhavakāraṇāyāḥ
saṃpūjayāmi paramāmṛtasārabhūtāṃ || 1 || (exps. 38b3–5)

End

stotraṃ ye ye paṭhanti tribhuvaṇajananīṃ pūjayitvātmaśaktyā
te te lakṣmīṃ kavitvaṃ surapuravanitākṣobhanaṃ prāpnuvanti |
tatvaṃ jñānaṃ vivāde jayam ativibhavaṃ dīrghajīva tvam ambā
dhyānād ānandasindhuṃ kulakamalapadaṃ kāmarājaprasādāt || 14 || (exps. 41b6–42t3)

Colophon

iti śrīśaṅkarācāryyaviracitaṃ śrītripurasundaryāparāmayūṣastotraṃ (!) samāptaḥ (!) || ○ || (exp. 42t3–4)
