Excerpts

Beginning

|| ❖ oṁ namaḥ śrīkṛṣṇāya ||

oṁ kāśīpataye namaḥ ||


nārāyaṇaṃ namaskṛtya, naraṃ caiva narottamaṃ |

devīṃ sarasvatīñ caiva, tato jayam udīrayet ||

tan manmahe maheśānaṃ maheśānapriyārbhakaṃ ||

gaṇeśānaṃ karigaṇeśānānanam anāmayaṃ ||

bhūmiṣṭhāpi nayātra bhūs tridivato py uccair adhasthāpi yā,

yāvad dhātuvimuktidā syur amṛtaṃ⟪ā⟫ yasyāṃ mṛtā jantavaḥ |

yā nityaṃ trijagatpavitrataṭinītīre suraiḥ sevyate,

sā kāśī tripurārirājanagarī pāyād apāyāj jagat || (fol. 1v1–3)


End

śṛnuyād ūredeśe ʼpi yaḥ kāśīkhaṇḍam uttamaṃ, sa kāśīvāsapuṇyasya, bhājanaṃ syāc chivājñayā ||

etac chravaṇataḥ puṃsāṃ, sarvatra vijayo bhavet |

saubhāgyaṃ vāpi sarvatra, prāpnuyān nirmmalāśrayaḥ ||

yasya viśveśvaraṃ(!) tuṣṭā(!), tasyaitac chravaṇe matiḥ |

jāyate puṇyayuktasya, mahānirmmalacetasaḥ ||

sarvveṣāṃ maṃgalānāṃ ca, mahāmaṃgalam uttamaṃ |

gṛhe ʼpi likhitaṃ pūjyaṃ, sarvvamaṃgalasiddhaye || (fol. 371r5–7)


Colophon

iti śrīskandapurāṇe kāśīkhaṇḍe anukramaṇikādhyāyo nāma śatatamo ʼdhyāyaḥ samāptaṃ(!) || || śrīśāke 1580 māghamāse kṛṣṇapakṣe caturthyāṃ hastanakṣatre śūlayoge bhaumavāsare kuṃbharāśigate sūryye, kanyārāśigate candramasi, mahāparvvaṇi śrīmati(!)bhakttapaṭṭanādhivāsī(!)daivajñainduvarmmaṇā likhitam idaṃ kāśīkhaṇḍapustakaṃ saṃpūrṇaṃ ||

yādṛśī(!) pustaketi || likhanaparī(!)śramavettyeti(!) || śivam astu śubhaṃ || (fol. 371r7–9)
