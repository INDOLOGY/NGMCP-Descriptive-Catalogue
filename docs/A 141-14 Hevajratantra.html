❖ namaḥ śrīherukāyaḥ || evaṃ mayā śrutam ekasmin samaye bhagavān


sarvatathāgatakāyavākcittavajrayoṣitbhageṣu vijahāra || tatra bhagavān āha ||


sarvatathāgatakāyavākcittahṛdayaḥ bhaṭṭārakaṃ guhyātiguhyataram īti(!)


(etajagarhaḥ sādhu sādhu mahākāyaḥ mahābodhisatvaḥ vajrasatvasya mahāsatvasya samay /// )


satvasya hṛdayaṃ hevajrākhyaṃ śṛṇu ||


vajragarbha uvāca ||


he vajras tu ‥‥‥skena


idṛśaṃ nāma saṃguhaṃ hekāreṇa kim ākhyātaṃ vajreṇāpi tayā kimu ||


bhagavān āha ||


hakāreṇa mahākaruṇā vajraṃ prajñā ca bhaṇyate ||


prajñopāyātmakaṃ tatvaṃ tan me hi gaditaṃ śṛṇu ||


(dṛṣṭvākṛṣṭimahācchātmasādhambahuvidhaṃ viduḥ)


stambhanoccāṭanaś caivaḥ(!) sainyastambhābhicārukaṃ ||


yoginīnāṃ yathānyāyaṃ utpattisthitikāraṇaṃ || (fol. 1v1–2v2)
