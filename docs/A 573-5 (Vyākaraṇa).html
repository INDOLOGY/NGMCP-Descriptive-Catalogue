Manuscript Features

The text starts in 134t and ends in 133b.

The text in this manuscript starts from the beginning and ends in anukrāntās tāvat svarāḥ sūtra of saṃjñāprakaraṇa.

Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

pranamya (!) paramātmānaṃ, bāladhīvṛddhisiddhaye |

sārasvatīm ṛjuṃ kurvve, prakriyān nātivistarā (!) ||

indrādayo pi yasyāntaṃ na yayuḥ śabdavāridheḥ |

prakriyān tasya kṛtsnasya, kṣamo vaktuṃ naraḥ kathaṃ ||

tatra tāvat saṃjñā saṃvyavahārāya saṃgṛhyate || (exp. 134t)

End

anukrāntās tāvat svarāḥ || pratyāhāraṃ jigrāhayiṣayā vyaṃjanāmyanukrāmati || haya///(exp. 133b)
