«Beginning»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

ekadā munayaḥ sarve sarvalokahite ratāḥ ||

suramye naimiṣāraṇye goṣṭhīṃ cakrur manoramām || 1 ||

tatrāṃtare mahātejā vyāsaśiṣyo mahāyaśāḥ ||

sūtaḥ śiṣyagaṇair yuktaḥ samāyāto hariṃ smaran || 2 ||

tam āyāṃtaṃ samālokya sūtaṃ śāstrārthapāragaṃ ||

muniṃ sarve ⌠⌠sa⌡⌡mutthāya śaunakādyās tapodhanāḥ || 3 ||

so ʼpi tān sahasā bhaktyā munīn paramavaiṣṇavān ||

nanāma daṇḍavad bhūmau sarvadharmavidām varaḥ || (fol. 1v, 1–5)


«End»

dhanadhānyādikaṃ tasya bhavet satyaprasādataḥ ||

daridro labhate vittaṃ baddho mucyeta bandhanāt || 7 ||

bhīto bhayāt pramucyeta satyam eva na saṃśayaḥ ||

īpsitaṃ ca phalaṃ bhuktvā cānte satyapuraṃ vrajet || 76 ||

iti vaḥ kathitaṃ viprāḥ satyanārāyaṇavratam ||

yat kṛtvā sarvaduḥkhebhyo mukto bhavati mānavaḥ || 77 ||

viśeṣataḥ kaliyuge satyapūjāphalapradaḥ ||

kecit kālaṃ vadiṣyanti satyam īśaṃ tam eva ca || 78 ||

satyanārāyaṇaṃ kecit satyadevaṃ tathāpare ||

nānārūpadharo bhūtvā sarveṣāmīpsitapradaḥ || 79 ||

bhaviṣyati kalau satyavratarūpī sanātanaḥ ||

ya idaṃ paṭhate nityaṃ śṛṇoti munisattamāḥ || 80 ||

satyaṃ naśyanti pāpāni satyadevaprasādataḥ ||

⌠⌠bhaviṣyaty akṣayaṃ puṇyaṃ satyam etan na saṃśayaḥ⌡⌡ (fol. 10r4–v3)


«Colophon»

iti śrīskandapurāṇe revākhaṃḍe śrīsatyanārāyaṇavratakathāyāṃ saptamo dhyāyaḥ || 5 || || saṃpūrṇaṃ || śubham astu || śrīsatyanārāyaṇārpaṇam astu || || (fol. 10v2–3)
