Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||     ||

dhīmatā harivaṃśena kriyate vedamātaram ||

devīm praṇamya gāyatrīm eṣā praśnottarāvalī || 1 ||

bhāti kā pratidinaṃ vivasvataḥ

kam padaṃ nanu vikalpavācakam ||

praśnavācakapadaṃ kim akāyaṃ

kidṛśo dinamukhe divākaraḥ || 2 ||

bhāvānuraktaḥ || vyasta jātiḥ ||     ||

viyoginīṃ kṛśodarīṃ prahartum udyataḥ kadā ||

bhaved †ananyajo† dṛḍhaṃ svakīyapaṃcabhiḥ śaraiḥ || 3 || (fol. 1v1–6)

End

viṣṇo[[ḥ]] kā hṛdi kiṃ niṣedha uditaṃ kaṃcic ca vargākṣaraṃ

nanv āmaṃtraya kiṃ padaṃ nigaditaṃ praśne vyayaṃ paṇḍitaiḥ ||

āhvāne kiraṇasya kiṃ vada padaṃ kiṃ ca sphuṭārthe padaṃ

kiṃ brūte mithilāpurīyuvajanaḥ kāṃtāṃ sakopāṃ prati || 41 ||

māna(‥)nukaraha || citrajātiḥ || (fol. 7v3–7)

Colophon

iti śrīharivaṃśakṛtau praśnottarāvalī samāptā || || śubham || || (fol. 7v7–8r1)
