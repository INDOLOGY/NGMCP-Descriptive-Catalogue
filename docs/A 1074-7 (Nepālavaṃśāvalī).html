Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || śrīgurubhyo namaḥ ||    ||

guruṃ gaṇapatiṃ durgā vaṭukaṃ śivam a(2)cyutam ||
brahmaṇāṃ girijāṃ lakṣmīṃ vāṇī vaṃde vibhūtaya ||    ||
anādyāya khilā(3)dyāya māyivegatamāmite ||
arūpāyaś ca rūpāya śivāya gurave namaḥ ||    ||

a(4)tha nepālike devān prādurbhāva rājabhogamālā ||
yathākrameṇa vartamāna vidhau saka(5)la dukhārtajanānāṃ varebhyo śṛṇavaṃtesati pāpānāṃ haraṇahetu kāraṇaṃ tasmāt brāhma(6)ṇādhikārijanasyā mukhāt sakala catuvarṇa śrotā rasam uccayaṃ bhavaṃti yogyāni(7)tyaṃ kilvī sakalau kṛtva nāśayatiś cāśraddhāyai kurvaṃti tebhyo gati prāpnoti bha(8)ktibhāvena yaḥ śṛṇvaṃti athavā ya paṭhaṃti gaṃgādi tīrtha snāna saṃbha phalaṃ prāpnuyā(9)t dine dine smaraṇena sakala tīrtha devarājatīrthā vaṃśāvalī saṃgrahaṃ kuryāt || (exp. 5right1–9)
