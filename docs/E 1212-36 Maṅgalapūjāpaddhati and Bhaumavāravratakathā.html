«Beginning»


śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

bhaumavāsareʼruṇodayavelāyām samutthāyāpāmārgeṇa dantadhāvanam kṛtvācamya || paścāt tilāmalakacu(!)rṇena nadyādau gṛhe vā snātvā dhautam raktavastram paridhāya raktottarīyam ca dhārayet || strī cāpy evam parī(!)dadyāt || agnim(!) mūrddheti aṣṭottaraśatavārān (!) tarpaṇam kuryyāt ||

oṁ agnim(!)mūrddhā divaḥ kakutpatiḥ pṛthivyā ayam apāṃ retāṃssi jinvaṃ(!)ti ||

tata〈ḥ〉starpaṇānte tāmrapātre raktacandanādi nikṣipyāgnimūrddheti mantreṇārgham aṣṭottaraśatam ddyāt || (fol. 1v1–2r1)


«End»


evam uktā ca tatraiva maṅgaloʼpi divam gataḥ ||

evam vratam samākhyātam sarvasaukhyapradāyakam || 33 ||


idam vratam kariṣyanti teṣām pīḍā na jāyate ||

strībhir vratam prakartavyam puruṣe(!) ca viśeṣataḥ || 34 ||


teṣām muktir bhavet (ye ca) svargavāso na śam(!)śayaḥ || (fol. 13r7–13v4)
