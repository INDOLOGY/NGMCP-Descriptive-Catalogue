yathā bhaviṣyatau liṃgaṃ riṣṭaṃ mṛtyos tathā dhruvaṃ | 1 |

yāyuṣmati kriyāḥ sarvāḥ saphalāḥ saṃprayojitāḥ |

ayaśo dehasaṃdehaṃ svārthahāniṃ ca yachati(!) | 3 | (fol.1r1-3)
