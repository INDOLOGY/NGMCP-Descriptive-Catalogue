Excerpts

Beginning

-cānupalabdha‥kathanupandha ity ubhaya yogyatām āha || kāla iti || yataḥ kālo yaṃ niravadhir vigatāvasānaḥ || vi(2)pulā mahatī ca gṛdhvī bhūmi || tathā ca tasyedānīm anutpannasya kālāntare utpāda ihānupalabdhasya deśānta(3)re upalaṃbhasyād iti bhāvaḥ || iha prathamārddhe vahutvena prayogaḥ || dvitīyārddhe ekatvena prayogaś ca na doṣaḥ (fol. 9r1–3)

End

prāsūtamoyam amalaṃ guṇinaṃ suputraṃ

taratna(8)pūrvadharam eṣa jagaddharaṃ ca

yanā(!) paṭhito ragoṃtamamataṃ vaiśeṣikaṃ khaṃḍanaṃ

yenāśrāvisakoṣakāvya nivahaṃ tatpāṇinīyaṃ matāṃ

chaṃdolaṇkara(9)ṇaṃ ca bhavya bharataṃ yenādhyagāyi śrutis

tasyā muradbhūṣya jagaddharasya kavi tuṣṭikeyam ujjṛbhatāṃ || (!)

asūta yaṃ ratnadharoti bhavyo ʼati bha(10)vyarupā dayāti kāpi ||(!)

jagaddgaraṃ tatkṛtaṭippaṇeṃko samāptimāyad daśamonavadhyaḥ || (fol. 87v7–10)

Colophon

iti śrīmahādāmodarapaṇḍitadharmmādhika(11)raṇikaśrījagaddharakṛtaṭīkāyāṃ daśamoṃkaḥ samāptaḥ || ||

śrīvikramābde karasapta nāga

bhūvāre ravau bhādinipūrṇatithau ||

tannāṭa(12)kaṃ sarvalikheti nūnaṃ

padhye ca nāthasya pustakam idaṃ || || (!)

(14) samvat 936 pauṣa śudi 15 roja 1 śubhāni santu || || (fol. 87v10–14)
