Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śivāya || ||

ṛṣaya ūcuḥ ||

bhavatā kathitaḥ samyak sarggaḥ svāyaṃbhuvas tataḥ |

brahmāṇḍasyāsya vistāro manvantaraviniścayaḥ ||

tatraiśvarośvaro (!) devo varṇṇibhir dharmmatatparaiḥ (2) |

jñānayogaratair nnityam ārādhyaḥ kathitas tvayā ||

tadvad āśeṣasaṃsāre duḥkhanāśanam uttamaṃ |

jñānaṃ brahmekaviṣayaḥ (!) yena paśyema tatparaṃ ||

tvaṃ hi nārāyaṇā (!) sākṣāt kṛṣṇadvai(3)pāyanāt prabhoḥ |

avāptākhilavijñānas tu (!) tvāṃ pṛcchāmahe punaḥ ||

śrutvā munīnāṃ tad vākyaṃ kṛṣṇadvaipāyanaṃ prabhuṃ |

sūtaḥ paurāṇikaḥ smṛtvā bhāṣituṃ hy upracakrame (!) ||

athāsminn a(4)ntare vyāsaḥ kṛṣṇadvaipāyanaḥ svayaṃ |

ājagāma muniśreṣṭhā yatra satraṃ samāsate ||

taṃ dṛṣṭvā vedavidvāṃsaṃ kālameghasamadyutiṃ |

vyāsaṃ kamalapatrākṣaṃ praṇemur dvijapuṅgavāḥ ||

papā(5)ta daṇḍavad bhūmau dṛṣṭvā sau romaharṣaṇaḥ |

pradakṣaṇīkṛtya guruṃ prāñjaliḥ pārśvago ʼbhavat || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

ya idaṃ (!) paṭhate nityaṃ, saṃvādaṃ kṛrttivāsasaḥ |

sanatkumārapramukhaḥ (!) sarvvapāpaiḥ pramucya(4)te |

śrāvayeta dvijān brahma, brahmacaryyasamanvitān |

yo vā vicārayed arthān, sa yāti paramāṅ gatiṃ |

yathetac chṛṇuyān nityaṃ, bha(5)ktiyuktadṛḍhavrataḥ |

sarvvapāpavinirmukto, brahmaloke mahīyate ||

tasmāt sarvaprayatnena, paṭhitavyo manīṣibhiḥ |

śro(6)tavyaś cānumantavyo, viśeṣād brāhmaṇaiḥ sadā || 137 ||  || (fol. 16v3–6)

Sub-colophon

iti śrīkūrmapurāṇe ṣatsāhasryāṃ (!) saṃhitāyāṃ īśvaragītāsūpaniṣatsu (7) daśamo dhyāyaḥ || 10 || ❁ ||

samavat 808 vaisāṣakṛṣṇayā asti thva kuhnu coyā juro || śubha || ❁ || ❁ ||
