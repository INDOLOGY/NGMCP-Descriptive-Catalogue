Beginning

❖ oṃ namo gaṇeśāya ||

śrīkṛṣṇāya namaḥ ||

śrīsarsvatyai namaḥ ||

bhagavatpadabhāṣyārtham ālocyātiprayatnataḥ ||

prāyaḥ pratipadaṃ kurvve gītāgūḍhārthadīpikāṃ || 1 ||

sahetu(2)kasya saṃsārasyātyanto paramātmakaṃ ||

paraṃ niḥśreyasaṃ gītā[[śā]]strasyoktaṃ prayojanaṃ || 2 ||

saccidānantarūpaṃ tat pūrṇṇaṃ viṣṇoḥ paraṃ padaṃ ||

yat prāptaye samārabdhāḥ vedāḥ kā(3)ṇḍatrayātmakāḥ || 4 || ? (fol. 1v1–3)

End

kāṃtatrayātmakaṃ (!) śāstraṃ gītākhyaṃ yena nirmmitaṃ |

ādimadhyāntaṣaṭkeṣu tasmai bhaṃgavate namaḥ || 2 ||

śrīgovindamukhāravindamadhunām iṣṭhaṃ mahābhārate

gītākhyaṃ paramaṃ rahasyam ṛṣiṇā vyāsena vikhyāpitaṃ |

vyākhyā(4)naṃ bhagavatpadaiḥ pratipadaṃ śrīśaṃkarākhyaiḥ punar

viṣpaṣṭaṃ madhusūdanena muninā svajñānalubdhai kṛtaṃ || 3 ||

iha yosti vimohyan manaḥ paramānandaghanaḥ sanātanaḥ |

guṇadoṣaṃ bhṛdeṣaṃ eva nas tṛṇatulyau yad ayaṃjanaḥ || 4 || (!)

(5)śrīrāma viśveśvaramādhavānāṃ prasādam āsādya mayā gurūṇāṃ |

vyākhyānam etad vihitaṃ subodha, samarpitaṃ tac caraṇāmbujeṣu || 5 || (fol. 226r3–5)
