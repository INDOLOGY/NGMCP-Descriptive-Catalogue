Manuscript Features

The extant fols. appear in the following order: 25–27; ?; ?; 1 empty fol.; 2–11; 15; 13; 16–17; 24; 29–36; ?; 37–53; 55–93.

The MS starts amidst the rāśibhedādhyāya on fol. 2 numbered by a later hand on the verso (exp. 10 top) and ends amidst the karmacintādhyāya.

Exposure 2 shows a cover leaf with some diagrams drawn on it.

Fols. 2–6 are damaged in the margins. The writing on some fols. is partly rubbed off.
