Beginning

/// viṣṇur nirjabhāna muhur muhuḥ 4

sṛṣṭiṃ vitanute brahmā yad dhṛtvā paṭanāghataḥ (!)

saṃharttāhaṃ yato devi deveśo vasavo (!) yataḥ 5

dhanādhipaḥ kubero pi yataḥ sarve digīśvarāḥ ||

na deyaṃ paraśiṣyebhyo deyaṃ śiṣyebhya eva ca 6

abhaktebhya (!) pi putrebhyo datvā mṛtyum avāpnuyāt 7 (fol. 3r1–3v4)

End

vajrāṇi kusumasyeva bhūṣaṇābhibhavaṃti hi 55

satkīrttibhuvane lakṣmī vāṇī vaktre vaset tataḥ

idaṃ kavacam ajñātvā yo japet suṃdarī (!) parāṃ 56

navalakṣaṃ prajaptvāpi tasya vidyā na sidhyati

sa śastraghātam āpnoti socirān mṛtyum āpnuyāt 57 (fol. *14r1–*14 v18)

Colophon

iti rudrayāmalataṃtre umāmaheśvarasaṃvāde trailokyamohanaṃ nāmakavacaṃ saṃpūrṇaṃ || śrīgurudevatārpaṇam astu || || śrīr astu || ||

saṃvat 1811 tāraṇasaṃvatsare mārgaśīṣa (!) kṛṣṇa dvitīyā taddine kāśīnāthena likhita (fol. *14v4–*15r5)
