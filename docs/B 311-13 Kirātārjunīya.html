Manuscript Features

Fols. 22–33, 72, 74–84 are available.

The text starts from the two letters of the 56th stanza of the 3rd sarga and runs up to the 1st pāda of the 60th stanza of the 11th sarga.

There are two exposures of fols. 23v–24r and 78v–79r.

Fols. 72v/r is in reverse order.
