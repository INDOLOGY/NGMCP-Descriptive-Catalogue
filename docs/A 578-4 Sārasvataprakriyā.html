hāntāt śāntāt ṣāntāt nāmy(!)upadhād aniṭaḥ sak divādiparaḥ | na tu dṛśeḥ ser apavadaḥ | akṛkṣyat(!) sṛpa gatau || sarpati || sraptā | sarptā | asṛpat || || kruśa āhvāne rodane || ca || krośati | kroṣṭā | akrukṣat | || ruṣa riṣa śiṣa hiṃsāyāṃ ||

iṣusahalubhariṣaruṣam anapi tasyeḍ vā || roṣitā || roṣṭā | reṣitā | reṣṭā | śeṣṭā | aśiṣyat || uṣa dāhe || oṣāṃcakāra | uvoṣa | auṣīt || || miha secane || ḍhi ḍho lopo dīrghaś ca ḍhe pare ḍhasya lopaḥ pūrvvasya cānṛto dīrghaḥ | meḍhā | amikṣat ||

daha bhasmīkaraṇe || dehitha | dadagdha | dhakṣati (!) | adhākṣīt || || jhasāt || adāgdhāṃ || || ivi vyāptau || invati | invāṃcakāra | ainvīt || | || hivi divi dhivi jivi prīṇane || dhinvikṛṇvyorvvasya lopo guṇābhāva uppratyayaś cābviṣaye | dhinoti || ||
