Beginning

oṃ asya śrīsaptaśatīstotramaṃtrasya madhyamacaritrasya viṣnuṛṣir uṣṇik chaṃdo (4) mahālakṣmīr devatā śākaṃbharī śaktiḥ durgā bījaṃ vāyus tatvaṃ (!) yajurvedamūrttiḥ bālārkanibhaṃ dhyānaṃ śrīmahā(5)lakṣmīprītyarthe madhyamacaritrajape viniyogaḥ ||

oṃ viṣṇuṛṣaye namaḥ śirasi ||

oṃ uṣṇikchaṃdase namo (6) mukhe ||

oṃ mahālakṣmīdevatāyai namo hṛdi || (fol. 19r3–6)…

(19v1) ṛṣir uvāca || 1 ||

devāsuram abhūd yuddhaṃ pūrṇam abdaśataṃ purā ||
maheṣe surāṇām adhipe devānāñ ca purandare || (2) 2 ||

tatrāsurair mahāvīryair devasainyaṃ parājitam ||
jitva ca sakalanān devān indro bhūn mahiṣā(3)suraḥ || 3 || (fol. 19v1–3)

End

ity etat kathitaṃ bhūpa saṃbhū(2)tā sā yathā purā ||
devi (!) devaśarīrebhyo jagattrayahitaiṣinī || 41 ||

punaś ca gaurīdehāt sā samudbhūtā yathābhavat ||
va(3)dhāya duṣṭadaityānāṃ tathā śuṃbhaniśuṃbhayoḥ || 42 ||

rakṣaṇāya ca lokānāṃ devānām upakāṛiṇī ||
