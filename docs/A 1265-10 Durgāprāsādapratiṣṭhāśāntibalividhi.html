Beginning

❖ oṁ namaḥ śṛīgurubhyo namaḥ ||

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

atha durggāprāsāda(2)praṣṭisthā śāntibalyārccana vidhir llikhyate || ||

asthāna ādina mālakva bā the(3)lake || ||

vasape vidhi ||

pavāsanava(!) dvoṇa mālako coya ||

jā pūjā boya gaṇa vaṭu(4)ka ādina pañcabaliṃ ||

hanubhairava balisa, karṇṇapatākā, pañcapatākā, dṛṣṭi (5) aduvāla, jajamakā, svāna, siṃdhrara kune, thvate vasape || || (fol. 1v1-5)

End

bali choya ||

vidyā kaṇṭhaṇaṃ thava 2 disasa choya ||

mūla hanumantra jukva (1) śatruyā bhūsa, phaleto vāṅāva,

svakacakā, kāyāva hole, bali śatruyā nagara (2) socakaṃ tāthe || ||

mūlanasaṃ bali visarjana yāya || || bali choya thavali disasa(3)

hārāvalikvātha ṅuyakaṃ horake || || sākṣi thāya || (fol. 78r5-78v3)
