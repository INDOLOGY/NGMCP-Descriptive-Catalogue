dmesthitāya brahmaṇe sarasvatīyuktāya

dvā(4)daśāṃte ayodhyāpurimadhye brahmarandhramaṇḍape nānāvarṇa saha(5)stradalapadme sthitāya paramātmane cicchaktiyuktāya sarvavarṇā(6)ya mayākṛtam, ajapā gāyatrīsahasram ekaṃ nivedayāmi || paṃ(7)capūjā ||

yatkṛtaṃ japasaṃkhyānaṃ pūjanaṃ tu maheśvaraḥ ||

anayā śadṛśī vidyā ||  a(1) || nayā śadṛśo japa (!)  ||
