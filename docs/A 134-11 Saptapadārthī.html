Manuscript Features

Miśra.

Excerpts

Beginning

śrīmahāgaṇapataye namaḥ

vedāṃtavijñānasuniścitārthair

yogīśvarair dhyeyam ajaṃ viśuddhaṃ |

umānumābhyāṃ anugamyamānaṃ

vande maheśaṃ sadanugraheśaṃ || 1 ||

vighneśādīn namaskṛtya mādhavākhyasarasvatī ||

śivādityakṛtes ṭīkāṃ kariti mitabhāṣiṇīṃ || 2 ||

viśiṣṭaśiṣtācārānumitaśrute[r] bodhitakarttavyatākaṃ karttavyaviṣayakavighnavighātakaṃ parameśvaranamaskāralakṣaṇaṃ maṅgalam ācaran arthād viṣayaprayojane darśayati hetava iti ( fol. 1v1–4)

…

ata āha hetava iti jagaṃta iti jagaṃti utpattivināśadharmakāni kāryāṇi teṣāṃ hetave nimittakāraṇāyetyarthaḥ | (fol. 2v13–14)

End

pakṣasyaiva vipakṣatvenānaikāntika eva vā ato nāyaṃ kālātyayāpadiṣṭa iti cet satyaṃ . tathāpi pakṣe sādhyābhāva pramām apareṇā ʼsiddhyāder anavagamāt . sādhyābhāvapramārūpabādhasyopajīvyatvāt pṛthagoktam tathāpyasaṃkīrṇodāharaṇaṃ kim iti cet śrūyatām . parvato yam vahnimān dhūmavatvād iti tadevam anu (fol. 9v15–17 )
