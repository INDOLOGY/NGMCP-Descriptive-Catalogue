Excerpts

«Beginning»


oṁ namaḥ śrīsambhave(!) jyotirūpāya ||


namo buddhāya dharmāya saṃgharūpāya cai(!) namaḥ ||


svayambhuve viyac chāntabhānave dharmadhātave ||


bhavārṇavasamuttāre kāruṇyāgāramūrttaye ||


jagadāhlādarūpāya namastu(!) sambhave(!) sadā ||


asti nāsti kharūpāya tānarūpasvarūpiṇe ||


śūnyarūpasvarūpāya nānārūpāya te namaḥ || (fol. 1v1-2)


«End»


yathāgataḥ śākyasiṃha atra nepālamaṇḍale ||


tathāgatas trayastriṃśe(!) śiṣyagaṇaiḥ parivṛtaḥ || (!)


yatra mātā pratiṣṭhanti tatra nāke gata(!) api ||


svalpamātraṃ saddharmaṃ ca deśayati tathāgataḥ ||


tataḥ prabhṛti kāle ʼtra anyajā atrajā api ||


paṃcopacāravidhibhiḥ sadā tasmai prapūjitaṃ ||


pratidine pratimāse prativarṣaṃ mudā sadā ||


tat purāṇaṃ pratiśrutvā śrīsvayambhoś ca pūjitaṃ ||


pṛthau sumangalavare śubhapūrṇayukte ||


svarge ʼpi uṣṇiṣapade suraloke śreṣṭha(ḥ) || (!)


śambhoḥ purāṇamahimāṃ praśutā janāya (!)


saṃtiṣṭhate buddhaguṇe suguṇe na bhukte || || (93r2-4)


«Colophon»


iti śrī svayambhucaityabhaṭṭārakoddeśe mahāprabhāvavarṇano nāmāṣṭamo ʼdhyāyaḥ parisamāptaḥ || || śubham || ||


ye dharmmā hetuprabhavā


hetus teṣāṃ tathāgataḥ hy avadat


teṣāṃ ca yo nirodha[ḥ]


evaṃ vādi mahāśramaṇaḥ || (fol. 93r5-6)
