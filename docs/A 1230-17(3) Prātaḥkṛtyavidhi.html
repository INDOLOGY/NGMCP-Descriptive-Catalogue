Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namaḥ śrīnāthāya ||

śikhābandhanamantraḥ ||
phaṁ, hūṁ hrīṁ śrīṁ śaktya brahmaśaktya śivaśaktyā sarvvavighnānutsāra(5)ya ucchādaya sarvvato me rakṣāṃ kuru svāhā ||
vibhutiṃ gṛhītvā abhimantraṇaṃ || (exp. 13t4–5)

End

prātar utthāya ||
u(4)kāraṃ vāyuvījaṃ tad upari varuṇaṃ vajrapāṇiṃ tad ūrddhaṃ
kālaṃ varṇṇāntayuktaṃ tad upari paramaṃ vahnivījaṃ sahaṃsaṃ || (5)
induṃ vinduṃ layāntaṃ sitakamalaparaṃ kṣīradhārā sravantaṃ,
dṛṣṭāṃ kūṭaṃ tu nitya dahati kulamalaṃ merutulyaṃ (6) hi pāpaṃ ||
iti kūṭaṃ svānte arkkarāsisaḍrśaṃ pāpanāsāya dhyāyet ||
mūlavatī simantrena yathāśākti (7) japaṃ kṛtvā ||
sta[[vaṃ]] paṭhet || (exp. 14t3–7)

Colophon

iti prātakṛtyaṃ ||    || o ||    || (exp. 17t7)
