Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīgurave ||

praṇamya paramātmānaṃ bāladhīvṛddhisiddhaye |

sārasvatīm ṛjuṃ kurvve prakriyāṃ nātivistarāṃ ||

indrādayo pi yasyāṃtaṃ na yayuḥ śabdavāridheḥ |

prakriyān tasya kṛtsnasya kṣamo vaktuṃ naraḥ kathaṃ ||

tatra tāvat saṃjñā saṃvyavahārāya saṃgṛhyate || (fol. 1v1–3, exp. 11t)

End

veñ tantusantāne || veñor vayarnipi || veñor dhātorv vayādeśo bhavati || uvāya ūyatuḥ ūyuḥ | uvayitha | vyadha tāḍane || vivyādha vividhatuḥ | vividhuḥ || vivyayitha || praccha jñīpsā- (fol. 100r4–5, exp. 111b)
