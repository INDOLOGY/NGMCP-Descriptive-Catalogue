❖ oṃ namaḥ siddhināthāya || ||

nṛtye rūpaṃ prakāsinaṃ uttale palisaṃsthitaṃ |

vṛṣārūḍhāsanāsinaṃ nṛtyamānaṃ sutejasaṃ ||

pūjayad devamūrtti saḥ caturvvaktramahābalaṃ |

aṣṭabhūjā mahākṣātā vatulā cakṣuśi yutaṃ ||

khaṭvāṇgaratnadaṇḍaś ca ratnadā hastam ekata,

ḍamaru mudgarāśūlaṃ saṃkhaṃ dhyātaya mekata ||

trailikye krūrayat sarvva digadikṣu śca pūratā ||

vighnabhūtā mahānīdrā visūtāmūdire vata ||

śrāvaya ugutaṃ kṛtvā mudrāś caiva pradarśanā |

ājñayā devadevesa nāṭheśvaravigrahāt ||

iti dhyāna || ||

manu ||

oṃ kalālivikalālāvī kaleśvara hūṃ lūkhi śrīrañca mahā hūṃ mahānāṭanāṭeśvarāya svāhā || oṃ hūṃ ▒ nṛtyalakṣmīsahitāya hūṃ hrīṃ hrāṃ hrūṃ oṃ namaḥ svāhā || ṣaḍaṅgeṇa pūjayet ||

iti nāṭheśvaraḥ || (fol. 1v1–8, exp. 2t)
