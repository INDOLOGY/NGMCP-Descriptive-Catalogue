Manuscript Features

The available folios are 1, 6–12 and 14–18.

|| śrī 3 bhavānīśaṃkarapro ḍamarupramāṇaṃ ||

jambūkacarmmadakṣe tu vāmacarmmaṃ ca karmaṭam ||

ajacarmmena graṃthitaṃ (vā)dyacarmmaṃ tu meṣakam ||

etatprasāṇasaṃyuktaṃ damaru vādyate nṛṇām ||

śabda ⟪i⟫ śravaṇ(e)mātreṇa devadevīpriyaṃ bhavet || ||

iti mahākālasaṃhitāyāṃ || || śubham || || (fol. 18v3­–5)

Fol. 6r was microfilmed twice on the same exposure as fol. 1v. All other exposures consist of the recto of the following available folio at the top and the verso of the preceding folio at the bottom.

Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namaḥ śrīgaṇeśāya ||

|| kāmadaṃ kāmadaṃ natvā nītvā natimatāṃ mataṃ |

puṣpacintāmaṇiṃ kurve vāṃchitārthaniketanaṃ ||

tatrādau sāmānyataḥ puṣpamāhātmyaṃ likhyate || tad uktaṃ śivarahasya || (fol. 1v1–2)

End

kṣetre pāśupataḥ pade himavato guhyaśvarīsannidhau |

trājadbhānuvibha pratāpan yate rājyeti saukhyān pade |

kṛṣṇācāryyasutaḥ || satām iha vinā kaṣṭena vāñchāptaye |

māyāsiṃha imaṃ manojñam akaroc chrīpuṣpacintāmaṇim ||

iṃdhachoṣānvitaṃ granthaṃ satkaviḥ kin na manyate

sakalaṃkaṃ yathā loke su(ddhā)snaṃ sukṛtī janaḥ || (fol. 18r8–18v2, exps. 15t and 16)
