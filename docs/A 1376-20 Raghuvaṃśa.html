Manuscript Features

The available folios are 1–4, 2, 3, 4, 6, 13, 17, 19 and 34–44.

Some of the folios are from the different MS of the same text.

In some folios there is no commentary.

The MS contains the text from the first to the 64th śloka of the second sarga.

The folios of the MS are misplaced.

Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

atha prajānām adhipa prabhāte jāyāpratigrāhitagandhamālyāṃ ||

vanāya pītapratibaddhavatsāṃ yaśodhano dhenum ṛṣer mumoca || 1 ||

tasyāḥ khuranyāsapavitrapāṃśum apāṃśulānāṃ dhurikīrtanīyā ||

mārgaṃ manuṣyeśvaradharmapatni(!) śruter ivārthaṃ smṛtir anvaga[c]chat || 2 || (fol. 1v1–3)

«End of the root text:»

tataḥ samānīya samānitārthī hastau svahastārjitavīraśabdaḥ ||

«End of the commentary:»

tataḥ mānitārthī svahastārjito vīra iti śabdo yena saḥ | etenāsya dātṛtvaṃ dainyarāhityaṃ coktam | sa rājā hastau samānīya saṃdhāya | aṃjaliṃ badhvety arthaḥ | vaṃśasya kartāraṃ pravartayitāraṃ ata eva raghukulam iti prasiddhiḥ || anantakīrtiṃ sthirayaśasaṃ tanayaṃ sudakṣiṇāyāṃ tanayaṃ yayāce || 64 || (fol. 44v8–10)
