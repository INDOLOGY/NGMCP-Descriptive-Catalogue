Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīugracaṇḍāyai ||     ||

atha raṇayātrāpūjāvidhi likhyate ||     ||

tatrādau saṃkalpaṃ kuryyāt || oṃ adyehatyādide(2)śakālo smṛtāḥ virṣṇu 3 śrīvirṣṇuprajāpatikṣatre varttamānasaṃvatsare amukāyane amukaṃṛtau amukamāsā amukapakṣe (3) amukatithau yathā nakṣetravāraṇakara(‥)ṇakaranasamanvitāyāṃ amukagotrasya amukanāmabhūpasya svasainyarakṣārthaṃ para(4)sainyaparāyanārthaṃ ṣatriṃṣadadhikaśataṃ devatāpūjanaṃ raṇayātrām ahaṃ kalikhyat || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

adīkṣitā(7)ya no dadyā,t kucailāya durātmane ||

anyaśiṣyāya duṣṭāya, niṃdakāya kusāthināṃ ||

dīkṣitāya kulīnāya gu(8)rubhaktiratāya ca ||

śāṃtāya kulabhaktāya, śāṃtāya kulakāmine ||

idaṃ karma śive dadyā,t kulabhāgī bhavet naraḥ ||

(9) idaṃ rahasyaṃ paramaṃ, durgākavacam uttamaṃ |

guhyaṃ gopyatamaṃ gopyaṃ, gopanīyaṃ svayonivat ||     || (fol. 90v6–9)

iti śrīdurggāsti(10)raharīyamahātantre ugracaṇḍīkavacākhyānaṃ nāmāṣṭādaśapaṭalaḥ || 18 || (fol. 90v9–10)
