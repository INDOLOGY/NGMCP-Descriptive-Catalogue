Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||    ||

yathā grīṣmarttugharmmeṇa, himānī pravilī(2)yate ||
evam asyopadeśena, līyante janmakoṭayaḥ ||

praviśantaḥ samudre(3)ṣu, yathā nadanadīgaṇāḥ ||
svarūpaṃ parimucyaiva, yānti tanmayarūpatāṃ (4) ||

evaṃ nāmā ʼkārajāti,deśakālavivarjjitaḥ ||
etan mantropāsaka(5)s tu, yāti brahmamayātmatāṃ || (exp. 3t1–5)

End

nātaḥ parataro hy asti, ko pi maṃtro mahītale ||
prakā(4)śitaḥ puraghnena, yatinām eva muktaye ||

iti krameṇa sarvveṣāṃ, maṃtrā(5)ṇāṃ nyāsa īritaḥ ||
evaṃ nyaset sureśāni, saṃnyāsī vātha bhūpatiḥ || (6) || (exp. 21b3–6)

Colophon

ity ādināthaviracitāyāṃ mahākālasaṃhitāyā (!) śāṃbhavādiṣa(7)ṇmaṃtrasyāṃganyāsoddhāraḥ samāptaḥ ||    || (exp. 21b6–7)
