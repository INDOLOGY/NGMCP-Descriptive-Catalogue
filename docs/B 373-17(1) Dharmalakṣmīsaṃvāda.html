Excerpts

Beginning

khvāra juyāva jyā sayāva strīyā guṇa thvalāva jñāni juyāva manuṣya ja(2)nma kāyiva thvatena samastaṃ thava puruṣa bināna chuṃna madu thava puruṣayāke ni(3)ścayana sevā yāya māla ||     || (exp. 6t1–3)

End

brahmāna samasta sasāra sṛṣṭi yātaṃ jhāva madisyaṃ vi(3)ṣṇu . daḥśāvatāra satru marapāva juraṃ jhāva madisyaṃ mahādeva naṃ bhi(4)kṣāpātra joṅāva diga helāva juraṃ karmmana yāṅāna || hanoṃ suyāna (5) akāśa ḍamarapāva juraṃ karmmana yāṅāna || thvatena karma bināna chu(8t1)naṃ madu juraṃ karma yāta namaskāra || (exp.7b2–8t1)

«Sub–colophons:»

iti dha(3)rmmalakṣmīsamvāda catuthyoṃdhyā ||     || (exp. 6b2–3)

iti dharmalakṣmīsaṃvāda paṃcamodhyāya ||      || (exp. 7t3)

iti śrīdharmmalakṣmī saṃ(2)mvāda samāptaḥ || śubhamastu || (exp. 8t1–2)
