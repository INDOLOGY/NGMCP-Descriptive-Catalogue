Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

śrīlakṣmīnṛsiṃhāya (!) namaḥ ||

bhujagapativimukta svacha (!)nirmokavallī

vila(2)sitam akurvan yasya gaṃgāpravāhaḥ ||

śirasi sarasabhāsvan mālatīdāmalakṣmīṃ

lagayati himagau(3)raḥ sostu vaḥ sādhyasiddhyai || 1 ||

kalpāvasānasamaye sthitaye kavīnāṃ

dehātaraṃ kim api yā sṛ(4)jati prasannā ||

yasyāḥ prasādaparamāṇur api pratiṣṭhām

abhyeti kām api namāmi sarasvatīṃ tāṃ || 2 || (fol.1r1–4)

End

nānā kā(10)vyapurāṇanāṭakakathā kośetihāsasmṛti-

jyotiśāstragajāśvamānavabhiṣak kośān prayatnā(11)d iyam ||

dṛṣṭvānyāni ca śābdikaiḥ sahakṛtā hārāvalī yatnataḥ

kartavyotra na saṃśayaḥ sumanasaḥ śabdā(1)rthaliṃgeṣvataḥ || 76 ||

sudhiyā janamejayayena yatnād

dhṛtisiṃhena samaṃ nirūpitoyaṃ ||

vidito bahudṛśvabhiḥ(2) kavīndrair

bhuvi kośānumataḥ śramo madīyaḥ ||

hitvā mahāśābdikatābhimānaṃ

mātsaryam anyatra muhu(3)r nidhāya ||

hārāvalīṃ yaḥ prakaroti kaṃṭhe

vidagdhagoṣṭīṣu (!) paraṃ sa bhāti || 78 || (fol. 13r11–13v3)

Colophon
