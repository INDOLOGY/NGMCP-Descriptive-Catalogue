Excerpts

Beginning

śrīganeśāya namaḥ || śrīparamātmane namaḥ|| śrīgurave namaḥ ||

vīrabrahmahaya cho(2)ḍā javahiṃ,

vanaparvata bahu pahucā tavahiṃ,

piccha 2 arjuna jāṃhiṃ,

balasame(3)ta haya khojakarahiṃ

haya āge nahi dekhā javahi,

dahadiha khojana dhāya ta(4)vahi,

yahi antra nārada muni āyo,

amṛtaphalasabahi, jahni pāyo (exp.3b:1–4)

End

āsana garuḍa sadā tu a āhaya,

nahi baḍha āsana yahi taha āhaya,

yatanā vacana rājavabhāṣi,

bhagata dayāla bahuta santoṣā

jasa dhuva bhagata āha nṛpoa mohi,

aisana kayama yathā pye tohi

tīni sahasa te pāyiya rāju,

dhyāhahu kṛṣṇa avara nahi kāju (exp.26b5:26a2)

Colophon

iti candrahāsavyākhyāṇam (exp.26a2)
