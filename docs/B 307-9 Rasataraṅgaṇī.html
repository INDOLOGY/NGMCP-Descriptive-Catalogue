Beginning

|| śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

lakṣmīm ālokya lubhyan nigamam upahasan śocayan yajñajaṃtūn

kṣatraṃ śoḍākṣI(2)paśyan sam iti daśamukhaṃ vīkṣya romāṃca maṃcan ||

hṛtvā haiyyaṅgavīnaṃ cakitam apasaran mlecha (!) raktair digaṃtā(3)n

siṃcaṃ daṃtena bhūmiṃ tilam iva tulayan pātu māṃ pītavāsāḥ || 1 ||

bhāratyāḥ śāstrakāṃtā⟪yā⟫ra śrāṃtāyāḥ śāṃ(4)tikariṇī ||

kriyate bhānunā bhūrirasā rasataraṃgiṇī || 2 ||

vāṇī kamalinī bhānor eṣā rasataraṃgi(5)ṇī ||

haṃsāḥ kṛtadhiyas tatra yuktam atra pratīyatāṃ || 3 ||

girāṃ devi taraṃgiṇyāṃ vāraya kruravāraṇān ||

yad bha(6)viṣyati lokānām āvilo viralo rasaḥ || 4 ||(fol. 1v1–6)

«Sub: colophon:»

iti śrībhānudattaviracitāyāṃ rasataraṃgiṇyāṃ vibhāvanirū(8)paṇaṃ nāma dvitīyas taraṃgaḥ || (fol. 6v7–8)
