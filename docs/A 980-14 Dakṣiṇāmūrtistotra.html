Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya numaḥ || ||

śrīdakṣiṇāmūrttaye namaḥ || ||

paraśakty uvāca ||

deveśa śrotum ichāmi rahasyātirahasyakaṃ ||

suguptam api me deva kathayasva maheśvara || 1 ||

īśvara uvāca ||

rahasyātirahasyaṃ ca gopyaṃ gopyaṃ mahattaraṃ ||

na kutrāpi mayā proktaṃ sarvasvam api pārvati || 2 ||

kathyate sārabhūtaṃ hi sarvataṃtreṣu durlabhaṃ ||

tava prītyā maheśāni yathāvad avadhāraya || 3 || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

brahmacaryyarato maṃtrī mādhūkaraparāyaṇa(!) ||

sahasraṃ prajapen nityaṃ tato nāmasahasrakaṃ || 59 ||

prapaṭhet parameśāni sākṣāchivasamo bhavet ||

gurubhaktāya dātavyaṃ nābhaktāya kadācana || 60 ||

paraniṃdāparadrohaparivādaparāya ca ||

parastrīniratorthāya na deyaṃ sarvadā priye || 61 ||

śiṣyāya gurubhaktāya śivādvaijaratāya ca ||

upā(!)kāyaṃ deyaṃ hi nānyathā naśyati dhruvaṃ || 62 ||

gopanīyaṃ gopanīyaṃ gopanīyaṃ prayatnataḥ ||

svayonir iva goptavyaṃ na deyaṃ yasya kasyacit || 63 ||

iti saṃkṣepataḥ proktaṃ kim anyac chrotum ichasi || || (fol. 11v1–6)

Colophon

iti śrīcidaṃbaranaṭataṃtre dakṣiṇāmūrttināmastotraṃ saṃpūrṇam || || śubham || †jānīgaṇapatajīsyedaṃ† || (fol. 11v5–6)
