Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

mautrapābījaṃ hṛdi pītam aruṇaṃ vibhāvya dahanena pāpaṃ dahet,

oṃ tat sat hṛdi vadhūbījaṃ pītavarṇaṃ vāyunā tasmād utsārya śabdabījaṃ sitacchāyaṃ lalāṭoparī(!) bhāvayet ||

tasmād uttaram amṛtenaiva śarīropari pūjayet ||

tatra vyāpakanīrābdhau ākārād ra[[kta]]paṃkajaṃ ||

bhūyas tasyopari dhyāyed oṃkārāt padmabhājanaṃ,

tasyopari punar dhyāyet kūrcaṃ nīlaṃ ghanaprabhaṃ ||

(fol. 1v1-5)

End

athaināṃ brahmaraṃdhre tārinī māṃ pratitārayatīti tārāsārddha///vākṣararūpā brahmaviṣṇumaheśvarasadāśivavindumelanarūpā etāṃ nīlākhyāṃ sarasvatīm adhīyānas tarkāgamapurāṇādikāvyādivāgīśo bhavati || tavāstrāṃ tāvottarā tārā bhavati || etasya jñānamātreṇa jīvanmukto bhavati || etām etya sarvavidyāṃ(!) jñānavān bhavatyi || sa tu sanyāsī(!) bhavati || sa tu śiva eva sarvadevasamo bhavti || || (fol. 2r3-7)

ityātharvaṇīye tāropaniṣat || ❁ || lekhakapāṭhakayoḥ śubham bhūyāt || ❁ || ||                                                                       (fol. 2r7)
