The foliation of the upper left-hand margin is continuous. But the foliation of the lower right-hand margin is given for each chapter (sometime two or there chapters are kept in one foliation). The aforementioned folio numbers of the text is cited from the lower right-hand margin.

There are two exposures of fols. 19v–20r, 67v–68r. (it is mentioned according to the foliation of the upper left-hand margin)

Excerpts

«Beginning of the Bhairavāṣṭākastotra:»

❖ oṁ namaḥ śrībhairavāya ||

pāṇau brahmakapālamujjvalatare kaṇṭhe murārer vvapuḥ,

sarvvāṅgeṣu tuṣārahāradhavalā devāsthimālā prajaḥ |

vidyujihva iva prasādhanadhiyo bhūyo pi saṃbhūtayaḥ

pāyān naḥ pralayāvaśānasamaye kāpālikaḥ śaṅkaraḥ || 1 ||

krodhadhvastamano(!)dbhavāndhakaripur vvāmāṅgaśatiḥ prabhuḥ,

sṛṣṭī rakṣaka saṃharan jagad idaṃ helāvasānirmmitaṃ |

dhyānāśaktavilīnalocanavibhur yyāgānvitaḥ siddhidaḥ

kāleśaḥ kulanāyako vijayate devo māhābhairavaḥ || 2 || (fol. 1r1–3)

«End of the Apaduddhāra:»

bhairavasya prahṛṣṭābhūt sarvvajñasya meśvari |

evaṃ śrutvā tato devi nāmāṣṭaśatam uttamaṃ (fol. 3v12)

«Colophon of the Apaduddhāra:»

iti āpaduddhāra[ḥ] samāpta[ḥ] śubha (fol. 3v12)
