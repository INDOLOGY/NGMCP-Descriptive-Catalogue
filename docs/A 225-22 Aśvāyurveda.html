Excerpts

Beginning

(❖ śrīganeśāya namaḥ ||)

praṇipatya dhavalatanu-

timiraharaṃ gopatiṃ haraśeśāṅkau ca |

aśvāyurvvedanidhiṃ

mahāmatiśālihotraṃ ca || 1 ||

(2)hnapaṃ samasta andhakāranāśayāka, thathiṅ hmaṃ śrīsūryyato yuḍovarṇṇaju sevijyākatha thiṅa hma candramā mahādevaṃ aśva cikitsāśāstrayā khāniju se(3)vijyāka tava dhāṅa jñāna thulāvo vijyāka, thathiṅa hma śālihotra thvateyā namaskārayāṅā o śrīdurllabhasuta gaṇaśarmmāna, thva mahārājana ādarana (4)ṅeṅāna rājāyā adhyakṣasa suśrutādimahāmunisa nānāśvaśāstrasa nānāmati pikāyā o aśvāyurvvedasaṃgrahāyātā || (fol.1v1–4)

End

yatkīrtyā dhavalīkṛte trijagati smṛtvā svamoghodayaṃ

kārṣyaṃ kiṃ nadadhātya sā vaharahaḥ pakṣe site candramāḥ |

tasya śrī(1)raṇajinnṛpasya vipulā vidyādayānīradheḥ

śrīviṣṇor iva viśvapālanara te rastvakṣayā saṃtataṃ || 9 || (!) (fol.594r8;594v1)

Colophon

|| atha nepālabhāṣā ||

saṃvat 850 mārgasira kṛṣṇa daśamī bṛhaspativārakuhnu śrī śrījayaraṇajitamalla mahārājasana, yajñanārāyaṇapradhānāṃgayā valasa thva guli siddhayoga saṃgrahanāma aśvaśāstrayā nepālabhāṣāna ṭīkādayakaṃ sidhayakā julo || thva guli grantha mahākaṣṭana dayakā thvatena uttamaprakārana rakṣāyāṅa taya māla || pūnarvādajñānījanana (!) aśuddha ju o thāsa vicārayāṅā o śuddha juyake māla, jīpanisayā makhu, svāmiyā ājñāmātra yathā śaktina praṃāṇa yāṅā || || ❁ || śubham stu || || (fol.594v1–5)
