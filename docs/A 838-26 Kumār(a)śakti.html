Excerpts

Beginning

śrīmahāgaṇapataye namaḥ

bhavadavadahanaduranta-

jvālāvalikalitaśaithilyam ||

vi(dyullatā)śatāvṛtam

aham acyutavāridaṃ vande 1

bhūmīmaṇḍalakalpa(‥‥)lanṛpatiśrīrājasāhātmaja-

śrīpṛthvīpatisāhadevavacanān mandātmacetobhṛtaḥ

alpāyāsata eva bhūrikalahaṃ śrīdharmaśāstraṃ drutaṃ

samyag bodhayituṃ karomi kutukāt kañcin nibaṃdhan navam 2 (fol. 1v1–3)

End

kastu(!)rītilakāyitaṃ hi gaganaṃ yasyā imām uttamāṃ

śrīpṛthvīpatisāhakīrttim amitāṃ skaṃde samādāya yā ||

dhāvaṃtī trijagat prayāti sahasā tām aśramais tnvator

yo jasraṃ śaraṇaṃ vicitrakṛtaye kṛṣṇāya tasmai namaḥ ||

itthaṃ śrīmadapārabhūsu[[ra]]carad daurgatyadoṣātyaya-

prāgalbhyadyumaṇidyuteḥ kṣitipateḥ pṛthvīpateḥ prītaye ||

śrīmatpremanidhidvijo vitanute yāṃ sanmanas saṃśayac-

chedrekakumāraśaktim amalāṃ dhārādim āsyāgatā(!) || (6) || ❁ || (fol. 76r3–6)

Colophon

iti śrīkūrmācalākhaṃḍaladvāragīrvāṇaguruśrīpremanidhipaṃḍitanirmitāyāṃ kumāraśaktau prathamadhārā saṃpūrṇā || ❁ ||      || ❁ || (fol. 76r6–7)
