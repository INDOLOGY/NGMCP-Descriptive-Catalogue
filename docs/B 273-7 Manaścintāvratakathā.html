Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śivāya ||

yudhiṣṭhira uvāca ||

yasya prasādāt strī sarvvā vidhavā na kadācana |

dhanadhānyasamutpannā bahuputravatī bhavet ||

saubhāgya guṇasaṃpannā paśubhṛtyasamanvitā |

kena dharmmeṇa bhagavan vrūhi me munisattama || ○ || 

vyāsa uvāca ||

śruyatāṃ nṛpaśārd-dūla dharm-maputra yudhiṣṭhira |

dharmmasyāsya ca māhātmyaṃ manaścintāvratasya ca ||

mano maheśvara (!) sākṣāc-cintā bhavati pārv-vatī ||

tābyāṃ +++ yaḥ pūjayati strīṇāṃ siddhir bhaviṣyati || (37r1–4)

End

|| vyāsa uvāca ||

iti te kathitaṃ bhūpa manaścintāvratasya me |

māhātmyaṃ sukhadaṃ ceha strīṇāṃ paratramokṣadaṃ ||

|| yudhiṣṭhira uvāca ||

śrīmat prasādato ʼsmābhiḥ śrutaṃ strīdharm-mam uttamaṃ |

dharm-mam ācarayiśyāmi draupadīn sarv-vakāmadāṃ || (!)

karm-māṇi loke na viceṣṭitāni

daivodyatas tena vibhūṣitāni |

akarm-makṛtvā narakaṃ prayānti

puṇyena svarggaḥ manujāḥ prayānti || || (!) (fol. 44r2–5)

Colophon

|| iti yudhiṣṭhiravyāsasaṃvāde manaścintāvratakathā samāptaṃ || || || śubha ||  samvat 887 kārttika śudi 5 budhavāra thva kunhu likhita sapūrṇṇaḥ (!) ||

||  śubhaṃ bhavatu sarv-vadā || (fol. 44r5–6 )
