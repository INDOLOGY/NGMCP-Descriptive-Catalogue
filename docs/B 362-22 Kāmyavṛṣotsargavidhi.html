śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||


atha kāmyavṛṣotsargavidhir likhyate ||


[[natvā maheśvaraṃ devaṃ girijā ca vināyakam ||


nīloḍvāhavidhiṃ vakṣye nāradīyapurāṇataḥ || 1 ||


tatra viṣṇupurāṇe ||


ekādaśāhe pretasya yasya cotsṛjyate bṛṣaḥ


pretalokaṃ parityajya devalokaṃ sa gacchati ||


tathaiva manuḥ ||


ekādaśāhe pretasya yasya notsṛjyate vṛṣaḥ ||


pretatvaṃ sasthiraṃ dattaiśrāddhaśatair api ||


++++ bahavaḥ putrāḥ śilavanto guṇānvitāḥ ||


teṣāṃ santatiyuktānāṃ yadyekopi gavāṃ vrajet ||


yajeta cāśvamedhena bahusauvarṇadakṣiṇāḥ ||


gaurim udvāhayed kanyāṃ nīlaṃ + vṛṣam utsṛjyet (!) ||


sarveṣām eva devānāṃ nīlotsargaḥ praśasyate ||


spardhate yośvamedhena kratu rājyena tatra vaiḥ ||


vṛṣabheyāni romāṇi yāvanti vatsikāsu ca ||


tāvat varṣasahasrāṇī svargaloke mahīyate || (fol. 1v1 and added on the margin)

tataḥ pūrvoktamantrair abhiṣikto yajamānaḥ sāñjali prārthayet ||


yatkṛtaṃ tatsukṛtam astu yan na kṛtaṃ tad viṣṇoḥ prasādāt brāhmaṇavacanāt sarvaṃ paripūrṇam astu ||


pramādāt kurvatāṃ karma pracyavetādhvareṣu ca ||

nyūnaṃ saṃpūrṇatāṃ yāti sadyo vande tam acyutam ||
