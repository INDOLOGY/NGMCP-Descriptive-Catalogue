Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

jayat(!) paramavaṃditā tripurasuṃdarī devatā

vibhogavibhavānvitā paramatatvaciṃtā(!) citā ||

upādhirahitā hitā sakalaśāstranirddhāritā

prapaṃcaparavaṃditā tribhuvanaikarmatā matā || 1 ||

rasaciṃtāmaṇir nāma rasaratnakaraṃjakaḥ ||

bhaviṣyaty eṣa me graṃthaḥ śrīmatāṃ hastapustakaḥ || 2 ||

phalaṃ svādugata(!) svāmidāridraḥ kṣutprapīḍitaḥ ||

yathā prāpyamanaḥ kuryāj jano graṃtham amuṃ śamī || 3 ||

(fol.1v1-5)

End

kaṃguṇī sarṣapair aṃḍatailaṃ vātha kusuṃbhakaṃ

meṣākṣīraṃ ghṛtaṃ vāpya gokṣīraṃ cāranālakaṃ |

eteṣv ekaṃ tu bhāṃḍāṃtaḥ kiṃ dūnaṃ prapūrayet |

badhvā vastreṇa tadvaktraṃ gaṃdhacūrṇaṃ tatopari(!)|

lohapātre nirudhyātha (pṛṣṭeṣṭa ppaṃca) kharpparaṃ |

sāgnim upalakaiḥ pūrṇaṃ evyaṃ drāvyaṃ samuddharet |

taṃ dhartūradravai(!) piṣṭvā spuṣkaṃ drāvyaṃ ca pūrvavat |

punar eva prakarttavyaṃ saśuddho gaṃdhako bhavet || ||

(fol.113r3-7)

Colophon

iti rasaciṃtāmaṇau gaṃdhakaśuddhiḥ |

samāptoyaṃ graṃthaḥ || (fol.113r7)
