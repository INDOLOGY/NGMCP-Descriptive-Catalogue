Excerpts

Beginning

rajasvalāmukhaṃ dṛṣṭvā sarvapāpair vyapohati ||

saṃbhāṣaṇaṃ ca kurute rājasūyādikaṃ phalaṃ || 1 ||

tasyā sparśanamātreṇa labhen muktiṃ caturvidhāṃ ||

tasyāḥ saṃgamamātreṇa trailokyotpāṭane kṣamaḥ 2 ||

śraddhayā pūjayet tasyāḥ bhagaṃ sarajaśobhitaṃ ||

nyāsaṃ kṛtvā svadeheṣu kalābhiḥ kāmaṣoḍaśaiḥ || 3 || (fol. 108r3–6, exps.109b)

End

stotrapāṭhaprabhāvena (!) sṛṣṭisthitir bhaved dhruvaṃ ||

tasmāt sarvaprayatnena devi gopyakaraṃ śubhaṃ || 17 ||

smaret prathamapuṣpiṇīṃ rudhiraviṃḍanīlāṃvaraṃ

gṛhītamadhupātrikāṃ madavighūrṇanetrāṃcalāṃ || 18 ||

ghanastanabharānatāṃ galitanīvikāṃ śyāmalāṃ

karasphuritavallarīviṣaḍaśaṃkhatāṭaṃkinīṃ || 19 ||

iti dhyātvā || || (fol. 109v1–4, exps.111t)

Colophon

iti kālīprastāve īśvarapārvatīsaṃvāde puṣpiṇīstotraṃ saṃpūrṇaṃ || 30 ||

asya śrīkālikāgāyatrīmaṃtrasya kṛṣṇa ṛṣiḥ gāyatrī chaṃdaḥ śivaśaktisvarūpā kālikādevatā sarvapāpakṣayārthe jape viniyogaḥ ||

kṛṣṇa ṛṣaye namaḥ śirasi gāyatrī chaṃdase namo mukhe || śivaśaktisvarūpāyai kālikādevyai namaḥ hṛdaye || || dhyānaṃ || ||

śmaśāne tu mahākālīṃ devī (!) dhyāyed digaṃvarāṃ ||

daṃṣṭrākarālavadanāṃ muṃḍamālāvibhūṣaṇāṃ ||

dakṣiṇerddhi dhanur dhatte vāme caiva tathā śarān ||

adhaḥ pāśaṃ ca khaḍgaṃ ca tatkramāt parikīrttitaṃ ||

caturlakṣaṃ japaḥ || raktāktair vilvapatrair daśāṃśena homaḥ || ||

śubham || gurvarpaṇam astu || (fol. 109v4–110r5, exps.111t–b)
