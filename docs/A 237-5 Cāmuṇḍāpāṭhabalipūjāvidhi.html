Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīgurugaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||


arthaḥ dathuyā gulibali likhyate || brāhmaṇa ācāryya josi thvate senaṃ sthirahārāvava yāva || maṇḍapanapi

urttarasāyamajirasā pūrvvasāyaṃteva || bali viya pāṭhayāya caṇḍi || || balipāta 1 dathusa pūrvvasapā 1 dakṣiṇapā

1 paścimasapā 1 uttarasapā 1 īśānasapā 1 || arghapātra || jalapātra || paṃcabali || gaṇavatuka(!) || thvate

paṭavāsana coyāva jīyake || laṃkhana hāya ||


ūkālaṃ(!) vāyubījaṃ tadupari varuṇaṃ vajrapāṇi tadūrdhvaṃ |

kālaṃ varṇāntayuktaṃ tadupari paramaṃ vahnibījaṃ sahaṃsaṃ ||

induṃ vinduṃ layānta sitakamalavaraṃ kṣīradhārāśravantaṃ |

dṛṣṭvā kūṭaṃ tu nitya dahati kulamalaṃ merutulyaṃ hi pāpaṃ || || (fol. 1v1–2r1)


End

saṃhāramudrāṇa(!) balivisarjjana || sākṣithāya || || balicchoyavidhi || dathupāṭagalālalaṃkhu ||

(pūrvvapāṭha)balisapaṃmagāva || dakṣiṇa(pāṭha)bali bheraju || urttarapāṭha balipulako || īśānabali golajā dako

sālāṅa || paścimapāṭhabali vaṭuka dumāju || parasekadakosakalaṃ dukhā pikhā cchoya || || (fol. 46v1-5)


Colophon

thvate dathupātacaṇḍīpāṭhabalipūjāvidhi(!) samāptaḥ || śubham astu sarvvadā || || || ❁ || (fol. 46v5–6)
