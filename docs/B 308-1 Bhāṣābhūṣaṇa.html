Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

atha bhāṣābhūṣa(2)ṇa (!) liṣyate (!) dohā

vidhanaharana tuma ho (3) sadā gaṇapati hou sahāya

vinai karoṃ ka(4)ri jorikai dījai graṃtha vanāya 1

jihakī(5)no parapaṃca sava apanī ichāpāi

tākau(6)huṃ varanana karuṃ hātha jori paripāi 2

(7) karuṇākara poṣita sadā sakala rūpā(8)na

aise īśvarako bhalai raho raina dina dhyā(9)na 3

mere mana me tuma vasau aisī kyoṃ ka(10)hijāi

tātaiṃ yaha mana āpasoṃ lī jaise (11) vagalāi 4 (fol. 4v1–11)

End

tāhī narake hetaya ha(8)kīnho graṃtha navīna

jo paṃḍita bhāṣā ni(9)puna kavitā viṣai pravīna 13

lakṣana ti(10)ya aru puruṣa ke hāvabhāva rasa dhāma

a(11)laṃkāra saṃyoga teṃ bhāṣābhūṣaṇa nāma 14

(12)bhāṣābhūṣanagraṃtha ko paṭhai sunai citalāi

(13)vividha artha sāhitya rasa samakai sabai (14)banāi 15 (fol. 14v7–14)

Colophon

iti śrīmanmahārājādhi(15)rājavaṃsāvataṃśayaśavaṃtasiṃharāṭhauravi(16)racitāyāṃ bhāṣābhūṣaṇa (!) samāptam || (fol. 14v14–16)
