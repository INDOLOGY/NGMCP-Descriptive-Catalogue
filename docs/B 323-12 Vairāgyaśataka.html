Excerpts

«Beginning of the root text:»

yāñ citayāmi (!) satataṃ mayi sā viraktā

sāpyanyam ichati (!) janaṃ sa janonyasaktaḥ ||

asmatkṛte ca parituṣyati (6) kācid anyā

dhik tāṃ ca taṃ madanaṃ ca imāṃ ca māṃ ca || 1 || (fol. 2r5–6)

«Beginning of the commentary:»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

atha vairāgyaśatake katicit ślokā likhyate (!) || ||

eka koī yogī āyekarke (2) bhartṛhari rājā ko amṛtaphala eka cadḥāyā ye phala jo ṣāvai so ajaṃvara ho vai etnā jāna ke rājāne (3) rāniko diyā (fol. 1v1–3)

«End of the root text:»

nagamyo maṃtrāṇāṃ na ca bhavati bhaiṣajyaviṣayo

na cāpi pradhvaṃsaṃ vrajati vivi(6)dhaiḥ śāntikaśataiḥ

samāveśād aṃge kim api vidadhad bhaṃgam aśamaṃ

smarāpasmāro yaṃ bhramayati dṛśaṃ dhūrṇayati ca ||

(7) vairāgye saṃcaraty ekā nītau bhramati cāparaḥ ||

śṛṃgāre ramate ka[[ści]]d huvi bhedāt parasparaṃ || 40 || (fol. 19r5–7)

«End of the commentary:»

kohi vairāgyame cal te haiṃ kohi nīti me rahate haiṃ kohi śṛṃgārame ramate haiṃ iha saṃsārameṃ tinauṃ śa(10)takake bheda nyārā nyārā hai jo jinke ichā he sohi meṃ cittalagāvatā hai || (fol. 19r9–10)

«Colophon of the root text:»

iti śrībhartṛ(8)hariviracite śṛṃgāraśatake sāraślokāḥ saṃgṛhītāḥ samāptam || śrīśāke 1769 śubham || || (fol. 19r7–8)

«Colophon of the commentary:»

iti śrībhartṛhariviracitaṃ (!) śṛṅgāraśatakaṃ sāraślokāḥ (11) samāptāḥ || ❁❁ || ❁ raghunāthavināduḥkha kaunahare || raghunāthavinā suḥkha kauna hare || || (fol. 19r10–11)
