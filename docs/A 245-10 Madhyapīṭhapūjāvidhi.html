❖ oṃ namaḥ śrī3mahāgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||


śrīgurupādukābhyāṃ namaḥ ||


śrī3kubjikādevī namaḥ || ||


madhyapīṭhapūjāparddhati || ||


yajamānapuṣpabhājana ||


oṃ adyādi || vākya ||


mānavagotrayajamānasya śrīśrījayabhūpatīndramallavarmmaṇaḥ śrīśrīkulacakreśvarīdevatā


prītyarthaṃ viśvajātrāmahādīpaprajvālitapañcopacārapūjā kattuṃ(!) puṣpabhājanaṃ samarpayāmi || ||


śrī saṃvarttāmaṇḍalānte kramapadanihitānandaśaktiḥ subhīmā ||


sṛṣṭaṃ nyāyacatuṣkaṃ akulakulagataṃ pañcakaṃ cānyaṣaṭkaṃ ||


catvāraḥ pañcakonyaṃ punar api caturaṃ tattvato maṇḍalena ||


saṃsṛṣṭaṃ yena tasmai namata guruvaraṃ bhairavaśrīkujeśaṃ || (fol. 1v1–2r1)
