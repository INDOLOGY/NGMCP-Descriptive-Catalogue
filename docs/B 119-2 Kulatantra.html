Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

durgāyai namaḥ ||

namo gurubhyo namaḥ

daśaguṇaśatapatra prollasadvrahmapadma
pravimilitaguṇakoṇa pronmiladviṃdupīṭhaṃ |
bhajata gurupadābjaṃ nyakṛtābjaṃ sadābhaṃ
hṛdayakaluṣaśāṃtyai sarvaśāstrārthakāṃtyai ||

kanakekaṭajñāptar iva yasmin jagatrayaṃ |
kāyātīta vacotītaṃ manotītam upāsmahe || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

nava cha sanyāsi dharmmaviditaṃ nijapadapīta nepālavāsaḥ
śrīdevī padapadmagalitahṛdayaḥ karmma sa śuddhadetā
pūrṇānaṃlabhidhānaḥ praśamarasahṛdaḥ prāptabhāvā guroyaṃ
soyaṃ śrīkaulikānāṃ hitakaraṇamatiḥ kaulavṛttiṃ vidadhyai || (fol. 172v6–7)

Colophon

śrīpūrṇānaṃdanāthena kṛtāyāṃ gurubhāvata |
kulavṛttau svataṃtrāyāṃ triṃśatpaṭala īritaḥ || 40 || (fol. 172v7–8)
