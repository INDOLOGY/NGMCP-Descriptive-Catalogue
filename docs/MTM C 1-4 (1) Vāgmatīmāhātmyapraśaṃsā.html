Manuscript Features

  • This MTM contains the following texts:

1. Vāgmatīmāhātmyapraśaṃsā (fols. 1–44, exps. 3–50)

2. Gokarṇāṣṭaka (exps. 51–52)

  • From the fol. 29 onwards, the MS is slightly damaged with the lost of a few letters.
  • There are two exposures of fols. 12v–14r and 18v–19r.

Excerpts

«Beginning»


❁ || ❖ || namaḥ paśupataye ||

yasya vaktrād viniṣkrāntā vāgmatī lokapāviṃ(!)nī |

namāmi śirasā devaṃ śaṅkaraṃ bhuvaneśvaraṃ |

yena dharmmārthinā proktaṃ purāṇam amṛtopamaṃ |

namāmi mūrddhnā sarvvajñaṃ pulastyamunisattamaṃ ||

yasya kāvyaṃ samālokya purāṇam idam uddhṛtaṃ |

namo ‘stu satataṃ tasmai vyāsāya ca mahātmane || (fol. 1v1–3)



«End»


vāgmatyāḥ maṇivatyāś ca (saṃvedye) lokapūjite |

smaya(!)te satataṃ yas tu puṇyamān kīrttyate bhuvi |

/// pasamudbhūtā rudradhāreti cocyate |

kalpitā devadevena lokānugrahakāriṇā |

muktibhuktipradaṃ puṇyaṃ śivamāyāya(!) lakṣitaṃ |

rudradhārodayaṃ dṛṣṭvā sarvvapāpaiḥ vimucyate |

gayāyāḥ namiṣā(ṇya)pi kurukṣetrā ca ‥ skarāt |

snānaṃ darśaguṇaṃ ‥ paṃ? maṇivatyāḥ samāgame ||

brahmavaktrasamudbhūtā tī (fol. 44v3–5)



«Colophon»

The colophon is lost.
