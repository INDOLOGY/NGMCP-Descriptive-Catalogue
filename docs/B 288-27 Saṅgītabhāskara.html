«Beginning of the root text: »

[[ viśvaṃ sthāvarajaṅgamātmakam idaṃ yasyāhnikaṃ vāṅmayaṃ

sarvaṃ vācikam indusūryabhagaṇādyāhāryam āryā jaguḥ |

dīkṣā mārakṛtākhila tribhuvano yaḥ kevalaṃ svā(!)tviko

rudraḥ śrīkṛtasavyabhāgasubhago bhūyāt sa vaḥ śreyase || ]] (fol. exp. 1top)

«Beginning of the commentary: »

❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

ajñānatimiradinakaram

ānandasudhāsamudrapūrṇenduṃ

tāpatrayāpaharaṇaṃ

vande paradevatācaraṇaṃ ||

natvā gurūn atigurūn

kṛtī jagajjyotir avanīśaḥ

saṅgītabhāskaram amuṃ kurute

sudhiyaṃ sametya vaṅ(!)śamaṇiṃ ||

nāṭyaśāstrāraṃbhe nirbighnasamāptyarthaṃ śiṣṭācāraparaṃparāprāptakarttavyatākam etacchāstrapravarttakasamucitaśiveṣṭadevatākīrtta[[na]]rūpaṃ maṃṅgalam ācarati granṭha(!)kāraḥ viśvamityādi (fol. 1v1–3)

«End of root text: »

evaṃ sālagasūtrasthair ekatālpavaśānakaiḥ |

rūpakair narttanaṃ kṛtvā pātraṃ tyāge supāśrayet |

narttakyasayaivam ādāya kathitā goṇḍalīvidhiḥ || (fol. 191v6–7)

«End of commentary: »

evam anena prakāreṇa ekatālpavaśānakaiḥ śeṣa ekatālītālakanṛtyayuktaiḥ śālagasūtraiḥ gītaprabaṃdhaiḥ rūpakaiś ca tālair nṛtyaṃ kṛtvā pātraṃ tyāgaṃ nṛtyavirāmaṃ kuryād ity artha || evaṃ anena prakāreṇa narttakasyānandāya mayā gōṇḍalīvidhiḥ kathita ity arthaḥ | (fol. 191v7–192r1)

«Colophon: »

iti saṃgītabhāskare goṇḍalīvidhiḥ || || idānīm avaśyakartta⟨vya⟩vyārtham āha yathā yathā prekṣakānā(!)ṃ camatkāeas tathā tathā ratim utpādayen nṛtya iti saṃgītasaṅgrahaḥ ||

...

śrīmajjagajjyotyir adhīśvarasya

nideśam āsādya guṇottarasya |

saṃgītaśāstrasya cakāra ṭīkā

śrīmaithilo vaṅśamaṇir manīṣāṃ ||

sudhiyo pi hi nandanti jalpinair bbālakalpitaiḥ |

tadeṣa paritoṣāya viduṣāṃ me śa(!)mudyamaḥ ||

nepālābde śaśiśaramunivyañjite śuklapakṣe |

puṣye gaṇḍe suragurudine drākṣapakṣe daśamyāṃ |

labdhvādeśaṃ guṇagaṇanidheḥ śrījagajjyotir īśāt |

saṃgītādyaṃ samalikhad asau bhāskaraṃ vijña induḥ || śubha || (fol. 192r1–192v3)
