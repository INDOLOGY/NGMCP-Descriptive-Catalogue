Beginning

/// tasya cikitsitaṃ |

jyeṣṭhāyāaṃ pañcame mṛtyur ūrdhvaṃdvādaśahāt sukhaṃ | muneṃ na copamṛṣṭa(2)sya paṃcarātraṃ bhavet dvāraḥ |(!)

tadūrddhvaṃ varttamānasya ekaviṃśa (!) bhavet sukhaṃ |

āṣāḍhāyāṃ tu pū(3)rvvāyāṃ navamehanim ucyate || (X.4a1–3)

End

drākṣāpatrakavālaparyyatakanā śṛṃgīkarī keśaraaṃ,

kṣaudre sātiviṣāturālabhasramālehaṃ parakuryyā// (X. 183:8–9)
