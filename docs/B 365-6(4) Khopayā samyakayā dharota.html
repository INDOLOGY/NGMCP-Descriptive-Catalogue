Title Khopayā samyakayā dharota

Remarks This is the fourth part of a MTM which also contains the text of the Gṛhavāstulakṣaṇa, Vātāyamsthāpanalakṣaṇa and Nāgavāstulakṣaṇa

Author

Subject vividha

Language Newari

Text features This is the list of food materials of the great Samyak festival accomplished in Bhaktapur. King Jagajjyotir Malla (NS 734–756) was invited in the function. King Śivasiṃha (NS 698–740) of Kathmandu was also seems to be presented there. A dasapati (letter of thanks and presentation) was presented him sixth day of feeding of Samyak. Ruling period of both of king is seen NS 734–740 (AD 1614–1620).
