Excerpts

«Beginning:»


śrīgaṇeśāyanamaḥ ||


vande kleśādyasaṃsṛṣṭaṃ purāṇapuruṣaṃ harim


prakṛtyā sītayā juṣṭaṃ yogeśaṃ yogadāyinam 1



patañjaliṃ sūtrakṛtaṃ praṇamya


vyāsaṃ munīṃ bhāsyakṛtaṃ ca bhaktyā


bhāṣyānugā yogamaṇiprabhākhyāṃ


vṛttiṃ vidhāsyāmi yathā manīṣā 2


iha khalu bhagavān patañjaliḥ prekṣāvat pravṛttyaṅgaśāstrapratipādyaṃ darśayati |


atha yogānuśāsanam | atra thaśabda ārambhārthaḥ | yogaśāstraṃ ārabhyata ity arthaḥ | (fol. 1v1–3)


«End:»


iti śabdaḥ śāstraparisamāptidarśanārthaḥ


yadvāci praṇavapratipattidahanaḥ sadyottarāpāṭavīṃ | (!)

dagdhvā svātmani bodhadīpam acalaṃ sūte tamaḥ pāṭanam |

yatra nyasta samastakarmajanaye dyogaṃ tadāgaṃ vinā

vande siddhibhuvaṃ tam īśam aniśam kaiva(lya)daṃ rāghavaṃ |


yatprasādalavaḥ sūte mokṣādyā sarvasaṃpadaḥ

umādhavaṃ maheśānaṃ taṃ kāśīnilayaṃ bhaje |

phaṇīndrasūtrasannaddhāḥ vyāsavāṅmaṇibhūṣitā

mad vāṅmauktikī mālā syāt sadā śrīrāmapādayoḥ(!) 3


kvāhaṃ pramādatirataḥ kva vātsalyam guror idaṃ

nūnaṃ mahātmanādīne svataścittakripānvitam (fol. 34v6–10)


«Colophon(s)»


iti śrīmadparamahaṃsaparivrājakācāryaśrīgovindāna(nda)bhagavatpūjyapādaśiṣya śrīrāmanandasarasvatī

kṛtau sāṃkhyapravacane yogamaṇiprabhāyāṃ kaivalyapādaś caturthaḥ samāptaḥ śubhm (fol. 34v10–12)
