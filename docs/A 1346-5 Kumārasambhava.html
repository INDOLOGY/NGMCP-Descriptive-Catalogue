Manuscript Features

The text runs form the very beginning up to the end of the 7th chapter.

The ekāras of an akṣara extend up only halfway from the base to the mātrā.

Some marginal corrections appear at the beginning of the text.

Words divisions are marked up to fol. 16r by small vertical strokes.

The prose order (anvaya) of the words of verses is provided in the form of numbers above them up to fol. 13.
