Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

kailāśottuṅgaśikhare ratnarājavirājite ||

kalpadrumavanākīrṇe svarṇapāṣāṇamaṇḍite || 1 ||

padmarāgaśilayukte (!) maṇimāṇīkyabhūṣite ||

vasantakusumāmodagandhavāhaikavāhinī || 2 ||

navaratnaśilabaddhapīṭhastham parameśvaram ||

devadevajagannāthaṃ pārvatīsahitam vibhum || 3 ||

vasantañ ca patañtañ ca dhyāyantam brahma śāśvatam ||

yantagābharaṇopetaṃ jaṭāmukuṭamaṇḍitam || 4 || (fol. 1v1–3, exp. 5t)

End

pativratānāṃ sarvāṣāṃ stotram etac chubhāvaham

idaṃ stotram mahāpuṇyaṃ ya śṛṇoti pativratā || 16 ||

naronyo vāpi nārovī (!) labhate sarvavāñchitam ||

aputrī labhate putraṃ nirdhanī labhate dhanam || 17 ||

rogī ca mucyate rogāt baddho mucyeta bandhanāt ||

pativratā tu paṭhanād gaṅgā snānaphalaṃ labhet || 18 ||

phalañ ca sarvatapasāṃ pratānāñ ca vrajeśvaraḥ ||

idaṃ paṭhitvā natvā ca bhuktvā ca tadanujñayā || 19 ||

yā nārī ca sthitā gehe jñemaṃ (!) sā bhuvaneśvarī ||

uktas (!) pativratādharmmaṃ gṛhīṇāṃ śrūyatāṃ vraja || 20 || || (fol. 15v4–7, exp. 19t)

iti śrīrudrayāmale devīrahasye lakṣmīnārāyaṇasya mantranāmasahasrakathanaṃ nāmaikonacatvāriṃśati paṭalaḥ || || 41 || || ❁ || || (fol. 15r3, exp. 18b)
