Excerpts

Beginning of the root text

graṃthāraṃbho. (!) vighnavighātāya samuciteṣṭadevatāṃ graṃthakṛt parā⁅mṛ⁆śatīti || (fol. 2v11–12)

Beginning of the commentary

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

jāgrattrailokyarājyodbhavavibhavaparīraṃbhasaṃbhāvitāṃ hi
kṣīrākūpārakalpālalitakaratalonmṛṣṭapādāravindam ||
kāṃcīśrīvatsahārāṃgadamukuṭalasatkaustubhodbhāsitāṃgaṃ
lakṣmīkāntaṃ prasannaṃ hṛdayasarasijāntasthitaṃ saṃsmarāmi || 1 ||

śrīmac chāṇḍilyavaṃśe kṛtavividhamakhaḥ kīrttimān dīkṣito bhūd
gaṃgādāsaḥ prasiddhaḥ suragurusadṛśaḥ (!) ⁅kā⁆nyakubjāgragaṇyaḥ ||
tasmād vīreśvarākhyaḥ tanaya iha mahābhāgyavān vihyuubhakto (!)
jātaḥ saṃkīrtanīyaḥ sakalabudhajanair bhūpatīnāṃ sabhāsu || 2 ||

tasmāc chrīmuralīdharo hi kavitāpāṃḍityapuṇyāvadhir
jātas tasya sutau trilocanaśivānandau guṇais tatsamau ||
śaive vā pathi vaiṣṇave samarasaḥ śrīmac chivānandataḥ
saṃjātaḥ kila bhīmasena iti sad vidyāvinodī kaviḥ || 3 || (fol. 1v1–8)

End of the root text

svayaṃ ca pallavātāmrabhāsvatkaravirājinī ||
prabhātasaṃdhye vā svāpaphalaludhve (!) hitapradā ||

āhūteṣu vihaṃgameṣu maśako nāyān puro vāryate
madhye vāridhi vā vasaṃstṛṇamaṇir dhatte maṇīnāṃ [ru]caṃ ||
khadyoto pi na kalpate pracalituṃ madhye pi tejasvināṃ
dhik sāmānyam acenataṃ prabhum ivānāmṛṣṭatatvāntaram || (fol. 282v8–9, 11–14)

End of the commentary

rājāno svavivekinaḥ kṣititale prāyo dvijāḥ paṃḍitaṃ
manyāna (!) stutim ācaranti sudhiyoṃ (!) no vā vidanti śramam ||
sāphalyaṃ tu tathāpi⟨|⟩gopaśiśukaḥ kutrāpi kasmiścid apy
ārdrāntaḥ karaṇe kariṣyati sudhāsiṃdhoḥ paraṃ sajjane || 3 ||

saṃvadgrahāśvamuni (!) nū (!) jñāte māse madhau śucau ||
trayodaśyāṃ somavāre samāptoʼ yaṃ sudhāṃ dadhiḥ ||    || ❁ ||    || (fol. 284r13–v2)

Sub-colophon

iti śrīmatpadavākyapramāṇapārāvārīṇadīkṣitabhīmasenakṛte kāvyapraśavivaraṇe sudhāsāgarākhye bhidheyaprayojanakāraṇasvarūpabhedalakṣaṇanirṇaya. prathama ullāsaḥ || (fol. 21r1–3)

Colophon
