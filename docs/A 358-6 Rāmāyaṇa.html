Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||    || ❁ ||

jitaṃ bhagavatānena hariṇā lokadhāriṇā |
ajena viśvarupeṇa nirguṇena guṇātmanā ||

pratiprayāte bhrate vasan rāmas tapovane ||
udvegaṃ lakṣayāmāsa tatra chānāntapasvināṃ |

ye tatra citrakuṭastha(!) snā(!) tāpasāśramāḥ |
rāmam āśritya niratās tānalakṣaya utsukān |

nayanair bhṛkuṭībhiśva rāmam udiśya śadvitāḥ |
anyonyam abhisaṃgamya śanaiḥ cakrur mmithaḥ kathāḥ | (fol. 1v1–4)

End

hariviṃśatismarālokās tām iva paryyaye
spaṃdanaiḥ sapaṃdanagatā gajaigajadhuraṃgatāḥ
aśvārohā sadaśvaiva pādātāśva padātibhiḥ
rākṣaseṃdrāgirinibhā śarataṃ vṛṣṭitomaraiḥ
nirichvāsaṃ hariṃ cakruṃ prāṇāyāma iva dvijān
niśāvarai hanyamāno mīnair iva mahāṃtibhiḥ (fol. 8v9¬–11)

Colophon

|| ityārṣe rāmāyaṇe uttarakāṃḍe rākṣasaniryānaṃ nāmasargaḥ || 6 || (fol. 8v7)
