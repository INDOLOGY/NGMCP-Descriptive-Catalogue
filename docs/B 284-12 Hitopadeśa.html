Excerpts

Beginning of the commentary

śrī gaṇeśāya namaḥ || atha bhāṣā ṭippaṇī ||    ||
yastā dhūrjaṭI bhannā mahārudrakā prasādale sajjana savaiko kāryya siddhi havas. sajjana mahārudrakā śiramā vasyāki gaṃgākā phena jasto. seto candramāko kalāsobhāmānbhai rahyakāchan. tastaimahārudrale savaiko rakṣā garun || 1 ||
(fol. 1v1–3)

Beginning of the root text

|| siddhi sādhye satāmastu prasādāttasya dhūrjaṭeḥ ||
janhavī pheṇarekheva yanmurddhi śaśinaṃkalā || 1 ||
(fol. 1v4)

End

rāja putraharu bhanchan. he guru ------ sukhasita suhṛd bheda sunyāṃ || viṣṇu śarmā bhancha. pherieso havas timiharukana || he rājaputraharu ho. tamrā śa ----- tamrā duṣṭa jana kālakāvaśa parun || tamrā śatrūko cāḍai nāśahavas || tamrā prāṇijana harukana saṃ ----- yo kathārupi vagaicā viṣe. sadai vālaṣa pani atiṣusi rahalān bhani āśirvāda gardābhayā || dinai --------
(fol. 115r7–115v1)

Colophon

vikamārka navananda gajabhūmita kārtike asitapakṣe caturthike ❁ iti hitopadeśesuhṛdbhededo nāma dvitīyaḥ kathāsaṃgraha samāptaḥ || ❁ -------- || iti hitopadeśe suhṛdbhededo nāma bhāṣāyāṃ dvitīyaḥ kathā saṃgraha samāpta || śubham || ❁
(fol. 115v2–5)
