Excerpts

Beginning

❖ atha ṣoḍasopacālaphalaṃ || ||


tathā ca āgamasāre ||


dhūpaṃ dahati pāpāni dīpo mṛtyuvināsanaṃ ||

alakṣmī(!) nāsayet puṣpaṃ arghadusvapnanāśanaṃ ||


tilakaṃ nāsaye[t] śatru(!) naivedyaṃ kalināsa(!)naṃ ||

vighnaṃ ca tutiśabdena dhyānaṃ caiva mahātbhayaṃ(!) ||


jāpaṃ hā(!)kaṃ vinasyanti vyādhināsaṃ visarjjanaṃ ||

ghā‥na samayojena bhasmena putrado bhavet ||


pranāmaṃ(!) siddhisaṃprāptaṃ maṃtrai(!) naiva tu riddhidā |

mudrāyā āyuvṛddhi[ḥ] syāt snānakāmam abhīṣṭhitā ||


ṣoḍaśe tān nireceta devyarccanam idaṃ mataṃ || ||


iti ṣoḍaśopacālavidhiḥ || || (fol. 1r1–4)


End

samūhaṃ yasya te maṃtrī dūraṃ gatvā prayatnataḥ ||

samūhe tu ahaṃ sākṣāt samūhas tu pitāmahaḥ ||


samūhaḥ keśavo devi samūhas tu puraṃdaraḥ ||

nārījanasamūhas tu svabhāvajaṃ tu suṃdari ||


tvayā sārddhaṃ(!)m ahaṃ devi viharāṃi yathepsayā ||

evaṃ stutvā tato maṃtrī kāyakleśena pūjayet ||


yathālābhānusāreṇa patrapuṣpaphalādibhiḥ |

kanyājanasamūhe tu mahāsiddhir vyavasthitāḥ ||


tasya vārccnapuṇyaya lopam ādṛśyate kvacit ||

yas tv evaṃ pūjayed devi sidhyate nātra saṃśayaḥ || ||(fol. 6v2–6)


Colophon

iti śrīhāhārāvatantre uttaramate guhyakāḷikāvidhāne devyotparttipaṭalaḥ || śrī || śrīdūrgā || (fol. 6v6)
