Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

nyāyamālāyā ādau svakīyagraṃthatvadyotanāya svamudrārūpam anekārthagarbhaṃ devatānamaskārapratipādakaṃ ślokaṃ paṭhati

kāṭhakam adhītam iti (2) prayogāt graṃthanāmety avagaṃtavyaṃ bhūbhojanādir adhyayanadharmaḥ punar uktir adhyetṛbhedān na duṣyati alpāyuṣāpi śākhāṃtarastho(3)pasaṃhāranyāyena karmānuṣṭhātuṃ śakyate tasmād ananyathāsiddharūpapratyabhijñānāc chākhābhede pi karma na bhidyate 2 (fol. 11r1–3)
