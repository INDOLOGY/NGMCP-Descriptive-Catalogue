Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṃgāyai namaḥ ||

bhagavati bhava līlāmauli māle tavāmbhaḥ-

kaṇam aṇuparimāne prāṇino yaṃ spṛśaṃti⟨ḥ⟩ ||

amaraṇagaranārī cāmaragrāhiṇīnāṃ

vigatakalikalaṃkā taṃkam aṃke luṭhaṃti⟨ḥ⟩ || 1 ||

brahmāṃḍaṃ khaṃḍayaṃtī haraśirasi yadā vallīm ullāsayaṃtī

svarlokād āpataṃti kanakagiriguhāgaṃḍaśailāt skhalaṃti ||

kṣoṇipṛṣṭhe luṭhaṃti duritacayacamū nirbharaṃ bhartsayaṃtī

pāthodhīṃ pūrayaṃti suranagarasarītpāvani(!) naḥ punātu⟨ḥ⟩ || 2 || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

mātar jāhnavi śaṃbhusaṃgavalite maulau nidhāyāṃjilaṃ

tvattīre vapuṣo vasānasame nārāyaṇaṃ hṛd vayaṃ |

sānaṃdaṃ smarato bhaviṣyati mamaḥ (!) prāṇaḥ prayānotsavo

bhūyād bhaktir avicyutā hariharā dvaitātmikāḥ sāśvatī || 8 || (fol. 2v1–3)

Colophon

iti śrīmacchaṃkarācāryaviracitaṃ gaṃgāṣṭakaṃ saṃpūrṇam || ❖ || || || || || || || || || || || || || || (fol. 2r5)
