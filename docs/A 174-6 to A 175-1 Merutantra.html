Beginning

❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || sarasvatyai namaḥ || śrīgurūbhyo namaḥ || śrī Iṣṭadevatāyai namaḥ ||

yāyā (!) sat sad ivābhāti na ca bhāpi (!) kadāpi sat |
yasmin jñāte na sā bhāti tasyai tasmai namo namaḥ || 1 ||

jalaṃdhareṇa vijite surāsuranaro khile ||
sūkṣmarūpeṇa te sarve maheśaṃ śaṃkaraṃ gatāḥ || 2 ||

divyānandasahastrāṇI samādhisthaṃ vilokayataṃ (!) ||
yārthitā cārcitā tais tu vāmadakṣIṇamārgibhiḥ || 3 || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

maṃtrārṇasaṃkhyadīpeṣu jvalite thatadvine ||
ṣunadīpe (!) kṛte sā tu dīpeśaṃ dṛśyate sphuṭā ||

Iṣaṃ svakīyaṃ kathaye (!) dyāvayen manase sthite ||
vadāti (!) devatā tuṣṭā guhyam eta (!) prakāśitaṃ ||

kārttaīryasamo nyona (!) bhaved īya (!) priyasuraḥ ||
taṃtreṣu tadmipavidhir (!) vahudhā parikīrtitaḥ ||

iti tte (!) bhāskarasyokte vidhidīpaṃ samanvitaḥ ||
ataḥ paraṃ yatra cecchā tatara pṛcchaṃtu māṃ prati || (fol. 88v6–8)

Colophon

iti śrīmahāmāyāmahākālānumate merutaṃtre śivapraṇīte dīpavidhiprakāśaḥ paṃcaviṃśaḥ ||    ||
