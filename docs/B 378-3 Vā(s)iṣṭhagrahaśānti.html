Excerpts

Beginning

svasti śrīgaṇapataye namaḥ || ||


lāṃgalīlāṃ diśaṃ sarvvāṃ bhūmiśleṣeṇa tāḍayet ||

sarṣapā vā japemantra(!) sarvabījānujāpayet ||

rakṣohaṇaṃ japen mantraṃ bhūtoccāṭanam eva ca ||

bhūmiśuddhyaṃ(!) prakurvīta bhūtānāmas(!) tathaiva ca ||

purūṣasūktaṃ japen nityaṃ bhūmiśudhyaṃ (!) tu kārayet ||

gāyatrī ca japen mantraṃ paścād maṇḍalam ārabhet ||

iti bhūtoccāṭanaṃ | (exp. 2 t1–4)


End

anena mantreṇa ādarśayati abhiṣekam bṛṣabharohaṇamukhaphalabhakṣaṇakūṭasāchim(!) ityāvādi anṛtabhāṣaṇa dugdhapāla kalavanacaraṇa(!) paranindā parahāsyavivarjitādveṣarahitāparaśucivivarjjitā pitṛmātṛbhakti devabrāhmaṇapūjā akulaśoṣaka śaraṇapālaka stridhāna(!)pararahitā svadhanasaṃtoṣaḥ kulācāraṇa(!) anācāra (!) vivarjjayet

hasaṃti jalpāṇi(!) vadaṃti gacche gataṃ na socāni kṛtan na mannyai (!)(!)

dvābhyāṃ tṛtīyai bhavaṃti rājyaṃ kathaṃti bhūpāla vadaṃti mūrṣa (!)(!) (exp. 36b1–6)
