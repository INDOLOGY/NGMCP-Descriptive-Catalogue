Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||    ||

yo rāvaṇaṃ raṇamukhe bhuvanaikabhāraṃ
hatvā cakāra jagataḥ paramopakāram ||
yam brahma cātidadhire paratopipāraṃ
tan naumi maithilasutā hṛdayaikahāram || 1 ||

nārikela phaleṣu taṇḍulam atha kṣīraṃ rasāle hitaṃ
jamvīrottha raso ghṛte samucita(!) sarpis tu mocāphale ||
godhūmeṣu ca karkaṭī hitatamā māṃsāt paye(!) kāṃjika
nāraṃge guḍabhakṣaṇaṃ ca kathitaṃ piṇḍāluke kodravaṃ(!) || 2 || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

bhaṃgāmade phenamade krameṇa dūrvāmlavargaṃ kaṭunāgaraṃ vā ||
gareṣu hemaprathitaṃ guḍaś ca madātyaye kṣīramade ca khaṇḍam || 38 ||

cayaprakopopasamāvāyor grīṣmādiṣu triṣu ||
varṣādiṣu ca pittasya kaphasya śiśirādiṣu || 39 ||

ekaśāyī dvibhojī ca ṣaṇmūtrī tripurīṣakṛt ||
ṛtusaṃgamakārī ca vyādhibhir nābhibhūyate || 40 || (fol. 4r2–4)

Colophon

iti kāśīrājaviracitāyāṃ ajīrṇamaṃjari samāptam || ❁ || śubham || (fol. 4r5)
