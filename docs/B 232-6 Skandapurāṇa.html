«Beginning: »


svasti śrīmahāgaṇādhipataye namaḥ ||


oṃ namo bhagavate mahārudrāya ||


yasyājñayā jagatsraṣṭā viriñciḥ pālako hariḥ |


saṃharttā kālarudrākhyo tamas tasmai pināṅine ||


tīrthānām uttamaṃ tīrthaṃ kṣetrāṇāṃ kṣetram uttamaṃ ||


tatraiva naimiṣāraṇye śaunakādyās tapodhanāḥ ||


dīrghasatraṃ prakurvvantaḥ satriṇāṃ saṃmatena ca ||


teṣāṃ saṃdarśanotsukyād āgato hi mahātapāḥ ||


vyāsaśiṣyo mahāprājño lomaśo nāma nāmataḥ |


tatrāgataṃ te dadṛśur munayo dīrghasatriṇaḥ ||


uttasthur yugapat srvve sarve hastāḥ samutsukāḥ |


datvārghyapādyam asakṛn munayo vītakalmaṣāḥ ||


taṃ paprachur mahābhāgāḥ śivadharmaṃ suvistaraṃ ||


munayaḥ ūcuḥ || (fol. 1v1–7)


«End: »


kathāṃ śayānāḥ śṛṇvaṃto bhavaṃtyajagarā narāḥ ||


yaḥ śṛṇvaṃti kathāṃ vaktrā samānāsanasaṃsthitaḥ ||


gurutalpasamaṃ pāpaṃ saṃprāpya narakaṃ vrajet ||


ye nindaṃti purāṇajaṃ kathāṃ vā pāpahāriṇīṃ || (!)


te vai janmaśataṃ martyā mūkās syur nātra saṃśayaḥ ||


idaṃ yaḥ śrāvayed vidvān samasta khaṇḍam eva vā ||


pādamātraṃ tadardhaṃ vā tvekaṃ vākhyānam uttamam ||


sa manasasya (!) prayatnena śaṃkaraprītaye sadā ||


tasmiṃs tuṣṭe hi saṃtuṣṭaḥ śaṃkaro nātra saṃśayaḥ || (fol. 232r1–5)



«Colophon:»


īti (!) śrīpurāṇaśravaṇānuvarṇanakathanaṃ nāma kṣepakodhyāyaḥ || 1 || svasti śrīśāke 1759


śrīsaṃvat 1894 sāla miti āṣāḍha vadi 4 roja 5 mā śrīpaśupati prite kedārakhaṇḍaśāstreḥ(!)


vāgmativiṣṇumatimadhyasthale kāṃtipure liṣitaṃ methyāṃkāṭavaṣṇyā ramākānta upādhyā vo


śubham astu ||


yādṛśī pustakaṃ dṛṣṭvā tādṛśī liṣitaṃ mayā ||


yadi śu(ddham aśuddhaṃ vā mam doṣo na dīyate ) śiva (fol. 132r5–9)
