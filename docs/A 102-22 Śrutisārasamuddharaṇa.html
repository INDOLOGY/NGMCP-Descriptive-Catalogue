Beginning

[ṭīkāṃśaḥ]

svasti śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

vilasaccitibalataḥ sphurad akhilaḥ svakatamasāparinirmitavipuladvayapihitasvakamahimāvyavahārikavapuṣā vidhi(2)vacanādikaviṣayo mama mānasanilayo harir avatāj jagad akhilaṃ || 1 ||

śrīkṛṣṇaṃ pratyagātmānaṃ bhaktyā natvā gurun api ||

ṭīkām ahaṃ kariṣyāmi toṭakaślokagāminī (!)  || (fol. 1v1–3)

[mūlāṃśaḥ]

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

trailokyānātha harim īḍayam udātasatvaṃ

śāktes tanūjatanayaṃ parameṣṭhikalpaṃ ||

jīmūtamuktavimalāṃvaracāruvarṇaṃ

vāsiṣṭham ugratapasaṃ praṇato(9)smi nityaṃ || 1 || (fol. 2v8–9)

End

[mūlāṃśaḥ]

bhūḥ pādau yasya khaṃ codaram asuravilas caṃdrassū(8)ryau ca netre

karṇāvāśāḥ śirodyaur mukham api dahano yasya vāstavyam abdhiḥ ||

aṃtasthaṃ yasya viśvaṃ suranarakhagago bhogighaṃdharvadaityaiś

citraṃ raṃramya(9)te taṃ tribhuvanavapuṣaṃ viṣṇum īḍayaṃ natosmi || 80 || 

(fol. 30r7–9)

[ṭīkāṃśaḥ]

śisnasthānaṃ samudraḥ yasyāṃtaḥ sthaṃ viśvaṃ suravarakhagago bhogigaṃdharvadaityaiś citraṃ nānāvidhaṃ viśvaṃ raṃramyate atiśayena ramate tam etādṛśaṃ tribhuvanavapuṣam īśaṃ niyaṃ(13)tāraṃ sahastraśīrṣetyādi śrutisiddhaṃ viṣṇuṃ mumukṣur ahaṃ namāmīty arthaḥ || (fol. 30r12–13)

Colophon

[mūlāṃśaḥ]

iti śrīmatbhagavatpūjyapādaśiṣya śrītoṭakācāryaviracitaṃ śrutisārasamuddharaṇaprakaraṇaṃ samāptam || ||(fol. 30r9)

[ṭīkāṃśaḥ]

|| iti śrīpūjyayogīṃdraśiṣyeṇa saccidānaṃdayoginā racitā tatvadīpikā toṭakaślokadīpikā (14) samāptāḥ || || śubham || ❁ || (fol. 30r13–14)
