Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||


atha sāmagajapyāni ||


dvairājaṃ pauruṣaṃ sūktaṃ sauparṇaṃ rudrasaṃhtām ||

śaiśavaṃ pañcanidhanaṃ gāyatraṃ jyeṣṭha sāma ca ||


vāmadevaṃ bṛhatsaumyaṃ rauravaṃ rathantaram ||

gavāṃ vrataṃ vikarṇaṃ ca rakṣoghnaṃ ca yaśas tathā ||

māṣeyuḥ sāmagā rājan paścimadvāram āśritāḥ || || (fol. 1v1–4)
