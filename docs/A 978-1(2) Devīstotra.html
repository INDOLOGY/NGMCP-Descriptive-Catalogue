Excerpts

Beginning

śrīdurggā ||

īśvara uvāca ||

stava....mahā.... sarvvasamptkaraṃ śubhaṃ ||

namo devyai mahādevyai cchinnāyai satatan namaḥ |

nābhau śubhrāravindaṃ tadupari kamalaṃ maṇḍalañcaṇḍarasmaiḥ

saṃsārasyaikasārāṃ tribhuvanajananīṃ dharmmakarmmodayāya | (fol. 1r13–1v1)

End

idaṃ stotraṃ mahāpuṇyaṃ brahmaṇā bhāṣitaṃ purā ||

sarvvasiddhipradaṃ sākṣān mahāpātakanāśanaṃ ||

yaḥ paṭhet prātar utthāya devyāḥ sannidhigopi vā |

tasya siddhir bhaved āśu vāñchitārthapradāyinī ||

dhanam putran tathā jāyā hayaṃ hastīnam eva ca | ||

masundharām mahāvidyām aṣṭau siddhir bhaved dhruvaṃ || (fol. 1v10–12)

Colophon

iti brahmaṇā bhāṣitaṃ devīstotraṃ samāptaṃ || || (fol. 1v12)
