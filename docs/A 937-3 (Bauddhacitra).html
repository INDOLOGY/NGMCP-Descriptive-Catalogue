Manuscript Features

1. This manuscript includes two types of texts;

i. Written text (exps. 3–5) ii. Sketches with tālamāna (6–15)

2. Written text includes devatālalakṣaṇa (exps. 3–4) and random notes in Newari by artists, who sketched deities and others. (exps. 5) There are dates of sketch from NS 755 to 966.
