Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ sāradāyai ||

nāgo bhāti madena kaṃ jalaruhaiḥ pūrṇṇendunā śarvvarī,

śīlena pramadā javena turago nityotsavair mandiraṃ |

vāṇī vyākaraṇena haṃsamithunair nnadyaḥ sabhā paṇḍitaiḥ

satputrreṇa kulaṃ tvayā masumatī lokatrayam viṣṇunā || 1 ||

bhāti śobhate, kosau, nāgo hastī, kena madena, harṣālaṅkāreṇa, athavā mattasvabhāvena || tathā bhāti, kin tat, kaṃ jalaṃ , kaiḥ jalaruhaiḥ,

padmotpalādibhiḥ | (fol.1v1-2r2)

End

karkkoṭakasya nāgasya damayantyā nalasya ca |

ṛtuparṇṇasya rājarṣeḥ smaraṇāt kalināśanaṃ ||

vehalaḍasiṃkosa, vane sapaṃḍayethva, vehala ḍasiṃko

sa cone mateva, puṇyakṣaya juyuva || ❁ || || śubhaṃ || (fol. 30r3-30v1)

Colophon

iti kuśopadeśanītisāre lokāṣṭakas tṛtīyaḥ || (fol. 30r1) saṃvat 812 vaiśāṣavadi 4 thva kunhu śrībhūpatīndramalladena dayakā dina || || śubham astu || (fol. 30v2-3)
