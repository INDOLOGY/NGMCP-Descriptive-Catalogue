❖ namo mañjughoṣāya ||


diśi diśi mañjughoṣa yadi nāma tathāgatakoṭibhiḥ stutas tad api jaḍāśavyāpi gurubhaktivaśād abhinaumi te guṇena |


unmadarājahansa yūthādhipasevita payasi mānase sarasi ratin niveśya vicaranti caranti sārasā api ||


prakaṭitacīrakaḥ kana(ṅga)kuṇḍalamaṇḍita lalitagaṇḍakaḥ kuvalaya karṇapūra yācīti bhavān aprathitaḥ kumārakaḥ || atha ca


samantabhadra lokeśvara vajraparādibhibu(ddhai) gurur iva sevi(tā)śmi parigha asamuddhṛta cārucāmaraiḥ ||


kvajhaṇajhaṇāyamānakārāvalir atiśaya parasya kvaṇḍalaḥ syur ita (misva+ṇḍara)bhūrucira dyutiravi na ca yauvannādaḥ ||


kūcabhagavasuva(!) tribhuvanaika namaskaraṇīya mādarād paśu mayātra kṛtam ācāryakam idam iti kasya++dbhutaṃ ||


ativikṛtānanena layīkṛtaḥ bhujagasahasrapāri(toṣa)tānā ++9vat | dāsabhītikāriṇā ārya yamātu prāha abhijvalita


sadasaṃgrāha+vyomarormipratyumālakamalakalikā sarmasamyānnayasya | tasyaivaivaṃ suracitavacaḥ puṇyapīyūṣa


sekoddesa +ma++++ vajrabhṛt mūrttilakṣmī || ❁ || iti mahāpaṇḍitābhayākaraguptapādaviracitā jyotimañjarīnāma homapāyikā


samāpta || ❁ || yathā dṛṣṭaṃ tathā likṣitaṃ lekhako nāsti doṣaṇāya yadi suddhaṃ masuddhaṃ vā mama doṣā na dīyate ||


jīrṇodhā samvat 456 āṣāḍha || ( exp. 3 and 2)
