Excerpts

Beginning

|| ||

lasat pītapaṭtāmbaraṃ kṛṣṇacaṃdraṃ

mudā rādhayāliṃgitaṃ vidyuteva |

ghanaṃ saṃpraṇamyātra nārāyaṇākhyaś

camatkāraciṃtāmaṇiṃ saṃpravakṣye || 1 ||

kvaṇat kiṃki(2)ṇījālakolāhalāḍhyaṃ

lasat pītavāso vasānaṃ calaṃtaṃ |

yasodāṃgaṇe yoginām apyagamyaṃ

bhaje haṃ mukuṃdaṃ ghanaśyāmavarṇaṃ || 2 ||

caturlakṣajyotir mahābo(3)dham uccaiḥ

pramathyaiva vidvajjanānaṃdahetoḥ |

paraṃ yuktiramyaṃ tu saṃkṣiptaśabdaṃ

bhujaṃgaprayātaiḥ prabaṃdhaṃ karomi || 3 ||(fol. 1v1–3)

End

subhāgyaḥ suvidyādhiko darśanīyaḥ

sugātraḥ sutejā suvastro pi tasya |

dane (!) pīḍyate saṃtatir nubhagaś ca

śikhī lābhagaḥ sarvalābhaṃ karo(2)ti || 11 ||

śikhī śuṣyago (!) vastiguhyaṃghrinetre (!)

rujā pīḍanaṃ mātulān naiva śarma |

sadā rājatulyaṃ naraṃ sad vyayaṃ tad

ripūṇāṃ vināśo raṇe sau karo(3)ti || 112 || (fol. 6v1–3)

Colophon

| iti śrīrājarṣibhaṭṭaviracite camatkāraciṃtāmaṇau grahabhāvaphalādhyāyaḥ samāptaḥ |

dvijakulatilakaḥ śrīcakrapāṇir guṇajño

narapatinṛpapṛthvīpūrvanārāyaṇākhyaḥ |

nivasati navakoṭe tasya pūjyo rasādri

nṛpamitaśakakāle cālikhad ddīśavṛtaṃ  | 1 | |

śalivāhanaśako bhūśunyāṣṭahīnaḥ śakau naipālīya iti |

| rāmorpaṇam (!) astu | | karakṛtam aparādhaṃ kṣaṃtum arhaṃti saṃtaḥ || (fol. 6v3–5)
