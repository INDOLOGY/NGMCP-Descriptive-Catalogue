Manuscript Features

MS is written in different hands; from the exp. 92 handwriting changes in another.


second chapter is available in descending order.


Sixth chapter is not foliated is not in order.


Text is available up to the eighth of the sixth chapter.


On the exp. 318 is written:


svasti śrīkamaladevaharirāmajagannāthapaṃta āge tina bhāiko sāji pustaka kākājyū


nīladevapaṃḍīta sīta baṃbeko chāpā śrīmadbhāgavat li diyuṃ sākṣī devībhaktapaṃta


iti samvat 1922 sāla ti māgha śudi 3 roja 6 śubhm


Text is divided into the 6 aṃśas (chapters)and the foliation given accordingly chapter wise.


Foliation on last six folios are discontinued . Two folios are missing in the middle of the text.


Excerpts

Beginning

«Beginning of the root text:»


oṃ namo bhagavate vāsudevāya


jitaṃ te puṃḍarīkākṣa namaste viśvabhāvana |

namaste stu hṛṣīkeśa mahāpuruṣapūrvaja 1 (fol. 1v7)


«Beginning of the commentary:»


|| śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||


śrīvindumādhavaṃ [vaṃ]de paramānandavigrahaṃ

vācaṃ viśveśvaraṃ gaṃgāṃ parāśaramukhān munīn 1


śrīmaccitsukhayogimukhyaracitavyākhyāṃ nirīkṣa sphuṭaṃ

tanmārgeṇa subodhasaṃgrahavatīm ātmaprakāśabhidhāṃ śrīmadviṣṇupurāṇasāravivṛtiṃ karttā yatiḥ śrīdhara-

svāmī madgurupādapadmamadhupaḥ sādhusvā(!)dhīśuddhaye 2 (fol. 1v1–3)


«End of the root text:»


iti vividham ajasya yasya rūpaṃ

prakṛtiparātmamayaṃ sanātanasya

pradiśatu bhagavān aśeṣapuṃsāṃ

harir apajanmajarādikāṃ sasiddhiṃ 60 (fol. 28r7–8)


«End of the commentary:»


prakṛtiḥ vyaktāvyaktasvarūpā paraḥ īśvaraḥ ātmā puruṣaḥ tanmayaṃ apagatajanmajarādilakṣaṇāiddhiprādeśatv ity āśirvādaḥ 60 (fol. 28r11–12)


«Colophon of the root text:»


iti śrīviṣṇupurāṇe ṣaṣṭeṃśe aṣṭamo dhyāyaḥ 8 samāptāmedaṃ(!) purāṇaṃ saṃvat 1873 mītī(!) ❁❁❁❁ (fol. 28r8–9)


«Colophon of the commentary:»


iti ṣaṣṭeṃśe ʼṣṭamo dhyāyaḥ 8 śubhaṃ (fol. 28v12)
