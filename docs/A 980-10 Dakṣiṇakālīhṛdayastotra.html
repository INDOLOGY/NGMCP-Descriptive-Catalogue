Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

namo dakṣiṇakālikāyai ||

śrīmahākāla uvāca ||

mahākautuhalaṃ (!) stotraṃ hṛdayākhyaṃ mahottamaṃ ||

śṛṇu priye mahāgopyaṃ dakṣiṇāyāḥ sugopitam || 1 ||

avācyam api vakṣyāmi tava prītyā prakāśitam ||

anyebhyaḥ kuru gopyaṃ ca satyaṃ satyaṃ ca śailaje || 2 || (fol. 1v1–2)

End

paṭhet stotra (!) kuleśaṃni (!) jīvanmuktaḥ sa ucyate ||

rajasvalābhagaṃ dṛṣṭvā paṭhed ekāgramānasaḥ || 39 ||

labhate paramaṃ sthānaṃ devī loke varānane ||

mahāduḥkhe mahāroge mahāśaṃkaṭake (!) dine || 40 ||

mahābhaye mahāghore paṭhet stotraṃ mahottamaṃ ||

satyaṃ satyaṃ punaḥ satyaṃ gopayan mātṛjāravat || || (fol. 2v9–11)

Colophon

iti śrīkālīkulārṇave dakṣiṇakālyā hṛdayastotraṃ || (fol. 2v11)
