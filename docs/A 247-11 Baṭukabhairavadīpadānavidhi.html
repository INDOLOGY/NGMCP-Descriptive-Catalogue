śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||


śrīvaṭukabhairavadīpadānavidhiḥ ||


ekāṃte caturasraṃ maṇḍalaṃ viracya gomayenopalipyāṣṭadikṣu yajñabṛkṣīyāṣṭakīlān


pūrvādivyastakrameṇa huṃ astrāya phaḍ iti manunā kṛtvā teṣu nenaiva krameṇa raktacandanādibhir


upacāraiḥ || karavīrakusumaiḥ || hrīṃ jayaṃtāya namaḥ || evaṃ aghorāyaḥ || cāmīkarāya namaḥ ||


asitāṃgāya nama || bhīṣaṇāya namaḥ || prapaṃḍāya namaḥ || kapilāyeti saṃpūjya etaireva manubhiḥ


|| (fol. 1v1–2r4)


«End: »


… dakṣeṇa jalaṃ gṛhītvā deśakālau saṃkīrtya amukabādhānivṛtyarthaṃ vaṭukāya baliṃ samarpayāmi


namaḥ || iti baliṃ datvosyāyamūlaṃ || 80 || japtā(!) samarpya merupṛṭheti stotraṃ paṭhitvāṃte


puṣpāṃjaliṃ datvā tadagre kṣetrapālaṃ bhairavarūpaṃ dhyātvā palapramāṇamṃ ekaṃ piṇḍaṃ datvā


ehyehi viduṣi kuru bhaṃjaya 2 narttaya 2 bighnaya 2 mahābhairavakṣetrapāla baliṃgṛhṇa 2 svāhā


(fol. 7r2–7v4)


«Colophon:»


iti kṣetrapālabaliṃ datvā dīpādevaṃ svahṛdayediti vaṭukabalividhiḥ || (fol. 7v4–5)


iti bhairavataṃtre vaṭukadīpavidhiḥ samāptam || śubham || saṃvat 1890 || … (fol. 4v1–2)
