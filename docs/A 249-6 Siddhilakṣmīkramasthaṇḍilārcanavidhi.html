❖ oṁ namaḥ śrīgurave namaḥ ||

tataḥ śrīsiddhilakṣmīkrama thaṇḍilārccanaṃ || (fol. 1v1) || ||

marjjātātheṃ vasape ||

thaṇḍili hṅavane, hnasakana tayāva, chāyā(2) sanāna yāya || || tritatvenācamanaṃ ||

khṅreṁ hrāṃ ātmatatvāya svāhā || (3)

khṅreṃ hrīṁ vidyātatvāya svāhā ||

khṅreṁ hrūṁ śivatatvāya svāhā || ||

thaṃṇḍi(4)lisa guru namaskāra ||

aiṃ 5 akhaṇḍamaṇḍalākāraṃ vyāptaṃ yena carā(5)caraṃ |

tatpadaṃ darśitaṃ yana, tasmai śrīguruve namaḥ ||

iti chāyāsnāna vidhi sa(fol. 6v11)maptaḥ || ||

iti mālinīdaṇḍakastrotraṃ samāptaṃ || || (fol. 11r9)

iti vaṭuka kalaśapūjā vidhiḥ || || (fol. 20r1)

iti paścima deguri sa(fol. 30v2)māpta || ||

iti jayatumbeśvarī deguli samāptaṃ || || (fol. 33v4)

iti śikhā(fol. 37r3)mahākāla deguli samāptaḥ || ||

iti ugracaṇḍā ugracaṇḍi degu(fol. 41v9)ri samāptaḥ || ||

iti śrītripurasundarī deguli samāptaḥ ||(fol. 139v12)

yaja(fol. 168r2)mānasya mānavagotra śrīśrījayabhūpatīndra malladeva varmmaṇaḥ śrīśrī(3)śrīsveṣṭadevatā prītyarthaṃ pāṣāṇanirmmita prāsāda pratiṣṭhā tadupari(4)

suvarṇṇakalaśadhvajāvarohaṇa koṭyāhuti siddhāgni yajña adhivāsa(5)na karmmaṇi thaṇḍilārccana nimityārthena yajamānasya sagaṇasparivā(6)rāṇāṃ āyurārogyamaiśvaryya janadhanalakṣmī santatisantāna vṛddhir astu(7)

yathā śāstrokta phalavaladāyano bhavantu || || (8)
