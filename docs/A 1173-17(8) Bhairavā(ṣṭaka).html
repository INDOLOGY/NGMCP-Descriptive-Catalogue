Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrībhairavāya namaḥ ||

jaṃ jaṃ jaṃ jakṣarūpaṃ daśadigidiśitaṃ bhūmikamyāyamānaṃ

saṃ saṃ saṃhālamūrtti śaśimukuṭajātrā saikhara candrabimvaṃ

draṃ draṃ draṃ didyakāyaṃ vikṛtanakhamukhaṃ dṛriraktaṃ karāraṃ

paṃ paṃ paṃ pāpanāśaṃ praṇamaḥ ta śatataṃ bhairavaṃ kṣatrapālaṃ || 1 ||(!)

(exp. 18b:2-5)

End

haṃ haṃ haṃ haṃṣarūpaṃ haṃsaṃ kahakahahā mukti ghoghoghorāṭṭahā 1 saṃ jaṃjaṃjaṃ jakṣarūpaṃ kalina jatimukhaṃ khargakha⟪‥⟫khargāṅgadhāraṃ raṃraṃraṃ raṅganagaṃ prahasitabahanaṃ (!) pigakesaṃ kalālaṃ ḥ saṃ saṃ saṃ svargganāthaṃ || pranamaḥ taº || 8 || bhairavāṣṭakamidaṃ puṇyaṃ yaḥ pathecchādvayānvita śivamavāpnoti, sarvvaśatrunivāranaṃ || (!)(exp. 19b7:20a3)
