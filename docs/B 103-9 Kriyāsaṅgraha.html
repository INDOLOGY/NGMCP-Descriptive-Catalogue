Manuscript Features

001. Kriyāsaṅgraha (fols. 1v–210v and 211r–212v)

002. Vajravārāhīsādhana (fol. 210v)

Fols. 211r–212v contain a table of contents (patrāṅka) of the Kriyāsaṅgraha.

On the front cover-leaf of the manuscript is written kriyāsaṅgraha (kuladattapañjikā) and vajravārāhītaṃtra.

On the last folio (212v) of the manuscript there is a drawing of a man who is sitting and counting a mālā.

On the right-hand margin of fols. 1v, 64v, 159v and 209v, and on the middle of 210r there are stamps of Bhīma Śamśera dated 1986.

There are two exposures of fols. 206v–207r.

On the middle of fol. 212r is written ❖ samvat 764 śu di.
