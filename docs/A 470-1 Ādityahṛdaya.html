Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

śrīsūryāya namaḥ ||

tato yuddhapariśrāṃtaṃ samare ciṃtayāsthitaṃ
rāvaṇaṃ cāgrato dṛṣṭvā yuddhāya samupasthitam | 1 |

daivataiś ca samāgamya draṣṭum abhyāgato raṇam
upagamyābravīd rāmam agastyo bhagavāṃs tadā | 2 |

rāma rāma ma[[hābā]]ho śṛṇu guhyaṃ sanātanaṃ |
yena sarvān arīn vatsa samare vijayiṣyasi | 3 |

ādityahṛdayaṃ puṇyaṃ sarvaśatruvināśanaṃ
jayāvahaṃ ja⟪ya⟫[[pe]]n nityam akṣayaṃ paramaṃ śivaṃ | 4 |

sarvamaṃgalamāṃgalyaṃ sarvapāpapraṇāśanam
ciṃtāśokapraśamanam āyurvarddhanam uttamaṃ | 5 |

raśmimaṃtaṃ samudyaṃtaṃ devāsuranamaskṛtaṃ
pūjayasva vivasvaṃtaṃ bhāskaraṃ bhuvaneśvaram | 6 | (fol. 1v1–7)

End

etac chrutvā mahātejā naṣṭaśoko [ʼ]bhavat tadā ||
dhārayāmāsa suprīto rāghavaḥ prayatātmavān | 28 |

ādityahṛdayaṃ japtvā triḥ paraṃ harṣam āptavān
trir ācamya śucir bhūtvā dhanur ādāya vīryavān | 29 |

rāvaṇaṃ prekṣya hṛṣṭāt sā yuddhāya samupāgamat
sarvayatnena mahatā vadhe tasya dhṛto [ʼ]bhavat | 30 |

atha ravir avadan nirīkṣya rāmaṃ
muditamanāḥ paramaṃ prahṛṣyamāṇaḥ |
niśicarapatisaṃkṣayaṃ viditvā
suragaṇamadhyagato vacas tvareti 31 (exp. 3b, ll. 3–8)

Colophon

ity ārṣe vālmīkīye rāmāyaṇe yuddhakāṃḍe ādityahṛdayaṃ nāma saptaśatatamaḥ sargaḥ | (exp. 3b, l. 8 up to right margin )
