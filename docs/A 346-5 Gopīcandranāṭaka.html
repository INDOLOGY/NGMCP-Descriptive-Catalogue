Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ nṛtyanāthāya || ||

udyaccandrārddhacuḍo viṣadhara valayo dvīpicarmmottarīyo
bhūtapretādisaṃgo (2) vṛṣabhatanugato gaurakarppūrakāyaḥ |
paṃcāsyo bhālanetro ḍamaru japadharo maṅgalo jīvanātho
hāḍāruṃ(2)ḍāṅgadābho navarasakuśalaḥ pātuvo nṛtya(3)nāthaḥ || ||

atha gopīcandanāṭakaṃ likhyate || || (fol. 1v1–3)

Extracts

gopīcandra rājāyā bhāsā ||

ahe udanā padumā amāra (4) vacana suno ||

udanā padumāyā – ahe mahārājeśvara ājñā karo || || rā , śloka ||

lakṣālakṣa vipakṣaya karaḥ śrīkṛṣṇa sevāparo
bhūyāmbhoruha phullamevadina kṛcchrī rājavṛndeśva(5)raḥ |
yāvad vaṅgavibhūṣaśo vahuguṇo gāmbhīryya sauryyākaro,
gopīcandra mahīndra sundara tanurvvarvva(6)smi sarvvopari ||

etādṛśa vaṃgadeśera adhipati gopīcandra rājā āmī āche || || (fol. 2r3–6)

End

bhāsa ||

ahe udanā padumā māharājādhi(3)rāja śrīśrīsiddhinarasiṃhamalladeva śarīra kuśala haiyā caitanya jñāna saṃyukta īsvara ke sevā ka(4)riyā sarvvadā jaya ho ||

hameloka nṛtya bhāvate īsvara ke sevā kailo samasta aparādha guṇi (5) kṣamā karo || || || paṃcamāna || lu 5 || (fol. 76r2–5)

Colophon

iti śrigopīcandranāmanāṭaka tṛtīyāṅkaḥ sa(6)māptaḥ || ||

karānala bhe[ra?]ṅkayutesu māse,
śucau’site visvatithau kujesmin |
lilekha vaṃgeśva(7)ra nāṭakaṃ taṃ,
kaṣṭena govinda dvijaśca[tū]rṇṇaṃ || ||

saṃ 832 jye, kṛ, 10, aṃ || saṃpūrṇṇam alikhat śu[bhaṃ] (fol. 76r5–7)
