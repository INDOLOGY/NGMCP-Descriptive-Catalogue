Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

atha taṃtrākhyānakathā likhyate ||

taṃtrākhyānakathā śreṣṭhā buddhpadma pravodhinī ||

ādityadīdhiter eva tāni vakṣyāmi yatnataḥ || 1 ||

yo taṃtrākhyāna kathā bhanyāko kasto ho bhanyā saṃpūrṇa kathāmā śreṣṭha bhayāko || jasto ādityakā kiraṇale savai prakāśa garāuṃchan || tastai buddhiprakāśa garchaṃ ||

rakṣitavyaṃ sadā vākyaṃ vākyād bhavati nāśanam ||

haṃsābhyāṃ nīyamānasya kūrmasya patanaṃ yathā || 1 || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

tasartha aghi prīti nabhayākāsaṃga prītigaryā nāśa garcha ||

tasartha taṃ sita maile prīti garnuchaina bhani musāle birālā cheu bhanyo ra dulābhitra pasyo || 46 ||

rātrau svasti divā svasti svasti madhyaṃ dine tathā ||

svasti bhūyād bhave nityaṃ svasti sarvatra sarvathā ||

taṃtrākhyāna kathāṃ śrutvā smṛtvā kāryaṃ karoti yaḥ ||

sa kadācin na cāpadbhir bādhyate buddhimān naraḥ || || (fol. 32r8–32v3)

Colophon

|| iti taṃtrākhyāna kathā saṃpūrṇam || || śrīrāmāya rāmacaṃdrāya namaḥ (fol. 32v3)
