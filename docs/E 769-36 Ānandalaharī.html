śrīparadevatāyai namaḥ || ||

oṃ namaḥ || śrīparadevatāye namaḥ || ||

catubhuje caturkalāvataṃśe kucconnate (3) kurkkuma rāmaśome ||

pūrṇekṣupāśāṅkuśaśca vāmona hasta samaste jagadeka(4)mātā || 1 || (5)

sāmartha dava thathīṅahma parameśvarīyāke śevā mayāseṃ meva deva bhālapāva juyī(6)va julo thvahmāyā kuśala juya madataṃ || thuli yākenāna || 1 || (7)

he mātā he jananī he jagadambe chalapola ādhāracakraśa bijyāka velasa || thvagulī śamayasa (22r1) kālasa devadevīgaṇa muṅāva || thva thāyasa vayāva parameśvarī śivaśakti svarupa kāyāva || (2) arddhanārīśvara dharalapava nṛtya yātaṃ || thvapani ṇehmāsyana chahmāṃ juyāva ānandana bijyā (3)

navātmānaṃ vande navarasamuha (4) taṇḍavanaṭaṃ ||

taṃ || he jajña janaka thva jagatsaṃsāra samastaṃ chalapola || jagatamātā dhakaṃ chalapolayā śarī(7)ra dubitaṃ || navātmā dharalapāva coṅa devadevī dakosyana stuti yātaṃ || 42 ||
