Manuscript Features

In the colophon, the place of copying has been mentioned as navākoṭagrāme triśūlagaṃgāyāḥ paścimabhāge …. This place is the modern Nuwakot District, western part from the river Trishuli.

There are two exposures of fols. 1v–2r.

Excerpts

Beginning

|| śrīgaṇapataye namaḥ ||     ||

śrīsarasvatyai namaḥ ||     ||

naumi lakṣmiṃ(!) mahādeviṃ(!) tathā tātapadāṃbujaṃ ||

nirvighnam astu te nātra dharmmasaṃcayane(!) namaḥ || 1 ||

vaṃde gopālacaraṇau cānaṃtau ca tathāparau ||

vaimalyaṃ kāritaṃ yais tu manaśo mama śāstrataḥ || 2 ||

praṇamya śrīnṛsiṃhākhyasaccidānaṃdam avyayaṃ ||

krī(!)yate dhamatattvaṃ ca sāram uddhṛtya sarvataḥ || 3 || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

rātrau jāgaraṇaṃ kāṛyyaṃ janmadānaṃ yathā baliḥ ||

puruṣāḥ śastrahastāś ca nṛtyā(!)gītaiś ca yoṣita iti ||

pārāśarapuraṃ cāsti †pāranera† iti smṛtaṃ ||

tatrasthena ca kūrmmeṇa racitaṃ dharmmabaṃdhanaṃ ||    ||    ||     || (fol. 43v4–44r2)
