Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śivāya ||

siddhiḥ sādhye satām astu prasādāt tasya dhūrjjaṭeḥ |
jāhnavīphenalekheva yanmūrddhni śaśinaḥ kalā ||
śruto hitopadeśo 'yaṃ pāṭavaṃ saṃskṛtoktiṣu |
vācāṃ sarvvatra vaicitryaṃ nītividyāṃ dadāti ca ||
ajarāmaravat prājño vidyām arthañ ca cintayet |
gṛhīta 〇 iva keśeṣu mṛtyunā dharmam ācaret ||
sarvvadarvyeṣu vidyaiva darvyam āhur anuttamaṃ |
ahāryatvād anarghatvād akṣyatvāc ca sarvvadā ||
saṃgamayati vidyaiva nīcagāpi naraṃ sarit |
samudram iva du[r]darśan nṛpam bhāgyam ataḥ paraṃ ||
vidyā śastraṃ ca śā[s]trañ ca dve vidyai(!) pratipattaye |
ādyā hāsyāya vṛddhatve dvitīyādriyate sadā ||
yan nave bhājane lagnaḥ 〇 saṃskāro nānyathā bhavet |
kathācchalena bālānāṃ nītis tad iha kathyate ||
mitralābhaḥ suhṛdbhedo vigrahaḥ saṃdhir eva ca |
pañcatantrāt tathānyasmād graṃthād ākṛṣya likhyate || ○ ||

asti bhāgīrathītīre pāṭhaliputranāmadheyan nagaraṃ ||
tatra ca sarvvasvāmiguṇopetaḥ sudarśano nāma narapatir āsīt ||
(fol. 1v1–2r1)
