Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

mayarasatajamanalagasaṃmitaṃ bhramati vāṅmayaṃ jagati yasya |
sa jayati piṃgalanāgaḥ śivaprasādād viśuddhama(2)tiḥ ||

triguruṃ viddhi makāraṃ ladhvādisamanvitaṃ yakārākhayaṃ |
laghumadhyamaṃ ca rephaṃ sakāram aṃte gurunibaddhaṃ ||

ladhvaṃtyaṃ hi takāraṃ jakā(3)ram ubhayor laghuṃ vijānīyāt |
ādiguruṃ ca bhakāraṃ nakāram iha, paiṃgale trilaghuṃ,
dīrghaṃ saṃyohaparaṃ tathā, plutaṃ (hyaṃtaṃ nāṃtam) ūṣmāṃ
sānu(4)svāraṃ [[ca]] guruṃ kvacid avasāne pi ladhvaṃtyaṃ || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

(gaṃdhāṇam udāharatti jahākasāvalaṃ te ku(13)pācala iyuṇa vijam āvalate mahiṃ cala ibhu aṇabha akaraṇā 18 mahi avalaṃ te mahi ha ha ha taha asara aṇā 17 caghuvaha calateñcalā vai badhuna hati hu aṇāḥ) (fol. 13r12–13)
