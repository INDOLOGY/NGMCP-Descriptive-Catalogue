Beginning

-krāṃtā śakuṃtalā sahetarabhiḥ |

rājā pūrvaschānam upetya saniśvāsaṃ aho vignavatyaḥ prārthitārthasiddhayaḥ | mayā hi

muhur (2) aṇgulisaṃvṛtādharoṣṭhaṃ pratiṣedhākṣraviklavābhirāmaṃ |

mukhamaṃsavivartti pakṣmalākṣyā (!) katham apy unnamitaṃ na cuṃbitaṃ (3) tat || 33 || (fol. 19r1–3)

End

rājā, bhagavān (!) ya(6)thāśakti śreyase prayatiṣye |

mārīcaḥ | vatsa kiṃ te bhūyaḥ priyam upaharāmi |

rājā saharṣaṃ praṇaman | ataḥ param api (7) bhagavān me prasādaṃ karttum arhati | yataḥ |

pravarttatāṃ prakṛtihitāya pāthiviḥ sarasvatī śrutimahatī mahīyatāṃ |

mamāpi ca kṣa(8)payatu nīlalohitaḥ punarbhavaṃ parigatabhaktir ātmabhūḥ || 2 || iti niṣkrāṃtāḥ sarve saptamoṃkaḥ || || (fol. 46r5–8)

Colophon

samāptaṃ cedaṃ mahā(9)kaviviracitam abhijñānaśākuṃtalaṃ nāma nāṭakaṃ || || likhitam idaṃ kāśyāṃ śukadevābhidhenopādhyāyopanā(10)mnā || || saṃbat 1787 || || śrī bhavānīviśveśvarābhyāṃ namaḥ || || śrīrāmacaṃdro vijayate || || śubhaṃ bhūyāt || || (fol. 46r8–10)
