Manuscript Features

The manuscript contains marginal glosses and comments, probably made by the first hand. The word division is indicated by small strokes above the akṣaras.

Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīharāya ||

jitaḥ sucaritocchedī smaro 'yaṃ duḥsaho mayā |
itīva deham akarod abhinnaṃ yaḥ saho mayā ||
bibhartti hāram anaghaṃ yo bhasmabhir ahiṃsitaṃ |
tejaś ca manmathaśarair indriyārthair ahiṃsitaṃ ||
yasyāhur atigambhīra jaladapratimaṅ galaṃ |
sa vaḥ karotu niḥśaṅkam udayaṃ prati maṅgalaṃ ||
yā trilokanatā mūrttir upāyena jugopa tāṃ |
yo vahat kaṃsanidhanaprayuktām ṛjugopatāṃ ||
babhāra vapurambhojaprabhayā pūtanābhi yaḥ |
papāta karmmaṇā yasya prabhayā pūtanā bhiyaḥ || (fol. 1v1-6)


End

ity uktvā sa nivavṛte nigṛhya roṣaṃ
sambhedaṃ nayavihitasya<ref name="ftn1">in the margin: nītinā kathitasya</ref> ca kramasya |
sadvāhāḥ katham api te rathaṃ taṃ uhūr(!)
gambhīrakṣatajaniruddhacakram asya |

ravir api śikharaṃ rathāṅgānāmnām<ref name="ftn2">in the margin: cakravākānāṃ</ref>
apahatamānasamastaparvvatasya |
drutagatir abhajat śramaṃ vimoktuṃ
dhanur iva khinnasamastaparvva tasya ||

pathiṣu ca kuravo jalāśayānāṃ saha kusumaprabhayāraviṃdinān te |
bhayapihitadṛśaḥ praṇaṣṭadikkā dadṛśur astamitaṃ raviṃ dinānte ||

iti bhavatu daśā tavāhitānāṃ
sakalamahījayabhūta vāhitānāṃ |
vasa sukham udayād ahīyamānaḥ<ref name="ftn3">in the margin: ahir iva ācaratīti ahīyamānaḥ </ref>
sakaladharādharaṇād ahīyamānaḥ || || (fol. 19r2-19v2)
