Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namo siddhanāgārjuṇāya (!) namaḥ || ||

yaḥ śānta paramānvayaḥ paraśivaḥ kaṃkālakolātakoḥ

dhyānātītam anādinityanicaya saṃkalpasaṃkocakaḥ ||

ābhāsāntarabhāsakaḥ samarasaḥ sarvātmajābodhakaṃḥ (!)

soyaṃ sarvamayo dadotu (!) jagatāṃ vidi (!) siddhyaṣṭakaṃ || ||

yā nityā kulakeliśobhitavapuḥ pūrvvoditā jṛṃbhateḥ (!)

pūrṇṇābhāmṛtakuṇḍalī paraparā mantrātmikā siddhidāḥ (!)

mālā pusṭaka (!) dhāriṇī triṇayaṇā (!) kundedu (!) varṇṇācalā

nityānandakule prakāśajananī vāgdevamāśreya (!) || || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

guṇaikāmaiśvarairudrairaṃkulītaila miśitaṃ (!)

triloha veṣṭitaṃ vaktre (!) dhāritaṃ puṣyabhāṣkarai

adṛśyo jāyate satyaṃ devir (!) api na dṛśyete ||

digveda gubhikāmaiś ca hyaṃgulītaila lepitam

puṣpa (!) bhāṣkara jogena (!) trilohe ca tu veṣṭaya (!)

mūrddhasthaiṣe ca ratvaṃ ca yojanānāṃ śatāvadhi

ete sarva mahāyogāś caṃdramaṃtreṇa siddhiti (!)

mani (!) maṃtrauṣadhaṃ jñātvā ʼsyaka gurupade gata (!) || || sāṃpratāyāt prayogāgnyā jñātavyāḥ tata (!) kvacit siddhasya siddho bhavat || || (fol. 156r1–4)

iti śrīsiddhanāgārjunaviracite kakṣapuṭe alpāhāra sāṃgvapāṃga (!) nāma pañcaviṃsati (!) paṭalaṃ || samāt (!) (fol. 156r7)
