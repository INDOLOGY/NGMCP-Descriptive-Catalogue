Manuscript Features

Most of the folios are tore on right-hand margin.

An unspecified text, appearing after the colophon, reads:

sarvāsāṃ nakṣatreṣṭīnāṃ havyavāhaṃ sviṣṭam agne ete(!) eva saṃyājye || sarvāsāṃ nakṣatreṣṭīnām anvāhāryeṇa saha gaur dakṣiṇā | nakṣatretyupakta(!)divasaprabhṛti samāptiparyaṃtaṃ patnīyajamānau vratacāriṇau bhavataḥ || adhaḥ śāyinau brahmacāriṇau syātāṃ | yajamāno havir ucchiṣṭaṃ bhuṃjīta | haviṣyaṃ patnī | yadyobhāv api haviṣyam evāśnīyātau || iti pradīpe || || 6 || vatsarāṃtiprakāśe(!) nu.. .. .. tu pavitreṣṭitapor bhavet gopālaḥ || nakṣatrasatre vṛṭṭānām ṛtvijām.anyayajamānasyārtvijaniṣiddhaṃ(!) | evaṃevaṃ cātur māsyādi sarvatra karma


Excerpts

«Beginning»


|| śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

atha nakṣatreṣṭayaḥ ||

tāś ca puroḍāśabhedenādinimitte āyuṣkara.. .. karttavyā iti naimittikavidhiviṣayatvalābhāya prāyaścittaprakaraṇe baudhāyane noktā api kāmyā api bhavaṃti || aṃtenaiva nakṣatrasatreṇa dyutirānaṃ? (fol. 1v1-2)


«End»

tato ʼnena nakṣatrasatreṇa yajñapuruṣaḥ priyatām iti īśvarārpaṇaṃ kuryāt | vaiśvānarīṣṭiḥ | tataḥ sati prakṛty atikrame.. .. kṛtīm iṣṭiṃ tat sthāne pūrṇāhutiṃ vā hutvātikrāṃtadarśapūrṇamāsau kuryāt | śrutisūtrānusāreṇa nakṣatreṣṭinirūpaṇaṃ | akāroktam anaṃtena vakāris(!) tena tuṣyatu || || (fol. 10r12-10v1)


«Colophon»

iti śrīmadārpadevasūnunānaṃtadevena kṛto nakṣatrasatraprayogaḥ samāptaḥ || || saṃvat 1744 || || 6 || śrīrāmāya || (fol. 10v1)
