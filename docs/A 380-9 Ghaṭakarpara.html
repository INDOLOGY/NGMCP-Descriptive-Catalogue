Excerpts

Beginning

śrī gaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

nicittaṃ kham upetya nīradaiḥ

priyahīnāhṛdayāvanīradaiḥ ||

salilair nihitaṃ rajaḥ kṣitau

ravicaṃdrāv api nopalakṣitau || 1 ||

haṃsā nadanmeghabhayān dravaṃti

niśāmukhāny adya na candravaṃti |

na cāṃbumattāḥ śikhino nadaṃti

meghāgame kuṃdasamānavaṃti || 2 || (fol. 1r1–3)


End

bhāvānurāgavanitāsarataiḥ śapeyam

ā(labh)ya cāṃbuṭṛṣitaḥ karakośapeyaṃ |

jīyema(!) yena kavinā yamakaiḥ pareṇa

tasmai vaheyam udakaṃ ghaṭakharpareṇa || 22 || (fol. 3r4‒6)


Colophon

iti śrima⟪ ‥‥‥‥⟫racitaṃghaṭakharparākhyaṃ kāvyaṃ saṃpūrṇaṃ || cha || || śrī || śrī || śrīmahāgaṇamati(!)sarasvatyārpaṇam astu || || (fol. 3r6‒7)
