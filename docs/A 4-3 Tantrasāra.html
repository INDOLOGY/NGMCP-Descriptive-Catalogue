Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

natvā kṛṣṇapadadvaṃdvaṃ brahmādisuravaṃditaṃ

guruṃ ca jñānadātāraṃ kṛṣṇānandena dhīmatā

tattadgranthagatād vākyān nānārthaṃ pratipadya ca

saukaryyārthaṃ ca saṃkṣepāt taṃtrasāraḥ pratanyate |

ucyate tatra prathamaṃ lakṣaṇaṃ guruśiṣyayoḥ |

śānto dāntaḥ kulīnaś ca vinītaḥ śuddhaveśavān etc. (fol. 1v)

Sub-colophons

iti śrīmahāmahopādhyāyaśrīkṛṣṇānandavāgīśabhaṭṭācāryyaviracite taṃtrasāre prathamaḥ parichedaḥ (!) || (fol. 37v)

iti śrīvidyāprakaraṇaṃ samāptaṃ || (fol. 159r)

iti śrīmahāmahopādhyāyaśrīkṛṣṇānandavidyāvāgīśabhaṭṭācāryyaviracite tṛtīyaḥ paricchedaḥ || (fol. 233r)

[[iti taṃtrasāre - tīyaparicchedaḥ |]] (fol. 251v)

End

śrīkṛṣṇānandavāgīśabhaṭṭācāryīyasaṃgrahaṃ |

dṛṣṭvānadhītaśāstrāṇi dhīrān adhyāpaya(!) sāṃprataṃ |

kāvyaśāstrapurāṇādi sāmānyagaṇikā iva |

iyaṃ hi śāṃbhavī vidyā gopyā kulavadhūr iva ||    ||

iti mahāmahopādhyāyaśrīkṛṣṇānandavidyāvāgīśabhaṭṭācāryaviracite taṃtrasāre caturthapaṭalaḥ samāptaḥ || (fol. 354v)

Colophon

Samvat 1827 kārtika badi 14 roja 4 śubham ||    ||

pustakaṃ taṃtrasārasya ṣaṇmudrāṃ putrahetave |

maulyaṃ datvā brāhmaṇāya gṛhītaṃ dīrghadarśinā |

devadattadvijendreṇa vedabāṇāgnipatrakaṃ ||    || (fol. 354v)
