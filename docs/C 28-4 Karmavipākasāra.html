Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

prāsūto māptasūryaṃ tanayam iha pitā rāmakṛṣṇaś ca soyaṃ

prāyaścitteṣu sāraṃ dinakaravibudhaḥ śāṃtikānāṃ ca sāraṃ

kṛtvātho karmapākeṣ vakhilabudhamude sāram etaṃ tanoti

śrīrāmāṃghriprabhāvād ayam api bhajatāṃ khyātim uccair dharāyaṃ (!) 2

graṃthe karmavipākīye ye mayārthā nirūpitāḥ

teṣām ādau suvovāpa kriyatenukramaḥ sphuṭaḥ

prārabdhanāśo dānādeḥ prāyaś cittaiḥ samuccayaḥ

rogāṇāṃ tāratamyena dvādaśābdaṃ tad ardhakaṃ

tadardhaṃ vā tadardhaṃ vā prāyaścittam ihoditaṃ (fol. 1v1–5)

End

ārdrā puṣkariṇīṃ puṣṭiṃ suvarṇāṃ homanālinīṃ

sūryāṃ hiraṇmayīṃ lakṣmīṃ jātavedo mamāvaha

tāṃ makṣāvahajātavedo (!) lakṣmīm anayagāminīṃ yasyāṃ hiraṇyaṃ prabhūtaṃ gāvo dāsyośvan videyaṃ puruṣān ahaṃ athāsyaiva sakrasya phalaśrutiḥ yaḥ śuciḥ prayato bhūtvā juhūyād ājyam anvahaṃ śriyaḥ paṃcadaśaṃ vacaḥ śrīkāmaḥ savataṃ japet padmāsane padmarūpe padmākṣī padmasaṃbhave taṃ (fol. 141r4–6)

«Sub-colophons:»

iti śrīmadrāmeśvarasūrisūnuśrīmannārāyaṇasutaśrīmadrāmakṛṣṇasutadinakarakṛte karmavipākasāre rogaharaprakaraṇaṃ (fols. 109r7–9)
