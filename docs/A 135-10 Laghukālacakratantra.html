Excerpts

«Beginning»

oṁ oṁ namaḥ śrīkālacakrāya ||

sarvajñaṃ jñānakāyaṃ dinakaravapuṣaṃ padmapatrāyatākṣaṃ

buddhaṃ siṃhāsanasthaṃ suravanamitaṃ mastakena praṇamya |

pṛcched rājāsu candraḥ karakamalapuṭaṃ sthāpayitvottamāṅgaṃ

yogaṃ śrīkālacakra[ṃ] kaliyugasamaye muktihetor narāṇāṃ || 1 ||


śūnyaṃ jñānañ ca bindūvarakuliśadharaṃ buddhadevāsurāś ca

vacye dehe ʼpare ca prakṛtiṣu puruṣaṃ pañcaviṃśātmakaṃ ca |

dehe viśvasya mānaṃ tribhuvanaracanāṃ bhuktidevāsurāṇām

etad vyākhyā hi samyak tridaśanaraguror maṇḍalaṃ cābhiṣekaṃ || 2 || (fol. 1r1-3)


«End»

satvānāṃ mokṣahetoj(!) jinapatigaditaṃ deśitaṃ janma yā ca

tantraṃ śrīkālacakraṃ laghutalam(!) akhilaṃ vajrasatvādhidaivaṃ |

prajñāpāryekayogaṃ(!) jinapatikuliśaiḥ ṣoḍaṣākāratatvaṃ

satvāḥ puṇyena tenākṣaraparamasukhaṃ yāntu tasmai namo ʼstu || 261 || (fol. 113r2-4)


«Colophon»

iti dvādaśasahasrādihbuddhoddhṛte śrīlaghukālacakrajñānapaṭalaḥ samāptaḥ || 50 ||

ye dharmā hetuprabhavā

hetu[s] teṣān tathāgato hy avadat

teṣāṃ ca yo nirodha

evaṃ vādī mahāśramaṇaḥ || ||

śreyo ʼstu || saṃvat 809 āṣāḍhaśuklapañcamyāṃ tithau bhaumadine vidūyati devācāryena(!) lekhayet(!) sampūrṇa[ṃ] karomi ||

etat puṇyānubhāvena mama saparivārāṇāṃ kalyāṇam astu || || śubham astu sarvajagatāṃ || (fol. 113r3-6)
