trailokayā adhipati julaṃ buddhatvaṃ na(3)maskāra yānāo nānāśāstraṃṇa pikāsyaṃ tayā, rājanītiyā kha jīna hlā(4)ya || 1 ||

adhītaivam idaṃ śāstraṃ narā jñāsyaṃti tatvataḥ || dharmma upadeśa vina(5)yaṃ kāryyākāryya śubhāśubhaṃ ||

niścayanaṃ manuṣyana thva sāstra dharalapio, ta(6)tvajñāna silanāo, dharmma adharmma siyu, upadeśavaṃta juyu, jñāni juyuo || (fol. 1v1-6)
