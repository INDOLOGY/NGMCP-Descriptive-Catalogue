-danādodayaṃ

vandehaṃ sakalaṃ kalaṃkarahitaṃ sthāṇor mukhaṃ paścimaṃ || 1 ||

gauraṃ kuṃkumapaṃkilaṃ sutilakaṃ vyāpāṃḍugaṃḍasthalaṃ

bhrūvikṣepakaṭākṣavikṣaṇalasat saṃsaktaraktotpalaṃ ||

snigdhaṃ bimvaphalādharaṃ prahasitaṃ nīlālakālaṃkṛtaṃ

vande yā juṣavedanādajanitaṃ vaktraṃ harasyottaram || || 2 ||

saṃvarttāgnitaḍitprataptakanakaiḥ saṃsparddhitejāruṇaṃ

gaṃbhīradhvanisāmaghoṣajanitaṃ tāmrādharaṃ suṃdaraṃ

arddhendudyutilolapiṅgalajaṭābhāraprabaddhoragaṃ

vande siddhasurāsurendranamitaṃ pūrvaṃ mukhaṃ śūlinaḥ || 3 ||

kālābhrabhramarāṃjanadyutinibhaṃ vyāvarttapiṃgekṣaṇaṃ

karṇābhūṣaṇabhogimastakamaṇiprodbhinnadraṃṣṭṛāṃkuraṃ ||

sarpaprotakapālasūktisakalavyākīrṇamāśekharaṃ

vande dakṣiṇamīśvarasya vadanaṃ cātharvanādodayam || 4 ||

vyaktāvyaktanirūpitaṃ ca paramaṃ ṣaṭtriṃśatattvādhikaṃ

tasmāduttaratattvamakṣaram iti dhyeyaṃ sadā yogibhiḥ ||

oṃkārādisamastamaṃtrajanitaṃ sūkṣmātisūkṣmaṃ paraṃ

vande paṃcamamīśvarasya vadanaṃ khaṃvyāpītejomayam || 5 ||

etāni paṃcavadanāni maheśvarasya

ye kīrttayanti manujāḥ satataṃ prabhāte ||

gacchanti te śivapuraṃ ruciraiḥr vimānaiḥ

krīḍaṃti śaṃkaragaṇaiḥ satataṃ śivāgre || 6 ||

hara hara bhavabhīmabhargaśaṃbho

trinayanamaheśaviśvaśaṃbho ||

hara hara duritaṃ triśūlapāṇe

śiva śiva śaṃkara kiṃkaras tavāhaṃ || 7 ||

iti śrīpaśupatipaṃcavaktrastotraṃ śubhaṃ ||
