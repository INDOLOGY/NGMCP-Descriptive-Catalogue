Excerpts

Beginning

śrīmadanagopālāya namaḥ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

śrīsarasvatyai namaḥ oṃ namaḥ śivāya

(2)yasya prabhāvād bhuvanaṃ śāśvate pathi tiṣṭhati

devaḥ sa jayati śrīmān daṇḍadhāro mahī(3)patiḥ 1

vaṃśe viśālavaṃsyānām ṛṣīnām iva bhūbhujām

apratigrāhakāṇāṃ yo ba(4)bhūva bhuvi viśrutaḥ 2

jāvedā ivārciṣmān vedān vedavidāṃvaraḥ

yo ʼdhītavān (5)sucaturaś caturopy ekakarmakṛt 3

yasyābhicāravajreṇa vajrajvalanatejasaḥ

papātā(6)mūlataḥ śrīmān suparvānandaparvataḥ 4 (fol. 1v1–6)

End

durjayān kariṇaḥ siṃhavasāsiktair mahāgajaiḥ

prahanyāt (!) kariṇīnāṃ (4)vā samūhaiḥ sādhvadhiṣṭhitaiḥ 63

salohajālair dṛḍhabaddhadaṃtaiḥ

sukalpitair ūrjitapādarakṣaiḥ

(5)pravīrayodhair madadurnivārair

hanyād gajeṃdrer (!) dviṣatām anīkam 64

eko pi vāraṇapati (!) (6) diṣatām anīkaṃ

vyaktaṃ nihaṃti madasatvaguṇopapannaḥ

nāgeṣu hi kṣitibhujāṃ vijayo ni(124v1)baddhas

tasmād gajādhikabalo nṛpatiḥ sadā syāt 65 (fol. 124r3–124v1)

Colophon

iti kāmaṃdakīye (2)nītisāre rājopadeśe gajāśvarathapattikarmāṇi pattyaśvarathagajabhūdānakalpaprakā(3)śayuddhayuddhaṃ ca viṃśatitamaḥ sarggaḥ 20 śubham bhūyāt ❁ (fol. 124v1–3)
