Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||     ||

nārāyaṇaṃ namaskṛtya narañ caiva narottamam ||

devī(!) sarasvatīñ caiva tato jayam udīrayet ||     ||

namas tasmai varāhāya līlayoddharate mahīm ||

khuramadhyagato yasya meruḥ khaṇakhaṇāyate || 1 ||

daṃṣṭrāgreṇo ʼddhṛtā gaur udadhiparivṛtā parvatair nimnagābhiḥ

sākaṃ mṛtpiṇḍavat prāg vṛhad uruvapuṣānantarūpeṇa yena ||

so ʼyaṃ kaṃsāsurārir muranarakadaśāsyāntakṛt sarvasaṃsthaḥ ||

kṛṣṇo viṣṇuḥ sureśo nudatu mama ripūn ādidevo varāhaḥ || 2 || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

śubhāśubhasya kathanaṃ śubhakarmmaphalodayam ||

lobhanaṃ puruṣasyāpi nimer ākhyānam adbhutam || 47 ||

prabodhini(!) kathā divyād gokarṇeśa samudbhavām(!) ||

nandinī varadāne ca jaleśaśailayos tathā || 48 ||

śṛṅgeśvarasya mahimā evaṃ vṛttāntasaṅgrahaḥ ||

etac chrutvāpnuyān marttyo varāhaśravaṇāt phalam || 49 || (fol. 289v12–290r1)

Colophon

ity anukramaṇikā nāma aṣṭādaśādhikadviśatatamo ʼdhyāya(!) ||     ||

samāptaṃ varāhapurāṇa(!) ||    || śubhm(!) || (fol. 290r1–2)
