«Beginning»


oṁ namaḥ śrīmahāgaṇapataye || ||

yasyājñayā jagat sraṣṭā viriṃciḥ pālako hariḥ |

saṃharttā kālarudrākhyo namas tasmai pinākine || 1 ||

tīrthānām uttamaṃ tīrthaṃ kṣetrāṇāṃ kṣetram uttamaṃ |

munīnām āśrayo nityaṃ naimiṣāraṇyam uttamaṃ || 2 ||

śaunakādyā mahātmānaḥ śivabhaktā mahaujasaḥ |

dīrghasatraṃ prakurvantas tatreśānasya tuṣṭaye || 3 ||

tasmin satre mahābhogo munīnāṃ bhāgyagauravāt |

ājagāma munīṃ draṣṭuṃ sūtaḥ paurāṇikottamaḥ || 4 || (fol. 1v1–3)


«End»


dadyāt svarṇaṃ yathāśakti yācakāya ca candanam |

yady ekasīramātrāpi dattvā bhūmiṃ śivārthine | 79 ||

sa tārayati dātur hi pūrvajān sakalān api ||

śrutvā grantham imaṃ samyak dadyād dānāni śaktitaḥ |

tāny akṣayaphalāny āhur munayo vedavādinaḥ || || (fol. 119v1–3)
