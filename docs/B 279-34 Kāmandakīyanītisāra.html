Beginning

oṃ namo gurave namaḥ ||

yasya prasādād bhuvanaṃ śāśvate pathi tiṣṭhati |

devaḥ sa jayati śrīmān daṇḍadhāro mahīpatiḥ ||

vaṃśe viśālavaṃśyānāṃ ṛṣīṇā(2)m iva bhūyasāṃ |

apratigrāhakāṇāṃ yo babhūva bhuvi viśrutaḥ || 2 ||

End

tatra prayogaḥ |
