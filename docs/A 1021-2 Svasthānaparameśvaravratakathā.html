Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīgaṇeśāya || || śrīgurave naḥ (!) ||

oṃ nama (!) śivāya || oṃ namaḥ śrīsvasthānyai ||

gurugaṇapatidurggā adduktaṃti (!) vamacyutaṃ (!)

tvajjñānaṃgini lakṣmiṃ vā nivaṃ devi |(!)

bhūtaṃ devi bhūtayanaṃ namaskṛtyaṃ, naracaio (!) narottamam,

devī sarasvatiś | caiva tato jayam udinayat (!) || ||

prathamasaṃ śrī 3 svasthānaparameśvarayakaṃ hlāya, nhasakanasa || oṃ kāla cosyaṃ sthāpanāyāya || (fol. 1v1–6)

End

thva saṃsāra hamāna samudra thva samudra taya māmyayāta śrī 3 thāni (!) parameśvariyā japa dhyāna totra pātha vāhikana …juva, thvatenaṃ samasta vartayātpinaṃ thva guli dharmma vrata ... kaṃ śrī 3 nārdamuniśvaranaṃ ājñādayakā kha || || thvate śrī 3 svasthāniparameśvariyā dharmmavratakathā julaḥ || ○ ||

(fol. 190v2–6)

Colophon

iti śrīliṃgapurāṇe śrī 3 svasthānimahātme (!) dharmavratakathā samāpataḥ (!) || || śrī3svasthāniparameśvarivrata .. sudo aśudho vā mama doṣo nadīyateḥ śrīḥ ||

(fol. 190v6–8)
