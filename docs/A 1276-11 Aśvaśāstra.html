Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrībhavānīśaṅkarābhyāṃ ||

vātayā śubhaphalasalaṃ yā, hmaṃsaco yāta tina, thanāpi kāsyaṃ tayā ||

uttaroṣṭha, thaṃthū hmathūsi || kṛkva (ṅve), sarvvakāmaviva ||

lalāṭadhāya, hmosata, the 2, 3, 4 dhanya theṅaduna 3 conasā,

niśreṇī nāma kāmanāphalaviva || (fol.1v1-3)

End

karnaṇeti thvagukiprakāraṇa cāmatho lasalaṃyāke prayoga yāta ṅāva,

salaṃ cātholayiva, salaṃcātholake karmasa, thva mahāprayoga atyantabhiṅa ||

iti garbhadhāraṇopāyaḥ || || (fol.87v1-3)

Colophon

iti nānāgrancha(!)samuccayam aśvaśāstraṃ samāptaṃ || ||

nepālavatsaragate, vasuvedāśvasaṃyute

śrāvaṇe ʼsitapakṣe ca, daśamyāṃ guruvāsare ||

daivajñaviśvasiṃhena, likhyate hayaśāstrakaṃ || ||

bhavānī prīṇātu || || śubham astu || ||

(fol.87v3-5)
