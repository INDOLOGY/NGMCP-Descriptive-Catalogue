Manuscript Features

The text starts from the beginning and ends in the nāmino raḥ sūtra of the Visargasandhi chapter.

On fol. 1r is written

❖ yo gatir yogayuktasya vārāṇasyāṃ mṛtasya ca |

sā gatiḥ snānamātreṇa vāgmatyāṃ tu nivāsane ||

❖ anātkāle (!) kṛtā pūjā balātkāreṇa maithunaṃ |

daṃbhārthe pūjayet śaṃbhūṃ rauravaṃ narakaṃ vrajet ||

Four parallel lines run around and mark out the four margins, the text being confined to the space within.

Two sets of four short parallel lines are drawn to the left and right of the binding hole. Short notes are written in the margins.
