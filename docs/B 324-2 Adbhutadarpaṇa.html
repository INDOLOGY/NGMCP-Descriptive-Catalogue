Beginning

oṃ namo mahāgaṇeśāya ||

saṃjñāne ‥ ‥ ‥ ‥ ‥ ‥ tu †kinānī | vīṃ† kareṇa ślathāṃ

dhṛtvā bhugnitavāmabāhulatayā cāvekṣya va(2)kṣasthalīṃ ||

kiṃcit kuñcitamadhyamañjulataraṃ paścād vrajaṃtyāḥ śanair

ddor ddolāyitacakṣu(ṣombadhibhuvaḥ) kākūktayaḥ pāntu (3) naḥ ||

kanyādātari saṃprayoktari tayor anyonyavīkṣāvidhe (!)

namre devapurodhasāmukhavidhau devyāḥ samu(jjī)vate ||

antaḥska(4)ndalita (!) spṛhan diviṣadā saty arṇasīty ākulaṃ

premnollāsavita (!) punantu śivayor | vrīḍāvalīḍādṛḍhāḥ || 2 || (fol. 1v1–4)

…

sūnunā (7) raghunāthasya budhabālakulādbhutaḥ ||

śrīkṛṣṇārādhanavyagram atināthānujanmanā ||

mahāmahopadād yasya go(8)vindasyāgrajanmanā ||

kriyate mādhavenāsau saṃkṣiptādbhunadarppaṇaḥ (!) || (fol. 1v6–8)

karmmaṇāṃ (7) hāniḥ pīḍā manasi mānase ||

mūrttidraviṇavastūnāṃ hāni (!) sāṃghātike hate ||

vaināsike vināśaḥ syāt putradravisapadāṃ (!) || ||

parāśaraḥ ||

eteṣv anupatapteṣu (9) mānavā nopatapyati (!) ||

viṣṇudharmottare tu || pīḍite /// (fol. 70v6–9)
