Excerpts

Beginning

❖ nṛtyeśvarāya namaḥ ||

pāṇau brahmakapālam ujjvalatanuṃ kaṇṭhe murārer vapuḥ

sarvvāṃgeṣu tuṣārahāradhavalā devāsthimālādharaṃ |

viṣṇur bbījam iva prasāditabhujo bhūyo pi saṃbhūtaye

pāyāduḥ (!) pralayāvasānasamaye kāpālikaḥ śaṃkaraḥ || 1 ||

krodhādhvastamanobhavāndhakaripu vāmāṅgaśaktiḥ prabhuḥ

sṛṣṭiṃ rakṣakasaṃharaṃ jagad idaṃ helā vasā nirmmitaṃ |

dhyānāśaktanimīlalocanavibhur yogānvitaṃ siddhidaṃ

kāleśo kulanāyako vijayate devo mahābhairavaḥ || 2 || (exp. 23t1–6)

End

hemābhāsāddhyajihvā triguṇitavilasad vāmahastāṃgulīkā

tadvakṣāṃtarnnighāṭasṛtiruṣitalasad rakṣahastā bhavāniṃ |

dhyātavyā sādhakendrair jjvalad analalasat keśayālolasantī (!)

vārttālīnām adheyā sakalajanamanovṛtti saṃstaṃbhayanti ||

hrīṃ namo vārāhi ghore svapnaṃ vada svāhā || ||

mālāyate |

trīllokām (!) iva rikṣituṃ vahati yo visphuryya dakṣotrayaṃ (!)

sastrai (!) gunchaparicchado vijayate devas triśūlāyudhaḥ || 8 || (exps. 25b4–26t2)

Colophon

iti śrībhairavāṣṭakaṃ samāptaṃ || || (exp. 26t2–3)
