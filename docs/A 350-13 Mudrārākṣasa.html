Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīgaṇeśāye (!) namaḥ ||    ||

kalyānaṃ kalpavṛkṣaḥ paricaraṇaparasyāpibhikṣuḥ kathaṃ syād,
yogī vā tat kim aketanum tanuguṇām eṣa rāgād vibhartti |
kaṇṭhe hālāhalaṃ ce kim amṛtakaraṇaṃ veti tarkkaṃ janānāṃ,
śṛṇvasmerānana śrīr giripatitanayā vallabho vo vidhattāṃ || (fol. 1v1–2)

End

tathā hi tat sādhanaṃ sahetuḥ siddhaye sādhyasya pakṣavṛttitvena jñānārtham iti upāyat jñānāyeti yāvat bhavati saṃpadyate tat kīdṛśaṃ | śādhya niścitam iti || parv-vato yaṃ vahnimān dhūmavatvāt || yo yo dhūmavān sa sa vahnimān yathā mahān saṃ (!) vyāpya ||    || (fol. 71v3–5)
