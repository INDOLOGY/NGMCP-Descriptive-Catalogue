Excerpts

«Beginning of Caṇḍamahāroṣaṇa»

oṃ nanaḥ śrīvajrasatvāya || oṃ namo(!) śrīcaṇḍāmahāroṣaṇāya || ||

evaṃ mayā śrutam ekasmin samaya(!) bhagavān vajradhāteśvarī †bhujdhavihāraḥ† anekaiś ca vajrayogigogiṇigaṇai(!) ||

tatra bhagavān sarvvam ālayarājaya nāmasamādhi(!) †samāpadya damūdājahāraḥ† || tad yathā || oṃ phāṃ phrīṃ phroṃ 

caṇḍarūpa caṭ 2 pracata 2 kaha 2 praspharaya 2 grasa 2 (fol.1v1–5)


«Beginning of Āryataarastotra:»


oṃ namas tārāyaḥ ||

namaḥ -s- tāreturevīre janavyuti nibhakṣaṇe ||

trailokyanāthavaktākṣe vikasatke śarodbhave || 1 ||

namaḥ śatasaraccaṃdra saṃpūrṇṇapaṭalānane ||

tārāsahasranikare prahasatkiraṇojvare || 2 || (fol.1v1–4)


«End of Caṇḍamahāroṣaṇa »


oṃ rāgavajrīya svāhā oṃ iṣyāvajrīya svāhā imāṃ dhāreani yo nityaṃ dhārayiṣyanti ||


paryavāpsyati pravartayīṣyanti svaparebhyaś ca vistarena saṃkāsayanti tasya sarva bhayopadravo


prasaṃgdhe śtrūn, sarvavyādhiroga nāśayanti || || (fol.4r3–4v1)


«End of Āryataarastotra:»


viṣaṃ tsya mahāghoraṃ sthāvaraṃ vāthajaṃgamaṃ ||

smaraṇāt pralayaṃ yānti †khadirapītanaṃva† vā || 25 ||

grahajvalaviṣantānāṃ paramārttivināśanī ||

aneṣāṃ caiva satvānāṃ dvitrisaptābhivarttināṃ || 26 ||

putrakāmo labhet putraṃ dhanakāmo bhaverd(!) dhanaṃ(!) ||

sarvakāmān avāpnoti na viṣai(!) pratihanyate || 27 || (fol.5r2–5v1)


«Colophon of Caṇḍamahāroṣaṇa »


ity akallavirāṣpaśrīcaṇḍamahāroṣaṇataṃtramantrapaṭalanāmadhāraṇī parisamāpta || ||


jadharmāhetuprabhavā hetus teṣā tathāgata ||


«Colophon of Āryataarastotra:»


iti śrīsamyakttaṃ buddhabhāṣitaṃ bhagavaty āryatārābhaṭṭārikāyā


namaskāraikaviṃśatitotraṃ samāptaḥ(!) || || yedharmmātyādi || ❁ || ❁


oṃ namo(!) śrīvajrasatvāyaḥ(!) || oṃ nama(!) śrī oṃ nama(!) śrīvarjasa(!) (fol.5v2–4)


hyavas teṣāṃ ca joneroroccha evaṃ vādi mahāśravaṇam || ||


mālagathaṃ duṣṭagana śatrūgana vighna paracakra aṣṭamahābhayāty ādi sarvabhaya śātyarthaṃ || || śubham || ||

(fol.4v1–5)
