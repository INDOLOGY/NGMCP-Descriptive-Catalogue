Excerpts

Beginning

atha stavaṃ pravakṣyāmi brahmaṇā nirmmitaṃ purā |

taṃ śṛṇuṣva maheśāni vidhānaṃ suradurlabhaṃ ||

nābhau śubhrasarojaraktavilasanvandhūkapuṣpāruṇaṃ |

bhāsvad bhāskaramaṇḍalāntam udare yadyogacakraṃ mahaṃ (!) ||

tanmadhye viparītamaithunaratapratyudgatā kāminī ||

dṛṣṭasthāṃ tarunārkkakoṭivilasat tejasvarūpāṃ śivāṃ || (fol. 1v12–2r1)

End

niśāyām pūjayed devīṃ kumārīm parvvatātmajāṃ |

tadā bhavati mantreṣṭhā skandamā (!) sarasvatī ||

kāmādyāmbājaped vidyāṃ lakṣmyādyāmbā japet sadā ||

sarvvadā sarvvakāleṣu lakṣmyādyā sarvvasiddhidā |

santoṣyavidhinā nārī pūjayitvā kumārikāṃ ||

tadyonau prajaped vidyāṃ śatasahasraṃ dine dine ||

na doṣo vidyate tatra brahmaṇā bhāṣitam purāḥ (!) |

ācārāṣṭakarūpeṇa siddhirūpanna saṃśayaḥ ||

kimannyat tu mahādevī kathitavyam ataḥ paraṃ || || (fol. 6v, 5–7)

Colophon

iti viśvasāratantre pracaṇḍacaṇḍikādevyāḥ sahasranāmastotraṃ samāptaṃ || || (fol. 6v, 8)
