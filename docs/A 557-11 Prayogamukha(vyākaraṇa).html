Manuscript Features

There are two folios numbered 67. The second one is the continuation of the text, while the first one is in fact fol. 97, which was misnumbered and misplaced directly before the properly numbered fol. 67.

There is a stamp of the Nepal Rastriya Pustakalaya on the right margin of fol. 2r.

The first folio, containing the beginning of the text, is lost. The manuscript reaches up to the end of the Kṛtprakriyā chapter.
