||    || mahābhūtādhipeśāya śuklavarṇṇa ca śūladhṛk |
iśānādhipa(2)tīdevaṃ iśvarañ ca namostute ||    ||
śūbhrāṅgaṃ śubhradehaṃ ahikulabha(3)laṇaṃ
rudra hāḍāvibhūṣaṃ khaḍgaṃ phetakañ ca śūlābjaṃ
kaladhṛta tiśu(4)bhaṃ vindukāpālamudrā ||
haṃ haṃ haṃ mantravijaṃ pra(ṇa)tija
gaditaṃ kṣetra(5)pālaṃ namāmiḥ ||    ||
devīkoṭan tu kṣetrāṇāṃ lakṣmī ca siṃhavāhīnī |
śū(6)lābjaṃ vindumudrā ca hemaratna sulaṃkṛtaṃ ||
vatavṛkṣanivāsinyā saṃhā(7)rakṣetrapālakaṃ ||
śavarggaṃ nilayādevaṃ mahālākṣmī namostute ||    ||
na(8)mostute mīsavāśibhyo sarvvadevā hi devatā
mayā ca jajanakāmyaṃ ca de(b1)hi me kṛpayā mamaḥ ||    ||
śubham astu sarvvadā || śrī 3 bhairava ||    || (exp. 44t1–44b1)
