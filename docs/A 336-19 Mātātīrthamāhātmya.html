śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

skanda uvāca ||

bhūyo gamad virūpākṣo muninā tena vai samam ||

mīnatīrthaṃ ca⟪ta⟫ [[ya]](2)trāsīn mīnarūpaiḥ suraiḥ saha || 1 ||

caitrakṛṣṇasya ⟪paṃca⟫[[sapta]]myāṃ sasnau bhaktyā yathāvidhi ||

saṃkalpya vidhi(3)vat tīrthe kuśahasto virūpadṛk || 2 ||

tatra snātvā ⟪sama⟫ [[ca cā]]ṣṭamyāṃ mātṛtīthe ghaṭodbhava ||

agamat sa dvi(4)jaḥ siddhaḥ sasnau tatra yathāsukham || 3 ||

tato pṛchan(!) muniṃ siddho māhātmyaṃ tīrthasaṃbhavaṃ ||

ā(5)karṇya tad vacas tasmai vyājahāra vaco muniḥ || 4 || (fol. 1v1–5)
