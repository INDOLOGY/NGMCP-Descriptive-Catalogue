Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrītripurāyai || ||

kailāsaśikharāsinaṃ(!) devadevaṃ jagadguruṃ |

śaṃkaraṃ paripapraccha parvatī parameśvarī ||

pārvvaty uvāca ||

bhagavan sarvvadharmmajña sarvvaśāstrāgamādiṣu |

āpaduddhāraṇaṃ mantraṃ sarvvasiddhipradaṃ nṛṇāṃ || 2 ||

sarvveṣāṃ ścaiva tu tārāṃ hitārthaṃ vāñchitaṃ mayā ||

viśeṣatas tu rājñā vai śāntipuṣṭiprasādhanaṃ || 3 || (exps. 24b6–25t3)

End

jāpyaṃ śāntikaraṃ vatsa satvārthapratisādhakaṃ |

nāstikāya na dātavyaṃ devadrākṣara(!) nindite ||

gurubhaktyā ktāyāyādatavyaṃ(!) nānyaketyo(!) ʼdhikaṃ tathā ||

prātar ādityakīrtyan tu sarvvapāpapranāśanaṃ(!) |

goghnaś caiva kṛtaghnaś ca mucyate sarvvapātakai(!) |

trikāla(!) saikakālaṃ vā yaḥ paṭhed ravisannidhau ||

sobhīṣṭaphalam āpnoti nātra kāryyā vicāraṇā || || (exp. 29t5–8)

Colophon

iti śivokte mahāmantre sūryyāvatāre sūryaśāntiḥ samāpta(!) || (exp. 29t8–9)
