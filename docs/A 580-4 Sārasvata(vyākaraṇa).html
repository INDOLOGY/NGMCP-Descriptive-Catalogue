Language Sanskrit

Manuscript Details

Script Devanagari

Material paper

State incomplete; the available folios are 1, 10, 12, 18, 24, 32–34, 37, 39–43, 47, 49, 51, 55–59, 61, 69, 74, 79–81 and 85–87. Some folios have been eaten by insects on the sides.

Size 32.5 x 13.6 cm

Folios 32

Lines per Folio 8–12

Foliation figures in the lower right and upper left margins of the verso; the marginal title sāra is written above the folio number on the left side.

Place of Deposit NAK

Accession No. 4/1405

Manuscript Features

The manuscript starts from the beginning of the work and reaches up to the beginning of the Strīpratyayayādhikāra subchapter of the Kṛdantaprakaraṇa chapter.

Short commentaries are written on the sides of some folios. There is a stamp of Chandra Shumshere on fols. 1r, 58r and 87v. The top half of fols. 80v and 33v is left blank.
