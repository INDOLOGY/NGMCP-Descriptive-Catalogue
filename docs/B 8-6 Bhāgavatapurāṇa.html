asya purāṇasya bhedam eva nirdiśati purāṇeti vācyaṃ viṣayaḥ tadartham evānyapurāṇasaṃkhyāṃ tāvad āha brāhmam ityādinā prastutaprapañcam āha harī(!)līlākathānāṃ vrātaḥ samūhaḥ sa evāmṛtaṃ tenāninditāḥ(!) pramuditā ye teṣāṃ (śo)bhanaṃ śravaḥ śravaṇaṃ yasya tatra hetuḥ sarvveti etasya dānamāhātmyam āha , prauṣṭheti , prauṣṭhapadyāṃ bhādrapadyāṃ tat stauti , rājanta iti tatra hetuḥ sarvveti kiñ ca nimneti vibhāgakathanam asya phalam āha , śrīma(!) divyā pāramahaṃsyāṃ saṃhitāṃ nirvvarṇṇyeta jñānaviṣayam āha yatreti jñānaṃ vāsudevaḥ sarvvam iti sākṣātkāraḥ vināśo bhāveṣu nāśaktiḥ bhaktir anana(!)premalakṣaṇā tābhiḥ sahitaṃ tadupalakṣitaṃ neṣkarmmyaṃ(!) nniṣkarmma brahma ..........<ref name="ftn1">looks like svārthe taddhitaḥ (?)</ref> yad yatrāviḥkṛtaṃ tad bhāgavataṃ bhaktyā suśravaṇādi kurvvan ta(tto) vimucyeta uparato bhavet || || (fol. 124r5-124v5)
