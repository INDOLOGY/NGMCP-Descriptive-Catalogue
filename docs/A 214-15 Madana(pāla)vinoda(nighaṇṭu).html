Excerpts

Beginning

śrīmahāmaṃgalamūrttaye gaṇeśāya namaḥ ||    ||

bījaṃ śrutīnāṃ sudhanaṃ munīnāṃ
jīvaṃ jaḍānāṃ mahadādikānāṃ ||
āgneyam astraṃ bhava pātakānāṃ
kiñcit mahaḥ syāmalam āśrayāmi || 1 ||

lubdhvā kapola madhuvāri madhuvratālī
kumbhasthali madhuvibhūṣaṇa lohitāṅgī |
māṇikyamaulir iva rājati yasya maulau
vighnaṃ sa dhūnayatu vighnapatiḥ sadā vaḥ || 2 ||

yo dhvāṃta saṃtati viśāra payodhi madhyān
niḥkkāsayat ya saraṇaṃ bhuvanaṃ nimajjat
datvā jagattrayahitaḥ svakarāvalaṃvaṃ
vāṃchāṃ sa vo dinakaraḥ saphalī karotu || 3 || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

/// tukān ||
yo rājñāṃ mukhatilakaḥ kaṭāramallas tena śrīmadanṛpeṇa nirmitetra |
graṃthe bhūnmadananāmni pūrṇaḥ karṇānāmamṛtarasapradā praśāstiḥ (!) | (fol. 39r1)

Colophon

iti śrīmadanapā[la]viracite madanavinode nighaṃṭau praśastivargaś caturdaśaḥ | | samāptaś cāyaṃ nighaṃṭuḥ | | dṛṅnetracārvāha śake madhauśitau pakṣe maheśasyatithau kaver dine nārānidhisvasya vilokanārthaṃ saṃprālikhatturṇam imaṃ nighaṃṭuṃ | yathāpratilikhitaṃ sadbhiḥ saṃśodhanīyaṃ | śubhamastu satatam | | ❁ | |śivaprasādāc chubham astu |
trikaṭuka ajamodāsaiṃdhavaṃ jirake he samadhāraṇādhṛtānām aṣṭamohiṃgughāgaḥ || (fol. 39r1–3)
