Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namo ||

brahmauvāca (!) ||
aṇekamatrakoṭiśa nṛsiṃhanāmamācaret ||
anyo vidhi rakṣāṃtiḥ viṣarogādi nivāraṇa ||

oṃ asya śrīlakṣmīḥ nasiṃ (!) kavacaṃ brahmā ṛṣiraḥ raḥ nuṣṭaprachandaḥ (!) śrīnaraha (!) devatāḥ mama sasarvarogānāṃ devadoṣānāṃḥ caurapatraḥ gavyāghra viścika (!) bhutaḥ preta piśācikaḥ ḍāṃkiniḥ śāṃkiniḥ aṇekanivāranārtheḥ (!) jape vinijogaḥ (!) |

oṃ kṣauṃ aṃguṣṭhābhyaṃ nama oṃ praum tarjanibhyaṃ namaḥḥ oṃ brauṃ maṃdhemābhyāṃ (!) nama oṃ sauṃ anāmikābhyām namaḥ oṃ vrauṃ kaṇiṣṭhikābhyāṃ (!) nama oṃ jauṃ karatala 2 pṛṣṭābhyāṃ nama oṃ kṣauṃ hṛdayāya nama oṃ prauṃ silasya svāhā oṃ hrauṃ siṣāya vauṣaṭ oṃ rauṃ kavacāya huṃ oṃ brauṃ ṇetra trayāyā vauṣaṭaḥ (!) oṃ jrauṃ astāya phaṭaḥ jauṃ au hrauṃ namaḥ || atha dhyānaḥ (!) (exp. 24a1–25a1)

End

oṃ jrīṃ hrīṃ nṛsiṃhāya namaḥ oṃ nama nṛsiṃhāya kapilajaḥ jatāyaḥ (!) amoghavācāḥ satvaṃḥ suvrataṃḥ mahāugrarūpāyaḥ oṃ hrāṃ hrīṃ hrauṃ phaṭa svāhāḥ (!) || sarvarogāṇāṃ baṃdha baṃdhaḥ sarvacaurāṇāṃ baṃdha baṃdha sarvagrahānāṃ baṃdha 2 sarvadevadoṣānāṃ baṃdha 2 sarva viścikānāṃ baṃdha 2 sarva vyāgrānāṃ (!) baṃdha 2 sarvabhutapraṭa pisācikaḥ ḍāṃkiniḥ sāṃkiniḥ yatra maṃtraḥ baṃdha baṃdhaya kilaya kilayaḥ marddaya (ma)rddaḥ curṇaya curṇaya curṇayaḥ (!) yama u virodhitāyāṃḥ(!) sarvaviṣaharaṇaṃḥ dahadahaḥ mathamathaḥ pacapacaḥ gadā cakreṇa vajreṇaḥ bhasmī kurukuru svāhā oṃ hrāṃ hrīṃ hruṃ phaṭa svāhāḥ (exp. 25b3–26a6)

Colophon

iti śrī nṛsiṃhapurāṇe brahmāsāvitri saṃvāde nṛsiṃha kavacaṃ samāpat śubhmaṃ (!) || ❁ ❁ ❁ ❁ (exp. 26a6–7)
