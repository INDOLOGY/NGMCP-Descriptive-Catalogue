Manuscript Features

On the folios of 1v–18v holds the text of Vāstuśāstra. ( up to exp. 19) exp. 19b–21t holds the text of Gurubhujaṅgaprayātastotra. (up to exp. 21t) exp. 21b–26t holds the text of the Devyā daṇḍaka(stotra).(up to exp- ) exp. 26b – holds the text of tripurāsārasamuccayavyākhyā by Nāga Bhaṭṭa.

Excerpts

Beginning

❖ devyā daṇḍaka ||


jaya jaya hariharakamalāsanābhyarccite devi māte namo devi durge śive śambhuvaktrodbhave caṇḍike caṇḍarūpe

svarūpe suvaktre sunetre sugātre suveśe [[ssukeśe]] subimbodharoṣṭhī mahāyoginī vahanapiñjadhvaje

tuhinakamalakundenduvarṇābhirutyantatīkṣṇābhi draṃṣṭrābhiḥ saṃnṝṇyase bhīṣaṇī bhairavī vīrabhadre subhadre

śmaśānapriye padmapatrekṣaṇe ghorarūpe jayantī jayamānasī mānavīm etya mātre | (exp. 21b1–5)


End

atha tanuvinipātakāle vrajatvāgnikālāgnirūdraṃ śataṃ nārakaṃ viṃśaticāṣṭakoṭisametaṃ tathā bhūr bhuvaḥ svar mahar janas tapaḥ satyalokāntikaṃ brahmaṇoṇḍaṃ vinirbhidya rudrādvipañcāśat saṃkhyaṃ tathā toyaṃ tejonirākāśaguhyāṣṭamaṣṭaṣaṣṭimatikramya yatprākṛtaṃ pauruṣaṃ niyati kāraṃ samādyaṃ vidyeśvaraṃ cakravartti svaśaktiṃ kālāṃ vāpi susūkṣmaṃ tyajitvo vrajante paraṃ yatra nityaṃ padaṃ sarvabhūtādhipaṃ sarvabhūtottamaṃ sarvaniṣkalaṃ dhyānahīnaṃ viśante sadā || || (exp. 25b4–26t2)


Colophon

iti śrīdevyavatāre ghoravadhe mahādevakṛto devyā daṇḍakaṃ samāptaḥ(!) || ❁ || śubhaḥ || || (exp. 26t2–3)
