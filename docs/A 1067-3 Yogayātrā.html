Beginning

/// te māyāvatī kulapāṃśanaś cakitavo
doṣārṇṇavo niḥsukhaḥ || 7 ||

daivajñamaṃtri suhṛdāptavacāṃsi
rājā yonādriyet svam iti ceṣvita hṛṣṭabuddhiḥ |
sannāyakena rahitodha ivācireṇa
hāsyatvam eti patito viṣaye ripuṇām || 8 ||

medhāvī matimānadīnavacano dakṣakṣamā vā nṛjur (!)
ddharmmātmā jyanasūyako laghukaraḥ ṣāḍguṇyavicchaktimān ||
utsāhī pararaṃdhravitkṛtadhṛtir vṛddhikṣayasthānavi(?)
roanavya sanīsmaratyupakṛtaṃ vṛddhopasevī ca ya || 9 || (fol. 5r1–5)

End

sarāṃsyaśoṣyāṇI saritasamudrān
drenduloke śamahīdhranāgāḥ ||
diśo grahar kṣāṇi śivaḥ suparṇṇaḥ skaṃdāviśāṣo marutaś ca sādhyāḥ || 7 ||

rurddhā daśārkkā vasavośvināṃ ca svāhā śacīmā
hṛgaṇaṃ bhavānī | patyojanaṃnyaś ca suroḥ surāṇāṃ sādhyogaṇāś cā /// (fol. 34v6–8)
