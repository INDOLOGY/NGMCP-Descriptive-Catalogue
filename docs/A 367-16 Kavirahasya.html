Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

ekārthā iti yathā poṣati puṣyati puṣṇati nānārthaś caikavācakā iti yathā śliṣyati | śleṣyati | āliṃgana dahanārthau sadṛśārthābhidhānāś ceti sadṛśārthāḥ sadṛśaśabdāś ca yathā puṣyati pūṣati atra puṣapūṣaśabdau puṣṭivṛdhyarthau nānārthabhedepi visadṛśau bhinnārthāḥ (fol. 1v1–3)

śrīrāmacandracaraṇam avalamve

jayaṃti murajitpāda nakhadīdhiti dīpikāḥ

mohāṃdhakāravidhvaṃsān mukttimārgaprakāśikāḥ 1

lokeṣu śāstreṣu ca ye prasiddhāḥ

kāvyeṣu ye sat kavibhiḥ prayuktāḥ

niścitya tāṃś cittavinodanārthaṃ

śabdān ahaṃ dhātubhir uddharāmi 2 (fol. 1v4–6)

End

nayante yad guṇān sarve yas tān nayati diṅmukhaṃ

śriyāmṛḍati mṛḍnāti sarasvatyā ca saṃpadā 73

iti samāptam avāptaguṇodayaṃ

kavirahasyam idaṃ rasikapriyam

sad abhidhānanidhānahalāyudha

dvijavarasya kṛtiḥ sukṛt ātmanaḥ 74 (fol. 31r3–5)

nayante iti ṇī āpaṇe bhvādiḥ sanmānety ādinātmanepadaṃ

ubhayapaditvān nayatīti mṛḍatīti sukhane tudādiḥ mṛmṛdhmīti |

mṛdha sukhacalane kriyādiḥ 74 (fol. 31r2–6)
