Manuscript Features

Some folios are missing in the beginning and at the end. Extant folios: 23, 41, 44-52, 60-69, 71-72 and some with broken edges, placed at the beginning. The MS ends in the middle of vers No. 672, which is No. 603 in Weber's edition (1881). In the beginning there are some partly damaged additional folios with other other texts. One of them contains names of the goddess (exp. 006)
