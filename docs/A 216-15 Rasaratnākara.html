Manuscript Features

The manuscript constitutes a whole along with two others, photographed in A 214-14 and A 216-1. This manuscript consists of two khaṇḍas, each starting the numbering from 1: Rasakhaṇḍa fols. 1–25 and Rasendrakhanḍā 1–163.

Āyurvedīyayantracitra fols. 4,5,7,35,40r

Index in last 4 expos.

Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

svargāpavargadātārau sāmarasyudarodare ||

bhavarogaharo vande caḍikā (!) candraśekharau || 1 ||

prathamaṃ pīthikā nāma dvitīyaṃ(2) rasaśodhanaṃ

tṛtīyaṃ jāraṇo nāma rasamūrcchā caturthakaṃ ||

śodhano paramaṃ nāma pañcaiva puṭale sthitaṃ ||

māraṇaṃ abhrakādiś ca ṣaṣṭḥopadeśasaṃsthi(3)taṃ ||(fol.1v–3)

End

sthūlānām kurute kārṣsyaṃ kṛśānāṃ sthaulyakārakaṃ ||

anupānaṃ viśeṣais tu ta(9)tra yoge niyojayet ||

sādhyāsādhyaṃ nihaṃty āśu bhakṣaṇān nātra saṃśayaḥ || ||(fol.163v8–9)

Colophon

iti kālāgnirūdrorasaḥ || || iti pārvatīputra(10) nityanāthasiddhaviracite rasaratnākare raseṃdrakhaṃde (!) cikitsānāmāṣtaviṃśodhyāya (!) || samāptaḥ || samvat 1905 phalgu vadi 2 ro 3 śubham (fol.163v9–10)
