Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

pravahya vanavegadyaditopi mavalati najajagad aṇupiṇubhāraḥ |
laghugurutulanā tulāprakārā nu iṃṭhimaguṇaḥ prabhavat
guṇatrayasya hiraṇyagarbhasya guru śarīrasaṃkhyā(2)vapuryya puruṣapurāṇaḥ |
aṇor aṇīyān mahato mahīyān paśyatya cakṣu sa śaṇāṃtya karṇaḥ |
sakalāṃkurakārivījānugataṃ yatad (!) akāri vyājñanaṃ kurvadrupatvaṃ nāmasāmānyaṃ na kalpayitu(3)m ucitaṃ goravāt (!) (fol. 1v1–3)

End

viśiṣṭajñānavṛtter api dhūmaliṃgakānumiti kāraṇatāyā avachedakatvānamānena nāpi kulajñānadvayatvādivṛttir niruktā(13)vachedaka taiva viṣayī kriyate na tu pūrvāprasiddhaviśiṣṭajñānatvāvṛtir avachedakatāktasā eveti gauravagrahasatvād iti vibhāvyate tadā vijayatām †āṃbhākarāḥ †| mimatrakarma iti.○ prāhur iti saṃ(14)kṣepaḥ ||    || (!) (fol. 16v12–14)

iti gauravalāghavavicāraḥ samāptaḥ || śubham || (fol. 16v14)
