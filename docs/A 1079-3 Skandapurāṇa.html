Manuscript Features

Folios are missing here and there. The available folios are as follows: 20, 46–48, 50–69, 71–77, 81–83, 85–117, 119–179, 270, 272–274, 289–299 and 20 damaged folios without folio numbers. From the fourth line of 299r text is written in Devanāgarī, so the damaged folios placed at the end most fall before 289th folio.
