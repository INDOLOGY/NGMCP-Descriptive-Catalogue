Excerpts

Beginning

ghnaṃ vajrakandasudarśanāḥ |

jaṭā raudrī viṣaṃ dugdham āvikaṃ ceti leparāṭ ||


yojyo yam api saṅkā ced garbhalakṣaṇasamvide |

lakṣaṇaṃ vikṛtir vāpyā śrutiḥ ṣaḍā⟪ṇā⟫dga(!)bimbakam ||


jānīyād iti vā garbhaṃ tad vijñāya viśodhayet |

niṣpannāvayave nīte mṛdutāṃ parimārjjite ||


dhārādhautata(!) tatau(!) lakṣyaṃ lakṣmasastam athetaram |

śaṅkhasvastikamālābja⟪ja⟫hayebhavṛṣabhūdharāḥ |


mṛgādhipātapatrenduhārasiṃhāsanādayaḥ |

maṇidarpaṇakeyūradhvajadevālayadrumāḥ | (fol. 8r1–3)


End

marīcaye pulastyāya bhṛgave pulahāya ca ||

svāyaṃbhuvāya manave kratave nāradāya ca |

jagus te ʼpi svaśiṣyebhya[[ḥ]] samāsavyāsayogataḥ ||

bhaktebhyaḥ prāthayadbhyaś ca tadicchāto maharṣayaḥ |

tadekadeśam iṣṭārthasiddhibījam anākulam ||

mayā tadicchayaivoktaṃ bhavadbhyaḥ śivam astu namaḥ || ❁ || (fol. 56v1–3)

Colophon

iti mayasaṅgrahe pratiṣṭhāsūtraṃ samāptam || || || || mayasūtra || || (fol. 56v3)
