Manuscript Features

The first foliation runs up to nearly the end of the Dṛṣṭāntālaṃkāra chapter. The second foliation runs from the middle of the Virodhābhāsa chapter to nearly the end of the Arthāntarānyāsaprakaraṇa chapter.

There is no folio number on fol. 49.
