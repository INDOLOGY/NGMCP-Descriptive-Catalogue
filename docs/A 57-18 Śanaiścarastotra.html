Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namo mahābhairavāya ||

guhyastavaṃ pravakṣāmi(!) mahābhairavanirggataṃ |
bhāṣitaṃ devadevena bhairaveṇa mahātmanā ||
gurupūrvvakrameṇaiva sādhakānā(!) hitāya vai |
bhuktimuktipradadivyadevāsuranamaskṛtaṃ ||
siddhidaṃ sukhadaṃ caiva vilomaghnaṃ malāpahaṃ |
yoginīhṛdayānandavīraiṣā(!)marddakai(!) nutam ||
vāmadakṣiṇasāmānyaṃ pavitrakalmaśāpahaṃ |
namitasiddhasaṃghais tu guhyā guhyataraṃ paraṃ ||
vallabhaṃ sarvvadevānā(!) sarvvakṣetrābhisammataṃ |
tatra bhuktigatānān tu sādhakānāṃ hitāya vai ||
ye ca maṇḍalavāriṇyaḥ parabhukticarās tu ye |
yoginīgaṇavīrāṇāṃ catuṣpīṭhāntavāsināṃ ||
ataḥ śrutvā vidhisthānaṃ mataṃ vai kaulikaṃ sthitaṃ |
pañcavaktravibhāgena parāparavibhāgaśaḥ (fol. 1v1-2r2)

End

jayate paṭhamānasya traisthālaṃ vimalam subhaṃ |
maṇḍalīkanarendrānāṃ priyo bhavati nityasaḥ
divyastrīṇā(!) viśeṣeṇa samaye nevā(!)bhibādhyate |
vibhūtiṃ vardhanaṃ tasya duṣṭai(!) naivābhibhūyate ||
draṃṣṭrino(!) vyālavetālaiḥ rākṣaśair bhūtanāyakaiḥ |
vighnair nānāvidhair jitvā saubhāgyam atulaṃ labhet ||
yaśaḥkīrttibalan tejo varddhate satataṃ bhuvi |
mahocchuṣmamate khyāto yoginīvijayastavaḥ ||
pippalādena muninā pṛthivyām avatāritaṃ
bhairavena purākhyātaṃ devyā yātṛvidhātmanā ||
na deyaṃ tarkadagdhānāṃ śaṭhānān tu viseṣataḥ
gurudevāgnibhaktānān dātavyam iti niścayaḥ || ❁ || (fol. 32r1-32v2)

Colophon

iti vidyāpīṭhe brahmayāmale mantrottarasaṃgraho yoginīvijayastavaḥ samāptaḥ || ❁ || śreyo stu (fol. 32v2-3)
