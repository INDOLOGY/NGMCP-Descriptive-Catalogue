Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgurave namaḥ

śrīgajavadanāya namaḥ ||

śrīsarasvati(!)bhyo namaḥ ||

śrīparadevatāyai namaḥ ||

oṁ pṛthvi merupṛṣṭe ṛṣiḥ kūrmo devatā sūtalaṃ chaṃdaḥ āsane viniyogaḥ ||

vṛthvi tvayā dhṛtā loke(!) devī tvaṃ viṣṇunā dhṛtā ||

tvaṃ ca dhāraya māṃ devi pavitraṃ kuru cāsanaṃ || (fol. 1r1–7)


End

devatānāṃ ca devānāṃ brahmādyair yāś ca pūjitā ||

bhūyāt sā varadā loke sādhūnāṃ sarvamaṃgalā || 49 || (fol. 41r4–6)
