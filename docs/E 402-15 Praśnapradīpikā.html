Beginning

|| atha praśnapradipikā (!) ||

❖ jāmitre ca yadā saurīr lagne vā hivukethavā ||

navame dvādaśe caiva bhaumadoṣo na vidyate || 1

kendre koṇe śubhāḍhyaś ca triṣaḍāyepy asaṅgrahāḥ ||

tadā bhaumasya doṣo na madane madanas tadā ||

❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ❖ ||

śrīsāradāyai (!) namaḥ || ❖ ||

timīrāvradhithau magnaṃ karairudrapratyayojayet |

prīṇāyetyāturaṃ prītyā tasmai sarvāṃtamaye namaḥ || 1 || (fol. 1r–1v)

End

punar āha || rekhāpaṃcaguṇāṃ dvibāhu 22 haraye ʼtra .. daś 10 ghnaṃ gajai (!) 8 saṃkhyativī avadestu vicakṣaṇa riti devīprasānmudāḥ || 3 || (fol. 18v)

Colophon

iti praśnamāyāṃ kuṣmāṃḍavīja || ❁ ||

śrīnārāyaṇārpaṇamastu || ❁ ||

svasti śrīsamvat 1949 sālamāghasuklapakṣa (!) dvādaśi (!) tithau ravivāre likhitam samāptaṃ || ❁ || (fol. 18v)
