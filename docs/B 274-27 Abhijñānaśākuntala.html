Excerpts

Beginning

yā sraṣṭus sṛṣṭir ādyā vahati vidhihutaṃ yā havir yā ca hotrī

yad dvekālaṃ vidhattaḥ śrutiviṣayaguṇā yā sthitā vyāpya viśvaṃ ||

yām āhuḥ sarvabījaprakṛtir iti yayā prāṇinaḥ prāṇavantaḥ

pratyakṣābhiḥ prasannas tanubhir abatu vas tābhir aṣṭābhir īśaḥ ||

|| nāndynte sūtradhāraḥ ||

alam ativistareṇa || nepathyābhimukham ālokya ||

ārye yadi nepathyavidhānam avasitaṃ tad ihāgamyatāṃ ||

praviśya naṭī || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

rāja || yathāśakti śreyase yatiṣye ||

māricaḥ || kin te bhūyaḥ priyaṃ karoti ||

rājā || ataḥ paramapai priyam asti ||

tathāpi yadi prasanno bhagavā (!) tadaitad astu || ||

pravarttatāṃ prakṛtihitāya pārthivaḥ

sarasvatī śrutimahatāṃ(!) mahīyatāṃ ||

mamāpi ca kṣapayatu nīlalohitaḥ

punar bhavaṃ pratigatabhaktir ātmabhūḥ || || (fol. 86v7–87r3)

Colophon

|| iti niṣkrāntāḥ sarve saptamoṃ ʼkaḥ || ||

ityabhijñānaśākuṃtalaṃ nāṭakapūrṇam agāt || ||

audīcyo gurjjarāṇām ativimalamatiṣṭolakīyo niśaṃ yo

vedadhyānāvaśakto vibudhagaṇavaro vyāsagopāsaśarmā ||

tatputreṇātiramyaṃ likhitamalamagān nāṭakaṃ śrīśukena

pūrṇaṃ śākuntalaṃ tat pramudakaram idaṃ cāstu pustaṃ budhānāṃ || ||

rāma || || || kāśīnāthāya namaḥ || (fol. 87r3–7)
