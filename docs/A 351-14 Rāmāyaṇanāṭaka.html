Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrī 3 arddhanārīnṛtyeśvarābhyāṃ namaḥ || ||

ādau nāndīślokaḥ ||

sānandaṃ candramauliḥ kumudahimasudhāhīrakarppūragauro,

dehārddhe (2) yasya gaurī virasati (!) rucitā kāṃcanastomagarurī |

maulau cābhāti gaṃgā tribhuvanajananī nāgabhūṣābhirāmo,

gīrvvāṇastomavandyo na(3)ṭajanasukhadaḥ pātu vo nṛtyanathaḥ || ||

tatas tadanantaraṃ ṇāndīgītaṃ || || rājavijaya || jati bādhā ||

jaya suravandita mau(4)li kalānidhi vāsakare guṇagāne |

nāga kaṃka⟪na⟫dhara, dekhite sundara, kautuke jagata bakhāne || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

īsavejāni girijā śaraṇalaya, sā(3)dhava nijahita kāma ||

eka bhalosa mora śaṃkarakāmini bhajanao hi paranāma ||

viśva lakṣimisuta raṇajita narapati, kalaya (4) viditaguṇagāna, |

karu karuṇāmayi tribhuvanajanani, vinati karu avadhāna || mepu 18 || || (fol. 260v2–4)
