Beginning

oṁ namaḥ śrīgaṇeśāya ||


śrīmaddakṣiṇakālyai namaḥ || ||


kailāsaśikhare ramye nānādevagaṇāvṛte ||

nānāvṛkṣalatākīrṇe nānāpakṣiravair yute || 1 ||


caturmaṇḍalasaṃyukte śṛṃgāramaṇḍape sthitaṃ ||

samādhau saṃsthitaṃ śāntaṃ krīḍantaṃ yoginīpriyam || 2 ||


tatra maunadharaṃ dṛṣṭvā devī pṛ[c]chati śaṃkaraṃ || ||


devy uvāca ||


kīṃ tvayā japyate nārthaṃ kiṃ tvayā smaryate sadā || 3 || (1v1–5)

krīṁ svāhā prajapen maṃtram ayutaṃ nāma pāṭḥakaḥ ||

ākarṣaṇaṃ cared devi jalakhecarabhūgatāḥ || 86 ||

kālike pūrvabījāni pūrvavat prajapan paṭhet || 87 ||


urvaśīṃ cāpi vaśāyen(!) nātra kāryyā vicāraṇā ||

krīṁ dakṣiṇe kālike ceti svāhāyutaṃ japen naraḥ || 88 ||


paṭḥen nāmasahasraṃ ca trailokya[ṃ] pārayed dhruvaṃ ||

satputrāya pradātavyam vidyārājñīpravedine || 89 ||
