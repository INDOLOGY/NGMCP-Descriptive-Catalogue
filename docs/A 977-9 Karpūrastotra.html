Reel No. A 977-9 Inventory No. 119010

Title Karpūrastotra and Karpūrastavaṭīkā

Remarks Alternative titles of the root text are Karpūrastava and Dakṣīṇakālīstotra.

Author The root text is ascribed in the colophon to Mahākala; the commentary is ascribed in the colophon to Lakṣmaṇa.

Subject Stotra

Language Sanskrit

Manuscript Details

Script Devanagari

Material Nepali paper

State complete

Size 28.6 x 12.2 cm

Folios 6

Lines per Folio 14–17

Foliation figures in the right- and left-hand margins of the verso

Place of Deposit NAK

Accession No. 1/1185

Manuscript Features

In the colophon the root text is ascribed to the Bhairavatantra.

Excerpts

«Beginning of the root text:»

karpūraṃ madhyamāṃtyasvarapararahitaṃ seṃduvāmākṣiyuktaṃ

bījaṃ te mātar etas(!) tripuraharavadhu triḥkṛtaṃ ye paṭhaṃti ||

teṣāṃ gadyāni padyāni ca mukhakuharād ullasaṃty eva vācaḥ

svacchaṃdaṃ dhvāṃtadhārādhararucirucire sarvasiddhiṃ gatānāṃ || 2 || (fol. 1v8, 2r8)

«Beginning of the commentary:»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

ajādibhir abhiṣṭutaṃ hṛdikṛtaṃ mahendrādibhir namaskṛtam ahardivaṃ †vitayato† munīndrair api || trikālam aham arcita⟨ṃ⟩manujamānave sādibhir nato smi jagadaṃvikālalitapādapaṃ ruhaṃ || 1 || (fol. 1v1–2)

«End of the root text:»

kuraṃgākṣīvṛṃdaṃ tam anusarati premataralaṃ

vaśas tasya kṣoṇīpatir api kuverapratinidhiḥ ||

ripuḥ kārāgāraṃ kalayati sakṛt kelikalayā

ciraṃ jīvanmuktaḥ sa bhavati subhaktaḥ pratijanuḥ || 22 || (fol. 6v, 5–7)

«End of the commentary:»

sa janaḥ ciraṃ dīrghakālaṃ jīvanmuktaḥ kīrtimān dhanavān dharmavāṃś ca bhavatīty arthaḥ pratijanur janmani janmani śobhano bhakto bhaktaśreṣṭho bhavati || 22 || (fol. 6v8–10)

«Colophon of the root text:»

iti śrībhairavataṃtre mahākā[la]viracitaṃ karpūrastotraṃ samāptaṃ śubhaṃ || ❖ || ❖ || ❖ || ❖ || ❖ || ❖ || ❖ || ❖ || ❖ || (fol. 6v, 5–7)

«Colophon of the commentary:»

iti śrīlakṣmaṇaviracitā karpūrastavasya ṭīkā samāptā || ❖ || ❖ || ❖ || ❖ || ❖ || ❖ || ❖ || ❖ || ❖ || ❖ || ❖ || ❖ || (fol. 6v8–10)
