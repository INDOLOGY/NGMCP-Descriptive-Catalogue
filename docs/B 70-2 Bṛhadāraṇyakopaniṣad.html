Beginning

Śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

natvā viśveśvaraṃ devaṃ gaṇeśaṃ gurudaivataṃ
paropakṛtaye nūnaṃ vākyārtho [ʼ]tra nirūpyate

tatropaniṣa[c]chabdena brahmavidyaiva vācyopasamīpasthaṃ pratyagātmaikatvaṃ niniścitaṃ sahetukaṃ saṃsāraṃ sādayatīty upaniṣat (fol. 1v1–2)
