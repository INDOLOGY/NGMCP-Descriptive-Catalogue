Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrī 3 gaṇeśāya namaḥ ||
śrīgurubhyo namaḥ ||    ||

atha gṛhapraveśa valyārcca(2)navidhi llikyate ||    ||
patavāsana coyāva mālako jiyake ||    ||
yajamāna pu(3)ṣpabhājana || adyādi || vākya ||
mānavagotra yajamānasya śrī 2 jayabhūpatī(4)ndramarllavarmmaṇa,
śrī 3 svaṣṭadevatā prītyarthaṃ navagṛha praveśa śāntivalyāarccanna (5)
katuṃ puṣpabhājana samarppayāmiḥ ||    || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

kuhmarapūjā choya ||
sarvvamaṅgala māṅgalya, śive sarvvā(2)rtha sādhake |
śaraṇya trambike gaurī, nārāyaṇī namostute ||
komārīdevyai svasthāna (3) vāso bhavantu ||    ||
sākṣi thāya ||    ||
pehnuto yajana ārogyaṃ ālasaṃ thva chesaḥ ||    || (fol. 100v1–3)

Colophon

iti gṛhapraveśa śāntivalyārccanavidhi samāptaḥ ||    ||
śubhamastu sarvvadā || (fol. 100v4)
