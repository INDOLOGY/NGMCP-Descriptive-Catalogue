Excerpts

Beginning

śrī gaṇeśāya namaḥ

gaurīśravaḥ ketaka patrabhṛṃgam ākṛṣya hastena dadanmukhāgre ||
vighnaṃ muhūrttā kalita dvitīya daṃta proho haratu dvipāsyaḥ(!) || 1 ||

kriyākalāpa pratipattihetuṃ saṃkṣipta sārārtha vilāsa garbhaṃ ||
anaṃta daivajña sutaḥ sarāmo muhūrttā ciṃtāmaṇI yātanoti || 2 ||

tithīśā vanhikau gaurī gaṇeśo hir guro raviḥ ||
śivo durgāṃtako viśve hariḥ kāmaḥ śivaḥ śaśī || 3 || (fol. 1v1-4)

End

tadātmaja rudāradhīr vivudha nīlakaṃṭhānujo
gaṇeśapada paṃkajaṃ hṛdi nidhāya rāmābhidhaḥ ||

girīśanagare varabhujabhujeṣu caṃdrair mite śake
viniram ādimaṃ khalu muhūrttaciṃtāmaṇIṃ ||    || 10 || (fol. 50v6-8)

Colophon

iti śrī daivajñānaṃta suta daivajñarāma viracite muhūrtta ciṃtāmaṇI gṛhapraveśaprakaraṇaṃ samāptaṃ || samāptaśca ayaṃ muhūrttaciṃtāmaṇIḥ ||    || ❖ ||
saṃvat 1824 śake(!) 1689 bhādrakṛṣṇa 5 śukravāsare || ❖ || śrībhavānīśaṃkarābhyāṃ namaḥ (fol. 50v8-51r2)
