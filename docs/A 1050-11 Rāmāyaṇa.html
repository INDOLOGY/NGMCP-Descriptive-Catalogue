«Beginning of the commentary text:»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || atha ṛṣidarśitavartmanā rāmasya daṃḍakāraṇyapraveśam āha ||

praviśyati (!) ||

ṛṣidarśitavartmāneti śeṣaḥ || daṃḍakāraṇyaṃ tatsaṃjñakaṃ mahāraṇyaṃ (2) daṃḍakasya rājño deśaḥ | śukraśāpavaśād araṇyam abhūt || (fol. 1v1–2)

«Beginning of the root text:»

śrīgaṇaśāya (!) namaḥ ||

praviśyatu mahāraṇyaṃ daṃḍakāraṇya ātmavān ||

rāmo dadarśa durdharṣas tāpasāśramamaṃḍalaṃ | 1 |

kuśa cīra(6)parikṣiptaṃ brāhmyā lakṣmyā samāvṛtam |

yathā pradīptaṃ durdarśaṃ gagane sūryamaṃḍalaṃ || 2 || (fol. 1v5–6)

«End of the root text:»

krameṇa gatvā pravilokayan vanaṃ

dadarśa paṃpāṃ (8) śubhadarśakananāṃ (!) ||

aneka nānāvidhapakṣisaṃkulāṃ

viveśa rāmaḥ saha lakṣmenaḥ (!) | 30 | (fol. 101r7–8)

«End of the commentary text:»

upasaṃharati || (2) itīti || ananyacetanaḥ || nāsty anyatra sītāyāḥ || cetanaṃ cittaṃ yasya saḥ | nalinibhir manoramāṃ || paṃpāṃ mataṃgasarasapradeśavartinīṃ viveśaḥ || 29 || śubha(9)darśaṃ śubhadarśanaṃ | kananaṃ (!) yasyāṃ tāṃ pāṃpāṃ (!) dadarśa || sarvata iti śe(10)ṣaḥ | viveśa ca mataṃgasarasāvachedeneti (!) bodhyaṃ || 30 || (fol. 101r1–2and 9–10)
