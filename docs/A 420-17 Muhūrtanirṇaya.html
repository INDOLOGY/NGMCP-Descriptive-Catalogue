Excerpts

Beginning

///gamanaiṃ syātmaveśaś ca pratiśukravāraśūlaṃ na citayed (!) yoginīpūrva (!) garggaḥ

praveśān nirggamaṃ caiva nirggamāc ca praveśanaṃ

nava(2)mo jātuno kuryād diṣṇye vāre tithāv api

daivajñavallabhe

pramatto vyādhito bhītaḥ śrāṃtaḥ kruddho bubhukṣitaḥ

adhvānaṃ na pra(3)padyeta klībavepas tathaiva ca

ratnāvalyāṃ

svakīyāṃ parakīyāṃ vā striyaṃ puruṣam eva vā

tāḍayitvā tu yo gachet (!) tadaṃtaṃ tasya (4) jīvitaṃ (fol. 66r1–4)

End

atha śastravadanaṃ sārasāgare

kṛttikāsu viśākhāsu bhaumārkkaśanivāsa(6)re

tarddine (!) ghaṭitaṃ śastraṃ nṛpāṇāṃ jayadāyakaṃ

atha dhāraṇāṃ tatra dīpikā

mūleṃdupūrvātrayapauṣṇapitrya-

śakrāśvi(7)sarpānalaśūlinaś ca

khaḍgādisaṃdhāranam (!) eṣu kuryāt

tithau vilagne ca śubhe śubhāhā (!) (fol. 101v5–7)

Colophon

iti śrīviśvanāthamiśrasaṃgṛhī (!) muhūrttanirnnaye (!) prakīrṇakaprakaraṇaṃ paṃcakaṃ samāptaś cāyaṃ graṃthaḥ

yad atihṛdayavegāl lekhanībhrāṃtibhāvāl

likhitam akṛtabuddhyā proktaśabdāvalaṃbāt

nayanavadanajaṃghād yan mayā pustake ʼsmin

karakṛtam aparādhaṃ kṣaṃturm (!) arhaṃti saṃtaḥ

saṃvat 1849 vaiśākhaśuklapakṣa duijī ālikhitaṃ samāptaha (!) aślokasaṃkhā 3681 (fol. 101v7–102r3)
