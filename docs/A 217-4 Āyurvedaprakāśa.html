Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

rogāḥ saptavidhāḥ proktāḥ sahajāścāpacārajāḥ ||
āgaṃtavastathaivānye kālajāśca prabhāvajāḥ || 1 ||
svabhāvajā doṣajāśca lakṣāste lakṣaṇaiḥ kramāt ||
(śukrārtava) gatair doṣainayaṃte sahajāgadāḥ || 2 ||
kubjavāmanatādyāśca māturgarbhāpacārajāḥ ||
bhūtādyā veśavāyvāgne mukhyai rāgah smṛtāḥ || 3 ||
vṛṣṭI śītādibhirjātāḥ kālajāḥ parikīrtitāḥ ||
deva dvijādiśāyādyaiḥ saṃbhavaṃti prabhāvajāḥ || 4 || (fol. 1v1–5)

Ending

bhavennidrāsukhaṃ yasya smṛtibhraśo na jāyate ||
nākāra varṇahāniḥ syāt sa rogī jīvati dhruvaṃ || 28 ||
yasyādaro bhavedvaidye prītirbheṣajakarmaṇi ||
pathyerucirmahādharmo sa rogī jīvati dhruvam || 29 ||
ityādi lakṣaṇaivaidyaḥ sādhyaṃ vijñāya rogiṇaṃ ||
āyurvedokta mārgeṇa cikitsāamyagācaret || 30 || (fol…………)
