Excerpts

«Beginning of the root text:»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

śrīdey uvāca

devadeva mahādeva kṛpāṃ kṛtvā mamopariḥ

sarvasiddhikaraṃ jñānaṃ kathayasva mama prabho 1

kathaṃ brahmāṇḍam utpannaṃ kathaṃ vā parivarttate

kathaṃ vili(!)yate deva vada brahmāṇḍanirṇayam2 (fol. 1v3–5)

«Beginning of the commentary:»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

devy uvāca

devadeveti.

he devatākā pani devatā bhayākā mahādeva.ma upara kṛpā gari savai siddhi hunyā jñana kana he prabho malāi kahanu havas 1 (fol. 1v1–2)

«End of the root text:»

jīvitavyo jape lābhe kṛṣṇa(!) ca dha(!)nakarṣaśe

maṃtrārthe yuddhapraśne ca ⟪‥⟫gamanāgamane tathā 42 || (fol. 31v1–2)

«End of the commentary:»

kṛṣi gardāmā lābha cha chaina dhanaprāpti huṃcha huṃdaina bhani yeti thoka praśna gadāmā 42 āyātīti.|| ❁ || ❁ || ❁ || - (fol. 31v8–9)
