Manuscript Features

The text contains many scribal mistakes. These mistakes are not marked below in the Excerpts.

Excerpts

«Beginning of the Kudṛṣṭinirghātana»


++mo buddhāya |

vakṣe kudṛṣṭinirghātanodikarmavidhānataḥ |

anenaiva vihāreṇa yatnaina labhate padam |

iha hi dvidhā satvāḥ śaikṣyā aśaikṣāś ca | tatrāśaya adhimuktiprayogaḥ bhūmiprapannaprayo //ñca vasitāptiparyantaḥ hetvavasthāsthinānāṃ śaikṣāṇāṃ śuviśuddhādikarmāvidhānena sambhāradvayasambhṛtasamyaksambodhigampallābhaḥ | (fol. 1v:1–2)


«End of the last folio, containing the end of the Amanasikārādhāra, and beginning of the the Sahajaṣaṭka»

sukhan na sahajād anyat sukhañ cāsaṅgalakṣaṇaḥ |

jñātvānisaṃgatāma nāmni bedhāgatasatsukhaṃ

viśvaṃ sūsamayaṅ kṛtvā magna sahajasāgare

mantratvasthito yogī nissaṅgārthaḥ pratisṭhitaḥ

bhāvāṃ cāsau gurūn kṛtvā nisaṅgaviṣayām bhavet | (fol. 39v: 5–6)


«Colophon»

kṛtir iyaṃ paṇḍitāvadhūtaśrīmadadvayavajrapādānam iti || sahaja (fol. 39v:6)
