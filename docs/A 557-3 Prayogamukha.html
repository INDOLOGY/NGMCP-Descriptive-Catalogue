Manuscript Details

Script Maithili and Newari

Material paper

State incomplete; partially damaged on the sides but with no damage to the text

Size 31.0 x 7.2 cm

Folios 56

Lines per Folio 5–6

Foliation figures in the left-hand margin of the verso; on fols. 1–34 śrī is written before the folio number.

Place of Deposit NAK

Accession No. 1/1590

Manuscript Features

Available folios 1–7, 7–38, 40–56

The text in this MS starts from the beginning of the text and reaches up to nearly the end of the Kṛtpaṭala chapter.

On the right side of the first covert-leaf and on fol. 56v are impressed stamps of Chandra Shumshere.

At the top of the first cover-leaf there is a śloka, under which is written ❖ prayogamukhapusta ||

This manuscript is written in Maithili script on fols. 1v–34v; from fol. 35r to fol. 56v the manuscript is in Newari script. The text is, however, continuous. On the last line of fol. 34v there is one correction done in Newari script.

The 39th folio is lost, but the folio number 39 is written on fol. 40r.

There are two exposures of fols. 2v–3r.

Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ❖ ||

prayogam icchatā jñātuṃ, jñeyaṅ kārakam āditaḥ ||

saṃjñayā ṣaḍvidhaṃ bhedāḥ, trayoviṃśatidhā punaḥ ||

tatra paṃcavidhaṃ karttā, karmma saptavidhaṃ bhavet ||

karaṇaṃ dvividhaṃ caiva, saṃpradānaṃ tridhā mataṃ ||

apādānan dvidhā caiva tathādhāraś caturvidhaḥ ||

karoti kārakaṃ sarvvaṃ, tatsvātantryavivakṣayā || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

ity anena bhāve, ghañpratyayaḥ śitvād acoñṇitīti vṛddhiḥ ātvaṃ || yuc yathā || kaṭasya kāraṇā, bhārasya hāraṇā, odanasya pācanā | evaṃ chedanā, bhedanā, ityādiḥ | nyāsasrantho yuj iti yuc pratyayaḥ | cakāraḥ svarārthaḥ | yu dhāraṇā kāv itya/// (fol. 56v5–6)
