Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

hetvābhāsanirūpaṇe prasaṃgasyāpi saṃgatitvaṃ saṃbhavati | vyāptipakṣadharmatāviśiṣṭahetunirūpaṇe sati vyāptipakṣadharmatāvirodhitadvirahavato duṣṭahetoḥ smaraṇād atas tadapradarśenena mūlasya nyūnatāṃ parijihīrṣuḥ prasaṅgasyāpi tannirūpaṇaprayojakatvam āha | etāvateti || parikaro vyāpitipakṣadharmate nirūpyetantam uktakrameṇa duṣṭahetoḥ smṛtatvasya prasaṅgavirvāhakasya lābhāya tatprasaṃgād iti || saparikarahetunirupitaprasaṅgasaṃgater ity arthaḥ || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

parvate vahnisādhane dūmo duṣṭa ityādiprayogavāraṇāya viṣayitāyāṃ bhramātya(!)vṛttitvaṃ niveśitaṃ | doṣaghaṭakakevalasādhyādau doṣavyavahārābhāvād ayaṃ doṣa ityādau svaviṣayakaniścayāvyavahṛtottarānumitiniṣṭhatādṛśobhayābhāvādhikaraṇatātvavyāpakavirodhiviṣayatāprayojyatākadharma eva doṣapadārtha iti dik || (fol. 18r11–12)

Colophon

iti śrīgadādharabhaṭṭācāryaviracitā sāmānyaniruktiṭippaṇī samāptā || ||

karaśaraghanaśāke taiṣa kṛṣṇena tithyāṃ

śaraśaraparapūryāṃ lālaratnākareṇa ||

likhitam idam araṃśrīhetusāmānya cihnaṃ

sad asad iti jagatsatkartṛkaṃ neti hetoḥ || 1 || || || (fol. 18r12–13)
