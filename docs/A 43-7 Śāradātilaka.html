Excerpts

Beginning

n pūjayet tataḥ |

⟪pīṭhādhaiḥ⟫ [[gandhādyaiḥ]] pīṭhamanvaṃntaṃ (!) tasmiṃś ca paradevatāṃ |

pañcakṛtvaḥ punaḥ kuryyāt puṣpāñjalim ananyadhīḥ |

uttamāṅgahṛdādhārapādasarvvāṅgake kramāt |

vinā nivedyaṃ gandhādyair upacāraiḥ samarccayet |

gurūpadiṣṭavidhinā śeṣam anyat samāpayet |

sarvvam etat prayuṃ〇jīta prokṣiṇīsthena vāriṇā |

visṛjya toyaṃ prokṣiṇyāḥ pūrayet tāṃ yathā purā |

tatas tanmaṇḍalaṃ mantrī gandhādyaiḥ sādhu pūjayet |

śālibhiḥ karṇikāmadhyam āpūryyopari taṇḍulai〇ḥ |

alaṅkṛtya punas teṣu darbbhān āstīryya tantravit |

kūrccam akṣatasaṃyuktaṃ nyaset teṣām athopari |

ādhāraśaktim ārabhya pīṭhaṃ mantramayaṃ yajet | (fol. 20r1–4)

End

madhye tāraṃ sasādhyaṃ manum atha vili(khe)t ṣaṭsu koṇeṣu sandhiṣv

aṅgāny ante kalānāṃ yugayugavilasat〇keśarāṣṭārṇṇapatram |

kiñjalkān kādivarṇair dvivasudalalasatṣoḍaśārṇaṃ svanāmnā

hakṣā⁅bhyāṃ⁆ triḥpravītaṃ likhatu parivṛtaṃ tat tripāśāṅkuśābhyām ||

tāraṃ na〇mo bhagavate mahāsudarśanāya ca ||

varmāstrāntaś cakramantraḥ ṣoḍaśākṣara īritaḥ ||

cakrayantra⁅m i⁆daṃ proktaṃ sarvvabhītinivāraṇaṃ |

kṣudrāpamṛtyuśamanaṃ rājñāṃ vijayavarddhanaṃ || ||

rekhā vilikhyāṣṭaśirāṃsi tāsām ābadhya bāhye śrutiśaḥ krameṇa |

sthā (fol. 173v3–5)
