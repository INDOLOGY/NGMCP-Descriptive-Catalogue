Excerpts

«Beginning: »

❖ oṃ namo gaṇeśāya ||

praṇamya śirasā devau pri(!)tāmaheśvarau(!) |

saṃgītaśāstre saṃkṣepas sārato yaṃ mayocyate ||

bharatādimataṃ sarrvam āloḍyātiprayatnataḥ ||

śrīmatāharibhaṭtena sajjanānandahetunā |

pracarad(‥ ‥)dgitasārodrālo(!) vidhīyate ||

gītaṃ vādyañ ca narttanañ ca trayaṃ saṃjñātam ucyate ||<ref name="ftn1">unmetrical</ref>

mā(‥)deśīvibhāgena saṅgīta[ṃ] dvibhidaṃ(!) mataṃ ||

druhiṇeṇa yad u[d]dīṣṭaṃ prayuktaṃ bharatena ca ||

mahādevasya puratas tan mokṣākhyaṃ vimuktidaṃ

taddeśas †tayavityata† syāllokānuvaṃjakaṃ || (fol. 1v1–3)

«End: »

atha gondalī |

vāsye prabandhau kaṭhinaiḥ muktam enādibhis tathā |

gītir mana ustaiṣyabandhaiś ca dhruvādibhiḥ | <ref name="ftn2">unmetrical</ref>

nāsyāṅge bandhitaṃ yatra nṛtyaṃ ca drutamānataḥ ||

svayaṃ gāyati vādyaṃ ...†vyatvamovīvāmevam vān† mūkagoṇḍanī |

goṇḍanyā maṇḍanaṃ proktaṃ tad gaiḥ kāṇḍīṭadeśajaṃ || iti goṇḍanī || (fol. 50r6–9)

«Colophon: »

iti śrīmatpaṇḍitalakṣmīdharasuta catu[ra]dāmodarabhaṭṭaviracite saṅgītadarpane(!) nṛttā(!)dhyāya[ḥ] (fol. 50r9)
