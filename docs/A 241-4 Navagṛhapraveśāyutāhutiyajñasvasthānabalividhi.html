❖ oṃ namaḥ siddheśvaramahābhairavāya ||


śrīgurupādukābhyāṃ namaḥ || ||


javapīṭhācāryayā vidhi likhyate || ||


gṛhe pratiṣṭhā devapratiṣṭhā deva pratiṣṭhāyātaṃ dayakā || ||


vidhithyaṃ patavāsana coya || mūlabaliyātaṃ saṭkoṇa aṣṭaśṛṇga ||


arghapātrayātaṃ trikona || jalapātrayātaṃ catiurasra || paṃcabali gaṇavaṭuka || ||


atha puṣpabhājana || adyādivākya ||


śrīsaṃvarttāmaṇḍalānte kramapadanihitānandaśaktiḥ subhīmā ||


sṛṣṭaṃ nyāyacatuṣkaṃ akulakulagataṃ pañcakaṃ cānyaṣaṭkaṃ ||


catvāraḥ pañcakonyaṃ punar api caturas tattvato maṇḍaledaṃ ||


saṃsṛṣṭaṃ yena tasmai namati (!) guruvaraṃʼ bhairavaḥ(!) śrīkujeśaṃ || (fol. 1v1–2r1)


«End: »


agrabali chhoyakātha ||


mūlapāṭhabalidathupāta galālasa javalāpātabalidulamājusa || khavalāpātabalina kiṃju āgama ||
