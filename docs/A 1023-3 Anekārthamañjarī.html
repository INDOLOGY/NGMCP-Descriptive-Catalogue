Beginning

srīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

śabdāṃbhodhir yyato nantaḥ kuto py agamad avyayāt ||
svānubodhaikam ānāya tasmai vāgātmane namaḥ || 1 ||

sarasvatyāḥ prasādena kavir badhnāti yat padaṃ ||
prasiddham aprasiddhaṃ vā tvatpramāṇaṃ ca sādhu ca || 2 ||

śīvaṃ bhadraṃ śivaḥ śaṃbhuḥ śivā gaurī śivābhayā ||
śivaḥ kolaḥ śīvā kroṣṭī bhaved āmalakī śivā || 3 || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

ṣakāraḥ kathitaḥ śreṣṭaḥ ṣo lakṣmīr iti gadyate ||
