Excerpts

Beginning

śrigaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

śrīśvetakālyai namaḥ ||

jalasodhanam (!) || phra astraya phaṭ || hriṃ yaṃ yaṃ soṣaṇaṃ (!) hriṃ raṃ raṃ dahanaṃ || hrīṃ vaṃ vaṃ plāvanaṃ || jale trikoṇaṃ tanmadhye hrīṃ vilikhe || gāyatryā saṃprokṣya || hrīṃ cāmuṇḍāyai vidmahe śrīṃ śvetakālyai dhīmahi tanno devī pracodayāt || hriṃ iti tridhābhimaṃtrya || dhenuliṅgayonimudrāṃ pradarśya || hriṃ ātmatattvāya svāhā || hriṃ vidyātattvāya svāhā || hriṃ śivatattvāya svāhā || sarvattvena hastaprakṣyālanam || (fols. 1v1–7)

End

itidaṃ (!) mātṛkāstotraṃ pavitraṃ pāpanāsanam ||

yaḥ paṭhet mānavo bhyaktyā sarvasiddhiṃ sa vindati || 13 ||

āpadaḥ sarvapāpānī (!) rogaśokabhayāni ca ||

śatravo grahapīḍāś ca vinaśyanti na saṃśayaḥ || 14 ||

vidyākāmo labhed vidyāṃ dhanalābho labhe (!) dhanam |

jayaṃ ca jayakāmopi putrārthī labhate sutam || 15 ||

iha loke sukhī bhūtvā sarvaiśvaryasamanvitaḥ ||

ante vimānam āruhya devī loke mahīyate || 16 || (fols. 12v4–13r2)

Colophon

iti śrīmātṛkāstotraṃ samāptam || || śrī samvat 1952 sāla āṣāḍa vadi daśamyāṃ vudhavāsare likhitam idaṃ tārānidhiupādhyā arjyāla śarmaṇaḥ śubham || || śrīmātṛkāśaraṇa || śrīśvetakālīsaraṇa || || (fol. 13r2–5)
