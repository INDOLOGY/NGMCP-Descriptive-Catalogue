Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśaḥ śaraṇam

praṇamya paramaṃ jyotir vāmanena kavipriyā ||

kāvyālaṅkārasūtrāṇāṃ sveṣāṃ vṛttir vidhīyate || 1 ||


kāvyaṅ grāhyam alaṅkārāt 1 kāvyaṃ khalu grāhyam upādeyaṃ bhavaty alaṅkārāt kāvyaśabdo yaṅ guṇālaṅkārasaṃskṛtayoḥ śabdārthayo[r] vartate bhaktyā tu śabdārthamātravacao gṛhyate 1 ko sāv alaṅkāra ity āha sauṃdaryam alaṅkāraḥ 2 alaṅkṛtir ala⟨ṃ⟩ṅkāraḥ (fol. 1v1–5)


End

iṃdravāhanaśabde vāhanam āhitād iti ṇatvan na bhavati āhitattvasyā vivakṣitatvāt svasvāmibhāvamātraṃ vivakṣitatan tena siddham iṃdravāhanam iti sadasattobhayā[ḥ] śabdā vivicyā †dralpena† darśitāḥ anayaiva diśā kāryyaṃ śeṣāṇām apy avekṣaṇam 1 (fol. 73r6–73v2)


Colophon

iti śrīvāmanācāryakṛtau kāvyālaṃkārasūtravṛttau paṃcame ʼdhikaraṇe dvitīyo ʼdhyāyaḥ 2 samāptaṃ prāyogikaṃ paṃcamam adhikaraṇam kāvyālaṃkārasūtravṛttiḥ samāptāḥ śubham (fol. 73v2–4)
