Excerpts

Beginning

śaśilekhā ||

jayati śrīmūladeva

yasya yaśo bharitabhuvanavistāraṃ ||

adyāpi dhūrtānāṃ(2)

manasi karoti (‥) rukaṃ camatkāraṃ

āsī bahusiddhinilayo

haracaraṇārādhanaikatā(3)tparyavān ||

mūlaśrīvaṃśatilako

durjjayabhaṭṭa iti nāmnā ||(!)

yena virajanagare

naranā(4)thonmatta kesarīnaḥ ||

purataḥ vidyādhavalo

hyagatāgatena ko vā

na vismāpitena ||(!) (fol.2r1–4)

End

mahāmāyāprabhā(3)vaś ca nirghaṇṭaś ca pṛṭhak sthitaḥ

vidhayaḥ saptamasyaite paricchedasya kīrtti(4)tāḥ || 7 ||

thvahna sapo śroka hma hlā ko saptama pariccheda saco ga juroṃ || || (fol.173r2–4)

ha(5)ramekhalāyāḥ saptamaḥ paricchedaḥ samāptaḥ || iti haramekhalāyāṃ yogamā(6)lāyāṃ nepārabhāṣāṭīkā parisamāpta || || śubha ❁ ||○ || hara || śubha || (fol.173r4–6)
