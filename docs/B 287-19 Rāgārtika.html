Excerpts

Beginning

rāga ārati ||

ārati joti jagadambe māi ke ||

niraṃjanarūpa va rāma jotike ||

raviśaśi uta mana ārati sāje

anahada sabda ghaṇṭa vajāvai || 1 ||

paṃcabhūta mili caṃvaṃra ḍolāvai

joti tilakamaiṃ tanamana bhāvai || 2 ||

sevaka bhakti jana ārati gāvai

aṃta dharma karma mokṣaphala pāvai || 3 || (exp. 2t1–2b2)

End

īndradamana siṃdhu vāje rohine ṣaḍi

mārakaṇḍe sesa gaṃgā ānaṃda bhara-I ||3 ||

kaṃcana maṃcana dipadāna jarmaga kari

navaniti aṃgatura vāti kājari || 4 ||

suranaramunī dvāre ṭhāḍe brahmaveda ucari

dhande dhande rāgasura ājuki ghari || 5 || (exp. 5t5–5b4)
