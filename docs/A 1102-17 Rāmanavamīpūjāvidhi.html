Excerpts

Beginning

śrīmahāgaṇapataye namaḥ || ||

atha rāmanavamīvratapūjāpaddhatiḥ || ||

tatrādau aṣṭamyāṃ daṃtadhāvanapūrvakaṃ nadyādau snātvā nityaṃ nirvarttya ācāryavaraṇaṃ kuryāt tatra maṃtraḥ

śrīrāmapratimādānaṃ kariṣyehaṃ dvijottama |

tatrācāryo bhava prītaḥ śrīrāmopi tvam eva ca||

ājyena taṃ snāpayitvā svayaṃ tailenābhyajya taṃ vipraṃ sātvikānnair bhojayitvā svayaṃ bhuṃjīta ||

tataḥ navamyā(!) ekabhaktena aṃgabhūtena rāghava ||

ikṣvākuvaṃśatilaka prīto bhava raghūdvaha ||

ity ekabhaktaṃ nivedya sāyaṃ saṃdhyāṃ kṛtvā ācāryasahitoḥ(!)

vā śayanaṃ kuryāt || ity aṣṭamīdinakṛtyam || ||

(fol. 1v1-6)

End

iti saṃkalpaḥ ||

śrīrāmaṃ smaran anena maṃtreṇa pratiṃāṃ dadyāt ||

imāṃ svarṇamayīṃ⟪śrī⟫rāma pratimāṃ samalaṃkṛtām ||

cittravastrayugacchannāṃ rāmohaṃ rāghavātmane ||

śrīrāmaprītaye dāsye tuṣṭo bhavatu rāghavaḥ ||

tato dānapratiṣṭḥāṃ kṛtvā abhiṣekaṃ kārayitvā prasādaṃ gṛhītvā brāhmaṇaiḥ saha bhuṃjīta || pāraṇāmaṃtra ||

tava prasādaṃ svīkṛtya kriyate pāraṇā mayā |

vratenānena saṃtuṣṭaḥ svāmin bhaktiṃ prayaccha me ||

(fol. 25v2-6)

Colophon

iti daśamīkṛtyaṃ

samāptoyaṃ rāmanavamīpūjāvidhiḥ || ||

likhitaṃ rāmacandraśarmaṇā ||

yadi śuddham aśuddho(!) vā mama doṣo na dīyate ||

śrīśāke 1726 bhādrapadamāse dina 30 gataḥ samāptam śubham || ||(fol. 26v1-2)
