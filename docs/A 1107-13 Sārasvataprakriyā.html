The MS contains the text from the ākhyātaprakriyā to the end of ktvāpratyayādhikāra chapter.


Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || || śrīsarasvatyai namaḥ || ❖ || śrīgurubhyo namaḥ ||

athākhyātapratyayā nirūpyaṃte || ||

sūtrasaptasatīṃ yasmai dadau sākṣāt sarasvatī ||

anubhūtisvarūpāya tasmai śrīgurave namaḥ || ||

natvā lakṣmīkā[n]tamīśaṃ prakṛtyā (dhyā)tvādhyātmakhyātibhājo mukuṃdaṃ ||

ākhyātākhyakprakṛ(!)yā tanyate sā sārasvatyā bālabodho yayā syāt || 2 ||

dhātoḥ || vakṣamāṇāḥ pratyayā dhātor jñeyāḥ || bhvādiḥ || bhū sattāyām ityādir gaṇo dhātusaṃjño bhavati || (fol. 1v1–4)


End

saṃmūlākṛtajīveṣu hankṛñgrahāṃ ṇam vācyaḥ ||

eteṣām anuprayogaś ca || samūlaghātaṃ haṃti || ākṛtikāraṃ karoti || jīvagrāhaṃ gṛhṇāti || ityādi || varṇāt kāraḥ || akāraḥ || kakāraḥ || rād ipho vā rephaḥ || rakāraḥ || lokācheṣasya siddhir yathā mātarādeḥ |

svarūpāṃtonubhūtyādiḥ śabdobhūd yatra sārthakaḥ ||

samaskarī śubhāṃ cakre prakriyāṃ caturocitāṃ || 1 ||

ayatād vo hayagrīvaḥ kamalākara īśvaraḥ ||

surāsuranarākāramadhupā pītapatkajaḥ(!) || 2 || || (fols. 90v3–91r2)


Colophon

iti śrīmatparamahaṃsaparivrājakācārya-anubhūtisvarūpācāryaviracitāyāṃ sārasvati(!)prakriyā samāptā || || jagadīśvarakṛpayā likhitam iyaṃ pustakī || svasti śrīsaṃvat 1780 || trayodaśī śubhadina āṣāḍhe tu budhe sati || liṣitaṃ nīlakaṃṭhena śubhadaṃ pustakaṃ kila || 1 || || ❖ || ❖ || (fol. 91r2–5)
