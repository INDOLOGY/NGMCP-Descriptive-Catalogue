Manuscript Features

Fol. 25 is missing. There are another two lines of text on fol. 27v, but largely illegible. On some other pages the writing is also rubbed off.

The manuscript contains two texts:

  • Caturdaśīvratakathā and
  • Umāmaheśvarastotra

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śivāya ||
dṛṣṭvā prabhāsapurāṇe caturddaśīkathām imāṃ |
likhyate sāram ākṛṣya bhuktimuktiphalapradāṃ ||
kailāsaśikhare ramye nānāratnavinirmmite |
suvarṇṇarajatākīrṇṇe vajravidrumabhāsure ||
tatrāsī[[naṃ]] mahādevaṃ devadevaṃ jagadguruṃ |
sṛṣṭisaṃhārakarttāraṃ pālanasya ca kāraṇaṃ ||
vṛtaṃ devagaṇaiḥ sarvvaiḥ sendrair api surāsuraiḥ |
brahmādibhis tathā devaṃ munimukhyais tathaiva ca ||
evaṃvidhe samāje vai varttamāne divaukasāṃ |
nivārya niḥsvanan tañ ca gaurī vacanam abravīt ||
devy uvāca ||
devadeva mahādeva sṛṣṭisaṃhārakārakaḥ |
tvaṃ sraṣṭā sarvvabhūtānāṃ tvaṃ gatiḥ parameśvara ||
tvaṃ yeyaṃ(!) sakalaṃ vyāptaṃ nāsti kiñ cit vadan yathā ||
bhuktimuktipada śrīmān sarvvalokeka(!)bāndhavaḥ ||
atītavarttamānañ ca bhaviṣyaṃ vetsi līlayā |
śrutyāni(!) sarvvaśāstrāṇi tvatprasādāt svayaṃ vibho || (fol. 1v1-4)

End

nītaṃ svargge śivasthāne tasya vrataprabhāvataḥ |
tad vratasya phalaṃ naiva sthitā svargge śivālaye ||
yeḥ(!) śṛṇvanti kathā divyaṃ(!) cadaturddaśīvratasya ca |
saptajanmakṛtaṃ pāpaṃ mucyate(!) nātra saṃśayaḥ ||
suvarṇṇagosahasrāṇi dānadātāratatphalaṃ |
