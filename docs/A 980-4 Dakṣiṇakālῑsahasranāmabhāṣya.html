śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||


śrīdakṣiṇākālīsahasranāma bhāṣya ||


kailāsaśikhare devī śaṃkaraṃ paripṛcchati ||


kiṃ bhūte || kailsaramaṇīye || nānā ratnavibhūṣite || nānā bṛkṣalatākīrṇe vyāpte || nānā pakṣiravair


yute || 1 || caturmaṇḍape ti caturmaṃḍapasaṃyukte || caturmaṇḍalasaṃyukte iti kvacit pāṭhaḥ ||


śṛṅgāramaṇḍape vihārārthakarma upairnvite || śaṃkaraṃ kiṃ bhūtaṃ samādhau saṃsthitaṃ


samādhir nādharmetyā ityamaraḥ || punaḥ kīdṛśaṃ śāṃtaṃ yoginīpriyaṃ || maunavṛttidharaṃ || śrī


devyuvāca || he nātha || kiṃ tvayā japyate || kiṃ tvayā sadā smaryate || sṛṣṭiḥ kutra vilīnāsti miśritā


bhavati || (fol. 1r-8)


«End:»


caturvrdādhipo bhūtvā trivarṣato trivarṣamadhye trikālajño bhavati | caturvidhaṃ pāṇḍityaṃ tasya


hastagataṃ bhavati || iti sarvadā śūnyaṃ śivābaliṃ pradātavyā || kālikādhyānaṃ maṃtraciṃtanānvitā


nīlasādhanaṃ || sādhanasahasranāmapāṭhaś ca kālīnāma prakīrttitaṃ ||


tasyāḥ kālīmantropāsakasya etatkṛtaṃ tad anyad abhyudayam iti | 335 || vīrasādhanaṃ karmeti |

vīrasādhanakarmakuryāt || śivā baliṃ dadyāt || siṃdūratilakaṃ kuryāt || veśyālāpayukto bhavet || 337 ||


veśyārohe niśācaraḥ niśāyāṃ gaṃtavyaṃ | rātrau paryaṭan śmaśānādau pari sarvatobhāvena |
