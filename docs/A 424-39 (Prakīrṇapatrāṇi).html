Manuscript Features

This MS is a portion of the text Kriyākalpatarū, assigned to the one of the Tantra which contains three different incomplete portion of the imagery text related to the various god and goddess, kumārīvaṭukayāga and incomplete and undefined portion of the Devīstotra.

On the exp. 3b4t scattered chapter is about the appearance and sign of the kumārī. and vaṭuka, available colophon only.
