Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrī3gaṃṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

natvā nṛsiṃhaṃ jagadekasākṣiṇaṃ

kṛtvā praṇāmaṃ gurave mahātmane

natvā gaṇeśaṃ girijāsutaṃ prabhuṃ

karomi ṭīkāṃ ca nalodayasya vai ||

nalodayasya kāvyasya ṭīkeyaṃ ca pratanyate |

nṛsiṃhākhyena muninā sarvveṣām upakārataḥ ||

hṛdayeti || he hṛdaya citta sadā sarvvadā tvaṃ yādayataḥ śrīkṛṣṇāt māgāḥ pṛthag mābhūḥ | yadoḥ gātrasyāpatyaṃ yādavaḥ yādav[[ā]]t iti yādavataḥ | sārvvavibhakti⟨ṣu⟩kastas || kiṃbhūtāt pāpāṭavyāḥ pāpānāṃ aṭavī tasyāḥ davāt iti davataḥ kiṃbhūtāyāḥ pāpāṭavyāḥ dūrasadāyāḥduṣkhena(!) soḍhuṃ śakyāyāḥ |  (fol. 1r1–6)

End

bhīmaduhitṛmāyāṃ itayā, yaśasāṃ kīrttīnāṃ āyāmino bhāvaḥ āyāmitā, tayā śriyā yaśodairghyarūpayā lakṣmyā hṛtaḥ vaśīkṛtaḥ ahaṃ tadadhigamāya tasyāḥ bhīmaduhitur adhigamas tasmai tat prāptyai amitayā apracurayā tayā spṛhayā vāñchayā manuṣyalokaṃ āyāmi āgato ʼsmi || || itīti || amalo niṣkapaṭo jano devajanaḥ iti vakṣyamāṇaṃ ūce uktavān kīdṛśo janaḥ māyāyāḥ kapaṭāt vikalo rahitaḥ punaḥ kīdṛśo janaḥ tad uktaḥ tena kalinā uktaḥ iti kiṃ- (fol. 26r1–6)

Colophon

iti nalodayaṭīkāyāṃ dvitīyollāsaḥ || || (fol. 25v8)
