Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ gaṇapataye namaḥ ||

vṛkṣādiva[[di]]tyādi || vṛkṣādaya iva vṛkṣādivad yathā vṛkṣādayaḥ saṃjñāśabdā rūḍhā loke viśiṣṭaviṣayatayā prasiddhās tathā ʼmīkṛtaḥ kṛdantā api śabdā iti kṛtvā kṛtinā na kṛtāḥ | prastāvād iha kṛtīṃ paṃḍitaḥ śarvvavarmmaiṣocdyate | śarvvavarmmaṇā kṛṣṇalakṣāṇaṃ sakṛtam ity arthaḥ || tathā cāhuḥ | avidhānalakṣaṇā hi kṛttaddhitasamāsā iti || avidhānaṃ śabdan tad eva lakṣaṃ niyāmakaṃ na sūtram ity arthaḥ | kena nāma tarhi kṛtā ity āha | kātyāyanena vararūcinety arthaḥ | (fol. 1v1–4)

End

tata (!) pūrvvādavato kvipi kṛtepīti | nanu cāvarttino do vṛddhir ity ucyatām avarṇena saho do vṛddhir bhaviṣyatīti | evaṃ satyākāre aścakāre ceti prakriyāgauravam api nirastam bhaviṣyatīti | satyam avarṇād iti paṃcamīnirddeśo ʼvarṇamātrapratipatyartho ʼanyathā dhātus tatprastāvād dhātor evāvarṇāt syaṃdity ucyata eva dhātor adhātor vvity uktam || || || (fol. 115v2–5)

Colophon

iti śrītrilocanadāsaviracitāyāṃ kātantravṛttivivaraṇapañjikāyāṃ kṛtsu ṣaṣṭhaḥ pādaḥ samāptaḥ || || ❖ || || saṃvat 1632 samaye vaiśāṣa śudi 6 sanivāsare || || pāṭhārthaṃ liṣāpitaṃ pāṭhakam itā(!) nandasutapadmanābhaliṣyāpitaṃ pāṭhārthaṃ liṣitaṃ pustaka(!) śrīvāstavyaṃ pāṃḍe madanaputra gosāi dāsena || viśvanāṭhasaranaṃ || śubham astu || rāma || (fol. 115v5–8)
