Excerpts

«Beginning»

śrīrāmāya 〈n〉namaḥ || ||

nārada uvāca || ||

vāsudevaṃ hṛṣīkeśaṃ vāvanaṃ(!) jalaśāyanaṃ ||

janārddanaṃ hariṃ kṛṣṇaṃ śrīvatsaṃ garuḍadhvajaṃ || 1 ||

vārāhaṃ puṃḍarīkākṣaṃ narasiṃhaṃ narakāṃtakaṃ ||

avyaktaṃ śāśvataṃ viṣṇum anaṃtam ajam avyayaṃ || 2 ||

nārāyaṇagadādhyakṣaṃ goviṃdaṃ kīrtta(!)bhājanaṃ ||

govarddhana〈ṃ〉dharaṃ devaṃ bhūdharaṃ bhuvaneśvaraṃ || 3 || (fol. 1v1–4)


«End»

iti vyāsamukhodbhūtaṃ ye paṭhaṃti samāhitā ||

divā ⌠vā⌡ yadi vā rātrau śāntis teṣāṃ prajāyate ||

aiśvaryam atulaṃ caiva ājña(!)puṣṭivivarddhanam ||

naranārīnṛpāṇāṃ ca bhave duḥsvapnadarśanam ||

grahanakṣatrapīḍāś ca taskarāgnisamudbhavāṃ ||

tāṃ sarvāṃ praśamaṃ yāṃti vyāso brute na śaṃsayaḥ || (fol. 5r1–5)


«Colophon»

iti vyāsa〈〈va〉〉kṛtaṃ navagrahastotraṃ saṃpūrṇam || || (fol. 5r5)
