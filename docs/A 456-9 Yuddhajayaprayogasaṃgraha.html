Manuscript Features

On the cover-leaf is a name list “pitṛnāmāvalī” of Nepal Royal family. There are separate list of the

name of father and forefather of kink Rajendra and Pṛthvīvīravikrama Sāha.


Text written in Newari language, contains various “Guṭikāprayoga”, “Mantraprayoga” “śatrustaṃbhanaprayoga “ etc.

regarding to the battle field to defeat enemies.
