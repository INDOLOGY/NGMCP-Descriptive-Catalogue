Excerpts

Beginning

oṁ namo vajradākāya || ||

evam mayā śutam ekasmin samaye bhagavān sarvatathāgatakāyavākcittavajrayoṣidbhageṣu vijahāra | tatra khalu bhagavān aśītikoṭiyogīśvaramadhye vajragarbham avalokya smitam akārṣīt || samanakarasmiṃ tasmin vajragarbhaṃ utthāyāsanād ekāṃ samuttarāsaṃgaṃ kṛtvā dakṣiṇajānumaṇḍalaṃ pṛthivyāṃ pratisthā(!)pya kṛtāñjalipuṭo bhūtvā bhagavantam etad avocat ||

śrotum icchāmi jñānendra sarvatantranidānakaṃ ||

rahasyaṃ sampuṭodbhūtaṃ lakṣālakṣaṇasaṃpuṭaṃ || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

yathāṃgaṇābālakathānumoditaṃ

saṃlāpasaṃparkkakathāñ ca khyāpitaṃ ||

mārgo ʼpi khyāpitaṃ, na ca bandhanañ ca

evaṃ vidhaṃ yogi kumārggadarśitaṃ⟨ḥ⟩ || <ref name="ftn1">Pāda c is unmetrical.</ref>

atha sarvopariṣat yogīyogīni ḍākaḍākinī asi(!)tikoṭibodhisatvās tathāgatāś ca anekaśaḥ || pratipahlādacittas tu sarvatathāgatajñānalābhi(!) tu sarvataḥ || vajragarbhapramukhā mahābodhisatvāḥ sarve ca devā nāgā yakṣā gaṃdharvāḥ sā ca sarvāvati(!)pariṣad bhagavato bhāṣitam abhyanaṃdan iti ||     || (fol. 65v3–6)

Colophon

iti śrīsampuṭodbhavasarvataṃtranidānamahākalparāja(!) daśama(!) samāpta⟨ṃ⟩ḥ || samvat 975 miti bhādrapadaśuklapaṃcami ādityavāra thvaṣunu sampūrṇṇaṃ coyā śiddhayānādina jula śubhaṃ || || sarvadākāle śubhar(!) astuḥ(!) || || ślokagraṃṭha(!) 3200 || (fol. 65v6–8)
