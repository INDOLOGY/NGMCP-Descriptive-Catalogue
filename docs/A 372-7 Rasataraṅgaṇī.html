Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

lakṣmīm ālokya lubhyan nigamam upahasan śocayan yajñajaṃtūn

kṣatraṃ śoṇākṣipaśyan sami(2)tidaśamukhaṃ vīkṣya romāṃcamaṃcan ||

hṛtvā haiyaṃgavīnaṃ cakitam apasaran mlecharaktair digaṃtān

siṃcan daṃtena bhūmiṃ tilam iva tu(3)layan pātu māṃ pītavāsāḥ || 1 ||

bhāratyāḥ śāstrakāṃtāraśrāṃtāyāḥ śaityakāriṇī ||

kriyate bhanunā bhurirasārasataraṃgiṇī (!) || 2 ||

(4) vāṇīkamalinī bhānor eṣā rasataraṃgiṇī ||

haṃsāḥ kṛtadhiyas tatra yuktam atra pradīyatām || 3 ||

girāṃ devi taragiṇyāṃ vāraya krūravā(5)raṇān

yad bhaviṣyati lokānām āvilo viralo rasaḥ || 4 ||

hetoḥ prūvavartittvaniyamāt prūvam eva tasyopanyāsaḥ samucita iti rasa(6)sya hetavo bhāvādayas tāvan nirūpyaṃte || (fol. 1v1–6)

End

vidvadvāridharāḥ snehaṃ tathā varṣaṃtu saṃtatam ||

labhate vipulāṃ vṛddhiṃ yathā rasata(2)raṅgiṇī || 21 ||

avagāhasva vāgdevi divyāṃ rasataraṃgiṇīṃ ||

asmatpadyena padmena racaya śrutibhūṣaṇam || 22 ||

yāvad bhānoḥ sutā kāpi (3)kālindī bhuvi varttate ||

tāvat tiṣṭhatu me bhānor eṣā rasataraṃgiṇī || 23 || || (fol. 28r1–3)

Colophon

iti śrīmaithilabhānudattamiśraviracitāyāṃ rasataraṃgi(4)ṇyām aṣṭamas taraṃgaḥ ||

|| samāpteyaṃ rasataraṃgiṇī likhitā jagannāthajyotirvidā śrīmadraṇabahāduraśyenasya (!) pāṭhārthaṃ || || (5)śrīśāke 1717 saṃvati (!) 1852 bhādrapadādhikaśuklatṛtīyāyāṃ somavāsare śrīnagarābhidhāyāṃ pālapādhiparājadhānyāṃ || || (fol. 38r3–6 )
