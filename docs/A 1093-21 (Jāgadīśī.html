Beginning

oṃ namaḥ kālikāyai ||

idaṃ ca yena rūpeṇa yena saṃbaṃdhena sādhyatā tadrūpāvacchinnapratiyogitākasādhyābhāvasya praveśam ādṛtya anyathā viśiṣṭābhāvādikam ā(2)dāya doṣo bodhya iti jāgadīśī | atreyam āśaṃkā sādhyaniṣṭhapratiyogitākamahānasīyavahnyabhāvādhikaraṇanirūpitavṛttitvābhāvasya hetāv asatvepi vahnitveta(3)ra dharmānavacchinnapratiyogitākābhāvasyāpi sādhya pratiyogitākatayā tad adhikaraṇanirūpitavṛttitvābhāvasya dhūmahetau satvenaiva lakṣaṇasamanvayād | alaṃ yena (4) rupeṇetyādiviśeṣeṇeneti (fol. 1r1–4)
