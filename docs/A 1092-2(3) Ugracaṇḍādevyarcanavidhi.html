Manuscript Features

This MS contains the following texts:

  • A 1092-2(1) Tumbeśvarīnityakarmavidhi (exps. 3b–9t)
  • A 1092-2(2) Śikhāmahākālanityārcanavidhi (exps. 9t–17b)
  • A 1092-2(3) Ugracaṇḍādevyarcanavidhi (exps. 18t–38b)
  • A 1092-2(4) (Parādevīpūjāvidhi) (exps. 39t–40b)
  • A 1092-2(5) (Brahmāviṣṇudhyāna) (exps. 41t–42b)


1. On the front cover-leaf is written: Degu sāphu
2. On the other side cover-leaf is written: Ugracaṇḍādevārcanam
3. This is a copy of the Ugracaṇḍādevyarcanavidhi microfilmed in A 1229/3.
