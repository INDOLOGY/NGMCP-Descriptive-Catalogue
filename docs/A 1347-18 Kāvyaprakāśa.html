Beginning

|| śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

graṃthāraṃbhe vighnavighātāya samuciteṣṭadevatāṃ graṃthakṛt parāmṛśati || ||

niya(2)tikṛtaniyamarahitāṃ

hlādaikamayīm ana[[nya]]parataṃtrāṃ

navarasarucirāṃ nirmitim

ādadhatī bhāratī kaver jja(3)yati ||1 ||

niyatiśaktyā niyatarūpā sukhaduḥkhamohasvabhāvā

paramāṇvādi (!) upādānakarmādisahakā(4)rikāraṇaparataṃtrā ||

ṣaḍraṣā na ca hṛdyaiva taiti (!) | etādṛśīr brahmaṇo nirmitir nirmāṇaṃ etad vilakṣaṇā tu (5) kavivāṅnirmitiḥ | ata eva jayati | jayaty arthena ca namaskāra ākṣipyate | tena tāṃ praty asmi praṇata iti (6) labhyate || 1 || (fol. 1v1–6)

End

padaikade(7)śe yathāsaṃbhavaṃ krameṇodā[haraṇam] ||

alam aticapalattvāt svapnamāyopamattvāt

pariṇati virasattvāt saṃgamena priyāyāḥ |

iti yadi śatakṛtvas tatvam (!) ālocayāma (!)

tad api na hariṇākṣīṃ vismaraty antarātmā ||

atra tvād iti | yathā vā |

tad gacha (!) siddhyai kuru devakāryam

artho yam arthāntaralabhya eva |

apekṣate pratyayam uttamaṃ tvāṃ

bījāṃkuraḥ prāg udayādivāṃbhaḥ |

atra ddhyai iti ca kaṣṭaṃ |

yaścāpsarovibhramamaṃḍanānāṃ

saṃpādayitrīṃ śikharair bibhartti |
