Manuscript Features

There are carved wooden covers. The last folio is missing. The following is written on fol. 1r in Newari script: ❖ śrīnārāyaṇasiṃghadevasya pustakaṃ bhavatu ||.

Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śivāya ||

siddhiḥ sādhye satām astu prasādāt tasya dhūrjjaṭeḥ |jāhnavīphenalekheva yanmūrdhni śaśinaḥ kalā ||

śruto hitopadeśo yaṃ pāṭavaṃ saṃskṛtoktiṣu |vācāṃ sarvvatra vaicitryaṃ nītividyāṃ dadāti ca || ajarāmaravat prājño vidyā〇m arthañ ca cintayet |gṛhīta iva keśeṣu mṛtyunā dharmam ācaret ||sarvadravyeṣu vidyaiva dravyam āhur anuttamam |ahāryatvād anarghatvād akṣayatvāc ca sarvvadā ||saṃ〇gamayati vidyaiva nīcagāpi naraṃ śarit |samudram iva durdarśan nṛpaṃ bhāgyam ataḥ param || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

ataḥ satyābhidhānadivyapuraḥsaro dvayor apy anayor bhūpālayoḥ 〇 kāñcanābhidhānaḥ sandhir vvidhīyatām | sarvajño brūte || evam astu || tato rājahaṃsena rājñā vastrālaṅkāropahāraiḥ sa mantrī dūradarśī pūjitaḥ | prahṛṣṭamanāś cakravākaṃ gṛhītvā rājño 〇 mayūrasya sanidhānaṃ (!) gataḥ | tatas tena citravarṇena rājñā sa sarvajño gṛdhravacanād bahumānadānapuraḥsaraṃ saṃbhāṣitaḥ | sandhiṃ svīkṛtya suśiṣṭadvitīyo rājahaṃsakaṭakaṃ praviṣṭaḥ | dūradarśī brūte || deva siddhaṃ vaḥ samīhitam | vainyabalo paśyatām idānīṃ || atha sarvve svasthānaṃ (vā) (fol. 95v3–5)
