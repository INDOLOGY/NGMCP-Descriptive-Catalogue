Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||


atha kānicit tu yaṃtrāṇi vinaiva sādhyasādhakanāmacarṇāṃkamelanādisaṃskāraṃ śivatāṇḍavādiṣu


tantreṣū paṭalāṃtare praṇītāni || tathā hi uttarakhaṇḍe tṛtīyapaṭale mātaṃgīdaivatyāni vaśyayaṃtrāṇi


viṃśatyaṃkamayaṃ yaṃtraṃ vaśayed akhilaṃ jagat

triṃśadāṃkānvitaṃ(!) yaṃtraṃ dūrād ākarṣayet striyam


ākarṣayet ākarṣaṇākārakavaśyatādhikaraṇī kuryāt tena vaśyayaṃtrāṇītyupakramasya na virodhaḥ ||


catvāriṃśad aṃkayaṃtre patiṃ vaśayate dhruvam

paṃcāśadaṃkayaṃtre ca rājānaṃ vaśam ānayet


ṣaṣṭyaṃkalikhitaṃ yaṃtraṃ sarvaśatruvaśaṃ nayet

saptyatyaṃkānvite rājapatnīṃ tu vaśam ānayet (fol. 1v1–4)


End

adhītaṃ naivaitan na ca sugamavākyaṃ na milito

madāśaṃkāchedī na hi ca mama siddhiṣu gaṇanā ||

tathāpyājñāpūrttiḥ katham api malaivammanṛpater

yad atrāste hetuś caraṇakaruṇāhaihayahareḥ || 1 ||


atikliṣṭaśabdaiḥ śivādiṣṭadharmā

budhānāṃ tu matyā pyasaṃspṛṣṭakaṣṭe ||

yadasmiṃstu tantre dhiyo me prakāśaḥ

prabhāvo styayaṃ dīpadānasya viṣṇoḥ || 2 ||


yasyoda(!)tamatiḥ satī guṇavatī (guṇavatī) mātāpitormāpatir

nāma premanidhīti paṃthakulabhūḥ kūrmācalo janmabhūḥ

titā(!)ṣṭābdhiṣaḍindu 1648 śākagakṣitānyorjñādyatithyakaniṭ (!)

kaliśrīśivatāṇḍavasya vihitaṃ saṭṭippaṇaṃ sattvaraṃ || 3 || (!)


śivatāṇḍavaṭīkāsau mallādarśābhidhācārcīḥ || premanidhiśarmavihitā pūrṇā syāt tuṣṭidā viṣṇoḥ || 4 || (fol. 18r7–18v3)


Colophon

iti premanidhipaṇḍitanirmito mallādarśaḥ || || śub ham || (fol. 18v3)
