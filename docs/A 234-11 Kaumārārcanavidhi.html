mānavagotrayajamānasya śrīśrī jayabhūpatīndramalladevavarmmaṇa(!) śrīsveṣṭadevatāprītyarthaṃ navagṛhapraveśasiddhāgniyajñakaumārīja⟪jā⟫gārccananimityārthena(!) puṣpabhājanaṃ samarppayāmi || ||

śrī saṃvarttāmaṇḍalānte kramapadanihitānandaśaktisubhīmā

sṛṣṭaṃ nyāyacatuṣkaṃ akulakulagataṃ paṃcakaṃ cānyaṣaṭkaṃ ||

saṃsṛṣṭaṃ yena tasmai namata guruvaraṃ bhairavaṃ śrīkujeśaḥ(!) || || (fol 1v1–2r1)
