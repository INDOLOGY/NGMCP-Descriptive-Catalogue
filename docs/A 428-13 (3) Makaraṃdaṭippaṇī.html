Beginning

|| śrīmoreśvaro jayati || || śrī || ḥ ||

kaumārakekalakūjitam aiṃdranīla

kailāsaśailaśikhare kṣaṇajaṃ niśamya ||

saṃjātakāṃtapṛṣadaṃ vada(2)naṃ bhavānyā

dṛṣṭvā smitāṃcitamukho avatu viśvam īśaḥ 1

sevaṃtu san madhuliho makaraṃdalubdhā

nityaṃ śubhābhinavatāmarasaṃ rasādhyaṃ ||

sadvā(3)kyarājikararājivirājitā cha (!)-

tarkārka saṃkarakṛtātivikāśakāśi 2 (fol. 1v1–3)

End

agre akṣatrasaṃcāraṛjau bhāṃśāyake grahe ||

adhike vakrage prokto nyathā vyastaṃ budhaiḥ smṛtaṃ ||

atra (7) yuktiḥ sphuṭā ||

ke ke no bhuvi sati (!) teti vikaṭajyotir mahābhonidhi (!)

mārgaṃ setum ivodbhaṭaṃ sukhakaraṃ ye kurvate helayā

(8) tat prajñāṃbudhim agnam ujjvalatamaṃ sadyuktiratnasvayaṃ

loke kiṃ prakaṭī karoti manujaḥ kṛṣṇākṣipātād ṛte || 1 || (fol. 11r6–8)

Colophon

iti śrī(9)kṛṣṇasūnur gurjarapuruṣottamabhaṭṭaracitaṃ makaraṃdaṭippaṇam abhinavatāmarasākhyaṃ saṃpūrṇaṃ || || śubham astu || ❁ || || (fol. 11r8–9)
