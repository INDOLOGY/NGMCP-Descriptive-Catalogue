«Beginning»

oṃ namo ratnatrayāya punarvāra hanaṃ he bhagavāna, chalapola(2) parāpūrvakālasa dakṣiṇa dhāyā bhuvanasa svetaketu bodhisatva(3) dhāyakāo bijyāka vyalasa, thao mantri pramukhanaṃ, bodhisatvapa(4)nita, dharmma upadeśa biya dhunakāo jambudvīpasa, deśadeśāntara(5)yā lakṣana parimāna svayāo hanaṃ māma, babāyā lakṣana svayāo(6) nyagrodha dhāyā nāma ujhānasa sabhāmaṇḍpa danāo, bijyāsyaṃli, chala(1)pola julasāṃ guguli prakārana, bijyāta dhālasā, samasta, lakṣaṇa saṃjukta juohma, jhimaṣupu daṃta duhma tu(2)īo kisiyā rūpa juyāo, śrīmāyādevīyā garbhasa bijyāya yāta || śrīmāyādevī mahārāni julasāṃ aṃ(3)tapurasa, sukha ānaṃdana sayana juyāo, nidrāna hneḍa oyakāo bijyātaṃ || he bhagavāna dhakaṃ upoṣaṭ deva(4) putrana prārthanā yātaṃ || || (fol. 1v1-2r4)


«End»


thvanaṃli uguli bhaṃḍāsanasaṃ julasāṃ, nāgakaṃnyā thamanaṃ bijyā(4)nāo, svacākala ulāo, ugu āśramasa, namaskāra yānāo, dhaṃnya bhāgyayā prabhāvanaṃ, gathiṃgu āśramasa lāta(5) dhakaṃ dhayāo, ugu parameśvarayā, āśramasa jvanāo nāgakaṃnyā lihāṃ onaṃ || bho parameśvara, thathiṃhma chalapolayā(6)ta bāraṃbāra namaskāra he īśvara thva velasa chalapolayā thugu prakāraṇaṃ aṣṭa janmayā vārttā ināpe (fol. 53v3-6)
