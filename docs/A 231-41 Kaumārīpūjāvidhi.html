bhotayākvāṭhaptatisthā dayakā || yajamāna puṣpabhājana || adyādi || vākya ||


śrīsaṃvarttāmaṇḍalānte kramapadanihitānandaśaktiḥ subhīmā ||


sṛṣṭaṃ nyāyan catuskaṃ akulakulagataṃ paṃcakaṃ cānyaṣaṭkaṃ ||


catvāraḥ pañcakonyaṃ punar api caturaṃ tattvato maṇḍarena (!) ||


saṃsṛṣṭaṃ yena tasmai nama(ta) guruvaraṃ bhairavaṃ śrīkujeśaḥ (!) ||


śyāmā raktā trinetrā sṭaṇabhara(!)namitā martta(!) mātaṃgagāmi


vimboṣṭhī cārunetrā pṛthutarajaghanā mekharā ratnaśobhe ||


devāṅge vastraśobhe varavarakusume varvvare keśabhāre ||


sā me śrīkubjikākhyā vitaratu tarasā jñānadivyaughamoghaṃ || (fol. 1v1–7)
