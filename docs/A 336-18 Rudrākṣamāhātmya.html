Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śivāya || ||

kārttika (!) uvāca ||

rudrākṣaṃ kīdṛ(2)śaṃ deva ko devaḥ kiṃ phaṃ (!) mayi |

dhāraṇo(!) kiṃ phalaṃ geyaṃ sthā(3)naṃdehe kathaṃ kutaḥ ||

bhagavan śrotum icchāmi vrūhi me (4)parameśvara || ||

īśvara uvāca ||

śirāsā(!) dhāraṇaṃ(5) koṭi karṇṇayor daśakotījaṃ |

sahsrakoṭI grīvāyāṃ a(6)nantaṃ bāhumūlake || (fol. 1v1–6)

End

ekavaktraḥ śivaḥ sā(4)kṣād brahmahatyām vyapohati ||

dvivaktra devadevo hi (5)govadhaṃ nāśaye(!) dhruvam |

trivaktraṃ mūrdhni devaṃ me strī(6)hatyāṃ harate kṣaṇat||

caturmukhas tu brahmā ca–(fol. 2v3–6)
