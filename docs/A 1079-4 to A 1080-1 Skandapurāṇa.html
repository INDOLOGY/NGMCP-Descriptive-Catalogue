Manuscript Features

It covers only the second half (uttarārdha) of the text and begins with the 50th adhyāya. One folio in the beginning and folios 256, 342 and 404 are missing. This MS has a wooden cover. Some folios in the end are broken in the right margin. There are altogether three almost blank folios in the beginning and the end.
