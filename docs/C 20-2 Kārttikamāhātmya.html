Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

śriyapatim athāmaṃtrya devadevarṣisattamaḥ |

harṣotphullānanāṃ satyāṃ vāsudevam (!) athāvravit (!) ||

dhanyosmi kṛtakṛtyosmi saphalaṃ jīvitaṃ ca me ||

majjanmani nidānau ca dhanyau tau pitarau mama ||

yo māṃ trailokyasubhagā janayāmāsa tu dhruvaṃ ||

ṣoḍaśīti sahasrāṇāṃ vallabhohaṃ yatas tava || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

yo śvattham arccayed evam udakena samantataṃ |

kulānām ayanaṃ tena tāritaṃ syān na saṃsayaḥ (!) ||

alakṣmīḥ kāraka..ca duḥsvapnaṃ durvicintitaṃ |

aśvatthatarppaṇāt tatra sarvvaduḥkhaṃ vilīyate ||

tarpitāḥ pitaras tena viṣṇūs tena samarccitaḥ |

yo .. .. rccayed evaṃ grahās tenaiva pūjitaḥ (!) || || (fol. 49v6–8)

Sub-colophon

iti śrīpadmapurāṇe kāttikamahātmye (!) kṛṣṇasatyabhāmāsaṃvāde ekonatriṃśodhyāyaḥ samāptaḥ || 29 || (fol. 49v8–9)
