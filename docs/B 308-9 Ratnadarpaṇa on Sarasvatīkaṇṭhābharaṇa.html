Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||    ||

ekena yasya yaminaḥ pramadeva deham
arddhena rājati yumān iva cāpareṇa ||
tattvakramā(2)d athacana pramadā pumān vā
śreyaṃsi varddhayatu sa smaraśāno vaḥ 1

śrīrāmasiṃhadevena dordaṃḍadalitadvi(3)ṣā ||
kriyate ʼvaṃtibhūpālakaṃṭhābharaṇadarpaṇaḥ 2

kaṃṭhābharaṇam anarghyaṃ vāgdevīratnadarpaṇotsaṃge ||
(4) asmin paśyatu nibhṛtaṃ prakāśa†savaṃgi†lāvavyaṃ 3

graṃthāraṃbhe samuciteṣṭadevatānamaskāre śiṣṭā(5)cāram anuvarttate ||

dhvanir iti, vācām adhiṣṭhātrī devī dyotamānā svaprakāśaśabdabrahmmarūpā bhāratī katham (6) upāsyate, sūkṣmādibhedena dhvanyādibhedena ca vivakṣito namaskāraḥ | (fol. 1v1–5)

End

aṣṭapa(6)cam eva kavināmāṃkaṃ yathā, rātāvagheti, gīrvāṇasubhaṭaḥ kaścid yuyutsu (!) ddaityasenayā | tatkalakṛtasāṃnidhyāṃ (7) māyādevīm apūjayat | tathā hi vyājo māyā saiva vāggūpā vyājaśabdopapadaṃ vāgabhidhānaṃ yasyāḥ sā mahyaṃ rakṣā(8)stv (!) iti saṃbaṃdhaḥ rātaṃ dattaṃ avadyaṃ doṣabahulam ādhirājyaṃ avadyā ādhayo yatra tādṛśaṃ vā rājyaṃ, yathā viśi- (fol. 177v5–8)

Colophon

iti śrīmiśraratneśvaraviracite ratnadarpaṇanāmni sarasvatīkaṃṭhābharaṇavivara(5)ṇe doṣaguṇavivecano nāma prathamaḥ paricchedaḥ ||    || (fol. 102r4–5)
