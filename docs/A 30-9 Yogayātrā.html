Manuscript Features

One of few manuscripts written in the Śāradā script in the NGMPP collection. The numbering of folios use the decimal notation system (10, 11, 20, 22, etc.). That is, no distinct symbols for 10, 20, 30, etc., are used (only numerals 0 to 9 are used). The manuscript is fragmentary and not in order.
