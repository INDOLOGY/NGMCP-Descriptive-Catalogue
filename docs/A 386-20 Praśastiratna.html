Excerpts

Beginning

tu saṃvaṃdhavati tad gata śrīyolekhyāḥ ||

asaṃvaṃdhe tu putraḥ dharmatvenaikeva śrīḥ |

strīṣu tu yeṣāṃ yādṛśāṃ yā yādṛśas triya |

stāsu tattatpuruṣagatā śrīsaṃkhyā prayoktavyā iti vivecanīyaṃ || svastiśabdānamtaraṃ dīyamānaṃ śrīśabdam api śrīsaṃkhyāyāṃ kecid gaṇayaṃti tan na yuktaṃ, tasya śiṣṭair maṃgalāṃtaraṃgīkārāt ayaṃ ca śrīśabdo matuvaṃto lekhyaḥ || (fol. 9r1–7)

End

svabhāvokti praśastābhi praśastaṃ ca praśastibhiḥ ||

praśastiratnaṃ keśānāṃ kavīnāṃ prītayes tvadaḥ (!) || 1 || (fol. 53v8–54r1)

Colophon

śakedharāṇikhāgabhūparimite 1701 śrucaumāsibhe

samīraṇa suresite haritithijyavārānvite ||

girīśacaraṇāṃbūbhūśrayaṇasārthakālopi yaḥ

praśasti parikalpanāṃ sukṛti rāmabhadraḥ kṛtī || 2 ||

iti śrīmadvijakulatilakakaruṇākaropādhyāyasuta kavirāmabhadrakṛtaṃ kaviprītidaṃ praśāstiratnākhyaṃ kāvyaṃ samāptim agamat || ||  saṃvat 931 mārgaśira kṛºº paṃcami ādityavāºº tha kuhnu śidhayāṅā rucirānaṃdajuna || śubham || (fol. 54v9–2)
