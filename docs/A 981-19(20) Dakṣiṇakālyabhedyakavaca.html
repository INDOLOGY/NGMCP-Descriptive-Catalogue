Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

dakṣiṇakālīkavacasya mahākālabhairavaṛṣiḥ anuṣṭupchaṃdaḥ || śmaśānakālikādevatā || sarvakāryyārthasādhane jape viniyogaḥ || ||

oṃ śmaśānakālikā pātu pūrvaśyāṃ diśi māṃ sadā ||

dvāviṃśatparṇarūpā ca bhaktavaśyā digaṃvarā || 1 ||

bhadrakālī tathāgneyyāṃ pāyān nityaṃ jaganmayī ||

rudrakālī mahākālī kaladvaṃdvāsvarapriyā || 2 ||

vāgeśvarī pātu yāmyāṃ bhayabhītivināśinī ||

vadadvaṃdvaṃ vāgvādinī vahnijāyāsvarūpiṇī || 3 || (fol. 36r5–9)

End

sarve tasya vaśaṃ yāṃti bhūtagrāmāś caturvidhāḥ ||

nityaṃ paṭhati ye bhaktyā kavacaṃ haranirmitaṃ || 46 ||

soham eva mahādevo mahākālī ca mātṛkāḥ ||

dharmmārthakāmamokṣāś ca tasya nityaṃ karaṃ sthitāḥ || 47 ||

anyasya varadaḥ sopi nityaṃ bhavati paṃḍitaḥ ||

kavitvaṃ satyavāditvaṃ satataṃ tasya jāyate || 48 ||

vadet ślokaṃ sahaśrāṇi (!) bhavec chrutidharas tathā ||

likhitaṃ yasya gehe tu kavacaṃ bhairavoditaṃ || 49 ||

na tasya durgatiḥ kācit jāyate pāpadapraṇe (!) ||

grahāś ca sarve tuṣyaṃti vasaṃ gachaṃti bhūmipā (!) || 50 ||

yadrājye vattvacajñosti jāyate tatra netrayaḥ ||

etat te sarvamākhyatam abhedyaṃ kavacaṃ śubhaṃ || 51 || (fol. 38v1–6)

Colophon

iti śrīrudrayāmaloktaṃ kālabhairavaviracitaṃ dakṣiṇakālī abhedyakavacaṃ saṃpūrṇam || 20 || (fol. 38v6–7)
