Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrīparadevatāyai namaḥ ||

śrīśivaśaktibhyāṃ namaḥ ||

vyomātītaṃ paraṃ śāntaṃ śivena śaktiśāmbhavaṃ |

bhāvābhāvavinirmuktaṃ śivaśaktiṃ namāmy ahaṃ || ||

oṃ namos tu te mahāmāye iti ||

asyārtho vyākhyeyaḥ ||

oṃkārasya prathamam āha ||

akāraukāramakāraś ceti bhedena oṃkāra ucyate || akāro brahmā ukāro viṣṇur mmakāro maheśvaraḥ || tasmāt oṃkāra ucyate || (fol. 1r1–3)

End

lākulyendusubinduvedasvarabhir nāgākṣarūḍhāvidher

mmadhye nāmavibheditābhṛguśivārūḍho vidhenāntago ||

sūryoyaṃ svarabhedatendu nabhaso mantre ṣayā māyayā

nityādheya namāmi duḥkhadahanīṃ saṃsārabhītacchriyāṃ ||

caturvvarggapradā mantrā caturakṣarasaṃjñakā |

gaditaṃ bhairavo mantraṃ mahāmāyā namos tu te || || (fol. 55v2–5)

Colophon

iti mokṣasopānaṭīkā samāptaḥ (!) || || (fol. 55v5)
