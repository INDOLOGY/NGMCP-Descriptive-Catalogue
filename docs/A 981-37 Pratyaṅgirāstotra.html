Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

oṃ asya śrīpratyaṅgirāstotramaṃtrasya mahādeva ṛṣir anuṣṭupchandaḥ pratyaṅgirādevatā hūṃ bījaṃ svāhā śaktiḥ mamābhiṣṭasiddhyarthe jape viniyogaḥ ||

śrīkubjikovāca ||

mandarasthaṃ sukhāsīnaṃ bhagavantaṃ maheśvaraṃ ||
samupāgamya caraṇau pārvatī paryyapṛcchata || 1 ||

śrīdevy uvāca ||

dhāriṇī paramā vidyā pratyaṅgirā mahottamā ||
naranārīhitārthāya bālānāṃ rakṣaṇāya ca || 2 ||

rājñāṃ maṇḍalikānāṃ ca dīnānāṃ ca maheśvarī ||
viduṣāṃ ca dvijātīnāṃ viśeṣād dharmmasādhinī || 3 || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

vilvaṃ vā pūjayed devīṃ japed aṣṭasahasrakaṃ ||

sahasraṃ labhate svarṇaṃ na daridro bhavet tataḥ ||

śālibhiḥ svarṇalābhaḥ syād godhūmaiḥ puṣṭisaṃpade ||

yavahomena dhānyaṃ syād ikṣubhiḥ caturaṃgulaiḥ ||

kumudair janavātsalyaṃ vakulai caturāṃ śriyaṃ ||

aśokaiḥ putralābhaḥ syāt pāṭalībhiḥ śubhāṃganāḥ ||

māṣahomena kaphaḥ syāt kodravair vyādhito bhavet ||

phalair ve(!) bhainakair bhrānto mriyate śālmalīhūtāt ||

kaṭutailena vidveṣaḥ staṃbhaḥ pītaprasūnakaiḥ ||

vāyuvegāt patatpatrair homenoccāṭanaṃ ripoḥ ||

homo nyeṣāṃ padārthām aṣṭo- (fols. 5v5–6v5)

Colophon
