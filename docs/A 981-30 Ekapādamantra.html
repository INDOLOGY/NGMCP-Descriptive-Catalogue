Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

oṃ hariḥ oṃ yo kalpayaṃti norayaḥ krūrāṃ kṛtyāṃ vadhūm iva tāṃ brahmaṇā pānirṇughdraḥ pratyakartāram ṛcchatu || 1 ||

śīrṣaṇvatīṃ karṇavatīṃ vi[[ṣṇu]]rūpāṃ bhayaṃkarīṃ

yaḥ prāhiṇod ihādyatvāṃ vitaṃtvaṃ yojayāsubhiḥ 2

yenādiṣṭeha vadasi pratikūlamadhāyini

tam eveto nivarttās tvam āsmān ṛcche anāgasaḥ 3

abhivartasvakarttāraṃ nirastāsmābhir ojasā

āyurasya nikṛntasva prajāṃ ca puruṣādinī 4 (fol. 1v1–5)

End

ye te jahyaṃ jasān javo mahyam agne sadā dhiyo durmadā anagrāsaḥ

āvidhyaināṃ chociṣāvidhya taṃtūn vaivasvatasya sadanaṃ yasya 43

yenorapiṃ duṣcaritāso agne jahumartyāso anṛtaṃ vadantaḥ

teṣāṃ vapūṃṣyarciṣā jātavedaḥ śuṣkaṃ na vṛkṣam abhisaṃdahasva 44

yo na svoñjaraṇo yaś ca niṣṭhayo jijhāṃsati

devās te sarve dhūrṣatuṃ brahma varmayamāntaraṃ śarma varma mamāntaram 45

ava brahma dviṣo jahi aṃgirā anuṣṭup ugrakṛtyā khaṭpaṭ jahi mahākṛtye vidhūmāgni samaprabhe jahi śatrūṃs triśūlenakṛn dhāsye piba śoṇitaṃ 46

kuvaraṃ te mukhaṃ raudraṃ naṃdimānaṃ dimāvaha

jvaraṃ mṛtyubhayaṃ ghoraṃ viṣaṃ nāśayate jvara 47

eko maṃtraprayogāya ava brahma dviṣo jahi (fol. 5v6–6v2)

Colophon

ity ekapādamaṃtraḥ samāptam śubham ❖ (fol. 6v2)
