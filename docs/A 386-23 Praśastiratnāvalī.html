Excerpts

Beginning

|| || śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

aviralamadadhārā bhāsitotuṅgagaṇḍasthala-

madhuratarodyad dhānaguñjadvirephaḥ ||

bhavajalanidhimagnārādhitāṅghridvayābjo

gaṇapatir avatu tvāṃ vighvaballīkuṭḥāraḥ || 1 ||

jagati yā kamalā kamālāsana smaraharādisurair asuraiḥ stutā ||

nijagaṇaiḥ sahasā sahasā ʼʼsitā bhagavatī bhavatāṃ bhavatān mude || 2 ||

śrīḥ ||

mama bhavantu bhavāntu bhavāntare dadhati ye hṛdaye sadaye na ye ||

kuśalino ʼnnaghino ʼnaghṛṇodayā kuru suteṣu gurun na tayo niśam || 3 || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

ajānatā hī racitā maye yaṃ buddhiṃ svakīyām iha ced bhaveyuḥ ||

abhud va śad bhramatas tademāṃ viśodhayantām avanau [[hi]] santaḥ || 65 ||

kaviśreṇikṣoṇīpatimukuṭamāṇikyavilasat

padadvando lebhe tanayam atha yaṃ rāmahṛ[da]yaḥ ||

asāre saṃsāre śruti vihitatārādyamanuja

prabhāvāt saṃpūrṇāṃ sa bha[[va]]diyaṃ tena racitā || 66 || (fol. 7v6–9)
