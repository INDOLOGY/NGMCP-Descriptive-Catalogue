Manuscript Features

The text in this manuscript runs from the beginning of the text up to the end of the fourth taraṅga.

On fols. 122v–123v, following the Puraścaryārṇava, there is an advertisement for four books, either published or shortly to be published the Nepālamāhātmya, the Nityakarmaprakāśikā, the Dānakhaṇḍa of the Caturvargacintāmaṇi and the Himavatkhaṇḍa.

There are two exposures of fols. 35v–37r, 53v–54r, 58v–59r, 63v–64r, 74v–75r, 85v–86r and 99v–100r.
