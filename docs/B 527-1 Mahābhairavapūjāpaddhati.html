Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīśrīmahāgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

śrīgurupādukābhyāṃ namaḥ ||

śrīśrīśrīmahābhairavāya nama(2)ḥ || ||

yajamāna puṣpabhājana ||

oṃ adyādi || vākya ||

yajamānasya mānavagotra śrīśrījaya bhūpa(3)tīndramallavarmmaṇaḥ

tolana pratiṣṭhā śrīśrīśrī ākāśamahābhairava prītyarthaṃ paṃcopacā(4)ra

pūjā kartuṃ puṣpabhājanaṃ samarppayāmi || ||

śrī saṃmvarttā maṇḍalānte kramapadanihitā nandaśa(5)ktiḥ subhimā

sṛṣṭaṃmyāya catuskaṃ akula kulagataṃ paṃcakaṃ cānya ṣaṭkaṃ |

catvāraḥ pañcakonyaṃ punara(6)pi caturaṃ tatvato maṇḍalena

saṃsṛṣṭaṃ yena tasmai namata guruvaraṃ bhairavaṃ śrīkujeśa || ||

(fol.1v1-6))

End

svāna taya || kvākāśa kvasamīraṇa kvadahanaḥ kvāpaḥ kvavi(3)śvambharāḥ kvabrahmā kvajanārddanaḥ kvabujagaḥ kvendraḥ kvadevāsurāḥ kalpāntālabhaṭī (4) nata pramuditaḥ śrīsiddhiyogeśvaraḥ krīḍā nāṭakanāyako vijayate devo mahābhai(5)ravaḥ || || svāna mālakosta biya || || astra mantrana vali thvaya || vali visarjjana (6) yāya || || nosiya || vali bhokaruya || sākṣi thāya || ||

(fol.63v2-6)

Colophon

iti śrīśrīśrī mahābhairava (7) pūjāparddhati samāpataḥ || ||

(fol.63v6-7)
