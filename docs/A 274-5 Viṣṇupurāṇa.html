Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namo bhagavate vāsudevāya || ||

nārāyaṇaṃ namaskṛtya naraṃ caiva narottamam |

devīṃ sarasvatīṃ caiva tato jayam udīrayet ||

namostv anantāya sahasramūrttaye sahasrapādākṣiśirorubāhave |

sahasranāmne puruṣāya śāśvate sahasrakoṭīyugadhāriṇe namaḥ || ||

jitan te puṇḍarīkākṣa namas te viśvabhāvana |

namas te tu hṛṣīkeśa mahāpuruṣapūrvaja ||

sadkṣaraṃ brahma ya īśvaraḥ pumān

guṇormmisṛṣṭisthitikālasaṃlayaḥ |

pradhānabuddhyādijagatprapaṃcasūḥ

sa nostu viṣṇur mmatibhūtimuktidaḥ || 2 ||

praṇamya viṣṇuṃ viśveśaṃ brahmādīn praṇipatya ca |

guruṃ praṇamya vakṣyāmi purāṇaṃ veda(sammi)tam |

itihāsapurāṇajñaṃ vedavedāṃgapāragam |

dharmmaśāstrāditatvajñaṃ vaśiṣṭhatanayātmajam |

parāśara(muniva)raṃ kṛtapūrvvāhṇikakriyam |

maitreyaḥ paripapraccha praṇipatyābhivādya ca |

tvatto hi vedādhyayanam adhītam (akhilaṃ) guro |

(fol.1v1-8 )

End

yaḥ śṛṇoti naraḥ pāpair ddija sarvvaiḥ pramucyate |

pitṛ(pakṣa)manuṣyebhyaḥ samastāmarasaṃstutiḥ |

kṛtā tena bhaved etat yaḥ śṛṇoti dine dine |

kapilādānajanitaṃ puṇyam atyantadurllabham |

(śrutvaitasya daśādhyāyān avāpnoti na saṃśayaḥ) |

yastvetat sakalaṃ śrṇoti puruṣaḥ kṛtvā manasy acyutam |

sarvvaṃ sarvvamayaṃ samastajagatām ādhāram ātmāvyam

jñānaṃ jñeyam anantam ādirahitaṃ sarvvāmarāṇāṃ hitam |

sa prāpnoti na saṃśayo sty (avikalaṃ yad vājimedhe) phala |

yatrādau bhagavāṃś carācaragurur mmadhye ta///

(fol.194v10-13 )
