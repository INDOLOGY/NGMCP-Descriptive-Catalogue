Manuscript Features

The MS contains the text from the beginning to the end of taddhita chapter.

Two exposures of fols. 5v–6r, 8v–9r, 68v–69r and 126v–127r.

In fol. no. 91 fol. number is mistakenly written 92 and the following folios count the same number.

In fol. no. 106 fol. number is mistakenly written 107 and the following folios count the same number.

Excerpts

Beginning

|| śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

śrīmadviṭhṭhalam ānamya pāṇinyādimunīn gurūn ||

prakriyākaumudīṃ kurmaḥ pāṇinīyānusāriṇīṃ || 1 || ||

aiuṇ ṛḷk eoṅ aiauc hayavaraṭ laṇ ñamaṅaṇanam jhabhañ ghaḍhadhaṣ jabagaḍadaś khaphachaṭhathacaṭatav kapay śaṣasar hal ||

iti pratyāhārasūtrāṇi | hakārādiṣv akāra uccāraṇārthaḥ |

hakāro dvirūpāttoyam aṭi śalypi vāṃchatā ||

arheṇādhukṣad ity atra dvayaṃ siddhaṃ bhaviṣyati || (fol. 1v1–4)

End
