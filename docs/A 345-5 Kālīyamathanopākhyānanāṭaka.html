Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīnāṭeśvarāya ||

kelyāna || co ||

bhāvahu śaṅkaragauri aradhaṅgā || dhru ||

bhava(1v1)bhaya harahara dhara japa mālā,

kānana kuṃḍali gala muṇḍamālā ||

canda tilaka śira gaṅga(2) bhujaṅgā,

piṃgala jaṭa naṭahana anaṃgā ||

sūtra - nepāla ko adhipati mahārājādhirāja śrīśrī sumati jaya jitā(2v3)mitramalladeva ko parameśvarī prītikāmanāsaño kṛṣṇacarita nāca nācave ko ājñā bha(4)yī haya ||

iti nepālamahīmaṇḍala mahīpatimukuṭamaṇī śrīśrī sumati jaya ji(17v1)tāmitramalla viracite kālīmathanopākhyānanāṭake prathamoṅkaṃ saṃpūrṇṇaṃ || ||

iti śrīmahārājādhirāja śrīśrī jaya jitāmitramallaviracite kālīmathanopākhyāna nāma nā(33v6)ṭake dvitīyoṅkaṃ saṃpūrṇṇaṃ || ||

End

iti nepālamahīmaṇḍa(44v5)la mahīpatimukuṭamaṇī śrīśrī sumati jaya jitāmitramallaviracite kālīmathanopākhyānanāṭake tṛtīyāṃkaṃ ||

Colophon

samvat 804 māghasudi 15 thvakuhnu śrīśrī sumati jaya jitāmitramalladevasana bhā(45r1)girāma pradhānāṅgayā velasa, thva kālīyamathanopākhyānanāṭaka pyākhana daya(2)kāva cocakāva tayā, anahelā yāya mateva, mahāyatnana nidāna yāyamāla ||
