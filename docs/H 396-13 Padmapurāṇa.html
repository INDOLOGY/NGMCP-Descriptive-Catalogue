Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ mahākālāya namaḥ || * ||

ānandavit sindhir ananta ity asā
vajñsya muktiṃ pradiśasy alīpsoḥ ||

avadhyatāho ayam eva goppād
dāmodaro vo',vanurudhya (!) hṛn satāṃ ||

ṛṣayo naimiṣāraṇye śrutvā nāmasahasrakaṃ ||
mukhyaṃ śivarahasyaṅ ca bhūyo devarṣim abruvan ||….. (fol. 1r1–4)

|| nārada uvāca ||

śṛṇvantur (!) munayaḥ sarvve guhyād guhyataraṃ mahat ||
vrataṃ yad abhidhāsyāmi śrutaṃ me brahmaṇo mukhāt || (fol. 1r7–v1)

End

pūjanāl labhqte satyaḥ sarvān kāmān abhīpsitān ||
viṣṇoś caritraśravaṇāt tatvajñām avāpnuyāt ||
ananyacetās taṃ dhyāyan mānavo muktibhāg bhavet ||
tasmāt sarvvaprayatnena kārtike viṣṇu (folios missing) (fol. 93r5–7)
