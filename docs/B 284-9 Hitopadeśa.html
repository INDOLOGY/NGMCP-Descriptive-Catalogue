Excerpts

Beginning

–d iha kathyate ||

mitralābhaḥ suhṛdbhedo, vigrahaḥ sandhir eva caḥ (!) |

pañcatantrāt tathānyasmād granthād ākṛ(2)ṣya liṣyate ||

asti bhāgīrathītīre, pāṭalīputraṇāmadheyaṃ(!) nagaraṃ |

tatra sarvvasvāmi guṇopeta(3)ḥ sudarśano nāma narapatir āsīt, sa ca nṛpati, ekadā kenāpi paṭhyamānaṃ, ślokadvayaṃ śuśrāva(4) ||

anekasaṃśayocchedi, parokṣārthasya darśakaṃ |

sarvvasya locanaṃ śāstraṃ, yasya nāsty andha eva saḥ || (!) (fol. 2r1–4)

End

prāleyādreḥ sutāyāḥ praṇayani(1)vasatiś candramauliḥ sa yāvad

yāvallakṣmīr m-murāre jalada iva taḍin mānase visphuliṃgas

tāva(2)n nārāyaṇena pracaratu caritaḥ saṃgrahoyaṃ kathānāṃ ||

aprañ ca ||

śrīmān dhavalacandrosau, jīyā(3)n māṇḍaliko ripūn |

yenāyaṃ saṃgraho yatnāl-lekhayitvā pracāritaḥ || || (fol. 132v7–133r3)

Colophon

|| iti hitopadeśe(4) sandhirnnāma caturthaḥ kathāsaṃgrahaḥ samāptaḥ || || samvat 781 pauṣa śukla daśamyān tithau ||(5) saṃpūrṇṇā likhitaṃ || śubham astu || (fol.133r3–5)
