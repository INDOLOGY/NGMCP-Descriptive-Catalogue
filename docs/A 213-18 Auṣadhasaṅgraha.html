Manuscript Features

The folios 8,10 and 12 are missing. In the second last folio there is a different text of

Vopadeva whose scrive is Sataślokī saṃ of the date1650 (śake 1515), and belong

to Narayaṇa. At last there is one folio whit different names medical herbs.

Excerpts

Beginning

|| śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

methī guḍucī musalī saṭī ca vidārikaṃda trisugaṃdhi siṃdhuḥ |

dhātrī lavaṃgekṣuragokṣurāś ca śatāvarī movarasau ca yaṣṭī |

kṛṣṇāśvagaṃdhā kadalīpakaṃda nāgāhvajātīphala jātipatraṃ |

śṛṅgī dhanīyā hvayakabphale ca cūrṇān mṛnābhaṃ dviguṇaṃ niyojyaṃ |

sarvaturyāṃśa vijayā sarvadviguṇa śarkkarā |

piṃḍī ca madhusarppiḥs sami māṣo ṭaṃko ʼtha vāmiti || (fol.1r1-4)

End

doṣān dūṣyān alabalavaya kāladeśā [[ʼ]]navasthāḥ

satvaṃ sātmyaṃ prakṛtim asakṛdyaḥ parīkṣa prayuṃkte ||

roge yogaṃ bhiṣaganala svasya sisidhyaty abhīṣṭaṃ

tasmād eṣā liṣita paṭhitā dhīradhībhir nnibodhyā || 99 ||

deśānāṃ varadātaṭaṃtaramataḥ sārthābhidhānaṃ mahā

sthānaṃ devapadaṃ sadāgrajagaṇā gaṇyāḥ sahasraṃ dvijāḥ ||

tatrā ʼmīṣu dhaneśakeśavavidoḥ śiṣyaḥ sutaś ca kramāc

cakre ślokaśataṃ bhiṣaṅmadapaviḥ śrīvopadevaḥ kaviḥ || || 100 ||(fol. 21r1-5)

Colophon

iti śrīvopadevaviracitā śataślokī samāptā || ❁ || || || || || śake 1515 svasti śrīmannṛpavikramārkarājyātīta saṃvat 1650 vaiśākhyām amāvāsyām citravavaṃśotpanna bhiṣagvaryelhopaṃḍitasuta nārāytaṇena kāśyām idaṃ ślokīpustakaṃ likhitaṃ svārthaṃ parārthaṃ ca || (fol.21r6-8)
