Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namo bhagavate vāśudevāye || brahmayajñartha ||

brhma yeka | suddhacaitanya | advitiye dvitiye brahmako māyā ho | brahmamāyā kyā ho bhanyā || bṛkṣa ra chāñā jastai | chāṃñāṃ ra bṛkṣa (nsārā) hoīnan | bṛkṣa vinā chāñā kahāṃ | yestai samjoga cha | brahma suddha caitanne chan | māyā anitya caitannye chan | tava māyā upara brahma chan | brahma upar kohi chainan | tava parabrahma aṣaṇḍa chan | (fol.1v1-4)

End

maithunakana kāma kahincha || 1 || krodhalāī kāla kahincha || 2 || mamatālāī lobha kahiṃcha || 3 || bandhanakana moha kahincha ||4 || jobhanalāī mada kahincha || 5 || māyākana makṣara kahincha || 6 || iti ṣaṭrūpā vicāra ardhuti || gaṃgādyo sanyāye, rahaṭha | ghaṭikānyāye | mṛgatriṣṇānyāye | bhṛgānyāye | lohacūmbakanyāye | yesto ṣaṭmundrā jityā || tyo jivanmukta kahāuchan tesaikana paramātma sorupa bhanchan | vedavedāntamā pani kahyāko cha || || (fol. 3v7-11)
