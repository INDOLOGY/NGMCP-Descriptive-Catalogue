❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīnṛtyanāthāya || tritattvenācamya || kuśabalakha o kāyāva saṅkalpa ||


adyetyādi || amukagotra yajamānasya śrī3sveṣṭadevatāprītikāmanayā amukanāmanāṭakasāṅgopāṅgasiddhyarthyaṃ


śrī3sadānandanṛtyeśvaramahābhairavā(rā)dhanapaṃcopacārapūjanamahaṃ kariṣye || adyetyādivākya || sūryārghaḥ ||


akhaṇḍamaṇḍalākālam ityādi gurunamaskāraṃ || prāṇāyāmaḥ || mātṛkānyāsaḥ || atha kalaṣaḍaṅganyāsaḥ || (fol. 1v1–5)



«End: »


thvanali yajamānādi sakalaṃ thava thāyasa tayāva || ekāneka yāṅāva svāna viya ||


duvaneyāpaṃcasūtrakā svāna thaṇḍiliyā sidharanapaṃ viya || balivisarjjana sākṣi thāya || ||


devanamaskāra yāṅāva saṃpākhana hūyāva cchelihāvaya || ccheṃsa śṇānakāyāva sūtralāsārāva


svastikasa taya || pyākhamp gāyana vāyana khaṭajāyake || khaṃṭajālūya || nṛvacchanādi dīparoharakṣā ||


sagona || cepanathiyāva ghāghara toya || pyākha mosakarasaṃ thathe juraṃ || || thvana pehnu kuhnu khaṭa jāya ||


svastika coyāva 1 āśirvvādaḥ thava thava sagona viya || sephaṃrūpa ārati pratiṣṭhā || devahlāye || (( pyākhana hlūyake )) ||


thvate dhunaṅāva pyākhana thāyayā dvārasa laṃ soyāva akhāla duhāya || devahlāya || matāviya || sagonacchaju viya


thvanaṃ rivakuhmala pūjāviya || mālako satā samaya viya || || (fol. 39v4–40r3)



«Colophon:»


iti nṛtyeśvarasamūlasiddhi samāptaṃ || (fol. 40r3)
