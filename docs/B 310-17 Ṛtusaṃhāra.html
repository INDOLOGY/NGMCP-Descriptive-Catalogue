Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

pracaṃḍasūryaḥ spṛhaṇīyacandramāḥ sadāvagāhakṣatavārisaṃcayaḥ ||

dināṃtaramyobhyupaśāntamanmatho nidāghakālaḥ samupā[[ga]]ta priye || 1 ||

niśāṃśaśāṃkakṣatanīlarājayaḥ kvacidvicitraṃ jalayaṃtramasdiram ||

maṇiprakārāḥ sarasaṃ ca candanaṃ śucau priye yāṃti janasya sevyatāṃ-m || 2 ||

suvāsitaṃ harmyatalaṃ manoharaṃ priyāmukhochvāsavikaṃpitaṃmadhu ||

sutaṃtrigītaṃ madanasya dīpanaṃ śucau niśithe ʼnubhavaṃti kāminaḥ || 3 ||

(fol. 1v1-5)

End

ramyaḥ pradoṣasamayaḥ paṭucaṃdrakāṃtiḥ

puṃskokilasya virutaṃ pavanaḥ sugandhiḥ ||

mattadvirephavirutaṃ niśi sīdhupānaṃ

sarvaṃ hi sādhanam I[[ti]] kusumāyudhasya || 35 ||

bahukusuma suramyaḥ kāmināṃ kāmabhūtaḥ

pathikajanavadhūnāṃ cittasaṃtāpahetuḥ ||

madhukarapikanādaiḥ jātavādaprapaṃcaḥ

pradiśatu sukhamāsu prāyaśas te vasantaḥ || 36 ||

malayapavanaviddhaḥ kokilārāvaramyo

madhurasurabhisaṃgāllabdhagandhaprabandhaḥ ||

vivudhakaramayukhaiś ceṣṭamānaḥ samaṃtād

bhavatu tava vasaṃtaḥ kāla eṣopi vṛddhyai || 37 || (fol. 11v10–12r5)
