Manuscript Features

The text covers from the beginning of the work to the end of the Dviruktaprakṛyā chapter.

Foliation restarts after fol. 75r at the end of the Vibhaktyartha chapter.

Fols. 1–57, 56, 58–75 (first foliation) and 1–51(second foliation) are on reel no. 580/18; fols. 44–125 (second foliation) are on reel no. 581/1.

The sūtras are highlighted in another ink. The numbers of the sūtras are written above them.

Marginal notes are written in all four margins and in some places between two lines.

On fol. 1r is written

(vyākaraṇam) siddhāntakaumudī (pūrvārddham) 74 patra

śrirāmaḥ ||

nṛtyāvasāne naṭarājarājo

nanāda ḍhakkāṃ navapaṃ[ca]vāraṃ ||

uddhartukāmaḥ sanakādisiddhān

etad vimarśe śivasūtrajālaṃ || 1 ||

stanamaṃḍalam āmāgha (!) nakhasya varayoṣitām

kadākarṇayate gītiṃ ramayā saha raṃgarāṭ || 2 ||

On fol. 125v there is a stamp of the Nepal Rastriya Pustakalaya.
