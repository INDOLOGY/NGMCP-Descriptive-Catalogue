Title Sundarīvaradānākhyakakārādisahasranāmabhāṣya

Remarks a commentary on the Sundarīvaradānākhya­kakārādisahasranāma; <<<Cf. 977/13>>> the text is ascribed in the colophon to the Kālītantra.

Subject Stotra

Manuscript Features

The available folios are 10–59, 61–62.

The marginal title kā. sa. bhā. is written in the upper left-hand, and rāmaḥ in the lower right-hand, margin of the verso.

Excerpts

Beginning

///kṣiṇarekhikā ||

adhorekhā bhaved rudromā.. sākṣāt sarasvat⟨i⟩ī ||

kuṇḍalī-aṅkuśākārā madhyaśūnyaptarāśim

kadambagolakākāraṃ kakāraṃ bhāvayan sudhīḥ ||

kakara--- va---rdevāsekatvāt sacarācarākhilabrahmo †drayy†āpteyam iti ||

pralaye brahmādayo devāḥ sthāvarajaṃgamādyāś ca sarve mahākāle līnā bhavanti taṃ kālam asau saṃgrahasya kāli(!)ti || (fol. 10r, 1–5)

End

iti ⟨ti⟩ gaditam aśeṣaṃ kālikāvarṇarūpaṃ

prapaṭhati yadi bhaktyā sarvasiddhi(!)śvaraḥ syāt

abhinavasukhakāmaṃ sarvavidyābhirāmaḥ

bhavati sa[ka]lasiddhiṃ sarvavīrāsamṛddhiḥ

sarvavīrāsataṃtreṇa sa[r]vādhī(!)keti yāvat evaṃ bhūtā samṛddhi[ḥ] bhavati⟨d⟩iti saṃkṣepataḥ proktaṃ kim anyat śrotum ichā⟪t⟫si || 50 || (fol. 62v, 2–7)

Colophon

iti śrīkālītaṃtre īśvarapārvatīsaṃvāde suṃdarīvaradānākhyamedhāsā///

svasti śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || (fol. 62v, 7–8)
