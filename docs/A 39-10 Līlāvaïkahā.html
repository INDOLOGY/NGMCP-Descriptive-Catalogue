Manuscript Features

Fols. 1–32 are damaged in the right-hand margin, and the text on the verso of 83rd folio seems rubbed away, and a label with “Śālivāhanasaptaśatī” in modern Nagari is pasted on it. The last verse available in this MS is the 1305th of the edition. Thus, only two leaves covering the last 28 verses are missing.
