Excerpts

«Text:»

tiryag ūrdhvagatā rekhā una(!)viṃśatisaṃkhyakāḥ

khaṇḍendus tripadaḥ koṇe śṛṃkhalā paṃcabhiḥ padaiḥ 1

ekādaśapadāvallī bhadran tu navabhiḥ padiḥ

caturviṃśatpadāvāpī paridhir viṃśatiḥ smṛtā 2

madhye ṣoḍaśabhiḥ kaṣṭaiḥ padmam aṣṭadalam smṛtam

khaṇḍendavo vedasaṃkhyā vāpyopi(!) śvetavarṇakāḥ 3

catasraḥ śṛṃkhalāḥ śyāmā raktaṃ bhadrāṣṭakaṃ smṛtam

valyaṣṭakaṃ nīlarūpaṃ pītas tu paridhir bhavet 4

kamalaṃ paṃcaraṅgan tu yathāśobhaṃ budho likhet

tasyopari (kalaśaṃ) paṃcaratnaṃ (samanvitaṃ) 5…
