Beginning

satyaṃ kuṭilasaṃkāsaṃ mucyaṃte nātra saṃśaya (!) |

gobhaṭārasakastuthaṃ (!) kṣitikiṭorasodbhavaḥ ||

kharpari netrarogāṇi iti kṛtvā turaṃgakaḥ |

rasako rajako rukṣo vātakṛ(2)t śleṣmanāśakaḥ ||

tridoṣaghnaṃ tu tatsatvaṃ netrarogavināśanaṃ ||

iti rasakaśuddhiḥ || ❁ || (fol.2r1–2)

End

abhrakaṃ ṣoḍaśāṃsena ekaikaṃ tatra nikṣipet (!) ||

nidhāya tāmrapatreṣu gharṣaye(9)t taṃ ca suvrate |

nijavāri tato devi lohapātre tu jārayet ||

rasena saha devesi canākāṃlena (!) kāṃjike |

mṛdv agninā tu niḥkvāthyaṃ praharārdhena (10) jārayet ||

somavallirasaiḥ piṣṭvā vyomasīvaccalānvitaṃ |

śanai śanai (!) haṃsapādyā dāpayec ca puṭatrayaṃ

somavallirasenaiva saptavārāś ca tāpayet |
