❖ laṃbhāvatīpatiṣṭhāvidhiḥ || marjātāthyaṃ vasape || pathame pañcagavyasādhanaṃ ||

sūryyārgha || balipradānaṃ || oṃ athaitān aṣṭau pūrvādikrameṇa pūjanaṃ || || dadhi || umāpataye

namaḥ || gomaya liṃga || śivāya 2 || oṃ śivo nāmāsi || gomūtra || oṃ vahnaye 2 || oṃ agnimūrddhā ||

puṣpa || śṛīyai 2 || oṃ śrścate || dugdha || somāya 2 || oṃ āpyāya || gomaya || janārddanāya 2 ||

oṃ gaṃdhadvārāṃ || ghṛta || bhāskarāya 2 || oṃ tejosi || kuśodaka varuṇāya 2 || oṃ varuṇasyo ||

khādyabhojyopakaraṇasametāṃ gṛhakṣetra gomahikha turaṅga chāgādi ttaṃsa pāta pañjara

kāsāranodi sarrvagṛhopakaraṇa sametāṃ jñānājñānakṛtakarmukha kṣayārthaṃ śaṭakoṭivarṣa

svargārthaṃ brahmaloka viṣṇuloka śivaloka saṃprāptaye yathā sambhava vedaśākhābhyaḥ

ṛgyajusāmātharva vedadhyāyinebhyaḥ sadaiva jñācāryabhaḥ imāṃ khaṭoparisthitāṃ mṛtakaśayyāṃ

dāna tubhyam mahaṃ utsṛje || oṃ kodāta pathet || svasti dakṣiṇā || (exp. 18A5–19A1)
