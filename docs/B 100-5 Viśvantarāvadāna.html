Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namo ratnatrayāya || ||

hnāpāṃ purāpurvakālasa hanaṃ chalapola, vaṃka dhāyā nāma(2) tapovanasa bijyāka vyalasayāgu kha vistālaṃ jinaṃ vinati yāya || gathya dhālasā ||

he (3) bhagavāna virdagyā nāma nagalasa sivi rājāyā kāya visvaṃtara rājakumāra dhā(4)yakāo bijyāka vyarasa chalapolayā padākarmayāgu kha gathya dhārasā bho paramya(5)svara || (fol. 25v, ll. 1-5)

End

thvanaṃli sivi lājānaṃ julasāṃ putrayāgu nāma juka ka(fol. 80v5)yāo dharma yānāo jāliṃ kumāla maṇḍi lāni kṛṣṇṇājini rājakumāli thvapani(6)sahitaṃ || ||

palivāla sakalyaṃ ānaṃdanaṃ bijyākatvaṃ jula || ۞ || (fol. 81r1)

bho palamesvala thva vyalasa chalapolayā dharmmaputra ji thukāḥ || || || (fol. 80v4-81r1)

Colophon

śubha samvat 1055 sāla miti āṣā gā 5 roja 7 sa likhitaṃ thahititola kvābāhālasa co(3)nahma vajrācāryya jogavaja dānavaja cinimāyā cinikumāli thuti hmaṃ 4 samucaya juyā(4)o conagu velasa ati dharmacitta utpati juyāo thva visvaṃtarayāgu astamivratayāgu(5) vāṣaṃ saphu coyā sidhayakā dina jula śubhaṃḥ || || || ۞ ||

dānavajayā||(6)ta coyā biyā sunānaṃ lobha yātasā paṃcapāpa rāyigu jula śubhaṃ || || ۞ || (fol. 81r, ll. 2-6)
