Manuscript Features

Only ten folios of this MS are available. The preserved part of the MS contains the following portions of the Raghuvaṃśa.

Raghuvaṃśa 1.54 – 1.66(exp. 7b)

Raghuvaṃśa 1.66 – 1.78(exp. 8a)

Raghuvaṃśa 4.22 – 4.32(exp. 7a)

Raghuvaṃśa 4.33 – 4.42(exp. 6b)

Raghuvaṃśa 4.89 – 5.8(exp. 9a)

Raghuvaṃśa 5.8 – 5.16(exp. 8b)

Raghuvaṃśa 10.4 – 10.14(exp. 9b)

Raghuvaṃśa 10.14 – 10.26(exp. 10a)

Raghuvaṃśa 14.85 – 15.7(exp. 12)

Raghuvaṃśa 15.8 – 15.20(exp. 11b)

Raghuvaṃśa 15.68 – 15.79(exp. 10b)

Raghuvaṃśa 15.79 – 15.91(exp. 11a)

Raghuvaṃśa 15.91 – 15.102(exp. 5a)

Raghuvaṃśa 15.102 – 16.7(exp. 4b)

Raghuvaṃśa 16.78 – 16.87(exp. 5b)

Raghuvaṃśa 16.87 – 17.8(exp. 6a)

Raghuvaṃśa 17.8 – 17.20(exp. 4a)

Raghuvaṃśa 17.20 – 17.32(exp. 3b)

Raghuvaṃśa 17.32 – 17.44(exp. 3a)

Raghuvaṃśa 17.44 – 17.56(exp. 2)

The folios must have been numbered in the right-hand margin of the verso. However, since the leaves are torn at the right, no foliation has been preserved. Only on three leaves can figure-numerals be observed, which may have been added by a second hand. The leaf on exp. 2 has the figure 78, the leaf on exp. 3b is numbered 77, and on the leaf on exp. 6a – which is a recto side (!) – a hardly legible number 76 can be seen.

The MS contains a considerable number of scribal errors, only a very few of which have been corrected by a second hand.
