Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||


śarabhamālāmaṃtram ||


asya śrījagatkṣobhaṇamahākālamaṃtrasya tatpuruṣeśvara ṛṣir jagatī chandaḥ śarabheśvaro devatā oṁ bījaṃ prakṛtiḥ śaktiḥ [[pakṣirājeti kīlakaṃ]] śarabheśvaraprītyarthe jape viniyogaḥ ||

śirasi tatpuruṣeśvarāya ṛṣaye namaḥ || mukhe jagatī chandase namaḥ || hṛdi śarabheśvarāya devatāyai namaḥ || guhye oṁ bījāya namaḥ || pādayoḥ prakṛtiśaktyai namaḥ || sarvāṅge pakṣirājāya ki(!)lakāya namaḥ || atha nyāsaḥ || (1v1–4)


End

nīlakaṃṭhāya rudrāya śivāya śaśimauline ||

bhavāya bhavanāsā(!)ya pakṣirājāya te namaḥ || 9 ||


parāt parāya ghorāya śambhave paramātmane ||

śivāya nirmalāṅgāya śāluvāya namo namaḥ || 10 ||


gaṃgādharāya saṃdhyāya paramānandatejase ||

sarveśvarāya śāntāya śarabhāya namo namaḥ || 11 ||


varadāya varāṅgāya kāmadevāya śūline ||

giriśāya giriśāya girijāpataye namaḥ || 12 || (fol. 4v9–4r4)
