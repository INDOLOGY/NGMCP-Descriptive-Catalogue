Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṁ namo bhagavate vāsudevāya ||


nārāyaṇaṃ namaskṛtya, narañ caiva narottamaṃ |

devīṃ sarasvatīṃ caiva, tato jayam udīrayet ||


dhṛtarāṣṭra uvāca ||


hateṣu sarvvasainyeṣu, pāṇḍuputrai raṇājire |

mama sainyā vaśiṣṭā⟪ni⟫[[s te]], kim akurvvata saṃjaya ||


kṛtavarmmā kṛpaś caiva, droṇaputraḥ pratāpavān |

duryyodhanaś ca mandātmā, rājā kim akarot tadā || || (fol. 1v1–3)


End

śṛṇu cedaṃ vaco mahyaṃ, satyena vadataḥ prabho |

iṣṭāpūrttena dānena, dharmmeṇa sukṛtena ca ||


adyāhaṃ sarvvapāñcālān, vāsudevaśya paśyataḥ |

sarvvopāyair hi neṣyāmi, pretarājaniveśanaṃ ||


anujñān tu mahārāja, bhavān me dātum arhasi |

iti (fol. 102v6–7)
