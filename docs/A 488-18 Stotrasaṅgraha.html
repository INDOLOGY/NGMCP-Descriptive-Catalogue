Excerpts

«Beginning»

na badhnāti mama niṣṭhuratāṃ manaḥ ||

yaiḥ santyajyapi bhṛtsneha dhanalubdhair nnirākṛtaḥ |

patisvajanahārddañ ca hārdditeṣveva me naḥ ||

vim etan nābhijānāmi jānann api mahāmate |

yat premapravaṇaṃ cittaṃ viguṇeṣvapi bandhuṣu || (exp. 2, 1–3)


«End»

etair nnāmapadair ddivyaiḥ stutā śakreṇa dhīmatā |

tamāvattaṃ yaśas tu mucyate vyādhibaṃdhanāt ||

... yatsahasra tu vāṃchitaṃ labhate phalaṃ || (exp. 64, 4–5)


«Colophon»

iti indrākṣīstavaḥ samāptaḥ || || (exp. 64, 5)
