Excerpts

Beginning

śrī ❖

sauvarcalako varṇana

sauvarcalaṃ tu rucakaṃ tilakaṃ hṛdyagaṃdhakam

akṣaṃ ca kṛṣṇalavaṇaṃ rucyaṃ kodravikaṃ nṛpe ||

tat kālavavaṇaṃ (!) dhanve māgadhaṃ hṛdyasaindhavaṃ

sugandhi rucakaṃ medhyaṃ cūtagandhaṃ ca mecakam ||

tilakaṃ jaraṇaṃ keye sugandhalavaṇaṃ made

pākyaṃ bhāvaprakāśe ca tathā gaṇanighaṃṭake ||

sugandhaṃ kālalavaṇaṃ magadhaṃ kṛṣṇa saṃjñitaṃ (exp. 1a:1–9)

End

(3) jirā, marica, suṭho, lvāṅ, ādhādhā tolā, dālacini, tejapāta, kṛṣṇajirā, pipalāmūla, ekeka tolā, amlaveta 4 tolā, anāradānā 8 tolā, virenun 2 tolā, sauvarcala 20 tolā li cūrṇa garnu yo cūrṇa mahi, tātopāni, madirāsaṃga khānāle peṭako golā, kapha, śūla, arśa, udararoga, dūra garāyera vāyulāi anulomana garāucha ruci baḍhāucha mukha svādilo tulāucha shī (!) pa u de (exp.1a:49–55)
