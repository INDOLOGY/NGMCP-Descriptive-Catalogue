Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namo nārāyaṇāya

satpakṣaprasaraḥ satām parimala prodvo‥ ‥ ‥
ndamādhvīkabhūḥ
īśasyaiva niveśitaḥ padayu(2)ge bhṛṃgāyamāṇaṃ bhramac
ceto me ramayattvavighnam anagha nyāyaprasūnāṃjaliḥ | 1 |

(svargāpavargayo mārgam āmananti) manīṣIṇaḥ |
yad upāstim asāvatra paramātmā (3) nirūpyate | 2 | (fol. 1v1–3)

End

asmākaṃ tu nisargasundaracirāc ceto nimagnaṃ tvayī
tvad dhānaṃdanidhau tathāpi taralaṃ nādyāpi saṃtaptaye
tan nātha (11) tvaritaṃ vidhe hi karuṇāṃ yena tvad ekāgratāṃ
yāte cetasi nāṇuvānyaśataśo yāmyāḥ punar yātanāḥ

ityeṣa nītikusumāṃjalir ujjvalaśrīr
yad vāsayed api ca dakṣiṇa vāmakau dvau
no (12) vā tataḥ kim amareśaguror gurus tu
prītos tvanena padapīṭhasamarpaṇena | (fol. 33r10–12)

Colophon

ityācāryaśrīmadudayanaviracitaṃ nyāyakusumāṃjaliprakaraṇaṃ samāptaṃ śubham astu parameśvaro vijayate (13) śāke 1803 saṃvat 1937 miti caitrakṛṣṇadvādaśyāṃ ravau samāpitam atrigotreṇa naiyāyikena didvadvareṇa gaṃgādattaśarmaṇā ’dhyayanārtham iti vidantu vidvāṃsaḥ laṃbodaro vijayate (fol. 33r12–13)
