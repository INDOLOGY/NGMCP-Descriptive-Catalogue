Manuscript Features

The title of the text is wrongly mentioned as the Skandapurāṇa(Kāśīkhaṇḍa) in the catalogue cards and PTL.

Fols. 19, 25, 68–69 and 72–110 are missing.

Few lines are missing on fol. 71v.

There are two exposures of fols. 119v–120r, 152v–153r and 162v–164r.
