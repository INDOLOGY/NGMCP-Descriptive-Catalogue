Beginning

oṃ namo yeka(!)jaṭāyaiḥ

ekajaṭot(!)bhavaṃ sadyaḥ sādhanaṃ ca bhaviṣyati ||

jñātena cādhunā tena sarvvakarmma prasiddhyati || 1 ||

āmityakṣaram uccārya śūnyatā kriyate sadā ||

tadevākṣaram uccārya viśvapadmasya bhāvanā || 2 ||

viśvapadmasya madhye tu tāmikṣarasya(!) bhāvanā ||

kapālaṃ bhāvyate śuklaṃ huṃkāraṃ tasya madhyate(!) || (fol. 1v1–5)

«End: »

paṃcarakṣāye svāhā || samasvākariye svāhā || abhayapradebhyaḥ svāhā || rakṣa 2 māṃ sarvasattvānāṃ ca bhūtapretapiśācaḍākinyāpasmārabhayebhyaḥ sarvatra sarvadā mama sarvasattvānāṃ śāntiṃ kuru pauṣṭiṃ kuru rakṣāṃ kuru bhagavatyai piṃgalokaikajaṭī hūṃ 3 phaṭ 3 svāhā (fol. 15r3–6)

«Colophon: »

iti śrī ārya ugratāyā ekajaṭānāmadhāraṇī parisamāptaṃ

ye dharmmā hetuprabhavā

hetuṃ(!) teṣāṃ tathāgataḥ | he vada(!)

teṣāṃ ca yo nirodhaḥ

evaṃ vādī mahāśramaṇaṃ || ❁ || samvat 1015 miti āśuna kṛṣṇa 11 somavāradine saṃpūrṇam likhiteyaṃ nepālamaṇḍale ṭhahiṭiṭole maitrīpuramahāvihāre sthita śrī vajrācārya jogapālavarṣa 55 gate idaṃ pustakaṃ likhāpitaṃ yadi śuddham aśuddhaṃ vā śodhanīyaṃ mahadbudhaiḥ śubhaṃ || (fol. 15v1–6)
