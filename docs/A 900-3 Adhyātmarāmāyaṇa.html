Beginning

śrīganeśāya namaḥ ||

atha rāmagītā ||

śrīmahādeva uvāca ||

tato jaganmaṃgalamaṅgalātmanā
vidhāya rā(2)māyaṇakīrttim uttamam ||
cacāra pūrvācaritaṃ raghūttamo
rājarṣivaryair abhisevitaṃ yathā || 1 ||

saumitri(3)ṇā pṛṣṭa udārabuddhinā
rāmaḥ kathā prāha purātanīś śubhāḥ ||
rājñaḥ pramattasya nṛgasya śāpato
dvijasya (4) tiryaktvam athāha rāghavaḥ || 2 || (fol. 1v1–4)

(3) sohaṃ svapādāmcitareṇubhiḥ spṛśan
