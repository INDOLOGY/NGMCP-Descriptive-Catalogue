Excerpts

Beginning of the root text

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

śrīmahādeva uvāca ||

tato jaganmaṃgalamaṃgalātmanā
vidhāya rāmāyaṇakīrttim uttamām ||
cacāra pūrvācaritaṃ raghūttamo
rājarṣivaryar abhisevitaṃ yathā || 1 || (fol. 1v3–5)

Beginning of the commentary

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

sakala saṃsāramā maṃgala rūpa bhayākā | estā rāmacandrale | rāmāyaṇaki kirti ra | rāvaṇādi duṣṭa daityakā vadhale | āphnu jasa calāi rājarṣimā kā śreṣṭha kakusthādi herule sevā gariyāko | paraṃparādharmakana sevā gardā bhayā || 1 || (fol. 1v1–2 and 7)

End of the root text

vijñānam etad akhilaṃ śrutisāram ekaṃ
vedāṃtavedyacaraṇena mayaiva gītam ||
yaḥ śraddhayā parip⟪e⟫aṭhed gurubhaktiyukto
madrūpam eti yadi madvacaneṣu bhaktiḥ || 62 || (fol. 19v4–5 and 20r2–3)

End of the commentary

yo jñāna vedako sāra | vedāṃtakana vedāṃtaile jāninyā rāma nāma bhayākā brahmale baṭāyāko jo manuṣya śraddhāle paḍhos tyo pani gurumā bhakti bhayāko yasto bhakta aṃtakālamā ma viṣe lina huṃcha bhani rāmacaṃdrale lakṣmaṇa prati ājñā gardā bhayā he pārvatiī bhani kana mahādeva ājñāgardā bhayā || 62 ||    || (fol. 19v1, 6 and 20r1, 5–6)

Colophon of the root text

iti śrīmadadhyātmarāmāyaṇe umāmaheśvarasaṃvāde uttarakāṃḍe śrīrāmagītā samāptāḥ(!) ||    || śubham ||    || (fol. 20r3–4)

Colophon of the commentary

iti śrīrāmagītābhāṣā saṃpūrṇam(!) ||    || śubham ||    || (fol. 20r6)
