Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

kailāsaśikharāsīnaṃ praṇipatya jagadguruṃ ||

papracha (!) pārvatīnāthaṃ vinayānamrakaṃ dharā || 1 ||

devy uvāca ||

deva deva parabrahma saṃsārārṇavatāraka ||

kena siddhir bhavec chīghraṃ kālikāmaṃtram uttamaṃ || 2 || (fol. 20v4–6, exp. 22t)

End

paṭhanān maṃtrasiddhiḥ syād dhāraṇāc ca na saṃśayaḥ ||

yasya gehe sthitaṃ cedaṃ caṃdanena vileritam (!) || 29 ||

kamalā niścalā tatra sadā tiṣṭhati pārvati || 29 ||

yaḥ śṛṇoti nirāhāraḥ śucis tadgatamānasaḥ ||

aṣṭamyāṃ ca caturdaśyāṃ tasya siddhiḥ kare sthitā || 30 || (fol. 23r3–6, exp. 24b)

Colophon

iti śrīkālīkulāmṛte kālīkavacaṃ saṃpūrṇaṃ || 4 || (fol. 23r6, exp. 24b)
