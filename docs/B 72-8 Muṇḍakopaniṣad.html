Excerpts

Beginning

[Ṭīkāṃśa]

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

oṃ brahmādevānām ityādyātharvaṇopaniṣadasyāś (!) ca vidyāsaṃpradāyakartṛpāraṃparyalakṣaṇaṃ saṃbandham ādāvevāha svayam evaṃ stutyartham evaṃ hi mahadbhiḥ paramapuruṣārthasā(2)dhanatvena guruṇā māsena labdhvā vidyeti śrotṛbuddhiprarocanāya †vidyāṃ mahīkaroti† stutyā prarocitāyāṃ vidyāyāṃ hi sādarāḥ pravartteyur iti prayojanena tu vidyāyā sādhyasādhanalakṣa(3)ṇaṃ saṃbaṃdham uttaratra vakṣyati |

bhidyate hṛdayagrathir (!) ityādinā | (fol. 1v1–3)

[Mūlāṃśa]

oṃ brahmā devānāṃ prathamaḥ saṃvbabhūva viśvasya karttā bhuvanasya goptā |

sa brahmavidyāṃ sarvavidyāpratiṣṭhāṃm (!) atharvāya jyeṣṭhaputrāya prāha | a(7)tharvaṇe yāṃ pravadeta brahmātharvā tāṃ purovācāṃgire brahmavidyāṃ |

sa bhāradvājāya satyavāhāya prāha bhāradvājoṃgirase parāvarāṃ || 1 || (fol. 2r6–7)

End

[Mūlāṃśa]

teṣām evaitāṃ brahmavidyāṃ vadeta śirovrataṃ vidhivad yais tu cīrṇaṃ tad etat satyam ṛṣī(8)raṃgirāḥ purovāca naitad acīrṇavrato [ʼ]dhīte | namaḥ paramaṛṣibhyo namaḥ paramaṛṣibhyaḥ (fol. 20v7–8)

[Ṭīkāṃśa]

pāraṃparyakrameṇa saṃprātebhyo namaḥ paramarṣibhyaḥ paramaṃ brahmasākṣād dṛṣṭavaṃto ye brahmādayo ʼvagatavṛta(12)ś ca te paramarṣayas tebhyo bhūyo pi namaḥ dvirvacanam atyādarārthaṃ muṃḍakaparisamārthaṃ (!) ca 13 (fol. 20v11–12)

Colophon

[Mūlāṃśa]

iti muṃḍakopaniṣat || || || || (fol. 20v8)

[Ṭīkāṃśa]

iti śrīgoviṃdabhagavatpūjyapādaśiṣyasya paramahaṃsaparivrājakācāryasya śrīmacchaṃ(13)karabhagavata kṛtāvātharvaṇopaniṣadvivaraṇaṃ samāptaṃ || (fol. 20v12–13)
