Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṁ namo maitreyanāthāya ||

evam mayā śrutam ekasmiṃ samaye bhagavān rājagṛhe viharati sma || veṇuvane kalandakanivāse mahatā bhikṣusaṃghena sārddham arddhatrayodaśabhir bhikṣuśataiḥ || atha khalv āyuṣmāñ

chāriputro mahāprājño dharmasenāpatir vibhuḥ |

lokasyānukampārthaṃ śāstāraṃ paripṛcchati ||

yo ʼsāv ā(!)nāgato buddho nirddiṣṭo lokanāyakaḥ |

maitreya iti nāmnāʼsau sūtre pūrvāparāntike |

tasyāhaṃ vistarañ caivaṃ śrotum icchāmi nāyaka |

ṛddhiñ caityānubhāvañ ca tan me brūhi narottama ||

athainam avadac chāstā vyākariṣyāmy ahan tava |

vistaran tasya buddhasya maitreyasya śṛṇo(!) hi me || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

parinirvṛttasya tasyaiva maitreyasya mahāmuneḥ |

da⟨r⟩śavarṣasahasrāṇi saddhadharmaḥ sthāsyate tadā |

praśadayiṣyatha cittāni tasmiṃ śākyamunau jine ||

tatro†(drantatha)† maitreyaṃ sambuddhaṃ dvipadottamaṃ ||

idam āścaryakaṃ śrutvā iām ṛddhim anuttamāṃ |

ko ⟨d⟩ vidān na prasīdeta api kṛṣṇā hi jātikaḥ ||

tasmād ihātmakāmena mahātmyam abhikāṃkṣatā |

sarddharmo guru(!) ka[r]ttavyaḥ smaratāṃ buddhaśāsanaṃ || ❁ || (fol. 6r5–6v1)

Colophon

iti maitreyavyākaraṇaṃ nāma mahāyānasūtraṃ samāptaṃ ||      ||

ye dharmā hetuprabhavā

hetu[s] teṣān tathāgato hy avadat

teṣāṃ ca yo nirodha

evaṃvādī mahāśramaṇaḥ ||     ||

śubham astu || ○ ||

abde glāvagnidvirade śitamāghe guhānane |

pūṣāyāṃ pūṣṇadivase, sarvvānandasamāptakṛt ||     || (fol. 1–3)
