Manuscript Features

The text runs from the very beginning to the beginning of the Ajantapuṃliṅga­prakaraṇa chapter (up to the first half of arthavadadhāturapratyayaḥ prātipadikam (Pāṇ 1. 2. 45)).

In the middle of the first page is written manoramā śeṣānandasyedaṃ.

At the top and in the left margin of fol. 1v are some sentences from the Śabdaratna, a commentary on the Prauḍhamanoramā.
