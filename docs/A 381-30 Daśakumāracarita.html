Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

sāṃvāya namaḥ || 

śrīmad gurucaraṇasmaraṇaṃ śaraṇaṃ mamās tu ||

śratve bhuvanavṛttāṃtaṃ pātālavṛttām ityarthaḥ viṣṭapaṃ bhavanaṃ gajad ityamaraḥ vāntī prakṛttir vṛttāṃta ity api uttamāṃganānāyikā strī yoṣid acalenyuprakamya viśeṣās tvaṃganā bhīrur ity amaraḥ vismayena vika |sate akṣiṇī locane yasyāḥ seti vismayodbhutam āścāryam ity amaraḥ locanaṃ nayanaṃ netram īkṣaṇaṃ cakṣuraṇīty (!) amaraḥ sasmitam iti kriyāviśeṣaṇaṃ sahāsam ity arthaḥ (fol. 1v1–5)

End

yogān vividhopāyān |

yogoyuktec esagatyāṃ kārmaṇālaś ca lābhayoḥ |

dehadārghaprayoge ca viskabhādau tathātmani | (!)

upāye bheṣaje vidbhir dṛṣṭi sahanane dhane | (!)

vissrabdhe patini dhyāne yukti nyāye ca yottane (!)

iti mahāyaḥ || || (!) (fol. 81r8–81v1)

Colophon

iti sakalaśāstrārthasārthakīkṛta śemaṣī (!) vilāsasāṃdrapravarttitāsaṃkhyayaśaḥ sama dra (!) vidvad pariṣac caṃdra yatīndra sarvavidyānidhāna kavīndrācāryasarasvatīkṛtāyāṃ daśakumāravyākhyāyāṃ padacaṃdrikābhidhāyāṃ viśrutacaritaṃ nāmāṣṭama ullāsaḥ || 8 ||  samāptaś cāyaṃ daṃḍikṛto graṃthaḥ ||

|| śrīgaṇapataye ṇamaḥ (!) śrīviśeśarāya (!) (fol. 81v1–4)
