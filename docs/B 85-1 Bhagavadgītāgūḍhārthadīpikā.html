saccidānaṃdarūpaṃ tat pūrṇaṃ viṣṇoḥ paraṃ padaṃ ||

yatprāptaye samārabdhāḥ vedāḥ kāṃḍatrayātmakāḥ 4 ||


karmmopāstis tathā jñānam iti kāṃḍatrayaṃ kramāt ||

tadrūpāṣṭādaśādhyāyī gītākāṃḍatrayātmikā ||


ekam ekena ṣaḍkena kāṃḍam atropalakṣayet ||

karmma⟪‥⟫niṣṭhājñānaniṣṭhe kathite prathamāntyayoḥ ||

yataḥ samuccayo nāsti tayor ativirodhataḥ ||

bhagavadbhaktiniṣṭhā tu madhyame parikīrttitā


ubhayānugatā sā hi sarvvavighāpanodinī ||

karmmamiśrā ca śuddhā ca jñānamiśrā ca sā tridhā ||


tatra tu prathame kāṃḍe karma tattyāgavartmanā ||

tvaṃ padāṛtho viśuddhātmā sopapatti nirūpyate ||


dvitīye bhagavadbhaktiniṣṭhāvarṇanavartmanā ||

bhagavān paramānaṃdas tatpadārtho ʼvadhāryate || (fol. 1v1–2r1)
