Title *Pañcadaśīpadadīpikāsahita

Remarks This is the commentary by Rāmakṛṣṇa on the basic text Pañcadaśī by Vidyāraṇya.

Author Vidyāraṇya, Rāmakṛṣṇa

Subject Vedānta

Language Sanskrit

Material paper

State incomplete

Lines per Folio 8– 9, 13–16

Foliation figures in the upper left-hand margin of the verso under the abbreviation paṃ. da. and lower right-hand margin of the verso under the word rāma.

Manuscript Features

The number 5 is double maintained but text is not repeated.

Foll. 38-63 are missing.

The commentary has been written above and below of the basic text.

The text bears these following chapters Tattvaviveka, Mahābhūtaviveka, Pañcakośaviveka, vdaitaviveka, Mahāvākyaviveka, Citradīpa, Tṛptidīpa, Kūṭasthadīpa, Dhyānadīpa, Nāṭkadīpa, Yogānanda, Ātmānanda, Avdaitānanda Vidyānanda, Viṣayānanda.

Excerpts

«Beginning of the basic text:»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

namaḥ śrīśaṃkarānaṃdagurupādāmbujanmane

savilāsamahāmohagrāhagrāsaikakarmaṇe || 1 || (fol. 1v7)

«Beginning of the commentary of the basic text:»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

natvā śrībhāratī[[tī]]rthavidyāraṇyamunīśvarau

pratyaktattvavivekasya kriyate padadīpikā || 1 ||

prārīpsitasya (!) graṃthasyā(2)vidhnena parisamāptipracayagamanābhyāṃ viśiṣṭācārapariprāptam iṣṭadevatāgurunamaskāralakṣaṇaṃ maṃgalācaraṇaṃ svenānuṣṭhitaṃ śiṣyaśikṣā(3)rthaṃ ślokenopanibadhnāti. | arthād viṣayaprayojane sūcayati namaḥ iti . || śaṃ sukhaṃ karotīti śaṃkaraḥ | sakalajagadānandakaraḥ paramātmā. eṣa (4) hy evānandayatīti śruteḥ. ānandaḥ. niratiśayapremāspadatvena paramānandarūpaḥ pratyagātmā. (fol. 1v1–4)

«End of the basic text:»

brahmānaṃdābhide graṃthe paṃcamo dhyāya īritaḥ

viṣayānaṃda e(5)tena dvāreṇāṃtaḥ praviśyatāṃ 34

priyād (!) dhariharau tena brahmānaṃdena sarvadā

pāyāc ca prāṇinaḥ sarvvān cāśritān chuddhamānasā(6)n 35 (fol. 140r4–6)

«End of the commentary of the basic text:»

bhedakopādhivarjjanād ity uktaṃ tāneva bhedakopādhīn āha śāṃtā iti eteṣāṃ parihāraḥ kenopāyenety āśaṃkyāha | yogād i⁅ti⁆/// (2) tam āha | nirupādhīti tripuṭībhānābhāvād bhūmānaṃdo yam ity ucyate ity arthaḥ 33 graṃtham upasaṃharati brahmānaṃdeti 34 priyā (!) /// (7) || (fol. 140r1–7)

«Colophon of the basic text:»

iti vidyāraṇyabhāratītīrthakṛtau brahmānaṃde paṃcamo ʼdhyāyaḥ samāpto yaṃ graṃthaḥ śubham astu saṃvat 1857 || || (fol. 140r6)

«Colophon of the commentary of the basic text:»

iti śrīmatparamahaṃsaparivrājakācāryyaśrībhāratītīrthavidyāraṇyamuniśvarakiṃkareṇa (!) rāmakṛṣṇākhyaviduṣā viracitām (!) upadeśagraṃtha(8)vivaraṇaṃ saṃpūrṇaṃ || || (fol. 140r7–8)
