Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||


athātaḥ saṃpravakṣāmi kupitākṣaṃgajñānanaṃ ||

pāpahantrī yoginī tu putradā rūpadhāriṇī ||


kupitākṣasya śaktis tu bālā syād vāmalocanā ||

asya ceṭī samādhyātā tayoḥ sādhanam ucyate ||


ṛṣis tu dakṣiṇāmūrttih paṃktiś chando ʼsya devatā ||

bālā bījaṃ vābhavaṃ syāt ārtīyaṃ śaktir ucyate ||


kīlakaṃ kāmarājaḥ syād divāvṛt ṣoṣa(!)ḍaṇgakaṃ ||

yathā tripurabhairavyās parṇanyāsāḥ purohitāḥ || (fol. 1v1–5)


End

kevalaṃ dhṛtahomena varadāḥ sarvvaśaktayaḥ ||

vāmāyāḥ saṃgamāt pūrvvaṃ prajaped vāmalocanāṃ ||


śatamātraṃ paurṇamāsyāṃ viśākhādityasaṃgame ||

sadyaḥ prasannā devī syād vīryastaṃbhaṃ dadāti sā ||


api kāntā satāṃ saṃgenāsau skharati mānavaḥ

ratidevī ratiprītiṃ kāmakīrtiṃ manoramāṃ ||


mama kāmapradāṃ sadyaḥ saṃbhave vāmalocanāṃ

iti bhīraṃ samuccārya vasupktā(!) vāmalocane ||


vīryyaṃ staṃbhaya viproktvā nādaṃ sā kuru cocyate

paṃcaviṃśativarṇo ʼyaṃ svāhānto mantra īritaḥ ||


madanādholakṣājapāt sidhyed vā vāmalocanā || || (fol. *3r5–3v2)


Colophon

iti śrīmerutantre krodhagaṇeśastotraṃ samāptam || || (fol. *3v2)
