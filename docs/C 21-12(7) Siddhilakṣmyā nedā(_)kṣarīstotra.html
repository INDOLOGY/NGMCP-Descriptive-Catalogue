Manuscript Features

After the text Siddhilakṣmyā Navākṣarīstotra in exp. 46t there are some sentences of some unknown text in exp. 46 top and bottom and exp. 47. Letters of exp. 46b are disrupted.

Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namaḥ śrīsiddhilakṣmyai ||

oṃkārarūpā himaṇḍabhadrahāṃ (!)

pañcānanāṃ pañcadaśāyatākṣīṃ |

virājamānāṃ daśabhiḥ karābjaiḥ

śrīsiddhilakṣmīṃ praṇamāmi nityaṃ || (exps. 45t1–2)

End

hariharamukhapadmānniḥsṛtaṃ (!) siddhilakṣmī

stavam iva matiguhyaṃ (!) rājitaṃ mantrarājaiḥ |

paṭhati bhuvi sadā yaḥ kṣīṇacitto ʼpi marttyaḥ

sa bhavati naranāthaḥ sarvvarājendrasevāḥ || 10 || (exps. 46t2–4)

Colophon

iti śrīsiddhilakṣmyā navākṣarīstotraṃ samāptaḥ || || (exps. 46t4–5)
