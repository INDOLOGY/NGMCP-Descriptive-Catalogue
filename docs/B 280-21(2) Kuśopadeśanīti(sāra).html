Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ sāladāyai ||

lāgvo hāti madekaṃ jaraluḥ pūrṇṇandurāsarvvali (2)

śīraṇa pramadā javeṇa turage nitonmavaimadiraṃ |

vāni vyākaranena haṃsa mi(3)thunenna desabhā paṇditaiḥ

satputreṇa kuraṃ toyāva śumaṭilokaṃ traya viṣṇunā (4) ||

thvayā artha ||

hastiyā śva juraṃ mandagīri tava raṃkhayā sobhā juraṃ parena rā(5)triyā sobhā juraṃ purṇṇacandrana stīyā sobhāna juraṃ svabhāvana śahrayā so(6)bhā juraṃ vegana cheyā sobhā juraṃ nitya uchāhāna vacanayā svabhā juraṃ vyāka(exp. 38b1)ranana pṛthīviyā sobhā juraṃ chena trailokeyā sobhā juraṃ nārāyanaṭoṃ || 1 ||

(exp. 38t1-b1)

End

avajyānāṭalojño bhava matihina palijanastraṭastran

prādhānyāghra(5)jatina samipye budhajanaḥ |

budhaistvākta lājyaṃḥ bhavana hiniṭi guṇavati

virppa(6)nā yānitau sakalamava sasidanti jagat ||

thvayā artha ||

rājāna masalaṅā(exp. 48b1)va maṭi hina juyu palijanayā julaṃ thvatena thva palijaṇana syaya pradhāna(2)yāke jyāṇijana pāsasa mavaṃṅa jyānijanaṇa rājā ṭoḍatalaṃ gunavaṭi (3) madayu niti madaṭaṅāva loka vinaṣṭha juyu || 8 ||

(exp. 48t4-b3)

iti kuśoupaese ni(4)tiśāle agaḍhakumālāṣṭakaṃ samāpta || ۞ ||

samvat 801 phālguṇa śu(5)di pūrṇṇamāśi purvvaphālguna ṇakṣate dhṛtṛyoga aṃgāra thvakuhnu thva saṃ(6)phuli coyā juro deva kamācāryyana saṃpurṇṇa yāṅā juro || śubhamastu savvadā ||
