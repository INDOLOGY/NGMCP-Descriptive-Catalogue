Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

yan nāma smaraṇaṃ samasta duritadhvaṃsāvahaṃ puṇyadaṃ

bhaktānām abhayaṃkaraṃ sukhakaraṃ sarvārthasiddhipradaṃ ||

taṃ natvārjunamīśvaraṃ gurukṛpāvāptaiḥ susiddhair varair

yogair vaidyarahasyam adyatanu[[te]] vidyāpatiḥ kautukāt || 1 ||

yataḥ samasta rogāṇāṃ jvaro rājeti viśrutaḥ ||

atojvarādhikārotra prathamaṃ likhyate mayā || 2 ||

aṃśāṃśaṃ yatra doṣāṇāṃ vivektuṃ naiva śaknuyāt ||

sādhāraṇī kriyāṃ tatra vidadhīta cikitsakaḥ || 3 || (fol.1v1-6)

End

iti sāmānyatonupānāni

dīrghadīrghāssubahavo graṃthā saṃti tathāpi me ||

sāṃpradāyikayogānāṃ saṃgrahārthaṃ- m- ayaṃ kṛtaḥ || 6 ||

catuḥ paṃcāśadbhir muni vidhuśate nādhi sahitair

gate ʼbde bhūpārkān nabhasi sitaḥ pakṣe phaṇi tithau ||

iti śrīmad vaṃśīdharatanuja vidyāpati kṛto

bhavat pūrṇo graṃthaḥ sakala bhiṣagānanda janakaḥ || 2 ||(fol.201r4-201v2)

Colophon

iti śrīmaddupādhyāya vaṃśīdharātmaja vidyāpatibudhanirmito vaidyarahasyābhidho yogaḥ || || śubham || (fol.201v2-3)
