Manuscript Features

It is a transcript of some old palm-leaf MS as it is stated on fol. 10v: idam apūrṇapustam aviditasamayāt purātanatāḍapatrapustakād uddhṛtaṃ vikramasamvat 1985 jyeṣṭhaśuklagurupaṃcamyāṃ. This palm-leaf appears different than the one filmed under reel no. B 29/2.

Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namo bhagavate vāsudevāya ||

merupṛṣṭhe sukhāsīnaṃ brahmalokapitāmahaṃ |
sanatkumāraḥ śrīmantam pṛcchate parameśvaraṃ ||

sanatkumāra uvāca ||

ko yaṃ viṣṇuḥ paro devaḥ sarvaśāstreṣu paṭhyate |
kin nu mūrttir amūrttir vā ajāto jāta eva vā ||
saguṇas tu kathaṃ prokto nirguṇo vā jadguruḥ |
kin nu sūkṣmaṃ vijānīyāt kiṃ vā sthūlam ihocyate ||
kiṃ viṣṇuḥ sakalaḥ proktaḥ kin tu niṣkalam eva vā ||
kim avyaktaṃ vijānīyād vyaktarūpaṃ tu kiṃ bhavet ||
kin tu nityam anityaṃ vākṣaro vā ’kṣara eva vā ||
sarvagaḥ kin tu vijñeyaḥ kiṃ vāsarvagataḥ smṛtaḥ |
kin tu varṇātmako viṣṇuḥ kiṃ vā varṇavivarjitaḥ ||
kathaṃ vā sthāpyate viṣṇur lokanātha jjagadguruḥ ||
pratiṣṭhāṃ tasya devasya śrotum icchāmi tattvataḥ || (fol. 1r1–5)

End

iti devāmṛte pañcarātre dvādaśamaḥ paṭalaḥ ||
sahasraśīrṣayārcāyāṃ viṣṇumūrttiṃ tato nyaset |
indrāya gāvo sīty asyāḥ śiromūrttiṃ tu sandhayet |
udvayantīti mantreṇa mukuṭamūrttin tu ⟪vinyaset⟫ sandhayet |
etā arveti mantreṇa lalāṭamūrttiṃ tu sandhayet |
bodhamānīti mantreṇa bhruvor mūrttiṃ tu sandhayet |
akṣannamīti mantreṇa akṣimūrttiṃ tu sandhayet ||
nāsadāsīti mantreṇa nāsikāmūrtti sandhayet ||
bhadraṃ karṇṇeti mantreṇa karṇṇamūrttiṃ ca sandhayet |
gāyani tveti mantreṇa gaṃḍamū (fol. 9v12–10r4)

Colophon

idam apūrṇapustakaṃm (!) aviditalekhasamayāt purātanatāḍapatrapustakād uddhṛtaṃ vikramasamvat 1985 jyeṣṭhaśukragurupañcamyāṃ, (fol. 10r5)
