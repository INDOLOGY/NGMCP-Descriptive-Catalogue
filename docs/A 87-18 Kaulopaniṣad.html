|| śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || oṃm || [[ātmānam akhaṇḍamaṇdalākāraṃ āvṛtyā sakalabrahmāṃḍamamḍalaṃ || svaprakāśamayaṃ dhyāyet || śrī ]]guruḥ sarvakāraṇabhūtāśaktiḥ || tena navaraṃdhrarūpo dehaḥ || navaśaktirūpaṃ || śrīcakraṃ || vārāhī pitṛrūpā || kuru kullāvali devatāṃ || mātā || puruṣārthasāgaraḥ || deho navaratnadvīpaḥ | tvagādi saptadhātu romasaṃyuktaḥ || saṃkalpākalpataravaḥ || tejaḥ kalpakodyānaṃ || rasanayā bhāvyamānaṃ || madhurām[[lalavaṇa]]ktikka[[ṭuka]]ṣāya ṣaṭ (!)rasāḥ || (fol. 1r1–4)

End

paṭhedetāni sūtrāṇi prātarabdhopadeśikaḥ ||

ājñāsiddhir bhavet tasya ityājñā parameśvarī (!) ||

yaś cācāravihīnopi yo vā pūjān na kurvate ||

yadi jyeṣthaṃ na manyetā nandate naṃdane vane ||

oṃ śāṃtiḥ śāṃtiḥ śāṃtiḥ || (fol. 3r7–9)

[ tripurasundarī upaniṣad ]

jayate kāmarūpaś ca kāmyaḥ || iyaṃ mahopaniṣat traipurīpāpam akṣayaṃ ||

paraṃ yogīmiriṣṭe(!) || eṣargyajuḥ parameyaś cātharvaṇeyaś ca mātādi vidyom || || oṃ śāṃtiḥ śāṃṭiḥ śāṃtiḥ || ||

pūrṇamadaḥ pūrṇami[[daṃ]] pūrṇāt pūrṇam udacyate ||

pūrṇasya pūrṇam ādāya pūrṇam evāvaśiṣtayate || ||

oṃ śāṃtiḥ śāṃṭiḥ śāṃtiḥ || || || ❁ || (fol. 3r7:3v3)
