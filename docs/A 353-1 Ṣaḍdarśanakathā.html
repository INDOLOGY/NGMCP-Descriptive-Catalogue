Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrī 3 nṛtyanāthāya namaḥ ||

nāndi me ||

amara apanā || māllava || co ||

namalape sānaṃda naṭeśa || dhru ||

jayajaya tri(fol. 1v1)bhuvananāthadayāla,

yāhune maṃgala raṇajītana hlāla ||

me pu 1 || ||

mahādeva gaṃgā praveśa || ||

nāyasunā || va(2)santa || co ||

paraveśa yāya thani naṭanayā thāsa,

sura nara munigaṇayāta jike āsa ||

gaṃgā sahita vayā deva ma(3)heśa

hlākahma śrīraṇajīta rasika nareśa ||

me pu 2 || ||

mahāaho gaṃgā ji vacana ṅeṅa ||

gaṃgāīśvara ājñā(4) dayakine || ||

mahāślokaḥ ||

maheśvara lokanātha, gaṃgāyā prāṇavallabhaḥ ||

saṃsāra pālana yāṅā, nṛtyabhūmī(5) vayā thani ||

End

mahāgaṃgā, pariṃ duṃ || || (fol. 11r8) ||

mahāaho prāṇa vallabhā thva saṃsārasa bhaktavatsara soyāva aneka loka uddhāra yāya dhuno āva niraṃjana(fol. 11v1) bhāvanā yāya ||

gaṃgājagadīśvara uttama ājñā || ||

mahāgaṃgā, devabhāva me || || || ||

jaya 2 (2) duṣṭanāśini || dhru ||

motiyā hālana tise āsana vetāla,

teja thula ravi, śaśi agginio jora ||

devi(3)yā caraṇa mati nṛpa raṇajīta

ajitasiṃhana hlāla guṇiyā suhīrta || me pu 21 || ||

Colophon

iti śriśrijaya(fol. 11v4)raṇajita malladeva viracite ṣaddarśanakathā prathamo'ṅka || || || śubhaṃ ||
