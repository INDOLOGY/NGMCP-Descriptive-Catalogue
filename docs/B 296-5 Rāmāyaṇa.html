Excerpts

Beginning

atha te vānarāḥ śūrās tadātyarthaṃ praharṣitā[[ḥ]] |

guṇapūrvvābhir āśībhir hanūmaṃtaṃ pratuṣṭuvuḥ |

yenaudāryeṇa maghavā devā(2)surasamāgame |

krāṃtavān yuddhyamānas tu kramatvaṃ tena tejasā

yathā viṣṇur mahātejāḥ pṛthiviṃ krāṃtavān purā ||

trivi(3)kramo mahāsatvas taddhi tejobalaṃ ca te |

yathā iṃdro (!) mahābāhur yajñe vividhadakṣiṇe |

yogasiddho mahāvīryas tat te bhavatu maṃ(4)galaṃ | (fol. 1v1–4)

End

abhiṣikte tu kākutsthe dharmeṇa viditātmani |

vyatītā sā niśā pūrvā paurāṇāṃ harṣadarśinī |

tasyāṃ (7) rajanyāṃ vyuṣṭāyāṃ prātar evāpare hani |

vaṃdinaḥ samupātiṣṭhan saumyā nṛpativeśmani |

te raktakaṃṭhinaḥ sarve kinnarā (8) iva śikṣitāḥ |

tuṣṭuvur nṛpatiṃ vīraṃ yathāvat saṃpraharṣiṇaḥ |

vīrasaumya prayudhyasva (!) kauśalyānaṃdavardhana |

jagaddhi sa-(fol. 78v6–8)
