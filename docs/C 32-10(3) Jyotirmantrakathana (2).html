Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

pārvaty uvāca

deva deva mahādevaṃ sthitisaṃhārakārakaṃ

kathayasva mahāsāram agamajyotiruttamaṃ 1

yasya smaraṇamātreṇa garbhavāsavimocanaṃ

agamyāgamane caiva abhakṣaṃ takṣanaṃ tathā 2

akṛtyākaraṇaṃ caiva apriyapriyajaṃ tathā

asyoccāraṇamātreṇa bhasmibhavati tatkṣaṇaṃ 3

īśvara ucāca

sādhu sādhu mahādevi sāvadhānāndrinandinī

kathayāmi rahasyaṃ ca devānām api durlabhaṃ 4

gopanīyaṃ prayatnena prathanīyaṃ parāt paraṃ

na deyaṃ paraśiṣyāya bhraṣṭāya sādhakāya ca 5 (exp. 22t1–5)

End

yaṃ yaṃ kāmayate kāmaṃ yāṃ yāṃ siddhiṃ cikīrṣati

te sarvve tasya vasagāḥ śambhor iva jagatrayaṃ 30

paṃcaviṃśatibhiḥ ślokaiḥr guhyakālīḍitā (!) yadi

kṛtakṛtyas tadā jātaḥ ārirādhayiṣur hitāṃ 31

paṭhitavyaḥ prayatnena tasmād eṣas tavottamaḥ

stavo yaṃ tripuraghnena lokānāṃ hitakāmyayā 32

prakāśito varārohe tasmāt pāṭhyaḥ prayatnataḥ

vidyāṃ prāpnoti vidyārthi dhanārthī dhanam āpnuyāt 33

muktikāmas tu labhate muktiṃ nāsty atra saṃśayaḥ

āvaśyakam idaṃ stotraṃ guhyakālyarpitātmanāṃ 34

etatstotrānvitā pūjā sahasraguṇitā bhavet

śrīdevy uvāca

yad uktaṃ bhavatā pūrvvaṃ prāṇeśa karuṇāvaśāt

nāmnāṃ sahasraṃ devyāstu tad idānīṃ vada prabho

śrīmahākāla uvāca

ati prīto smi deveśi tavāhaṃ vacasāmunā

sahasranāmastotraṃ yat sarveṣām uttamottamaṃ

sugopitaṃ yadyapi syāt kathayiṣye tathāpi te

devyāḥ sahasra- (exps. 30t4–10)

Sub-colophon

iti hāhārāvatantre atharvaṇasaṃhitāyāṃ uttarakhaṃḍe paṃcamapāṃḍe (!) sadāśivaviracite hariharaśaktiḥ paramavidyāgamaḥ jyotir maṃtrakathanaṃ nāmaḥ trayoviṃśatitamaḥ paṭalaḥ samāptaṃ śubham (exps. 24t1–3)
