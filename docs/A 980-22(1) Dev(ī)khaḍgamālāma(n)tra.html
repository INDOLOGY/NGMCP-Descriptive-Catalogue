Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namaḥ śrīdevy uvāca || ||

bhagavan devadeveśa sarvajña karuṇānidhe |

yuddhaśaktimahāmaṃtraṃ vadasva parameṣṭadam || 1 ||

śrī īśvara uvāca || ||

śṛṇu devi pravakṣyāmi yanmātvaṃ (!) paripṛcchasi ||

tasya śravaṇamātreṇa cakrapūjāphalaṃ labhet || ||

oṃ asya śrīyuddhaśaktikhaḍgamālāmaṃtrasya upasthendriyādhiṣṭhātrī varuṇādityaṛṣir jagatī chaṃdaḥ sātvikākārabhaṭṭārakapīṭhasthitaśivakāmeśvarāṃkanilayā kāmeśvarīlalitāmahābhaṭṭārikā devatā aiṃ bījaṃ sauṃ śkati klīṃ kīlakaṃ khaḍgasiddhyarthe viniyogaḥ || || (fol. 12v1–6, exp. 4t)

End

prapaṭhe (!) yaḥ paṃcadhā syur mālā sunikhilās vapi ||

mālāmaṃtro maho bhāga sarvasiddhipradāyakaḥ || 8 ||

ekatriṃśat sahasrāṇi trilokī mohanakṣamaḥ ||

śuddhaḥ saṃsiddhimātrasyājapas tena vidhīyate || 9 ||

namasā (!) pūjanaṃ devi pādukāṃ pūjayāmi te ||

svāheti homaparatas tarpayāmiti (!) tarpṇam || 10 ||

jayadvaṃdvaṃ samāyuktā stutir uktāṃ tu paṃcamī || || (exp. 13r5–13v1, exp. 4b–5t)

Colophon

iti śrīrudrayāmale śrīdevyā (!) kaḍgamālāmaṃtram || ||

śubham bhavatu sarvadā || || || || (exp. 13v1, exp. 5t)
