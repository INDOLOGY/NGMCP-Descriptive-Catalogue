Excerpts

«Beginning»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

cakrika uvāca ||

govindakeśava hare jagadīśa viṣṇo

jānāmi yady api na te stutiyogyavākyaṃ ||

stotuṃ tathāpi rasanā mama vāṃchati tvāṃ

svāmin prasīda hara doṣm imaṃ prabuddhaṃ || 1 ||

tyattkā(!)bhavaṃ tam akhileśvaracakrapāṇe

anyān yajaṃti manujā jagatīha yaṃ ca ||

mūḍhās tu eva duritaprakaraikadhāmni

sānugrahas tvam api mayy api deva yasmāt || (fol. 1v1–5)


«End»

taṇḍulakṣodapaṃkena viṣṇor āyataneṣu yaḥ ||

anyair anyaiś ca vā citrair likhet tasya phalaṃ śṛṇu || 3 ||

putrapautraprapautrādyair bhukteha sakalaṃ sukhaṃ ||

śeṣe viṣṇuṃ puraṃ gatvā naro mokṣam avāpnuyāt || 4 || (fol. 5r4–7)


«Colophon»

iti śrīviṣṇupūjāśaṃkhacakrapūjāvidhiḥ samā⌠sya⌡ptam || śubham astu || śrīśāke 1770 śrīsaṃvat 1905 sāla miti māghamāse śuklapakṣe 8 roja 5 śubhaṃ (fol. 5r7–8)
