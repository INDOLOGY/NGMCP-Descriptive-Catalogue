Beginning

///svagukṣupayukte muñcaiti śulī tridoṣajaṃ śūlaṃ ||

triphalā lau[[ha]] || ||

lohasya rajasāgābhās triphalāyā tathā traye |

guḍasyāṣṭau tathā bhāgā guḍāmūtraṃ caturguṇaṃ ||

etat sarvaṃ ca vipacet guḍapākavidhānavit ||

lihec caitad yathāśakti, kṣaye śūle ca pākaje ||

loha guṭikā || ||

dhātrīcūrṇasyāṣṭau palāni lohacūrṇasya catvāri ||

jaṣṭīmadhukarejaś ca dvipalaṃ dadyāt paced ghṛṣṭaṃ ||

(fol. 1v1–4)

End

śūlaṃ sarvvodbhavaṃ hanti, paktiśūlaṃ viśeṣataḥ ||

raktapittāṃgadāhaṃ ca, sāmlapittaṃ vamin tathā |

hṛcchūlaṃ pārśvaśūlaṃ ca kukṣivastiguḍeṣu ca |

droga(!)vamanair āmavātanipīḍitaṃ ||

khaṇḍa śuṃṭhi || ||

jīrṇṇe jīrṇṇetya(!)jīrṇṇe vā , yac chūlam upajāyate |

pathyāpathayaprayogena(!) bhojanābhojanena ca ||

samaṃ na jātiniyamāt sonnadravam udāhṛtaḥ ||(!)

(fol. 130r7–9)
