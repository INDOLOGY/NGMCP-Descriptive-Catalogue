Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || oṃ namaḥ śrī atharvavedāya

śaunakīyānāṃ mantrāṇāṃ yathā yathākāma viniyogavidhiṃ vyākhyāsyāmaḥ tatra ye triṣaptā iti sūktena vacājihvāṃ saṃpātyābhimantrya badhnāti medhākāmaḥ || 1 || ye triṣaptā iti sūktena audumbarasamidha ādadhāti badhnāti sarvasaṃpatkāmaḥ || 2 || ye triṣaptā iti sūktena audumbarasamidha ādadhāti grāmakāmaḥ || 3 || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

vidyate svapneti sūktena mukhaṃ prakṣālayati duḥsvapnavināśanakāmaḥ || 1 ||
ajaiṣmeti caturbhiḥ sūktair antyāvasānavarjitāśatrupadeśān vṛścati abhicārakāmaḥ || 1 || agan masvar ity ṛcādvayenā ʼdityam upatiṣṭhate sarveṣu tantreṣu || iti viniyogamālāyāṃ ṣoḍaśakāṇḍaṃ samāptam || viṣāsahim iti kāṇḍena puruṣaśarīram abhimantryā ʼʼyuṣkāmaḥ || 1 || (fol. 30r3–6)

Colophon

iti viniyogamālāyāṃ saptadaśakāṇḍaṃ samāptam || oṃ citsakhāyām iti kāṇḍenāntyeṣṭipitṛmedhapiṇḍapitṛyajñe viniyogaḥ || 1 ||

iti viniyogamālāyām aṣṭamakāṇḍaṃ samāptam || brahmakāṇḍasya mahāśāntyādau śāntikalpe viniyogaḥ || 1 || iti viniyogamālāyām ekonaviṃśatikāṇḍaṃ samāptam || śastrakāṇḍasya jyotiṣṭomādiṣu yajñeṣu sahasrasaṃvatsaraparyantā viniyogāḥ || 1 || iti viniyogamālāyāṃ viṃśatikāṇḍaṃ samāptam || 20 || atharvavedīya viniyogaḥ samāptaḥ ||    || (fol. 30r6–v3)
