Excerpts

«Beginning of 1. Baṭukabhairavāṣṭaśatanāmāpaduddharaṇastotra»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ❁ ||

merupṛṣṭhe sukhāsīnaṃ devadevaṃ trilocanam ||

śaṅkaraṃ paripapraccha pārvatīparameśvaram || 1 ||

pārvaty uvāca ||

bhagavan sarvadharmajña sarvaśāstrāgamādiṣu ||

āpaduddhāraṇaṃ mantraṃ sarvasiddhipradaṃ nṛṇām || 2 || (fol. 1v1–3)


«End of 2. Ādityādinavagrahastotra»


naranārīnṛpāṇāṃ ca bhaved duḥsvapnanāśanam ||

aiśvaryam atulaṃ teṣām ārogyaṃ puṣtivardhanam || 11 ||

grahanakṣatrajāḥ pīḍās †takarāgni†samudbhavāḥ ||

tāḥ sarvā[ḥ] praśamaṃ yāṃti vyāso brūte na saṃśayaḥ || 12 || || || || || || || || || || || || (fol. 5v3–6)


«Colophon of 1. Baṭukabhairavāṣṭaśatanāmāpaduddharaṇastotra»


iti śrīviśvasāroddhāre rudrayāmale ḍamarutantre umāmaheśvarasaṃvāde śrīvaṭukabhairavāṣṭaśatanāmāpaduddhāraṇastotraṃ saṃpūrṇaṃ śubham || || || ||

asitāṅgo ruruś caṇḍaḥ krodhaś conmattabhairavaḥ

kapālī bhīṣaṇaś caiva saṃhārā(!)ś cāṣṭa bhairavāḥ || 1 ||

bhadram astu ❁ ❁ ❁ ❁ oṁ hrīṃ vaṭukāya āpaduddhāraṇāya vaṭukāya hrīṃ ❁ mūlamantraḥ || ❁ (fol. 4v1–5)


«Colophon of 2. Ādityādinavagrahastotra»


iti śrīvyāsaviracitaṃ ādityādinavagrahastotraṃ saṃpūrṇam ❁ ❁ (fol. 5v7)
