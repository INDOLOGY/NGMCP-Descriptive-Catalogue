«Beginning of root text»

dohā

merī bhavabādhā harau rādhā nāgari soya

jā tanakī jhāṃī parai syāma harita duti hya 1 (fol. 3v6–7)


«Beginning of the commentary»


śrīgurugaṇeśāya namaḥ

atha vihārī satasaī kī ṭīkā hariprakāśa liṣyate

savaiyyā

tulasī dala māla tamālaso syāma anaṃgate suṃdara rūpa suhāhiṃ

śrutikuṃḍala ke mani kī jhalakaiṃ muṣamaṃḍalapaiṃ varanī nahi jāṃhiṃ

saṣi deṣi piyūṣa mayūṣa huteṃ suṣamāṃ ati ānana kī sarasāṃhi[ṃ]

viharaiṃ harigopa sutāsaṃga kāhu nisīthini maiṃ vanavīthina māṃhiṃ 1 (fol1v1–4)

...

atha kai kī sthiti

rājala suve hihāramaiṃ hai sārani sarakāra

sālagrāmī surasarita sarajū sobha apāra 6

paragannā goālasaiṃ gāva cainapura nām〈sa〉[ma]

gaṃgā sauṃ uttara tarapha mo harikavi ko dhāma (fol. 2r6–2v1)



«End of root text»


yadapi hai sobhā sahaja muktanau sudesa (!)

poe ṭhaura kuṭhaura ke larame hota viśeṣa 15 ❁ (fol. 338r6–7)


«End of the commentary»

pūrahita śrinaṃda ke munisaṃdilya mahān

hama haiṃ tāke gota me honana mo jajamāna 5

mohana mahāudāra taji aura jāṭhie kāhi

saṃpatti sudāmāko daī iṃdra lahī nahi jāhi 6

gahī aṃkasamatu tāta teṃ vidhiko vaṃśa laṣāya

rādhā nāma kahai sunai ānana kāna caḍhāya 7

❁ saṃvata aṭḥāraha sau vite tāpara tīsarucāri

janmāṭḥai pūro kiyo kṛṣṇacaraṇa mana dhāri 8 (fol. 338v8–339r1)


«Colophon of root text»


iti śrīvihārīsotikṛta satasaī saṃpūrṇam ❁ (fol. 338r7–338v1)


«Colophon of the commentary»

iti haricaraṇadāsakṛtā vihārīviracita saptaśatīṭīkā hariprakāśākhyaṃ saṃpūrṇam
