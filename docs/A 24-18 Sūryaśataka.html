Manuscript Features

The available part of the MS contains part of the commentary on Sūryaśataka 18 and ends with the beginning of the commentary on Sūryaśataka 23. The MS is damaged on both its right-hand and left-hand margins, with loss of text. Due to the damage no foliation has been preserved.
