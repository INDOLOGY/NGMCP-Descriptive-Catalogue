Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||    ||

niyatikṛtamiyamarahitāṃ
hlādaikamayīm ananyaparataṃtrāṃ ||
navarasarucirāṃ nirmitim
ādadhatī bhā(2)ratī kaver jayati || 1 ||

kāvyaṃ yaśase ʼrthakṛte
vyavahāravide śivetarakṣataye ||
sadyaḥ paranirvṛtaye
kāṃtāsaṃmitatayopa(3)deśayuje || 2 ||

śaktir nipuṇatālokaśāstrakāvyādyavekṣaṇāt ||
kāvyajñaśikṣayā ʼbhyāsa iti hetus tadudbhave || 3 ||

tada(4)doṣau śabdārthau
saguṇāv analaṃkṛtī punaḥ kvāpi ||
(ida)m uttamam atiśayitivyaṃgye
vācyād budhair dhvaniḥ kathitaḥ || 4 || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

tathaiva yad vidhīye(6)ta sa vyāghāta iti smṛtaḥ ||
seṣṭā saṃsṛṣṭir eteṣāṃ bhedenaikatra yā sthitiḥ || 37 ||

aviśrāṃtijuṣām ātmany aṃgāṃgi(7)tvaṃ tu saṃkaraḥ ||
ekasya ca grahe nyāyadoṣābhāvād aniścayaḥ || 38 ||

sphuṭam ekatra viṣaye śabdārthālaṃkṛtidvayaṃ ||
vya(8)vasthitaṃ ca tenāsau trirūpaḥ parikīrttitaḥ || 39 ||

eṣāṃ doṣā yathāyogaṃ saṃbhavaṃto pi kecana ||
ukteṣv aṃtaḥpataṃtī(9)ti na pṛthak pratipāditāḥ || 40 ||    || (fol. 6r5–9)

Colophon

iti śrīkāvyaprakāśe daśamollāsaḥ ||    || śrīḥ || śrīḥ || ❁ ❁ ❁ || (fol. 6r9)
