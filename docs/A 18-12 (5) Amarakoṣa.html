Manuscript Features

These prakīrṇa folios contain Amarakoṣa prathamakāṇḍa nātyavarga śloka 19 to 38 and dvitīyakāṇḍa vanauṣadhivarga śloka 98 to 119 according to Rāmāśramī commentary of Bhānuji Dīkṣit
