Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṁ namaḥ śrīāryyāvalokiteśvarāya ||

bodhisatvāya 〇 mahāsatvāya mahāṁkārūṇikāya ||

evaṃ mayā śrutam eka(2)smin samaya bhagavān śrāvatyāṃ vihara〇tisma || 〇 jetavane anāthaviṇḍasyārāme mahatār bhirkṣusaṃ(3)ghena sārddhaṃ marddha trayodaśabhir bhikṣuḥ śa〇taiḥ ||

saṃ〇vahulai〇ś ca bodhisatvair mmahāsatvaiḥ || tadyathā || vajra(4)pāṇinā ca nāma bodhisatvena mahāsatvena || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

idam avocat bhagavāṃn ātmanā te ca (4) bhikṣavaḥ ste ca bodhisatvāḥ

sā caḥ sarvvāvatī patṣat sadevamānuṣāsuragaruḍa gaṃdharvvaloko bhagavato bhā(5)ṣitam abhyanandann iti || ||

āryyakāraṇḍavyūha māhāyāsūrtraṃ ratnarājaṃ samāpta || ۞ || (fol. 106v3–5)

Colophon

ye dharmmā hetu prabhāvā hetu teṣāṃ (fol. 107r1) tathāgataḥ

havartta teṣāñ ca yo nirordha evaṃm vādī mahāśramana || ||

deyaṃ dharmmayaṃ pravalamahāyānayīna paramapāśaka tasya dharmmā(2)tmā, śākyabhīkṣu śrīprabhākalasya 〇 jadata puṇya bhṛvvatācārya mātāpītṛpūrvvagamaṃ kṛtvā śakalasatvarāśi(3)nurttarajñāna phalaprāptayaṃntu || || 〇

svasti śrīmatpaśupaticalanakamara dhu〇lidhuśalita siloruha, śrīmatmā(4)nyasvarīṣṭadevatāvalabdha prasādaṃ de〇dipyamānamānonata, ravikulatīla〇ka hanumadhvaja, nepāleśvala ma(5)hārājādhirāja, rājarājendra sakalarājacakrādhiśvala śrīśrījaebhūpālendra malladevasya vijayarārjya || ||

dānapati (fol. 107v1) śrīkāṣṭhamaṇḍapamahānagale mandu śiṃkvamaṃguṇḍi śrītarumulamahāvīhārāvasthitaḥ tasya puṇyātmā, tavapuṣulī vatu ośvakarjyā(2)sala,

la yautā grahāstavyaṃ śākyabhīkṣu śrīprabhākala, bhātṛ śākyabhīkṣu śrī〇dhanarāja, dvitiyabhātṛ śākyabhī(3)kṣu śrīśākyamuṇideva, bhātṛmātrā śu〇maṃṅgarālakṣmi, prabhākalasya bhāryyā〇jñā sundaliṅamayi, dhanarājasya dārā(4) jamunāmayi, bhātṛputrī dhanaśvalī〇mayī || ||

ete sadānumaṃtena 〇 idaṃ kālaṇḍavyuha pustaka dayakā (5) ||

athaḥ paradeśa bhākhā dānapati jojamāna, prabhākala urtrāpaṇṭhaśa dhucīkṣatra, jhikhāche sanīdhānaśa, tāsirumbu mahāvī(fol. 108r1)hāle, nānā latna rupya caityasthānayā anugraśa ruphīṅaśa nānār jyā yāṅaṃ coṅa velasa,

mana irchā jusyaṃ vayāva, śrīśrīśrīkāla(2)ṇḍavyuha pustaka cocakā julo || e ||

〇 śubha samvat 814 mārgha māsya śukla〇 pakṣa śrīpañcamyāyāṃ tithau urttraphāgu(3)ṇīnakṣatre, ati gandhajogya, jathā ka〇rṇṇamuhutre,

śanīścalavālasale makrarāsigate śavitarī, siṃharāsi(4)gate candramasi || ||

thva dinakuhnu saṃpurṇṇa sirddha yāṅā julo || e ||

likhitaṃ mandu siṃkvamuguṇḍī vantāvvāhāra(5) khaṇḍacuka māhāpāṭakūla, śrīśrīśrīvajradevyā calanasevaka, vajrācārjya śrīśulapatidevana likhyantī || ۞ ||

yādṛsaṃ pustakaṃ(fol. 108v1) dṛṣṭā tādṛśaṃ likhitaṃ mayā

yadi śurddham aśurddhaṃ vvā svadhanīyaṃ muṇīskalaḥ || e ||

udakālana caurasyaṃ mukhikābhyāṃ tathaivva ca

ra(2)kṣatavyaṃ prayatneṇa mayā kastveṃ līkhi〇taṃm iti || ۞ ||

thuliyā puṇyaṇa jo〇jamānaṣya āyuvṛddhil astu śubha || (3)

daivyaṇa śakṛtaṅayatya, śulapatī〇deva he ṇāmnā kautulya yadaṃ

ve〇lekha, śrīpuṣṭakaṃ metadevaṃ dhī(4)rākṣamadhvaṃ

yadī śurddham atraṃ śa〇rvvaguṇadokhavivekā dhīrāgu〇vahītatparā mukheṇa līkhi(5)taṃ yattnā kṣamadhvaṃ paṇḍītām ihaṃ || ۞ || (fols. 106v5–108v5)
