Complete transcript of a separate folio

❖ oṃ nama śrīdhanantarīdevyau nama ||
oṃ nama śrīguruvyau nama ||    ||

matu viśekhaṃ ṇāṇā naḍi parikṣā iti ||    || (2)
vātekhu pāṇḍaraṃ mutraṃ saphenaṃ kapharomināṃ ||
raktavarṇṇa⟪.⟫ bhavet pitte, misitaṃ dvadvbajya bhavet || 1 ||
sannī ⟪.⟫ pātu kṛṣṇa (3) syāde tasya atrasya rakṣanaṃ ||
mutra rakṣana ||
jihvāpiḍākha⟪.⟫lasyas.. śā..tā.. mārudhādhike
raktāṇā(4)ma bhavet pitte kaphe śutrādavāghaṇā ||
kṛṣṇāguṣtāṭasa jñayaṃ sannī pātudhiṅke tu sā ||
miśrīte miśrītāṅgayo du(5)riṣṭa rakṣana vajjitā ||    ||
sthānāṇī codalpavātva hṛdaya pitam evaca ||
kaṇṭhādau śleṣma siraśī trayāṇāṃ tri(6)vidhaṣṭathā ||    ||
trikvanākālavāṭaś ca vartturākorapiṭatha cadurākāraṃ śleṣama ||    || ⟪.⟫ ||
maduś caiva bhave (7) vyāddhi mṛtu kuyaucca bhaikhajaṃ ||
tiṣṇā ca ṭiṣṇā bhaikhaje jvariteo pracālayat ||    || (exp. 3t1–7)
