«Beginning»


❖ namaḥ śrīsarvvajñāya ||


pūrṇṇabhadrayaśomatī kuśīdā vaṇijas tathā ||


somovatiś ca padmākṣaḥ | pañcālo dhūmra eva ca ||


rājānaṃ paścimaṃ kṛtvā varggo hy eṣa samuditaḥ ||


janapadacaryāyāṃ pūrṇṇabhadra iti | buddho bhagavān satkṛto mānitaḥ pūjito rājabhī rājamātrair dhanibhiḥ


pauraiḥ śreṣṭhibhiḥ sārthavāhaiḥ devair nāgair yakṣair asurair garuḍaiḥ || kinnarair mahoragair iti


devanāgayakṣāsuragaruḍakinnaramahoragābhyarcito buddho bhagavāṃ jñāto mahāpuṇyo nābhī


cīvarapiṇḍapātaśayanāsanaglānapratyayabhaiṣajyapariṣkārāṇāṃ saśrāvakasaṃgho maṇḍape yaṃ pravacanaṃ


pratinavaś ca buddhābhyadā yāvad eva manuṣyebhyaḥ samyakprakāśito rājagṛham upanisṛtya viharati veṇuvane


kalandakanivāpe | tatra bhagavato cirābhisaṃbuddhabodhair yaśasā ca sarvaloka āpūrṇa(!) | athādakṣiṇāgiriṣu


janapade saṃpūrṇo nāma brāhmaṇa(!) mahāśālaḥ prativasati | (fol. 1v1–6)


«End»


putra iti buddho bhagavāṃ satkṛto gurukṛto mānitaḥ pūjito rājabhī rājamātrair dhanibhiḥ pauraiḥ śeṣṭhibhiḥ


sārthavāhair devair nāgair yakṣair asurair garuḍaiḥ kinnarair mahoragair iti devanāgayakṣāsuragaruḍakinnaramahoragābhyarthito


buddho bhagavān jñāto mahāpuṇyo lābhī cīvarapiṇḍapātaśayanāsanaglānapratyayabhaiṣajyapariṣkārāṇāṃ saśrāvakasaṃghakapilavastuni


viharati nyagrodhārāme kapilavastuny anyatamaḥ śākya ādyo mahādhano mahābhāgo vistīrṇṇaviśālaparigraho vaiśravaṇadhanasamudito


vaiśravaṇadhanapratispardhi(!) tena sadṛśāt kulāt kalatram ānītaṃ sa tayā sārddhaṃ krīḍati ramate paricārayataḥ patnī āpannasatvā


saṃvṛttā sāṣṭānāṃ cānavānāṃ vā masānām atyayāt prasūtā mahati(!) mahatī māṃsapesī jātāyāṃ mātāpitarau viṣaṇṇāv anye ca gṛhavā


(exp.123b6–11)
