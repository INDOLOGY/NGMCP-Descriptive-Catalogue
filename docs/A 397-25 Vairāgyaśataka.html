Excerpts

Beginning

|| śrīgurugaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

dikkālād anavachinnā (!) naṃdacinmātramūrttaye ||

svānubhūtyaikamānāya namaḥ śāṃtāya vedhase || 1 ||

yoddhāro matsaragrastā prabhavaḥ smayadūṣitāḥ ||

avodhopahatāś cānye jīrṇamaṃge subhāṣitaṃ || 2 ||

ajñaḥ sukham ārādhyaḥ sukhataram ārādhyate viśeṣajñaḥ ||

jñānalavadurvidagdhaṃ brahmāpi naraṃ na raṃjayati || 3 || (!) (fol. 1v1–3)

End

vairāgye saṃcared eko nītau bhramati kaścana ||

śṛṃgāre ramate kaścid rucibhedāḥ parasparaṃ || 11 ||

nātirucitaṃ na tasya tasya spṛhā manojñepi ||

ramaṇiyepi sudhāṃśau na nāma kāmaḥ sarojinyāḥ || 12 || (fol. 33r5–6)

Colophon

iti śrībhartṛhariyogīndraviracitāyāṃ subhāṣitaratnāvalyāṃ vairāgyaśatake avadhūtacaryānāma daśamaṃ daśakaṃ || 10 || iti bhartṛhari śatatrayaṃ śubham śrīśāke 1727 vaiśāṣa 8 śubham || || (fol. 33r6–7)
