Excerpts

«Beginning»

atha gomūtrasādhanaṃ || ||

atha yajñapratiṣṭhādau vāstupūjādau sūtakāśaucāṃte vā nityaṃ kāyapavitrīkaraṇārthaṃ vāvaśyakaṃ gomūtraprāśanaṃ karttavyaṃ || (exp. 3t1–2)


«End»

oṃ sviṣṭakṛte svāhā | idaṃ sviṣṭakṛte || iti hutvā hutaśeṣasviṣṭakṛtasahitaṃ tṛtīyabhāgagomūtraṃ | suvarṇaṃ raupyatāmrapātrasthitabrahmatīrthena vā praṇavasahitaṃ karṣamātraṃ vā pibet || || (exp. 5t2–3)


«Colophon»

iti gomūtraprāśanavidhiḥ || || || (exp. 5t3)
