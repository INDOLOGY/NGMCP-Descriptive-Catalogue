Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgurugaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

devaṃ praṇamya gopālaṃ vaidyagopāladāsajaḥ |

santoṣātanayaś chando gaṃgādāsas tano(2)ty adaḥ || 1 ||

saṃnti (!) yadyapi bhūyāṃsaś chandogranthā manīṣiṇāṃ ||

tathāpi sāram ākṛṣya lavakārtho mamodyamaḥ || 2 ||

iya(3)m acyutalīlāḍhyā sadvṛttājāliśālinī ||

chandasāṃ mañjarīkāntā sabhyakaṇṭhe lagiṣyati || 3 ||

padyaṃ catuṣpadī tac ca, vṛttaṃ (4)jātir iti dvidhā ||

vṛttam akṣarasaṃkhyātaṃ jātir mātrākṛtā bhavet || 4 || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

vyavahāroditaṃ prāyo mayā cahṃdo ʼ[[tra]]kīrttitaṃ ||

prastārādi puna(4)r noktaṃ kevalaṃ kautukaṃ hi tat || 5 ||

sargaiḥ ṣoḍaśabhiḥ samujjvalapadair navyārthabhavyāśayair

yenākāri tad acyutasya caritaṃ kāvyaṃ kaviprī(5)tidaṃ ||

kaṃsāreḥ śatakaṃ deneśaśatakaṃ dvaṃdaṃ ca tasyāstv asau

gaṃgādāsakaveḥ śrutau †kuṭukināṃ† śrīchaṃdasāṃ maṃjarī || || || || (fol. 28r3–5)

Colophon

iti chaṃdomaṃjaryyāṃ gadyaprabhedo nāma ṣaṣṭhaḥ stavakaḥ samāptaḥ || || || śubham astu || || || || śubham || || ||

(7)śāke śailākṣijīmūte śuklayāṣāḍhasaṃjñake ||

sarpe tithau somavāre bhogānaṃdo lilekha tāṃ || || 1 || || śubhaṃ || (fol. 28r6–7)
