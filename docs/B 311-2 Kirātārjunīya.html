Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

śriyaḥ kuruṇām adhipasya pālanīṃ

prajāsu vṛttiṃ yam ayuṃkta vedituṃ ||

savarṇaliṃgī viditaḥ samāyayau,

yudhiṣṭhiraṃ dvaitavane vanecaraḥ || 1 || (fol. 1v1–2)

End

vajra jaya ripulokaṃ pādamadmānata śrīr<ref name="ftn2">Edition reads: pādapadmānataḥ san</ref>

ggadita iti bhavena<ref name="ftn3">different reading: śivena</ref> ślāghito devasaṃghaiḥ ||

nijagṛham atha gatvā sādaraṃ pāṇḍuputro

dhṛtagurujayalakṣmīr ddharmasūnuṃ nanāma || 48 ||     || (fol. 146r1–2)

Colophon

iti kirātārjjuṇī(!)ye mahākāvye lakṣmyaṅke bhāravīkṛtau kalyāṇasamyallābho nāmāṣṭadaśaḥ sarggaḥ samāptaḥ || 18 ||      ||

samvat 931 phālguṇaśuklatrayodaśyāṃ guruvāre taddine rūpanāyanaśarmmaṇā idaṃ pustakaṃ svārthaṃ sampūrṇaṃ kṛtaṃ ||

lekhakāya pāṭhakāya ca śubham astu ||     ||

śivāya namaḥ (fol. 146r2–5)
