Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || || atha vaśyādhikāraḥ ||

sitabhānuviṭapamūlaṃ maṃjiṣṭābhavanacaṭakakuṣṭaṃ ||

svāṅgakṣatabhavadigdhaiḥ stribhuvanam ebhir vaśīkurute || 1 ||

tālaṃ piśācabhavane bhūtenhi(!)pretavadanaparyyuṣitaṃ ||

rogendrasaṃprayuktaṃ tribhuvanavaśakārakaṃ || 2 ||

mṛtanaralocanajihvālalāttahṛnnāsighrāṇasādhitaṃ tailaṃ ||

sakalabhūbhujendralalanāvaśīkaraṇaṃ karālaye puṣpe ||

naratailanarakapāle pretāmbaravarttitaḥ kṣapādīyāt

kṣoṇīhetu mahendraśikhare samutpannaṃ kajjalaṃ jagat vaśakṛt || 4 || 

(fol.1v1-6)
