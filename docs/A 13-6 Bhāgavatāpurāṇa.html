Manuscript Features

These fragments appear to be part of the same MS microfilmed in reel nos. A 13/3 and A 13/4. Available fols. are 39–48 and 59–79. Marginal notes are found on almost every folio.

Excerpts

Beginning

(III.1.13)viṣṭo yam apatyabuddhyā |

puṣṇāsi kṛṣṇād vimukho gataśrīs tyajāśvacainaṃ kulakauśalāya ||

ity ūcivāṃś tatra suyodhanena pravṛddharoṣāsphuritādhareṇa |

asatkṛtaḥ satspṛhaṇīyaśīlaḥ kṣattā sa///25 linaiva puṣṭaḥ |

tasmin pratīpaḥ parakṛtya āste nirvvāsyatām āśu purāc chvaśānaḥ ||

sa ittham atyulbanakarṇṇabāṇair bhrātuḥ puro marmmasu tāḍito ’pi |

svayaṃ dhanurddhāri nidhāya māyāṃ gatavyatho yād urumānayānaḥ || (fol. tṛ 39r1–2)

End

evaṃ paretya bhagavantam anupraviṣṭā

ye yogino jitamarunmanaso virāgāḥ |

tenaiva sākam amṛtaṃ puruṣaṃ purāṇaṃ

brahma pradhānam upayānty agatābhimānāḥ ||

atha taṃ sarvabhūtānāṃ hṛtpadmeṣu kṛtālayam |

śrutānubhāvaṃ śaraṇaṃ vraja bhāvena bhāmini ||

ādyaḥ sthiracaraṇānāṃ yo vedagarbhaḥ saharṣibhiḥ |

yogeśvaraiḥ kumārādyaiḥ siddhair yogapravarttakaiḥ ||

bhedadṛṣṭyābhimānena niḥsaṅgenāpi karmaṇā |

kartṛtvāt saguṇaṃ brahma puruṣaṃ puruṣarṣabham |

sa saṃsṛtya punaḥ kāle kāleneśvara (fol. tṛ 79v4–5)

Sub-colophon

iti śrībhāgavate mahāpurāṇe tṛtīyaskandhe jīvagati ekatriṃśattamo dhyāyaḥ || (fol. 79v1)

iti śrībhāgavate mahāpurāṇe pāramahaṃsyāṃ saṃhitāyāṃ vaiyāsikyāṃ daśamaskandhe pañcasaptatitamo dhyāyaḥ ||

iti śrībhāgavate mahāpurāṇe pāramahaṃsyāṃ saṃhitāyāṃ vaiyāsikyāṃ daśamaskandhe saubhavadhe ṣaṭsaptatitamo dhyāyaḥ ||

iti śrībhāgavate mahāpurāṇe pāramahaṃsyāṃ saṃhitāyāṃ〇 vaiyāsikyāṃ daśamaskandhe trisaptatitamo dhyāyaḥ ||
