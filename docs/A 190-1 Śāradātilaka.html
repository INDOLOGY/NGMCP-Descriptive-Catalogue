Excerpts

Beginning

|| || svasti śrīgaṇapataye namaḥ ||


nityānandavapur niraṃtaragalat paṃcāśad arṇaiḥ kramād

vyāptaṃ yena carācarātmakam idaṃ śabdārtharūpaṃ jagat ||

śabdabrahma yad ūcire sukṛtinas caitanyam antargataṃ

tad vo ʼvyād aniśaṃ śaśāṃkasadanaṃ vācām adhīśaṃ mahaḥ || 1 ||

aṃtaḥsmitollasitamiṃdukalāvataṃsam

iṃdīvarodarasahodaranetraśobhi ||


((…))


hetus trilokavibhavasya navendumauler

antaḥpuraṃ diśatu maṃgalam ādarād vaḥ || 2 ||


saṃ(ṣā)rasiṃdhos taraṇaikahetūn

vande gurūn mūrdhni śivasvabhāvān ||

rajāṃṣi yeṣāṃ padapaṃkajānāṃ

tīrthābhiṣekaśriyam āvahaṃti || 3 || (fol. 1v1–3)


End

ācāryavidyāvibhavasya (tasya)

jātaḥ prabhor lakṣmaṇadeśikeṃdraḥ

vidyāsvaśeṣāsu kalāsu sarvā-

svapi prathāṃ yo mahatīṃ prapede || 84 ||


ādāya sāram akhilaṃ nikhilāgamebhyaḥ

śrīśāradātilakanāma cakāra tantraṃ ||

prātaḥ sa eṣa paṭalair iha tattvasaṃkhyaiḥ

prītipradānavidhaye viduṣāṃ cirāya || 85 ||


anādyaṃtā śaṃbhor vapuṣi kalitārddhena vapuṣā

jagadrūpaṃ śaśvat sṛjati mahanīyām api giraṃ ||

sadaryyāśabdārthastanabharanatā śaṃkaravadhūr

bhaved bhūtyai bhūyād bhavajanitaduḥkhaighaśamanī || 86 || (fol. 95r9-11)


Colophon

iti śāradātike lakṣamaṇācāryaviracite paṃcaviṃśatitamaḥ paṭalaḥ || || samāpto yaṃ granthaḥ || ||

(fol. 95r11)
