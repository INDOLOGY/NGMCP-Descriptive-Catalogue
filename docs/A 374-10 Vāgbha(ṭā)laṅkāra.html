Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

śriyaṃ diśatu vo devaḥ śrīnābheyajinaḥ sadā

mokṣamārgaṃ satāṃ brūte yadāgamapa(2)dāvalī 1

sādhuśabdārthasaṃdarbhaṃ guṇālaṃkārabhūṣitam

sphuṭarītirasopetaṃ kāvyaṃ kurvīta (3) kīrttaye 2

pratibhākāraṇan tasya vyutpattis tu vibhūṣaṇam

bhūyo tyaṃtakṛto bhyāsa<ref name="ftn1">Different reading: bhṛśotpattikṛdabhyāsaḥ</ref> (4) ityādyakavisaṃkathāḥ 3

prasannapadanavyārthayuktyudbodhavidhāyinī

sphuraṃtī satkave(5)r buddhiḥ pratibhā sarvatomukhī 4 (fol.1v1–5)

End

samyag(27v1)jñānasamutthānaḥ śānto nispṛhanāyakaḥ

rāgadveṣaparityāgaḥ samyagjñāna(2)sya codbhavaḥ 31

doṣair ujjhitam āśritaṃ guṇagaṇaiś cetaś camatkāriṇan

nānā(3)laṃkṛtibhiḥ parītam abhito rītyā sphuraṃnyā satām

tais tais tanmayatāṃ gataṃ nava(4)rasai (!) ākalpakālaṃ sadā

sraṣṭāro racayaṃtu kāvyapuruṣaṃ sārasvatādhyāyi(5)naḥ 32 (fol. 27r7–27v5)

Colophon

iti śrīvāgbhaṭālaṃkāre paṃcamaḥ parichedaḥ (!) samāptaḥ (6) śubham grathasaṇkhyā 325 (fol. 27v5–6)
