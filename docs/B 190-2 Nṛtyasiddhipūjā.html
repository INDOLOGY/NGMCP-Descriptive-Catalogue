cupe pūjā || paśu tarppaṇa || tāraṇa yācake || sa(3)maya biya || dakṣiṇā yācake || ceta, siṃdhra || ekāneka || deva āśirvvāda || (4) ambe pūrvva, svāna kokāya || svāna biya || bali visarjana yāya || sākṣī (5) thāya || nāṭeśvarasa svāna yaṃṅāva pyākhana syaṃne thāyasa āṅasa || ceta(6) sidharaṇa svaṃ phoṭā dayakāo ||
