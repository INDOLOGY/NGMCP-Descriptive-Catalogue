Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

samādhicyā abhyāsāceṃ prakāra || 1 || vākyasudheṃta samādhī sāṃgīta lyātyāṃta aṃtarī || 3 || bāhya || 3 || titayeka nirvikalpa || 1 || dona savikalpa || 2 || tyādoṃ ta || dṛśyānuviddha || 1 || śabdānuviddha || 1 || hecatina bāhya hecatina āṃtarhi || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

tadarthapravṛttinivṛtti vyavahāro asāvi prārabdhakṣaye deha paryaṃta indriya vyavahārapratyayā cāṇa īhī sva svarūpa saccidānaṃda ca pāhāto nāmarūpī mana jānudeta [[ca]] nahī ||

|| śloka ||
prathamaṃ saccidānaṃde bhāsamāne tha tāvatā ||
buddhiṃ niyamya naivordhvaṃ dhāraye nāmarūpayoḥ ||

iti dṛk dṛśya samādhiprakaraṇaṃ samāpti || ❁ ❁ ❁ ❁ ❁ ❁ ❁ (fol. 38v3–6)

Colophon

yena pustakaṃ śrīmajjagadguro prasādena gaṇeśātmaja viṭhala nāme vāstavya vasiṣṭhātiraṃ āṣṭināme grāmaprāṃtavarāu tena ātmavicārārtha dravyadvārā pustakaṃ saṃgṛhya śrīkṣetre karttavyaḥ saṃvat 1865 śake 1730 jeṣṭhamāse || ❁ || (fol. 38v7–8)
