Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || śrībhavānīśaṃkarābhyāṃ ||

hṭdaya sadā yādavanaḥ pāpāṭavyā durāsadā yadavanaḥ ||
arisamudāyādavataś trijanan bhāgāḥ smaraṇadāyādavataḥ || 1 ||

yojaninā gopītaś cārayo ballavāṃganā gopītaḥ ||
bhūr yenā gopītaḥ kaṃsādyo dveṣam eva nāgopītaḥ || 2 ||

padariṣu santāmānasthitayo yan nun na sudattasannāmānaḥ ||
yatra sannāmāna syur mavabhājañ ca paṭhitasannāmānaḥ || 3 || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

arisahatiasya vaneṣuśrucāṃ | padamāpadamāsudhadaṃ ca janāyapathaiva hariṃ | yatamā yatamā yatamā yatamā || 6 || punaśca naiṣadhena pūrvatāyatā yatā sahāyam unmahāmahā mahā mahāstas saṃpadaṃ || 7 || dha (fol. X)

Colophon

iti nalidaye mahākāvye kaviśrīkālidāsa kṛtau nalaprāptivarṇano nāma caturthaḥ sargaḥ || 6 ||
likhitaṃ mahātmā jīvaṇarāmeṇa kṛṣṇagaḍhamadhye ātmārthe ||
samāptaṃ nalodayādhyaṃ pustakam || (fol. 17v6–7)
