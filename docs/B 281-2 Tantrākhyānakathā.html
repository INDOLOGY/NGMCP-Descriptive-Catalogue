Excerpts

Beginning

oṁ namaḥ sarvvajñāya ||

taṃtrākhyānakathāṃcaiva buddhipadma pravodhinī |

ādityadīdhitimevuṃ tāni (2) dhāsyāmi yatnataḥ ||

taṃtrākhyāna dhāyā khaṃ thva, buddhi hātaṅā pare hoyakava ādityasa kiraṇavo u(3)thiṃga thva khaṃ, cho yatnanaṃ dhararape māla ||

rakṣatavyaṃ sadā vākyaṃ vākyaṃ bhavati nāśanaṃ |

haṃsyāṃ(4)bhyāṃ gīvamānnasya, kūrmmasya patanaṃ yathā ||

sadā vacana rakṣarapaṃ yane māla, velakāla maso(5)syaṃ hlāyā vacanana nāśa juphuvakha, haṃsa nehmaṃsyaṃ boyakaṃ yaṃle vacana hlāṭolana,

kāpare tā(6)ka theṃ tāya yevakha ||

thvayā upākhyāna || (fol. 1r1-6)

End

rātrau svastidine, svastisvastī madhyestu sarvvadā ||

svasti bhuyātvenni(fol. 43r7)bhyaṃ svastī sarvvata sarvvadā ||

thva taṃtrākhyāna ṅeṅayā naṃ, hlākayā naṃ,

thva tatva dhararape mālakha dhāraṅā || ||

trisa naṃ dinasa naṃ madhyāṃnasa naṃ (fol. 43v1) kalyāna juraṃ,

sarvvadā kālaṃ śubhamaṃgala kalyāna thajura ||

jitarāja bhārokasya hṛdaya kamala yāṅana thva taṃtrākhyānayā gatheṃ buddhi dvā(2)taṃ atheṃ kalyāna buddhivaṃta thajura || (fol. 43r6-43v2)

Colophon

(fol. 43v2) iti taṃtrākhyāna samāpta ||

samvata 638 pauṣa śuddhi 8 śubhamastu sarvvadā || ۞ : ||
