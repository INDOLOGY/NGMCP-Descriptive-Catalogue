Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

samālaṃve laṃbodaracaraṇapaṃkeruhayugaṃ

yadālaṃbya svairaṃ vijahati ca vighnāṃdhata(masān)

smarantaḥ saṃtoṣn nirupadhiparānaṃdalaharī

samākrāmtasvāṃtā hariharaviriṃciprabhṛtayaḥ || 1 ||

akhaṇḍabhūmaṇḍalamaṇḍalesmin

pratāpasaṃtāpitasaptasaptiḥ |

dayāmayāṃtaḥkaraṇe babhūba

sa siṃhamallaḥ pra(thi)taḥ pṛthivyāṃ || 2 ||

śrīrāmapaṇḍitasutaḥ paṇḍitaśrīvināyakaḥ |

ādiṣṭastena tanute śrāddhakalapalatām imāṃ || 3 ||

iaha khalu dharmārthakāmamokṣāṇām upāyāṃtarasādhanatvepy alasaprāya kalikaluṣakalaṃkapaṃkamagnadehaiḥ puruṣair anāyāsena teṣāṃ sādhayitum aśakyatvāt avaśyakartavyaśrāddhamātrasyaiva /// sādhanatvena tannirṇīyate || || (fol. 1r1-7)

End

gāvas tasya praṇaśyanti citakālam upasthitāṃ(!)||

vanaspatigate some (riśrayaṃ) vā yo dhigacchati |

svargasthāḥ pitaras tasya cyavante nātra saṃśayaḥ ||

vanaspatigate gatasvarūpam āha || vyāsaḥ ||

trimuhūrttaṃ vased arke ityādidvitīyastavakaprānte likhitaṃ ||

itihāsepi ||

dantadhāvanatāmbūlaṃ tailābhyaṃgam abhojanam ||

ratyauṣadhaparānnaṃ ca śrāddhakṛt sapta varjayet ||

viṣṇurahasye ||

śrāddhopavāsadivase khāditvā dantadhāvanam ||

gāyatryā śatasaṃpūtaṃ ambu prāśya viśuddhyatīti || 

(fol. 82r6-82v2)

Colophon

iti śrīmahārājasahagilānvayaikabhūṣaṇaparamānandādi(ṣṭa)dharmādhikāriśṛīrāmapaṇḍitāmtamajapaṇḍitavināyakakṛtāyāṃ śrāddhakalpalatāyāṃ paribhāṣāyāṃ nipatānipatādiśrāddhakarttṛbhojanāntakathananirūpaṇaś caturthastavakaḥ || ||

samāptoyaṃ śrāddhakalpalatā || śubham || || (fol. 82v6-10)
