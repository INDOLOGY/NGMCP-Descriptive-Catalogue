Excerpts

Beginning

|| śrīrāmāya namaḥ || śeṣovāca ||

ity uktam ākarṇya samīrajaḥ sadā subaddhamātmānam aveṣa vīrān ||

saṃmūrchitān śatruśarābhighātapīḍāyutān baṃdhanamuktaye smaran || 1 ||

śrīrāmacaṃdraṃ raghuvaṃśajātaṃ sītāpatiṃ paṃkajapatranetraṃ ||

svamuktaye baṃdhataḥ kṛpāluṃ sasmāra sarvaiḥ karuṇair viśokaiḥ || 2 || (fol. 1r1–6)

End

loko bhavaṃtam idam ullasito hasiṣyan

tasmād vilaṃbam iha mā cara mocayāśu || 6 ||

iti śrutvā jagannātho raghuvīraḥ kṛpānidhiḥ ||

bhaktaṃ mocayituṃ prāgāt puṣpakeṇa suveginā || 7 ||

śrīrāmaḥ kapivaryaṃ taṃ mocayāmāsa baṃdhanāt ||

mūrchitāṃs tān bhaṭān sarvān vīkṣa dṛṣṭyāśu jīvayan || 8 || (fol. 2r1–7)

Colophon

iti śrīrāmāśvamedhe hanumatkṛtaṃ śrīrāmastotraṃ saṃpūrṇaṃ || ❖ || ❖ || ❖ || ❖ || ❖ || ❖ || (fol. 2r7–8)
