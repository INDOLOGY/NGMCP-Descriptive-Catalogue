Manuscript Features

The first foliation ends with the 16th verse of the 26th taraṅga and the second foliation begins at the end of the 28th taraṅga. Fol. 98v (second foliation) is blank but with no loss of text.

padyāmṛtasarovaram

Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

śṛṃgārī girijānane sakaruṇo ratyāṃ pravīraḥ smare

bībhatso sthibhir utphaṇīti bhayakṛn mūrtyādbhutas tuṃgayā

raudro dakṣa(2)vimarddanena hasakṛn nagnaḥ praśāṃtaś cirād

itthaṃ sarvarasāśrayaḥ paśupatir bhūyāt satāṃ bhūtaye

dehi matkaṃdukaṃ rādhe paridhānanigūhitaṃ

iti vi(3)sraṃsayan nīvīṃ tasyāḥ kṛṣṇo mude stu naḥ 2

nītaṃ yadi navanītaṃ

kayad iti nītaṃ yaśodayā pṛṣṭaḥ |

iyad iti sarvasamakṣaṃ

karadhṛtarādhā(4)payodharaḥ pātu 3 (fol. 1v1–4)

End

vedābdhisaṃkhyais taralais taraṃgaiḥ

sarovaracātivaraṃviranirmitaṃ (!)

padyāmṛtaiḥ pūritam alpa(9)nalpaṃ (!)

bhajaṃtu vidvajjanarājahaṃsāḥ 78

śrīmān kāśyapagotrajo budhavaraḥ śrīrāmacaṃdro hy asau

vikhyāto bhuvi varṇanī(104v1)yavibhavas tasyātmajenādhunā

śrīmallakṣmaṇaśarmaṇā viracitaṃ padyāmṛtāḍhyaṃ saraḥ

prītyai sāṃbasadāśivasya bhava(2)tu śrīmādhavasyāniśam 79 (fol. 104r8–2)

Colophon

iti śrīmatkeśavāropāhvarāmacaṃdrasūnulakṣmaṇaviracite padyāmṛtasarovare

vai(3)rāgyakathanaṃ nāma catuścatvāriṃśat (!) taraṃgaḥ samāptaḥ 44 ||     ||     ||     || (fol. 104v2–3)
