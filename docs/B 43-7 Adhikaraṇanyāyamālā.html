Excerpts

Beginning

|| || śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

pramāṇam upajīvyatvāt prathame dhyāya īritāṃ (!) |

mānādhīnasya dharmasya .dvitīye bheda ucyate |

ane(2)na prathamadvitīyayor adhyāyayoḥ pūrvottarabhāvaḥ upapāditaḥ || |

dvitīyādhyāysya prathamapāde prathamādhikaraṇe prathamavarṇaka(3)m āracayati || |

vidhivākye padaiḥ sarvair apūrvaṃ pratipādyate

pratyekam athavaikena sarvais tat pratipādanaṃ | (exp.6, fol. 1v1–3)

End

tathā ca yājñavalkyaḥ |

prati(4)grahodhoko vipre yājanādhyāpane tatheti |

evam eva smṛtyaṃtarāṇyudāharaṇīyāni ||

tasmād viprasyaivārtv ijyam

devā(5)nāṃ sthitikṛt pūro hariharo bhūt sūtrakṛj jaiminis

tad bhāṣyaṃ śavaro vyadhād gaditavāṃs tad vistaraṃ mādhavaḥ |

so yaṃ ni(6)tyakalatraputrajanaka prajñādhipatyasthiti (!)

dīrghāyuḥ saha baṃdhubhir vijayato (!) ⟨m⟩ ācandram ātārakaṃ || (fol. 36r 3–6)

Colophon

iti śrīmat trikāṃḍamīmāsaṃmaṇḍanaprativasaṃtasomayājībhaṭṭamādhavaviracite jaiminīyanyāyamālāvistare dvādaśādhyāyasya caturthaḥ pādaḥ || samāpto dvādaśodhyāyḥ prasagaḥ (!)

yajñākarmaṇyajapan pūravasāmasu |

yajñākriyāyāṃ maṃtra ekaśrutusyāj japādīn varjjayitvā |

... japatvābhāvād ekaśrutir bhavatyeva śākhāchedanādikaṃ prati teṣāṃ śabdo lakṣaye || śrīrāmārpeṇām (!) astu samapta (!) || ❁ ❁ || (fol. 35r6–35v2)
