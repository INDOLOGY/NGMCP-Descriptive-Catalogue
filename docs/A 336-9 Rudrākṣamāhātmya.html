Excerpts

Beginning

[Ṭīkā]

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

mahādevale pārvvatīlāī sunāūchan ||

īśvara uvāca ||

atheti ||

ava uprāṃta vyakta gari rudrākṣakā māhātmya varṇa(2)na gardachu ||

mokṣakāmanā rudrākṣale kalyāṇa sādhanu pani rudrākṣaile huṃcha ||

rudrākṣale duḥkhakana naśagarchan (fol. 1v1–2)

[mūla]

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

īśvara uvāca ||

atha rudrākṣamāhātyaṃ varṇayāmi samāsataḥ ||

rudrākṣaṃ mo(5)kṣakāmas tu rudrākṣaṃ śreyasādhanaṃ (!) || ||

rudrākṣaṃ harate duḥkhaṃ rudrākṣaṃ pāpanāśanaṃ ||

sarvvapāpakṣayaka(6)raṃ paṭhatāṃ śṛṇvatām api || (fol. 1v4–6)

End

[mūla]

vakraṃ ca khaṃḍi(5)chidraśvaitaṃ caiva [[va]] viśeṣatsaḥ || (!)

kiṃcid kiṃcid bhaved devi phalaṃ doṣeṇa hanyate || || (fol. 8r4–5)

[ṭīkā]

vakram iti ||

vāgo paryāko khaṇḍita bhayāko aṃta pvālaparyāko he pārvvati suna jasto japyā dhāryā phala pāudainan ki(3)na bhanyā doṣale hāni dinyā cha bhani śrīmahādevale devī pārvvatīlāī sunāyā ||(fol. 8r2–3)

Colophon

iti rudākṣamāhātmyasya bhāṣāṭīkā samāptam || (!) (fol. 8r3)

iti śrītaṃtraciṃtāmaṇau rudrākṣamāhātmyaṃ || (fol. 8r5)

rudrākṣasya ca lakṣaṇaṃ suphaladaṃ māhātmyam evaṃ śubhaṃ

rudreṇoktam idaṃ prasastam aśubhaṃ tat tasya yā devatā ||

tat sarvaṃ suvi(7)cāryya nīlakavinā gairīya bhāṣānvitaṃ

rājendrādimavikramāya vibhave bhavatyārpitaṃ pustakaṃ || || || || (fol. 8r6–7)
