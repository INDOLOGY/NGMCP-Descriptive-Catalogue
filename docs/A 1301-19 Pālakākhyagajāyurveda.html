Manuscript Features

Fol. 38 has been mentioned twice to the two successive folios.

Fol. 44 has not been assigned but the text is continuous.

The colophon has been followed by a descriptive table of contents.

There are two exposures of fols. 21v–22r, 181v–182r, 193v–194r and 201.

Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

paihle hāti khedā garnyā karma lekhiṃchan || hātikā varṇana || hātikā śubha aśubhalakṣaṇa mada bahyākā haihleko vicāra || svabhāvako vicāra || āṃgamā kṛmi kīṭādile khāyi ṭoki ghātu bhayā pani na mānyāko vicāra nidrāko vicāra || (fol. 1v1–3)


End

jaun hātilāī nitya nitya pramāṇasita svād rāṣyāko pāni ṣuvāī rāṣyāko aghikā pramāṇamā ghaṭi badhi bhayo bhanyā viṣama bhaṃnu || tyo visama vāyuko vikāra jāṃnu bhani caṃpānagarakā aṃgarājālāī pālakākhya ṛṣīsvarale gajāyurvedabhanyāko kathāhā vistārasita kahadā bhayā || || (fol. 201v5–8)


Colophon

iti śrīpālakākhye gajāyurvede mahāpravacane śalyanāmni tṛtīyas thāme daṃtoddharaṇaṃ nāma aṣṭāviṃśo ʼdhy|āyaḥ samāptaḥ || || śubham astu sarvajagatām || || svasti śrīsaṃvat 1919 sālamiti kārttikaśudi pūrṇimā śubham || || (fol. 201v8–10)
