Excerpts

Beginning

svasti śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

śrīgurucaraṇakamalebhyo namaḥ || ||

śivaṃ pūrvaṃ paraṃ cava nityaṃ śuddhaṃ niraṃjanam ||

nidhanaṃ paramaṃ divyaṃ dattātraya (!) namāmy aham ||1 ||

oṃ asya śrīgurustotramaṃtrasya paramaśiva ṛṣi (!) virāṭ chaṃdaḥ śrīguruḥ paramātmā devatā haṃ bījaṃ saṃ śaktiḥ krauṃ kīlakaṃ śrīguruprasādasiddhyarthe jape viniyogaḥ ||

atha karanyāsaḥ || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

durllabho[ʼ]yaṃ guru (!) devi śiṣyasaṃtāpahārakaḥ ||

svāmimukhyā gaṇeśādivaiṣṇavādiṣu pārvati || 147 ||

manasāpi na vaktavyaṃ mama dṛṣṭyā spṛśāmy aham ||

atīva citte pakvasya śraddhābhaktisamanvite || 148 ||

vaktavyaṃ tu idaṃ devi mamātmāti (!) sadā priye ||

abhakto vaṃcako dhūrttaḥ pāṣaṃḍo (!) nāstikas tathā || 149 ||

manasāpi na vaktavyā gurugītā kadācanaḥ (!) ||

tathāpi tasya vaktavyā naṣṭā bhavati nānyathā || 150 ||

saṃṣārasāgarasamuddharaṇaikamaṃtraṃ

brahmādidevamunipūjitasiddhamaṃtraṃ ||

dāridryaduḥkhabhayaśokavināśamaṃtraṃ

vaṃḍe mahābhayaharaṃ gururājamaṃtraṃ || 151 ||  || (fol. 6r2–7)

Colophon

iti śrīskandapurāṇe īśvarapārvatisaṃvāde gurugītāstotraṃ saṃpūrṇam astu śubham || ||

gurur brahmā gurur viṣṇu(!) …
