Manuscript Features

kiṃ jyotis tava bhānu mānahanime rāṭro (!) padīvādikaṃ

syād evaṃ ravidīpadarśanavidhau kiṃ jyotir ā(8)khyā hi me ||

catus tasya nimīlanādi samaye kiṃ dhir dhiyo darśane

kiṃ tatrāha mato bhavān para(9)makaṃ jyotis tadasmi prabho || 1 || (fol. 5r7–9)

Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

vaṭatarunikaṭanivāsaṃ

paṭutaraṃ vijñānamudritakarābjaṃ ||

(2) kaṃcidyaiśikamādyaṃ

kaivalyānaṃdakaṃdalaṃ vaṃde || 1 ||

niravadhisaṃsṛtijalanidhi-

nipa(3)tita janatāraṇas phuran naukāṃ ||

paratatvabhedaghuṭikāṃ

paramaśiveṃdrāryapādukāṃ naumi || 2 ||

(4) daiśikaparamaśiveṃdrā

deśavaśād budhya divyamahimāhaṃ ||

svātmani viśrāṃti kṛte-

sara(5)saṃ prastaumi kiṃcid idaṃ || 3 || (fol. 1r1–5)

End

arasam agaṃdham arūpaṃ

virajaskam asatvam atamaskaṃ ||

nirupam anirbhayatatvaṃ |

tat kim a(3)pi dyotate tatvaṃ || 60 ||

iti gurukaruṇāpāṃgād

āryābhir dvyadhikaṣaṣṭisakhyābhiḥ (!) ||

niravadyā(4)nubhavotthaṃ

nigamaśiras taṃtrasārabhūtārthaṃ || 61 ||

gaditam idam ātmavidyā-

vilāsam anuvāsaraṃ (5) smarān vibudhaḥ ||

pariṇatapadam āptavidyaḥ

prapadyate sapadi param ārthaṃ || 62 || (fol. 5r2–5)
