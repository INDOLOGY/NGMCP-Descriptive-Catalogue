Beginning

(1)❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīsarvvabuddhabodhisatvebhyaḥ ||

śrīmanti sadguṇaparigrahamaṅgalāni
kīrtyāspadāny anavagītamanoharā(2)ṇi |
pūrvaprajanmasu muneś caritādbhutāni
bhaktyā svakāvyakusumāñjalinārccayiṣye ||

vyāghrījātakaṃ prathamaṃ || ○ || (fol. 8r1)

iti śibijātakaṃ dvitīyaṃ || 2 || (fol. 18v4)

iti kulmāṣapiṇḍījātakaṃ tṛtīyaṃ || 3 || (fol. 23v5)

…

ity ayogṛhajātakaṃ dvātriṃśattamaṃ || ❖ || (fol. 332v2–3)

mahiṣajātakaṃ trayastriṃśattamaṃ || (fol. 339v5)

End

evaṃ pratisaṃkhyānabahulāḥ svāṃ guṇa(3)śobhām anurakṣanti paṇḍitā iti saṃkhyāvarṇṇe vācyaṃ || tathāgatamāhātmye ca bhadraprakṛtyabhyāsavarṇṇe ca || evaṃ (4)bhadrākṛtir abhyastā tiryaggatānām api na nivarttata iti || ❖ ||
iti śatapatrajātakaṃ catustriṃśattamaṃ samā(5)ptam iti || ❁ || (fol. 343r2–5)

Colophon

ye dharmā hetuprabhāvā hetun teṣān tathā(1)gataḥ |
hy avadat teṣāṃ ca yo nirodha evaṃvādī mahāśramaṇaḥ ||
śubha[m a]stu sarvadā ||
samvat 769 poṣa-(2)kṛṣṇa-ṣaṣṭamyāyāṃ tithau utraphālguṇikṣatre saubhanayogya svamavārasare ||
noṃgabhota (3)viśvaṃtara nāma svārthaṃ leṣitā ||
iti śatapatrajātakaṃ prakriyāḥ saṃpūrṇṇaṃ jātā || śubhaṃ || (fol. 343r5–v3)
