Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrīgaṇapataye namaḥ || ||

namo raye vidhihlāya ||

vāṅa gaṃdhaka emasakhāra sedhu,

madatasā, sābhara, samabhāga. lāṣana naya nayati sayāya,

misacchoya gomatisa thone hanoṃ hanothathe tuhna [[sa]]yorayāya onanali neya bhassma juyiva || || namo raye || (fol. 1r1–2)

End

prātaruddhṛtya guṭikāṃ kṣīrabhāṇde vinikṣipet |

tata kṣīraṇḍaṣyati kṣipram etan pratyayakārakaṃ

ratikāre mukhe dhāryyā guṭikā vīryyarodhinī |

kṣīraṃ pītvā ramed rāmā, sundarī ca kalānvitāṃ (!)

mukhasthāṃ ddhārayed dhasti tadā vīryyaṃ pramuñcati ||

|| rasaratnākarāt || || (fol. 10r2–4)
