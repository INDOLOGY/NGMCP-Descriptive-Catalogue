Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || || atha viṣṇustavarāja (!) likhyate || ||

padmovāca ||

yogena siddhavibudhaiḥ paribhāvyamāna,(!)

lakṣyālayaṃ(!) tulasti kāntitabhaktabhṛṅgaṃ ||

proktāṃgaraktanayanāṅgulipatracitraṃ,

gaṅgārasaṃ haripadāmbuja nāśrayehaṃ(!) || 1 ||

gulphanmaṇipracayaghaṭṭitarājahaṃsa,(!)

siñjatsunūpurayutaṃ padapadmavṛttam ||

pītāmbarācalavilolacalatpatākā,

svarṇatrivaktā valayaṃ ca hareḥ smarāmi || 2 || (x.56a8-56b4 )

End

ye bhaktyādyāṃ dhyāyamānāṃ manojñāṃ

vyaktiṃ viṣṇoḥ ṣoḍśaślokapuṣpaiḥ ||

stutvā natvā pūjayitvā vidhijñāḥ

śraddhāyuktā brahmasaukhyaṃ prayānti || 18 ||

padmeritam idaṃ puṇyaṃ,śivenaparibhāṣitam ||

dhannyaṃ yaśasyam āyuṣyaṃ,svargyaṃ svastyayanaṃ param || 19 ||

paṭhanti ye māhābhāgās te mucyantyeha sokhilāṃ(!) || dharmmārthakāmamokṣāṇāṃ paratreha phalapradam || 20 || (x.58b4-9 )
