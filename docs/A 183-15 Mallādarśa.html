Excerpts

Beginning

atha kānicit tu yaṃtrāṇi vinaiva sādhyasādhakanāmavarnāṃkamilanādisaṃskāraṃ śivatāṇḍavādiṣu taṃtreṣu paṭalāṃtare praṇītāni | | tathā hi uttarakhaṇḍe tṛtīyapaṭale mātaṃgīdaivatyāni vaśyayaṃtrāṇi

viṃśatyaṅkamayaṃ yaṃtraṃ viśayed akhilaṃ jagat
triṃśad aṃkānvitaṃ yaṃtraṃ dūrād ākarṣayet striyam

ākarṣayet ākarṣaṇakārakavaśyatādhikaraṇī kuryāt tena vaśyayaṃtrāṇīty upakramasya na virodhaḥ |

catvāriṃśad aṃkayaṃtre patiṃ vaśayate dhruvam |
paṃcāśad aṃkayaṃtre ca rājānam vaśam ānayet

ṣaṣṭyaṃkalikhitaṃ yaṃtraṃ sarvaśatruvaśaṃ nayet (exp. 16:1–4)

End

adhītaṃ naivaitan na ca sugamavākyaṃ na milito
madāśaṃkā[c]chedī na hi ca mama siddheṣu gaṇanā |

tathāpy ājñāpūrttiḥ katham api malaivammanṛpater
yad atrāste heturś caraṇakaruṇā haihayahareḥ | 1 |

atikliṣṭaśabdaiḥ śivādiṣṭadharmo
budhānāṃ tu matyāpy asaṃspṛṣṭakaṣṭe |
yadasmiṃs tu taṃtre dhoyo me prakāśaḥ
prabhāvo ʼsty ayaṃ dīadānasya viṣṇoḥ | 2 |

yasyodyotamatiḥ satīguṇavatī mātā pitomāpatir
nāma premanidhīti paṃthakulabhūḥ kūrmācalo janmabhūḥ |
tenāṣṭābdhiṣaḍindu 1648 śākagasitānyor jādy atithyaṃkaniṭ-
kāle śrīśivatāṇḍavasya vihitaṃ saṭṭippaṇaṃ satvaraṃ | 3 |

śivatāṇḍavaṭīkāsau
mallādarśābhidhācārvī |
premanidhiśarmavihitā
pūrṇā syāt tuṣṭidā viṣṇoḥ | 4 ||    || (fol. 3t7–11)

Colophon

iti premanidhipaṇḍitanirmito mallādarśaḥ ||    || śrīrāmaḥ punātu naḥ || (exp 3t11)
