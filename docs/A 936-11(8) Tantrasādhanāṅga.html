Excerpts

«Begining: »

akṣobhyaa ā aṃ aḥ ka kha ga gha kṣa śūnya

amoghasiddhiḥ i ī e ai ca ccha ja jha ña ya śa vāyu

amitābhaṛ ṝ ara ār ṭa ṭha ḍa ḍha ṇa ra ṣa teja

ratnasaṃbhavau ū o au pa pha da bha ma va ṣya āpa

vairocanalṛ lṝ ala āla ta tha da dha na la sa pṛthvī

oṃ ādibuddhādevākṛṣya mantrasādhanai[ḥ] yad arimitrodāsyalakṣaṇam uktaṃ bhagavatā śākyasiṃhena tanmayā kṛpayā śrīśīlasāgareṇāśeṣajagadboddhau pratiṣṭhita[ḥ] likhyate || tatra yadi sādhyasya nāmākṣaraṃ sādhakanāmākṣarasya śatrur bhavati tadā sādhakasya maraṇaṃ bhavati || athodāsya bhavati tadā kleśo bhavati || atha mitraṃ bhavati tadā siddho bhavati devatā || (fol. 38v1–5, 39r1–4)

«End: »

nāmādyaṃ cittavajraṃ bhavati narapate devatā devatānāṃ

vāgvajraṃ sarvanāmākṣaram api ca tatas cādhikaṃ kāyavajraṃ |

tasmāt pratyaṅgamanto bhavati bahuvidhaḥ pāṭhasiddhaḥ kadācit

bhāvyo jā(ḍyu)ś ca jāpyaḥ svajinakulavaśāc cittavākkāyabhedaiḥ ||

pratyekam mantrajātaiḥ prabhavati niyataḥ koṭijāpaḥ [[sa]] siddho

homas tasmād daśāṃśaḥ prakṛtiguṇavaśāt siddhyate yāvad eva |

paścāc chāntyādikeṣu prabhavati phalado nānyathā siddhim eti

saddhyānair jjāpahomair vrataniyamayutair mantrayoniś ca sādhyā || (fol. 42r1–6)

«Colophon: »

ādibuddhamahāyānatantre mantrasādhanāṃgaḥ samāpta iti || ❁ || (fol. 42r6)
