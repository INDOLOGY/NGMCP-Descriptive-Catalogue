Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṁ namo (macchyaṃ)danāthāya ||

namas te kulanāthāya, kaulasiddhipradāyine |
jñānavijñānadehāya, śrīma(cchyaṃ)da namo stu te ||

anādighorasaṃsāra,vyādhidhvaṃsaikahetave |
namaḥ śrīnāthavaidyāya, kulauṣadhividhāyine ||

pātālādiśivāntavaktrakuhare yasyāsthitaṃ viśvagaṃ,
nityaṃ sāvararūparaṃjitatanuṃ devādibhiḥ saṃstutā |
(magnyā)vyomasahasranālpamahimānāgādivaṃdair vṛtta(!),
mīnākārasamānabhogavadanā śaktiḥ para pātu mām ||

śaktayaḥ kṣobhitā yena, pītaṃ †ca mṛtam† uttamam |
avāpu(!) paramānaṃdaṃ, taṃ vaṃde vīranāyakam ||    ||

śrīdevy uvāca ||

śrutaṃ mayā kulāmnāyaṃ, siddhapāraṃparāgatam |
ūrmikaulārṇṇavaṃ guhyaṃ bhogahasteti viśrutaṃ || (fol. 1v1–6)

End

evaṃ śvetācalādīś(!) ca, bhāvayet gāḍhayatnataḥ ||
vījenāpi vinā dhyātvā, devacittasamāhiḥ(!) ||<ref>Hypometrical stanza </ref>

piban bhu[ñ]jan svapais(!) tiṣṭha(!), na gacchet saṃkramann api ||
sarvva(!)vasthā sthitā(!) yogī, bhāvayed devatākṛtiṃ ||

athavā kevalaṃ saukhyaṃ yogī dvaṃdvena nanditaṃ ||
tāvad vibhāvayed gāḍhaṃ, yāvat sa(!)sphuṭatāṃ vrajet ||

gate tu prasphuṭe yogī mahāmudreṇa sidhyati ||    || (fol. 84v4–6)
