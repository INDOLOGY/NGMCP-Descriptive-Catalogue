Beginning

-dvayāṃ sadvayahṛtsu ca pradikṣiṇyena nyāsaṃ kṛtvā padāṣṭakam aṣṭādhāreṣu nyaset || padacatu(2)rddaśakaṃ caturddaśādhāreṣu navakaṃ cen navādhāreṣu daśakaṃ cerd (!) daśādhāreṣu sāmānyapaṭalo(3)ktāḥ nyāsā kāryā iti | (fol. 1r1–3)

vaśyādau tu viruddhāṃśaṃ puttalyāṃ saṃscchāpyāṃśāṃtarasvahatībhi(!) sahasva (5) hṛdaye schāpanīyam (!) ity āha kitviti sārvākṛṣṭādikarasyogamāha pāśā dyati kavyukteti aṣṭadalamadhyāgaśaktau (!) sādhyā(6)dim ālikhya pāśāṃkuśābhyāṃ tāṃ saṃveṣṭya pādyaṣṭakam (!) aṣṭadale likhitvā śiṣṭai (!) ta(tra) vissaṃveṣṭya (!) punaha (!) deye pāśāṃkuśādi li(7)khited (!) iti | (fol. 36v4–7)
