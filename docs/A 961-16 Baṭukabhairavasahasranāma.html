Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||


kailāsaśiṣare ramye devadeva jagadguruṃ ||

papra[c]cha pārvatī śaṃbhuṃ śaṃkaraṃ lokaśaṃkaraṃ || 1 ||


pārvaty uvāca || ||


deva deva mahādeva sarvatra sukhasaṃbhavaḥ ||

āpaduddhara daridrādipīḍitānāṃ nṛṇāṃ vibho || 2 ||


yaddhitaṃ sukhasampattidhanadhānyakaraṃ sadā ||

viśeṣato rājakule śāntipuṣṭipradāyakaṃ || 3 ||


bālagrahādiśamanaṃ nānāsiddhikaraṃ nṛṇāṃ ||

coktaṃ pūrvaṃ tu yan nātha dhyānapūjāsamanvitaṃ || 4 || (fol. 1v1–2r3)


End

bhūmikāmo bhūtikāmaḥ ṣaṇmāsaṃ ca japet sudhīḥ ||

patikṛtyā vināśārthaṃ japet triśatam uttamaṃ || 83 ||


māsatrayeṇa sarveṣāṃ vighnānāsaṃtako bhavet ||

māsam ekaṃ japed devi niraśvaramānasaḥ || 84 ||


dhanam putraṃ tathādārāṃ prāpnuyān nātra saṃśayaḥ ||

mahākārāgṛhe baddhaḥ piśāvaiḥ parivāritaḥ || 85 ||


nigaḍaiḥ śṛṃkhalābhiś ca yaṃ dhanaṃ paramaṃ gataḥ ||

paṭhanāc ca divārātrau sarvāṃ kāmān avāpnuyāt || 86 ||


śatam āvarttayed yas tu puraścaraṇam ucyate ||

yaṃ yaṃ kāmayate kāmaṃ taṃ taṃ prāpnoti niścitaṃ || 87 ||


satyaṃ satyaṃ punaḥ satyaṃ satyaṃ satyaṃ punaḥ punaḥ ||

sarvakāmaprado devī sarvajñaḥ sarvasiddhidaḥ || 88 ||


satkulīnāya śāṃtāya ṛjuve satyavādine ||

stotradānāt saḥ prahṛṣṭe bhairavo ʼbhūn maheśvara || 89 || ❁ || (fol. 30r1–30v6)


Colophon

iti śrīuḍḍāmarataṃtre umāmaheśvarasaṃvāde vaṭukabhairavasahasranāma samāptāḥ(!) || ❁ || 1941 sāla śrāvaṇa kṛṣṇa 2 roja 5 śubhaṃ (fol. 30v6–7)
