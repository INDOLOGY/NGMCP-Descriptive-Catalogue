Beginning

rttā prakhyātā dharaṇītale || 1 ||


bhrūṇagarteti vipreṃdrāḥ sarvapātakanāśinī ||

tatra kṛṣṇacaturdaśyāṃ ya[ḥ] śrāddhaṃ kurute naraḥ || 2 ||


sapitṛṃ srāracye(!) mūnaṃ daśapūrvān daśāparāṇ ||

tasmāt sarvaprayatnena tatra śrāddhaṃ samācaret || 3 ||


snānaṃ ca brāhmaṇaśreṣṭhādānaṃ vyāpi svaśaktitaḥ || 104 || 2914 || (fol. 92r1–3)


End

jayānaṃttaṃ tena sādhyeye garbhadehārddhasaṃśraye ||

tvaṃ ratis tvaṃ dhṛtis tuṣṭhis tvaṃ gaurī tvaṃ sureśvarī || 25 ||


tvaṃ lakṣmī tvaṃ ca sāvitrī gāyatrī tvam aśaṃsayaṃ ||

yaktāṃ(!) vit(!) triṣu lokeṣu strīrūpaṃ devi dṛśyate || 26 ||


tat sarvaṃ tvanmaye nātra vikalpo ʼsti mana(!) kvacit ||

yena satyena te nāthasya maṃtrā vāsaṃ utaṃ kuru |


sānnidhyaṃ bhaktitas tuṣṭā surāsuranamskṛte || 76 ||


sūta uvāca ||


evaṃ stutā ca sā devī nalena pṛthivī ‥rā

provāca darśanaṃ gatvā taṃ nṛpaṃ bhaktavatsalā || 29 ||


devy uvāca ||


parituṣṭo ʼsmi te vatsa stotreṇānena sāṃprataṃ ||

nala uvāca ||
