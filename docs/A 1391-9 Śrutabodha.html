Manuscript Features

Available folios are 1–4 and an unnumbered (the last) folio.

mani(!)rāmasyedaṃ pustakaṃ śrutabodhasya (!) mani(!)rāmasyedaṃ pustakam

On fol. 4r11, v. 31 of Vrajaratna Bhattacarya’s edition, describing the lakṣaṇa of toṭakavṛtta (toṭaka metre), is missing, and from then on the verse numbers differ from the edition by one.

Probably at most one folio is missing after fol. 4. The end of fol. 4v contains the first four akṣaras of the last pāda of v. 35 (Bhattacarya’s edition). The text from then until the last seven akṣaras of v. 41 (at the beginning of the unnumbered folio) is missing.

ādyaṃ dvitīyam api etc. (v. 35)

prathamamaguruṣaṭkaṃ etc. (v. 36)

sumukhi laghavaḥ pañca etc. (v. 37)

yadā pūrvo hrasvaḥ etc. (v. 38)

dvitīyam alikuntale etc. (v. 39)

catvāraḥ prāk sutanu guravo etc. (v. 40)

ādyāś ced guruvas trayaḥ priyatame etc. (v. 41)
