Manuscript Features

The first two folios are missing. Many folios are damaged by worms, but still legible throughout.

The manuscript is written by several hands.

The text breaks off in the middle of the commentary on 8.27. Possibly the folios with the remaining text have been lost, but they may as well never have existed, as the last line of the last page has been filled with decorating elements.
