Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaś caṇḍikāyai ||

śrīgaṇapataye namaḥ ||

gaṇapatiñ ca herambaṃ vighnarājaṃ vināyakaṃ |

devīputraṃ mahātejo mahābalaparākramaṃ ||

mahodaraṃ mahākāyam ekadaṃṣṭraṃ gajānanaṃ |

śvetavarṇṇaṃ mahādīptiṃ trinetraṃ gaṇanāyakaṃ ||

akṣamālāṃ svadantañ ca dakṣiṇena vidhāritaṃ | (fol. 1v1–4)

oṃ namaś caṇḍikāyai ||

mārkkaṇḍeya uvāca ||

sāvarṇṇiḥ sūryyatanayo yo manuḥ kathyate ʼṣṭamaḥ ||

niśāmaya tadutpattim vistarād gadato mama || (fol. 5r5–6)


End

mārkkaṇḍeya uvāca ||

iti dattvā tayor ddevī yathābhiliṣitaṃ(!) varaṃ |

babhūvāntarhitā sadyo bhaktyā tābhyām abhiṣṭutā |

evan devyā varaṃ labdhvā surathaḥ kṣatriyarṣabhaḥ |

sūryyāj janma samāsādya savarṇṇir bhavitā manuḥ || || (fol. 59v2–5)


Colophon

iti śrīmārkkaṇḍeyapuraṇe sāvarṇṇike manvantare devīmāhātmye saptaśatikānāma caṇḍīstavas samāptaḥ || ❖ || (fol. 59v5–6)


«Colophon of the Indrākṣīstava»

iti śrīskandapurāṇe idnrākṣīstotraṃ samāptaṃ || ||

yadakṣaraparibhraṣṭaṃ mātrāhīnañ ca yad bhavet |

kṣantum arhasi tenaiva kasya vai niścalaṃ manaḥ || ||

śrī 3 bhavānī prīṇātu ||

❖ śrī śrī jayabhūpatīndramalladevasanacūkacāsadevapratiṣṭhāsiddhāgni a itā hūti yajñayā thva sā hūri || saṃ 825 bhādrapadaśukladaśamīmūlaśaniścaravāra kuhnuḥ || śubha || (fol. 60v5–61r3)
