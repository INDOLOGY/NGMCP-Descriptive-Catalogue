Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīgaṇeśāya || ❖ oṃ śrīgurave namaḥ ||

||❖ śrīdhanvantarivaidyāya namaḥ ||

bījaṃ śrūtīnāṃ sudhanaṃ munīnāṃ

jīvaṃ jaḍānāṃ mahadādikānāṃ ||

āgneyamantraṃ (!) vaha (!) pātakānāṃ

kiñcitsahas sāmaramāśrayāmi (!)

labdhā kapola madhu vā madhuvratālī

kumbhasthalī madhuvibhūṣaṇa sāhitāṅgī (!) |

māṇikya mauli virājati yasya maulau

vighnāni vāḥ paridhunosi sa vighnarājaḥ || (!)

yo dhvānta santati viśāla payodhi madhyā (!)

niḥkāsa yasya saraṇaṃ bhuvanaṃ nimajjet ||

datvā jagattraya hitaḥ svakarāvalambaṃ

vāñchś sadhā(!) ni nakaraḥ sakalī karotu (!) (fol.1v1–4)

«Sub:Colophon:»

iti madanapāraviracite madavinode nirghaṇṭau madhuvarggaḥ navamaḥ || 9 || ❁ ||(!) (fol.38v1-2)

End

śastraṃ strīṇāṃ ca baṃdhyānāṃ dhanyāyubaladapradaṃ || (!)

alakṣmī pāparakṣoghnaṃ sarvvagraha nivāraṇaṃ ||

kalyānakaḥmidaṃ (!) sarppiḥ śreṣṭapuṃsavaneṣu ca ||

yānikalyāṇa ghṛtaḥ ||

tatra evasthirīdīni jalekalkekaviṃśati |

rase tasmin pacetsarpi, samtrīkṣīra caturguṇaṃ ||

vīrādvimāṣakā kolā, svayaṃ guptāṣarbhaka dvibhiḥ ||

dvemedocca same kalke, kalyāṇaka mahatparaḥ ||

mahākalyāṇakaghṛtaḥ || || (fol.45r5-7)
