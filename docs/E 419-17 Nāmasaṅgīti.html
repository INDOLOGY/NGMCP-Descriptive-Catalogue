Beginning

śākyamuni bhagavān ityādy ārabhya, yāvat tat sādhu bhagavānn iti ṣaḍbhir gāthābhiḥ prativacanaṃ | atha śākyamuni bhagavān sakalam ityādy ārabhya yāvad uṣṇīṣakulaṃ mahad iti dvābhyāṃ gāthābhyāṃ ṣaṭkulāvalokanaṃ | imāṃ ṣaṇmantretyādy ārabhya yāvad arapaṃcanāya te nama r-iti tisṛgāthābhir māyājālābhisaṃbodhikrama sūcitaṃ || (fol. 2r1–4)

End

gaṃbhīrodāravaipulyamahārtho jagadarthakṛt |
buddhānāṃ viṣayo hy eṣa, sarvasaṃbuddhadeśita iti |

tatra eṣo rtho hi yasmāt | gaṃbhīrodāravaipulyas tasmād buddhānām eva viṣayo gocaro nānyeṣāṃ śrāvakādīnāṃ | jagadarthakṛd iti | mahākaruṇāparigṛhītatvāt | sarvabuddhais t[r]aikālyavarhi(!)bhir ddeśitaḥ sarvasaṃbuddhadeśita iti ||   || (fol. 80v9–12)
