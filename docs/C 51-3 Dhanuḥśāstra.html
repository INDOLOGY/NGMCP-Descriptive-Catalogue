Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śivāya ||

natvāhaṃ devadeveśaṃ, śaśāṅkāṅkitaśekharaṃ |

dhnuḥśāstraṃ pravakṣyāmi, purā rudreṇa bhāṣitaṃ ||

[[purā vidyā śivenoktā, tatprabhāvena (!) labhyate ||

nārāyaṇaṃ devadeva sendrādigaṇakinnarāḥ ||]] (!)

ādau vāṇagatir jjñeyaṃ (!), dvitīyaṃ cāpalakṣaṇaṃ ||

sandhanan tu tṛtīyañ ca, vedhanañ ca caturthakaṃ ||

dhanurjyāvedhanaṃ pañca, dhaṣaḥ pañcalakṣaṇaṃ ||

parāṅgasphoṭanañ caiva, svāṅgaṃvāṇanivāraṇaṃ (!) ||

mantravidyāṃ dṛṣṭimuṣṭiṃ pāṃśuvikṣepanan (!) tathā ||

bhasmarekhā tatorddhan (!) tu, raṇadīkṣā tataḥ paraṃ || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

yathoktam ājyaṃ nikhilaṃ prayojyaṃ,

prayogasarvvān prakaroti yuddhe ||

anekaśāstran avalokya †lekhya-

śāstrādamāyur† ya (!) sa varddhanīyaḥ ||

saṃgrāmasvapnāśubhasūcakaḥ syād,

aniṣṭadīpyarthaharāśubhāś ca ||śāstrāvalokaṃ kurute nṛpasya,

pramānakāle (!) paricintanīyāḥ || 21 || ||  (fol. 19v4–6)

Colophon

iti śrībhūbhukdilīpaviracete kodaṇḍaśāstre dikkālapicchāpūjāmaṃtrādeśādhyāyaḥ (!) ṣaṣṭhaḥ paṭalaḥ samāptaḥ || 6 || ||

mūle granthi (!) hared vidyā, madhyeṣu jñānam eve ca |

śirasthāne haret sarvvaṃ, varjjayed dhanudhāraṇaṃ || ||

śubhaṃ || || saṃvat 852 pauṣa,kṛṣṇa,trayodaśī,śukrathva kuhnu thva dhanuśāstra coya dhunakā śrīlakṣmīrāyane, svanihma, śrīhanumantana niyā juro || śubhaṃ || śrīrāmaḥ || 

❖ parṇṇato dviguṇam yugaṃ, cūrṇṇan dadyān muhurmmuhuḥ | (!)

puṣpavat paripīḍyet, rasan dadyāt sudhopamaṃ || (fol. 19v6–20v1)
