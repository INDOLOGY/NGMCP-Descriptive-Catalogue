Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namaḥ śrīpratyaṅgirāyai ||    ||

atha pañcavaktradhyānam ||

pūrvavaktraṃ siddhilakṣmīr dakṣiṇaṃ guhyakālikā |
kālasuṅkarṣaṇī (!) vaktram uttaraṃ samudāhṛtam ||

paściman tu kujāvaktram ūrdhvaṃ (2) pratyaṃgirābhidham ||
dhyānamūrtim idaṃ jñeyaṃ saṃsārārṇavatāraṇam ||

bhuktimuktipradātāraṃ sukhasaubhāgyavarddhanam |
iti kālasaṅkarṣaṇītantre ||    || tasya dhyānam || (fol. 1v1–2)

End

kulena jāyate tasya dāridryakalahāvaham |
mahāpātakajān doṣān sarvavyādhīṃś ca nāśayet |

bahulāśriyam (!) āpnoti sādhakaḥ puṇyahetukām
(6) yasyedaṃ tiṣṭhate gehe tasya śrīharisannidhau |

namo rāśibhayaṃ ghoraṃ sarvabhogādisampadam |
yaś caitāṃ pūjayed bhaktyā pustake pi tathā vidhau

tena tuṣṭā bhaven nityaṃ trailokyaṃ sa carācaram (fol. 48v5–6)

Sub-colophon

iti śrīrudrayāmale śrīnandikeśvarasaṃvāde śrībhavānīsahasranāma ||    || (fol. 35v5)
