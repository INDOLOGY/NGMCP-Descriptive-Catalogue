Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namo gaṇeśāya ||

oṃ namo bhagavate vāsudevāya ||

prapadye devam īśānaṃ śāśvataṃ dhruvam avyayaṃ |

maheśvaraṃ mahātmānaṃ, viśvasya jagataḥ patim ||

prabhuṃ bhūtabhaviṣyasya, sāmpratasya ca satpatiṃ |

jñānam apratigantasya vairāgyañ ca jagatpateḥ |

sthairyyam aiśvaryyadharmāś ca satyaś(!) ca kṛpayā saha |

ya imām īkṣate bhāvān nityaṃ sadasadātmakān | (fol. 1v1–3)

End

apratyayam anāryyañ ca sthitaṃ mṛtakamatsyavat ||

pravṛttipūrvvake vṛtte punaḥ sargo bhaviṣyati |

jñānād guṇā na varddhante rasaḥ satvaṃ tathā tamaḥ ||

nimittakāre(!) rajaso jagad bhaved

viṣat pravṛtte sa viśeṣatā ca |

viśeṣatāñ ca triyatā(!)ñ ca yānti

laukāhyam īsatvarajaḥ pravṛttāḥ ||

satyābhidhāyinas tasya, dhyāyatas tvaṃ nimittataḥ || || (fol. 371r8–9)

Colophon

ity ādimahāpurāṇe brahmāṇḍe upasaṃhārapāde || 124 || || śrīśubham astu ||     || śrīgaṇeśā(ya) namaḥ ||     || (fol. 371r8–9)
