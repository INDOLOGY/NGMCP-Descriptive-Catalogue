Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||    || oṃ namaḥ śivāya ||

jo viviha matta sāara pāraṃ patto vivimala helaṃ
pachu maṃbhā sataraṇḍoṇā aso piṃgalo jaai ||

diho saṃ(2)jutta paro viṃdu jua pāli a a caraṇaṃte
saṃguru vaṃka dumatto asso luhu hoī suddha ekkaalo ||

jahā ||

mādri rueho ahisso jisso buhaadea saṃ(3)bhuṃ kāmanti sāgori gahi lattaṇa kvaṇaī || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

kilā cakkā caccari sahulā saha acaṃdumālā dhavalaṃgī sambhūgitāgaṇḍakāsuddharaṇā(12)redātahahaṃsi suṃdarī ādummilāsu ‥ ṇahu kiriḍāchaṃdā ○ lahutivvagi sālurā ācau aggala tathā sa a chaṃdā jampai kaiā avalla ballabhapiṃ(13)gala nāma prasiddha phaṇīṃdra ha piṃgala nāma prasiddha phaṇindra ||    || (fol. 8v11–13)

Colophon

iti śrīmatpiṃgalanāgakṛtaṃ piṃgalaṃ saṃpūrṇam ||    || śubham ||

caitre māsi ca pūrṇimātithiyute vāre śaśāṅke tathā ||
