Beginning

sāṃkhyāś caturviṃśatitatvāny ācakṣate || 5 || ||


icchā dveṣaḥ sukhaṃ duḥkhaṃ saṃghātaś cetanā dhṛtiḥ |

etat kṣatraṃ samāsena savikāram uvāhṛtaṃ || 6 || ||


icchā sukhaṃ tatsādha‥bhūd abhrūpād iti spṛhā lābhaś cittavṛttiḥ |


krodha iti iṣyati cocyate || sukhaṃ nirupādhīcchāviṣayībhūtā dharmasādharaṇakarinikā cittavṛttiḥ

paramātmasukhavyañjikāḥ duḥkhanirupādhidveṣaviṣayībhūtā cittavṛttir asdharmā sādhāraṇatāraṇikā

saṃghātaḥ paṃcamahābhūtapariṇāmaḥ sendriyaṃ śarīraṃ cetanāsvarūpajñānavyañjikā prakāmāṇā

sādhāraṇakāraṇīkā cittavṛttir jñānāṅhyadhṛtir eva sannānāṃ dehendiryāṇām acaṣṭaṃbhahetuḥ (48r1–5)


End

evam ajñānena prerito bhagavān hiṃsārūpadharmmam āśritya prāyaśo yu‥chvāntaṃ vyāvartayiṣyatīti

dhṛtarāṣṭābhiprāyam āśaṃkya tan nirācikīrtṣuḥ saṃjayo dhṛtarāṣṭraṃ pratyuktvavān ity āha

vaiśampāyanaḥ || ||


sañjaya uvāca ||


evam uktvā ṛṣīkeśo guṇākeśena bhārata |

ubhayoḥ senayor madhye sthāpayicchā(!) rathottamaṃ || 24 ||


bhi‥ ‥ ‥ prasukhataḥ sarve khāñ ca mahīkṣītī ||

uvāca pārtha paśyaivān samavetāṇ kurun iti || 25 ||


he bhārata dhṛtarāṣṭavaṃśamaryyādām anusaṃdhāyāpi drohaṃ prityaktajñānīnām iti

saṃbodhanābhiprāyaḥ || guḍākāyā nidrāyā īśena jitanidratayā sarvatra sāvadhānena arjunenaiva

mukto bhagavān ayaṃ hṛtyāpi shārathye māṃ niyojayatī (exp. 32b6–9)
