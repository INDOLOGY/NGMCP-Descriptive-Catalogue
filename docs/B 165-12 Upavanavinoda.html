Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇēśāya namaḥ || ||

puṣpadhanvā vijayatenaṅgenāpy akhilaṃ jagat ||

vaśīkṛtaṃ yena haṭhāt sasurāsuramānuṣam || 1 ||

puṃsāṃ sarvasukhaikasādhanaphalāḥ saundaryagarbhodgara

krīḍālolavilāsinī janamanasphī[[ta]]pramādāvahāḥ (!) ||

guñjadbhṛṅgavinindra(!)pañkajabharasphārollasaddīrghikā

yuktāḥ santi gṛheṣu yasya vipulārāmāḥ sa pṛthvīpatiḥ || 12 ||

navaṃ manohāri vapur varāṅganā sakhā kalāvit kalavallakīsvanaḥ ||

dhanāni sarvaṃ vipulaṃ sukhaiṣiṇo vinā vihāropavanaṃ na rājate || 13 ||

(exp. 4b:1–11)

«Ending:»

bījaṃ narapalāṅkolatailayogavibhāvitaṃ ||

udgacchati mṛdā pūrṇam ambhaḥ siktaṃ karekṣaṇāt || 34 ||

aṃkolatailabhāvitam uṣitaṃ gośakṛti kumudakandamalaṃ ||

karakāmbhasoñchitabhṛte kalaśe kusumaṃ samudbhavati ||

gokolāsthikarīṣais tu dagdho garte viśodhite ||

uptaś ca bālukāpūrṇaṃ mūlakaṃ gartavad bhavet || 36 ||

iṣṭakacitte samantāt puruṣanikhātevaṭe tarur jātaḥ

vāmana eva hi dhatte phalakusumaṃ sarvakālasamam || 36 || ||(exp. 22a:9–22b:6)

iti śrīmacchāraṅgadharakaviviracita upavanavinodaḥ samāptaḥ || || ||
