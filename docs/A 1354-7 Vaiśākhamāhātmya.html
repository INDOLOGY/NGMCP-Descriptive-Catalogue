Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namo gaṇapataye ||

amvarīṣa uvāca ||

yad etat paramaṃ brahma, vedavādibhir ucyate |
sa devaḥ puṇḍarīkākṣaḥ svayaṃ nārāyaṇaḥ paraḥ ||
yo (2) mūrtto mūrttimān īśo, vyakto 'vyaktaḥ sanātanaḥ |
sarvvadevamayo 'cintyo, dhyātavyaḥ sa kathaṃ hariḥ ||
yasmin sarvvam idaṃ viśvam, otaṃprotaṃ pratiṣṭhi(3)taṃ |
avyaktam ekaṃ paramaṃ, paramātmeti viśrutaṃ ||
yato janmādi jagatāṃ, yo nirmmāyasvayaṃbhuvaṃ |
dadau tasmai ca nigamān, ātmany eva vya(4)vasthitān || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

prasaṃgād idam ākhyātaṃ, māhātmyaṃ kiṃcid u(6)ttamaṃ |
bhānau meṣagate viprā vaiśākhasyāsya bodhanaṃ ||
ataḥ śṛṇvantu bhūdevā, yat pṛṣṭho haṃ purānaghāḥ |
prahlādasya cari(1)traṃ taṃ, tat saṃgrāmaṃ sa viṣṇunā || || (fol. 99r5–99v1)

Colophon

iti śrīpadmapurāṇe pātālakhaṇde vaiśākhamāhātmye yamabrāhamaṇasaṃvāde trayodaśo dhyā(2)yaḥ samāptaḥ || 13 || śubhaṃ || ||

yāte bhujaṃgāṣṭaturaṃgame bde
jyeṣṭhe site nāgatithau budhe hni |

patirggaṇānāṃtara(3)sā dvijo sau vaiśākhamāhātmyam idaṃ lilekha || samvat 788 jyeṣṭhaśukla paṃcamī budhavāra thva kuhnu balānihmayā (4)śrīgaṇapatina thva vaiśākhamāhātmya coya dhunakādina juro || thva velasa rājdhirāja śrī śrī śrīnivāsamallaprabhusana, (5) saptāṅkakaṃsabadhapyāṣaṃadayakaṃ, vokhata voyakaṃ huyakāvala, tavasamadāya, ju va juro || śubham || (fol. 99v1–5)
