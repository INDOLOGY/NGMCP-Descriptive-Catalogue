Excerpts

«Beginning»

oṁ namas tārāyai〈ḥ〉 ||

naṣṭe gate cāntarhite cantre(!) tārodbhave yogamahāsamudre tārārṇavo nāmamahādhitantre naṣṭe ca tasmin punar eṣa kalpaḥ ||

bhūdhṛtya tantreṣu ca vistareṣu

yogān prayogān bahusatvahetoḥ ||

lokeśvaraḥ kalpam idaṃ babhāṣe

śṛṇvantu sarve kila bodhisatvāḥ ||


imaṃ nayaṃ sarvajanārthakāri

lokaṃ vilokyā śaraṇaṃ mayādya

triduṣṭaduṣṭena vidheyam etad

grāhya[ṃ] bhavadbhir mahatādareṇa || (fol. 1v1-4)


«End»

evam avocan nāthaḥ parṣat madhye thitāś ca ye satvāḥ potalake nagarake nasarājye paraduṣṭaduḥkhito bhagavān tan nāsti saṃpannabuddhā bhūtāḥ satvārthakāriṇo niyataṃ || neyataṣṭavvīcaryā(!) naṣṭo nayanapunyaś cakraḥ || || idam avocado bhagavān āryāvalokiteśvara āttamanās te ca bodhisatvā mahāsatvāḥ sā ca sarvāvatiyasadasa(!) devamānuṣāsuragaruḍagandharvaś ca loko ʼvalokiteśvarabhāṣitam abhyanandann iti || (fol. 30v3-31r2)


«Colophon» śrībhagavaty āryatārāyāḥ kurukullā kalpo aṣṭamaḥ samaptaḥ || 8 || ye dharmā || ❁ || śubhasamvat 948 phagun śuklapaṃcami aśunīnakṣatre(!) (!)śukraṃbha(!)yoge buddhavāre saṃpūrṇṇam iti || || riṣitaṃ(!) kāntīpurinagare rājakṛṭīmahāvihāle(!) ʼvasthitavajrācāryasiddhānandena riṣāpitā || yadi sudhaṃ(!) asuddhaṃ vā śvadhaniya(!) mahāguṇaiḥ || || śubha || ❁ || (fol. 31r2-5)
