Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namaḥ avighnam astu

hariharahiraṇyagarbhebhyo namaḥ

svādhyāyavidhivākyārthavicāre prayatāmahe

prabhākaraguror dṛṣṭyā mīmāṃsāraṃbhasiddhaye

atra kecid āhuḥ na mīmāṃsāśāstram ārabdhavyam iti taddhi vedavākyārthavicāropāyabhūtanyāyanibaṃdhanaṃ na ca vedavākyārtho vicārārhaḥ na ca vicārasya viṣayabhūto[ʼ]rtho[ʼ]sti na cāpyasau vicāryanirṇītaḥ prayojanam avakalpate apuruṣārthatvat (fol. 1v1–4)

End

atha manvīran kṣaṇikatā pratyakṣam īkṣitāpi na niścayapatham avatīrṇā tenāgṛhītevāvabhātīti. tathāpi tasyāṃ na pratyakṣaṃ pramāṇaṃ | tatra hi pratyakṣasya svabhāvato vikalpavirahitasya tatpṛṣṭhabhāvinā pratyakṣavyāpāroʼnugamyate tatra pratya/// (fol. 164v4–6)

«Sub-colophon:»

iti mahāmahopādhyāyaśrīśālikanāthamiśrakṛtau prakaraṇapañjikāyāṃ nyāyaśuddhir nāma navamaprakaraṇam || 9 || || (fol. 164r3–4)
