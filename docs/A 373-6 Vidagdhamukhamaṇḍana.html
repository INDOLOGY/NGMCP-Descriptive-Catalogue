Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīgaṇeśāya ||

siddhauṣadhāni bhavaduḥkhamahāgatrānāṃ (!) ,

puṇyātmanāṃ paramakarṇṇarasāyaṇāni (!) |

prakṣā(2)lanaikaśalilāni manomalānāṃ

sauddhodaneḥ pravacanāni ciraṃ jayanti ||

jayanti santaḥ sukṛtaikabhājanaṃ (!)

parā(3)rthasaṃpādanasadvratasthitāḥ |

karasthanīropamaviśvadarśino

jayanti vaidagdhyabhuvaḥ kaver giraḥ || (fol. 1r1–3)

End

mātrācyutakaṃ || ||

sūśyām ācandanavaṃtī, (!) kāntātilakaruṣitā |

kasyaiṣānaṃga(6)bhūḥ prītiṃ, bhujaṅgasya karoti naḥ ||

yathā satprasavaḥ snigdhaḥ saṃsārggavihitaḥ (!) stutiḥ |

tathā sarvvāśrayaḥ satya,m ayaṃ ta(7)va kuladrutaḥ ||

biṃducyutakaṃ || ||

mahīruhā vihaṃgānāṃ, madhurais te kalāpināṃ |

virutaiḥ svāgatānīva, nīravāhā- (fol. 22v5–7)

Colophon

iti dharmmadāsaviracite vidagdhamukhamaṇḍane, bhā(5)ṣāvicitritaprākṛtalakṣaṇo nāma tṛtīyaḥ paricchedaḥ || (fol. 18v4–5)
