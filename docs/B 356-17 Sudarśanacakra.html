Excerpts

Beginning

Śrīgaṇeśena guravo jayanti || ||

viśvacakraṃ kālacakraṃ divyacakraṃ sudarśanaṃ ||

viṣṇoḥ karāmbujāvāsam īḍe tajjñānam adbhutam || 1 ||

atha samasta jyotiḥśāstratattvakāmadhenurūpasudarśanacakram āha ||

sudarśanaṃ dvādaśāraṃ janmabhendvarkarāśitaḥ ||

varṣamāsadinānāṃ hi phalaṃ syāt parivarttanāt || 2 ||

kendrakoṇāṣṭago rāhuḥ pāpā ārtyai śubhā mede ||

tanvādyair varṣamāsārdha hyekābhyāṃ tat prakalpayet || 3 || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

tatrāpi varṣamāsadinabhāgeṣu yasya śubhāśubhagrahasya yogordhadṛṣṭir vā tat tadgrahasya rasabhojanāni sa ceṣṭādyatra gurūpadeśād āveśyaṃ athaikaghrasā(!)n iti iṣṭamāsabhāvād aikaikaṃ dinaṃ vā pratibhāveṣu jñeyam evaṃ sārdhadvirāvarttanena dvādaśabhāveṣu trīṃśad dinānī(!) syuḥ tatrāpi sārdhadivasadvayasya dvādaśāṃśena ghaṭi12 pala30m ekadinasya dvādaśāṃśena 5 ghaṭīpaṃcakena ghaṭikāphalam iti vartṣapraveśaḥ (fol. 3v2–6)

Colophon

iti sudarśanacakram || || athātra pūrvapadyārthaḥ tatrādau karkāṭakena vṛttatrayam uparyuparyuparyatalaṃ vihāya lekhyam tatrārkacandralagnakuṇḍalir likhet kuṇḍalītrayepi pāpā(dhi)‥kasya bhāvo ʼśubhaḥ śubhādhikasya bhāvaḥ śubha ity ādi viṣamarśa[m](!) ūhanīyam || || (fol. 3v6–8)
