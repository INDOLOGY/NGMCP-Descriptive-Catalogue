śrīgurubhyo namaḥ || ||


atha pūjāvidhiḥ ||


vajrodake hūṁ phaṭ svāhā jalam ānīya | oṃ hrīṁ viśuddha sarvapāpāni samayāśeṣavikalpam


apanaya hūṁ | pādau prakṣālya | oṃ svāhā ityācamya 3 || āsane upaviśya ||



sūryaḥ somo yamaḥ kālo mahābhūtāni pañca ca ||


ete śubhāśubhasyeha karmaṇo navasākṣiṇaḥ ||


devīpādagataṃ citraṃ pāpākrāṃntam abhūn mama ||


tanniḥsāraya cittān me pāpāṃ phaṭ phaṭ ca te namaḥ ||


iti saṃprārthya || bhūmau trikoṇaṃ vṛttaṃ svadakṣiṇe vilikhya || oṃ ādhāraśaktaye namaḥ


iti saṃpūjya || pātraṃ prakṣālya || ādhāre nidhāya || krāṁ hṛdayāya namaḥ iti śuddhatoyenāpūrya ||


kroṁ aṃkuśamudrayā tīrthānyāvāhya (fol. 1v1–6)
