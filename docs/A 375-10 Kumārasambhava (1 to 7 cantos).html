Manuscript Features

The text runs form the very beginning up to the middle of the second pāda of śloka 94 of the 7th sarga.

After fol. 38 there is one folio numbered 65, which contains part of some unidentified commentary on the Kumārasaṃbhava. It starts from the middle of śloka 64 and goes up to the middle of sloka 68 of the 3rd sarga.
