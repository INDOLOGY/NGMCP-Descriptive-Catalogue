Beginning

śrīgaṇeśṃ vande ||

śrīsūryyā[ya] namaḥ ||

āditya[[ḥ]] pūjyate yena †tadayaṃti† dine dine ||
ṛṇaṃ pāpaṃ kṣayaṃ yāti (!) prabhāte sūryyadarśanāt || 1 ||

ahaṃ brahmā ahaṃ viṣṇur ahaṃ rudraḥ śivālaye ||
tad ebhiḥ sahito deva (!) prabhāte sūryadarśanāt || 2 ||

kārttikyāṃ ca samaṃ puṇyaṃ bhīṣmapaṃcadineṣu ca ||
śataśo pi sahasreṣu prabhāte sūryadarśanāt || 3 || (fol. 1v1–2r3)

End

sūryyāṣṭakam paṭhen nityaṃ sarvapāpaiḥ pramucyate ||
dhanapuṇyaphalaṃ dānaṃ labhate nātra saṃśayaḥ || 9 ||

rājadvāre paṭhed ghore saṃgrāme śatruśaṃkaṭe ||
vyādhicaurabhyaṃ nāsti sūryyāya (!) praṇamāmy aham || 10 || (fol. 3r4–3v3)

Colophon

iti śrīmacchaṃkarācāryaviracitaṃ sūryyāśṭakaṃ saṃpūrṇam ||    || (fol. 3v3–4)
