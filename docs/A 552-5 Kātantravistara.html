Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ❖ ||

maheśvaraṃ namaskṛtya kumāraṃ tadanantaraṃ

sugamaḥ kriyate smābhir ayaṃ kātantravistaraḥ |

abhiyogaparāḥ pūrvve bhāṣāyāṃ yat prapedire |

prāyeṇa tad ihāsmābhiḥ parityaktaṃ na kiñcana |

siddho varṇasamāmnāyaḥ || tatra caturddaśādau svarāḥ | daśa samānāḥ | teṣāṃ dvau dvāv anyonyasya savarṇau | (fol. 1v1–3)

End

ser grāse karmakarttari | ṛlvādibhyo ʼpṛṇaḥ || teḥ kṣoḥ | vraśceḥ kac | kṣer ddīrvān | śyo ʼsparśe | anapādāne ʼñceḥ | na prakāśe | avijigīṣāyāṃ divaḥ | hrīr ghotrondanudavidāṃ vā | devatrātaḥ | kṣai śuṣipacāṃ makavāḥ | vā prastyomaḥ | nirvvāṇo ʼvāte | bhinnrṇavikṣāḥ śakalāghamarṇabhogeṣu | vikṣaḥ pratīte | anupasargāt phullakṣībakṛśollāghāḥ | utphullasaṃphullaparikṛśāḥ | avarṇahaṭo vṛddhiḥ || || (fol. 85r1–4)

Colophon

iti śrīmatkarṇadevopādhyāya śrīvarddhamānamiśraviracite kātaṃtravistare kṛtprakaraṇe ṣaṣṭhaḥ pādaḥ samāptaḥ || || || saṃvat 1589 samae kārttikasudī tṛtīyā gurau || miśra śrī .. .. .. .. .. .. kapakarārthaṃ paropakārārthaṃ sūtrapustakaliṣāpitaṃ || || śubhaṃ bhavatu || (fol. 85r4–6)
