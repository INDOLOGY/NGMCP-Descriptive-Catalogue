Foliation The MS has two different foliation systems. Both foliations are on the verso. The first one (in the lower left-hand margin) starts from 279 and runs up to 290 under the title ❖ dharma­putrikā, while the second one (in the middle right-hand margin) starts from 1 and runs up to 12.

It seems that it was a multi-text MS, since the first foliation starts from 279.

Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namaḥ śivāya ||

vande devaṃ purāṇaṃ śivam amaraguruṃ bhūtalasthena mūrddhnā

nityaṃ śāntaṃ suśūkṣmaṃ tribhuvananamitaṃ sarvvasatvaikanāthaṃ |

nissaṃgaṃ nirvvikāraṃ prajananamaraṇakleśadoṣair vvimuktaṃ,

yogābhyāsaikavedyaṃ triguṇavirahitaṃ na prasahyasvarūpaṃ ||

nirvyāpāraṃ susūkṣmaṃ kṣitijaladahanākāśavātair vvimuktaṃ

pratyakṣādipramāṇaiḥ kṛtibhir aviditaṃ hetudṛṣṭāntasū(!)nyaṃ |

rūpādīnām abhāvād avayavavikalād indriyair aprameyaṃ,

pratyātmajñānagamyaṃ paṭubhir api girā niṣprapaṃcasvabhāvaṃ ||

śāstrasyādipraṇetāraṃ sanakaṃ munipuṃgavaṃ |

namaskṛtya pravakṣyāmi saṃhitāṃ dharmmaputrikāṃ |

siddhiṃ prāpnoti yogasya yuṃjan yogī na saṃśayaḥ ||

viditvā sādhanopāyaṃ ṣoḍaśānāṃ tu nirṇṇayaṃ |

catvāri yogaprakaraṇāny antarāyāś caturvvidhā(!) |

teṣāṃ jaya(!) cikitsā ca dve liṃge siddhisūcake | (fol. 1v1–4)

End

tān saṃpaśyābhijānāti sa yogī hy abhidhīyate |

ālocyābhyantagūḍhārthaśāstraṃ sanakanirmmitaṃ |

vastumātraṃ samādāya dharmmaśīlasya sūnunā |

sukhāvabodhabālānāṃ graṃthitā saṃhitā mayā |

nirūpya dātum arhanti doṣān tyaktvā maṇī(!)ṣiṇaḥ |

ye cintā nisutṛṣṇayeva, satataṃ kliṣyanti ye cāpare

svāmyājñāśravaṇaspṛhād anudinaṃ tiṣṭhanti rājāṅga[[ṇāḥ]] |

ye cānye madirāsavādivividha[[s]]vādena saṃmūrcchitāḥ |

tair yyogasya phalaṃ śa(!)tā bahumataṃ nāsvādya(!) ta[[t sa]]t sukhaṃ ||     || (fol. 12r3–5)
