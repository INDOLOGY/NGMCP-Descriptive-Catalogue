Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namo govindāya ||

jayati parāsara sūnuḥ satyavatī hṛdayanandano vyāsaḥ |
yasyāsya kamala galitaṃ vāṅmadhupuṇyaṃ janaḥ pivati ||

pārāsarya vacaḥ sarojam amalaṃ gītārthagandhotkadaṃ
nānākhyānaka keśaraṃ harikathā savodhanāvodhitam |

lokaiḥ sajjana ṣaṭpadairaharahaḥ pepīyamānaṃ mudā
bhūyādbhāratapaṃkajaṃ kalimala pradhvaṃsivaḥ śreyase || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

kanyādānaṃ vṛṣotsarga, tīrthasevā sabhāprayāḥ |
annadānasya sarvvāṇI, kalānārganti ṣoḍaśīṃ ||

hṛdayaśvaratha dānāni, maṇiratnaṃ vasuṃdharā |
agniṣṭo mastri (!) rātraśca, ye cānye vividhābhakhāḥ (!) |

sarvvapyannā (!) ------- /// (fol. 117r1–3)

Colophon

|| itihāsasamuccaye annadānaṃ || (fol. 115v5)
