Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīkṛṣṇāya ||

nārāyaṇaṃ namaskṛtya, naraṃ caiva narottamaṃ |

devīṃ sarasvatīñ caiva, tato jayam udīrayet ||     ||

janamejaya uvāca ||

gate bhagavati vyāse, dhṛtarāṣṭro mahīpatiḥ |

kim aceṣṭhata viprarṣe, tan me vyākhyātum arhati ||

tathaiva kauravo rājā, dharmmaputro mahāmanāḥ |

kṛpaḥ(!)prabhṛtayaś caiva, kim akurvvata te trayaḥ |

aśvatthāmnaḥ śrutaṃ karmma, śāpabhyo(!) nyā(!)nyakāritaḥ |

vṛttāntam uttraṃ brūhi, yad abhāṣata saṃjaya ||      || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

na hi naḥ kiṃcid aprāpyaṃ, bhaved api divi sthitaṃ |

na cemaṃ vaiśasaṃ ghoraṃ, kuravāntakaraṃ bhavet |

evaṃ vilapya bahulaṃ, dharmmarājo yudhiṣṭhiraḥ |

prarudan duṣ(!)khito rājan, cakārāsyodakaṃ prabhuḥ |

tato vineduḥ sahasā, striyaḥs(!) tāḥ khalu sarvvaśaḥ |

abhito yo(!) sthitas tatra, tasminn udakakarmmaṇi |

tatas tā ānayāmāsa, karṇṇasya sa paricchadāḥ ||

striyaḥ kurupatir ddhīmān, bhrātuḥ premnā(!) yudhiṣṭhiraḥ |

sa tābhiḥ saha dharmmār(!)tmā, pretakṛtyam anantaraṃ |

kṛtto(!)ttatāra gaṃgāyāṃ, śa(!)lilād ākulendriyaḥ ||     || (fol. 20v3–6)

Colophon

iti śrīmahābhārate śatasāhasryāṃ saṃhitāyāṃ vaiyāśikyāṃ strīparvva samāptaṃ ||     ||     || (fol. 20v6)
