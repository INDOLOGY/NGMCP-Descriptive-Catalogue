Beginning of the root text

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

Beginning of the commentary

śrīgaṇapataye namaḥ ||

śrīgurucaraṇakamalebhyo namaḥ |

śrīkāśīśayor jayati ||

saccidānaṃdasaṃdoha(!) bhaktai(!) kāmodamaṃdiraṃ ||
sādaraṃ naumi taṃ bhaktyā śrīgopījanavallabhaṃ || 1 || (fol. 1v1–2)

End of the root text

naraḥ śrutvaitad ākhyānaṃ khakholkādityasaṃbhavaṃ ||

End of the commentary
