Excerpts

Beginning

(fol. 1r1) ❖ namo ratnatrayāya ||   ||

natvā triratnaṃ jagadekaratnaṃ
†sāpunyapāśaṃ† karuṇāmayaṃ ca
guruṃ mahat-jñānapradaṃ pravakṣye
'ṣṭamīvrataṃ tan mahimānan(!) uttamaṃ

gu(2)rubhaṭṭārakaṃ jayaśriyaṃ bhikṣāga(!)nānte yogānte dharmmāsanaṣṭham apṛcchad aṣṭamīmāhātmyam āryyāvalokiteśvaramukhyāṣṭamīdivase jinaśrīḥ saśiṣyaḥ śrāvaya(3)satyāc(!) chuklāṣṭamīmāhātmyam adyāṣṭamīparvaṇi guro ||   ||

jayaśrībhikṣur āha śrūyatāṃ bho || gurubhaṭṭārakopadeśaṃ yathā kathayāmīti ||

End

a(fol. 31r2)tha śakro devānām indraḥ sārddham anekaiḥ devaputrasahasraiḥ svāgatādibhir anvamodayad āha ca

virūpo yaṃ mahīpālaḥ sundarīballabho priyaḥ
virū(3)patvān mumurṣu[r] yo mahāhradam upācarat

lohitamuktāvalī kvayam(!) ity uktavatīndre svātmajasyopavāsakuśasya haste ity uktvāryāvaloki(4)kiteśvaraḥ kutreti pṛṣṭe sukhāvatīṃ pratyāgamat tata āryāṣṭāṃgopoṣitas tvam asīti tāṃ strīn mandāramālābhir amṛtena divyabhojanena ca (5) divyālaṃkāravāsobhir upoṣadhapūrvābhidhānaṃ tam arcayām āsa mahendraḥ atha kecit sumupoṣitāḥ sukhamṛtāś cakravarttipadaṃ kecid divyaṃ padaṃ (6) kecit sukhāvatī tathāgataṃ bhaikṣukaṃ śrāvakayānikaṃ pratyekayānikaṃ mahāyānikaṃ bodhisātvikaṃ samavāpnuvan svasvakāmunātaḥ(!)

upavāsakuśaś cakravarttī pitṛśāsanam ādhāyāṣṭamīm upāsya māhīndraṃ padaṃ mahotsavato dhyatiṣṭhat
