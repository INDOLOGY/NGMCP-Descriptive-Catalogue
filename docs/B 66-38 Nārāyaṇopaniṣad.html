Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||    ||

oṃ atha puruṣo ha vai nāṛāyaṇo kāmayata || prajāḥ sṛjyeti || nārāyaṇāt prāṇo jāyate manaḥ sarvvendriyāni ca || kha[ṃ] vāyur jyotir āpaḥ pṛthvī viśvasya dhāriṇī⟨ṃ⟩ || nārāyaṇād brahmā jāyate nārāyaṇād rudro jāyate nārāyaṇā[t] prajāpatiḥ prajāyate || nārāyaṇād indro jāyate nārāyaṇād aṣṭau vasavo nārāyaṇād⟨d⟩ ekādaśā rudro(!) jāyaṃte nārāyaṇā[d] dvādaśādityāḥ sarvvā devatāḥ sarvve ṛṣayaḥ sarvvāṇi chaṃḍāṃsi sarvvāṇi bhūtāni nārāyaṇād eva samutpadyaṃte || (fol. 1v1–2r2)

End

oṃ atharvaśiro yo dhīte prātar⟨m⟩ adhīyāno rātrīkṛtaṃ pāpaṃ nāśayati śāyam adhīyāno divasakṛtaṃ pāpaṃ nāśayati mādhyaṃdinam ādityābhimukho dhīyānaḥ || paṃcamahāpātakopapātakāna(!) pramucyaṃte || sa sarvavedapārāyaṇaṃ puṇyaṃ labhate || nārāyaṇe śāyujyam āpnoti ya evaṃ veda ||    || (fol. 4v1–5)

Colophon

iti nārāyaṇopanīṣadaṃ saṃpurṇama(!) śubhma (fol. 4v5)
