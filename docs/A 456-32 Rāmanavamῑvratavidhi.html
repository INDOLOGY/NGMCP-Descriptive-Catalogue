«Beginning»


klīṃḥ ||

tara caitra śukla navamyāṃ rāmanavamīvrataṃ |

tac ca parividdhāyāṃ madhyāhnavyāpinyāṃ kāryaṃ tad uktaṃ


agastyasaṃhitāyāṃ


caitra śuklā tu navamai punarvasuyutā yadi ||

saiva madhyāhnayogena mahāpuṇyatamā bhavet ||


dinadvaya ṛkṣayukta tad ayukta madhyāhnavyāptāvavyāptāvekadeśavyāptau vā uttarā anyathā parā

tatra caitra śukla navamyāṃ rāmanavamīvrataṃ ity uktvā tad uktaṃ tatraiva


navamī cāṣṭamī viddhā tyājyā viṣṇuparāyaṇaiḥ ||

upoṣaṇaṃ navamyāṃ vai daśamyāṃ pāraṇaṃ bhaved iti | (fol. 1v1–4)


«End:»


dvādaśābdaśatenāpi yat pāpaṃ naiva dahyati ||

vilayaṃ yāti tatpāpaṃ śrīrāmanavamaivrataṃ ||

japena rāmamantrrāṇāṃ yogyatāṃ yāti tasya tu ||

upoṣya ca smaran rāmaṃ nyāsapūrvam ananyadhīḥ ||

guror ājñām anuprāpya tasya rāmam anusmaran ||

ya evaṃ vidivad kuryād rāmapūjāṃ samāhitaḥ ||

antakāle tadā mokṣaṃ prāpya vaikuṣṭham āpnuyāt ||

mumukṣavo hi satataṃ śrīrāmanavamīvratam ||

na jānanti surā sarve devendro pi viśeṣataḥ ||

tasmāt sarvātmanā sarvaiḥ karttavyaṃ navamīvratam ||

mucyaṃte sarvvapāpebhyo yānti brahma sanātanam || || (fol. 9v2–10r2)



«Colophon»

ity agastyasaṃhitāyāṃ sāroddhāre rāmanavamīvratapūjākathā samāptā || || śrīḥ śītāpatī rāmacandrāḥ

(!) namaḥ || ❁ || ❁ || (fol. 10r2–3)
