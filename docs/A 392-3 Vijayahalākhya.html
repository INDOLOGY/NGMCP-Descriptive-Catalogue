Beginning

|| oṃ ||

savva sma vayaṇapaṃkayanivāsiṇīṃ paṇamiṃ kraṇamuya deviṃ

dhammāyativagga jayaṃ suyaṇāṇamudāsiyaṃ vodhaṃ 1

vividaka ivira īyāi gābhāṇaṃ vaṃhakālā iṣin nūṇaṃ

raiyaṃvvakṣā . . . vibhiṇā jayavallahaṃ nāma 2

rāgākṣapakṣāvajakṣa paḍhijhaṃti paturagāhātuṃ

vaṃpibhavachālagjaṃ pakṣastīpadddhva ilaiṇīyā 3 (fol. 1v1–3)

End

vibhaḍiya hakālāmayāṃkāvaṃdostarassa maṇdalaṃ yāttā

nīsaritraṃ tārasitraṃ gayavihāvākāsam udvarai || 8

harasirasaraṇam migatrastu kāṃtā taha jaḍāṇa majkammi

tahavigali jjāyi vaṃdovihinyāṃ kā paṇā sai 9 | 1000 (fol. 34r5–6)

Colophon

iti padyālayaśāstram idaṃ likhāpitaṃ bhaṭṭaśrīmukuṃdarāyaputrabhaṭṭa śrīnaṃda lālena svasuputrapotrādi (!) pāṭhārtham || śreyaskaraṃ bhavatu || (fol. 34r7)
