Beginning

oṃ namaḥ śivāya ||

yadā dāruṇā bhāṣaṇā bhīṣaṇā me
vadiṣyaṃty upānte kṛtāntasya dūtāḥ |
tadā manmathas tvatpadāmbhoruhasthaṃ(!)
kathaṃ niścaraṃ(!) syān namas te stu śambho || 1 ||

yadā dunnirvvāra(!)vyatho haṃ śayāno
ruṭhan nistaraṃ niṣkṛto vyaktavāṇī |
tadā jahnukanyājalālaṃkṛtaṃn(!) te
jaṭāmaśulaṃ(?)<ref>unmetrical</ref> manmano mandiraṃ syāt || 2 ||

yadā pāram asmā⟪dya⟫d yamasthānamahir
jjanair vvā vihīnam gamiṣyāmi dūraṃ
tadā tan niruddhaṃ kṛtāntasya mārggaṃ
mahādeva mahyaṃ manojñaṃ prayaccha || 3 || (fol. 18v8-10) <references/>

End

mahādeva śambho girīśa triśūlīn(!)
tvadīyaṃ samastaṃ vibhātīti yasmāt |
śivād anyathā daivataṃ no hi jāne
tasmāc chivo haṃ śivo haṃ śivo haṃ || 14 ||

anena stave(n ā)darād ambikeśaṃ
parāṃ bhaktim ātanvato ye namanti |
nṛtau nirbhayās te lasantaṃ bhajante
hṛdāmbhojamadhye samāsīnam īśaṃ || 15 || (fol. 19r9-19v1)
