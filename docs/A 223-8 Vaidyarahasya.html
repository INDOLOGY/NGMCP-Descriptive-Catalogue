Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

yan nāma smaraṇaṃ samastaduritadhvaṃsāvahaṃ puṇyadaṃ

bhaktānām abhayaṃkaraṃ sukhakaraṃ sarvārthasiddhiḥ pradaṃ || (!)

taṃ natvārjjunam īśvaraṃ gurukṛpā ʼvāptaiḥ susiddhair varair

yogaṃ vaidyarahasyam adya tanute vidyāpatiḥ kautukāt || 2 || (!)

yataḥ samastarogāṇāṃ jvaro rājeti viśrutaḥ ||

ato jvarādhikārotra prathamaṃ likhyate mayā || 2 ||

aṃśāṃśaṃ yatra doṣāṇāṃ vivektuṃ naiva śaknuyāt ||

sādhāraṇīṃ kriyāṃ tatra vidadhīta cikitsakaḥ || 3 || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

dīrghadīrghāsu bahavo graṃthā saṃti tathāpi me ||

sāṃpradāyikayogānāṃ saṃgrahārthaṃ mayaṃ (!) kṛtaḥ || 6 ||

catuḥpaṃcāśadbhir munividhuśatenādhisahitair

gadebde (!) 1754 bhūpārkān nabhasi sitapakṣe phaṇitithau

iti śrīmad vaṃśīdharatanujavidyāpatikṛto

bhavat pūrṇo graṃthaḥ sakalabhiṣagānaṇdajanakaḥ || 7 ||(fol. 97r6–8)

Colophon

iti śrīmad upādhyāya vaṃśīdharātmaja vidyāpatinirmmito vaidyarahasyābhidhoyoga samupaset (!) || likhitaṃ radhunaṃdane (fol. 97r8–9)
