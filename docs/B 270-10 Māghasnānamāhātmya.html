Manuscript Features

Fol. 162 is maintained twice but text is not repeated.

Numbers 99 and 144 are added by a second hand in the verso side of folios 89 and 143 respectively but the text is not interrupted.

MS is dated saṃvat 777 māghakṛṣṇatrayodaśī ādityavāra.

Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ ||

nārāyaṇan namaskṛtya narañ caiva narottamam |

deviṃ sarasvatiñ caiva, tato jayam udīrayet ||

(2) dvaipāyanauṣṭhapuṭaniḥsṛtam aprameyaṃ

puṇyaṃ pavitram atha pāpaharaṃ śivaṃ ca |

yo bhārataṃ sama(3)dhigachati vācyamānaṃ,

ki (!) tasya puṣkalajalair abhiṣecanena ||

pārāśaryyavacaḥsarojam ama(4)laṃ gītārthagaṃdhotkaṭaṃ,

nānākhyānakakeśaraṃ harikathāsaṃbodhanodbodhitaṃ |

loke sajja(2r1)naṣaḍpadair aharahaḥ pepīyamānaṃ mudā,

bhūyād bhāratapaṃkajaṃ kalimalapradhvaṃsinaḥ śreyase || (fol. 1v1–2r1)

End

imaṃ mantraṃ (4) samuccāryya, śāntikumbhāmbusecanaṃ |

anena brahmabījena, prabhāvojjvalatejasā |

dūritoghavināśārthaṃ,(!) (1)mantrapūtena siṃjayet (!) |

haviṣyāneṣu (!) bhuṃjīta, māghasnānaphalaṃ labhet ||

labhate sarvvakāmaṃ ca, mucyate sarvvakilviṣā(2)t ||

phalam ekaṃ tyajed yac ca, tatphalaṃ pūrṇṇamādine (!) |

brāhmaṇāya pradātavyaṃ, paścāt prāśanam ārabhe(3)t || (fol. 175r3–175v3)

Colophon

iti śrīvidyāpurāṇe māghasnānamāhātmyaṃ samāptaṃ || ||

saṃvat 777 māghakṛṣṇatrayo(4)daśī, ādityavāra kunhu dhunakā || likhiti, vipra śrīgaṃgārāmana, paśracheyā || (fol. 175v3–4)
