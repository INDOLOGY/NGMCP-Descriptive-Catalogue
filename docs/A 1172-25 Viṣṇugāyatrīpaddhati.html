Excerpts

«Beginning»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

atha viṣṇugāyatrīpaddhatiḥ ||

prātar utthāya guruṃ namaskṛtya ||

gurur brahmā gurur viṣṇuḥ gurur devo maheśvaraḥ ||

gurur eva paraṃ brahma tasmai śrīgurave namaḥ ||

ajñānatimiraṃdhasya jñānāṃjanasalākayā

cakṣur unṃmilitaṃ (!) yena tasmai śrīgurave namaḥ ||

akhaṃḍamaṃḍalākāraṃ vyāptaṃ yena carācaraṃ ||

tatpadaṃ darśitaṃ yena tasmai śrīgurave namaḥ || (exp. 2t1–4)


«End»

bhāsvatkoṭisamadyutiṃ jaladharaśyāmaṃ supītāṃvaraṃ

nānāratnavibhūṣaṇojjvalatanuṃ vakṣollasatkaustubham ||

śaṃkhaṃ cakragadābjamaṇyasidhanur bāṇān karāṃbhoruhai

vibhrāṇaṃ garuḍasthitaṃ kamalayāśliṣṭaṃ bhaje keśavam ||

iti dhyātvā aṣṭottaraśataṃ japet ||

paścāt punaḥ pūrvoktaprāṇāyāmatrayaṃ ca karaṃnyāsaṃ aṃganyāsaṃ ca kṛtvā jalamādāya guhyātiguhyagoptṛ tvaṃ gṛhāsthāsmatkṛtaṃ japaṃ || siddhir bhavatu me deva tvatprasādāt kare sthitā || iti maṃtreṇa tajjalaṃ japaṃ ca viṣṇave namaḥ iti samarpya visarjayet || ācamya || (exp. 2b, 27–34)


«Colophon»

iti viṣṇugāyatrīpaddhatiḥ || (exp. 2b, 34–35)
