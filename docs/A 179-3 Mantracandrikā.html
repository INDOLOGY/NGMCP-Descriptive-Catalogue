Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||     ||

natvā gurūpadāṃbhojaṃ gaṇeśaṃ sāradāṃ śivāṃ |

śīvaṃ sūryyaṃ hṛṣīkeśaṃ tanyate maṃtracaṃdrikāṃ(!) || 1 ||

graṃthaṃ graṃthāṃtarād vākyān nānārthaṃ pratibudhya ca |

saukaryyārthaṃ ca saṃkṣepāt sārasāraṃ pratanyate || 2 ||

vande [ʼ]haṃ sakalāṃ sveṣṭāṃ paramātmasvarūpiṇīṃ |

yasyāḥ kaṭākṣamātreṇa sādhakaḥ kin na sādhayet || 3 || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

śāṃtidaṃ suhṛdaṃ strīṇāṃ, bhūtapretabhayāpahaṃ ||

garbharakṣākaraṃ śuddhaṃ param asyā(!)n na vidyate ||

mustāmothaṃ niśāharidrā vahniś citrakamūlaṃ yaṣṭiḥ madhuyaṣṭiḥ rohiṇī kaṭukī phalatrayaṃ haritakyādiprasthaparimitaṃ ghṛtaṃ samyak pācayet maṃtrasādhitam uktaphaladaṃ || iti paṃcagavyaṃ ||     || (fol. 250r1–3)

Colophon

iti śrīmaṃtracandrikāyāṃ yaṃtrakaraṇādhikāraḥ saptaviṃśas taraṃgaḥ ||     ||     || śūbhaṃ ||     || śrīśakarāje 1695 vikaramābde 1830 caitramāse kṛṣṇapakṣe paṃcamyān tithau viśāṣā(!) nakṣatre vṛhaspativāsare śubhalagne || taddine daivajñakṛṣṇena likhitam idaṃ pustakaṃ saṃpūrṇaṃ ||     ||

śrīśivāya namaḥ ||     || (fol. 250r3–5)
