Beginning

oṃ namaḥ śrītripurasundarīkalyāṇavṛṣṭibhir ivāmṛtapū[[ri]]tābhiḥ

End

hrīṃkāraṃ trayasaṃpuṭenamahatā mantreṇa saṃdīpitaṃ

stotraṃ yaḥ prati .. saraṃ tava puro mātar jjapan mantravit |

tasyoktau vibhujo bhavanti vasugo lakṣmī cirasthāpanī

vāṇī nirmmalaśuktibhāribharitā jāgartti dīrghaṃ yaśaḥ || 16 || (exps. 44b3–5)

iti śrīrudrayāmale śrīparāmbāmahātripurasundaryyai bhaṭṭārakās kalyāṇavṛṣṭistotraṃ samāptaṃ || ○ || (exps. 44b5–6)
