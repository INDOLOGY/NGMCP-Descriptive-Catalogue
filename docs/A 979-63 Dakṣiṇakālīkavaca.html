Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

krīṃ ātmatatvāya svāhā || krīṃ vidyātatvāya svāhā || krīṃ śivatatvāya svāhā || ity ācamaṇaṃ (!) || kālyai namaḥ || kapālinyai namaḥ || iti oṣṭhaprokṣaṇam || balāyai karaṃ prakṣyālayet || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

idaṃ kavacam ajñātvā yo japet kālikāstavam ||

dhyānena koṭijāpena tasya vidyā na siddhyati ||

pade pade bhaved duḥkhaṃ lokānāṃ nindito dhruvam ||

iha loke sadā duḥkhī pare ca narakaṃ vrajet ||

gurumaṃtrasamaṃ jñātvā yo japet kavacottamam ||

tasya vidyā bhavet siddhā satyaṃ satyaṃ varānane || (fols. 10v1–5)
