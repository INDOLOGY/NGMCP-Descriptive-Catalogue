Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||    ||

oṃ namaḥ śivāya ||    ||

atha devīṃ pravakṣyāmi vidyāṃ khecarisaṃjñitāṃ ||
yayā vijñātamātreṇa trailokye smin jarāmaraḥ || 1 ||

mṛtyuvyādhijarāgrastaṃ dṛṣṭvā viśvam imaṃ priye ||
buddhiṃ dṛḍhatarāṃ kṛtvā khecarīṃ tu samāśrayet || 2 || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

bhṛṃgaṃ samūlaṃ pariśodhya cūrṇaṃ kṛṣṇaṃ tilaṃ cāmalakaṃ dadhi ca |
madhutrayaiḥ svādya narottmāni vyādhoparogā na jarā na mṛtyuḥ || 9 ||

nirguṃṭhapatram ekaikaṃ trikālaṃ paribhakṣayet ||
dvādaśābdād bhaved devi jarāmaraṇavarjitaḥ || 10 ||

niguhyānalanirmuṃḍi sāmyaṃ saṃsādhayed rajaḥ
śarkarāghṛtamadhvarkavatsarāpalitāpaham || 11 ||

ṣaṇmāsagaṃdhakaṃ svarṇaṃ tilakaṃ rudralocanaṃ ||
madhuratrayayutaṃ cūrṇam ajarāmarapadapradam || 12 ||

rasaśālmaliniryāsagaṃdhakamadhuratrayaiḥ ||
bhakṣayet prātar utthāya ṣaṇmāsād ajarāmaraḥ || 13 ||    || (fols. 16v6–17r3)

Sub-colophons

iti śrīmahāādināthena śekte (!) mahākālayogaśāstre umāmaheśvarasaṃvāde prathamaḥ paṭalaḥ || 2 || (fol. 5v4–5)

iti śrīmahāādināthena prokte mahākālayogaśāstre umāmaheśvarasaṃvāde khecaryā vidyāyām dvitīyaḥ paṭalaḥ || 2 || (fol. 12r9–12v1)

iti śrīmahāādināthena nirūpite mahākālayogaśāstre umāmaheśvarasaṃvāde khecarīvidyāyām tṛtīyaḥ paṭalaḥ || 3 || (fol. 16r4–6)

Colophon

iti śrīmahāādināthena nirūpite mahākālayogaśāstre khecaryāṃ vidyāyām oṣadhayogo nāma caturthaḥ paṭalaḥ || 4 || samāptā || śubham || ❖ || (fol. 17r3–5)
