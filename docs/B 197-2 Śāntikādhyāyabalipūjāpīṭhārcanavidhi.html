oṁ namaḥ śrīmahāgaṇeśāyai(!) namaḥ ||

brāhmaṇa ārcāryya(!) jośi thvate syana sthila hārāva vayāva | uttala soya || majilasā pūrvva soyaṃ teva || sārccayanadakṣiṇa || balipāṭa 1 madhyasa kṣatramūlana yāya || ṅūba bali pūrvvādikramana brahmāṇyā dina ṅūyake || pāṭha śāntikādhyāya || || jalapātra || arghapātra || pañcabali || gaṇa vaṭūka || thvate paṭavāsana coyava jiyake || (fol. 1v 1-6)


«End»

ambe pūrvva || aiṃ 5

ambe pūrvvagataṃ padaṃ bhagavatī caitanyarūpātmikā

jñānecchā bahulā tathā hariharau brahmā marīcitrayaṃ |

bhāsvad bhairavapaṃcakaṃ tad anu ca śrīyoginīpaṃcakaṃ

candrākkāci(!) marīciṣaṭkavimalaṃ māṃ pātu nityaṃ śrīkūjā ||(!)

bali visarjjana yāya || || pūrvvādikramena || || aṣṭapīṭhasa balicchoya || || dathū pāṭajūko galāḍalaṃkhu sacchoya || gaṇādhyākhā ināyasa || vaṭūka dūmājusa choya || dvadudako dukhāpikhā || sākṣi thāya || (fol. 53v1-6)
