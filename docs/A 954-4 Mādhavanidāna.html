«Beginning of the root text:»

praṇamya jagad utpattisthitisaṃhārakāraṇaṃ

svargāpavargayor dvāraṃ trailokyaśaraṇaṃ śivaṃ || 1 ||

(5) nānā munīnāṃ vacanair idānīṃ

samāsataḥ sadbhiṣajāṃ niyogāt ||

sopadravāriṣṭanidānaliṃ(6)go

nibadhyate rogaviniścayo yaṃ || 2 || (fol. 2r4–6)

«Beginning of the commentary:»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || śrīkṛṣṇāya namaḥ ||

tulasīkusumaṃ dadhat tamāla-

pratimaṃ valla pi gopabālasaktam ||

kimapi tribhavaikamūlakaṃdaṃ

paramānaṃda(2)mahaṃ mahaḥ smarāmi || 1 ||

śrīhammīranareśvaraḥ samabhavad vīraḥ pratāpānala-

jvālājālakarāladaśvayavana prāgbhāra śuṣkendhanaḥ |

yasya prauḍhasabhāgaṇavapi (!) yaśaḥ svo(3)rllokakallolinī (!)

kallolāmalamaṃganāḥ sumanasāṃ gāyaṃti romāṃcitāḥ || 2 || (fol. 1v1–3)

«End of the root text:»

yatkṛtaṃ sukṛtaṃ kiṃcit kṛtvainaṃ rugviniścayaṃ ||

muṃcaṃtu jaṃtavas tena nityam ātaṃkasaṃtati (!) || 64 ||  śrīrāmaḥ (fol. 158v6)

«End of the commentary:»

medo doṣo rogaḥ || 59 || tvagāmayaḥ kuṣṭarogaḥ || 60 ||

romāṃto masūrikābhedaḥ || kṣudraḥ kṣudrarogaḥ |

āsyo mukhaṃ ⟪ru⟫rugviniścaye ayam uddeśa⟨ḥ⟩kathanaṃ graṃthakārasya (7) prāmāṇyam āha subhāṣitam iti dvayaṃ || 64 || (fol. 158v1,7)
