«Beginning:»'


oṃ siddhi śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

atha vairāṭapurāṇa pravakṣyāmi || piṃḍabrahmāṃḍa viseṣitaṃ pañca tattva pacīsa prakṛti triśunyaḥ

paṃca devatā || triyaguṇaḥ triyo devatā || saptapātāla caturddaśa bhuvanaḥ || sapta brahmāṇḍa

ekaviṃśa svarga || ekavīṃśa brahmāṃḍaḥ || nava kulī nāga aṣṭa parvataḥ navalāṣa tārāmaṇḍala +

madhye candrasūrya devatā || aṭhāraha bhāra vanaspati || navaṣaḍasaptadīpāvatī prathimi

saptasāgaraḥ nava saina vāsī nadī || sāta vāraḥ satāvīsa nakṣatra (fol. 1v1–6)


«End:»


sukṣama āhāraṃ alpanidrā samasarīca sādhanaṃ || ṣaḍamāsāntareṣu ṣaḍacakrakaṃ ||

vāśīramati urdhvamaṇḍaleḥ suṣamanā mārgeṣu bhave pavanaṃ ṣaḍamuṣa asteṣu prāptaṃ || tatra

paścāt caḍhata pavanaṃ meruparvataṃ ca garjite || aikavīsa purīmadhye svāsaṃ gagana maṇḍalaṃ

ca prāptaṃ ||

paramasunyaṃ ramite yogī parabrahma yatra gatālayaṃ ||

dhyāna samādhimayaṃ yogī satyaṃ satyaṃ ca pārvvatī ||

aneka yugagatās tatraḥ syaṃ bhūdhyanaṃ ca +ścalaṃ || śuṇo devi pārvatī || iśvaro kathaṃta mahājñānaṃ || (fol. 23r2–23v2)


«Colophon:»

iti śīmahādevapārvatīsaṃvāde vairāṭapurāṇe yogaśāstre ṣaṣṭamo paṭala || 6 ||

itī śrīkūmārīpāvī racitaṃ

paṭhate hartte pāpaṃ śutvā mokṣi dāyakaṃ

yogāraṃbhaṃ bhave siddhaṃ āvāgavananṛvarttate ||

aṭhasatīrtha bhave punyaṃ gaṃgāsnānaṃ dine dine ||

svāsā ko pramān ||

vaiṭhe aṃgula 12 bolate aṃgula 14 mathanu karate aṃgula 48 calate aṃgula 64 sovate aṃgula 100 ||

iti svāsā ko pramāṇa ||

mayā sudhaṃ asudhaṃ vā mama doṣo na dīyate iti vairāṭapurāṇe saṃpurṇaṃ śvabha mastu || ❁ ||

(fol. 23v223r3)
