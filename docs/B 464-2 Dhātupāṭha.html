Excerpts

Beginning

|| śrīmahāgaṇapataye namaḥ ||

bhū sattāyāṃ udāttaḥ parasmaibhāṣaḥ | edha vṛddhau || sparddha saṃgharṣe || gādhṛ pratiṣṭhālipsayor graṃthe ca || bādhṛ loḍane | nāthṛ nādhṛ yācñopatāpaiśvaryāśīḥṣu || dadha dhāraṇe skudi āpravaṇe || (fol. 1v1–3)


End

tiras aṃtardhau | agada nīrogatve | uras balārthaḥ | taraṇa varaṇa gatau⟨ḥ⟩ | payas prasṛtau | saṃbhūyas prabhūtabhāve | aṃbara saṃvara saṃbhara saṃbharaṇe | iti kaṃḍvādayaḥ || || (fol. 26r5–7)


Colophon

iti dhātupāṭha[ḥ] samāptaḥ || || śubhaṃ bhavatu || || śrīsītārāmacaṃdrārpaṇam astu || (fol. 26r7)
