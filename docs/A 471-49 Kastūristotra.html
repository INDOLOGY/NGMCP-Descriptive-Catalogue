Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||    ||

kastūrīkuṃkumāktāgurusitakiraṇānāndravair liptagātrīṃ
vidyotadraktapaṭṭāmvaram abhidadhatīṃ nāsikāmauktikāḍhyām ||
ratnair añcatkirīṭāṃ ruciramaṇilasatkuṇḍalodyatkapolaṃ |
vibhrājadhemamuktāmaṇiracitamahābhūṣaṇair bhūṣitāṃgīm || 1 ||

ratnaśreṇīvicitrāvanitalavilasatkalpavṛkṣasya mūle
ratnadvipasya madhye sphuradamalamaṇispaṣṭasaṃliṣṭapīṭhe
svāstīrṇe sadvitāne trisaraparimite koṇavarge manojñe
devādevābhivaṃdye ruciraśavahṛdambhoruhe rājamānām || 2 ||

visphūrjadratnkāṃtisphuradamalatulākoṭiyuktāṃghripadmāṃ
hṛtpadmāntarvibhāvyārcanavidhivihitābhyarcanair arcanīyām ||
muktāratnapravālaprakaraṇigaṇaprollasatkaṃcukīṃ tāṃ
naumyādyāṃ brahmamūrttiṃ hariharavidhibhiḥ pūjitāṃ sarvadātrīm || 3 || (fols. 1v1–2r3)

End

bhūpālaśreṇibhūpo bhavatu raṇabahādurasāhas tadīyā-
rirnāśaṃ yātu jīvyādayamathasaradāṃ tvatprasādāt sahasram ||
bhūdevān devidānais tava padakamalaṃ tatsaparyāvidhānair
mānair bandhūnvitānair akhilasuravarāṃs tarpayan sarvakālam || 28 ||

āyu(!)ddīrghaṃ dayārdraṃ hṛdayam anudinaṃ dīnavargo ṣu(!)bhargo
bhaktiḥ śaktiḥ sadaiva pratibhaṭadalane garva urvīśvareṣu
prītir dharme pratitir guruvacasi guṇiṣ vādaro [ʼ]bhīṣṭasaṃpat
syād devi tvatprasādān nṛpatiraṇabahādurasāhasya śaśvat || 29 || (fols. 7v6–8r5)

Colophon

iti śrīmanmahārājādhirājamahārāje(!)raṇabahādurasāhabahādurasamseraja..devājñaptaśrīvācaspatimiśraviracitaṃ kastūrīstotraṃ saṃpūrṇam || ❁ || ❁ || ❁ || ❁ || (fol. 8v5–7)
