Excerpts

Beginning

tam ||

bhagavas tvatprasādena tadakhaṇḍam ihās tu me |

yathākhaṇḍaṃ jagat sarvaṃ tvam eva puruṣottama ||

tathākhilānyakhaṇḍāni vratāni mama santu vai

evam evānumāsamvai cāturmmāsyavidhi smṛtaḥ ||

caturbhir eva māsais ca pāraṇaṃ prathamaṃ smṛtam ||

prīṇanañ ca hareḥ kuryyāt pārite pāraṇe tataḥ ||

caitrādiṣu ca māsesu caturṣvamyantu pāraṇam |

tatrāpi śakvapātrāṇi dadyāc chudro samanvitaḥ ||

śrāvaṇādiṣu māsesu kārttikānteṣu pāraṇam |

yaṃ snāntu ghṛtapātrāṇi dadyād viprāya bhaktitaḥ || (fol. 15r1–4)

End

bhūyo rūpas tadāpnoti pāraṇe pāraṇe gate ||

bhaktyā paṭhati yaś caitān devasya purato hareḥ |

so ʼrccayaty avanīpālajñānayajñena keśavam ||

sarvvā badhās tathā pāpam akhilaṃ manujeśvara |

viṣṇudharmmā vyapohanti saṃsmṛtāḥ paṭhitāḥ śrutāḥ ||

etat te sarvvam ākhyātaṃ rahasyaṃ paramaṃ hareḥ |

nātaḥ parataraṃ kiñcic chrāvyaṃ śrutisukhāvaham ||

atroktavidhibhaktasya puruṣasya vipaścitaḥ |

na durllabhaṃ naravyāghra paramaṃ brahmaṇā śrutam || || (fols. 146v5–8)

Colophon

iti viṣṇudharmmeṣu śāstre māhātmye paramāmṛtaṃ dharmottamaṃ parisamāptam iti || || viṣṇudharmmeṣu mīvṛttāntāḥ || kriyāyoge pravṛttiḥ || .. .. .. .. .. ṣa samvādaḥ || śukrapralhādasamvādaḥ || sugati dvādaśīḥ || yāmyakleśavimuktiḥ || ekabhaktavidhiḥ varṣamāsavratāḥ .. .. .. dvādaśī || vijaya dvādaśī || jayantyaṣṭamī || ati jayaikādaśī || uttarāyaṇa snāpanam || viṣṇuvratam || samprāpti dvādaśī || (fols. 146v8–11)
