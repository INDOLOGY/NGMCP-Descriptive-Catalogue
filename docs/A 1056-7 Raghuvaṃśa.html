Excerpts

Beginning of the root text

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

vāgarthāv iva saṃpṛktau vāgarthapratipattye
jagataḥ pitarau vaṃde pārvatīparameśvarau 1 (fol. 2r6)

Beginning of the commentary

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ |
śrīvakratuṃḍāya namaḥ nirvighnam astu |

mātāpitṛbhyāṃ jagato namo vāmārdhajānaye |
sadyo dakṣiṇadṛkpātasaṃkucad vāmadṛṣṭaye 1

aṃtarāya timiropaśāṃtaye śāṃ[tapā]vanam aciṃtyavaibhavat(!) |
taṃ naraṃ vapuṣi kuṃjaraṃ mukhe manmahe kim api tuṃdilaṃ mahaḥ 2 (fol. 1v1–2)

End of the root text

taṃ bhāvāya prava [sa]mayā kāṃkṣiṇīnāṃ prajānām
aṃtargūḍhaṃ kṣitir iva babhau bījamuṣṭiṃ dadhānā
maulaiḥ sārddhaṃ scha(!)virasacivair hemasiṃhāsanaschā(!)
rājīrājyaṃ vidhivad aśiṣad bharttur avyāhatājñā 57 (fol. 9v4–6, exp. 212)

End of the commentary

aklīvau muṣṭimustakā iti yādavaḥ
aṃtargūḍhaṃ aṃtargataṃ garbhaṃ dadhānā hemasiṃhāsanaschā(!) avyāhatārājñī maulai bhavaiḥ mūlād āgatair vāptair ity arthaḥ sthavirasacivair vṛddhāmātyaiḥ sārdhaṃ bharttuḥ rājyaṃ vidhivad vidhyarthaṃ yathāśāstram ity arthaḥ arhārthe vatipratyayaḥ aśiṣat śāstisya | śartiśāstyartibhyaś ceti cler aṅ 57 (fol. 9v3, 7–8, exp. 212)

Colophon of the root text

iti śrīraghuvaṃśe mahākāvye kaviśrīkālidāsakṛtau agnivarṇavarṇano nāmaikonaviṃśatiḥ sargaḥ ||    || (fol. 9v5–6, exp. 212)

Colophon of the commentary

iti śrīpadavākyapramāṇapārāvārapārīṇa-śrīmahopādhyāyakolacalamallināthasūriviracitāyāṃ samāptaraghuvaṃśaṭīkāyāṃ nāmaikonaviṃśatiḥ sargaḥ ||    || (fol. 9v8–9, exp. 212)
