Excerpts

Beginning

|| umāvratatithī(!)māhātmyam ||
agastya uvāca ||
deva tava prasādād vai śutam umāvrataṃ mayā ||
tasmād dānopavāsaś ca strīṇāṃ bhaved vratottamam ||
bhūyaḥ śrouṃ ca vāṃchāmitvatto brahmavidām varāt ||
vratānāṃ caiva dānānāṃ māhātmyaṃ brūhi medhunā ||
skaṃda uvāca
tithinakṣatrarāśīnāṃ dinānāṃ ca tathaiva ca ||
sarveṣām ātmarūpā sā jñāyatāṃ hi tvayā mune ||
tathāpi caritaṃ dharmmaṃ tayā lokahitāya ca ||
dharmmātmarūpayā devyā saṃsārasthitaye punaḥ ||
yeṣu yeṣu ca kāleṣu dhyānadānopavāsakān ||
cakāra girijāśreṣṭhas tattatkālobhavan mune ||
snānadānopavāseṣu tasyāḥ prāptaś ca yais tadā ||
tithyahorāśinakṣatrā(!) suśreṣṭhās(!) te(!) bhavanti vai ||
tataḥ prabhṛti cādyāpi gaurīvratād dvijottama || (fol. 1v5–15)

End

śraddhayā japyate mantraṃ śraddhayā dhyāyate hariḥ ||
śraddhayā labhyate dharmmaḥ śraddhayā labhyate phalaṃ ||
krodhenā[[ ʼ]]śraddhayā yac ca kriyate dharmmamānuṣaiḥ ||
vilīyate ca tat sarvvaṃ bhasmanīva yathā hutaṃ ||
nāsti dharmasamo(!) mitro(!) nāsti dharmmasamā gatiḥ ||
nāsti rūdrasamo devo nāsti mokṣasama(!) sukhaḥ(!) ||
strīṇāṃ paraṃ dharmmaphalāḍhyam etat jānīhi vipreśa sadācareyaṃ ||
turīyavarggottamasaṃpradāyaṃ punaś ca kiṃ śrotumanā asi tvam || (fol. 5r18–5v4)

Colophon

iti śrīskandapurāṇe himavatkhaṇḍe umāvratakathanaṃ nāma
unacatvāriśodhyāyaḥ(!) ślokāḥ 108 (fol. 5v4–5)
