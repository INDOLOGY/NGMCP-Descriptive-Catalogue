Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

śriyaḥ patim athāmantrya, gate devarṣisattame |

harṣotphullānanā satyā, vāsudevam athāvravīt || 1 ||

dhanyā 'smi kṛtakṛtyāsmi, saphalaṃ jī(2)vitaṃ ca me |

majjanmani nidānau ca, dhanyau tau pitarau mama ||

yau māṃ trailokyasubhagāṃ, janayāmāsatur dhruvaṃ |

ṣoḍaśastrīsahasrāṇāṃ, vallabhāhaṃ yatas tava ||

yasmā(3)t mayādipuruṣaḥ kalpavṛkṣasamanvitaḥ |

yathoktavidhinā samyak nāradāya samarpitaḥ || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

yośvattham arccayed deva,m udakena saman tataḥ ||

kulānām ayutaṃ tena, tāritaṃsyān na saṃśayaḥ ||

alakṣmīḥ kālakarṇṇī ca (5) duḥsvapnaṃ durvvicintitaṃ |

aśvatthatarpaṇāt tatra, sarvvaṃ dukhaṃ (!) vilīyate ||

tarpitāḥ pitaras tena, viṣṇus tena samarccitaḥ ||

yośvattham arccayed devaṃ gra(6)hās tenaiva pūjitāḥ || || (fol. 39v4–6)

Colophon

iti śrīpadmapurāṇe kārttikamāhātmye ekonatriṃśaddhyāyaḥ (!) samāptaḥ || || śubhaṃ bhavatu || ||

(7) nepālahāyene jāte svaraśailādrisaṃyute |

kārttike kṛṣṇapakṣe ca paṃcamyāṃ kujavāsare, ||

ājñayā devidāsasya, bhaktapaṭṭanavāsi(8)naḥ (!) |

likhitaṃ parśurāmena (!) kārttikākhyānam uttamaṃ || || samvat 777 || || śrīhariharaprīṇātu (!) || || (fol. 39v6–8)
