Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

atha ha[[ya]]tagraṃtho likhyate ||

(tatrādau saṃjñādhyāyaḥ ||)<ref name="ftn1">darken</ref>

madhyeʼdhyāyadvayaṃ aṃte prakīrṇakaḥ ||

tatra saṃjñādhyāye khaṃḍadvayaṃ(+27 ) nirūpaṇam asti || tatra saṃjñādhyāye prathamarekhāgaṇitavātī(!) nirūpyaṃte || upadeśasya isāratasaṃjñā || dṛśyasya hirasīsaṃhjñā || dṛśyopadeśoviṃduḥ | (fol. 1v1–9)

End

tadā madhyānhānāt pūrvaṃ horātulye madhyāhne devapūrvayā śaṃkuchāyā makāyāḥ simatakhata eva digrekhā bhavati | yadi chāyā pūrve tadā kivalā paścime sti | chāyā paścime tadā kivalā pūrvesti || (fol. 60v9–18)

Colophon

iti hayātagraṃtho samāptaḥ || || || ❖ || (fol. 6v18–19)
