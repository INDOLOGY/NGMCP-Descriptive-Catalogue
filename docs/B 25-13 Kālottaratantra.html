Excerpts

Beginning

///vet | cintayā rahitaṃ yac ca tat paraṃ parikīrttitaṃ |

evaṃ jñātvā tu maṃtrajñaḥ sarvvapāpakṣayo bhavet |

śāntaṃ sarvvagataṃ śūnyaṃ maṃtrair dvādaśabhiṃ(!) sthitaṃ |

akṣarāṃtaritaṃ kētvā ṣaḍaṃgaṃ sa ⟪mi⟫ [[śi]]vo bhavet |

prathamaṃ hṛdayāṃtasya dvitīyaṃ tu śiraḥ smṛtaṃ ||

tṛtīyaṃ vai śikhā caiva caturthaṃ kavacaṃ bhavet |

paṃcamaṃ tu śivaṃ vidyāt ṣaṣṭham astravisargajam |

ṣaḍaṃgam etat kathitaṃ śivena paramātmanā |

kathitaṃ guhyasaṃjñā(vaṃsādākhyena) mahātmanā |

mayā tu kathitaṃ tubhyaṃ sadyomuktipradāyakam |

dīrghayuktaṃ śivasyāṅgaṃ hṛdayādyaṃ yathāsthitam |

sadyādikramayogena hrasve hrasvaṃ prakīrttitam

sānusvāraṃ bhaven netraṃ sarveṣāṃ coparisthitam |

savisargaṃ bhaved astraṃ sānusāravivarjjitam |

(fol.2r1-5 )

suṣumnā devatāyānaṃ | piṃgalā asuraṃ puram |

iḍāyāṃ mānuṣaṃ lokaṃ (jaṃghānī kṛmisaṃbhavā) |

hastinī pretalokaṃ vai (hyavā ca erakaṃ) dhruvaṃ |

ity eteṣāṃ gatir (lāvaṃ) mokṣa bhaved (vahya tha tha tha tha tha tha tha tha

na bhūyo janmasaṃbhavaḥ || (fol.38v4-39r2)
