Excerpts

Beginning

|| || śrīgurumūrttir jayati || ||

atha vacmi mahāmaṃtraṃ haridrāgaṇapaśya(!) tu ||
jagatrayahitaṃ bhogamokṣadaṃ kavitākaraṃ || 1 ||

nānā mantragaṇasyāśusiddhidaṃ bhuvi durllabhaṃ
gariṣṭhaṃ saphalaṃ cātha kāryamātraprasādhanam || 2 ||

gopanīyaṃ prayatnena vāñchitārthasuradrumaṃ ||
saubhāgyapuṣṭilakṣmīdaṃ dīrghajīvipradaṃ nṛṇāṃ || 3 ||

naranārīnareṃdrāṇāṃ paraṃ vaśyaphalapradaṃ ||
bighnaughadhvaṃsane dakṣaṃ kṛtyādrohanivāraṇaṃ || 4 || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

śivabhaktivihināya (!) gurubhaktivivarjjite ||
nindakāya kumārgasthe dīrghasūtradharāya ca || 30 ||

na deyaṃ śivasadbhāvamanyāyapathavarttine ||
dātāro narakaṃ yāti punarāvṛttivarjjitaṃ || 31 ||

tasmāt sarvaprayatnena gopanīyaṃ svaguhyavat ||
śivād gurutarī yasya gurubhakt suniścalā || 32 ||

tasya deyam idaṃ śāstraṃ nānyasya tu kadācana ||
tasmāt sarvaprayatnena gopane saṃsukhī bhavet || 33 || || ❁ || (fol. 757r1–5)

Colophon

iti śrīśambhunityātaṃtreṣu śrīśrīnāthamate ādikhaṇḍe śrīvidyāgurunirūpaṇaṃ nāma śatottara ekaviṃśatimaṃ paṭalaṃ || ❁ || || || ❁ || || 〇 || śubham astu || samāptoyaṃ khaṇḍaḥ || śrīgurudevatārpaṇam astu || || ❁ || || ❁ || || ❁ || || (fol. 757r5-8)
