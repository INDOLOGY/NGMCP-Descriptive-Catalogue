(❖ oṃ namo bhagavatya(!) āryyamahāpratyaṅgirāyaiḥ(!) ||

evaṃ mayā śrutam ekasmin samayad(!) bhagavān || devya(!) khuṃtāyanti(!) śeṣa viharanti(!) sma || sa dharmāyā devasa⟨ṃ⟩bhāyāṃ mahatā⟨bhiḥ⟩ bhikṣusaṃghena sārddhamahatā ca bodhisattvasaṃghena | sakrena(!) devānām iṃdena(!) sārddha[ṃ] || tatra khalu bhagavān prajñapta eṣāsane niṣadya || || (fol. 1v1–4)

«End: »

oṃ sarvvatathāgatoṣṇīṣa sitāntapatre hūṃ phaṭ svāhā || tadyathā || oṃ anare 2 acale 2 khakhame 2 vile 2 sāmye 2 sarvvaburddhānāṃdhiṣṭhinād dhiṣṭhita sarvvatathāgatoṣṇīṣa sitātapatre sarvvaduṣṭān praduṣṭān hūṃ phaṭ svāhā || buddhayogena sarvopradraveṣu sunti japtā kattavyāṃ (!) || || idam avocado bhagavān āttamanā sarvvabuddhabodhisattvāś ca sadevamānukhāsurarokogaruḍakirnnara mahoraga bhagavato bhāṣitam abhyanandasnīnti (!) || || (fol. 21v1–22r1)
