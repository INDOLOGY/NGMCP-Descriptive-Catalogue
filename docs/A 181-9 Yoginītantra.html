Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namas tasyai ||    ||

pradhānam ādhāravikalpasattā
svabhāvabhāvā bhuvanatrayasya ||
sā vidyayāvyaktam apīha māyā
jyotiṣ paraṃ pātu jagaṃti nityam || 1 ||

śrīdevy uvāca ||

oḍḍiyānādikaṃ pīṭhaṃ śruto haṃ tārapīṭḥakam ||
idānīṃ śrotum icchāmi kāmarūpasya nirṇayam ||

yad uktaṃ tat tvayā nātha ghorapāpavināśanam ||
kāmākhyāsaṃjñakaṃ pīṭhaṃ prakāśaṃ tu kalau gatam ||

kalisarpapradaṣṭānāṃ vicitrānāṃ cikitsakam ||
yat tejaḥ paramaṃ divyaṃ kiṃrūpaṃ kathyatāṃ vibho || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

tantraḥ puraḥsaraṃ kṛtvā todārādikam uccaret ||
pādyārghācamanīyaṃ ca madhuparkaṃ nivedayet ||

svamudrayā svataṃtreṇa aṃgapūjāṃ samācaret ||
ṣaḍaṃgena tato bhūti raktapuṣpeṇa pūjayet ||

pīṭhaṃ ca upapīṭhaṃ ca siddhakṣetraṃ ca yā nadī ||
ṣaṭkoṇeṣv arcayet paścād yathā dṛṣṭaṃ tathā phalaṃ || (fol. 120r1–3)

Colophon

iti śrīyoginītaṃtre sarvataṃtrottame dvāviṃśatisāhastre prathamatame dvitīyabhāge kāmarūpādhikāre paṃcadaśamaḥ paṭalaḥ || 15 ||    ||
śūbham astu sarvajagatām ||
saṃvat 1898 āṣāḍhaśuklapūrṇimā asmiṃ dine likhitam idaṃ pustakaṃ ||
vijayānandena sampū[r]ṇam || śūbham ||    || (fol. 120r4–6)

iti śrīyoginītaṃtre sarvataṃtrottame dvāviṃśatisāhastre kāmarūpādhikāre prathamatame dvitīyabhāge dvitīyaḥ paṭalaḥ || 2 || (fol. 7v5–6)
