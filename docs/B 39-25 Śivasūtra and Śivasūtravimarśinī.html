Manuscript Features

A scattered folio in a different hand form an unspecified text appears at the very beginning of the MS reading as follows:

tāro naiti rathābharāḥ |

vidaṃbaraṃ brahmavidyā chandar vyādikam eva vā |

sarvatra samam uktaṃ syān maṃtramātra[ṃ] hi bhidyate |

oṁ śivāya namaś c[e]ti saumyaḥ paṃcākṣaraḥ smṛtaḥ |

saumo ʼtra ṛṣir ākhyātaḥ pūrvavat sarvam īryate | 3 |

haṃso ʼtra ṛṣir ākhyāto brahmānaṃdaikasiddhidā | 4 |

Excerpts

«Beginning of the root text»

caitanyam ātmā || 1 ||

jñānaṃ baṃdhaḥ || 2 || (fol. 1r10 and1v9-10)


«Beginning of the commentary

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

oṁ rudrakṣetrajñavargaḥ samudayati yato yatra viśrāṃti mṛdye?

yatratvaṃ yasya viśvaṃ sphuritamayamiyad yan mayaṃ viśvam etat ||

svāc chaṃdyānaṃdavṛṃdo(!) chaladamṛtamayānutrar aspandatatvaṃ

caitanyaṃ śāṃkaraṃ taj jayati yad akhilaṃ dvaitabhāsādvayātmā ||

āsamaṃjasyam ālocyavṛttīnām iha tatvataḥ |

śivasūtraṃ vyākaromi gurvāmnāyavigānataḥ(!) (fol. 1r1-3)


«End of the root text»

nāsikāntar madhyasaṃyamāḥ || bhūyaḥ syāt pratimīlanam || (fol. 12r9 and 12r14)


«End of the commentary»

śivarahasyanidarśanasaṃstavan

navanavāmṛtasārarasolvaṇaṃ |

sukṛtino ramayaṃtu bhavac chide

sphuṭam imām śivasūtravimaśinīm |

iyam arocakināṃ rucivardhinīṃ

pariṇatiṃ tanute paramāṃ mateḥ ||

rasanamātra sa eva sudhaughavan

mṛtijarājananādi na pāpahṛt |

dehaprāṇasukhādibhiḥ parimitāṃ haṃtādibhiḥ saṃvṛtāś-

caitanyaṃ cinute nijaṃ na sumahan māheśvaraṃ khaṃjanaḥ ||

madhye bodhasudhābdhiviśvaṃ abhitaḥ tat pheṇa(!)piṃḍopamaṃ

yaḥ paśyed upadeśatas tu kathitaḥ sākṣāt śivaḥ saṣṭakaḥ |

naratatarasā(!)saṃsārābdhiṃ vidhan napare pade |

padam avicalaṃ nityālokaṃ pramodasunirbhare

vibhavāta? śivaproktaṃ sūtraṃ rahasyasamujvalaṃ |

prasabhavilasat laghuktyām na samut plavadāyitavat || = || | (fol. 12r21-12v5)


«Colophon root text»

iti śrīśivasūtravivṛttāv āṇavopāyaprakāśanaṃnāma tṛtīya unmeṣaḥ || (fol. 11v20)


«Colophon of the commentary»

iti śrīmanmahāmaheśvarābhinavaguptācāryapādapātropajīvinaḥ śrīkhemarājyasyaeti(!) śivasūtravimarśinī saṃpūrṇā || = || | likhitaṃ rāghavendraśiṣyaśrīrāmeṃndreṇa || śrīr astu || = || (fol. 12v5-6)
