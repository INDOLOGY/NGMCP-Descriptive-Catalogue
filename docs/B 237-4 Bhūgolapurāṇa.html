Beginning

oṃ svasti gaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

oṃ thvi(!) bhūgolapurāṇa liṣyate ||

avyaktāya namaḥ ||

taṃ yadīyasāsakai(!) sabrahmāṃḍa ||

yadi vā viṣṇu ||

śivapāṃtayo yathā ||

ākāsate vāyu utpannaṃ || vāute teja utpannaṃ || vāyute teja utpannaṃ || tejate brahmāṇḍa utpaṃ(!) || pānimaddhe(!) aṃḍa upaje || te brahmāṃḍa phuṭikuṭakā bhaye || te jalamaddhe viṣṇu rahe hai || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

cārilakṣa vattisa sahasrakalīyugapramāna ||

sūryaparvasahasra eka || caṃdraparvasahasra dui ||

mānuṣya pramāna āpane sāḍhe tīni hātha ||

āyurbalavisāsau varṣako || tei jo satya vacana volai || astrī prasūti ... ||| (fol. 7v6–8)

Colophon

iti śrībho(!)galapurāṇasamaptam śubham astu || (fol. 6r4–5)
