«Beginning: »


❖ śrīgurumūrttir jayati ||


atha mahāṣoḍhānyāsaḥ ||


ays śrīparāprāsādamantrasya paraśambhuṛṣiḥ avyaktagāyatrīchandaḥ sarvamantreśvarī


śrīparāmbādevatā hsāṃ bījaṃ hsīṃ śaktiḥ hsūṃ kīlakaṃ mama śrīgurudevatāprāsādasiddhyarthe


jape viniyogaḥ || pañcabrahmamantrair agulīnyāsaṃ vidhāya hsāmityādityādinā ṣaḍaṅgaṃ kuryāt || ||


tadyathā || (exp. 2t1–3)


«End: »


aṃgulyādau tadagre ca nyaset pāṇitalepi ca ||


vāmabāhudaśasthāne nyased evaṃ krameṇa hi |


āsane dakṣapādasya pādamūle tato pyadhaḥ ||


jānudeśe tatodhastāt tadadhaḥ prapadepi ca ||


aṃgulyādau tad agre ca nyaset pādatale pi ca ||


vāmapādadaśasthāne nyased evaṃ krameṇa hi |


evaṃ vinyastagātre yaḥ sa sākṣād devatā samaḥ ||


mantrasiddhir bhavet tasya triṃśad vāreṇa maṇdalāt ||


iti guhyam idaṃ devi tāṃśaye kulanāyake ||


ityuktarītyā japtvā japaṃ śrīgurave nivedya ||


svadehaṃ mahāṣoḍhoktam akhilaṃ smare ||


śivoham iti niściṃtya ṣoḍaśārṇāṃ japet || (exp. 9b8–10t2)


«Colophon:»


iti śrīmaheśānandanāthaśrīcaraṇaparicaryāparāyaṇa śrīśaṃkarānaṃdanāthena viracitāyāṃ


śivārccanadīpikoddhṛtāyāṃ śrīguruśāṃkaryāṃ nyāsakāṇḍe mahāṣoḍhānyāsanirūpaṇaṃ nāma


caturthopadeśaḥ samāptaṃ || || (!) (exp. 10t2–4)
