Excerpts

Beginning

|| || śrīgaṇeśāya na[ma]ḥ(!) || ||

vighneśvaraṃ namaskṛtya devīṃ caiva sarasvatīṃ ||

guruṃ ca devatānāṃ ca prasādād uddhṛto mayā || 1 ||

nānā śāstrasamuddhṛtya ki(!)yate bālabodhakaṃ

muṃjādityena vipreṇa śiṣyārthe sārasaṃgrahaḥ || 2 ||

vidur(!)ṣā brāhmaṇānāṃ ca gaṇakānāṃ tathaiva ca ||

eteṣāṃ karalagno [']sminbālabodhaṃ ca vistaret(!) || 3 ||

udayācalaparyyaṃtam astācala(!) mahīm imāṃ ||

vikhyāto bālabodho [']yaṃ muṃtā(!)dityena kārayet(!) || 4 || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

yātrākāle nirīkṣeta śakunaṃ ca śubhāśubhaṃ

ekavastunimittāni vitte(!) śuddhe(!) tathaiva ca 1

atha dikśūlaṃ vyākhyāyate

maṃdeṃdunā tyajet pūrvā(!) guruvāreṇa dakṣiṇaṃ(!) |

paścimāṃ bhṛgubhānubhyāṃ uttarāṃ-(fol. 8v6–8)

«Sub-colophon:»

iti viṣṭicakraṃ || (fol. 8v6)
