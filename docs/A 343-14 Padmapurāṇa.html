Beginning

❖ namoḥ vāgbhavāyet ||

tan namomi maheśāna ganēśa ṣatmukhādaya |
svaddhuṃnīñca mṛdānīñca (2) pārambhavaḥ vadāmīha ||    ||

mahādevatvaṃ gaṇeśaṃ komālatvaṃ pārvvatītvaṃ gaṃgātvaṃ thva ṅāhmaṃ (3) namoskāra yāṅava thva bākhaṃna hlāya || 1 ||

vyāsa uvāca ||
śṛṇu rājan mahāprājño dhalmaputra sa(4)nātanaṃ |
kūtuhala kathāmete śuthāhivrata pradhvati ||    ||

vyāsa ṛṣisyaṃ hlāra
thva khaṃ ṅeṅo rājā (5) yudhisthira ka’ustaka khaṃ hlāya śuṭhāyīprameśvara dhakaṃ dhayā khaṃ hlāya ṅeṅo (fol. 1r1–5)

End

yaḥ śṛṇo(2)tikathāmēṣā sarvva saṃpatti saṃbhavāḥ |
tat kathān kathayāmāsa, aśvameḍhādhikaṃ la(3)bhet || o ||

thva bākhaṃ ṅeṅahmaṃyā anekaḥ saṃmpati rāyu thva khaṃ hlākahmayā āśvamedha ja(4)jñayā phala rāyu dhakaṃ vyāśa ṛṣisyanaṃ yudhisthila rājāsa hṅavane hlālaḥ || (5) || 160 || (fol. 35v1–5)

Colophon

iti śrīpadmapurāṇe urttarorttarakhaṇḍe śova kumāra samvādya paṃñca ||(6)dhikaśatodhyāyaḥ | śrīmahādevaṃ prītirastuḥ ||    || ⟪ma⟫ śubhamastu sarvadā ślaiyostu || (fol. 35v5–6)
