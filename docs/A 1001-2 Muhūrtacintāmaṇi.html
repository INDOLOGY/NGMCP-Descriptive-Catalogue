Manuscript Features

Text is available from the chapter of Vivāhaprakaraṇa and up to the end gṛhapraveśaprakaraṇa. Published version of Muhūrtaciṃtāmaṇī contains the 14th chapter titled vaṃśavarṇanaprakaraṇa.
