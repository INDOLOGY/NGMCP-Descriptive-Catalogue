Excerpts

Beginning

.ne jistunaṃ jivathe sānaī ||

sātosa vedana kokhayā helana (2) ghāla sosetu kheṅāyī,

cetasa yetole peṭa makhāṭole pyāna hi (3) toṅāthe ṭonayī ||

vāpe niyāva tātha soyāva nāyana cupena sūyī (4)

dona phusalapai do sose behelapai thototheṃ durjanana yāyī ||

suja(5)na kujana nehmā parama virodha lithe nipula picālayī

lithe li(6)malātaṅāva bhālapāva chāya, sarava saṃghosa susyaṃ vanayī || (exp. 2:1–6)


«Middle»

dhanāśrī || pra || (6)

prathama prema jata hari borala, āna anādara bhela

bolala (7) janama bhari je jāyata, dine dine dura gela ||

sājani mādha(3b1)va nahi gamāra adhika prema parābhava pāiya,

karamadosa (2) hamāra || dhru ||

dūra so dekhiya pāvaya nonuva, basare cihni(3)ya gāma,

ati agati mati bisarali, suni suni tasu nāma (4) ||

supahu vacana kuñjara daśana, mohia chalayi bhāna,

ā(5)pana vacana boli bisarata, tathi ki karata āna ||

bhane vi(6)dyāpati sunaha jovati nahi manoratha ora,

hṛdaya vedana (7) jāke nivediya, taisena medani thora || || (exp. 3t5–3b7)


End

dhanāśrī || pra ||

durajana jatana kapati(5)yā mana seye parama kaṭhīna,

dukhasa hṅeliva sukhasa khoyiva (6) sahaja mana malīna ||

hari harisara ||

vacana racana parajīva (7) haraṇa avasara sosetu conaī

kosana tutole iriṣyā ma (exp. 5b4–7)
