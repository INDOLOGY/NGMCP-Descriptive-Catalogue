Beginning of the root text

oṃ namo bhagavate vāsudevāya ||   ||

jitaṃ te puṇḍarīkākṣa namaste viśvabhāvana |
namaste stu hṛṣīkeśa mahāpuruṣa pūrvaja || 1 || (fol. 2r6)

Beginning of the commentary

śrīgurugaṇeśāya namaḥ ||    ||

ya āste hṛdayagraṃthigūḍhaciṃtāmaṇidyutiḥ ||
cetayan nakhilaṃ viśvaṃ tasmai śrīviṣṇave namaḥ ||

hiraṇyagarbhatanayo mādhavasyātmajātmajaḥ |
śrīratnagarbhas tanute viṣna(!)vākūṭacandrikāṃ | (fol. 1v1–2

End of the root text

iti vividhabhajasya yasya rūpam prakṛtiparātmamayasya sanātanasya ||
pradiśatu bhagavān śeṣapuṃsāṃ harira(!)pajanmajarādikāsa(!)siddhiḥ || 30 || (fol. 449r5–6)

End of the root text

Sub-colophon

iti śrīratnagarbhācāryaviracitāyāṃ śrīmad vaiṣṇavākūṭacaṃdrikāyāṃ ṣaṣṭheśe(!) ʼṣṭamo dhyāyaḥ ||   || samāpto yaṃ viṣṇupurāṇaṭīkā ||   || (fol. 449r10–11)
