Excerpts

Begining

(oṃ namaḥ śrīmate bhagavate śākyamunaye tasmai ‥ ‥ namo namaḥ ||)

vaṃde śrīśākyasiṃhaṃ suragaṇasahitaṃ devadevādhipeśaṃ,
saṃsārābdheḥ plavatvaṃ sakalaguṇanidhiṃ gautamaṃ buddhanāthaṃ ||
saṃsāre siṃhanādaṃ sakalabhayaharaṃ, sthāpitaṃ dharmachatraṃ
trailokye dharmavarṣaṃ guṇaśatanilayaṃ tarpitaṃ devalokaṃ || 1 ||

anuviharati bhagavān saṃmyaksaṃbuddho jetavane vihāre anāthapiṇḍadasyārāme mahatā bhikṣusaṃghena sārddhaṃ trayodaśabhir bhikṣuśatai[r] mahadbhi[ḥ] kṣīṇāśravais tapoyuktaiḥ samādhivaśitāprāptaiḥ jiteṃdriyaiḥ taiś ca saṃbahulair bodhisatvaiḥ evaṃ ca maha[d]bhi[r] mahāśrāvakagaṇaiḥ anekaṛddhivaśitāprāptaiḥ || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

atha khalv āyuṣmān rāṣṭrapālo bhagavantaṃ etad avocat || kin nāmāyaṃ bhagavan dharmaparyāyaḥ kathaṃ caina[ṃ] dhārayāmi || evaṃm ukte bhagavān āyuṣmā(!)ntaṃ rāṣṭrapālam etad avocat || amoghapratijñāviśuddham iti nāma dhāraya || satpuruṣavikri(!)ḍitaṃ bodhisatvacaryāviniścayaṃ nāma dhāraya || artha(pāripurīya) nāma dhāraya || idam avocat bhagavā[n ā]ttamanā āyuṣmān rāṣṭrapālaḥ sadevamānuṣāsuragandharvaś ca loko bhagavato bhāṣitam a[bhya][[na]]ndann iti | (fol. 82v15–83r2)

Colophon

puṇyara(!)kṣmīḥ satpuruṣasya pūrvayāgasu(!)traratnarājaṃ samāptaṃ ||     || iti śrīvicitrakarṇikāvadāne paripṛcchā nāma mahāyānasu(!)traṃ samāptaṃ || iti samvat 1958 sāla miti kārtika sudi 9 roja śubhm (fol. 83r2–4)
