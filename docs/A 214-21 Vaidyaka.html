Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ sarvajñāya ||

dhyātvā śivaṃ paramatatvavicāravaidyaṃ

caṇḍimabhiṣṭaphaladā sagaṇaṃ gaṇesa(!) |

dhanvantariṃ munivaraṃ munisūśrūtādi

nātreyasūgratapalaṃ(!) manasā pranamya(!) ||

nānāmataprathita dṛṣṭaphalaprayogaiḥ

prastāvavākyasahitair iha siddhiyogaḥ |

vṛndenamandamatinātmahitānvitāyaḥ

saṃlikhyate gaḍa(!) viniścayaja krameṇa || 2 || (fol.1r1-3)

End

hnasakacchūyāḥ ateriḥ hallajiraḥ sevarā svātharahāḥ apāmārgaharaḥ

kūṭaḥ mahāraṅaḥ thvate samabhāg thutakaṃna yāvḥ cekanaṣuneḥ phoyaḥ thane, hnasakacchunāsaḥ dūvane vavaṃnāsa || ||

lvāhāmālanaḥ yāyaḥ dharīpāṅna vālaḥ (fol.72v6-7)
