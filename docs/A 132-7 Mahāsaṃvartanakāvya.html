Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namaḥ sarvajñāya || ||

śriyaṃ parārddhyaṃ vidadhad vidhātṛjit tamo nirasyan nabhibhūtabhānubhṛt ||

nudannidāghaṃ jitacārucandramā(!) sa vandyate ʼrhan niha yasya nopamā || 1 ||

hitāya tenaiva nṛṇāṃ pravarttitā vivarttasaṃvarttakathānuvarṇyate ||

prakāśanīyebhipihimaunam āśrayan nasādhuvīthīm †paramācigāyate† || 2 ||

iyaṃ pradīpasya śikheva dehināṃ puraskṛtā jñānatamo nirasyati ||

prakāśane syā ata eva dhīmatāṃ parāṅmukhatvaṃ na niṣeviḍaṃ kṣamaṃ || 3 ||

nidānabhāg bhajati kathā mahāśriyaṃ (yathābjinī)rūciramṛṇālasaṃsthitā ||

tadāditaḥ (pradyaṭita)karṇapurevan manoharaṃ śrutiviṣayeti veśyatāṃ || 4 ||

upodghāto nāma prathamo viśrāmaḥ || 1 || (fol.1v1-6 )

End

vidhātum arthaṃ ruciraṃ ciraṃ nṛṇāṃm(!) avāpa bodhiṃ dayayā yayā vibhuḥ ||

adhiṣṭhitaṃ bhṛtayā tayā vapur ddadhasya dhātuprakarākarālatāṃ || 12 ||

munau praśānte jananetraśāsanaṃ gata(!)stam āditya ivāvabhāsanaṃ

vidhātum ujjagmur ūrūdīrṇagarjanā akālameghā iva bhūri durjjanāḥ || 13 ||

sametya teṣāṃ pratipakṣatā ʼkṣatā munīndrasaṃgho ʼśubharaṇoraṇaḥ(!) ||

yathoktasaṃgītimayī diśaṃ diśā vyadhāj jinājñām atibhāsurāsvarāṃ || 14 ||

saṅgītikārādhigatārthanirṇayaḥ || prajñādayābhyām avadhūtadurṇayaiḥ ||

saṃkīrttyamānā sujanair anekadhā lokārthakām enakṛtā mayā kathā || 15 ||

sūktaṃ yad evaṃ bhavitātra devaṃ bauddhaṃ syāc ced dvir uktam api kiñcana tan madīyaṃ || ālokam eva viddhāti sad eva bhāna(ś cakṣtaḥ) prabhāvaharaṇan na tamas tadīyaṃ || 16 ||

abhyāsayogavidhinā vacanetrabauddhe puṇyaṃ viśuddhisubhagaṃ yad upārjitaṃ me | tenāvagamya nikhilān svayam eva bhāna(!) kṛtvā nṛṇāṃ karagatān iva darśayeyaṃ || 17 || (fol.37r6-38r2 )

Colophon

ṣāṣṭhakāṇḍe guruparvakramo nāma caramo viśrāmaḥ samāpteti(!) || 4 ||

ṣaṣṭhañ ca vicitrārthakāṇḍaṃ || || samāptā ca mahāsamvarttanī kathā ||

kṛtir ācātyyaśrībhantasarvarakṣitasya mahākaveḥ || ||

vṛttāni daśaunāni (!) catvāri śatāni anuṣṭupchandasā gaṃṇyamānāni(!) pañcaśatāni

catustrīṃśad(!) adhikāni pramāṇavanti bhavantīti || ❖ || || || || || ||                                                                                                        (fol.38r2-5 )
