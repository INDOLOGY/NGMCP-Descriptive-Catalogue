«Text:»

❖ oṃ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

umāṃgaṃgajakarṇavaktraṃ gaṇeśaṃ

bhujākaṃkanaṃ (!) śobhitaṃ dhūmaketuṃ ||

gale hārayuktā phalāśobhayataṃ (!)

namo jñānarūpaṃ gaṇeśaṃ namaste || 1 ||

gaṇeśaiyakadaṃtaṃ (!) subhaṃ sarvvakāryyaṃ

smaraṃ ṣāṇmukhaṃ jñanadaṃ (!) sarvvasiddhi ||

manaścititaṃ (!) kāryasiddhiṃ bhavantaṃ

namo buddhikātaṃ (!) gaṇeśaṃ namaste || 2 ||

mahāsundaraṃ vikṛtaṃ vighnarājaṃ

caturbhirttajair ekadantaika varṇaṃ ||

idaṃ daivarūpaṃ gaṇāsiddhināthaṃ

namo bhālanetraṃ ganeśaṃ namaste || 3 ||

śiraḥ sindūraṃ kuṃkumaṃ dehavarṇa

śubhaṃ modakaṃ prītaye vighnarājaṃ

mahāśaṃkaṭaṃ cheditaṃ dhūmaketuṃ

namo gauriputraṃ gaṇeśaṃ namaste || 4 ||

yathā dhātakacheditaṃ viṣṇunāmaṃ (!)

tathā dhyāyataṃ (!) śaṃkaraṃ pāpanāśaṃ ||

yathā pūjitaṃ ṣaṇmukhaṃ śokanāśaṃ

namo vighnarājaṃ gaṇeśaṃ namaste || 5 ||

sadā sarvvadā dhyāyataṃ ekadaṃntaṃ

sadā pūjitaṃ siṃdūraṃ raktapuṣpa (!) ||

sadā arccitaṃ caṃdanaṃ kuṃkumāgaṃ (!)

namaḥ siddhināthaṃ gaṇeśaṃ namaste || 6 ||

namo gauri aṃgaṃ malotpannatubhyaṃ

namo jñānarūpaṃ namaḥ siddhipatyaṃ ||

namo dhyāyataṃ pūjitaṃ buddhipatyaṃ

namo vakratuṃḍaṃ gaṇeśaṃ namaste || 7 ||

bhujaṃgaprayātaṃ paṭhed yastu bhaktyā

prabhāte paṭhed buddhinaikāgracittaṃ ||

ayaṃ yāti vighnaṃ diśeśo bhayaṃtaṃ

namo jñānarūpaṃ gaṇeśaṃ namaste || 8 ||

iti śrīparamahaṃsaparivīrāryya śrīsaṃkarācāryya viracitaṃ śrīgaṇeśabhujaṃga saṃpūrṇa || śubhaṃ || 9 || || (exp. 3a1:3b6)
