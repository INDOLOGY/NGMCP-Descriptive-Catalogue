Beginning

śrī bhūtesa (!) uvāca || ||

End

na deyaṃ nindakebhyo hi na deyaṃ garvvasayuti (!) ||

deyaṃ śiṣyāya bhaktāya gurubhaktiparāya ca ||

jñānayuktāya dhīrāya dātavyam iti niścayaṃ ||

gopanīyaṃ suyatnena gopanīyaṃ sadā bhavet ||

gopanīyaṃ gopanīyaṃ svayonīr iva pārvvatī || || (exp. 18, l. 1–3)
