Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

atha pṛthvirāmeśvarārccanaṃ ||

dvārasamaṇḍaladayakāva kṛrttimukhapūjā || ||

tatrādau nosiya || cetasvāna chuye cetasvāna akṣatajonāvacote ||

adyādi || asmatśrīpṛthvīrāmeśvarārccananimityarthaṃ kartuṃ śrīsūryyāyārghaṃ namaḥ || puṣyaṃ namaḥ veda || oṃ ākṛṣṇe || guru namaskāra || oṃ akaṇḍamaṇḍaleti || prāṇāyāmanyāsaṃ kārayet || (exp. 6t1–5)

End

oṃ hrauṃ netratrayāya vaṣaṭ || oṃ hraḥ astrāya phaṭ || ||

saṃhāramudrānavalithoye || || nirmālya iśānakoṇasachoye pūjā || oṃ caṃ caṃdamūrttahe namaḥ || sūryasākṣi iti ācamanaṃ || || mahādevathānanapihā vayā dohacāpūjāyānā valivāye uttaradvāra pūjā || pūrvadvārapūjā || dakṣiṇadvārapūjā || paścimadvārapūjā || || āgaṃchesaduhāvanāvajyālayā kosapūjā || (exp. 8t1–5)

Colophon

iti pṛthvirāmeśvaranityārccanaṃ || || (exp. 8t1–5)
