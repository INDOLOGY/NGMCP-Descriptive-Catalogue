Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ sarvvajñāya ||

siddhi(!) sādhye satāy astu, praśādāt tasya dhūrjjaṭeḥ |

jānhavīpheṇalekheva yanmūrddhni śaśinaḥ kalā |

śruto hitopadesoyaṃ pātavaṃ(!) saṃskṛtoktiṣu |

vācāṃ sarvatra vaicitryaṃ nītividhyāṃ dadāti ca ||

ajarāmaravat prājño vidhyām arthaṃ ca cintayet |

gṛhīta iva keśeṣu mṛtyunā dharmmam ācaret ||

sarvvadravyeṣu vidhyaiva dravyam āhur anuttamaṃ |

ahāryatvād anarghatvād akṣayatvāc ca sarvvadā ||

(fol. 1v1–4 )

End

prāleyādriḥ(!) sutāyāḥ pranaya(!)nivaśatiś candramauliḥ sa yāvad

yāvallakṣmīr murāre(!) jalada iva taḍit mānase visphuraṃtī |

yāvat svarṇṇācaloyaṃ davadanasamo yasya sūrya sphūliṃgo(!)

tāvan nārāyaṇena pracaratu caritaḥ samgraho ʼyaṃ kathānāṃ ||

paraṃ ca ||

śrīmāṃ(!) dhavalacandro ʼsau jāyā(!)maṇḍaliko ripūn |

yenāyaṃ saṃgraho yatnāl lekhayitvā pracārataḥ || ||

(fol. 140r4–140v2 )

Colophon

iti hitopadeśe saṃdhir nnāmaḥ(!) caturthakathāsamgrahaḥ samāpta(!) || || ❖ || || śubha || saṃvat 804 (4) jyeṣṭha śukla || (fol. 140v3–4 )
