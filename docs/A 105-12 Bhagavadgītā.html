Excerpts

Beginning

[ Beginning quoted from śrīdharīṭīkā ]

śrīmahāmaṃgalamūrttaye namaḥ

śeṣāśeṣamukhavyākhyā cāturyaṃ tv ekavakrataḥ

dadhānam adbhutaṃ vaṃde param ānaṃdamādhavaṃ 1

(2)natvā viśveśvaraṃ caiva prabhuṃ viśveśam ādarāt

yad bhakti yaṃtritaḥ kurve gītāvyākhyāṃ sobodhinīm 2

bhāṣyakāramataṃ sa(3)myak tadvyākhyātṛgiras tathā

yathāmati samālocya gītāvyākhyāṃ samārabhe 3 (fol. 1v1–3)

[mūla aṃśaḥ]

asya śrībhagavadgītāmālāmaṃtrasya śrībhagavān vedavyāsaṛṣir anuṣṭup chaṃdaḥ śrīkṛṣṇaḥ paramātmā devatā (5) aśocyā nanvaśocas tvaṃ prajñāvādāṃś ca bhāṣase iti bījaṃ sarvadharmān parityajya mām ekaṃ śaraṇaṃ vrajeti śaktiḥ

(6) ahaṃ tvāṃ sarvapāpebhyo mokṣayiṣyāmi mā śuca iti kīlakam śrīkṛṣṇaprītyarthe gītāpāṭhe viniyogaḥ (fol. 2r4–6)

End

[mūla aṃśaḥ]

tacca saṃsmṛtya saṃsmṛ(7)tya rupam atyadbhutaṃ hareḥ

vismayo me mahān rājān hṛṣyāmi ca punaḥ punaḥ 77

yatra yogeśvaraḥ kṛṣṇo yatra pārtho dhanu(8)rdharaḥ

tatra śrīr vijayobhūtir dhruvānītir matir mama 78 (fol. 211r6–8)

[ṭīkā aṃśaḥ]

tacceti

he rājan āścaryale yukta bhayāko esto jo śrīkṛṣṇako viśvarūpa cha (4) tasko vāraṃvārasmaraṇa gari merā manamā baḍo camatkārahuncha vāraṃvāra harṣalāi pāuṃcha 77 (fol. 211r3–4)

svaprāgalbhyabalād viloḍaya bhagavadgītāṃ tad aṃtargataṃ

tatvaṃ (!) prepsur upaiti kiṃ (1) gurukṛpāpīyuṣa dṛṣṭivinā

aṃbusvāṃjalinā nirasya jaladherāditsur aṃtarmaṇīn

āvartteṣu na kiṃ nimajjati janaḥ satkarṇadhāraṃ vinā (fol. 211v12 and 212r1)

Colophon

[ṭīkā aṃśaḥ]
