Excerpts

«Beginning»


śrī gaṇeśāya namaḥ ||


saccidānaṃdāya namaḥ ||


kṛṣṇānaṃdaṃ guruṃ natvā paramānaṃdam advayaṃ ||

vakṣye vedāṃtasārasya ṭīkāṃ nāmnā subodhinī(!) || 1 ||


iha khalu kaścit mahāpuruṣo nitya adhyayana vidhyadhīta(!) sakala veda rāśīnāṃ

cinmātrāśraya(!) tad rupa advayānaṃda viṣayānādhya nirvacanīya bhāvarupā jñāna

vilasitānaṃta bhavānuṣṭhita kāmya niṣiddha varjita nitya naimittika prāyaścittopāsanā

karmabhiḥ samyak prasanne śvarāṇām Iṣṭikā karṇādi saṃgharṣitā darśatala(!) vadeti nirmalāśayānāṃ (fol. 1v1-3)


«End»


kathaṃ tad darśanaṃ dehavināśād ūrdhvam ucyate tasmāt brahmā vijñānaṃ dṛḍhaṃ caramavigrahe

jāyate muktidaṃ jñānaṃ prasādādeva mucyata iti 3 tasmāt sūṣṭhūktaṃ vimuktaś ca vimucyata iti ||

nitya śuddha paripūrṇam advaya saccidātmakam akhaṇḍam akṣaram sarvadā sukham abodha tat kṛtair

varjitaṃ sad aham asti tat paraṃ 1 gobarddhanapreraṇayā vimuktakṣetre pavitre narasiṃhayogī

vedāntasārasya cakāra ṭīkāṃ subodhinīṃ viśvapate purastāt 2


jāte paṃcaśatādhike daśaśate saṃvatsarāṇāṃ punaḥ

saṃjāte daśavatsare prabhuvara śrī śālivāhe śake


prāte durmukhavatsare śubhaśucau māse anumatyāṃ tithau (!)


prāpte bhārgavavāsare narahariṣ ṭīkāṃ cakārojvalām 3 || (fol. 38r1-5)


«Colophon»


iti śrīparamahaṃsaparivrājakācāryaśrīmatkṛṣṇananda(!)bhagavatpūjyapādaśiṣyanṛsiṃhasarasvatīkṛtā

vedāntasāraṭīkā samāptā || || atha saṃkhyā 1300 (fol. 38v5-6)
