Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīgaṇeśāya ||

praṇamya śāradāṃ devīṃ divyavijñānaśālinīṃ |

vacmi ceto vinodārtha(!), sudhāraṃ kīrasaptateḥ || 1 ||

yathā || asti candrakalaṃ nāma nagaraṃ || tatra vikramaseno nāma rājā, tatra haridattaḥ śreṣṭhiḥ (!) || tasya bhāryyā śṛṃgārasundarī,[[ divyāvaraṇābhūṣitām (!)]] tayoḥ suto madanaḥ || tatpatni (!) somadattaśreṣṭhinoḥ (!) putrī prabhāvatī nāma, sa tasyām anurāgavān, sa ca madano viṣayāśaktaḥ || tatpratibodhāya śreṣṭhimitravikrameṇānītau, gaṃdharvvapuṣpāpīḍāmālatījīto(!) sūkasārike (!) || (fol. 1v1-4)

End

śukenoktaṃ || kutra yāsi hasitvā proktaṃ | paragṛhe | tatonena paragṛhagamane eka[[sa]]ptatikathābhir bahavo doṣā uktāḥ | ayam evopakāraḥ | tato madanaḥ sukhaṃ gṛhī(!) vasati | prabhāvaty api śukaṃ nirvvacanaṃ kṛtvā tiṣṭhati sma || (fol. 58v2-4)

Colophon

iti śukasaptatau, dvisaptatitamī kathā || iti strīcaritaprāptave(!) mahadākhyana(!) śukadvisaptatitamī kathā samāptaḥ(!) || || śubhaṃ || samvat 749 śrāvaṇakṛṣṇāṣṭamyāṃ, rohiṇī somavāsare śrīśrījayanareśamalladevaprabhunā(!) likhāpitaṃ (!) daivajñasumeruṇā likhitaṃ saṃpūrṇṇaṃ || (fol. 58v4-5)
