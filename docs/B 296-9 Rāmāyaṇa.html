śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || śrīrāmacandrāya namaḥ ||

kyā sodhachau timi so(5)dha bhaṃchu ma bhanī marjī bhayothyo jasai |

brahmāko (6) karuṇā bujhera ṛṣile vintīgaryā yo tasai || (7)1
