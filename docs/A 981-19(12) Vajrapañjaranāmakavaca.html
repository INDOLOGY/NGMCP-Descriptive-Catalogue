Excerpts

«Complete Transcript:»

adhunā devadeveśa kavacaṃ vajrapaṃjaraṃ ||

kālyā kṛpākaṭākṣormi kirmīritadṛśaṃ bhṛśaṃ || 1 ||

īśvara uvāca ||

śṛṇu devi pravakṣyāmi tavāgre kavacottamaṃ

yasmin mṛte bhavaty eva śrīkāliṃ tanayo naraḥ || 2 ||

vinā pūjā na siddhiḥ syād dakṣiṇā dakṣiṇānanā ||

tataḥ kavacinā bhāvyaṃ sādhakeṃdreṇa siddhaye || 3 ||

ṛṣir asya smṛtaḥ śaṃbhuḥ chaṃdaḥ sukṛtir uttama ||

devatāṃ dakṣiṇāṃ dhyātvā kālī pratyakṣaraṃ nyaset || 4 ||

aṃbhodaḥ śyāmalāgī (!) mativikaṭadaṃtī lolajihvāṃ jatālāṃ (!)

muṃḍaṃ vāme dadhānāṃ karakamalatale dakṣiṇe caṃdrahāsaṃ ||

sṛkkvadvaṃdvāstradhārādharamukhakamalāṃ pretador baddhakāṃcīṃ

mādhvī konmattanetrāṃ śavahṛdayagatāṃ naumi kālīṃ karālīṃ || 5 ||

krāṃ bījaṃ mūrttigaṃḍadvitayam anu punas tad vayaṃ hraṃ dvayaṃ tu

netradvaṃdveva lajjā yugalam api muhuḥ karṇayor ninyasec ca ||

nāsādvaṃdve ca rakṣir adanavasanayor ṇecakādaṃ tapaṃktyor (!)

like bhrū dvadgrade ca tritayam api punac (!) bījam ādyaṃ nyasec ca || 6 ||

bāhuṃ dvaṃdve ca hbhūṃ hbhūṃ caraṇayugalake śaktibījadvayaṃ ca

svāhā sarvatra dehe viśasatarapatir vinyaset vyāpakena ||

evaṃ nyāsaṃ vidhāya tribhuvanavalayakhyātakīrttikaviṃdro

vajrāgojīvaloke jayati bahujanaḥ sādhakaṃ siddhamaṃtraḥ || 7 ||

krīṃ krīṃ krīṃ devi huṃ huṃ kahakaha hahahe hehi hrīṃ hrīṃ hahāhā

daṃdaṃdaṃ dakṣiṇekaṃ kakakaka kalite kālike kālapatnī ||

krīṃ krīṃ krīṃ yoni hūṃ hūṃ hahahaha mahile kāli hrīṃ hrīṃ hariśe

svāhā sāhe śanetre bhabhabhabha bhavatī bhūtaye vo bhae tu || 8 ||

bhūtapretapiśāca pannagavarapratyarthisenābhaye

raṇye raṇyatarakṣu bhīṣu ca tathā cānyeṣvabhīṣṭaṃ sphutaṃ

smṛtvainaṃ manumuttamaṃ gatabhayo jāyeta vīrottamo

viśvāpaṃcakasādhayed anudinaṃ svāṃte dadhat sādhakaḥ || 9 || ||

iti kālīkalpe vajrapaṃjaranāmakavacaṃ saṃpūrṇam || 12 || (fols. 17r9–18r5)
