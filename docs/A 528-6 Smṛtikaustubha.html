Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ


divākaraniśākarāv analavidyutas tārakā

yadīyaparatejasā jagati satprakāśakṣamāḥ |

yadātmasukhaleśataḥ sukhina āha vedo ʼparān

dhanaṃjayajyapradaṃ jayati sūtaveṣa mahaḥ 1


yadīyamukhacaṇdrataḥ sravati sādhuśabdāmṛte

sabhāsthitabudhāḥ sadā dadadhi citrabuddhiṃ mudā

sa pāṇinipatañjaliprabhṛtibhūripuṇyātmanāṃ

kadācid api yadhṛdā ʼkhilakṛtir na saṃjyajyate 2


manumukhamunivaravacasām anusāraṃ yaḥ

sadā sadācaraṇaiḥ kurvann avanigatānāṃ mahatā mahatāṃ dhiyaṃ vaśe tanute 3 (fol. 1v1–4)


End

tatraivānyo [ʼ]pi viśeṣaḥ bhadrāsanaṃ ca chatraṃ ca vālavyajanam eva ca | khaḍgaṃ vaktraṃ tathā

cāparatrāni vividhāni ca | rājñan pūrvasya ye tv āsat(!) sarva eva narāḍhipena te kāryā nareṃdrasya

ta tadaiva vidhā tadā | kāmaṃ saṃvatsaraḥ kāryo ʼlābhe [ʼ]nyasya bhūbhujā guṇādhikāś cetroº kāryo

ye [ʼ]nyetrābhihitām apeti pūrve vehuṇādhikās tadā ta eva grāhyā dutyāśayaḥ | acatā ʼvanitalanilayaṃ

vividhamadodyaṃ †kalerale† vilayaṃ | nītaṃ vatanacanītaṃ ghṛtam iva rūpaṃ kṛtaṃ kṛtaṃ yena 1

vājavāhāduravaṃdubhūpateḥ kaustubhe [ʼ]tra nṛpadharmagocare |

dīdhitiprathamāgardradṛśastoṣākṛtsuṃ kṛtināṃ vyajāyata 2 (fol. 46v2–6)


Colophon

iti śrīmatsakalabhūmaṃḍalamaṃḍanāyamānavājavāhāduracaṃdradevājñāpravṛttena

prathamadīdhitiḥ samāptā || || saṃvat 1752 samanāma phāgu (fol. 46v6–8)
