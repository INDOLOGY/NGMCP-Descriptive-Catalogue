Reel No. A 562-2 Inventory No. 34897

Title #Prauḍhamanoramāṭīkā

Remarks This manuscript contains a refutative commentary on the Prauḍhamanoramā. This commentary is not the Laghuśabdaratna, Bṛhatśabdaratna, Jyotsnā, Kucamardinī, Vibhā, Prabhā, Bhairavī or Paramatakhaṇḍana.

The root text is not given separately.

Lines per Folio 7–10

Foliation figures in the upper left and lower right margins of the verso; marginal titles: ka. ṭī., ka. la. ṭī., mano. ṭī., mano; rāma is written above the right-hand folio number.

Manuscript Features

The text goes up to prādūhoḍhoḍhyeṣaiṣyeṣu of the Acsandhiprakaraṇa chapter.

On fol. 18v the handwriting changes.
