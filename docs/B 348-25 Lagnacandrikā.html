Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīsūryyāya ||

tamisrā jagaragrastaṃ, yo jīvayati bhūtalaṃ |

taṃ vande paramānandaṃ, sarvasākṣina(!)m īśvaraṃ || 1 ||

tanur dhanaṃ ca bhrātā ca, suhṛt putro ripuḥ striyaḥ |

mṛtyuś ca dharmmakarmāyavyayā bhāvāḥ prakīrttitāḥ || 2 ||

viṣamo tha samaḥ puṃ stri(!) krūrākrūraś ca nāmataḥ |

cara sthiro dviśvabhāvo meṣādyā rāsa(!)yaḥ smṛtāḥ || 3 ||

duścikyaṃ syā tṛtīye ca, caturthe sukhasadma ca |

baṃdhusaṃjñañ ca pātālaṃ hibukaṃ paṃcamaṃ ca dhiḥ || 4 || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

daśāṃśerkaḥ śaśī ++++ṣṭādaśamāṃke

budhaḥ paṃcadaśāṃśe ca, paṃcamāṃśe bṛhaspatiḥ || 833 ||

saptaviṃśāṃśake śukro, viṃśatyaṃśe śanaiścaraḥ |

saiṃhike pañcaviṃśāṃśe, paramocco nigadyate || 834 || (fol. 78v6–79r2)

Colophon

iti śrīsarvvaśāstra viśārada śrīkāśīnāthakṛtau lagnacaṃdrikā samāptaṃ(!) || || yādṛśaṃpustakaṃ

...kṣamaśva jagadīśvara ||

nepālābde ṣaḍrasebhe surārātigrau yute |

māse nabhasi indau ca supañcamyāṃ tithau dine ||

kāśīnāthakṛtaṃ granthaṃ kṛṣṇākhyena prabodhakā(!) |

śaivarāmasya pautrena(!), cālikhallagnacandrikā ||

|| saṃvat 866 śrāvanamāse śukle paṃcamyāṃ śukravāre śubha ||

pādaikadṛṣṭiḥ daśama(!) tṛtīye

dvipādadṛṣṭiḥ navapañcameṣu |

tṛ(!)pādadṛṣṭiḥ caturāṣṭakeṣu

saṃpūrṇadṛṣṭiḥ samasaptakeṣu || (fol. 79r3–79v2)
