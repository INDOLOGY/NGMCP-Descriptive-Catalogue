oṃ namo narasiṃhāya ||

End

kiṃ kiṃ siṃhas tataḥ kiṃ narasadṛśavapur ddevacitraṃ gṛhītaṃ
naivaṃ dhikko nujīvet kramam anupatitaḥ so pī(!) satyaṃ hariśaḥ

cāpaṃ cāpaṃ nakhāṅkaṃ jhaṭiti hahahahaṃ karkaśatvaṃ nakhānām
