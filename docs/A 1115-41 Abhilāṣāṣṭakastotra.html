Excerpts

Beginning

|| śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

tam ālokya ⟨|⟩ stutiṃ cakre romakaṃcukito mudā

proccaran gadga[[dā]]lāpān namo stv iti punaḥ punaḥ 1

ekaṃ brahmaivādvitīyaṃ samastaṃ

satyaṃ satyaṃ neha nānāsti kiṃcit

eko rudro na dvitīyo vatasthe

tasmād ekaṃ tvāṃ prapadye maheśaṃ 2

ekaḥ kartā tvaṃ hi sarvasya śaṃbho

nānā⟨ṃ⟩rūpeṣv ekarūpo ⟪sya⟫[[py a]]rūpaḥ

yadvat †pratyamvvarka† eko py anekas

tasmān nānyaṃ tvāṃ vineśaṃ prapadye 3 (fol. 1v1–4)

End

gopanīyaṃ prayatnena mahāvaṃdhyāprasūtikṛt

striyā vā puroṣeṇāpi niyamāll iṃgasannidhau 22

abdaṃ japtam idaṃ stotraṃ putradaṃ nātra saṃśayaḥ

ity uktvāntarhito bālaḥ so pi vipro gṛhaṃ yayau || 23 || (fol. 2v6–7 (the 7th line continues to the margin as well))

Colophon

iti śrīskandapurāṇe kāśīkhaṇḍe indrāgnilokavarṇane ʼbhilāṣāṣṭakaṃ vīreśvarastotraṃ sampūrṇam || (fol. 2v7 (the 7th lines continues to the margin as well and the colophon is written in the margin)
