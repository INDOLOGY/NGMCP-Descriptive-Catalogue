Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

namas tasyai jaganmāta[ḥ] kuṃbhayonipriyāśriye ||

namaskṛtya śivāṃ devīṃ sarvabhāvagatāṃ śubhāṃ ||

purāṇaṃ saṃpravakṣyāmi yathoktam ṛṣibhiḥ purā ||

ṛṣaya ūcuḥ ||

bhagavaṃs tvaṃ samastasya dṛṣṭādṛṣṭasya tattvavit ||

purāṇārthaṃ vayaṃ sarve āgatā bhāvabhāvitāḥ ||

kathyatāṃ yatra ghorārthā bhūtāḥ sāṃpratadānavāḥ || (fol. 1v1–4)


End

devyaḥ<ref>accusative case-ending is aspected</ref> saṃpūjayitvā<ref>apāṇinīyaprayogaḥ</ref> tu vidhinā nṛpasattama ||

ṣaḍāvarttaṃ prakurvvaṃti pratyakṣā bhavate śivā ||<ref>This stanza contains syntactical errors; according to Pāṇini.</ref>

sadācāraḥ śubhāhāraḥ sarvvasaṃgavivarjitaḥ ||

vācayan śatam arddham vā patyakṣaṃ prāpnuyāc chivaṃ ||

etac chābhyudayaṃ pādaṃ śataiḥ tribhi(!) navottaraiḥ ||

sahasrair dvādaśair vatsa kathitaṃ sarvvasiddhidaṃ || || (fol. 231v11–12)


Colophon

ityādye devyavatāre devīpurāṇaṃ samātam || 27 || ❁ || || śubhaṃ || ||

śrīgaurīdattasya (It seems that, this line was added latter in a different hand) (fol. 231v12)
