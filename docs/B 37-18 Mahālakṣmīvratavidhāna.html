Manuscript Features

This MTM contains the following texts:

1. Mahālakṣmῑvratavidhāna 2. Karmakāṇḍapustaka 3. Namaskārātmakagrantha

The text is copied in corrupt Sanskrit and contains numerous errors, and these errors are not marked in the excerpt below.

Excerpts

«Beginning»

❖ oṁ namo mahālakṣmīdevyai namaḥ ||

sūryāghaṃ datvā || aṅgaṃnyāsa || arghapātrapūjā || dhenumudrāṃ darśayet || nivañcanam ||

imaṃ dīpaṃ mahājyoti puspasarṣapasaṃyutam |

sarvvavyādhivinimuktaṃ pāpavṛndaharāya ca ||

oṁ mahālakṣmī astrāya namaḥ | ācamya || dvārapūjā || (fol. 1v1–3)


«End»

paścāt pāraṇam | puna prabhāta pratyārantapūjā visarjjanaṃ kārayet || ○ || yathoktaṣoḍasaṃ varṣaṃ sampūrṇā cādiṣṭajanaṃ | mamāvāsagṛhasthe vā ṣoḍasaṃ kamalālaye ||

ṣoḍasaḥ ṣoḍasīvāyaṃ saṃbhāraṃ pratigṛhyatām ||

yathoktaguruṇā mahyaṃ bhuktimuktipraśādatu ||

siṃdhūrāṃjanapātrañ ca caṃdanaṃ dāḍikopari |

brāhyaṃ kulaṃ ca pakkāraṃ vāyanaṃ dattam uttamam || ||

thvate vani thaṃneyā pāṭha || ○ || ○ || (fol. 14r1–5)


«Colophon»

iti śrīmahālakṣmīvidhanavrata samāptaṃ || śubham astu ||

samvat 620 caitra śuddhi 2 śrīrāmajusyaṃ kāyapanistaṃ coyā thva vidhāna || (fol. 14r6)
