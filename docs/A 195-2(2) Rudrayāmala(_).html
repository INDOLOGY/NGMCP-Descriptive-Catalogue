Beginning

sarvāgamarahasyāḍhyaṃ sarvadevaikavanditaṃ ||

śrībhairava uvāca

etad rahasyaṃ paraṃ(!)maṃ mantranāmasahasrakaṃ ||

lakṣmīnārāyaṇaśreṣṭhaṃ sarvasvaṃ tattvato mama ||

guhyaṃ gopyaṃ(!)tamaṃ devi sukhadaṃ dharmmavarddhanaṃ ||

lakṣmīśaṃ(!)vacanaṃ loke paratra paramārthadaṃ ||

End

viṃduṃ guruṃ ca saṃpūjya gaṃdhākṣataprasūnakaiḥ ||

tatra viṃdau guruṃ devi sthāpayed bhaktipūrvakaṃ ||

saṃpūjayet svamūlena dakṣiṇāṃ kālikāṃ bhajet ||

mahākālaṃ yajet tatra kāmaṃ kāmeśvari(!) tathā ||

guruṃ ca paramaṃ devi parāparaguruṃ tataḥ ||
