Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ siddhiḥ śrīgaṇeśāya ||

maheśvaram namaskṛtya kumāran tadanantaraṃ |

sugamaḥ kriyate ʼsmābhir ayaṅ kātantravistaraḥ |

abhiyogaparāḥ pūrvve bhāṣāyāṃ yat prapedire |

prāyeṇa tad ihāsmābhiḥ parityaktan na kiñcana || ||

siddho varṇasamāmnāyaḥ || siddhaḥ sakalalokaprasiddhasaṃjñāsahita iha śāstravarṇṇasamāmnāyo veditavyaḥ | varṇṇā akārādayas teṣāṃ samāmnāyaḥ pāṭhakramaḥ || || tatra caturddaśādau svarāḥ || tatra prasiddhe varṇṇasamāmnāyaviṣaye ādau ye caturddaśavarṇās te svarasaṃjñā bhavanti || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

supathi nalopaḥ || suvidvan virāmavyañjanādiṣv iti datvaṃ supuṃ pūntso naśabdalopaḥ sūdyūkūlaṃ || diva udvyañjane evamūkhāśrat || śrasiddhasoś ca || harāṣakṣāntebhyo dadyo bhavanti || napuṃsakād iti vihitaviśeṣaṇan tena bahūni śreyāṃsi yasya vamihū śreyāt || virāmasūbheṣv iti siddhe vyañjanagrahaṇaṃ vyañjanaśabdoccāritakāryyapratipattyarthaṃ || tena tatkūlam ityādi tyadādyatvan na syāt || || (fol. 54r6–v1)

Colophon

iti śrīmatkarṇadevopādhyāya śrīvarddhamānamiśraviracita .. vistare nāmni tṛtīyaḥ pādaḥ samāptaḥ || || || śrīśrīśrī śāradāyai namaḥ || || śrī śrī śrī bhavānyai pritir astu saṃpūrṇam idam pustakaṃ kṛtohaṃ yas teno bhūyāḥ || || ❖ || (fol. 54v1–2)
