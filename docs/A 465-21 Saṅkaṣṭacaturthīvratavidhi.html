Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

ṛṣaya ūcuḥ ||

dāridryaśokakaṣṭādyaiḥ pīḍitānāṃ ca vairibhiḥ ||

rājyabhraṣṭair nṛpaiḥ sarvaiḥ kriyate kiṃ śubhārthibhiḥ || 1 ||

dhanahīnair naraiḥ skaṃdasarvopadravapīḍitaiḥ ||

vidyāputragṛhabhraṣṭaiḥ rogayuktaiḥ śivārthibhiḥ || 2 ||

karttavyaṃ kiṃ vadopāyaṃ punaḥ kṣemārthasiddhaye || ||

skaṃda uvāca || ||

śṛṇudhvaṃ munayaḥ sarve vratānām uttamaṃ vrataṃ || 3 ||

saṃkaṣṭatāraṇaṃ nāma putreha sukhadāyakaṃ || (fol. 1v1–5)


End

ahalyāpi patiṃ lebhe gautamaṃ prāṇavallabhaṃ ||

vidyārthī labhate vidyāṃ dhanārthī dhanam āpnuyāt || 79 ||

putrārthī ⟪prāpnuyā⟫ labhate putram ⟨āpnoti⟩ rogī rogād vimucyate || 80 || || (fols. 8v6–9r1)


Colophon

iti śrīskaṃdapurāṇoktaṃ saṃkaṭacaturthīvrataṃ samāptaṃ || || ||

saṃvat 1760 śāke 1625 śrāvaṇa vadi 13 || sahasi māsike caturthīsaṃjñakatithau some saṃkaṣṭanāśanaṃ || || || likhitaṃ bhīmapuroparāmagoviṃdabhaṭṭasutasarvottameṇa svārthaṃ parārthaṃ ca ||

yādṛśaṃ pustakaṃ dṛṣṭvā tādṛśaṃ likhitaṃ mayā

yadi śuddham aśuddhaṃ vā mama doṣo na dīyate ||

śrīr astu || śubham astu || || (fol. 9r1–5)
