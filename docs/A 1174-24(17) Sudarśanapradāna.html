Manuscript Features

The manuscript contains 32 Stotras, see A 1174-24 Stotrasaṃgraha for a complete list.

In many places a and e are indistinguishable, likewise ā and o.

Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śivāya ||

ṛṣaya ūcuḥ ||
sudarśanākhyaṃ yac cakraṃ labdhavāns(!) tat kathaṃ hariḥ |
mahādevād bhagavata(!) sūta tad vaktum arhasi || ||

sūta uvāca ||
devāsurāṇām abhavat saṃgrāmo dbhutadarśanaḥ |
devāś ca nirjjitā daityair vviṣṇuśaraṇam āgatā ||
stutvā taṃ vividhai stotraiḥ praṇamya purasasthitāḥ(!) |
bhayabhītāś ca te sarvve dānavaiḥ kleśitā bhṛśaṃ ||
tāṃ dṛṣṭvā prāha bhagavān devadevo janārddinaḥ |
kimartham āgatā devā vaktum arhatha sāmprataṃ ||
vacaḥ śrutvā harer ddevāḥ praṇamyocuḥ surottama |
nirjjitā dānavaiḥ sarvve śaraṇaṃ tvām ihāgatā(!) ||
gatis tvam eva devānāṃ trātā tvaṃ puruṣottama |
hantum arhasi tān kṣipram avadhyān vārijekṣaṇa || (fol. 58r1-4)

End

aśvamedhasahasrasya phalam bhavati niścitaṃ |
paṭhate sarvvabhāvena vidyā ca mahatī bhavet ||
jāyate mahad aiśvaryyaṃ śivasya dayito bhavet |
dustare jalasaṃghāte taj jalaṃ sthalatāṃ vrajet ||
hārāyante mahāsarppāḥ siṃhakrīḍā mṛgāyate |
tasmān nāmnāṃ sahasreṇa stotavyo bhagavān śivaḥ ||
prayacchate khilāt[[kā]]mān(!) dehān te ca parāṃ gatiṃ || || (fol. 58r1-3) <references/>

Colophon

ity ādityapurāṇe mānavīyasaṃhitāyāṃ nārāyaṇasya sudarśanapradānaṃ nāma ekonacatvariṃśatitamo dhyāyaḥ || || śrīsadāśivaprītir astu || || śubhaṃ bhūyāt sarvvadā || || (fol. 62r3-4)
