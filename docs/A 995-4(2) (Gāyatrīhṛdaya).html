Manuscript Features

The language of the text is not standard, and so, irregularities are not marked. There are three extra folios of some Tantra text written in maithili script.

Excerpts

Beginning

❖ takārañ camyākā(!)pītaṃ brahmaviṣṇuśivātmakaṃ

sāntaṃ padmaṃ samārūḍhaṃ dhyātvā dahati pātakaṃ |

takārasya ca vaśiṣṭha ṛṣi (!) gāyitrī(!)cchandaḥ agnir ddevatā pṛthvībījaṃ 〇 praharān saktiḥ †ṣaṇṇvaṣasutrā† pītavarṇṇaṃ dhyātvā pādāṅguṣṭhaṃ | vianse (!) mahā (!) dahati pātakaṃ | cchakāra (!) vinaye (!) ṅguṣṭha (!) atasīpuṣpasanibhaṃ padmamadhye sitā (!) dhyātvā dahati copapātakaṃ | cchakārasya viśvāmitra ṛṣi (!) 〇 triṣṭubchandaḥ prajāpatidevatā āpo bījaṃ viśvasakti etc. (fol. 1v1–4)

End

aṣṭau brāhmaṇa grāhayitvā | brahmaloke mahītale |

ṣāṣṭiśatasahasrāṇi 〇 gāyitrī japetvā yat phalaṃ tat labhate gāyitrīhṛdayasmaraṇamātraṃñ ca iti gāyitrīhṛdaya samāpta || ||

samaṃ saṃpuṭa 〇 caiva | vicitrā virtīrṇṇam eva ca |

muṣaṃ dvimuṣaṃ trimuṣaṃ caturmuṣaṃ pañcamuṣaṃ ṣaṭmuṣaṃ adhomuṣaṃ saṃmuṣaṃ vyāpakāñjatiko 〇 tathā |

sakaṭaṃ jamapānaṃñ ca granthitaṃ saṃmuṣo nimuṣaṃ

vilambaṃ muṣtikaṃñ caiva po kūrmmo varāhakaṃ |

siṃhakrāntam mahakrānte | mudgaraṃ praṇame tathā |

ete mudrā caturviṅśati gāyitrī ārādhye |

gāyitrīmudrā yo yo na jānāti tat sarvvaṃ niphalaṃ bhavet (fol. 4r1–v1)

Colophon

iti mudrā samāpta || (fol. 4v1)
