Beginning

❖ oṁ namaḥ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||


sū(!)drasya śrāddhaṃ likhyate ||


tatrādau pādaprakhyā(!)lanaṃ ||


tāmrapātre candanākṣatakuśapuṣpaṃ datvā || pūrvvābhimukhena sūryyārghyaṃ kārayet || adyādi ||

gotrasūryyārghya || amuko deśaśāmbatsarīkaśrāddhe kartuṃ śrīsūryyāya arghaṃ nama[ḥ] ||


oṁ mohāndhakāma[ma]gnānaṃ janānāṃ jñānaraśmibhih |

kṛtam uddhalanaṃ je(!)na ta[ṃ] naumi śivabhāskala(!) || (exp. 4t1–5)


End

āyuprajādhanaṃ vidyā svarggamokṣa[va]sūni ca |

prayacchantu tathā rājñā nṛṇāṃ caiva pitāmahā(!) ||


āyuvṛddhi[r] yaśovṛddhi[r] vṛddhiprajñāsukhaśriyā |

Dharmasantānavṛddhis tu saṃtu te saptavṛddhayaḥ ||
