Excerpts

Beginning

śrīmanmahāgaṇādhipataye namaḥ ||    ||

śrīsarasvatyai namaḥ ||     ||

yogīśvaraṃ yājñavalkyaṃ saṃpūjya munayo bruvan ||

varṇāśrametarāṇāṃ no brūhi dharmān aśeṣataḥ || 1 ||

milāsthaḥ sa yogīndraḥ kṣaṇaṃ dhyātvābravīn munīn ||

yasmin deśe mṛgaḥ kṛṣṇas tasmin dharmān nibodhata || 2 ||

purāṇānyāyamīmāṃṣādharmaśāstrāṅgamiśritāḥ ||

vedāḥ sthānāni vidyānāṃ dharmasya ca caturdaśa || 3 || (fol. 1v1–3; 1st foliation)

«End: »

aśvamedhapahlaṃ tasya tad bhavān anumanyatām ||

śrutvaitad yājñavalkyo pi prītātmā munibhāṣitam || 335 ||

evam astv iti hovāca namaskṛtya svayaṃbhuve ||     || (fol. 17r6–7; 3rd foliation)

«Sub-colophons:»

iti śrīyājñavalkyamunipraṇītadharmaśāstre prathamo dhyāyaḥ samāptaḥ ||    ||    || (fol. 18r2–3; 1st foliation)

iti śrīyājñavalkyamunipraṇītadharmaśāstre dvitīyo dhyāyaḥ ||    ||    || (fol. 16v4; 2nd foliation)

Colophon

iti śrīyājñavalkyamunipraṇītadharmaśāstre tṛtīyo dhyāyaḥ ||    ||

śrīkṛṣṇaḥ ||     ||     ||     ||      || śrīḥ ||     ||     ||     ||     || śubham || (fol. 17r7–8; 3rd foliation)
