Manuscript Features

The MS contains the following texts:

1. Sarasvatīstotra

2. Sarasvatīstuti

3. Sāradāstava

4. Gaṇeśastotra

5. Bhīmarājastotra

6. Pāṇḍavagītāstotra

7. Bhīmāṣṭaka

8. Aparādhastava

9. Mṛtyuñjayastotra

10. Bhavānīśaṅkarāṣṭakastotra

11. Nārāyaṇaśoḍaśanāma

12. Viṣṇupañjalakastotra

13. Nārāyaṇastotra

14. Tulasīkāṣṭhamāhātmya

15. Tulasīstava

16. Viṣṇunārāyaṇastotra

These texts contain many scribal errors, apparent errors are not marked in the Excerpt below.



Excerpts

«Beginning of 1. Sarasvatīstotra»

❖ oṃ namo śrīsarasvatyai namaḥ ||

sarasvati namas tubhyaṃ varade kāmarūpini |

vidyāraṃbhaṃ kaliṣyāmi siddhir bbhavatu me sadā || 1 ||

vāgathāviva saṃpṛktau vāgarthapratipattaye |

jagataḥ pitarau vande pārvvatīparameśvarau || 2 || (exp. 3t1–4)


«End of 16. Viṣṇunārāyaṇastotra»

saṃjyo uvāca ||

nārāyano kṛto rājā vihnumāyā kuranāhaliḥ śivāyaḥ prītri brahmāyanī vihnu de guru || (exp. 50b1–3)


«Colophon of 16. Viṣṇunārāyaṇastotra»

iti śrīskandhapurānya vihnunnārāyaṇāya samāpta || śubham astu ||

jādṛṣṭaṃ pustakaṃ dṛṣṭvā tādṛṣṭaṃ likhitaṃ mayā

jadda śuddha asudhaṃ vā mama doṣo na diyate ||

thva sarasvati devayā śirokaḥ thava gvaṃ stikadhanikarmmācājyayāke kāyāvaḥ devyandrakarmmācājyena thava āṣalasena vavamacāto śenayātā hleyā julo śubha || ❖ || (exp. 50b3–7)
