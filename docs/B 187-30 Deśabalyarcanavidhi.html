Beginning

❖ śrīgurubhyo namaḥ || ||

deśabalyārccaṇavidhi likṣate || ||

yajamāna puṣpabhājanaṃ || adyādi(2) vākya ||

śrīsamvarttā, śyāmāraktā ||

utphullāṃ || nirvvāṇaṃni ||

siddhilastu kriyā || laṃkhana hāya, canda(3)nādi svāna taya || || nośiya ||

khphreṁ hrāṁ ātmatatvāya svāhā ||

khphreṁ hrīṁ vidyātatvāya svāhā (4)||

khphreṁ hruṁ śivatatvāya svāhā ||

khphreṁ hrāṁ hrīṁ hūṁ sarvvatatvāya svāhā || ||

sūryyārgha || adyādi || vā(5)kye ||

śrī 3 siddhilakṣmyādidevī sthāpana varṣavarddhana dhvajāvarohana deśabalyārccaṇa || (fol. 1v1-5)
