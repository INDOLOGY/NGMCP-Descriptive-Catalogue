Excerpts

«Beginning of the root text:»

❖ oṃ namaḥ kṛṣṇāya ||     || rājovāca ||

nivṛttimārgaḥ kathitaḥ(!) ādau bhavatā yathā || (!)

kramayogopalabdhena, brahmaṇāya yad asaṃsṛtiḥ || 1 ||

pravṛttilakṣaṇaś caiva, traiguṇyaviṣayo mune ||

yo sābalīnaprakṛter gguṇasargaḥ punaḥ punaḥ || 2 || (fol. 1v5–6)

«Beginning of the commentary:»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

puṇyāraṇye nṛsiṃhaikanāmasiṃho virājate ||

yan nāmataḥ palāyante mahākalmaṣakuṃjarāḥ || 1 ||

visargasaṃvā(!)n jīvān svamaryādosu (!) saṃyutān ||

vi(la)yābhyakhilai rūpair ity eva paṃcame sthitaṃ || 2 || (fol. 1v1–2)

«End of the root text:»

nānubhūya na jānāti pumān viṣayatīṣṇatāṃ ||

nirvidyeta svayaṃ tasmāt na tathā bhinnadhīḥ paraiḥ || 41 ||

yan nastvaṃ kartṛsaṃdhānāṃ sādhūnāṃ gṛhamedhinām ||

kṛtavān asi durmarṣaṃ vipriyaṃ tava me(!)rṣitaṃ || 42 ||

taṃtukṛṃtanam asvastyam(!) abhadram acaraḥ punaḥ ||

tasmāṃllokeṣu te mūḍha na bhaved bhramataḥ padaṃ || 43 || (fol. 20v10–11)

«End of the commentary:»

itthaṃ virāgo na syād ityatra hetum āha |

nānubhūya ananubhūyaviṣayāṇāṃ tīkṣṇatāṃ duḥkhahetutvaṃ na jānāti tasmād viṣayānubhavena tīkṣṇatvajñānāt || 41 || atas tvayāsmākam apri-(fol. 20v12–13)
