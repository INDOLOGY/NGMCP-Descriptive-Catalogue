Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śṛīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

bho agastamuni thvate prakāranaṃ candramāyā īhipā dhunakāva tākāranaṃ sukhabhoga yānāva cona jula ||
thvanaṃli thva candramāna jula(2)sāṃ thava kṣa kalaha kaciṃgala juyāva rohinī dhayāhma karātava bilvadha juyāva conaṃ || bāki 26 hma kalātava nāpa jaka rasa yānāva ro(3)hinī dhayāhma rāniyā māhādukha juyāva vayāya he masiyāva tatākehepanista chuṃchuṃ madhāsyaṃ || māhāduḥkhanaṃ tākāraṃ hanāva conaṃ || tatākehe(4)panisyanaṃ nasuddhāṃ namatusya talaṃ thulita duḥkha juyāva ana vane he masiyāva mahāduḥkhanaṃ thava kṣa vanaṃ ||    ||
(fol. candramāº1r1–5)

End

thanaṃli śrīmahādevanaṃ antapurayā kha jāraṃdharanāpa juddha yānāgu(7) kha viṃdyāyāta viśnunaṃ chalaya yānāva prativratā dharma nṣṭa yākaguyā kha viṃdyāyā sasapa viśnunaṃ bhoga yākagu ādiṃ saṃpu(8)rṇa kha pārvatidevīyāta kanāva padma ānaṃdanaṃ bijyātaṃ thanayā kha thuli || bho agastamunisana kumāranaṃ ājñā dayakalaṃ ||    || (fol. jālaṃdharaº26r6–8)

Sub–colophons

śrī 3 svasthāniparameśvarava śrīskaṃdakumārayāke ṅenāva cona jula || (fol. candramāº4v9)

iti śrīskaṃndapurāṇe māgha(8)māhātmye (kedārakhaṃṇḍe) kumāra (agasta)saṃmvāde śrīmahārudra mṛga rupa juyāva śrīpārvatiyāta chalaya yānāva bijyākagu kha thvate śleṣamāntaka vana (9) …samāpta ||    ||

iti śrīska | pūrane keḍarakhande māghamātmane śrīsvasthāniparameśvara vrartta īndrāsana sāmyaga svarggayā kathā samāṃptaṃ || ۞ ||(7) ||    ||
liṣitaṃ jhvālabāhālayā śrīvrajrāścāryya, viśvaratnaṃ dalici ṛṣiyā kha || ki⟪lāsa⟫[[tesvala]] māhādevayā kha || māhādeva lucarā ju(8)vaguyā kha || kumāraṃ tārakāśulayāta juddha thayāva śyākaguyā kha. thva pyahmasyā kha magāgu tanā tayā thavake maduyā niṃtiṃ jula || śubhaṃ || (fol. kumāraº14v6–8)

iti śrīsakaṃṇḍapurāne keḍāra(10)khaṃṇḍe māghamātmya kumāra agasta saṃvāde ṭrīpurāśūla daitya juddha kṛta īndra amarāvati pravyaśanāma ||    || śrīsvasthāniparamyaśvararyyā kathā samāptāṃ || ۞ || (fol. trīpurāº10v9–10)

īti śrīskaṃḍapurāṇye kedārakhaṃṇḍe kumā. agastasaṃvāde jāraṃdharasaṃhāra viṃdyāsahagāmini viśnu sarāpa bhoga kṛtaṃ nāma śrīsvasthāni parameśvara(right margin1)ryyā kathāyāṃ paṃcadaśamodhyāya || 15 || bho agastamuni || (fol. jālaṃdharaºjālaṃdharaº26r9–right margin1)
