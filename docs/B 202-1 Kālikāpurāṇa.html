Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||     ||

oṃ namaś caṇḍikāyai ||     ||

yad yogibhir bhavabhayārttivināśayogyam

āsādya vaṃditam atīva viviktacittaiḥ

tad vaḥ punātu haripādasarojayugmam

āvirbhavat kramavilaṃghitabhūr bhuvaḥ svaḥ ||

sā pātu vaḥ sakalayogijanasya citte

[ʼ]vidyātamiśra(!)taraṇir bhuvi muktihetuḥ

yā cānyajaṃtuvi(!)vahasya vimohanīti-

māyāvidher jjagati śuddhasu(!)buddhihaṃtrī || ||

i(!)śvaraṃ jagatām ādyaṃ praṇamya puruṣottamaṃ ||

nityajñānamayaṃ vakṣye purāṇaṃ kālikāhvayaṃ ||

mārkaṃḍeyaṃ muniśreṣṭhaṃ sthitaṃ himadharāṃtike

munayaḥ paripapracchuḥ praṇamya kamaṭhādayaḥ ||

bhagavan samyag ākhyātaṃ sarvaśāstrāṇi tattvataḥ ||

vedān sarvān(!)s tathā śā(!)ṃgān sārabhūtaṃ pramathya ca (fol. 1v1–6)

End

yo loka[[m ī]]śaḥ satataṃ bibharttiḥ

yaḥ pālayaty antakaras tathānte ||

idaṃ samastaṃ hi samudrasaptaṃ

tadīyarūpaṃ ca namo [ʼ]stu tasmai ||

pradhānapuruṣau yasya prapaṃcau yogināṃ hṛdi ||

yaḥ purāṇādhipo viṣṇuḥ prasīdatu sanātanaḥ ||

yo hetur ugraḥ puruṣaḥ purāṇaḥ

sanātanaḥ śāśvata i(!)śvaraḥ paraḥ ||

purāṇakṛd vedapurāṇavedyaḥ

prastaumi taṃ naumi purāṇaśeṣe ||

iti sakalajagad bibhartti yā sā

madhuripumohakarī namo [ʼ]stu tasyai ||

vahati ca vapuṣā maheśvaro yāṃ

praṇamata prāṇihi[[tāṃ śivāṃ tāṃ]] ||     || (fol. 232r8–11)

Colophon

iti kālikāpurāṇa(!) samāptam ||     || śubham || ❁ || śubham astu sarvadāt(!) ||     || śubham || ❁ || (fol. 232r11–12)
