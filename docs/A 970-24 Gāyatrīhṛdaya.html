Beginning

/// hṛtibhī pravartate. vaśiṣṭhaḥ kāś ca vyāhṛtayaḥ kā ca gāyatrī ||

brahmovāca ||

oṃ bhūr oṃ bhuvaḥ oṃ sva oṃ mahaḥ oṃ janaḥ oṃ (2) tapaḥ oṃ satyam iti vyāhṛtayaḥ oṃ tatsaḥvitur (!) vareṇīyaṃ bhargo devasya dhīmahi dhiyo yo na (!) pracodayāt. ity eṣā (3) gāyatrī. vaśiṣṭhaḥ kiṃ vai bhūr. kiṃ bhuvaḥ kiṃ svaḥ kiṃ mahaḥ kiṃ janaḥ kiṃ tapa (!) kiṃ satyam kiṃ tatsavituḥ kiṃ vareṇīyaṃ (4) kiṃ bhargaḥ kiṃ devasya kiṃ dhīmahi kiṃ dhiyaḥ kiṃ yaḥ kiṃ naḥ kiṃ pracodayāt. || (fol. 2r1–4)

End

uttiṣṭha devi gantavyaṃ punarāgamanāya ca.

gāyatrī ca mahādevī praviśya hṛdayaṃ mamaḥ (!) ||

tayādaco pranaḥ yo yo(7)dhi himadhī sya vadergo bhyaṃ ṇīrervatu vitsata āvāyavya yāvāyavyā naurvāyavya yāvā. | harosi pādhmānaṃ

(9v1) surabhir jñānavairāgyaṃ yoniśaṃkho ʼthapaṃkaja. (!) ||

liṃgaṃ nirvāṇamudrāṣṭau japānte ca pradarśayet

(2) me viddhi || asāvādityo brahma ||     || (fol. 9r6–9v2)
