Excerpts

Beginning

|| śrīmadgaṇeśaśrīrgurubhyo namaḥ ||

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || oṃ ||

goviṃdamādhavamukuṃda hare murāre

śaṃbho śiveśaśaśiśekharaśūlapāṇe ||

dāmodarācyutajanārdanavāsudevaṃ

lakṣmīnṛsiṃha mama dehi karāvalaṃbaṃ || 1 ||

gaṃgādharāṃdhakaripo haranīlakaṃṭhaṃ

vaikuṃṭhakaiṭabharipo kamaṭhābjapāṇe ||

bhūteśakhaṃḍaparaśomṛḍacaṃḍikeśaṃ

kedārasāṃbaśivadehi karāvalaṃbaṃ || 2 || (fol. 1r1–4)

End

agastya uvāca ||

yo dharmarājaracitāṃ [[lalitaprabaṃdhāṃ]]

nāmāvalīṃ sakalakalmaṣabījahaṃtrīṃ ||

dhīrotra kaustubhabhṛtaḥ śaśibhūṣaṇasya

nityaṃ japet stanarasaṃ na pibet sa mātuḥ || 13 ||

iti śrutvā kathāṃ ramyāṃ śivaśarmāgaṇair yutaḥ ||

puro gacchan vimānena dadarśāpsarasāṃ purīṃ || 14 || || (fol. 2r2–6)

Colophon
