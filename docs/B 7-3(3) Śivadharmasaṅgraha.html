Manuscript Features

The following information is given in PTL: I, A 1082/ The MTM contains the following texts: 1. Śivadharmaśāstra (exps. 8–53) 2. Śivadharmottara (exps. 55–110) 3. Śivadharmasaṅgraha (exps. 111–162) 4. Umāmaheśvarasaṃvāda (exps. 163–197) 5. Śivopaniṣad (exps.200 –219) 6. Vṛṣasārasaṅgraha (exps. 220–266) 7. Uttarottaramahāsaṃvāda (exps. 267–291) 8. Dharmaputrikā (exps. 292–303) There are two exposures of fols. 48v–49r.

Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṁ namaḥ śivāya ||

namaḥ paramadevāya traiguṇyavijitātmane | sarvatoyogarūpāya saṃsārābhāvahetave ||

sthitisaṃhārasargāṇāṃ kartre hartre prasāriṇe | ṣaḍvi(!)śāya pradhānāya mahādevāya dhīmatā(!) ||

śaṃbho⟨sa⟩ḥ sanatkumārasya vāyor dvaipāyanasya ca | granthasāraṃ samuddhṛtya kriyate dharmasaṃgrahaḥ || (fol. 1v1–2)


End

amadyapaś ca ya śūdro bhavabhakto jitendriyaḥ | ābhūtasaṃplavaṃ sthāyī apratīghātalakṣaṇam ||

gāṇāpatyaṃ labhet sthānaṃ pārthivaṃ sārvakāmikam | madyayo(!) madyapaiḥ sārddhaṃ bhūtasaṃghais tu modate ||

so ‘rcyamāno mahīpṛṣṭhe martyānāṃ varado bhavet | iti hovāca bhagavān vāyur vākyaviśāradaḥ || || (fol. 51r6–51v2)


«Colophon: »

śivadharmasaṅgrahe dvādaśamo dhyāyaḥ || || || śivadharmasaṅgraha[ḥ] samāptaḥ || || || || || || || || (fol. 51v 2–3)
