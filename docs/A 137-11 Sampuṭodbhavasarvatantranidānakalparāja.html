Excerpts

«Beginning»


❖ namo vajrasatvāya ||


evaṃ mayā śrutam ekasmin samaye bhagavān sarvatathāgatakāyavākcittahṛdayavajrayoṣid bhageṣu vijahāra ||

tatra khalu bhagavān aśītikoṭiyogeśvaramadhye vajragarbham avalokṛ(!) smitam akāṣit(!) mamanturasmitesmin(!)

vajragarbha utthāyāsanād ekāṃsam(!) uttarāsagaṃ(!) kṛtvā dakṣiṇaṃ jānumaṇḍalaṃ pṛthivyāṃ pratisthāpya

kṛtāñjanipuṭo(!) bhūtvā bhagavantam etad avocat || (fol. 1v1-3)



«End»


yathā kṣīraṃ sayād iti rūpabhujyamānaṃ viṣāditā(!) jena(!) parinamati(!) | śūnyaiś ca punaḥ

puruṣarūpabhuktamānaṃ āpadyate | evan te kulaputrā rāgādaya āśayaviśeṣa vāvinā viśiṣṭaphalāvāhakā

na vettīti prakṛti niravadyatvāt prayoga yeṣa(!) visuddhi samantā sabhavinas te viśiṣṭaphalāhakā yathā

te ketaky ādayo viśiṣṭamantāna(!) caturaś ca rāgādaya iti svabhāvaśuddhāḥ || || (fol. 93v10-94r2)



«Colophon» iti śrīsaṃpuṭodbhavaṃ sarvatantrarāje sarvvakalpanidānatilaka ekādaśamaḥ || iti saṃpuṭatikto nāma

mahātantrarāja samāptaḥ || samvat 778 jyeṣṭhakṛṣṇa aṣṭamyāyāṃ(!) tithau utrabhadranakṣatre(!) sobhanayoge

ādityavāre siddhaṃ śubham astu sadā〈ḥ〉 || (fol. 94r2-4)
