Excerpts

Beginning

mīnanātho śrīvatsacihno varamīnacihnaḥ |

śrīviśvanātho varamindranātho rudraś ca viśṇuś ca sadā namaste || 12 ||

ye rudraviṣṇoḥ śatatantukāle paṭhanti nityaṃ susamāhitena nas(!)yanti rogaṃ kaluṣādi sarvvaṃ viyogaduḥkhañ ca samasta sā(!)ntiḥ || 23 || iti śrī hariharastava(!) samāptaṃ || || (exp. 2, 1–4)

End

tīrthayātrādigamanaiḥ kin tasya kliśitaṃ vapuḥ |

ye ʼvrayanti(!) harer mūrttiṃ tulaśīdalakomalai(!) || 4 ||

samaṃ jalidalair yuktaṃ keśavāyehamaṇḍale |

kurvvanti pūjanaṃ ye ca te yanti paramāṃ gatiṃ || 7 ||

snāne dāne tathā dhyane prāśane keśavārcane |

tulaśī kīrttanāt taṃ sarvve vāyubhyo śivaloke sa gacchati || (exp. 42, 6–9)

Colophon

iti śivadvādaśanāmastotraṃ samāptaṃ || || (exp. 42, 9)
