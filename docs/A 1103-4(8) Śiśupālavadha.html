Excerpts

Beginning

śrīḥ ||

anugiram ṛtubhir vitāyamānām atha sa vilokayituṃ vanāṃtalakṣmīṃ
niragamamad abhirāddhum ādṛtānāṃ bhavati mahatsu na niṣphalaḥ prayāsaḥ 1
dadha///vanāni vahvir yuvatiyutā yadavaḥ prayātim īṣuḥ
manasuśayamahāstram anyathāmī na kusumapaṃcakam apy alaṃ viśoḍhum 2 (fol. 1r1-2)

End

dadhty urojadvayam urvaśītalaṃ bhuvo gateva svayam urvaśītalaṃ
babhau mukhenāpratimena kācana śriyādhikāyāṃ prati menakācana 86

itthaṃ nārīr ghaṭayitum alaṃ kāmibhiḥ kāmam āsan
prāleyāṃsoḥ sapadi rucayaḥ śāṃtamānāṃtarāyāḥ
ācāryatvaṃ ratiṣu vilasaṃn(!)manmathaśrīvilāsāḥ
hrīpratyūhapraśamakuśalāḥ sīdhavaś cakrur āsāṃ 87 || (fol. 10v6-8)

Colophon

iti śrīśiśupālavadhe mahākāvye śrīśabdāṃke candrodayavarṇanaṃ nāma navamasargoḥ(!) śubham (fol. 10v8-9)
