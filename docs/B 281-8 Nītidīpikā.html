Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

praṇamya śaṃkaraṃ devaṃ śāṃkarīṃ śaṃkarātmajaṃ ||

ajñānadhvāntanāśāya krīyate nītidīpikāṃ ||

nānā granthāt samālokya dhīraisaha vicāryya ca ||

kṛta yaṃ sāram udhṛtya, sarvvaloka hitaiṣiṇī ||

caturāśīti lakṣeṣu śarīreṣu śarīriṇāṃ ||

na mānuṣyaṃ vinā netraṃ, na svajñānaṃ tu labhyate ||

atra janmasahasrāṇāṃ, sahasrair api niścitaṃ ||

kadācit labhate jantu mānuṣyaṃ puṇya saṃcayāt || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

nāsti jñānasamaṃ tīrthaṃ nāsti jñānasamaṃ tapaḥ ||

nāsti jñānasamo yajña stasmājjñānaṃ samabhyaset ||

deva bhāskara mallpasmin dyājānī(!)svaratāṃ gate |

nītiśāstreṇa nepālaṃ, samun(!) pālitu mudhyate ||

śuviyugma gaje saṃkhye, gate nepāla sammate ||

divyarātrau paraṃdhīrā saṃmpūrṇṇā nīti dīpikā || 120 || (fol. 54v7–55r3)

Colophon

|| || iti nītidīpikāyāṃ ṣaṣṭhamo(!)dhyāyaḥ || || śubham astu sarvvapākānnaṃ bhavatu || sasvat (!) 869 śrāvaṇakṣṇa, pratipadā, śravaṇanakṣetra (!) āyuṣyamānayoga, budhavāra thva kunhu ṣāyadhunakāmiti || lekhata śyāmānaṃda || || śubha ||

(fol. 55r4–6)
