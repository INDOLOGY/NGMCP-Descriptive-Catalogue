Manuscript Features

This manuscript has two texts: Annapurṇastotra(fols. 1v–3r) a devotional story about Māgha kṛṣṇa caturthī, a sacred day for Gaṇeśa.

Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||
nityānaṃdakari parābhayakati sauṃdaryyaratnākarī
nirdhutāṣilaghorapāvanakarī pratyalāmāheśvarī
prāpte yācalavaṃsapāvanakarī sāsīpurādhisvarī
bhikṣāṃ dehi kṛpāvilaṃvanakarī mātān na pūrṇeśvarī 1 (fol. 1v1–5)

End

śrīkṛṣṇa uvāca
tasmāt pārtha tvaā kāryaṃ vratam uttamaṃ
etat prabhābvato nūnaṃ labdhakāmo bhaviṣyati 29
rājyaṃprāpta tayā mitra pautrapotresukhāvaham
karoti vratam etac ca sarvasiddhim avāpnuyāt 40 (fol. 7v5–9)

Colophon

iti śrīpadmapurāṇe pārvatīgaṇeśasaṃvāde māghakṛṣṇapakṣecaturthīvrata saṃpṇaṃ ||
saṃvat 1886 śrāvaṇakṛṣṇapakṣe 11 ravivāsare śubhaṃm astu kalyāṇam astu || 6 || śrī || 6 || (fol. )
