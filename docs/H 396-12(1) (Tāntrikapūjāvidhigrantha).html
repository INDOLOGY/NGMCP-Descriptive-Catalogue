Excerpts

Beginning

|| samudramekhale devī (!) parvvatastanumaṇḍale ||
viṣṇupatnī (!) namas tubhyaṃ pādasparśa (!) kṣamasva me ||
bhūmau praṇamya || tataḥ svāsānusāreṇa, yādaṃ nidhāya ||
kavacādistavaṃ paṭhet || iti prāta(!)kṛtyavidhiḥ saṃpūrṇaṃ (!) ||    || (x43a:2–4)

End

|| dhyānaṃ || utpattisthitisaṃhṛtir ghaṭayituṃ dhatya (!) trirūpitaḥ (!) |
niḥstraiguṇyāṃ jagataḥ yadi ūdikirītibrahmācyutaḥ mūladhṛk(!) |
tām ādyā (!) prakṛti (!) smarāmi manasā sarvvārthasiddhipra..
praṇamet || kṣamasva || namaskāra || (x42:7–x43a:1)

Colophon

|| iti śrīūrdhvāmnāya, śrīsadāśiva, śrīrāja, śrīparameśvara, ājñācakravidhiḥ saṃpūrṇaṃ (!) ||    || ❁ ||    ||    || ❁ ||    || ❁ || ❁ ||    || (X43a:1–2)
