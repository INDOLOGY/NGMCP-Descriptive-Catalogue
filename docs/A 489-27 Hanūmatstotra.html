Excerpts

Beginning

|| śrīmate rāmānujāya namaḥ ||

śrīmad hanumate namaḥ oṃ asya śrīāpadduddhāravibhīṣaṇaproktahanumatstotra­maṃtrasya vibhīṣaṇa ṛṣir anuṣṭupchaṃdaḥ | hanūmān devatā | hrīṃ bījaṃ || svāhā śaktiḥ || klīṃ kīlakaṃ | mama āsannasarvopadravanivṛttidvārā sarvābhīṣṭārthe jape viniyogaḥ || (fol. 1v1–6)

End

japtvā stotram imaṃ maṃtraṃ vasuvāraṃ paṭhen naraḥ

rājadvāre sabhāsthāne prāptavādaṃ jayed dhruvaṃ || 13 ||

vibhīṣaṇakṛtaṃ stotraṃ yaḥ paṭhet satataṃ naraḥ |

sarvāpadbhyo vimucyeta nātra kāryā vicāraṇā || 14 || (fol. 3v1–4)

Colophon

iti vibhīṣaṇaproktaṃ hanumatstotraṃ saṃpūrṇaṃ || || ❖ || ❖ || saṃvat 1848 || paṭhanārthaṃ likhyanārthaṃ śubham astu || || || paṭhanārthaṃ khūbarāma misra || śubham astu || (fol. 3v4–7)
