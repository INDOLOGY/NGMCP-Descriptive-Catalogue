«Beginning»

śrīgaṇesāya namaḥ ||

bhagavati bhagavatpadapaṃkajaṃ bhramarabhūtaṣurāśurasevitaṃ ||

sujanamāsahaṃsdapariskṛtaṃ kamalayāmalayānibhṛte bhajet || 1 ||

tāṃ vubhāvapi vadehaṃ vighneśakuladevate

naranāgānanaste ko narasiṃhānanopara || 2 || (fol. 1v1–4)


«End»

raṃ duramyo vahavigamyor marubaṃdhuhṛdyataḥ sādhupadyas tata || śrīpatainunākahi satsununāṃ vikhamātu pade pādyapuṣpāṃjali te || 32 || (fol. 8r6–v2)


«Colophon»

iti śrīmatbhagavatīpūjyapādāraviṃdamakaraṃda sāiṣṇudhā mullasan madhukaramaṃjurarapūravāribhaṭ śuriśrīpatitanujarāmakṛṣṇakavikṛpato bhagavatipādyapuṣpāṃjali te || 33 || prācinācāryavaryāmadudayanācāryasiddhāṃ tasiddhamaṃgalaṃ || phalagocaratāmalayāṃsakaramaṇīyamukumudiramapāramyastaradharmā samadharmam adadhāt || 34 || iti śrībhagavati pādyapuṣpāṃjali || bhavāni sakarābhyāṃ namaḥ śrībhavāṇīprītaye namaḥ || śubham astu || śrīr astu || || || || || (fol. 8v2–9r4)
