Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ supi vibhaktyarthao nirūpyate tatra prathamādvitīyātṛtīyācaturthīpañcamī ṣaṣṭhīsaptamītyā(!) vyākhyāvibhaktyasya(!) saḥ tatra prathamāyā śaktigrāhakam sūtram prātipadikārthe prathameti yasmin prātipadike uccarite yasyārthasya niyamenopasthitiḥ sa tatprātipadikārthaḥ | tena śakyalakṣyagauṇānāṃ sarvveṣāṃ saṃgrahaḥ kṛṣṇaḥ sundara ityādau prakṛtyarthānuvādikā vibhaktiḥ | (fol. 1v1–3)
