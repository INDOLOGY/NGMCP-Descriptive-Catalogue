Beginning

-pamāna saṃkuddha (!) rudraniśvāsasaṃbhavaḥ (!) ||

jvaroṣṭadhā pṛthak dvadva (!) saghātāgaṃtujaḥ ||

dakṣaprajāpatisyaṃ īśvaratvaṃ yajña sāmā vo(2)sye ○ apamāna yāna

krodharapā va iśvarasya svāsaṃ tayā va śvasa svāsa(laya) layo jvara, thva jvara, vāta, pita, (!)śleṣma vāta(3)pitta vādaśleṣma (!) sannipāta āgaṃtu vā ||

mithyāhāravihārasya doṣāhy āmāśayāśrayā ||

bahin nirasya koṣyagniḥ (4) jvaradāḥ syu rasānugā || (fol. 2r1–4)

Sub-colophon

iti nidānoktaparikrame śrīmādhavavira(3)cite rugviniścayaḥ samāptaḥ || || (fol. 311r2–3)

End

ity annapānavidhiḥ || ||

royayāye yā dhātu julasanoṃ hnasa hnuto śitala palicā(1)laparaṇa ○ mevatācārasateṃ va || || ity annapānavidhiḥ || || thvate pracāravidhi dravyaguṇasa nirṇaya yā(2)hnā kha || || (fol. 315r7–315v2)
