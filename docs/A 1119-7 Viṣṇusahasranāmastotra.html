Many folios have been microfilmed twice in this reel number. The order of folios is as follows: 1v, 1v, 4r, 4v–8r, 8v–9r, 8v–9r, 8v–9r, 1r–1v, 4r, 4v–7r, 7v–8r, 7v–8r, 8v–35v, 37r–42v.
