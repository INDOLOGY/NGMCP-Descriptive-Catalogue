Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

nyāyaḥ paṃcānanaḥ śrīmān jayarāmaḥ samāsataḥ ||

samāsatattvam ācaṣṭe vibbudhunāṃ(!) sudhopamaḥ(!) || 1 ||

tatra samāsamātva(!)vibhaktiśūnyapūrvapadakanāmasaṃmudāutvaṃ(!) dadhi mabhuram(!) astītyādau bhāvanānvaye prathamāṃtapadajanyopasthiteḥ prayojakatvena luptavibhaktaḥ(!) satvād adoṣaḥ nīlotpalam ityādisamāsebhu(!) vibhaktir yathā na hi rājapuruṣa ityādau luptaṣaṣṭhyādismṛtyā śākṣavosaḥ(!) (fol. 1v1–4)

vibhaktir evā(!) pustvasya(!) lakṣaṇayā strītvasyāpi bodhikā brāhmaṇapadam eva vā śalyā (!) brāhaṇatvena lakṣaṇayā brāhmaṇītvena bodhakaṃ | evaṃ hasāv(!) ityādāv api | śivā(!) namaskuryād ityādau śivapadaṃ lakṣaṇayā śivadurgānyataratvena kiṃ vā śktyā śivatvena lakṣaṇayā durgātvena bodhakāṃ(!) || evam anyatrāpy asvam(!) iti || || (fol. 13v8–14r1)
