Excerpts

«Beginning of the root text:»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

jayati raghuvaṃśatilakaḥ

kauśalyāhṛdayanaṃdano rāmaḥ

(3) daśavadananidhanakārī-

dāśarathiḥ puṃḍarīkākṣaḥ 1

pārvatyuvāca

atha rāmaḥ (4) kim akarot kauśalyānaṃdavarddhanaḥ

hatvā mṛdhe rāvaṇādīn rākṣasān bhīmavikra(5)maḥ 2 (fol. 1v2–5)

«Beginning of the commentary:»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

śrīgaṇapataye namaḥ

1 mṛdhe yuddhe 2 3 kathaṃ kena prakāreṇa 4 śraddhadhanyāḥ (8) etacchravaṇe śraddhāvatyāḥ kathāmṛtam āsvādya me ma (!) tad āsvādanaviṣayā tṛṣṇā tyaṃ (!) baṃrdharte (!) 5 (fol. 1v1, 8)

«End of the root text:»

bhaktyā paṭhed yaḥ śṛṇu(4)yāt sa pāpair

vimucyate janmagatodbhavaiś ca

adhyātmarāmaṃ paṭhataś ca nit(5)yaṃ

śrotuś ca bhaktyā likhataś ca rāmaḥ (!)

atiprasannaś ca sadāsamīpe

sītā(6)sametaḥ śriyam ātanoti 72 (fol. 104v3–6)

«End of the commentary:»

bhagavaṃtaṃ sarva(7)gataṃ rāmaṃ śrāvayāmīti buddhyā yo vācayati taṃ tatsarvagatvāt śrutvā vācakasyoparitoṣam eti (8) tat paritoṣena ca tasya sarvārthasiddhir iti bhāvaḥ anaṃtapuṇyaṃ vācakaśrotor anaṃtapuṇyada-(fol. 104v1,7–8)

«Sub-colophon of the root text:»

iti śrīmadadhyātmarāmā(7)yaṇe umāmaheśvarasaṃvāde uttarakāṃḍe aṣṭamaḥ sargaḥ 8 (fol. 94v6–7)

«Sub-colophon of the commentary:»

iti śrīmatsakalarājavipad uddharaṇasamarthetyādivirudāvalīvirājamānasya himmativa(9)rmaṇaḥ putrasya śrīrāmavarmaṇa (!) kṛtāvadhyātmarāmāyaṇe setā uttarakāṃḍe (!) aṣṭamaḥ sargaḥ 8 (fol. 94v8–9)
