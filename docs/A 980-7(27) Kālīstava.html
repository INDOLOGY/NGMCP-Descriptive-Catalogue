Excerpts

Beginning

sukhasthitaṃ jagannāthaṃ kailāśe girijaikadā ||

āliṃgy āha kathaṃ vāsaṃ datvārddhāṃge tha vaṃcase || 1 ||

majjatiṃ kāntikāṃ prāhur brahmādyās tattvadarśinaḥ ||

jñātāsannāṃ kathaṃ kasmai kena vādya prasīdati || 2 ||

śrutveti kavacanaṃ devyā vismitotphullalocanaṃ ||

uvāca prahasan devas tanmano naṃdayann iva || 3 || (fol. 93r6–93v3, exp. 94b–95t)

End

mohanoccāṭanādīni yāni karmāṇī cechati (!) ||

tāni siddhyaṃti manasā japan maṃtraṃ stavaṃ sadā || 48 ||

parameśvarasaudaryāparamārcāparāyaṇaḥ ||

parameśṭhīpadaṃ prāpya parameśitvam ṛchati (!) || 49 ||

vipulapulakayukta evam aṃtrī yadi bhavati sadā maharddhisiddhiṃ ||

sa bhavati naradeva ekavaṃdyaḥ sakalasukham avāpya mokṣam eti || 50 || (fol. 97v4–98r1, exp. 99t–b)
