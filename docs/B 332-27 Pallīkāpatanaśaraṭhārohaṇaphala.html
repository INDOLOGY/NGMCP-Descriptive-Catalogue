Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

atha pallīpatanaśarathārohaṇayoḥ pahalam ||

pulastyovā(2)ca

athāṃtaḥ (!) saṃpravakṣāmi (!) śṛṇu śaunaka yatnataḥ ||
pallyā prapatanaṃ caiva śaraṭasya praro(3)haṇam || 1 ||

pallī ca prathame yāme mastake sukhadāyinī ||
mukhe miṣṭānnabhojī (4) syāt pādābhyāṃ maraṇaṃ dhruvam || 2 ||

madhyāhne patite pallī dakṣiṇe sukhasaṃpadā ||
vā(5)māṅge kurute vyādhiṃ brahmadvāre tu mṛtyudā || 3 ||

pallī tṛtīyayāme ca pṛṣṭhavaṃśe ca (6) rogadā ||
karotu kurute vyādhiṃ bamhadvāre tu vastradā || 4 || (fol. 1r1–6)

End

homadānaṃ vastradānaṃ tiladānaṃ svaśaktitaḥ ||
taddoṣa śamanārthāya kuryyād brāhmaṇabhojanam || 39 ||

(2)itthaṃ samyagvidhānena śāntikarma samācaret ||
tamāyuvijayo (!) nityaṃ kīrtivṛ(3)ddhiś ca jāyate || 40 ||

pallī śaraṭayoś caiva vidhānaṃ śobhanaṃ bhavet ||
kīrttane (4) ca phalo yena sarvāriṣṭanivāraṇam || 41 ||

sarvaśatrukṣayaṃkaraṃ proktaṃ garge(5)ṇa dhīmatām (!) || ❁ (fol. 3v8–4r5)

Colophon

iti śrīgargokta pallīpatanaśaraṭātohaṇaṃ samāptam ||
(6) likhitam idam utprabhātīpayonāmaka (!) durgādattena likhitam ||
idaṃ pustam | (7) samvat || 1900 || śāke | 17 | 65 | pauṣamāse | śuklapakṣe || 8 || myāṃ samāptam idaṃ (fol. 4r5–7)
