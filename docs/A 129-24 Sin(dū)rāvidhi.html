«Beginning»

❖ oṁ namaḥ śrīvajrasatvāya || ||

siṃdhūracāyā kaṃtha vidhi ||

nityayā thyaṁ hni validhūna kāva || majā tathyaṁ thāpanā dhūnakāva || paścimābhimukhaṃ mūlamaṇḍala vanāpaṃta yaṁ du chatha satayaṁ du || siṃdhūramaṇḍala ṣadvirani maṇḍala coya dhūna kāva || hyā u rājala hākurā jala siṃdhūrasāra opa raktasūtra nīlasūtrahyā u aduo || paṃcaśāli mūlamaṇḍala saṁtaya || (exp. 3t1-5)


«End»

māsāhutiyā vidhi thyaṁ dhūnakāva || mahāmaṇḍala sakasyanaṁ svacākala ulāo sakalaṁ pheka tunā o kalapīvaṁ sophāla dhūna kāva satākṣara vonāo maṇḍala lopanayāya || vajrarakṣā || kiviya || maṇḍala rasa joya || maṇḍalamadhye kilaṁ devatayāva dhamūtha kālinaṁ argha yoke abhiseṣa viya || sakasyātaṁ gvapala ohnāya || sālādevakṣe māpana yāya || || sālā devalitaya || || jābhūla || śubhaṃ || (exp. 24t1-24b1)
