Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||


oṁ namo dakṣiṇakālikāyai ||


kalānidhikalollāsimaulīṁ smeramukhāmbujām

kālindīlolakallokadomalāṃśyāmalāṃ bhaje ||


praṇamya dakṣiṇāpadāraviṃdam ādarāt ||

karoti ko [ʼ]pi pūjakaprayogasaṃgrahaṃ budhaḥ ||


prāta[ḥ] kṛtyam akṛtvā tu yo devīṃ bhaktito [ʼ]rcayet ||

viphalā tasya pūjā tu śaucahīnā yathākṛpā || (fol. 1v1–5)


End

bījaprasādam uccārya mahādevāya tatparam ||

kridhīśṃ bahnim ārūḍhaṃ vāmanetraṃ sabiṃdukam ||


kālikāyai padaṃ paścāt prāsādabījam uccaret ||

mahādeva mahākālaḥ sarvasiddhipradāyakaḥ ||


oṁ hrīṁ kṣmīṁ hū mahākālāya hauṁḥ(!) mahādevāya krīṁ kālikāyai hauṁḥ iti mahākālamaṃtraḥ

mahākālaṃ saṃpūjya bhairavapātrāmṛtena [[pūrvoktamaṃtreṇa]] triḥ samtarpya || mūlam uccārya

sāṃge sā (exp. 57b3–7)
