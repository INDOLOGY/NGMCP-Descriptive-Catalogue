Excerpts

Beginning

oṁ namaḥ śrīgaṇeśāya || ||

śrīvīranātha uvāca ||


devadevamahāde[[vaṃ]] devaṃ tribhuvaneśvaraṃ ||

praṇamya śirasā devī pṛchate kālikā haraṃ ||


oṁ namo guhyakālikāyai || ||

athāto ʼtharvaṇasaṃhitāyāṃ ||

śrīdevy āha ||

oṁ marmābhighātinyai namo namaḥ || ||


svāmyaṃkasaṃsthitā devī dvaitādvaitavivarjitā ||

pṛchyate(!) kālikā devī bhairavaṃ bhuvaneśvarī || (fol. 1v1–3)


End

sarvadharmaṃ parityajya kuladharmaṃ samācaret ||

kuladharmaṃ parityajya rairavaṃ narakaṃ vrajet ||

uttamā nityapūjā syān madhyamaṃ parvapūjanaṃ ||

māsapūjādhamā proktā mā (fol. 155v8–9)


Sub-colophon

iti śrīhāhārāvataṃtre atharvaṇasaṃhitāyāṃ mahākarmārccanapaddhatau nānādevatādhyānakathanapaṭalaḥ || || (fol. 76v7–8)
