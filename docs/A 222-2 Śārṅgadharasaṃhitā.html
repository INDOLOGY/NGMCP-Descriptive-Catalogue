Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāye (!) namaḥ ||

śrīyaṃ(!) sa ṭhadyād bhavatāṃ purārir

yadaṅgatejaḥ prasare bhavānī ||

virājate nirmalacaṃdrikāyāṃ

mahauṣadhīvajjvalitā himādrau || 1 ||

prasiddhayogā munibhiḥ prayuktāś

cikitsakair ye bahuśonubhūtāḥ

vidhīya śārṅgadhareṇa teṣāṃ

susaṃgrahaḥ sajjanarajanāya || 2 || (!)

hetvādirūpād yuti sātmyajāti

bhedaiḥ saminnyātura sarvvarogān ||

cikitsitaṃ kurṣaṇa bṛṃhaṇākhyaṃ

kurvvīta vaidyo vidhivat sa jogaiḥ || 3 (fol. 1v1–4)

End

kṛṣṇāṃjanaṃtayostulyaṃ sarvam ekatra cūrṇayet

dasamāsena kapura tasmiṃ cūrṇe pratāpayet

etat pratyajanaṃ netra gachanājanāmṛgabhāvanayāmṛtaṃ (!) patraṃ janam || (!) (fol. 138r6–7)

Colophon

iti śrīśārṅgadhareṇa viracitāyāṃ cikitsāsthāne /// rasaṃ cūrṇaṃ saṃpūrṇa (śubham astu) ||

vikrama karasaradharāvāṇayuktaṃ pauṣa śuklāṣṭamī budhe leṣanīyam kṛpālanāmaṃ chatri upādhyākaisya pūtraṃ prathamam samaye gatavaraṣa vedi vasu || || ||(fol.138r7-10)
