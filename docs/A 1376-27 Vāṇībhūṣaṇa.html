Manuscript Features

The available fols. are 1–30 and 33.

The exposures are misplaced. So the exposures of first and last card-catalogues are in exposure numbers 21 and 22 and fols. 1–11 are in exposure number 23–34. Fols. 12–30 and 33 are in exposure number 1–20.

Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīgaṇeśāya ||

rājat kuṃjarakṛttirājavimalo daṃcaddūkūlāvṛti

bhrājadbhogiphaṇāmaṇi pravilasan muktāmaṇi bhrāmati |

phullāṃ bhrājabhujaṃgaphūtkṛtiraṇan maṃjīramaṃjūdyutiḥ

śreyo vaḥ śivaśailarājasutayor deyād abhinnaṃ vapuḥ ||

santīha yadyapi mahākavisaṃpraṇīta-

graṃthās tathāpi mama vāṇi/// tāvilāḥ ||

saṃpūrṇṇaṃ śāradavidhāu udayaṃ prapanne

kin nāma nābhyudayamaṃcati tārakāpi || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

nandālaye nivasanakṛtavanavilāsanavihitavividhanasarbhasapanaṃ saṃpītavasanavaratarūṇakaracaraṇamanasarasarasi janayanadharaṃ || śāsūnaṃ || 29 || || mātrāvṛttam iha proktaṃ catvāriṃśattrayodhikaṃ | trayodaśādhikaśataṃ varṇṇavṛttam ihoditaṃ || || (fol. 33v3–5)

Colophon

iti śrīdāmodaraviracitaṃ vāṇībhūṣaṇe varṇṇavṛttakathanaṃ nāma dvitīyaḥ paricchedaḥ || || samāptoyaṃ vāṇībhūṣaṇābhidhānaś cchando/// (fol. 33v5)
