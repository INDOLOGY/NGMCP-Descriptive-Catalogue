Manuscript Features

śrīpañcākṣarībhāṣyatātparyya

…

namaḥ śivābhyām śivāpahābhyām

śeṣakalyāṇa (!)viśeṣitābhyāṃ

asaṃbhavābhyāṃ śrutisaṃbhavābhyāṃ

namo namaḥ śaṃkarapārvatībhyām 4

namaḥ śivābhyāṃ navayauvanābhyāṃ

parasparāśliṣṭavapur dharābhyāṃ

nagedrakanyāvṛṣaketanābhyāṃ

namo namaḥ śaṃkarapārvatībhyāṃ 5 

virodhivacaso mūkān vāgīśān api kurvata

jaḍān apy anulomārthān pravācaḥ kṛtināṅgiraḥ 

idaṃ likhītam śālagrāmeṇa goḍadese || śloka ❁ 750 || = ||  (fol. 31r5–8)

Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

namo rudrāya devāya svatantrāyākhilātmane |

pradhānapuruṣeśāya jagatsargādihetave 1

yā vi(2)bhāti sadāsānkṣisvarūpeṇa svabhāvataḥ |

tān namāmyādareṇaiva śivām āṃnaṃdadāyinīm 2

viśvanāthapadāmbho(3)janakhacaṃdramarīcayaḥ

sañcariṣṇos tam asyandhe mama san tv avalambanam 3 (fol. 1v1–3)

End

tathā ih tavad akṣadaśakaṃ manasā saha buddhitattvaṃ atha vāyugaṇaḥ iti liṅgametad amu(9)nā puruṣaḥ khalu saṃgato bhavati jīva iti tathā purāṣṭakena rahitasya kvacida⟨ḥ⟩sti saṃsaraṇaduḥkhayujāna purāṣṭakena sahi(1)tasya tathā kvacid asti duḥkhavigamaś ca tavetyādi tathānyatrāpi jīvo bhavet karaṇapṛthagavaśīkṛtya cid iti (fol. 30v8–31r1)

Colophon

iti śrīpādapadma(2)bhagavatpūjyapādācāryakṛta mantrarājadivyamahāmantra śrīpañcākṣarībhāṣyatātparyasaṃgrahaḥ saṃpūrṇam (fol. 31r1–2)

idaṃ likhītam śālagrāmeṇa goḍadese || śloka ❁ 750 || = || (fol. 31r8)
