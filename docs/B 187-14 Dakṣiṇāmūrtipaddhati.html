«Beginning»


śīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

brāhme muhūrte cotthāya guruṃ sādhakasattamaḥ ||

dhyāyet śirasi yogīṃdraṃ prasannaṃ svaṣṭasiddhaye || 1 ||

īṣadunmiṣadālokadhvastānāditamottaraṃ ||

nityānaṃdātmakaṃ vaṃde dakṣiṇāmu(!)rttim avyayaṃ || 2 ||

sādhako brāhme muhūrtte cotthāya mānasaṃ snānaṃ kuryāt || yathā ||

namaḥ śivāyai gaṃgāyai śivāyai ca namo namaḥ ||

namaḥ tripathagāma(!)nyai viśvamūrtyai namo namaḥ ||

iḍā bhagavati(!) gaṃgā piṃgalā yamunā nadī ||

tayor madhye gatā nāḍī suṣumṇākhyā sarasvatī || 2 ||

jñānahrade dhyānajale rāgadveṣamalāpahe ||

yaḥ snāti mānase tīrthe sa yāti paramā(!) gatiṃ || 3 || (fol 1r1–5)


«End»


gaccha gaccha paraṃ sthāna(!) svasthānaṃ parameśvara |

yatra brahmādayo devā na viduḥ paramaṃ padam ||

iti paṭhitvā puṣpādi āghrāya svaśvāsānusāreṇa śivaṃ svahṛdy ānīya yathā sukhaṃ viharet || (fol. 15r9–11)


«Colophon»

iti caturbhujācāryaviracitā śrīmedhadakṣiṇāmūrttipaddhatiḥ samāptā || || śrī(!) || || śubham astu || || hāṃ ātmatatvāya svāhā || hrīṃ vidyātatvāya svāhā | hūṃ śivatatvāya svāhā || ||

śrīḥ || śrīḥ || ❁ || śrīḥ || ❁ || smṛtaṃ yaduktaṃ yatkṛtaṃ tatsarvaṃ brahmārpaṇaṃ bhavatu svāhā ❁ (fol. 15r11–14)
