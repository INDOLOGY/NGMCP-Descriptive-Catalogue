Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

sānaṃdaṃ praṇipatya siddhisadanaṃ lambodaraṃ bhāratīṃ

sūryādigrahamaṃḍalaṃ nijaguruṃ bhaktyā hṛda(2)bje sthitaṃ [[||]]

yeṣām aṃghrisaroruhasmaraṇato nānāvidhāḥ siddhayaḥ

siddhiṃ yāṃti laghuprayāṃti vilayaṃ pratyūhaśailavrajāḥ (3)1

yat pūrvaṃ paramaṃ śukāsyagalitaṃ sajjātakaṃ phakkikā-

rūpaṃ gūḍhatamaṃ tam eva viṣadaṃ kurve gaṇeśosmy ahaṃ ○

dai(4)vajñaḥ sutarāṃ yaśaḥ sukhamatiḥ śrīharṣadaṃ sragdharā-

vṛtais cāru nṛṇāṃ śubhāyanapadaṃ śrīmac chivānujñayā (5) 2 (fol. 1v1–5)

End

ye paṭhisyaṃti daivajñās teṣā(5)m āyuḥ sukhaṃ śivaṃ ||

bhūyāt kairavkuṃdābhā sukīrtiḥ sarvato diśaṃ || 4 ||

hṛdyaiḥ padyair guṃphite sūritoṣe-

laṃkārākhye jātake (6) maṃjulesmiṃ (!) |

vaṃśādhyāyaḥ saṃpraṇīta[[ḥ] suvṛttair

vedair yuktaḥ śrīgaṇeśena varyyaḥ | 5 | (fol. 15v4–6)

Colophon

iti śrījātakālaṃkāre vaṃśādhyāyaḥ saptamaḥ ||

vasuyuge gaja indra ca vatsare [1848]

iṣa siteṣṭatithau budhavāse (!)

likhitamadhīrakomalasuṃdaraṃ (!)

ramati me mana (!) pustakajātakaṃ

hastākṣaraṃ gaṃga hastākṣara ojhopanāma gaṃgāviṣṇu kartrikaḥ || śavata (!) 1851 (fol. 15v5–7)
