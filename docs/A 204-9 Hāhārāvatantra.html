Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṁ namaḥ śrīgaṇeśāya || ||

Śrīvīranāthau(!)vāca ||

kailāśaśikharāśīnaṃ, devaṃ tribhuvaneśvaraṃ ||

praṇamya śirasā devī, pṛcchate kālikā haraṃ || ||

oṁ guhyakālikāyai || ||

athāto atharvaṇasaṃhitāyāṃ ||

śrīdevy āha ||

oṁ marmmābhighātani(!) namo namaḥ || ||

sāmarasahito devi, dvaitādvaitavivarjjitaḥ ||

pṛcchate kālikā devī, bhairavaṃ bhūvaneśvaraṃ || (fol. 1v1–2)


End

Guhyakāli(!) maṅgarī(!) ca, siddhikālī jayeśvarī ||

carccikā tripureśi(!) ca, kālaṃ ca bhuvaneśvarī ||

śrīmaṅgalā mahākāli(!), nirvvāṇapadadāyinī |

rājyadā dhanadā nityaṃ, ⟨ja⟩[ya]śa(!)vaktraṃ parām bhajet || ||(exp. 50b ll. 8–9)


Colophon

iti śrīhāhārāvatantre ṣaṭtriṃśa[t]sāhasre mahārthava⟨na⟩[ṇa]saṃhitāyāṃ śivapārvvati(!)samarahasyaśrīguhyakālidevyā sahasranāmapaṭalaḥ || || || || (exp. 50b ll. 9–10)
