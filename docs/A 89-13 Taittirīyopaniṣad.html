Title Taittirīyopaniṣad,Taittirīyopaniṣadbhāṣya, Taittirīyakaṭippaṇa

Remarks The basic text is the Taittirīyopaniṣad on which Śaṅkarācārya made the Bhāṣya named Taittirīyopaniṣadbhāṣya and on this Taittirīyopaniṣadbhāṣya again Ānandajñāna made Ṭippaṇa named Taittirīyakabhāṣyaṭippaṇa.

|| || Taittirīyabhāṣyaṭīkayaḥ (!) prāraṃbhaḥ || || has been written by the same scribe on the middle of the fol.1r and consequentially || || Taittirīyabhāṣyaṭīkayoḥ samāpti (!) || || on the fol. 66v.

Excerpts

«Beginning of theTaittirīyopaniṣd:»

(6r6) oṃ śan no mitraḥ śaṃ varuṇaḥ śan no bhavatv aryamā śan na iṃdro bṛhaspatiḥ || śan no viṣṇur urukramaḥ || namo brahmaṇe namas te vāyo |||

(6v6) tvam eva pratyakṣaṃ brahmāsi tvām eva pratyakṣaṃ brahma vadiṣyāmi | satyaṃ vadiṣyādi (!) | tan mām avatu | tad vaktāram avatu māṃ | ava(7)tu vakāraṃ | oṃ śāṃtiḥ śāṃtiḥ śāṃtiḥ prathamo nuvāka (!) || (fol. 6r6–6v7)

«Beginning of theTaittirīyopaniṣdbhāṣya:»

oṃ namo bhagavate śrīvāsudevāya sarvadhīsākṣiṇe mahyam eva namaḥ ||

yasmāj jātaṃ jagat sarvaṃ yasminn eva ca līyate ||

yenedaṃ dhāryate caiva tasmai (6) jñānātmane namaḥ || 1 ||

yair ime gurubhiḥ pūrvaṃ padavākyapramāṇataḥ ||

vyākhyātāḥ sarvavedāṃtās tān nityaṃ praṇato smy ahaṃ || 2 ||

(7) taittirīyakasārasya mayācāryaprasādataḥ ||

vispaṣṭārtharucīnāṃ hi vyākhyeyaṃ saṃpraṇīyate || 3 || (fol. 1v5–7)

«Beginning of the Taittirīyopaniṣdbhāṣyaṭippaṇa:»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

yat prakāśasukhābhinnaṃ yan maṃtreṇa prakāśitaṃ ||

vivṛtaṃ brāhmaṇe tat syām adṛśyaṃ brahma nirbhayaṃ || 1 ||

yajurvedasākhābhedataittirī(2)yakopaniṣadaṃ vyācikhyāsur bhagavān bhāṣyakāras tatpratipādyaṃ brahma jagajjanmādikāraṇatvena taṭasthalakṣaṇena maṃdamatī (!) atisāmānyenopalakṣitaṃ | satyajñā(3)nāditvā (!) ca svarūpalakṣaṇena viśeṣato viniścitaṃ namaskārachalena saṃkṣepato darśayati yasmāj jātam iti nimittopādānatvayoḥ paṃcamyāḥ sādharaṇād ubhayavi(4)dham api kāraṇatvam iha vivakṣitaṃ || (fol. 1v1–4)

«End of the Taittirīyopaniṣd:»

(65v7) aham annam annam adaṃtamā || 3 || dmi || ||

(66r6) ahaṃ viśvaṃ bhuvanam abhyabhavāṃ || suvar na jyotiḥ ya evaṃ veda ity upaniṣat || 10 || saha nāv avatu saha nau bhunaktu saha (7) vīryaṃ karavāvahai || tejasvi nāv adhītam astu mā vidviṣāvahai || || oṃ śāṃtiḥ śāṃtiḥ śāṃtiḥ hariḥ om || || 

(fol. 65v7–66r7)

«End of theTaittirīyopaniṣdbhāṣya:»

pareṇeśvareṇa śvarūpeṇa suvar na (8) jyotīḥ suvar na suvar ādityaḥ nakāra upamārthe āditya iva sakṛd vibhānam asmadīyaṃ jyotir jyotiḥ prakāśa ity arthaḥ ||

iti vallīdvayavihi(9)to (!) upaniṣatparātmajñānaṃ (!) tām etāṃ yathoktāṃ upaniṣadaṃ śāṃto dāṃta uparatas titikṣuḥ samāhito bhūtvā bhṛguvat tapo mahad āsthāya ya evaṃ veda (10) tasyedaṃ phalaṃ yathoktam om iti || (fol. 66r5–10)

«End of the Taittirīyakabhāṣyaṭippaṇa:»

abhibhavāmi upasaṃharāmīty arthaḥ īśvarātmanā jñānenāhaṃbādhe dvaitaṃ tato (13) nāsti bhayakāraṇam ity arthaḥ || nakāra ivety arthaḥ || ||

(66r1) taittirīyakabhāṣyasya śāṃkarasya daṭīyasaḥ (!) ||

sphuṭārthabodhakāmebhyo niramāyi suṭippaṇaṃ || || (fol. 65v12–66r1)

«Colophon of the Taittirīyopaniṣdbhāṣya:»

iti hariḥ oṃ tat sad iti śrīmatparamahaṃsaparivrājakācāryaśrīmadgoviṃdabhagavatpūjyapādaśiṣya(11)śrīmacchaṃkarabhagavatpūjyapādakṛtau taittirīyopaniṣadbhāṣyaṃ samāptaṃ || (fol. 66r10–11)

«Colophon of the Taittirīyakabhāṣyaṭippaṇa:»

iti śrīparamahaṃsaparivrājakācārya(2)śrīśuddhānaṃdapūjyapādaśiṣyaśrībhagavadānaṃdajñānaviracitā (!) taittirīyakabhāṣyaṭippaṇaṃ saṃpūrṇam astu || || || (fol. 66r1–2)
