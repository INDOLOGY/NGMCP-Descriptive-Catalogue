Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

viśvaṃ darpaṇadṛṣyamānanagarītulyaṃ nijāntargataṃ

paśyan nātmani māyayā bahir ivod bhūtaṃ yathā nidrayā ||

yaḥ sākṣāt kurute prabodhasamaye svātmānam evādvayaṃ

tasmai śrīgurumūrtaye nama idaṃ śrīdakṣiṇāmūrtaye || 1 || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

maunavyākhyāprakaṭitaparabrahmatatvaṃ yuvānaṃ

varṣiṣtāṃte vasa ṛṣigaṇair āvṛtaṃ brahmaniṣṭaiḥ ||

ācāryaidraṃkarakalitacinmudramānaṃdarūpaṃ

svātmārāmaṃ muditavadanaṃ dakṣiṇāmūrttim īḍe || 15 || (fol. 5r5–5v3)

Colophon

iti śrīmatparamahaṃsaparivrājakācāryaśrīmac chaṃkarācāryaviracitaṃ dakṣiṇāmūrtistotram saṃpūrṇa (!) || śubham || (fol. 5v3–5)
