Excerpts

«Beginning»


❖ namo buddhaya ||

praṇamya sadgurun nāthaṃ satvatyebho hitam āśrayam |

jaladārāṃ pravakṣāmi pūrvādidiṃ(!)śakramāt ||

padmāṅkurapāṇḍarayāḥ sugasamvādam uttamam |

gandharaktajā? .. viśvavāridhārāṃ prakathyate ||

saddharmaśīla<<mahā>>vaihāre pāṇḍarāpūcchaṇīyā tat |

deśito yatpadmāṅkuraḥ sarvagaṇasamanvite ||

pāṇḍa⌠⌠rā⌡⌡devā pṛcchati sadānukampakamune |

sarvatra kalyānakārin(!) dharmagañjasamudbhava ||

keṇe(!) ghaṭitan digbhāge kathaṃ jātisarūpakam |

kathaṃ phalānusaṃsāraṅ katham eva sulakṣaṇam || (fol. 1r1–4)


«End»

devālaye maṇḍape vai vā tirthā vāsadharmastha |

nimittāśapannāṣyeṣu? kalyāneṣu tīrtheṣu ca ||

idānīṃ sabhāsañj janā kapālayaṃ pravodhitāḥ |

padmāṅkuro padena kuśadhārasamudbhavam ||

lokaprasiddhaviṣyāte lokacāran tu kalpayet |

lokadharmaikabhāvatvāt trailokyaṃ saṃmatāmatam || ❁ || (fol. 3r4–3v1)


«Colophon»

iti padmāṅkuśe(!)padeśajaladhārā samāptā || ❁ || kṛtir iyan nāma buddhipādānām iti śubham || ❁ || (fol. 3v1–2)
