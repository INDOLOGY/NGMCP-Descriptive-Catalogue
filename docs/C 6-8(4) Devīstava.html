Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaś caṇḍikāyai ||

jaya jaya hariharakamalāsanābhyarccite devi matar namo devi durge

śive śaṃbhuvaktrodbhave caṇḍike carcārūpe

surūpe suvaktre sunetre sugātre

sukeśe suvese subimbādharauṣṭhī

mahāyoginī barhinī(!) barhipucchadhvaje

tuhinakarakumudakundenduvarṇābhir ugrābhir

atyantatīkṣṇābhir draṣṭrābhi(!) saṃdṛśyate mahābhairavī

bhīṣaṇī vīrabhadre subhadre śmaśānapriye padmapatrekṣaṇe

ghorarūpe jayantī jaye mānasī mānavī martyamātṛ(mukhetrya) dhvaje

sarvvasiddhiprade jananidhir avaradundubhir meghanirghoṣagambhīrahāsasvane

brāhmi kaumāri māhendri māheśvarī(!) vaiṣṇavī vāruṇī vāyur agnyātmaje hemakūṭe

     (fol.1v1-5 )

End

ṣaṭhe daṇḍakaṃ daṇḍakenāpi siddho divi krīḍate |

brahmaviṣṇutrilokottamair divyamānair vimānair

mahāsiddhisaṃghai(!) kalpakoṭisahasrāṇi cāvṛtteś

sādhakaṃ aghatanuvinipātakāle vrajaty agnikālāgnirudraṃ

śataṃ nārakaṃ viṃśati (cāṣṭkaṃ) koṭisa(netaṃ) sāmāyaṃ

aṇḍaṃ vinirbhidya rūdrān (dvivyārca) || śatasaṃkhyāto(yate yā nirākāśa guhyāṣṭakaṃ aṣṭasaṣṭim atikramya yat kṛtaṃ prākṛtaṃ yo rūṣaṃ niyatikālaṃ

samāpyaṃ avidyeśvaraṃ cakravarttiṃ(!)saśaktikalāvāṃ sūkṣmāṃ sadā)

                                                                                          (fol.5r4-5v2 )

Colophon

iti || bhagavati(!)purāṇe ghoravadhe devyā stavadaṇḍakaṃ samāptaṃ || ||

rājādhirājapara ❁ meśvaraparamaśaivaparabhaṭṭārakaśrīmat rūdradevasya

vijayarājye || śrīudai brahmavardhanasya pusta || ❁ || kaṃ ||

samvat 293 caitraśuklatṛtīyāyāṃ ādityane(!)likhanasamāptīkṛtam iti || ❁ ||

(fol.5v2-5 )
