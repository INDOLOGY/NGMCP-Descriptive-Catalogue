Beginning

❖ śrīdevy uvāca ||

bhāvās tu trividhā deva divyavīrapaśukramāt |
guravaś ca tathaivātra tathaiva maṃtradevatāḥ ||

ādyo bhāvo mahādeva (2) śreyāt sarvvasamṛddhihaḥ (!) |
dvitīyo madhyamaś caiva tṛtīya (!) sarvaniṃditaḥ |

bahujāyās (!) tathā homāt kāyakleśādivistaraiḥ |
na bhā(3)vena vinā deva taṃtramaṃtraphalapradāḥ (!) | (fol. 1r1–3)

End

guggulai (!) karavīreṇa ghṛtaṃ varttimadhuplutaṃ |
kṛtvā valiṃ ca caivedyaṃ śarkalāmadhusaṃyutaṃ |

vāruṇīmadhurā tatra naivedyaṃ pūpasaṃyutaṃ |
evaṃ saṃpūjya vidhivat jñātavyā jñānavedibhiḥ |

ity uktaṃ śāṃbhavaṃ śāstraṃ jñātvā śiva (!) svayaṃ bhavet |
ato nyaṃ sarvvam evā (!) syuḥ kathitaṃ sarvvakamadaṃ || (fol. 52r3–6)

Colophon

|| iti śrīrudrayāmale śivapārvati samvvāde śāṃvarādhikāre maṃtraśāstradīpake bhairavopāsanādhikāre dvāviṃśatipaṭalaḥ ||    ||

śubham astu || rāma rāma rāma rāma rāma || rāma || śubhaṃ || (fol. 52r6–7)
