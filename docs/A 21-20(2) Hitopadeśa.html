Manuscript Features

In this MTM only two folios from the beginning of a MS of Nārāyaṇa’s Hitopadeśa are preserved. Fol. 3v, which is almost totally illegible, ended probably somewhere within stanza 30 (kīṭo ʼpi sumanaḥsaṅgād etc.) of the prastāvanā.
