Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namaḥ śivāya ||    ||

skanda uvāca ||    ||

niścaleṣupaviṣṭeṣu teṣu yogiṣu śaṃkaraṃ |
vyājahārātha deveśaṃ kuvero yogisattamaḥ || 1 ||

kuvera uvāca ||    ||

devadeva mahādeva mahāpuruṣasattama
(kā)ṇimādiguṇair yukta jñānavijñānasāgara || 2 ||

yadi māṃ sarvadā śaṃbho skaṃdād anyaṃ na manyase ||
mama kāruṇyabhāvena yat pṛcchāmi tad ucyatāṃ || 3 ||

tvatprasādopapannena rājyena parameśvara ||
rājasaḥ śrīmadāṃdho [ʼ]haṃ tvāṃ na jānāmi tatvataḥ || 4 ||

ko bhavān kim idaṃ sarvaṃ jagad etac carācaraṃ ||
kathyā(!)tām iti me yena protsared āśu durmatiḥ || 5 ||

prakāśaṃ satatātmānaṃ paraṃ saṃvitsamāśrayaṃ ||
yair nāmabhir upāsyo [ʼ]si yathārthena vada prabho || 6 ||    || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

ya tvaṃ karoṣi sa brahmā sa candraḥ sa ca bhāskaraḥ ||
surāsuramayī deva tava śaktir jagattraye || 22 ||

kṣarākṣaramayaṃ devaṃ śivaṃ śivakalātmakaṃ ||
api brahmā na jānāti tvāṃ vai jānāty anugrahāt || 23 ||

manasām apy agamyas tvaṃ vacasām apy agocaraḥ |
ataḥ śivaśiveśānety ucyate vāgvilepanaiḥ || 24 ||

śaṃbho maheśvara vibhartti sadā tripuṃḍraṃ
sadmanā saliladhautalalāṭapaṭṭaḥ ||

yo [ʼ]nukṣaṇaṃ śivaśiveti japaty asau vā
lokatrayopari sadāśiva(!) te priyo [ʼ]stu || 25 ||

śivo dātā śivo bhoktā śivo bhuktivarapradaḥ ||
śiva eva tu sarvaṃ hi jagad etat pralīyate || 26 ||    || 146 ||    || (fols. 7v5–8r1)

Colophon
