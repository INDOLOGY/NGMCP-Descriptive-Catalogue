Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namaḥ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

śrīdakṣiṇakālikāyai namaḥ

śrīmahākāla uvāca

mahākautuhalaṃ stotraṃ hṛdayākhyaṃ mahottamaṃ

śṛṇu priye mahāgopyaṃ dakṣiṇāyāṃ sugopitaṃ 1

avācyam api vakṣyāmi tava prītyā prakāśitaṃ

anyebhya kuru gopyaṃ ca satyaṃ satyaṃ ca śailaje 2 (fol. 5r1–3, exp. 5b1–3)

End

labhate paramaṃ sthānaṃ devī loke varānane

mahāduḥkhe mahāghore mahāsaṃkaṭake dine 41

mahābhaye mahābhīte paṭhet stotraṃ mahottamam

satyaṃ satyaṃ puna[[ḥ]] satyaṃ gopayen mātṛjāravat 42 (fol. 8r1–3, exp. 8b)

Colophon

iti śrīparamarahasyakālīkulārṇave mahātaṃtre śrīdakṣiṇakālīkāyā hṛdayastotraṃ samāptaṃ (fol. 8r4, exp. 8b)
