Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

atha brahmavārtamahābhāṃḍe vaṭamule mahāsatrāya sametāḥ maharṣayaḥ śaunakādayaḥ || te(2) ha vai samiptāṇayas tatvajījñāsavo mārkaṃḍeyaṃ cīrajīvinam upetya papracchuḥ (!) || kena tvaṃ ciraṃ jivasi || kena vā(3)naṃdam anubhavasīti | paramarahasya śivatatvajñāneneti hovāca || kiṃ tat paramarahasya śivatatvajñānaṃ || ko devaḥ(4) | ke maṃtrāḥ || kā niṣṭhā | kiṃ tad jñānasādhanaṃ || kaḥ parikaraḥ || ko baliḥ || kaḥ kālaḥ || kiṃ sthānaṃ || yena dakṣīṇābhimu(5)khaḥ śikhāpārākṣīkṛtā bhavati || tatparamarahasya śivatatvajñānaṃ || (!) (!) (fol. 1v1–5)

End

mohajālakasaṃkrāṃtaṃ veivekārthaṃ mṛkaṃ(2)ṭujaṃ (!) ||

datvā vicārapāśena baddhadvaitabhayāturaṃ ||

ujjīvayanijānaṃde svasvarūpeṇa saṃsthitiḥ ||

śemuṣī da(3)kṣiṇā proktā sā yasyām īkṣaṇe mukhaṃ ||

dakṣiṇābhimukhaḥ proktaḥ sivosau brahmavādibhiḥ ||

sargādikāle bha(4)gavān viraṃcyopāsyaṃnaṃ (!) sargasāmarthyam āpyatu toṣā citte vāṃchitārthaṃ svaṃ labdhvā sosyopāsako bha(5)vati || ya imāṃ paramarahasya śivatatva vidyām adhīte ya evaṃ veda sa kaivalyam anubhavati sa kaivalyam anubhavatī(6)ty oṃ (sadāsi.) ty upaniṣat || (fol. 3v1–6)
