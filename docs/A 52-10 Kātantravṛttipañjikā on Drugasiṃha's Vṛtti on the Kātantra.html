Manuscript Features

This incomplete MS gives a portion of Trilocanadāsa's sub-commentary on Durga-siṃha's vṛtti on the sandhivṛtti (i.e. the section on sandhi) of the Kātantra sūtras. It starts on fol. 20r amidst the commentary on Kātantra sūtra 1.2.15 and ends in the middle of the commentary on sūtra 1.5.16 (fol. 36v).

The following fols. are slightly damaged: fol. 20; 24; 30.
