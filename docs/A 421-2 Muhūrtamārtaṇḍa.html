Manuscript Features

A folio appears after the colophon, which is not related to the main text.

The folio number 23 is mentioned twice but text is not repeated. So, the second should be the folio number 24 and should be run soforth.

In the most of the folio, the main text is in middle and commentary is at the top and below of the folio.

In some folio text is written in the margin.

There are different date for the main text and commentary.

Excerpts

«Beginning of the root text:»

śrīḥ ||     ||

sindūrollasitam ibhendravaktram ambāṃ

śrīviṣṇuṃ viyati varān gurūn praṇamya

bahvarthaṃ vividhamude laghuṃ muhūrtta-

mārttaṃḍaṃ sugamam ahaṃ tanomi siddhyai 1 (fol. 1v8)

«Beginning of the commentary:»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

mārttaṇdo vatu vācannaḥ pādair jāḍyatamoharaḥ

vṛttavaddhatanuḥ sevo bhīṣṭado viśvalocanaḥ

kurve muhūrttamārttaṇḍaṭīkāṃ mārttaṃḍava[[lla]]bhām

iyam ākalpam etena di(2)vyād viprasya veśmasu 2 (fol. 1v1–2)

«End of the root text:»

tryaṃkendrapramite varṣe śālivāhanarājyataḥ ||

pūrvavākyārtham ādāya graṃtho yam (5) racito laghuḥ ||

paṭhanārtham aśaktānāṃ aṃgīkāryaḥ sadā budhaiḥ || 4 || ❁ || (fol. 48r4–5)
