Excerpts

Beginning

śrīmahākāla uvāca ||

acintyā ʼmitākāraśaktisvarūpā

prativyaktyadhiṣṭhānasattaikamūrttiḥ (!) ||

guṇātītanirdvaṃdvabodhaikagamyā

tvam ekā paraṃbrahmarūpeṇa siddhā || 1 ||

agotrāʼ kṛtitvād anaikāntikatvād

alakṣāgamatvād aśeṣāʼ karatvāt || 2 ||

prapaṃcālayatvād anāraṃbhakatvāt

tvam ekā paraṃbrahmarūpeṇa siddhā || 2 || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

mahākālarudroditaṃ stotram etat

sadā bhaktibhāvena yodhyeti bhaktaḥ ||

na cāpannarogo na śoko na mṛtyur

bhavet siddhirante ca kaivalyalābhaḥ || 25 ||

iti te kathito divyāḥ sudhādhārāhvayaḥ stavaḥ ||

etasya satatābhyāsāt siddhiḥ karatale sthitā || 26 || || (fol. 6r5–6v3)

Colophon

ity ādināthaviracitāyāṃ mahākālasaṃhitāyāṃ guhyakālyāḥ sudhādhārāhvayaṃ stotraṃ || śubham astu || (fol. 6v4–5)
