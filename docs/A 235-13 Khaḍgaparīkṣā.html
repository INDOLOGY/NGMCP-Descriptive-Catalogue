Beginning

❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||


atha khaḍgaparikṣā ||


aṃgaṃ rūpaṃ tathā jātir netrāriṣṭe ca bhūmikā ||

dhvanir mānam iti proktaṃ khaḍgajñānāṣṭakaṃ śubhaṃ || ||


tad yathā ||


abhinne dṛśyate yādṛk vibhidya ghaṛite tathā ||

yadaiva dṛśyate chihnaṃ tadaṃgaṃ saṃpracakṣyate ||


nīlakṛṣṇādikaṃ khaḍge rūpam ity abhidhīyate ||

te /// va yat pratītaṃ syāt tajjātir iti gadyate ||


aṃgādiriktaṃ cakrādi yanmāhātmyopasūcakaṃ ||

tan/// m iti jānīyāt khaḍge khaḍgaviśāradaḥ || (fol. 1v1–5)


End

niraṃgam atitīkṣṇaṃ ca gāḍhaṃ śāṇe vumet(!) kaṇaṃ ||

chinatyaṣṭavidhaṃ lauhaṃ vajrāṃgam iti tam vadet || 79 ||


kalāya puṣpasadṛśam amgaṃ bhūmiḥ sītāsitā ||

kalāya vajraṃ jājīyāt tatkṣate pāka ucyate ||


aṃgaṃ campakapuṣpābhaṃ bhūmiḥ kṛṣṇāsitā tathā ||

śikhāṃ śāṇe vame[c] chītāṃ tiktaṃ tasya jalaṃ bhavet ||


idaṃ campakavajraṃ syāt sarvatra vijayapradaṃ || 81 ||


aṃgaṃ valād alasamaṃ bhūmiḥ śuklā tatheta‥ ||

balāvajram idaṃ jñeyaṃ nānābhāvo bhaved drutaṃ ||


ity ayaṃ vātarogānān nāśane paramauṣadhaṃ ||
