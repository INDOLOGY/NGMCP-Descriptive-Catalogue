Foliation figures; on the first 16 folios the folio numbers are written in the lower right margin of the verso, but on the second set of folios numbered 2–21, the folio numbers are written in the middle of the right margin of the verso. rāma is written over the folio number in the second set of folios (numbered 2–21).

Place of Deposit NAK

Accession No. 1/240

Manuscript Features

The manuscript contains 2–15 sargas of the text.

The first 16 folios contains 10–15 (up to the 63rd śloka of the 15th sarga) sargas. Folios 2–21 contains 2–9 (some ślokas of the 2nd sarga are lost) sargas.
