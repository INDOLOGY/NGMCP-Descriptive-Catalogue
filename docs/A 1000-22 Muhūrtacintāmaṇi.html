Excerpts

Beginning

❖ om namaḥ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

kailāse pūrṇarākāhimakararūcire vīkṣya vimvaṃ svakīyaṃ
bhūyo bhūyo pidhānapratibhaṭakaraṭisparddhayā caṇḍatakṣuḥ (!) |
sādhāratvaṃ tadaṃghriprahṛtibhir abhito dhūyate sau dharitrī,
tvaṃ vā vāgbhir niruddhā kapaṭakaraṭinaḥ kelayo naḥ punaṃtu ||

muhūrttacintāmaṇisaṃjñakasya,
svayaṃ kṛtasya pramitāṃkṣarākhyā |
rāmau vidhakte vivṛtiṃ praṇamya,
viṣṇukṛt (!) rūdrān pitaro gurūṃśca || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

tadātmaja iti || tasyānantasūtadaivajñasyātmajo rāmābhidhāgirīśanagare vārānasyāṃ (!) muhūrtaciṃtāmaṇināmadheyaṃ graṃthaṃ bhujabhujeṣu camdrai 1622 rmite śake dvāviṃśatyadhikapaṃcadaśamite 1522 śake viniramāda kārṣīt ||

śeṣaṃ sphūṭaṃ || 10 || (fol. 255v7–9)

Sub-colophon

|| iti śrīdaivajñānantasūtadaivajñarāmaviracitāyāṃ svakṛtasūrtacintāmaṇiṭīkāyāṃ (!) pramitākṣarāyāṃ vāstuprakaraṇāṃ samāptaṃ || (fol. 247v1–2)

Colophon

iti śrīdaivajñānantasūtadaivajñarāmaviracitāyāṃ

svakṛtamuhūrttamaṇiṭīkāyāṃ pramitākṣarāyāṃ gṛhapraveśaprakaraṇaṃ samāptim agamat || ○ || samāptā ceyaṃ muhūrttacintāmaṇiṭīkā ||   || ❁ ||   || bhagna.

…..pratipālayet ||   || śrīkṛṣṇāya namaḥ ||   || samvat 916 āśvinaśudi 12 vṛhaspativālaśvakunhu || liṣiti daivajñadurgānandasiṇhanaṃ kṣeyā….
