Excerpts

Beginning

athāgnihotrahomaḥ || ||

anastamitānuditayoruddhareti yajamāno jū(!)yāt | uddharaṇaṃ gārhapatyād āhavanīyadakṣiṇāgnyoḥ | pratyaṅmukho vā sāyaṃ nidadhyāt | tasmin sāyaṃ prātar homam eke | tataḥ saṃdhyāvandanam | astamitānuditayor yajamānasya saṃcareṇa praveśanam | antareṇa parāgniṃ gatvā dakṣiṇena vā pradakṣiṇam āhavanīyaṃ parītyopaviṃśati yajamānaḥ | patnī ca yathādeśaṃ adya ācamati | (fol. 1r1–4)


End

ukhāyāṃ piṃḍāvadhānam | yajamānasyāvaprāṇam agnau sakṛdā chinnāṃ prāsanam | ulmukasya ca madhya sapiṃḍapatnī prāśnāti putrakāmā | anavaghrāṇaṃ ca tasmin pakṣe | udakasparśaḥ | tato darśaśrāddhaṃ dvipārvaṇaṃ jīvattitṛkasya piṃḍapitṛyajñābhāvaḥ | aparāhṇe atopāyanīyam aśnīta || || śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || śrīgurubhyo namaḥ || (fol. 5v1–4)
