Remarks

  • This bundle contains 2 MSS (B 39-21 (01) Yogataraṃginī and B 39-21 (02) Gorakṣaśataka), which are written by the same or several very similar hands. The layout of both the MSS is the same. The foliation is continuous and was, most probably, added after both the MSS were merged into one.
  • Please, see Microfilm Details below.
