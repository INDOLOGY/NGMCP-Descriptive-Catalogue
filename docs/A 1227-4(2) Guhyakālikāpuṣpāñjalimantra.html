Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṁ asya śrīguhyakālikā puṣpāṅjali mantrasya

hsaiḥ (t2) sadāśiva ṛṣi khpreṁ vṛhatī chanda hsauṁ

śivaśakti sama(3)rasanirvvāṇa kālikādevatā,

hskhrāṁ vījaṃ, hskhphrīṁ śakti khphreṁ (4) kīlakaṃ,

nityapūjā saununādhika prāyaś citta si(5)ddhyarthaṃ,

pūjādau viniyoga || (exp. 32t1-5)

End

oṁ namo hrīṁ khphraṁ hūṁ phreṁ bhairavī kṣo(t2)bhanī

mahāparayakālīnī marmme marmmābhighāte,

ki(3)li 2 kici 2 hrīṁ 2 chhrī, amuka kāryaṃ,

me kuru 2 (4) hūṁ 3 phaṭ 3 svāhāḥ ||

iti puṣpāñjarimantra || 0 || (exp. 34t1-4)

Colophon

iti puṣpāñjarimantra || 0 || (exp. 34t4)
