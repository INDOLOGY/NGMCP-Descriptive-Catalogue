Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ sarvajñāyaḥ (!) || ||

praṇamya śirasā buddhaṃ trailokyādhipatiprabhu (!) |

nānāśāstradhṛtaṃvakṣe, rājanītiḥ (2) samuccaye (!) || ||

trailokyayā adhipati, buddhatvaṃ namaskālayāṅāva, nānāśāstra,

śapikāsyaṃtayā, rāja(3)nitimuṅatayā, yajena lhāya || 1 ||

atitaivam idaṃ sāstraṃ, naro jñāsyati tattvata (!) |

dharmmopadeśavinayaṃ, kā(4)ryyākāryya (!) śubhāśubhaṃ || (fol. 1v1–4)

gobhir vipraiś ca (3) vedaiś ca, satyabhiḥ satyavādibhiḥ |

sāda(4)ko, brāhmaṇadako, veda satya, satyavādidake,
