Excerpts

Beginning

śrīsarasvatyai || ||

kailāsaśikhare ramye devadevaṃ maheśvaraṃ |

bhavānīrahasya (!) prītyā prayacha varadāyakaḥ || 1 ||

śrīdevy uvāca ||

katham īśāna sarvajña labhate śaktim uttamaṃ ||

sādhakāya prasādena tan me kathaya suprabho || 2 ||

śrībhairava uvāca ||

vinā pūjā vinā dhyānaṃ vinā jāpyaṃ vinā valiṃ ||

labhate yāni kalpāṇi tan me nigadita (!) śṛṇu || 3 ||

nāṃnāṃ sahasraṃ tārāyā (!) kathayāmi viśeṣataḥ ||

śṛṇu devi yathā bhaktyā bhaktānāṃ paramaṃ hitāṃ || 4 ||

vinā pūjopacāreṇa vinā jāpyena yatphalaṃ ||

tatphalaṃ sakalaṃ devi kathayāmi tataḥ śṛṇu || 5 || (fol. 1v1–7)

End

anenaiva vidhānena yojayet parameśvarī

sa bhūmivalaye devi sākṣad darśanasaṃśayaḥ ||

mahāśaṃkhena kalyāṇī kūrve kāryajayādikaṃ

kulasarvasya kasyai ca prabhavo caritaṃ tvayā ||

na śakyate samātaṃkavarṣakoṭiśatair api || 65 ||

kiṃcin mayā tu cāpalya kathitaṃ parameśvarīṃ ||

janmāṃtarasahasreṇa varṇito (!) naiva śakyate ||

kulīnāya pradātavyaṃ tava bhaktiparāya ca ||

na deyaṃ deva deveśi gopyaṃ sarvāgame sadā || 66 ||

paṭhanīyaṃ mahādevī sarvāvasthāsu durllabhā || 167 || (fol. 11r8–11v4)

Colophon

iti śrīnīlataṃtre śrīnīlatārāsarasvatīsahasranāmastotraṃ samāptam || ||

śubham astu ||

sampūrṇam ||

idaṃ pustakaṃ likhitvā amātyabharatalālasya dadau || hanumatapurapule nivāsī || (fol. 11v4–6)
