Manuscript Features

This MTM contains the following texts:

1. Mohamudgara 2. Gaṅgāṣṭaka 3. Gurupādukopadeśa 4. Bhavānībhujaṅgaprayāta 5. Gurubhujaṅgaprayāta 6. Ātmaprabodha 7. Nirañjanastava 8. Sarasvatīdvādaśanāmastotra 9. Śāntistava 10. Navapātrastotra 11. Sundarīpañcaratnaka

Exposures 20 and 21 are out of focus.
