Excerpts

«Beginning»

❖ oṁ namaḥ śivāya ||

kailāsaśikharāsīnaṃ bhairavaṃ vigatāmayaṃ |

caṇḍanandimahākālagaṇeṣa(!)(vṛṣabhṛdgibhiḥ) ||

kumārendrayamādityabrahmāviṣṇupurassaraiḥ ||

stūyamānaṃ (maheśānaṃ) gaṇamātṛniṣevitaṃ ||

sṛṣṭisaṃhāraka⌠ñ caiva⌡ sthitivila⌠⌠ya⌡⌡kārakaṃ ||

anugrahakaran devaṃ praṇatārttivināśanaṃ |

muditaṃ bhairavaṃ dṛṣṭvā devī vacanam abravīt ||

yat tvayā kathitaṃ mahyaṃ svacchandaṃ parameśvaraḥ |

śatakoṭipravistīrṇṇabhedānantavisarppitaṃ ||

catuṣpīṭham mahātantraṃ catuṣṭayaphalodayaṃ |

namaskurvanti manujā alpavīryaparākramāḥ || (fol. 1v1–4)


«End»

bhairaveṇa samākhyātā śaktayas tu varānane |

anyāś ca siddhayaś citrā apsaromadhyamottamāḥ ||

anyatantra(!) samucchāyā sādhayanti na saṃśayaḥ ||

evaṃ saṃkṣepataḥ prokta(!) melakan tu varānane

satatābhyāsayogena dadanti varukaṃ svakam ||

yasya saṃprāsanād devi vīreśasadṛśo bhavet ||

tasmād dhyānārcanaṃ homaṃ japaś ca varavarṇṇini ||

kartavyaṃ bhāvitātmena (!) tataḥ siddhyanti mantriṇaḥ || ❁ || (fol. 161v2–4)


«Colophon»

iti svacchandalalitabhairave mahātantre mantrapīṭhe chomādhikāro nama pañcādaśamaḥ(!) paṭalas trisahasrapañcaśatādhikaṃ samāptam iti || ❁ ||

samvat 198? śrīmahārājanāgārjunadevasya rājñi | kulajaśrīmādhavasiṃhasya pustakaṃ lekhakakulodbhavabhīmasiṃhena likhitam iti || ❁ || (fol 161v4–5)
