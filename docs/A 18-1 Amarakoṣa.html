Beginning

///bjo jaivātṛkaḥ somo glaur mṛgāṅkaḥ kalānidhiḥ |

/// bhittaṃ śakalakhaṇḍe vā puṃsy arddo〇 ’rddhaṃ same ’ṃśake |

bahivrīhir adiṅnāmnām unneyaṃ tad udāhṛtam |
