Manuscript Features

The fol. 74 is not mentioned. We could see the fol. 75 after the fol. 73. But, text is not lost. So, it clearly shows that the scriber mentioned the fol. 75 after the fol. 74 by mistake. The fol. 75 must be the fol. 74 and it must be run so on.

Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namaḥ kṛṣṇakālikāmahāgaṇeśāya (!) ||

saunāsīramaṇipravīṇajaladaśyāmābhirāmachavi-

nṛtyatītapaṭadvaikakapaṭaprodyatparāgojvalam ||

vaṃśīvādanakaitavapravilasad gambhīradhīradhvanim

vande kañcid (!) ahaṃ mudāmadhulihaṃ hṛtpadmamadhyasthitaṃ || (fol. 1v1–2)

End

mahendramaṇimudrikāmadhu(7)karīparītodaraṃ

namas tripurasundarīcaraṇapuṇḍarīkadvayaṃ

āsīt kīrttikaras tatottaraharas tasmāt sa kaviḥ

śrīmānpaṇḍitapuṅgavo ruci(8)karaḥ śrīkṛṣṇaśarmmā tataḥ

tadmād eva gadādharaḥ samabhavat tarkkāmbudheḥ pāragas

tārābhaktisudhārṇavaṃ samatanot tan nandanaḥ kautukāt ||      || (fol. 246v6–8)

Colophon

iti śrīmahāmahopādhyāyasatṭhakkuraśrīnarasiṃhaviracite tārābhaktisudhārṇave viṃśatitamas taraṅgas samāptaḥ ||     || śubham || (fol. 247v9)
