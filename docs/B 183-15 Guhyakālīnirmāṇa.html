Manuscript Features

There are some notes in three separate folios. One at the beginning and two at the last folios. The following note of warning is given in the initial folio not to take out and copy this manuscript :

thva saphu devayā gṛhena pita yene madu thva pūjāsa thyākapi(2)syaṃ meva sunānaṃ saphū sopisyaṃ ākha chago hleyā yaṃsā thva (3) dayācoko devatāyā kudṛṣṭi juyu thva coyā tayā bamo(4)jiṃ pita mayaṃsya hleyā makāsya jyā calā yānācohmayā(5)ta thva dayācoko devatāyā sudṛṣṭi juyū || śubham ||    || (exp.1)

Excerpts

Beginning

śrīguhyakālyai namaḥ ||    ||

atha śrīguhyakālyānityārcana pa(2)ddhatirlikhyate ||
sveṣṭeśvarī pūjāmaṇḍapasa vane ||
maṃtra bonāva lā(3)hāta tuti siya ||
oṃ gaṃdhodake hūṁ phaṭ ||
oṃ hrīṁ klīṁ svāhā ||
ā(4)camana yāya || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

uttarasyāṃ ca cāmuṃḍā ma(7)hāghora terāvatu ||
ūrddhaṃ cādho vidigdiśo tvaṃ sarvatraiva pāhi māṃ (8) ||
śaktayo yāśvate devi parivāragaṇā śvayeṃ ||
sarvatra sarvadā (9) pāṃtu māṃ tvaccaraṇasevakaṃ ||
sadā māma kuto bhītiṃ kurutvaṃ guhya(10)kālike || 10 || (fol. 53r6–10)

Colophon

iti śrīmahākālasaṃhitāyāṃ duḥsvapnadhvaṃsakasto(11)traṃ ||    || śubhaṃ ||    ||
