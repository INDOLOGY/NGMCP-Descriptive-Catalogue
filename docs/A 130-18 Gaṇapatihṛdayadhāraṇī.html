Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrī bhagavatyai ārya uṣṇīṣavijayāyai ||

evamāhā(!) śrutam ekasmin samaye bhagavān sukhāvatyāṃ dharmasaṃgītimahāguhyaprāsāde bhagavān viharati sma | sukhapratiṣṭhite bhagavān amitāyus tathāgato(!) āryāvalokiteśvaraṃ bodhisattvaṃ mahāsattvam āmantrayate sma | santi kulaputra duḥkhitā[[ḥ]] sattvāḥ alpāyuskāḥ teṣāṃ satvānāṃ arthāya hitāya sukhāya imāṃ sarvatathāgatoṣṇīṣavijayānāmadhāraṇīṃ dhāraya vācaya deśaya paryavāpnuyā[t] parebhyaś ca vistareṇa saṃprakāśaya dīrghāyuskānām upādāya iti || (exp. 3v1–4)

«End: »

mahāsaubhāgyo bhavati | rājyakule mahāprasādo bhaviṣyati || śrutidharo bhaviṣuyati na cāsya kasyacit avatāra(mavekṣī avatāraṃ pratira(!)psyati na cāsya bodhicittāntarāyā bhaviṣyati [[jātau jātau]] jātismaro bhaviṣyati || idam avocado bhagavān āttamanās te ca bhikṣavas te bodhisattvā sā ca sarvāvatīparṣat sadevamānuṣāsuragaruḍagandhavaś ca loko bhāgavato bhāṣitam abhyanandann iti || || (exp. 18:2–5)
