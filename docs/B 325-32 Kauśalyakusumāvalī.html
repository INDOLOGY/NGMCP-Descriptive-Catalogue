Excerpts

«Beginning of rudrakuśalaṭīkā:»

śrī 3 ganeśāya namaḥ || ||

umāpatiṃ viśvapatiṃ praṇamya

samastavidyārtham idaṃ svabhāvataḥ

bālaprabodhāya mayābhidhīyate

yuddhopayogārthaviśeśasaṃgrahaḥ || 1 || ||

(2) umāpatim ity ādi || umāpatiṃ mahādevaṃ viśvapatisaṃsārapatisvabhāvata (!) srvavidyārthavistāraṃ praṇamyaṃ bālaprabodhāya yuddhopayogārthaviśeṣaṃgrahaḥ mayā avidhiyate (!) || || (fol.1v1–2)

«Beginning of kauśalyakusumākara:»

oṃ namo gaṇapataye ||

sindūrapūrakumbhasthala-

ravividhvastavighnaghanatimiram ||

gagananibhaṃ gajavadanaṃ

namāmi sattvārthasiddhaye śuddhaṃ || || (exp.6v1)

«Sub: colophon:»

ity arthanirṇayaḥ || iti rājaṣusvasagrathitāyāṃ (!) kauśalyakusumāvalyāṃ vicitrānāmaḥ prathomoṃśaḥ samāptiḥ || || (fol. 21r2)

End

lagnaṃ yadā nāsti viśuddham anyo

godhūlikāṃ sādhutatā diśanti ||

(4) lagne viśuddhe sati vīryayukte

godhūlikā naiva phalaṃ vidhatte || || godhūliguṇāḥ || ||

ataḥ paraṃ niibodhemaṃ śacīyāgavidhiṃ śubhaṃ ||

praśastatithinakṣatra kṣaṇavāreṣu śilpinā ||

kumbhakāramṛ(5)dā(‥)ṃ kārayitvā pulomalā || (fol. 3–5)

Colophon

iti śrīrudrakauśalaṭīkā samāptaḥ (!) || ||… (fol. 5r11)
