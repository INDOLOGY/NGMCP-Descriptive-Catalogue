Beginning

|| śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

yasyāḥ patir deśāṃtaraṃ gatas tāṃ nāyikāṃ paṃcabhiḥ ślokair abhidhatte nicitetyādi he kuṃdasamānadaṃti nīradai (!) upetya khaṃ nicitaṃ kuṃdānāṃ puṣpāṇi kuṃdāni saiḥ(!) samānā daṃtās tāṃbūlābhāvād vihiraṇyāḥ(!) .prāgatya gaganaṃ vyātpaṃ (!) sarasair meghaiḥ | kham iva tava nāyakaḥ || (fol. 1v1–2)

nicitaṃ kham upetya nīradaiḥ
priyahīnā hṛdayāvanīradaiḥ ||
salilair nihitaṃ rahaḥ kṣitau
ravicandrāv api nopalakṣitau || 1 || (fol. 1v4)
