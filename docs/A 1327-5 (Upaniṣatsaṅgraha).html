Manuscript Features

This text contains Muṇḍakopaniṣad, Praśnopaniṣad, Brahmavidyopaniṣad, Kṣurikopaniṣad, Cūllikopaniṣad, Atharvaśiraupaniṣad, Atharvaśikhopaniṣad, Garbhopaniṣad, Mahopaniṣad, Brahmopaniṣad, Prāṇāgnihotropaniṣad and Māṇḍūkyopaniṣad similarly Vaitathyaprakaraṇa and Advaitaprakaraṇa of the Māṇḍūkyakārikā.

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || oṃn (!) namo atharvavedāya (!) [[||]]

oṃ brahmā devānāṃ prathamaḥ saṃbabhūva
viśvasya karttā bhuvanasya goptā
sa brahmavidyāṃ sarvavidyāpratiṣṭhāṃ
atharvāya jyeṣṭhaputrā(2)ya prāha ||

atharvaṇe yāṃ pravadeta brahmā-
tharvā tāṃ purovācāṃgire brahmavidyāṃ
sa bhāradvājāya satyavahāya prāha
bhāradvājoṃgirase parāvarāṃ | (fol. 1v1–2)
