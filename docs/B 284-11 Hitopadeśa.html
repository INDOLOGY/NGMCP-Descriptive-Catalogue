Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namo hariharābhyaḥ (!) ||

siddhiḥ sādhye satām astu prasādāt ta(2)sya dhūjaṭeḥ (!) |

jāhnavīphenalekheva, yan muddhnii śaśina (!) kalā(3)ḥ ||

śruto hitopadeśoyaṃ, pāṭavaṃ saṃskṛtoktiṣūḥ (!) |

vācāṃ sarvvatra(4) vaicitryaṃ nītividyāṃ [da]dāti ca ||

ajarāmaravat prājño, vidyām a(5)rthañ ca cintayetḥ (!) |

gṛhīta iva keśeṣu, mṛtyunā dharmmam ācaretḥ (!) (6) ||

sarvvadravyeṣu vidyaiva dravyam āhur anuttamaṃḥ(!) |

ahāyatvā dakṣa(7)yatvāc ca sarvvadaḥ (!) (fol. 1v1–7)

End

anenāśritānāṃ vātsalyena, trailokyasyāpi(9) prabhu (!) tvayi yujyate |

evam uktvā sarvvesāṃ bandhanāni tena chinnāni |

a(1)nantaraṃ, hiraṇyakaḥ sādaraṃ sarvvān saṃpūjyāha sakhe citragrīva,(2)

sarvvathātra jālabandhanavidhau matidoṣam āsaṃkyātmanyavajñā na katrtta(3)vyaḥ

(fol. 12r8–12v3)
