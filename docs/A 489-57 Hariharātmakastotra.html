Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

dharmarāja uvāca ❖

goviṃdamādhavamukuṃda hare murāre

śaṃbho śiveśa śaśiseṣaraśūlapāṇe ||

dāmodarācyutajanārddana vāsudeva

tyājyā bhaṭāya iti saṃtatam āmanaṃti || 1 ||

gaṃgādharāṃdhakaripo haranīlakaṃṭha

vaikuṃṭha kaiṭabha ripo kamaṭhābjapāṇe ||

bhūteśaṣaṇḍaparaśo mṛḍacaṇḍakeśa

tyājyā bhaṭāya iti saṃtatam āmanaṃti || 2 || (fols. 1v1–2r2)

End

agastir uvāca ||

yo dharmarājaracitāṃ lalitaprabaṃdhāṃ

nāmāvaliṃ sakalakalmaṣabījahaṃtrīṃ ||

dhīrotra kaustubhabhṛtaḥ śaśiśeṣarasya

nityaṃ japet stanarasaṃ na pibet sa mātu(!) || 13 ||

iti śṛ....t kathāṃ ramyāṃ śivaśarmāpriye ʼnnavāṃ ||

prahṛṣṭavakraḥ purato dadarśāpsarasāṃ purāṃ || 14 || (fol. 5r2–v3)

Colophon

iti śrīdharmarājaviracitaṃ hariharātmakastotram samāptam || || śubham astu sarvadā || || rāma rāma rāma rāma (fol. 5v3–4)
