Size 22.5 x 17.1 cm

Folios 13 (pages)

Foliation The MS contains two foliation. The first one starts (figure is written in the middle top-hand margin) from the each chapter and the second one (figure is written in the upper right-hand margin) starts from the beginning and runs up to the end.

Here in the "Excerpts", the foliation is cited from the second one or the regular foliation.

Place of Deposit NAK

Accession No. 6/2101

Manuscript Features

The MS is in modern book form. Probably, it is a collection. It doesn't contain any colophons and sub colophons. Each chapter starts with the new foliation and new maṇṅalācaraṇa.

Excerpts

Beginning

kālīkulapaṃcaśata(!)

I sadbhāvabhāvaki(!)kalā(!) praṇamāmi kālim

II svakalāṃtasukhāśaktām

III atitejaḥ subṛṃhaṃtī

IV pādau jagrāha devyās tu bhairavo bhairaveśvaraḥ ||

praṇamya śirasā devīṃ stutipūrvaṃ varo ʼbravīt ||

namaḥ śūnyātiśūnyasthe namaś cidacidojjhite ||

namo vijñānavibhave namaḥ saṃhārakāriṇī(!) || (page 1 l. 1–7)

End

khakale khacare khasthe svabhāve khamāśrite || (!)

raviprakāśasaṃkāśe dvādaśārkasamaprabhe ||
