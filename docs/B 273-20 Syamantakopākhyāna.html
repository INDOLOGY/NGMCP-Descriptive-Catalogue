Excerpts

Beginning

semamtakovyāṣyāna (!) 1

❖ śrīnṛtyanāthāya namaḥ ||

ādiślokaḥ ||

jaya jaya hara gaurī, nāṭa thānaṃ vijyātā |

śiraśa śitalagaṃgā, candramān vānarātā

balavihunechalapolaṃ nṛtyesiddhiṃ jimistā ||

nāndime || ||

mālava || cauºº ||

jaya harana kasaṅana maraye dhyāna |

vacchi sarira sa o sa gauri sohāna ||

śirasa śitara aśitara jura akāsa gaṃgāna,

mho sata sanicalata jura candramāna ||

dhuri dhu ccheguri dhuti khokhi vina

vānalaya da va khava tavado hara vahāna || (fol. 1r1–6)

End

kṛṣna, thva saṃsāra suyāṅa makhu

indrajāra māyāna to kapuyakaṃtara,

sāravastu cchu, śrī 3 caṇḍeśvarīyā

sevāyāya prātubhāva ||

sarvva, śrīkṛṣṇa ucita ājñā dayaku || ||

bhiravi || khacoºº ||

jaya caṇḍeśvarī devī jagatayā sāra,

chalaporasena yāva ji pani udhāra ||

nṛpa bhupatīndrayā che chahma adhārara,

yāse vijyāhune bhavasamudrara pāra || mepu 66 || || ārati || (fol. 67r3–67v2)

Colophon

samvat 826 jyeṣṭha vadi 5 śrīśrījayabhūpatīndradevasana, śrī śrī śrīmūlacukaya gajuli chāna bhota sase syamantakopākhyāna dayaka sa vijyāṅā || || (fol. 67v2–3)
