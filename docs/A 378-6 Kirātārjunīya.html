Manuscript Features

This text runs from the very beginning up to Pañcamasarga.

After the end of each chapter there are some verses regarding Dharmaśāstra and Sāhitya.

Excerpts

«Beginning of the root text:»

śriyaḥ kurūṇām adhipasya pālanīṃ

prajāsu vṛttiṃ yam ayuṃkta vedituṃ

sa varṇiliṃgī viditaḥ samāya(6)yau

yudhiṣṭhiraṃ dvaitavane vanecaraḥ 1

(3v4) kṛtapraṃṇāmasya (!) mahīṃ mahībhuje

jitāṃ sapatnena nivedayiṣyataḥ

na vivyathe tasya mano na hi priyaṃ

pravaktum i(5)cchaṃti mṛṣā hitaiṣiṇaḥ 2 ❁

(fol. 2r4–3v5)

«Beginning of the commentary:»

śrīgaṇeśāya nasamaḥ (!)

arddhāṃgīkṛtadāṃpatyam api gāḍhānurāgi yat

pitṛbhyāṃ jagatas tasmai kasmaicin mahase namaḥ 1

ālaṃbe ja(2)gadālaṃbe herambacaraṇāṃbuje

śuṣyanti yadrajaḥsparśāt sadyaḥ pratyūhavārddhayaḥ 2 (fol. 1v1–2)

...

(8) atha tatrabha[[ga]]vān bhāravināmā kaviḥ

kā(9)vyaṇ yaśase ʼrthakṛte

vyavahāravide śivetarakṣataye

sadyaḥ paranirvṛtaye

kāṃtāsaṃmitatayopadeśayuje

ityādyalaṃkārikavacanaprā(10)māṇyāt (!) kāvyasyānekaśreyaḥsādhanatāṃ kāvyālāpāṃś ca varjayed iti niṣedhaśāstrasya cāsatkāvyavi⟪‥‥‥⟫ ||  1  || (2r1)ṣayatā (!) ca paśyan kirātārjjunīyākhyaṃ mahākāvyaṃ cikīrṣuś cikīrṣitārthāvighnaparisamāpti[[ḥ]]saṃprdāyāvicchedalakṣaṇapakṣa(2)sādhanatvād (!) āśīrnamaskriyāvastunirddeśo vāpi tanmukham ity āśīrvādādanyataramasya (!) prabandhamukhalakṣaṇatvāc ca vanecarasya yudhi(3)ṣṭhiraprāptirūpaṃ vastu nirddiśan kathām u[[pakṣi]]pati || śriya iti || (fol. 1v8–2r3)

«End of the root text:»

tam anatiśayanīyaṃ sarvataḥ sārayogād

avirahitam a(6)neke nāṃkabhājā phalena |

akṛśam akṛśalakṣmīś cetasā śaṃsitaṃ sa

svam iva puruṣakāraṃ śailam abhyāsasāda 52 (fol. 14v5–6)

«End of the commentary:»

tam iti [[||]] akṛśāḥ pūrṇāḥ lakṣmyaḥ śobhā yasya so ʼkṛśalakṣmīr iti bahubacanā(2)śrito bahuvrīhiḥ [[||]] evam uraḥprbhṛtibhya (!) kab iti pappratyayānavakāśaḥ [[||]] tadarthaṃ lakṣmīśabdasyeva (!) bahuvacanāṃtasyāpāṭhāt [[||]] nāpi nadyṛtaś caetyasā(3)vakāśaḥ [[||]] uraprabhṛtipāṭhasāmarthyād (!) eva śaiṣikas tu vaibhāṣika ity avirodhaḥ [[||]] so rjunaḥ sarvatra sārayogād utkṛṣṭavastu prayogāt [[||]] sāro bale sthi(4)rāṃśe ca nyāyye klībe vare striṣv ity amaraḥ [[||]] anatiśayanīyam anatikramaṇīyaṃ anekena bahunā aṃkabhājā samīpagatena śīghrabhāvineti yā(8)vat [[||]] phalena kāryasidhyāvirahitam aśūnyakāryasiddher avaśyaṃ kāryasādhakam ity arthaḥ || akṛśam atanu⟪‥⟫bhūtam iti yāvat [[||]] cetasā ʼʼśaṃsitaṃ prāptu(9)m iṣṭaṃ śailam iṃdrakīlaṃ svam ātmanīnaṃ puruṣasya kāraṃ (!) karma taṃ puruṣākāraṃ (!) uktaviśeṣaṇaviśiṣṭaṃ pauruṣam ivābhyasasāda [[prāptavān]] mālinīvṛttaṃ 52 (fol. 14v1–9)
