Beginning

oṃ namaḥ śrīgaṇeśāya ||

praṇamya jagatāmīśaṃ rāmaṃ kāmadamīśvaraṃ ||

tithyāditattvaṃ tatprītyai vakti śrīraghunandanaḥ ||

tatr⟨ā⟩a tithirūpam āha || hemādrikālamādhavīyayoḥ || skānde prābhāsakhaṇḍaṃ ||

amā ṣoḍaśabhāgena deviproktā mahābalāḥ ||

saṃsthitā paramā māyā dehināṃ dehadhāriṇā | (fol. 1r1–4)


End

manvādau ca yugadau ca yugādau ca māsatrayaphalaṃ smṛtaṃ |

viruddhaṃ guruvākyasya yadatra bhāṣitaṃ mayā |

tatkṣaṃtavyaṃ budhenaiva smṛtitattvaṃ bhbhutsubhiḥ |

smṛtitattve pramodāya dviruddhaṃ bahubhāṣitaṃ |

guṇadoṣānurāgena tacchādhyaṃ dharmmadarśibhiḥ || || (fols. 222v8–223r2)
