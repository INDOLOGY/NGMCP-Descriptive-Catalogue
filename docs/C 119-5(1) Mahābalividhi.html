Excerpts

Beginning

❖ namo gaṇeśāya ||

5 siṃhāsanasthitaṃ devaṃ paṃcavaktra gajānanaṃ |

pūrvva kuṃkuma saṃkāśaṃ dakṣiṇaṃ kṛṣṇa sobhi(2)taṃ ||

raktam unttaram ity āhuḥ kṣīravarṇan tu pacchimaṃ |

urddhaṃ kuṇdvendu saṃkāśaṃ śuddhasphuṭika saṃninaṃ |

tripañcanaya(3)nai yuktaṃ daśavāhuṃ gaṇeśvaraṃ || (exp. 42t1–3)

sarvvahiraṇya dhanadhānyapaśu sarvvadāsān dāsyādī(2)n vṛddhaya 2 sarvvaduritān nāśaya 2 sarvvaśāntiṃ kuru 2 imāṃ atipiśitaṃ puṣpa dhūpa dīpa oḍana pū(3)jāṃ yathāyanīsāṃ baliṃ gṛhna 2 kuru 2 glāṃ glāṃ gluṃ mahāgaṇapataya svāhā kṣamasva varadā ma(4)ma svāhā  || o || (exp. 45t1–4)
