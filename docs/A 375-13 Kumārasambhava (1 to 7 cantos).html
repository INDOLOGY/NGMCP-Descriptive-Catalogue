Manuscript Features

The text runs form the very beginning up to śloka 75 of the 7th sarga.

Word divisions are marked by small vertical strokes from the beginning of the text up to fol. 20v and from fol. 42r to the end.

Ṭippaṇas (short glosses) appear regularly in the first 17 folios, intermittently from there up to fol. 37r, and again regularly from fol. 37v onwards.

On fol. 1r is written:

❖ rāmo hemamṛgan na vetti naghuṣo yāne yunakti dvijāṃ (!), viprasyaikasavatsa[[dhenu]]haraṇe yātā matiś cārjune || dyūte bhrātṛścatuṣṭayaṃ (!) samahiṣīṃ dharmmātmajo dattavāṃ (!) praye satpuro (!) visanosamaye (!)<ref>Perhaps for: prāye satpuruṣo vipattisamaye</ref> buddhyā parityajyate ||
