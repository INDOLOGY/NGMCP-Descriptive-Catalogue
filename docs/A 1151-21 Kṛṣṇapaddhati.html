Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

śrīgopālo jayati || ||

atha sādhako brāhme muhūrtte utthāyāvaśyakīṃ kriyāṃ kṛtvā rātrivāsaḥ parityajya vāsontaraṃ paridhāya svaśirasi [[sthitaṃ]] svaguruṃ dhāyed yathā ||


sahasradalapaṃkaje tuhinaraśmibiṃbodare

srutākṣarakathādibhiḥ prasramare halakṣāṃtare |

varābhayakaraṃ guruṃ hṛdi sugaṃdhimālyāṃbaraṃ

bhaje śirasi bhāsuraṃ taruṇaraktaśaktyākulaṃ |<ref>pāda b is unmetrical.</ref>(fol. 1v1–4)

End

gaccha gaccha paraṃ sthānaṃ svasthānaṃ parameśvara ||

yatra brahmādayo devā na viduḥ paramaṃ padam

iti paṭhitvā puṣpaṃ nāsāṃtikam ānīya nāsāsvāsānusāreṇa ⟪‥⟫ śrīkṛṣṇaṃ hṛdy ānīya śrīkṛṣṇarūpo ʼham iti bhāvayed iti || || (fol. 21v2–4)


Colophon

iti śrīcaturbhujācāryaviracitā śrīkṛṣṇapaddhatiḥ samāptā || || || likhitaṃ śrīsatyadharaśarmaṇā kāśyāṃ || || saṃvat 1786 bhādraśukladvādaśyāṃ || ❁ || śubhaṃ || ||
