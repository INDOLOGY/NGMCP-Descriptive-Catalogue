Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namo bhagavate vāsudevāya || oṃ namo nārāyaṇāyaḥ (!) || śrīkṛṣṇāya namaḥ || rājovāca ||

kathito vaṃśavistāro, bhavatā somasūryayoḥ |
rājñā (!) cobhayavaṃśyānāṃ caritaṃ paramādbhutaṃ ||

yadoś ca dharmmaśīlasya nitarāṃ munisattama |
tatrāṃśenāvatīrṇṇasya viṣṇor vvīryyāṇi śaṃsa naḥ ||

avatīryya yador vvaṃśe bhagavān bhūtabhāvanaḥ |
kṛtavān yāni viśvātā tāni no vada vistarāt ||

nivṛrttatarṣair upagīyamānād
bhavauṣadhāc chrotramanobhirāmāt |
ka uttamaḥślokaguṇānuvādā,t (!)
pumān virajjyeta vinā paśudhnāt || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

itthaṃm (!) parasya nijadharmmarirakṣayātta-
līlātanos tadanurūpaviḍambanāni |
karmāṇi kramakaṣaṇāni yadūttamasya
śrūyād amuṣya padayor anuvṛttim icchan |

matyas tathā nanū samedhitayā mukunda,
śrīmatkathāśravaṇakīrttanacintayeti (!) |
daddhāmadustarakṛtāntajarāpavarggaṃ, (!)
grāmād vanaṃ kṣitibhojo pi yaur yadārathāḥ (!) || (fol. 203v2–3)

… haribhaktir astu śubhaṃ || sambat 844 māghakṛṣṇāpaṃcamī citrānakṣatra bhājudevanathavatāro cakāvatayā || (ṅākṣa 1036 vida) || (fol. 203v4–5)
