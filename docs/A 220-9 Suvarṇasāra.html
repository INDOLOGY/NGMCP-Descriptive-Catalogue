Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

natvā śivaṃ prathamataḥ praṇipatya caṇḍī

vāgdevatā tadanu tātapadauñ ca ||

saṃgṛhya ya(2)t dyatitamantrasuvarṇasāraṃ

tatkarṇayoḥ kurutajīvitavedavijñāḥ ||

parisekāṃ pradehāś ca snehāt saṃśodha(3)nāni ca || (!)

divāsvapnaṃ vyavāyaṃ ca vyāyāmaśiśiraṃ jalaṃ || (!)

krodhapravātabhojyāni varjjayet taruṇī(4)varī || ||

arucī mātuluṃgasya keśaram ājyasaindhavaṃ ||

dhātrīdrākṣāsitānām vā kalkam āsye vadhāra(5)yet ||

mustaparppaṭakośīla candanādīvyānāgaraiḥ || (!) (fol.1v1–5)

End

amṛtādya gugguluḥ ||

pratyekaṃ tripha(7)lāprastho jalaṃ cātra ṣaḍāṭakaṃ ||

kuḍavaṃ gugguloḥ pākaḥ saṃbaddhas tu viśeṣataḥ

śivā vibhītā(8)malakī phalā pratyekaśo muṣiṭicatuṣayaṃ ca ||

toyāṭakeṣu kvathitaṃ vidhāya pādāvaśiṣṭaṃ tvava(9)tālavīta ||

eraṇḍataila dvipalaṃ vidhāya

picūtrayaṃ gandhakasaṃjñakasya ||

pacet puna syātra pala dve (fol. 125v6–9)
