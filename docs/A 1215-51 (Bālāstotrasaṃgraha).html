Manuscript Features

The MS contains the following four texts:

  • Bālāhṛdya (fol. 1v1–4r3) [fols. 2–3 are missing]
  • (Bālāparameśvarī)Mālāmantra (fol. 4r3–5r5)
  • Bālāstavarāja (fol. 5r5–7r3)
  • (Tripurā)Bālāṣṭottaraśatanāma (fol. 7r4–8v6)

bālāhṛdayamaṃtrasya prayogaṃ vada staprada(!) || 1 ||

īśvara uvāca ||
bālāyā hṛdayaṃ maṃtraṃ guptā[[t]] guptataraṃ tathā ||
(e)tacchravaṇamātreṇa maṃtrasiddhim avāpnuyāt || 2 ||
na dhyā(naṃ) .. .. .. .. .. na japena ca tarpaṇaṃ ||
sakṛt tu cāraṇā[t] tasya ciṃtitaṃ ca bhaved dhruvaṃ || 3 ||
