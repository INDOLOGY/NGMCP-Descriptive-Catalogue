Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||    ||

gaṇeśaṃ hariṃ padmayoniṃ ca natvā
haraṃ bhāratīṃ khecarā(2)n sūryapūrvān ||
vilokyākhilaṃ tājakaṃ padmakośaṃ
pravakṣye phalaṃ varṣalagne grahāṇām 1

(3) atha pravakṣye yavanādisaṃmataṃ
