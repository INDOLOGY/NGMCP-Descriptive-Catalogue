Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ mahābhairavāya ||

saṃvarttā maṇḍalānte kramapadanihitānandaśaktiḥ subhīmā |

śṛsṛkṣādyaṃ(!) catuṣkaṃ akulakulagataṃ pañcakaṃ cā ʼnyaṣaṭkaṃ

catvāraḥ pañcakonyaḥ punar api caturas tatvato maṇḍaledaṃ |

saṃsṛṣṭaṃ yena tasmai namataṃ(!) gurutaraṃ bhairavaṃ śrīkujeśaṃ ||

śrīmad dhimavataḥ pṛṣṭhe trikūṭasikharāntagaṃ |

santānapuramadhyastham aṇekā(!)kārabhūṣiṇaṃ |

(tṛsratresu) triḥprakāran nu triśaktitriguṇojjvalaṃ |

candrasūryakṛtā(śeṣam) vahnidedīpyavarccasaṃ |

trisandhyāveṣṭitan divyaṃ triprakārapathānvitaṃ |

dvārapālatrayopetaṃ trikapāṭārgalānvitaṃ |

anekaratnasandīptaṃ udyānavanamaṇḍitaṃ |

vasantaguṇasaṃpannaṃ satatānandapūritaṃ ||

santānabhuvanan divyaṃ divyādivyai(!) nisevitaṃ |

tatra taṃ bhuvanan divyaṃ vyaktāvyaktaṃ sanātanaṃ ||

kāryakāraṇabhedena kiñcit kālam apekṣayā |

tiṣṭhate bhairavīśānaṃ maunam ādāya niścalaṃ ||

(fol.1v1-6 )

End

gajaś caiva tu kūṣmāṇḍapalāṇḍuñ ca viśeṣataḥ |

paryyuṣitācchālitahyagaruṃ pippallyāḥ talās(!) tathā |

kṛṣṇacchāgo mahānetrī palalaṃ meṣātmakaṃ smṛtaṃ |

sāmarthajñavidānāñ ca iti pūjā prakīrttitāḥ ||

siddhadravyasamākhyātaṃ prasaṅgād yoginīkule ||

nānena rahitā siddhi(!) bhuktimuktiṃ na vidyate |

nirācapadaṃ(!) hy etat tadbhedaparamaṃ smṛtaṃ || ❁ ||

(fol. )

Colophon

iti kulālikāmnāye śrīmatkubjikāmate samasta(jñānāvavopaścaryā) nirddeśo nāma

pañcaviṃśatimaḥ paṭalaḥ samāptaḥ || ❁ || ❖ samvat ā # o phālguṇa kṛsṇa ||                                                                                (fol.156v5-6)
