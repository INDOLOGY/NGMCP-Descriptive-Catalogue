Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ tasmai namo vighnavināśanāya ||

anādibhavasaṃbhūta pāpapraśamakāraṇaṃ |

smaraṇaṃ vāsudevasya yasya tasmai namo namaḥ ||

manvādi smṛtimālā .. suvivicya yathākramaṃ |

kriyate bhavadevena prāyaścittanirūpaṇaṃ ||

tatra prāyaścitte nirūpanī(!)ye gurutvāt paṃcamahāpātakaprāyaścittanirūpaṇapurassaramevetanraprāyaścittanirūpaṇe karttavye manvādivacanakramānurodhe prathamaṃ brahmavadhaprāyaścittam eva nurūpyate || || (fol. 1v1–5)


End

gavām āhāranirmmuktair yyavaiḥ śaktūn sanannataḥ |

yāmyaṃ kṛcchaṃ samuddiṣṭam āsanaśrutipāragaiḥ ||

sarvvakāmapradaṃ hyetat sarvvakalmaṣanāśanaṃ ||

atrāpi pañcadhenavaḥ || iti kṛcchraparicchedaḥ || || (fol. 115v5–8)


Colophon

iti bālavatabhībhujaṅgāyananāmnā bhaṭṭaśrībhavadevasya kṛtau prāyaścittaprakaraṇaṃ samāptaṃ || samāptaś cāyaṃ granthaḥ ||

❖ bhujanetragahe varṣe cāṣāḍha śuklapakṣake |

tithau sūryyasya nakṣatraṃ haste ʼrkkasya dine prare |

bhavadevakṛtaṃ granthaṃ bhavānīśaṅkaro dvijaḥ |

lilekha brāhmaṇahitaṃ bhavānīpatisevakaḥ ||

samvat 822 āṣāḍha śukla saptamī hastanakṣatra āditya vāra thva kuhnu śrībhavānīśaṅkarabrāhmaṇena bhavadevasmṛti cāyadhunakā dina juro || (fols. 115v7–116r3)
