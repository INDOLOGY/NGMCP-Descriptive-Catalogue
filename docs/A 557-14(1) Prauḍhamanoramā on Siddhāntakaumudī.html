Manuscript Features

Exps. 2–5t contain the text from the commentary on the last part of akathitaṃ ca (Pāṇ. 1.4.51) to the commentary on abhiniviśaś ca (Pāṇ. 1.4.47) in the Kārakaprakaraṇa chapter. The text starts on exp. 4t and ends on exp. 2.
Exps. 4b–5t contain the last part of the commentary on maghavā bahulam (Pāṇ. 6.4.138) to the initial part of the commentary on tho nthaḥ (Pāṇ. 7.1.87) in the Halantapuṃliṅga chapter. The text starts on exp. 5t and ends on exp. 4b. On exps. 5b–6t is commentary on the last part of tho nthaḥ to the last part of ṣṇāntā ṣaṭ (Pāṇ. 1.1.24). The text starts on exp. 5b and ends on exp. 6t.
