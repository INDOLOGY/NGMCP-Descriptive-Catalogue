Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇe+ya namaḥ || ||

śrīgurubhyo nama (!)

nāke nākeśamukhyāḥ sukharanivahāḥ saṃti yenaṃtasaṃkhyā

nākhyām ākhyātam īṣāṃ katham api ca manaḥ pūrvakaṃ vāk ⟨a⟩ ma(2)dīyā |

ekaṃ hi tv ekadamtaṃ sakalasuraśiraḥ sadya (!) saṃgharśitāghriṃ

śīghraṃ bhakeṣṭāsiddhipradam (!) iha hi suraṃ sādaraṃ taṃ namāmi || 1 ||

mallārikulanāyakaṃ ra(3)vimukhān kheṭāṃś ca natvā guroḥ

smṛttvā pādayugaṃ hy avāpya ca tataḥ kiṃcit subodhāṃśakaṃ ||

māllārir grahalāghavasya kurute ṭīkāṃ sa sadvāsanāṃ

yasmā(4)d alpamatiś ca kuṃṭhitamatiḥ sthāt pūrva(vaicitryataḥ) || 2 || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

kiciṃt avabodhāṃśakaṃ jñānalava (!) labdhvā prajñā idaṃ karaṇaṃ spaṣṭārthaṃ kṛtee nānādyaṃkā+(9) vicitraṃ arthena ba(hu)laṃ ca cachuś (!) tat prakarot | kṛtavān ityarthaḥ | it pūrvaśakāt grahānyanaprakāraḥ | graṃthālaṃkāraś ca kṛtaḥ  (fol. 105v8–9)

Colophon
