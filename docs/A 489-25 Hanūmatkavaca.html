Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrīrādhākṛṣṇāya namaḥ ||

oṃ asya śrīhanumaṃtapaṃcamukhīkavacastotramaṃtrasya śrīrāmacaṃdraṛṣiḥ anuṣṭupchaṃdaḥ paṃcamukhīhanumānadevatā hanumāneti bijaṃ vāyuputrāyeti śaktiḥ oṃ aṃjanisutāyeti kīlakaṃ śrīrāmacaṃdraprasādasiddhyarthe sakalalokapakārārthaṃ paṃcamukhīhanumaṃtakavacastotrajape viniyogaḥ || (fol. 1v1–7)

End

aṣṭavāraṃ paṭhe[n] nityaṃ iṣṭakāmārthasiddhaye

navavāra[ṃ] paṭhe[n] nityaṃ rājyabhogasamācaret ||

daśavāraṃ paṭhe[n] nityaṃ trailokyajñānadarśanaṃ ||

ekādaśavāraṃ jape[n] nityaṃ sarvasiddhi bhaven naraḥ ||

idaṃ kavacasmaraṇenaiva mahābalasamācaret || (fol. 5v3–7)

Colophon

iti śrīpaṃcamukhīhanumaṃtakavacaṃ saṃpūrṇaṃ || ||

śubhaṃ bhavatu || śrīniṃvādityāya namo namaḥ || || (fol. 5v7–8)
