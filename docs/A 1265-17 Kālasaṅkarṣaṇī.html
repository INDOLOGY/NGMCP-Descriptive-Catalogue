Beginning

❖ oṁ namaḥ śrīdevyai ||

yasyāṃ krīḍārtham akhilaṃ brahmāṇḍaṃ kaṇḍukāyate ||

tāṅ kālagrāsarasikāṃ kālasaṃkarṣaṇīn na(2)maḥ ||

śrīpārvvatyuvāca |

deva deva jagannātha śaśāṅkakṛta śekhara ||

mantrapūjā sādhanañ ca śrutaṃ sarvvam aśeṣataḥ | (3)

idānīṃ śrotum icchāmi dhāraṇāyantum uttamaṃ ||

yasya dhāraṇamātreṇa trailokya vijayī bhavet ||

śrībhagavānuvāca || (fol. 1v1-3)

End

khaṭvāṅgāyudhasthas taṃ ca ghorakālānaropamā ||

devīrūpe parāsarvvā sarvvāṇām iṣasampaḍhā ||

yathaivaikā tathā (8)sarvvā yathā sarvvāsta..kiṃkā ||

bhinnābhinnasvarūpeṇa dheyāḥ sarvvāḥ sadaiva hi || (fol. 54v7-8)
