Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṁ namo bhagavate vāsudevāya ||

namaskṛtyāprameyāya, viṣaṇave kūrmmarūpiṇe [|]

purāṇaṃ saṃpravakṣyāmi yad uktaṃ viśvayoninā ||

satrānte sūtam anaghaṃ, naimiṣe yā maharṣayaḥ ||

purāṇasaṃhitāṃ puṇyaṃ papraccha romaharṣaṇaṃ ||

tvayā sūta mahābuddhe bhagavān brahmavittamaḥ ||

itihāsapurāṇārthaṃ vyāsaḥ samyag upāsitaḥ ||

tasya te sarvvaromāṇi vacasā hṛṣitāni yat ||

dvaipāyanasya tu bhavāṃs tato vai romaharṣaṇaḥ ||

bha⟪‥⟫vantam eva bhagavān vyājahāra svayaṃ prabhuḥ || (fol. 1v1–4)


End

tasmau(!) vyāsāya gurave sarvajñāya maharṣaya(!) ||

parāśarāya śāntāya namo nārāyaṇātmane ||

yasmāt saṃjāyate kṛtsnaṃ tatraiva pravilīyate ||

namas tasyai(!) sureśāya viṣṇave kūrmmarūpiṇe || || || || (fol. 226r7–8)


Colophon

iti śrīkūrmmapurāṇe ṣaṭsāhasryāṃ saṃhitāyāṃ saṭcatvāriṃśatamo ʼdhyāya[ḥ] || || bhāgadvayasahitakūrmmapurāṇa[ṃ] samāptaṃ || || (fol. 226r9)
