Excerpts

Beginning of the root text

śrīkṛṣṇāya gurave gaṇeśāya namaḥ ||    ||

oṃ namaḥ śrīparamahaṃsāsvāditacaraṇakamalacinmakaraṃdāya ||
bhaktajanamānasanivāsāya śrīrāmāya || 1 ||

vāgīśāyasya vadane lakṣmiir yasya ca vakṣasi ||
yasyās te hṛdaye saṃvitaṃ nṛsiṃham ahaṃ bhaje || 2 || (fol. 1v7–8)

Beginning of the commentary

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

gaṇeśaṃ vighnahartāraṃ bhāratī vāk prakāśinīm ||
gurūn hṛdvāṃta (!) haṃsāś ca daṇḍavat praṇamāmy ahaṃ || 1 || (fol. 1v1)

End of the root text

|| sūta uvāca ||

evam ābhāṣitaḥ pṛṣṭhaḥ sarājñā ślakṣṇayā girā ||
pratyabhāṣitadharmajño bhagavān vādarāyaṇiḥ || 40 || (fol. 76r7–8)

End of the commentary

evam anena prakāreṇa ābhāṣita (!) abhimukhīkṛtaḥ pṛṣṭadharmajño vādarāyaṇiḥ śukraślakṣṇayā madhurayā girāḥ pratyabhāṣata vakṣyamāṇaprakāreṇa praśnottaraṃ jñānam iti śeṣaḥ || 40 ||

śrīmadbhāgavate bhaktijñāno yodyāt (!) lakṣaṇe || śrīdharoktā ʼrthaskaṃdhe noccāṭito (!) mayā || 1 || (fol. 76r13–14)

Sub-colophon

|| iti śrībhāgavate prathamaskaṃdhe subodhinīṭīkāyāṃ ekonaviṃśo dhyāyaḥ || 19 || (fol. 76r14)

Colophon

iti śrībhāgavate mahāpurāṇe prathamaskaṃdhe ʼṣṭādaśasāhasryāṃ pāramahaṃsyāṃ saṃhitāyāṃ śukāgamanaṃ nāma ekonaviṃśo dhyāyaḥ || 19 ||    || śubham ||    || (fol. 76r8–9)
