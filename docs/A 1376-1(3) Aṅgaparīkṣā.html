Manuscript Features

1. The MTM contains following texts:

(1) Sahadevanaṃ dayakā chāya (exp. 2–5)

(2) Dharmalakṣmīsaṃvāda (exps. 6t–11b)

(3) Aṅgaparīkṣā (exps. 11b–17t)

(4) Vaidyakacikitsā (exps. (exp. 20 left–34)

2. Exps 13 and 14 are two exposures of the same folio.

3. There are some notes on Saptavāra and Sattvavirodha after this text.

Excerpts

Beginning

❖ namo mañjughokhāya namo 3 ||

moḍa tuka(4)yā, mānyaṃ lābha dayio, jao hnasapota tukayā, strī lābha dayu, khao hnasapata (5) tukayā jolaṇa kayu || nipā tukayā, lakṣmī rābha dayu || jao mikhā tu(6)tasā dhana rābha dayu || khao mikhā tutasā, nugvaḍasa vyādhi madayu || (exp. 11b3–6)

End

khao tutasā pāhāna oio || barahni tutasā, jao tuka bhiṅa || khao tutasā (5) pāhāna oyio || byāli yirasā, jao mikhā tutasā mabhiṅa || khao mikhā tutasā bhiṅa (6) bācāsa jao mikhā tusā rājāna mānya yāyio juro || (exp. 13t4–6)

Colophon

iti aṃgaparikṣā samāpta || (exp. 13t6)
