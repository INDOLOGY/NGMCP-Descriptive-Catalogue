Manuscript Features

Fols. 8 and 10 are Missing. There are seven extra folios in the end, which contain visualisations of various śaiva deities and part of a devyaṣṭakastotra. Some folios are broken.

Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrīdevy uvāca ||

sādākṣa(!)tatvamaṇḍalaṃ pramāṇātītagocaram ||

yad uktaṃ sarvaśiddhāntais tvatprasādāvadhāritaṃ ||

antaḥkaulā bahiśsaivā śaucācāreṣu brāhmaṇā (!) |

tribhi (!) kkarma tva〇yā deva suguptaṃ prakaṭīkṛtaṃ |

jñātan tam astrarājaṃ ca matakāryavibhedataḥ ||

idānī (!) śrotum icchāmi vidyāsadanapūjanaṃ |

triṣv oliṣu ca sānva(yaṃ) yathāvidhy anupūrvaśaḥ ||

āsanaṃ mūrttimūlañ ca mudrāvṛkṣādhidaivataṃ || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

āgameṣu bahutve pi karttavyaṃ svagurumaṃ (!) ||

kathitam va (!) deveśi mayā śrutaṃ yathā 〇 priye ||

na kathed yasya kasyāpi rahasyaṃ paramādbhutaṃ ||

guhyād guhyataraṃ kārya jananījāragarbhavan ||

rakṣaṇīyaṃ praya〇tnena caurebhyo dravinaṃ yathā || ❁ || (fol. 15r2–4)

Colophon

iti śrīcaturāśītisahasrasaṃhitāyāṃ kulālikāmnāye śrīsiddhapīṭhāvatāre śrīmatkukārāmate vidyāpīṭhārccanaprakaraṇan nāma pañcatriṃśatimapaṭalaḥ samāptaḥ |||| (fol. 15r4–5)
