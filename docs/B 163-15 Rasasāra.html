Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ |

śrīgaṇeśaṃ śivaṃ kāḷīṃ vāgīśvarīṃ tathā guruṃ ||

bahugada vināśāya dhanvaṃtariṃ namāmyaham || 1 ||

rogarāṭ sarvabhūtānāṃ maṃtakṛddāruṇojvaraḥ ||

tasmāt tasya viśeṣeṇa yataste prathamaṃ bhiṣag || 2 ||

kaṣāya guṭikā karṇa vīryyanāhyarddhavarṣataḥ ||

tasmād vaidyopakārāya rasameva pravakṣate || 3 || (fol.1v1–3)
