Excerpts

Beginning of the root text:


nārāyaṇaṃ namaskṛtya naraṃ caiva narottamaṃ ||


devīṃ sarasvatīṃ caiva tato jayam udīrayet || 1 ||



yudhiṣṭhira uvāca ||



dharmmāḥ pitāmahenoktā rājadharmmāś citāḥ śubhāḥ


dharmam āśramiṇāṃ śreṣṭhaṃ vaktum arhasi pārthiva || 1 || (fol. 1v6–7)


Beginning of the commentary:


śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ


śrīgopālāya namaḥ


taraṇi mukuranetro trottejanābhaṃ (!) śarīrapratikṛtim anumāpābhunni (!) caṃhāṃtarāmaḥ


(schiradagaṇḍamasaṃgaṃ) yaṃnapatyatyathāttaṃ tadabhayam amṛte satpratyagīḍe gavendra || 1 || (fol. 1v1–2)


End of the root text:


sa ca kila kṛtaniścayo dvijo bhujagapatimatiha (!) |


rātāvakṛtyaḥ pamāneyamasaho (!) vanāṃtaraṃ parigaṇito chaśilāśataḥ praviṣṭaḥ 9 (fol. 570r6–7)


End of the commentary:


vyākaron maukṣadharmānma (!) nīlakaṃṭhaḥ samāsanaḥ


anena prīyatāṃ devo girijāpati ʼvyayaḥ (!) 2 (fol. 570r11–12)


Sub-colophon:


iti śrīmatpadavākya†mamāṇājña†maryādādhuraṃdharacaturdharavaṃśāvasataṃsaśrīgovidasūrisūnoḥ (!)


nīlakaṃṭhasya kṛtau bhāratabhāvadīpe mokṣadharmārthaprakāśaḥ samāptim agamat | (fol. 570r12–13)


Colophon:


iti śrīmahābhārate śatasāhasryāṃ saṃhitāyāṃ vaiyāsikyāṃ śāṃtiparvaṇi mokṣadharmeṣu


uṃchavvatyupākhyānaṃ samāptamiti samāptaṃ cedaṃ śāṃtiparva (!) śubham astu asyānuśāsanikaṃ parva (!) bhaviṣyati || (fol. 570r7–9)
