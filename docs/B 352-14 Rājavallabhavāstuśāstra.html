The MS is probably written with a different hand after fol. 13r.

Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

ānandaṃ vo gaṇeśārkaviṣṇugaurīmaheśvrāḥ
devāḥ kuryuḥ śriyaṃ saukhyam ārogyaṃ ca gṛhe sadā 1

de[vaṃ] namāmi girijātmajam ekadantaṃ
sindūracarcitavapuḥ suviśālaśuṇḍam
nāgena maṇḍitatanuṃ yutasiddhibuddhyaṃ(!)
sevyaṃ suroraganaraiḥ sakalārthasiddhyai 2

yā brahmādyair alakṣyā tribhuvananamitā brahmaputrīśi[[vā]]dyā
brahmā viṣṇuś ca rudraḥ praṇamati bahuśo yāṃ sadānandarūpām
vāṇī caitanyarupe ca vasati sakalaprāṇinidrāsudhātṛṭ (!)<ref>breaking of meter</ref>
sā nityaṃ suprasannā vitaratu vibhavaṃ ⟪‥‥‥⟫ viśvarūpā ca loke 3 || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

śrīmedapāṭhe nṛpakuṃbhakarṣas
tad ahnirājīvaparā[[ga]]sevī
samaṃdunākhyo bhuvi sūtradhāraḥ
kṛtomuṃ(!)nā bhūpativallabho[ʼ]yaṃ 44

tonati (!) gurubhaktyā bhāratīpādatuṣṭyā
munimatam idam uktaṃ vāstuśāstraṃ suvṛttaṃ
gaṇitam api ca sāraṃ śākunaṃ sārabhūtaṃ
bhavatu bhavatu yogyaṃ viśvakarmaprasādāt 45 (fol. 32v3–5)

Colophon

iti śrīsūtradhāramaṇḍanaviracitāyāṃ(!) vāstuśāstre rājavallabhe śākunalakṣaṇaṃ nāma caturdaśamaṃ(!)prakaraṇaṃ 14

śrir astu śubham astu

yāval lavaṇasamudro
yāvantakṣatramaṃḍito meruḥ
yāvac caṃdrādityau
tāvad idaṃ pustkaṃ jayatu 1

śrīsūtradhāradāmodareṇa likhitam idaṃ śubhaṃ bhūyāt ||

haddācakraṃ pravakṣyāmi sūryabhāc caṃdrabhe phalam (fol. 32v5–9)
