brahmā ta(6)dā prāha kṛtāṃjalis taṃ

tathāpi dā(7)sasya mamākhileśa

kṛtaṃ vaco bhaktaparosi vidvan

(t)vaṃ bhrātṛbhir vaiṣṇavam ekam ādyam

praviśya dehaṃ paripāhi devān 54 || ○ || (exp. 329.fol. 53v5–7)

«End of the commentary:»

he parātman parameśvara jagatsṛṣṭisthitilayakarttām (!) evety arthaḥ tvam aiśam ekaṃ nijaṃ tattvaṃ brahmarūpaṃ jā(9)nāsi 53

nanv evaṃ brahmajñasya brahmarūpasya kathaṃ śarīraparigraho ta āha tathāpīti bhaktānugrahārthaṃ māyayā sarvam idaṃ kṛtam iti bhā(10)vaḥ ato vaiṣṇavaṃ dehaṃ praviśya devān pāhi rakṣa 54 || (exp.329.fol. 53v8-10)
