Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

atha saṃkṣepato grahaśāntir(!)vidhir likhyate

skandapurāṇe

sukhopaviṣṭhaṃ gāryan tu kauśikaḥ paripṛcchati

nikhilāṃ grahaśāmtim bho bhagavan vaktum arhasi 1

gārgya uvāca

devadānavagaṃdharvā yakṣarākṣasakiṃnarā(!)

pīḍitā grahapīḍābhiḥ kiṃ punar bhuvi mānavāḥ 2

śanaiścareṇa saudāso naramāṃse niyojitaḥ

rāhuāṇā pīḍito rājan nalo bhrānto mahītale

aṅgārakavirodhena rāmo rāṣṭrad vivāsitaḥ

aṣṭamena śaśāṅkena hiraṇyakāśipur hataḥ 4

raniṇā saptamasthena hato rājasuyodhanaḥ

pāṇḍavā budhapīḍāyāṃ vikarmaṇi niyojitā(!)

ṣāṣṭenośanisā(!) yuddhe hiraṇākṣo nipātitaḥ

ete cānye bahabo grahadoṣaiḥ supīḍitāḥ

atha prayoga(!)

kusayavajalāny ādaya

adyetyādidedakālau smṛtvā amukasarmaṇo vivāhakarmaṇi nirvvighnakāmo navagrahamaṣenāha(!) kṣayeti(!) saṃkalpaḥ

pūjopakaraṇāny upaniya(!)

oṃ adyetyādidesakālau smṛtvā navagrahamakhe nirvighnārthaṃ gaṇe(!) pūjanam ahaṃ kariṣet(!) iti saṃkalpaḥ(fol. 1v1-2r7)

End

oṃ yeṣate niṛta iti varūriṣi(!) nirito(!) devatā yayaunirīta(!) pritaye(!) ājyahome caruhomaviniyoga(!) || yaṣate nirīte bhāṣadtvaṃ juṣa svāhā idaṃ niritaye(!) atra praṇīto || 4 || imameti sephaṛṣi(!)varuṇo devatā gāyaṭṛ(!) chaṃdo varutrapritaye(!) ājyahome caruhome viniyoga(!)|| idaṃ me rācake svāhā | idaṃ varutraṇāya || atra praṇitodakasprsa(!)|| 5 || vāto veti gandharvā ṛṣayo vāyor devatā usnik(!)chaṃdo vāyupritaye(!) ājyahome caruhome viniyoga(!)/// (fol.30v3-9)
