Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīmahāgaṇeśāyai namaḥ ||

brāhmaṇa, ārccāryya, jośi, thvatesyana sthila hārāva (2) vayāva |

uttala soya || majilasā, pūrvva soyaṃ teva || sārccayana dakṣiṇa ||

bali pāṭa 1 madhya(3)sa kṣatra mūlana yāya ||

ṅuva bali pūrvvādikramana, brahmāṇyādina ṅuyake ||

pāṭha śāntikā(4)dhyāya || ||

arghapātra || jalāpātra || pañcabali || gaṇa vaṭuka ||

thvate patavāsana coyāva (5) jiyake || || (fol. 1v, ll. 1-5)

End

ai 5 a(fol. 39v5)mbe pūrvvagataṃ bhagavatī caitanyarūpātmikā

jñānechā vahulā tathā hariharau, brahmā ma(6)rici trayaṃ |

bhāsvad bhairava paṃcakaṃ tadanu ca śrīyoginīpaṃcakaṃ

candrākkaucimarīci ṣaṭka(7)manalaṃ māṃ pātu nityaṃ śrīkujā || ||

bali visarjjana yāya || || purvvādikramena || || (fol. 40r1)

aṣṭapīṭhasa bali choya || ||
