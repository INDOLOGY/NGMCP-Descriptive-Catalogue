Beginning

namo lokanāthāya ||

natvā guru triratnaṃ ca kathayāmi samāsataḥ (2)

aṭṭhamīvratramāhātmyaṃ nepālobhāṣayā mahat || 1 ||

Buddhagayāsa bo(3)dhimaṇḍapa mahāvihārasa jayaśrī bhikṣusena āgyā dayakara || kurkuṭā(4)rāma vihārasa upagupta bhikṣusenaṃ aśoka rājā pramukhaṃ jhimacyā la(5)ka sabhālokayāta āgyā dayakara || ṅaṅo aneka aṣṭamīvratrayā(fol. 2r1) kathā kane dhuno āo virupa rājā udhāra juoguri aṣṭamīvratrayā ma(2)himā kane || (fols. 1v, ll. 1-2r2)

End

thvanaṃriguriṃ prajāloka kālamṛtyu juyā(fol. 98r3)o cakravartti rājā juraṃguriṃ devaputra juraṃguriṃ sukhāvatī thenaṃguri mo(4)kṣa juraṃguriṃ tathāgata... gurīṃ bodhisattva pratyeka buddha thvate tā(5)prakāranaṃ thaotha(kāmunāna pada)vi rātaṃ || upavāsa kuśa mahārājā(6)naṃ aṭṭhamī upavāsa yāṅa sa(kara) (pra)jāloka pratipālanā yānā rajāyi yā(fol.98v1)ka juro || (fols. 98r, ll. 2-v1)

Colophon

iti śrīdivyāvadānordhṛṭāṣtamīmāhātme vīrakuśāvadā(fol.98v2)naṃ samāptaṃ ||
