Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||
śrīsarasvatyai namaḥ ||
śrīgurave namaḥ ||

śrīnārada uvāca ||

śrutaṃ prakṛtikhaṇḍaṃ tad amṛtārṇavam uttamaṃ ||
sarvotkṛṣṭaṃm īpsitaṃ ca mūḍhānāṃ jñānavarddhanaṃ ||

adhunā śrotum ichā(!)mi gaṇeśakhaṇḍam īśvara ||
taj janmacaritaṃ nṛṇāṃ sarvamaṃgalamaṃgalaṃ ||

kathaṃ vrajet suraśreṣṭhaḥ pārvatyā udare vibho ||
devī kena prakāreṇa lalābha tādṛśaṃ [[sutaṃ]] || (fol. 1v1–3)

Sub-colophon

iti śrībrahmavaivartte mahāpurāṇe nārāyaṇanāradasaṃvāde gaṇeśakhaṇḍe prathamo dhyāyaḥ || 1 || (fol. 2v5)

iti śrībrahmavai° gaṇeśakhaṇḍe dvitīyo dhyāyaḥ || 2 || (fol. 3r10–11)

iti śrībrahmavai° gaṇeśakhaṇḍe śivapārvatīsaṃvāde tṛtīyo dhyāyaḥ || 3 || (fol. 4r6–7)

…

iti śrībrahmavai° gaṇeśakhaṇḍe° durgāstotraṃ paṃcacatvāriṃśatitamo dhyāyaḥ || 45 || (fol. 74v2–3)

End

mṛtavatsā kākavaṃdhyā brahmāt putraṃ labhed dhruvaṃ ||
yā dūṣitā ʼdūṣitāyā yā ca śuddhā labhet sakham(!) ||

kātsryaṃ(!) ca brahmavaivarttaṃ śrutvā yal labhate phalaṃ ||
tat phalaṃ labhate marttyaḥ śrutvaidaṃ khaṇḍam uttamaṃ ||

vāṃchāṃ kṛtvā tu manasi śṛṇoti paramāstikaḥ(!) ||
tasmai dadāti sarveṣṭaṃ suraśreṣṭho gaṇeśvaraḥ || 49 ||   || (fol. 75v10–76r1)

Colophon

iti śrībrahmavaivarte mahāpurāṇe nārāyaṇanāradasaṃvāde pūrve śrīgaṇeśajanmakhaṇḍe sūtaśaunakasaṃvāde tulasī gaṇapati parasparaśāpodbhavaśāpamomānaṃ(!) nāma ṣaṭcatvāriṃśatitamo dhyāyaḥ || 46 ||   || śubham ||   ||   ||   ||   ||   ||   ||   ||   ||   || (fol. 76r1–2)
