Manuscript Features

The first folio of the manuscript contains the content of the manuscript.


In the top hand margin, there is the text of dvādaśanāmastotra:


oṁ gāyatryai namaḥ ||


prathame savitā devī dvitīyaṃ parameśvarī ||

tṛtīyaṃ jñānarūpā ca caturthaṃ laksam ucyate || 1 ||


pañamaṃ siddhiputrī ca ṣaṣṭhaṃ ca bodharūpiṇī ||

saptaṃ dīnakaṃ nāma aṣṭamaṃ vijayā tathā ||


auṣadhaṃ navamaṃ nāma daśamam mukhaśodhinī ||

nirgandhakā daśadhāma dvādaśaṃ ślokatāriṇī ||


dvādaśaitāni nāmāni nāmāṃ japan nityaṃ yato budhaḥ ||

koṭiphalan nityaṃ ānandaṃ ʼtra(!) mucyate ||


śrībrahmapurāṇe brahmanāradasamvāde śrīgāyatrīdvādaśanāmastotram

svasti śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ


śrīnārada uvāca⟨ḥ⟩

sarvapāpaharaṃ deva kenāvidyā nivarttate
