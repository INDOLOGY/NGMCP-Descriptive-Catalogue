Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||
oṃ namo bhagavate vāsudevāya ||    ||
prapadye devam īśānaṃ śāśvataṃ dhruvam avyayaṃ ||
maheśvaraṃ mahātmānaṃ viśvasya jagataḥ patim ||
prabhuṃ bhūtabhaviṣyasya sāmpratasya ca satpatiṃ ||
jñānam apratimaṃ tasya vairāgyaṃ ca jagatpateḥ || …
brahmavāyumaheśebhyo namaskṛtvā samāhitaḥ ||
ṛṣīṇāṃ ca variṣṭhāya vaṣiṣṭhāya mahātmane ||
tannaptre cātiyaśase jātūkarṇyāya varṣaye ||
vāsaveyāya śucaye kṛṣṇadvaipāyanāya ca ||
purāṇaṃ saṃpravakṣyāmi brahmāṇḍaṃ vedasasmitaṃ || (fol. 1v1–3, 6–8)

End

hṛnmadhye vinyased devīm akhaṃḍajagadātmikāṃ ||
cakreśvarī(!) hṛdi nyasya kṛtvā cakrasamarpaṇaṃ ||
mudrāṃ pradarśya yonyākhyāṃ sarvānandam anunyaset
ity ātmani nyastacakraḥ sākṣād devī bhaviṣyati ||    || (fol. 105v1–3)

Colophon

iti śrībrahmāṃḍottarapurāṇe hayagrīvāgastyasaṃvāde lalitākhyāne nyāsavidhir nāma vilāso ʼdhyāyaḥ || 40 ||    || śubham astu sarvadā samāptam ||    || śubhm || (fol. 105v3–4)
