Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

vāsudevaṃ ramākāṃtaṃ natvā gālaukṣi(!)bhāskaraḥ ||

kurute jaimininayepraveśāyārthasaṃgraham || 1||

atha paramakāruṇiko bhagavānjaiminir dharmavivekāya dvādaśalakṣaṇīṃ praṇināya tatrādau dharmajijñāsāṃ sūtrayāmāsa || athāto dharmajijñāseti || (fol. 1v1‒3)


End

so yaṃ dharmo yadudyeśya(!) vihi⁅ta⁆s taduddeśena kriyamāṇas taddetuḥ ||

īśvarārpaṇabuddhyā kriyamāṇas tu niśreya(!)sahetuḥ

na ca tadarpaṇabuddhyānuṣṭhāne pramāṇābhāvaḥ ||

yat karoṣi yadaśnāsi yajjuhoṣi dadāsi yat ||

yat tapasyasi kauṃteya tat kṛṣva madarpaṇam

iti bhagavadgītāsmṛter eva pramāṇatvāt ||

smṛticaraṇe tatpramāṇyasya śrutimūlakatvena vyavasthāpanād iti śīvam || ||

bālānāṃ sukhabodhāya bhāskareṇa sumedhasā

racito yaṃ samāsena jaiminīyārthasaṃgrahaḥ || 1 || || (fol. 17v7‒19r3)


Colophon

iti śrīmahopādhyāyalaugākṣibhāskaraviracitaṃ pūrvamīmāṃsārthasaṃgrahanāmakaṃ prakaraṇam agāccaramavarṇadhvaṃsam || ||

svapāṭhārthaṃ sanmatinā durgādattena sūriṇā ||

likhitaṃ sūkṣmam etad vai mīmāṃsāmatidīpakam || 1 ||

vārāṇasīṃ nivasatāṃ niyataṃ hi jahnuputrī taṭodakadhutākhilakalmaṣāṇām ||

pādābjasevanapareṇa mayā gurūṇāṃ kiṃ kiṃ na sādhitam adhītam idaṃ tu tūrṇam || 2 ||

cha || cha || cha || cha || iti saṃvat 1921 || ravināthena tajjena(!) asmin granthe suṭippaṇam || tat kṛtaṃ nyastam etac ca śodhitaṃ dvijahetave || 1 || saṃvat 1924 śubham (fol. 19r3‒8)
