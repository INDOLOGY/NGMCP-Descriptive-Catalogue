Excerpts

Beginning

śrīkṛṣṇāya namaḥ ||    ||

bhāvārthāḥ karmaśabdās tebhyaḥ kriyā pratīye taiṣatyartho vidhīyate |
prathamedhyāye dharmasya pramāṇaṃ nirupitaṃ prathame pāde codanāyā anaṃtaraṃ tvarthavādamaṃtranāmadheyasmṛtyā cārāṇāṃ vākyaśeṣasāmarthyayoś ca saṃdigdhārthanirṇaye prāmāṇyam uktaṃ | evaṃ samastenādhyāyena dharmapramāṇānirupaṇāt kathaṃ lakṣaṇo dharma ityayam (!) aṃśo vicāritaḥ | (fol. 1v1–4)

End

agnihotrādivākyam ekasya puruṣasya vihitam eva maparasyāpi (!) vidhattena karmātaram evam atrāpyadhyetṛbhedāt tān prati sarvāṇy eva vākyāny ekam eva karma vidadhati na karmāṃtaram evam ekādaśadvādaśakpālayor ubhayor upatti śiṣṭatvānna valāvalaṃ tasmādbhedaḥ || śabdāṃtarābhyāsaguṇaiḥ sasaṃkhyair nāmnā ʼtidvareṇa ca sāpavādaḥ | bhedaḥ kriyāṇām iti sādhitoya mataḥ paraṃ tadviniyogarcitā || (fol. 46v10–12 and 47r1)

Colophon

upādhyāyaśrīpārthasārathimiśrakṛtau śāstradīpikāyāṃ dvitīyasyādhyāyasya caturthapādaḥ | dvitīyādhyāyasamāptaḥ (fol. 47v1–2)
