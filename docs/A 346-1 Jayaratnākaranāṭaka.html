Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśo jayati ||    ||
sphūrjjaścandrakalā naṭī suradhunīdhārā sakhī nirmmalā
dīpaś cāʼhiśiromaṇir jjavanikā śārddūlacarmmāmbaram ||
nandī yatra vidūṣako gaṇapatiḥ śrīsūtradhāraḥ svayaṃ,
nityaṃ tāṇḍavamaṇḍale vijayate śrīnṛtyanāthaḥ śivaḥ || 1 ||
nāṃdyante sūtradhāraḥ || alam ativistareṇa || samastabhūmaṇḍalanagaratilakanepālanagareśvaraśrīmanmahārājādhirājaraṇabahādūrapaṃcānana[[sya]] satsabhāṃ praviśya, ... (fol. 1v)

End

natvādyaṃ girijāṃ guruṃ gaṇapatiṃ śrīnīlakaṇṭhaṃ tato
goviṃdaṃ taraṇiṃ tathaiva pitaraṃ mātāmahaṃ mātaram ||
vidyālaṃkṛtaśaktivallabhakaviślāghyena satprītaye
nepālaṃdrayaśo(!) vinirmitam idaṃ bhūyān mudā nāṭakam || 11||
goviṃdasya yathā ramā priyatamā gaṃgādharasyāmbikā,
tārā devaguroḥ punar ddinamaṇeś chāyā vidho rohiṇī ||
svāhāgneś ca pulomajā maghavataḥ satpaṇḍitānāṃ tathā,
vidyāpallavaśaktivallabhakaver bhūyād iyaṃ satkṛtiḥ || 12 || (fols. 96rv)

Colophon

iti śaktivallabhabhaṭṭācāryyaviracite śrīraṇabahādūrasāhapratāpavarṇane jayaratnākaranāmanāṭake ekādaśakallolaḥ ||    || samāpto yaṃ jayaratnākaranāma nāṭakagranthaḥ ||    || śubham bhūyāt ||    || (fol. 96v)
