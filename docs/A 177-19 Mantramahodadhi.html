śrigaṇeśāya namaḥ

praṇamya lakṣmīṃ nṛhariṃ mahāgaṇapatiṃ gurūṃ

taṃtrāṇy anekāny ālokya vakṣye maṃtramahodadhiṃ  1

prātar utthāya śirasi dhyātvā gurūpa(2)dāṃbujaṃ

smārtasaṃdhyāṃ maṃtrasaṃdhyāṃ kṛtvā devaṃ viciṃtaye(3)t 3 (fol. 1v1–3)

abde vikramato yāte vāṇavede nṛpaimiti (!)
