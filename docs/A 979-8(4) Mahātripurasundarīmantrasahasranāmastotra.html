Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namaḥ śrīgaṇeśāya || ||

śrīmahārājarājeśvarītripurasundaryyai namaḥ || ||

kailāśaśikhare ramye nānāratnopaśobhite |

kalpapādapamadhyasthe nānāpuṣpopaśobhite || 1 ||

munimaṇḍapamadhyasthe munigandharvvasevite |

taṃ kadācit sukhāsīnaṃ devadevaṃ jagadguruṃ || 2 || (exp. 17t1–4)

End

sarvvavedeṣu yatproktaṃ tatphalaṃ palikīrttitaṃ (!) |

śrutavān varavān putra dhanavān sarvvasampadaḥ || 188 ||

dehānte paramaṃ sthānaṃ yat surair api vallabhaṃ |

sa yāsyati na śaṃdehastavarājasya kīrttanāt || 189 || (exp. 38b1–4)

Colophon

iti śrīvāmakeśvaratantre harakumārasamvāde śrīmanmahātripurasundarīmantrasahaśranāmastotraṃ saṃpūrṇṇaṃ || || (exp. 38b4–5)
