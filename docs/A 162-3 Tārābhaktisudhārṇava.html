Beginning

oṃ namaḥ śrīkṛṣṇakāli[[kā]]mahāgaṇeśebhyaḥ

saunāsīramaṇipravīṇajaladagrāmābhirāmacchaviṃ

nṛtyat pītapaṭadvayaikakapaṭaprodyat parāgojjvalaṃ ||

vaṃśīvādanakaitavapravilasad ga(2)mbhīradhīradhvaniṃ |

End

svagātrarudhiraṃ dātuṃ kṣatriyāder bhaved vidhiḥ ||

sātviko jīvahatyāṃ hi kadācid no caret ||

ikṣudaṇḍañ ca kuṣmāṇḍaṃ tathā ⟨m⟩anya phalādikam ||

kṣīrapiṇḍai(5)ḥ śālicūrṇaiḥ paśu (!) kṛtvā cared baliṃ ||

tattadphalaviśena (!) tattat paśum upānayet ||

kuṣmāṇḍaṃ mahiṣatvenac chagalatvena karkkaṭīṃ || 0 ||(fol. 227r4–5)
