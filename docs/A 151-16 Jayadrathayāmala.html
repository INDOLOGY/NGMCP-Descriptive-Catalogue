Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namaḥ śrīśivādibhyo gurubhyaḥ || ||

pāyādvo kālakālakṣyaraṇisamahā rāvaraudrāṭṭahāsā-

haṭṭāhaṭṭāyuvā(2)ś ca pravimathitamahābhairavā ghoradīptā ||

ekākṣīti prasarpad ravipathavigalad raśmicakrapravāhā

mudrāsphoṭāsphuṭau(3)ghasphuṭitadaśadiśā kālarātrī pracaṇḍā || 1 || (fol. 1v1–3)

«Sub-colophon:»

iti śrībhairavaśrotasi vidyāpīṭhe śiraśchede jayadrathayāmale mahā(9)tantre caturviṃśatisāhastre caturthaṣaṭke mahālakṣmīvidhau rasadvayasiddhipaṭalaḥ || || samāptoyaṃ mudrāṣa(1)ṭkajayadrathāvatāraḥ || (fol. 246r8–9 and fol. 246v1)

End

prapūjyamānaḥ surasiddhavṛndaiḥ

sad yoginībhiś ca tathāpi vīraiḥ ||

jegīyamāno (6) varavāraṇaughaiḥ

saṃlālyamānaś ca tathāpsarogaṇaiḥ ||

devī tathā sarvasurendravanyā

jagrāhadevāṅkagatāṃ cakāra ||

āliṅya (!) saṃcumbya (7) punaḥ punaiva

papau ca cāsyā adharau maheśaḥ ||

tato jaganmauligataṃ svalakṣmā (!)

rūḍhā maṇau devavaraś cakāra ||

tasyāḥ sutuṣtokhila(8)siddhidātā

sarvottamaḥ sarvasurendravandyaḥ || (fol. 246r5–8)
