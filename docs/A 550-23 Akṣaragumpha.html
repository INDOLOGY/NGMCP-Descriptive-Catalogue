śrīgaṇeśo vijayate ||      ||

lakṣmīpāṇisparśa

kāṅkṣann utsājilaṃ

tvaritan natakandharo harir jayati || 1 ||

anyonyekṣaṇajanita-

prakampaparikampitasuraughaḥ

pariṇayakālaḥ śriyaṃ diśatu || 2 ||

kovāruṇi ā oṇa aśiro hi ||
