Script Newari

Material palm-leaf

Size 31.0 x 4.0 cm

Folios 35

Manuscript Features

The following folios are extant: 1-35. Fols. 28-35 are slightly worm-eaten. On the back of fol.1 the title lehasamuccayaḥ has been inscribed in modern Nagari characters.
