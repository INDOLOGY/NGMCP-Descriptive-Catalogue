Excerpts

Beginning

|| śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || oṃ namo gaṇapataye ||

yenā ʼviyadvikaraṇair ākhyātaiḥ ddhātuloditaiḥ |

prakāśaiḥ saṃprakāśyaṃte kriyāḥ saṃstaumi gopatiṃ ||

kavikalpadrumaṃ ⟪tu⟫ pāṭhaṃ vivarituṃ nijaṃ |

dhātuvṛttiḥ kāvyakāmadhenur nāma vidhīyate || 2 ||

yatra prayogaḥ dhātunāmakramādit phalārthakaḥ |

śaptipos tvanitāṃ tulyaprayogāṇāṃ sabhedakāḥ || 3 ||

tatrādau graṃthakṛduciteṣṭadevatām ādityam upāste |

śabdākarakaragrāmam arthamaṃḍalamaṃḍalaṃ |

jñānātmānam namanādyaṃtam ādityaṃ tamupāsmahe | (fol. 1v1–4)

End

ṛṣati stutyāḥ || ṛśyaḥ | na śasvane | naśanābhiṣanugrahe | bhiṣak bheṣajaṃ puṣu bhajane | yoṣit | yuṣmat | lusa hiṃsane | lusabhaḥ ity asau dhātūnām iha sauśaṇāṃ dvicatvāriṃśadīritāḥ | iti dhātūn nirdhāryopasaṃharati | iti sphītaḥ saptadaśaśatyāpaṅkau na ṣaṣṭyā | dhātuskaṃdhair budhāḥ sevyaḥ kavikalpadrumaḥ phalan | saptadaśānāṃ śatānāṃ samāhāraḥ saptadaśasatī ṣaṭko naḥ ṣaṣṭho yasyās tayā dhātavaskaṃdhāḥ dhātuskaṃdhās tai kalan pratiskaṃdhaṃ prayogaḥ | atha graṃthakṛdātmānaṃ khyāpayaṃti vidvadvane śaśipyenabhiṣakveśevasanunāṃ teneveda padesthe navo pade vadviśeje nayaḥ || saṃpāditaḥ || || (fol. 36v6–10)
