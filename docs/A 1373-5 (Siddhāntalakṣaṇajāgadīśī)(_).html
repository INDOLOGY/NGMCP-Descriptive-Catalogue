Excerpts

Beginning

atha kālo ghaṭavān mahākālatvād ity ādāv avyāptiḥ pratiyogivyadhikaraṇābhāvāprasiddhyā asmad gurucaraṇāsvādhikaraṇānuyogikasvābhāvaviśiṣṭo yaḥ saṃbaṃdhaḥ tena ghaṭatva prati(2)yogyadhikaraṇahetvadhikaraṇavṛttisvarūpasaṃbaṃdhena tadvṛttitvābhāvaviśiṣṭahetv abhāvaḥ sa eva pratiyogivyadhikaraṇa ity arthaḥ (fol. 1r1–2)

End

tathā hi hetumann iṣṭapratiyogitva saṃbaṃdhāvachinnāvacchedakatāka vahnyāde (!) bhedasya pratiyogitāyāṃ va(6)hnitvāvacchinnapratiyogitānirūpitattvapratiyogitvasaṃbaṃdhāvacchinnāvachedakatā (!) nirūpitatvobhayoḥ satvāt na ca vahnyābhāvavad bhediya(!)pratiyogitāyāṃ va(7)hnitvāvacchinnanirūpitatvasatvenobhayābhāvasatvāt nāvyāptir iti vācyam pratiyogitāyāḥ pratiyogitāvacchedakatvamate tatraiva doṣa iti (fol. 10v5–7)
