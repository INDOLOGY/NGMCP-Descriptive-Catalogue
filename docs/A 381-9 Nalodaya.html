Excerpts

Beginning

[Ṭīkā]

❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||  || 

natvā nṛsiṃhaṃ jagadekasākṣiṇaṃ

kṛtvā praṇāmaṃ gurave mahātmane |

natvā gaṇeśaṃ girijāsutaṃ prabhuṃ

karomi ṭīkāṃ ca naloda[ya]sya vai ||

nalodayasya kāvyasya ṭīkeyaṃ ca pratanyate |

nṛsiṃhākhyena muninā sarvveṣām upakārataḥ || (fol. 1v1–3)

[Mūla]

hṛdaya sadā yādavataḥ

pāpāṭavyā durāsadāyā davataḥ |

arisamudāyādavatas

trijaganmāgāḥ smaraṇa dāyā davataḥ || 1 || (1v3–4)

[Ṭikā]

he hṛdaya citta sadā sarvadā tvaṃ yādayataḥ śrīkṛṣṇāt māgāḥ pṛthak mā bhūḥ yadoḥ gotrasyāpatyaṃ yādavaḥ yādavāditi yādavataḥ sārvavibhaktikas tas | (1v4–5)

End

kiṃ ca ayaṃ nalaḥ sadāsaṃpadaṃ ahāsta | dohād gatau | asya /// yoga || ye gatyasrthās te prāptyarthāḥ jñānārthāś ca | samṛddhi (!) prāpa kathaṃ bhūtāṃ saṃpadaṃ mahāmāhānto mahā utsavā yasyā ///mahāmahānāṃ mahadaiśvaryyayuktām ity arthaḥ kathaṃ bhūtoyaṃ nalaḥ unmahāḥ ucchrito maha utsavo yasyāsau u /// hāḥ || || (fol. 55r5–8)

[Mūla]

nalena pūryatāyatā yatāyatā cirāyasā |

sadāyamunmahāmahā mahāmahāsta saṃpadaṃ || 47 || (fol. 54v9–55r1)

Colophon

iti śrīkālidāsakṛte nalodayakāvye caturthollāsaḥ samāptaḥ || (fol. 55r1–2)

iti śrīkālidāsakṛtasya nalodayakāvyasya nṛsiṃhāśramaviracitāṭīkā samāptā || śubham astu sarvvadā ||  || (fol. 55r8–9)
