Excerpts

Beginning

❖ nṛtyeśvarāya namaḥ ||

sānandaṃ nandihastād dhṛtamurajare bāhū te huṃkārabarhi

strosāmnāśāprace praviśati phaṇipatau bhogasaṃkocabhāji |

gaṃḍāstrī naulimālāmukhalitakakubhas tāṇḍave śūlapāṇau

vaiśāṃ yajyāś ciraṃ vā vadananiṣūtayaḥ pāntu cītkāravatyaḥ || 1 || (fol. 1r1–4)

End

maulau nāmaya kuntalaṃ tava nabho gaṅgādharādūpyatrī

tyevaṃ siddhaniṣiddhatāṇḍavanaya vyāgro haraḥ pātu vaḥ ||

pāṇau kaṅkaṇam ūtphaṇaḥ phaṇipatir nnetre jvalat pāvakaṃ

kaṇṭhe kuṇṭhitakālakūṭakūharo vastraṃ gajendrājinaṃ

gaurīlocanalobhayaḥ sūbhago deśo na me tisphura

ṅgaṇḍollāsavibhāvito paśupate hāsyoṅgamaḥ pātu vaḥ || 26 || (fol. 3v3–6)

Colophon

iti śrīnṛtyeśvarastotraṃ || ❖ ❖ ||

oṃ namo nāṭeśvarāya ||

nityānandaparaṃ śāntaṃ niṣphalaṃ ca nirāmayaṃ

vyomātītaṃ paraṃ śāmyaṃ bhairavaṃ taṃ namāmyahaṃ || ||

śṛgāṇāraṃ caiva vīraṃ sakaruṇamudadhiṃ krodhahāsyādibhīmaṃ

vībhatsaṃ raudraśāntaṃ na[[va]]rasanirayaṃ nṛtyam ānandarūpaṃ |

kṛtvā nityāvatāraṃ vṛṣabhatarugataṃ tāṇḍavaṃ nṛtyanāthaṃ

devaṃ viśveśarūpaṃ tripuraripuharaṃ naumi nṛtyeśvareṣaṃ || ||

mañjilaṃ maṇikaṅkaṇaṃ mṛgamadaṃ kāśmīrajaṃ kajjalaṃ

dharmmiṣṇaṃ navamaṃ śukaṃ ca vapumā vāme na vastrārūhāṃ |

nāmendraṃ karicarmmabhasmagaralaṃ ṣaṃbho jaṭāmaṇḍalaṃ

krīḍānāṭakanāyako vijayatu devorddhanārīśvaraḥ || || (fol. 3v6–4r3)
