Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

hariḥ ʼʼom gurucaraṇābhyāṃn namaḥ || ||

sūta uvāca ||

kailāsaśikhare ramye bhaktisādhanahetave ||

praṇamya pārvatībhaktyā śaṃkaraṃ paripṛcchati || 1 ||

pārvaty uvāca ||

[[oṃ]] namo devadevaśa (!) parāt para jagadguro ||

sadāśiva mahādeva gurudīkṣāṃ pradehi me || 2 ||

kena mārgeṇa bho svāmin dehabrahmamayo bhavet ||

⟪yat⟫[[tvaṃ]] kṛpāṃ kuru me svāmin namāmi caraṇau tava || 3 ||

īśvara uvāca ||

yasya deve parā bhaktir yathā deve tathā gurau ||

tasyaite kathitā hy arthāḥ prakāśaṃte mahātmanaḥ || (fol. 1v1–2r1)

End

guravo bahavaḥ saṃti śiṣyavittāpahārakāḥ

durlabho[ʼ]yaṃ gurur devi śiṣyacittāpahārakāḥ(!) 45

saṃsārasāgarasamuddharaṇaikamaṃtraṃ

brahmādidevamunipūjitasiddhamamtraṃ

dāridra(!)duḥkhabhayaśokavināśamaṃtraṃ

vaṃde mahābhayaharaṃ gururājamaṃtraṃ (fol. 18r3–7)

Colophon

iti śrīskaṃdapurāṇe īśvarapārvatīsaṃvāde gurumamtraṃ (!) śrī(!) | (fol.18r8)
