Excerpts

Beginning

oṁ namaḥ paradevatāyai namaḥ ||

kailāsa(!)śikharārūḍhaṃ śaṃkaraṃ parameśvaraṃ ||
papraccha girijākāṃtaṃ gaurī taṃ vṛṣabhadhvajaṃ || ||

śrīdevy uvāca ||

bhagavan sarvvadharmmajña sarvvajñānamaya prabho ||
sūcitaṃ yoginītaṃtraṃ tan me vada jagadguro ||

māhātmyaṃ ki(!)rttitaṃ tasya purā śrīśailamaṃdire
vārāṇasyāṃ kāmarūpe nepāle maṃdarācale || ||

śrīi(!)śvara uvāca ||

śṛṇu devi pravakṣyāmi yoginītaṃtram uttamaṃ ||
pāvanaṃ paramaṃ dhanyaṃ mokṣaikaphaladāyakaṃ (fol. 1v1–3)

End

śrīkāly uvāca

brahmaviṣṇvādikānāṃ ca brahmāṃḍe naravāsināṃ
nityaṃ nigrāhakas tvaṃ hi tava bhairavasattama

kulācāreṇa yaḥ ko ʼpi mām arcayati putrakaḥ(!)
sa me putra svabhāgacche (!) satyaṃ satyaṃ na saṃśayaḥ

gṛhṇātu vaktraṃ dāsyāmi brahmāṇḍabhayadaṃ bhavān
tava vaktraṃ kālarūpaṃ sarvvanigrāhakaṃ paraṃ

i(!)śvara uvāca

vaktraṃ datvā bhairavāya kṣaṇāt †sāṃtaragāt śi†
mahākālī bhairavo ʼpi vajrapāṇir bbabhūva saḥ

karālabhairavaṃ rūpaṃ krodhavaktro babhūva saḥ
vajrapāṇir mahākālī prasādīśvarābhidaḥ (!)

iti śrīyoginītaṃ(!) pūrvvārddhakathitaṃ mayā
gopanīyaṃ sadā bhadre yoniṃ †paramare† yathā (fol. 46v4–8)

Colophon

iti śrīyoginītaṃtre devīśvarasamvāde unaviṃśatiḥ paṭalaḥ 19
samvat 961 ti(!) jyeṣṭhaśudi 5 roja 3 likhitaṃ śrīdhaneśvararājena śubham ślokasaṃkhyā 1460 (fol. 46v9–10)
