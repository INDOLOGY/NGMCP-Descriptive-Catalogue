Excerpts

Beginning

svasti śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||


athāmṛteśīyiginīmaṃtravidhānam || ||


oṁ juṁ saḥ hrauṁ saṃ kaṭe rogaṃ me paraṃ nāśaya naśaya hrauṁ saḥ jūṁ oṁ 23 || asya śrī īśvara

ṛṣiḥ yathoktacchandaḥ amṛteśvarī yoginī devatā juṁ bījaṃ saḥ śaktiḥ oṁ kīlakaṃ mṛtyuṃjayārthe

caturvidhapuruṣārthasidhyarthaṃ ca japārcane viniyogaḥ || oṁ oṁ hṛt oṁ jūṁ śira oṁ saḥ śikhā oṁ

oṁ kavacaṃ oṃ juṁ netratrayaṃ oṁ saḥ astraṃ || (fol. 1v1–5)


End

tat tanmaṃtravaraṃ japtvā vidhnaśāntyai sahastradhā ||

saptāhena bhaved devī trividhotpātanāśane ||


graṃthāntare ||


ṣaṭbhujādhyānan tu piṃgalā bhrāmarī ulkā saṃkaṭānām eva || tad api grakarmaṇy eva || haumyan tu ||


vilvapatrais tatphalaaiś ca kamalair nāgakeśaraiḥ ||

juhuyāt pāyasānnnnena tathaiva kulavastubhiḥ ||


svasvavastubhih ||


sahastraṃ ayutaṃ vāpi lakṣaṃ vā kāminīkramāt ||

athavopakrameṇaiva juhūyāt svasvavastubhiḥ ||


dakṣiṇāntaṃ samāpyaiva tan taddiṣṭam anuṃ japed iti || || (fol. 7v3–7)


Colophon

iti bhaṭṭopanāmakadakṣiṇāmūrtiśivārādhakakāśīnāthaviracite maṃtrarājasamuccaye uttarārddhe

ekādaśa paṭalaḥ || || 11 || śubham astu || || || (fol. 7v7–9)
