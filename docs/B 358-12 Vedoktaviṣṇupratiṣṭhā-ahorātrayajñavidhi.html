= Manuscript Features

MS contains the text about the Mūrttipratiṣṭhāvidhi of the God Viṣṇu and its Caturthīkarma. Ekādaśīvratakathā is not available.

exp. 60-64 contains the text related to the karmakāṇḍa and Mantra.

Excerpts

«Beginning: »


oṃ namas sāradāyai namaḥ ||


atha ananta ekādaśīvratādiviṣṇupratiṣṭhāprāsādasuvarṇakalaśadhvajāvarohaṇaviṣṇumate ahorātra

yajñavidhir likhyate ||


gaṇajāgādi māṭrśārdhaṃ māha || (!)

ekaviṃśatiyajñeṣu vivāhe daśabhirddine ||

trirātre ca vratādeśe seṣās sadyaṃ tu kārayet ||

agnipādavidhāna ||


pūrvvasin divase kṣūrakarmmakhalisnāna sicivastrana hele ||

yajñamaṇḍapanirmmacchana kīlikāvarohana yathāvidhānaṃ || (exp. 2A1–14)


«End:»


atra gandhādi ||


oṃ devāgātum iti || pūrṇā || oṃ āpyāyasveti || pathanaṃ ||

oṃ āsaṃsā paṭhet yathāvidhānaṃ yajamānādi candanādipuṣpāntaṃ | brahmāpraṇītavisarjjanaṃ || yajñavisarjanaṃ nyāsalikāya || arghapātraṃ adhomukhaṃ kārayet || yathāvidhānena kaumārīpūjanaṃ || iti caturthīyajñavidhānaṃ || (exp. 59:9–10)

«Colophon»


iti vedoktaviṣṇupratiṣṭhā ahorātrayajñavidhis samāptaṃ || || ❖ atha caturthīkarmaṃ(!) kuryāt || (exp. 58A7–8)
