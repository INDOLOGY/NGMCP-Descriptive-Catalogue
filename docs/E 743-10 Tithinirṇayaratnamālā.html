Manuscript Features

The foliation of the text seems to be added later. Thus, it is not always regular, therefore not cited below in the Excerpt.

The text contains many scribal errors. Apparent errors are not always marked below in the Excerpt.

Excerpts

«Beginning»

yma icchanti mānavā(!) ||

muhu(!)rttam api navamyāṃ, daśamī yadā †hiṃśrā† |

sadā devī cālanīyā, sarvvasyaṃrttidāyikā (!) ||


ṣaṭmuhutte (!) yadā bhū(?)ne, daśamī vyāyapanī yadi |

yena devī coranīyā (!), sarvvaśatrubhayāpahā || (exp. 2 ll. 1–2)

«End»


upākarmma tataḥ kuryāt, nakṣatre sūryadevate ||

†śrāṇi†nirṇaya iti ||

granthāny anakāni tithinirṇṇayeṣu,

tathāpi yuktā kṛtir asmadīyaḥ ||

†saṃtyavanāvo† bahavaḥ mamuṃdraḥ(!),

Tathāpi kurvvanti hi †nāva†madhya† || (fol. 42r4–6)
