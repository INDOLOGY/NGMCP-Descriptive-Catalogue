Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrī 3 mahāgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

atha niśivalyārccanavidhir likhyate || ||

snāna(!) ||

ūkā(2)raṃ vāyuvījaṃ tadupari paramaṃ vajrapāṇiṃ

tadurddhaṃ kālaṃ varṇṇāntayuktaṃ

tadupari para(3)maṃ vahnivījaṃ sahaṃsaṃ |

induṃ vinduṃ layāntaṃ śitakamalavarakṣīradhārāśravantaṃ,

dṛ(4)ṣṭvā kūṭantra nityaṃ dahati kulavaraṃ bhairavaṃ śrīkujeśaṃ || || (fol. 1v1-4)


«Middle»

iti tridaśaḍāmare kanavīre śrīsiddhināthāvatāritaḥ śrīsiddhila(6)kṣmīmahāmāyāstavaḥ dvāviṃśatitamaḥ patalaḥ samāptaḥ || || (fol. 26r5-6)

iti hārāvarāvatantre navākṣarī(fol. 29r5)stotra samāptaḥ || ||


End

ātma āśīrvvāda || ambe pūrvvagataṃ padaṃ bhagavatī caitanyarūpātmikā,

jñāne(2)cchā bahulā tathā hariharau brahmā marīcitrayaṃ |

bhāsvad bhairavapaṃcakaṃ tadanu ca (3) śrīyoginīpaṃcakaṃ,

candrārkkau ca marīci ṣaṭkam amalaṃ māṃ pātu nityaṃ kujā || ||


yajamānādina mālakostaṃ svāna biya || vācana laṃkhana hāya || ūkāraṃ vāyu(4)vījaṃ || ceta || śrīkhaṇḍaś candanaṃ divyaṃ || siṃdhra || vīreśvarīmahāvīra || svāna biya (5) || utphullāṃ || mālakostā biya || || astramantrana bali visarjjana yāya || no(6)siye || || bali bhokaluya || || sākṣī thāya || || bali choya || || (fol. 36v1-6)


Colophon

iti śrī(1)śrīśrīsiddhilakṣmīdevyai niśibalyārccanavidhiḥ samāptaḥ || || śubham astu || (fols. 36v6-37r1)
