Beginning

❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || śrīpiṃgalanāgāya ||

jo vivihamatrasā ara, pāraṃ pattovivimalamaduhelaṃ |

paḍhamaṃ bhāsatraṃraṇḍo, ṇāoso piṅgalo ja a I || 1 ||

dīho saṃjuktaparo, vindu juo pālo o acaraṇanti |

soguvaṃkadumatto, aṇṇola huho isuddha ekkalalo || 2 ||

jahā || bhāīru eheo, hiṣṇo jiṣṇo abuḍḍhao deo |

saṃbhuṃ kāmaṃtīsā, gaurīgahi lattaṇaṃ kuṇai ||3 ||

katthavisaṃjuktaparo, vaṣṇola huho idaṃ sa ṇenajahā |.
