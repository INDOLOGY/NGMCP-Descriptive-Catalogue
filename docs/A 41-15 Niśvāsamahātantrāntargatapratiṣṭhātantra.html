Beginning

/// (vite ||

tatrāsīno mahādeva)ḥ śrīkaṇṭheśvarapūjitaḥ |

praṇamya caraṇau tasya devī vacanam abravīt ||

bhagavan sarvadharmajña lokanātha jagatpate |

pratiśṭhān devadevasya śrotum icchāmi tatvataḥ ||

dṛśṭvā bahuvidhā [dha]rmās tvatprasādāt mayā vibho |

śivadharmāt paro dharmo na bhūto na bhaviṣyati || (fol. 1v1)

End

evaṃ yathākrameṇoktaṃ pratiṣṭhātantram uttamaṃ |

śāstrārthasaṃgrahārthan tu paṭalaṃ samudāhṛtaṃ |

iti yathākrameṇaiva vāstuśāstrasamanvitaṃ 〇|

yuktiyuktaṃ susaṃpūrṇṇaṃ nirapekṣaṃ sudurllabhaṃ |

niśvāsākhye mahātantre bījabhede mahātape |

pratiṣṭhātantra (!) samākhyātaṃ samāptaṃ (śā)stram uttamaṃ |

rakṣaṇīyaṃ prayatnena śivoktaṃ jñānam uttamaṃ |

dātavyaṃ gurubhaktāya nānyasmai saṃpradāpayet |

tantraṃ pa(ñca)sahasraṃ tu śāstraṃ vai ślokasaṃkhyayā |

kathi[taṃ] saviśeṣeṇa nisandigdham anākulaṃ || || (fol. 121r2–4)

Colophon

iti niśvāsā〇khye mahātantre prati[ṣṭhā]tantraṃ parisamāptaṃ || ||

nṛpānvaye śrībalade[va]rājake tri+++duḥkhavināśahetoḥ

nirbhya (!) mayā likhitam eva sumaṅgalārtham (fol. 121r4–5)
