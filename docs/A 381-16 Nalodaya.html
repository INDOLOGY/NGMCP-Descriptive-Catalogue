Manuscript Features

The first exposure bears some parts of Kāvyaprakāśaṭīkā so this not related to the text Nalodayakāvya and the last one bears remarkably only a stamp of NAK.

Marginal title is just over the left foliation and just over the right foliation Rama is maintained.

Some corrected letters can be found in margins of the text.
