Excerpts

Beginning

❖ || oṃ namo mahābhairavāya ||

prathamaṃ caṃdrakuryākhyaṃ (!) guhyanāthaṃ dvitīyakaṃ |

tṛtīyaṃ sumbhadevaṃ ca kalmāśā〇khyaṃ caturthakam |

paṃcamaṃ vaṭapuryākhyaṃ virājaṃ ṣaṣṭham eva ca |

pravvāsaṃ (!) saptamaś caiva kāṃśālākhyaṃ tu cāṣṭamaṃ |

nava〇maṃ bhogadevaṃ ca biṃbaṃ devi dvipaṃcamā |

ekādaśaś ca khaḍgākhyai (!) dvādaśaṃ rudram eva ca |

dhārāśivaṃ trayodaśaṃ ca mudrāsphoṭaṃ caturddaśaṃ |

baṃkāpuraṃ paṃcadaśaṃ bhujasphoṭa ṣaḍ ucyate |

ṣoḍaśaite mahāsiddhā kuṃkaṇe hy adhikāriṇaḥ | (fol. 1v1–2)

End

pūjānte arccayet sarvvān guru (!) sarvvopacārakaiḥ || 〇

vastrair ābharaṇaiḥ hṛdyaiḥ gobhūaśvahemagajādibhiḥ |

pradakṣiṇaśataṃ kuryāt sāṣṭāṃgaṃ ca namaskāraṃ | 〇

kṛtam ārambha (!) śāstrasya vyākhyālekhanakeṣu vā ||

ye samāptaṃ varārohe kṛtvā siddhiprado bhavet |

athavā na kṛte devi vilopāt maraṇaṃ bhavet ||

pacyate niraye ghore pā⟪da⟫vādākhya〇m amaṣṭakaṃ |

idaṃ rahasyaṃ kaulāgamo gopanīyaṃ prayatnataḥ |

gopanā siddhyate sarvvaṃ satyaṃ satyaṃ 〇 na saṃśayaḥ |

idānīṃ paramaṃ guhyaṃ caturvviṃśasahasrakaṃ |

kule taṃtre catuḥ proktaṃ aṣṭāviṃśasahasrakaṃ || ❁ || (fol. 199v4–200r2)

Colophon

iti mahāmanthānabhairava⟪ā⟫yajñe śatakoṭipravistīrṇṇe 〇 anvaye navakoṭyāvatāre śrīcaturviṃśatisahasre taṃtrābhidhāne namaskārāśīrvādava〇rṇṇano nāma trinavatitamaḥ paṭalaḥ || ❁ || ❁ || ❁ ||

samāptaṃ śrīśrīmatabhaṭṭārakaṃ || ❁ || ❁ || ❁ || ❁ || ❁ || ❁ 〇 ||

iti śubham || ❁ || ❁ || ❁ || maṃgalamahāśrī || ❁ || ❁ || (fol. 200r2–3)
