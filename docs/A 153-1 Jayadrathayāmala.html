nam ṛtubhuktadayitās tā smaraṃ[[ti]] vaśīkṛtāḥ |

kva cid ratisukhā[[svā]]damadakībair udāharan |

suślīlasā(ndu)kalye (!) vai kubjavīkṣa (!) narottamaṃ |

kva cit kṛtakakopādisamadai (!) mānivījanaṃ |

vallabhaiḥ prārthyamānaṃ tat na prasīdati satvaraṃ |

kva cid ratisamāklāntamithunairdakṣiṇānilāḥ ||

sevyante ketakīmodamantharā makaraṃdinaḥ (!) | etc. (fol. 2r1–3)

sākṣādyogeśvarītantraṃ parameśamukhācchrutaṃ |

mayā pūrvve mahātyugraṃ kramasaṃkrāmasaṃkulaṃ |

jayadrathāt krameṇaiva bhūmāv avatariṣyati |

tasmād deveśi pūrvvasya ṣaṭkasyotkīlanaṃ mayā |

kṛtaṃ suravareśāni tan mahāśvāsya sāṃprataṃ |

iti śrutvaiva sā devi praharṣotphullalocanā |

praṇamya parameśānaṃ kṛtāṃjalipuṭāgrataḥ |

sthitvovāca surādhyakṣaṃ kṛtakṛtyāsmi śaṃkara | (fol. 5v5–7)

athātaḥ saṃpravakṣyāmi sarvasiddhiprasādhanaṃ ||
