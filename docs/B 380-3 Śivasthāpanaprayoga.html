Excerpts

Beginning

Śrīgaṇapataye namaḥ || ||


Oṁ svastiḥ ||


oṁ śāṃtiḥ || ||


atha śivasthāpanaprayogo likhyate || ||


tataḥ prāṅmukho yajamānaḥ kṛtanityakriyaḥ śuklavāsāṃsi paridhāya sagurvācāryyaḥ

savaidikabrāhmaṇasamaṅgalaḥ sattīrthe kṣetre vā svagṛhādisakalasthale vā saparivāro gatvā śuklāṃ

varadharam iti paṭhitvā sveṣṭadevatāṃ kuladevatāṃ ca praṇamya svstino masītām ityādisvastivāca

itvā pratijñāsaṃkalpaṃ kuryāt || tataḥ sarvaprāyaścittārthe gosuvarṇadānaṃ paṃcagavyaprāsanaṃ

kuryyāt || (fol. 1r1–4)


End

Vṛṣakandhaṃ samārūḍhaṃ śūlahastaṃ trilocanaṃ ||

Āvāhayāmi yajñe ʼsmin pūjeyaṃ pratigṛhyatāṃ ||


Sarvādhipo mahādeva iśānaḥ śukla iśvaraḥ ||

Śūlapāṇivirūpākṣa tasmai nityaṃ namo namaḥ ||


Iti natvā sāṃgāyasaparivārāyeti eṣa māṣabhaktavalir namaḥ ||


Īśānapūrvayor madhye †kuṃmaṃ† sthāpyānaṃtaṃ dhyātvā


ehyehi pātāladharāmarendu-

nāgaṅganākinnaragīyamāna(!) ||

rakṣoragendrāmaralokasaṃghair

anamtakṣādhvaram asmadīyaṃ ||


bho anamtehāgach(!)eha tiṣṭety āvāhya raktāṃ patākāṃ dhvajāṃ cochricya āyaṃ gauḥ anaṃ (fol.19r9–19v2)
