Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrīādināthapādukebhyo namaḥ ||

śrīmanmahendrācale ramye khaṭvāṅṭaśikharesthitaṃ |

sarvvajñaṃ sabaraṃ vīraṃ śivaṃ paramakāraṇaṃ |

upasaṃgamya vidhinā devadevaṃ jagadguruṃ |

praṇipatya maheśānī pṛcchati sma mahe[śva]raṃ |

siddhāntaṃ caiva ḍāmaraṃ dakṣiṇaṃ caiva gāruḍaṃ |

bhūtatantrañ ca deveśa kulaśāstrāṇy aṇekadhā |

mayā śrutāni śrīnātha tvayā nigaditāni ca |

idānīṃ śrotum icchāmi kulamūlāvatārakaṃ |

ūrddhasṛṣṭim adhosṛṣṭim adhodhaḥsṛṣṭir eva ca |

kathayasva praśādena yady ahaṃ tava vallabhā ||

śrīnāthovāca ||

sādhu sādhu varārohe śrūyatāṃ kamalekṣaṇe |

kathayāmi mahādevi kulamūlāvatārakaṃ |

salakṣakoṭibhedena siddhakaulaṃ mayoddhṛtaṃ |

amūlaṃ kulamūla (!) tu kulapañcāśikocyate | (fol. 1v1–5)

End

siddhānandaḥ kramo hy eṣaḥ pāraṃparyakramāgataṃ |

karaṇecchāsamāyātaḥ sarvaṃ tasmād bhaviṣyati |

mayā tubhyaṃ tvayā mahyaṃ mayā tubhyaṃ punas tv idaṃ |

tvaṃ mama vai punas tvaṃ vai ahaṃ dāsyāmi sarvvataḥ |

sūtrabhūta (!) mayākhyātaṃ alā(?)granthārthavistaraṃ |

bhaviṣyanti purākalpe laghvītantre savistarā |

sapādakoṭilakṣasya sūtram etat prakīrttitaṃ |

saṃgrahopagrahādibhyaś ca caturvviṅśasahasrakaṃ |

pañcāṣṭādaśaśataṃ caturvviṃśasahasrakaṃ |

etatprakāśitaṃ tubhyaṃ tvam anyena yathāruciḥ |

kambalādicatuṣkan tu mālāvidyā kulodbhavaṃ |

pāśastobhakarās tāṃ tu pīṭhasiddhāntagopitaṃ || ❁ || (fol. 9v1–4)

Colophon

iti siddhapañcāśikāyāṃ candrapīṭhavinirggatāyāṃ mūlanāthāvatāritāyāṃ divyājñāguṇaprakāśikāyāṃ kulālītantramūlagatāyāṃ pañcamaḥ paṭalaḥ samāptā ceyaṃ śrīsiddhapañcāsikā || ❁ || śrīmahādevenālekhi || (fol. 9v4–5)
