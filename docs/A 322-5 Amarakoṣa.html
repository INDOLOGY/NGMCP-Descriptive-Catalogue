Excerpts

Beginning

śrī gaṇeśāya namaḥ

rāmaṃramyaṃ samaṃkāmyaṃ saccidānanda madvayam ||
natvāgajā nanaṃ cāsmi karomyāmaradīpikām 1
prārīpsita graṃtha vighātāya kṛtamaṃgalaṃ śiṣya
śikṣārtha mādau nivavaṃdha | yasyeti | he dhīrāḥ vikāra
hettāvapi vikriyaṃte yeṣāṃ na cetāṃsita evadhīrā ityukta lakṣaṇāḥ |
sa anirvacana sāmarthyaḥ kaścit | śriyetrivarga saṃpatyai amṛtāya mokṣāya
sevyataḥ m (!) |
(fol. 1v1–4 )

End

kauśalyo darasaṃbhavodaragadā cakrābjavibhratkaraḥ
śrī vatsādibhiranvitaścakitayā kauśalyayā prārthitaḥ ||
rupaṃcāyayanayan nijaṃ prakaṭayan vālasvabhāvanrudan
naṃvāṃke samavasthitaḥ sakaruṇo rāmovibhupātu vaḥ 4 graṃtha saṃkhyā
(fol. 97v8–10 )

Colophon

iti śrī dāṇIṇātya śiromaṇībhūta citta pāvana śrī mukuṃda bhaṭṭātmajaḥ śrī viśvanāthabhaṭṭāṃ ghrilaghavidhyena śrī manmahārādhirājapārvata viṣayādhipa śrī trivikramasena pramvāśritena pāṃḍe ityupanāmaka viracitāyāmamaradīpikāyāṃ mamarasiṃhakṛta nāmaliṃgānuśāsana paṃjikāyāṃ tṛtīyaḥ kāṇḍaḥ saṃpūrṇaḥ śubham śrī śāke 1727 māse 6 likhitamidaṃ pustakaṃ rupanārāyaṇātmaja revatīramaṇa śarmaṇā śubham gokhuragrāme
(fol. 97v10–98r4 )
