Beginning

atītaḥ paṃthānaṃ tava ca mahimā vāṅmanasayor
atadvyāvṛtyā yaṃ cakitam abhidhate(!) stutir(!) api ||
sa kasya stotavyaḥ katividhagunaḥ kasya viṣayaḥ
pade || || tv arvācīne patati na manaḥ kasya na vacaḥ || 2 || (fol. 2r5-7)

he bhagavan yasya mahimā vāṅmanasayoḥ paṃthānam atīta eva || vāk ca manaś ca vāṅmanase(!) tayo(!) [[etena vāṅmanase(!)]] sarvārthaviṣayiṇī tābhyām api tava mahimā avadhārayituṃ na śakyate na ca pratyakṣeṇānubhāvena gṛhyate sa kathaṃ tava mahimānaṃ śrutir api || vedo pi ..kitam abhidhate(!) bhītabhītaṃ (śru?)tena niścayataḥ || kayā || atadvāvṛtyā(!) na tat(!)atadrūpā cāsau vṛtiś(!) cā(!) yā tadvyāvṛttis tayā || sa ('nyaṃ) bhūto mahimā kasya stotavyo 'pi tu na kasyāpīty arthaḥ || tathā katividhaguṇas tasya bhagavataḥ kiyaṃto guṇās te parijñātuṃ śakyate || kasya viṣayaḥ kasya gocaro bhavet | na kasyāpīty arthaḥ || etena niṣkalasvarūpeṇa stuter anadhikāritvaṃ pratipāditaṃ || sakalasyāvakāśatvaṃ pratipādayann āha || (fol. 2r1-4, 8-9)

End

kuśumadaśananāmā sarvagaṃdharvarājaḥ
śaśidharavaramauler ddevadevasyā(!) dāsaḥ ||
svagurunijamahimno bhraṣṭa ivāsa(!) roṣāt
stavanam idam akāṣṣī(!) divyadivyaṃ mahimnaḥ || 34 ||

suravaramunipūjaṃ svargamokṣaikahetu(!)
paṭhati yadi manuṣyaḥ prāṃjalī nānyacetāḥ ||
vrajati śivasamīpaṃ kinnaraiḥ stūyamānaḥ
stavanam idam amoghaṃ puṣpadaṃtapraṇītaṃ || 35 || (fol. 12v2-5)
