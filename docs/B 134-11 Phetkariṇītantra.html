Manuscript Features

The number 65 has been assigned to two consecutive folios. From the verso side of the second of these folios, two foliations are given, an older one in the upper left-hand and lower right-hand corners (the latter of the two being crossed out) and a newer, corrected one in the lower right-hand corner.

Fols. 1–9v are in Devanagari script; fols. 9r– 65r (exp. 65b) is in Newari script. Fol. 65v (exp. 66t) is blank; fol. 65r (= fol. *66r, exp. 66b) to the end is again in Devanagari script. The first four lines of fol. 65r (= fol. *66r, exp. 66b) repeat the last portion of the Jananādisaṃskārapaṭala recorded on fol. 65r (exp. 65b). The Devanagari and Newari portions are written in different ink and seem to be in different hands.

Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namo gaṇeśāya ||

oṃ namo durgāyai ||

śrīgaṇapataye namaḥ ||     ||

atha phetkāriṇītaṃtraṃ ||

krodhāt jvalantīṃ jvalataṃ rasantīṃ

sṛṣṭiṃ dahaṃtīṃ jagatgrasaṃtīṃ || (!)

bhīmaṃ nadaṃtīṃ praṇamāmi kṛtyāṃ

rorūyamānāṃ sudhayograkālīṃ ||

unmattabhairavaṃ nārasiṃhaṃ bhairavaḍāmaraṃ ||

śīvārāvaṃ jālikādyam asitāṃgādiyāmalaṃ || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

iyaṃ mayoktā kṛtyākṛtyā samastāgamasārabhūtā

śatror vināśāya dhanāya dharmamokṣāya samyak prayāto niyojyā ||

duṣṭāniṣṭadurjana-

hiṃsāparaghātakāriṇīkṛtyā

gaditeṣṭasiddhidātrī

mahātra phetkāriṇītaṃtre ||

sṛṣṭisthitivinā[śā]ya dvitīyādibhairavī ||(!)

mahākṛtyā smṛtā nityaśubhārivimardinī ||(!)    || (fol. 70v1–3)

Colophon

śrīkrodhabhairavaḥ śaraṇaṃ(!) ||     || śubhaṃ || ❁ || ❁ || ❁ || ❁ || ○ || ○ || (fol. 70v3–4)
