Beginning

❖ Oṃ namoḥ sarvvajñāya || ||

jato lakṣmi tato dharmma tato satya bijyāka jato dharmma tato jaya || ||

gana satya ana (1v1) lakṣmi gana rakṣmi bijyāta ana dharmma bijyāta svahma nāpaṃ bijyāka thāsa jasa juyio bhāgi juyio purukhāsta ju(2)o sadā nirmmara juyio niścayanaṃ || 1 ||

dharmmovāca ||

hnāpāṃ dharmmana ājñā dayakaraṃ bho rakṣmi manuche rokapa(3)ni sakarayāṃ dharmma uti majuo dharmma uti mjuohma rithu jarmmasa drāridra jula drāridrahma rithya taomi jura (4) dhanaontahma rithya drāridra jusa china gathya uti makhanā dhaka lakṣmi⟪na⟫yākya dharmmana ṅasya bijyātaṃ || || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

karmmana thukā brahmādi devaroka (4) lokapārapani thathya juyamāra ⟪cvaka⟫ dvaha yāya mateo, thvatena || dvaha yānā yāya ṅēhune, gurudvaha, mitradvaha, svāmi(5)doha, māmadoho, babudvaho, sasara, oaṃca, viśvāsa, dyātaka thuti dvaho yākahma dohiko juyuo, naraka vāsa candra śu(8v1)ryya ākāsasa, devarokana, pṛthidatarana, narakavāsa phāna ṅānāo tao || || parvvata kubuyāṃ naṃ bhārata makhu || thu(8v1)tina dharmma satya rakṣmi vināna suṃ madu || ||
