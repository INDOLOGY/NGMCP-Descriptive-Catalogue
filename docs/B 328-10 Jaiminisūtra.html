Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

anālaṃbe śāstrādhyanikaruṇayā tena rahane(!)

na kiṃ me saṃcāro bhavatu vipatitvaṃ (!) kimu na (2) me |

asau sadviśāvasa (!) prabhavatitarāṃ graṃtharacane

hy anabhyaste thatrā (!) subhaparibodhe pi vaduḥ (!) || 1 ||

svaduḥkhoddhārārthavi(3)vidhatarapatnādaravidhau

pravṛttir nṛṇāṃ syāt katham aśubhavijñāyanam (!) ṛte |

subodhā no vāṇī bhavati bhagavajjaimini(4)muner

ata stokāṃ karttuṃ nṛpativara eṣa prayatate || 2 || 

bhāvyarthaṃ vijñānavaśān mahīyasāṃ

kāśīnivāsādividhau sva(5)pṛttaye (!) |

śrīmanmalaivamānṛpaḥ (!) kṛpāṃbudhiḥ

karoty araṃ (!) jaimi[[ni]]sūtrakāśikā (!) || 3 ||

atha prāktanakarmaparidāsyamā(6)naphalajñāne sati tannirākaraṇakāśīvāsādikarmakaraṇena jagatām evoddhāracikīrṣuḥ paramakāriṇiko (7) jaiminiḥ muniḥ samyakśravaṇādyabhāve ʼraṇyānūditatvāpattibhir yyā śiṣyāṇāṃ viṣayāṃtaramanovicchedarūpāvadhānasiddhaye paṭhitṛśrotṛṇāṃ prasaṃgato maṃgalasiddhaye sakalaśāstrakarttṛśiromaṇibhagavatsadāśivaprati⟨(2r1)bhagavatsadāśivaprati⟩pādakopadanibaṃdhapūrvakaṃ tṝṇāṃ (!) śubhāśubhajñāpakagraṃthaviśeṣam anuṣṭhātum ārakṣate u(2)padeśa (!) vyākhyāsyāmaḥ (fol. 1v1–2r2)

End

api ca yasyā rāśe (!) daśāṃsamāraddhāmavatitadrā(7)śito dvitīyāthabdalāghavā (!) edānīm (!) eva daśārāśy adhīnaḥ sayoga uddiṣṭaḥ sthalāṃtare etatsūtrakaraṇe bhāgyarogayo (!) sāmye kema ima ity avaśye (!) vaktum āyete (!) viviśabde (!) gaurava (!) tyāt kiṃ ca prārthamikārthopade- (fol. 32v6–8)
