Manuscript Features

MS contains the chapter of the pratyakṣakhaṇḍa, anumānakhaṇḍa and up to the beginning of the śabdakhaṇḍa.

On the exposure 2 is written: śrī kecittu vuakter abhedaḥ vyakter aikyaṃ ghaṭatva ka[[la]]śatvādīnāṃ jātīnāṃ bhede bādhakaṃ | tulyatvaṃ dravyatva guṇavatvādīnāṃ jātitve | rūpaṃ lakṣaṇaṃ tejjo arthāṃs tasya hāniḥ ākāśatvāder jātitve | asaṃbaṃdhaḥ saṃbaṃdhābhāvaḥ samavāysya jātitve | samavāyasya svarūpasaṃbaṃdhasatve pi tasmin saṃbandhibhinnatvādighaṭitasaṃbaṃdha[[la]]kṣa[[ṇa]]syābhāvāt | anyatvasiddhavadeva vyākhyeyam ityāhuḥ ||

sa śreyasi bahūni …
