Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namaḥ śrīmahābhairavāya ||    ||

mahākāla uvāca ||

guhyastavaṃ pravakṣyāmi mahābhairavanirgataṃ ||
bhāṣitaṃ devadevena bhairavena mahātmanā || 1 ||

gurupūrvakrameṇaiva sādhakānāṃ hitāya vai ||
bhuktimuktipradaṃ divyaṃ devāsuranamaskṛtaṃ || 2 ||

siddhidaṃ sukhadaṃ caiva vilomagnaṃ vilāpahaṃ ||
yoginīhṛdayānandaṃ vīrasāmayikair nutaṃ || 3 ||

vāmadakṣiṇasāmānyaṃ pavitraṃ kalmaṣāpahaṃ |
namitaṃ siddhasamghais tu guhyād guhyataraṃ paraṃ || 4 ||

vallabhaṃ sarvadevānāṃ sarvakṣetrābhisammataṃ ||
tatvabhuktigatānāṃ tu sādhakānāṃ hitāya vai || 5 || (fol. 1v1–7, 2r1)

End

mahocchuṣmamate khyāto yoginīvijayastavaḥ ||
pippalādena muninā pṛthivyām a[[va]]tāritaḥ || 304 ||

mahāśāṃtikam adhyāyaṃ durnimittanivāraṇaṃ ||
sarvapāpaharaṃ nityaṃ sarvaduḥsvapnanāśanaṃ || 305 ||

bhairavena purā khyātaṃ devyai ca trividhātmanā ||
na deyaṃ tarkadarpāṇāṃ śaṭhānāṃ ca viśeṣataḥ || 306 ||

devāgnigurubhaktānāṃ dātavyam iti niścayaṃ ||    ||     || (fol. 31v6–7, 32r4)

Colophon

iti śrīvidyāpīṭhe brahmayāmale maṃtrottarasaṃgrahe yoginīvijayastavaḥ samāptaḥ ||    ||    ||
śubham bhūyāt ||    ||    ||

grahajaladhinande 'smin varṣe tapasi kṛṣṇake |
śarvatithyāṃ tad alikhad rāmaśaṃkaraśaramakaḥ ||    ||    || ❖ ||
