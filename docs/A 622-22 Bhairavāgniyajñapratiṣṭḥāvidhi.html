Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīmahābhairavāya ||

atha bhairavāgnidinayajñavidhir li(2)khyate ||

yajamāna puṣpabhājana || oṃ adyetyādi || vākya ||

mānava go(3)tra yajamānasya śrī 3 jayabhūpatīndramallavarmmaṇaḥ śrī 3 bhīmasena (4) māhābhairavaprītyarthaṃ suvarṇṇalipta mukhapratiṣṭhā bhairavāgniyajñe ni(5)mityarthaṃ katuṃ puṣpabhājanaṃ samarppayāmi ||     ||

(fol. 1v1–5)

hnasaka(5)na kene ||

ā(6)tmavindudharaṃ yasya saṃpradāya jayāya ca ||    ||

sākṣī tha(295v1)ya || vākya ||

oṃ hrāṃ kṛtakarmmaṇe sākṣiṇe śrī sūryyāya a(2)rghaṃ namaḥ puṣpaṃ namaḥ || sākṣine namaḥ ||     ||
