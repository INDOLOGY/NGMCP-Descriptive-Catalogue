Beginning

[Ṭīkākāṃśa]

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

śatamukha(!)pūjitapādāṃ(!) śatamukhamanasopyagocarākāraṃ ||

vikaśi(!)jalaruhanetram umunuchāyāṃkam(!)āśrayaśaṃbhuṃ

iha khalu bhagavān śaṃkarācārya uttamādhikāriṇāṃ vedāṃtaprasthānatrayaṃ nirmāya tadavalokanāsamarthānāṃ maṃdabuddhīnāṃ anugrahārthaṃ sarvavedāṃtasiddhāṃtasaṃgraham ātmabodhākhyaṃ prakaraṇaṃ nidarśayiṣuḥ(!) pratijānīte || (fol. 1r1-4)

[Mūlāśa]

tapobhiḥ kṣīṇapāpānāṃ śāṃtānāṃ vītarāgiṇāṃ ||

mumukṣūṇām apekṣyo(!)yam ātmabodhobhidhīyate || 1 ||

bodhonyasādhanebhyo hi sākṣānmokṣaikasādhanaṃ ||

pākasya vahniva(!) jñānaṃ vināmokṣo na siddhyati || 2 ||

(fol. 1r5-7)

End

hṛdākāśodito hyātmā bodhabhānus tamopahṛt ||

sarvaṃ(!)vyāpī sarvadhārī yena sarvaṃ prakāśate || 67 ||

digdeśakālādyanapekṣasarvagaṃ śitādi(!)hṛn nityasukhaṃ niraṃjanaṃ ||

ya(!) svātmatīrthaṃ bhajate viniḥṣkriya(!) sa sarvavit sarvagatomṛto bhavet || 68 || 

(fol. 12v6-8)

[Ṭīkāṃśa]

yo vinaḥkriyaḥ(!) paramahaṃsaḥ svātmatīrthaṃ bhajate sa sarvavit sarvajñaḥ sarvatra paramātmasvarūpatvād amṛtaḥ mukto bhavet kathaṃbhūtaṃ svātmatīrthaṃ degdeśakālādyanapekṣam eva sarvagaṃ śītādidvaṃdvaduḥkhanir(!) haratīti

śītādihṛṃ nityasukhaṃ mokṣānaṃdaprāpakatvāt, itaratīrtheṣu tadviparitaṃ(!) draṣṭavyaṃ tasmād ātmatīrthe snātasya na kiṃcid avaśiṣya(!) iti bhāvaḥ || 68 || (fol. 13r4-8)
