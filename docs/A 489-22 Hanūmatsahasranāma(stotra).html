Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śivāya namaḥ || śrīvāsudeva uvāca ||

tataḥ sa prayato bhūtvā mama tāta yudhiṣṭhira |

prāñjali prāha viprarṣir nāmasaṅgraham āditaḥ || ||

upamanyūvāca ||

brahmaproktai ṛṣir vvedair vvedavedāṅgasambhavaiḥ |

sarvvalokeṣu vikhyātaṃ stutyaṃ stoṣyāmi nāmabhiḥ ||

mahadbhir vviditaiḥ smṛtyaiḥ siddhaiḥ sarvvārthasādhakaiḥ |

ṛṣiṇā taṇḍitā bhaktyā kṛtair vvedakṛtātmabhiḥ | (exp. 5, 1–3)

End

idaṃ nāmnāṃ sahasran tu yo [ʼ]dhīte pratyahaṃ naraḥ |

duḥkhaugho naśyate tasya saṃpattir varddhate ciraṃ || 131 ||

bandhaṃ caturvidhaṃ tasya bhavaty eva na saṃśayaḥ |

rājāno rājaputrāś ca rājakrīyāś ca mantrīnaḥ || 132 ||

trikālapaṭhanāttasya siddhiḥ syāt karasaṃsthitaḥ |

brāhme muhūrtte cotthāya pratyahaṃ yaḥ paṭhen nara || 133 ||

na hi kāmūṣmika sopi labhate nātra saṃśayaḥ |

saṃgrāmasanniviṣṭānāṃ vairividrāvaṇaṃ paramaḥ || 134 ||

jvarāpattyāraśamanaṃ gulmādiplīhavāraṇaṃ |

sāmrājyasukhasaṃpattir dāyakaṃ jayatāṃ nṛṇā || 135 ||

ya idaṃ paṭhate nityaṃ pāṭhayed vā samāhitaḥ

sarvān kāmān avāpnoti vāyuputraprasādataḥ || 136 || (exp. 22, 5–9)

Colophon

iti śrīmadrāmāyaṇe vālmīkināpraṇītaṃ hanumatsahasranāmastotraṃ saṃpūrṇam samāptaṃ || 7 || || śrī 3 hanūbhairavaprītir astu || || śubham astu sarvvadā || (exp. 22, 9–10)
