|| || oṃ praśnopaniṣat |

oṃ sukeśā ca bhāradvājaḥ saivyaś ca satyakāmaḥ

sauryāyaṇī ca gārgyaḥ kauśalyaś cāśvalāyano bhārgavo vaida(2)bhī (!) kavaṃdhī kātyāyanas te hai te brahmaparā brahmaniṣṭhāḥ paraṃbrahmānveṣamāṇā eṣa ha vai tatsarvvaṃ vakṣatīti te ha samit pāṇayo bhagavaṃtaṃ(3)pippalādam upasannās tān ha sarṣir (!) uvāca bhūya eva tapasā brahmacaryeṇa śraddhayā saṃvatsaraṃ savatsyatha | (fol. 1v1–3)
