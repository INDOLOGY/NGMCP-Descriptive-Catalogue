Manuscript Features

Three folios which do not belong to this manuscript are added at the beginning. The first two of them are illegible because the script is rubbed off, the third is half broken. It begins:

aupālambhamātravyāpāratvaramāṇasahacarī sārthavivacito panītakamalinīdalajalārddraśamanīye tannidrakeṇa rajaṇīṅ gamayati , katham apy u⁅palabdha⁆ ++///

On top of the folio the following is written:

calavīcirāgavacanāpaṭavaḥ |


Excerpts

Beginning

‥‥‥maṇitaśrutair mṛgadṛśāṃ mālāvadāne valat
pañceṣor nniśitaḥ śaro nidhuvanakrīḍātaror aṅkuraḥ |
ātodyadhvananaṃ manobhavamanorājyābhiṣekaḥ śriyaḥ |
svānte śātasurī kariṣyati cirāt kaṃsārivaṃśī varaḥ

adharanihitavaṃśī nādapīyūṣasārair
vrajayuvatijanānāṃ mohayan mānasāni |
hṛdayakamalamadhye yogibhir bbhāvanīyo
haratu duritajātaṃ devakīnandano naḥ ||

yo bhūt purā ravikaraḥ sakalāgamajñas
tasyātmajo (vuṣi)(?)karo sya ca keśavo pi |
tasyātmajaḥ sakalatantrasarojabhāsvān
govinda eva bhuvane viditaś cakāsti ||

End

maukunde pādapadme sarabhasam adharībhūtacetāḥ purastād
ārād āliṅgya pādāmbujayugalarajogauravaṃ gauraveṇa |
mātuḥ pādāravindopari luṭhitaśirāḥ sādaraṃ śūlapāṇeś
cūtāpītāvalambī vidhur iva vidadhe kaumudīn devanāthaḥ || (103v6-104r2)

Colophon

yas tarkkatantraramaṇīkamanīyakānto
⟪yo⟫ govinda eva bhuvane viditaḥ sukīrttiḥ |
