Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

udayāstamanaṃketor gaṇitena praśasyate ||

jñā⟨ṃ⟩tuṃ (2) khadivyabhaumāś ca yatas te trividhā matāḥ || 1 ||

ekonattaraśataṃ tv eke sa(3)hasram apare viduḥ ||

ekaś ca bahudhā bhāti prāha vai nārado muniḥ || 2 ||

yā(4)vaṃty ahāni dṛśyeta tāvan māsaiḥ phalaṃ bhavet ||

māsaiḥ samāś ca vijñeyā (5) ity ūcur munayaḥ kilaḥ (!) || 3 ||

susnigdho ruciraḥ sūkṣmo ṛjuḥ śukra(6)śubhaprada ||

viparito (!) śubhaḥ ketus triśikhedradhanuprabhaḥ || 4 || (fol. 1v1–6)
