Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

atha gaṇapati (!) atharvaśīrṣa (!) prāraṃbhaḥ || hariḥ oṃ || m ||

tan mām avatu || tad vaktāram avatu || avatu māṃ || avatu vaktāraṃ ||

oṃ śāṃtiḥ śāṃṭiḥ śāṃtiḥ || oṃ atha gaṇeśātharvaśīrṣa [[vyākhyā]]syāmaḥ || oṃ names te gaṇapataye || tvam eva pratyakṣaṃ tat tvam asi || tvam eva kevalaṃ kartāsi || tvam eva kevalaṃ dhartāsi || tvam eva kevalaṃ hartāsi || tvam eva sarvaṃ khalv idaṃ brahmāsi || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

saha nāv avatu || saha nau bhunaktu || saha vīryaṃ karavāvahai || tejasvi nāv adhītam astu mā vidviṣāvahai || oṃ śāṃtiḥ śāṃtiḥ śāṃtiḥ saṃpūram atharvaśīrṣaṃ ||

yādṛśaṃ pustakaṃ dṛṣṭvā tādṛśaṃ likhitaṃ mayā ||

yadi śuddham aśuddhaṃ vā mama doṣo nadīyate || 1 || (fol. 3v5–8)

Colophon

saṃvat 1942 śrāvaṇa śu. 4 Śukravāsare dvī(!)tīyaprahare saṃpurṇaṃ likhitaṃ śrīkṛṣṇajośī rāmanagaravāle (sapta) || ❁ || (fol. 3v8–9)
