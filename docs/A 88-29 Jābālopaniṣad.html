Excerpts

Beginning

śrījābālopaniṣatprāraṃbhaḥ oṃ |

bṛhaspatir uvāca yājñavalkyaṃ yadanu kurukṣetraṃ devānāṃ devayajanaṃ sarveṣāṃ bhūtānā brahmasadanaṃ avimuktaṃ vaikuṇṭhakṣetraṃ devānāṃ devayajanaṃ sarveṣāṃ bhūtānāṃ brahmasanam(!) tasmād yatrakvacana gachet(!) tat tadabhimanye tā vimukto vai devānāṃ devayajanaṃ sarveṣāṃ bhūtānāṃ brahmasadanaṃ atra hi jaṃtoḥ māṇeṣ(!)ūtkramamāṇeṣu rudrastārakaṃ brahmavyācaṣṭe yenā sau amṛtībhūtvā mokṣī bhavati tasmād avimuktam eva niṣevetā vimuktaṃ vai na vimucyetevam evaiṣa bhagavan niti yajñavalkyaḥ || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

śikhāṃ yajñopavītaṃ cetyetet sarvaṃ bhūḥ svāhetyapsu parityajyātmanam anvicched yayā jātarūpadharo nirdvaṃdo niḥparigrahas tatvābrahmamārge samyak saṃyaṃnaḥ śuddhamānasaḥ māṇasaṃ (!) shāraṇārthe yathoktakāle vimukto maikṣa (!) mācaran-nudarapātreṇa vā māvābhau (!) samau bhūtvā śūnyāgārado (!) ca gṛhe tṛṇakūṭavalmīkavṛkṣamūlakulālaśālāgnihotranadīpulinagiriguhā kaṃdhara (!) koṭaranirjharasthaṇḍilye ca niketavāsyāmayatno nirmmamaḥ śuṣkadhyānaparāyaṇo ʼdhyātmaniṣṭho ʼ śubhakarmanirmūlanaparaḥ sanyāsena dehatyāgaṃ karoti sa paramahaṃso nāma sa paramahaṃso nāmeti || || (fol. 3r1–6)

Colophon

iti jābālopaniṣat samāptā || || rāmakṛṣṇa || (fol. 3r6–7)
