Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namo nāṭeśvarāya (!) || nāndīgītaṃ || rājavijaya || cauºº ||

prathamahi sumirañe gurugaṇēśa dethu abhayavara harathu kaleśa ||

kara tiriśūla ḍamaru maṇihāra premadhariy śiva aradhadhaṃgadāra ||

nandī bhṛṃgi saṃge nirita vihāra oḍhi iśara sundara bādha chāla ||

cāṃdatilaka jaṭā niramara gaṃga tribhuvana ṭḥākura bhuṣanabhujaṃga ||

toha hi sohāvana moramanabhāva jagataprakāśabhūpa kautuka gāva || || (exp. 2a1–6)

Sub-colophon

iti śṛīdevicaraṇakamalamadhukara mahārāja jayajagatprakāśamallakṛtaṃ nāndīgītaṃ samāpam || (exp. 3b5–6)

iti śṛīdevicaraṇakamalamadhukara mahārāja jayajagatprakāśamallakṛtaṃ nānārthapuruṣoktigītaṃ samāptaṃ || || (exp. 14a6–14b1)

iti śṛīdevicaraṇakamalamadhukara mahārā… (exp. 27a6)

iti śrīgītāṣtake nṛpacandraprakāśe daśāvatāra kīrttanabhāvaḥ samāpta || || (exp. 46b3–4)

End

jagataprakāśanṛpatikara vinati cāṃdaśeṣarasiṃha paramasumatī || 6 ||
