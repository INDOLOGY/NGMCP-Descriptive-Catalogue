Manuscript Features

This MS alone was used for the edition of the Kāśyapasaṃhitā by Ācharya and Śarmā in 1938 (cf. bibliography). The following folios are extant: 22 (this folio has not yet been edited); 29; 33-34; 37-39; 41-46; 49; 75-76; 79; 81; 84-85; 92; 94; 101; 104-105; 114; 117-118; 120; 125-126; 128; 132-133; 136-141; 143-154; 167-191; 193-234; 236-246; 250; 253; 259; 261-264. The actual order of folios is as follows: 29; 22; 33; 37-39; 41-46; 49; 34; 75-76 etc. Fol. 178 comes between fols. 179 and 180. Some folios are damaged in the margin. The foliation of these damaged folios has been indicated in the space of the left binding hole, probably by the editor of the text. Fols. 141-164 seem to have been written by another hand than the previous folios. Almost every folio is damaged, if only slightly so.
