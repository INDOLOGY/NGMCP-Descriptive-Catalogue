Beginning

|| śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

atha syamaṃtopākhyānaṃ likhyate ||

naṃdi(2)keśvara uvāca ||

śṛṇuṣvaikāgracittaḥ san vrataṃ gāṇeśvaraṃ mahat ||

(3) caturyyāṃ śuklapakṣe tu sadārcyo yatnataḥ⟨s⟩ tadā || 1 ||

sanatkumārayo(4)gīṃdra yadicheddhitam (!) ātmanaḥ ||

nārī vā puruṣo vā pi sa kuryād vra(5)tam uttamaṃ || 2 ||

mocayatyāśu vipreṃdra saṃkaṣṭād vratinaṃ narāṃ ||

apa(6)vādaharaṃ caiva sarvavighnavināśanaṃ || 3 || (fol. 1v1–6)

End

vrataṃ kuryād gaṇeśasya *4y āvan māsacatuṣṭayaṃ ||

sarvasiddhikaraṃ puṇyaṃ nṛṇāṃ caiva mahā(5)mune || 138 ||

etad vrataṃ samākhyātaṃ parāpavādanāśanaṃ ||

ataḥ pa(6)raṃ mahāprājña kim anya chrotum (!) arhasi || 139 || (fol. 11r3–6)

Colophon

iti śrīskaṃdapurāṇe (7) syamaṃtakopākhyānaṃ samāptaṃ || śrīgajānanārpaṇam astu || idaṃ pu(8)stakaṃ jāmakheḍakaropāhvaya śrīpatibhaṭṭātmjatryaṃbakabhaṭṭasya (9) saṃvat 1879 tathā śāke 1744 ahikāśvine māsi śuklapakṣe (10) caturthyāṃ vārāṇasyāṃ brahma ghaṭṭe samāptim agamat || śrīgajā[na]nā(11)rpaṇam astu ||

ekadaṃtaṃ śūrpakarṇaṃ gajavakraṃ caturbhujaṃ ||

pāśāṃ(ku)śadharaṃ devaṃ dhyāyet siddhivināyakṃ || 1 || (fol.11r6–11)
