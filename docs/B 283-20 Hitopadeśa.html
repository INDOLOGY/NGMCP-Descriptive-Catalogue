Excerpts

Beginning

ntareṇa,, balād dhṛtaḥ san,, punar āgamanāya sapathaṃ kṛtvā svāmini(!) saṃnivedayitum āgataḥ || siṃhaḥ sa kopam āha | satvaraṃ gatvā darśaya taṃ kva sa durātmā tiṣṭhati || tatas taṃ gṛhītvā śaśako gaṃbhīrakūpaṃ gataḥ || atrāgatya paśyatu svāmīty uktvā tasmin kūpajale tasyaiva siṃhasya pratibimbaṃ darśi⁅ta⁆vān || tatosau darppād dhmātaḥ | kopāt tasyopary ātmānan nikṣipya paṃcatvaṅ gataḥ ||

(fol. 37r1–3 )

End

jalāntaścandracapalaṃ jīvitaṃ khalu dehināṃ |

tathā vidham iti jñātvā śaśvat kalyāṇam ācaret ||

mṛgatṛṣṇāsamaṃ vīkṣya jagac ca kṣaṇabhaṃguraṃ |

sujanaiḥ saṃgatiṃ kuryād dharmmāya ca sukhāya ca ||

(fol. 76v9–10 )

|| iti hitopadeśe vigrahonāma tṛtīyaḥ kathā saṃgrahaḥ || ❁ || ❁
