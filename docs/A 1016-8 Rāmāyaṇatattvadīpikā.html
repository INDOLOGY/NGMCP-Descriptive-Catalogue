ṭīkā aṃśa

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||
|| atha śrīmadyuddhakāṃḍe vyākhyāyāni vyākrīyaṃte | evam apārapārāvāraṃ govatsam uttīrya nirātaṃkaṃ laṃkāṃ praviṣṭena sītānveṣaṇapūrvakam aśokavanabhaṃgādikam atimārurṣe pauruṣaṃ kṛtvā pratinivṛttena hanumatā prabhijñānapradāna(!) sarvaṃ karmābhibhāṣitaṃ sītāvṛttaṃ | taṃ śrutvā rāmas tadākarṇena muditamanāḥ kṛtakāryabhṛtyajane svāmitā evaṃ bhavitavyam iti sadācāram upadaṣṭaṃ(!) sugrīvādiharivīrāṇāṃ utsāham utpādayituṃ ca samāhatapreyasi vṛttāṃtāṃ hanumaṃtam astauṣīd ity āha śrutveti | (fol. 1v1-3)

mūla aṃśa
.... hanumatye(!) vākyaṃ yathāvad abhibhāṣitaṃ ||
rāma(!) prītisamāyukto vākyam uttaram abravīt || 1 ||
kṛtaṃ hanumatā kāryaṃ sumahad bhuvi durlabhaṃ ||
manasāpi yad anyena na śakyaṃ dharaṇītale || 2 ||
na hi taṃ paripaśyāmi yas tareta mahārṇavaṃ ||
anyatra garuḍād vāyor anyan na ca hanūmataḥ || 3 ||
devadānavaca(kṣā)ṇāṃ(?) gaṃdharvoragarakṣasāṃ ||
adhṛṣyāṃ ca purīṃ laṃkāṃ rāvaṇena surakṣitāṃ || 4 || (fol. 1v6-8)
