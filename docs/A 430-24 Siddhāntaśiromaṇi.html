Excerpts

Beginning

|| || śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

yatra trātum idaṃ jagajjalajinībaṃdhau samabhyudgataṃ

[[dhvāṃta]]dhvaṃsavidhau vidhūtavinaman niḥśeṣadoṣoccaye ||

varttaṃte (2) kratavaḥ śatakratumukhā divyaṃti devādivid

drāṅ naḥ sūktim ucaṃ vyanaktu sagiraṃ gīrvāṇavaṃdyo raviḥ || 1 ||

kṛtī jayati jiṣṇujo gaṇakacakra(3)cūḍāmaṇir

jayaṃti lalitoktayaḥ prathita taṃtrasadyuktayaḥ ||

varāhamihirādayaḥ samavalokya yeṣāṃ kṛtīḥ

kṛtī bhavati mādṛśo pyatanutaṃ(4)trabaṃdhelpadhīḥ || 2 || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

tajjanmaccararaṇāraviṃdayugalaprāptaprasādaḥ sudhīr

mugdhodbodhaka(2)‥ vidagdhagaṇakaprītipradaṃ prasphuṭaṃ ||

vyaktāvyaktaviviktayuktibahalaṃ (!) helāvagamyaṃ vidāṃ

siddhāṃtagrathanaṃ kubuddhimathanaṃ cakre kavir bhāskaraḥ || 60 ||

(3) praśnān amun prapaṭhato gaṇakasya gola-

kaṃdollasat saralayuktiśatapravālaiḥ ||

praśnottarārthapariciṃttanavārisikta- (!)

mūlāmalāmatilābha samupaiti (dṛ(4)ṣṭiṃ) (!) || 61 || (fol. 69v1–4)

Colophon

iti śrībhāskarācāryaviracite siddhāṃtaśiromaṇau praśnādhyāyaḥ || || śubham astuḥ (!) || || saṃvat (1734) āṣāḍhamāse kṛṣṇapakṣe (5) līṣitaṃ (!) (hemarājakāyastha kāśī‥ ‥ madhye bhutabhairavasamipe (!) dakhinadīśā (!) pustaka vidyārthī jośīkī || || śrīrāmarāmarāmarāmarāmarāma || (fol. 69v4–5)
