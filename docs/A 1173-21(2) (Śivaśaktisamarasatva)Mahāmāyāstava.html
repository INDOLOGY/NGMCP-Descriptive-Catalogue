Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namo ʼstu te mahāmāye śūkṣmadehe parāpare |

ekākini viśuddhātmai nādākhye bindumāliṇi (!) |

adehāc ca samutpanne acale viśvadhāriṇī |

mahākuṇḍalinī nityaihaṃsamadhye vyavasthite ||

somasūryāgnimadhyasthe vyomavyāpi parāpare |

ḍekāravigrahāvasthe hakārārddhārddhadhāriṇi || (exp.5a:1–4)

End

tathā kālī umā devī devadūti namo ʼstu te |

rudrakālī mahādevi carmmamuṇḍe bhayāvahe ||

mahocchuṣme mahāśānte namaste śaktirupiṇi |

bhūrbhuvaḥ sveti svāhānte dayāṃ nāthe kuruṣva me ||

jñānārthino mahāmāye etad icchāmi vedintaṃ (!) ||

yasvidaṃ paṭhate stotraṃ trisaṃdhyo ceva mānavaḥ ||

prāpnoti cintitān kāmān strīṇāṃ bhavati vallabhaḥ | (exp.6b:6–exp7a:3)

Colophon

iti śrīśivaśakti samarasatve māhāmāyāstavaḥ samāptaḥ || ❁ || (exp.7a: 3–4)
