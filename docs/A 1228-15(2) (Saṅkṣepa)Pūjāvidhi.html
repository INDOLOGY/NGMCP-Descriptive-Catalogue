Beginning

❖ oṃ namo guhyaśvaryyai ||

atha saṃkṣepa paddhati llikhyate ||

kamaṇḍalu ||

laṃ (2) phra astrāya phaṭ ||

iti saṃprokṣa ||

phreṃ yhrīṃ 2 śoṣaṇaṃ ||

phreṃ rhrīṃ 2 dahanaṃ ||

phreṃ (3) vhrīṃ plāvanaṃ || gāyatryā saṃprokṣa ||

gandhākṣataṃ datvā || dhenuliṃga yonimudrāṃ(4) ||

ācaṃmya || (exp. 3t1-4)

End

mūlamantraṃ yathāśakti saṃjapya japa(4) samarppaṇaṃ ||

kphreṃ kāmakalākālyai mūlamantra japakṛtaṃ samarppayā(5)mi ||

stuti paṭhet ||

yogeśvarī mahāmāya yogalakṣmī maheśvarī (b1)tvām ahaṃ

saraṇa vande prasīda parameśvarī ||

yonimudrā namaskṛ(2)tya ||

phraṃḥ astrāya phaṭ || bali visarjjanaṃ ||

candana puṣpa ātmaśi(3)rasi gṛhnīyāt || || (exp. 11t3-b3)

Colophon

iti saṃkṣepapujāvidhi samāptaṃ || || (exp. 11b3)
