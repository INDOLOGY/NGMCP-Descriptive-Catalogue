Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīrāmacandrāya ||

kauśalyānayanakumudenduṃ

daśarathahṛdayāravindamārttaṇḍaṃ |

sītāmānasahaṃsa (!)

rāmaṃ rājīvalocanaṃ vande ||

prāptarājyasya rāmasya, rākṣasānāṃ vadhe kṛte |

ājagmur ṛṣayaḥ siddhā rā(2)ghavaṃ pratinandituṃ |

aśito[[ṅgirā]]thayavakrītas caityas cyavana eva ca |

kando medhātitheḥ putraḥ pūrvvāṃ ye saṃsthitāṃ diśaṃ ||

svastyātreyotha bhagavān amucovimucas tathā ||

ājanmus te sahāgastyā yeśritā dakṣiṇāṃ diśaṃ || (fol. 1r1–2)

End

evam etad yathāvṛttaṃ, samutthāya samāhitaḥ |

śṛṇvan khyātiṃ ca kīrttiṃ ca, dharmmārthau ca samaśnūte |

rāmāyaṇaṃ govisargge, madhyāhne vā (3) samāhitaḥ |

sandhyāyām aparāhne (!) ca vācayan nāvasīdati || || 

gavāṃ śataṃ sakanaka śṛṃgiṇāṃ dade, (!)

ddine dine ceha phalaṃ samāpnuyāt | (!)

tad āpnuyād vigatabhayo vahūśrutaḥ, (!)

paṭhetu yo daśa(4)rathaputrasaṃbhavaṃ || (fol. 112r2–4)

Colophon

ity ārṣe rāmāyeṇe caturviṃśatisāhasrayāṃ saṃhitāyāṃ vālmīkīye śrīmad uttarakāṇḍe śrīrāmasvarggārohaṇaṃ nāma sarggaḥ || uttarakāṇḍaḥ samāptaḥ ||

|| śubhaṃ || ❖ samvat 813 mārggaśila (!) kṛṣṇacaturddaśī somavāra thva kuhnu svanihmavantāgṛhadvijaśrīdevaśaṃkareṇa rāmāyaṇa uttarakāṇda saṃpūrṇṇana cosyaṃ, vaṃlā nihma dvijavaraśrīyadunandanaju yāta viyā dina juro ||  || (fol. 112r4–6)
