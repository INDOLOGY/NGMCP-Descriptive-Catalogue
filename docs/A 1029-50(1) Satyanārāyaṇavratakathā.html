Excerpts

Beginning

atha satyanārāyaṇavratakathā likhyate ||

śrīvyāsa uvāca ||

navāṃbhojanetraṃ ramākelipātraṃ caturvāhu cāmīkaraṃ cārugātraṃ

jagantrāṇahetuṃ ripau dhūmaketuṃ sadāsatyanārāyaṇaṃ staumidevaṃ 1

śrīrāmaṃ saha lakṣmaṇaṃ sakaruṇaṃ sītānvitaṃ sātvikaṃ

vaidehīmukhampadma lubdhamadhupaṃ paulastyasaṃhāriṇaṃ

vaṃde vaṃdya pasaṃ ʼvujaṃ(!) sukhakaraṃ bhaktā ʼnukaṃpākaraṃ

śatrughnena hanūmātā ca bharatenāsevitaṃ rāghavaṃऽ (!) 2 (fol. 1v1–4)

Sub-colophon

iti śrīskaṃdapuraṇe revākhaṃde sūtaśaunakasaṃvāde satyanārāyaṇavratetihāse prathamodhyāyaḥ 1 (fol. 4v6–7)

iti śrīmatskaṃdapuraṇe satyanārāyaṇavratetihāse revākhaṃde sādhuvaṇikcaritakathanaṃ nāma dvitīyodhyāyaḥ (fol. 7v4–5)

iti śrīskaṃdapuraṇe revākhaṃde satyanārāyaṇavratetihāse sādhuprayāṇaṃ tṛtīyodhyāyaḥ 3 (fol. 10r1–2)

iti śrīskaṃdapuraṇe revākhaṃde satyanārāyaṇavratetihāse caturthodhyāyaḥ 4

(fol. 12v7–8)

End

viśeṣataḥ kaliyuge satyapūjāphalapradā

kecit kālaṃ vadaṃtyetaṃ satyamīśaṃ tadevahi 14

satyanārāyaṇaṃ kecit satyadevaṃ tathāpare

nānārupadharo bhūtvā sarveṣām īkṣitapradaḥ 15

bhaviṣyati kalau satyavratarūpīsanātanaḥ

ya idaṃ paṭhate (!) nityaṃ śruṇoti munisattamāḥ 16

tasya nasyaṃti pāpāni satyadevaprasādataḥ (fol. 12r10–13v3)

Colophon

iti śrīmatskandapurāṇe revākhaṃḍe satyanārāyaṇā ʼparaparyāya śrīmaddāśarathirāmacaṃdravratetihāse paṃpamodhyāyaḥ

śrīmatsatyanārāyā ʼbhidha śrīrāmacandrārpaṇam astu

idaṃ ca daśapaṃcadaśapustakāni dṛṣṭvā tatratya samīcīnaṃ pāṭḥānsaṃgṛhya likhitaṃ na kapolakalpitaṃ sāhasam iti sudhibhir bodhyaṃ

maṃgalastvālocakānāṃ śivaṃ śivaṃ śivam iti ||

adya amuka . . . vratamahaṃ kariṣye (fol. 13v3–9)
