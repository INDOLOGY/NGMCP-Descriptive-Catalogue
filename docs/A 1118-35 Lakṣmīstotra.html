Excerpts

«Beginning»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

agastir uvāca || ||

mātar namāmi kamale kamalāyatākṣī(!)

śrīviṣṇuhṛtkamalavāsini viśvamātaḥ ||

kṣīrodaje kamalakomalagarbhagaurī(!)

lakṣmi prasīda satataṃ namatāṃ śaraṇye || 1 ||

tvaṃ śrīr upeṃdrasadane madanaikamātar jjyotsnāsi caṃdramasi caṃdramanoharāsye ||

sūryye prabhāsi ca jagattritame(!) prabhāsi

lakṣmī. || 2 || (fol.1v1–4)


«End»

vārāṇasyāṃ rahasyaṃ ca yathāvac chivabhāṣitaṃ ||

tava tuṣṭikaraṃ bramhan kathayiṣyati ṣaṇmukhaḥ || 29 ||

iti labdhvā varaṃ so ⌠ʼ⌡tha mahālakṣmīṃ praṇamya ca ||

yayāv agastir yatrāsti kumāraḥ śikhivāhinaḥ || 30 || (fol. 2v5–7)


.«Colophon»

iti śrīskandapurāṇe kāśīkhaṃḍe agastikṛtaṃ lakṣmistotraṃ(!) samāptaṃ || || śrīlakṣmyaī namaḥ || || (fol.2v7– 8)
