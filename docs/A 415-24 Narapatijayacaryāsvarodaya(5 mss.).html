Excerpts

Beginning

|| śṛīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

natiṃ kṛtvā gaṇeśāya vighnavidhvaṃśakariṇe ||

nirvighnakṛtisārthāya kamalāpataye namaḥ ||

harivaṃśaḥ kaviḥ svārthaṃ na parārthaṃ svaro(2)daye ||

vyākhyānaṃ mātṛkādīnāṃ svarāṇāṃ nāma janmanam || 2 ||

gurubhyo bahudhā śrutvā yathājñānaṃ yathādhiyā ||

jayalakṣmīr iti māyā ṭīkā rājñī viracyate || 3 || (fol. 1v1–2)

End

atriva rāhuśiṣinau rājño baṃdhanadāyakau ||

dadāti sakṛta bhaumas taraṇir bhaṃgadāyakaḥ ||

nābher adha sthitāḥ pāpār urdhve saumyā jayāvahāḥ ||

(10) viparītā mṛtyukarāḥ sainikasya nṛpasya ca ||

miśrābhagapradā yuddhe pāpā yadi balānvitāḥ ||

vijayo jāyate rājñaḥ sāmyāsaupadi balādhikāḥ || 1 || || (!) (fol. 27r9–10)

Colophon

iti senācakram || || iti svarodayaṭīkā samāptaḥ (!) || || saṃvat 1750 sāke likheka śāvaladāsakāyestha vāsīṃdā rāmāpuramaḥ || (!)

(11) dai. bhāu daivajñasyedaṃ pustakam || 1 || (fol. 27r10–11)
