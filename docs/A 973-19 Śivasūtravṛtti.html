Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||


idānīṃ śāktopāyaḥ prakāśyate || ||


tatra śaktir maṃtravīryasphārarūpeti prathamonmeṣāṃtasūtritatatsvarūpavivecanapuraḥsaram unmeṣāṃtaram ārabhamāṇo maṃtrasvarūpaṃ tāvan nirūpayati || cittaṃ maṃtraḥ || cetyate vimṛśyate anena paraṃ tattvam iti cittaṃ pūrṇasphuranttā satattvaprāsādapraṇavādimaṃtrāḥ vimarśarūpaṃ saṃvedanaṃ || (fol. 1v1–4)


End

ataḥ satatam ukyuktaḥ spaṃdatattvaviviktaye ||

jāgrād eva vijṛṃbhāvam acireṇādhigacchati


ity evaṃ cittaṃ maṃtraḥ || iti ataḥ prabhṛtimaṃtravīryamudrāvīryānusaṃdhipradhānaṃ ||

uccārarahitaṃ vastu cetasaiva viciṃtaya(!)t ||

yaṃ samāveśam āpnoti śāktaḥ sotrābhidhīyate


ity āmnātaṃ śāktopāyaṃ vivicya avadhānāvaliptaṃ pratividyāsaṃhāre tadutthasvadarśanam iti sūtreṇaitad anuṣaṃgenā(!)ṇavopāyapratipādanasyāvakāśo datta iti śivaṃ || || (fol. 11r1–5)


Colophon

iti śrīśivasūtravṛttau śāktopāyaprakāśanaṃ nāma dvitīyonmeṣaḥ || 2 || (fol. 11r5)
