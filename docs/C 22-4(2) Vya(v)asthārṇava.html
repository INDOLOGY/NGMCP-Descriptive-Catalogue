Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

tithisaṃkrāṃtyaśauceṣu dravyaśudhyadhikāriṇoḥ

prāyaścittodvāhadāyeṣv api vācyotra nirṇayaḥ ❖

tatrādau tithivyavasthā |

tithis tu rāśitrayodaśāṃśaś caṃdrabhogopalakṣitaḥ kālaviśeṣaḥ yathā sūryasiddhāṃtaḥ arkād viniḥsṛtaḥ prāvīyadyātyaharahaḥ (!) śaśībhāgair dvādaśabhis tat syāt tithiś cāṃdramasaṃ dinam iti asyārthaḥ | sūryācaṃdramasāryaḥ sannikarṣaḥ sāmāvāsyeti gomilasūtrād amāvāsyāyāṃ sūryācaṃdramasor ekāṃśāvachedenaikarāśyavasthānaṃ tad anantaraṃ candrasya || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

atha sahavasatāṃ bhrātṝṇāṃ madyeko (!) mṛtas tadā tatputrasyāpy aṃśo dātavyaṃ yadi mṛtasyānekaputrās tadā pitraṃśas teṣāṃ vibhajya dātavyaḥ evaṃ sahavasatāṃ bhrātṝṇāṃ mṛtasya putro yadi nāsti tadā makṣyaḥ (!) pautrāya de (!) pautrābhāve prapautrāya na deyaḥ prāg uktasaptamapuruṣaparyyaṃtānāṃ vibhāgadānaṃ tu bhinnadeśād āgatānām ity aviruddham iti śivam || || iti dāyabhāgaḥ || || (fols. 70v10–71r3)

Colophon

iti gauḍadeśīya raghunāthasārvabhaumaviracito vyavasthārṇavaḥ samāptaṃ saṃpūrṇam śrīśāke || 1700 || || śrīsaṃvat || 18 || 35 ||| miti dutīā āṣānhavaditerasī || †ke prasataka puraṇa bhaye ena somaradeṣyā somaralīṣyā ||† (fol. 71r3–5)
