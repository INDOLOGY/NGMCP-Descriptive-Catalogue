bhagavate bhīmasenāya tubhyaṃm ahaṃ sraṃpradade ||    ||
kṛtai ta(6)t sarvvatra sthānarābhaṃ, dānapatiṣṭharthaṃ,
rajata dakṣiṇā tubhyam ahaṃ sapradade ||    ||
śijara (7) oha tayāyā vākya ||    || adyatyādi ||
jajomānasya, amuka dānasya amuka gotrasya (21t1) śrī 3 sveṣṭadevatā prītikāmanayā śrī 3 sveṣṭadevatai imaṃ ḍamaruṃ viṣṇudevatāṃ
tubhyaṃ(2)m aha saṃpradrade ||    ||
