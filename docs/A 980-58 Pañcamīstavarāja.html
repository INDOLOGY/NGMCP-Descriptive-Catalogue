Excerpts

Beginning

-hāṃt mayā khyātaṃ na deyaṃ yasya kasyacit |

bhūgṛhaṃ guṇa⟪re⟫rekhā ca vedadvāraṃ ca śobhitaṃ ||

trivartta ṣoḍaśadalaṃ tathāṣṭadalakarṇṇikā ||

śakrakoṇaṃ digdhruvañ ca vasūtrikoṇavaindavaṃ ||

evam etat mahācakraṃ mahāśrītripurāmayaṃ ||

śive sindūrasaṃdohaḥ svāṅgasundarabhāsurāṃ |

karuṇāpūrṇṇakallolaḥ kaṭākṣaṃ kulanāyikāṃ ||

yā vidyā paramā śaktiḥ jagaccaitanyakāriṇī ||

tān namāmi mahādevīṃ pañcamīm ātmarūpiṇī ||

yasyā sarvvasamutpannam yasyām adyāpi tiṣṭhati |

layam eṣyati tām eva paṃcamīṃ praṇamāmy ahaṃ || (exp. 2,1–5)

End

chatrṃ mūrddhni sahasrapatrakamalaṃ rakṣāmṛtaṃ varṣaṇaḥ

divyastrīparicaryyamānam akhilaṃ ātmānam (!) evaṃ sadā ||

dhyāyet mānasapaṃkaje bhavatīṃ (!) raktāṃ mū (!) pūrvāsane

svāhaṃ yastu samastam āṇḍa (!) labhate rājyāspadaṃ bhūtale ||

ākāśe svaprakāśe sakalaguṇamayīṃ sundarīṃ raktanetrāṃ

nityaṃ padmāsanasthā (!) taruṇaravinibhāṃ raktacandraprabhābhāṃ ||

dhyātvaivaṃ sarvvabhāvaiḥ sakalaguṇayutāṃ sarvvādā (!) yo hi paśat (!) ||

sā savo (!) vāmadena salahaḥ (!) khecaro jāyate sa (!) ||

idaṃ stripurasadaryyāḥ (!) stavarājaṃ maṇohara (!)

rikṣamantra ca śubhadaṃ śivena parikīrttitaṃ || (exp. 11, 2–6)

Colophon

iti śrīrudrajā[[ma]]le śrīśivabhāṣitaṃ daśamaḥ sahitāyāṃ (!) śrītripurasundaryyā krame pañcamīstavarājañ samāpta || śubham astu sarvvadā || 112 || ❖ oṃ namaḥ paradeva || (exp. 11, 6–7)
