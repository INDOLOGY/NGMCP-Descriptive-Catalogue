Manuscript Features

After the end of the main text, the remaining part of the last folio is covered with a Garuḍadvādaśanāmastotra which begins on fol. 15v3:

suparṇavainateyaṃ ca nāgarināgabhīṣaṇam |
jitāṃtakaviṣāriṃ ca ajitaviśvarūpiṇam |

and ends: iti śrīkāśiṣaṃḍe garuḍadvādaśanāmastotraṃ.

Excerpts

Beginning

śrībhavānyai namaḥ ||

śrīśaṅkaraṅ gurun natvā audya(!)nāthān api kramāt ||
sādhakānāṃ hitārthāya nṛpāṇāñ jayadāyakam ||
sādhanam vividhaiś caiva vidyānandam vitanyate ||
kalpamudrādhanaprāpte guṭikātilakāñjana(m) ||
pādukākhaḍgavetālavīrasādhanapūrvvakam ||
kṛtrvā gṛhe śmaśāne vā nirbhayaṃ raṇam āviśet ||
asādhyas tasya naivāsti śatruṣ ko pi mahītale ||

śrīdevy uvāca ||
bhagavan sarvva[[dha]]rmmajña sṛṣṭisthityantakāraka ||
devadeva sadādeva sarvvajñānamahodadhe ||
sarvvaughadhīnām madhye yā paramā siddhimūlikā ||
tasyāḥ kalpam mahādeva kathayasva trilocana ||

śrī īśvara uvāca ||
sādhu sādhu mahāprājñe sarvvatatvārjite śive ||
atigūḍhataraṃ pṛṣṭo brahmādīnāṃ sudurlabhaḥ ||
sarvvakalpeṣv imaṃ sāraṃ sarvvasiddhipradāyakam ||
kathayāmi na sandehas tava snehād varānane ||
vicitrabodhā vijayā samvic cātisusiddhidā ||
saṃjīvanīti vikhyātā mama prāṇapradhāriṇī || (fol. 1v1-2v1)

End

nāsti tasmāt paraṃ loke siddhamūlamahauṣadham ||
pratyabhūtāmṛtaṃn(!) divyam idam stotraṃ na śakyate ||
siddhamūlī mukhe yasya bhuktibhuktir(!) bhave(!) dhruvam ||
caitanyasphuṭikan ta[[sya]] hṛdaye harayel labhet ||
īdṛśaḥ paramaḥ kalpaḥ kalpānāṃ sārasaṃgrahe ||
⟪māyasu....kalpanāṃ sārasaṃgrahe ||⟫
satyam uktam mahādevi tava snehād idam vacaḥ || || (fol. 15r2-15r6)

Colophon

iti śrīparākramasaṅgrahasāre atirahasye śivahṛdayasthite samastasandehopaśamane kanyādhipāvatāre citāmbaravivaraṇe siddhimūlikākalpa samāptaṃ || śubham || śrīrāmaḥ (fol. 15r6-15v3)
