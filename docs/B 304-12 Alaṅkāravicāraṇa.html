Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgurave namaḥ

jāgrat tralokyarājyodbhavavibhavapariraṃbhasaṃbhāvirāghri- (!)
†jñīrā†kūpārakanyā lalitakaratalonmṛṣṭpādāraviṃdam
kāṃcīkoṭīrahārāṃgadamukuṭala(2)sat kaustubhodbhāsitāṃgaṃ
lakṣmīkāṃtaṃ prasannaṃ svakṛtivicchittaya (!) saṃsmarāmi 1

prāyaḥ kuvalayānaṃdaśraddhājādhyajaḍīkṛtāḥ
prakāśe vidhyamāne pi na pa(3)śyaṃti vipaścitaḥ 2

ataḥ kuvalayānaṃde nyūnādhikya (!) prapaśyatā
ekaṣaṣṭīr alaṃkārāḥ sthāpyaṃte kṣamyatā (!) budhaiḥ
upamā yatra sādṛśya lakṣmīr ullasati(4)dvayor
ity upamālakṣaṇe lakṣmīr ullasattidvayor iti vyartham (fol. 1v1–4)

End

kalpanātiśayoktim apahāya kalpitānām ūhasaṃbhāvanamithyādhya(4)vasitīnāṃ saṃdehasaṃkarodāha[ra]ṇatayā vyākhyānam atyaṃtān ucittam iti prapaśyaṃtv abhiyuktāḥ evam anyatrāpy ūhyaṃ tathā ca sūtrasiddhāḥ paramapra(5)tiṣṭhitālaṃkārikālaṃkāra śrīkāvyaprakāśakāra vyavasthāpitāḥ śrīdevanārthapaṃcānana śrīmahopādhyāyagoviṃdaṭhakkura śrījayarāmanyāyapaṃ(6)cānanādy anekavidvadvayākhyātā ekaṣaṣṭir arthālaṃkārā evopakurvaṃtīty anavadyaṃ ||    || (fol. 14r3–6)

Colophon

iti bhīmasenī alaṃkāravicāraṇā ||    || (one line is covered with different ink) (fol. 14r6)
