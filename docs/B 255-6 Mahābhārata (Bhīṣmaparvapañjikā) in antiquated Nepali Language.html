Manuscript Features

The first folio, a table of contents, of the text explains the warriors and page numbers of the Bhīṣmaparvan. Rest of the folios describe the major wars of the Bhīṣmaparvan.

Beginning

śvetako ra bhīṣmako bado yuddha bhayo yudhhamā virathi bhayākā śvetako rakṣā garna āyākā sātyaki bhīmasena dṛṣṭadyumna kaikeya dhṛṣtaketu abhimanyu eti mahārthikana droṇa śalya kṛpa vīrale saṃgabhai bhīṣmale eti vīralāī cheki brahmāstrale śvetalāī mārdā bhayā (fol. 2r1–3)
