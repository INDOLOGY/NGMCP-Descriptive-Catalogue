Remarks This is the first part of a MTM which also contains the text the Pitṛnāmāvalī.

Subject Nāṭaka

Language Newari

Text Features The text of this Vīradhvajopākhyānanāṭaka are deposited in two archives. Most of the previous part is in Asha Archives and the some of the later part is in National Archives. There are some folios still missing even after combining both of the manuscripts (see Kansakar 1994:8-14). The text of the nātaka is published as Kansakar 2000 (?)
