yajamāna puṣpabhājana ||

adyādi ||

vākya ||

mānavagotra yajamānyā śrī3 anantavratodyāpanaābhyodakabṛddhiśrārddhakartuṃ puṣpabhājanaṃ

samarpayāmi ||


siddhir astu kriyārambhe bddhir astu dhanāgame

puṣṭireṣu(!) śarīreṣu śāntireṣu gṛhe tava ||

sarvvabighnipraśamanaṃ sarvvaśāntikaraṃ śubham ||

āyuputrañ ca kāmañ ca lakṣmī santati barddhanaṃ (!) ||

tat tad vaiva vidhātrāṇāṃ śāntir bhavantu vāriṇaṃ ||
