Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ ratnatrayāya ||

devy uvāca ||

mahāśirāntagan deva..hākailāsamadhyagam |

mahāntayogapīṭhaṃ tu mahāpadā....tālayaṃ ||

mahāntapīṭhajaiḥ sārddham mahābharaṇamaṇḍitam ||

mahāsamādhim āsaktam mahāvyāptiniṣannadhī(!) ||

mahāmantragaṇās tatra mahāvidyādy anekadhā ||

mahāmaṇḍalamadhyasthā mahāmudrābhi(!)muditāḥ(!) ||

(sadhanadhraśanābhṛtyāṃ kṛtvāgre maṇḍalakulam )

mahāstotraṃ paṭhaty agre †takkṣyatīnās tu kuścitāḥ† ||

kuścitāni(!) śarīrāṇi kubjikākaraṇātmakam ||

urasā śirasākucta tathā (coktendukaiḥ) pṛthak ||

†nānā nānāvaṣṭhosamāviṣṭair nānāvaṣṭhoravākulaiḥ† ||

(fol.1v1-5 )

End

stutvā nānāvidhaiḥ stotrair yathā devis(!) triśūkadhṛk ||

draṣṭavyaḥ sarvakālaṃ tu trividhenāntarātmānā(!) ||

āgamoktās tu yaṃ devi ghrabhyayāsya(!) pravarttate ||

na cedam āgamaṃ kvāpiu darśayet khyāpayet priye ||

etad vigamaya mukhyam (mamoktā) tu punaḥ punaḥ ||

ekasya yogiśiṣyasya mama yas tasya dāpayet || ○ ||

(fol.38v6-39r2 )

Colophon

iti mahāmudrāpīṭhe śrīkubjikāmate paramarahasye guhyātiguhye aṣṭamaḥ paṭalaḥ samāptaḥ || ○ || samvat 212 phālguṇaśuklaaśvanī(!) nakṣatre śukradine śrīmandarathasya rāja (śrīhallapiṅgaviṣaye kāthadhuṭōllakāpicāsinā kulaputravajābhāvokasya pūjārthas puṇyārthinā paramarahasya) pustakam idaṃ khitam(!) iti || ○ ||śivam astu || (fol.39r2-5 )
