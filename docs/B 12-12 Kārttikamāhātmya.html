Manuscript Features

After the colophon the last folio is filled with stanzas, for example:

aye vāram vāram ⟪‥‥⟫bhavajaladhipāraṃ jigamiṣo
payaḥ pāyaṃ pāyaṃ nivasanikaṭe jahnuduhituḥ
 mano dāyaṃ dāyan danujadamanañ cintaya ciraṃ
yaśo gāyaṃ gāyaṃ gamaya samayaṅ kaiṭabharipoḥ || (fol. 30r3-4)

Excerpts

Beginning

namo bhagavate vāsudevāya ||

nārāyaṇaṃ namaskṛtya narañ caiva narottamaṃ |
devīṃ sarasvatīñ caiva tato jayam udīrayet ||
sūta uvāca ||
... brahmalokaṅ gato muniḥ |
dadarśa lokanātheśaṃ dhyāyamānaṃ janārddanaṃ ||
pitāmahaṃ mahāprājñaṃ sarvvabhūtahite rataṃ |
stūyamāṇaṃ(!) ṛṣigaṇair ggīyamāṇaṃ surāsuraiḥ |
apsarogaṇasaṅkīrṇṇaṃ kinnaraiḥ sarvvato vṛtaṃ |
mūrttimadbhiś caturvvedais sitihāsaiḥ(!) purāṇakaiḥ ||
saptodadhisamāyuktaṃ nadībhiḥ parivāriṃ(!) |
sarvvadharmmapratīkāśaṃ sarvadharmmasamā(śra)yaṃ |
praṇipatya mahābhaktyā nāradas tu pitāmahaṃ |
vinayenopasaṅgamya papraccha munisattamaḥ |
nārada uvāca ||
bhagavan tava dāso smi bhṛtyo smi harivasena |
vaiṣṇavaṃ brūhi me dharmmaṃ sarvvajño si pitāmaha |
phalaṃ vaiṣṇavadharmmasya kathayasva suvistaraṃ |
yenāhaṃ tvatprasādena padaṃ yāmi paraṃ hareḥ | (fol. 1v1-5)

Sub-Colophons

iti skandapurāṇīye dvitīyo dhyāyaḥ || 2 || (fol. 7v5)

iti skandapurāṇīye tṛtīyo dhyāyaḥ || 3 || (fol. 11r3)

iti skandapurāṇīye caturtho dhyāyaḥ || 4 || (fol. 16v2)

iti śrīskandapurāṇīye mañcamo(!)dhyāyaḥ || (5) || (fol. 22r1)

iti skandapurāṇīye ṣaṣṭho dhyāyaḥ || || (fol. 25r2)

iti skandapurāṇīye saptamo dhyāyaḥ || || (fol. 27v4)

iti skandapurāṇīye ṣṭamo dhyāyaḥ || 8 || (fol. 29v1)

End

mahābhāgavataṃ nityaṃ kalau kurvvanti kīrttanaṃ |
jananī putragītena pitṝṇāṃ sa dhurandharaḥ |
kalau hi bhārataṃ nāma durllabhaṃ na ca lapsyate |
brahmarudrapadotkṛṣṭaṃ guruṇā kethitam(!) mama |
yeṣāṃ bhāgavaṃ cihnaṃ dṛśyate gīyate mune |
śrūyate ca karṇau devāḥ te gacchanti hare ggṛhaṃ ||

Colophon

iti skandapurāṇīye navame dhyāye vinā darśena samāptam idaṃ kārttikamāhātmyaṃ || || la saṃ 447 śrāvaṇabadi 5 candravāsare edine viarāgrāme karaṇa śrīkāśināthena vaiṣṇavabāṇakadāsapaṭhanārthaṃ yathā dṛṣṭaṃ pustakam alekhi || iti || (fol. 29v4-30r1)
