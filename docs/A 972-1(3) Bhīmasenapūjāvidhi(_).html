Manuscript Features

MTM contains the sub-chapter dānaphala with samayācāra, mantrasaṃgrahaḥ, bhīmasenapūjāvidhiḥ and pāśavapuraścaraṇa .

MTM is dated NS 810.

On the exp. 18b-20t is available incomplete portion of the Saṃvarttārcana, navadurgāstotra? … available after the colophon of Bhīmasenapūjāvidhiḥ
