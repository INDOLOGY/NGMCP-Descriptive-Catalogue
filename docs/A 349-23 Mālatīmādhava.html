Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

sānaṃdaṃ nandihastāhatamurajaravāhūtakaumārabarhi

trāsān-nāsāgraraṃdhraṃ viśati phaṇipatau bhogasṃkocabhāji |

gaṃḍoḍḍīnālimālāmukharitakakubhas tāṃḍaveśūlapāṇer

vvināyakyaścirāṃvo vadanavidhutayaḥ pāntu cītkāravatyaḥ || 1 ||

api ca ||

cūḍāpīḍakapālasaṃkulagalan maṃdākinī vārayo

vidyutprāpalalāṭalocanapuṭajyutir vimiśra tviṣaḥ ||

pāṃtu tvāṃ akaṭhoraketakiśikhā saṃdigdhamugdhendavo

bhūteśays bhujaṅgavallivalaya sraṅnaddhajūṭājaṭā || 2 || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

kāmaṃ vatsa madhava

yat prāgeva manorathair vṛtam abhūt kalyāṇam āyus matos

tat puṇyair mad upakramaiś ca phalitaṃ kleśopi macchiṣyayoḥ |

nismātaś ca samāgamopi vihitas tvat preyasaḥ kāṃtayā

saṃprītau nṛpanaṃdanauyadaparaṃ śreys tadapyacyutam |

mādhavaḥ saharaṣaṃ |

praṇaman nagavati jñānavijñānanidhe guro ataḥ param api priyam asti |

kāma | tathāpidam astu |

saṃtaḥ saṃnu niraṃtaraṃ sukṛtino vidhvastapāyodayaḥ

rājānaḥ paripālayṃ vasudhāṃ dharmesthitā sarvvdā |

kāle saṃtatavarṣīṇo jalabhuvaḥ saṃtu kṣitau puṇyato

modaṃtāṃ dhvanabudda(!)bāṃdhavasuhṛd goṣṭhīpramodāḥ prajāḥ | (fol. 51r8–51v2)

Colophon

iti parikramya niḥ (!) krāṃtāḥ sarvve mālatīmādhave mālatī prāptir nāma daśamoṃkaḥ || || samāptaṃ cedaṃ mālatīmādhavābhidheyaṃ prakaraṇaṃ || (fol. 51v2)
