Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śri3herukādyaśrīvajravārāhījogarājottamottamam āha || ||

athāta[ḥ] saṃpravakṣyāmi yogarājottamottamaṃ ||

ṣaḍcakrasya yathāyogaṃ yogatantrād vinirggataṃ ||

jihvā tālugatāṃ kṛtvā yogi(!) yogaparo bhavet ||

vīrasya vīrajihvāyāṃ jihvā tālutale punaḥ ||

yogaja sphārayed buddhaṃ sarvvāsākāśavastuṣu

siddhyate janmani haiva mandapuṇyopi mānavaḥ ||

gurupādaprasādena yog(!)abhyāsavu(!)śena tu ||

uktañ ca ||

vi(!)rasya vīrajihvāyāṃ jihvātālvatare punaḥ ||

tāludeśe tu vīreṇa kṛtvā dhyānaparo bhavet || || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

tasyādyācamana[ṃ] datvā jānu⟨r⟩bhir dharanī(!)gataḥ ||

praṇipatya(!) tataḥ kṛtvā pūjayet sādhakaḥ sadā ||

kāmayat sādhakaḥ prahṛṣṭātmā tatkṣaṇād vīra sarvajñaḥ sarvakṛt prabhuḥ ||<ref name="ftn1">unmetrical Stanza</ref>

sarvatrailokyaṃ carī ca bhagavantaṃ sādhakottamaḥ ||<ref name="ftn2">unmetrical Stanza</ref>

eṣa vīro mahāvīra sarvvadvaṃdvavivarjjitaḥ ||

yena vijñānamātreṇa pṛthivī<ref name="ftn3">unmetrical Stanza</ref> sāgaramekhalāḥ ||

vaśam āyānti te kṣipraṃ sadevāsuramānuṣāḥ || || (fol. 23r7–23v3)

Colophon

iti śṛīherukādyavajravārāhīyogarājottamottamaparamarahasya(!) daśamanirddeśa(!) samāptaḥ || ||

ye dhamā hetuprabhavā

hetus teṣāṃ tathāgataḥ || hyavada[t]

teṣāṃ ca yo nirodhaḥ

evaṃ vādī mahāśramaṇaḥ || (fol. 23v3–4)
