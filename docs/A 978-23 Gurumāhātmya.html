śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

śrīmahāgaṇapatiprīty arthaṃ prāṇapratiṣṭhāṃ kariṣye

tatrastha śrīprāṇapratiṣṭhāmaṃtrasya brahmāviṣṇumaheśvarā ṛṣaya gaṇapatidevatā parāprāṇaśaktir devatāyai hṛdayai ṛgyajuḥsāmārthavaṇichaṃdasi mukhe | oṃ bījaṃ guhye | hrīṃ śaktiḥ pādayoḥ || krauṃ kilakaṃ sarvāṅge mahāgaṇapatiprīty arthaṃ prāṇapratiṣṭhāpane viniyogaḥ | (fol. 1r1–5)

na bhaktesadṛśaṃ (!) jñānaṃ na bhaktesadṛśaṃ (!) tapaḥ ||

na bhaktesadṛśaṃ (!) tīrthaṃ tasmād bhaktiṃ samācara || 55 ||

idaṃ sadgurumāhātmyaṃ kathitaṃ tava nārada ||

ya[j]jñātvā labhate jaṃtu (!) paramaṃ padam avyayaṃ || 56 ||

na dhūrttāya kṛtaghnāya na paradveṣakāriṇe || 57 ||

svaśiṣyāyāpi duṣṭāya na deyaṃ ṛṣisattama ||
