Manuscript Features

The available folios are 5, 7–21, 33–36, 39, 40, 70, 71 and 85.

The text in this MS begins from the 24th śloka of the first sargaḥ and ends in the 12th śloka of the 12th sarga. Many folios of the middle are missing so the text in this MS is not continuous.
