Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||    ||

maṃgalo bhūmiputraś ca ṛṇaharttā dhanapradaḥ ||
sthirāsano mahākāyaḥ sarvakāmavirodhakaḥ || 1 ||

lohito lihitākṣaś ca sāmagānāṃ kṛpākaraḥ ||
dharātmajaḥ kujo bhauma (!) bhūmijo bhūminandanaḥ || 2 || (fol. 1v1–5)

Extracts

etāni kujanāmāni nityaṃ yaḥ śraddhayā paṭhet ||

End

putrān dehi dhanaṃ dehi tvām asmi śaraṇaṃ gataḥ ||
ṛṇadāridryaduḥkhena śatrūṇāṃ ca bhyāt tataḥ || 11 ||

ebhir dvādaśabhir ślokair yaḥ stauti ca dharāsutam ||
mahati śriyam āpnoti svaparo dhanado yuvā || 12 ||
