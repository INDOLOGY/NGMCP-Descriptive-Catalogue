Excerpts

Beginning

śrīīśvaruvāca (!) ||

merupṛṣṭe sukhāsinaṃ (!) devadeva maheśvaraṛḥ||

śaṃkaraṃ paripapracchaḥ

Pārvvatīvāca

bhagavān sarvvadharmajñaḥ sarvvśāstrāgamādiḥ (!) ||

āpaduddhārakaṃ mantraṃ sarvvasi[ddhi] pradaṃ nṛṇāṃ ||

sarvveṣā[ñ] caiva bhutānām (!) hitoyaṃ vāṃchito mayā ||

saviśeṣa rājā vaiḥ śānti puṣṭi vivarddhanaṃ ||

aṃganyāsaḥ karanyarasaḥ (!) deda(!) nyāsa ḥ samaṃvitaṃḥ (!) ||

vaktumarhasi deveśa mama harṣa vivarddhanaṃ || (exp. 8a2–7)

End

nigadaiścāpi (!) vaddho yaḥ kārāmgṛhaḥ (!) nipātitaḥ ||

śṛṅvalālābandhanaṃ prāpta paṭhecaiva divāniśiṃ ||

mayaṃ(!) kāmayate kāmaṃḥ paṭhet stotramanuttamaṃ |

taṃ taṃ kāmamavāpnoti sādhakātra saṃśaya || (!)

aprakāsya paraṃguhyaṃḥ nadeyayaṃ yasyakasyacit ||

satkulīnāya śāṃtāya ṛjave dambhavarjite ||

dadyāstotramidam (!) puṇyaṃ sarvvakāmaphalapradaṃḥ ||

evaṃ śrutvā tato devī nāmāṣtaśatam uttamam |

|

santoṣaṃ pa[ra]maṃ prāpya bhairavasya mahātmana ||

yayā ca parayā bhaktyāḥ sadā sarvveśvareśvari ||

bhairavasya prasannobhuta yasvayaṃ ca maheśvaraḥ || (!) (exp. 15a9:15b8)

Colophon

iti śrīrudrayāmaleḥ (!) amṛtasāroddhāreḥ (!) āpadudhārastotraṃ (!) samāṃptaḥ (!) ||

saṃva[[t]] 1928 sāla miti śrāvanaśudi 4 roja 6 śubhm leṣaka rūpanarān nāṃcākṣa (!) śubhmaṃ (exp. 15b8:16a2)
