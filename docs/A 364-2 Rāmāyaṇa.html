Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīrāmacandrāya ||

jayati raghuvaṃśatilaka kauśalyānandavarddhano rāmaḥ |
daśāvadananidhanakārī dāśarathiḥ puṇḍarīkākṣaḥ ||

jitaṃ bhagavatā tena hariṇā lokadhāriṇā |
ajena viśvarupeṇa nirguṇena guṇātmanā ||

namas tasmai munīśāya, śrīyutāya tapasvine |
sarvvajñānādhivāsāya, tasmai vālmīkaye namaḥ ||

tapaḥ svādhyāya nirataḥ tapasvīvāvidāmvaraḥ |
nāradaṃ paripapracha vālmīkir munisattamaḥ || (fol. 1v1–3)
