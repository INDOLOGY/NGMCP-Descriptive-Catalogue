Beginning

oṃ namo ratnatrayāya

evaṃ mayā śrutam ekasmin samaye bhagavān campakapurīmahānagaryyāṃ viharati sma anekai(!) dde(2)vaganā(!)yakṣāsuragaruḍagandharvvakinna(!)manuṣyāmanuṣyaiḥ pañcaśatabhir bhikṣugaṇaiḥ sārddhaṃ bodhisatvai’(!) parṣatbhir abhyarccitaḥ mā(3)nitaḥ pūjito bhagavān puṣpaiḥ paricchanna(!) babhūva tadā bhagavān pramuditaḥ prītisaumanasyajāto bhikṣuṇām(!) āmaṃtrayati sma(4) bhikṣava(!) dharmmam ācarataḥ(!) sarvvajñamitrabhūtaḥ sa dharmmacārī kintu

dharmeṇa sūtam(!) āpnoti dharmeṇa dhanavān bhavet
dharmeṇa(5) cepsitaṃ prāptaṃ lakṣmī vasati dharmmataḥ
taṃ(!)nagaryyāṃ nivāsaḥ śrīcandrasena udīritaḥ
paśya tasya prabhāvaṃ ya(!) vakṣāmi(!) saka(6)laṃ yataḥ (fol. 1v1-6)

Sub-Colophons

iti śrīlakṣacaityabimbakathā tṛtīyaḥ (fol. 50r7)

iti śrīsarvvajñamitrāvadānaṃ śṛṃgabherīkathā paṃcamaḥ (fol. 59r2)

iti sarvvajñamitrapādāvadāne caturthaḥ (fol. 66v8)

iti sarvvajñamitrapādāvadāne piṇḍapātrāvadānaṃnāma pañcamaḥ (fol. 75r7)

iti (fol. 84v1) śrīsarvvajñamitrapādāvadāne pravrajyāvrata ṣaṣṭhaḥ

End

asu prasannāḥ kakubho manoramāḥ uvāśa śuddhyā śaradīva bhānunām
paribhramat keśarapuṣpavarṇitaṃ papāta(fol. 89v1) divyaṃ kusumaṃ vihāya sa
sadā yayuḥ vismayaphullalocanā vaṃdākās(!) tatra sahāpsarogaṇāḥ
papau manojñān saguṇaḥ prabhajano(2) manaḥpramodaṃ jagatāṃ vyajṛmbhataḥ(!)

tataḥ sarvvajñaḥ rājānam āha sādhu mahārāja

anena puṇyena janā bhavaṃtu jarārujāmṛtyu(3)bhaya(!) muktāḥ
dharāḥ(!) sadā śasyagaṇina(!?)pūrṇās(!) tadi(?) pinaddhastanitāpayodā
praśāstu dharmeṇa mahī(!) mahīndra(!) lokās tu ratnatra(4)yapūjane mahān
vaiduryamuktāmaṇibhūṣitāṃgān aṃtaḥpuraṃ paduma(!)dalāyatākṣaḥ

ityādiṣṭaṃ munīndreṇa śrutvā śoka(!) sa bhū(5)patiḥ tatheti pratiśrutvā ca prātyanaṃdat sapārṣadaḥ (fol. 90r9-90v5)

Colophon

iti sarvvajñamitrapādāvadāne puṣpārohaṇaṃ nāma saptamaḥ (fol. 90v5)
