Excerpts

Beginning

śrigaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

oṃ parāparaguruye (!) namaḥ ||

kailāśaśikharāsīnaṃ prasannamukhapaṃkajam ||

gaṇeśaskaṃdanaṃdyādipramathaiḥ parisevitam || 1 ||

surāsurai (!) stūyamānaṃ mahābhairavarūpiṇam ||

saṃkaraṃ paripapraccha praṇamya girinandinī || 2 ||

śrīdevy uvāca ||

bhagavan sarvadharmajña sarvabhūtahite rataḥ ||

sarvāgamārthatatvajña sarveśvarajagatpate || 3 ||

śvetakālyā mahādevyā yattvayā sūcitaṃ purā ||

stotraṃ sahasranāmākhyaṃ tad idānīm vada prabho || 4 ||

īśvara uvāca ||

kathayāmi vararohe rahasyātirahasyakam ||

sārāt sārataraś caiva sarvataṃtreṣu gopitam || 5 ||

bhogamokṣapradaṃ divyaṃ devyā nāmasahasrakam ||

yasya kīrtanamātreṇa prīṇāti śvetakālikā || 6 || (fols. 1v1–7)

End

vinā maṃtraṃ paṭhitvā tu rauravan narakaṃ vrajet ||

tasmāt ⟨pūrvat⟩ pūrvaṃ maheśāni maṃtradīkṣāṃ samācaret || 192 ||

dvādaśārṇamahāmaṃtre mālāmaṃtraṃ śatākṣaram ||

trisakṛcoccaret pūrvam anyathā nisphalaṃ bhavet || 193 ||

tato divyañ ca kavacam etan nāmasahasrakam ||

paṭhanāt siddhim āpnoti śvetakālyā prasādataḥ || 194 || (fols. 12v1–4)

Colophon

hariḥ oṃ tacchat iti śrīvāḍavānalīyamahātantreṣṭādaśasāhasrikāyāṃ saṃhitāyāṃ śvetakālyā sahasranāmastotram samāptam (fol. 13r4–6)
