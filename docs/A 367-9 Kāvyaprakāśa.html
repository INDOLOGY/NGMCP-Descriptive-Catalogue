Excerpts

Beginning

śrīsiddhivināyakāya namaḥ || ||

praṇamya śāradāṃ kāvyaprakāśod bodhasiddhaye ||

padārthavivṛtidvārā svaśiṣyebhyaḥ pradarśyate || 1 ||

athātra śivaśakttisadāśiveśvaraśuddhavidyāmāyākalāvidyārāgakālaniyatipuruṣaprakṛtividdhimanohaṃkāraśrotratvakcakṣujihvāghrāṇavākpāṇī⟪pāda⟫pāyūpasthaśabdasparśa ruparasagaṃdhākāśavāyuvahnisalilabhūmayaḥ ṣaṭtriṃśat tatvāni vedy bhūmi patitāni,saṃvid rūpaṃ tu saptatriṃśaṃ tad api upadeśādau vedyam upacāreṇeti sarvathā yad avedyaṃ svatatraṃta (!) tad aṣṭātriṃśaṃ, tāny etāni sarvatatvāni sarvatra sthitāni, (fol. 1v1–4)

End

paripoṣaṇād vipralaṃbharasam api dyotayatīty ekasyaiva

vyaṃjakasyobhayatrānupraveśād anutkaṭabhedatvena saṃkaraḥ,

tathā cokttam ālocane, sarvatra kāvye rasādi tātparyan tāvad asti, tatra rasadhvaner bhāvadhvaneś caikenābhivyaṃjanaṃ snigdhaśyāmalety atra vipralaṃbhaśṛgārasya tad vyabhicāriṇaś ca śokavegātmanaś carvaṇīyatvād iti, upasaṃharati, evam anyad api udāhāryaṃ,avaśiṣṭā api bhedā lakṣyato lakṣīyāḥ iti saiddhiḥ || || (fol. 34v12–35r1)

Colophon

|| iti śrīkāvyaprakāśadarśane caturtha ullāsaḥ samāptaḥ ||

|| śrīgurugaṇapatigīrbhyo namaḥ ||

|| śrīrāmacanḍāya namaḥ || || (fol. 35r1)
