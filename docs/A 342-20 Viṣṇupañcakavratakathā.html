Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

udayasparśinau daśāḥ paurṇamāsyaḥs tu yāmikāḥ ||

madhyāhnayāpinī śroṇā upoṣyā Iṣṇutatparaiḥ || 

yudhiṣṭhi(ra uvāca ||)

yat kṛtvā mucyate jaṃtur mahataś caia pātakāt ||

tadrataṃ brūhi gauinda(!) (ya)di tuṣṭo si keśaaḥ || (fol. 1r)

End

mūrttīḥ saṣṭi(!) suarṇasya idhiat pūjayet punaḥ ||

pradakṣIṇatrayaṃ kṛṭvā sarvajñāyātisarjayet ||

yaḥ karoti idhānena naro vai viṣṇupaṃcakaṃ || 

praiśya vaiṣṇulokaṃ(!) viṣṇunā saha modate || 

bhojayed brāhmaṇān rājan paṃcadādaśasaṃkhyayā || (fol. 2r)

Colophon

iti śrībhaiṣyottarapurāṇe kṛṣṇayudhiṣṭhirasaṃāde viṣṇupaṃcakaṃ samāptaṃ || ❁ ||
