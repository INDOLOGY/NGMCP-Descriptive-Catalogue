Manuscript Features

In this MS folios are misplaced and microfilmed. In exps. 124b–126 there is vāgdevatāpūjāvidhi.

In fol. 1r there is written: || oṃ śrīchavilālasya pustakam ||

Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ gaṇādhipataye namaḥ ||    ||

vāgarthāv iva saṃpṛktau vāgarthapratipattaye ||
jagataḥ pitarau vaṃde pārvatīparameśvarau || 1 ||

kva sūryaprabhavo vaṃśaḥ kva cālpaviṣayā matiḥ ||
titīrṣur dustaraṃ mohād uḍupenāsmi sāgaram || 2 || (fol. 1v1–2)

End

taṃ bhāvāya prasavasamayākāṃkṣiṇīnāṃ prajānām
aṃtargūḍhaṃ kṣitir iva babhau bījamuṣṭiṃ dadhānā ||
maulau sārddhaṃ sthavirasacivair hemasiṃhāsanasthaṃ
rājñī rājyaṃ vidhivadaśiṣad bhartur avyāhatājñī ||    || (exp. 119b1–3)

Colophon

iti śrīraghuvaṃśe mahākāvye kaviśrīkālidāsakṛtau agnivarṇavarṇano nāmaikonaviṃśatis sargaḥ || ❖ ||
śrī śāke 1690 samvat 1825 miti kārttike di 3 likhitam idaṃ pustakaṃ kāśīnāmanagaryām paphāṇeti || ❖ || ❖ || ❖ || ❖ ||
idaṃ pustakaṃ śrīvajranāthopādhyāyasya || (exp. 119b3–6)
