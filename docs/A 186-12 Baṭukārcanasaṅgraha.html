Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

yan mantra (!) japato vipraḥ siddho bhūd iha bhūtale ||

vaṭukaṃ siddhidaṃ kāṃtaṃ bhairavaṃ taṃ namāmy ahaṃ || ||

vipraḥ || bṛhadāraṇyakaḥ || vaṭukaṃ vaṭūaknāmānaṃ ||

āpadgatamanuṣyāṇām āpad uddhārahetuke ||

maṃtrastotraṃ bhairavasya ṛṣibhiḥ samudīrite ||

atas tadviṣayaṃ baddhaṃ nānā bhaktyā matir mamaḥ ||

yaṃ dṛṣṭvā sādhakāḥ sarve bhaviṣyaṃti gatavyathāḥ || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

nityaṃ samarcanād devaṃ vaṭukaṃ nātra śaṃsayaḥ ||

pūrvoktam dhyānam āsādya pūjāvarṇaiḥ samācaret || 8 ||

⟪||8 ||⟫

tathā pūrvoditāṃ pūjāṃ vidhānena samācaret ||

karakalitakaālaḥ kuṇḍalīdaṇḍapāṇis

taruṇatimiranīlavyālayajñopavītī ||

kratusamayasaparyāvighnavicchedahetur

jayati vaṭukanā- (fol. 122v6–8)
