Excerpts

Beginning

/// hiṃsākaivakopasya brahmacaryyādayo mama lābhasya purataḥ kemīsatyas te ya parigrahāḥ | yama niramāsana praṇāyāmapratyahāradharaṇādhyānasamadhayantu nirvvikāra citttaika sadhyatvā ‥ satkara samunmulanā eva apica, striya evām īsāṃ kṛtvas tanaita asmad gocarā pravavanten te || yatah santu vilokanabha || ṣaṇa vilāsaparihāsa keli parimbhāḥ || (fol. 5r1–3)

End

tathā pādastu, parjjanyosmin jagati mahatīṃ vṛṣṭim Iṣṭhāmbidhattāṃ rājānaḥ kṣyāmvigatavividhopalpavaṃ pālayantu ‥. Tsaiṣā pahatamanu sa ‥prasadatmahāntaḥ saṃsārāddhi viṣayamamatā tajva yajva tarantu || viṣṇubhaktiḥ sarvvabhed bhaviṣyatīti, nikrāntāḥ sarvve ṣaṣṭhāṅkaḥ samāptaḥ || (fol. 58v4–6)

Colophon

iti śrīmahāmahopādhyāyamiśra śrī śrīkṛṣṇaviracitaṃ prabodhacandrodayan nāma nāṭakaṃ samāptaḥ || ❁ || (fol. 58v6)
