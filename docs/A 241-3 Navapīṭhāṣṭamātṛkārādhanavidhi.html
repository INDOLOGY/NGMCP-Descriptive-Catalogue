❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||


atha navapīṭha aṣṭamātṛkā ārādhanavidhi (li)khyate || ||


nakasaṃ pūjājāpakaṃ pūrvasevācchoya || kumāramisā thaṃkālina puṣpabhājana yācake ||


adyādi || vākya || yajamānasya amukasya vivāhoddeśena aṣṭamātṛkā ārādhana balyārcana


pūjākarttuṃ puṣpabhājanaṃ samarpayāmi || ||


śrīsaṃvarttāmaṇḍalānte kramapadanimitānandaśaktiḥ subhīmā ||


sṛṣṭaṃ nyāyacatuṣkaṃ akulakulagataṃ paṃkañcānyaṣaṭkaṃ ||


catvāro pañcakonyaṃ punar api caturaṃ stattvato (!) maṇḍaledaṃ ||


saṃsṛṣṭaṃ yena tasmai namata gurupadaṃ bhairavaṃ śrīkujeśaṃ || (fol. 1v1–7)



«End: »


śrīśrīsiddhāgnibhaṭtāraka yathāśāstroktaphaladāyino bhavantu || gogurubrahmaṇebhyaḥ śubhaṃ


bhavatu || ācāryamantrasiddhilastu || rājā dharmavijayī bhavatu prajāḥ sukhinaḥ santu devaśa


śubhodaryobhu(!) sarve satvvāḥ sukhino bhavantu paryyanyaḥ kāle varṣā bhavatu rāṣṭraḥ śubha


śubhavatu || dvipadacatuṣpadānāṃ śāntir bhavatu puṣṭir bhavatu || asmat rājādhirāja
