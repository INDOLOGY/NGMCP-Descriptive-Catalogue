Excerpts

Beginning

oṁ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || || ||

oṁ namaḥ śivāya || ||

oṃ

mahimnaḥ pāraṃ te paramaviduṣo yady asadṛśī

stutir brahmādīnām api tadavasannās tvayi giraḥ ||

athāvācyaḥ sarvāḥ(!) svamatipariṇā[mā]vadhi gṛṇan

mamāpy eṣa stotre hara nirapavādaḥ parikaraḥ || 1 || (fol. 1r1–4)


End

śrīpuṣpadaṃtamukhapaṃkajanirgatena

stotreṇa kilviṣahareṇa harapriyeṇa ||

kaṃṭhasthitena paṭhitena samāhitena

saṃprīṇito bhavati bhūtapatir maheśaḥ 42 (fol. 7r5–8)


Colophon

iti śrīpuṣpadaṃtaviracitaṃ mahimnaḥ pārastotraṃ samāptam || || || śrī (fol. 8)
