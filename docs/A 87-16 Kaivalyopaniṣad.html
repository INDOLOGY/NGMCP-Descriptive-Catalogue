Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

kaivalyākhyopaniṣadaṃ kaivalyārthāvabodhinīm ||

vyākhyāsye kevalas tena kaivalyātmā prasīdatu || 1 ||

bhagavatiśrutimukhapratipatyarthaṃ kaṃcanāśvalāyanam urarīkṛtya nimittīkṛtya ākhyāyikām avatārayati brahmavi[[dyā]]yām āstikyaṃ janayituṃ,

atha sādhanacatuṣṭayasaṃpannānaṃtaraṃ, āśvalāyano ṛgvedācāryo bhagavantaṃ pūjārhaṃ parameṣṭhinaṃ sarvotkṛṣṭasthānavivāsinaṃ(!) parisametya, śāstrīyena vidhinā samīpamāgatyo vāoiktavān papracha(!) || 1 || (fol. 1v1–4,8)

śriḥ ||

athāśvalāyano bhagavaṃtaṃ

parameṣṭhinaṃ parisametyovāca || 1 ||

adhī hi bhagavan brahmavidyāṃ variṣṭhāṃ

sadāsadbhiḥ sevyamānāṃ nirūḍhām || 2 ||

yayā cirāt sarvapāpaṃ vyapohya parātparaṃ puruṣam upaiti vidvān || 3 || (fol. 1v5–7)

End

antyāśramī sarvadā sakṛdvā japet ||

anena jñānam āpnoti saṃsārārṇava nāśanam||

tasmād evaṃ viditvainaṃ kaivalyapadam aśnutae ||

kaivalyapadam aśnuta iti || 30 || (fol.10r8–9)

…enaṃ paramānandakaivalyaṃ kevalasyātmanobhāvaḥ puruṣābhilāṣaḥ viṣaya sarvapuruṣārthasamāptibhūtaṃ śrute prāpnoti, kaivalyaṃ padaṃ kaivalyaṃ padaṃ aśnute vyākhātam, padābhyāsa upaniṣat parisamāptyarthaḥ || 30 ||(fol.10r12–13)

Colophon

iti kaivalyopaniṣat || (fol. 10r9)

|| || iti śrīmat paramahaṃsaparivrājakā[[cā]]rya śrīmad vidyāraṇyaviracitā kaivalyopaniṣat ṭīkā samāptā || śāke [[1776 mārga kṛṣṇa 9 tithau roja 3 likhitaṃ kṛṣṇarāmaśarmaṇā śubham ||]] (fol. 10r14–16)
