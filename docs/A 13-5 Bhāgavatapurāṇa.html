(janiṣya)te tatpri(yārthaṃ) [saṃbhavantu surastriyaḥ |

vāsudevaka]lā〇nantaḥ sahasra(va)danaḥ svarāṭ |

End

niryāta tyajata trāsaṃ gopāḥ sastrīdhanārbhakāḥ |

tatas te niryayur gopāḥ svaṃ svam ādāya godhanam |
