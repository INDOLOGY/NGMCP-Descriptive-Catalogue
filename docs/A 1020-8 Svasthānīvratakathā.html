Beginning

oṃ namo (!) śivāyai || [[svasthāniikathā]] ||

kailāsaśikhare ramye nānādhātuvirājate ||

kalpavṛkṣavane ramye kāma(2)dhenuviājite ||

cintāmaṇiśiris tatra sarvvatra suvirājite ||

tatrāsīnaṃ jagan-nāthaṃ sarvajñaṃ parame(3)śvaraṃ ||

vāmāṅgasaṃsthitām adritanayā viśvamaditā (!) ||

vināyakena skandena nandinā viśvandi(4)nā ||

saṃveṣṭitaṃ sukhāsīnaṃ jñānamudropaśobhitaṃ ||

śuddhasphaṭikasaṃkāśaṃ trinetraṃ candraśekharaṃ ||

(5) jñātvāvasaraṃ deveśī vacanaṃ cedam avravīt || (!) (fol.1v1–5)

End

etat phalaṃ mahāgaurī svasthānīvratam uttamaṃ ||

yaḥ karoty adhunā(2) martyo bhoginyā bhāvaniścalaḥ

lakṣmiṃ paśus tathā putrā vidyā āyur yaśobalaṃ || (!)

svathānīnāṃ prasā(3)dena sarvaṃ bhavati niścitaṃ ||

svasthānīnāṃ kathāṃ nityaṃ kathayanti ca mānavāḥ ||

vaktā śrotāra lokānāṃ(4) sarvvapāpaṃ praṇasyati ||

dhanadhānyasamṛddhiś ca putrapautrasamanvitaḥ || (!)154 ||(fol. 12r1–4)

Colophon

iti śrīsvasthānī(5)vratakathā samāptā || || iti śrīsamvat 1883 sāla miti māgha sudi 8 roja 1(6) saṃpūrṇam iti || śubham astu sarvadā || rāma || rāma || śubham || (fol.12r4–6)
