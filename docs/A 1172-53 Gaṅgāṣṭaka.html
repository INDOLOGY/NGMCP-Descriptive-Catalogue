Excerpts

«Beginning»


śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

kalpakṣoṇikarodayaḥ kati kati dvīpi dvipānāṃ tvacaḥ |

kā kolāḥ katican(!) nagāḥ kati sudhā dhāmnaś ca khaṃḍā kati ||

kiṃ ca tvaṃ ca kati trilokajanani tvaddvāripūrodare

majjajjaṃtukadaṃbakaṃ samudayaty ekaikam ādāya yat || 1 ||

devi tvatpulināṃgaṇe sthitijuṣāṃ nirmānināṃ jñānināṃ

svalpāhāranibaddhaśuddhavapuṣāṃ vāṇīṃ guhaṃ śreyase ||

nānyatra kṣitimaṃḍaleśvaraśataiḥ saṃrakṣito bhūpateḥ

praāsādo lalanāgaṇair adhigato bhogīndrabhogonnataḥ || 2 || (fol. 1v1–5)


«End»


vācālaṃ vikalaṃ khalaṃ śritamalaṃ kāmākulaṃ vyākulaṃ

cāṃḍālaṃ taralaṃ nipītagaralaṃ doṣāvilaṃ cākhilam ||

kuṃbhīpākagataṃ tam aṃtakakarād ākṛṣya kas tārayen

mātar jahnunarendranaṃdini tava svalpodabinduṃ vinā || 7 ||

śleṣmaśleṣaṇayāmalemṛtavile kāśākule vyākule

kaṃṭhe ghargharaghoṣanādamaline kāye ca unmi(!)lati ||

yāṃ dhyāyann api bhārabhaṃguratarāṃ prāpnoti muktiṃ naraḥ

snātuś cetasi jāhnavī nivasatāṃ saṃsārasaṃtāpahṛt || 8 || (fol. 2v3–7)


«Colophon»


iti kālidāsasya gaṃgāṣṭakaṃ (fol. 2v7)
