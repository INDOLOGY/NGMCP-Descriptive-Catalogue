Excerpts

«Beginning of the root text:»

viśeṣāviśeṣaliṃgamātrā ligāni (!) guṇaparvāṇi || iṣṭādṛśimātraḥ śuddho pi pratyayānupaśyaḥ || 20 || (6)tadartha eva dṛśyaśyātmā || 21 || (fol. 12r5–6)

«Beginning of the commentary:»

-tkāra apavargo mokṣaḥ tau arthaḥ (!) prayojanṃ yasya tat tathety arthaḥ || 18 ||

tasya ca dṛśyasya nānārūpapariṇāmakasya heyatve(2)na jñātavyatvād avasthāḥ kathayitum āhā viśeṣeti || (fol. 12r1–2)

«End of the root text:»

puruṣārthaśūnyānāṃ guṇānāṃ pratiprasavaḥ

kaivalyaṃ svarūpapratiṣṭhāvāci chaktir (!) iti || 33 || (fol. 31r7)

«End of the commentary:»

gaṃgātaṭe paṭṭatapantane smi(8)n

viśvaṃ vapur viśvasṛjaḥ samīkṣya ||

nirīkṣya bhāṣyādikṛtaṃ pravīṇair

anyair mayā bhāṣyam idaṃ vyadhāyi || (fol. 31v7–8)

«Colophon of the root text:»

iti śrīpātaṃjale caturthaḥ kaivalyapādaḥ samāptaṃ ca pātaṃjalanāmakaṃ yogaśāstram || (fol. 31v6)

«Colophon of the commentary:»

iti maithi(9)lasanmiśraśrīkṛṣṇadevatanayamahāmahopādhyāyasaṭhṭhakura śrībhavadevapriyaśiṣyamahāmahopādhyāya sanmiśra (10) śrībhavadevakēte pātaṃjalīyābhinavabhāvye kaivalyanirūpaṇaṃ caturthacaraṇavyākhyāsaṃpūrṇā || śubhm (!) || (fol. 31v8–10)
