Excerpts

«Beginning»


śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||


candragrahaṇa nepālabhākhāna jyāya grahalāghavayā matayāgu || ||

pūrṇṇimāyā ghaṭi rātrī(2) kenāconi suryyava rāhuva ṣaṣṭvāṣṭaka dvidvādaśa sama saptaka eka rāsī lānāconi rāhuyā sphuṭana suryyasphuṭasa

pā(3)da yānā bhuja yāya rāhu śunya juyā aṃśā 14 hṅā juyā oyī thathya jusā grahaṇa du dhāya || thathya majusā grahaṇa madu dhā(4)ya

|| thuli chatākhunu majusā madu dhāya || || grahaṇa datasā grahaṇa jyāya || || (fol. 1v1-4/exp. 26)


«End»


candramāyā kalā aṃgu 15 (6) yā kalā madhya kalā jyāya || candrabimba ghaṭisa 50 guṇayā palāna tane 15 bhāga kayā labdha chakhe taya

|| grāsayā ghaṭi bigha(7)ṭi pikayā ghaṭi 50 guṇa yāya vighaṭina tane || thukī chakhe tayāgūna bhāga kāya, labdha ṣodaśa kalā madhye

grāsāṃguli śe(8)ṣasa 8 guṇa yāya, bhāga unaṃlabdha yava thva grāsāṃguli yava || || (fol. 8v5-8/exp. 33)


«Colophon»


iti grahalāghavayā nepālabhākhā candragrahaṇa jyāyagu jula (fol. 8v8/exp. 33)
