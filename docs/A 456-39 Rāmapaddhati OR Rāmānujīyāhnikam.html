Excerpts

«Beginning»


śrīmate rāmānujāya namaḥ ||


oṃ gurur brahmā gurur viṇur gurur deva maheśvaraḥ ||


gurur eva praṃbrahma tasmai śrīgurave namaḥ ||


ajñānatimirāndhasya jñānam asti śalākayā ||


cakṣur unmilitaṃ yena tasmai śrīgurave namaḥ ||


yasya smaraṇamātreṇa jñānam utpadyate svayam ||


sa eva sarvasampattis tasmai śṛīgurave namaḥ || 5 ||


sarvaśrutiśiroratnam virājitam pādāmbujam ||


vedāṃtāmbujasūryyāya tasmai śrīgurave namaḥ || atha gurudhyānaṃ || (fol. 1v1–2r2)



«End:»


nāsikākeśaparyantan daṇḍākāropaśobhitam ||


madhyacchidrasamāyuktaṃ vathyate(!) harimandiram ||


ūrdhvapuṇḍram ṛjuṃ saumyaṃ lalāṭe pariyaṃtaṃ(!) śubham ||


caṇḍālo pi viśuddhātmā pūjayen nātra saṃśayaḥ ||


sarpan draṣṭvā (!) yathā loke durddarā(!) bhayakampitā ||


ūrdhvapuṇḍrakṛtaṃ tadvat kampate yamakiṃkarāḥ || (fol. 30v6–31r5)


«Colophon»


iti śrīrāmānujakṛtaṃ vedoktaṃ śrīrāmapaddhatiḥ saṃpūrṇam || śubhamat (!) ||


saṃ 1915 līkhitaṃ hanumanadasasya || (fol. 31r5–7)
