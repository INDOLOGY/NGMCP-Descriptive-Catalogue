Beginning

❖ oṃ svasti || śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

sphurattripurasundarīcaraṇayugmam ārādhaya,t

praṇamya (2) varujāripuṃ prathamavaidyam ambhodhvijam (!) ||

vilokya vividhāgamān karaṇavīrakṣubhānmaja (!)

(3)kinsiścit amitiṃ nijāṃ prathayatīha cūḍābhiṣaṃ || 1 ||

kāraṇaṃ maṃḍalavitataṃ lakṣmīr a(4)tha bhaiṣajodyotaṃ ||

jvaratimirabhāskarāhvaṃ grathaṃ(!) tanute sa rogiṇas trātum || 2 || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

ity agnisādaḥ || ||

deśe śrīmeda(5)pāṭe rasayugasarabhumānavarṣe daśamyāṃ

śuklāyām āśvinasya tridaśagurudine yogi(157v1)nīpantanasthaḥ (!) ||

bhūpe śrīrājamalle vilasati vasudhām atadale kumbhasūnu, (!)

kāyastya (!) cuṇḍanāmā jva(2)ratimiraharaṃ bhāskaraṃ saṃnyadhatta || || (fol. 157r4–157v2)

Colophon

iti kāyasthacuṃḍakṛte, jvaratimirabhāskare jvaropadravacikitsā(3)paripūrṇṇatā nāma ṣoḍaśo dhyāyaḥ || || samāpto yaṃ jvaratimirabhāskaro nā(4)ma graṃthaḥ || ||

rasāṣṭavasbhir llakṣye, yāte nepālahāyane ||

māghe māsy aśite pakṣe, dvādaśyāṅ guruvāsa(5)re ||

jvarastimirahaṃsākhyaṃ, (!) raṃmyāsyākhyena (!) dhīmatāḥ (!) ||

vyalekhīdaṃ hitārthāya, sutārthaṃ jagatā (!) mudā || ||

(158r1) atha nepālabhākhā || samvat 886 māghakṛṣṇayā dvādasi,(!) bṛhaspatīvāra thva kuhnu vaidya bhinakhvāra(2)na, thva jvarastimirabhāskara (!) nāma grantha coya sidhakādina, thvaverasa, gorakhārina, kiṃdosa, segu(3)sa, hnuti sathā nātayāo edeśa sagheraya, yāṅāḍo dukhaviyāo tao juro || || śubha || || (fol. 157v2–158r3)
