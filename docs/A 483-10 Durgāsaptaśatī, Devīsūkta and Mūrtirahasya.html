Beginning

svasti śrīmahāgaṇapataye namaḥ || ||

oṃ namaś caṇḍikāyai avighnam astu ||

nityaṃ snātvā śuciḥ kuryāj devarṣipitṛtarppaṇam ||

devatābhyarcanaṃ caiva sa - - - midhānam eva ca || 1 ||

asnātas tu pumān [n]ārho japāgnihavanādiṣu ||

snāto vi(!)kārī sarvatra daivapitre ca karmaṇi || 2 ||

atyaṃtamalinaḥ kāyo navachidrasamanvitaḥ ||

sravaty eṣa divārātrau prātaḥ snānena śudhyati || 3 || (fol.1v1–4)


End

ajñānād vismṛter bhrāṃtyā yan nyūnam adhikaṃ kṛtam ||

viparītaṃ tu tat sarvaṃ kṣamasva parameśvari || ||

tvadatra pāṭhe jagadambike mayā

visargabindvakṣarahīnam īritaṃ ||

tad atra sampūrṇatamaṃ prayāti

saṃkalpasiddhiś ca mamaiva jāyatām || ||

śrījagadambikāyai namo namaḥ || || śubham bhūyāt (fol. 85v3–6)
