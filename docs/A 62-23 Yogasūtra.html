«Beginning:»'


muner vivṛtir mayeya(m)

prātanyate budhajanapratibodhahetuḥ ||


atha yogānuśāsanam ||


anena sūtreṇa śāstrasya saṃbandhābhidheyaprayojanāny ākhyāyante |

athaśabdo ‘dhikāradyotako

maṅgalārthaś ca | yuktir yogasamādhānaṃ anuśiṣyate vyākhyāyate

lakṣaṇabhedopāyaphalair yena tadanuśāsanam yogasyānuśāsanam yogānuśāsanam |

tadā śāstraparisamāpter adhikṛtaṃ boddhavyam ity arthaḥ tatra śāstrasya

vyutpādyatayā yogasamādhānaḥ saphalobhideyaḥ(!)

tadvyutpādanaṃ ca phalam vyutpāditasya yogasya kaivalyaṃ phalaṃ |

śāstrābhidheyayaoḥ pratipādyapratipādakabhāvalakṣaṇaḥ sambandhaḥ (fol. 2r1–6)


«End:»


hānam upadarśyātītānāgatādi sudharmāṇāṃ sadbhāvam upapādya vijñānavṃ nirākṛtya sākāravādaṃ ca

pratiṣṭhāpya puruṣasya jñātṛtvam uktvā cittadvārā sakalavyavahāraniṣpattim upapādya puṣasiddhau

pramā upadarśya kaivalyanirṇayāya daśabhiḥ krameṇopayogino arthān abhidhyayaya(!) śāstrāntareṇaitadeva

kaivalyam upapannam ītyabhidhāya(!) kaivalyasvarūpam nirṇītam iti vyākṛtaḥ kaivalyapādaḥ (exp. 30t 3–7)


«Colophon:»


itiśrī(dhū)reśvaviracitāyāṃ pātañjalayogaśāstravṛttau kaivalyapādaś caturthaḥ 4

saṃvat 1920 li❁khā va||nā❁rasa ||mai śu❁bham || || miti mārga śudi 7 roja 5 śubham (exp. 30t7–30b2)
