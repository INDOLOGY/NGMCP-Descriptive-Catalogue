Beginning

❖ oṃ namaś caṇḍikāyai ||

brahmāyanyai (!) namaḥ ||

brahmāṇī brahmaśāvitrī brahmaṇatvavideyinī |

caturbhujā caturvaktrā caturvvedaparāyaṇī |

caturddaśadiso vyāptā caturyugaparāyaṇī |

haṃsayuktavimānasthā saumyarūpā pitāmahī |

pustakajapamālāya varadābhayapāṇinī |

pītapuṣparatān (!) devī pītāṃgīpītavāsasā |

pītopahārasaṃtuṣṭā pītagandhānulepīṇī |

uduṃbaratalāvasthā prayāgan kṣatravāsinī |

pūrvvapī[[ṭhe]] sthitā nityaṃ brahmaśakti namostu te || 1 || (fol. 1r1–5)

End

ekakālan dvikālam vā trikālaṃ yaḥ paṭhen naraḥ |

śatam āvartta[[ya]]dyastu prāpnoti śubham uttamaṃ ||

nāśayed bhayadāridraṃ nāsayed ripusantati |

nāśayed agnicaurāṇI nāsayed rājakrodhajaṃ ||

nāśayet saviṣarghoraṃ nāsayed abhicārakaṃ |

nāsayel lokadoṣāṇI nāśayetsarvvapātakaṃ ||

āyurārogyam aiśvaryaṃ dhanadhānyaṃ vivarddnaṃ |

dharmmārthakāmakokṣāṇāṃ yaśaḥ saubhāgyasādhanaṃ ||

riddhisiddhiṃ (!) śriyaṃ lakṣṃī vidyājñānasutīnvitaḥ |

buddhiprajñāṃ sumitraṃ ca vandaṃnte (!) ca dine dine ||

nākāle maraṇan tasya utpātan nāśayet sadā |

sarvvalokaiḥ prasasante (!) dīrghāyur api labhyate || (fol. 8r1–8v3)
