Title *Pañcadaśītātparyabodhinīṭīkāsahita

Remarks This is the commentary by Rāmakṛṣṇa on the basic text Pañcadaśī of Vidyāraṇya.

Author Vidyāraṇya, Rāmakṛṣṇa

Subject Vedānta

Language Sanskrit

Manuscript Details

Script Devanagari

Material paper

State incomplete

Size 26.0 x 12.5 cm

Folios 207

Lines per Folio 5–16

Foliation figures in the upper left-hand margin of the verso under the abbreviation || paṃ. ca.|| and lower right-hand margin of the verso under the word || rāmaḥ ||

Date of Copying VS1838

Place of Deposit NAK

Accession No. 5/6991

Manuscript Features

Fol 2 is missing of the chapter Dvitaviveka.

There are different marginal title according to different chapters.

New foliations begin in every different chapter.

The text holds chapters Pañcakośaviveka, Ātmānanda, Advitānanda, some part of Viṣayānanda, Dvetaviveka Citradīpa and some part of Nāṭakadīpa.

The commentary has been written above and below of the basic text.

«Beginning of the basic text:»

guhāhitaṃ brahma yat tat paṃcakośavivekataḥ 0

boddhuṃ śakyaṃ tataḥ kośapaṃcakaṃ pravivicyate || 1 ||

de(6)hād ābhyaṃtaraḥ prāṇaḥ prāṇād ābhyaṃtaraṃ manaḥ ||

tataḥ karttā tato bhoktā guhā seyaṃ paraṃparā || 2 ||

rāma (!) || (fol. 1v5–6)

«Beginning of the commentary on the basic text:»

śrīrāmacandrāya namaḥ ||

natvā śrībhāratītīrthavidyāraṇyaminīśvarau |

pañcakośavivekasya kurve vyākhyāṃ (2) samāsataḥ || 1 ||

taittirīyopaniṣattātparyavyākhyānarūpaṃ paṃcakośavive[[kā]]khyaṃ prakaraṇam ārabhamāṇa āryas tatra (3) śrotṛpravṛttisiddhaye saprayojanam abhidheyaṃ sūcayan sukhataś cikīrṣitaṃ graṃthaṃ pratijānīte. guhāhitam iti. (fol. 1v1–3)

«End of the commentary of the basic text:»
