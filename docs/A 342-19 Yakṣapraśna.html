Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

yudhiṣṭhira uvāca ||

na cāhaṃ kāmaye yakṣa taa pūraparigrahaṃ ||

kāmaṃ naitat praśaṃsaṃti saṃto hi puruṣāḥ sadā || 1 ||

yadātmanā svam ātmānaṃ praśaṃset puruṣarṣabha ||

yathāprajñaṃ tu te praśnān pratiakṣyāmi pṛccha māṃ || 2 || (fol. 1r)

End

yathā kuṃtī tathā mādrī viśeṣo nāsti me tayoḥ ||

mātṛbhyāṃ samabhicchāmi nakulo yakṣa jīvatu ||

yasya te rthāc ca kāmāc ca ānṛśaṃsyaṃ paraṃ mataṃ ||

tasmā(fol. t te bhrā)taraḥ sare jīvaṃtu bharatarṣabha || 86 || (fol. 6r)
