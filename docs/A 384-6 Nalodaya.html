Beginning

. . . sya arayaḥ yadarayaḥ teṣu yadariṣu kṛṣṇavairiṣu mānasthitayaḥ mānasyābhimānasyasthitayo iva sthānāni sannānaṣṭe ityarthaḥ || nāma prasiddhaṃ | yannunnaṃ yena śrīkṛṣṇena nunnaṃ praritaṃ ataḥ śakaṭaṃ udarasat ullalitaṃ ūrddhamūkham abhūt | ataḥ śakaṭe ca mātari iti yatra śrīkṛṣṇena sannāmāḥ samyaṅanamanaṃ sannāmaṃ stana(!) saha varttamānāḥ sa sannāmāḥ samyak kṛta nasaṃbhārā ityarthaḥ bhavabhājaḥ bhavaṃ saṃsāraṃ bhajante tādṛśā na syun na bhaveyuḥ | (!) (fol. 2r1–4)

«Sub: Colophon:»

iti nalodayakāvya ṭīkāyāṃ tṛtīyollāsaḥ || 3 || (fol. 52v3)

End

sa iti yugmaṃ || tan matena tasyā bhaimyāmatena janena brāhmanena (!) iti agādi uktaṃ | iti kiṃ he vaśanāṃ sasyastena caurakāsikka varttase, ayaṃ māmabhyāga rakṣaṇo vidhis tena vayaśasyo yaśaḥprado ṇa (!) svajanena mallajaṇaḥ (!) nāstena tektena hetunā chadma visati śati bhṛtena pāṣI . . . (fol. 53v4–6)
