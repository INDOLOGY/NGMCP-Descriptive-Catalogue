Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namaḥ śrīāryyāvalokiteśvarāya

vasantatilakavṛttam

stutvā praṇamya abhayaṃkarasarvasatvān

sapūrṇacandravadanāmbujapadmanetraṃ (!) ||

sarvajñajñānamukuṭoddhṛtamuktimārgaṃ

taṃ padmapāṇivarabhūṣitajñānarāśiṃ || 1 || (exp. 34 left, l. 9–right l. 2)

End

saṃkīrttiyaṃti (!) tava vīryamahānubhāvaṃ

rātrau divā ca tava nāmam anusmaranti ||

duḥsvapnavighnabhayapāpavināśayaṃti

tuṣyanti deva satataṃ tava rakṣayanti || 25 || (exp. 38 right, ll. 4–7)

Colophon

iti śrī āryyāvalokiteśvarasya bandhudattavajrācāryyaviracitaṃ karuṇāstavastotraṃ samāptam || 16 || ❁ || (exp. 38 right, ll. 7–10)
