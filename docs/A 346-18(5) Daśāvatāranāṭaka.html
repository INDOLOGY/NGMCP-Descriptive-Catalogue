matsya, varāha, paraśurāma, rāma avatāras are included in this Daśāvatāranāṭaka.

There is similar nāṭaka (NS 834) of the same title microfilmed as E 1136/4 from private collection, now in Asha Archives, Kathmandu.The Descriptive catalogue of the same is published by J. Vaidya and P. Kansakar (1991: 165–167).

Beginning

❖ oṃ namo nātyaśvarāya namaḥ ||    ||
sunya me ||
pravesa viṣṇu, lakṣmī, sarasva(3)ti ||    ||
āsāori || co ||
saṃsārayā sāra yāya rajoguṇa joṇā,
pṛthi(4)viyā bhārā kāya śamathana boṅā || (exp. 20b2–4)

Extracts

iti marccha aotāra samāptaḥ ||    || (exp. 22b6)

iti prasurāma aotāra samāpta || (exp. 28b3)

iti rāmāyaṇa prathamāṃka ||    || (exp. 33t7)

thi (9) thiṃ rasa anega yāṅā lokanāthyā śrīnivāsaṃ khaṅā ||    || (exp. 33b8–9)

rāma parikṣapaṃ pikāya ||    ||
hanumaṃta oo bātrā kaṅa ||    ||

māraśrī || ja ||
