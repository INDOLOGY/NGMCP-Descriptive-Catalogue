Beginning

srīgudaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

sāyaṃ tāṇḍavanirbharabhramicayodbhrāṃtakṣapānāyako-

dvāṃtānekasudhābhuṣecanavaśāt saṃjīvite dvīpini ||

nagnaḥ śailasutāpuraḥpaśupatir lajjābharavyākulaḥ

pāyān narttanatālarakṣaṇaparo lokādhipo naḥ sadā ||
