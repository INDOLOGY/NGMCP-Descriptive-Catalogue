Manuscript Features

There is notes on area of pṛthvī and pātāla at the right hand margin in the end of the manuscript as below:

❖ ṅaya koṭī śahaśra yojana prīthviyā vistāra || ❖ hnasa dora śahaśra yojana, pātārana tha prīthvi || (fol. 211)

There is notes on wages(?) in grain and cash of any work written in second hand at the end of manuscript:

māthaṃ ❖ vā phaṃ 14 chapataka ❖ vā phaṃ 14 chapataka ❖ vā phaṃ 20 chapataka

ke phaṃ 6 chapataka ke phaṃ 13 chapataka ke phaṃ 9 chapataka

mo ṭaṃ 5 chapataka mo ṭaṃ 4 ke phaṃ 11 chamohora bhāva mo ṭaṃ 2 misāto biyāva hara mo ṭaṃ 1 kātika śu 4 ❖ saṃ 848 mo ṭaṃ 1 kātika kṛ 15 mo ṭaṃ 1 daṃ 100 chapataka mo ṭaṃ 1 śuki 3 chapataka (fol. 211)
