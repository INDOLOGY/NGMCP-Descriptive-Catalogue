Manuscript Features

As stated in the colophon, this MS is a transcript of one palm-leaf MS dated in NS 307, but lacuna in that palm-leaf MS has been filled in with portions of text from some another palm-leaf MS. Fols. 1–9, 77–84.

Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namo bhagavate vāsudevāya || ||

atha dīkṣā ||

pūrvavat snānam ācamanaṇ kṛtvā paścād arghapātraṃ dhāraṇādvitayena saṃśodhya dhenumudrayā, tenārghapātrodakena sarvāṇi dravyāṇi saṃprosya | tata ātmānaṃm (!) arcayet | puṣpāñjalinā śirasi mūlamantreṇa tatorghapātraṃ pūjayet, mūlamantreṇāṅgasahitena | tato dvārabāhyaṃ gandhā dvārapālān pūjayet | puṣpārghatoyahastaṃ praviśed yāgamandiraṃ, nṛsiṃhacakraastreṇa parijaptaṃ siddhārthakaṃ kṣiped dikṣu | (fol. 1v1-3)

End

ācāryaprabhṛtin mūrttipān pūjayet| rātrau jāgaraṇaṃ kuryāt | caturthe divase deva (!) snapayet pūjayed dhomaḥ ca kuryāt, samastamantrāṇāṃ svena mantreṇa pūrṇāṃ dadyāt | yajñamaṇḍapeśānabhāge kalaśasthaṃ viśvaksenaṃ pūjayet | nirvarttya sarvasaṃbhāraṃ gurave vinivedayet | tataḥ avabhṛthaṃ gacchet || ||

śrījayākhye devasthāpanavidhi (!) viṃśaḥ paṭalaḥ || || (fol. 5v1–3)
