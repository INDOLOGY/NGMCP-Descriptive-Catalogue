Manuscript Features

On the exposure 2 is written ❖ śubha || samvat 809 śrāvaṇakṛṣṇaḥ || pratipadā || bṛhaspativāra || thva kṛti(syaṃ) ārambha dinaḥ ||

On the exp. 161b continues the text on the Newari script.

After the fol. 247, MS is in disordered and repeated sometimes, text seems copied in same time by two scribers. Text is available in mixed scripts Devanagari and Newāri.
