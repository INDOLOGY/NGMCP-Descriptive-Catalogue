Beginning

oṃ namaḥ kamaladalavipula[na]yanābhirāmāya (!) śrīrāmacamdrāya namaḥ

oṃ divi bhūmau tathākāśe bahir aṃtaś ca me vibhuḥ ||

yova(2)bhāty avabhāsātmā tasmai viśvātmane namaḥ ||

ahaṃ baddho vimuktasyām iti yasyāsti niścayaḥ ||

nāty aṃtatajjño no mūrkha sosmya śāstredhikāravān ||

(3)kathopāyān vicāryādau mokṣopāyān imān atha ||

yo vicārayati prājño na sa bhūyobhijāyate || (fol.1v1–3)

End

karmātmakaṃ prathamam eva manobhy udeti

saṃkalpataḥ kamalaja prakṛtis tadetya ||

nānābhidhaṃ jagad idaṃ hi mudhā tanoti
