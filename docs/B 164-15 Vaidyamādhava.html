Excerpts

Beginning

śrīsūryanārāyaṇāya namaḥ || ||

svargāpavargavistāro sāmarasyodarodare ||

bhavarogaharo (!) vande caṃḍikā caṃdraśekhare (!) ||

rasoparasalohānāṃ tailamūlaviṣaiḥ saha ||

susādhyaṃ pratyayopetaṃ kalpāṃte rasasādhane ||

vaidyānāṇ yaśaḥ svārthāya vyādhitānāṃ hitāya ca

vādīnāṃ kautukārthāya vṛddhānāṃ deha siddhaye ||

maṃtriṇāṃ maṃtrasiddhyarthaṃ vividhāścaya kārakaṃ ||

yato haṃti jarāmṛtyur murchito vyādhi ghātakaṃ || (fol. 1r1–5)

End
