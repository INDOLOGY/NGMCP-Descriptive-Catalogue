Manuscript Features

Two incomplete MSS of the Ekākṣarakoṣa and some Liṅgānuśāsana text are mixed up and are filmed under reel nos. A 18/11 and A 18/14. The first folio is missing.

Excerpts

Beginning

ṣvittinvusisvarvvajravārbhṛtāṃ |

paraśu⟪ṇa⟫kratudantānāṃ kuśūlaravayoḥ ghṛ(ṇe)ḥ ||

dhānyānāñ cāsamāhāre to nakṣe dundubhiḥ |

aro ’pajananāḍībhyo yugam aṅgaṃ padaṃ vraṇaṃ |

(A 18/14, fol. 2r1)

❖ striyāṃ dīrghānta ukto pi prākśabdo nāmayoninā |

druhnirṇṇudanuktañ ca vairamaithunikādi vuḥ |

bhāḥ dvāḥ duparatprabhṛtayo(!) mṛṇāladikam alpajāṃ ||

jātyādidīdhitidaratadmuviṭakarddis tathā samā ||

sikatām vā bahutve pi viṃśatyādyāviṃśatyādyaikyavācikā |

(A 18/11, last exp.(fol. 3v)1–2)

End

viṃśatyāder anekatve vṛttir dvitvādikeṣv api |

ācatus triṣu liṅgeṣu nirlliṅgas tu daśāvadhiḥ |

striyāṃ cānavates tāsu śatādis tu napuṃsake ||

nānā lakṣaṃ sahasrādi sārbbudaṃ puṃnapuṃsake |

striyām eva bhavet koṭir bhūmni katir aliṅgakaḥ |

yatis tatir apīty eke striyām paktir daśādhiketi ||

(A 18/11, exp. 2top(fol.4r?)6–8)

Colophon

iti saṅgrahaślokaḥ || ○ ||

samvat 262 phā, śu, e, uttarā, mīnādityadi[ne] (A 18/11, exp. 1top(fol.4r?)8)
