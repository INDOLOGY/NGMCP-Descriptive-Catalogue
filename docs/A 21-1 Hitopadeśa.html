Manuscript Features

Two folios, which originally belonged to another Hitopadeśa manuscript, have been added at the end. Both are numbered in letters and figures on the verso side and are written upon in 5-6 lines to a page in Newari script. Both are damaged with some loss of text. The first of them is numbered as 14 in letters and 104 in figures, the second as 17 in letters and 10+ in figures (the last digit of the number is lost).

Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namaḥ śivāya ||

siddhiḥ sādhye satām astu prasādāt tasya dhūrjjaṭeḥ |

jāhnavīphenalekheva yanmūrddhni śaśinaḥ kalā ||

śruto hitopadeśo 'yaṃ pāṭavaṃ saṃṣkṛtoktiṣu(!) |

vācāṃ sarvvatra vaici(tryaṃ) nītividyān dadāti ca ||

ajarāmaravat prājño vidyām arthañ ca ciṃtayet |

gṛhīta iva keśeṣu mṛtyunā dharmmam ācaret ||

sarvvadravyeṣu vidyaiva dravyam āhur anuttamaṃ |

ahāryatvād anarghatvād akṣayatvāc ca sarvvadā || (fol. 1v1-3)


End
