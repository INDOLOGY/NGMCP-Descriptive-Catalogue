Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

natvā śivaṃ sakalatatva kalādiruḍhaṃ

jñānāmṛtaika bahulaṃ paramātmarupaṃ ||

rāgādi rogaśamanaṃ damanaṃ smarasya

śātaṃ (!) kṣayādhipadharaṃ triguṇātmarupaṃ || 1 ||

himavaduttare kūle siddharṣigaṇasevite ||

śātaṃ (!) maṇigaṇākīrṇe nānā pādapaśobhite || 2 ||

tatrasthaṃ tapasāyuktaṃ taruṇāditya tejasaṃ ||

śuddhasphaṭikavacchubhraṃ mani(!)bhūṣita vigrahaṃ || 3 || (fol.1v1–3)

End

mārkkaṃḍeyo ca kathitā kathitā yogadhīmātāḥ (!)

saṃhitā ṛṣibhi (!) proktā maṃtrer nānāvidher vibhoḥ ||(!)

agniveśaś ca bheḍaś ca jātukarṇya parāsaraḥ (!)

hāritaḥ śalyapāṇiś ca saddharaṇā munibhāṣitāḥ (!)

yathā siṃho mṛgāṇāṃ ca yathā ʼnaṃto bhujaṃgameḥ (!)

devānāṃ ca yathā śaṃbhus tathātreyo ʼsti vaidyake ||

tasmād yatnena sad vaidyaiḥ sākṣād bhedarā (!) sumānasaiḥ

arccanīya sumaṃtavyo dāsyate sukhasaṃpadaḥ (fol.132r6-10)

Colophon

iti śrīmaharṣir ātreyabhāṣite hāritottare śāerīrādhyāyaḥ atha graṃthasaṃkhyā 6200 svasti saṃvataḥ (!) 1857 bhādra māśi (!) pūrṇamyāṃḥ (!) || śrīr astuḥ (!) śrīkṛṣṇāya namaḥ śrīvallabhāye(!) namaḥ śrī śrī śrī śrī rāmaḥ (fol. 132r10–11)
