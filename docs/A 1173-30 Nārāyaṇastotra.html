śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

kecitsvadehāntar hṛdayāvakāse prādeśamātraṃ puruṣamvasantam |

caturbhujaṃ kañjarathāṅga saṅkha gadādharan dhāraṇayā smarāmi || 1 ||

prasannavaktraṃ [[na]]līnāyatekṣaṇaṃ (!) kadambakiṃjalkapisaṃgavāsasam ||

lasan mahāratnahiranmayāṃ (!)gadas phuran mahāratnakirīṭakuṇḍalam || 2 ||

un hṛdrarityamkajakarṇikālayet (!) yogeśvarāsthāpita pādapallavam ||

śrīlakṣmaṇam kaustubharatnakaṃdharaṃ amlānalakṣmā vanamālayācitam || 3 ||

vibhūṣitāmmekhalayāṃgulīyakair mahādhanair nupura kamkaṇādiviḥ (!) ||

snigdhāmalākuñcita nīlakuntalai vircamānāna (!) hāsapesalam || 4 ||

adīnalīlā hasidekṣaṇollasat bhūbhaṃga saṃśucibhuryanugraham || (!)

ikṣetacintāmaya me na miśvaraṃ (!) yāvanmanodhāraṇa yāvasiṣṭate || 5 ||

ekaikaśogāni (!) dhiyānubhāvaet pādādipāladhasitaṃ gadābhṛtaḥ || (!)

jitaṃ jitaṃ sthānamayo svadhārayet paraṃ paraṃ sudhati (!) dhīryathā yathā || 6 ||

yāvannajāṃmeta (!) parāvaresmin vīsveśvare (!) draṣṭari bhaktiyogaḥ ||

yāvatsavīyaḥ puruṣasya rūpaṃ kṛyāvasāne prayata (!) smaretat (!) || 7 ||

iti njārāyaṇastotraṃ śubhaṃ ||
