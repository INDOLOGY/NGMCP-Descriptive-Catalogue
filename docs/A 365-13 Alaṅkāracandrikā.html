Manuscript Features

The text is continued from the starting to some portion of Prativastūpamālaṃkāraḥ. After this, the middle of the text is missing. After this, some fols.of Arthāntaranyāsālaṃkāraprakaraṇaḥ are available. Foliation is disorder.
