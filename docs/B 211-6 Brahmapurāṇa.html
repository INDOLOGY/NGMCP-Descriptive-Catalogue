Beginning

śataśo tha jalair dhautaṃ sur⟪o⟫ābhāṇḍam ivāśuciḥ ||

na tīrthāni na dānāni na vratāni na cāśramāḥ |

duṣṭāśayaṃ dambharuciṃ punanti vyathitendriyaṃ ||

indriyāṇi vaśīkṛtvā yatra tatra vaśen naraḥ ||

tatra tasya kurukṣetraṃ prayāgam puṣkaraṃ tathā ||

tac chṛṇudhvaṃ pravakṣyāmi tīrthāny āyatanāni ca |

saṃkṣepeṇa muniśreṣṭhāḥ pṛthivyāṃ yāni kāni vai | 9 || (fol. 2r1–3)

End

evaṃ mayā muniśreṣthāḥ kūrmmabhūmir udāhṛtāḥ |

mokṣakṣetrañ ca paraman devañ ca puruṣottamaṃ ||

ye paśyanti vibhuṃ stuvanti varadaṃ dhyāyanti muktipradaṃ

bhaktyā śrīpuruṣottamākhyaṃ ajaraṃ saṃsāraduḥkhāpahaṃ |

te bhuktvā manujendrabhogam amalāḥ svarge ca divyaṃ sukhaṃ

paścād yānti samastadoṣarahitāḥ sthānaṃ harer avyayaṃ || ❁ || || (fol. 179r7–179v2)
