Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namaḥ śivāya ||

dhyātvā śivaṃ prathamatatva vicāravaidyaṃ
caṇḍīm abhīṣṭa phaladāṃ sagaṇaṃ gaṇēśaṃ ||
dhanvannarīṃ(!) munivaraṃ muni suśrutādīn
ātreya mugratapasāṃ śirasā praṇamya ||

oṃkāra ādi śakti || dhyāo rayaṃ prathamatatva vicāra yāka vaidyapatista parameśvara śivatvano dūkṣāphala vio pārvatītva nirvighnayāka gaṇepatitvano vaidya dhanvantarītvaṃ saśrutatotvaṃ ādi na ugratayā ātreya munitvaṃ noṃ namaskāra yāṅā || 1 || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

(lāhā jhipacitayālu sitati jhipa cita lusi) ikā ākhe hnāyakana saconi piṃkī ādityavāra khuhna yoka cinā vadhāyake, adhicāva vaṃ yā || 1 ||
|| haritāramasa manāśiramaṃsa gaṃdhakamasa sihlāyamaṃsa halumaṃsa pādhāmaṃsa vāsatolā 1 sādhela tolā 4 lakhatayā va bhiṃkaniya cvāsu kachu va vayānāsa ||    || (fol. 220r4–7)
