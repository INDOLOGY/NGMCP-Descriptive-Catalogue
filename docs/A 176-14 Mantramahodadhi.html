pātālapañcadaśavahnihimāṃśukoṣṭe varṇāl likhel lipi (!) bhavān kramaśaḥ sudhīmān || 1 || śubhaṃ

The fol. 48 is not mentioned. But, text is not lost. It means that the scriber might give the number 49 instead of 48. So, the fol. 49 should be the fol. 48 and it should be till the end of MS.

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||     ||

natvā lakṣmīpatiṃ devaṃ svīye maṃtramahodadhū ||

nāvaṃ viracaye (!) ramyāṃ tadguṇāya guṇai (!) (2) yutāṃ. 1

tatra tāvan mahodadhināmakaṃ taṃtraṃ cikīrṣur ācāryaḥ śiṣṭācārapālanāya nirvighnagraṃthasamā(3)ptaye iṣṭadevatā (!) namaskārapūrvakaṃ graṃthakaraṇaṃ pratijānīte. | (fol. 1v1–3)

iti sahasracaṃḍīvidhānaṃ. sahasracaṃḍīvidheḥ phalam āha. evaṃ sahasrasaṃkhyāka (7) iti. 120 etad ayutaṃ caṃḍīvidhānaṃ lakṣacaṃḍīvidhānayor upalakṣakaṃ. sahasracaṃḍīdaśaguṇo ʼyu(8)tacaṃḍīvidhiḥ tad daśaguṇo [[la]]kṣacaṃḍīvidhiḥ jape home dakṣiṇāyāṃ viprabhojane ca daśaguṇatam. (9) || 221 || (fol. 57v6–9)
