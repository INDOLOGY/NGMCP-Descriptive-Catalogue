Beginning

«In title page:»

dharmao lakṣmīo saṃvādayāgu saphu

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

jato lakṣmi(exp. 2rt2) tato dharma

tato sate : jato dharma tatva jaya(3)

gana sate bijyāta ana lakṣmi bijyā(4)ī : gana lakṣmi bijyāta : ana dharma bi(4)jyāī thvapanī khalanāpa bijyāka : (kṣa)(5)sa jadāī : bhāgemāni juīva purūṣāṣṭa (6) daīva sadāṃ nirmala juīva

dharma uvāca(7)

nhāpāṃ dharmana ājñā dayakalaṃ :

bho lakṣmi (8) manukṣelokayā dharma uti majuvagu thugu(8) jarmasa tavamihma : lithu janma⟪⟫(9)ṇa daridra jula

End

thvatena doha yāya mateva he la(exp. 10 lft 15)kṣmi dohayā pāpanasya

bijyāhune (rt 1) gurudoha, mitradoha, svāmidoha, mā(2)madoha, babuyāke paṃcavisva dyātaka(3) thuli doha yākahma kvadhi juīva na(4)rkavāsa juīva candra suryya ākāsa(5)śa datale, lokapāla datalaṃ phānā(6)va talagu narkasa vāśa juīva parvata (7) kubiyānaṃ bhāra thamaṣṭa thuli dha(8)rma satya lakṣmi bināṃ su maḍu, iti śrīdharmasatyalakṣmisamvāda aṣṭa(9)madhyāyo 8
