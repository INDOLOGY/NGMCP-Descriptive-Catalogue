Manuscript Features

The MS is damaged on the upper left-hand and lower right-hand margins. The right-hand damage on 1v, 7r, and 7v causes a slight loss of text.

In the middle of folio 1v and the middle of folio 2r, next to the binding hole, there are two sketches of some Buddhist deities.

There are two exposures of fols. 6v–7r.

The front title card has been microfilmed twice.

Excerpts

«Beginning»

❖ namo mañjunāthāya ||

atha vajradharaḥ śrīmān durdāntadamakaḥ paraḥ |

trilokavijayī vīro guhyarāṭ kuliśeśvaraḥ ||

vibuddhapuṇḍarīkākṣaḥ protphullakamalānanaḥ |

prollālayan vajravaraṃ svakareṇa muhur mmuhuḥ ||

bhṛkuṭītaraṅgapramukhair(!) anantair vajrapāṇibhiḥ |

durdāntadamakair vīrair vīrabībhatsarūpibhīḥ || (fol. 1v1–4)


«End»


jagataś cāsyanāthasya vimuktiphalakāṃkṣiṇaḥ

śreyomārggo viśuddho ⌠ʼ⌡yaṃ māyājālanayoditaḥ ||

gambhīrodāravaipulya(!) mahārtho jagadarthakṛta(!) |

buddhānāṃ viṣayo hy eṣa samyaksambuddhadeśita iti ||

upasaṃhāraḥ || (fol. 24v1–3)


«Colophon»


āryamāyājālāt ṣoḍaśasāhasrikān mahāyogatantrāntaḥpātisamādhijālapaṭalād bhagavattathāgataśākyamunibhāṣitā bhagavato mañjuśrījñānasatvasya paramārthā nāmasaṅgītiḥ parisamāptā || ❁ ||


ye dharmā hetuprabhavā

hetun teṣān tathāgato hy avadat

teṣāñ ca yo nirodha

evaṃvādī mahāśramaṇaḥ ||

śubham astu || (fol. 24v3–6)
