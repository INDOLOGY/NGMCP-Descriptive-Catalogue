Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ yonimudrātmikāyai namaḥ ||

svarggaṅgāya manīpaṭī yati taḍid dī(2)pīyati dyostalaṃ,

yad raṅgī yati marddalī atighanas tārā ca puṣpīyati(3) |

gaurī yatra naṭī yatīndu taraṇī yat kāṃsyatālī yataḥ

śambhor adbhuta nā(4)ṭyasaṃgatir iyaṃ bhūyāt tava śreyase ||

eka eva rasaḥ sthāyī, rasānāṃ (5) yatra śaṃkaraḥ |

anye cāsyopakārāya, jāyate vyabhicāriṇaḥ ||

⟪⟫ ha(6)stakānāṃ vidhiṃ vakṣe, bharatenoditaṃ yathā |

asaṃyutaṃ saṃyutaṃ ca, nṛtya(7)hastam iti tridhā ||

ekahastena yat karmma, tad asaṃyutam īritaṃ |

hastā(8)bhyāṃ saṃyutaṃ jṇeyaṃ, nṛtyamātrasthitaṃ paraṃ ||

patāka tripatākaś ca, karttarī(9)mukha eva ca |

arddhacandrohyarālaś ca, śukatuṇḍas tathaiva ca ||

muṣṭikaḥ (10) śikharaś caiva, kapinthaḥ khaṭa kāmu⟪ ⟫khaḥ |

sūcīmukhaḥ padmakokha(fol. 66v1)ś ca, sarppaśirā mṛgaśīrṣakaḥ || (fols. 66r1-v1)

End

madhyāṅguṣṭhāgramilanā, dūrddhāvakrā(2) pradeśanī |

anye dvetalasaṃlagne, tāmracūḍakaraḥ smṛtaḥ ||

śrīghrā(3)rthe caiva jyambhāyāṃ, kalākāṣṭhā nirūpane ||

vālālāpatha viśvāse(4) saṃkate bhatsanādiṣu ||

saśabdakaḥ prakarttavyas tāmracūḍotra hasta(5)kaḥ || 24 ||

ete kalā caturvviṃśatya saṃyutā kathitā mayā ||

kathyante saṃ(fol. 77v1)yutā anye, karā iha manoharāḥ || (fols.77r1-v1)

Colophon

iti nṛtyaśāstrahastakādhyā(fol. 77v2)ya samāptaḥ || || || ||
