Manuscript Features

Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||    ||

praṇamya devadeveśaṃ sarvagrahanivāraṇaṃ ||
śaniścarasya (!) śāṃtyarthaṃ ciṃtayāmāsa pārthivaḥ ||

laghuvaṃśeti (!) vikhyātā rājā daśaratha (!) purāḥ (!) ||
cakravartti (!) sa vijñeyaḥ saptadvīpodhipobhavat ||

kṛttikānte śani (!) jñātvā daivajñajñāpito hi saḥ ||
rohiṇī (!) bhedayitvā tu śanir yāsyaṃti (!) sāṃprataṃ ||

sā kathaṃ bhedam ity uktaṃ surāsurabhayaṃkaraṃ ||
dvādaśābdaṃ tu durbhikṣaṃ bhaviṣyaṃti (!) sudāruṇaṃ || (fol. 10v1–10)

Extracts

dhyātvā sarasvatī (!) devī (!) gaṇanāthaṃ vināyakaṃ ||
rājā daśaratha (!) stotraṃ śaurer idam athākarot ||
namaḥ kṛṣṇāya nīlāya śitikaṃṭha nibhāya ca ||
namo nirmāṃsadehāya dīrgham aśru (!) jatāya (!) ca || (fol. 11v8–13)

End

gocare janmalagne vā daśāsvaṃtardaśāsu ca ||
tyajāmi satataṃ tasya pīḍā cānya grahasya ca ||

anenaiva prakāreṇa pīḍāmuktaṃ jagadbhavet ||
varadvayaṃ tu saṃprāpya rājā daśarathas tathā ||
manya (!) kṛtārtham ātmānaṃ namaskṛtya śaniścaraṃ ||

śaninā cātyanujñātaṃ svasthānaṃ gaccha pārthiva
svsthānaṃ sa tato gatvā prāptakāmo bhavet tadā ||

yaḥ (!) idaṃ prātar utthāya varaṃ stotram idaṃ paṭhet ||
pūjayitvā śani (!) cāpi tasya tuṣyaṃti (!) bhāskarī ||
sarvābhiṣṭaprado nityaṃ bhaviṣyati na saṃśayaḥ ||    || (fol. 14r2–12)

Colophon

iti śrīskandapurāṇe daśarathakṛtaṃ śanaiścarastotraṃ samāptam ||    || śubhm || (fol. 14v13–14)
