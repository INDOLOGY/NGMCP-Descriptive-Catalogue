Manuscript Features

It starts from the description of the death of Raṇa Bahādur Śāha's queen Kāntivatī in NS 920 Kārtika śukla 4 (November 1, 1799 AD) and ends with the inauguration function of Bīradhārā of VS 1948 Kartika badi 6 (October 23, 1891 AD).
This incomplete Vaṃśāvalī covers description of early Śāha and early Rāṇā period.
