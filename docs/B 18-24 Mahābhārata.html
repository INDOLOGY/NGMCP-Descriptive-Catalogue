Manuscript Features

Missing folios: 1-9, 29, 176ff.

There are two folios with the number 16.

A number of folios are broken in the margin with some loss of text (fols. 103-121, 169-175).

At the end there is one displaced folio without foliation which belongs to earlier on in the manuscript.
