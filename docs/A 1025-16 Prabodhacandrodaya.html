Excerpts

Beginning

❖oṃ namaḥ śrīkṛṣṇāya ||

madhyāhnārkamarīcikāsv iva payas pūro yad ajñānataḥ

khaṃ vāyur jvalano jalaṃ kṣitir iti trailokyam unmīlati ||

yat tatvaṃ viduṣān nimīlati punaḥ sragbhogibhogopamaṃ,

sāndrānandam upāsmahe tad amalaṃ svātmāvabodhaṃ mahaḥ ||

api ca ||

antarnāḍīniyamitamarul laṃghita brahmaraṃdhraṃ

svānte śāntipraṇayinisamunmīladānanda(!)sāndram |

pratyagjyotir jjayati yaminaḥ spaṣṭalālāṭanetra-

vyājavyaktīkṛtam iva jagadvyāpicandrārdhamaule (!) || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

yataḥ ||

praśāṃtārātir agamad vivekaḥ kṛtakṛtyatāṃ |

nīrajaske sadānande, pade cāhaṃ niyojitaḥ ||

tathāpyetad astu ||

paryya (!) nyosmin jagati mahatīṃ vṛṣṭim Iṣṭāṃ vidhattāṃ,

rājānaḥ kṣmāṃ vigatavividhopaplavāṃ pālayaṃtu |

tattvonmeṣopahatatamasas tvatprasādān mahāntaḥ,

saṃsārābdhiṃ viṣayamamatātaṃkapaṃkaṃ taraṃtu ||

bhaktiḥ sarvvam etad bhaviṣyatīti niṣkrāntāḥ sarvve ṣaṣṭho ʼṃkaḥ samāptaḥ ||

(fol. 39v4–7)

Colophon

iti śrīmahāmahopādhyāyamiśraśrīkṛṣṇaviracitaṃ prabodhacaṃdrodayanāma nāṭakaḥ (!) ṣaṣṭhāṃkaḥ (!) samāptaḥ || 6 || ||

samvat 902 phālguṇa vadi aṣṭamī śukravāra saṃyukta dine yoganārāyaṇena svapaṭha (!) nimittaṃ saṃpūrṇaṃ likhitam idan nāṭakaśreṣṭhapravodhacaṃdrodayaḥ (!) || || śubham bhavatu ||

|| śrīśrīśrīrudrāya namaḥ ||

śrī 3 gurave namaḥ || śrī 3 vāgdevyai namaḥ || || (fol. 39v7–9)
