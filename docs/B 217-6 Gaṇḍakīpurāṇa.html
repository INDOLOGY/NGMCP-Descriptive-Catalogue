Manuscript Features

The MS is written in corrupt Sanskrit. Here in the Excerpt, error has not been indicated.

The cover-leaf is decorated with different pictures.

Margins of the all other folios are also decorated with the beautiful pictures.

Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ si(dhi)śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

śrīdevya uvāca ||

kathaṃ to prabaṣyāmi || narānāṃ hitkākaranaṃ ||

duṣināṃ suṣīnāṃ caiva || dālidrināṃ vyādhīpīṇḍītā || [[1]] ||

bahudūṣadālīdrīṃsya || sāṣā tasya vidhiyate ||

pramātmā duṣito jasyu || so pī kaṣṭiḥ puna puna || 2 ||

tad ahaṃ saṃpravadhyāmī || pāpapunya tu saṃsaye ||

kenapāye prabhaven || dūkhino drisyate narā || 3 || (fol. 1v1–6)

End

tulasi darsanaṃ punyaṃ || sarire koṭitirthakaṃ ||

pāyo pānyaṃ samātulyaṃ vadate moccham eva ca || 34 ||

sā kathā tulasi loke pujito jadi je narā ||

gāvo koṭisa0000hasrāni asvamedha satāni ca || 35 ||

koṭikaṃnyā mahāpunyaṃ || kujaralakṣam eva ca || (fol. 21v9–22r5)

Colophon

īti śrīgaṃḍakipurāṇe || tulasimahātamaḥ || caturthe adhyāye || 4 ||

saṃpurna smapta ||

jāthā pratti tathā liṣitaṃ | ||    ||    || ⟪taṃ⟫ || śrīkeśavabhāṃrati || prasādasyā ma bhārati || svoyaṃ paṭanāratī || śrīsyāmabhārati liṣitaṃ || mama doṣo na diyete || liṣitaṃ ṇepāladesa || phulaḍolakoṭa || (fol. 22r5–10)
