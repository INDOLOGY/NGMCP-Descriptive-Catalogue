Excerpts

Beginning

|| śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||


oṁ namaḥ parameśānīṃ kālīṃ durge sanātanīṃ ||

aśeṣājagatāṃ mātāṃ kālameghasamadyutiṃ ||


muṃḍamālāvalīramyāṃ muktakeśīṃ digaṃbarāṃ ||

lalajjihvāṃ ghorarāvām āraktalocanatrayīṃ || (fol. 1v1–5)


End

sudhāpūrṇasvannahastayoginībhir virājitāṃ ||

āraktamukhamālābhir †maṃḍābhir† asṛgarṇavaiḥ ||


ghorarūpair mahānādaiś caṃḍatāpaiś ca bhairavaiḥ ||

gṛhītaśavakaṃkālijayaśabdaparāyaṇaiḥ


nṛtyadbhir vādanaparair aniśaṃ ca digaṃbaraiḥ ||

śmaśānālayamadhyasthāṃ brahmāyutaniśe(!)vitāṃ || (fol. 3v1–6)


Colophon

iti śrīyoginītaṃtre dhyānam || samāptam || śubham astu || kalyāṇam astu || ||


māsi bhādre trayodaśyāṃ vāsare ca niśākare ||

haradevena likhitaṃ mahāmāyāprasādataḥ || śubham || || ❁ || || (3v6–4r4)
