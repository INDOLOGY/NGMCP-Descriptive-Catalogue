Excerpts

«Beginning»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

oṃ nama ādityāya || ||

navagrahāṇāṃ sarveṣāṃ sūryyādīnāṃ pṛthak pṛthak ||

pi(!)ḍā ca duḥsahā rājan jāyate satataṃ nṛṇāṃ || 1 ||

pi(!)ḍānāśāya rājeṃdre nāmāni śṛṇu bhāsvataḥ ||

sūryyādīnāṃ ca sarveṣāṃ pi(!)ḍā naśyati śṛṇvataḥ || 2 || (fol. 1v1–4)


«End»

oṃ namo ketave

ketuḥ kalayitā dhūma dhūmraketur vivarṇakaḥ ||

lokeketur mmahāketuḥ sarvaketur bhayapradaḥ || 1 ||

raudro rudraprī(!)yo rudraḥ krūrakarmmā sugaṃdhadhṛk ||

palāladhūmasaṃkāśaś citravastradharoʼntakṛt || 2 ||

citrachāgavalīprītir vicitrachāgavāhanaḥ ||

vicitravāma.. ladaścitrayajñāya vittadhṛk || 3 ||

tārāgaṇavimarddī ca jaimineyo grahādhipaḥ ||

paṃcaviṃśati nāmāni ketor yaḥ satataṃ paṭhet || 4 ||

tasya naśyanti bādhā ca sarvā ketuprasādataḥ ||

dhanadhānyaṃ paśūnāṃ ca bhaved vṛddhir nna saṃśayaḥ || 5 || (fol. 7v5–8r2)


«Colophon»

iti śrīskaṃdapurāṇe ketoḥ paṃcaviṃśatināmastotraṃ samāptaṃ śubham || ||

śrī śāke 1720 saṃvat liṣitamidaṃ navagrahapustakaṃ vidyādhara upādhyā sarmaṇam śubham || || miti jyeṣṭha kṛṣṇa 2 roja 4 śubham || || || || || (fol. 8r2–5)
