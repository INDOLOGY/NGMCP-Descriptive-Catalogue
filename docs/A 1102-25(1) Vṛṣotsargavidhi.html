Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namo brahmaṇe namaḥ || ||

vṛṣotsargavidhiṃ vakṣye || ||

hnāyāṃ agnikuṇḍa dane agnikuṇḍayāṃ naiṛtyakoṇasapaṃcagavyasādhanaṃ ||

pītacūrṇena navakoṣṭḥaṃ likhet || || oṃ adyādi || sūryārghyaṃ(!) || amukoddeśavṛṣotsargayajñanimittakapaṃcagavyasādhanaṃ karttuṃ śrīsūryāy ārgho(!) namaḥ || puṣpaṃ 2 || oṃ ākṛṣṇe || || gurunamaskāranyāsārghyapātrapūjā || bali(!) || oṃ namo babhluśāya || oṃ gaṇānāṃ tvā || oṃ ambe ambike oṃ ye bhūtānāṃ || oṃ ghṛtaṃ(!) ghṛta || sarvebhyo bhūtapaye(!) baliṃ gṛhṇa 2 svāhā || dhūpadīpajāpastotraṃ || balivisarjanaṃ || || tataḥ paṃcagavyapūjanaṃ || abhyukṣaṇaṃ oṃ tatvāyāmi || oṃ devasya tvā || pūrve dadhi || (fol. 1v1-15)

End

brahmaṇābhiṣekaḥ || yajamānādīnām abhiṣeka āśīrvādaḥ || yajamānayāta sepha ārati || pratiṣṭhā || || kalaśavoti kāya hotā puṭhi soka josi ācārya yāta || śrīlakṣmī yajamānayāta || kalaśana thavathe duthe sakasyātaṃ kalaśa viya || hmethā saṣo sāvahna yāta viya || nāgapva tuthisa tayake || yakṣayakṣiṇī dvārasa ghāya || toraṇa ghāya chesa || || (fol. 23v2-11)

Colophon

iti vṛṣotsargavidhiḥ samāptaḥ || || śubhm || 6 ||

(fol.23v11-12 )
