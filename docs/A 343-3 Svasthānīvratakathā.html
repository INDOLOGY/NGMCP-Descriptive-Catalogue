Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namaḥ śrīgaṇeśāya ||    ||

gaṇapatiṃ ca heraṃbaṃ vidhnarājaṃ vināyakaṃ ||
devīputro mahāteja mahāvala parākrama || (2)
mahodara mahākāya ekadaṃtaṃ gajānanaṃ ||
śvetavarṇaṃ mahādṛṣṭIṃ trinetraṃ gaṇanāyakaṃ ||
akṣamālāśca daṃtaśca dakṣiṇe karaśaṃ (3) tathā ||
parśumodakapātraṃ ca vāmahaste vidhāraṇaṃ ||
nānāpuṣparataṃ devī nānā gaṃdhānulepanaṃ ||
nāgayatopavītāṃgaḥ nānāvi(4)ghnavināśanaṃ ||
devāsuramanuṣyānāṃ siddhagandharvavanditaṃ ||
trailokyavighnaharttāraṃ māravvārudhaṃ(!) namāmyahaṃ || 1 || (fol. 1v1–4)

śrīśrīśrīsvasthānīparameśvarāya namaḥ ||
namastute mahādevī (8) salānāṃ pāpahāriṇī ||
trailokyajananīs tubhyaṃ svasthānīparameśvarī ||
siṃhāsanasamārūḍhā khaḍgaphetakadhā(9)raṇī || (fol. 1v7–9)

End

thva saṃsāla hānā(2)na samudra thva samudra taraya yāyayāta īśvara śrī 3 svasthānīparameśvarayā japa tapa dhyā(3)na stotra pāṭha bāhikanaṃ mevatānaṃ taraya majuva īśvara śrī 3 svasthānīparameśvarayā dharma(4)vrata bāhikanaṃ thva samasta vratayāsinaṃ thvaguli dharmavrata mahāuttama thuti ⟪kha⟫ manuṣyalo(5)kanaṃ govelasaṃ matolatasyaṃ thava nugalasa taya māla dhakaṃ pittāmaha śrībrahmāna julasāṃ ā(6)jñā dayakasya bijyātaṃ ||    || (fol. 333v2–6)

Colophon

iti śrībra(hmā) ājñā kumāra agaṣtya munīśvara sambā(7)de navarāja gomayaju caṃdrāvatī uddhāra śrīsvasthānīparameśvarayā kathā saṃpūrṇam || 28 || (8) śubhamastu sadā ||    || (fol. 333v6–8)
