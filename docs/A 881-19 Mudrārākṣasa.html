Excerpts

Beginning

gā(!) badhyatāṃ badhasamaye lohitā kusumamālā ābadhyata iti pūrvasaṃpradāyaḥ ye ca rākṣasaḥ sabāṣpaṃ pari⟨ri⟩tāpaṃ karoti idāniṃ vidher mmāṃ prati duḥkham anubhāvayituṃ yatna ity ahaṃ manye prathamatas tāvac chatroḥ śriyaṃ śrāvito smi abhinayaṃ kṛtvā darśitaś cāyam abhiprāyaḥ śatrur yathā kiṃ cit kiṃ cid upatāpaṃ prāpnoti (fol. 74r1–3)


End
