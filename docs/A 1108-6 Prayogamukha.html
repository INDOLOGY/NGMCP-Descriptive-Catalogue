Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namo bhagavate vāsudevāya ||

prayogam icchatā jñātuṃ jñeyaṃ ka(!)rakam āditaḥ ||

saṃjñayā ṣaḍvidhaṃ bhedāt trayoviṃśatidhā punaḥ ||

tatra paṃcavidhaḥ karttā karmma saptavidhaṃ bhavet ||

karaṇaṃ dvividhaṃ caiva saṃpradānaṃ tridhā mataṃ ||

apādānaṃ dvidhā caiva tathādhāraś caturvidhaḥ ||

karoti kārakaṃ sarvvaṃ tatsvātantryavivakṣayā ||

tatra karttary abhihite prathamaiva vidhīyate ||

tṛtīyā cāthavā ṣaṣṭhī smṛtānabhihite dvidhā || (fol. 1r1–5)


End

śatṛ yathā || kaṭasya kuvann ity evam ādau || śānaca yathā || kaṭasya kurvvāṇa ity evam ādau || samāso naiva siddhyatīty āha tavyasatsaṃjñakābhyāṃ ca samāso na tu siddhyati kathaṃ purāṇanāṭakakāvyādiṣu prasiddhāḥ || || (fol. 49v4–6)


Colophon

iti prayogamukhe kṛtapaṭalaparicchedaḥ samāptaḥ || || ❖ ||

ṣaṣṭha .. .. bhaṃ mite śāke pañcamyāṃ śukravāsare |

kārttike kṛṣṇapakṣe tu prayogamukhyaṃ (!) marādayat (!) ||

cakrarāja dvijātīnām cakravarttisupaṇḍitaḥ |

kena puṃsā pāṃdunā vai bhaktagrāmanivāsinā ||

saṃ 805 kārttika va di 5 śubha || (fol. 45r3–4)
