Manuscript Features

On the exp. 3 is written in Newari language

❖ dhanī yadhāvarāyirava khadiyārayajiva chatapaṭI rāva jāyithāyi rāyi dhaniḍā dhatāhi thāyi nāma thādha | khāci prakā gvara sohata

Excerpts

Beginning

śubham astu

śrīgurūgaṇapatibhyāṃ namaḥ ||

śrīdakṣiṇāmūrttaye namaḥ ||

trilocanaṃ nama⟪stu⟫skṛtya brahmāṇaṃ viṣṭaraśravaṃ |

pārvatīṃ bhāratīṃ lakṣmīṃ vankṣye mantraprakāśikāṃ.

vaṃde vṛṃdāvanāsīnam indirānandavi(2)grahaṃ ||

upendraṃ sāṇḍakāruṇyam (!) anaṃtam ajam avyayaṃ ||

oṃkārātmākṣarādhīr bhuvanapatiravir dakṣiṇāmūrtticintā-

ratnatraiyambakodyat smṛtiharaṇamahāvākyatattvādirūpin (!) |

aṣṭārṇadvārahārṇapri(3)yanṛharitano śrīvarāhasya cakrin

kṛṣṇātman rāmaviṣṇo vividhaguṇamahāśaktibhāsvan namaste || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

atha nyāsaḥ ||

asya śrīśatruparājayamantrasya tāpasāmanyuṛṣiḥ || jagatīcchandaḥ || śatruhantro (!) de(6)vatā mama samastaśatruparajayārthe (!) viniyogaḥ || saṃsṛṣṭaṃ dhanaṃ || lāṃ | vajrahastāya aṃguṣṭhābhyāṃ namaḥ || ubhayaṃ samākṛtaṃ līṃ sacīpataye tarjjanībhyāṃ || smabhyaṃ (!) dhattāṃ || purandarāya madhyamābhyāṃ || varuścamanyuḥ (!) || laṃ magha(7)vate anāmikābhyāṃ || bhiyaṃ dadhānād vada yeṣa (!) śatravaḥ || lauṃ | pākaśāsanāya kaniṣṭḥikābhyāṃ || parājitāso apanilayantāṃ || aḥ || vṛtraghne karatalakarapṛṣṭhābhyāṃ || evaṃ hṛdayādināsaḥ || indram airāvatarūḍhaṃ (!) vajra(8)hastaṃ śacīpatiṃ ||

upāsmahe devarājaḥ bhaktaśatū niṣṭhadanaṃ (!) || || śubham (fol. 105r5–8)

«Sub-colophon:»

|| iti śrīmantradaivatāyāṃ prakāśikāyāṃ āgneyākhya ekonatriṃśat paṭalaḥ (7) || 29 (fol. 100v6–7)
