«Beginning»


piṃgākṣaṃ gajavaktraṃ caturthakaṃ || 2 ||

laṃbodaraṃ paṃcamaṃ ca ṣaṣṭhaṃ vikatam eva ca ||

saptamaṃ vighnarājaṃ ca dhūmravarṇaṃ tathāṣṭamaṃ || 3 ||

navamaṃ bhālacaṃdraṃ ca daśamaṃ tu vināyakaṃ ||

ekādaśaṃ gaṇapatiṃ dvādaśaṃ tu gajānanaṃ || 4 ||

etad dvādaśanāmāni trisaṃdhyaṃ ya(!) paṭhen naraḥ ||

na ca vighnabhayaṃ tasya sarvasiddhikaraṃ prabho || 5 || (fol. 2r1–2v3)


«End»
