❖ śrīpuruṣottamāya namaḥ ||

atha jaiṣṭhīpūrṇṇimāyāṃ mahāsnānavidhiḥ ||

hnanaṃ sijalaghalacā, gva (2) 3 sali melā gva 3 dvāphalasvāna akṣata,

brāhmaṇa hmaṃ 3 thvate joṅāva khohodo one ||

tute lāhā(3)ta siye || no siye 3 || prāṇāyāma, nyāsa gāyatrīna ||

laṃkhahosa boṅāva, snāna, akṣata, kāyāva (4) balabhadrayā mūlamantra palapāva laṃkhasa te, svāna vayakāva, ghalasa laṃkha tuye || svahmāsaṃ u(theṃ) (5) || joṅa brāhmaṇatoṃ nyāsa yāya, nona mavāsyaṃ jonantaṃ he ||     || (exp.2/fol. *1v1–2 )
