Manuscript Features

On the exposure 2 is written:

rājavijayagraṃtholakṣmipatiśarmaṇaṃ pustaṃ
iyaṃ kaumudī garvavidhvaṃśakartrī
kṛtaiḥ dīkṣitaiḥ paṃḍītānāmpṛthivyāṃ |
tataḥ śaṃkamānā sudhīrāḥ suśiṣyāḥ
bhrameti bhramamtau prayoge prayoge || 1 ||

After the colophon is available index and navagrahamantra.
