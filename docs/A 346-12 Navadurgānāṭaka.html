Excerpts

«Beginning»


❖ oṃ namaḥ nāṭeśvarāya ||


navadurggādevyai namaḥ ||


atha navadurggānāṭakakrama(!) liṣyate || ||


āṣāḍa māṃdalaṃ kālayet || nāṭeśvarapaṃcoprahārapūjāyuko nakhāya ||


cūpe pūjāpaṇḍutarppaṇa || tāraṇayā cakre samaya viya || dakṣiṇāyācaka ||


cetasindhrara || ekāntaka || deva āśīrvvāda || ambe pūrvvasvāna kvakāya ||


svānaviya || marjjātathyeṃ valivisarjanayāya || sākṣithāya || devasvānayaṅāvapyākhana che sa deva sone || (fol.1v1-4)



«End»


samayacchāya || svānake tan nethvate navami vidhi || daśami kunhu gaṇavosyaṃ taya ||


jiyakāvacchāyanatiyāva pāyāta kyāṅavane || devahlāya, koratokalhāya || gaṇapyākhana huyatvārasa ||


devahlāyāva kyāṅa vanevalaśvayā pāyāta napārāya || thvate dhunaṅāvacchesalaṃ svayāvaduṃtāyane thanā vila bhojane ||


thvate daśami kunhu || || (fol. 25r3-25v1)


«Colophon»


|| iti navadurggānāṭakasaṃpūrṇṇa(!)samāptaḥ(!) || || śubhaṃ || ||

saṃvat 806 āṣāḍhakṛṣṇa aṣṭamī śrīśrīsumati jayajitāmitramalladevasana thva sāphuladayakā || || (fol. 25v1-2)
