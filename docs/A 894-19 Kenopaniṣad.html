Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namo brahmaṇe ||

prayatnādyaṃtareṇaiva mana ādeḥ pravarttakaṃ ||

viditāviditānyatvasiddhaṃ brahmāhamadvayaṃ || 1 ||

keneṣitam ityādikāṃ sāmavedaśākhābhedabrāhmaṇopaniṣadaṃ padaśo vyākhyāyāpi na tutoṣa bhagavān bhāṣyakāraḥ śārīrakair nyāyair anirṇitārthatvād iti nyāyapradhānaiḥ śrutyarthasaṃgrāhakair vyācikhyāsuḥ pūrvakāṃḍena saṃbandham avidhitsuḥ pūrvakāṅḍāṃḍārthaṃ saṃkṣepato darśayati samāptam iti ||

(fol. 1v1–3)

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || samāptaṃ karmātmabhūtaprāṇaviṣayaṃ vijñānaṃ karmacānekaprakāraṃ yayor vikalpasamuccayānuṣṭḥānāt ||

dakṣiṇottarābhyāṃ sṛtibhyāṃ āvṛtyanāvṛtī bhavataḥ || (fol. 1v6–7)

End

upaniṣadaṃ bho vrūhītyuktāta upaniṣadbrāhmī vāvata upaniṣadam avrūmeti tasyai tapo damaḥ karmeti pratiṣṭhā vedāḥ sarvāṃgāni satyam āyatanaṃ yo vā etām evaṃ veda apahatya pāpmānam anaṃte svarge loke jyeye pratitiṣṭhati pratitiṣṭḥati || 4 || ||

caturthakhaṇḍaḥ || (fol. 21v6–7)

apahatya pāpmānaṃ apakṣayyadharmādharmāvityarthaḥ | anamte apāre avidyamānāṃte svarge loke sukhaprāye nirduḥkhātmani pare brahmaṇi jyeye mahati sarvamahattare pratitiṣṭhati sarvavedāṃtavedyaṃ brahmātmatvenāvagamya tadeva brahma pratipadyata ityarthaḥ || (fol. 21v 9–11)

Colophon

iti śrīmatparamahamsaparivrājakācārya śrīgoviṃdabhagavatpādāśiṣya paramahaṃṣaparivrājakācārya śrīmac (!) haṃkarabhagavataḥ kṛtau talavakāropaniṣat kṣudragaṇe vākyavivaraṇaṃ saṃpūrṇam || ❁ ❁ ❁ ❁ ❁ ❁ ❁ (fol. 22r2–4)

kenaiṣitavākyabhāṣya ṭippaṇaṃ samāptaṃ || ❁ || ❁ ❁ ❁ ❁ ❁ (fol. 22r1)

iti sāmavedīyā talavakāraśākhoktā kenopaiṣat samāptā || (fol. 22r3)
