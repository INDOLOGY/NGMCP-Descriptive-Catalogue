asārepihi saṃsāre, sārametaccatuṣṭayaṃ |

kāśyāṃ vāsaḥ satāṃ saṃ(3)go gaṅgāmbhaḥ śambhupūjanaṃ ||

chuchu dḥārasā | kāsīvāsa, satpuruṣa saṃ(4)ga yāya, gaṃgāmbhalaṃkha, mahādeva pūjā yāya, thvate sāra datā || 100 ||     || (exp. 46b2-4)
