Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śivādibhyo gurubhyaḥ ||

yasya prabhāvād bhuvana sāsvate pathi tiṣṭhati |

devaḥ sa jayati śrīmān daṇḍadhāro mahīpatiḥ ||

vaṃse viśālavaṃsānām ṛṣīṇām iva bhūyasā ||

apratigrāhakānāṃ yo babhuva bhuvi viśrutaḥ ||

jātavedā ivarciṣmān vedān vedavidām varaḥ |

yodhītavān sucaturaś caturo py ekavedavat || (fol. 1v1–3)
