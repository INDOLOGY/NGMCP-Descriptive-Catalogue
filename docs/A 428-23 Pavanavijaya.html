Beginning

❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

maheśvaraṃ namaskṛtya, śailajāṃ gaṇanāyakam ||

guruṃ ca paramātmānaṃ, saṃsārabhayatāraṇaṃ || 1 ||

(2) śrīdevy uvāca ||

deva deva mahādeva, kṛpāṃ kṛtvā mamopari |

sarvasiddhikaraṃ jñānaṃ, kathayasva mama prabho || 2 ||

kathaṃ bra(3)mhāṇḍam utpannaṃ, kathaṃ vā parivarttate ||

kathaṃ vilīyate deva, vada bramhāṇḍanirṇayaṃ || 3 ||

īśvara uvāca || (!)

tattvād bramhāṇḍa(4)m utpannaṃ, tattvena parivarttate ||

tattve vilīyate devi, tattvād bramhāṇḍanirṇayaḥ || 4 || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

ekākṣarapradātāraṃ nā(5)ḍībhedanivedakaṃ ||

pṛthivyāṃ nāsti tad-dravyaṃ, yadatvā (!) cānṛṇī bhavet || 57 ||

svaras (!) tattvaṃ tathā yuddhaṃ devi, vasyāṃ striyaṃ tathā ||

ga(6)rbhāṃgarogakālākhyaṃ, navaprakaranānvitaṃ || 58 ||

evaṃ pravartitaṃ loke, prasiddhaṃ siddhayogibhiḥ ||

ācaṃdrārka grahaṃ jīyā (!) (7) paṭhatāṃ siddhidāyakaṃ || 59 || (fol. 18v4–7)

Colophon

|| iti śrīśivomāsaṃvādokta navaprakaraṇānvitaṃ pavanavijayo nāma svarodayaṃ saṃpūrṇaṃ || (8) || atha kāṃsyapātre salilaṃ nikṣipya madhyāhne sūryāvalokanaṃ kāryaṃ ||

lakṣaṃ lakṣitalakṣaṇena salile bhānur yadā dṛśya(9)te ||

kṣīṇe paścimadakṣIṇottarapuraḥ ṣaṭtriddhimāsaikataḥ |

madhye chidram idaṃ bhaved daśadinaṃ dhūmākulaṃ tad dine,

sarvvañaira(10)(va)bhāṣitaṃ munivarair āyuḥ pramāṇaṃ sphuṭam || ||

saṃ 899 jyeṣṭhavadī 7 ravivāre saṃpūrṇam idaṃ svaraśāstram || || śubhaṃ bhūyāt || || (fol. 18v7–10)
