Excerpts

Beginning

atha strī vaśīkaraṇaṃ

puṣpepuṣpaṃ bhusaṃgṛhya bharaṇaṃ tu phalaṃ tathā

śākhāṃ caiva viśākhāyāṃ haste patraṃ tathaiva ca ||

mulemūlaṃ samuhṛtya kṛsnomantasya tatkramāt ||

piṣṭākarpūrasaṃ yuktaṃ kuṃkuṃmaṃ rocanaṃ samaṃ ||

tilake strīvaśaṃ yāti yadi sākṣādarundhatī ||

kākajamghāvacākuṣṭaṃ śukraśoṇita miśrītaṃ ||

taddate bhojane vālā śmasonerodate sadā ||

(fol. 12r1–4)

End

vastraṃpūtaṃ jalaṃ grāhyaṃ gaṃdhakaṃ tatrabhāvayet ||

viḍoyaṃ hārayāśreṣṭo vījānāṃ drāvaṇe hitaṃ ||

ciṃcājaṃ vīranāraṃga bhātulugaplavetasaṃ ||

cāṃgerī canakaṃ cūka cāmlavargā prakīrttitaḥ ||

ityāmla vargāḥ ||

vālmīka mṛttikā bhāgaṃ narāsthituṃṣabhaśmanoḥ ||

bhāgaṃ bhāgaṃ samādāya vajnamukhā samucyate ||

iti vajramūṣā ||

(fol. 121v4–7)

Colophon

|| iti śrīnātha viracite kāmaratne rasādiśodhanaṃ māraṇaṃ nāma śoḍaṣopadeśaḥ ||    ||    || 6 || gopāla ||    ||

(fol. 121v7–8)
