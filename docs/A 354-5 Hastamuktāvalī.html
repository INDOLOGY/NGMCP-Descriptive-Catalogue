Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namas tasyai ||

gauri tvaṃ kimu vetsi hastakarasaṃ ko vetti no yady ahaṃ,

ced evaṃ bhaṇa tarhi kiṃ hṛdi nanūttānau ca padmau sta(2)nau ||

bhūyo nyaṃ vada kartarīmukhayugālekhena kiṃ syād iti,

smerā⟪ṇapra⟫[[namra]]mukhīṃ hasan girisutāṃ cumban mṛḍaḥ pātu naḥ | (3) ||

dhīraktaḥ guṇasiṃdhudurddharadarogāḍhāvagāḍhasphuraj-

jyotirsmāṭakakāmaśāstravilasatsagītavidhyādharaḥ (!) ||

kāvyāmbho(4)dhikalānidhir vimalatā saṃgītamuktāvalīṃ,

sammodāya śubhaṃkaro vitanute śrīhastamuktāvalīṃ || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

tathā purāṇakavibhiḥ kathitam ūcyate ma (!)

yo yatrābhinayo yogyaḥ so nukto pi niyujyate ||

nṛtye (2) karāṇāṃ śuddhānāṃ, prayogāt tu phalaṃ bhavet ||

uktaṃ svayaṃ brahmaṇaiva, likhitaṃ tan nirūpyate ||

narttakānāṃ bhavel lakṣmī (!), niścalā satato(3)jvalā (!) ||

kīrttir digantaviśrāntā, putrapautrair asocyate (!),

sarvvalokānurāgaś ca, nṛtyacitte (!) sadā sthitiḥ || || || ❁ || ❁ || (fol. 42v1–3)

Colophon

iti śubhaṃkaviracitāyāṃ (!) hastamuktāvalyāṃ samāpto yaṃ graṃthaḥ || || samvat 902 bhādraśudi 1 hastamuktāvali saṃpūrṇa julo śubhaṃ || ||

(5) yeka rūpaiyāle kinyāko pustaka ho (fol. 42v4–5)
