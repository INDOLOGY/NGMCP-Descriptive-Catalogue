Excerpts

Beginning

||    || śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||    || śrībhavānīśaṃkarābhyāṃ namaḥ ||

aṃvarīṣa uvāca ||    ||

yad etat paramaṃ brahma vedavādibhir ucyate |
sa devaḥ puṇḍarīkākṣaḥ svayaṃ nārāyaṇaḥ paraḥ |
yo mūrtto mūrttimān īśo vyakto vyaktaḥ sanātanaḥ |
sarvvabhūtamayo ciṃtyo dhyātavyaḥ sa kathaṃ hariḥ |
yasmin sarvvam idaṃ viśvam otaṃ protaṃ pratiṣṭhitaṃ |
avyaktam ekaṃ paramaṃ paramātmeti viśrutaṃ |
yato janmādi jagato yo nirmāya svayaṃbhuvaṃ |
dadau tasmai ca nigamān ātmany eva vyavasthitām | (fol. 1v1–5)

End

dvipa maja(!)brāhmaṇasaṃvādo mayāyaṃ vo niveditaḥ |
tasya mādhavamāsasya puṇyākhyānaprasaṃgataḥ |
prasaṃgād idam ākhyātaṃ māhātmyaṃ kiṃcid uttamaṃ |
bhānau meṣagato(!) vipra vaiśākhasyāpy abodhanaṃ |
ataḥ śṛṇvantu bhūpas tvaṃ yat pṛṣṭo 'haṃ purā⟪ta a⟫[[naghā]]ḥ |(?)
prahlādasya caritraṃ tu saṃgrāmaṃ sva[[ha]] viṣṇunā || (fol. 130r4–130v2)

Colophon

iti śrīpadmapurāṇe pātāla(sa)khaṃḍe vaiśākhamāhātmye navatimo dhyāyaḥ ||    ||    ||

vaiśākhamāhātmyaṃ samāptaṃ ||    ||    || śubhaṃm astu ||    ||
liṣāpitaṃ sukula anaṃnta || liṣitaṃ nārāyana dāsa || śubham astu || rāma || (fol. 130v2–4)
