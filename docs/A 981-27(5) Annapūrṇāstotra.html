Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrī devyūvāca ||

kailāsaśikhalāsīnaṃ mahādevaṃ maheśvaraṃ |

pārvvatī parayā prītyā prapaccha śaśisekharaṃ ||

pārvvaty uvāca ||

katham īśāna sarvvajña lokeśa su[[kha]]m uttamaṃ ||

labhaṃte mānavā nityaṃ bādhyaṃte pīḍayā na ca ||

dāridryahatacintānāṃ na buddhiḥ pratibhāti hi |

alasāḥ karmahīnāś ca na jāpyate prapiḍanāt ||

kathaṃ sāmrājyasaukhyan te labhante niścalaṃ vibhu |

tan me kathaya deveśa yadi snehosti māṃ prati || (exp. 37t1–4)

End

bāhau śirasi kaṃṭhe vā saṃpūjya dhārayed yadi ||

bhaktā bhogān yathā kāmān aṃte divyāṃgaṇo bhavet ||

tasya syur vvaśagā bhūpā lokavaśyaṃ prajanti hi ||

śatravo nāśamāyāṃ[[nti]] mitrānāṃ vardhate sukham ||

yāni cecchaṃti kāmāni haṭhastāni labhen naraḥ ||

bhūtapratapiśācāś ca rākṣasā dānavāś ca ye ||

na draṣṭum api śaktās te dhṛtaṃ yena mahāstavaṃ ||

sarvvatra caṃ[[ca]]lā lakṣmī niścalā tasya maṃdire |

vāṇī tasya vased vaktre devīṃ satyaṃ na saṃśayaḥ ||

idaṃ stotraṃ paṭhitvā tu yatra santeva gacchati ||

tatra tatra labhen nityaṃ vijayaṃ bhogam uttamaṃ || || (exp. 45t3–8)

Colophon

iti śra rudrayāmale umāmaheśvarasaṃvāde annapūrṇṇāsahasranāmastotraṃ samāptam || || || (exp. 45t8–9)
