Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

nanu vyāptinirūpaṇaprastāve sāmānyābhāvavyavasthāpanam arthāntaragrāhyam ity upodghātasaṃgatyā tad avatārayati nanv iti atra tattadvahnitvāvacchinnapratiyogitākābhāvo vahnitvāvacchinnapratiyogitāko na veti pratipattir draṣṭavyā, ya evetyādinā vahnitvasyāvacchedakatvaṃ, ta evetyādinā cāvyāptyupayuktaṃ tadavacchinnābhāvasya hetusāmānādhikaraṇyaṃ prakāśitaṃ viśeṣābhāvīyatattadvahnitvāvacchinnapratiyogitvāny eva vahnitvāvacchinnāni (fol. 1v1–4)

End

tattadrūpādiviśeṣābhāvo’ py atirikto na siddhet tattadrūpaṃ nāstīty anugatapratītyātiriktaviśeṣābhāvasiddhau satyāṃ tatrānekādhikaraṇasaṃbaṃdhakalpanāgauravasya phalamukhatvād adoṣatvavat prakṛte[ʼ]pi sāmānyābhāve tat suvacam iti bhāvaḥ | atra prativandir(!) anuttaram ty asvarasaḥ kecid āhuḥ ity ābhyāṃ sūcitaḥ || ❁ || (fol. 11r4–6)
