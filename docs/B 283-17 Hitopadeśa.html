na gathetvaṃ dhārasā gvachinaṃ thāyasa vanasa siṃhana āhāra māla juva varaśa śaśana ā(2) /// thva vanaśa śaśana siṃha khaṅava dhāra āva ji mvāya madato dhakaṃ huhu (siṃ)hana je mocakiva (3)/// ya yātani upāyani yāya dhakaṃ cittana bhārapāva thvanali (siṃ)ha vayāva dhāra bhochi śa (4) /// pityāka dhāra je vaya libvāto dhakaṃ mevatāna maṣu dhakaṃ je thava rājā siṃhayāke śīvā(5)/// dhāyā mevahma sīhana dhāra amohmayāsiṃ je thukā rājā dhāra thathyeṃ thukā lībvāta śaśa (6) /// siṃhayā krodha vayāva siṃhana dhāra amohma siṃha je keṅo dhakaṃ dhāra śaśana siṃha bo(exp. 2t1)/// na mora madu tuthisa coṅa yanava kośvacakala lithe thva siṃhana bhārapāva meva siṃha thukā thama yā(2)/// varavaṃta du dhaka bhārapāva guḍā ḷpā chāsyaṃ kena hatakara thva kṣanasa thama saṅātheṃ kepā śana (3) /// pā ṅātheṃ yāta rithe thva siṃhayā krodha vayāva tuthisa dubvāta thahā vaya maphayāva thva siṃha mṛ(4)///na thvatina gohmayā buddhi data thvahma valavaṃta dhāya ||    ||
(exp. 1:1–2t4)
