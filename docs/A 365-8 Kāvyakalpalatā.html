Excerpts

Beginning

|| śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

oṃ namaḥ śārdāyai namaḥ ||

vivṛśya vāṅmayaṃ jyotir amreṇa ya⟨ja⟩tīṃdunā

kāvyakalpa[[la]]tākhyeyaṃ kaviśikṣā pratanyate || 1 ||

sārasvatāmṛtamahārṇavapūrṇimeṃdor

matvārisiṃhasukaveḥ kavitārahasyaṃ ||

kicic (!) ca tad racitam ātmakṛtena kiṃcid

vyākhyāsyate tvaritakāvyakṛte ʼtra sūtraṃ || 2 ||

śāstrārambhe śāstrakāraḥ samuciteṣṭadevatānamaskārapūrvaṃ śāstrasyākhyāṃ prekṣāvat pravṛttyaṃgaprayojanayuktam abhidheyaṃ cāha ||

vācaṃ natvā mahānaṃdakara satkāvyasaṃpade

kaviśikṣām imāṃ vacmi kāvyakalpatāhvayāṃ || 1 || (fol. 1v1–7)

End

padārthasya guṇadadhika guṇapadārthona samasyā pūryato yathā trilokyāṃ sphurataḥ śuddha yaśasaḥ puratas tathāso ʼpiviśvaṃbharānātha somaḥ śyāma vastu pūrvabhau || 62 ||

tad guṇādhikaṃ yaśaḥ | sad gāṃbhīryaguṇasyāgre samudrogoḥ padāyate sanmāma samarojāṃtargaganaṃ bhramaṇāyate || 63 ||

evamaucityena tad guṇādhikatvam āropyaṃ || || (fol. 233v2–6)

Colophon

iti śrījīvanadattasūriśiṣya mahākavi paṃḍitacakracūḍāmaṇi śrīmadamaracaṃdraviracitāyaṃ kāvyakalpalatā kaviśikṣāvṛtau artha siddhi /// samasyāstavakaḥ saptamaḥ samāptaḥ || /// graṃthāgraṃ || saṃpūrṇaścā ///siddhitān ścaturthaḥ (!) || graṃ 2122 śrīmadvāya /// kāvyakalpalatāvṛtau sasūtrayāmanuṣṭutāṃ || || iti samāptoyaṃ kāvyakalpalatā (fol. 232v6–233r9)
