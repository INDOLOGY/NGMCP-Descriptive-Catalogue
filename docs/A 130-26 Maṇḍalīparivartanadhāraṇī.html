Manuscript Features

On the exp. 2, possibly scattered one folio from the Guhyeśvarimantranāmadhāraṇī? appears.

nā buddhi || || oṃ namaḥ śrīnairātmānai(!) || ||

iṣṭaguru namas tubhyaṃ taptakāñcanasannibhaṃ ||

sarvvasattvahitārthāya vidyārājñī namo stu te ||

…

guhyeśvarim (!) iyaṃ mātā mahāmāyeti viśrutāḥ ||

māṅgalyatrāsanī vidyā prapadyāya guhyeśvarī ||

yāyābhijñānamātreṇa vidyate sādhaneśvarī ||

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīcinya(!)sāgarāya || ||

evaṃ mayā śrutam ekasmin samaye bhagavān nandopanandanāgarājābhavane viharati sma || śrīmaṇiratnagarbhamahāmeghamaṇḍalakūṭāgāre mahatā bhikṣusaṃghena sārddha[ṃ] mahatā ca bodhisattvasaṃghena sārdhaṃ mahatā ca nāgarājena sārdhaṃ || tadyathā || nandena ca nāgarājena || upanandena ca || sāgareṇa ca || anavataptena ca || manasvinā ca || varuṇena ca || takṣakena ca || (fol. 1v1–5)

«End: »

nilātrānyupajaratkāri mahānāgahṛdaye || guma 2 ghumāpaya 2 stokajāṅgalibhujaṅgame vikaṭaghola(!)rūpe visphuragarjana vijṛmbhane(!) āvāhayāmiḥ(!) sarvanāgānāṃḥ(!) sarvabuddhādhiṣṭhānataḥ sarvatyaṣṭatathāgatasatyena prabardha jambudvīpa svāhā || namaḥ śrīsarvvabuddhabodhisattvebhyaḥ siddhyantu mantrapadāni svāhā || || (fol. 9r4–9v3)

«Colophon: »

ityāryamahāmeghān mahāyānasūtrād vātamaṇḍaliparivarttonāmadhāraṇi buddhabhāṣitaṃ samāptaḥ(!) || ||

ye dharmā hetuprabhavā

hetus teṣāṃ tathāgataḥ || hyavadat

teṣāṃ [ca] yo nirodha

evaṃ vādī mahāśramaṇaḥ || || (fol. 9v3–5)
