Excerpts

Beginning

śrī mahākālyai

oṃ namo dakṣiṇakāliyai (!) || ||

śrīmahākāla uvāca ||

mahākautūhalastotra (!) hṛdayākhyaṃ mahottamaṃ

śṛṇu priye mahāgopyaṃ dakṣiṇāyā sugopitā ||

avācām api vakṣyāmi tava prītyā prakāśitaṃ ||

anyeṣāṃ kṛtagopyaṃ ca satya (!) satyaṃ ca śailaje || || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

paṭhet stotraṃ kuleśāni jīvanmukta (!) sa ucyate ||

rajasvarābhagaṃ (!) dṛṣṭvā paṭhed ekāgramānasaḥ ||

labhate pamaṃ sthānaṃ devī loke varānane ||

mahāduḥkhamahāroge mahāśaṃkaṭake (!) dine ||

mahābhaye mahāghore paṭhet stotraṃ mahottamaṃ ||

satyaṃ satya (!) puna (!) satyaṃ gopayan mātṛjāravat || || (fol. 8r1–8v1)

Colophon

iti śrīkālikāṃ (!) kulārṇave dakṣiṇakālyā hṛdayaṃ samāpta śubham || || śubhaṃ bhūyāt || (fol. 8v1–2)
