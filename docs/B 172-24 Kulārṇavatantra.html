Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

guruṃ gaṇapatiṃ durggāṃ vaṭukaṃ śivam acyutaṃ |
brahmāṇaṃ girijāṃ lakṣmīṃ vāṇīṃ vande vibhūtaye ||

anādyāyākhilādyāya, māyine gatamāyine |
arūpāya sarūpāya, śivāya gurave namaḥ |

parāprāsādamantrāya saccidānandatejase
agnī ṣomasvarūpāya (!) tryamvakāya namo namaḥ ||

|| śrī umovāca || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

rahasyātirahasyānāṃ, rahasyoyaṃ kuleśvari ||
urddhāmnāyaḥ (!) samākhyātaḥ samāsena ca vistarāt ||

kulārṇṇavam idaṃ śāstraṃ yogiṇīnī hṛdisthitaṃ ||
prakāśitaṃ mayā cādya, gopanīyaṃ prayatnataḥ ||

pustakaṃ ca kuleśāni, paśugehena niḥkṣipet ||
na dadyāt paśuhaste ca, na paṭhet paśusannidhau ||

na paṭhennāsavollāsa, gratthaṃ (!) bhūmau na liḥ . . .
. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . /// (fol. 164v2–5)

Colophon

|| iti śrīkulārṇṇave mahārahasye kāmyakarmmakathanaṃnāma ṣoḍaśollāsaḥ || (fol. 158r1–2)
