Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

nīlodbhavapriayavivarddhitabhānubhālaṃ

sarvasvabhaktijana(2)vighnasamūhakālaṃ ||

viśvaṃbharādharapateḥ duhitasuvālaṃ (!)

vaṃde vināyakam ahaṃ guphitendra(3)jālam || 1 ||

nakhāgraiḥ sadā vādayaṃti satānaiḥ

svaraiḥ ṣaṭjakaiḥ (!) saṃyutānāṃ ca maṃtrī ||

pravṛ(4)ddhiṃ sataṃ me sadā de[[va]]vaṃdyā

sarojā[[sanā]] devi buddhiṃ karotu || 2 ||

samastān śiśun saṃvilokyālpa(5)buddhin (!)

jhaṭity atra pūrve prabaṃdhe niveśyaḥ ||

ato bhairavasyātmajo vacmi samyak

satāṃ saṃmude (6) prasphuṭaṃ praśnabaṃdhaṃ || 3 ||

asakyas tarituṃ bālai (!) praśnanīranidhir yataḥ ||

gaṃgādharo gurūn natvā (7) karoti praśnabhairavaḥ (!) || 4 ||  (fol. 1v1–7)

End

eka śeṣe ca lābhaḥ syād vayād (vyā)diṣvagne phalaṃ vadet 62

(5) khado (!) jayaś ca saukhyaṃ ca bhojanaṃ ca dhanāgamaḥ

niścitaṃ maraṇaṃ ceti kākavācyaṃ phalaṃ tv idam 63 (fol. 20v4–5)

Colophon

iti śrībhairavapraśne bhairavoktaṃ śubham samvat 1956 sāla miti śrāvaṇavadi 10 roja 2 śubha(7)m (fol. 20v6–7)
