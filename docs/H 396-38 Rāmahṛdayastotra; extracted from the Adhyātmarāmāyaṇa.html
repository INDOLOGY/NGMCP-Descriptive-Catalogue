|| śrīrāmahṛdayastotra (!) śrīrāmahṛdaya (!) || (fol.1r1 )

śrīgaṇeśāya nama (!) || || sītovāca || ||

rāmaṃ viddhi parabrahma saccidānandam advayaṃ |

savo(!)pādhivinirmuktaṃ<ref>for sarvopādhivinirmuktaṃ</ref> sattāmātram agocaraṃ || 1 ||


ānandaṃ nirmalaṃ śāntaṃ nirvikāraṃ nirañjanam |

sarvavyāpinam ātmānaṃ svaprakāśam akalmaṣam || 2 ||


māṃ viddhi mūlaprakṛtiṃ sargasthityantakāṃtiṇīm (!) <ref>for sargasthityantakāriṇīm</ref> |

tasya sanidhi(!)mātreṇa<ref>for sannidhimātreṇa</ref> sṛjāmīdam atandritā || 3 ||


tatsannidhyāt (!)<ref>for tatsānnidhyāt</ref> mayā sṛṣṭaṃ tasminn āropyate ʼbudhaiḥ

ayodhyānagare janma raghuvaṃśe tinirmale || 4 || (fol.1v1-5)
