Manuscript Features

The available folios are 1, 3, 8, 11–13, 16, 24, 59 and 65–69.

The MS contains the different small portions of the text from different aṅkas of the Nāṭaka.

The text in this MS starts from the beginning of the Nāṭaka and ends on the nearly the end of the seventh (last) act.

Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

dhanyā keyaṃ sthitā te śirasi śaśikalā kin nu nāmaitadasyāḥ

nāmaivāsyāttadetat paricitam api te vismṛtan tasya hetoḥ |

nārīṃ pṛcchāmi nenduṃ kathayatu vijayā na pramāṇaṃ yadīndur

devyā nihnotum icchor iti surasaritaṃ śāṭhyamavyād vibhor vvaḥ || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

rākṣasaḥ || ātmagataṃ || kathaṃ dāsīkṛtya mām idānīṃ vijñāpanāyāṃ mukharīkaroti kauṭilyaḥ || kā gatiḥ | prakāśaṃ || rājan caṃdragupta viditam eva taṃ yathā vayaṃ malayaketo kiṃcitkālāṃtaramūṣitā iti || tat parirakṣantām asya prāṇāḥ ||

rājā || cāṇakyamukham avalokayati || cāṇakya (fol. 69v5–7)
