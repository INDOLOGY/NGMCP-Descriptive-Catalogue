Manuscript Features

The available folios are: 1–3, 5–18, and 19–29.

Fol. 29 is placed after the fol. 18 and microfilmed in exps. 19b–20t.

In this reel number different portions of two manuscripts containing the same text are collected. The first 17 folios are from one manuscript and the rest folios are from another manuscript. The first 17 folios contain the text from the beginning of the siddhāntakaumudī to the ajantapuṃliṅga chapter. The rest 11 folios contain the tatpuruṣasamāsa chapter.

On fol. 1r there is written:

śrīgaṇapataye namaḥ ||

devarājasevyamānapāvanāṃghripaṃkajaṃ

vyālayajñasūtram induśeṣaraṃ kṛpākaram ||

nāradādiyogivṛndavaṃditaṃ digaṃvaraṃ

kāśikāpurādhināthakālabhairam bhaje || 1 || ...

śrīr varccasvamāyuṣyaṃ pavamānaṃ mahīyate

dhanadhānyaṃ bahuputralābhaṃ śatasaṃvatsaraṃ dīrgham āyuḥ ||

avyādhinā śarīreṇa manasā ca nirādhinā

pūrayann ardināmaāra (!) tvaṃ jīva śaradaṃ śataṃ || 1 || rāmakṛṣṇāye namaḥ || || ||
