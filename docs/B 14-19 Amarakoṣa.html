Manuscript Features

The following folios are extant: 1–17; 19–20; 21–23 only as fragments; 24; 26–54; 56–75; 78–84. Slightly damaged are fols. 1; 14–15; 19; 24–37; 74–76; 83. The writing on a few folios has been rubbed off to varying degrees. There are corrections carried out by the scribe himself and glosses by another hand.

A short description of this MS is to be found in BSP vol. IX p. 29 no. 102.
