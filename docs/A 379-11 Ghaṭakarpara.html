Manuscript Features

On the cover-leaf is the stamp of Chandra Shamsere dated (VS) 1970.

The text as given in the present MS consists of 21 stanzas, whereas several editions of the text consist of 22 stanzas. Stanza 21 of these editions (beginning with etan niśamya) is missing in the present MS.

Fols. 3r and 3v are in reverse order.

Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namo gaṇeśāya || ||

nicitaṃ kham upetya nīradaiḥ

priyahīnāhṛdayāvanīradaiḥ

salilair nihitaṃ rajaḥ kṣitau

ravicaṃdrāv api nopalakṣitau || 1 ||

haṃsā nadanmeghabhayād dravanti

niśāmukhāny adya na candravanti |

na cāmbumattā śikhino nadanti

meghāgame kundasamānadanti || 2 || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

bhāvānuraktavanitāsurataiḥ śapeyam

ālabhya cāmbutṛṣitaḥ karakośapeyaṃ |

jīvena yena kavinā yamakaiḥ pareṇa

tasmai vaheyam udakaṃ ghaṭakharppareṇa || ○ || 21 || (fol. 3v2–3)

Colophon

iti śrīkālidāsakṛtau ghaṭakharparaḥ samāptaḥ || ❁ || ❁ || || ❁ || || śubham astu || (fol. 3v3)
