Excerpts

Beginning

svastiḥ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

śrī sarasvatyai(!) namaḥ || śrī gurubhyo namaḥ || sūta uvāca ||

athātaḥ saṃpravakṣāmi śuddhaṃ kaivalya muktidaṃ ||
anugrahān maheśasya bhavaduḥkhasya bheṣajaṃ || 1 ||

na karmmaṇām anuṣṭānai(!) nadānair tapasāpi vā ||
kaivalyaṃ labhate kiṃtu jñānena kevalaṃ || 2 ||

rāmāya daṃḍakāriṇye pārvatīpatinā purā ||
yā proktā śiva gītākhyā guhyād guhyatamāhisā || 3 || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

|| ṛṣaya ucu(!) || atha prabhṛtinaḥ sūta tvamācārya pitā guru ||
a | avidhyāyā paraṃpāraṃ yasmāttārayitā sinaḥ || 67 ||

utpāda vrahmadātānno gariyan mahatā pitā ||
tasmāt sutātmaja tvaktaḥ satyaṃ tānyosti no guru || 68 ||

ityuktā munayaḥ sarvve sāyaṃ saṃdhyām upāsituṃ ||
stuvaṃtaḥ sūta putraṃ taṃ saṃtuṣṭa gautamī taṭe || (fol. 36r8 & 36v1–3)

Colophon

iti śrī padhmapurāṇe śivagītāsūpaniṣatsu vrahma(!) vidhyāyāṃ yoga śāstre(śivarāghava) saṃvāde sakalādhyāya yogo nāma ṣoḍaśodhyāyaḥ || 16 ||

|| iti śiva rahasye saṃvat 1780 śrī śāke 1645 || jyeṣṭha śuklidaśamyāṃ tithau ravivāsare kāṃtipura sthāne likhitaṃ bharata bharata dvijena samāptaṃ śubhaṃ bhūyāta (fol. 36v2–6)
