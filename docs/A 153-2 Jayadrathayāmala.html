Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ svasti || oṃ namo gaṇapataye || oṃ namaḥ śivāya ||

oṃ namaḥ śivābhyāṃ sānucarābhyāṃ ||

garbhākalpakṣayābdhikṣubhitaghanaghaṭāṭopasaṃghaṭṭacaṇḍaiḥ

jhaṃkārais troṭayantī sakalavibudhamṛtkoṭarālīva liṅgam ||

sā ghorā sarvaviśvakṣayam api yā kālakālī karālā

pāyād vau (!) balā (!) mātā kulakamalavanodbhāsakī kālikākhyā || 1

himavatparvvataśreṣṭhe sarvvasiddhisurārcite ||

śailarāje suraśreṣṭha gunau (!) bhavet tisottame (!) ||

tasyātisundarataraṃ aṅgam atyantasundaram ||

sarvasaṃpadguṇaudhāryasundaraṃ surasevitam, (fol. 1v1–3)

End

iti rahasyam idaṃ tava sundari tridaśavanditam etad alaṃ mayā ||

nigaditaṃ paramārthavicitritaṃ sakalam eva jayadrathavatsale ||

iha tu kāni(!)kulārṇṇavamadhyato nigaditaṃ tridaśeṃdravilāsini |

kramavicitrakalātuvijṛmbhitaṃ tridaśabhedagataṃ praṇatuṃ yathā |

tad iha bāṇakathāpravicārato raṇavivekaguhāgatam adbhutam |

tridaśaḍāmarapānamahotsave kim aparaṃ na hi yat kathitaṃ tava |

atra mantravipulāśayatvatā vidyate nikhilatāvagocarā |

yā na kutra cid ihopavarṇitā vīryabhāvavilayodayeśvari |

ṣaṭkam etad upavarṇṇitaṃ mayā kālisaṃjñakam alaṃ sureśvari |

viṃśatidvidaśabhāgavicitritaṃ (!) bhūrikalpaśatavasturājitaṃ || ||

(fol. 131v7–132r2)

Colophon

iti bhairavaśotasi vidyāpīṭhe śiracchede śrījayadrathayāmale mahātantre caturviṃśatisāhasre mahātaṃtre dvitīyaṣaṭke siddhalakṣmīvidhāno nāma kalpaṃ samāptam iti || ||

prācīnatāḍapatrapustakāt svakīyabhāratībhavanārthaṃ śrīmadrājaguruhemarājavidvadvareṇa pūrṇaprasādaśarmadvārā pratirūpakīkṛtya 1982 vaikramābde sampāditam idaṃ pustakam iti śubham || (fol. 132r2–5)
