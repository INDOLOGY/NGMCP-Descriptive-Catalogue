Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namo guruyoginībhyaḥ ||

saṃ⟪ka⟫varttā maṇḍalānte kramapadanihitānandaśaktiḥ subhīmā |

sṛksṛkcādyaṃ(!) catuṣkaṃ akulakulagataṃ paṇcakaṃ(!) cānyaṣaṭkaṃ ||

catvāra(!) paṇcakonyaḥ(!) punar api caturas tatvato maṇḍaledaṃ |

saṃśṛṣṭaṃ yena tasmai namatha gurutaraṃ bhairavaṃ śrīkujeśam ||

śrīmad dhimavataḥ pṛṣṭhe trikūṭasikharāngaṃ(!) |

santānapuramadhyastham anekākārabhūṣiṇaṃ ||

(trśsrasṃvai) triprakāraṃ tu tṛ(!)śaktitṛ(!)guṇojjvalaṃ ||

candrasūryakṛtālokaṃ vahnidīdīpya(!)varcasaṃ ||

trisandhyāveṣṭitaṃ divyaṃ prakāratripathānvitaṃ ||

dvārapālatrayopetaṃ trikapāṭārggalānvitaṃ ||

anekaratnasaṃdīptaṃ udyānavanamaṇḍitaṃ ||

vasantaguṇasaṃpannaṃ satatānandapūritaṃ ||

santānabhuvanaṃ divyaṃ divyādivyair niśevitaṃ ||

tatrastham bhuvaneśānaṃ vyaktāvyaktaṃ sanātanaṃ ||

kāryakāraṇabhādena kiṇcit(!) kālam upekṣayet ||

tiṣṭhate bhairavīśāno maunam ādāya niścalaṃ ||

(fol.1v1-6 )

End

gajaś caiva tu kūṣmāṇḍaṃ palāṇḍuṇ(!) ca viśeṣataḥ |

paryyuṣitācchālitahyagaruṃ pippallyā(!) talās(!) tathā |

kṛṣṇacchāgā mahānetrī palalaṃ meṣātmakaṃ smṛtaṃ |

sāmartha(ntu)vidhānāś ca iti pūjā prakīrttitāḥ(!) |

siddhadravyasamākhyātaṃ prasaṅgād yoginīkule |

nānena rahitā siddhibhuktimuktiṃ na vidyate |

nirācapadaṃ(!) hyetattu bhedaparamaṃ smṛtaṃ || ||

(fol.129v2-4 )

Colophon

iti kulālikāmnāye śrīkubjikāmate samastajñānāvabodhaācaryānirddeso nāma paṇcatriṃśatimaḥ paṭalaḥ || || iti caturviṃśatisārātsārataraṃ kubji | ||kāmate śrīkulapīṭhavinirgataṃ paṇcaviṃśati paṭa///

(fol.129v4-5)
