Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

kvaṇat kiṃkiṇījālakolāhalāḍhyaṃ

lasat pītavā(2)so vasānaṃ calaṃtaṃ ||

yaśodāṃgaṇe yoginām apyagamyaṃ

bhajehaṃ mukuṃdaṃ gha(3)naśyāmalāṃgaṃ || 1 ||

na cet khecarāḥ sthāpite kiṃ bhacakre

na cet spaṣṭagāḥ sthāpi(4)taiḥ kiṃ graheṃdraiḥ ||

abhāvāditaḥ spaṣṭatā kotra hetuḥ

phalair eva sarvaṃ vruve tāni ta(5)smāt || 2 || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

śikhī riṣphago (10) pādanetre ca pīḍā

svayaṃ rājatulyo vyayaṃ satkaroti ||

ripur nāśa(11)naṃ mātule nai[[va]] śarma

rujai (!) pīḍyate vastiguhye sadaiva || 12 || (fol. 13r9–11)

|| || iti ketoḥ dvādaśabhāvaphalaṃ || itī (!) śrīya cama(2)tkāraciṃtāmaṇau navagrahaṇāṃ dvādaśabhāvaphalaṃ sa(3)māptaṃ śubhaṃ bhavatu || kalyāṇam astu || saṃvat 1716 va(4)rṣe phālguṇavadi 1 some liṣitaṃ (!) bhaṭavīreśvareṇa (!) bhaṭalakṣma(5)ṇasutena (!)  ||

yādṛśaṃ pustakaṃ dṛṣṭvā tādṛśaṃ liṣitaṃ mayā |
