Beginning

❖ oṁ namaḥ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||


atha(ḥ) bhūtacaturdaśīvusādhanavidhiḥ likhyate ||

nityayātisādakvaśirake | duthusavaṃthelake hṅathukuhnu || ghaṇṭāvādyadalutha ohoyāvasujātadakvavuyake || ||

lasayā ca vadaśane bhālamavivaladhūnake māla || lasayājāpūjādakkva ṅāyāvapithūsate || || mūlavāhikana ||

sakalabhiṃsindhrarāyarape dṛṣṭiyajamakā aduvālakarṇapatākā pañcapatākā tāyapatavāsanacoyamālako vasape || ||

(exp. 2.1–3t1)


End

samastacakracakreśi yutadevīnavatmike |

ātrātrikam idaṃ puṇyaṃ gṛhāna mama siddhaye ||


iti pathanadevyā samastakoparitribrāmapūrvvakaṃ || māpādatalasamastaṃ navadhanirājayad iti purasaḥ sthāpanaṃ ||

yathāśaktajapastotraṃ || || nānyam vadāmit(!)yādi || || tarttadijaṃ pūrvvakaṃ pañcamudrāyā praṇamya ditvā

rātrikavidhiḥ || || samayanyānacchaya || || arghāhāyakanta || mūlavidyānāṃ || putanarppana || hastana ||

prītapretātyādi || pañcamudrā || svānakokāya || ekānekamūrtit(!)tyādi || || (exp. 56b1–57b1)


«Sub-Colophon(s)»


iti caturdaśībusādhanapaddhati vidhiḥ samāptaḥ || || ❁ || || śubham astu sarvvadā || || (exp. 55t3–4)


«Colophon(s)»


iti ātrātrikavidhiḥ samāptaḥ || || śrīśrībhavānyai namaḥ || samvat 822 caitraśuklapurṇimā kuhnu likhitaṃ

vidhyāpīṭhayā karmmācārya dhanasiṃha || || ❁ || || śubhaṃ bhavatu sarvvadā || śubha || (exp. 57b2–4)
