Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrīśrīśrīmahāgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||


śrīgurubhyo namaḥ || ||


atha dīkṣāvidhiḥ || ||


nekasa dina soyā va guruyāke goya tasyaṃ ājñā kāyā va śiṣyana guru ādina sakaleṃ ṅamūnakāva


dina soyā va dina vyakta yāya || dhvākhā ināya ādina aṣṭamātṛkā dūmāju ādina ṅābhiṃ goya cchoya ||


guru ādina pūjā vāli josi ācārya sakastāṃ goyacchakoṭa dakṣiṇā viya || (exp. 3t1–5)



End

+lakuṭhi māneśvarī sa+na ṅāva | pūkuṭhi yātaṃ pūjā jopayake thava 2 pūjābalina ghūtacchāyā homa


yāṅā thāyasa jujuna || || navam,ī kuhnu meśasa lākhatalavane jujuna khālameśa syāya madhuvala || ||


khaṃḍasva kuhnu yātaṃ dakṣiṇā daṃ 5 || navamī kuhnu yātaṃ daṃ 3 || cālana kuhnuyātaṃ daṃ 5 ||


khaṃḍakṣepeye yātaṃ daṃ 2 || || aṃtalavānasāhaṃsa sahmaṃcchi 1 || dakṣiṇā daṃ 3 || svāna taya


pūjājoyaya kāthāyasa || (exp. 37t3-37b1)



Colophon

|| || iti dīkṣāyā vidhiḥ samāptaḥ || saṃvat 777 mārggaśiravadi 10 thva kuhnu rājopādhyāya


śrībhāskararājena liṣitaṃ saṃpūrṇaṃ || śubhaṃ || śrīśrīśrī bhavāṇīprīṇātu || ||


❖ śuklapakṣe śubhadine śubhavāre vārānane |


mantrasyāvaraṇaṃ kuryād grahaṇe candrasūryayoḥ ||


navāranāstu(!) doṣāni(!) grahane (!) candrasūryayoḥ |


pavitraparvva deveśi ārambhena śubhe dine ||


caitrāniḥ … ||


vaiśākhe kṣemalābhastu jyeṣṭhe ca maraṇaṃ dhruvaṃ |


ākhāḍhe bandhunāśaṃ ca śrāvaṇe vasatāyutaṃ ||


kṣayaṃ bhādrapade māsi āśvine ratnasaṃcaya |


kārttike ca bhavel lakṣmī mārgaśīrṣadhanaṃ mahat ||


pauṣe tu sakalād rogāt māghe saṃpatti mattamā |


phāgguṇe sarvvakāryyāṇi jānāhi parameśvarī (!) ||


śiṣyasya prathamārṇaṃ hi saphalā mandirapriye |


nāmasaṃkalpayeddevaṃ manoharamanindite ||
