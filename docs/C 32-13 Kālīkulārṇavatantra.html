Beginning

oṃ namaḥ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

namaḥ śrīmaṅgalāyai ||

śrīvīranātha uvāca ||

kailāśaśikharāśīnaṃ (!) devaṃ tribhuvaneśvaraṃ |

praṇamya śirasā devī kālikā pṛcchate haraṃ || ||

oṃ guhyakālikāyai ||

athāto ʼrthasaṃhitāyāṃ || ||

śrīdevyāha ||

oṃ marmmābhighātaṃ nīnamrā nāmnaḥ || ||

sāmarasyā sthitā devo dvaitādvaitavivarjjitā

pṛcchate kālikā devī bhairavaṃ bhuvaneśvaraṃ ||

praṇayānalasaṃkāśaṃ jagadrakṣaṇatatparaṃ ||

devādhidevataṃ bhīṣmaṃ dvaitadvaitasya sthāpanaṃ | (fol. 1v1–5)

End

devyā utkāṣā mantrajapitena tatra kṣaṇāt kāma tena devī kāmarūpī maheśvarī ||

raktanetraraktavāsāṃ sarvvayakṣajātīnāṃ gṛhṇāti na saṃdehaḥ || adho trayakṣarān tvaṃ mantra śayakṣamārjjārīraktena yasyā nāmaṃ likhyate utkoṣābhiyojitā || uccāṭayatvā yati ākarṣati || bhrāmayati || tena raktena pratikṛtvālanīyā || adhenūjāṃ japet || utkoṣayati || akasmād viṃtā akasmādviddhi raṃtyavimitra tena likheta || yathā pradeśasthāpayet || utkoṣāyuttaliko || || iti śrīkālikūlārṇṇave trayodaśaṭīkā || || śrībhairava uvāca ||

yasyā krīḍārtham akhilaṃ brahmāṇḍekaṃ .. kāyate ||

cintya rūpāṃ śirobhāsāṃ vande kālīṃ jagatpatīṃ ||

raktacarmmāmbare sthitvā ṣoḍhā nyāsaṃ samācaret || ||

tataḥ krame ṣoḍhā nyāsa ucyate || || ugramātṛ (fol. 45v2–8)
