Excerpts

Beginning

|| śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

yaḥ padmodabhavadevadānavapitṛkṣoṇītalastha kṣamā-

haḥ saṃdhyādivibhāgavocitajagat prāgbhārama(2)dhyāṃtakṛt ||

gāḍhadhvāṃtatirohitākhilapadārthān ātmabhābhirjjanod-

vegotpatyavyaye ca bhāsayati taṃ vaṃde grahāṇāṃ pati (!) || 1 ||

śruti(3)smṛtivicāraṇācaraṇacārucittaḥ śucir

vivekavinayāspadaṃ sad upamanyugotraḥ kṛtī ||

mukaṃdacaraṇārccanā viracanā prapaṃcaḥ (4) sudhī

pūrvavibhavārcitārthijanasaṃcayaḥ keśavaḥ || 2 ||  (!) (fol. 1v1–4)

«Sub-colophon:»

iti śrīmaheśvaropādhyāyasutaśrībhāskarācāryaviracite siddhāntaśiromaṇivyā(6)khyāne tattvaciṃtāmaṇau madhyamādhikāraḥ prathamaḥ samāptaḥ || || ❁ || (fol. 60r5–6)
