Excerpts

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

umāgaṃ(!) gajaṃ karṇavaktraṃ gaṇeśaṃ

bhujākaṃkaṇaṃ śobhitaṃ dhumraketu(!)

gale hāramuktāphalāśo (!) bhavantaṃ

namo jñānarūpaṃ gaṇeśaṃ namas te || 1 ||     ||

gaṇai(!)śaikadaṃtaṃ śubhaṃ sarvakārya

smaraṇasumukhaṃ (!) jñānadaṃ sarvasiddhi(!) ||

manaś ciṃtitaṃ kāryyasiddhir bhavantaṃ

namo jñānarūpaṃ gaṇeśaṃ namas te || 2 ||

kare puṣṭakaṃ (!) śaktivighnavirājaṃ(!)

caturbhī(!)bhujām ekadaṃtaikavarṇaṃ ||

idaṃ devarūpaṃ gaṇai(!)siddhināthaṃ

namo bhālacaṃdraṃ gaṇeśaṃ namas te || 3 ||     ||

śire siṃduraṃ kuṃkumaṃ hemavarṇaṃ

śubhaṃ modakaṃ prīyate vighnarāja ||

mahāsaṃkaṭacchedinaṃ dhumraketu(!)

namo gauriputraṃ gaṇeśaṃ namas te || 4 ||    ||

yathā cheditaṃ pātakaṃ viṣṇunāmaṃ(!)

yathā dhyāyeta śaṃkaraṃ pāpanāśaṃ |

yathā pūjitaṃ sū(!)mukhaṃ śokanāśaṃ

namo vighnarājaṃ gaṇeśaṃ namas te || 5 ||     ||

namo gauriaṃgaṃmalotpannabhūtaṃ(!)

namo jñānabhūtaṃ namo siddhipatyaṃ (!)||

namo dhyāyate pūjitaṃ ṛddhipatyaṃ(!)

namo jñānapatyaṃ (!) gaṇeśaṃ namas te || 6 ||

sadā sarvadā dhyāyate m (!) ekadaṃtaṃ

sadā pūjitaṃ sinduraṃ(!) raktapuṣpaṃ ||

sadā m (!) arccitaṃ caṃdanaṃ kuṃkumoktaṃ

sadā vaṃditaṃ gaurīputraṃ namas te || 7 ||

bhujaṃgaprayāta(!) paṭhed yas tu bhaktyā

prabhāta (!) paṭhe (!) śuddhacaikāgracittā(!) ||

kṣayaṃ yāṃti vighna (!) diśāśo (!) bhavaṃtaṃ

namo jñānarūpaṃ gaṇeśaṃ namas te || 8 ||    ||

iti śrīmatśaṃkarācāryyaviracitaṃ śrīgaṇeśabhujaṃ(!) stotraṃ samāptam
