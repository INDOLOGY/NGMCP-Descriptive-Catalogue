Manuscript Features

This MS consists of the sūtrapāṭha of the Kātantra from 1.1.1.–3.8.35, that is till the end of the section on the verb or ākhyāta.

Fols. 6 and 24 are missing. The writing is partly rubbed off on a few fols.
