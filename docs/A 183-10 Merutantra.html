Excerpts

Beginning

oṁ namaḥ śrīgaṇeśāya

yayāsat sad ivābhāti ((na ca)) kadāpi sat
yasmin syāneti(!) sā bhāti tasmai tasmai namo namaḥ || (!)

jalaṃ dhareṇa vijite surāsuranare ʼkhile ||
sūkṣmarūpeṇa te sarve maheśaṃ śaraṇaṃ gatāḥ || 2 ||

divyāṇy abdasahasrāṇi samādhisthaṃ vilokya tam ||
prārthitācārarcitā tais tu vāmadakṣiṇamārggibhi[ḥ]

amba tadā bodhayutā natvā taṃ saṃvyajijñapat ||
dhyayaṃti vidhiharyyādyās tvāṃ tvaṃ dhyāyasi kaṃ punaḥ || 4 ||

māṃ vadaṃti parāṃ śaktiṃ tvāṃ vadaṃti paraṃ śivaṃ ||
ādhayor api kiṃ dhyeyaṃ tatvaṃ me kṛpayā vada || 5 ||(fol. 1v1-4)

End

śāpena kīlito maṃtro rājadevena pārvati
japtas tu kīlito maṃtro na sidhim adhigacchati

madhye eko lakāro vā phaṭcānte ladvayaṃ yadi
phaṭdvayaṃ vālatrayaṃ vā na cet sastaṃbhito manuḥ

śāpena bhairaveṇāsmāt siddhaṃ bhavati śobhanaṃ
kadāpi naiva duḥkhaṃ ca prāyaśo bahulaṃ bhavet

ādu tu yasya maṃtrasya kramāt saptākṣarāṇi cet
repheṇa vā yakāreṇa saṃyuktāni manuḥ sa tu

vāyv agniḥ sāpasaṃdagdhaḥ sādhakaṃ paritāpayet
tasāṃt saṃsārayed devi siddhaye sādhakottamaḥ

dvābhyāṃ tribhir atho ṣaḍbhir aṣṭabhir vāpadṛśyate
yadākṣarair antaritaḥ phaṭ (fol. 8r9–13)
