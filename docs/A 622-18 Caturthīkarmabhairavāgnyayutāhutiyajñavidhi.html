Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||    ||

tatoś catupīṭhakarmmavidhiḥ ||

yajña vidhāna mālako vasape ||
patavāśa(2)na coya || kalaśa gora | boye ||
digdvārādi kalaśayātā thāya 2 śa kebo coye |
bali mālako bo(3)yaḥ ||    ||
yajamāna puṣpabhājanaṃ || adyādi || vākya ||

mānavagotra yajamānasya śrīśrī jaya bhūpatī(4)ndramallamarmmaṇa śrī 3 ākāśamahābhaivavaprīrtyathaṃ jīrṇṇoddhāra dharmmaśālā suvarṇṇakaraśa dhva(5)jāvalohanabhairavāgni ayutāhutijajñe caturthikarmmani puṣpajanaṃ(!)
samarpayāmi || (fol. 1v1–6)

End

pratiṣṭhā ||
pratiṣthitosi (4) devesi, supratiṣṭhā bhuvaṇatrayaṃ |
svāyutvaṃ parivarṇṇate, yajamānādi vṛddhayet ||
sarostutipa(5)dedinā, sanno rājyes tathaiva ca |
yajamānāsya bhūtoyaṃ suputrapaśubāndhavā |
rakṣatvaṃ sarvvakā(6)reṣu deśanabhairava namostute ||
supratiṣṭhā varadā bhavantu ||    ||

hnasakana kene ||

pūrṇṇacandrani(7)bhaṃ śutraṃ darppaṇa śatrudarppahā ||
ātmavindu dharaṃ yasya, saṃpradāya jayāya ca ||    ||

sakṣī thoya(!) || (8) || vākya ||

oṃ hrāṃ kṛtakarmmaṇe sākṣi śrīsūryyāya arghaṃ namaḥ ||
puṣpaṃ namaḥ || sākṣīṇṇai namaḥ ||    || (80v1)

thanā mālako bali || gaṇa || gogrāsa || kaumāri || dukhāpikhā ||
thvate thava thavaṃ thāyasa choya juro (2) || (fol. 80r3–80v2)

Colophon

iti caturthīkarmmabhairavāgni ayutāhutiyajñavidhiḥ samāptaṃ || ۞ || śubhaṃ bhūyāt ||    || (fol. 80v2)
