Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || śrīgurubhyo namaḥ ||

nidhāya hṛdi viśveśaṃ vidhāya guruvandanam |

bālānāṃ su(2)khabodhāya kriyate tarkasaṃgrahaḥ || 1 ||

viśveśvaraṃ sāmbamūrtiṃ praṇipatya girāṃ gurum |

ṭīkāṃ śi(3)śuhitāṃ kurve tarkasaṃgrahadīpikām ||

cikīrṣitasya granthasya nirvighnaparisamāptyarthaṃ śi(4)ṣṭācārānumitaśrutibodhitakartavyatākam iṣṭadevatānamaskāralakṣaṇaṃ maṅgalaṃ śi(5)ṣyaśikṣārthaṃ nibadhnaṃś cikīrṣitaṃ granthādau pratijānīte | nidhāyeti (fol. 1r1–5)

End
