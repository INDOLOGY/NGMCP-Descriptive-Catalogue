Manuscript Features

Fols. 85–88 are missing.

Fols. 91–93 has been mentioned twice to the two successive folios.

Fol. 131v is blank.

The manuscript number is wrongly written as 1/2097 in catalogue cards.

This MS was retaken.

The date of copying of the MS was misread as NS 807 in PTL.

Fol. 71 is not mentioned but the text continues.

This reel number does not exist in PTL.

The text contains considerable corrections and marginal notes.

There are two exposures of fols. 177v–178r, 214v–215r and 228v–229r.


Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṁ namaḥ śivāya ||

namas tasyai jaganmātre, kumbhayonipriyāśriye |

namaskṛtyā(!) śivān devīṃ, sarvvabhāgavatāṃ śubhāṃ ||

purāṇaṃ saṃpravakṣyāmi, yathoktam ṛṣibhiḥ purā || ||

ṛṣaya ūcu(!) ||

bhagavaṃs tvaṃ samastasya, dṛṣṭādṛṣṭasya tattvavit |

purāṇārtham vayaṃ sarvva āgatā bhāvabhāvitāḥ ||

kathyatāṃ yatra ghorādyā bhūtāḥ sāṃpratadānavāḥ |

bhaviṣyāś ca vinā śiṣye devī devanamaskṛtā ||

indrasya ca divaḥ prāptir hṛtarājyasya dānavaiḥ | (fol. 1v1–4)


End

devyaḥ saṃpūjayitvā tu vidhinā nṛpasattamaḥ(!) |

ṣaḍāvarttaṃ prakurvvanti pratyakṣā bhavate śivā |

sadācāraḥ śubhāhāraḥ sarvvasaṅgavivarjitaḥ |

vācayan śatam arddham vā pratyakṣaṃ prāpnuyāc chivaṃ |

tatra cābhyudayaṃ pādaṃ śataiḥ tribhir navottaraiḥ ||

sahasrair dvādaśair vatsa kathitaṃ sarvvasiddhidaṃ || ○ || (fol. 232v5–7)


Colophon

ity ādye devyavatāre devīpurāṇaṃ samāptaṃ || ○ || śrīśrīśrīguru(!)ve namaḥ || ○ || || ❖ samvat 907 mārggasirasudi || || śubham astu || || (fol. 232v7)
