Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

pratyaṃgirā⟨ā⟩yai namaḥ ||

oṃ asya śrīpratyaṅgirāstotrasya mahadeva ṛṣir anuṣṭupchandaḥ śrīpratyaṅgirādevatā hūṃ bījaṃ svāhā śaktir mamābhiṣṭa (!) siddhyarthe yape viniyogaḥ ||

kubjikovāca ||

mandarasthaṃ sukhāsīnaṃ bhagavantaṃ maheśvaraṃ
samupāgamya caraṇau pārvatī paripṛchati ||

devy uvāca ||

dhāriṇī paramā vidyā pratyaṅgirā mahottamā ||
naranārīhitārthāya bālānāṃ rakṣaṇāya ca || (fol. 1v1–7)

End

oṃ staṃbhi[[nī]] sphreṃ sphreṃ mama śatrūn staṃbhaya 2 svāhā oṃ sphreṃ sphreṃ mama śatrūn mohaya 2 svāhā | oṃ kṣobhinī sphreṃ sphreṃ mama śatrūn kṣobhaya 2 svāhā oṃ drāviṇi sphreṃ sphreṃ mama śatrūn drāvaya drāvaya svāhā | oṃ jṛṃbhiṇi mama śatrūn jṛṃbhaya 2 svāhā oṃ bhrāmiṇī sphreṃ sphreṃ mama śatrūn bhrāmaya bhrāmaya svāhā || oṃ raudriṇi sphreṃ sphreṃ mama [[śatrūn]] raudraya 2 svāhā | oṃ saṃhāriṇi sphreṃ sphreṃ mama śatrūn saṃhāraya 2 svāhā | oṃ śoṣiṇī sphreṃ sphreṃ mama śatrūn śoṣaya śoṣaya svāhā |

sopi duṣṭāṃtako devi hanyāc chatrūn na saṃśayaḥ ||

sarvato rakṣayed vidyāṃ mahābhayavipa[[tti]]ṣu |
mahābhayeṣu ghoreṣu na bhayaṃ vidyate kvacit ||

iti ku[bji]kāmate caṃdrograśūlapāṇīvadananirgatamahātaṃtre pratyaṃgirānāmamaṃtroddhāraḥ smāraṃ smāraṃ bhāratipādayugmaṃ kāraṃ kāraṃ sadgurubhyo namas (ca[[ṇḍi]]) graṃthasyādau vighnarājaprasādāt †svalpāraṃbhāṃñjamakarājavaṃnditi† (fol. 5r3–5v5)
