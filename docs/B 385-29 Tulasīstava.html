Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṁ namastulaśīdevyai ||

śaunaka uvāca ||

nāmoccāre kṛtā yasyā, prīṇātya suradarppahā |(t2)

sā kathaṃ tulaśī loke, pūjyate vandya tena hi ||

darśanenāpi yasyāstu, puṇyaṃ koṭi guṇaṃ śataṃ |

dhanyā(3)ste mānavā loke, yeṣāṃ saṃpadyate kalau ||

śāligrāmaśilārccārthaṃ, tuṣyahaṃ tulaśī kṣitau ||

tulaśīṃ ya (4) vicinvanti, dhanyāste kalepallavāḥ ||

keśavārthaṃ kalau ye ca, lopayantīha bhūtale |

kiṃ kariṣyatya (5) saṃtuṣṭo, yatopi sahakiṃkaraiḥ |

tulaśīdalena deveṇa, pūjitānena daityahā || (exp. 21t1-5)

End

jīve vyādhi vinirmmukto, dharmmanca varddhate mati |

tulaśīstavaṃ tu paṭhitaṃ (b6) phala bhavati mānavaḥ ||

tīrtha koṭisahasrāṇi, tatphalaṃ labhate phalaṃ |

tatphalaṃ(7) sama vāpnoti, paṭhitvā tulaśīstavaṃ || (exp. 23b5-7)

Colophon

iti śrīskandapurāṇe tulaśīstava samāptaḥ || (b8)

tulaśī prītirastu || ||

saṃ 823 kārttika vadi 13 || (exp. 23b7–8)
