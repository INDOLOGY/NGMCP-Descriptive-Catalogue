Manuscript Features

The first folio is broken in the left margin with a loss of 3 to 5 akṣaras per line. Missing folios: 2-4, 12, 58, 66-68. Several other folios are broken in the margin, too.

After folio 72 follow folios numbered 16 to 30, 34, 36, 37, 39, 42, 43, 44, 46, 47, 50, 51, 53, 13, 14. It is not quite clear whether these originally belonged to another manuscript. It is also possible, that a new foliation started within the same manuscript. With regard to the physical appearance of the manuscript and to the writing, there is no difference between the earlier and the later parts.
