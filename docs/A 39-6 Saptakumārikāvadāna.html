Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namo buddhāya ||

śrīmatkṛpāpreraṇayāvatāraṃ saṃbhārabhāraṃ jagato hito yaṃ |
yaś cāgadantatvam ajasram asmai vāgīśacūḍāma[ṇa]ye namo ’stu ||

hitāya tenaiva nṛṇām praṇītaṃ khyātāvadānaṃ yad ihāvadānaṃ |
kariṣyate ta〇sya gurūpadeśād vibhāgamātraṃ suhṛdarthanena ||

ihāyam ācāryo gopadattaḥ saptakumārikāvadānam bhagavata(!) praṇītam anupraṇinīyur ana〇rthasambhāvanānirāsārtham abhidheyādana(!) vaktukāmaḥ prathaman tā[va]d amuṃ ślokam upsnicikṣepa || ○ || vairāgyayuktam ityādi ||

(fol. 1v1–4)

End

jagad aśeṣaṃ tena vai puṇyadāsaṃ ||

sūktapriyasya preśalaṃ(!) hṛdayasyādaravaṃśacaritasyācāryaśubhadā tasyaivam iyaṃ prārthanā || sadvi ||

idam agrato bhavantī ṭīkā abhjñāpradīpamañjarikā |

śaṃsayati〇mire yam avaśyam ihanti viduṣā samatsariṇāṃ ||    || (fol. 62r1–2)

Colophon

samāptā ca sptakumārikāvadānaṭīketi || kṛtir ācāryagopadattasyeti |〇|

bhīmasyāpi bhaved bhaṅgo muner api matibhramaḥ |

yathādṛṣṭan tathā likhitaṃ lekhako nāsti doṣa[bhāg] iti ||    ||

samvat 608 jeṣṭabadi daśathyāṃ 〇| revatinakṣatre śobhanayoge budavāsare ||    ||

śubham astu sarvvajagataḥ parahitaniratā bhavantu bhūtagaṇāḥ |

doṣāḥ prayāntu nikhilaṃ sarvatra sukhī bhavatu lokaḥ || (fol. 62r2–5)
