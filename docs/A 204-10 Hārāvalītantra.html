Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

śrī īśvara uvāca ||

atha naimittikānuṣṭhānakramo yathā ||

tad uktaṃ hārāvalītantre ||

nityaṃ naimittaṃ kāmyaṃ sāpekṣaṃ pūrvvapūrvvataḥ |

anyathā cintayed iyaṃ kuryyād āparamparā ||

nityācārarato mantrī kuryyān naimi[tti]kārccanaṃ |

naimittikārccanasiddh⟪i⟫aḥ kuryyāt kāmyam akhaṇḍitaṃ || (fol. 1v1–3)


End

(jye)ṣthādī(!)jitamātras tu abhiṣeka[ṃ] visarjjayet |

kaniṣṭho yadi sampūrṇṇaḥ kramapūjā vidhīyate ||

evaṃ siddhiparo ⟪‥ ‥⟫ mantrī (sā)dhako bhūvi durllabhaḥ |

na deyaṃ yatra kutrāpi goptavyaṃ paśusaṅkaṭe || || ❁ || || (fol. 31v6–7)


Colophon

iti śrīhārāvalītantre mātṛkāpūjāvidhiphalasamāptaḥ || ❁ || (fol. 31v7)
