Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

ādau karmaprasaṃgāt kalayati kaluṣaṃ mātṛkukṣau sthitasya

tanmūtrāmedhyamadhye kvathayati nitarāṃ jāṭharo jātavedāḥ ||

yad yad vā tatra duḥkhaṃ viṣagativiṣamaṃ<ref name="ftn1">For viṣayati viṣamaṃ</ref> śakyate kena vaktuṃ

kṣaṃ(1v1)tavyo me parādhaḥ śiva śiva śiva bho śrīmahādevaśaṃbho || 1 ||

bālye duḥkhātireko malalulitavapuḥ stanyapāne pipāsā

no śakyaṃ ceṃdriyebhyo bhavaguṇajanitā jaṃtavo māṃ tudaṃti ||

nānārogādiduḥkhād tudanaparava(2r1)śaḥ śaṃkaraṃ na smarāmi

kṣaṃtavyo me parādhaḥ śiva śiva śiva bho śrīmahādevaśaṃbho || 2 || (fol. 1r1–2r2)

End

kiṃ dānena dhanena vājikaribhiḥ prāptena rājyena kiṃ

kiṃ vā putrakalatramitrapaśubhir ddehena gehena kiṃ ||

jñātvaitat kṣaṇabhaṃguraṃ sapadi re tyājyaṃ mano dūrataḥ

svātmasthaṃ guruvākyato bhaja bhaja śrīpārvatīvallabhaṃ || 13 ||

āyur naśyati paśyatāṃ pratidinaṃ yāti kṣayaṃ yauvanaṃ

pratyāyāṃti gatāḥ punar na⟨r⟩ divasāḥ kālo jagadbhakṣakaḥ ||

lakṣmīs toyataraṃgabhaṃgacapalā vidyuccalaṃ jīvitaṃ

tasmān māṃ śaraṇāgataṃ śaraṇada tvaṃ rakṣa rakṣādhunā (fol. 6v5–7v5)

Colophon

iti śrīśaṃkarācāryaviracitaṃ śiva(!)ʼparādhasuṃdarastotraṃ saṃpūrṇaṃ ||     || 235 ||     ||
