Beginning

||    || śrīmanmaṅgalamūrttaye gaṇeśvarāya namaḥ ||    ||

śrīmahākālīmahālakṣmīmahāsarasvatībhyo namaḥ ||    ||

oṃ namaś caṇḍikāyai

avighnam astu ||
atha prāṇapratiṣṭhāḥ || oṃ āṃ hrīṃ yaṃ raṃ laṃ vaṃ śaṃ yaṃ saṃ haṃ haṃ saḥ sohaṃ durgāyāḥ prāṇāḥ iha sthitāḥ || (fol. 1v1–3)

Extracts

oṃ āṃ hrīṃ krauṃ yaṃ raṃ laṃ vaṃ śaṃ ṣaṃ saṃ haṃ haṃsaḥ sohaṃ durgāyā vāṅmanaś cakṣuḥ śrotaghrāṇaprāṇā ihāgatya sukhaṃ tiṣṭhantu svāhā ||    || iti prāṇapratiṣṭhāṃ kṛtvā || tato dhyānam || (fol. 1v5–7)

iti dhyātvā mānasopacāraiḥ saṃpūjya || atha saptaśatīśāpamocanam (!) || (fol. 2r5–6)

End

dhūpaṃ dīpaṃ gandhaṃ puṣpaṃ oṃ hrīṃ śrīṃ caṇḍike cāmuṇḍe gaṇapatikṣetrapālasahite baliṃ gṛṇha gṛṇha namaḥ
śūlapāṇaye kṣetrapālāya (fol. 8r6–7)

Colophon

iti pūjāvidhānaṃ saṃpūrṇam || (fol. 8r7)
