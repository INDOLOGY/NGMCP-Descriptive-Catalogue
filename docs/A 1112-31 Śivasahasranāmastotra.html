Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

ṛṣaya ucuḥ (!) ||

sūta vedārthatatvajña śivadhyānaparāyaṇa ||

muktyupāyaṃ vadāsmabhyaṃ kṛpālo munisattamaḥ (!) || 1 ||

kaḥ sevyaḥ sarvadeveṣu ko vā japyo manuḥ sadā ||

sthātavyaṃ kutra vāṃ (!) nityaṃ kiṃ vā sarvvārthasādhakaṃ || 2 || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

ye śaṃbhuṃ su(1)ra⟪sa⟫ttamaṃ suragaṇair ārādhyam īśaṃ śivaṃ

śailādhīśasutāsametam amalaṃ saṃpūjayaṃtyādarāt ||

te dhanyāḥ śivapādapūjanaparā anyonya(!)dhanyo janaḥ ||

satyaṃ satyam ihocyate munivarāḥ satyaṃ punaḥ sarvathāḥ (!) || 258 || (fol. 46r5–46v4)

Colophon

iti śrīśivarahasye saptamāṃśe mahāpurāṇe ṣaṇmukhaśrīsadāśiva(1)saṃvāde śivasahasranāmastotraṃ sampūrṇasamāptaṃ śubham || || ||

śrīsāṃvvasadāśi[vā]rppaṇam astuḥ (!) || || śrīr astu ||

likhitaṃ śrībhairavarājadevajuju upādhyāyaśarmmaṇaḥ (!)
