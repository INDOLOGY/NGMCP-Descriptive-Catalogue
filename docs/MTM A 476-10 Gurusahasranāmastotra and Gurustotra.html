Manuscript Features

Fols. 16–18 and 23 of the first text are missing.

This MTM contains the following texts:

1. Gurusahasranāmastotra (exps. 24)

3. Gurustotra (exps. 25–)

Each text contains separate foliation.

There are two exposures of fols. 7v–8r (the 2nd foliation).

Excerpts

«Beginning of 1. Gurusahasranāmastotra»


❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||


kailāśaśikharāsīnaṃ, candrakhaṇḍavirājitaṃ |

papraccha vinayā bhaktyā, gaurī natvā vṛṣadhvajaṃ || ||


śrīdevy uvāca ||


bhagavan sarvvadharmmajña, sarvvaśāstraviśārada |

kenopāyena ca kalau, lokā yānti hi saḍgatiṃ || (fol. 1v1–5)


«End of 2. Gurustotra»

iti te kathitaṃ devi, tvatsnehān naganandini | brahmāviṣṇumaheśānāṃ, vandanīyo guruḥ smṛtaḥ || || (fol. 10r4–6; 1st foliation)


«Colophon of 1. Gurusahasranāmastotra»

iti śrīmohanatantre pārvvatīmahādevasamvāde śrīguroḥ sahasranāmastotraṃ saṃpūrṇṇaṃ || ||

saṃvat 840 āṣāḍhaśuklapūrṇṇamāsi śukrabāla thva kuhnu vipra śrīpurarājena likhita[ṃ] pustakaṃ || (fol. 26r5–26v4; 1st foliation)


«Colophon of 2. Gurustotra»

iti śrīrudrajāmale tripurāmaheśvarasaṃvāde gaurīkalpe gurustotraṃ samāptaṃ || sahajānaṃdāya, śrīguruḥ prīṇātu⟨ḥ⟩ || ❁ || (fol. 10r6–10v2; 2nd foliation)
