Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

agastya uvāca ||

aśvānanamahābuddhe sarvaśāstraviśārada ||

kathitaṃ lalitādevyāś caritaṃ paramādbhutaṃ || 1 ||

pūrvaṃ prādurbhavo mātus tataḥ paṭṭābhiṣecanaṃ ||

bhaṃḍās(!) turavadhaś caiva vistareṇa tvayoditaḥ || 2 || (1v1–5)


End

tena tuṣṭā mahādevī tavāhbīṣṭaṃ pradāsyati ||

ity uktvā śrīhayagrīvo dhyātvā śrīlalitāṃbikāṃ || 87 ||

ānaṃdamagnahṛdayaḥ sadyaḥ pulakito bhavet || 88 || (exp. 491b7–492t1)


Colophon

iti śrībrahmāṇḍapurāṇe uttarakhaṃḍe lalitopākhyāne stotrakhaṃḍe śrīlalitāsahasranāmastotraṃ saṃpūrṇaṃ || śrīparadevatārpaṇam astu || svārthaṃ parārthaṃ ca saṃvat 1924 pramāthī nāmābde bhādrapadaśukladaśamyām idaṃ pustakaṃ āṃbrekaropanāmanārāyaṇena likhitaṃ ||

arjitaṃ bhūrikaṣṭena pustakaṃ likhitaṃ mayā ||

hartum icchatu(!) yaḥ pāpī tasya vaṃśaḥ kṣayo bhavet || 1 ||

bhagnapṛṣṭhīkaṭigrīvā stabdhadṛṣṭir adhomukhaṃ ||

kaṣṭena likhitaṃ graṃthaṃ yatnena paripālayet || 2 || (exp. 492t1–b3)
