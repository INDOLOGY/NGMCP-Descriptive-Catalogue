Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya nama(!) ||

svargāpavargadātārau sāmadṛsyodarodare ||

bhavarogaharau vaṃde(2) caṃdrikā(!) candraśekharau || 1 ||

hato haṃti jarāmṛtyuṃ mūrcchito vyādhighātakaḥ |

dhatte ca khedati vaṃddhaḥ(3) konyaḥ sūtā kṛpākaraḥ || 2 ||(!)

mārayejjāritaṃ sūtaṃ gaṃdhakenaiva mūrcchayet

baddhasyādṛtira(4)tnībhyāṃ(!) rasasauva(!) tridhā gati || 3 ||(!)

doṣahīno raso brahmā murcchitas tu janārdanaḥ |

mārito(5) hararūpī syād baddhaḥ sāsyā(!) maheśvaraḥ || 4 || (fol.1v1–5)

End

trikaṭu triphalā vahni cātujātakasaiṃdhavaṃ ||

sauvarcalaṃ dhūmasā(10)raṃ cūrṇam etat samaṃ samaṃ ||

kṛtvā yojyaṃ bhāgam ekaṃ tat sarvaṃ cādrakadravaiḥ ||

sigrutthair mātu(11)ligotthai(!) lālayitvā vaṭīkṛtaṃ ||

rasaḥ kālāgnirudroyaṃ triguṃja bhakṣayet sadā ||

a(1)gnidīptikaraṃ khyātaṃ sarvavātakulāṃtakaṃ ||

sthūlānāṃ kurutaḥ kāśya(!) kṛśānyaṃ shaulyakāra(2)kaṃ ||

anupānaṃ viśeṣas tu tatra yoge niyojayet ||

sādhyāsādhyaṃ nihaṃtyāśu bhakṣaṇā(3)n-nātra saṃśayaḥ ||

iti kālāgnirudrorasa || (fol.195v9–196r3)

Colophon

iti pārvatīputra nityanātha nā(4)thasiddhaviracite rasaratnākare rasendrakhaṇḍe cikitsānāmāṣṭaviṃsodhyāya samā(5)ptaṃ (!) || || śrī śrī śrīviṣṇu prītir astu || || śāke 1705 phālguṇa (!) sudi 8 || || śubhaṃ ||(6) ślokasaṃkhyā 4058 || (fol.196r3–6)
