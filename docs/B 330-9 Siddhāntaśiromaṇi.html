Manuscript Features

On the exposure 2 is written atha bhāskarīyasiddhāṃtaśiromaṇivyākhyātattvacintāmaṇinā || mako grathmaḥ prāraṃbhaḥ || patre 176 || pustakamidaṃ śrīkṛṣnajośī rāmanagaravāle || gaṇitādhyāyaḥ

and on the exposure 21 is written iti bhāskarīyasiddhāntaśiromaṇivyākhyātattvaciṃtāmaṇIḥ saṃpūrNAKṇaḥ || śrīkṛṣṇajośī rāmanagaravāle

Excerpts

Beginning

ḥ || śrīharir jjayati || || śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

oṃ namo bhagavate purāṇapuruṣottamāya sūryāya ||

(2) yaḥ padmodbhavadevadānavapitṛkṣoṇītalasthakṣapāhaḥ

saṃdhyādivibhāgabodhitajagat prāgbhāramadhyāṃ(3)takṛt gāḍhadhvāṃtatirohitākhilapadārthānātmalābherjjanod

vegotthavyathaye vabhāsayati taṃ vaṃde gra(4)hāṇāṃ patiṃ 1

śrutismṛtivicāraṇacaraṇacārucittaḥ śucir

vivekavinayāspadaṃ sad upamanyugotra (!) kṛ(5)tī

mukuṃdacaraṇārcanāvicaraṇāprapaṃcaḥ sudhīr

babhūva vibhavārcitārthijanasaṃcayaḥ keśavaḥ 2 (fol. 1v1–5)

End

siddhāṃtoktapragaṇaguṇitajñānago dyāṃ matiś ced

golechede py ama(9)lavipulollāsabhṛd vāsanāyāṃ

teṣāṃ cite nivasatimiyā (!) yuktiyuktā mamaiṣā

vyākhyāvidyābhiratini(10)ratasyā (!) cyutārcā parasya 1 || (fol. 175r8–10)

Colophon

iti vācaspatīśiramasūnu lakṣmīdāsaviracite bhāskarīyasiddhāṃtaśiromaṇivyākhyāne gaṇitatattvaciṃtāmaṇau pātādhikāraḥ samāptaḥ || || samāptoyaṃ graṃthaḥ || saṃkhyā 7500 || ||

varṣe paṃcāgnivasv iṃdu mitaharidine kārttike kṛṣṇapakṣe

vāre sūryasya sāṃghrir jayaharinagare gomatītīravāsī ||

jñātau śrīpārvatī yaḥ śukatanayavaraḥ śaṃkaro bhāskarīya

ṭīkāṃ tattvākhya ciṃtāmaṇim iyam alikhat svārthaputrārthasiddhayai || 1 ||

śrīr astu || śubhāni bhavaṃtu sarvajatām ||  putakam idaṃ śrīkṛṣṇajośī rāmanagaravāle (fol. 175r10–14)
