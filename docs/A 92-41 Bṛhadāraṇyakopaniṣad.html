Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namo bhagavate vāsudevāya || ||

oṃ uṣā vā asvasya medhyasya śiraḥ sūryaś cakṣuḥr vātaḥ prāṇo (2) vyāttam agnir vaiśvānaraḥ saṃvatsara ātmāśvasya medhyasya dyauḥ pṛṣṭham aṃtarikṣam udare pṛthivī pāja(3)syaṃ diśaḥ pārśve avāṃtaradiśaḥ parśava ṛtavoṃgāni māsāś cārddhamāsāś ca parvāṇy ahorā(4)trāṇi pratiṣṭhā nakṣatrāṇy asthīni nabho mā guṃ sānyuvadhya guṃ sikatāḥ siṃdhavo gudāyakṛc ca klo(5)mānaś (!) ca parvatā (fol.1v1–5)

End

hiraṇmayena (3) pātreṇa satyasyāpihitaṃ mukhaṃ |

tatvaṃ (!) pūṣan napāvṛṇu satyadharmāya dṛṣṭaye |

pūṣan nakarṣe yama(4)sūryaprājāpatyavyaharaśmīn samūhatejo yat te rūpaṃ kalyāṇatamaṃ tat te paśyāmi | yo sā(5)vasau puruṣaḥ soham asmi | vāyur anilam amṛtam athadaṃ bhasmāṃta guṃ śarīraṃ | oṃ kratosmara kṛta (6) guṃ smara oṃ kratosmara kṛta guṃ smara | agnenaya supathā rāye asmān viśvāni deva vayunāni vidvā(7)n | yuyoddhyasmaj-ju[[hu]]rāṇameno bhūyiṣṭhāṃ te nama uktiṃ vidhema || 1 || saptadaśamaṃ brāhmaṇaṃ || ❁ || (fol. 78r2–7)

Colophon

iti bṛhadāraṇyake upaniṣat pāṭhe paṃcamodhyāyaḥ || || ❁ || || oṃ tatsad brahmārpaṇam astu || || (fol. 78v1)

oṃ yo ha vai jyeṣṭhaṃ śreṣṭhaṃ ca veda… yo ha vai saṃpadaṃ veda sa guṃ hāsmai padya–
