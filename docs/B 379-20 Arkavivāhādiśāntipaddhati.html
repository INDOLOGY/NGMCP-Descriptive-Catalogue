«Beginning»


śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||


atha paricchedadvayānuktakarmavidhāyakas tṛtīyapariccheda ucyate tatrādau tṛtīyā


vivāhavidhānārthaṃ arkavivāhaḥ kathyate tatra tṛtīyā vivāha niṣedha ukto mātsye |


udvahed ratisiddhyarthaṃ tṛtīyāṃ na kadācana |


mohād ajñānato vāpi yadi gacchet tu mānuṣīṃ |


naśyatyeva na sandeho gargasya vacanaṃ yatheti |


saṃgrahe |


tṛtīyāṃ yadi codvāhet tarhi sā vidhavā bhavet |


caturthādivivāhārthaṃ tṛtīye ‘rkaṃ samudvahed iti


athārkavivāhaprayogaḥ | (fol. 1v1–4)


«End»


viṣṇudharmottare


gajapṛṣṭhagataṃ bālaṃ nausthaṃ vā snāpayed dvija

tad abhāve tu dharmajñaḥ kāṃcane tu varāsane

tad abhāve gajābhāve sarvauṣadhaiḥ

sarvagaṃdhair bījaiḥ puṣpaiḥ phalais tathā

paṃcagavyena ratnaiś ca mṛttikābhyaś ca bhārgava ||


snāpayed ity anvayaḥ ||


sthālīpākena dhātāraṃ pūjayet tadanantaraṃ ||


saptāhaṃ cātra karttavyaṃ tathā brāhmaṇabhojanaṃ ||


aṣṭame hani viprāṇāṃ tathā deyā ca dakṣiṇā


kāṃcanaṃ rajataṃ gāś ca bhuvaṃ cāsanam eva ca |


+++++++++++ pūjayet havane tu trayāṇāṃ devānāṃ aṣṭottarasahasram aṣṭottaraśataṃ vā

hunet bhadrāsane upaviśya bālakaṃ snāpayet sadhavā striyaḥ pūjyāḥ (fol. 71r5–8)



«Sub-colophon»


iti kākamaithunādidarśanaśāntividhḥ ||


iiti dantajananaśāntiḥ || (fol. 71r4,8)
