Manuscript Features

In fols. 1v, 2r and on the top of 2v there is commentary bhāvārthadīpikā of Śrīdhara. In other folios there is only the root text.

Folio numbers restart after the end of every skandha.

Excerpts

Beginning

|| śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

śrīkṛṣṇāya namaḥ ||

oṃ namaḥ śrīparamahaṃsāsvāditacaraṇakamalacinmakaraṃdāya bhaktajanamānasanivāsāya śrīrāmacaṃdrāya || 1 ||

vāgīśā yasya vadane lakṣṃīr yasya ca vakṣasi ||

yasyāste hṛdaye saṃvit taṃ nṛsiṃham ahaṃ bhaje || 2 ||

viśva[ṃ] sargavisargādinavalakṣaṇalakṣitaṃ ||

śrīkṛṣṇākhyaṃ paraṃ dhāma jagaddhāma namāmi tat || 2 || (fol. 1v1–4, exp. 3t of reel no. A 285/4)


«Beginning of the root text»

|| oṃ namo bhagavate vāsudevāya || ||

janmādy asya yatonva[yā]d itarataś cārtheṣv abhijñaḥ svarāṭ

tene brahma hṛdā ya ādikavaye muhyanti yatsūrayaḥ ||

tejovārimṛḍāṃ yathā vinimayo yatra trisargomṛṣā

dhāmnā svena sadā nirastakuhakaṃ satyaṃ paraṃ dhīmahi || (fol. 2v1–4, exp. 4t of reel no. A 285/4)


End

namas tasmai bhagavate vāsudevāya sākṣiṇe ||

ya idaṃ kṛpayā kasmai vyācacakṣe mumukṣave || 20 ||

yogīṃdrāya namas tasmai śukāya brahmarūpiṇe ||

saṃsārasarpadaṣṭaṃ yo viṣṇurātam amūmucat || 21 ||

bhave bhave yathā bhaktiḥ pādayos tava jāyate ||

tathā kuruṣva yogeśanāthas tvaṃ me yataḥ prabho || 22 ||

nāmasaṃkīrttanaṃ yasya sarvapāpapraṇāśanaṃ ||

praṇāmo duḥkhaśamanas ta[ṃ] namāmi hariṃ paraṃ || 23 || (fol. 28r8–11, exp. 436b of reel no. A 286/1)


Colophon

iti śrībhāgavate mahāpurāṇe dvā (fol. 28r11, exp. 436b of reel no. A 286/1)
