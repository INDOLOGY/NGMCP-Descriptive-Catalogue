Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ | |

natvā tāṃ śrutidevatāṃ trisamayajñānodgateḥ kāraṇaṃ

tatpādāṃburuhaprasādavikasad bodho budhaḥ śrīpatiḥ ||

śiṣyaprā(2)rthanayā vicārya sakalān horāgamārthān muhur

vakṣye jātakakarmapaddhatim ahaṃ horāvidāṃ prītaye | 1 |

jñeyo tra prathamaṃ hi janmasamaya(3)ś chāyādi yaṃtraiḥ sphuṭās

tatkālaprabhavā vilagnasahitāḥ kāryās tataś ca grahāḥ ||

siddhāṃtoktaparisphuṭopakaraṇais te cāsakṛtka(4)rmaṇā

bhāvāḥ kheṭadṛśobalāni ca tatas teṣāṃ viciṃtyāni ṣaṭ | 2 | (fol. 1v1–4)

End

ye dharmakarmaniratā vijiteṃdriyā ye

ye pathyabhojanajuṣo dvija(3)devabhaktāḥ ||

loke narā dadhati ye kila śiīlā

teṣām idaṃ kathitam āyur udāradhībhiḥ || 37 || (fol. 7v2–3)

Colophon

iti śrīśrīpatipadaviraci(4)tāyāṃ jātakakarmapaddhatau āyurdāyādhyāyaḥ paṃcamaḥ || 5 || ❁ ||

bhavanti cej janmani riṣṭayogā

na syāt tadāyur gaṇitāgamaṃ yat ||

(5) syād riṣṭabhaṅgād athariṣṭabhaṅgair

vidhīyate saṃ[pra]tinirṇayo yaṃ || 1 ||

yacchubhāśubhaphalottarābdalaṃ

riṣṭabhaṃgakarakheṭayoḥ punaḥ ||

(6) saikavargadalahṛdbhasaṃjñitaṃ

tad dalaṃ bhavati śeṣavargajaḥ || 2 ||

bhādivargapativīryasaṃguṇaṃ

jāyate balam ariṣtakāriṇaḥ ||

riṣṭabhaṃga(7)rapi (!) saptavargajaṃ

taddhi sarvabalasaṃjñitaṃ tayoḥ || 3 ||

śubhāśubhatvaṃ ca hitāhitatvaṃ

tuṃgādinīcoccasamāśritatvam || (fol. 7v3–7)
