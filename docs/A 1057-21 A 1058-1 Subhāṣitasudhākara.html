Excerpts

Beginning

|| śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

śubhasadanasametāṃ bhaktavṛṃdāya dātuṃ

kalitalalitapadmā līlarā .. .. putrīṃ ||

suraracitasaparyyā vighnavidhvaṃsadakṣā

bhavatu mama vibhūtyai hāriheraṃbaśuṃḍāḥ || 1 ||

samastakavimaṃḍalīsarasapadyapāthonidhiṃ

pramathya puruṣottamo hṛdayamaṃdareṇa sphuṭaṃ ||

subhāṣitasudhākaraṃ vibudhavṛṃdajīvātave

prakāśayati bhāsuraṃ kuvalayapramodapradaṃ || 2 ||

mīmāṃsārṇavamajjanair upaniṣad devaṃ drumasyāpi vā

chāyābhiḥ śrutivārivāhavacanāṃbhobhir niśāṃtātmanāṃ ||

tarkagrīṣmamahoṣmataptamanasāṃ vidvajjanānāṃ mude

bhūpādeśasudhākarapratidinaṃ sarvair guṇair maṃḍitaḥ || 3 || (fols. 1v1–6)


End

yāvaj jahnusutāṃ dadhāti giriśo yāvad dharāmaṃḍalaṃ

śeṣaś caṃdradivākarau suragiriṃ yāvat parikrāmataḥ ||

lakṣmī śrīpuruṣottamasya hṛdaye yāvad varīvarttate

tāvat padyasudhākaraṃ kṣititale rātriṃdivaṃ dyotitāṃ || 89 || (fol. 96r2–4)


Colophon

iti śrīmannārāyaṇacaraṇaparicaraṇaparāyaṇavīsalanagarīyanāṃ rāvalanārāyaṇātmajapuruṣottamaviracite subhāṣitasudhākare ṣoḍaṣaḥ prakāśaḥ || || saṃvat 1734 samaye āṣāḍha śu da 1 śukre likhitam idaṃ pustakaṃ tra. śivaśaṃkareṇa ātmapaṭhanārthaṃ paropakā[rā]rthaṃ ca || || śubham astu || ❖ || ❖ || (fol. 96r4–6)
