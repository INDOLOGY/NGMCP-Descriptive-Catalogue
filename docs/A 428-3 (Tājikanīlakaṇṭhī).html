Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||    ||

praṇamya heraṃvamatho divākaraṃ guroranaṃtasyatathāpadāṃvujaṃ ||
śrīnīlakaṃṭho vivinaktisūktibhistattājikisūrimanaḥ prasādakṛt || 1 ||

pumāṃścarogniḥ sudṛḍhaśca tuṣyā drakroṣṇapittā tiravodrirudraḥ ||
pītodinaṃ prāgviṣamodayolpa, saṃgaprajorukṣa nṛpaḥ samojaḥ || 2 || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

āsīdasīmaguṇamaṃḍitapaṃḍitāgryo, vyākhyadbhutaṃ pagavīḥ śrutivitsuvṛttaḥ ||
sāhītyarīti(!) nipuṇo gaṇitāgamajña, ściṃtāmaṇirvipulagarga kulāvataṃsaḥ || 61 ||

tadātmajonaṃta guṇostyanaṃto, yoʼdhok saduktī kilakāmadhenuṃ ||
saṃtuṣṭaye jātaka paddhatiṃ canyarupayadduṣṭamatiṃ nirasya || 62 ||

padyāṃvayāsāvitato vipaścicchrīnīlakaṃṭhaḥ śrutiśāstraniṣṭaḥ ||
vidvacchivaprītikaraṃ vyadhāsī,tsaṃjñā vivekaṃ sahamāvataṃsaṃ || 63 || (fol. 21v4–22r1)

Colophon

iti śrī daivajñānaṃtasute nīlakaṃṭha viracitaṃ saṃjñātaṃtraṃ samāptaṃ ||    ||    || (fol. 22r2)
