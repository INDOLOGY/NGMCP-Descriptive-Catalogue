Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrīmahālakṣmyai namaḥ || yudhiṣṭhira uvāca ||

svasthānalābhaputrāyuḥ sarvvaiśvarya sukhapradaṃ |

vratam ekaṃ samācakṣva, vicāry-ya puruṣottama || ||

śrīkṛṣṇa uvāca ||

durvvārevṛtta daiteya (!) pāravyāpte (!) triviṣṭape |

etad eva kṛtasyādau, devendraḥ prāhanāradaṃ ||

tasya śrutvā tato vākyaṃ, sa muniḥ pratyabhāṣata || ||

nārada uvāca ||

purandara purā pūrv-vaṃ puramāsīt śubhānvitaṃ |

ratnagarbhābhavad bhūmir yatra ratn-nā vasuṃdharā | (fol. 1r1–1v1)

End

priyāga iva tīrtheṣu devatās tu bhavān iva |

nadīṣu ca yathā gaṃgā, vrateṣvaṃ tat tathāvrataṃ (!) |

dharm-maṃ cārthaṃ ca kāmaṃ ca mokṣaṃ ca yadi vāṃchasi |

tad etat tvaṃ vrataṃ śakra, kuru śraddhāsamanvitaḥ |

vratamidamatha cakre nāradenopadiṣṭhaṃ

surapatir api tasmād vāṃchitārthaṃ sa lebhe |

tvam api kuru tad etad dharm-masūno yathāsmād

abhimataphalasiddhiḥ putrapautrādivṛddhiḥ || ○ || (fol. 16v3–17r2)

Colophon

iti śrībhaviṣyottare mahālakṣmīvratakathā samāptā || ||

|| tribhiḥ pārāvatā paṃca paṃcabhiḥ saptasārsā | saptabhir navahaṃsāśca navabhir varhiṇastrayaṃ || rājakanyā vivārthe śatena śatamāharet ||… samvat 749 bhādrapadakṛṣṇa 14 caturdaśyāṃ tithau ādityavāsare liṣitam idaṃ || ○ || śubham astu || (fol. 17r3:17v5)
