Manuscript Features

The text starts from the beginning and ends in the commentary of the saisāddhase sūtra of the Visargasandhi chapter.
On fol. 1r is written: sārasvatavyākhyā.
On fols. 1r and 28v there is a stamp of Chandra Shumsere.

Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

ānaṃdaikanidhiṃ devam aṃtarāyatamo raviṃ |
dayānilaparaṃ vaṃde, varadaṃ dviradānaṃna (!) ||1

vāgdevatāyāścaraṇāraviṃdamānaṃdasāṃdre hṛdi saṃnidhāya |
śrīpuṃjarājaḥ kurute manojñāṃ, sārasvatavyākaraṇasya ṭīkāṃ || 2 ||

iha graṃthasya karttānarantarāyābhīpsitārthasiddhau śiṣṭācārapratipālanāya ca iṣṭadevatānamaskārarūpamaṃgalācaraṇapūrvvakaṃ śrotripratipattidvārā saprayojaṃ cikirṣitaṃ | pratijānite | (fol. 1v)

End

saiṣāddhase || ṣo na karmmaṇi | adeḥ ṣṇaḥ snaḥ | upratyayaḥ | saḥ iṣu icchāyāṃ | eṣaṇam eṣaḥ | anaṣor nirākaraṇārthaṃ saśabdā eṣaḥ śabdāc ceti kuvyārate | tadaḥ saśabdaḥ enedaḥ eṣaśabdaḥ | kilātṣaḥ saḥ kṛtasya niṣabdaiṣaśabdāty api rasya visarjjanīya/// (fol. 28v)
