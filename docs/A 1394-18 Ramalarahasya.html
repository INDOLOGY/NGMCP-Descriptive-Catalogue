Beginning

vaṃdhva praśnaṃ rāvaṃ yadi sthirarupī patitaḥ syātadādvividha praśne saṃpvirupe bhinnatvarupe ca sthiratva kravyaṃ || evaṃ yadi dvisvabhāva rupī patitaḥ syātadā dvividhva praśne viphalaṃ vaktavyamakiṃ ca || ādau manobhilaśvitadā ramalaṃ paśve (!) || tatprastuḥ (!) praśnaṃ kasmādgrahā varttate || apara tasmin gṛhekorupī pratitosti || (fol. 2r1–3)
