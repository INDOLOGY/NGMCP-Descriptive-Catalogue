Manuscript Features

On the first exposure available few lines of the atharvaśikhopaniṣad

|| śrī || atharvaśokhopaniṣat prāraṇbhaḥ || oṃ bhadraṃ karṇeºº svasti na indroºº || hariḥ oṃ || atha hainaṃ paippalādaoṃgirāḥ sanatkumāraś cātharvāṇam uvāca bhagavan kim ādau prayuktadhyānaṃ dhyāyitavyaṃ kiṃ tat dhyānaṃ ko vā dhyātā kaś ca dhyeyaḥ sa ebhyotharvā praty uvāca || oṃm ity etadakṣaram ādau prayuktaṃ dhyānaṃ dhyāyitavyam ity etad akṣaraṃ…(exp.1)

athāyaṃ puruṣaḥ…

jānvācya sruveṇājyāhutīr juhoti || 4 || (exp.2a)

After the colophon is available few stanzas related to the devy upaniṣad

ekaiva viśvarūpiṇī | tasmād ucyate anekā | 6 |
