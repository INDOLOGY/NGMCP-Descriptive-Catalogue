Beginning

|| śrīvedavyāsāya namaḥ
samahasta guṇapūrṇāya doṣadūrāya viṣṇave namaḥ
śrīprāṇanāthāya viśvādi caturātmane 1
asyā upaniṣado maṃḍūkarūpī api caturūpī harirdevatā | keṣāṃcichaṃdovaddhānāṃ akṣaṇaṇanayānuṣṭup chaṃdas taṃ jñeyaṃ (fol. 1v1–2)

śrīvedavyāsāya namaḥ | hariḥ oṃ m oṃmitye tadakṣaraṃ Iṃdraṃ sarvaṃ tasyopavyākhyānaṃ bhūtaṃ bhavadbhaviṣyad iti 004 (fol. 1v6)

End

sarvasya (raṇavot dyādiḥ) madhyamaṃtastathaiva ca
evaṃ hi praṇavaṃ jñātvā vyaśnute tadanaṃtaraṃ
praṇavaṃ hīśvaraṃ vidyāt sarvasya hṛdayesthitaṃ
sarvavyāpi namoṃkāraṃ matvādhīro na śocati
amātronaṃtamātraś ca daitasyopaśamaḥ śivaḥ
oṃkāroṃ vidito yena sa munir netaro janaḥ
sa munir netaro janaḥ 7 khaṃḍaḥ 4 (fol. 15r5–7)

praṇava pratipādyāya caturmurtisvarupiṇe |
namaḥ śrīprāṇanāthāya bhaktābhīṣṭhapradāyine
iti māṃḍakopaniṣadarthānāṃ saṃgrahotraya trayarāghavoṃdrakṛtastena prīyatāṃ kamalāpatiḥ 2 gaṃgātīre samāsādya viṃdumādhavasannidhau trikramo likhat prītyai madhvamādhavayor mudā 1 (fol. 15r8–10)

Colophon

iti maṃḍūkopaniṣat | (fol. 15r7)
