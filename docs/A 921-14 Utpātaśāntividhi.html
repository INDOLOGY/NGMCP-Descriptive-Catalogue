Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namo lokeśvarāya ||
śrīmatpātarokeśvarasyaṃ ||

lokanāthaṃ pravicchatimāne pānihitāthāya tārāde(2)vī mahākṛpā ||

śrīmatpāta raktaparvvatasa lokeśvara bijyāta ana kṛpāvanta tārādevina manukṣa(3)yāta kalyāna yāya arthana lokeśvarayāke utpāta kha ṅena ||    ||
bho thākura manukṣayā utpāta bhiṅa (4) mabhiṅa sānti asānti chalaporasyaṃ ādesa dayake māra pujā vidhithyaṃ chalapo juva gathiṃ (5) dhālasā kṛpā samudrathyaṃ ||    || (fol. 1v15)

End

kurasaṃ thajule busaṃ thajule koṣana kheja choo (12r1) khanio paracakra bhaya dhana phuya phao, thvateyā sānti yāya mahābali biya paṃcarakṣā pātha (2) yācake thvatenaṃ sānti juyio || 40 ||
teposaṃ thajule dhalaposaṃ thajule thaṅā lakha suta(3)sā svāmi mṛtyu juyu dhana phuyu thvayā sānti jaladāna biya saṃnyāsi nake ghela saka(4)tāṃ dayakaṃ nake thvate sānti juyuo || 41 || (fols. 11v6–12r4)

Colophon

samvat 1046 mti vaisākha kṛṣṇayā tṛ(5)tiyā ādityavāra thvakuhnu sidhayekā julo || śubhaṃ || (fol. 12r4–5)
