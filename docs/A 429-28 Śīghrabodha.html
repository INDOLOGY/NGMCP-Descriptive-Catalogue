❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

bhāsayaṃtaṃ jagadbhāsā, natvā bhāsvaṃtam avyayaṃ |

kriyate kāśi(2)nāthena, śīghraṃ bodhāya saṃgrahaḥ || 1 ||

rohiṇyuttararevatyo, mūlaṃ svātī mṛgo maghā (3) |

anurādhā ca hastaś ca, vivāhe maṃgalapradāḥ || 2 ||
