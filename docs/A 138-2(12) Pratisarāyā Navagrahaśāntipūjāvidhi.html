Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīpratisarāyai⟨ḥ⟩ ||
oṃ namaḥ grahamātṛkāyai⟨ḥ⟩ ||    ||

pañcarakṣāyāgu śānti navagrahapūjājajñadaśāṃśāhuti yāyagu vidhim āha ||    ||

hnāpāṃ pañcarakṣāyātha saṃkalpa yāsyaṃ, pātha dhāla 1,3,5, pramānaṃ (!) pāthayā(‥) jajña sthāpanā yāya, jajñakalaśa, sagvaṃmata, nyagapanakī cakraciṃhna, pañcagavyapātramakuṭa, salilasa, baji thane thvayā de o na rogiyā guñja takataya, cāku dhāla gva1 decchāya, vyāgva (exp. 97t, fol. 1v1–5)

End

svastivākyetyādi(‥) kalaśābhiṣeka viya, jāta kala o hlāya, baji dhāla ci paṃthi yake || daśapatratayake moha1 daṃ4 julo || gaṇacakradhūmāṃgādi cchoya, svāna kāya mukhaśuddhi ||    || (fol. 12v2–4)

Colophon

thuti pratiśarāyāgu navagrahayā śāntipūjāvidhi jula ||    || cotasā grahamaṇḍala coya, maphatasā, pati bharāḍamaṇḍala taya, caityu cchahma pratimā taya māla || candrabimba sūryabimba, ātmābimba dāna viya māla, roga śānta juyu || japaṃ vidhi dūyagu, tha o 2gu mantrana jula śubhaṃ ||    || (fol. 12v4–13r1)
