Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

natvā devaṃ gaṇeśaṃ ca śrīmad vedāṃgarāyajaḥ ||

naṃdīkeśvarasaṃjño haṃ vakṣye gaṇakamaṃḍanaṃ || 1 ||

(2)aśvinī bharaṇī caiva kṛttikārohiṇīmṛgaḥ ||

ārdrā punarvaśuḥ puṣyo śleṣā caiva maghā tathā || 2 ||

pūrvophottraphāhasta(3)ś citrasvātiviśākhikā ||

rādhā jyeṣṭhā ca mūlaṃ ca pūrvāṣāḍhottarātathā || 3 ||

abhijicchravaṇaś caiva dhaniṣṭhā śatatā(4)rakā ||

pūrvabhādrottarābhādre cāṃtyamaṃ bhaṃ tu revatī || 4 || (!) (fol. 1v1–4)

End

tat putromajalitsaṃjño vedavedāṃga(3)pāragaḥ ||

yena vedāṃgarāyeti prāptaṃ dillīśvarāt padaṃ || 14 ||

pitṛbhakttirataḥ prājñas tat sūnur naṃdikeśvaraḥ ||

dvijaprītyai vyadhāt pūrṇaṃ graṃthaṃ(4) gaṇakamaṃḍanaṃ || 15 ||

jyotir nivaṃdhamakhilaṃ ca tathā muhurta-

ciṃtāmaṇiṃ gaṇakamaṃḍanaratnanāle ||

jyotirvi(5)dābharaṇasajjanavallabhākhyo

dṛṣṭvā trivikramaśatādimayedam ukttam || 16 || (fol. 23v2–5)

Colophon

iti śrīmad vedāṃgarāyātma(6)ja naṃdikeśvaraviracite gaṇakamaṇḍane gaṇitvādikriyā nāmāṣṭamodhyāyaḥ || 8 || gaṇakamaṃḍanam idaṃ saṃpūrṇaṃ || (fol. 23v5–6)
