Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ mahābhairavāyaḥ atha bhairavāgniyañjavidhir likhyate ||


tato kalaśārccanaṃ || suryyārgha || gurunamaskāra || nyāsa || oṃ jūṁ saḥ kaniṣṭakāyai namaḥ


oṁ jūṁ saḥ anāmikāyai svāhā || oṃ jūṁ saḥ madhyamāyai vauṣaṭ || oṃ jūṁ saḥ tarjjanyāyai hūṁ || oṃ


jūṁ saḥ anṅguṣṭāya vaṣaṭ || oṃ jūṁ saḥ astrāya phaṭ || iti karaṃ nyāsaḥ || (exp. 3t1–12)


End

śikhāsvsśchana || stotra || saṃvarttā trideva yogniyā asta kṣatra pibanayā tvākatika mālakva || nirvvāṇaṃ

nrvvikalpaṃ tyādi || vākya || śrīśrīśrī īṣṭadevatāyā caturdaśīmahāpārvanya niśi balyārccanapūjānimittyārthena
