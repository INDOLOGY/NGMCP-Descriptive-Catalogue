Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

ātmīyaṃ caraṇaṃ dadhāti purato nimnonnatāyāṃ bhuvi

svīyenaiva kareṇa karṣati taro (!) puṣpaṃ śramāsaṃkayā ||

talpe kiṃ (2) ca mṛgatvacā viracite nidrāti bhāgair nijair

aṃta (!) premarasālasāṃ priyatamām aṃge dadhāno haraḥ 1

vidvajjanamanobhṛṇgarasavyāsaṃgahe(3)tave |

eṣā prakāśyate śrimadbhānunā rasamaṃjarī 2

tatra raseṣu śṛṅgārasyābhyarhitatvena tadālambanavibhāvatvena nāyikā tāva(4)n nirūpyate || sā ca tridhā | svīyā parakīyā sāmānyā ceti | (fol. 1v1–4)

End

mādhvīkasyadasaṃdhohasuṃdarā (!) rasamaṃrīm ||

(8) kurvaṃtu kavayaḥ karṇabhūṣṇaṃ kṛpayā mama ||

tāto yasya gaṇeśvaraḥ kavikulālaṃkāracūḍāmaṇie

deśvo (!) yasya videhabhū (!) su(9)rasatkallolakirmīrutā (!) ||
