«Begining: »

oṃ namo buddhāya || ||

yonte dvayāvāsa vidhu(!)tavāsaḥ

saṃbuddhadhīsāgalalabdhajanamā ||

sarvadharmato(!) yasya ga⟨m⟩bhīrabhāvaṃ<ref name="ftn1">Unmetrical</ref>

yathānubuddhaṃ kṛpayā jagāda || 1 ||

yasya darśanatejā[ṃ]si paravādimateṃdhanaṃ |

dahatyadyāpi lokasya mānasāni tamāṃsi ca || 2 ||

yasyā sama jñānavacaḥ śaraughā[n]

nighnanti⟨ti⟩niḥśeṣabhavārisanyaṃ(!) ||

tridhāturājyaśriyam ādadhānā

vineyalokasya sadevakasya || 3 ||

nāgārjunāya praṇipatya tasmai

tatkārikāṇāṃ vivṛtiṃ kariṣye |

uttānasatprakriyavākyanaddhāṃ

tarkānilāvyākulitāṃ prasannāṃ || 4 || (fol. 1v1–4)

«End: »

kutra gamiṣyati(!)ti yānyekeṣāṃ śravaṇabrāhmaṇānāṃ pṛthagloke dṛṣṭigatāni bhaviṣyati(!) | tadyathā | ātmavādapratisaṃyuktāni jīvavādapratisaṃyuktāni kautuka⟨tuka⟩ma[ṃ]galapratisaṃyuktāni tānyasya tasmin samaye prahīnā(!)ni bhavanti parijñātāni saṃmucchinnamūlāni tālamastakavadanābhāsagatā || āyatyānutpādāni niro[dha]dharmāṇi || || atha khalvāyuṣmān śāriputro maitreyasya bodhisattvasya mahāsattvasya bhāvitam abhinandyotthāyāsanāt prakrāṃtānyās te ca bhikṣavaś [ca] || || (fol. 206v3–6 )

«Colophon: »

ity ācāryacandrakīrttipādoparicintāyāṃ prasannapadāyāṃ madhyamakavṛttau dṛṣṭiparīkṣā nāma saptaviṃśatitamaḥ(!) prakaraṇaṃ samāptaṃ samāpteñcedaṃ madhyamakaśāstraṃ sakalalaukikalokottarapravacananītaneyārthavyākhyānanaipuṇyaviśāradaṃ śrāvakapratyekabuddhānuttarasamyaktaṃ buddhabodhimaṇḍāśanadāyakam iti || || śubham || (fol. 206v6–9)
