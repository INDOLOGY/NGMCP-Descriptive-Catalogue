Excerpts

Beginning

(gdeva)tāṃ dhyāyet,

yathā pañcāśallipibhir vibhaktamukhadoṣpanmadhyavakṣaḥsthalām

bhāsvanmaulinibaddhacandraśakalām āpīnatuṅgastanīṃ |

mudrām akṣaguṇaṃ surāḍhyakalaśaṃ vidyāñ ca hastāmbujair

vvi〇brāṇāṃ viśadaprabhāṃ trinayanāṃ vāgdevatām āśraye ||

iti dhyātvā

lalāṭamukhavṛtākṣiśrutighrāṇeṣu gaṇdayoḥ |

oṣṭhadanto〇ttamāṅgāsye doṣpatsakthyagrakeṣu ca |

pārśvayoṣ pṛṣṭhavaṃśe ca nābhau ca jaṭhare hṛdi |

aṃse kaṅśādikṛtpūrvaṃ pāṇipādayu〇ge tathā |

jaṭharānanayor nyasyen mātṛkārṇṇān yathākramaṃ || (fol. 1r1–4)

End

anyonyaṃ nibiḍaṃ badhvā aṅguṣṭhāgre anāmike |

daityānāṃ dhūmaketvākhyā mudraiṣā kathitā priye |

vakṣaṃ nissāritaṃ kṛtvā (adho) jihvāñ ca cālayet |

pārśvan tu muṣṭim bagaṇaṃ (!) lelihāneti kīrttitā ||

lelihāmudrayā prāṇapratiṣṭheti sakalasaṃpradāyaḥ (fol. 3v5–6)

Colophon

541 cai ba (fol. 3v6)
