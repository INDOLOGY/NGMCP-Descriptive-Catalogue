Manuscript Features

The text runs from the beginning of the Bhvādi chapter to the end of the Lakārārtha chapter. Sutras and vārttikas are highlighted in another ink.

On the first page of the manuscript is written bhinnapravṛttinimittānāṃ śabdānām ekasminn arthe vṛttīḥ sāmānādhikaraṇyam | uru uttarapādo yasya sa u[[rū]]ttarapāda tasmāt urūttarapādāt upamiyate anayā iti upamā upamāyā bhāvaḥ aupamyaṃ tasmin aupamye.

In the middle of the page is written śrīmadhya; at the bottom of the page is written: śrīmadhyakaumuditiṅantakaupustak (!).

The handwriting of this manuscript and of fols. 9r–39v (first foliation) of reel no. A 560/10 (Inventory no. 28579) seems to be the same, with the text continuing from the latter to the former.

The handwriting of this manuscript and of reel no. A 560/12 (Inventory no. 28619) is also the same, with the text this time continuing from the former to the latter.
