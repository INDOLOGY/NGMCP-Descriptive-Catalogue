Excerpts

Beginning of the commentary

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

sānaṃdāmādine ye sphuradaśanisamā mallavarge sagarvāḥ
kaṃse sāsūkṣaṇaṃ svapriyakulamanasī (!) janmabhāsvatsvarūpa
‥(2)saṃmugdhastrīśaliṃgan madanaśararucāṃ rāśayo raṃgabhūmau
kalyāṇaṃ kalpayaṃtām alaghumadhupater nnaikalakṣyāḥ kaṭākṣyāḥ 1

śrīmadgopālabha(3)ṭtena draviḍasthāsuparvaṇā
kriyate rasamaṃja‥‥‥kā rasikaraṃjinī 2 (fol. 1r1–3)

Beginning of the root text

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ
ātmīyaṃ caraṇaṃ dadhāti purato nimnonnatāyāṃ bhuvi
svīyenaiva kareṇa karṣati taroḥ puṣpaṃ śramā(8)śaṃkayā
talpe kiṃ ca mṛgatvacā viracite nidrārti (!) bhāgair nijair
aṃtaḥ premarasālasāṃ priyatamām aṃge (!) dadhāno haraḥ 1 (fol. 1v7–8)

End of the root text

tāto yasya gaṇeśvara kavikulālaṃkāra cūḍāmaṇirdeśoyasya videhabhūḥ surasaritkallolakirmmīritā padhyena svakṛtena tena kavinā śrī bhānunā yojitā vāgdevī śruti pārijāta kusumasyarddhāṃkarī maṃjarī (fol. 54r6–8)

End of the commentary

matsaraparihṛta manasāṃ sudhiyāṃ purataḥ parisphuṭāveṣārasika lalāṭa taṭasthāramyā rasamaṃjarī 2 (fol. 54r9–10)

Colophon

iti śri bhānudatta miśra viracitā rasamaṃjarī samāptā śrīrāmovijayate tarāma śubhaṃ (fol. 54r8)

tāta śrī harivaṃśa bhaṭṭaika caraṇaśaraṇa gopāla bhṭṭakṛtā rasika raṃjanī ṭīkā samāptā śubhamastu leṣaka pāṭhayo(!) netrāṃkabhūpa pramite śāke māghe gurau dvikegatyā(!) śrīnagarabhye likhatsvayama 1693 śubhaṃ likhitā viṣṇu vallabhena śubham (fol. 54r8–10)
