Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṁ namaḥ śrī 3 mahāgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||


śrīgurupādukābhyāṃ namaḥ ||


atha (bhoṅayā kṣa)tha pratiṣṭhā patādayakā || ||

patavāsana coya vāstu suddhā bali mālako coya || ||


yajamāna puṣpa bhājana yācake ||

adyādi || vākya || mānava gotra yajamānasya śrī 2 jaya bhūpatīndra malla varmaṇa śrī 3 svaṣṭa devatā prītyarthaṃ śrī 3 caṇḍikādevyā navagṛhapraveśapratiṣṭhābalyarccana katuṃ puṣpabhājanaṃ samarpayāmiḥ || || (fol. 1v1–5)



End

pratiṣṭhā ||

pratisthito si deveśi supratiṣṭhābhuvanatrayaṃ |

sānidhyaṃ pratigṛhṇāmi yajamānādivṛddhaye ||


sanno stu dvipade nityaṃ sanno rājyas tathaiva ca |

yajamānasya saṃbhūto yaṃ suputrapaśubā[n]dhavaḥ ||


rakṣa tvaṃ sarvakālekhu(!) daśanabhairava namo stu te ||

supratiṣṭhāvaradā bhavantu || ||


kuhnura pūjā choya ||


sarvvamaṅgalamāṅgalya(!) śive sarvārthasādhake(!)

śaraṇye tra(!)mbike gauri nārāyaṇi namo stu te ||


komārīdevyai svasthānavāso bhavantu || sākṣithāya || ||


pehnuto yajamāna ārogyaṃ ālasaṃ thvacchesaḥ || (fol. 60v1–6)


Colophon

iti gṛhapratiṣṭhāśāntibalyārccanavidhiḥ samāptaḥ || || śubham astu sarvvadā || || || || (fol. 60v6–7)
