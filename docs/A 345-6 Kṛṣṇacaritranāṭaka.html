Excerpts

Beginning

❖ śrī 3 arddhanārīśvarābhyāṃ namaḥ ||

|| nāndi ślokaḥ ||

sānandaṃ candramaulissuragaṇa mahitaḥ kunde karppūra gauraḥ (1v1) ḥ

smerāsyo lāsyayukto girivaratanayāśliṣṭadeho vilāsī |

kandarppoddarppahārī phaṇimaṇi vilasaccāru(2)bhūṣābhirāmo,

bhaktānāṃ kalpaśākhī bhavabhaya samanaḥ pātu nṛtyeśvarovaḥ || ||

sūtra- he pri(2r7)ya śrī 3 sveṣṭadevatā prītikāmanayā vṛhadghaṃṭā nivedanārthaṃ nepālamahīmaṇḍalena, śrīśrī jaya raṇa(8)jitmalla mahārājādhirājenāpūrvva kṛṣṇacaritaṃnāmanāṭakamabhinetu mahayājñeptāsmi || ||

iti prathamo’ṅkaḥ || || (9v3)

iti dvitīyo’ṅkaḥ || || (19v8)

iti tṛtīyo’ṅka || || (30r4)

iti caturddaśoṅkaḥ || ۞ ||( 126v1)

End

iti śrīśrī jaya Raṇajitmalla mahārāja viracite kṛṣṇacaritranāmanāṭake paṃcadaśo’ṅka(132v3)ssapūrṇṇaḥ || ||

Colophon

samvat 858 phālguṇa śudi 1 budhavāla saṃpūrṇṇa yāṅā dina śubha || || ۞ ||śubhaṃ ||(132v4)
