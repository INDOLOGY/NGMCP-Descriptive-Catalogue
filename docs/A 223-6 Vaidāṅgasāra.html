Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namaḥ śivāya || ||

dhyātvā śivaṃ paramatatvavicāravaidyaṃ

caṇḍīm abhīṣṭaphaladāṃ sagaṇaṃ gaṇeśaṃ |

dhanvaṃtarīṃ munivaram munisuśrutādīn

ātreyam ugratapaso manasā praṇamya || 1 ||

ādivyādhiharaṃ vande parāśaktiyutaṃ śivaṃ

ādāvaṃtorujaṃ jñāne prayatetac cikitsakaḥ ||(!)

sādhyāsādhyavibhāgajñaḥ tataḥ kuryāc cikitsitaṃ

kṛcchropāya sukhopāyo dvividhaḥ sādhya ucyate || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

vaidyasmaraṇaḥ || ||

brahma tvam eva viṣṇuś ca rudraś ca sahadurgayā ||

ārttasya roganāśāya pratyakṣo bhavapāyaka || (!)

agnau prārthanā || ||

atha sva duṣṭadevatāṃ smṛtya (!) amṛtīkṛtya auṣadhaṃ bhakṣayet || ||

vasvaṃkavedasahite śubhavatsare vai

īṣasya śukla phaṇirājatithau niśīśe (!)

bhīṃdyāla vaidya sujano harikṛṣṇasevī

vaidyāṃgasāra lalitaṃ bhiṣajāṃ lileṣa || || (!) (fol. 144r4–6)

Colophon

iti śrīvaidyāṃgasāre jvarādi viṣaparyaṃtaṃ bājīkaraṇaṃ saṃpūrṇam || || śubham astu sarvajagatām || (fol. 144r7)
