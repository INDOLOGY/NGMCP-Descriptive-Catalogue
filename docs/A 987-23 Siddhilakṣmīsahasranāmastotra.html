Excerpts

Beginning

sya maheśi tu ||

skandhasthitā mahādevi trailokyaiśvaryyadāyinī ||

mātṛkāṣṭakamadhyasthāṃ paṃcapretāparisthitaḥ || ||

oṃ asya śrīpratyaṅgirākavacasya nārāyaṇaṛṣi gāyatrīcchandaḥ pratyangirāsiddhilakṣmīdevatā hrīṃ bījaṃ hruṃ tilakaṃ mama sarvvarakṣārthe jape viniyogaḥ || || (fol. 1r1–4)

End

idaṃ stotraṃ maheśāni na deyāt paraśiṣyake ||

śaṭhāya bhaktihīnāya nindakāya varānane ||

saubhāgyaṃ .. .. .. .. ñca rūpaṃ ca pīvaran (!) tathā ||

putrān api varārohe yoṣid āpnoty aśaṃsaya || 142 || ||

idaṃ stotraṃ maheśāni śaṭhāya ca sureśvari ||

tathā mṛtyum avāpnoti satyaṃ satyaṃ vadāmy ahaṃ || || (fol. 22r4–22v3)

Colophon

iti śrīrudrayāmale devīśvarasaṃvāde siddhilakṣmīsahasranāmastotraṃ samāptam || || śubham astu śaṃ || || (fol. 22v3–4)
