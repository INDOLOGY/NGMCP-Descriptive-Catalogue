Manuscript Features

The MTM contains three texts: 1. Mṛtyuñjayastotra (exp. 4t–7t) 2. Meṣaviṣuvacchāntividhi (exp. 8t–13t) 3. Tulāviṣuvacchāntividhi (exp. 13b–15b)

The MS contains many scribal errors. So the errors are not marked.

Excerpts

«Beginning»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || atha viṣuvacchāntiḥ ||

atha śāntiṃ pravakṣyāmi yaduktaṃ rudrayāmale ||

yena vijñānamātreṇa naraḥ saukhyam avāptavān || 1 ||

meṣasaṃkramaṇe duṣṭaṃ yadā saṃkramaṇapriye ||

mādhavaḥ pratimā kāryā svarṇaraupyavidhānataḥ || 2 || (exp. 8t1–5)


«End»

tadvastraṃ parityajya anyadvāsasī paridhāya | brāhmaṇamukhādāśīrvādaṃ prasādaṃ ca gṛhītvā karmeśvarārpaṇaṃ kuryāt || tataḥ brāhmaṇān bhojayitvā svayam api bhuṃjīto yathāsukhaṃ viharet || (exp. 13t2–6)


«Colophon»

iti meṣaviṣuvacchāṃtiḥ || (exp. 13t6)
