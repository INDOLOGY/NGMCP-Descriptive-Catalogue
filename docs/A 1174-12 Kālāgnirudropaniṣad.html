Manuscript Features

The manuscript is written by several hands and all the handwritings are untidy. The end of the second text cannot be found. In the excerpts below mistakes have not been marked.

Excerpts

Beginning

svasti śrīgaṇeśāya nnamaḥ || ||

oṃ viṣṇur viṣṇur viṣṇur a[[tr]]ācamyaḥ vāk vā vāk ghrāṇa ghrāṇaḥ cakṣu cakṣuḥ śrotraṃ śrotraḥ nābhi hṛdaya kaṃṭhe śirasīḥ śikhā bāhubhyām yaśobalaṃ 1 (fol. 1v1-3)

End

sarvatirtheṣu yat puṇyaṃ sarvajajñeṣu yat phalaṃ
tat phalaṃ samavāpnotī bhasmasnānā samācaret ||

bhasmasnānaṃ paraṃ tirthaṃ gaṃgāsnānaṃ dīne dine ||
bhasmarūpī śīvā sākṣāt bhasma trailokyapāvanaṃ || (exp. 14b6-15a3)
