Excerpts

«Beginning of the root text:»

jo viviha matta sāarapāraṃ yatto vivi(2)la maī helaṃ ||

pahamaṃ bhāsataraṇḍo ṇāo sā piṅgalo jaai || (fol. 2r1–2)

«Beginning of the commentary:»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

gopīpīnapayodha[ra]dvayamilac ⟪calā⟫ daśākarṣaṇa-

kṣelinyādhṛta cārucaṃcalakarāṃbho(2)jaṃ vrajat kānane ||

drākṣāmañjulamādhurīpariṇamad vāgvibhramaṃ tanmanāg

advaitaṃ samupāsmahe yadukulālambaṃ vicitraṃ mahaḥ ||

(3) lambodaram avalambe staṃberamavadanam ekadantavaraṃ ||

ambe kṣitamukhakamalaṃ yaṃ vedo nāpi tattvato veda (!) || 2 ||

…

bhaṭṭaḥśrīrā(7)macandraḥ kavi[[vi]]budhakule labdhadha śruto yaḥ ||

śrīmānnārāyaṇākhyaḥ kavimukuṭamaṇis tat tanūjo janiṣṭa

tatputro (8) rāmabhaṭṭaḥ sakala kavikulakhyātakīrttis tadīyo

lakṣmīnāthas tanūjo racayati ruciraṃ piṅgalārthapradīpam || 5 || (fol. 1–3 and 6–8)

«End of the root text:»

athodgāthā chandaḥ ||

(5) pūvvaddetu tad ve mattātī sati ra suhaa saṃbhaṇiā |

so ugāho vutto (6) piṅgala kai diṭṭha saṭṭhimattaṃ || (fol. 27v4–6)

«End of the commentary:»

he subhageti śiṣyasaṃbodhanaṃ pūrvvārdhe uttarārdhe (7) yatra mā[[trā]] triṃśat samyak bhaṇitā 30 yad vā subhageti mātrāviśeṣaṇaṃ daladvaye (8) pi sundarās triṃśatkalāḥ sā piṃgalakavidṛṣṭā sa(9)ṣṭi 67 (!) mātrāṃgākalā ṣaṣṭiśarīrā udgāthāvṛtā (!) atra sarvatrā vahaṭṭhabhāṣāyāṃ liṃgavyatyaya prā(10)tipadikanirdeśo vā na doṣādhāyaka iti tu ⟨gu⟩ guravaḥ (fol. )
