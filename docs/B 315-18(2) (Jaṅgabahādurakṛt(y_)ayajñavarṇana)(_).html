Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

śrīcakrasthā samastapravarasuraśirassaṃghasaṃgharṣitāṅghir(!)

nānālaṃkārahārāṃkuśakusumaśarāṃś cekṣukodaṇḍapāśau ||

hastābjais sandadhānā ravinikarakarā bālabhālenduvindu

prodyadvaktrā karotu tripuraharabadhūḥ kāmadhāmaprapūrṇam || 1 ||

asti prasastavibhavābhavavāsabūtir

bhūmī(!)dharādhipatidurgagaṇair agamyā |

ramyā ramāramaṇalokasamānakāntiḥ

kānṭīpurī(!) vasumatī tilakā ʼlakābhā || 2 ||

(fol. 1v1-5)

End

rājaṃs tvatkṛpayānayā trijagatāṃ gīrvāṇanirvāṇatā

loke nāsti mumukṣur adya bhavato dordaṇḍasaṃrakṣite |

maddāridryam ṛte vimocaya vibho taṃ ceti vijñaptikṛt

tvatkīrtivrajaleśavarṇanakaraḥ śrīrāmacandraḥ kaviḥ || 55

(fol.7r8-10)

Colophon

śrīmadatipracaṇḍabhujadaṇḍetyādiśrīmanmahārājajaṅgabahāduradevasyagoraṇḍamaṇḍalādhipatilabdha jī sī bi prāyamminiṣṭarayāṇḍakamyāṇḍaraincīphapadaprakaṭīkṛtakīrtipuṃjasya ḍoṭīpadapariṭīkitadeśaviśeṣavāsinā rāmacandraśarmaṇā viracitakoṭyāhutitrayavarṇanaikadeśaleśaḥ samāptaḥ ||

vasvaṣṭādrimṛgāṃkasammitaśake yajñatrayaṃ bhūjujā

tenākāri tatas tadīyavacasā sadgadyapadyānvitaḥ |

graṃthoyaṃ racito yathāmati mayā kṣantavya eva kvacid

doṣaś ced vibudhaiḥ paroktirasikair etat kaviprāthanam || 56 ||

śubham svasti śrīmadrājaguruhemarājaśarmaṇā tripañcanavabhūmite hāyane pauṣakṛṣṇadvādaśyāṃ likhitam idaṃ [[pustaka]]m iti jānīhi śubham astu

(fol. 7r10-7v4)
