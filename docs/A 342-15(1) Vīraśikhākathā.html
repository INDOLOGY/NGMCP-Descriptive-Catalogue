Manuscript Features

This microfilm contains two prakīrṇa folios written in Nepali and Newari (A 342-15(1) Vīraśikhākathā and A 342-15(2) Mantrīcāturyakathā). These are two different folios from two texts separated from the original bundle. Similarities and dissimilarities between these two prakīrṇa texts:

Similarities
Size is the same.
Number of lines (8) is the same.
Pagination system of counting Rāma in the right margin is the same.
Short title is written in the left margin.

Dissimilarities
Ink of Vīraśikhākathā is blacker than the ink of Mantrīcāturyakathā.
Short title in Vīra° is Vī: śī, while in Mantrī° it is [Vī:] śi. The extant number of folios of Vīra° is 1 and of Mantrī° is 13.
Vīra°is in Nepali, while Mantrī° is in Newari.
Vīra° is available only on the verso while Mantrī° appears on both the recto and verso.
