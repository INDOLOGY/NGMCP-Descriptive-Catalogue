Excerpts

Beginning

|| śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

śrītripurasuṃdayyai(!) namaḥ ||

namāmi sadguruṃ śāṃtaṃ pratyakṣaṃ śivarūpiṇaṃ ||

śirasā yogapīṭhasya muktikāmārthasiddhaye || 1 ||

seve siṃdūrasaṃdoha suṃdaraṃ svāṃgabhāsuraṃ ||

karuṇāpūrṇapūrṇapīyūṣakaṭākṣāṃkularūpiṇīṃ || 2 ||

yasyāḥ sarvaṃ samutpannaṃ yasyām adyāpi tiṣṭhati ||

layam eṣyati yasyāṃ tāṃ paṃcamīṃ praṇamāmy ahaṃ || 4 || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

yadyad vāṃchati kāmāni bhuktimuktikarāṇi ca ||

labhate nātra sandeho bhuvi svarge rasātale || 51 ||

dṛṣṭvā taṃ sādhakaṃ śreṣṭaṃ graharākṣasahiṃsakāḥ ||

dūrād eva layaṃ yāṃti palāyaṃte [[di]]śo daśa || 52 ||

viṣaṃ nirviṣatāṃ yāṃti yāṃti pāpāni saṃkṣayaṃ ||

devan mānuṣo bhūtvā bhunakti bahulaṃ sukhaṃ || 53 ||

tasmān nityaṃ paṭhe[t] stotraṃ muktikāmārthasiddhaye ||

bhaktitaḥ pūjayed devīṃ svarakṣāṃ sarvadā paṭhet || 54 ||

pūrva jātismaraṃ jñānaṃ divyaṃ janmasahaśrakaṃ(!) ||

punar na jāyate yonau maraṇaṃ nāsti cāparaṃ || 55 ||

gaṃdharvarūpavān bhūtvā saṃpūjya [[para]]meśvarī ||

rakāṃmaṃtraṃ paṭhitvā tu devatvaṃ labhate dhruvaṃ || 56 ||

aputro labhate putraṃ dari (fol. 9v3–9)
