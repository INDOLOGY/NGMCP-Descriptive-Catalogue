Excerpts

Beginning

❖ dhanāśrī || cauºº ||

tanayaka dokhaka taya nahi hoya jananī kṛpākare dūra karadhoya ||

karejoḍipayāpaḍI vinavamaya tohi ehi dukhabhāra santāra mohi ||

katapahataya nahi kayala udhāra pāvana māri hakaraha vicāra ||

bhaya bhañjani tohe māya bhavāni ava kiya visaraha adharuki vāni ||

nṛpa jagajoti kahata kara udāsa jatahi katahi jaga tohare āsa || || śubham astu sarvvadā || || (exp.1:1–4)

End

anūparūpa sarūpadhāriṇi bhuvaṇa sṛṣṭi kapālike harana pāraṇa jananakāriṇi sadhu naṭapaṭū bālike ||

nṛpa jitāmitra bhāvabhāvita bhanayini amati ambike je kicchu devi …(exp.14:5–7)
