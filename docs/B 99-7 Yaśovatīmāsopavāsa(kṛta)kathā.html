Excerpts

«Begining:»

oṃ namaḥ sarvajñāya || ||

athāśoko mahīpāla tam arhantaṃ punaḥ praṇipatyovāca ||

aho dharma aho puṇyaṃ sarvaduḥkhavināśanaṃ ||

aparaṃ śrotum icchāmi tadājñāṃ vaktu marhasi ||

prasīdasva mahābhijñaḥ sarvasatvārthalkāraṇām ||

yathāhaṃ desayikhyāmi (!) tathāhaṃ kriyate dhunā ||

ityākarṇya vaca[ḥ] śrutvā uvāca bhikṣusattama ||

ekasmin samaye tatra vaisālī(mumagratyūmarcchata)hradas tīre kṛtāṅgāre gālāyāṃ viharati sma || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

yasovatyādyanikayā ʼnena kusalamūlena cittotpādena deyadharmaparityāgena trikalpāsaṃkhyā samudānītāṃ bodhi samudānīya mahākaruṇāparibhāvitā || ṣat(!)pāramitā paripūrya yasovatyā nāma samyaksaṃbuddho bhaviṣyasi || dāsabhir balayī caturbhir ākārai[ś] ca⟨|⟩turbhir sāradyais tribhir āveśikai smṛtyupasthānair mahākaruna(!)yā yam asyā deyadharmo yo mamāntike cittam abhiprasādya || idam avocad bhagavān āttamanās te ca bhikṣavo sāś(!) ca sarvavatīparṣat sadevāsuragaruḍakinnara bhagavato bhāṣitam abhyanandann iti || || (fol. 10v1–5)

Colophon

iti yasovatimāsopavāsakṛtakathā samāpta(!) || || śubhaṃ || || śubham astu sarvadā || || ❁ || ❁ || ❁ || ❁ || ❁ || (fol. 10v5–6)
