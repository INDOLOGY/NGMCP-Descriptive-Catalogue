Beginning

///mṛtaṃ mahābhāga tan me karuayā vada || 2 ||

vyāsa uvāca ||

atha gītāṃ pravakṣyāmi yogamārgaprakāśinīṃ ||

niyuktā pṛcchate sūta rājñe majamukhena yā || (!) 3 ||

vareṇya uvāca ||

vighneśvara mahābāho sarvavidyāviśārada ||

sarvaśāstrārthatattvajña yogaṃ me vaktum arhasi || 4 ||

śrīgajānana uvāca || ||

samyagvyavasitā rājan matis te[ʼ]nugrahān mama ||

śṛṇu gītāṃ pravakṣyāmi yogāmṛtamayīṃ nṛpa || 5 ||

na yogaṃ yogam ity āhur yogo yogo na ca śriyaḥ ||

na yogo viṣayair yogo na ca mātrādibhis tathā || 6 || (fol.2r1–2v5)

End

savāhanāṃ sāyudhāṃ ca samabhya (!) ca yathāvidhi ||
