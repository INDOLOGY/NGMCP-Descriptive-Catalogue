«Beginning: »


|| atha sūtasaṃhitā || ||


|| || sūta uvāca || ||


athātaḥ saṃpravakṣyāmi śivasthānaṃ samāsataḥ |


yatra saṃciṃtya deveśaṃ kaivalyaṃ labhate naraḥ || 1 ||


purā nārāyaṇaḥ śrīmān kirīṭī garuḍadhvajaḥ |


śrīman kailāśaparyante tatāpa paramaṃ tapaḥ | (2)


bhasmoddhūlitasarvāṅgas tripuṇḍrāṅkitamastakaḥ |


rudrākṣamālāsaraṇo jaṭāvalkalasaṃyutaḥ | (3)


tapasā tasya devasya keśavasya mahātmanaḥ |


prādur āsīn mahādevaḥ śaṃkaraḥ karuṇānidhiḥ | (4 ) (fol. 1v1–4)



«End: »


bhasmadhāraṇamāhātmyaṃ mayā vaktuṃ na śakyate |


guruṇāpi ca me viprāḥ kalpakoṭuśatair api |


brahmaṇā viṣṇunā vāpi rudreṇāpi munīśvarāḥ |


bhasmadhāraṇamāhātmyaṃ na śakyaṃ paribhāṣituṃ ||


aho mohasya māhātmyaṃ munīṃdrābrahmavādinaḥ |


yan narā bhasmasaṃtyajya kurvatyanyat samīhitaṃ |


atirahasyam idaṃ munipuṃgavāḥ


kathitam eva samasta jagaddhitaṃ |


yadi samācaritaṃ manujair mudā


saphalam eva hi janama na saṃśayaḥ || 61 || (fol. 10r1–5)



«Colophon:»


iti sūtassaṃhitāyāṃ yajñavaibhavakhaṃḍe ekonatriṃśodhyāyaḥ || || (fol. 6v9–7r1)


iti sūtasaṃhitāyāṃ yajñavaibhavakhaṃḍe triṃśodhyāyaḥ vibhūti(māhātmyaṃ) samāptaṃ || (fol. 10r5)
