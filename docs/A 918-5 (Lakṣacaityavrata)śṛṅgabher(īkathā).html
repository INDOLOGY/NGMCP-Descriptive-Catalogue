śṛṃgabheryyā phalaṃ śreṣṭhaṃ turyānāṃ turyam uttamaṃ ||


phalaṃ ..tā anākāle vrateṣu dharmmasaṃmate |


santi śṛṃgabherīś(!) ca divyaṃ gotraṃ bhaviṣyati || (!)
