Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namo gaṇapataye || ||

sa jayati dainya sadā phalam akhilam idaṃ paśyato jagat yasyā

hṛdayasyeva gaṇitvā(!) jātā rasarūpiṇīkaruṇā ||

natvā śrīpārvatīṃ devīṃ bhairavaṃ siddhasaṅgatim |

rasaratnaṃ prakāśyāmi dehe lohe śubhaṃkaram ||

sūte sūtavaro varaṃ hi nayanaṃ śabdātparaṃ sparśanād

dhrūmād dīpyati tatkṣaṇād atha saṃkhyā‥‥‥‥

(fol.1v1-2)

ath rasasya gatibhedam āha ||

dhūmaḥ śuddhaś ca kaṃ paś ca maṇḍuka(!)plutir eva ca |

ctuś caiva gatis tasya aṛśyā paṃcamī gatiḥ ||

praṇāmaḥ ||

rasendroparasas tubhyaṃ haravīryāya vai namaḥ |

namas te hararūpāyā pāradāya namo ʼstu te |

samastam etatkathitaṃ prayatnāt satvādikānāṃ sukhakāmadharmadam |

rasāṅkuśā vā dhanatatparāṇāṃ || ||                                               (fol.30v5-7)

«Sub-colophon:»

iti śrīnāthaviracite rasaratne sādhakānāṃ rasasya siddhikaraṇaṃ nāma dvādaśopadeśaḥ || || || (fol.30v7-8)

End

abhrakaṃ harabījan tu. ṣoḍaśośena kāñcanam ||

dhmātaṃ prakāśabhūṣāyāṃ tantujīrṇaṃ sa vedhayet ||

dakṣiṇāvarttasaṃ…ntaṃ harabījena melakam ||

lākapāla(!)śataṃ tyaktvā dattvā bhairavadundubhiḥ ||

jayaśabdañ ca nirghoṣair arghyahastair abāhutaiḥ ||

rājimātrasūtena vrajet kailāsamandiram ||

krīḍataḥ saptalokeṣu śivartulyaparākramaḥ || ||

(fol.64r5-7)

Colophon

iti śrīmūlasūtre rasasvacchande caturviṃśatisāhasre dravaṇādhikāro nāma ṣaṣṭhaḥ paṭalaḥ || || (fol.64r7-8)
