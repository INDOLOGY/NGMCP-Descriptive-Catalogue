Excerpts

Beginning

/// krāṃtā śatāvarī ||

sarppākṣī girikarṇī ca muṃdri(!)ca sahadevikā ||

triphalā khaṇḍaśaḥ kṛtvā sarvāny etāni nikṣipet ||

dhānyasya ca caturthāṃśaṃ (dhātvāmlam idam) uttamaṃ ||

svedanādiṣu kāryyeṣu pāradasya viśiṣyate ||

satapte kāṃtaje khalve rasarājaṃ vinikṣipet ||

cūrṇaṃ ca gṛhadhūmaṃ ca citrakaṃ triphalā guḍam ||

dagdhorṇām iṣṭikāṃ kanyāṃ lavaṇaṃ bṛhatīdvayaṃ ||

(fol. 2r1-3)

End

bhāvayed ātape tīvre vimalā śuddhyati dhruvaṃ ||

vimalaṃ śignujo yena kāṃjiko sīsaṭaṃkaṇaiḥ ||

vajrakaṃdasamāyuktair bhāvayet kadalīrasaiḥ ||

māsikakṣārasaṃyuktaṃ aṃdhramūṣāgataṃ dhamet ||

satvaṃ candrārkasaṃkāśaṃ pataty asya na saṃśayaḥ || ||

haritāśma ca ye rojo viṣārārtir harir maṇiḥ ||

ravivīryyavimākeṣu sasyakasya guṇānugaḥ ||

(fol. 106v5-7)
