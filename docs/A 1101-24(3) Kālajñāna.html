Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

atha kālajñānam ||

rocanaiḥ kuṃkumair lakṣā saptikāraktasaṃpytaiḥ ||

dvādaśāraṃ likhet padmaṃ tad bahiś caiva tatsamaṃ ||

ṣoḍaśārakalābāhye mūlaṃ bījaṃ dale likhet ||

prathamasya dale varṣa(!) māsāś caiva bahir ddale ||

divasā ṣoḍaśāre tu sādhyanāma ca karṇike ||

pūjayec cakravantaṃ tu kālajñānaṃ śivoditaṃ ||

oṃ udyatkālapuruṣottamasaṃhāramūrte kālākṣakāya aṃtaḥ kālaṃ pradarśaya pradhānakālaṃ pradarśaya svāhā || (fol. 1v1-5)

End

paścimāmnāya(!) sakrasya prokteyaṃ kālavaṃcanā ||

nāmito brahmaraṃdhrāṃtaṃ sarpābhāṃ jyotirūpiṇīṃ ||

prollasaṃtīṃ japen nityaṃ māyāṃ kālasya vaṃcanāṃ ||

hrīṃ māyām iti ||

(svakīkyaṃ grasapta yo sau) cittaṃ kālakalākulāṃ ||

grāsaṃ te na smaret kiṃcit kālas tasya karoti kim || (fol. 3v5-7)

Colophon

iti kālajñānaṃ śubhm || || || || || (fol. 3v7)
