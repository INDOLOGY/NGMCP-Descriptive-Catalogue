Manuscript Features

The text runs form the very beginning up to the middle of the verse 52 of the first sarga then it breaks and again starts form the end of the verse 4 of the fourth sarga and goes up to verse 139 of the sixth sarga.

Fol. 21 is wrongly numbered 22, and vice versa. The verso side of what has been numbered fol. 21 is blank, but there is no lost of text.

lokā ity amoghamaṃtram uktavān ity arthaḥ | tataḥ prabhṛti sarvasya tejaḥ apahṛtaṃ | 53 | jite nṛpālāv iti |

iti śrībhartṛkāvye (!) ṭīkāyāṃ prakīrṇakāṇḍe rāmapariṇayo dvitīyaḥ sargaḥ |     |
