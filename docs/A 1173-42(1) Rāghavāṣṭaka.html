«Text:»

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

bhuvanaguptaye brahmaṇārthitaṃ

daśarathālaye vyaktimāgataṃ ||

janakanaṃdinī lakṣaṇānvitaṃ

bhajamanaḥ sadā rāmamadbhutaṃ || 1 ||

kamalalocanaṃ pāpamocanaṃ

svajana raṃjanaṃ dvaitabhaṃjanaṃ ||

ṣaḍarimardanaṃ buddhi varddhanaṃ

bhajamana saº || 2 ||

agaṇitaujasaṃ daṃḍasāgaraṃ

praṇana (!) tāpasaṃ sādhumānasaṃ ||

amitabhāsasaṃ pītavāsasaṃ

bhajaº || 3 ||

nijatanuprabhābhāsitākhilaṃ

bāliratnayuk divyakuṃdulaṃ ||

padavirājitaṃ kṣoṇimaṇḍalaṃ

bhajaº || 4 ||

madanakoṭisauṃdarya mohanaṃ

paramabhaktidaṃ kāmadohanaṃ ||

hṛdaya paṃkajedhyātnābhavaṃ

bhaº || 5 ||

surapatistvayodhyāpure vare

suratarusthale maṃḍapāṃtare ||

maṇimayāsane saumyamāsthitaṃ

bhajamanaḥ saº || 6 ||

amarakiṃnnaraiḥ siddhacāraṇair

munivarair nararr apsarogaṇaiḥ

paramaśraddhayā bhaktitorcitaṃ

bhajamaº || 7 ||

caritavistaraṃ koṭibhiḥ śataṃ

śatrubhaṃjanaṃ rāmasaṃsthitaṃ ||

bhajanaśaṃkaraṃ prāpya hṛt kathaṃ ||

bhaja8 ||

evamadbhutaṃ rāghavāṣṭakaṃ

pratidinaṃ paṭhet bhaktipūrvakaṃ ||

ahilayārcitaṃ pādapaṃkajaṃ ||

bhaja maº || 9 ||

iti śrīmat paramahaṃ ○ paº śrīmachaṃ (!) karācāryaviracitaṃ rāghavāṣṭakaṃ saṃpuṇaṃ (!) || (fol. 1a:b)
