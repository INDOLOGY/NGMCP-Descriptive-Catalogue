Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṁ namaḥ śrīvajasatvāyaḥ ||

svasti śrīgaṇesahāya namaḥ ||

oṁ śrīguru ājñā || ||

❖ raviśaśi(2)śaśisūryaḥ kāmakaṃ darpaṇīye, malayaja,

girilohedigguhaṃ śihlakaṃcana khajalaghanake śī(3)taṃ

pranāṇaṃ ca pūrvvakamala kṛta dhupaḥ sarvvabhutāniha nyāt || ||

śita hlāca msaṃ 16 kapura msaṃ 12(4) kasturī msaṃ 7 śrīkhaṇḍa msaṃ 10 manaśīla msaṃ 10 nakī msaṃ 16 jaṭāmāsa(5) msaṃ 6 śrīra msaṃ 9 ||

thva dhupa thaṅana, sarvvabhūta gaṇa mocake,

bhūtamāraṇa dhūpa thva juro(6) || 1 || (exp. 17/fol. 1v1-6)

End

okhadi pādhāraṃ, vāsara utiṃ, kṣeja jukva(8) visekhaṃ juro || 1 ||

hanumantraguṭikāyāta, varahaṃsayā kṣejasa duthaṅanaṃ,

khāna tā(exp. 19/fol. 3v1)cake hnu 21 || 2 ||

devīguṭīkā dayake, yimāyā kṣejasa duthaṅanaṃ,

khāna tācake (2) hnu 7 || 3 ||

kāmaguṭikā dayake, bohorayā kṣejasa duthaṅanaṃ,

khāna tācake hnu (3) 21 || 4 ||

lakṣmīguṭikā dayake sirddhayāya thatheṃ sirddhi yāya juro || 5 || (exp. 18/fol. 3r7-fol. 3v3)

Colophon

I(exp. 19/fol. 3v4)ti paṃcaguṭikā sirddhayake vidhi samāpta mitiḥ || ۞ ||
