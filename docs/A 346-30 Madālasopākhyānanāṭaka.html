Manuscript Features

This nāṭaka is seen written by Jagajjyoti Malla in ŚS 1550 and staged in the time of Jagatprakāśa (NS 765–795) according to the Āśir vacana at the last. But there is the date of gagaṇa vindu rasa – śaśikara (śs 1600) with the name of Jagajjyoti in the song of Ārati.
