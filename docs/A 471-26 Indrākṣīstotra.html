Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namaś caṇḍikāyai |

oṃ asya śrī-indrākṣīstotramantrasya purandara-ṛṣir anuṣṭupchandaḥ śrī-indrākṣīdevatā śrīṃ bījaṃ hrīṃ śaktir bhavānīkīlakaṃ mama sarvābhīṣṭasiddhyarthe pāṭhe viniyogaḥ ||

oṃ indra-ṛṣaye namaḥ śirasi

oṃ anuṣṭupchaṃdase namo mukhe

oṃ iṃdrākṣīdevatāyai namo hṛdi

oṃ śrībījāya namo guhye

oṃ hrīṃ śaktaye namaḥ pādayoḥ

oṃ bhavānīkīlakāya namaḥ sarvāṅge |

…

dhyānam ||

oṃ indrākṣīṃ dvibhujāṃ devīṃ pītavastravibhūṣitām ||
vāme vajradharāṃ devīṃ savyahaste bhayapradāṃ || 1 ||

sahasranetrāṃ sūryābhāṃ nānālaṃkārabhūṣitāṃ |
prasannavadanān nityam apsarogaṇasevitāṃ | [|| 2 ||]

śrīdurgāṃ saumyavadanāṃ pāśāṃkuśadharāṃ parām |
trailokyamohinīṃ devīṃ bhavānīm praṇamāmy aham || 3 || (exp. 2, ll. 1–9)

End

āyur ārogyam aiśvaryam jñānaṃ vittaṃ yaśobalaṃ |
śatam āvarttayed yas tu mucyate vyādhibandhanāt |

āvarttānāṃ sahasreṇa labhate vāṃchitaṃ phalaṃ |
rājā vaśyam avāpnoti sarvam eva na saṃśayaḥ | 10 ||

lakṣam ekaṃ japed yas tu sākṣād devī bhaviṣyati ||
trikālaṃ paṭhate nityaṃ dhanan dhānyaṃ ca saṃpadaḥ | 12 |

rājadvāre sabhāmadhye gṛhamadhye gṛhāṃgaṇe |
vidyāvāde vivāde ca devī rakṣatu sarvadā | 13 |

arddharātrau paṭhen nityaṃ mucyate vyādhibaṃdhanāt |
aindrastotram idaṃ puṇyaṃ japet tu phalabarddhanaṃ | 1(‥) ||

vināśāyāti rogāṇām alpamṛtyuharāya ca |
rājyārthī labhate rājyaṃ dhanārthī labhate dhanaṃ | 14 |

icchākāman tu kāmārthī dharmārthī dharmam akṣayaṃ |
vidyārthī labhate vidyāṃ mokṣārthī paramam padaṃ | 15 | | (exps. 2, ll. 15–3, ll. 3)

Colophon

iti śrīrudrayāmalataṃtre umāmaheśvarasamvāde śrīṃdrākṣīstotraṃ | |

aśāpitam idaṃ stotram akīlitam idaṃ dhruvaṃ |
paṭhed vā pāṭhayed vāpi sarvān kāmān avāpnuyāt |

prātaḥ kāle ca madhyāhne pradoṣe cārdharātrake |
catuḥ sahasrāvarttena bhuvanaṃ vaśam ānayet |

sarvarogāt pramucyarthaṃ śatam āvarttayed dhruvaṃ | | (exp. 3, ll. 3–6)
