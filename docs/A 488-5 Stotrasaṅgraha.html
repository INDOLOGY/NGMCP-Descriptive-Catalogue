Manuscript Features

The MS contains the following texts: 1. Mālinīdaṇḍakastava 2. Mahāmāyāstotra 3. Hanubhaivarastotra (Halāyudhaviracita) 4. Siddhilakṣmīpañcamīstava

In the third exp. there is written: ❖ samvat 897 miti caitra śudi 15 śrī 3 vajra jogniguhāsapunaścaranayāta oṇā caitra vadi dvitīyābṛspati bāla kuhnu śrī 3 gaṇanāthayā vighnanivārana dau 10000 siddho layāṅā dā 100000 mūlayā gu. aṣṭami kuhnuṃ saṃkarya yāṅā viṣṇunānāṃ bhairavānanda ācārya

yadi śuddham aśuddhaṃ vā naiṣa ko nāstidokhayet | ādaraseyatra likṣyaṃ tatra liṣyate mayyat || vasau durggā bhūvanaś ca āṣāḍe dvitiyātithi | leṣakaviṣṇunārāyanabhavānīprītir astu ||

«Beginning»

❖ ... || oṃ namaś caṇḍikāyai namaḥ || ||

śrībhairava uvāca ||

jaya tvaṃ mālinī devi nirmmale malanāśinī |

jñānaśaktiprabhur ddevī buddhisthaṃ tejavarddhanī ||

jananī sarvvabhūtānāṃ saṃsāre smin vyavasthitā |

mātā vīrāvalī devī kāruṇyaṃ kuru vatsale || (exp. 4b1–4)

ata śrīpuṇyasaṃkāśāṃ ratnabhūṣaṇabhūṣitāṃ |
