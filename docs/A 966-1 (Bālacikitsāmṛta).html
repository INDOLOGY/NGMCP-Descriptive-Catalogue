Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || śrīdhanaṃtrāya (!) nama (!) ||…

nānā padyapayodhimanthananavamodaṃ budhānaṃ hṛdaḥ

sāraṃ bālacikitsitāmṛtamidaṃ satyāṃvidhatte paraḥ

yogyaṃ no vibudhadviṣā punaridaṃ nindaṃtu te vecchayaḥ

reṇur malinī karoti‥‥‥‥khalaiḥ preritaḥ 3

graṃthān vilokya pracura prayogān

vaidyaśvakīyaiḥ katibhistadīyaiḥ

ya(!) vakṣe cikitsāṃ rucirāṃ śiśūnāṣāṃ (!)

deṣakālāgnisamīkṣya kuryāt 4 (exp.1b1, 9–14)

End

kiyaṃtā kālena jana (!) naṃpusakam api surata sasamaṃ kunute ‥‥ śuṃṭhīkvātha ṣoḍaśaśeṣā śitāyuto (!) pītaḥ kurute tena puṃso retaḥ tanaṃ vināśena ārdre varaṭī chanena vyekaṃde sudarśanakhyākhyaṃ sādhitasādhitam atasī tailaṃ viṃdu nayanābhilepato dhate (!) jātīphalārka kṛmahāraka vaṃga śuṭhī kaṃko kuṃkumakaṇā rajanī haricaṃdanāni…(exp. 54a:5–11)
