Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

kālīrahasye ||

vinā pītvā surāṃ bhuktvā māṃsaṃ gatvā rajasvalām ||

yo japed dakṣiṇāṃ devīṃ tasya duḥkhaṃ pade pade ||

iti vacanād idam āvaśyakam ||

rajasvalāmukhaṃ dṛṣṭvā sarvvapāpair vyapohati ||

saṃbhāṣaṇaṃ ca kurute rājasūyādikaṃ phalam || 1 ||

tasyāḥ sparśanamātreṇa labhen muktiṃ caturvvidhām |

tasyāḥ saṃgamamātreṇa trailokyotpāṭane kṣamaḥ || 2 ||

śraddhayā pūjayet tasyā bhagaṃ sarajaśobhitam ||

nyāsaṃ kṛtvā svadeheṣu kalābhiḥ kāmaṣoḍaśaiḥ || 3 ||

mātṛkāṃ nyasya deheṣu anulomavilomataḥ |

śivarūpaṃ vicintya svaṃ bhagapūjāṃ samācaret || 4 ||

koṭijanmārjitaiḥ puṇyair bhaktyā yaiḥ pūjitaṃ bhagam |

viśeṣaiḥ pūjitā yoniḥ puṇyasaṃkhyā na vidyate || 5 || (fol. 1r1–6)

End

satyaṃ satyaṃ punaḥ satyaṃ satyaṃ satyaṃ na saṃśayaḥ ||

sapuṣpabhagapūjā ca brahmalokepi durllabhā || 15 ||

asya stotrasya māhātmyaṃ mayā vaktuṃ na śakyate ||

viraṃcir maghavā viṣṇuḥ paṭhate ca niraṃtaram || 16 ||

stotrapāṭhaprabhāveṇa sṛṣṭisthitir bhaved dhruvam ||

tasmāt sarvvaprayatnena devi gopyakaraṃ śubham || 17 ||

smaret prathamapuṣpiṇīṃ rudhirabiṃdunīlāṃvarāṃ gṛhītamadhupātrikāṃ madavighūrṇanetrāṃcalām || 18 ||

ghanastachabharonnatāṃ galitacolikāṃ śyāmalāṃ karasphuritavallakīṃ viśadaśaṃkhanāṭaṃkinīṃ || 19 || (fol. 1v4–9)

Colophon

iti śrīkālīprastāre īśvarapārvvatīsaṃvāde puṣpiṇīstotram || catuḥpathādau arddharātraṃ prayoge ca oṃ padme padme mahapadme padmādhanimāye svāhā || siddhasārasvate || eiṃ hrīṃ śrīṃ hraṃ hmauḥ [[sauḥ]] shauḥ vada vada vāgvādini trāṃ trī trīṃ nīlasarasvati eiṃ eiṃ eiṃ kāhi kāhi kalaṃkī svāhā || 34 || maṃtraḥ || (fol. 1v4–9)
