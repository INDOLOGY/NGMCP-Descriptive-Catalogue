Manuscript Features

The MS contains the text of Ānandalaharī, Vagalādevyā stava and Viṣṇupañjarastotra.

On the cover leaf in a different hand is written:


❖ gajamukhaṃ vāmakūṭaṃ ca ekavaktraṃ trilocanaṃ |

gaṇapatiṃ vighnaharttāraṃ gaṅgāputraṃ namāmy aham ||
