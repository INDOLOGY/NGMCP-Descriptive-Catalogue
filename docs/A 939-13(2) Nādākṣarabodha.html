Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya nama ||

atha ṣaḍcakrako vistāra ||

prathama mūlādhāra cakre gaṇeśa deva riddhi buddhi śaktiḥ teṣu caturdalakamala śaṃti(!) || vaṃ śaṃ ṣaṃ śaṃ || dvitīya svādhiṣṭhāna cakre brahmādevatā gāyatri śakti teṣu ṣaḍdalakamala || vaṃ bhaṃ maṃ yaṃ raṃ laṃ || (fol. 1v1–4)
