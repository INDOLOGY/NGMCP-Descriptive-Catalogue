Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

atha gocaraphalaṃ kathyate || ||


gaurādi⟪vya⟫[[tya]]s tasya cāraś candrāder upalakṣaṇam ||

tatphalaṃ gocaraphalaṃ grahā yacchanti janmabhāt || 1 ||


sūryaḥ sthānavināśaṃ

bhayaṃ śriyaṃ mānahānim atha dainyam ||

sukṛtahatiṃ siddhim āyam atha hānim || 2 || (fol. 1v1–2)
