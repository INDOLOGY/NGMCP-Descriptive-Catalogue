Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

nyāyapaṃcānanaśrīmān jayarāmaḥ samāsataḥ
ākhyātavādavyākhyā⟨khyā⟩nam āta⟨ce⟩noti mā(!)noramaṃ 2 ||

anukūru(!)vyāpāratvaṃ ākhyātaśakyatāvacchedakaṃ anukūlatvasya saṃsargatvāt vyāpāratvamātraṃ tathety eke anukūlunamātraṃ tathety anye || dhātor arthaḥ phalaṃ anukūlavyāpāradikam ākhyātasyeti maṃḍanaḥ ta(!)tatphalāvachinnavyāpāraviśeṣaḥ tattaddhātvarthaḥ sa(!)khyāvarttamānatvādikaṃ kvacid āśrayatvādikaṃ cākhyātau(!)rtha iti guravaḥ ta⟪sya⟫sarvaṃ nirācikīrṣuḥ pratijānīte | (fol. 1v1–3)

End

ādipadātra syato(!)tyādau pratiyogitvaparigrahaḥ | karmākhyāte phalārthake bhāvākhyāte cāśrayatvādyarthatvābhāvāt kvacid ity uktaṃ | tatra tatra caitraḥ pacati suṃdaraḥ pacatītyādau maṃḍanamatahṛṣayo naiva tanmataṃ hṛsitaprāyam iti asmatkataśavdālokarahasyai(!) vistaraḥ

apūri jayārāmeṇa vibudānaṃdadāyinī |
