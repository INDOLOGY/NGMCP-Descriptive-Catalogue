Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namastripurāyaiḥ (!) ||

herambañ ca namaskṛtya viśvarūpaṃ trilocanaṃ |

brahmāviṣṇus tathā śakra śabdarūpā sarasvatī ||

svarasaṃkhyā gaṇāḥ(2) sarvve anilaḥ sūryyacandramā |

pṛthivyāṃ pañcatattvāni sūrān (!) mahiṣamarddinī ||

saptārddhapīṭhadevyāś caś (!) catuṣaṣṭhistu yoginī |

nama(3)skṛtya mayā sarvvaṃ darśanād guru vanditam || (fol.1v1–3)

End

|| tāravaṃgeśvararasa || ||

roga saṃkha trikaṭuka granthī,

dipyaka (6)vahni kaṭphala hiṅguḥ |

lavaṇau viḍiyā sāṃbhari yuktaṃ,

cūrṇṇita ślakṣṇaṃ samatā grāhyaṃ, |

samyak taptaṃ sajalaṃ pītvā

bahuvi(7)dhi rogo kurute nāśāṃ (!) |

kāśā śvāsā, gulmā śūlā,

sarvvaṃ mukto kurute dīptā ||

sa eva pītvā hṛḍi jadi hāsaṃ

bahulā vijayā duhu miri johyam

…(fol.55v5–7)

Colophon

vaṃga dvādaśarase || || ❁ || (fol.55v7)
