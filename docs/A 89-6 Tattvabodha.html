Manuscript Features

In the fol. 12r, after the colophon there are some stanzas have been written which are related to panegyric.

tatvabodhko (!) a⟪gha⟫rtha ṣolyeko pai:(2)hleko patra ho ātmā tvam jīva:

tatvabodhko (!) bhākā ṣolyāko (2) pachillo patra ho harā (!) rāma

Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || hariparamātmanebhya (!) namaḥ ||

vāsudevendra nāuṃ garyākā yogi yogi(2)mākā sreṣṭha (!) bhayākā.

jñān dine bhayākā. yestā. gurukana. namaskār (!) garera. ||

mokṣe(‥)(3)ko ichā (!) garneharukā. hita havas bhannā nimitta.

tatvabodha (!) nāuṃ garyāko graṃtha. jo cha (4)so. banāīncha || 1 ||

cārvedkana. (!) paḍhdachan. anek (!) dharmaśāstrakana pani paḍhdacha(5)n. ātmākana jāndainan. jasto. ḍāḍu pākeko raska (!) jāndaina. tyastai aviveki pu(6)ruṣ (!) pani ātmākana jāndaina || 2 || (fol. 1v1–6)

End

antakaraṇakā (!) sandehakana nāsincha. i ādi garekā (3) śravaṇāt sundā ⟪tāhā⟫ ||

jñāniko śarir (!) gayākṣetramā cāṇḍālkā (!) baghaicāmā marda(4)cha. testo cha tāpani. jñāni puru (!) jo cha so mukta bhayāko chaṃdo ānandarūp (!) bhayācha(5)ko kṛtārtha huncha: || (fol. 11v2–5)

Colophon

iti śrīmatparamahaṅsaparivrājakācāryye(!) śrīvāsudevendravi(6)racitam ⟪ā⟫ tatvabodhākhyam (!) vedāntaprakaraṇam sampurṇam (!) śubham ||

śrīśāke (7) 17 saye śrīsamvat 1917 sāla miti phāguṇasudi (!) 11 roja śukavāśare (!) li(12r1)ṣete (!) || (fol. 11v5–12r1)
