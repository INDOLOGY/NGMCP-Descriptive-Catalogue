Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇēśāya nama(!) ||    ||

gaṇeśaṃ śāradāṃ vande vande puruṣamavyayam ||
karomi cātmabodhasya bhāvām caiva subodhinī || 1 ||

artha ||
paihle siddhidātā gaṇeśa | aura śāradādevī | aura avyaya arthāt | kaihle nāśa nahunyā yastā avināśi paramapuruṣa bhagavānlāī praṇāṃ gari || ātmabodha nāu bhayākā granthako praṣṭa bijhinyā bhāṣā gardachū || 1 || (fol. 1v1–10)

End

digdeśakālādyanapekṣa sarvagaṃ śītādihṛnnityasukhaṃ niraṃjanam ||
yastvātmatīrtha bhajate viniṣkriyaḥ | sasarvagaṃ vitsarvagato ʼ mṛto bhavet || 68 ||

artha ||
jo sarva kriyā dekhi rahit | paramahaṃsa āphnu ātmasvarūpa | tīrthaviṣe snāna garnevālā | arthāt | bhajanechan| sohī | sarvavettā | … puruṣakana | svātmatīrtha sevanu avasya cha | dosro kartavya aru chaina || 68 || (fol. 48v11:49v6)

Colophon
