Manuscript Features

This text is in three parts as pūrva-khaṇḍa F.30, madhyamakhaṇḍa F44 and uttarakhaṇḍa F60. Total folios are 134 although registered as 124.

Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

nārāyaṇaṃ namaskṛtya naraṃ caiva narottamam ||

devīsarasvatī (!) caiva tato jayam udīrayat(!) || 1 ||

brahmāsmṛtvāyuṣovedaṃ prajāpatīm ajīgrahat ||

śośvinau tau sahassrāksaṃ sotriputrādikān(!) munim || 2 ||

tegnivesādikāste tu pṛthak taṃtrāṇi teṇire(!) ||

tebhyopi viprakīrṇebhyaḥ prāyasāratarośrayaḥ(!) || 3 ||

bālānāṃ sukhabodhāya rogīnāṃ(!) ca hitāya vaiḥ(!) ||

sūkṣmaṃ karoti carakaḥ graṃthaṃ tu carakākhyayā || 4 ||

iti vedotpatti(!)

yas tu rogam avijñāya karmānyārabhate(!) bhiṣak ||

jñātavyo vakṣate(!) yoyaṃ jvarādīnāṃ viniścayaḥ || 5 ||(fol.1v1-6)

End

śālimudgādimaṣnīyāt(!) pived dugdhaṃ saśarkkaram ||

sevyaṃ nadī saro vāri karajālaṃ ca śītagoḥ || 21 ||

ikṣavaḥ śālayo muṃgāḥ(!) saroṃbhaḥ kvathitaṃ payaḥ ||

śaradetāni pathyāni pradoṣe ceṃduraśmayaḥ || 22 ||

tīkṣṇamamlaṃ salavaṇaṃ dadhitailāsavātapaṃ ||

divāsvapnaṃ purovātaṃ śaratkāle vivarjayet || 23 || (fol.30v1-4)

Colophon

iti śrīsahasravadanasyāṃsa(!) carakācārya viracitā(!) saṃhitāyāṃ carakākhye bhūta ciṃtādi kathanan nāma trayadaśo(!) dhyāyaḥ || 13 || samāptaṃ pūrvakhaṇḍaḥ || ||śubham || (fol. 44v4-6)

iti śrīsahasravadanasyāṃsa(!) carakācāryyaviracitāyāṃ viracitāyām saṃhitāyām carakākhye rasauṣadhi bhakṣaṇe sāmānya pathyāpathya kathanaṃ nāma ṣoḍaśodhyāyaḥ || 16 || samāpto(!) madhyamakhaṇḍaḥ || 2 || śubham || ||( fol. 60v4-6)

iti śrīsahasravadanasyāṃsa(!) carakācāryyaviracitāyāṃ saṃhitāyāṃ carakākhye svsthavṛttikathanaṃ nāmaikādaśodhyāyaḥ || 11 || samāptoyaṃ uttarakhaṇḍaḥ(!) || || śubham || fol. 30r4-6)
