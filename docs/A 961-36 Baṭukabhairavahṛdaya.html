Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||


kailāśasi(!)kharāsīnaṃ devadevaṃ jagadgurum ||

devī papraccha sarvajñaṃ śaṃkaraṃ varadaṃś śivam || 1 ||


devy uvāca ||


deva deva pareśāna bhaktābhīṣṭapradāyaka ||

prabrūhi me mahābhāga gopyaṃ yadyapi me prabho || 2 ||


vaṭukasyaiva hṛdayaṃ sādhakānāṃ hitāya ca || 3 ||


śiva uvāca ||


śṛṇu devi pravakṣāmi hṛdayaṃ vaṭukasya ca || 3 ||(fol. 1v1–3)


End

niśāyāṃ maṃgaladine śanivāre viśeṣataḥ ||

catuṣpathagato bhūtvā nagro(!) bhūtvā varānane || 22 ||


yadi bhāgyavaśād devi rātrau maithunasaṃyutaḥ ||

sauktakeśo maheśāni bhūtvā cāṣṭaśataṃ japet || 23 ||


saṃbhavet parameśāni sākṣād viṣṇur na saṃśayaḥ ||

sarvavidyānidhir devi deveśo vaṭukaḥ paraḥ || 24 ||


bhūrjapatre tv imaṃ maṃtraṃ gaṃdhacamḍanacarcitam ||

likhitvā cāṣṭagaṃdhena dhārayet sādhakottamaḥ || 25 ||


kare kaṃṭḥe bāhumūle dharayed vā samāhitaḥ ||

tasya sarvārthasiddhi[ḥ] syāt satyaṃ satyaṃ na saṃsayaḥ(!) || 26 ||


paṭhanād dhāraṇād devi svayaṃ siddhīsvaro bhavet || 27 || (fol. 2v4–9)


Colophon

iti viśvasāroddhāre vaṭukabhairavahṛdayaṃ samāptam śubham || (fol. 2v9 and on the side)
