Excerpts

Beginning

oṁ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||     ||

manu ekāgram āsīnam abhigamya maharṣayaḥ ||

pratipūjya yathyānyāyam idaṃ vacanam abruvan || 1 ||

bhagavan sarvavarṇānāṃ yathāvad anupūrvaśaḥ ||

aṃtaraprabhavāṇāṃ ca dharmmān no vaktum arhasi || 2 ||

tvam eko hy asya sarvasya vidhānasya svayaṃbhuvaḥ ||

aciṃtyasyāprameyasya kārtyyatattvārthavit prabho || 3 || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

eṣa sarvāṇi bhūtāni paṃcabhi[r] vyāpyamūrttibhiḥ ||

janmavṛddhikṣayai[r] nityaṃ saṃsārayati cakravat || 24 ||

evaṃ yaḥ sarvabhūteṣu paśyaty ātmānam ātmanā ||

sa sarvasamatām etya brahmābhyeti paraṃ padaṃ || 25 ||

ity etan mānavaṃ śāstraṃ bhṛguproktaṃ paṭhan dvijaḥ ||

bhavaty ācāravān nityaṃ yatheṣṭāṃ prāpnuyād gatiṃ || 125 || (fol. 114r7–114v1)

Colophon

iti śrīmānave dharmaśāstre bhṛguproktāyāṃ saṃhitāyāṃ dvādaśo dhyāyaḥ || 12 ||    ||     ||      ||

samvat 1903 sālamiti śrāvaśudi 13 roja 4 śubham ||     ||     || 2600 ||     || ❁ (fol. 114v1–2)
