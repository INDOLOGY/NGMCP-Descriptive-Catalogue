Manuscript Features

The manuscript contains altogether 120 texts. See E 1774-3(1)–(120).

Missing folios are: 9–14, 51, 89, 100, 105–109, 111–113, 127–131, 162, 178–179.

This MS is quite corrupt:

- Many mistakes regarding consonants

- Frequent omission of visarga and anusvāra.
