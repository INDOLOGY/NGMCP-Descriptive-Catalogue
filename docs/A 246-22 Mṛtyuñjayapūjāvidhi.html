Excerpts

«Beginning:»


❖ oṃ namaḥ śivāya ||


tato mṛtyuñjayapūjā ||

ṛṣiś chandaḥ || oṃ asya śrītryaṃbakamantrasya baśiṣṭha ṛṣiḥ śrīmṛtyuñjayarudro

devatā anuṣṭubchandaḥ devadevyautraṇa plauṃ bījaṃ śaktiḥ pujane viniyogaḥ ||


atra mūlamantreṇa karaśuddhiṃ kṛtvā praṇavaṃ karatalayoḥ vinyasya byāhṛtimantracatuṣṭayam

karatalayor aṅguṣṭhādikaniṣṭhikāntam aṅguliṣu vinyaset || tataḥ sarvveṇa vyāpakanyāsaṃ kṛtvā

dehāṅganyāsam ārabhet || (exp. 2:1–5)


«End:»


evaṃ karanyāsaṃ kṛtvā dehāṅganyāsam ārabhet || oṃ tryaṃbakam iti śirasi || oṃ yajāmahe iti

bhrubor madhye || oṃ sugandhim iti netrayoḥ || oṃ puṣṭibarddhanam iti mukhe || oṃ udvārukam iti

kaṇṭhe || oṃ miva hṛdaye || oṃ bandhanād iti udare || oṃ mṛtyor guhye || oṃ mukṣī urvvoḥ || oṃ yamā

jānvoḥ || oṃ mṛtād iti pādayoḥ || evaṃ dehāṅganyāsaṃ kṛṭvā ṣaḍaṇganyāsam ārabhet || oṁ hrāṃ jāṁ

namo bhagavate tryaṃbakāya śūrapāṇaye svāhā hṛdayāya namaḥ || oṃṁ hrīṁ jīṁ namo (bhagavate)

rudrāya amṛtamūrttaye māṃ jīva śirase svāhā || oṁ hrīṁ jrīṁ namo bhagavate << missing >> madhye

japen mṛtyuhare tryaṃbakaṃ ||


japet phalārthī vidhivat prayogaṃ

prāsādabījaṃ jayasaṃpuṭena || (exp. 4t2–4b1)


«Colophon(s):»


iti mṛtyuñjayavidhānaṃ samāptaṃ || || oṁ hūṁ jūṁ saḥ || kecit pāṭhaḥ || || (exp. 4b2)
