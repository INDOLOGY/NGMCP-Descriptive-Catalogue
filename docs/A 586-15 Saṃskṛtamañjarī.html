Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

bho svāmin || paramayā kṛpayā prathame lusume vibhaktiliṅgavacanānāṃ vistaraḥ pradarśito bhavtāḥ(!) || dvitīye śṛṃgārarasānāṃ vistaraḥ uktaḥ tat sarvaṃ śrutvā jñātvā ca paramānandasāgare ahaṃ nimagnāsmi || ataḥ paraṃ asmin saṃsāre prātaḥ kālam ārabhya sandhyākālaparyaṃtaṃ janaiḥ kiṃ kiṃ kartavyam ||

tad ahaṃ sarvaṃ śrotum icchāmi || bhavantaḥ atidīnāyai svapriyāyai (hyaṃ) vaktuṃ arhanti || he iduvadane(!) priye || samakarṇaya tava prītyai ahaṃ kathayāmi ||

yadā caturghaṭikā rātriḥ avaśiṣyate tadā utthāya tatrādau bhagavatsmaraṇaṃ kartavyaṃ prātaḥ stutiḥ paṭhitavyā punaḥ svapūruṣapādau (na natvā) bhūmer namaskāraṃ ca kṛtvā kapāṭaṃ udghāṭayet || (fol.1-v1-2v1)

End

yāvat tiṣṭhanti bhūloke śrīmadbhāgavatādayaḥ ||

yāyāt prakāśatāṃ tāvat caiṣā saṃskṛtamañjarī || 29 ||

nidhividhutanayitnuprānvite śrīśake ʼbde

śivabhuva upakaṇṭhe dugdhavipraudyahantuḥ ||

gaganavipathacakṣuḥ kṣmānvitagranthamānā

supuriviraciteyaṃ mañjarīsaskṛtānam(!) || 30 ||

(fol.96r3-5)

Colophon

iti śrīmad akhaṇḍapaṇḍitamaṇḍalāyaṇī(!) śrīmadghṛtakauśikavaṃśāvaṃśāvataṃsam

avarandasūrisutaśivaśarmaśarmaṇā kṛtasaskṛta(!)mañjaryāṃ dayitayoḥ savāde(!) sāṃsārikavyavahārasaṃgrahe tṛtīyaṃ kusumaṃ granthaś ca samāptatām ahaiṣīt ||
