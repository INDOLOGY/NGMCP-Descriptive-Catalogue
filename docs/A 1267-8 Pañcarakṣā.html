Title: Pañcarakṣā

Subject: Bauddha, Bauddhasūtra

Language: Sanskrit


Manuscript Details

Script: Newari

Material: palm-leaf

State: slightly damaged

Size: 28.0 x 4.0 cm

Binding Hole: 1, left of the centre

Folios: 150

Lines per Folio: 6

Foliation: letters in the left margin and figures in the right (by a later hand)

Date of Copying:

Place of Deposit: NAK

Accession No.: 3-688

Manuscript Features

1. scribe: fols. 1-39, 41v-54r, 55v-152v
2. scribe: fols. 40v-41r
3. scribe: fol. 54v-55r (maybe identical with 2.)

The script used by the first scribe makes an older impression than that of the others, yet they have to be regarded as contemporaries.

Missing folios: 58, 111, 139. Writing is rubbed off at the end of the manuscript. One additional folio at the end with foliation lost.
