Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

himavad viṃdhyaśailābhyāṃ prāyo vyāptā vasundharā ||

saumyaṃ pathyañ ca tatrāpyam āgneyaṃ vaidyam auṣadhaṃ ||

gopālas tāpasā vyādhā ye cānye vanacāriṇaḥ ||

mūlā(2)hārāś ca ye tebhyo bheṣaja vyaktir iṣyate ||

valmīkakūparathyā

tarutaruladevasadanaśmaśāneṣu ||

jātā vidhināpi hṛtā

oṣadhyaḥ siddhidā na syuḥ || (fol.1v1–2)

End

raseneva (!) kaṣāyan tu tiktakene(3)ti niścayaḥ ||

yatkiñcid doṣamuktoyaṃ na nirharati kāyataḥ ||

ara-jāta manyadvā tat sarvam ahitaṃ smṛtam || (!)

vyādhimindriyadaurbalyair maraṇaṃ cādhigacchati ||

(4)viruddharasavīryāstu bhuṃjāno nātmavān-naraḥ ||

viruddhāśanajān rogān pratihanti virecanam ||

vamanaṃ śamanaṃ vāpi satvaraṃ kriyate yadi ||

sātmyatolpatayā(5) vāpi dīptāgnes taruṇasya ca ||

snigdha vyāyāmibalināṃ viruddhaṃ vitathaṃ bhavet || || (fol.49v2–5)

Colophon

iti śrīvaidyaratnadyote nānādravyanāmaguṇavijñāno nāma tṛtīyaḥ(6) paricchedaḥ || || || || (fol.49v5–6)
