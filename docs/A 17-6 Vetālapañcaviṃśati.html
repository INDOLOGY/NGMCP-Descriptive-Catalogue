Manuscript Features

Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ sarvvajñāya ||

pṛthivimaṇḍalasa rājā pratāpi vijyāna(ṇḍa) ujonīnagalayā

vikramā(2)ditatvaṃ osake khyāntiśila kaṃpālīkatvaṃ

vayāva dvārasa conatvaṃ, rājā pura thva (cha)(3)hmaṃṇa

navaratna vyālasa duṃthaṅāva rājāsasaṃ biyakara choyātvaṃ,

rājā syavā yāka bhaṃḍI(4)riyāke tayakara cholatvaṃ | … (fol. 1v1–4)

Extracts

thva khaṃyā pāra yāṅā, vikramāditasyaṃ vetāla (2) lilāvaṃṅatvaṃ juro || ۞ || hanoṅa vetāla hayā || 1 || kāriṃdīnadīyā tirasa coṃgva (3) … (fol. 1v1–3)

End

Tribhuvanasaṃ vyāparapala aṣṭa mahāsiddhi yāṅa bhāgī juyu | brahmā viṣṇu maheśvarasavo (2) ule yāṇa mvāyutoṃ | rājāsa ojana dhāyāthe rā , che śalāma yātaṃ | olapane cheṇa | thvate si(3)ddhiona lāyu, che śariḍayā hī lāṇa mātṛgaṇa dvākoṃ tṛpti yāṅaṇa, thvateoṇa mahāsi(4)ddhi rāyu jana hlāko yāhuṇa āva jena hlākotvaṃ lumoṇakaṃ te | vikramāditasane che sāhā(5)sana thanu mānana hṅā che caritra soyā the | nīyaṅahmaṃ, vetārayā khaṃ /// (6) rājā dvākonavuṃ che mahemā seharape maphoyu karma, artha, mokṣayā kāma /// (fol. 119v1–6)
