Manuscript Features

This MS contains the following texts:

  • Gaṇapatistotra (fol. 1v1–6)
  • Kavaca-argalā-kīlakasahitadurgāsaptaśatī (fol. 1v6–65r1)
  • Indrākṣīstuti (fol. 65r1–65v6)
  • Prarthanā (fol. 65v6–66r5)

In the preliminary Title List and in the NAK catalogue card the title is wrongly mentioned as: Aṣṭamātṛkāstuti

On the cover-leaf appears a strange seal of "viśeṣa puliṣa vibhāga".

Beginning

❖ oṁ namaḥ śrīgaṇapataye ||

gaṇapatiṃ ca herambaṃ vighnarājaṃ vināyakaṃ |
devīputraṃ mahātejaṃ(!) mahābalaparākramaṃ ||

mahodaraṃ mahākāyam ekadraṣṭraṃ gajānanaṃ |
śvetavarṇṇaṃ mahādīptaṃ trinetraṃ gaṇanāyakaṃ ||

akṣamālāsvadantañ ca dakṣiṇe(!)karasaṃsthitaṃ |
parśumodakapātraṃ ca vāmahaste vidhāriṇaṃ || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

māhātmyaṃ tvadvicitraṃ danujavinihi(!)taṃ saptasatyās tavedaṃ

vausarggā bindumātrākṣarapadarahitāvākpradoṣāvihīnaṃ
