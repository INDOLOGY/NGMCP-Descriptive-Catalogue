Manuscript Features

This Ms. has many scribal errors.

Available folios: 1–4, 6, 8–18.

The root text is written in Sanskrit and the commentary is in Maithili.

Root text is written in the middle of the folio while the commentary is up and below of the root text.

Excerpts

Beginning of the root text

|| śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

śrīgoviṃdagurubhyo namaḥ ||

tha(!) atha janmapraśnalagnayo balābalatvaṃ gurur eṣābhi mā(!)ha

pāpaḥ ṣaṭ āyagobhavyaḥ sarvagas tu subho graha
sarvo niṣṭo vyayāʼṣṭastha(!) caṃdraś cārau tu cāṃgapa 1

trikoṇakaṃṭako casthai jñejyaśukro yadarkṣata
pāloʼpyaniṃdabhāvastho nariṣṭāyāʼnyathāʼdhama 2 (fol. 1v4–6)

Beginning of the commentary

|| atha janmakā praśnako balābalatiṃkā phala kahī ja-ichai | phalam āha || pāpagrahai 6 chaṭhai nutīsarai ra pāramaiṃ paḍaitau ācho sava hī subhagrahai kahī ara āṭhamai ṭha bāramai 12 saba dīneṣṭaka hījenirphala (fol. 1v1–2)

End of the root text

prakāsagrahayogādyai śrīyate sa svayaṃ śrīyā 9

śrīkāśahradagaṇeśo dyotanasuṃrī .. ṣṭasiṃhasuribhṛt
naracaṃdropādhyayau sāstraṃ cakrer tha bahulam idaṃ 10

vedadvitryekasaṃkhye tra māghe śukla-aṣṭamyāṃ ||
varivau varye śrīnaracaṃdropādhyāyau vikramādyavṛtya śrīstuṃ 11 (fol. 18r3–5)

End of the commentary

bhagnapiṣṭakaṭigrīvā adho driṣṭi adho muṣākṛṣṭena likṣi mayā jatanena paripālayet 1 śrīnaracaṃdropādhyāvikramākratapraśnaśate vṛṣṭivārttā prakiṣṭalakṣaṇaṃ prakāsa 7 saptamasaṃpūrṇam ida idaṃ pustakaṃ līkhītaṃ jyānīlakṣaṇamaṇaśrīpuramadhya saṃvat 1856 varṣa śāke 1620 mītī māghavadi 8 ravivāsare (fol. 18r9–v3)

Colophon of the root text

iti śrīkāsahṛada || ❁ || śrīsiṃhaśuriśiṣyasvenāṃvaramate || (fol. 18r6–7)
