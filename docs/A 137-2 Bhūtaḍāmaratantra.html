«Beginning»


❖ oṁ namaḥ śrībhūtadāṃbarāyaḥ (!)


samaṃnvā harttumābuddhā asekhādikṣusaṃsthitāḥ


rudrārudracaturbhujaṃ gurutanuṃ brahmādibhītipradaṃ


ghaṇṭāvajradharaṃ (ca)turbhujamayaṃ mudrāśriyāraṃkṛtaṃ


krodhasarvavidan namāmi śirasā śrībhūtanāthaṃ prabhuṃ


kṛṣṇāvānāgadharaśraturaṃgatanayataṃ devadevādhipaṃ (!)


oṁ maitrīkaruṇāmuditopekṣācaturbrahmavihārān bhāvayat


oṁ svabhāvaśuddhāsarvadharmānāṃ svabhāvaśuddho haṃ tataḥ karadvayaṃ akārena


caṃdramaṇḍaloapari jvāsākuraṃ nīrahuṃ kānadullāsaṃyutāṃjaliṃ vidhāya maṃtram ucārayat (!)


oṁ siddhivajra huṁ tadanu hṛdi śitadidhitāṃ maṇḍastharaktarakārastha


haritaviranāvalibhiraktavarṇaya rāsimaya sārayat (!) citayeta (!) (fol. 1v1–2r1)


«End»


oṁ āḥ huṁ hoḥ 3 vāsthajabhya kṣetrapālebhya (!) 3 vājataṃ dadāmi te cāgabhye saparivārā (!)


śrīghraṃmaṃdambaliṃ gṛhṇaṃtu khādaṃtu pibaṃtu yaḥ huṃ vaṃ hoḥ satṛptaṃ mama


sarvasatvānāṃ ca śāṃtipuṣṭirakṣāmbaranaguptiṃ kurvatu(!) huṁ 2 phat 2 ḍāmalavajradhara


ājñāpayati svāhā pra. yaṃ lā. ghaṃ. tu. ta śatākṣara kṣamāpanaṃ amṛtāśādhana(!) tata vaca oṁ sara


sara visara 2 gaccha 2 sarvadevatāna śrī vajradhara samājñāpayati svāhā kavaca krodhā hrīṁkāraṃ

jathā sukham viharatu oṁ sara 2 gaccha 2 sarvadevatā śrīvajradharasamājñāpayati oṁ muḥ svāhā


miriti visarjanam iti (fol. 14v2–15v2)


«Colophon»


iti śrībhūtaḍāmalamukhyā ṣyana(!) samāptaḥ bhu(!)taḍāmalasamadhisamāptaḥ


śubham (fol. 15v2–3)
