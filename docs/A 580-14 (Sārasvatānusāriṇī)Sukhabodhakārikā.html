Manuscript Features

Almost the whole Sanskrit text is accompanied by interlinear notes in Newari.
There are many scribal errors.
On fol. 1r there is a stamp of the Nepal Rastriya Pustalalaya and the words: sārasvatānusāriṇī sukhabodhakārikā.
