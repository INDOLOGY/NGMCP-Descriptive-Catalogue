Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ kālarātryaiḥ ||

nāsti nyāsādikaṃ tatraḥ (!) nāsti nirbandhanaṃ tathā

(2) punaścaryākriyā nāsti smaraṇāt pūjanāt phalam ||

maṃtrasyetasya māhātmyaṃ///(3)marthyaṃ stutim eva ca ||

vaktuṃ na śakyate devi kalpakoṭisatair (!) api || (exp. 3t,1–3)

End

pratyahaṃ prajapen maṃtraṃ muyutaṃ (!) dakṣiṇāmukham ||

satyaṃ satyaṃ bhaved devi saṃśayāsā (!) (2) vinaśyati ||

nyāsaṃ dehasya kurvvīta pūrvoktapaddhatau tatā (!)

atha maṃtra || ❖ oṃ (3) mrāṃ mrīṃ mruṃ mṛtyeśvari kṛto devattaṃ māraya kroṃ || ❁ ||  (exp. 30b1–3)

Colophon

iti śrīkālarā(4)trīkalpe īśvarapārvatīsaṃvāde mṛttināmai(!)dvādaśa (!) paṭala (!) || ❖ || ❖ || (exp. 30b3–4)
