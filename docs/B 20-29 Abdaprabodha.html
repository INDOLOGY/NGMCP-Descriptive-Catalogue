ābdaprabodhamudayajñamudā++++

svalpābhidheyav(!) iva

vipulābhidhāna bahusaṅgrahair ajātamudaḥ |

laghum alaghu vācyasaṅgra[ha]m

śrībhojadevanṛpasaṅgrahasarvasāraṃ

sārañ ca saṅgrahagaṇasya va[[rā]]hasāmyāt |

yogīśvarādibudhasādhumataṃ gṛhītvā

grantho yathāgamakṛto na vikalpanīyaḥ || (fol. 1v1–4)
