Beginning

❖ oṃ khuṃ śrīparamaśivaśaktiśrīśrīnātha aśeṣapāraṃparyyakramasvagurupādāṃbujaṃ yāvat praṇaumi ||

yā candre bhūtibhinno raviharisada(2)ne gotracandraiś ca rudrai

hṛnnābhau kandacakre tridahanakuhare dvādaiśaikena saṃsthā |

sṛṣṭis thairyāntakarttṛ trilakhakhayutāṃ sarvvatasvaikavṛttim

tāṃ(3) kālīṃ naumi nityaṃ paramapadamayīṃ satsadānandadātriṃ || (fol. 1v1–3)

... śrīvimalaprabodhoham ekadaṇḍī śivātmakaḥ (5)

saṃvīkṣya vidhivat jñātvā paṃcacāmaraśekharaṃ |

paṃcatriṃśatibhedākhyaṃ (!) vakṣye śrīkālikārccanaṃ | (fol. 1v4–5)
