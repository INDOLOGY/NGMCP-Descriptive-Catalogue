Text Features This text explains about aṣṭāṃgacikitsā is: śalyacikitsā, viṣacikitsā, bhūtacikitsā, rasāyanacikitsā, paṃcakarmacikitsā, and also methods to cure vāta pitta, kapha, pradara and prameha in 100 methods according to suśruta and caraka.

Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇēśāya namaḥ ||

kṛtsnasya tantrasya gṛhīta dhāmnaś cikitsataḥ prasṛtasya dūram ||(!)

vidagdhavaidyaṃ pratipūjitasya kariṣyate yogaśatasya bandhaḥ || 1 ||

kṛtsnasya nirataśeṣataṃtrasya caraka suśrutādi vaidyaśāstrasya gṛhītadhāmnā || atra dhāma śabdo yogavācī || yogāraṣadjhasamūhaāḥ (!) gṛhītaṃ dhāma yoga samūhāḥ tena gṛhīta dhāmnā || kena cikitsitā vaidyena vararucinā yogaśatasya baṃdha kariṣyate | kīṃbhūtasya prasṛtasya vikhyātasya kvadūraṃ nānādeśeṣu || (fol.1v1-5)

End

grahaṇīṃ ca hanyā cirajāṃ samūlaṃ ||

grahaṇī kapāṭa su (!) tu lokasidhaḥ || 2 ||

pathyā śuṃthī guṃḍa cānu |

palārddhaṃ bhakṣayetsadā ||

agni muṃḍavaṭī nāma sarvarogalulāṃtakā || 3 ||

bahuyogā hyanaṃtāś ca bahugraṃthodbhavān śubhān ||

śrīvatsenātra sakṛtān || 1 ||

bālāvabodhanārthāya | saṃkṣepeṇa subodhakā ||

bahubhyāsā sahānāṃ hi |

siṣyāṇāṃ (!) suṣa(!)hetave || 2 ||

nahuṣyo hamato loke budhaistya karakohyasau ||

yato ratnarṇavād ratnān || gṛhṇaṃti vibudhā api || ❁ || (fol.52v6–11)

Colophon

iti śrīvaidyakaśāstra yogasaṃgraha samāptaḥ || || (fol.52v11)
