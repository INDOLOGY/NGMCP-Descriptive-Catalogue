Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namoḥ(!) śrīgaṇeśāya || ||


kākacalitra khaṃ hlāsyaṃ tayā || ||

darkṣina svayāva hālasā(2) mabhiṃṅa khā ṅene māliva ||

naiṛtya soyāva hālasā || anega vastu naya dayūva ||

āgne(3) svayāva hālasā śatru nāśa juyiva ||

utra soyāva hālasā satiyā khaṃ ṅene dayiva ||

i(4)sāna soyāva hālasā siddhi khaṃ ṅene dayūva ||

bhusa coṅāva hārasā śatru dayūvaḥ || (fol. 1r1-4)


End

koṃ koṃ dhakaṃ hālasā, vā phasa vayūva ||

kāṃ kāṃ dhadhakaṃ hālasā aneka rābha dayīva (fol. 4r1) ||

bāhnisa hālasā rājāna prasāda biyiva ||

chesa coṅāva [[hāla]]sā samasta rābha dayiva || (2)

kauṃ kauṃ dhakaṃ hārasā tvane vastu dayūva ||

kāṃ kāṃ dhakaṃ hālasā suvarṇṇa thavatā dayūva || (fols. 3v4-r2)


Colophon

iti nandikeśvarāya kākacarītra kathā khaṃ samāptaḥ || || || śubha || (fol. 4r3)
