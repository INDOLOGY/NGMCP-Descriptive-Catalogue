Beginning

dhanvantaraye (!) namaḥ || ||

hariṃ śivaṃ vidhātāraṃ tatpatnīstu sutān gurun ||

namaḥ samastānpratyūha śāṃtaye maṃgalāya ca || 1 ||

ādau sarveṣu rogeṣu nāḍī jihvā ca mūtrakam ||

parīkṣayet svayaṃ vaidyas tad vidhānaṃ nigadyate || 2 ||

kare tu dakṣiṇe puṃsāṃ vāma haste tu yoṣitāṃ ||

prakoṣṭhe maṇibaṃdhādhaḥ sparśayet tribhir aṅgulaiḥ || 3 ||

śiva śaktyātmakatvena puruṣāṇāṃ ca yoṣitām ||

śivāṃśo dakṣiṇebhāge vāmabhāgas tu śaktijaḥ || 4 ||

tasmāt puṃṣāṃ dakṣahaste vāme strīṇāṃ parikṣayet ||

aṅgūṣṭhamūlamāśritya cāṅgulatraya mātrakam || 5 || (fol.1v1-4)

pretabādhārayodoṣo dhruvaṃ jñeyo vicakṣaṇaiḥ ||
