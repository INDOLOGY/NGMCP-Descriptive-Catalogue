Excerpts

Beginning of the Vyākaraṇa text

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ |

ārohaṇāvarohaṇabhedena dvividhaḥ kramaḥ || tatra mūlakāreṇāvarohaṇakramasya svīkārāt pūrvaṃ vākyasya sādhutvabodhakapaṃcasaṃdhiprakaraṇaṃ nirūpitaṃ (2) tataḥ vākyaghaṭitapadasya sādhutvabodhanasyāvaśyakatvena supas tat prakṛtikaprātipadikasya ca sādhutvabodhanaṃ kṛtam | (fol. 1r1–2)

Beginning of the Nyāya text

oṃ namo harāya (!) paritaḥ pratiyogyupalabdheḥ doṣād vā [i]tyādi didhitigraṃthasya (!) paritaḥ sarvāvayavāva[c]chedena prtiyogigrahātmakād doṣād vā ityarthaḥ na caivam api pratiyogigrahotpattidaśāyāṃ (2) tat pratyakṣaṃ durvāraṃ pratiyogyupalambhakasāmagryā api doṣatvopagamāt || (exp. 3t1–2)

End of the Nyāya text

yad avacchedenābhapratya(2)kṣaṃ tad avacchedena pratiyogy upalaṃbhakasāmagryā yogyānupalabdhi saṃpādakatayā āvaśyakatvena tad avacchedena pratiyogitāgrahasyāvaśyakatvād ityasmad gurucaraṇāḥ || ○ || (exp. 5t1–3)

End of the Vyākaraṇa text

nanu yadyapi māṇavaka agniṃ saṃyunaktu itī[c]chā tathāpi (9) svāmibhayena nivartate ca tathā ca bhṛtyavṛttipradhānībhūtavāraṇānukūlavyāpārāśrayībhūtabhṛtyavattinī tad vṛttivyāpāravācakavāridhātuvācyaphalāśrayatvaprakārakai[c]choddeśyatva- (fol. 7v8–9)
