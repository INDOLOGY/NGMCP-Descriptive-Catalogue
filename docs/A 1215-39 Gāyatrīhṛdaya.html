Beginning

śrīgurugaṇeśāya namaḥ ||   ||

atha gāyatrīhṛdayam ||

athāto vasiṣṭaḥ svāyaṃbhuvaṃ pṛcchati tāvad bhagavantaṃ gāyatrīṃ bho anubrūhīti || brahmovāca || brahmajñānotpat[t]iṃ prakṛtiṃ vyākhyāsyāma[ḥ] || ko mahāpuruṣa iti || tenāṅgulyā mathyamānāt salilam abhavat salilād budbudam abhavat || budbudāt phenam abhavat || phenād aṇḍam abhavat || aṇḍād brahmābhavat || brahmaṇo gni[[r a]]bhavat || agner vāyur abhavat || vāyor oṅkāro bhavat || oṃkārād aggāyatryam(!) abhavat || gāyatryāḥ sāvitry abhavat || sāvitryā sarasvaty abhavat || (fol. 1v1–2r2)

End

abhakṣabhakṣaṇāt pūto bhavati || apeyapānāt pūto bhavati || aspṛśyasparśanāt pūto bhavati || asaṃbhāsya(!)saṃbhāsyaṇāt(!) pūto bhavati || duḥpratigrahaṇāt pūto bhavati || abrahmacāri(!) brahmacāri(!) bhavati || paṃktisahaśrapāvano bhavati || sarvakratuvariṣṭaphalabhāg bhavati || aṣṭau brāhmaṇān grāhayitvā brahmaloke mahi(!)yate || gāyatrījapaphalabhāg bhavati ||   || (fol. 10r4–v4)

Colophon

iti gāyatrṛ(!)hṛdayaṃ samāptam śubham ||   ||

kaṣṭena liṣi(!)tam graṃthaṃ yatnena paripālaya⟨ḥ⟩ ||   || (fol. 10v4–7) <references/>
