Excerpts

Beginning

⟪śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ⟫ || || 12 || o vā vṛṣarāśikam || oṃ klīṃ śrīṃ klīṃ hrīṃ brahmasvarūpāya namaḥ || brahmasvarūpamaṃtraḥ || 13 || oṃ gopālāya śrīṃ gocarāya vaṃśa śabdanamo namaḥ | vaṃśagopālamaṃtraḥ || 14 || (fol. 1v1-2)

End

iti śikṣāhomamaṃtraḥ || oṃ rudravaṃtī mātāśikṣācchedaṃ karomy aham | iti śikṣācchedanamaṃtraḥ || oṃ graṃ graṃ graṃ śāṃtiṃ klīṃ | vraṃ vraṃ vraṃ śrīmahāpuruṣasvāmine namaḥ | vramidayāya namaḥ svāhā | iti kiṃ vyāghramaṃtra āsanamaṃtraḥ || || (fol. 2v3-4)

Colophon

iti śrīsanakasanandanasanatkumāraviracitam ādiśāstrasiddhānte ṛṣīśvaramatam || (fol. 2v4-5)
