Beginning

śrīgaṇapataye namaḥ || ||

svasvābhilāṣaṃ na hi labdhum īśā

nirvighnam īśānamukhāḥ suraughāḥ ||

vināprasādaṃ kila yasya naumi

taṃ ḍhuṃḍhirājaṃ matilābhahetoḥ (2) || 1 ||

jātakoditadaśāphalaṃ yataḥ

sthūlakālaphaladaṃ sphuṭaṃ nṛṇāṃ ||

tatra na sphusrati daivavin matis

tadvruvebdaphalam āditājakāt || 2 || (fol. 1v1–2)

End

tadātmajo 'naṃtaguṇosty anaṃto

yo dhuk sad uktiḥ kila kāmadhenuṃ ||

svarūpayad duṣṭamataṃ nirasya || 52 ||

pa(9)dyāṃvayā sā vitato vipaścit

śrīnīlakaṇṭhaḥ śrutiśāstraniṣṭṣhaḥ ||
