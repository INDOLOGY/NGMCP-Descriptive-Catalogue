Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrībhṛṅgeśvarāya || ❁ ||

yad yogibhir bbhavabhayārttivināśayogyam
āsādya vanditam atīvaviri(!)kracittaiḥ |
tadvat punātu haripādasarojayugmam
āvirbbhavat kramavilaṅghita bhūr bhuvaḥ svaḥ ||

pāyāt savaḥ sakalakalmaṣabhedadakṣaḥ
kṣīrodakukṣiphaṇibhoganiviṣṭamūrttiḥ |
śvāsāvadhūtasalilot kalikākarālaḥ
sinndhusyu(!) nṛtyam iva yasya karoti saṅgāt || (fol. 1v1–2)

End

na cālayej jalaṃ dviṣṭhāṃ dhīrahīnāṃ tathā striyaṃ |
devatā pitṛsachāstra(!) yabdi(!) saṃtrādintakaiḥ ||

kṛtvā tu sparśanālāpaṃ śuddhaye tārkkāvalokanāt |
avalokya tathodakyā manhyajaṃ patitaṃ śavaṃ ||

vidharmmisūtikākhaṇḍa(!) vivastrāntyārasāyinaḥ |
mṛtaniryyātakāś caiva paradāra ... ||    || (fol. 73v6–7)

Colophon

iti śrīmārkkaṇḍeyapurāṇe pakṣiṇām utpanno nāma dviti(!)yo dhyāya || 2 || (fol. 5v3)
