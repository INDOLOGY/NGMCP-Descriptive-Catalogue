Excerpts

«Beginning»

śrīnātha uvāca ||

kiṃ kulaṃ kiṃ bhavet sailaṃ kathaṃ tasyaiva lakṣaṇaṃ |
kathayasva prasādena yady ahaṃ tava valabhā ||

śrīcakr(!)ovāca ||

ataḥ paraṃ pravakṣyāmi na ca kaulaṃ kulāṣṭakaṃ |
yena vijñātamātreṇa bhuktimukti prajāyate ||

ni⟪ra⟫rmalaṃ vīrja(!) śuddhaṃ hi niprapaṃca(!) sa sarvvagaṃ |
bhāvābhāvavinimuktaṃ(!) vaṃde kaulakulorjitaṃ | (fol. 1r1-1v1)


«End»

tatra madhye guru tiṣṭhed gurusthāna sa ucyate |
ādyaṃte praṇavāḥ paṃcamahāsthānaṃ ca madhyataḥ ||

mūlaṃ sūkṣmaṃ paraṃ hrasvaplutaṃ paṃkti krameśvaram |
tanmadhye śrīgurusthāna bhuktimuktiphalapradaṃ ||

gurur vvodyasya ye bhedāḥ vṛddhakaumālabālakāḥ |
unmanā bhṛgu saṃjātaṃ | samānānakuleśvaraṃ ||

vyādhiṃ nyā (fol. 14r4-14v4)
