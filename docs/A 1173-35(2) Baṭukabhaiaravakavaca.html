Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya nama (!) ||

śrībaṭūkabhairavebho (!) namaḥ ||

devy uvāca ||

bhagavan sarva[ta]tvajña devānāṃ priyakārakaṃ ||

bhairavaṃ kavacaṃ brūhiḥ yadi cāstīkṛpā mayi ||

prānatyāgaṃ(!) kariṣyāmi yadi no kathyase vit vame (!) ||

saṃte(!) satyaṃ puṇa(!) satyaṃḥ(!) satyam meva na saṃśaya ||(!)

īti(!) daityā(!) vacaḥ śrutvāḥ vihasan parameśvara || (!)

bhairavaṃ kavaca prāha catuvarggaphalapradaṃ (!) ||

śrī īśvarruvācaḥ (!) ||

kavacaṃ baṭukakhyasya śṛṇu mat prānavallabhe ||

caṃdrikātaṃtrasarvvasvaṃḥ (!) baṭūko yaṃ visyaṣata (!) ||

tatra maṃtrādyakṣarantuḥ vāsudevasvarūpaka ||

varṇya dvitiyo brahmaiva baṭukaścadraśe ʼṣara (!) ||

caturvvargasya siddhyarthaḥ āpaduddhāraṇāya ca ||

kuru dvayaṃ mahaśāniḥ (!) mohanaṃ parikīrttita (!)

vatukāya maheśāniḥ (!) bhuvanaṃ parikīrttitam ||

lajjābījaṃ mahāvidyā muktidā parikīrttitā || 1 || (exp.5b2:6a5)

End

īdaṃ kavacamajñatvāḥ(!) yo jaṭodvaṭukaṃ ṇara (!) || ||

na prāpnoti phalaṃ syāsyaḥ paramaṃ dukhamāpnuyāt (!) ||

manvantaratrayaṃ sthitvāḥ tiryyagyoniṣu jāyate || ||

īha loke mahārōgīḥ drāridryeṇāti piḍitaḥ ||

śatruṇāṃ vaśago bhutvāḥ karapātraṃ bhaveṃ naraḥ ||

yaḥ parikṣya pradātā vaiḥ sāstiddhi (!) labhate naraḥ ||

āyur ścityā yaśo dharmmaṃḥ varaṃ mokṣa na saṃśayaḥ || || (exp.7b5:8a1)

Colophon

īti śrībaṭukabhairavakavacaṃ saptaṃḥ (!) || || (exp.8a2)
