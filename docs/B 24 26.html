Manuscript Features

In the middle of folio 1v and the middle of folio 2r, next to the binding hole, there are two sketches of some Buddhist deities.

On the front cover-leaf is written: 

   "vi.saṃ"14

 "patra." 24

  "paramārthanāmasaṅgītiḥ"

   ("patrāṇi" 1–24)


After this text proper, here is one folio of some unidentified Buddhist text on exps. 26–27.

Excerpts

«Beginning»

❖ namo mañjunāthāya ||

atha vajradharaḥ śrīmān durdāntadamakaḥ paraḥ |

trilokavijayī vīro guhyarāṭ kuliśeśvaraḥ ||

vibuddhapuṇḍarīkākṣaḥ protphullakamalānanaḥ |

prollālayan vajravaraṃ svakareṇa muhur mmuhuḥ ||

bhṛkuṭitaraṅgapramukhair anantair vajrapāṇibhiḥ |

durdāntadamakair vīrai(!) vīrabīhatsarūpibhiḥ || (fol. 1v1–4)


«End»

jagataś cāsya nāthasya bimukttiphalakāṃkṣiṇaḥ |

śreyo mārgo viśuddhoʼ yaṃ māyājālena yoditaḥ (!) ||

gambhīrodāravaipulyamahārtho jagadarthakṛt |

buddhānāṃ viṣayo hy eṣa samyak sambuddhadeśita iti || upasaṃhāraḥ || || (24r1–3)


«Colophon»

āryamāyājālāt ṣoḍaśasāhaśrikāt mahāyogatantrāntaḥpātisamādhijālapaṭalād bhagavattathāgataśākyamunibhāṣitā bhagavato mañjuśrijñānasatvasya paramārthā nāmasaṅgītiḥ parisamāptā || ❁ || 

      ye dharmā hetuprabhavā 

              hetun teṣāṃ tathāgato

      hy avadat teṣāñ ca yo nirodha 

               evaṃvādī mahāśramaṇaḥ ||  śubham astu  || ❁  || (fol.24r3–5)
