Excerpts

Beginning

śrīkālikā jayatu || ||

śrīśaṃkara uvāca ||

kathito ʼyaṃ mahāmaṃtraḥ sarvamaṃtrottamottamaḥ ||

yam āsādya mayā prāptam aiśvaryapadam uttamaṃ || 1 ||


saṃyuktaḥ parayā bhaktyā yathoktavidhinā bhavān ||

kurutām arcanaṃ devyās trailokyavijigīṣayā || 2 || ||


paraśurāma uvāca ||


prasanno yadi me deva parameśa purātana ||

rahasyaṃ parayā devyā kṛpayā kathaya prabho || 3 || (fol. 1v1–5)


End

dukhito ʼpi sukhī nityaṃ daridro dhanam āpnuyāt ||

mūrkho ʼpi labhate vāṇīm anargalavijṛṃbhanī || 15 ||


acirāj jāyate siddhi †vādu† kāṃcanakhecarī ||

aṣṭamyām arddharājtre tu paṭhed aṣṭottaraṃ śatam || 16 ||


tadā ṣaṭkarmasaṃsiddhir jāyate lokasuṃdarī ||

sabhāyāṃ vyasane caiva saṃgrāme saṃkaṭe tathā || 17 ||

mahābhaye samutpanne mahāvaṃdhavipattiṣu ||

paṭhet stotraṃ hṛdi dhyātvā kālikāṃ sādhakottamaḥ || 18 ||


te sarve nāśam āyāṃti mahādevavaco yathā || 19 || || (fol. 21r4–21v2)


Colophon

iti śrīkālīkūlasarvasve śrīmaddakṣīṇakālyaṣṭottaraśatanāmastotraṃ samāptam || || śubham ||(fol. 21v2–3)
