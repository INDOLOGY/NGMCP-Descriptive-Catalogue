Description of the maṇḍala

Ṣaṭkoṇa with trikoṇa at the centre
Names of 8 mātṛkās in first circle
Names of 24  ? (śṛṣṭi, puṣti etc.) in second circle
Names of 72 ? (vi, ka, kṣe etc.) in third circle
Names in four directions out of 8 quarters:

east south west north
vajra daṇḍa dhārā gadā
indra yama varuṇa kuvera
asitāṃgabhaira rurubhairava caṇḍabhairava krodhanabhaira
unmattabhairava ānanda kapālī bhīṣaṇabhaira
saṃhārabhairava

(exp. 8b)
