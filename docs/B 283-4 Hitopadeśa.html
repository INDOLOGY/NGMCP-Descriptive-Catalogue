Excerpts

Beginning

///ṣanmāsābhyantareṇa(!) tava putrān nītiśāstrābhijñān ahaṃ karomi |

rājā savinayaṃ punar uvāca ||

kīṭopi sumanaḥsaṃgād ārohati satāṃ śiraḥ |

aśmāpi devatāṃ yāti, mahadbhiḥ supratiṣṭhitaḥ ||

anyac ca ||

yathodayagirau dravyaṃ, ravisaṃgeṇa dīvyate |

tathā satsannikarṣeṇa hīnavarṇṇopi dīpyate ||

tad eteṣām asmatputrāṇām adhunā bhavantaḥ pramāṇam ity utvā tasya

viṣṇuśarmmaṇḥ sabahumānapurassaraṃ rājaputrān samarthitavān ||

(fol. 4r1–5 )

End

anya ------ ḥ candramauliḥ sa yāvat

yāval lakṣmīmurāre(!) jjalada iva talir(!)mmānase visphurantī |

yāvat svarṇṇācaloyaṃ davadahanasamesya(!) sūryaḥ sphuli[[ga]]s

tāvan nārāyaṇena pracaratu satataṃ samgraho yaṃ kathānāṃ ||

aparaṃca ||

śrīmān kīrtt candrosau jīyān māṇḍaliko ripūn |

---------- yitvā pracāritaḥ || ||

(fol. 105r7–105v3 )

iti hitopadeśe sandhir nnāma caturthaḥ saṅgrahaḥ samāptaḥ || ||
