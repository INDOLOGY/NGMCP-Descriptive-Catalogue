Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namaḥ samyaksaṃbuddhāya ||

buddhadharmma ca saṃghañ ca triratnāgram anuttaraṃ |

praṇamya sahasovāca mañjuśrīkarūṇa(!)tmakaḥ ||

saṃsārābdhimahāghore nimagnās sarvajantavaḥ ||

tārayasva kathaṃ nātha darśayasva mahāmune || (fol. 1r1–2)

End

anena sādhitaṃ gavyaṃ indrasya hi ivopamaṃ ||

maṃtrato jāpayaṃ gavyaṃ sarvapāpavināśakaṃ |

garbbhapravaṇamātreṇa pāpā gacchanti nāśanaṃ ||

varṇṇānurūpabhedena pañcagavyaṃ pradāpayet ||

saṃjapato mantram āha ||

tad yathā || oṃ oṃ oṃ amoghapariśodhanarājāya tathāgatāryāhaṃta samyaksambuddhaya || tad yathā || oṃ śodhaya 2 samanteradhiri 2 śuddhasatvamahāpadme huṃ huṃ huṃ phaṭ svāhā || || (fol. 21v5–22r1)

Colophon

iti sarvatathāgatoktadvādaśa sahasrikāyāṃ pārājikāyāṃ vinayasūttroddhṛtaśrīmūnindramukhakamalavinirggataṃ pāpaparimocano nāma nirddeśaḥ paṭala samapta || ❖ ||

śrīman nepālasambat caturanavanava māsakṛṣṇe ca āśvina tat kṛṣṇāyāṃ ṣaṣṭhitithau paridivaśivabhaṃ siddhiyogaś ca ghasro tat ghasro vāra śukre ravinivasa tulārāśi mithune candra tat divase lekhya pūrṇṇaṃ paribhavati śuddhā śuddhā jānāti likhyaṃ || || suramye lalitāpuryyāṃ śrīmānigalasaṃjñake || jaśodhanākhyaṃ vihārāsya lekhyaṃ śrīvajradhareṇa yā likhitapustaka saṃti tādṛṣṭa likhitaṃ mayā śuddhāśuddhaṃ na jānāti śodhanīya mahadbudhaiḥ || || śubham astu sarvadā maṅgalaṃ || || (fol. 22r1–7)
