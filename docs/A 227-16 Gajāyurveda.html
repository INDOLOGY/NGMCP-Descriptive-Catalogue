Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ

tatrādau gajapraśaṃsā ucyate

viṣṇudharmottare

puṣkara uvāca

kuñjarāḥ paramā śobhā śivirasya balasya ca

āyataḥ kuñjareṣveva vijayaḥ pṛthivīkṣitāṃ (!)

śiviraṃ gṛhaṃ teṣāṃ samarjane yalaṃ phalameva bhṛgūttamaḥ (!)

yathāvannṛpatiḥ kuryāt gaṃdharvā kuṃjarā matāḥ

tāvaṃtaś ca tathā dhāryā yāvaṃ (!) tāṃ poṣaṇaṃ sukhaṃ

kartuśakyaṃ na dhāryās te kṣudhitāḥ duḥkhitās tathā (fol. 1v1–4)

End

tanuvaṃśodaraś caiva tanuvyakta tanūruhaḥ

dīrghajihvā viṣāṇaś ca dīrghagātras tathoravaḥ (!)

pradhvastaromakarṇatvak parastāc cāpi saṃvṛtaḥ

nipābhe gocaras thānaṃ śayyāyāṃ cāpya nirvṛttaḥ

rupābhibhavasaṃkī ca nityam evān avasthitaḥ

hrasvaśravaṇalāṃgūlo dīrghaṃ vikramate–(fol. 16v7–9)
