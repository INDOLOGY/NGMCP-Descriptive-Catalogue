Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namo ratnatrayāya ||

namo daśadiganantāparyyaṃtalokadhātupratiṣṭhitasarvabuddhabodhisatvāryaśrāvakapratyekabuddhebhyotītānāgatapratyutpannebhyaḥ || ||

suravarārthanayā muninā svayaṃ

sucaritaṃ pṛthudarśanidarśitaṃ ||

lalitavistaraṇā(!)makam uttamaṃ

śṛṇuta sūtram idaṃ tad ihādarāt || ||

evam mayā śrutam ekasmin samaye bhagavān śrāvastyāṃ viharati sma || jetavane anāthapiṇḍadasyārāme mahātā bhikṣusaṃghena sārdhaṃ dvādaśabhir bhikṣusahasraiḥ || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

idam avocad bhagavān āttamanās te maheśvaradavaputrapū[rvaṃ]gamāḥ śuddhāvāsakāyikadevaputrā maitreyapūrvaṃgamā sarvabodhisattvāmahāsatvāmahākāśyapūrvaṃgamāś ca sarvamahāśrāvakāḥ sarvadevamānuṣāsuragandharvaś ca loko bhagavato bhāṣitam abhyanandann iti || (fol. 252r4–6)

Colophon

nigamaparivartto nāma saptā(!)viṃśatimaḥ(!) || || samāptā(!)cedaṃ lalitavistaramahāyānasūtraṃ sarvabodhisattvacaryāprasthānam iti || || ye dharmā ityādi || || śubhaṃ || || granṭha 6000 (dyā) 100 (fol. 252r6–7)
