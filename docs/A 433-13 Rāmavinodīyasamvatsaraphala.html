Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||


yo nirguṇo guṇamayaṃ vitanoti viśvaṃ

tāpatrayaṃ harati yas tapanomy ajasram ||

kālātmako jagati jīvayate ca jaṃtuṃ

brahmāṇḍasaṃpuṭamaṇiṃ dyumaṇiṃ tam īḍe || 1 ||


yā tābdā yamavarjitā naga7guṇā śūnyāṃvarāgo‥ 600ddhṛtā

bhāḍhyaṃ labdhamitābdanetradahanāḍhyaṃ sābdaśākrendutaḥ ||

digbhāgāptakalāyutaṃ prabhavato ʼbdāḥ ṣaṣṭhiśeṣāḥ smṛtāḥ

śeṣāṃśā ravibhir hatā dinamukhaṃ meṣārkataḥ prāg bhavet || (fol. 1v1–5)
