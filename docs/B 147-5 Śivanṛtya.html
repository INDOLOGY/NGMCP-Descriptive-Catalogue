Title Śivanṛtyacarāṅka

Remarks ascribed to the Nagendraprayāṇa, which itself is regarded as a part of the Śivatāṇḍavatantra; in the Preliminary Title List, the title was given as Śivanṛtya.

Subject Śaiva Tantra

Language Sanskrit

Manuscript Details

Script Newari

Material paper

State incomplete, available folios: 17–50 and 52–57; the upper middle part of fol. 39r is slightly damaged;

Size 30.0 x 12.5 cm

Folios 40

Lines per Folio 8

Foliation figures on the verso, in the lower right-hand margin under the half title tāṇḍava, while the word śiva has been written in the upper left-hand margin; on the verso of the last folio, in the upper left-hand corner, is written śiva tāṇḍa taṃtraṃ.

Place of Deposit NAK

Accession No. 5/5084 (NAK viṣayasaṃkhyā: Śaivatantra 1478)

Manuscript Features

The date VS 1478 is written on the cover-leaf, but it is not clear whether this is the date of copying.
