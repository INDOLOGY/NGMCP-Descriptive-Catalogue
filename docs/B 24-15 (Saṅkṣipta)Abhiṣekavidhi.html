«Beginning»

❖ namaḥ śrīvajrasatvāya ||

udakamukūṭavajrārāvidyaṇḍo sanāma

trikulasamayabhavyācāryaguhye turādyaiḥ |

navabhir amitapuṇyair yābhiṣekaprakārair

janayati bhavauddhiṃ tāriṇīn tān namāmi ||

jānāti saṃkṣiptarucir nirūdyamo

vihīnam evaḥ(!) kalikālasaṅgamāt |

ato⌠ʼ⌡bhiṣekakrama eṣa vistaram

bihā⌠ra⌡vāgīśvarakīrtinocyate ||

na tīrthyāya na mūrkhāya na śuṣkatarkaratāya ca |

na śrāvakāya na ṣaṇḍhāya na vṛddhāyārbhāya ca ||

na rājñe⌠ʼ⌡pi na putrāya na śraddhārahitāya ca |

(saptāṣṭamau) pradātavyau śāsane hitam icchatā || (fol. 1v1–3)


«End»

rūpādisarvvapadārthānāṃ svacittapratibhāsamātrapratipattau svapnāṅganāpratibhāsābhinnam aṅgavajrādisvābhā⌠va⌡rūpapariṇatāyāṃ karmmamudrāyāṃ māyopamatayā viśuddhāyāṃ niravadyābhiṣekalābhe bhikṣūṇāṃ satvara (bhrasmo) bhavatīti caturthābhiṣekavidhiḥ ||

śaṃśyendukundakumudāmbujakātihāri (!)

haṃ sthiptam (!) ekavacanan mama saṃprasūtam |

(yanye śā svasvarasamajāpadasvatena)

prajñāravindabhavavajradharasvabhāvaṃ || (fol. 6v6–7r2)

iti saṃkṣiptābhiṣekavidhiḥ samājikaḥ samāptaḥ || ❁ || kṛtir iyam mahāpaṇḍitācārya ….dānām iti || granthasya pramāṇaṃ śloka 170 || śrīruda(da)sya vijarājyaiḥ(!) || sakalasatvārthahetu(!)nām || (fol. 7r2–3)
