Excerpts

Beginning

svasti śrīsahasrāṃśave namaḥ ||

yo nirguṇo guṇamayo vitanoti viśvaṃ
tāpatrayaṃ harati yas tapano py ajasraṃ ||
kālātmakotprāti (!) jīvayate ca jaṃtūn
bra(2)hmāṃḍasaṃpuṭamaṇiṃ dyumaṇiṃ tam īḍe ||

jayaṃti saṃtaḥ kuśalaṃ prajānāṃ
namo muṃnīdrāya (!) surāḥ smṛtāḥ stha ||
smṛtāsi vāgdevi dayasva mātar
vidhehi vighnā(3)padhimaṃgalāni (!) || 1 ||

alaukikajñād amaraḥ svakoṣe
na yāni nāmāni samullilekha ||
vilokya tair apy adhunā pracāram
ayaṃ prayatnaḥ puruṣotamasya (!) || 2 ||

(4) vargakramas tathā nāmaliṃgayoḥ sūpadeśatā ||
paribhāṣādikaṃ sarvam atrāpy amarakośavat || 3 || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

karaṇe lyuṭ triṣu yathā tasya vyākhyāna (!) (9) ity adaḥ ||
sūtrakṛd bhāṣyakṛt kophagavanumāta (!) itīcchati ||

iti liṃgādi saṃgrahavergaḥ (!) ||

dṛṣṭaprayogā ye śabdāḥ prāyas ta iha kīrtitāḥ
aprayuktās tūnya(10)linyādidṛṣṭā (!) apy upekṣitāḥ || (fol. 37r8–10)

Colophon

iti śrīpuruṣottamadevaviracitas trikāṃḍaśeṣābhidhānakośagraṃthaḥ samaptaḥ (!) ||

śrīraviḥ prīyatāṃ ||

nabhasi bahu(37v1)lapakṣe vāsare sūryasūno (!)
sarajvananatithau (!) śrīviśvanāthopakaṃṭho (!) ||
likhitam idam apūrvaṃ kośakāṃḍatriśeṣaṃ
muninamivaryyāḥ svāmināmtaḥ sute(2)na || 1 ||

likhita iha mahārtho yas trikāṃḍyāstuśeṣo (!) ||

saṃvat 1863 vaiśākhamāse kṛṣṇapakṣe 13 rāma rāma rāma śrīrāma rāmaḥ (fol. 37r10–37v2)
