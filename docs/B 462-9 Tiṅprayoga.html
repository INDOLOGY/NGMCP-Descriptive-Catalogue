Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

atha tiṅprayogam abhidhāṣyāmaḥ ||

tiṅāṃ ca navapūrvārddhaṃ lādeśāv aparau dvidhāḥ

parasmaipadam iti ko rthaḥ || tip, tas, jhi, || sip, thas, tha, || mip, vas, mas || ta, ātāṃ, jha, || thās, āthāṃ, dhvam, || iḍ, vahi, mahiṅ iti lādeśās tiṅo veditavyā[ḥ] || (fol. 1v1–3)
