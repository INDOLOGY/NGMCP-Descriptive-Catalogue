Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

oṃ asya śrīajapāgāyatrīmaṃtrasya brahmaṛṣir avyaktagāyatrīchandaparamahaṃso devatā haṃ bijaṃ saḥ śaktiḥ sohaṃ kīlakaṃ gaṇeśabrahmāviṣṇumahārudrajīvātmaguruparamātmabhyo brahmaṇo mokṣārthe viniyogaḥ ||

oṃ haṃ haṃsāya vidmahe paramahaṃsāya dhīmahi sohaṃ haṃsaḥ pracodayāt ||
hakāreṇa bahir yāti sakāreṇa viset punaḥ ||
haṃsa ittyeva maṃtraṃ ca jīvo japati sarvadā ||
asya saṃkalpamātreṇa punarjanmo na vidyate ||

atha sūryodayam ārabhya sūryodayaparyantocchvāsaniśvāsāttmakamajapāgāyatryāḥ ṣaṭśatottaraikaviṃśatisahasraṃ ajapāgāyatrīmaṃtram ahorātrayo⟨ḥ⟩r ahaṃ kariṣye || asya śrī ajapāgāyatryāḥ

ṣaṭśataṃ tu gaṇeśasya ṣaṭsahasraṃ prajāpateḥ
ṣaṭsahasraṃ gadāpāṇeḥ ṣaṭsahasraṃ pinākinaḥ ||
sahasram ātmanaś caiva guroś caiva sahasrakam ||
paramātmanaḥ sahasraṃ ca yathāvidhi nivedayet || (fol. 1v1–3r2)

End

ākāśaṃ biṃdusaṃyuktaṃ bhṛgusargasamanvitaḥ |
maṃtrarājas tv ayaṃ devi sarvasiddhiphalaprada || 16 ||

sarvakāleṣu siddho sau sarvamaṃtreṣu durllabhaḥ |
yojanātiviśeṣeṇa mucyate janmabaṃdhanāt || 17 ||

ity etat kavacaṃ devi kathitaṃ devadurllabhaṃ ||
gopanīya⟨ṃ⟩m idaṃ bhadre prāṇabuddhiprayatnataḥ || 18 ||

yaḥ paṭhed bhaktibhāvajñaḥ sarvakāleṣu sarvadā ||
na tasya duḥkhasaṃghātaṃ yamasyāpi kuto bhayam || 19 ||

prātaḥ kāle snānakāle pūjākāle viśeṣataḥ ||
aṇimādir bhavet tasya maṃtrasiddhi na saṃśayaḥ || 20 ||

aśucir vā śucir vāpi gacchat bhuñjāna eva vā ||
manasā yaḥ smared yas tu mucyate bhavasāgarāt || 21 || (fol. 6v1–7v1)

Colophon

iti śrīrudrayāmale haragaurīsaṃvāde parārddhe paramahaṃsakavacaṃ saṃpūrṇaṃ samāptam śubham (fol. 7v1–3)
