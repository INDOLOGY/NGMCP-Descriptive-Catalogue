Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

oṃ asya śrīpaṃcamīstarājamaṃtrasya dakṣiṇāmūrttir ṛṣiḥr anuṣṭubpchaṃdaḥ śrīmattripurasuṃdarīdevatā śrīṃ bījaṃ hrīṃ śaktiḥ mama caturvidhapuruṣārthasiddhyarthaṃ jape viniyogaḥ ||

mūlena ṣaḍaṃgaṃ kṛtvā

guruṃ gaṇapatiṃ durgāṃ baṭu[[kaṃ]] śivam acyutaṃ

brahmāṇaṃ girijāṃ lakṣmīṃ vāṇīṃ vaṃde vibhūtaye || 1 ||

hetave majjatām eva saṃsārārṇavasetave ||

prabhave sarvavidyānāṃ śaṃbhave gurave namaḥ || 2 || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

ākāśe suprakāśe sakalaguṇamayīṃ suṃdarīṃ raktanetrāṃ

nityāṃ padmāsanaschāṃ (!) taruṇaravinibhāṃ raktacaṃdraprabhābhāṃ ||

ātmānaṃ sarvabhāvaiḥ sakalaguṇayutāṃ sarvadā yo hi paśyet

sākṣād devaḥ śivo vā madanadahanakṛt khecaro jāyate saḥ || 175 || (fol. 10r5–7)

Colophon

iti śrīrudrayāmalamahātaṃtre śivaśaktisaṃvāde paṃcamīstavarājaḥ samāptaḥ || ||

yadakṣarapadabhraṣṭam mātrāhīnaṃ ca yad bhavet ||

tatsarvaṃ kṣamyatāṃ devi prasīda parameśvari || 1 ||

bhūmau skhalitapādānāṃ bhūmir evāvalaṃvanaṃ ||

tvayi jātāparādhānāṃ tvam eva śaraṇaṃ śive || 2 ||

yad atra pāṭhe jagadaṃbike mayā

visargaviṃdvakṣarahīnam īritaṃ ||

tad astu saṃpūrṇaphalaṃ prasādataḥ

saṃkalpasiddhis tu sadaiva jāyatāṃ || 3 ||

śubham astu || || śrīr astu || ❁ || ❁ || ❁ || ❁ || ❁ || ❁ || (fol. 10r5–10v4)
