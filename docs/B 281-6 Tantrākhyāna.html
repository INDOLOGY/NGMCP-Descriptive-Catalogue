Beginning

❖…… namaḥ sarvvajñāya ||

tatrākhyāna kathāṃ caiva, buddhipadma pravedhinī |

ādityadīdhitiṃ medaṃ tā nivvo(2)dhāsyāmi yatnataḥ ||

tatākhyāna dhāyā khaṃ thva, buddhi hātaṅā pele hoyakava ādityasa kilanava uthiṃga (3) thva khaṃ chu yatnanaṃ dhararape māla ||

End

rātrau svasti dine svasti svasti madhye dine sthite |

svasti bhūyad bhave (3) nityaṃ svastī sarvvatra sarvvadā ||

thva traṃtrākhyāna ṅēgayā naṃ hlākayā naṃ thva ta..tva dhararapuyāna rātrisa naṃ dinasa noṃ ma(4)dhyānasa noṃ kalyāna juroṃ sarvvadā kālaṃ śubhanaṃgala(!) , kalyāna thajura ||

(fol. 33r2-4)

Colophon

iti taṃtrākhyāna samāptaṃ ||     || śubhamastu ||
