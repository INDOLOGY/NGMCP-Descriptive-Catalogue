Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||     ||

atha pitṛgītā likhyate ||     ||

ṛṣaya ūcu[ḥ] ||

bhajanaṃ nidhirūpaṃ te pitṛbhaktividhiṃ mudā ||

ṛṣayaḥ paripapraccha vyāsaṃ dharmārthakovidam || 1 ||
