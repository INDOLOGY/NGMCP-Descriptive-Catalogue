Excerpts

Beginning

namāmi sadguru (!) śāṃtaṃ prasannamukhapaṃkajaṃ ||

vāmoruśaktisaṃyuktaṃ jñānabuddhiprakāśakaṃ || 1 ||

gajavaktra (!) sadā naumi raktābhaṃ vighnanāyakaṃ ||

yasya smaraṇamātreṇa sādhakaḥ siddhim āpnuyāt || 2 ||

śrībhairava uvāca ||
