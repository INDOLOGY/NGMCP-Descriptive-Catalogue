śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

tataḥ karttā ||

hāṁ ātmatattvāya svāhā || hrīṁ vidyātattvāya svāhā || hrūṁ śivatattvāya svāhā || heṁ sarvatattvāya namaḥ || iti karaprakṣālanam || ityācamya ||


āsanaṃ spṛṣṭvā || āsanam abhimantrayet ||


oṁ pṛthvītvayeti mantrasya merupṛṣṭhaṛṣiḥ sutalaṃ chandaḥ kūrmo devatā āsanābhimantraṇe

viniyogaḥ ||

oṃ pṛtvī tvayā dhṛtā lokā devi tvaṃ viṣṇunā dhṛtā ||

tvā ca dhāraya māṃ nityaṃ pavitraṃ kuru cāsanam ||

iti prārthya || tato haste jalaṃ gṛhītvā ||

oṃ asya śrīmahāmṛtyuñjayamantrasya vāmadevakaholavśiṣṭhā ṛṣayaḥ

paṃktigāyatryanuṣṭupchaṃdāṃsi śrīsadāśivamahāmṛtyuñjaya rudro devatā

hrīṁ śaktiḥ śrīṁ bījaṁ śrī mahāmṛtyuñjaya rudradevatā prītidvārā mama mama

yajamānasya ca abhīṣṭa siddhaye jape home viniyogaḥ || (fol. 1v1–2r1)

atha mūlamantram ||

oṁ hrauṁ oṁ jūṁ saḥ bhurbhuvaḥ svaḥ trayaṃbakaṃ yajāmahe sugandhiṃ puṣṭibarddhanam || ūrvvārukam iva bandhanān mṛtyor mukṣīya mām mṛtāt || bhurbhuvaḥ svarom jūṁ saḥ hrauṁ oṃ iti mūlamantraḥ ||

haste jalaṃ gṛhītvā

oṁ guhyātiguhyagoptrī tvaṃ gṛhāṇāsmat kṛtaṃ japam ||

siddhir bhavatu me deva tvatprasādān maheśvara ||

iti jalaṃ utsṛjya || prārthayet ||

janmamṛtyujarārogaiḥ pīḍitaṃ janmabandhanaiḥ || 1 ||
