Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ nama śrīnṛtyanāthāya namaḥ ||    ||
sunya me ||    ||
gaurī || ja ||
kapūrapu me(8)sa osa ||    ||
praveśa || mahādeva, jama ||    ||
vasanta goḍagiri || e ||
sarīra(9)sa lepana vibhūti rasatāo
ābharaṇa nāgana kosana ṅacāo || (exp. 11b7–9)

End

|| maṃgala || jati ||
śivadisti sivasatura, asatara munina sahita
nepā(8)ra saṃvatachara ||
saṃsāra ati athira manasa tayāo,
kāratika bhagata bhā(9)va dayāva ||
gopinātha varanana piriti kārana
ekādasi mahimāna (17t1) tavara kāsana ||
siddhinarasiṃhamalla sahita sabhāna
maṃgalamaya prasa(2)na juya kṛpāna ||    ||    || (exps. 16b7–17t2)

Colophon

iti ekādasivratanātaka samāptaḥ ||    || (exp. 17t2)
