Beginning

|| śrīgaṇēśāya namaḥ ||

yasyai namo parayati yasa guṇāḥ samagrā (!)

nārāyaṇopaniṣadā yadupāsanoܠ㄀ā ||

yo naḥ pracodayāti buddhimadhikratau yas

taṃ tvām ananya gatir īśvara saṃśrayāmi || 1 ||

vedeṣu vāladanuyāyisū vā vicārya

taṃtreṣu vākyam akhilaṃ pratipādakaṃ te ||

tātparya saṃgraham ahaṃ vidadhe maheśa

mahyaṃ prasīda nayamām ṛjunāpathā tvaṃ || 2 || (fol. 1v1–4)
