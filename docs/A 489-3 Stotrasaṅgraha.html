Excerpts

Beginning

dhyānam ||

padmadvayābhayavarān dadhataṃ karābjair

māṇikyamaulim aruṇāṅgaruciṃ trinetram ||

raktāmbujāsamam aśeṣaguṇaikasindhuṃ

bhānuṃ samastajagatām adhipaṃ namāmi || 1 || ||

śṛṇuṣva muniśārdūla sūryasya kavacaṃ śubham ||

śarīrārogyadaṃ divyaṃ sarvasaubhāgyadāyakam || 1 ||

dedīpyamānamukuṭaṃ sphuran makarakuṇḍalam ||

dhyātvā sahasrakiraṇaṃ stotram etad udīrayet || 2 || (exp. 3t1–b4)

End

apakrāmantu bhūtani piśācāḥ sarvato diśam ||

sarveṣām avirodhena sandhyākarmasamārabhe || 2 ||

iti mantrābhyāṃ jalena bhūtāny utsārayitvā || choṭikāmudrayā tālatrayeṇa ca digbandhanaṃ kuryāt || ||

tīkṣṇadaṃṣṭra mahākāya kalpāntadahanopama ||

bhairavāya namas tubhyam anujñāṃ dātum arhasi ||

iti prārthya prāṇāyāmaṃ kuryāt || tataḥ sveṣṭadevatāyāḥ nyāsaṃ dhyānaṃ kṛtva japaṅ kuryāt || (exps. 43t4–44t1)
