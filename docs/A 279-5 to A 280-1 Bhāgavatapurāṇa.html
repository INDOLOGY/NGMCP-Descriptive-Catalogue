Excerpts

Beginning of the root text

janmādyasya yato nvayāditarataś cārtheṣv abhijñaḥ svarāṭ
tene brahmahṛdāya ādikavaye muhyaṃti yat sūrayaḥ ||

tejo vārimṛdāṃ yathā vinimayo yatra trisargo mṛṣā
dhāmnāsvena sadā nirastakuhakaṃ satyaṃ paraṃ dhīmahi || 1 || (fol. 2r7¬–8)

Beginning of the commentary

vakratuṇḍāya namaḥ ||    ||

śrīparamahaṃsā svādita caraṇakamalacinmakaraṃdāya ||
bhaktajanamānasanivāsāya śrīrāmacaṃdrāya || 1 ||

vāgīśā yasya vadane lakṣmīr yasya ca vakṣyasi ||
yasyās te hṛdaye saṃvittaṃ nṛsiṃham ahaṃ bhaje || 2 || (fol. 1v1–2)

End of the root text

sūta uvāca ||

ya idam anuśṛṇoti śrāvayed vā murāreś
caritam amṛtakīrter varṇitaṃ vyāsaputraiḥ ||
jagad adyabhi(!) dalaṃ tad bhaktakarṇāvataṃ saṃ
bhagavati kṛtacitro yāti tat kṣemadhāma || 42 || (fol. 124r10–11)

End of the commentary

ayam api śloka(!) pūrvam eva paṭhita iti kaiścitatrātrapaṣaṃte(!) śrīkṛṣṇavṛtaḥ skaṃdhārtha(!) śrīmadbhāgavate mayā || śrīdharoktā vaśiṣṭhas tu śrīpaḥ prītyai prakāśitaḥ || 1 || (fol. 124r15)

Sub-colophon

iti śrīmadbhāgavate mahāpurāṇe daśamaskaṃdhaṭīppaṇikāyāṃ navatitamo dhyāyaḥ || 90 || śrīsamvat || 1921 phālgunamāse śuklapakṣe dvādaśyāṃ guruvāsare saṃpūrṇaṃ śubham ||    || śrīgurvarppaṇam astu || śrīpaḥ prītir astu || śubham || (fol. 124r14–15)

Colophon

iti śrībhāgavate mahāpurāṇe aṣṭādaśasāhasryāṃ saṃhitāyāṃ vaiyāsikyāṃ daśamaskaṃdhe śrīkṛṣṇalīlācaritānuvarṇane navatitamo dhyāyaḥ (fol. 124r11)
