Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīvajrasatvāya ||

śrīguru(!)ve namaḥ || oṃ namaḥ śrīsarvvabuddhabodhisatvebhyaḥ || ||

suga⟪‥⟫tān sasutān sadharmakāyān

praṇipatyādaratoʼkhilāṃś ca vaṃdyān |

sugatātmajasaṃvarāvatāraṃ

kathayiṣyāmi yathāgamaṃ samāsāt ||

na hi kiṃcid apūrvvam anuvācyaṃ

na ca saṃgranthanakauśalyaṃ(!) mamāsti ||

ata eva na me parārthacintā

svamano bhāvayituṃ kṛtaṃ mayedaṃ || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

bodhisatvasukhaiḥ sarvvair jagat sukhitam astu ca ||

jagadduḥkhaikabhaiṣajyaṃ sarvasa[ṃ]pat sukhākaraṃ ||

lābhasatkārasahitaṃ ciraṃ tiṣṭhatu śāsanaṃ ||

mañjughoṣaṃ namasyāmi yatprasādāt mati<ref name="ftn1">Unmetrical Stanza</ref> śubhe ||

kalyāṇamitraṃ vandehaṃ yatprasādāc ca barddhate ||○ || (fol. 66r5–66v1)

Colophon

iti bodhicaryāvatāre pariṇāmanāparicchedo daśamaḥ || || samāptā ceyaṃ bodhicaryyāvatārparikathā || ○ || kṛtir iyaṃ ācāryaśrīśrīśāntidevapādānām iti || ❁ || || || śreyostu saṃvat 950 mti naṣṭavaiśāṣa śudi trayodaśyāṃ ravivāra (!) pūrṇā bhavataḥ || || likhitaṃ ratnakṛtivihārā(!)vajrācārya (na⟪‥⟫ryā) nandeneti śubha || (fol. 66v1–3)
