Excerpts

Beginning

|| śrī .. .. sauṃdaryyai namaḥ || ||

asya śrīpaṃcamīstavarājastotrasya rudrayāmalācāryya ṛṣiḥ anuṣṭup chaṃdaḥ śrītripurā devatāṃ namāmi

śrīguruṃ iti bījaṃ yā vidyā paramā śaktir iti śaktiḥ |

yasyāṃ sarvvaṃ samutpannam iti kīlakam ||

sarvamānorathasidhyarthe jape viniyogaḥ |

śailasiṃdurasaṃdohasauṃdaraṃ svāṃgabhāsuraṃ ||

karuṇāpūrṇakallolakaṭākṣaṃ kulanāyakam || 1 ||

namāmi śrīguruṃ śāṃtaṃ pratyakṣaśivarūpiṇam ||

śirasā yogapīṭhasthaṃ sarvakāmārthasiddhaye || 2 || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

mohanaṃ staṃbhanākarṣamāraṇoccāṭaṇaṃ tathā ||

kalpanādeva jāyaṃte sarvasiddhim upālabhet ||

.. .. .. .. daryā stavarājamanoharam ||

rakṣāmaṃtraṃ ca śubhadaṃ śivena parikīrttitam ||

chatraṃ mūrddhni sahasrapatrakamalaṃ raktasudhākarṣaṇaṃ

divyaśrīparicāryamānam aniśaṃ cātmānam evaṃ sadā ||

dhyāyan vaganasaṃkaje bhagavati raktāṃ raktāṃ cupūrvotane

stotraṃ yastu samastamāsalabhate rājyāspadaṃ bhūtale ||/

prakāśasakalaśubhamayīsuṃdarīraktanetrāṃ

nityaṃ padmāsanasthāṃ taruṇaravinibhāṃ / ṣāṃ ||

dhyāyet tāṃ sarvabhāvaiḥ sakalaguṇayutaṃ sarvade ye hi paśyet sākṣāt ddevaḥ śivo vā / khecaro jāyate saḥ || (fol. 12v2–7)

Colophon

śrīrudrayāmale kāṃḍe vadgarūpāṣṭakaṃ paṃcamī stavarājastotra / saṃvat 1656 .. .. caitra va di paṃcami (fol. 12v7–8)
