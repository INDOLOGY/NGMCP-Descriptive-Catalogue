❖ śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

bhavaccaraṇaprasādāt, bhannu tapāikā caraṇaprasādale, idaṃ vibhktijñānaṃ

[Saṃkṛta]

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

bho svāmiṃ(!) kṛpāsāgara || bhavaccaraṇaprasādāt, idaṃ vibhaktijñānaṃ, liṃgajñānaṃ vaca(!) jñānaṃ vistareṇa mama jātaṃ ||

[Artha]

yo vibhaktijñāna, liṃgajñānaṃ bhannu , puliṃga strīliṃga napuṃsakaliṃgako (jñānapāyā) , vacanaṃ jñāna bhannu , ekavacana, dvivacana, bahuvacanako jñāna pami merā bhayo || || 6 ||
