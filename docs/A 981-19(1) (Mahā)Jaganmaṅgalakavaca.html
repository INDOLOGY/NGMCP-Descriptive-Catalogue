Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || ||

pārvaty uvāca ||

oṃ asyā devamahādevaḥ vidyāyām anudīritaḥ ||

dvāviṃśatyakṣarī vidyā bahudhā bhedataḥ kathaṃ || 1 ||

kāṃ vidyāṃ kva nu gātre tu rakṣaṃ (!) kuryyā (!) ca sādhaka ||

ekākṣarādim ārabhyaṃ tryakṣarī ca ṣaḍakṣarī || 2 ||

sāṃgā saparivārādyā tathā saptadaśākṣarī ||

aṣṭākṣarī ca triguṇā tathā ca daśamākṣarī || 3 ||

ekādaśī mahāvidyāṃ (!) tathā ca dvādaśākṣarī ||

trayodaśākṣarī vidyā yogināṃ (!) mokṣadāyinī || 4 || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

ekakālaṃ dvikālaṃ vā trikālaṃ śraddhayānvitaḥ ||

niśāyāṃ ca viśeṣena śaktyā saha paṭhet sudhī (!) || 12 ||

sarvakāmam avāpnoti ciraṃjīvī bhaven naraḥ ||

apasmāre bhave kuṣṭe jvare grahe sadāruṇe || 13 ||

[[|| nadyāḥ paścimavāhinyāḥ nābhimātrodake sthitaḥ ||

maṃḍalān muktim āpnoti kālī bhaktiprasādataḥ || 14 ||]]

bhūtapretamahāsaṃghe ḍākinī pīḍite pi ca ||

paṭhitvā nāśam āyāti na ca pīḍā karoty alaṃ || 15 ||

liṣitaṃ (!) tiṣṭhate yatra na ca duḥkhaṃ kaciḍ (!) bhavet ||

sarvaduḥkhavinimukto (!) devī putropamaḥ svayaṃ || 16 ||

devatāgurubhāvena gurudaivasya bhāvataḥ ||

daivataṃ vaṃcayad yas tu guruḥ paramadaivataḥ || 17 ||

alpāyuś ca bhavet satyaṃ kālīkavacam uttamaṃ ||

ajñātvā kavacaṃ devi jaganmaṃgalasaṃjñakaṃ || 18 ||

prajapet koṭilakṣaṃ tu na siddhyati varānane ||

maṃtraṃ gurumukhāl labdhvā datvā tasmai tu dakṣiṇāṃ || 19 ||

tena jñapto japen maṃtraṃ tadā siddhyati vai punaḥ ||

anyathā stotrarājo yaṃ na siddhyati varānane || 20 ||

pūjanāt paṭhanā (!) dhyānā (!) satyaṃ satyaṃ varānane || 22 || (fols. 6v4–7r2)

Colophon

iti śrīkālīkulārṇavokta mahājaganmaṃgalakavacaṃ saṃpūrṇaṃ || 1 || (fol. 7r2–3)
