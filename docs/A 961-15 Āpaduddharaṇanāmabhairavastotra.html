Excerpts

Beginning

svasti śrīparabrahmaṇe namaḥ ||


avinayam apanaya viṣṇo damaya

manaḥ samaya viṣayamṛgatṛṣṇāṃ ||

bhūtadayāṃ vistāraya

tāraya saṃsārasāgarataḥ ||


divyadhūnimakaraṃde parimalaparibhogasaccidānande ||

śrīpatipadāravinde bhavabhayakhedacchide vande ||(fol. 1v1–4)


End

yān yān kāmayate kāmān stāṃ stāṃ prāpnoty aśaṃsayam ||

aprakāśyaṃ paraṃ guhyaṃ na deyaṃ yasya kasyacit || 64 ||


satkulīnāya śāṃtāya ṛjave daṃbhavarjite ||

dadyāt stotram idaṃ puṇyaṃ sarvakāmaphalapradaṃ || 65 ||


iti śrutvā tato devī nāmāṣṭaśatam uttamam ||

jajāpa parayā bhaktyā sadā sarveśvareśvarī || 66 ||


saṃtoṣaṃ paramaṃ prāpya bhairavasya prasādataḥ ||

bhairavo ʼpi prasanno ʼbhūt sarvalokamaheśvaraḥ || 67 || (fol. 9r3–9v1)


Colophon

ī(!)ti śrīrudrayāmale umāmaheśvarasaṃvāde āpadudhāraṇaṃ nāma bhairavastotraṃ saṃpūrṇam śubham || śāke 1780 samvat 1915 phālguṇaśukla 28 nandā tithi ‥manakṣatre vaidhṛtiyoge guruvāśa(!)re kauḍiṃnyagotra ‥ ‥ ‥ krena(!) padmalālena likhitaṃ pustakaṃ samāptaṃ saṃpūrṇam śubham || rā rā rā (fol. 9v1–5)
