Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namo sarvvajñāyai ||

saṃdhigas tandriyaś caikasādhyaś ca cittavibhramaḥ |

kaṣṭasādhyaḥ kaṇṭhakubjaḥ karṇiko jihmakas tathā |

kaṣṭāt kaṣṭataraṃ jñeyā(!) rugdāho hanti vā na vā

antako bhagnanetraś ca raktaṣṭīvīpralāpakaḥ ||

śītāṃṣako hmabhinyāsaḥ kaṣṭasādhyaś ca māyakaḥ ||

jñātavyo hi sadā vaidyaiḥ sannipātās trayodaśaḥ(!) ||

(x.1:1-3)

End

kaṃpapralāpaparitāpanavarddhapīḍā prottaptapādayugalaḥ prasṛtāṃgadāhaḥ |

prajñāpranāśa(!)vikalaḥ pracurapravādī nūnaṃ prayāti pitṛpālagṛhaṃ pralāpī ||

uttiṣṭhati vālātkāre(!) kriyāṃ brūte yadīcchayā |

yāmīti vadate nityaṃ sa tyājyo bhiṣaguttamaiḥ || 12 ||

śvasanaṃ kāsaparitāpanavihvalaḥ kaṭhinakaṇṭhakasaṃvṛtātijihvakaḥ |

badhiraśūlabalahānilakṣaṇo bhavati kaṣṭatara eva jikaḥ(!) ||

prathamena ca jihvāyām utpādayati kaṇṭhakān |

jihvakaḥ saṃnipātoyaṃ saṣṭa(!)sādhyo na saṃśayaḥ || 13 ||

(x.18a:1-18b2)

Colophon

iti sannipātalakṣaṇa(!) || (x.18b2)
