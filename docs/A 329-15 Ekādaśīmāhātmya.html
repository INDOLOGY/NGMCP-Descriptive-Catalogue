Excerpts

Beginning

śrīmahāgaṇapataye namaḥ || ||

jitaṃ bhagavatā yena hariṇā lokadhāriṇā ||

ajena viśvarūpeṇa nirguṇena guṇātmanā || 1 || 

māṃdhātā uvāca ||

pāpeṃdhanasya ghorasya śuṣkārdrasya ca bhūriśa (!) ||

ko vahnir dahate tasya, tad bhavān vaktum arhasi || 2 ||

nājñātaṃ triṣu lokeṣu caturmukha gṛhodbhava ||

vidyate tava vipreṃdra trividhaṃ ca suniścitaṃ || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

na paṭe (!) goghnamadhye tu deve dviṣṭeṣu mā paṭhet |

ye ca dharmaparā rājan devadvijaparāyaṇāḥ |

teṣāṃ madhye harer agre paṭhanīyaṃ dvijātinā || (fol. 67v4–5)

Colophon

|| iti śrīnāradīye purāṇe vaiṣṇavopaniṣade (!) rukmāṃgada caritre ekādaśīmāhātme (!) phalaśrutir n-nāmaḥ || || 39 || śubhamstuḥ || || śubhaṃ || śrīśāke 1681 saṃvat 1816 māse 1 tithau 28 vāsare 4 nakṣatre 27 rāśau 12 yoge 1 lagne 3 likhitam idaṃ

pustakaṃ padmanābhaḥ (!) daivajñena ||

baddhamuṣṭikaṭigrīvār adhidṛṣṭir adhomukhṃ ||

kaṣṭēn likhitaṃ grṃthaṃ yatnena paripālayet ||

vijñapti (!) iti stsu vijñpti (!) ||  || śubhaṃ || || rāma || || rāma || ||

bhūnāgabhūpaiḥ pariomāṇa śāke vaiśākhamāse ʼthtithāvanaṃ ge ||

hīnasthite caṃdrmasi ca pauṣṇe viṣkumbhayoge karṇe vaṇīje || ||

lilekha manmathe lagne padmanābhena dhīmatā

ekādaśī mahātmaṃ (!) ca pavitraṃ pāpanāśanaṃ || || śubham astu || || śrīr astu || śubhaṃ kalyāṇaṃ bhavatu || || (fol. 67v5–68r1)
