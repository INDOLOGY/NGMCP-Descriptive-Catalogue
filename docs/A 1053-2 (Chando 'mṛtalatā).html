Excerpts

Beginning

oṃ namo ratnatrayāya ||    || paramagurubhyo namaḥ ||

devaṃ praṇamya śākyendraṃ jayalakṣmītanūbhūvaṃ ||
śrīnāmānandatanadyomṛtānando tanodimāṃ || 1 ||
bahuni viduṣāṃ saṃti chaṃdaśāstrāṇi yadyapi ||
sāramākasya sarvebhyaś chedanārthe(!) sma ddyamaḥ(!) || 2 ||
śākyen māhatmyakusumāpadya gadyālisamkulā ||
iyaṃ chandomṛtalatāṣid vatkaṃṭhe laśiṣyatu || 3 ||
padyaṃ caturbhiś caraṇaiḥ sametaṃ vṛtaṃ tathā jātyabhidhānam etat ||
vṛtrābhidhaṃ rājanasaṃkhyayuktaṃ mātrākṛtaṃ jātyabhidhaṃ vidhātatu || 4 || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

dharmanivṛttapravṛttaviniyojitanivṛtta namste namaste
śauddhodane mahāśuddhodana nikhi vā jñānānalabhitaptatanuṃ māmanadhigat manorathaṃ raṅga raṅga || vyavahārreditaṃ prāyo mayā chandotra kīrttitaṃ | prastārādipunarnīrktaṃ kevalaṃ kautukaṃ hitat || (fol. 45r3–6)

Colophon

iti chandomṛtalatāyām amṛtaviracitāyāṃ chandomṛtagadya prabhedāṣaṣṭīmaṃjarī samāpta (fol. 45r6)
