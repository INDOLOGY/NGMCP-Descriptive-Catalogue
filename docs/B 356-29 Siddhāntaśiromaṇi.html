Excerpts

Beginning

|| śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

śrīmārttaṇḍa prasanno stu ||

oṃ jayati jagati gūḍhān andhakāre padārthān

janaghanaghṛṇayā yaṃ vyaṃjayan nātmabhābhiḥ ||

vimalitamanasāṃ sadvāsanābhyāsayogair

api ca paramatattvaṃ yogināṃ bhānur ekaḥ || 1 ||

jayati sarvotkarṣeṇa varttate⟨ḥ⟩ || kaḥ || ayaṃ bhānuḥ sūryaḥ kiṃ viśiṣṭaḥ ekaḥ advitīyaḥ kiṃ kurvan vyaṃjayan prakāśayan padārthān kābhiḥ ātmabhābhiḥ svadīptibhiḥ kva jagati kiṃ viśiṣṭān padārthān gūḍhān adṛśyān kasmin sati andhakāre kayā hetubhūtayā janaghanaghṛṇayā ghanācāsau ghṛṇā ca ghanaghṛṇā janānāṃ ghṛṇāṃ janaghanaghṛṇā tayetyarthaḥ na kevalaṃ ghaṭapaṭādīn padārthān vyaṃjayan api ca paramatattvaṃ parabrahma keṣāṃ yogīnāṃ kathaṃbhūtaṃ kaluṣitamanobhāvād ajñānarūpeṇa tamasā atigūḍhaṃ kiṃviśiṣṭāṃ yogināṃ vimalitamanasāṃ nirmalīkṛtacetasāṃ kaiḥ sadvāsanābhyāsayogaiḥ (fol. 1v1–8)

End

adhidinair dinakṛd dinasañcayaḥ

sahita indu dinānyatha tā niśa(!) ||

virahitāni ca tāni dinakṣayaiḥ

kṣiti dināny ata utkramato paraṃ || 12 ||

evam anayā vāsanayā ghaṭitārkacandramāsāntaram adhimāsāḥ ||

kiṃ pāṭeneti vā śabdārthaḥ || evam adhimāsadinaiḥ sahitāḥ saurāhās cāṃdrāhāḥ bhavaṃti kiṃ tat pāṭhena vā || te ||(fol. 15v8–10)
