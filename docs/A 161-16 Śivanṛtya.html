Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||    ||

oṃ mahānṛtyāvasāne tu paripapraccha (!) pārvatiḥ (!) ||
girīśaṃ śirasā natvā candrārddhakṛtaśekharaṃ || 1 ||

śrīdevy uvāca ||

devadeva ma(2)hādeva sṛṣṭisthityaṃtakāraka ||
pūrvam uktāni yaṃtrāṇi kathayasva kṛpānidheḥ || 2 ||

śrīdakṣināmūrttir uvācaḥ (!) ||

śṛṇuṣvāvahitā bhūtvā girije (3) prāṇavallabhe
akathyaṃ paramārthena tathāpi kathayāmy ahaṃ || 3 || (fol. 1v1–3)

Sub-colophons

iti śrīdakṣiṇāmūrtipārvatīsamvāde śivanṛtya(!) navakoṣṭavidhir nāmā(!) prathamaḥ paṭalaḥ || 1 || (fol. 1v9)

(fol. 2 is missing)

iti śrīśivanṛtye ekonapaṃcāśatkoṣṭavidhi[s] tṛtiyaḥ paṭalaḥ || 3 || (fol. 3r3)

iti śrīśivanṛtye ekaviṃśādhikaśatakoṣṭavidhiḥ paṃcamaḥ paṭalaḥ || 5 || (fol. 5r3)
