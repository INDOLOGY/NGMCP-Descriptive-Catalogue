❖ oṁ namo nārāyaṇāya ||

śrīrlachamitvaṃ dharmmava mvāṅā(6)va hṅavasa lachamisake ṅeṅā bhor lachami chana chu nimistīna samasta manukṣa ute makhāṃ (7) gurichinoṃr tomi tosana thya sardharmmar dharmmasamucayana ṅeṅāva lachamisena ājṇā(exp. 6b1) biraṃ bho chi dharma khavakṣa jena ute makhāṅā hetu kāne ṅehune gvarīchi[[va]]naṃ manukṣeyā(2) misātosa śuciśila madu chāya chandra madu hni rāla cā khi capañdhura adhāvata yāṅā(3)va coṅahmaṃ śuciśira madu lajyā madu dhapari tamakāli thapigva nilathāya jena vāsa(4) mayāṅā duśvayatvaṃ mayayā thathyāṃ tavami ṭosona jurā je macoṅā thāyasa hnichi hnichi (5) ṣu ṅyākatheṃ duhāvalasāṃ sora śuṅāva vanaiyiva lachamisena dharmmatva kāṅā || || (exp. 6t4-b5)

iti dharmmalachamisaṃvāde prathamodhyāya || || (exp. 6b6)
