Beginning

śrīgovindāya namaḥ ||

doṣaikadṛk purobhāgī nikṛtas tv anṛjuḥ śaṭhaḥ || 46 ||

(Second complete MS:)

Beginning

oṃ viśeṣyanighnaiḥ saṃkīrṇair nnānārthair avyayair api ||

liṅgādisaṃgrahair vvargāḥ sāmānye vvarggasaṃśrayāḥ |||| 1 ||||

strīdārādyair yad viśeṣyaṃ yādṛśaiḥ prastutaṃ padaiḥ ||

guṇadravyakriyāśabdās tathā syus tasya bhedakāḥ |||| 2 |||| (fol. 1r)

End

ṣaṭsaṃjñakākās (!) triṣu samā yuṣmad asmat tiṅ avyayaṃ || 

param virodhe śeṣan tu jñeyaṃ śiṣṭaprayogataḥ || 5 ||

iti liṅṅasaṃgrahādivargaḥ || || (fol. 23v)

Colophon

nānākavīnāṃ yadi nāma koṣāḥ santy eva śabdārthavidaṃ pradhānāḥ ||

tathāpi sūkte ʼmarasiṃhanāmnaḥ kaver atīva prathitaṃ mano naḥ || ...

ity amarasiṃhakṛtau nāmaliṃgānuśāsane ||

sāmānyakāṇḍas tṛtīyaḥ sāṃga eṣa samarthitaḥ || 

śrīśāke 1682 || śrāvaṇamāse || (fol. 23v)
