Excerpts

Beginning

❖oṃ namaḥ kṛṣṇāya ||

madhyāhnārkamrīcikā svivapaya pūrā yad ajñānataḥ

khaṃ vāyur jvalano jalam kṣitir iti trailokyam unmīlati ||

yat tatvaṃ viduṣāṃ nimīlati punaḥ sragbhohibhogopamaṃ

sāndrānadam upasmahe tad amalaṃ svātmāvabodhaṃ mahaḥ ||

api ca ||

antarn-nāḍī niyamita marulaṃghitabrahmaraṃdhraṃ,

svānte śānti praṇayini samūnmīlitānanda sāndraṃ |

pratyag jyotir jayati yaminaḥ spasṭalālāṭanetra,

vyājavyaktīkṛtam iva jagad vyāpicandrārddhamauleḥ ||

nāndyaṃte sūtradhāraḥ ||

alam it vistareṇādiṣṭāsmi || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

nīrajaske sadānande pade cāhaṃ niyojitaḥ |

tathāpyatantastu (!)

paryya (!) nyosmin jagati mahatīṃ vṛṣṭim iṣṭāṃ vidhattāṃ

rājānaḥ kṣmāṃ vigatavividhopaplavān pālayantu ||

tatvonmeṣāpahatamanasas tvatprasādān mahāntaḥ

saṃsārārddhiṃ viṣayamamatātaṃkapaṃka tarantu ||

bhaktiḥ sarvvametad bhaviṣyatīti niṣkrāntāḥ sarvve

ṣaṭho ʼṅkaḥ samāptaḥ || 6 || (fol.  52r4–6)

Colophon

iti śrīmāhāmohopādhyāya miśraśrīkṛṣṇaviracitaṃ prabodhacandrodayan nāma nāṭakaṃ ṣaṣṭhoṅka samāptaṃ || || || saṃ 988 miti mārgaśīrṣaśukla 7 roja thva kunhu bhagavatgītā 6 guviyā thva candrodayanāṭaka saphukayā jula || śrīgovindarāja viprena || || || (fol. 52r6–52v2)
