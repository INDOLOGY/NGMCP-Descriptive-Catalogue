Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

ya samastaviduṣāṃ śiromaṇir yena rājati sabhā vipaścitāṃ ||

taṃ mahīśvarasatāṃgribaṃdhanam nīlakaṃdḥa pitaraṃ namāmyahaṃ || 1 ||

śṛṃgāraika pradhānaṃ yadṛtusaṃhāra nāmakaṃ ||

kāvyaṃ tatkālidāsīyaṃ vyākhyāsyehaṃ yathāmati || 2 || (fol. 1v1–2)

śrigaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

pracaṇḍasūryaḥ spṛhaṇīyacandramāḥ sadāvagāha kṣatvārisaṃcayaḥ ||

dināṃtaramyo bhyupaśāṃta manmatho nidāghakālo yam upāgataḥ priye || 1 ||

(fol. 1v6–7)

End

āmrīmaṃjulamaṃjarīvaraśaraḥ yatkiṃ śukaṃ yatddhanur

jyā yasyālikulaṃ kalaṃkarahitaṃ chatraṃśitāṃśuḥ sitaṃ ||

mattebhomalamānilaḥ (!) parabhṛto yadvaṃdino lokajit

soyaṃ vo viparītarītuvitanur bhadraṃ vasantānvitaḥ || 28 || (fol. 30v5–7)

pūjito na jagadīśvaro mayā nāpi viśvajananī susevitā ||

kiṃtu matpralapitaṃ nijechayā(!) tatra sadgurukṛpaiva kāraṇaṃ || 1 || (fol. 31r1)

Colophon

iti śrīkavikālidāsakṛtau ṛtūsaṃhārakāvye vasaṃtavarṇanaṃ nāma ṣaṣtḥaḥ sargaḥ || 6 || (fol. 31r3)

vedacandravasubhūmivatsare māghasūryasita paṃcamī tithau ||1814 ||

vyoma puśkaranagairmita kṛtiḥ || 700 || pūritā hi maṇirāma śarmaṇā || 2 || iti śrībhāradvāja maṇirāma viracitāyāṃ caṃdrikābhidhāyām ṛtusaṃhāravyākhyāyāṃ ṣaṣtḥaḥ sargaḥ || 6 || saṃvat 1858 || || (fol.31r1–4)
