Manuscript Features

on the exposure two is written: vi.saṃ. 527 śāktapramoda tārātantra.

tārādhyāna, yantrddhāra, mantroddhāra, tārāsaparyyāsaraṇi, tārāstotra (ascribed to the Nīlatantra),, tārākavaca (ascribed to the Nīlatantra), tārāhṛdaya (ascribed to the Bhairavītantra), tāropaniṣat (ascribed to the Atharvaṇaveda-saubāgyakāṇḍa), tārā [or ] tāraṇīśatanāmastotra (ascribed to the Svarṇamālatantra), tārā⟨ṃ⟩takānādisahasranāma (ascribed to the Brahmayāmalatantra)

Excerpts

Beginning

śritārātantram

atha tārādhyānam

pratyālīḍhapadārppitāṅghriśabahṛd ghorāṭṭahāsā parā

khaḍgendībarakarttrikharpparabhujā huṃkārabījodbhavā ||

kharbānīlaviśālapiṅgalajatājuṭaikanāgair yyutā |

(jā annyasya) kapālakartṛjagatāṃ haṃty ūgratārā svayam || (exp. 3R,1–7)

End

iti nigaditam asyās tādinān(!) māṃ sahasraṃ

varadamanunidānan divyasāmrājyasaṃjñam ||

vidhiharigiriśādau śāktidānaikadakṣaṃ

samavidhipaṭhanīyaṃ kālitārā samakṣaiḥ(!) (fol. 19r1–4)

Colophon

iti śrībrahmayāmale tārāyās takārādisahasranāmastotraṃ sampūrṇam || 1960 (fol. 19r4–5)
