Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ śīvāya ||

kailāśaśikharāsīnaṃ nānā ratnopaśobhitam |

siddhividyāparākīrṇṇā, yakṣagandharvasevitaṃ ||

tatrāsīno mahādeva śrīkaṇṭhas sūrapūjitaḥ ||

praṇamya caraṇau tasya devī vacanam avravīt |

bhagavan sarvvadharmmajña lokanāthañ  (!) jagatpate ||
