Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||    ||
śrīrāmacandrāya namaḥ ||

atha śataślokīrāmāyaṇa likhyate ||    ||

śrīsāradācaraṇapadmayugaṃ vidhāya
citte (2) gaṇeśacaraṇau praṇayena natvā ||
rāmāyaṇīyam akhilaṃ munibhiḥ praṇītam
ādyair ahaṃ sakalasārataraṃ pravakṣye || 1 ||

pūrvaṃ yo ditijo narārddha(3)vapuṣā siṃhena vyāpāditaḥ
so yaṃ viśravasaḥ suto 'surasutāgarbhe bhavad rāvaṇaḥ ||
tenātīva varāj(!) jagattrayam idaṃ duṣṭena saṃpāditaṃ
jātas tadvadhaheta(4)ve daśarathāc chrīrāmanāmā hariḥ || 2 || (fol. 1v1–4)

End

bhūmau nirgatasītayā virahito rāmo mahīṃ kevalaṃ
varṣāṇām ayutaṃ tataḥ sabha(!)bhu(7)jac caikāṃtapatreṇa saḥ ||
rājyasyāspadasaṃsthito(!) svatanayau kṛtvā samaṃ bāṃdhavaiḥ
sārddhaṃ svīpṛpuraṃ(!) prajābhir amarasthānaṃ vimānai(8)r yayau || 102 ||

ity etad yaś carit[r]aṃ paṭha⟨ṃ⟩ti raghupater yo naro vā śṛṇoti
sāraṃ rāmāyaṇasya pratidinam ṛṣiṇā cāgni(9)vaiśyena gītaṃ ||
so yaṃ nirdhūtapāpo jaladharapadam āruhya yānena yāti
svargaṃ divyena devair abhimukham akhilair āgataiḥ (10) stūyamānaḥ || 103 || (fol. 8v6–10)

Colophon

ity agnivaiśyamunikṛtaṃ rāmāyaṇaṃ samāptam || ||    || ||
śrīśāke 1715 māse 11 likhitam idaṃ satyadhareṇa ||    || (fol. 8v10 and right margin)
