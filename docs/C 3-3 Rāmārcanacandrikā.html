Manuscript Features

Available folios: 1–9, 13–16, 20–46

Between fol. 16 and 20 two smaller folios with 7 lines on each side are photographed (exp. 17b–19t).
They are numbered 15 and 19 in the left margin and have no marginal title.
The script is a transitional form of Maithili and Newari.
The text runs as follows:
