Excerpts

Beginning

uṃ cchaṃ cchāṃ hrīṃ hrūṃ 2 phaṭ sarvvatra vasaṃ (!) kuru svāhā ||

parasiddhibandhanaṃ paścāj jāpena || uṃ kanda svāhā || japa anavāna (!) nyūne siddhipradaṃ || hrīṃ prajñā śrī baṃdhanāya kūṃ 3 phaṭ svāhā || vāmavat tena bandhanaṃ dakṣiṇāvat tena tyāgaḥ || (exp. 26t3–5)

End

haṃtākṣaṃ khodaduḥkhaṃ (!) samitabhavabhaye sarvvavighnāntaye

gāṃ gīṃ guṃ gaiṃ ṣaḍaṃge gagaṇagatiyute siddhisarvvārthadāye |

oṃ gluṃ mudrāgajākhyai gamapavanagate ākhuyā naikadaṇḍe uṃ hrīṃ hrūṃ gāṃ gaṇeśe dviradamukhagaṇe vighnanāthe namaste ||

devtāṣṭakaṃ tu yo devi paṭhate ca samāhitaḥ |

tasya siddhir bhavecchīghraṃ yoginīnāṃ prasādataḥ ||

kathitaṃ yoginīstotraṃ nityā tantre varānane |

olitraye (!) pi sāmānyaṃ surāṇām api durllabhaṃ || (exp. 34b2–7)

Colophon

iti śrī darppaṇaśiṣāyāṃ (!) siddhasārasaṃgrahe caturosīti sāhasre devyāṣṭakaṃ || || (exps. 34b1–35t1)
