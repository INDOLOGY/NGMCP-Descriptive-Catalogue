Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśo jayatu || ||

śrīguhyakālyai namaḥ || ||

śrīsadāśiva uvāca || ||

oṃ namas tribhuvaneśāya brahmaṇe viśvarūpiṇe ||

īśvarāya pareśāya gurave śivarūpiṇe || 1 ||

yasya prasādād devopi sarvvajñaḥ saśivo bhavet ||

tāṃ naumi pareśānīṃ sarvavyāpimaheśvarīṃ || 2 ||

brahmāṇḍakoṭilakṣāṇi bhasmasāt kurute (!) yayā ||

tāṃ kālagrāsaniratāṃ guhyakālīṃ namāmy ahaṃ || 3 || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

aṃjanādrinibhāṃ kālī (!) koṭisūryasamaprabhāṃ

trailokyarakṣaṇaratāṃ maṃgalāṃ praṇamāmy ahaṃ 201

guhyakālīṃ maṃgalāṃ ca siddhikālījapeśvarīṃ

carcikāṃ tripureśi ca kālī ca bhuvaneśvarīṃ 202

śrīmaṃgalāṃ mahākālīṃ nirvāṇapadadāyinīṃ

mokṣadāṃ rājyadāṃ nityāṃ daśavaktrāṃ parāṃ bhajet 203 (fol. 18r1–5)

Colophon

iti śrīhāhārāvataṃtre ṣaṭtriṃśati sāhasre mahāʼ tharvaṇasaṃhitāyāṃ śivapārvatisāmarasya (!) śrīguhyakālidevyāḥ sahasranāmaśrutirahasyaṃ sampūrṇam śubham samvat 964 nepāla mārgaśiraśukla 6 somavāra 2 joganarasiṃ naṃ coyā julo (fol. 18r5–7)
