Excerpts

Beginning

|| śrī gaṇeśāya namaḥ || śrī sūryyāya namaḥ ||

svasvābhilāṣaṃ na hi la(kṣa)mīśā
nirvighnam īśānamukhāḥ suraughāḥ ||
vinā prasādaṃ kila yasya naumi
taṃ ḍhuḍhirājaṃ matilābhahetoḥ 1

jātakoditadaśāphalaṃ yataḥ
schūlakālaphalapradaṃ sphuṭaṃ nṛṇāṃ ||
tatra na sphurati daivavinmatis
tad bruve bdaphalam āditājikāt 2 (fol. 1v1–4)

End

tadātmajo naṃtaguṇo sty anaṃto yo dhok saduktī kila kāmadhenuṃ ||
saṃtuṣṭaye jātakapaddhatiṃ ca nyarūpayaṃ duṣṭamatī nirasya || 53 ||

padmāvayāgnāvitato vipaści chrīnīlakaṃṭhaḥ śrutiśāstraniṣṭhaḥ ||
vidvachivaprītikaraṃ vyadhāgnāta tad varṣataṃtraṃ mṛgayādiyuktaṃ || 54 || (fol. 23r10–23v2)
