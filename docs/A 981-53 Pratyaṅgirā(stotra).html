Beginning

❖ śrīpratyaṃgirādevyai namaḥ || ||

oṃ namaḥ sarvasiddhiyoginībhyo namaḥ

sarvasiddhimātṛkobhyo namo nidyoditānandananditāyai sakalakulacakranāyikāyai ..gatādyai .. ṇḍakapāli tad yathā | oṃ hūṃ krūṃ hāṃ phreṃ ksauṃ krauṃ namaḥ paramahaṃsinī .. ṇamārgadevi viṣamopaplavapraśamani sakaladuritāriṣṭakleśadalati sarvāpadābhodhitaraṇi sakalaśatrupramarddani devi āgacha 2 hasa 2 vaṃja 2 nararudhirān tu vasābhakṣaṇi mama svaśatruṃ marda 2 khāhi 2 truśūlena ninda 2 chinda 2 khaḍgena tāḍaye 2 chedaya 2 tāvadyāvan mama sakalamanorathān sādhaya 2 paramakāruṇike bhagavati mahābhairavarūpadhāriṇi tridaśavaranamite sakalamaṃtramātaḥ praṇata janavatsale devi mahākāli kālanāśini hūṃ hūṃ hūṃ prasida madanāture kuru 2 surāsurakanyake hrīṃ śrīṃ krauṃ svāhā || || (fols. 1v1–2r4)
