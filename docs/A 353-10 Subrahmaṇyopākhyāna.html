Beginning

❖ śrī 3 nṛtyanāthāya namaḥ || ||

nāndi me ||

i sadanamā || vibhāsa || co ||

caṃdrasama nicarata bhiṅa varaṇa (fol. 1r1) maheśa,

triśūla ḍamaru josā gaji naya heśa,

gala molamālā īśa naṭeśa ||

śirasa gaṃgāyā śobhā(2) vibhutina busā,

dhucheguli jaṁsa hisā,

thānathāna bina tisā thathiṅahma raṇajitayā āsā ||

me pu 1(3) || ||

vidarbhanagarayā subrahmaṇya rājā kanakamaṃjarī rāṇī candravīra mantrī nīticaṇḍa ko(ṭavā)(4)ra śyāmā sakhī praveśa || ||

End

candraaye caṇdrāvatī mantrī koṣṭhapāla sakhī thva saṃsāra thīra makhu kāma krodha lo(fol. 14v1)bha moha tolatāva jagadambā bhāvanā yāya nuyo ||

sarvvemahārāja ati uttama || ||

caṃdra senādiyā (2) davala va || ||

bhairavī || e ||

jaya 2 tribhuvaṇa rāṇi || dhru ||

ḍamaru truśūla joṅa āsana betāla

tri(3)bhuvaṇapālini yāva udhāra ||

jagatavyāpi devi bihune mukuti,

hlāla raṇajita nṛpa devi uguti ||

me (4) pu 15 || ||

Colophon

iti śrīśrījayaraṇajitamalladeva mahārājādhirāja viracite subrahmaṇyupākhyāna ka(fol. 14v5)thā prathamo'ṅkassamāpta || || śubham astu || ||
