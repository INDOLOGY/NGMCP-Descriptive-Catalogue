Manuscript Features

The date in the MS reads śake 1711 pauṣe māsi kṛṣṇapakṣe maṃdavāsare 11.tadine. This corresponds to December 1st 1789 AD.

Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ || śrī gurubhyo namaḥ ||

nidhāya hṛdi viśveśaṃ vidhāya guruvaṃdanaṃ

bā(2)lānāṃ sukhabodhāya kriyate tarkasaṃgrahaḥ 1

dravyaguṇakarmasāmānyaviśeṣasama(3)vāyābhāvāḥ saptaiva padārthā (!)

tatra dravyāṇi || pṛthivyaptejovāyvākāśakāladigā(4)tmamanāṃsi navaiva ||

rūparasagaṃdhasparśasaṃkhyāparimāṇapṛthaktvasaṃyogavibhā(5)gaparatvāparatvagurutvadravatvasnehaśabdabuddhisukhaduḥkhechādveṣaprayatnadharmādha(6)rmasaṃskārāś caturviṃti (!) guṇāḥ || (fol. 1v1–6)

End

a(4)nādiḥ sāṃtaḥ prāgabhāvaḥ | utpatteḥ pūrvaṃ kāryaṃsyāsādir (!) nṛnaṃtaḥ (!) pradhvaṃsaḥ || utpattyanaṃra (!) kārthasya (!) || (5) traikālikaṃ saṃsagāvachinnapratiyogināko (!) tyaṃtābhāvaḥ || yathā bhūtale ghaṭo nāstīti || tadā(6)tmyaṃ saṃbaṃ(dhma)vachinnapratiyogitvāko (!) nyonyābhāvaḥ saptaiva padārthā iti siddham |

kāṇādanāya(7)mitinā (!) bālavyutpattisiddhaye ||

annabhaṭṭena (!) viduṣā racijā (!) tarkasaṃgrahaḥ || 1 || cha || śrī || cha || (8)

svārthaṃ paropakārārthaṃ ca || (fol. 8v3–7)

Colophon

śake 1711 pauṣe māsi kṛṣṇapakṣe maṃdavāsare 11.tadine idaṃ pustakaṃ samā(9)ptaṃ || hariḥ || || || || (fol. 8v8–9)
