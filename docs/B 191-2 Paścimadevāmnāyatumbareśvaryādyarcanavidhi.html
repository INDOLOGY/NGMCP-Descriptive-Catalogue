teṣāṃ siddhi na dūre sti devyā saṃtuṣṭisādhakaṃ ||

yaṃ nāsti taṃnnāsti syāt kaulike kulasādhane |


yaṃ 2 cintayate kāmaṃ taṃ taṃ siddhyati nānyathā ||

satyaṃ satyaṃ mahādevī kaulikī tatsamā na hī | (!)

ardharātrī samusthāye (!) devī pajvālyate niśī (!) ||


paṭhete (!) stotrametaṃ tu sarvvacintita siddhyatī || (!)

puracaryā(!) vininaiva (!) stotramātreṇa siddhyati ||


maṇḍale pratimāgre vā maṇḍalāgre paṭhed yati |

idaṃ mahāstava (!) proktaṃ acintitā na siddhaye || (!)


nānyaṃ deya ratāyān tu na dyaṃ tu kadācana |

dātavyaṃ bhaktayuktānāṃ kuladīksāratāya ca || || (!) (exp. 70t4–70b2)
