«Beginning: »


❖ dīpayāgavidhiḥ ||


āmantranaṃ ||



āmantritosi yoginyā sagaṇaṃ gurupūrvakaṃ |


puṣpadhūpādikaṃ caiva saumanasyaṃ prayacchatu ||


adya me pratigṛhṇantu nimantra parameśvari ||


puṣpabhājana || devārccana || paśuyāgaṃ || ācāryapūjā || śodhana || dinayā karma thvate || ||


tato rātriyā karma yāya ||


aiṃ 5 kṣīrārṇavasamudbhūtaṃ koṅkane yat prakāśitaṃ |


sahajānandasandoha nirmmitaṃ paramāmṛtaṃ ||


varṇānāṃ caiva sarveṣāṃ ekīkaraṇamamurttamam ||


kulaśāsanāsirdhyarthaṃ annapātraṃ pragṛhyatām || (fol. 1r1–4)


«End: »


kalasābhiṣekaṃ ||


devasyāgre akhaṇḍa dhārayet ||


akhaṇḍamaṇḍalāra pītarajasāyuktaṃ


rājāpuṣpākṣataṃ caiva jānubhyām bhūmisaṃsthitā ||


devyāsamārādhya āputrayaśaśriyam ||


bighnāntaṃ saṃpradā lakṣṃī maṅgalaṃ śiva sarvvadā ||


namaste bhairavaṃ rūpaṃ vīrarūpāvatārakam ||


vīreśī bhairavīyuktaṃ sarvvadeveṣu devatā ||


sāṃbhavī sagaṇā sarve vatāra parameśvarī ||


nyūnādhikaṃ yathā pūjā yacdi chidram achidrakam ||


saṃpūrṇaṃ sarvabhāvena kṣamyatāṃ parameśvara ||


gaccha gaccha gaṇāḥ sarve svasvasthānaṃ gataṃ layaṃ ||


rakṣa raklṣa maheśāni punarāvijayāya ca || ||


anyānyavīrajananamaskāraṃ || || tālatrayam || (fol. 8v2–6)


«Colophon:»


thvate dīpajāgaurttarayāmathaḥ || || (fol. 8v6–7)
