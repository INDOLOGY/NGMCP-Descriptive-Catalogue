(2) atha paramakāruṇiko bhagavān jaiminir dharmavivekāya dvādaśalakṣaṇīṃ praṇināya tatrādau dharmajijñāsāṃ sūtrayā(3)māsa athāto dharmajijñāseti (fol. 1v1–3)
