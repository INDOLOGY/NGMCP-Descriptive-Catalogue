«Beginning»

|| śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

yaṃ hi vairāgyasaṃpannās tattvam arthaṃ vivekinaḥ

labhaṃte sādhanairdātās taṃ sītānāyakaṃ bhaje || 1 ||


tadaṃtarapratipattau raṃhati(!) sai(!) pariṣvaktaḥ praśnanirupaṇābhyāṃ

vṛttamanughatṛtīyārthadhyāyām āha dvitīya ityādinā | aviruddhe vedāṃtārghe

tat jñānasādhanaciṃtāvasaradaṃtyanayor(!) adhyāyayor hetuhetumadbhāvaḥ (fol. 1v1-4)


«End»


aṃtaryāmī jagatsākṣī sarvakartā raghu udvahaḥ(!) |

ato tra doṣo śaṃkya syād eva (t)dhyaiveti (!) śāsanāt || 2 ||


vakṣasya kṣaṇoś (!) ca pārśveka rala(!) yugale kaustubhāvāṃdayāṃ ca sītāṃ

kodaṃḍadīkṣāmaramayavarayuto vīkṣya rāmāṃta saṃgaḥ | svasyāḥ kvasyā ditīyaṃ hṛdi kṛ

mananābhāṣya ratnaprabhākhyā svātmānedaika(!) luvdhvā raghuvaracaraṇāṃbhojayugmaṃ prapapannā || (fol. 30v4-8)


«Colophon»


iti śrīmatparamahaṃsaparivrājakācāryaśrīmatgopālasarasvatīpūjyapādaśiṣyaśrīgoviṃdabhagavatkṛtau

śārīrakamīṇā(!) bhojayugyaṃ māpannā || || iti śrīmat -------------- /// (fol. 30v8-10)
