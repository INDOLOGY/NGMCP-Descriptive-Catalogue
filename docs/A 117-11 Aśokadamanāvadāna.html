Excerpts

Beginning

(fol. 1v1) oṃ namo buddhāye(!) ||

atha te bhikṣavaḥ sarve jayaśriyaṃ j(!)atiguruṃ
kṛtāñjalipuṭo natvā prārthayaṃ ca tathāparaṃ ||
bhadanta śro(2)tum ichāmaḥ punar anyat subhāṣitaṃ ||
nṛpāśokāvadānaṃ yat tan samādeṣṭa(!)m arhati ||
iti taiḥ prārthyamānaḥ sa jayaśrīḥ st(!)avi(2)ro yatiḥ ||
sarvā[ṃ]s tā[ñ] chrāvakān bhikṣu(!)n samālokyaivam ādiśat ||
śṛṇudhvaṃ bhikṣavaḥ sādhū(!) yūyaṃ sarve samāhitāḥ ||
avadā(4)nam aśokasya narendrasya cakṣyate ||(metre!)
yad ayaṃ nṛpatī rājā cakravarttī nṛpādhipaḥ ||
aśoka[ḥ] sarvalokānāṃ śokahantā bhava(5)n babhau ||
yac cāśoko narendro sau yaśasas sadguror pateḥ ||
upadeśāt pure svasminn upaguptaṃ samānayan(!) ||
tasyopadeśa(6)m ākarṇya buddhabhaktiprasāditaḥ ||
saddharmadeśanāṃ śrutvā triratnaśaraṇaṃ gataḥ ||
satkṛtya śraddhayā nityaṃm arhatām abhaja(7)n mudā ||
caityaiś cāpi maho[c]ch[r]āyaiḥ pṛthivīn samaśodhayat ||
caturāśītisahasrāṇāṃ saddharmarājikā bhuvi ||
(8)yathāvidhi pratiṣṭḥāpya prācareyan samaṃtataḥ ||

End

(fol. 59v1) iti me guruṇā khyātaṃ tathā v⟨y⟩aś cakṣyate mayā ||
tad anumoda(ṇa)ṃ kṛtvā pracaradhvaṃ śubhe sadā ||
iti ja(2)yaśriyā khyātaṃ sarve te bhikṣavo mudā ||
śrutvā tatheti saṃbhāṣya prābhyacarañ chubhe sadā ||

aśokanāmnā nṛpater idaṃ ye vadānam a(3)trāvirataṃ śṛṇvanti ||
sadā parāṃ ye ca niśāmu(!)yanti prayānti sarve pi sukhā[[va]]tīṃ te || ❁ ||

Colophon

iti aśokadamu(!)nāvadānaṃ sa(4)māptam ||

ye dharmā hetuprabhavā hetus teṣāṃ tathāgata ||
hyevadan teṣāṃ yo nirodha evaṃvādi mahāśramaṇaḥ ||   ||

śubhaṃ || bhūyāt
