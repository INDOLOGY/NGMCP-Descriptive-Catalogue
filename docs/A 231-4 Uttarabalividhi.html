❖ oṃ namaḥ śrīgaṇeśāya nama(!) ||


gurabhyau(!) nama ||(!)


atha ṅāthā nayā urttara bali putha likhyate || panātiyā khāhoha coṅa kvāṭha pratiṣṭhāyānā dayakā ||

brāhmaṇa ācārya josi thvate neṃsthina hārāvava yāva || maṇḍapana pi urttara soyāya majina sā

pūrvva soyaṃteva || pratiṣṭhā kuhnuyā velasa yāya || thvana hṅāonali dinapatiṃ rajasā

rāsakarmmārccanayāya dhunaṅāva āya || (fol. 1v1–5)


«End:»


yajamānena laṃkhana (ycke) || adyādi || vākya ||


oṃ paśugṛhṇāśi deveśi prajñayā upavāsitaṃ |

asyāpi svarggasaṃprāpti sihrinaayaccha me || ||


|| mohāyakaṃ syāya || arghapātraṇa hāyāva śiradenakaṃ cchāya || || thvate dhuna ṅāva ||

ābharaṇa cetalāvali dakoskaṃ taya || samaya cchoya || vākya || namaḥ śrīnāthāya ||

namaḥ śrī siddhināthāya || namaḥ śrīkujeśanāthāya namo namaḥ || ||
