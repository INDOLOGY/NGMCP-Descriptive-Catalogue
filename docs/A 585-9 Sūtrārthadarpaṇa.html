Manuscript Features

The available folios are 36–99.

The MS contains the text from the bhvādi chapter to the end of the tiṅanta chapter.

The MS contains many scribal errors.

Excerpts

Beginning

❖ nekārthatvāt ||

ata eva yāgāt svarho bhavatītyādāv utpadyata ityādyartha upalabhyate || upasargās tv arthaviśeṣasya dyotakā na tu vācakāḥ || prabhavati parābhavati saṃbhavati anubhavati abhibhavati udbhavati paribhavatītyādau vilakṣaṇārthāvagateḥ || uktaṃ ca

upasargeṇa dhātvartho balād anyatra nīyate ||

prahārāhārasaṃhāravihāraparihāravad iti || 8 || (fol. 36v1–3, exp 3t)

End

kārakāṃtarasya kartṛtvaṃ yathā ||

asiṃś chinatti sādhvajaṃ kaṭāha ātmanā pacatyaudanena pacyate ʼrthabaudanaḥ sa pacyate || amīṣu kartṛtā yathā tathaiva cetane hare svataṃtratā tu kartṛtā tvayi sthitā hy ato ʼvamāṃ || 156 || ❖ || || iti karmakartṛprakriyā || samāptā ca tiṅantaprakriyā || || || (fol. 100r1–4)

Colophon

iti śrīnidhīdhireṇa kṛte sūtrārthadarpaṇe | 1 |
