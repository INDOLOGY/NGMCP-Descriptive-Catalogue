Beginning

|| || śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||


kailāsaśikhare ramye devadeva[ṃ] maheśvaraṃ |

dhyānoparatam āsīnaṃ prasannamukhapṃkajaṃ 1


surāsuraśiroratnaraṃjitāṃghriyugaṃ prabhuṃ |

praṇamya naṃdiko devaṃ baddhāṃjalir abhāṣata 2


naṃdi(!) uvāca ||


devadeva jagannātha saṃśayo sti mahān mama |

rahasyaṃ kiṃcid icchāmi praṣṭuṃ tvāṃ bhaktavatsala 3| (fol 1v1–4)


End

nātaḥ parataro maṃtro nātaḥ parataraḥ stavaḥ 205


nātaḥ paratarā vidyā tīrthaṃ nātaḥ parāt paraṃ |

te dhanyāḥ kṛtapuṇyās te ta eva bhuvi saṃsthitāḥ 206


ekabhāvaṃ sadā nityam ye rcayaṃti maheśvarīṃ |


devatānāṃ devatā yā brahmādyair yā ca pūjitā ||

bhūyāt sā varadā loke sādhūnāṃ viśvamaṃgalā 207 (fol 17v1–3)
