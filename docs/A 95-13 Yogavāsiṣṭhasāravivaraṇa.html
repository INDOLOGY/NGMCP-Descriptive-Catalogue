Excerpts

Beginning

śrigaṇeśāya namaḥ || śrīsarasvatyai namaḥ || śrīgurubhyo namaḥ ||

lakṣmīkāntaṃ namaskṛtya yathāmati viracyate |

vāśiṣṭhasāravivṛttiḥ (!) paropakṛtaye mayā || 1 ||

vaṣiṣṭhena yatīndreṇopadiṣṭād rāghavaṃ prati ||

sāram uddhṛtavān kaś cit brahmavidyāmahārṇṇavāt || 2 ||

tatrādau vaśiṣṭha(!)sārākhyaṃ grantham ārikṣus (!) tatpratipādyeṣṭadevatānatirūpaṃ maṅgalam ācarati || dikkāleti || evaṃvidhāya śāntāya guṇātītāya tejase brahmasvarūpāya namaḥ || tejaḥśabdenātra brahmaiva na bhautikaṃ tejaḥ tasya vakṣyamāṇalakṣaṇānupapatteḥ || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

viśvam avidyamānam api brahmaṇi sthitaṃ tena viśvena hīnaṃ tatpadaṃ brahmapadaṃ nāsti || 32 || yatheti || eka evārthaḥ || 33 || saumyeti || saumye sthite jale yathā vīcir asti nāsti ca tathedaṃ jagad brahmaṇi asti nāsti ca | ajñānadaśāyām astijñānadaśāyāṃ nāstīty arthaḥ tenedaṃ brahma śūnyāśūnyaṃ padaṃ gataṃ jagatā hīnaṃ sahitaṃ cety arthaḥ || vyāvahārikatātvikadaśā || bhyām (!) ity arthaḥ || 34 || (fol. 27r3, 9–10)

Colophon

iti śrīlaghuyogavāśiṣṭhasāre nirvāṇaprakaraṇaṃ ṭīkāyāṃ daśamaṃ || || || || yoºº sāºº || iti śrīyogavāsiṣṭhasāravivaraṇe mahīdharakṛte śūnyāśūnyaṃ nāma ṭīkāyāṃ daśamaṃ prakaraṇaṃ || (fol. 27r10–11)
