On the exposure 27 is written:

mṛdāharaṇasaṃghaṭṭapratiṣṭhāhvānam eva ca ||
snapanaṃ pūjanaṃ caiva visarjanam ataḥ paraṃ ||
haro māheśvaraś caiva śūlapāṇi (!) pinākadhṛk ||
paśupatiḥ śivaś caiva mahādeva iti smṛtaḥ ||
nāmakrameṇāpūrvokte karmāṇi kāryāṇītyarthah ||    ||

āraṃbhalakṣaṇāṃ ||

āraṃbho varaṇaṃ yajñe saṃkalpo †atasatrayoḥ†||
