Excerpts

Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

pracaṃḍasūryaḥ spṛhaṇīyacandramāḥ
sadavagāḍhakṣatavārisaṃcayaḥ ||
dināṃtaramyo ʼbhyupaśāṃtamanmatho
nidāghakālaḥ samupāgataḥ priye || 1 ||

niśāḥ śaśāṃkakṣatanīlarājayaḥ
kvacidvicitraṃ jalayaṃtri(!)maṃdiram ||
maṇiḥprakārāḥ sarasaṃ ca caṃdanaṃ
śucau priye yāti janasya sevyatām || 2 || (fol. 1v1–3)

End

malayapavanaviddhaḥ kokilārāvaramyo
madhukarasurabhisaṃgāl labdhagaṃdhaprabandhaḥ
vividhakarabhabhūṣaiś ceṣṭamānasamantād
bhavatu tava vasantaḥ kāla eṣopi vṛddhau || 33 ||

vasukusumasuramyaḥ kāmināṃ kāmyabhūtaḥ
pathikajanavadhūnāṃ cittasaṃtāpahetuḥ |
madhukarapi kanādair jātavādaprapaṃcaḥ
pradiśatu sukham āśu prāyasas te vasantaḥ ||    || (fols. 14r8–v3)

Colophon

iti śrīkālidasakṛtau ṛtusaṃhāre vasantavarṇano nāma ṣaṣṭaḥ sarggaḥ || saṃvat 1716 śāke 1581 [[āṣāḍhe masi kṛṣṇapakṣe kuru]] liṣitam idaṃ rāmakṛṣṇena caturvedaḥ | || śubham astu || śubham astu || gurave namaḥ || śrīrudramate namaḥ govindāya namaḥ kṛṣṇāya namaḥ śrībhavānyai namaḥ 151 parimāṇam śubham bhūyāt (fol. 14v3–6)
