Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṃ namaḥ siddhikamalāyai ||

devy uvāca ||

bhagavan bhāṣitā sarvaṃ guhyaṃ paramadurllabhaṃ |

idanī(!) śrotum icchāmi llabho(!) nāmasahasrakaṃ || 1 ||

siddhilakṣmyā mahādevyā sarvvadeveṣu durllabhaṃ |

prasīda kathyatāṃ tatvaṃ devadevo jagatpate || 2 ||

śrīsadāśiva uvāca ||

siddhilakṣmyā mahādevyāḥ śṛṇu nāmasahasrakaṃ |

yena smaraṇamātreṇa śivaṃ sākṣād bhaven naraḥ || 3 ||

nātaḥ parataraṃ kiṃcit stotrarakṣākaraṃ bhavet ||

kalau mahāvīryyavantaṃ sadya pratyayakārakaṃ || 4 ||

nyaset prathamato devi tato dhyāyan mananyadhī(!) |

iti dhyātvā japen mantraṃ paścāt stotraṃ paṭhen naraḥ || 5 || ||

oṃ asya śrīmahālakṣmyā sahasranāmastotrasya viśvāmitraṛṣir anuṣṭupchandaḥ śrīsiddhilakṣmī devatā klīṃ bījaṃ hrīṃ śaktiḥ hruḥ kīlakaṃ mama puruṣārthacatuṣṭayasiddhaye paṭhe(!) viniyogaḥ || || (fol. 1v1–5)

End

śaṭhāya bhaktihīnāya nindakāya varānane ||

saubhāgyaṃ bhartṛihārddaṃ ca rūpaṃ ca jauvanan(!) tathā || 158 ||

putrān api varārohe yoṣid āpnoty asaṃśayaḥ |

imaṃ stotraṃ maheśāni gopanīyaṃ prayatnataḥ || 159 ||

śiśyāya bhaktiyuktaya dātavyaṃ stavam uttamaṃ |

yadi dadyād abhaktāya śaṭhāya ca sureśvari || 160 ||

tadā mṛtyum avāpnoti satyaṃ satyaṃ vadāmy ahaṃ || .. .. .. .. || || ❁ || || (fol. 8r2–4)

Colophon

iti śrīrudrayāmale tantre śrīdevīśvarasaṃvāde śrī śrī śrī siddhilakṣmyāḥ sahasranāmastotraṃ samāptaṃ || || śrī 3 siddhilakṣmīdevī jayati || śubham astu || || samvat 812 kārttikaśuklatrayodaśī revatī nakṣatre vajrayoge śanaiścaravāra thvakūhnū rājadaivajñavṛṣadhvajena saṃpūrṇṇayāṅā juro || śrī 3 parameśvarī prītir astu || (fol. 8r5–6)
