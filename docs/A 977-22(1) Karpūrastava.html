Beginning

śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

athāsya puraścaraṇaṃ || ||

karpūrākhyam idaṃ stotrai yad uktaṃ bhavatā purā ||

ṣaṭkarmmasādhanaṃ tasya prayogaṃ vada śaṃkara || 1 ||

śrīśiva uvāca || ||
