Excerpts

Beginning

śrīśaṃ vaṃde ||


namaste śaraṇye śive sānukaṃpe

namaste jagadvyāpike viśvarūpe ||

namaste jagadvaṃdya pādāraviṃde

namaste jagattāriṇi trāhi durge || 1 ||


namaste jagacciṃtyamānasvarūpe

namste mahāyoginījñānarūpe ||

namaste namaste sadānaṃdarūpe

namasta jagattārini trāhi durge || 2 || (fol. 1r1–5)


End

idaṃ stotraṃ mayā proktam āpaduddhārahetukaṃ ||

trisaṃdhyam ekasaṃdhyaṃ vā paṭhanād eva saṃ[[ka]]ṭāt || 10 ||


mucyate nātra saṃdeho bhuvi svage(!) rasātale ||

samaślokam ekaṃ vā yaḥ paṭḥed bhaktitaḥ sadā || 11 ||


prasasarvaduḥ(!) kṛtaṃ tyaktvā prāpnoti paramāṃ padaṃ || 12 ||


paṭhanād eva deveśī(!) kin na sidhyati bhūtale ||

stavarājam imaṃ devī(!) saṃkṣepāt kathitaṃ tava || 13 || (fol.2r4–2v3)


Colophon

iti siddheśvarataṃtre āpaduddhārakalpe svatarājaṃ saṃpūrṇa[ṃ] || (fol. 2v3–4)
