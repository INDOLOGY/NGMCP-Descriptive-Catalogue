Excerpts

Beginning

śrīdevy uvāca ||

śrutaṃ mayā maheśāna mantratantrādivistaraṃ |

tvatprasādāt sureśāna sarvvaṃ tad avadhāritaṃ ||

idānīṃ śrotum icchāmi trailokyavijayāṃ śubhāṃ ||

yathā jānāmi vīreśa nissaṃdigdhaṃ yathā bhavet ||

sādhakānāṃ hitārthāya samāsena vada prabho |

sarvasaddhipradā devī surāsuranamaskṛtā || ○ ||

śrībhairava uvāca||

caṇḍāṃ caṇḍoṭṭahāsāṃ ghanarasitaravāṃ daṃṣṭriṇīṃ bhīmavaktrāṃ,

nirmāṃsāṃ raktanetrāṃ bhṛkuṭitabhrukuṭīṃ raktamedāvaliptāṃ |

nirmāṃsāghoghaghaṇṭāṃ raṇaruṇitatarāṃ nīlajīmūtavarṇṇāṃ,

hūṃhūṃhūṃkāranādāṃ tribhuvanavijayāṃ krodharūpāṃ namāmi || ○ ||

(fol. 1v1–5)

End

vīryhabhāvavilayo dayeśvarī|

ṣaṭkam etad upavarṇṇitaṃ mayā

kālisaṃjñakam alaṃ sureśvari ||

viṃśatitridaśabhāgacitritaṃ

bhūrikalpaśatavasturājitaṃ |

śatārdhayuktam akṣaraṃ sureśvarī

na kathanīyam idaṃ yatra kutra cit || ○ || (fol. Last1–2)

Colophon

bhairavaśrotasi vidyāpīṭhe śiracchede jayadrathayāmale mahātantre caturviṃśatisāhasre dvitīyaṣaṭke siddhalakṣmīvidhānaṃ ṣaṭtriṃśatimapaṭalaḥ || ○ ||

saṃ 309 pauṣaśu 5 || ○ || maṃgalamahāśrī || ○ || (fol. Last2–4)
