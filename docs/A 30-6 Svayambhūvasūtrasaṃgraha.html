Manuscript Features

The writing of this MS is very neatly done and of old appearance (approx. 12th ct.).

The following fols. are available: 4–5; 10; 17–19; 21; 25–41; 43–53.

Some fols. are darkened.

Exposures 2–3 show a wooden cover leaf.
