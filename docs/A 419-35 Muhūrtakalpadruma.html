|| śrīgaṇeśāya namaḥ ||

anāthabaṃdhuṃ gaṇanātham aṃtaś

citte niveśya (2) kṣapitāṃtarāyam ||

prācīnavākyāni vi[[cā]]rya samyaṅ

muhūrtakalpadru(3)mam ārabhe haṃ || 1 ||

śrīvāṇikalyāṇaparaṃparābhiḥ

saṃpūrayainaṃ mama vā(4)ṅ niguṃphaṃ || (!)

muhūrttakalpadrumanāmadheyaṃ

vijñāpane yaṃ tavapādapadme || (5)2 ||

śākaḥ sārkaḥ12 ṣaṣṭihṛn mānavobdaḥ

so ṣaṃkhyāto dakṣine (!) narma(6)dāyāḥ ||

so yaṃ naṃdair9 anvite jivasaṃjño

ny amin deśe vatsaraḥ saṃpra(7)diṣṭaḥ || 3 || (fol. 1v1–7)

tasūnunāvidṛla(!) dikṣitena muhurtakalpadruma eṣacakre ||

kalpadrumeścetkirannamālā ciṃtāmaṇikarkaramevamanye ||

padekadeśaḥ kilamaṃjariyaṃ sāraṃ vicārakrutatvavedin || 2 ||

nānā phalādhyaiḥ kusumaiḥ sametaḥ kalpadrumaḥ kalpita vastudāyī ||

ākalya mākalya vi --- ṣigehestanityaṃ vivudhādhipānāṃ || (fol. 87r1–6)
