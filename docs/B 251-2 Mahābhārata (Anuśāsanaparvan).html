Excerpts

Beginning

❖ oṁ namo bhagavate vāsudevāya ||


nārāyaṇaṃ namaskṛtya, narañ caiva narottamaṃ |


devīṃ sarasvatīṃ vyāsaṃ, tato jayam udīrayet || ||


yudhiṣṭhira uvāca ||


śamo bahuvidhākāraḥ sūkṣma uktaḥ pitāmaha |


na ca me hṛdaye śāntir asti kṛdvedam īdṛśaṃ ||


asminn arthe buhuvidhā, śāntir uktā pitāmaha |


sukṛte ko ʼnuśāntiḥ syāc, chamād bahuvidhād api ||


śarārccitaśarīraṃ hi, tīvravraṇam udīkṣya ca ||


śarmma ṇo(!)palabhe vīra, duḥkṛtāny eva cintayan |


rudhireṇāvasiktāṅgaṃ, śra(!)vantaṃ ca yathācalaṃ || (fol. 1v1–3)


End

na śaktā vinihantuṃ hi, raṇe taṃ sarvvadevatāḥ |


tasmān mā tvaṃ, saricchreṣṭhe, śocasva kurunandanaṃ ||


vasūn eṣa gato devi, putras te vilavo bhava ||


ity uktā sā tu kṛṣṇena, vyāsena ca saridvarā ||


tyaktvā śokaṃ mahārāja, svaṃ vāryy avatatāra ha ||


satkṛtya tāñ ca saritaṃ, tataḥ kṛṣṇamukho nṛpa ||


anujñātās tayā sarvve, vyavarttanta janādhipāḥ || || || || (fol. 261v1–3)


Colophon

iti śrīmahābhārate śatasāhasryāṃ saṃhitāyāṃ vaiyāśikyām ābhyuttamānu[śā]sane parvvaṇi dānadharmmaḥ samāptaś cāyaṃ ānusāsanikaṃ parvva || || (fol. 261v4–5)
